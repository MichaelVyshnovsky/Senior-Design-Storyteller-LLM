Aelarion Shadowstride

Character Profile

Character Name: Aelarion Shadowstride

Background

Aelarion Shadowstride was born into a world of shadows and magic in the misty High Rockies. Raised by a secretive group of rogue wizards known as the Silent Blades, he learned the art of stealth, deception, and void manipulation at an early age. His father, once a promising wizard who perished under mysterious circumstances, instilled within him a deep-seated distrust of those who sought to control or manipulate others. This mindset would later serve him well as he navigated the treacherous paths of both combat and intrigue.

Aelarion's journey has been marked by a blend of skills that allowed him to thrive in a world teetering on chaos. However, at the core of his being lies an internal conflict between his desire to protect those he loves and the necessity to survive as a hunted outsider. This internal struggle makes him both a formidable opponent and an unlikely ally.

Abilities

  • Arcane Trickster
  • Mastery of Misty Moon Lore spells (1st level)
  • Silence (1st level spell save)
  • Sound of Steel (1st level spell save)
  • Void Manipulation Trickery (trick ability)
  • Stealthy Trickster's Silhouette
  • Cunning Trickster's Deception
  • Trickster's Lure
  • Silent Blades Guild Member
  • Hide (1st level spell save)
  • Spellcraft

Relationships

Ally: Seraphina the Enchanter - A kind-hearted and wise mage who supports Aelarion in his quest for vengeance against corrupt nobles.

Rival: Lord Varick the Betrayer - A greedy noble who has crossed paths with Aelarion, seeking to eliminate him from power.

Personality

Aelarion is a man of sharp intellect and fiery temper. He is driven by a deep-seated hatred for corruption and those who perpetuate it. Despite his rough exterior, he possesses a clever mind and a moral compass that occasionally guides him. His motivation lies in balancing vengeance with justice, making him an unpredictable ally in both conflict and diplomacy.

Appearance

Aelarion Shadowstride stands at approximately 6'0", with piercing violet eyes that reflect the blood moon that cursed his lineage. His tall frame is adorned with scars that tell stories of countless battles, while his attire consists of dark robes stained with blood, symbolizing his violent past.

Faction Role

Aelarion Shadowstride is a member of The Silent Blades Guild, known for their stealth and mastery of shadow manipulation. As a trickster, he wields the power of the void in service of his allies, making him both a formidable opponent and an enigmatic ally.
