'''Aloe vera plants''' were tropical curative [[herbs]] found in the [[Malatra]]n jungles.{{Cite organized play/LJ/Shadows of Sin/Certificates}}

==Description==
Juice of aloe vera plants could be easily extracted from the leaves and used as a topical [[ointment]]. The juice protected the user's skin from irritation. This plant also allowed creatures like [[lizardfolk]] to keep their skin wet longer without daily baths.

==History==
In the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], aloe vera plant were known to be gifted to goodly travelers by the lesser [[Nature spirit (Kara-Tur)|nature spirit]]s of Malatran [[vanilla]] grove [[Timon]] and [[Pumba]].
