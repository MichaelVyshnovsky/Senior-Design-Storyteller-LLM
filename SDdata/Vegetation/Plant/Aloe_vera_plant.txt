Aloe vera plants were tropical curative herbs found in the Malatran jungles.
Juice of aloe vera plants could be easily extracted from the leaves and used as a topical ointment. The juice protected the users skin from irritation. This plant also allowed creatures like lizardfolk to keep their skin wet longer without daily baths.
In the 14 century DR, aloe vera plant were known to be gifted to goodly travelers by the lesser nature spirits of Malatran vanilla grove Timon and Pumba.
