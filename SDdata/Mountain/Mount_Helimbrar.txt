'''Mount Helimbrar''' was the southernmost peak of the [[Sword Mountains]].{{Template:Cite web enhancement/Environs of Waterdeep|14}}{{Template:Cite dragon/128/Welcome to Waterdeep|11}}

==Geography==
The mountain was located in the [[Sword Coast North]], just north of great metropolis [[Waterdeep]]. Along with [[Mount Sar]], it protected the city from the frigid winds of [[the North]].{{Template:Cite web enhancement/Environs of Waterdeep|3}}

==History==
Both Mount Helimbrar and Mount Sar were named after two great [[fomorian]] giants that lived atop the peaks before their deaths.

==Notable Locations==
The ancient wizard of the [[Shadow Thieves]] created a subterranean stronghold somewhere near the base of the mountain.
