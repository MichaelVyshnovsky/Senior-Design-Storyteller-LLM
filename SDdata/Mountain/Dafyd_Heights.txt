'''Dafyd Heights''' was a small mountain range located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Dafyd Heights was found enveloped by the [[Llyrath Forest]] in southern Gwynneth. The mountains ran east to west through the forest parallel to the nearby coast of the [[Sea of Swords]] for {{SI|30|mi|k|long=yes}}. A small unnamed river flowed south from the mountains to empty into the sea.
