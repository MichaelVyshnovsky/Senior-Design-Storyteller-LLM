'''The Highlands''' were a mountain range located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Highlands were a prominent feature in central Gwynneth, stretching for over {{SI|75|mi|k|long=yes}} and forming most of the southern border of [[Myrloch Vale]]. The Highlands were separated from [[Aspenheight]] in the west by the [[Aspenheight Gap]], but the Highlands ran into the [[Cambro Mountains]] in the east at [[Synnoria]].
