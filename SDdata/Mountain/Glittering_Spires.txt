The Glittering Spires was a small mountain range found in the northern reaches of the Hordelands.
thumb|A map showing the Glittering Spires within the Hordelands.
The Glittering Spires bordered Yal Tengri, shielding much of the Plain of Horses to the east from the frigid breeze of the sea. The mountain range was enclosed by the Winterflow River to the south and terminated near the mouth of the Arundi River in the north.
