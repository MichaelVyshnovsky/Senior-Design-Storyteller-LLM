'''Al-Sayaj Mountains''', also called the '''Mountains of Desolation''', were an important geographical feature of the [[Cities of the Pantheon]] in [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Geography==
Al-Sayaj Mountains rose along the southeastern region of the Cities of the Pantheon, creating a natural border with the [[Cities of the Ancients]].

The mountains, and surrounding foothills, were the source of several vital rivers of the region including [[Al-Naqus River|Al-Naqus]], [[Al-Hadi River|Al-Hadi]], [[River Abdo|Abdo]] and [[Al-Jama River|Al-Jama]].
