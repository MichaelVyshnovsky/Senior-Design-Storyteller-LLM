'''High Horn''' was a mountain 1000 feet high (310 m) situated in the [[Storm Horns]] mountain range, north-west of [[Cormyr]]. It was the site of the stronghold also named [[High Horn (hold)|High Horn]] and the [[High Horn Pass]] leading to the west and providing one of only two easy crossings through the mountain range.
