Shan Skar was a prominent peak of Katakoro Shan found in Khazari.
The peak sat high in the northwestern corner of the country near Manass.
Given the narrow breadth of Katakoro Shan in that region, Shan Skars altitude of  gave it an impressive view of Khazari as well as the Quoya Desert.
