The '''Orcskull Mountains'''<ref group="note">An alternate spelling or typo is shown as "Orcskill Mountains" in Dungeon Magazine #196. were a mountain range located on the island of [[Moray]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The mountains covered the southern tip of Moray. They were bordered on the east by the [[Strait of the Leviathan]], and by the [[Trackless Sea]] to the south and west. The [[Shannyth River]] flowed north from the center of the Orcskull Mountains into central Moray.

==Inhabitants==
The mountains were ruled by various tribes of [[orc]]s, [[ogre]]s, [[giant]]s, and other monstrous [[humanoid]]s.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|8}}
