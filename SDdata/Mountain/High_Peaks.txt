The '''High Peaks''' were the western spur of the [[Cloven Mountains]].

==Geography==
With the [[Deepwash]] to the north and the [[Thornwood]] to the south, the High Peaks were located in the middle of the barony of [[Ahlarkhem]] in [[Erlkazar]]. The tallest peak was around 10,000 feet (3,048 meters) above sea level.
