The '''Qadim Hills''' were a grouping of hills in the [[Troll Mountains (Amn)|Troll Mountains]] of [[Amn]].

==Geography==
These were the western hills of the Troll Mountains, beginning north of [[Trollford]] and running until the [[Greenfields (Western Heartlands)|Greenfields]]. and some of the waters composing the [[Esmelflow]] began atop them.

==Inhabitants==
Goblins used to inhabit these hills, and they warred against the [[troll]]s of the mountains.
