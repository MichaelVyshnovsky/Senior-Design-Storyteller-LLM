The '''Tuutsaas Chain''' was a mountain range located in the [[Alpuk]] region within the [[Great Glacier]].

==Geography==
The Tuutsaas Chain was the western border of Alpuk, diving the Great Glacier and the [[Tortured Lands]].

==Flora & Fauna==
[[Remorhaz]] have created an extensive network of tunnels below the mountain range. The southern reaches were home to [[white dragon]]s. A tribe of [[Yeti|gargantua yeti]] lived amond the most eastermost peaks.
