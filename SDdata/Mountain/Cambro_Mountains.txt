The '''Cambro Mountains''' were a mountain range located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Cambro Mountains ran north to south in northern Gwynneth, forming much of the western border of [[Myrloch Vale]]. They met [[the Highlands]] at [[Synnoria]] near the southeast arm of the [[Myrloch]]. The Cambro Mountains were approximately {{SI|75|mi|k|long=yes}} long. They were split in the north by the [[Cambrent Gap]].

==History==
The [[dwarven]] settlement of [[Highhome]] was founded within the mountains in {{Yearlink|-7000}}.{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|23-31}}
