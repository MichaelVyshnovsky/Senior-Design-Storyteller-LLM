The Cambro Mountains were a mountain range located on the island of Gwynneth within the Moonshae Isles.
The Cambro Mountains ran north to south in northern Gwynneth, forming much of the western border of Myrloch Vale. They met the Highlands at Synnoria near the southeast arm of the Myrloch. The Cambro Mountains were approximately  long. They were split in the north by the Cambrent Gap.
