The '''Adder Peaks''' was a mountain range found within [[Chessenta]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dlc}} 

==Geography==
The Adder Peaks were located along the southern border of Chessenta along the northeastern edge of the [[Chondalwood]]. Both the [[Winding River]] and [[Adder River]] originated in the Adder Peaks.
