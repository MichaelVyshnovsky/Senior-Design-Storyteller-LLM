Mount Thorog was one of the two tallest mountains east from the Moon Pass.
The mountain peak was named after a deceased orc chieftain. 
Thorog was located between Stone Stand and the stronghold of Hellgate Keep.  Guarded by perytons, Telkouns Tower was a fortress located between Mount Ieldroun and Mount Thorog.
