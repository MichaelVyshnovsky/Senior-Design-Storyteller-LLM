'''Kossuth's Ire''' was a long-dormant volcano located in the [[White Peaks]] mountain range in the [[Moonsea North]].{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|62}}

==Geography==
[[File:MoonseaNorth Dungeon170 lowres.png|thumb|''A 15{{th}} century map showing the location of Kossuth's Ire.'']]
The volcano was situated along with other peaks in the eastern end of the White Peaks, due north of the village of [[Verdigris]] in the [[Dragonspine Mountains]].{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|55}}

===Geographical Features===
After its most recent eruption, the area around Kossuth's Ire became a barren landscape that cracked open from overwhelming subterranean pressure.

==History==
After many years of inactivity, Kossuth's Ire erupted in the {{YearlinkName|1480}}, due to a "planar fissure" connected to the plane of [[Elemental Chaos]].{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|50}}{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|64}} Smoke, lava, and ash arose from its craterous opening, while its underground tunnels filled with magma.

==Notable Locations==
* [[Forharn]], a city of [[gnome]]s built within the darkest depths of the volcano.{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|79}}
