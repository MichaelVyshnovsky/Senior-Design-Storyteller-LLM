The '''Fang''' was a large peak near the southeastern coast of [[Melabrauth]] on [[Laerakond]].

This tooth-shaped peak was used by sailors for navigation in the [[Dragon Sea (Abeir)|Dragon Sea]]. However, the mountain was infested by monsters.
