The '''Vanishing Hills''' were a grouping of hills located along the northern coast of the [[Moonsea]] region of [[North Faerûn]].{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|56}}

==Geography==
They were located in the area of the Moonsea referred to some as the [[Moonsea North]].{{Cite adventurers league/Outlaws of the Iron Route|33}}

===Geographical Features===
The hills were the source of the [[Trank River]], which ran south and opened up into the [[Twilight Marsh]] and the actual [[Moonsea (lake)|Moonsea]].
