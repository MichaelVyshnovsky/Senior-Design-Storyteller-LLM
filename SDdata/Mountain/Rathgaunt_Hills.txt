The '''Rathgaunt Hills''' was a rocky outcropping located along the southern edge of the [[Shaar]].<ref name=FRA-9-10">

==Geography==
The Rathgaunt Hills were located just east of [[Lake Lhespen]] and south of the [[River Shaar]]. The [[Trader's Way]] passed through the hills near their midpoint near the town of [[Sebben]]. The hills ran west to east for approximately {{SI|200|mi|k|long=yes}} before levelling out near the [[Landrise]].<ref name=FRA-9-10"/>

==History==
The [[mage]] [[Phelpar]] had a hidden cache of [[magic item]]s stored in a cave within the Rathgaunt Hills.
