'''Kossuth's Eyes''' were a pair of dormant volcanoes in the [[Small Teeth]] mountains.

==Description==
These two volcanoes were located on the far eastern end of the mountain range.

==History==
The volcanoes were last active during the founding of the [[Dwarf|dwarven]] kingdom of [[Shanatar]].
