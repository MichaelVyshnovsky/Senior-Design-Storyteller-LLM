The '''White Peaks''' were a range of mountains located in the [[Moonsea North]] region of [[North Faerûn]].{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|56}}

==Geography==
The mountains divided the barren, desolate [[Tortured Land]]s in the north, from the grassy steppes of [[the Ride]] to the south.

===Geographical Features===
The most notable mountain of the White Peaks range were [[Whitehorn mountain|Whitehorn]], the peak that lent its name to a local [[Whitehorn|mining town]] at its base,{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|62}}

Two notable streams flowed down from the southern slopes of the mountains, the [[Amaratha Stream]], and the [[Crescentflow]] that snaked into the [[Border Forest]].

==Trade==
''Circa'' the mid–14{{th}} century, [[Melvaunt]] sponsored the constructions of mines in White Peaks.<ref group="note">While the source does not refer to "White Peaks" explicitly, it refers tot he mountains north of [[the Ride]].

==History==
In the {{yearlinkName|1480}}, the planar fissure to the [[Elemental Chaos]] opened up, casing Kossuth's Ire to erupt after years of no activity.{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|50}}

==Notable Locations==
* [[Forharn]], a hidden city of [[gnome]]s built beneath the seemingly-dormant volcano.
* [[Myth Ondath]], the former City of Peace that lay in ruin centuries after its founding.
* [[Whitehorn]], a small mining town that also fell to ruin.{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|49}}
