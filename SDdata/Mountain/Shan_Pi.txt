Shan Pi was a large peak of Katakoro Shan found in Ra-Khati.
Shan Pi was found in the southeast corner of Ra-Khati. It stood on the perimeter of Katakoro Shan southwest of the Katakoro Glacier.
At just shy of , Shan Pi had an impressive view of the Old Caravan Road as it passed by the Growing Mountains and the Katakoro Plateau.
