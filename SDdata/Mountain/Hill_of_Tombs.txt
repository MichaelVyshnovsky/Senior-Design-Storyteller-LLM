The '''Hill of Tombs''' was the southernmost point in the [[Earthfast Mountains|Earthfast Mountain]] range, near the coast of the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]]. [[Fire River]] originated in these mountains and flowed west through the [[Vast]] into the [[Dragon Reach]].<ref>
