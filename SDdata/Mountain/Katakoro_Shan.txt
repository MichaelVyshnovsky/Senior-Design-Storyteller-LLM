Katakoro Shan was an enormous mountain range located in the Hordelands. It was part of the larger Yehimal mountains.
Katakoro Shan formed part of the borders of the nations of Khazari and Ra-Khati, and also provided a formidable boundary between them and the Katakoro Plateau. Stretching along this mountain range was the Jumpa River Gorge.
