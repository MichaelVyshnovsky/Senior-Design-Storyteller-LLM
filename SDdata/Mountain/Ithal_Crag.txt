The '''Ithal Crag''' was a large hill in the [[Darromar/Royal Quarter|Royal Quarter]] of [[Darromar]] where the queen's royal palace of [[Faerntarn]] was built.<ref name=LoI1:T-p85/>

==Geography==
The Ithal Crag was a rocky headland overlooking the [[River Ith]] to the south.<ref name=LoI1:T-p85/>

==Sites==
The lower portion of the hill was surrounded by the [[Garden of Rhinda]].<ref name=LoI1:T-p85/> A winding path from led up the hill and through the garden to the buildings at the top.<ref name=LoI1:T-p83>

On the top of the hill rested the two main buildings of the palace complex, the main keep and the garrison building, along with other buildings, all surrounded by a [[granite]] wall.<ref name=LoI1:T-p83/> One of the city's four Watchtowers sat on the hill as well.<ref name=LoI1:T-p85/>

==History==
In {{Yearlink|-221}}, sixteen-year-old [[Darrom Ithal]]<ref name=LoI1:T-p25> defeated the [[Calishite]]s at this location, bringing freedom to [[Darromar|Ithmong]]&mdash;and ultimately [[Tethyr]]&mdash;again. The hill was named after him.<ref name=LoI1:T-p85/>

The first royal palace was built on the site in [[843 DR]], after the coronation of Queen [[Alisande]], and the palace of Faerntarn made use of the same structure.<ref name=LoI1:T-p83/>
