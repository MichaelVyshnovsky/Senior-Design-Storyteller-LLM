Mount Hlim was the southernmost of the Greypeak Mountains in the Savage Frontier.
Just west of the mountain was Highstar Lake, and the nearby underground dwarfhold of Hammer Hall.
* Xonthals Tower, a village built at the base of the mountain built around the tower after which it was named. Throughout its history the settlement has been taken over by both the Zhentarim, and many years later the Cult of the Dragon.
