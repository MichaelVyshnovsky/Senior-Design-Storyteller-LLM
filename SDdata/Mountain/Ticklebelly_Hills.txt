The '''Ticklebelly Hills''' was a grouping of rocky hills and cliffs found in the [[Moonsea North]] region of [[North Faer√ªn]].

==Description==
The Ticklebelly Hills comprised rocky mounds, large crags and sparse vegetation.{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|12}} The landscape was relatively difficult to traverse, at least for [[humanoid]] travelers.{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|11}}

==Geography==
The hills were situated along the southern slopes of the eastern [[Dragonspine Mountains]], north of the [[Grass Sea]] and the [[Giant's Cairn]].{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|56}}{{Cite dungeon/177/Realmslore: Vaasa|81}}

===Flora & Fauna===
[[Bulette]]s were known to dig their tunnels beneath the earth of the Ticklebelly Hills.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1489}}, a [[shooting star]] fell from the skies into the hills, causing unease among the local wildlife and forming a [[Kranun's Crater|massive crater]].{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|5}}

==Notable Locations==
* [[Kranun's Crater]], home to the exiled [[stone giant]] [[Kranun]] and the [[pool of radiance]] that formed within.{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|9}}

==Inhabitants==
As of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], [[orc]]s of the [[Burning Banner]] tribe were observed to be active throughout the hills.{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|8}}

Stone giants and their [[saber-toothed tiger]] pets were known to wander the hills and the surrounding lands.

===Notable Inhabitants===
* [[Coldborn]], a [[dragonborn]] [[druid]] associated with the [[Emerald Enclave]].
