'''Noko-Ji Peak''' was a mountain in the [[Po Yul Dzayul Range]] in [[Tabot]]. It stood above the town of [[Ji]] and its name meant "the blow high over Ji".

==Notable Inhabitants==
It was the home of [[Morka Fooztang]], a hermit and oracle, around {{YearlinkConvert|2607|SY}}.
