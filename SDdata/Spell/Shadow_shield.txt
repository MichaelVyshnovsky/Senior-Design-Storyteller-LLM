'''''Shadow shield''''' was a powerful [[spell]] used by [[sorcerer]]s and [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell created a temporary cloak of shadow for the caster. The cloak protected the caster from damage, [[necromancy|necromantic]] spells, negative energy and death-based magic.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
