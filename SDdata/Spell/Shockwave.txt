Shockwave was an utterance from the Lexicon of the Perfected Map that used truespeak to induce the air to push all targets to their feet.
Through the shockwave utterance, all those within a  radius of a given point were pushed off-balance, likely into the ground, by the air. The effects lasted a few seconds.
The recitation was known to word archons and loquasphinxes.
