'''''Mass blindness or deafness''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[sorcerer]]s and [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell affected an area with a {{SI|10|ft}} radius. All creatures within this area were rendered blind and [[deaf]] until the spell expired.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
