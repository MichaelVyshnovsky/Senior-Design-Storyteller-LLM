'''''Know greatest enemy''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[blackguard]]s and [[paladin]]s.

==Effects==
This spell allowed a caster to ascertain the power of each creature within {{SI|25|ft}}. This spell was vulnerable to spells that prevented [[scrying]].

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[divine focus]].
