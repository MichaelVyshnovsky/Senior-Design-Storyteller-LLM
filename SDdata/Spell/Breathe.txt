'''''Breathe''''' was an [[alteration]] spell of the [[southern magic]] tradition{{Southernmagic}}. 
==Effects==
When cast, the spell allowed the caster or the target creature to breathe normally in a location or substance it would normally suffocate. 
 
==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required a material component. A cloth of any material was held over the caster's mouth while reciting the verbal component of the spell.
