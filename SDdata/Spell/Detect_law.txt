'''''Detect law''''' was a [[divination]] spell that provided information about objects or creatures with a [[Alignment|lawful]] aura.

==Effects==
This spell functioned exactly like ''[[detect evil]]'' except it detected lawful auras from [[paladin]]s, lawful clerics, spells, magic items, etc.
