Fanatic retaliation was a unique evocation spell from the third circle of magic used in the late 15 century DR.
The fanatic retaliation spell was cast as a reaction, mere moments after the casters ally was slain. The spell always targeted the creature that landed the killing blow by launching a powerful psionic barrage that had a chance of rendering the target silenced for twelve seconds. Creatures of formidable intelligence had a chance of reduce the damage by half and shrug the magical silence off. This magical rebuke had the range of . The effects of fanatic retaliation could not be strengthened by a casting at a higher proficiency level.
This unique sell was known and was used by the drider Karniss, a worshiper of the Absolute in the . He would unleash the deadly spell when one of his spindleweb fanatics was slain.
