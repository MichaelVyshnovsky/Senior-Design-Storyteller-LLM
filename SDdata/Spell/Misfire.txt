Misfire was a divine spell used by priests devoted to Beshaba.
The victim of this spell was unable to hit any selected primary target of any ranged or missile attack. This spell lasted for several days unless it was nullified by a dispel magic or remove curse spell.
This spell required only somatic components to cast.
