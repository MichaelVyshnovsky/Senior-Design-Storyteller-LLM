'''Animal summoning I''' was a [[conjuration]] [[spell]].

==Effects==
When cast, the spell allowed the caster three chances to name a type of animal that might be found in the vicinity and summon up to eight of them to aid the caster.
==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[material component]].
