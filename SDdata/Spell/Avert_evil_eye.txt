Avert evil eye was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate. This popular spell was only usable by wizards.
This spell protected individuals from the evil eye. The caster would cast the spell on a blue bead while saying the name of the recipient. The bead was then sewn into the recipients clothing or crushed into a drink that they consumed. This spell lasted for several days depending on the casters power.
This spell was reversible and could be cast as attract evil eye.
This spell required a material component in the form of a blue bead to cast.
