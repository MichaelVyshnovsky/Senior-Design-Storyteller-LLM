'''''Animal shapes''''' was a [[transmutation]] spell that caused willing creatures to ''[[polymorph]]'' into a chosen animal. Besides [[druid]]s, the spell could be cast by some [[cleric]]s of any of the many deities of the Realms with [[Animal domain|influence over animals]].
