'''''Undead servant''''' was a [[necromancy]] [[spell]] that allowed the caster to temporarily reanimate the remains of a [[humanoid]] creature.

==Effects==
Upon casting this spell, a deceased person or creature was brought back as a [[skeleton]] or [[zombie]]. It affected the remains of any [[human]]-sized creature or smaller, as long as it was humanoid in form. The [[undead]] creature obeyed simple commands given by the caster for the duration of an hour or more, or until it was destroyed or ''[[dispel magic|dispelled]]''.

==Casting==
This spell required [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, as well as the [[material component]] of a pinch of dirt from a graveyard.

==History==
This spell was known to be used by wizards of the [[Cult of the Dragon]].
