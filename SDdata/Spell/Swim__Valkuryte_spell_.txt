'''''Swim''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[enchantment]] [[spell]] used by specialty priests devoted to [[Valkur]], allowing untrained swimmers to swim with ease for several hours.

==Effects==
If used on a recipient who did not know how to swim, this spell would grant them the ability to swim. If cast on a proficient swimmer, this spell would allow them to swim with ease. The number of recipients affected by this spell was determined by the caster's skill.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal component|verbal component]]s and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s a small pumice stone for each recipient to cast.
