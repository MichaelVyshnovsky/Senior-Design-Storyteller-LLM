'''''Paralyzation''''' was an [[Arcane magic|arcane]] [[illusion]] [[spell]] that created the illusion of muscle slowness in targets.

==Effects==
This spell would not function if used upon mindless creatures. The spell affected the weakest targets first within a {{SI|20|ft}} cube, becoming paralyzed for an hour if they were unable to resist the spell.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s to cast.

==History==
This spell was found within ''[[Selvar's Ineffable Conjurations, Magicks, and Phantasms]].
