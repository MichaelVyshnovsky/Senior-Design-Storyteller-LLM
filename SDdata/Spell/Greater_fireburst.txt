'''''Greater fireburst''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[sorcerer]]s and [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell created an explosive blast of flame. Any creature or object within {{SI|10|ft}} of the caster was vulnerable to the blast.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[material component]], a pinch of [[sulfur]]
