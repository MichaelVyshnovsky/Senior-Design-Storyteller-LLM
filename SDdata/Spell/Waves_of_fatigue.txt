'''''Waves of fatigue''''' was a [[necromancy]] [[spell]] that instilled fatigue in the targets.

==Effects==
On casting ''waves of fatigue'', the caster generated waves of [[negative energy]] within a 30 ft (9 m) cone. This negative energy instilled fatigue in everyone who was not so to begin with.

==Components==
''Waves of fatigue'' required only somatic and verbal components.
