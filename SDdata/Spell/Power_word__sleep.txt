'''''Power word, sleep''''' was a [[conjuration]] [[spell]] that put a creature or person into a light sleep.

==Effects==
This spell functioned in the exact manner as the [[enchantment]] spell ''[[sleep]]'', except that it only required [[verbal component]]s. Its effects lasted for anywhere from a one to several minutes.

==Components==
This spell required only  [[Verbal component|verbal]] components.
