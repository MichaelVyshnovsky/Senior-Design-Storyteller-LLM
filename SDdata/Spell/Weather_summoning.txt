'''Weather summoning''' was a [[conjuration]] spell that could bring forth any kind of weather that might normally occur for the location, climate, and season at the time of casting.

==History==
In the month of [[Eleasis]], of the {{YearlinkName|1371}}, the city of [[Ravens Bluff]] declared it illegal to cast ''weather summoning'' within city limits due to a raging unnatural storm at the time called the [[Soulstorm]], threatening arrest for those that broke this decree.{{Cite trumpeter/2-8|2}}{{LCRB}}
