'''''Sheela's entangle''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Sheela Peryroyl]].
 
==Effects==
This spell had the same effects of the ''[[entangle]]'' spell. It could be cast up to {{SI|80|yd|long=yes}} from the caster and affected an area of {{SI|40|cu ft|long=yes}}. What distinguished this spell from ''entangle'' was that the effects of this spell were cumulative, therefore could be cast several times within the same area.
 
==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required [[material component]]s, the holy symbol of Sheela Peryroyl and a piece of vine.
