'''''Ice walk''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[alteration]] [[spell]] used by specialty priests devoted to [[Ulutiu]], enabling them to allow targets to safely walk on ice.

==Effects==
This spell allowed the targets to safely walk upon ice of any thickness as if it were firm ground. The target's feet did not actually touch the ice, nor did their passing leave a trace.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal component|verbal component]]s and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s the priest's [[holy symbol]] and some snow or a piece of ice to cast.
