'''''Neverwinter: Spelljammer''''' is an expansion module for the massively-multiplayer role-playing game ''[[Neverwinter (game)|Neverwinter]]''. Released in November 2023, the expansion ties in with the 2022 boxed set ''[[Spelljammer: Adventures in Space]]''.

==Features==
* New Campaign: Light of Xaryxis
* New Adventure Zone: [[Wildspace]]
* New Dungeon: [[Demonweb Pits]]
