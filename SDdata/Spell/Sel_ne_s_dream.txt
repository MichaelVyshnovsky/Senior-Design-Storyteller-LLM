Selûnes dream was an evocation cantrip available to the devotees of Selûne the Moonmaiden.
The spell of Selûnes dream allowed the caster to bestow divine healing upon the recipient with a chance of the target falling into a deep, restful slumber.
This spell was ineffective when used against undead or construct creatures. The spells reach was .
In 1492 DR, Selûnes dream cantrip was tied to a powerful weave-connected Selûnite necklace, the amulet of Selûnes chosen. The amulet originated from the glory days of the Shattered Sanctum, a once great temple stronghold of Selûne, brought to ruin by Shars Dark Justiciars.
