'''''Prayer of healing''''' was a common [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, the spell healed up to six creatures in a 30 foot (9 meter) radius from the caster. [[Construct]]s and [[undead]] were immune to this spell.

==Components==
This spell required only a [[Verbal component|verbal]] component.
