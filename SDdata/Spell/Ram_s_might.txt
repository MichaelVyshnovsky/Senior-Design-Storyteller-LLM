'''''Ram's might''''' was a [[transmutation]] [[spell]] used by [[druid]]s and [[ranger]]s.

==Effects==
This spell added considerable force to any unarmed strikes made by the caster, transforming the caster's hands with the hardness and texture of a ram's horns. The caster was still able to perform other functions with their hands such as casting spells.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components to cast.
