'''''Rainbow''''' was an [[evocation]] or [[alteration]] spell that could either create a magic short composite [[bow]] and seven arrows, or a long bridge suitable for traveling over some obstacle.

The [[Crystrum of Tranquility]] had a copy of this spell written within it.<ref>
