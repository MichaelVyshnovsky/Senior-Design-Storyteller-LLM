Conjure celestial was a conjuration spell that summoned a celestial.
The summoned celestial followed the casters orders as long as they didnt go against the creatures beliefs. The summoned celestial considered the caster and the party its allies and would stay with the party for a hour or until "killed". The summoned celestial was a creature of the same power as a couatl, for example, whom after the Second Sundering was considered a celestial. unicorn, which was also considered celestial post-Second Sundering.
Only verbal and somatic components were needed.
