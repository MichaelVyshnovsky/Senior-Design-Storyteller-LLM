'''''Resist turning''''' was a rare [[spell]] used by [[necromancer]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, the spell protected [[undead]] creatures from being [[Command undead|commanded]] or [[Turn undead|turned]] by [[cleric]]s and [[paladin]]s. The spell protected all undead creatures in a {{SI|15|ft}} area, this area was either a set location or mobile as the caster moved. The caster was able to cast this spell on themselves.

==Components==
This spell required only a [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] component.

==History==
This spell was known to be used by wizards of the [[Cult of the Dragon]].
