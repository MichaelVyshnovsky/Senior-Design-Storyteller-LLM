Dumathoins rest was a spell used by clerics of Dumathoin.
When cast, any skeletons and zombies within  collapsed. Any attempts to reanimated them failed for at least a day.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material components, the holy symbol of Dumathoin.
