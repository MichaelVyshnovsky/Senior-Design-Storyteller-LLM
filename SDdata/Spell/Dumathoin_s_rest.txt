'''''Dumathoin's rest''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Dumathoin]].

==Effects==
When cast, any [[skeleton]]s and [[zombie]]s within {{SI|30|ft|long=yes}} collapsed. Any attempts to reanimated them failed for at least a day.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]]s, the holy symbol of Dumathoin.
