'''''Etherwalk''''' was a special [[divine magic|divine]] [[alteration]] [[quest spell]] from the [[Astral sphere|astral]] and [[travelers sphere]]s that transported the caster and their followers to the [[Ethereal plane]].

==Effects==
The caster could bring up to fifty followers with them provided they all joined hands when the spell was cast. Up to three round-trip journeys could then be made through the [[Inner Planes]] before the spell transported the participants back to the [[Prime Material plane]].

==Components==
This spell did not require [[spell component]]s of any kind.

==History==
This spell was used by [[druid]]s who served [[Moander]] during the height of ancient [[Netheril]]'s power.
