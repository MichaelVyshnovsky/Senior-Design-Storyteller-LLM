'''''Mordenkainen's defense against nonmagical reptiles and amphibians''''' was a [[spell]] that improved the chances of surviving an attack by reptiles and amphibians.

==Effects==
This spell allowed the caster or a targeted creature to have increased ability against all amphibians and reptiles. This included [[dinosaur]]s and giant versions of the species. This spell had no effect against magical amphibians and reptiles, such as [[dragon]]s.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required [[material component]]s, a piece of snake skin and a desiccated leg of a frog. These were to be placed in a pouch made from tough leather.
