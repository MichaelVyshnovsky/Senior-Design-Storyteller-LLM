'''Sohei''' ({{Pronounce|soh|heh|refs=}}), sometimes called '''''yamabushi''''' "mountain warrior",{{Cite dragon/404/Character Themes: Fringes of Kara-Tur|4}} were special warrior-priests that could be found in [[Kara-Tur]] and the [[Hordelands]]. ({{Pronounce|noh|sheng|refs=}}) Sohei were similar to [[paladin]]s found in [[Faerûn]].

==Culture==
Sohei usually served as guardians for [[monasteries]].

==Abilities==
To defend their monasteries or temples from attacks, Sohei trained extensively with weapons as well as martial arts. They also possessed limited magical powers. 

==Notable Sohei==
* [[Akirakeiko]] was a sohei adventurer in [[Miyama Province]] in [[Kozakura]] in [[1357 DR]].
* [[Changchub Zangpo]] was the Sergeant of the Guard for [[Saikhoi]] in [[Ra-Khati]] during the mid–14th century [[DR]].
* [[Hoseki]] was a disguised sohei follower of the [[Dai-Oni (tengu)|dai-oni]] in [[Kozakura]] in [[1357 DR]].
* [[Ichi]], a sohei leader who lead over 420 comrades in [[Miyama Province]].
* [[Kidera Ujisato]] was the commander of the sohei of the [[Winter Sect]] in [[Nakamaru]] around [[1357 DR]].
* [[Shyarn Feng]] was a sohei adventurer in [[Shou Lung]] in [[1358]].
* [[Tsao Ho]] was a sohei adventurer in [[Shou Lung]].
* [[Tu-shun]] was a sohei who administrated a [[shoen]] of the [[Konjo]] school near the [[Itsuki]] village in [[Kozakura]].
