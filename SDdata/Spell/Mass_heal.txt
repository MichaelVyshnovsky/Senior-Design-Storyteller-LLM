'''''Mass heal''''' was a [[conjuration]] spell that applied an enhanced ''[[heal]]'' spell to a small group of creatures.

==Effect==
This spell works the same as ''[[heal]]'', except it affects all creatures within range of the caster and within 30 ft. of each other.
