'''''Greater anyspell''''' was a [[transmutation]] [[spell]] unique to [[cleric]]s with the [[Spell domain]]. It allowed them to read and prepare [[arcane]] spells.

==Effect==
This spell functioned in the same manner as ''[[anyspell]]'', but for more powerful arcane spells.
