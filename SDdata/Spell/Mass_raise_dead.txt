'''''Mass raise dead''''' was a powerful [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell was able to resurrect up to five individuals. The spell also provided minimal healing to the newly resurrected individuals. This spell was only successful if cast on certain humanoids.
