}}'''''Return to sand''''' was a [[spell]] unique to [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.

==Effects==
This spell caused items made from metal, stone, or earth to crumple into [[sand]]. This spell targeted a single item or an entire area up to {{SI|5|ft}} by {{SI|5|ft}} by {{SIAdj|1|ft}}.

Items affected by this spell returned to their normal form after the spell ended.

This spell had no effect on magical items or creatures made from stone or earth, such as [[golem]]s.

==Components==
This spell required only [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components to cast.
