'''''Insanity''''', also known as '''''descent into madness''''',<ref> was an [[enchantment]] [[spell]] that drove a subject permanently insane.

==Effects==
The enchanted creature was under the constant effect of a ''[[confusion]]'' spell, causing them to act erratically.

The ''insanity'' spell could not be removed with ''[[remove curse]]'', but only with ''[[greater restoration]]'', ''[[limited wish]]'', ''[[miracle]]'', and ''[[wish]]''.
