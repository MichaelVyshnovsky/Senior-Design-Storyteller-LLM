'''''Monster summoning VII''''' was a powerful [[arcane magic|arcane]] [[conjuration]] [[spell]] that summoned creatures to serve the caster.

==Effects==
This spell summoned one or two powerful creatures to follow the caster's commands for the spell's duration. The creatures arrived one minute after the spell was cast. Some examples of potential creatures summoned via ''monster summoning VII'' included [[fire giant]]s, [[brown pudding]]s, [[hydra]]s, [[dragon turtle]]s, and [[purple worm]]s.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s a small bag an a candle to cast.

==History==
This spell was used by [[swamplord]]s, [[specialty priest]]s of [[Sebek]], to summon a pair of [[giant crocodile]]s.
