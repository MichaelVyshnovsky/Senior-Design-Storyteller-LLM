Fabricate (originally called Aksas contrivance
With this spell the caster would use a amount of material equal to the resulted product within a set amount of space. The size changed with every type of Weave, the Oldest known version had it based on skill to a minimum of a 1 yard (0.91M) cube, after that it changed to 10 cubic ft.(3.04M) maximum unless the caster was more skilled.After the Second Sundering it changed to a 10 foot cube or 5-foot (1.52M) cubes.
One that that remained was that the size got greatly reduced when using minerals or metals, to as low as a 1 foot cube.
This spell could have used to make wood or trees in to a bridge or wool in to cloths. If the caster wished to make more advanced items the caster needed to have the skills to make the intended item by hand.
The spell only needed vocal and somatic components and the original material in a appropriate amount.
