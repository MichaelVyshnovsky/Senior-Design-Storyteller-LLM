Stoneblend was a spell used by clerics of Laduguer.
When cast, this spell enable the caster to blend in with a stone wall, essentially becoming invisible. The invisibility effect only worked if the caster remained absolutely motionless. The spell did not function if the caster was physically touched by another creature.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required material components, the holy symbol of Laduguer and a pinch of dust. In addition, the casters clothing had to be earth tones, such as black or brown.
