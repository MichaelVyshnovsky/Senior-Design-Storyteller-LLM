'''Arcane gate''' was an [[arcane]] [[conjuration]] [[spell]] that created a portal between two points.

==Effects==
The caster picked two spots that they could see, the first within {{SI|10|ft}} and the other within {{SI|500|ft}}, and a portal would be created between the two. The portal was 10 feet in diameter and could only be seen and accessed on one side. The portal showed showed glowing mist on its surface. The portal could not be cast on a creature and the portal could also be turned on and off at the will of the caster.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
