'''''Gedlee's electric loop''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[sorcerer]]s and [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell caused a small strobe of lightning to cycle through all creatures within {{SI|5|ft}} of the caster. Creatures struck with the lightning were hurt by the electrical surge and were temporarily stunned. The strength of the lightning was determined by the caster's skill level.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, the spell required [[material component]]s, a small [[magnet]] and a some [[copper]] [[wire]].
