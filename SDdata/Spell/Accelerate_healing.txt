Accelerate healing was an uncommon divine healing spell granted to clerics by their deities.
Accelerate healing was a self-descriptive spell that, when cast, hastened the target creatures natural healing. The spells effects last from one to four days.
The spell did not affect other forms of magical healing, including spells, magic items, or potions of healing.
The spell required only verbal and somatic components.
*Shinthala Deepcrest, the Grand Cabal of the Emerald Enclave circa 1370 DR.
It was recorded in Flame of the Spirit, a holy artifact of the Tymoran faith. Occasionally, the goddess granted individuals who held the item access to spells, including accelerate healing. It was also recorded in Yornars Trail Companion, a holy text of the church of Mielikki.
