Conjure sand lion was a spell used by wizards in Anauroch and Zakhara, the Land of Fate.
This spell conjured a female spotted lion that was faithful to the caster. The caster could see through the lions eyes. The sand lion was immune to necromancy and enchantment or charm spells. Spells from the Elemental Plane of Earth could harm the creature.
It was impossible to cast this spell within the phlogiston.
This spell required a handful of sand to cast.
