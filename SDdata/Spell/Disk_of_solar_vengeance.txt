'''''Disk of solar vengeance''''' was a spell used by [[cleric]]s of [[Horus-Re]].
 
==Effects==
When cast, this spell created a brilliant disk of flame behind the caster's head. The spell gave the caster an increased resistance to cold.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required a [[divine focus]]
