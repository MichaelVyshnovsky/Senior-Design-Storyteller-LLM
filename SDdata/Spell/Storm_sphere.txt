Storm sphere was an evocation spell that created a ferocious spherical storm of loud winds and lightning.
The spell created a storm inside a radius of  centered at at point within  of the caster that lasted for up to one minute, or as long as the caster kept concentrating. Anyone caught within the storm was immediately bashed by violent winds that grew stronger the more powerful the caster. In addition, the storm generated bolts of lightning at the casters command, directed at creatures as far as  from the storms center.
Anyone nearby was partially deafened by the storms loud noise.
The spell required only verbal and somatic components.
