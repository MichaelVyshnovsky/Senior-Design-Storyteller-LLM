Sun scepter was a conjuration spell that created a magical scepter of light. This spell was used by specialty priests of Akadi.
When cast, this spell created a scepter made from light that had a mace-like shape. Usable as a weapon only by the caster, it struck with the powers of axiomatic, disruption, and flaming burst weapons.
This spell required only  verbal and material components in the form of a solid gold scepter or mace worth at least 2,500 gold pieces to cast. The scepter or mace was not consumed during the casting.
