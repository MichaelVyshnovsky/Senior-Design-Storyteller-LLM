'''''Sun scepter''''' was a [[conjuration]] spell that created a magical scepter of light. This spell was used by specialty priests of [[Akadi]].

==Effects==
When cast, this spell created a scepter made from light that had a [[mace|mace-like]] shape. Usable as a weapon only by the caster, it struck with the powers of [[axiomatic]], [[disruption]], and [[flaming burst]] weapons.

==Components==
This spell required only  [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[material component]]s in the form of a solid [[gold]] scepter or [[mace]] worth at least 2,500 [[gold piece]]s to cast. The scepter or mace was not consumed during the casting.
