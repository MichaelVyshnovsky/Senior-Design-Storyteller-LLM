Analyze item was an utterance from the Lexicon of the Crafted Tool that used truespeak to reveal all magic that enchanted or affected an item.
The utterance revealed all the spells that affected an item, if any, and if it was magical, it also revealed its charges, the way to activate it, and what its powers were. The utterance did nothing with artifacts.
The recitation was known to loquasphinxes.
