'''''Greater antidragon aura''''' was a protective spell used by [[cleric]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell created a protective aura around the targets that glowed with a phosphorescent green. It provided extra protection against attacks and and resistance to spells from all sources, not just dragons and their ilk. For them, it also provided significant shielding against their spells. Additionally, it protected against any attempt by a dragon to possess or influence a creature's mind, as with ''[[protection from evil]]''. Finally, if [[dragon]]s and related creatures did manage to strike a protected creature, then they would suffer a loss of strength.

The relic was often a piece from the sword of a dragon slayer. The sacred relic had to have a value of at least 500 [[gp]].
