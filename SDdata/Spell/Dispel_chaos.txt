Dispel chaos was an abjuration spell that worked exactly like dispel evil except it affected chaotic spells and creatures instead of evil ones.
The effects of this spell were the same as dispel evil except the aura surrounding the spellcaster was a solid, unwavering blue and made of lawful energy.
Verbal and somatic components were required to cast this spell plus the casters (un)holy symbol or divine focus.
Justiciars of Tyr, Nar demonbinders, and Olin Gisir were known to use this spell.
