'''''Pierce shield''''' was an incredibly powerful [[spell]] used by [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
A caster used this spell to overwhelm an opponent's magical defenses. It lowered the target's magical defense by a certain percentage, which was determined by the caster's skill level. Additionally, it also stripped one protection spell used by the target.
