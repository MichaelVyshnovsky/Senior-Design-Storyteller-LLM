'''''Daltim's fiery tentacles''''' was an arcane [[conjuration]] [[spell]] that caused flaming tentacles to emerge from the ground and grapple and burn creatures within reach. It was developed by [[Daltim Flamefist]] of [[Halruaa]].

==Effects==
The spell functioned in all ways like the well-known ''[[Evard's black tentacles]]'' To this, ''Daltim's fiery tentacles'' added a blue-white flame that wreathed the writhing members, burning as well as crushing the unfortunates trapped within.

==Components==
Like ''Evard's black tentacles'', ''Daltim's fiery tentacles'' required the usual verbal and somatic components and a piece of a tentacle of a giant octopus or squid.
