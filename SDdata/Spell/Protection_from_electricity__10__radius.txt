Protection from electricity, 10 radius was a spell used by the Red Wizards of Thay
This spell created a protective sphere with a radius of 10 feet (3 meters) that protected the recipient from all forms of electrical attacks.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component a piece of copper wire for each creature within the protected area.
