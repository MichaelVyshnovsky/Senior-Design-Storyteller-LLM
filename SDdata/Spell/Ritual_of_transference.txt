'''''Ritual of transference''''' was a cooperative [[divine magic|divine]] [[alteration]] [[spell]] that transfered charges of [[magic]]al energy between two items.

==Effects==
In addition to an item to item transfer, power could also be drawn from altars, [[artifact]]s, or other souces of great power. ''Ritual of transference'' was also rarely used to imbue individuals with extra power to help them defend an area.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s two consecrated [[holy symbol]]s to cast.
