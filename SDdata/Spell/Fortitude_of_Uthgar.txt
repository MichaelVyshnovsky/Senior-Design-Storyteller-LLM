'''''Fortitude of Uthgar''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[enchantment]] [[spell]] used by specialty priests devoted to [[Uthgar]], enabling them to endure pain beyond their norm.

[[Shaman]]s of Uthgar used this spell on themselves and members of this tribe.

==Effects==
This spell boosted the physical and mental fortitude of the recipient, allowing them endure pain with courage. The recipient also found it easier to resist spells that affected their mind. The spell would last for several days, depending on the skill of the shaman, so long as they carried a beast totem on their person for the spell's duration.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal component|verbal component]]s and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s the priest's [[holy symbol]] to cast.
