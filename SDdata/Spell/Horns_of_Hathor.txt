'''''Horns of Hathor''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[alteration]] [[spell]] used only by specialty priests devoted to [[Hathor]] called [[Revered Mother]]s.

==Effects==
This spell allowed a priestess to grow a pair of horns from her head and gorge her enemies. The priestess could even charge at enemies at least {{SI|5|ft}} tall and {{SI|30|ft}} away.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as a [[material component]] a piece of horn from a [[cow]], bull or [[minotaur]] to cast.
