'''''Power word, heal''''' was a powerful [[spell]] used by [[bard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell had the power to heal nearly all wounds. If the targeted individual was charmed, paralyzed, stunned or suffering from fear, this spell returned then to their normal mental state. The caster was required to physically touch the target. This spell had no effect on [[construct]]s or [[undead]] creatures.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[material component]], a skin shed from a lizard.
