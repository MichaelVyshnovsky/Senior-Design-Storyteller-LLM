}}'''''Calm''''' was an [[alteration]] [[spell]], available to [[dragon]]s, that allowed them to negate the effects of their natural ''fear aura''. A variant of the spell was used by [[shugenja]] in [[Kara-Tur]] and was very rare outside Kara-Tur.

==Effects==
While a dragon maintained concentration on this spell, the determination and morale of any person or creature within its presence was unaffected. Its effects could be ended at any time, by choice of the caster. The effects of the spell used by shugenja was similar, it calmed those affected by confusion, fear or insanity.

==Casting==
The spell used by dragons required only a [[verbal component]]s. The spell used by shugenja, in addition to verbal and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[material component]], either a page of sutras or a prayer wheel.
