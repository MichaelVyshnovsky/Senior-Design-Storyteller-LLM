'''''Whip of pain''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[necromancy]] [[spell]] used by priests devoted to [[Loviatar]], enabling them to create a whiplike line of force.

==Effects==
This spell created a thin lines of force extending from the caster's fingertips. The whips crackled with purple energy and could strike targets up to {{SI|10|ft|long=yes}} distant. The whips made loud cracking sounds each time they struck and harmed a target, wracking them with intense pain.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s a drop of [[unholy water]] and one of the caster's hairs to cast.
