Cool strength was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate. This spell was only usable by wizards.
This spell prevented heat stroke, heat exhaustion, sunburn, and other harmful effects caused by the sun. This spell could also keep moist creatures from loosing as much water from the suns heat.
This spell required a pinch of sand, a drop of water, and a drop of blood from the recipient.
