'''''Waves of exhaustion''''' was a [[necromancy]] [[spell]] that made the targets exhausted.

==Effects==
On casting ''waves of exhaustion'', the caster generated waves of [[negative energy]] within a 60 ft (18 m) cone. This negative energy instilled exhaustion in everyone who was not so to begin with.

==Components==
''Waves of exhaustion'' required only somatic and verbal components.
