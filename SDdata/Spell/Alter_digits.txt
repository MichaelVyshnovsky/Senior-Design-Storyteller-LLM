Alter digits was a spell. 
For a minute or more, the caster could alter the targets fingers and toes in size, hue, shape, texture, or any combination thereof. They could also be made to exude sweat that was sticky or slippery. Number of digits would not be changed by this spell.
The spell called only for somatic and verbal components.
