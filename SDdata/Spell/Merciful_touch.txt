'''''Merciful touch''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Sharindlar]].
 
==Effects==
A caster used this spell to not only heal injuries, but to alleviate pain and suffering. So if the spell was unable to cure a disease or completely heal an injury, it would ease the symptoms or pain from said conditions for up to a full day. ''Merciful touch'' also negated the effects of certain spells such as ''[[irritation]]''. 

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], the holy symbol of Sharindlar.
