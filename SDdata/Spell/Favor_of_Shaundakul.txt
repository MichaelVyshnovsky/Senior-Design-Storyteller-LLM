Favor of Shaundakul was a divine alteration spell used by priests devoted to Shaundakul, enabling them to create a beneficial event during a trying time.
This spell created a beneficial event during an otherwise trying time. The spell would only function out in the wild when traveling.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component the casters holy symbol to cast.
