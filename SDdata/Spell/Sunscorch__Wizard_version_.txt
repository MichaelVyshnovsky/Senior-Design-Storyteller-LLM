Sunscorch was a spell unique to Zakhara, the Land of Fate.
This spell caused an intense blast of heat to emanate from the casters hand. This beam of heat curved around obstacles to unerringly strike the intended target. The heat from this spell caused more damage if the target was wearing or carrying any metal. Only living flesh was affected by this spell.
This spell could not be cast underwater.
This spell did not require material components to cast.
