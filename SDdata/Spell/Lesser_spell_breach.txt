'''''Lesser spell breach''''' was an [[abjuration]] [[spell]] cast by [[Sorcerer|sorcerers]] and [[Wizard|wizards]].

==Effects==
This spell stripped the target of up to two magical defenses (such as ''[[globe of invulnerability]]'', ''[[stoneskin]]'', ''[[protection from elements]]'', ''[[death armor]]'', or ''[[mage armor]]''), starting from the most potent one. It also reduced the target's resistance against magic slightly for a minute.

==Components==
The spell required [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[Somatic component|somatic]] components.
