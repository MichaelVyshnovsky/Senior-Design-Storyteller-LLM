'''''General of undeath''''' was a powerful [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Velsharoon]].

==Effects==
This spell allowed the caster to increase the number of [[undead]] creatures they could control at a single time. The amount of undead the caster could control was determined by their skill level. The lasted a day, after which the caster lost control as if they had voluntarily given it up.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[divine focus]]. The caster focused on the darkness in their soul, willing it to increase and grant greater power over the undead.
