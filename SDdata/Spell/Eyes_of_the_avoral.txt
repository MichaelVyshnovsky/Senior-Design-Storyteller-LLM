'''''Eyes of the avoral''''' was a [[transmutation]] [[spell]] that vastly improved the subject's vision.

==Effect==
A touched target creature acquired the keen eyesight of an [[avoral]], significantly enhancing their ability to spot threats. This lasted ten minutes, or longer for more experienced casters.

==Components==
It required only a [[somatic component]].
