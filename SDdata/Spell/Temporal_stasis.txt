Temporal stasis, which was originally called Chronomancers stasis,
Whoever was touched by a mage casting this spell was placed into a state where time ceased flowing for him or her. The creature would not age, and no body functions would operate, nor would there be any consciousness. In addition, the target was protected from any force or effect, including those that might cause harm. This state of stasis would last permanently unless undone by other magic,
Temporal stasis required verbal and somatic components and an expensive powder (worth at least 5,000 gp) composed of the dust of several rare gems, including diamond, emerald, ruby, and sapphire, which were consumed in the casting process.
