'''''Amelior's restraint''''' was a defensive [[arcane]] [[spell]] developed by the [[mage]] and [[sage]] [[Amelior Amanitas]] of [[Secomber]]. Presumably, the spell could be used by [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, ''Amelior's restraint'' disabled all special abilities, skills, and magics of a target creature. The spell continued the arcane restraint for a certain period of time of at least a single minute.
