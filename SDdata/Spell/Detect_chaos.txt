'''''Detect chaos''''' was a [[divination]] spell that provided information about objects or creatures with a [[chaotic]] aura.

==Effects==
This spell functioned exactly like ''[[detect evil]]'' except it detected chaotic auras from [[demon]]s, chaotic clerics, spells, magic items, etc.
