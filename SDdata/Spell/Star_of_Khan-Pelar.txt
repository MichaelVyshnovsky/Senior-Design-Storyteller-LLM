The '''Star of Khan-Pelar''' was a gem that the wizard [[Martek]] was said to have imbued with an enchantment to retrieve his Sphere of Power, but would actually resurrect him. It was one of the five [[Star gem of Martek|Star Gems of Martek]].{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|4}}

==Description==
The [[Star of Khan-Pelar]] was an enormous [[rock crystal|clear crystal]].

==Powers==
The gem had also the ability to restore to a wizard the vigor to cast spells they'd cast before, taking between ten minutes and ninety depending on the power of the spell.These properties are not referenced in the [[Desert of Desolation]] module. This article assumes the gems are intended to be analogue to the ones in the core module.

==History==
The Star of Khan-Pelar was said to be buried beneath the [[Oasis of the White Palm]].{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|5}} In {{yearlink|1356}}, it was found by adventurers exploring the [[Temple of Set]],{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|78}} who used it to resurrect the wizard Martek.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|126}}
