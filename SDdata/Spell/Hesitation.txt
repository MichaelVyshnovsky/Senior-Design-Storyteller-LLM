'''''Hesitation''''' was a [[divine magic|divine]] [[enchantment]] [[spell]] from the [[time sphere]] that caused a target to pause before executing their planned course of action.

==Effects==
The slight delay caused by ''hesitation'' usually provided enough time for others to act before the target.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as a [[material component]] a piece of turtle shell to cast.

==History==
The ''[[Tome of Torment]]'', a holy book from the [[Church of Ilmater]], contained the ''hesitation'' spell.
