'''''Teleport field''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spells teleported creatures within an area at random. The caster was able to cast this spell in a location that was within visual range. Once a location was chosen and the spell cast, any creature within a {{SI|30|ft}} radius was affected by this spell.
