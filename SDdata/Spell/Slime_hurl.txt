'''''Smile hurl''''' was a [[conjuration]] [[spell]] that created three globs of dark, [[green slime]] within the hands of the caster which were subsequently launched towards one or a number of target creatures.

==Effects==
When the globe of slime struck a person or creature it could not be readily removed and had to be destroyed in a specific manner.

==Casting==
The casting of this spell required [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s as well as the use of a ''[[divine focus]]''.
