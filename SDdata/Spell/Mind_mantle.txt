Mind mantle was a spell that was commonly used by alhoons.
This spell protected the casters or targeted creatures mind against charm and mind-reading spells. It worked by covering the thoughts of the creature with random thoughts. The spell also protected against magical items and psionic influences. While protected, the targeted creature was able to cast spells. The spell was vulnerable to dispel magic.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
