'''''Embattlement''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[abjuration]] spell that made it more difficult to successfully attack the caster.

==History==
The ''embattlement'' spell appeared within the ''[[Tome of Torment]]'', the holiest book of the [[church of Ilmater]], which first appeared in the Realms in [[848 DR]].
