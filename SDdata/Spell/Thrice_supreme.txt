'''''Thrice supreme''''' was a more powerful version of the ''[[improved thrice]]'' [[spell]].

==Effects==
After casting, the caster's abilities in physical combat were temporarily increased. Their skill in hand-to-hand combat and use of a melee weapon were both increased. The spell also increased the ability to cast spells with a touch effect.

==Casting==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required [[material component]]s, three black [[sapphire]]s of equal size. Each sapphire had to be worth at least 250 [[gp]] each.
==History==
This spell was known to be used by wizards of the [[Cult of the Dragon]].
