}}'''''Murdock's feathery flyer''''' was an [[alteration]] [[spell]] used by [[wizard]]s. This spell was invented by [[Murdock]].

==Effects==
This spell created a feathery membrane between the caster's arms and extending to their feet. With arms and legs spread the caster could jump from a height and glide through the air. The membrane would disappear instantly when the spell ended.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required an eagle's feather to cast.
