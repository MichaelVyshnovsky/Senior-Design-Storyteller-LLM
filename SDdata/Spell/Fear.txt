'''''Fear''''' was a [[spell]] that caused subjects to flee, it was known as '''''cause fear''''' after the [[Spellplague]] and before the [[Sundering]].

==Effects==
When cast, a sense of total terror was created within a cone-shaped area of effect. Any creature within this area became overcome with fear, usually causing them to panic and flee. In their panicked flight, they often dropped their weapons and equipment. This spell had no effect on [[undead]] creatures.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[material component]], the heart of a hen or a white feather except after the Spellplague and before the Sundering.

==History==
The spell was attributed to [[Netherese arcanist]] [[Keonid]] in {{Yearlink|-1875}} and was originally known as ''Keonid's fear''. and in the ''[[Book of Bats]]''.
