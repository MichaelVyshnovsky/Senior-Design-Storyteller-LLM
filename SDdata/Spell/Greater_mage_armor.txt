'''''Greater mage armor''''' was a simple spell used by [[sorcerer]]s and [[wizard]]s.
==Effects==
Like ''[[mage armor]]'', this spell allowed the caster to create an armor for themselves out of magical force.
==Components==
Unlike ''mage armor'', this spell did not require a [[material component]], only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components. An earlier version required a material component, a tiny [[platinum]] shield worth 25 [[gp]].
