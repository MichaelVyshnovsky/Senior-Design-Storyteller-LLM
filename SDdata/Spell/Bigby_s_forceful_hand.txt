'''''Bigby's forceful hand''''' was an [[evocation]] [[spell]] similar to ''[[Bigby's interposing hand]]''.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell created a hand of force. This hand followed and shoved at a targeted creature, even a very strong one. The hand would push the targeted creature right to the distance limit of the spell. The spell was strong enough to slow a moving [[spelljammer]].

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a strong glove made from either cloth or leather.
