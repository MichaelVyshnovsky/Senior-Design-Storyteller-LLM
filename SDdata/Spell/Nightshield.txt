'''''Nightshield''''' was a protective [[arcane]] and [[divine]] [[abjuration]] [[spell]] from the first circle of magic. The spell could be used by [[cleric]]s, [[wizard]]s, and [[sorcerer]]s.

==Effects==
When the spell was cast, it enveloped the caster in a protective ''nighshield'' that boosted their resistance to harmful effects and increased their chances of avoiding harm or shrugging off harmful magics and effects. Additionally, the ''shield'' completely negated damage from all ''[[magic missile]]s''. The spell's protective properties increased with the caster's magical proficiencies.

==Components==
The spell required only a [[Somatic component|somatic]] and [[verbal component]]s.
