Sneeze was a person-affecting cantrip that acolyte wizards could learn. It caused a target creature to experience an irritation of the nasal passages, possibly forcing a spasmodic, violent exhalation.
This cantrip was similar to the scratch cantrip and had a range of . It caused one creature to experience a tickling in the nose that prompted a single sneeze to relieve the irritation. If the target managed to resist the spell, nothing happened.
This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster spoke the name of a breathing irritant (dust, pepper, etc.) and touched their nose.
