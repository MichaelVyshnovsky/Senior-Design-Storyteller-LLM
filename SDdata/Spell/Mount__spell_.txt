Mount, which was originally called Jarms mount, was a summoning spell to conjure an equine to carry a rider.
To within close range of the caster, this spell summoned a pony or light horse, which was willing an able to carry a rider. The mount was summoned with riding gear, such as bit and bridle and saddle, already equipped. The duration of the summons depended on the power of the spellcaster but was at least two hours.
A strand of horse hair was required to cast this spell, in addition to verbal and somatic components.
The spell was invented by the Netherese arcanist Jarm  in .
In the month of Eleasis, of the , the city of Ravens Bluffs Ministry of Art and Wizards Guild jointly declared it illegal to cast mount within city limits due to a raging unnatural storm at the time called the Soulstorm. In addition to threatening arrest for those that broke this decree, it was declared that breaking this prohibition would result in a permanent expulsion from either organization.
