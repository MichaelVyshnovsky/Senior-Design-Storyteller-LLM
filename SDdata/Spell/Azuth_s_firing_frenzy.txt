'''''Azuth's Firing Frenzy''''' was a [[divine]] [[evocation]] [[spell]] that discharged three volleys of ''[[magic missile]]s''.

==Effects==
When this spell was cast, the caster shot forth three separate barrages of ''magic missiles'', each of which included five projectiles. While each barrage targeted a different person or creature, the ''missiles'' of each barrage struck the same target.

In rare cases, it was possible for ''firing frenzy'' to inadvertently discharge [[magic]]al items carried by those it struck.

==History==
The specifications of this spell were contained within ''[[Vorthryn's Archivir]]''.

==Components==
This spell required [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s in order to be cast.
