'''''Comet''''' was a powerful [[spell]] used by [[sorcerer]]s and [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
The caster was able to use this spell to reign destruction on a target. When cast, a large comet or [[meteor]] would impact the targeted area, causing massive destruction and shock waves. The caster could choose a target within {{SI|90|yd}}. The caster and their allies were not harmed by this spell.
