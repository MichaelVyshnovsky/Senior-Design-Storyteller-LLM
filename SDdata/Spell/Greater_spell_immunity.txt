'''''Greater spell immunity''''', also known as '''''Serten's spell immunity''''',<ref> was an [[abjuration]] spell that was identical to ''[[spell immunity]]'' except that it bestowed complete resistance against one or more particular spells, spell-like effects of magic items, or spell-like abilities of creatures of 8th level or below.
