Purify food and drink was a transmutation or alteration spell that make food edible and water potable. The alteration version could be reversed, creating putrefy food and drink, which spoiled food and fouled water.
The transmutation version of this spell as an orison learned by beginning clerics and druids of any faith.For comparison water weighs about  per  or 1 kg per liter. The spell removed dirt, poisons, parasites, mold, fungus, and impurities of any kind, making it suitable for consumption by most humanoids. Unholy water was ruined by this spell.
When cast in reverse, putrefy food and drink rotted food and contaminated beverages in like amounts, making them unfit and possibly dangerous to consume. Holy water was ruined by this spell.
All versions of this spell only required verbal and somatic components.
