'''''Excessive indulgence''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[enchantment]] [[spell]] used by specialty priests devoted to [[Sharess]] called [[Sensate]]s, enabling them to distract a target by forcing them to over focus on their current activity.

==Effects==
This spell forced the target to focus on their current activity to a point of excessive indulgence, preventing them from taking any course of action other than repeatedly performing their current activity. An attack on the target during this time would cancel the spell.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, the [[material component]]s required for this spell were the priest's [[holy symbol]] and three freshly picked flower petals.
