'''''Malavon's rage''''' was an [[evocation]] [[spell]] that shot darts of [[magic]]al energy out from the caster in all directions.

==Description==
When cast, ''Malavon's rage'' generated small wedges of magic energy, which were then instantly issued forth in all directions within a radius of {{SI|7|ft|long=yes}} to {{SI|15|ft|long=yes}}, piercing anyone caught in their path. The spell was noted as being particularly useful when surrounded by enemies.

==History==
''Malavon's rage'' was created by the [[drow]] [[sorcerer]] [[Malavon Despana]] prior to [[1281 DR]].
