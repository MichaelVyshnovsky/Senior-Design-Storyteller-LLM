Protection from all lycanthropes, 10 radius was a spell that protected the recipient from all types of lycanthrope.
This spell protected the caster or another individual from all types of lycanthrope. Lycanthropes were unable to get within  of the recipient.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, namely wolfsbane. During casting, the wolfsbane was crushed and then cast in a circle around the caster.
* Ahmad, a Semphari fighter, carried a scroll of protection from lycanthropes.
* Radatha, leader of the Fire Knives, kept a scroll of protection from lycanthropes within her treasure vault.
