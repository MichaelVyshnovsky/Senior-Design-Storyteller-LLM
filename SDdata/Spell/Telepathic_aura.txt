'''''Telepathic aura''''' was a [[divine magic|divine]] [[divination]] [[spell]] used by [[paladin]]s that allowed the caster to connect with their companions telepathically.

==Effects==
''Telepathic aura'' only allowed one-way communication where the caster could send thoughts to companions within range, but the companions could not reply with their own thoughts.

==Components==
This spell required only [[somatic component]]s to cast.

==History==
''Telepathic aura'' was found within a [[spellbook]] from the [[Church of the Red Knight]] called ''[[Master Tactician]]''.{{Cite web/Master Tactician}}
