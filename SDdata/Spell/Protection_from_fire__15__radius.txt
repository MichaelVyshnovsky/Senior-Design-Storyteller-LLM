Protection from fire, 15 radius was a spell used by the Red Wizards of Thay.
When cast, this spell protected the caster from all forms of fire, both mundane and magical. The spell could even protect against elemental fire. It created a small area of protection around the caster, 15 feet (4.5 meters) in radius. This protected area moved with the caster.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, a vial of water.
