'''''Khelben's dweomerdoom''''' was a powerful [[arcane magic|arcane]] [[abjuration]] [[spell]] that countered an opponent's spell before it was cast.

==Effects==
This spell removed the most powerful spell currently prepared and memorized by the caster's target.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required as a [[material component]] a crystal lens worth at least 1,000 [[gp]] to cast. The lens could be reused.

==History==
This spell was developed by [[Khelben Arunsun]] of [[Waterdeep]].
