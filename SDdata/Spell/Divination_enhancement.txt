'''''Divination enhancement''''' was an [[evocation]] spell used by [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
This spell allowed the caster to extend the range and duration of the ''[[wizard eye]]'' spell specifically, as well as any beginner [[divination]] spell. The duration and range for these spells was increased by half. ''Divination enhancement'' affected any divination spell cast before the spell expired.

==Components==
These spells only required [[Verbal component|verbal]] components to cast.
