'''''Slashing darkness''''' was a [[divine]] [[evocation]] [[spell]] that shot forth a ribbon of pure darkness from the caster.

==Effects==
The dark swathe of energy created by this spell hissed as it flew from the caster's hand. It was quite harmful to living beings but had curative effects on [[undead]] creatures.

==Casting==
The casting of this spell required only [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s.
