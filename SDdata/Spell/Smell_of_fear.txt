'''''Smell of fear''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[ranger]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell placed a powerful aroma upon a targeted creature. This aroma mimicked the fear scent which attracted most predators. It greatly increased the chances of a targeted creature encountering a predator.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
