'''''Wyvern call''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
A caster used this spell to summon a [[wyvern]]. The caster had total control over the wyvern. There was a risk the wyvern could turn against the caster if ''[[charm monster]]'' was cast upon it by another individual.
