'''''Soften earth and stone''''' was a [[spell]] commonly used by [[cleric]]s of [[Urdlen]].

==Effects==
When cast, the spell transformed stone into [[clay]] and packed dirt into [[sand]] or mud.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required [[material component]]s, the holy symbol of Urdlen and wet clay from the wheel of a talented potter.
