'''''Ghastly hands''''' was a [[necromancy]] [[spell]] that transformed the [[spellcaster|spellcaster's]] hands to those of a [[ghast]].

==Effects==
For the duration of the spell, the caster's mere touch was capable of [[paralyze|paralyzation]]. They emitted a distinct, foul odor that was recognizable to anyone who had previous encountered a [[ghast]].

The caster could end the effects of the spell at will, but any paralyzation they caused could not abated, and ran its course.

==Casting==
The casting of this spell required [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s.

==History==
This spell was known to be used by wizards of the [[Cult of the Dragon]].
