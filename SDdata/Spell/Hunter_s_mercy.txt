'''''Hunter's mercy''''' was a [[transmutation]] spell granted uniquely to [[ranger]]s by their deities.

==Effects==
After a prayer for mercy, a ranger's next shot with a bow, if taken quickly, would not miss and had a good chance of inflicting critical damage to its target.

==Components==
To cast this spell required [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s.
