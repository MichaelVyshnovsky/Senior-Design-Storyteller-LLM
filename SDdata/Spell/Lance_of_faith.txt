'''''Lance of faith''''' was a [[prayer]] used by [[cleric]]s.

==Effects==
At will, the cleric could fire a ray of bright golden light towards a target within {{SI|25|ft|long=yes}}, searing them with painful radiance. Additionally, the target was surrounded by sparkles of lights, helping one visible ally of the cleric become more likely to strike true in their next attack against that target.
