'''''Still waves''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[invocation]] [[spell]] used by specialty priests devoted to [[Valkur]], enabling them to calm waves at sea.

==Effects==
This spell calmed the waves within a {{SI|20|ft}} by {{SI|20|ft}} area of water, allowing sailors and cargo to be fished out of the sea. Heavy chop waves were reduced to still water and raging waves from storms were reduced to heavy chop. The spell would have to overpower a [[water elemental]] if cast in the same area.

==Components==
This spell required only [[verbal component|verbal component]]s and [[somatic component]]s to cast.
