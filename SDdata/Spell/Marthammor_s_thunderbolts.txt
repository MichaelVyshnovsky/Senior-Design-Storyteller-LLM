'''''Marthammor's thunderbolts''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Marthammor Duin]].

==Effects==
When cast, this spell unleashed bolts of lightning from the eyes and hands of the caster. The caster was capable of striking a target up to {{SI|40|yd|long=yes}} away. The lightning had no effect on objects and could be blocked by barriers. They also did not fork nor rebound off of a surface.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required [[material component]]s, the holy symbol of Marthammor Duin.
