'''''Portent''''' was a [[divine magic|divine]] [[divination]] [[spell]] from the [[chaos sphere|chaos]] and [[divination sphere]]s that provided the caster with a glimpse of their luck in the future.

==Effects==
This spell gave the caster a glimpse of their luck in the near future for either themselves or another subject. The glimpse provided a brief insight into an event that would occur within one full day of the spell's casting, informing the subject on whether the outcome would be favorable or undesirable.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s either some [[tea]] leaves or a numbered wheel to cast.

==History==
[[Bando|Bando the Lame]], a [[halfling]] [[cleric]] of [[Tymora]] living in [[Daggerford]], could cast ''portent''.
