'''''Swift invisibility''''' was a type of [[illusion]] spell.
 
==Effects==
This spell was identical to ''[[invisibility]]'', but swifter: it could be done alongside another action, affected the caster alone, and endured for about six seconds.

==Components==
This spell required only a [[verbal component]].
