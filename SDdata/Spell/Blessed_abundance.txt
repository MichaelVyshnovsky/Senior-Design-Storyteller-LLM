'''''Blessed abundance''''' was a [[conjuration]] spell that allowed the caster to multiply a quantity of animal or vegetable matter.

==Effects==
This spell worked on raw or prepared food and live or harvested plants, not on live creatures, metals, minerals, or magical items. Up to one cubic foot (0.28 m<sup>3</sup>) of organic matter could be multiplied by the caster's level. The creations were real and permanent.

==Components==
Verbal and somatic components, as well as the priest's [[holy symbol]] were required to cast this spell.
