'''''Sixth sense''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Corellon Larethian]].
 
==Effects==
This spell gave the caster the ability to detect unexpected danger, up to {{SI|10|ft|long=yes}} from the caster, towards themself or their allies. The spell did reveal the exact nature of the threat, just a sense of impending danger.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required [[material component]]s, the holy symbol of Corellon Larethian and a single drop of sweat.
