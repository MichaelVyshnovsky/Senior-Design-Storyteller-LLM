Stoneflight was a spell unique to Maztica.
This spell was cast on slingers, allowing them to launch their missiles father and with greater force. This spell could be cast on three individuals at a time. The amount of individuals was determined by the casters spell level, up to a maximum of thirty people at a time. Any individual had to be within  of the caster to gain this ability.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, casting this spell required a material component in the form of a small ball made from bird guano.
