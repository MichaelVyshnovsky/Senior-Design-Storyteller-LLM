'''''Launch bolt''''' was a simple [[spell]] used by [[sorcerer]]s and [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell caused a [[crossbow]] bolt to launch itself at a target of the caster's choice.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
