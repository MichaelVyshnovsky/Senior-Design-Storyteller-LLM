Stitch was a useful cantrip that wizards-in-training could learn. It added or repaired a seam in either cloth or leather.
This cantrip either repaired torn seams or added new seams. Up to  of cloth or  of leather could be sewn with one application of this spell. The seam was the same as one done by a person with sufficient skill in sewing or leatherworking
This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster spoke a short, rhyming incantation while pantomiming sewing with needle and thread.
