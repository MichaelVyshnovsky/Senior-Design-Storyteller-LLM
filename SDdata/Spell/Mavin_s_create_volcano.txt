'''''Mavin's create volcano''''' was an [[arcanist]] spell of [[Epic magic|epic power]] created in the days of [[Netheril]]. 

==Effects==
Casting ''Mavin's create volcano'' caused a minor earthquake to erupt in the desired area. After a week’s time, ash and smoke exploded from the earth and, within a month, the mountain site would erupt into a powerful volcano.

===Drawbacks===
The spell was never cast in Netheril‚ but every arcanist sought the knowledge to conjure a devastating volcano, but they were stilted due to the terrifying effects the spell would have had on the ground-dwelling creatures, such as [[dwarf|dwarves]] and [[elf|elves]].

==Components==
The components needed for this spell were a mantle of sewn [[diamond]]s which acted like a [[ring of fire resistance]], a [[helm of brilliance]], and a [[mythallar]], which was placed at the location of the volcano.

==History==
The spell was created by [[Mavin]] around {{Yearlink|-601}} (3258 NY). This spell could no longer be cast due to the restrictions placed on magic by [[Mystra]] after the near cataclysmic destruction of the [[Weave]] by the casting of ''[[Karsus's avatar]]'' in {{Yearlink|-339}} (3520 NY). Later ages remembered the spell simply as '''''create volcano'''''.<ref >
