'''''Animal animosity''''', also known as '''''Rillifane's curse''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Rillifane Rallathil]].
 
==Effects==
When cast, this spell created an aura that caused animals to become violent and hostile towards a targeted creature. Animals within {{SI|50|ft|long=yes}} of the caster was affected. However, the only animals vulnerable to this spell were animals that were also vulnerable to ''[[animal friendship]]''. This spell was only partially effective if the targeted creature was a [[ranger]] or had a natural ability to affect an animal's emotional state. This spell was vulnerable to ''[[dispel magic]]'' and ''[[remove curse]]''.
 
==Components==
This spell required [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components.
