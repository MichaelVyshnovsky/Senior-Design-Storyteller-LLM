Animal animosity, also known as Rillifanes curse was a spell used by clerics of Rillifane Rallathil.
When cast, this spell created an aura that caused animals to become violent and hostile towards a targeted creature. Animals within  of the caster was affected. However, the only animals vulnerable to this spell were animals that were also vulnerable to animal friendship. This spell was only partially effective if the targeted creature was a ranger or had a natural ability to affect an animals emotional state. This spell was vulnerable to dispel magic and remove curse.
This spell required verbal and somatic components.
