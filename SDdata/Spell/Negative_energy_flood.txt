Negative energy flood was a necromancy spell that bombarded a creature with waves of negative energy.
The caster targeted a visible creature within a range of , sending bands of negative energy. If living and unable to resist the effect, the target creatures flesh shriveled away as the life force was drained from it. A creature killed by this spell rose as a zombie under the casters control within seconds.
If the target creature was already undead, the spell bolstered the targets vitality temporarily.
The spell only required verbal and material components. The materials required were a broken bone and a square of black silk.
