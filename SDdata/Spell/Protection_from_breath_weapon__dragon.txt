'''''Protection from breath weapon, dragon''''' was a [[spell]] used by the [[Red Wizards of Thay]].

==Effects==
This spell protected the caster from all forms of [[dragon breath]] weapons, regardless of the [[alignment]] of the [[dragon]].

==Casting==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, the spell required a [[material component]], a [[gold piece|gold coin]]. The coin was destroyed during casting.
