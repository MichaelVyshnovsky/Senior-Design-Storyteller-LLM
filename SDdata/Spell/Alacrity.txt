'''''Alacrity''''' was an [[alteration]] [[spell]] used by [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
This spell allowed the caster to increase the speed at which they cast spells of intermediate, or lower, ability.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required a miniature hourglass to cast. The hourglass was destroyed during the casting.
