Call lightning storm was a powerful evocation spell from the 5 circle of magic that dealt lightning damage. The spell could be used by druids and shugenjas of Kara-Tur.
Call lightning storm was an empowered version of the call lightning spell. Each strike of lightning dealt increased damage and the caster could summon up to fifteen lightning strikes from the skies. The spell could be cast at a distance of  and it increased with the casters magical powers.
The spell required only a somatic and verbal components.
* Aerosclughpalar, an old gold dragon druid of Teuveamanthaar.
* Chazzar Ne, an Uthgardt barbarian druid in the late 14 century DR.
* Lossarwyn the Ice Lich and a druid who preferred to use cold magic.
* Blue dragons possessed an innate magical ability to call lightning storms.
* Verdant princes could cast call lightning storm once per day.
