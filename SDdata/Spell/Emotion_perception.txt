'''''Emotion perception''''' was a [[divine magic|divine]] [[divination]] [[spell]] from the [[war sphere]] that allowed the caster to get a sense of the emotional state and morale of a military unit.

==Effects==
In order for the spell to function the caster needed to have an uninterrupted line of sight to the military unit.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as a [[material component]] the [[priest]]'s [[holy symbol]] to cast.

==History==
Watcher [[Tonorak Winthrax]], a priest of [[Helm]], could cast ''emotion perception''.<ref name=VR-p15">
