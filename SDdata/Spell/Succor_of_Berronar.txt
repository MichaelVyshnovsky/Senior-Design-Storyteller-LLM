Succor of Berronar was a spell used by clerics of Berronar Truesilver.
When cast, this powerful spell duplicated the effects of several other spells cure blindness or deafness, cure disease, cure serious wounds, neutralize poison and repair injury. In addition, the spell postponed lycanthropy and addictions for a whole day. The spell also restored sanity and gave the recipient a sense of general well being. This spell could only be cast once per month.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required material components, the holy symbol of Berronar Truesilver and a single drop of holy water.
