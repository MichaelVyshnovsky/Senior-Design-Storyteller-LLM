'''''Cure minor wounds''''' was a [[conjuration]] spell that clerics and druids learned as an [[orison]]. It provided the minimum noticeable amount of healing.

==Effects==
This spell function exactly like ''[[cure light wounds]]'', requiring the healer to touch the creature being healed. This spell could be used to do the minimum amount of damage to [[undead]].
