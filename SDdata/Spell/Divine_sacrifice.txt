Divine Sacrifice was a necromancy spell that traded the casters life force for increased damage to opponents.
The caster could sacrifice life force to increase the damage dealt to opponents. When cast, he or she could sacrifice a moderate amount of health in exchange for the ability to deal a significant amount of additional damage on the next successful attack.
Only verbal and somatic components are required to cast this spell.
