'''''Geyser''''' was a very rare [[spell]] used by [[druid]]s.
 
==Effects==
When cast on a target, the spell created a geyser in the targeted location. The geyser erupted, shooting steam and scalding water to a height of {{SI|90|ft|long=yes}}. The column of water could be up to {{SI|4|ft|long=yes}} in diameter.
 
==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required [[material component]]s.
