'''''Animal summoning II''''' was an improved version of ''[[animal summoning I]]''.

==Effects==
The spell operated in the same way as ''animal summoning I'' but could summon up to twelve animals of the same size or up to six animals approximately twice that size.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[material component]].
