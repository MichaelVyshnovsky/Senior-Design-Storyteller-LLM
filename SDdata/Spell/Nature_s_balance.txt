'''''Nature's balance''''' was a powerful [[spell]] used by [[druids]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell lowered the spell resistance of all opponents within a {{SI|5|ft}} radius. Additionally, all allies of the caster within the same area were healed. The amount of healing and spell resistance removed was determined by the caster's skill level.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
