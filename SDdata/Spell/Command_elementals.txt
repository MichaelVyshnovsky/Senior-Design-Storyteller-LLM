'''''Command elementals''''' was an [[enchantment]]/[[charm]] spell of the [[southern magic]] tradition{{Southernmagic}}. It was a very rare spell, but commonly used by [[Elementalist|elementalists]] and rarely used by [[Conjuration|conjurers]]. 
==Effects==
When cast, the spell allowed a caster to command, or take control of, an [[elemental]]. Only one creature from one of the elemental or para-elemental planes could be targeted. The spell had a range of 40 yards (36 meters). 

==Components==
The spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
