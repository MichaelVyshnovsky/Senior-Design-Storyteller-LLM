'''''Protection from winged flyers''''' was an [[abjuration]] spell that allowed the caster to protect someone from any creatures that had wings and flew. 

==Effects==
These flying beasts were completely unable to even touch the target of the spell. It worked similarly to the spell ''[[protection from evil]]''.

==Components==
The material component required to cast this spell was a simple feather, or [[bat]] wing.
