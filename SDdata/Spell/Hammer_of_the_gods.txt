'''''Hammer of the gods''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell covered an area with a {{SI|22|ft}} radius in divine light. The divine light was used by the caster to smite any opponent within the affected area.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
