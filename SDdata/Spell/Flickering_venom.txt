Flickering venom was an invocation used by some warlocks, which could be used to infect an enemy with deadly poison. When a warlock casted flickering venom, they conjured a dagger-like weapon of venomous poison which they then shot into a creature within  of them. Flickering venom was more easily cast than some spells, but required a short rest of a few minutes between castings regardless.
