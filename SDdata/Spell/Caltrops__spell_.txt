'''''Caltrops''''' was a simple spell.
==Effects==
When cast, this spell covered an area of {{SI|5|ft|long=yes}} by {{SI|5|ft|long=yes}} with [[caltrop]]s. The size of the area covered in caltrops was determined by the caster's skill level.
==Components==
This spell required only a [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] component.
