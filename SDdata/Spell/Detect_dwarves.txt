'''''Detect dwarves''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Sharindlar]].
 
==Effects==
When cast, this spell allowed the caster to detect any [[dwarves]] up to {{SI|90|ft|long=yes}} in front of the caster. The spell was capable of detecting [[azer]], [[derro]], [[duergar]], [[Half-dwarf|half-dwarves]] and even dead dwarves or dwarven blood. Even if hidden by an illusion-based spell, invisible, or shapechanged.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], the holy symbol of Sharindlar.
