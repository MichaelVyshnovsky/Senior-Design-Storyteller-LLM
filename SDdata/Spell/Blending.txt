'''''Blending''''' was an [[arcane magic|arcane]] [[illusion]] and [[alteration]] [[spell]] that allowed to caster to blend in with their surroundings.

==Effects==
This spell only worked on subjects of man-size or smaller. The spell's [[magic]] caused the subject to appear to be part of its surroundings. Their appearance could be changed for the duration of the spell if the subject conentrated on doing so. The subject's friends and allies would view them normally. Viewers with higher intelligence stood a greater chance of seeing through the spell when they came within {{SI|30|ft|long=yes}} of the caster.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as a [[material component]] a dried piece of [[chameleon]] skin to cast.

==History==
[[Knightengale]], a [[paladin]] in [[Cormyr]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], wore a suite of [[plate armor]] enchanted with the ''blending'' property.{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1992|462|Knightengale}}
