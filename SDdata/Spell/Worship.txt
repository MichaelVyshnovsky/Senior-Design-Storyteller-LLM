'''''Worship''''' was an uncommon spell of the [[southern magic]] tradition.{{Southernmagic}}

==Effects==
The spell increased the natural charisma of the caster. Weak-willed creatures who had a clear view of the caster fell to their knees and worshiped the caster. Use of this spell to impersonate a [[deity]] was incredibly dangerous. 
 
==Components==
The spell required only [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components.
