Curse of impending blades was a necromancy spell from the 2 circle of magic that made ones enemies vulnerable to damage. The spell was granted to rangers and was usable by bards, sorcerers, and mages.
When cast, the spells target, within  from the caster, found themselves having a difficult time of avoiding attacks and seemingly, stumbling into danger and the enemys blade. The spells effects were not affected by dispel magic, but could be remove via remove curse, break enchantment, limited wish, miracle, or wish spells. Curse of impending blades lated for two minutes and was prolonged per minute with each level of arcane mastery. The spells distance also increased with the casters magical prowess.
The spell required somatic, verbal, and material components. The material component was a leather thong, pierced with an iron nail.
