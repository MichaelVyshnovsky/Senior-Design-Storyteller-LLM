One with the land was a spell commonly used by druids, hathrans and rangers.
When cast, this spell allowed the caster to have a greater perceptiveness with nature. It increased the casters ability in empathy with animals, hiding and stealthy movement, searching and increased directional awareness, wilderness lore and handling animals.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
