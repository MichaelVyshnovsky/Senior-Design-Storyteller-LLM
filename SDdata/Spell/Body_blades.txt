Body blades was a divine alteration spell that caused a number of blades to emerge from the body of the caster.
The blades created this by spell emerged from all over the casters body, protruding through clothes and armor. Each one vaguely resembled the blade of a dagger. They were non-metallic in composition and did not obstruct the casters movement or vision.
Body blades was one of the spells whose specifications could be found within the Lash of Loviatar.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required two sharpened pieces of metal and a single shard of bone.
