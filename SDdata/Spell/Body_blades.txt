'''''Body blades''''' was a [[divine]] [[alteration]] [[spell]] that caused a number of blades to emerge from the body of the caster.

==Effects==
The ''blades'' created this by spell emerged from all over the caster's body, protruding through clothes and armor. Each one vaguely resembled the blade of a [[dagger]]. They were non-metallic in composition and did not obstruct the caster's movement or vision.

==History==
''Body blades'' was one of the spells whose specifications could be found within the ''[[Lash of Loviatar]]''.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required two sharpened pieces of metal and a single shard of bone.
