Prismatic ray was an evocation spell that fired a beam of colored light from the casters hand to a target creature. The beam could temporarily blind those affected, and had various other properties based on its color.

The interplay of arcane energies involved in this spell could cause sensations of nausea in the caster.
The spell had both verbal and somatic components.
