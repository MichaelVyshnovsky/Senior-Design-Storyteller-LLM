'''''Charmthwart''''' was an [[abjuration]] [[spell]] that protected its caster from [[charm]] spells that fell within the greater [[enchantment]] [[school of magic]].

==Effects==
Upon casting ''charmthwart'', a continuous singing sound surrounded the caster for a number of seconds. For its duration, they were protected against charm magic to which they were previously susceptible. The spell could be prematurely ended the will of the caster.

==Casting==
This spell only required [[somatic component]]s to cast.

==History==
Developed by the [[wizard]] [[Sammaster]] during his early years training in [[the Art]], the discovery of this spell was the result of [[arcane]] experimentation. This spell was known to be used by wizards of the [[Cult of the Dragon]].
