'''''Create spelljamming helm''''' was a [[spell]] that created a ''[[spelljamming helm]]''.

==Effects==
During casting, the caster touched a regular unoccupied chair. If cast successfully, the chair was transformed into a ''spelljamming helm''.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], a rod made of crystal with a value of at least 5,000 [[gp]]. It was consumed during the casting process.
