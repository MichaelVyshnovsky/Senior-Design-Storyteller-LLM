Protection from mechanical traps was a spell used by the Red Wizards of Thay.
When cast, this spell protected the caster from non-magical mechanical traps. The spell was unable to detect the presence of a mechanical trap.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, a small amount of axle grease.
