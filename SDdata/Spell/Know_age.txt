'''''Know age''''' was a common [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s.
 
==Effects==
When cast, this spell revealed instantly to the caster the age of a targeted creature, individual or object. The spell was accurate to within one year of the actual age.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], a page from a calendar.

==History==
''Know age'' was one of the spells contained within the ''[[Scepter of Mystra]]'', a relic of the faith of [[Mystra]].{{cite book/Prayers from the Faithful|77-80}} It was also found in ''[[Yornar's Trail Companion]]'', a holy [[spellbook]] of the [[Church of Mielikki]].
