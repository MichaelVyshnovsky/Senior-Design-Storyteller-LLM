'''''Detect enemies''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Vergadain]].
 
==Effects==
When cast, this spell detected the presence of a creature with hostile intent towards the caster. It was capable of detecting any said creature, even if astral, disguised, ethereal, hidden, invisible, out of phase, within {{SI|60|ft|long=yes}} of the caster.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], the holy symbol of Vergadain.
