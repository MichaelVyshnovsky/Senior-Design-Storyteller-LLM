'''''Greater spell trigger''''' was a stronger version of  ''[[spell trigger]]''.

==Effects==
The spell was almost identical to ''[[spell trigger]]'', except it had a range of {{SI|40|feet|long=yes}}, and could store any spell less complex than itself.

==Components==
Besides [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components and the ashes from a scroll, the spell required a drop of blood from a subject who had once cast, or been the target of, ''[[permanency]]'' or ''[[contingency]]''.

==History==
[[Azuth]] made this spell available through [[Elminster]] sometime prior to {{yearlink|1370}}.{{cite book/Secrets of the Magister|101}}
