'''''War cry''''' was a basic [[spell]] used by [[bard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell increased the courage of the caster. As a result, they appeared more intimidating to their opponents, who therefore couldn't defend themselves properly against melee attacks from the caster.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
