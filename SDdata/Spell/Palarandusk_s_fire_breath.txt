Palarandusks fire breath was an arcane evocation fire spell that allowed the caster to spit a gout of flame.
The gout of flame was a cone measuring  in length. The flames burned anything caught within the cone with increasing intensity based upon the casters skill. The caster experienced a nasty case of heartburn after casting this spell.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
This spell was developed by the gold dragon Palarandusk.
