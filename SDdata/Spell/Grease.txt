Grease (originally called Shans tallow Ilsensine, Laogzed, were granted the power of grease by their deities, and ooze genasi could create a similar effect.
This spell would coat a single object or a ten-foot-square surface within a close range with a layer of slippery grease, which would last for a number of seconds depending on the power of the caster.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, the caster required a piece of butter or a bit of pork rind, although any similarly greasy substance could in theory be substituted. This material component disappeared as the spell was cast.
The spell was invented by the Netherese arcanist Shan in .
In the month of Eleasis, of the , the city of Ravens Bluffs Ministry of Art and Wizards Guild jointly declared it illegal to cast grease within city limits due to a raging unnatural storm at the time called the Soulstorm. In addition to threatening arrest for those that broke this decree, it was declared that breaking this prohibition would result in a permanent expulsion from either organization.
