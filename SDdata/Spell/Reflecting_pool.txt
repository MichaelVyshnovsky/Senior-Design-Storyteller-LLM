'''''Reflecting pool''''' was a [[divination]] spell that allowed the caster to use a small pool of water as a [[scrying]] device.

==Components==
In addition to a [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]], this spell required a [[material component]] in the form of a [[mistletoe]] and an ounce of {{SI|1|oz|long=yes}} of [[oil]] extracted from a [[walnut]] or [[hickory]] nut.
