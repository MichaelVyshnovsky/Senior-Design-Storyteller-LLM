'''''Beacon of hope''''' was an [[abjuration]] [[spell]] that granted health and feelings of hope.

==Effects==
Any targeted person or creature within {{SI|30|ft|long=yes}} gained an advantage for surviving death effects and [[divine]] spells, like those of [[cleric]]s, [[druid]]s, and [[ranger]]s. The targets also gained the most out of [[heal]]ing spells cast on them.

==Components==
Casting this spell required [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components.
