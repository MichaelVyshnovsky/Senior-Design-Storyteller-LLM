'''''Master earth''''' was a [[transmutation]] [[spell]] used by [[druid]]s.

==Effects==
This spell allowed the caster to travel instantaneously through the earth to a destination of their choosing. There was no distance limitation for the spell provided the location was on the same plane of existence. ''Master earth'' only functioned on the [[Prime Material plane]].

This spell could be used to travel to both specific or general locations.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required a fossil of an animal to cast.
