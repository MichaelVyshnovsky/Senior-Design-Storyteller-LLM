Bones of the earth was a transmutation spell that raised pillars of stone from the ground.
The spell caused up to six pillars to rise within a range of . The pillars had a diameter of  and rose up to . A pillar could lift up a creature that could not move away quickly enough and could crush and pin a creature to a sufficiently low ceiling. Once emerged, the pillars could be attacked and destroyed, but were otherwise permanent. More powerful casters could create a larger number of pillars.
The spell only required verbal and somatic components.
