'''''Soul forge''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Moradin]].
 
==Effects==
When cast on a creature, this spell allowed said creature to be temporarily immune to ''[[fear]]'' and ''fear''-based effects. This spell could only be cast on lawful good creatures, and there was a chance the spell could fail if cast on creatures other than [[dwarves]].

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], the holy symbol of Moradin.
