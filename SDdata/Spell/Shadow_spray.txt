Shadow spray was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.
When cast, this spell created a directed explosion of a large number of shadow ribbons. The burst radius was . Any creature with the blast radius suffered damage. They were also dazed and became more vulnerable to fear-based spells.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, a small amount of black ribbons.
This spell was included in the Rudiments of the Shadow Weave, a controversial book about Sharran doctrine and the Shadow Weave, written by the wizard Vontallion Rose.
