'''''Mordenkainen's lucubration''''' was an uncommon [[spell]] that allowed a caster to instantly remember a spell.

==Effects==
When cast, the caster was able to remember any spell they had successfully cast in the previous 24 hours. They were only able to recall low-to-mid level spells and only a single spell. If the recalled spell required [[material component]]s to cast, the caster had to have them available to successfully re-cast the spell.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
