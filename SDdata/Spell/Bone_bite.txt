'''''Bone bite''''' was a many-schooled [[divine]] [[spell]] available to [[priest]]s of [[Myrkul]]. It would transform any bone or bone fragment held by the caster to transform into razor-sharp bone jaws that would bite any living thing with which it came into contact.

==Components==
The spell had [[verbal component|verbal]], [[somatic component|somatic]], and [[material component]]s, the last a partial or complete bone.
