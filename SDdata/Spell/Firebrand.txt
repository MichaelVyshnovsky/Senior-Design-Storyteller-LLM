'''''Firebrand''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[sorcerer]]s and [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell covered an area with a {{SI|5|ft}} radius in a mass of burning flame. Any creature caught within the burst of flame was burned. The caster was able to fire multiple bursts in different spots as far as {{SI|100|ft}} from their location. Both the range and the strength of this spell was determined by the caster's skill level.


==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[material component]], a vial of [[alchemist's fire]].
