Heralds call was a spell used by bards and harper scouts.
When cast, the spell allowed the caster to daze a crowd of creatures. During casting, the spell created a very loud authoritative shout. Any creature with  became dazed. Any creature beyond this radius heard the shout but were not affected by the spell.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
