}}'''''Call of despair''''' was an [[Elven High Magic]] [[wizard]] [[spell]] useful for defending [[elven]] homelands.

==Effects==
When cast this spell forced all but the bravest creatures to flee in absolute terror. This spell was used against enemies attacking an elven homeland and elven defenders and their allies were immune to the spell's effect. The effect of the ''call of despair'' lasted longer depending on the skill of the caster.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required as a [[material component]] a small [[brass]] [[gong]] to cast.
