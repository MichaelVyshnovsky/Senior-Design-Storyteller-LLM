'''''Melt''''' was a common [[spell]] used by [[wu jen]] in [[Kara-Tur]].

==Effects==
When cast, the spell raised the temperature of a small area, {{SI|3|ft|long=yes}} around the wu jen. Creatures from the [[Frostfell (plane)|Para-Elemental Plane of Ice]] or from colder climates were vulnerable to this spell. 

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, the spell required [[material component]]s, a pinch of soot and [[rock salt]].
