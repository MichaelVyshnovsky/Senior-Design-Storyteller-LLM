'''''Rend shadow weave''''' was an [[abjuration]] spell that affected the [[Shadow Weave]].
 
==Effects==
When cast, this spell created an area of dead magic measuring {{SI|10|cu ft|long=yes}} within the Shadow Weave. The spell had no effect on the [[Weave]].

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], a [[moonstone]] with a minimum value of 50 [[gp]].
