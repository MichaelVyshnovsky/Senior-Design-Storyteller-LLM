'''''Roots of the assassin''''' was a powerful [[Divine magic|divine]] [[conjuration]] [[spell]] used by priests devoted to [[Moander]], enabling them to create dangerous roots.

==Effects==
This spell created hundreds of vile black roots capable of springing from any surface and strangling whoever triggered the spell. The caster determined the specific trigger for the spell during its casting. The spell could not be triggered by [[undead]] or plant-based creatures.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s the priest's [[holy symbol]] and a rotting leaf or rotting piece of wood to cast.
