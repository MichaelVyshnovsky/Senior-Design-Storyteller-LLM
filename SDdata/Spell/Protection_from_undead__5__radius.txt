'''''Protection from undead, 5' radius''''' was an [[arcane magic|arcane]] [[abjuration]] and [[necromancy]] [[spell]] that protected the caster against one form of [[undead]].

==Effects==
This spell created a globe of protection against a single form of undead. This sphere only protected against physical attacks from the undead. The spell could be centered on any touched target when cast.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as a [[material component]] the dust from a destroyed undead creature of the type the caster wished to protect against to cast.

==History==
The entire structure of [[Tymora's Holy Hall of Good Fortune and Casino]] in [[Zhentil Keep]] was warded with a ''protection from undead'' spell.
