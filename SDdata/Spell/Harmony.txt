Harmony was a spell used by bards.
When cast, the spell inspired confidence into the casters companions. This was accomplished by the casters allies hearing the spell. It could be instrumental, sung, read as an epic poem, or spoken words. The effects lasted until the spell expired.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
