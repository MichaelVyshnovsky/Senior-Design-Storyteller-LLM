'''''Chill''''' was an [[evocation]] [[orison]].
 
==Effects==
When cast, this spell reduced the temperature of a liquid or material. The liquid had to be a non-living substance. The spell reduced the temperature by about {{SI|40|F|long=yes}} to a maximum of freezing temperature. The spell expired rapidly, lasting only an instant.
