Dispel fatigue was a necromancy spell that alleviated weariness and exhaustion in the recipient.
One creature within a range of 90 ft (27.4 m) could be reinvigorated by this spell. All effects of physical and mental exertion were removed, leaving the subject fully rested and refreshed.The Players Option Spells & Magic describes other systems of magic, including some using "spell points" and "fatigue points". The Players Option Combat & Tactics describes a combat system that uses fatigue points. In those systems, this spell removed all fatigue points from a creature.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a sprinkling of springwater that had been blessed.
