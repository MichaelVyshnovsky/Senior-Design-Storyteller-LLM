Legend lore was a divination spell that related legends about an important creature, location, or object.
The spell required verbal, somatic and material components. The materials required were four ivory strips and each casting of it also consumed high-quality incense.
The spell was attributed to Netherese arcanist Jarm in , and was originally called Jarms saga.
