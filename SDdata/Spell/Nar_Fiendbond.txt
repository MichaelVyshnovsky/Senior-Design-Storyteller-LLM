'''''Nar fiendbond''''' was a [[transmutation]] spell that transformed a person into a [[half-fiend]]. It was presumably developed by the [[Narfell|Nars]].

==Effects==
The spell imbued a volunteer or victim with the essence of the [[fiendish planes]], granting them powers as if they were the offspring of a full-blooded [[fiend]].

==Components==
The spell required a fortune in [[gem]]s.
