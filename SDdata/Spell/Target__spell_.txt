'''''Target''''' was an [[Arcane magic|arcane]] [[southern magic]] [[spell]] from the school of [[alteration]] used by [[wizard]]s that allowed them to mark a target for ranged weapons.

==Effects==
Once marked by the ''target'' spell, any missile weapons fired at the victim stood a greater chance of hitting and harming the victim. The spell lasted for several minutes depending on the skill of the caster.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s to cast.
