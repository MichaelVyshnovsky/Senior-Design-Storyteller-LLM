Path to the grave was a channel divinity power granted to clerics of the Grave domain.
The cleric cursed a target creature within a range of , rendering its life force more susceptible to being extinguished. For a few seconds, the cursed target became extremely vulnerable to damage from a single subsequent attack.
Only the clerics holy symbol was required to invoke this curse.
