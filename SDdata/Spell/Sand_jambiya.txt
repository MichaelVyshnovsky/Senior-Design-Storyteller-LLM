Sand jambiya was a spell used by wizards in Anauroch and Zakhara, the Land of Fate. This spell was only usable by wizards.
This spell turned a fistful of sand into a small sharp weapon. The new blade was no longer than twice the length of the casters hand. A sand jambiya could harm creatures that were immune to normal weapons.
This spell required a fistful of sand to cast.
