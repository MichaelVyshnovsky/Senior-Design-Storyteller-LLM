}}'''''Sand jambiya''''' was a [[spell]] used by wizards in [[Anauroch]] and [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate. This spell was only usable by [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
This spell turned a fistful of [[sand]] into a small sharp weapon. The new blade was no longer than twice the length of the caster's hand. A sand [[jambiya]] could harm creatures that were immune to normal weapons.

==Components==
This spell required a fistful of sand to cast.
