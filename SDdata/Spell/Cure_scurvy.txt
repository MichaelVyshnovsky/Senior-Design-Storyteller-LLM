'''''Cure scurvy''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[abjuration]] [[spell]] used by specialty priests devoted to [[Valkur]], enabling them to cure [[scurvy]].

==Effects==
This spell allowed the caster to instantly cure scurvyâ€“a common disease among malnourished sailors.

''Inflict scurvy'' could be cast to inflict a single target with the same disease if they were within {{SI|270|ft}} of the caster. The effects of scurvy could be negated by a ''[[cure disease]]'' spell or by including fresh fruit in the diet.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal component|verbal component]]s and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s the priest's [[holy symbol]] and a salt-encrusted pit of a fruit to cast.
