'''''Protection from plants, 10' radius''''' was a [[spell]] used by the [[Red Wizards of Thay]]

==Effects==
This spell created a protective sphere that prevented all forms of plant life from crossing. It was effective against [[fungi]], [[slime]]s, [[mold]]s, and [[Plant creature|plant-based creatures]].

==Casting==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required ash as a [[material component]]. This ash had to be from a harmful mold or slime that had been burned.
