'''''Azundel's purification''''' was an [[abjuration]] [[spell]] that destroyed all the inherent or transferred magic from a given item.

==Effects==
This spell would obliterate any [[curse]]s, tracers, dweomers, and magical remnants, including [[arcane]] spells and spell-like powers, from a single non-living item, a pile of dry or powdered materials or a body of liquid.

==Components==
The material components for this spell were a pinch of powder from a crushed ''[[focal stone]]'', a flawless [[diamond]], and three drops of a ''[[potion of sweetwater]]''.
