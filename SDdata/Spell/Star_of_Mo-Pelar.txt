The '''''Star of Mo-Pelar''''' was a star gem gifted by the wizard [[Martek]] to the [[Pharaoh]] [[Amun-Re]] of [[Bakar]].

==Description==
The ''Star of Mo-Pelar'' was a fist-sized [[opal]]. It was one of the five [[Star gem of Martek|''Star Gems of Martek'']]. 

==Powers==
The ''Star of Mo-Pelar'' had the same powers as a ''[[gem of true seeing]]''.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|40}}

==History==
The wizard Martek gave the star gem to the pharaoh Amun-Re of the house of Mo-Pelar, who had it placed in his [[Pyramid of Amun-Re|tomb]].{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|5}} The pharaoh would later use the Star Gem as part of a death curse uttered in [[Osiris]]' name which dried the [[River Athis]]; this barred his soul from the afterlife.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|30}} The curse stayed in place until [[adventurer]]s retrieved the ''Star of Mo-Pelar'' in {{yearlink|1356}}, using it to restore Martek to life.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|126}}
