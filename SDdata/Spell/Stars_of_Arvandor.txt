Stars of Arvandor was an evocation spell.
When cast, this spell created a number of sparkling stars that orbited the head of the caster, similar to ioun stones. The number of stars was determined by the casters skill level, to a maximum of ten stars at once. The stars were used as a ranged weapon, with the caster launching a star, one at a time, at a target. The stars inflicted significant damage on evil-aligned creatures.
This spell required verbal and somatic components.
