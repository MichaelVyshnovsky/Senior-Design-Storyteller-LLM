'''''Stars of Arvandor''''' was an [[evocation]] [[spell]].
 
==Effects==
When cast, this spell created a number of sparkling stars that orbited the head of the caster, similar to [[ioun stone]]s. The number of stars was determined by the caster's skill level, to a maximum of ten stars at once. The stars were used as a ranged weapon, with the caster launching a star, one at a time, at a target. The stars inflicted significant damage on evil-aligned creatures.

==Components==
This spell required [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
