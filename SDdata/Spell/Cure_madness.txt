'''''Cure madness''''' was a [[divine magic|divine]] [[abjuration]] [[spell]] from the [[necromantic sphere]] that removed the harmful effects of diseases that targeted the mind.

==Effects==
In order for the spell's [[magic]] to work the [[priest]] needed to place their hands upon the target's brow. The negative effects of madness dissipated within several minutes to several days depending on the severity of the disease. This spell also worked to combat the ''[[feeblemind]]'' spell and the aftereffects of [[psionic]]s that harmed the mind.

==Components==
This spell required only [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s to cast.

==History==
This spell was only granted to followers of the [[elf|elven]] [[goddess]] [[Sehanine Moonbow]].
