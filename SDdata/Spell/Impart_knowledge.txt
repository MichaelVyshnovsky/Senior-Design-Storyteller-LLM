Impart knowledge was a divine divination spell used by priests devoted to Oghma, enabling them to share complex thoughts with another through touch.
This spell allowed the priest to silently share complex knowledge with another through touch. Knowledge transferred by this spell occurred instantly, making the spell useful for a dying priest to share secrets quickly.
This spell required only somatic components to cast.
