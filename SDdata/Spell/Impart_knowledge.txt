'''''Impart knowledge''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[divination]] [[spell]] used by priests devoted to [[Oghma]], enabling them to share complex thoughts with another through touch.

==Effects==
This spell allowed the priest to silently share complex knowledge with another through touch. Knowledge transferred by this spell occurred instantly, making the spell useful for a dying priest to share secrets quickly.

==Components==
This spell required only [[somatic component]]s to cast.
