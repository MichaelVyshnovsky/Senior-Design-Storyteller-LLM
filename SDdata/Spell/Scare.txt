'''''Scare''''', which was originally called '''''Hamring's scare''''',

==Effects==
This spell caused a number of creatures within 30 feet of each other and within medium range of the caster to become frightened or shaken for a number of seconds.

==Components==
A bone from an [[undead]] creature, such as a [[ghoul]], [[ghast]], [[mummy]], [[skeleton]], or [[zombie]] was required to cast this spell, in addition to [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s.

==History==
The spell was invented by [[Netherese arcanist]] [[Hamring]] in {{Yearlink|-1294}}, the same year he invented his ''[[Ray of enfeeblement|enfeeblement]]'' spell.
