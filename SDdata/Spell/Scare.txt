Scare, which was originally called Hamrings scare,
This spell caused a number of creatures within 30 feet of each other and within medium range of the caster to become frightened or shaken for a number of seconds.
A bone from an undead creature, such as a ghoul, ghast, mummy, skeleton, or zombie was required to cast this spell, in addition to verbal and somatic components.
The spell was invented by Netherese arcanist Hamring in , the same year he invented his enfeeblement spell.
