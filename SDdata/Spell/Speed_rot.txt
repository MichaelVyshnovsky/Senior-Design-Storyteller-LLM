'''''Speed rot''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[abjuration]] [[spell]] used by priests devoted to [[Moander]], enabling them to cause rot in fruit and vegetables.

==Effects==
This spell caused fruit, vegetables, and grain to immediately ripen and rot. Up to {{SI|100|cu ft|long=yes}} of plant material could be affected with a single casting. This spell could also be used to cause physical harm in plant-based creatures.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as a [[material component]] a bit of mold to cast.
