Blade of disaster, also known as black blade of disaster, was a powerful conjuration spell that weaponized a rift in the very structure of the planes that could be used to cut through anything in its path. It could be learned by sorcerers, warlocks, and wizards
The spell summoned a  crack between the planes that was roughly shaped like a dark blade. The "blade" appeared at a point of the casters choosing within a range of  and lasted up to a minute, as long as the caster maintained concentration. It could be commanded by the caster to move around and perform muliple devastating attacks against opponents in quick succession. The blade dealt great damage against all creatures that touched it and could even disintegrate them if a vital spot was hit.
The version that had become most popular in  was vulnerable to dimensional anchor, and would collapse if struck with one.
The spell required only verbal and somatic components.
