'''''Alternate reality''''' was a [[wild magic]] [[alteration]] [[spell]] used by [[wild mage]]s.

==Effects==
This spell allowed the caster to create a small change in probabilities. Though this change lasted for just a moment, it had a profound effect on a single recent event.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required a small unmarked [[dice|die]] to cast.
