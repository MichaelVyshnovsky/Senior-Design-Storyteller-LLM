'''''Pierce magic resistance''''' was a very rare [[spell]].

==Effects==
This spell was used by a caster to strengthen another spell against magical resistance. It had to be cast before the intended spell. It was usually effective against mid-level magical resistance or barrier spells.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[material component]], a small knife or spike made from [[iron]].

==History==
This spell was known to be used by wizards of the [[Cult of the Dragon]].
