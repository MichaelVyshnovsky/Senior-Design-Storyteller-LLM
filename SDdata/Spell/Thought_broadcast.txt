Thought broadcast was a divine alteration spell from the thought sphere that allowed a subjects thoughts to be experienced by others.
While this spell was active the subjects thoughts were broadcasted to others within . This could be extremely useful or hindering depending on whether or not the subject was a willing participant. The spells magic crossed any language barriers so the thoughts could be understood by all races.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material components a small balloon to cast. The balloon was inflated during the casting and then consumed.
The Scepter of Mystra, a relic of the Mystran faith, could cast the thought broadcast spell.
