Loviatars caress was a divine alteration spell used by priests devoted to Loviatar, enabling them to succeed with a touch spell.
This spell ensured that the following touch-based spell would always succeed. The target spell needed to be cast immediately after Loviatars caress. This spell could also be used with the priests pain touch ability.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
