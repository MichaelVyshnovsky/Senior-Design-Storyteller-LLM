'''''Move earth''''' was a [[transmutation]] spell that excavated trenches and created hills.

==Components==
The spell required [[Verbal component|verbal]], [[Somatic component|somatic]] and [[material component]]s, in the form of a mix of different soils like [[clay]], [[loam]], and [[sand]] in a small bag, as well as an [[iron]] blade.

==History==
The spell was attributed to [[Netherese arcanist]] [[Proctiv]] in {{Yearlink|-2095}} and was originally called '''''Proctiv's earthmove'''''.
