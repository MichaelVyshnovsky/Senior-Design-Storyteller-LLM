'''''Sticks to crocodiles''''' was a spell granted by the [[Mulhorandi pantheon|Mulhorandi]] [[god]] [[Sebek]] to his [[werecrocodile]] [[priests]].

==Effects==
The spell functioned identically to ''[[sticks to snakes]]'', except that it transformed sticks into [[crocodile]]s rather than [[snake]]s.
