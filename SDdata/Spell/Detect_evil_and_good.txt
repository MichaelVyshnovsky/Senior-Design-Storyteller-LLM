'''''Detect evil and good''''' was a [[divination]] [[spell]] that detected [[outsider]]s of different types and other types of creatures within {{SI|30|ft|long=yes}} feet of the caster.

==Effect==
The caster could sense desecrated or consecrated ground as well as [[aberration]]s, [[celestial]]s, [[elemental]]s, [[fey]], [[fiend]]s, or [[undead]]. The spell could not penetrate {{SI|3|ft|long=yes}} feet of wood or dirt, {{SI|1|ft|long=yes}} of stone, {{SI|1|in|long=yes}} of [[steel]], or a thin sheet of [[lead]].

==Components==
Only [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s were needed for the casting of this spell.
