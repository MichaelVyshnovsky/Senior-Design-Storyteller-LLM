'''''Azuth's immobility''''' was an [[enchantment]] [[spell]] that allowed a [[cleric]] to temporarily immobilize a number of beings within a given area.

==Effects==
When this spell was cast the caster chose an area 40 feet (12.2 meters) in diameter. Every person or creature within that area of effect had a chance of being immobilized as per the spell ''[[hold person]]'' or ''[[hold monster]]''.

Beings were freed from the effects of ''Azuth's immobility'' if they were touched by the caster, targeted by their spells or struck by a weapon.

==History==
The specifications of this spell were contained within ''[[Vorthryn's Archivir]]''.

==Components==
This spell required [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s in order to be cast.
