'''''Branding smite''''' was an [[evocation]] [[spell]] that produced an astral radiant glow on a target when struck.

==Effects==
If the enchanted weapon struck an invisible target, then it would give off a soft glow to a distance of about {{SI|5|ft|long=yes}}. The target would be struck with radiant damage proportional to the power of the caster. The effect lasted for a minute.

==Components==
Only a [[verbal]] component was needed to cast this spell.
