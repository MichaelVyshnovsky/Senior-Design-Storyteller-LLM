Aura of flaming death was a powerful spell used by clerics, druids, and shamans.
When cast, this spell wrapped the caster in an aura of intense flame. This flaming barrier burned any nearby opponents as well as protected the caster from mundane attacks within a  radius. It also gave the caster a resistance to fire.
