'''''Balagarn's iron horn''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[bard]]s, [[sorcerer]]s and [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell created a sonic vibration strong enough to cause a creature to stumble or trip. The strength of the resonant vibration was determined by the caster's skill level.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
