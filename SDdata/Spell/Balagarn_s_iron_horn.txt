Balagarns iron horn was a spell used by bards, sorcerers and wizards.
When cast, this spell created a sonic vibration strong enough to cause a creature to stumble or trip. The strength of the resonant vibration was determined by the casters skill level.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
