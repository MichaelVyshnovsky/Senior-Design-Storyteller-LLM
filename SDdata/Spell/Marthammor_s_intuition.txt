'''''Marthammor's intuition''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Marthammor Duin]].
 
==Effects==
This spell enabled the caster to detect the nearest [[dwarf]], often one in need of immediate assistance. The spell had a radius of {{SI|1|mi|k|long=yes}}. The spell also allowed the caster to find the quickest route to the desired location. Rarely, the spell was capable of warning the caster of an event in the near future.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required [[material component]]s, the holy symbol of Marthammor Duin and a lock of the caster's hair.
