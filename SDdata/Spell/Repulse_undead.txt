'''''Repulse undead''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, waves of energy emitted outwards, centered on the caster. Any [[undead]] creature approaching the caster and encountering these waves was pushed back, unable to continue on their path.
