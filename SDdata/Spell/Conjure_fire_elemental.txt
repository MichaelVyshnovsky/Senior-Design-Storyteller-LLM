'''''Conjure fire elemental''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[conjuration]] spell that brought one or more creatures from the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]] to assist the caster. The reverse of this spell, '''''dismiss fire elemental''''', sent the summoned creatures back home.

==History==
