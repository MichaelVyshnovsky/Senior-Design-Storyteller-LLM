Beguiling rebuke was a unique enchantment charm spell from the sixth circle of magic used in the late 15 century DR.
The beguiling rebuke spell was cast as a reaction, mere moments after the caster was attacked. Once activated, the rebuked assailant and their nearby allies had a nigh chance of temporarily becoming beguiled. This charming effect, if successful, stopped the affected creatures from attacking the caster for twelve seconds. This magical rebuke had the range of . The effects of beguiling rebuke could not be strengthened by a casting at a higher proficiency level.
This unique sell was known and was used by the cambion spawn of Mephistopheles, Raphael during the Absolute crisis of the , were he to stand against the Absolute Adversaries during their foray into the House of Hope in Avernus.
