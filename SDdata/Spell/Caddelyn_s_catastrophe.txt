'''''Caddelyn's catastrophe''''' was a variation of the [[spell]] ''[[fumble]]'', developed by the [[mage]] [[Caddelyn]], that affected up to four creatures.

==Effects==
The spell could have a number of effects: the targets would either fall and drop all their belongings, trip and stumble, or fail if they were in the middle of spellcasting.

==Components==
The material components for this spell were a [[banana]] peel or a melon rind, and a [[candle]] made from tallow or a simple lump of animal fat.

==History==
This spell was originally inscribed in the Caddelyn's [[Caddelyn's Workbook|personal spellbook]].
