Stainless was a forge chant employed in the making of blueshine items by dwarves.
The chant modified an item made of metal to render it completely immune to rust, corrosion, acid, and decay. Even drow equipment gained greater resistance to sunlight when so treated. This chant was a prerequisite for the standard blueshine application around .
Besides the chanting common to all forge chants, it required a mass of platinum identical to that of the object to protect.
