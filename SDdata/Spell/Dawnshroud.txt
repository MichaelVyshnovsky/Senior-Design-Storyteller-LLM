'''''Dawnshroud''''' was a spell used by [[cleric]]s of [[Lathander]].

==Effects==
This spell was similar to the ''[[daylight]]'' spell. When cast, a targeted creature began glowing with the rose light of a new dawn. The light emitted by the spells was harmful to [[ooze]]s and the [[undead]]. Any creature within {{SI|60|ft|long=yes}} could be affected by the light from the spell.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required a [[divine focus]].

==History==
While ''dawnshroud'' was developed by clerics of Lathander, the spell was made avaible to be used by those outside the clergy.
