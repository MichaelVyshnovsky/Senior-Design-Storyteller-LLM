'''''Striking shadows''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[conjuration]] [[spell]] used by priests devoted to [[Mask]], enabling them to create a pair of tentacled shadows.

==Effects==
This spell created a pair of tentacled shadow monsters that resembled [[grell]]. The creatures would attack any targets nearby who weren't wearing a symbol of Mask.

==Components==
This spell required only [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s to cast.
