Striking shadows was a divine conjuration spell used by priests devoted to Mask, enabling them to create a pair of tentacled shadows.
This spell created a pair of tentacled shadow monsters that resembled grell. The creatures would attack any targets nearby who werent wearing a symbol of Mask.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
