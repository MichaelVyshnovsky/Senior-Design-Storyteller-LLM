Earth anchor was a spell used by clerics of Urogalan.
This spell magically anchored creatures to the ground. It could be cast up to  from the caster and affected an area with a  radius. Creatures in the area affect could not lift whatever appendage was in physical contact with the ground, only slowly shuffle along in a sliding motion. A creature leaving the area of effect could once again move normally. This spell only affected living and corporeal creatures.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material components the holy symbol of Urogalan and some mud.
