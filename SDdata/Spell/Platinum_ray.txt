'''''Platinum ray''''' was a spell used against evil [[dragon]]s. 

==Effects==
When cast, a radiant platinum ray, representing the strength of good dragons, shot out from the caster to a target. When used against an evil dragon, the spell suppressed their innate magical abilities. 

==Components==
This spell required [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components. [[Cleric]]s of [[Bahamut]] were capable of casting this spell at a stronger level.
