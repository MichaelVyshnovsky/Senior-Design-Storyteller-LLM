'''''Disarm''''' was an [[evocation]] [[spell]] that caused someone to drop any hand-held weapons or items they were carrying.

==Effects==
When cast, ''disarm'' caused a minor telekinetic burst that knocked any items from the hands of the affected individual, launching them a number of feet away. This spell had no effects on [[ring]]s or any other worn items that were not specifically carried.

==Usage==
In the city of [[Waterdeep]], this spell was commonly taught to members of [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]].

==Components==
This spell only required [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s.
