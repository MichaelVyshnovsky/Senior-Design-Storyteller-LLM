'''Thoaloat's Variant''' was an alteration to a [[spell]] taught by the [[Srinshee]] to [[Elminster Aumar]].

==Effects==
Thoaloat's Variant converted a given spell's effect into one that allowed the caster to change a [[tree]]'s shape.

==Components==
The [[verbal component]] had a limerick-like rhyme.

==History==
The [[Chosen of Mystra]] [[Elminster Aumar]] accidentally cast this spell in his exhaustion along the way between [[Moonshorn Tower]] and the [[Riven Stone]] in the {{yearlinkName|761}}.
