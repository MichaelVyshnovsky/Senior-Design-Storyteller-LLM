'''''Fire storm''''' was an [[evocation]] spell that filled  an area with sheets of flame.

==Effect==
This spell blasted all creatures within the area with fire similar to a ''[[wall of fire]]'', causing damage for a short time.

Some older versions had a reverse the spell, '''''[[fire quench]]''''', that extinguished an equal area of magical fire, or double the area of normal fire.

==Components==
This spell required [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[Somatic component]]s.

==Realmslore==
The [[arcane]] version of ''fire storm'' was originally common only to the [[Red Wizards of Thay]].
