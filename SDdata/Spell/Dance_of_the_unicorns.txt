Dance of the unicorns was a divine alteration spell used by specialty priests devoted to Lurue called Silvermaids.
This spell allowed the recipient to teleport without error to any location within , including up to  of equipment, every minute for six minutes. The movement of the teleport could occur whenever the recipient wanted, making it resembled the blink spell.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components the priestesss holy symbol and a vial of clear water from a river stirred by a unicorns horn to cast.
