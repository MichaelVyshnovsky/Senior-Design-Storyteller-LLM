'''''Burning talons''''' was a spell known to the [[archdevil]] [[Harhoring]].

==Effect==
Disembodied talons would swirl into existence at the beginning of this spell.

==History==
The spell was used by [[Harhoring]] against [[the Simbul]] in {{yearlink|1372}}, during her scouring of [[Avernus]] in search of [[Elminster]]. He employed it to augment his own talons.
