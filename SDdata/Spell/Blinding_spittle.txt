'''''Blinding spittle''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[druid]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, the spell turned the caster's saliva into a caustic liquid. The caster then spat into the eyes of a targeted creature. The caster was able to strike a creature within {{SI|25|ft}}, this range increased with the caster's skill level. The caustic salivia only harmed the eyes of a creature and had no effect if used on a creature without eyes.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
