Sakkratars triple strike was a transmutation spell that gave the target increased speed in combat allowing extra attacks. The spell also gave the targets weapon a special property Keen and caused it to be covered in pure white flame.
This spell was created by a Coronal of Cormanthyr, presumably Sakkratar.
