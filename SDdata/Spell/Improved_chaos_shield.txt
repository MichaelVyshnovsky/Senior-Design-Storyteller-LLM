'''''Improved chaos shield''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[wild mage]]s.

==Effects==
A wild mage used this spell to improve their casting results during a [[Wild surge|surge of magical energy]]. It was often cast after the ''[[Nahal's reckless dweomer]]'' spell.
