Air bubble was a spell that provided a breath of fresh air.
When cast, this spell created a sphere of air around a targeted creatures head. The caster could choose any creature they could see, up to a maximum range of . The amount of globes of fresh air created by the spell was determined by the casters skill level.
This spell required only a somatic component.
