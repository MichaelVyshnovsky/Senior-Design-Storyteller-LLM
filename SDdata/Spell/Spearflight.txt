'''''Spearflight''''' was a spell unique to [[Maztica]].

==Effects==
This spell allowed a spearman to hurl [[javelin]]s or [[spear]]s farther and with greater force. When cast, any person within 30 yards (27 meters) of the caster gained this ability.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[material component]]: a beak from a [[hawk]].
