'''''Thunderball''''' was an uncommon [[evocation]] spell of the [[southern magic]] tradition.{{Southernmagic}}

==Effects==
The spell created an explosive blast of lightning followed by a clap of thunder. It caused damage to all creatures within 20 feet (6 meters), the thunder causing hearing damage to some. A single creature could be targeted, and they were automatically deafened as well as taking twice the damage.   
 
==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], an [[amber]] rod.
