'''''Draconic transformation''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[druid]]s, [[sorcerer]]s, and [[wizard]]s to give themselves [[dragon]]-like features. 

==Effects==
This spell transformed its caster so that they gained dragon-like traits. They gained the ability to see in total darkness and detect invisible things, to breathe out a blast of energy, and to conjure incorporeal wings for flight. 

==Components==
The spell required [[verbal]], [[somatic]], and [[material component]]s. The material required was a statuette of a dragon worth at least 500 [[gp]]
