Slow cold was a spell devised by Nerren Prentiyuel before his time as the Magister.
The spell allowed for merchants to ship large quantities of chilled foodstuffs without spoilage, presumably by cooling down the food.The effect is gleaned and deduced from the title the spell in this article is assumed to be a cold-based evocation that uses cold to preserve food.
The spell was developed by Nerren Prentiyuel prior to . He made a fortune from it it was both lost and superseded by the 14 century DR.
