'''''Advanced illusion''''' was an [[arcane magic|arcane]] [[illusion]] [[spell]] that was similar to an improved version of the ''[[spectral force]]'' spell.

==Effects==
The illusion created by this spell operated on a program chosen by the caster. The illusion was complete with audio, visual, thermal, and olfactory components.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s some [[sand]] and a bit of [[fleece]] to cast.

==History==
''Advanced illusion'' was created by [[Toscudlo]], a [[Netherese]] [[Arcanist]], in {{YearlinkConvert|2359|NY}}.
