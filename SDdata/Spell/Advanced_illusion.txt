Advanced illusion was an arcane illusion spell that was similar to an improved version of the spectral force spell.
The illusion created by this spell operated on a program chosen by the caster. The illusion was complete with audio, visual, thermal, and olfactory components.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as material components some sand and a bit of fleece to cast.
