critical word of nurturing was a very advanced utterance from the Lexicon of the Evolving Mind that used truespeak to rejuvenate a target from its wounds, or to rend them apart utterly, if reversed.
In its straight form, healing energy suffused the target, slowly repairing them of a massive amount of damage. In its reversed form, it rended and tore at the target it could last for an instant, but with some concentration, damage would be doubled after a few seconds.
The recitation was known to logokron devils.
