Resistance was an abjuration cantrip that made a creature more resistant to harmful effects.
Target creatures were imbued with magical energy that served to protect them from harm.
In addition to verbal and somatic component, the arcane version required a miniature cloak as a material component, while the divine version required a divine focus.
