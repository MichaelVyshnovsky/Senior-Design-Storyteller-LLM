'''''Vision of glory''''' was a [[spell]] used to inspire creatures.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell gave a targeted creature a short glimpse of a [[deity]] or divine creature showing support towards them and their cause.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[divine focus]].
