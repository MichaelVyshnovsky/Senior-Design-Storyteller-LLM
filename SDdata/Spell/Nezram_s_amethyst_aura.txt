Nezrams amethyst aura was an abjuration spell shielding the subject from poison and disease. It was presumably developed by Nezram.
The subject of Nezrams amethyst aura was encompassed by a bright violet light that blocked all poisons and diseases from penetrating, whether by a weapon or a creatures touch or bite. It did not however cure such things already affecting the target.
The spell required a pinch of amethyst dust.
