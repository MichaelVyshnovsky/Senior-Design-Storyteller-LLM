'''''Enchanted hammer''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Laduguer]].

==Effects==
This spell was used to enchant a smithy's hammer. The hammer had to made from either [[mithral]] or [[silver]]. When this hammer was used the make armor or metal weapons such as swords, said items became stronger than if forged using a regular hammer. The spell allowed the smithy to make either one full suit of armor, two large weapons or four smaller weapons such as daggers  or arrow heads.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], the holy symbol of Laduguer.
