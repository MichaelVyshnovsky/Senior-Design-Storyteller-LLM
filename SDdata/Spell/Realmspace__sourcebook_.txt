Realmspace is an accessory for the Spelljammer setting using the Advanced Dungeons & Dragons 2-edition ruleset.
Realmspace describes the area of space near the planet Toril. The book describes the sun, as well as the planets Anadia, Coliar, Toril, Karpri, Chandos, Glyth, Garden, HCatha, as well as Elminsters Hideout. The book also details new magical items, new monsters, and new spelljamming ships.
