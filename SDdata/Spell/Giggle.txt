Giggle was a person-affecting cantrip that beginning wizards could learn. It caused a target creature to suffer a brief fit of laughter.
This cantrip had a range of  and could target a single person or creature. If the target did not resist the spell, then it broke out in laughter for a period of two to three seconds before they could recover. If the spell was resisted, only a momentary chuckle ensued.
This cantrip required verbal and somatic components to cast. The caster wiggled a finger rapidly back and forth while uttering a word or phrase.The verbal component of this cantrip was known as a "punch line".
