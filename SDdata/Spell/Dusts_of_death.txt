'''''Dusts of death''''' was a [[spell]] unique to [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.

==Effects==
This spelled turned dust or [[sand]] into a deadly [[poison]]. The poison was undetectable, but casting ''[[detect magic]]'' revealed a magical nature about the dust.

If consumed, this dust instantly killed the consumer. The only trace of foul play would be a bit of dust around the victim's lips and nostrils.

Many sorcerous societies in Zakhara used this spell as their prime method of assassination.

==Components==
This spell required a pinch of sand or dust to cast.
