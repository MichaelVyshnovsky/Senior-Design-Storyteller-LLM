'''''War god's blessing''''' was a ''[[channel divinity]]'' power granted to [[cleric]]s of the [[War domain]].

==Effects==
A cleric with the War domain could use this power to infuse a physical strike of an ally with supernatural accuracy.

==Components==
Only the cleric's skill was needed to evoke this power.
