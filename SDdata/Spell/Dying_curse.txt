'''''Dying curse''''' was a [[Protection sphere|protection]] spell of the [[southern magic]] tradition that allowed a caster to have vengeance after death.{{Southernmagic}}

==Effects==
Anyone who killed the caster would suffer the effects of a ''[[major curse]]'' spell. The spell took effect even if the caster was later resurrected. The curse was permanent until it was removed.

The only way to remove the curse was by a ''[[wish]]'', ''[[remove bane]]'', or ''[[remove major curse]]'' spell.

==Components==
This spell required verbal and [[somatic component]]s.
