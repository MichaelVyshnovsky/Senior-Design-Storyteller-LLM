'''''Spider poison''''' was a [[necromancy]] [[spell]] used by [[wizard]]s, [[sorcerer]]s and [[assassin]]s.

==Effects==
This spell allowed the caster to inject a target with a paralyzing poison if they were able to touch them. The poison quickly sapped the target's physical strength.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required a live or dead [[spider]] to cast.
