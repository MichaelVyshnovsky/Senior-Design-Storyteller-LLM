'''''Shield of Lathander''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Lathander]].

==Effects==
When cast, this spell protected the caster or a targeted individual from physical attacks by [[magic weapon|magical weapons]]. The protected individual was still vulnerable to the magical enhancement of magical weapons.

==Components==
This spell required only [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components.

==History==
In the month of [[Eleasis]], of the {{YearlinkName|1371}}, [[Chief Prelate]] [[Relarn Dayspring|Dayspring]] of the [[Clerical Circle]] of [[Ravens Bluff]] declared it illegal for priests to cast ''shield of Lathander'' within city limits due to a raging unnatural storm at the time called the [[Soulstorm]], threatening arrest for those that broke this decree.{{Cite trumpeter/2-8|2}}{{LCRB}}
