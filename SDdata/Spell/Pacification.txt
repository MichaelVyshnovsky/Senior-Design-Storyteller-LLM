'''''Pacification''''' was a spell used to calm a creature. 

==Effects==
When cast, the caster breathed out a calming, gentle wind. A single creature within {{SI|25|ft|long=yes}} of the caster was then calmed, as if the subject of a ''[[calm emotions]]'' spell. The spell did not function on creatures more powerful than the caster.

==Components==
This spell required [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components. If, during casting, the caster tossed blue [[spinel]] dust into the calming breeze, the spell last two minutes longer, with an additional two minutes per skill level.
