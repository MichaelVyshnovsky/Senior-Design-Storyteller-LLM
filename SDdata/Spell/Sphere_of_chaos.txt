Sphere of chaos was a spell used by wizards.
When cast, this spell created chaotic and random magical effects within a sphere that had a  radius. Everyone within the spherical area suffered from random magical effects, which ranged from minor such as being healed or being struck with confusion, to extreme, such as bursting into flames or total disintegration.
