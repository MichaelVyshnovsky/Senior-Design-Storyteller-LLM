'''''Life drain''''' was a [[divine magic|divine]] [[necromancy]] [[spell]] from the [[necromantic sphere]] that allowed the caster to strengthen a target's life force at the expense of another creature.

==Effects==
The amount of vitality drained with this spell depended on the skill of the caster and could be used by themselves or another target. ''Life drain'' did not function upon [[undead]] in any manner.

==Components==
This spell required only [[verbal component]]s to cast.
