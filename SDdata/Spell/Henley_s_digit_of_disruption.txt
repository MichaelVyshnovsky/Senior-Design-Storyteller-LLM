'''''Henley's digit of disruption''''' was a [[divine magic|divine]] [[evocation]] [[spell]] from the [[creation sphere]] that smited a target with holy energy.

==Effects==
This spell was used specifically against the [[undead]] and creatures of the [[Negative Energy plane]]. The casting [[priest]] gathered holy [[positive energy]] and released it through their fingertip, destroying the target creature. This spell did not harm [[demipower]]s.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s the caster's [[holy symbol]] and a powdered [[sunstone (gem)|sunstone]] worth at least 500 [[gp]] to cast.

==History==
This rare spell was known to be found within the ''[[Demonomicon of Iggwilv]]''.
