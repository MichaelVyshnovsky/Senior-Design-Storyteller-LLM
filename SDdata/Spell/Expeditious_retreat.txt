'''''Expeditious retreat''''' was an [[evocation]] [[spell]] that made the caster faster.
==Effect==
The caster became a lot quicker being able to walk, run or shift doubly as fast as normal.
The length of the spell's effect varied depending on what type of magical consistency was in the world.
Before the [[Spellplague]] it lasted longer depending on skill
==Components==
Only vocal and somatic components where needed.
