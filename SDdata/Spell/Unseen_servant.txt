An unseen servant was a conjuration spell that performed simple tasks at its users orders.
As the name suggested, unseen servant created an invisible mindless servant that could be ordered around for anything except attacking something or someone. It was physically weak and could only lift up to 20 pounds (9 kilogram), but could drag up to 100 pounds (45 kilogram). If brought outside of the spells range, the servant disappeared.
This spell was created by the arcanist Carbury in  and was originally known as Carburys servant.
Unseen servant was an uncommon spell in the Realms by the mid-14 century DR. In the month of Eleasis, of the , the city of Ravens Bluffs Ministry of Art and Wizards Guild jointly declared it illegal to cast unseen servant within city limits due to a raging unnatural storm at the time called the Soulstorm. In addition to threatening arrest for those that broke this decree, it was declared that breaking this prohibition would result in a permanent expulsion from either organization.
Drow house insignia tokens typically contained this spell.
