Insulation was a spell unique to Halruaa.
Insulation provided temporary resistance to electrical damage to the caster.
This spell required 1" (2.54 cm) square piece of rubber to cast. The rubber needed to be placed between the casters foot and the ground and remain in contact with the casters skin at all times for the spell to work.
