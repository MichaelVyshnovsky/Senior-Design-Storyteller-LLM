}}'''''Mechanical disruption''''' was a [[spell]] unique to [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.

==Effects==
This spell served as a paralysis spell when cast at mechanical creations, keeping them from moving or performing any function until the spell ended.

If placed within a mechanical upon its creation, there was a chance that the creation would fall to pieces.

==Components==
This spell did not require physical components to cast.
