'''''Fire burst''''' was an [[alteration]] and [[evocation]] [[spell]] used by [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
When cast upon any nonmagical flame, this spell caused a single burst of small fire arrows to lance from the flame, striking any creatures found within {{SI|10|ft|long=yes}} of the flame.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components to cast.
