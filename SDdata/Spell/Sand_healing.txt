'''''Sand healing''''' was a spell used by used by [[wizard]]s in [[Anauroch]]. It was extremely rare outside Anauroch.

==Effects==
The spell allowed a caster to heal wounds using [[sand]]. There were limitations to the spell. The caster was not able to use this spell to cure blindness, [[disease]]s or infections, mental ailments, or poison. 

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, the spell required several [[material component]]s. The caster needed to mix sand, water, and blood before application to a wound. If the caster did not have access to water, then saliva or tears were an acceptable alternative. Likewise, if the caster did not have access to blood, then sap from a plant was an acceptable alternative.
