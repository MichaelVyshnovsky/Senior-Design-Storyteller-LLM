'''''Summon nature's ally VI''''' was one of the series of ''[[summon nature's ally]]'' spells available to [[druid]]ic spellcasters.

==Effects==
This spell functioned just like all ''summon nature's ally'' spells, which in turn resembled the ''[[summon monster]]'' spells. It could summon a single [[dire bear]], huge [[elemental]] of any type, [[elephant]], [[girallon]], [[megaraptor]], giant [[octopus]], [[pixie]] (which favored casting ''[[Otto's irresistible dance]]''), [[salamander]], [[whale|baleen whale]], or [[xorn]]. It could also be used to summon a larger number of monsters from a lower level of a spell in the series.

==Components==
The spell required a [[divine focus]] and both [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s.
