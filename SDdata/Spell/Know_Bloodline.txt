'''''Know bloodline''''' is a [[divination]] spell available to [[cleric]]s. By casting this spell a cleric was able to determine the ancestry and racial type of the target.

==History==
Know bloodline was commonly used by [[Mulhorandi]] priests to determine the worthiness of Mulan humans following the path to becoming a [[glorious servitor]].
