}}'''''Protection from paralysis''''' was an [[abjuration]] [[spell]] used by [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
This spell offered complete protection to the recipient from all forms of magical paralysis. In addition to protecting from spells such as ''[[hold person]]'', the spell also protected against similar attacks from monsters such as a [[ghoul]]'s paralyzing touch. The spell offered no protection from physical harm from monster attacks.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required a bit of cloth from a [[cleric]]'s robes to cast.

==History==
This spell was originally developed in the [[Underdark]], but later on found usage by some on the surface.<ref>
