Talonas blessing was a divine abjuration necromancy spell used by priests devoted to Talona, enabling them to grant a touched target immunity to all poisons and diseases.
For this spell to work the caster had to touch the targets skin. The caster could also use this spell on themselves.
This spell granted temporary immunity to all poisons, venoms, gases, and diseases both normal and magical, including such things as lycanthropy and mummy rot. The spell also killed or expelled any parasites within the targets body as well as any molds, mildews, or fungi.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
