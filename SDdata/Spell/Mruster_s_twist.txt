Mrusters twist was a spell.
The spell changed incoming spells into other spells, and allowed its caster to redirect the latter.
By , Jhalavans fond return had been modified to create Mrusters Twist. Elminster Aumar, a new human armathor of Cormanthor, used the spell in his duel against Delmuth Echorn. He used it to convert a spell akin to acid rain into a dispel magic to strike at his opponents mantle.
