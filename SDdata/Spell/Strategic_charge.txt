'''''Strategic charge''''' was an [[abjuration]] spell that let one dodge blows better while armored. Originally, it was unique to [[blackguard]]s and [[paladin]]s of the [[Red Knight]],

==Effects==
For a few seconds (longer for more experienced knights), the caster was able to move into or out of combat with less chance of injury from opponents taking the opportunity that such a move granted.

==Components==
Only a verbal component and the use of the caster's [[holy symbol]] or [[divine focus]] were needed to cast this spell.
