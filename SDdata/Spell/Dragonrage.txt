Dragonrage was a powerful arcane elven high magic spell that temporarily granted the caster the powers of a dragon.
When this spell was cast the caster was given the powers of a metallic dragon of random type and age. Though the casters appearance did not change, they gained access to the selected dragons entire range of abilities including flight, breath weapon, fear aura, etc. The caster also gained the normal personality traits applicable for the selected dragon. This spell lasted for several hours depending on the skill of the caster.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
This spell was used by elves to protect homelands such as Evermeet.
