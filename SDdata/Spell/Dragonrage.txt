'''''Dragonrage''''' was a powerful [[Arcane magic|arcane]] [[elven high magic]] [[spell]] that temporarily granted the caster the powers of a [[dragon]].

==Effects==
When this spell was cast the caster was given the powers of a [[metallic dragon]] of random type and age. Though the caster's appearance did not change, they gained access to the selected dragon's entire range of abilities including flight, breath weapon, fear aura, etc. The caster also gained the normal personality traits applicable for the selected dragon. This spell lasted for several hours depending on the skill of the caster.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s to cast.

==History==
This spell was used by elves to protect homelands such as [[Evermeet]].
