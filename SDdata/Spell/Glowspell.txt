Glowspell was a spell that summoned forth a drifting cloud radiance that remained lit for a set period of time.
Glowspells were a good alternative no regular and enchanted oil lamps, providing gentle illumination when conjured.
The spell was known as far back as the . A Banite sorceress Dasumia of Galadorna, who once tutored Elminster Aumar, used glowspells to illuminate her home and its balconies.
As of the late 14 century DR, drifting glowspell clouds were used to illuminate the streets of Athkatlas glittering Gem District. like many other cities, Athkatla did not used ensorcelled oil lamps or magical globes of light.
