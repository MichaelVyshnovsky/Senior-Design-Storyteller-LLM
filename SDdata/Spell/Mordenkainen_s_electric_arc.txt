Mordenkainens electric arc was an uncommon spell that caused arcs of electrical energy to erupt from the casters fingers. 
The caster was able to target a single creature or multiple at once. The number of arcs created was determined by the casters skill level. The caster was only able to direct a single arc to each target creature.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required material components a rod made of glass and a piece of fur.
