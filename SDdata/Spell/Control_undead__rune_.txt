'''''Control undead''''' was a type of [[giant]] rune.

==Effects==
Once activated this rune allowed the caster to control nearby [[undead]] creatures.

==Components==
Like most giant runes, it required no [[material component]]s, though the caster needed to know the rune ''[[blinding light]]'' in order to cast it. The process of shaping this rune could take anywhere from one to eight hours.
