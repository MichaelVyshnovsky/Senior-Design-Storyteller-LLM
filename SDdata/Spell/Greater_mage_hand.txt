'''''Greater mage hand''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[sorcerer]]s and [[wizard]]s.

==Effects==
During casting, the caster pointed at a creature or object and move it {{SI|20|feet|long=yes}} in any direction. A creature with strong enough spell resistance could negate this spell. The target cannot be moved beyond the range of the spell. The spell was able to perform delicate tasks.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
