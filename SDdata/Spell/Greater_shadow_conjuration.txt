'''''Greater shadow conjuration''''', also known as '''''demi-shadow monsters'''''<ref> (or '''''demishadow monsters''''' and '''''demishadow monster'''''), was an [[illusion]] [[spell]] that created semi-real duplicates of [[conjuration]] spells.

==Effects==
This spell was a stronger version of ''[[shadow conjuration]]''. It allowed the duplication of stronger spells and was more powerful, even when seen through as an illusion.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
