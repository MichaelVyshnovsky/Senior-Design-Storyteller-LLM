Haelas battle blessing was a spell used by clerics of Haela Brightaxe.
The caster used this spell to provide aid to a comrade during combat. The targeted individuals melee attack against a creature significantly improved.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required a material component, the holy symbol of Haela Brightaxe.
