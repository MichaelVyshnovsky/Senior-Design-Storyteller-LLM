'''''Rising rot''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[necromancy]] and [[evocation]] [[spell]] used by priests devoted to [[Moander]], enabling them to infects others with rot.

==Effects==
This spell shot a beam of brown energy from the caster's palm to a target, infecting them with mold that sucked moisture and nutrients from their flesh. The mold would continue to infect the target until cured with a ''[[cure disease]]'' spell or the target's death. This spell had no effect on the [[undead]], incorporeal creatures, or creatures that lacked body tissues.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as a [[material component]] a handful of mold spores to cast.
