Net of shadows was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.
With this spell, a caster could blind a creature by using ordinary shadows. The shadows flew to the creature and wrapped them in a net of darkness. Within this net, the creature was unable to see anything beyond . Darkvision was rendered useless. Additionally, the creature was disoriented while within the net. The net of shadow did not hinder the creatures movement.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
