'''''Dispel mirage''''' was a [[spell]] unique to [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.

==Effects==
This spell created a magical field capable of destroying false images including mirages, illusions, etc. Natural invisibility and higher powered illusion spells were not affected by this spell.

==Components==
This spell required a handful of [[sand]] to cast.
