'''''Surefoot''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[ranger]]s.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell allowed the caster to increase their dexterity and balance. It granted them the ability to traverse even the most dangerous paths with ease.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
