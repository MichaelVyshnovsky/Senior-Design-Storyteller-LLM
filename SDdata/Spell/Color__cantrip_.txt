'''''Color''''' was a useful [[cantrip]] that beginning [[wizard]]s could learn.{{Cite dragon/59/Cantrips: Minor magics for would-be wizards|6}} It temporarily altered or enhanced the hue of one object, and could be used on hair or skin.

==Effects==
The target of this spell had to be within {{SI|30|ft|long=yes}} of the caster and the effects lasted thirty days. This cantrip could change the hue of one object no larger than {{SI|1|cu yd|long=yes}} and also worked on hair or the skin of one creature. Faded colors, such as dyed cloth or pigments in a painting, could be brought back to vibrancy, or a completely new color could be applied.

==Components==
The [[verbal component]] of this cantrip was a hum and [[somatic component]] could be either waving a hand back and forth as if painting, or making a wringing motion with both hands.
