'''''Airy water''''' was an [[arcane magic|arcane]] [[alteration]] [[spell]] that allowed the caster to transform liquids into breathable substances.

==Effects==
This spell transformed liquids such as water into breathable substances. The affected area was usually a bubble of airy water around the caster that moved with them. Water-breathing creatures could not breath or swim within the bubble.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required as a [[material component]] a handful of [[alkaline salt]]s or [[bromine salt]]s to cast.

==History==
''Airy water'' was invented in {{Yearlink|-531}} by the [[Netherese arcanist]] [[Fjord (arcanist)|Fjord]] with the name ''Fjord's distortion''.<ref name=NEoM2e-p23,28" /> This spell was used commonly by [[noble marid]]s.
