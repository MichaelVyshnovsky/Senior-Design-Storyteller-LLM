Blackmantle was an arcane spell from the schools of necromancy and enchantment that allowed a wizard to halt a targets healing.
This spell created a shimmering aura around all targets within , preventing them from healing or regenerating via normal or magical means for the spells duration.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell required as a material component a small mummified animal to cast. Toads and mice were commonly used.
This spell was found within the Book of Shadows, a tome based upon the oration of the necromancer Nebt Bhakau of Zakhara.
