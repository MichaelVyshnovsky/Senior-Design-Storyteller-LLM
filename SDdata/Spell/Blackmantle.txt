'''''Blackmantle''''' was an [[Arcane magic|arcane]] [[spell]] from the schools of [[necromancy]] and [[enchantment]] that allowed a [[wizard]] to halt a target's healing.

==Powers==
This spell created a shimmering aura around all targets within {{SI|15|ft|long=yes}}, preventing them from healing or regenerating via normal or magical means for the spell's duration.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as a [[material component]] a small mummified animal to cast. [[Toad]]s and [[mice]] were commonly used.

==History==
This spell was found within the ''[[Book of Shadows]]'', a [[tome]] based upon the oration of the [[necromancer]] [[Nebt Bhakau]] of [[Zakhara]].
