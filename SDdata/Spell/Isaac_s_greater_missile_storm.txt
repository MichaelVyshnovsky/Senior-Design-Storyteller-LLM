Isaacs greater missile storm was a spell used by sorcerers and wizards.
When cast, this spell created several missiles of energy. The caster was able to direct these missiles to multiple targets. Up to ten missiles could strike a target at a time. The number of energy missiles created was determined by the casters skill level.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
