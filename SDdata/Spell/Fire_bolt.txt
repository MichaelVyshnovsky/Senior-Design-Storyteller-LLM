'''''Fire bolt''''' was an [[evocation]] [[spell]] that cast a mote of flame at a target.

==Effect==
The spell could target any creature or object within a range of {{SI|120|ft|long=yes}}. It was very simple to cast and caused little damage, but it increased in power with the caster's experience.

==Components==
The spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s.
