'''''Greater shield of Lathander''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Lathander]].

==Effects==
When cast, this spell protected the caster or a targeted individual from magical and physical attacks as well as cancelling the effects of any compulsion on an individual, such as ''[[Geas/Quest|geas]]'' and [[psionic]] control.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
