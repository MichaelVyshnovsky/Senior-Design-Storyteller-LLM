'''''Symbol of insanity''''' was a [[necromancy]] spell that created a powerful rune that could drive one or more creatures insane when triggered.

==Notable Users==
Inside the [[Jade Palace of the Necromancers|Jade Palace]] of [[Ysawis]], the treasury room's thresold was warded with ''symbols of insanity'' that would discharge upon being approached within {{SI|5|ft|long=yes}}.<ref>
