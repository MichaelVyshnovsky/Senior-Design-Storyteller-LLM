'''''Sleep''''' was a type of [[giant]] rune that functioned similarly to the ''[[sleep]]'' [[spell]].

==History==
In [[1366 DR]],{{Cite web/Presenting...Seven Millennia of Realms Fiction}} seeking a way to delay the interference of [[Morten]] and the earls of [[Hartsvale]] in his group's rescue of [[Brianna Burdun|Princess Brianna]], the [[runecaster]] [[Basil (verbeeg)|Basil]] suggested and began work on a ''sleep'' rune.
