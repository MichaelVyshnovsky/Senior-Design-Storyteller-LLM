Protection from magical traps, 5 radius was a spell used by the Red Wizards of Thay
When cast, this spell protected the caster from magic-based traps. It created a small area of protection around the caster, 5 feet (1.5 meters) in radius. This protected area moved with the caster. The spell was unable to detect the presence of a magical trap.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, the spell required a material component, a small amount of axle grease.
