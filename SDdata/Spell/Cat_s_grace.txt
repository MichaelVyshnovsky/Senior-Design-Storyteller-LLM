Cats grace was a transmutation spell that increased the chance of performing any skill requiring dexterity.
The caster had to touch the target of this spell in order to cast it. The recipient was granted a few minutes of increased agility, coordination, and grace.
In addition to verbal and somatic components, this spell require a few hairs of cat fur.
