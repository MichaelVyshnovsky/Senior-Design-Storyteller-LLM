Bils fantastic ale was a little known spell of sardonic nature created by the wizard Bil long before 1261 DR.
Bils fantastic ale was developed by a wizard of small repute Bil, who was mostly known for creation a highly sough after jade shield ring. He disappeared from the Realms in 1261 DR with all of his belongings and works scavenged by his acquaintances of ill repute and sold off to the highest bidder.
