'''''Improved mind mantle''''' was a [[spell]] that was commonly used by [[alhoon]]s.

==Effects==
This was a more powerful version of ''[[mind mantle]]''. This spell protected the caster's or targeted creature's mind against charm and mind-reading spells. It worked by covering the thoughts of the creature with random thoughts. The spell also protected against [[magical item]]s and [[psionic]] influences. While protected, the targeted creature was able to cast spells. The spell was vulnerable to ''[[dispel magic]]''.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
