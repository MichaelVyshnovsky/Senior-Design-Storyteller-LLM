Insatiable thirst was an enchantment spell used by wizards.
The victim of this spell believed they were about to die of thirst and was struck with an uncontrollable urge to drink, driving them to seek potable fluids to consume with the utmost haste. The victims thirst was not satisfied, regardless of how much fluid they drank, until the spell ended.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components to cast.
