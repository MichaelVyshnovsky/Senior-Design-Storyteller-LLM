Fiendish quickening was a transmutation spell.
Fiendish quickening enhanced the casters inherent ability to cast teleport and/or greater teleport. These could be cast more swiftly, as though they were under the effect of metamagic.
The casting of fiendish quickening required somatic and verbal components. Only fiends could cast this spell.
*Asmodeus the archdevil could use fiendish quickening as an inherent ability.
*Baalzebul, the archdevil could use fiendish quickening as an inherent ability.
*Mephistopheles, the archdevil could use fiendish quickening as an inherent ability.
