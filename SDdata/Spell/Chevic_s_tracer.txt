'''''Chevic's tracer''''' was a spell used by [[Netheril|Netherese]] [[arcanist]]s.

==Effects==
The spell was designed to track a target. To cast the spell, the arcanist had to be within sight of the target. Once cast, the caster was able to ''[[teleport]]'' up to 10 feet (3 meters) from the target. The spell was ideal for bounty hunting.

==Components==
In addition to a [[Verbal component|verbal]] component, the spell required a [[material component]]: a [[lodestone]] shaped as a cube.

==History==
The spell was invented by Netherese arcanist and bounty hunter [[Chevic]].
