'''''Dispel law''''' was an [[abjuration]] spell that worked exactly like ''[[dispel evil]]'' except it affected lawful spells and creatures instead of evil ones.

==Effects==
The effects of this spell were the same as ''[[dispel evil]]'' except the aura surrounding the spellcaster was yellow, flickered like a candle, and made of chaotic energy.

==Components==
Verbal and somatic components were required to cast this spell plus the caster's (un)[[holy symbol]] or [[divine focus]].
