Detect thoughts, also known as ESP,
The caster was able to sense the surface thoughts of beings within range. How long they examined an area or subjected decided what kind of information was received. It could penetrate barriers, but  of stone,  of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or  of wood or dirt blocked it.

This spell could be used for interrogation and also to probe deeper into the targets mind. The spell could not detect or read the mind of very unintelligent creatures or creatures that did not speak any language. The spell reached 30 feet, and lasted 1 minute but could not penetrate  of wood or dirt,  of stone,  of regular metals, or a thin layer of lead.
The spell required verbal, somatic, and material components. The material required was a copper piece.
