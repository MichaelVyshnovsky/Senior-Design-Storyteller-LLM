Enhance ability was a transmutation spell that would enhance a targets abilities for up to a hour with effects generalized from the six famous improvement spells used by adventurers.
The caster would pick an effect that would grant improvements to the target in a predetermined way
* Bears Endurance The target was granted improved constitution and health.
* Bulls Strength The target gained an improvement to its strength and also was able to carry twice as much as before.
* Cats Grace The target gained an improvement to its dexterity and could fall from a height of 20 feet without getting hurt.
* Eagles Splendor The target would gain an increased advantage from its charisma.
* Foxs Cunning The target gained advantages to attempts that used its intelligence.
* Owls Wisdom The target would gain benefits to using wisdom.
For every spell level higher then the normal spell level used, another target could gain a benefit of this spell.
Verbal and somatic components were needed, as well as a feather or fur from a beast as a material component.
