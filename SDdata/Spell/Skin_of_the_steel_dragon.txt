'''''Skin of the steel dragon''''' was a type of [[abjuration]] spell.
 
==Effects==
When cast, this spell increased the caster's resistance to damage.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], either a holy symbol of [[Mystra]] made from [[steel]] or a seven-pointed star also made from steel.
