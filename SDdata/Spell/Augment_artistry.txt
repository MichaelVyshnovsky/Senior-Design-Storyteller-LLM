'''''Augment artistry''''' was a [[spell]] used by [[cleric]]s of [[Corellon Larethian]].

==Effects==
When cast, this spell temporarily enhanced the artistic skill of a targeted creature. Any skill that was generally considered artistic, such as painting, dancing, [[gem]] cutting, ''etc.'', was enhanced by this spell.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required as [[material component]]s a [[holy symbol]] of Corellon Larethian and a [[scroll]] that contained a poem.
