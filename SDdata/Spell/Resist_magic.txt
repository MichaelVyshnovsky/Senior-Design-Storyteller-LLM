Resist magic was a southern magic divine spell available to worshipers of Thoth.
When cast, this spell imbued the caster with a measure of magical resistance. The strength of the magical resistance was determined by the casters skill level. The caster could only use this spell on themselves.
This spell required only somatic and verbal components.
