'''''Purify self''''', also known as '''''cleanse''''', was a [[divine]] [[alteration]] [[spell]] that cleansed the caster's body and unsoiled their clothes.

==Effects==
When cast, this removed all of the dirt and filth from the caster's person and their apparel. Their clothes were made immaculate and any injuries they had became free of infection.

==Components==
This spell required [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s as well as a small piece of soap.
