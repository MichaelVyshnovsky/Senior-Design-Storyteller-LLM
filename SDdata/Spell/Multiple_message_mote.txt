'''''Multiple message mote''''' was an improved version of the [[spell]] ''[[message mote]]''.

==Effects==
This spell created a ''message mote'', as per the spell of the same name, with two significant improvements. First, the ''mote'' could be re-activated every time the caster's specifications were met. Second, the ''mote'' could utter a second, shorter message that would only be released in the presence of a [[Tyr]]ran clergy member.

==Components==
''Multiple message mote'' had both a [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]].

==History==
Along with ''message mote'', this spell appeared on one of the [[scroll]]s found within the ''[[Balance of Belaros]]''.
