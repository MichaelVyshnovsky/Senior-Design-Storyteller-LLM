'''''Mass healing word''''' was an [[evocation]] [[spell]] that could remotely mend the wounds of multiple people.

==Effects==
The spell provided minor healing for up to six creatures within a range of {{SI|60|ft|long=yes}} from the caster. The healing power increased with the caster's ability.

==Components==
The spell required only [[verbal component]]s.
