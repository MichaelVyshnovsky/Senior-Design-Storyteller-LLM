'''''Negative energy burst''''' was a [[spell]] that dealt negative energy damage.

==Effects==
When cast, this spell created a burst of negative energy. This burst was large, affecting an area with a {{SI|22|ft}} radius. Any creature within this area were weakened. [[Undead]] creatures were healed. The strength of the burst was determined by the caster's spell level.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components.
