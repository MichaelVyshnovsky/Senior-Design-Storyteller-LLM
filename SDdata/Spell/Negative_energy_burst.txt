Negative energy burst was a spell that dealt negative energy damage.
When cast, this spell created a burst of negative energy. This burst was large, affecting an area with a  radius. Any creature within this area were weakened. Undead creatures were healed. The strength of the burst was determined by the casters spell level.
This spell required only verbal and somatic components.
