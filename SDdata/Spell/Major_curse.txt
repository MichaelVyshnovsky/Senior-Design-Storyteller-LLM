'''''Major curse''''' was a southern magic [[divine magic|divine]] spell available to worshipers of [[Nephthys]].

==Effects==
This spell was identical to the ''[[remove curse]]'' spell. The key difference was this spell had a permanent effect. The spell could be cast on a creature or an item. If a cursed item was touched, the unfortunate individual became cursed.

The reverse of this spell, '''''remove major curse''''', removed the effects of both this spell as well as the ''[[bestow curse]]'' spell.

==Components==
This spell required only [[Somatic component|somatic]] and [[Verbal component|verbal]] components.
