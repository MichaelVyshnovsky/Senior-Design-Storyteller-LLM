'''''Home port''''' was a [[Divine magic|divine]] [[invocation]] [[spell]] used by specialty priests devoted to [[Valkur]], allowing the recipient to sail home.

==Effects==
This spell allowed the recipient to unerringly sail to their home port regardless of how far away they were or inclement weather conditions. The home port was whichever port the ship docked at the most. The spell lasted for several weeks once cast.

==Components==
In addition to [[verbal component|verbal component]]s and [[somatic component]]s, this spell required as [[material component]]s a pebble from the vessel's native port to cast. The pebble was held during the casting.
