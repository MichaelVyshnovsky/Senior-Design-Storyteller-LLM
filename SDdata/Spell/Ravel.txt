'''''Ravel''''' was a reversed [[cantrip]] that acolyte [[wizard]]s could learn.{{Cite dragon/59/Cantrips: Minor magics for would-be wizards|6}} It undid the results of the ''[[stitch]]'' cantrip, or exploited a flaw in a seam to further degrade the integrity of the binding.

==Effects==
This cantrip completely reversed a single ''stitch'' cantrip, but otherwise required a loose or broken thread as a starting place for the unweaving.{{Cite dragon/59/Cantrips: Minor magics for would-be wizards|8}} This cantrip had no effect on [[magic item]]s.

==Components==
This cantrip required [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s to cast. The caster spoke the name of the spell while pantomiming plucking at a loose thread.
