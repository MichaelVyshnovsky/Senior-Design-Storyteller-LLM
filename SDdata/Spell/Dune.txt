'''''Dune''''' was an uncommon [[conjuration]] spell of the [[southern magic]] tradition{{Southernmagic}} that created a magical sand dune.

==Effects==
The spell would create a sand dune which transported a target creature to the caster. The amount of creatures that could be transported by the spell was dependant on the caster's skill level. The spell only functioned in a sandy area. 
 
==Components==
In addition to [[verbal]] and [[somatic]] components, this spell required a [[material component]], a pinch of sand.
