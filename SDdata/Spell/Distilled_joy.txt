'''''Distilled joy''''' was a [[transmutation]] [[spell]] used to create [[ambrosia]], The spell was common among [[cleric]]s following the goddess [[Lliira]]

==Effects==
After an entire day of preparation, the caster had to touch someone in a moment of true joy, such as from the bliss of true love, the passion of a sexual encounter, the creative spark of an artist, or the daydream of a [[dryad]]. Once created, the ambrosia lasted until consumed.

==Components==
The ''distilled joy'' spell required [[verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s and the container to collect the ambrosia, usually a tiny vial.
