'''''Moondance''''' was a cooperative [[divine magic|divine]] [[conjuration]] [[spell]] that ''[[bless]]ed'' each participant.

==Effects==
A minimum of three [[priestess]]es were required to cast ''moondance''. The ''blessing'' imparted by the ''moondance'' lasted for several days depending on the combined skill of the casting priestesses.

==Components==
This spell required only [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s to cast.

==History==
''Moondance'' was a [[drow]] spell granted to [[priest]]s of [[Eilistraee]], the Dark Maiden.
