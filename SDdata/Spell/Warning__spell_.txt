'''''Warning''''' was a common [[spell]] used by [[shugenja]] in [[Kara-Tur]]. The spell was very rare outside Kara-Tur.

==Effects==
This spell increased an individuals sense of danger. They became aware of any creature in a 10 foot (3 meter) radius around them, which prevented ambushes.

==Components==
In addition to [[Verbal component|verbal]] and [[somatic component|somatic]] components, the spell required a [[material component]], a small metal staue coated in the dust from a crushed [[pearl]].
