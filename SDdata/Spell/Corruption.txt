'''''Corruption''''' was an [[arcane magic|arcane]] [[necromancy]] [[spell]] that temporarily corrupted a target's soul with greed and ambition.

==Effects==
The target of ''corruption'' was overwhelmed with dark power and halted in their steps, unable to move as they dreamed of delusions of grandeur. When the spell ended there was a chance the target would look favorably upon the caster.

==Components==
This spell required only [[verbal]] and [[somatic component]]s to cast.
