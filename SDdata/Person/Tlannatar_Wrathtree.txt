Tlannatar Wrathtree was an inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 century DR.
Tlannatar Wrathtree participated in the hunt of Elminster Aumar in . Though he was concerned that it involved entering Castle Dlardrageth, where Elminster was hidden, he was overruled by the others. During the hunt, he slew a number of his own companions with a spell he cast upon Elminster, which was reflected.
