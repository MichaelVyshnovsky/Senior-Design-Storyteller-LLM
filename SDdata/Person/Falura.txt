Falura was a halfling cook who worked under Chef Marjoram in Lady Jannaths Estate in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Falura was a young, dark-skinned hin woman with curly black hair and dark brown eyes.
During her time in Lady Jannaths estate, Falura strived to be a chef. She was supervised by Marjoram, who ran his kitchen with an iron fist, demanding perfection. Falura managed to serve Firelia Jannaths guests raw dishes, struggled with knife-work, and complained over the amount of mouths she had to cook for.
