'''Dorlan Nyrotha''' was [[Thoster]]'s first mate aboard the ''[[Green Siren]]'' during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was a hulking man. He was often barefoot.

==Personality==
He thought the crew was not very tough, and would not disguise his scorn for fools.

==Activities==
Those who defied him were sometimes keelhauled.{{cite book/Plague of Spells|88-89|8}}

==Relationships==
His captain, Thoster, considered him someone to whom [[Japheth]] could direct talk of ghosts.{{cite book/Plague of Spells|153|11}}

==History==
In {{yearlink|1396}}, he was the first mate aboard Thoster's ship.{{cite book/Plague of Spells|96-98|8}}{{cite book/Plague of Spells|87|7}}

When he woke up, they were under attack near the hidden island of [[Hegruth]] by the forces of the [[kraken]] [[Gethshemeth]]. The forces of the kraken struck at them as they met with [[Nogah]], a rebel [[kuo-toa]] whip who was the kraken's enemy. The pirate rallied the crew during the battle, personally slaying at least one enemy kuo-toa.{{cite book/Plague of Spells|147-150|11}}

Some time later, while accompanying [[Nogah]] in her attempt to wrest the ''[[Dreamheart]]'' back from the kraken Gethshemeth,{{cite book/Plague of Spells|211|15}} Nyrotha was slain by an ooze.{{cite book/Plague of Spells|219-220|15}}
