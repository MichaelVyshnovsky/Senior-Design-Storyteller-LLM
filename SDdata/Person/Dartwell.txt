Dartwell was a half-elf market grifter in Waterdeep in the 1470s DR.
In 1476 DR, Dartwell entered into Undermountain after fleeing from the City Watch after swindling some merchants. Feeling weary, he decided to sit for a moment in the Hall of Sleeping Kings, and stayed asleep
He may have been awoken in 1479 DR.
