Orgaal was a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave who served the Elemental Prince Olhydra during the late 15 century DR.
At some point during or before the , Orgaal was sent on a mission to spy upon the Temple of Eternal Flame for the Crushing Wave. Orgaal was caught in the act by the cultists of the Flame, imprisoned within their temple complex, and interrogated by Flamewrath Ignatia.
