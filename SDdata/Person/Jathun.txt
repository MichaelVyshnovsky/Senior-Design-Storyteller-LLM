'''Jathun''' was a caravan guard of the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In late {{yearlink|1357}}, Jathun was a guard working in [[Orthil Voldovan]]'s caravan bound for [[Waterdeep]] from [[Scornubel]], which [[Shandril Shessair]] and [[Narm Tamaraith]] joined; during an ambush, Narm appeared to panic, resulting in Orthil having him knocked out and bound.{{cite book/Hand of Fire/TPB 2002|103|7}} Per [[Arauntar]], Jathun was the one who knocked Narm out when it became necessary to restrain him during an ambush.
