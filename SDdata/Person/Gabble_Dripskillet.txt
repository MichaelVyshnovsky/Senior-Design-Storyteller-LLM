Gabble Dripskillet was the main deep gnome representative in Mantol-Derith in the late 15 century DR.
Gabble was a merchant and shopkeeper selling salt, gemstones, and rare minerals. She was surprised by a duergar attack on her section of the trading post.
She was ready to negotiate in order to stop the madness.
Gabble was on good terms with Yantha Coaxrock.
