'''Rowan Babar''' was the founder of the [[Order of the Dawn]] and the cult master in [[1479 DR]].

==History==
Rowan Babar founded the Order of the Dawn in the city of [[Mreyelundar]] a few years before 1479 DR. Some people believed he impersonated the skull of a massive [[Primordial]] during the cult's rites, but that people usually met untimely ends.
