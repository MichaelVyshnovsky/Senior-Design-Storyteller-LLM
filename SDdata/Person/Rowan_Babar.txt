Rowan Babar was the founder of the Order of the Dawn and the cult master in 1479 DR.
Rowan Babar founded the Order of the Dawn in the city of Mreyelundar a few years before 1479 DR. Some people believed he impersonated the skull of a massive Primordial during the cults rites, but that people usually met untimely ends.
