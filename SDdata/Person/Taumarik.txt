Taumarik was a prince of Hartsvale in 1489 DR.
Taumarik was the son of Grauman, the king of Hartsvale in 1489 DR. He was also married to Ylienna of Silverymoon, and took poorly to the various insulting names given to Ylienna by the earls of Hartsvale.
In 1486 DR, Taumarik left Hartsvale to spend three years exploring the North, upsetting many of the earls and nobility when he returned with a wife&mdashthe sorceress Ylienna of Silverymoon. Despite this political friction, Taurmarik still began taking on the responsibilities of the throne upon his return.
