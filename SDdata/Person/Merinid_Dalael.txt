'''Merinid Dalael''' was a [[drow]] [[assassin]] member of the [[Jaezred Chaulssin]] in [[Menzoberranzan]] in [[1479 DR]].
 
==History==
In 1479 DR, sent by [[Valan Jaelre]], one of the lairs used by members of the [[Council of Spiders]]. There he killed the Council's elders [[Raelaryn Xorlarrin]] and a minor scion of [[House Barrison Del'Armgo]] and captured [[Hoshtar Xorlarrin]], Raelaryn's cousin.
