Aladiam was the proprietor of Aladiams Imports, an imports shop found in Tegals Mark during the mid-14 century DR. In addition to providing Sembian spices and silks from his store, he moonlighted as a smuggler and a fence, selling and transporting stolen goods.
From his shop, he would often recruit adventurers and sell-swords to transport stolen goods for him.
Among his belongings, Aladiam possessed a scimitar of speed.
