'''Baelen Anastur''' was an elderly [[gnome]] businessman who owned and operated [[Anastur's Abrasives]] of [[Llorkh]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Baelen oversaw his gnome and [[human]] employees as they prospected mineral-based polishing agents used in metalworking.
