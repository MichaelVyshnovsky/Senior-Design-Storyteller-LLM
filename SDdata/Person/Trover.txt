'''Trover the Stout''' was a mercenary in [[Zhent]]-occupied [[Daggerdale]] in [[1367 DR]].

==History==
In 1367 DR, Trover and [[Larkell Greenspring]] escorted Constable [[Tren Noemfor|Tren]]'s envoy [[Marrakt]] to meet a just-arrived-in-town adventuring party.

==Personality==
Trover was a seasoned veteran, not easily intimidated by others.
