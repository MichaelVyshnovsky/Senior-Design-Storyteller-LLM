'''Coletti''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] who taught at the [[Institute for Sorcerous Imagery]] in [[Eileanar]] until its destruction in {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}.

==Activities==
Coletti taught alongside the other six resident teachers, made up of [[Driander]], [[Dillencraft]], [[Galanti]], [[Maleville]], [[Holstmann]], and [[Sylberg]].
