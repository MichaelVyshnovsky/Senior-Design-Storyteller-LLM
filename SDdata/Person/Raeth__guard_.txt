'''Raeth''' was a guard in [[Shadowdale]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was a late riser.

==Relationships==
He was teased at times for being a late riser.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1357}}, he was a young, new guard in the [[Twisted Tower of Ashaba]] of [[Shadowdale (town)|Shadowdale]], employed at least since some time before the night when [[Culthar]], another guard, was caught trying to kidnap [[Shandril Shessair]].
