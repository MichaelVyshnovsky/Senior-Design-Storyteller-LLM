'''Hah Li''' was a [[hill giant|coombe giant]] inhabiting the [[Rainbow Monastery]] in [[Ra-Khati]] during the mid 14th century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Hah Li helped lead a band of [[Tuigan]] deserters to the [[Rainbow Monastery]] which they took as their own.

==Abilities==
Like all coombe giants, Hah Li possessed impressive dexterity and balance. Because of this it was extremely difficult to knock him down.
