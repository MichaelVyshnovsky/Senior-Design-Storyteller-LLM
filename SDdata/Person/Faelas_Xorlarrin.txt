Faelas Xorlarrin was a drow wizard of House Xorlarrin in Menzoberranzan in the late 15 century DR.
Faelas was a competent Xorlarrin wizard. In the , he was a master of Sorcere and a coded letter was written to him about how Lolths current plan supported the idea of wizards joining the Ruling Council.
In the , Faelas and his cousin and fellow Sorcere master Jaemas Xorlarrin accompanied the demon army led by Marilith and Nalfeshnee. They witnessed the defeat of the two demon lords at the hands of Drizzt DoUrden, and Jarlaxle urged them to report the news to Matron Mother Zeerith QXorlarrin.
In the , Faelas was not chosen to join the reformed House DoUrden.
