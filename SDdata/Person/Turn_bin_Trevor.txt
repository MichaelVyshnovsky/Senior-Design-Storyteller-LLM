Turn bin Trevor, formerly known as Turon Daysender was a male human fighter from Waterdeep who came to Zakhara. He was Jayani al-Jasirs consort as of 1367 DR.
Turn was a handsome young outlander in Zakhara.
Turn gained the attentions and affections of Jayani al-Jasir and enjoyed life as her romantic consort.
Turn was originally from Waterdeep where he was called Turon Daysender.
Turn sailed all across the coastlines of the Realms, eventually settling in the Corsair Domains of Zakhara. Jayani planned to use him to help extend her holdings beyond the Land of Fate.
