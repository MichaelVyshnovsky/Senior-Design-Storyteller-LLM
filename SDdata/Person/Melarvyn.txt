Melarvyn was a doorguard for Haelithtorntowers, the home of Lady Joysil Ambrur in Marsember, Cormyr, in the late 14 centur DR.
He was quite long-winded with words, and had an active, suspicious mind.
One of his bracers could be used to ring a gong.
He watched the door for Lady Joysil Ambrur, under the supervision of the steward Elward Daunthideir.
