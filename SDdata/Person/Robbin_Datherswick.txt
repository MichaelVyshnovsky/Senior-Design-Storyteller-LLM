Robbin Datherswick was a hin entrepreneur from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Robbin was a strongheart halfling with a light skin tone, blonde hair, and dark brown eyes.
Circa the , Robbin Datherswick could often be seen visiting Felogyrs Fireworks in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate. The halfling was an aspiring competitor of the fireworks seller, who considered Avery Sonshal to be a genius salesman. Robbin concluded that it was an ingenious business decision to be the only licensed fireworks vendor in the entire city. The halfling was inspired to find something unique that only he could produce, and that was hard to replicate, in order to establish his own monopoly.
