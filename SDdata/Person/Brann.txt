'''Brann''' was a [[shepherd]] in [[Thundarlun]] during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was curious about adventurers, and thought to embark in one himself.

==Possessions==
He carried a signaling horn.

==Activities==
He was a shepherd during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In late {{yearlink|1357}}, Brann witnessed [[Shandril Shessair]] being carried towards [[Thundarlun]] by [[Delg Ironstar]] and [[Narm Tamaraith]]. He blew his signal horn so that the town could anticipate their arrival.
