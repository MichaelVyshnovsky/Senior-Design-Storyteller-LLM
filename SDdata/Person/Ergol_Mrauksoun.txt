Ergol Mrauksoun was a moneylender and landlord in Immerford in Cormyr.
In 1480 DR, Ergol and other wealthy citizens went to protest to Lord Lothan Durncaskyn about an unseen thief and murderer but the lord explained he was busy with two other troubles a group of outlaws preying upon caravans and a possible crazy wizard in Hullack Forest.
