'''Edraele Muirreste''' was a captain of the [[Silver Guard]] that replaced his sibling [[Elvath Muirreste]] in [[Seiveril Miritar]]'s crusade.

In [[1374 DR]], he was one of the captains summoned by Seiveril to plan the [[Cormanthor War]].
