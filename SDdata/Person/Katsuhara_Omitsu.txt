'''Katsuhara Omitsu''' was a [[spirit folk]] [[shugo-daimyo]] in [[Kozakura]] in [[1357 DR]].

==History==
Katsuhara was the daimyo of [[Dosaki Province]] and the main overseer of the construction of the imperial shipyards at [[Renkyu]] and the controlled exploitation of the [[Waturi Forest]].
==Personality==
Katsuhara was an outgoing and popular individual with a great talent for diplomacy. He was also a respected strategist.
