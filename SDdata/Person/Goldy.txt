Goldy was one of the horses that belonged to the noble Pharaboldi family of Arrabar circa 1373 DR.
Goldy was a gentle and smaller horse with a palamino coat, a tan-golden color.
Goldy and the other Pharaboldi horses were cared for by Turcan, the familys stablemaster. In the , Goldy was saddled up to be ridden by Emriana Matrell, the unwilling object of Denrick Pharaboldis affections. The young couple was to take a ride around the Pharaboldi family estate and partake in a picnic.
