'''Embro Cymrych I''' was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the early 7{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] and the first son of his predecessor, High Queen [[Miranda Cymrych|Miranda the Fair]]. His successor was his son, [[Embro Cymrych II|Embro II]].

==History==
Embro I assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|609}}, following the death of his mother.

He ruled for only five years before succumbing to injuries he received in a [[horse]]-riding accident in the {{YearlinkName|614}}.
