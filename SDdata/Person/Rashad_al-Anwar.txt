'''Rashad al-Anwar''' was an unskilled [[halfling]] [[sha'ir]] who relied on his [[gen]] to keep him out of trouble. He traveled [[Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1993 rare set|39|[[Rashad al-Anwar]]}}

==Description==
Rashad dressed like a typical [[Zakharan (ethnicity)|Zakharan]], wearing a long [[aba]]. He carried a [[jambiya]] and a small staff.

==Activites==
Rashad's lack of practical knowledge regarding his chosen profession was evident in his words and actions. His gen [[Sallah]] needed to constantly correct him. Rashad's lack of guile landed him into trouble with influential people during a trip to [[Qudra]].
