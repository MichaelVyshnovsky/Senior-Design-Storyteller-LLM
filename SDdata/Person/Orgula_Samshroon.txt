'''Orgula Samshroon''' was a clerk inhabiting the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the mid–1360's [[DR]].

==Personality==
She gave off a strong matronly vibe. Orgula never forgot a face and was quite cheerful. She was rather short.

==Activities==
Orgula worked as a counter clerk in [[Aurora's Realms Shop, Street of Tusks Catalogue Counter]] within Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]]. Her vast knowledge of [[Faerûn]], and the items carried within the shop, made her a valuable asset to the company.

==Rumors & Legends==
Orgula was a secret contact for the [[Harpers]] and served as a set of eyes for [[Piergeiron Paladinson]] himself.
