Lady '''Ahrendue Echorn''' was an inhabitant of [[Cormanthor]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]]. She was a [[high mage]] involved in the raising of the ''[[Mythal]]''.

==Abilities==
She was a [[High Mage]].{{cite book/Cormanthyr: Empire of the Elves|121}}

==Description==
She was wrinkled, walked with a cane, and had a husky voice.

==Personality==
She was happy to contribute her lifeforce to the mythal, and saw it as a good way to die.

==History==
In {{yearlink|261}}, Lady Ahrendue Echorn was welcome into the inner ring of the mages helping [[Mythanthor]] with the laying of the ''[[Mythal]]''.{{cite book/Cormanthyr: Empire of the Elves|144}}

After the city was renamed [[Myth Drannor]], she may have become one of the instructors at [[Windsong Tower]].{{cite book/Cormanthyr: Empire of the Elves|157}}
