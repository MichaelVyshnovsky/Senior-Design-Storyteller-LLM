King '''Adbar''' was a historical [[dwarf|dwarven]] ruler of [[Citadel Adbar]] in [[Luruar]] during the waning years of [[Delzoun]].

==History==
Adbar was known for constructing a road that connected Citadel Adbar with southern lands to expand their trade network.
