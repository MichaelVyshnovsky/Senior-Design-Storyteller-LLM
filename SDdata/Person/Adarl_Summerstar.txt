Lord Adarl Summerstar was a noble of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR. He was the head of House Summerstar.
Adarl Summerstar was a quiet man.
His old age was most salient about him.
He was the head of House Summerstar.
During the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the , Adarl Summerstar was among those nobles who called out to their peers to sit back down just as the situation degenerated into violence.
Among the comments of the crowd as he entered Castle Obarskyr, the general sentiment appeared to be that he was "a proper gentlesir".
