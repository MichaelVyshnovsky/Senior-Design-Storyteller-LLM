'''Diane Halistinar''' was an inhabitant of [[Hillsfar]] and former priest of [[Sune]] around [[1367 DR]].

==Activities==
Diane controlled a powerful merchant cartel within the city.

==History==
A falling out with Sune left Diane without access to her [[divine magic]].
