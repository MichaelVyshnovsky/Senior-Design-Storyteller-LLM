Zastreva was an Untaken of Umberlee, a member of the Bitch Queens clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queens House in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Zastreva was a fair-skinned human woman with blond hair and blue eyes. Her skin was freckled and hair was gathered in two ponytails. Zastrevas garb was a simple jerkin and a tunic, as well as a cooks apron and a toque blanche.
Circa the , during the Absolute crisis, Untaken Zastreva was serving in the Water Queens Houses kitchens. After the death of Holli Dylford, a member of the church, Untaken Padulla and Untaken Zastreva worked on a funeral feast to the served in the drowned priestess honor. Despite her affections for the deceased, Zastreva was appoyed by the service and its incessant singing.
