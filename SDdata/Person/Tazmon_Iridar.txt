'''Tazmon Iridar''' was a fisherman inhabiting the city of [[Yhep]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p170">

==Activities==
He was actually a spy sent by an unknown party from the city of [[Derlusk]] within the [[Border Kingdoms]]. By [[1370 DR]], Tazmon was tasked with discovering the secret behind Yhep's lucrative scarlet [[pearl]] trade. Barring that, Tazmon would attempt to steal several of the pearls and bring them back to Derlusk.<ref name=EotSS-p170"/>
