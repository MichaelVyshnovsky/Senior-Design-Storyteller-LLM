Skaug was a pirate captain and the founder of the port of Skaug on the island of Skaug in the Nelanther Isles.
The pirate had a hook-like nose.
With three ships, Skaug used his ill-gained fortune and supplies to build the port on the small island north of Ioma around 1169 DR.
