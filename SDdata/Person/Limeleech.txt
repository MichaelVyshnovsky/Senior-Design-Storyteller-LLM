Limeleech was a drow professor and a member of the Society of Brilliance during the late 15 century DR.
She had the self-assurance that she was both exceptionally smart and exceptionally good-looking. Her ambition was to attain fame for her scholarly achievements.
She couldnt help but feel jealous of Blurgs impressive intellect and she didnt hide it.
In the , Limeleech lived in the Lodge of the Society of Brilliance and was actively working on her research paper, revealing that gnolls heightened aggression could be attributed to hyena [https//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mite mite] infections.
