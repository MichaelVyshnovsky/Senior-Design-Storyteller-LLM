'''Dendybar the Mottled''' was a [[wizard]] and the guardian of the northern spire in the [[Host Tower of the Arcane|Hosttower of the Arcane]] in the seaport city of [[Luskan]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Dendybar was once former priest of [[Myrkul]], until one day he decided he could more effectively serve his god as a wizard.

==Abilities==
Beyond his spellcasting capabilities, his devotion to Myrkul allowed Dendybar to ''[[animate dead|animate]]'' [[skeleton]]s and [[zombie]]s.

==Relationships==
Dendybar held a deep sense of jealousy and hatred towards his fellow guild member [[Eldeluc]], due in part to him rising quickly within their ranks with little effort.
