Brawler Tiny was a goblin cultist of the Absolute who dwelled the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.
Tiny had yellow skin and light brown hair she wore in small brains. She wore a crude leather harness, a necklace of anomal bones, and a hide-and-leather loincloth.
The diminutive goblin woman was somewhat sensitive about her height.
She carried a crude, goblin-made scimitar into battle.
In the , cultists of the Absolute from the Shattered Sanctum launched a terrible raid on the Waukeens Rest inn. Tiny was among the many goblins that celebrated their victory in a grand feast.
