Ruby Hammerwhacker was a dwarven warrior who lived in the Sword Coast North during the late 15 century DR.
Ruby was an idealistic and upbeat person who always looked on the bright side of life.
She had an indomitable spirit, believing herself capable to overcome any obstacle. Her confidence originated from her personal drive to continually better herself.
Ruby believed that she should fight for those who were too weak or otherwise unable to do so themselves.
