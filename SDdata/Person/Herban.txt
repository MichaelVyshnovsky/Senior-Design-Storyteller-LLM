'''Herban''' was a [[human]] [[fighter]] who lived in [[Neverwinter]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|''You address me as "Sir" when you open your mouth, maggot! If this [[Wailing Death|plague]] hadn't made us recruits, I'd boot your worthless ass out of here for that type of attitude!''|Herban.}}

==History==
Herban was a stern and disciplined combat instructor at the [[Neverwinter Academy]] who worked tirelessly, educating all students in the art of combat tactics against various [[monster]]s. Corporals [[Dendy]] and [[Hewwet]] assisted him in his teachings. The [[Hero of Neverwinter]] was among his students.

Despite his exceptional skills, he tragically perished during the assault on the Academy in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.
