Adventurer
Belast Messandar was an elven adventurer that retired after a successful career, and enjoyed a second life as a merchant of Neverwinter.
During his life he fought in many military campaigns.
Belast Messandar was quite popular among the people of Neverwinter.
He was an adventuring companion of Cruven Gantara, a barbarian warrior from the isle of Tuern.
After his death in his 8 century of life, Belast was interred in the Merchants Landing section of Neverdeath Graveyard. His funeral was attended by many in the city.
Some time in the 1480s DR, agents of the Cult of the Dragon led by an individual named Malthus sought out Belasts resting place in order to find that of Cruven Gantara. They communed with numerous spirits in Neverdeath, from Merchants Landing to Soldiers Memorial.
