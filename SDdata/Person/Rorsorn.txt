'''Rorsorn''' was a highknight of [[Cormyr]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
During his last raid, he was in the same group as [[Kelgantor]], [[Tethlor]], [[Rorskryn Mreldrake]], and [[Jusprar]].

==History==
Rorsorn was one of the [[highknight]]s that took part in the raid on [[Elminster Aumar]]'s refuge at [[Tethgard]] in the [[Hullack Forest]] after the old sage had stolen the ''[[Royal Gorget of Battle]]'', on [[Mirtul 5]] of the {{yearlinkName|1479}}. He was not named among the known survivors afterwards.
