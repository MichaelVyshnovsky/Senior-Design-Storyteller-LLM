'''Sareene''' was a [[Harper]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Sareene participated in a meeting alongside [[Brammagar]] and others in the {{yearlinkName|1373}}, where they discussed their options regarding [[Vangerdahast]]'s coming scheme to trap and bind [[dragon]]s to service. Sareene was skeptical of Brammagar's proposal; unknown to them, they were eavesdropped on by [[Narnra Shalace]].
