Zillah was the lamia queen of the ruins of Unthalass in Unther in the late 15 century DR.
Zillah was a devoted follower of Grazzt.
Zillah ruled over the lamia inhabiting the ruins of Unthers former capital.
In the , Gilgeam and his army arrived in the city from the lands of Shyr in the world of Abeir. In the , Gilgeam, thanks to his pact with Grazzt, reclaimed rulership of the city from the lamias.
Later Gilgeam sent Utu and Zillah to steal Nanna-Sins divine spark. However, Utu died and Zillah was captured and put in jail.
