Steelclaw was a hairless cat, the feline slayer of illithid tadpoles from the Moonrise Towers during the late 15 century DR.
Steelclaw was a female hairless cat who thought herself to be a dangerous killer and a powerful foe. She referred to humanoids as "the furless ones."
