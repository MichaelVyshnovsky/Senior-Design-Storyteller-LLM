Alasturan Malatheer was a shopkeeper in Neverwinter in 1374 DR. 
He was also the local representative of the female adventuring company, the Galadran Company, and collaborated often with the Soft Claws. Unknown to him, his good friend Amundra Nelaerdra was the leader of the Soft Claws, and a steel dragon.
Alasturan was always in control of himself. His shop sold maps and charts.
