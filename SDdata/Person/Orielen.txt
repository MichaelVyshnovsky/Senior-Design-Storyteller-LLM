Orielen, also spelled Orielden, was a human hexblade and a Knight of Shar who served the Dark Ladys temple, the House of Night, in Shadowdale in the late 14 century DR.
As part of his duties as the House of Nights head guardsman, Orielen kept a watchful eye over the two mezzoloths that stood as sentinels at the temples front entrance.
Orielen served under High Priestess Esvele Greycastle. He led a small number of guardsmen, and was aided in his duties by two acolytes of the Sharran church, Telkin and Jorra.
