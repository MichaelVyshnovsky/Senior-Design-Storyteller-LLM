==History==
In {{yearlink|241}}, she accompanied [[Coronal]] [[Eltargrim Irithyl]] into the [[Vault of Ages]] alongside a number of other sorceresses.

In {{yearlink|261}}, she helped during the laying of the ''[[mythal]]'' by weaving spells that listened for the names and titles of [[Mythanthor]].{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|289}}
