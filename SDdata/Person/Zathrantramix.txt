'''Zathantramyx''' was a [[red dragon]] who lived in the [[Moonsea]] region in [[1373 DR]].
 
==Activities==
Zathantramyx laired with her mate, [[Malaritheos]], in a dormant volcano near [[Zhentil Keep]]. They were served by a [[frost giant]] tribe.

==History==
In [[1368 DR]], Malaritheos and Zathantramyx joined the monstrous army that raided Zhentil Keep. In later years, they were busy raising three young dragon children.
