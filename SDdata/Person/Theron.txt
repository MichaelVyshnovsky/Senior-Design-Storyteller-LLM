'''Theron''' was a cook in [[Neverwinter]].

==Personality==
Theron did not like [[human]]s because he thought they smelled.

==History==
Around [[1479 DR]], Theron was the cook of the [[Moonstone Mask]] inn in the [[Protector's Enclave]]. He also supervised the inn's staff.
