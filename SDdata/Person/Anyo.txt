'''Anyo''' was a very old [[chiang lung]] in [[Fochu Peninsula]] in [[Wa]].

==Activities==
Anyo was an ultimate loremaster; all seekers of knowledge tried to find her lair to gain the rarest information. Anyo usually granted information to all people who agreed to indulge her whims.

She had a fondness for scholarly discussion.

==Lair==
Anyo lived in a palace under an underground cave lake, which was located in another plane of existence. The gate was at the bottom of the lake.
