'''Jacinth "Jackie" Moonspring''' was the Society Editor who contributed to the [[Ravens Bluff Trumpeter]] [[broadsheet]] of [[Ravens Bluff]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Welcome all! I'm Jacinth "Jackie" Moonspring, your new society editor. Every month, look here for the latest scoop on the city's glitterati.''|Jackie Moonspring's first column's opening}}

==History==
Jacinth Moonspring worked for the editor-in-chief [[Fred Faber]].{{Cite polyhedron/118/The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter|28}} Three months later, Jacinth's column reported on [[Don Juan Montery]], a notorious [[Golden Rooster]] knight philanderer.{{Cite polyhedron/121/The Ravens Bluff Trumpeter|31}}
