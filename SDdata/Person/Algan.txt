'''Algan''' was a bandit preying on travelers through [[Skuldaskar]] in the mid-3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In {{yearlink|240}}, a number of bandits attacked [[Elminster Aumar]] while he rode through [[Skuldaskar]]. When the bandit captain was overmatched by Elminster's magic, he called on [[Drace (bandit)]] and Algan to shoot arrows at him, which they did, killing his horse.
