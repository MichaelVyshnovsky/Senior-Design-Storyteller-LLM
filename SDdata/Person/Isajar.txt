'''Isajar''' was the Keeper of Tomes of [[Candlekeep]] at some point.

==Personality==
Isajar had a reputation for disliking the usage of the hard 'c' in his writing, instead favoring the letter 'k'.

==Activities==
As Keeper of Tomes, Isajar was responsible for the governance of Candlekeep as well as appointing scholars to various leadership roles within the order of the [[Avowed]].

==History==
At some point, Isajar penned the tome ''[[Kandlekeep Dekonstruktion]]'', which detailed how Candlekeep was built and even its magical wards.
