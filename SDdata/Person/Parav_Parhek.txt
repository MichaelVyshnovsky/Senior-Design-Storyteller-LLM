'''Parav Parhek''' was an [[Amn]]ian son of the [[Parhek]] dynasty.

==Relationships==
Parav was the youngest child of Prince [[Malek Parhek (prince)|Malek the Coinmage]] by a different mother than the twins [[Rhinn Parhek|Rhinn]] and [[Savra Parhek|Savra]].

==History==
Parav grew up in [[Waterdeep]] as part of the exiled Parhek family. They were located by his cousin Prince [[Rohav Steelsar|Rohav]], and Parav was killed in [[1305 DR]] by his agents.
