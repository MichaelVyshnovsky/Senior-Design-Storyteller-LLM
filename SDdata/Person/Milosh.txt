Milosh the Impartial was a merchant who lived in Helms Hold during the late 15 century DR.
He sold a variety of goods useful to adventurers such healing kits that could help mend injuries, or those that helped them better understand magic, religion or the natural world. Milosh even sold items that aided in the art of thievery or helped uncover secrets found within ruined dungeons. 
During the Siege of Helms Hold circa the , Milosh became stuck outside the citys main entrance alongside several other prominent citizens, including Investigator Rannigan and the Helmite priest, Brother Satarin.
