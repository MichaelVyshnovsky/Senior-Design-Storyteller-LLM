'''Drykal''' was a blacksmith from [[Luskan]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. 

==History==
Some time around [[1490 DR]], Drykal had a blacksmith's shop in the Luskan market district, where he encountered the [[Champion of the Burning Dawn]].
