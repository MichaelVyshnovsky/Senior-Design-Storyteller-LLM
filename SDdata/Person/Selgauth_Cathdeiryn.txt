'''Selgauth Cathdeiryn''' was an inhabitant of [[Cormanthyr]] and a house heir in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Selgauth Cathdeiryn was one of the wizards staying back in the hunt for [[Elminster Aumar]] in {{yearlink|241}}. He later related the experience unto [[Earynspieir]] and the sorceresses in service of the Coronal; the meeting may have turned violent.
