'''Rilira''' was a former [[drow]] noble in [[Szith Morcane]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Szith Morcane Unbound|21}}
  
==History==
Rilira was the head of a noble house in Szith Morcane.

''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], after the [[fire giant]]s occupied the outpost, Rilira hid her identity when the nobles were slaughtered.

As a slave, Rilira tried to organize her fellow drow.
