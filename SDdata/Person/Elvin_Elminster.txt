'''Elvin Elminster''' (no relation) was co-owner alongside [[Dangus Barrowfold]] of [[Short and Tall Supplies]], an equipment supply shop in [[Halfhap]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He had no relation to the [[Elminster|other Elminster]].

==Description==
Elvin was a gangly 7-foot tall [[human]] man. He always said "no relation" after saying his name.
