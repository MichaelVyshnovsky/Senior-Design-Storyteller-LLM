Ixas was one of the Wearers of Purple, a wizardIxas is described as a wizard on SoTM-p5, however his stat block on p30 is that of a Cult Fanatic, with Cleric Spells. and a traditionalist follower of the Cult of the Dragon in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.
His master remained loyal to the old traditions of the Cult, in opposition to the majority of the Cult that had shifted to Severin Silrajins vision. In order to increase the power of his faction, in the  he sought the power hidden in a village on a small isle near Phlan. He had his kobold minions attack the village, but they were defeated by an adventuring party hired to investigate rumors of dracoliches and ghost ships wandering the area, as well as the villagers, who had mastered fiendish powers.
Soon after, Ixas ship was attacked both by the adventurers and fiendish pirates accidentally released by the adventurers. In the battle, Ixas and his crew were presumably killed.
