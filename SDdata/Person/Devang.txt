'''Devang''' was a vizier and counselor of Rajah [[Jasraj Seltrarir]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Vizier Devang dwelt within [[Seltrarir Palace]], and had a corrupting influence on the rajah, who trusted his vizier greatly. In truth, Devang was secretly a [[rakshasa]] and served [[Tirumala (rakshasa)|the rajah]] of [[Tirumala]].

==History==
During or shortly before the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Devang persuaded Rajah Seltrarir to sign a trade agreement with Tirumala.
