Jhandess Millomyr was a Tethyrian bard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Jhandess was a harp-maker who owned her own shop called Millomyr Harps located on Golden Serpent Street in Waterdeeps North Ward. She was affiliated with the New Olamn bardic college.
