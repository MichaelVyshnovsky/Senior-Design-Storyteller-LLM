Krrzaalt was a cleric and spiritual leader of the Kronikl tribe of pterafolk as of the .
Krrzaalts predecessor, a spirit naga chieftain, was slain by adventurers, leaving the Kronikl tribe in complete disarray. Krrzaalt had been taught the dogma of Shekinester and took control of the tribe when no other leaders stepped forward. Krrzaalt was affirmed several months later at a meeting of spirit naga chieftains. He later attended the same meeting, called the Great Conclave, on a regular basis and was the only pterafolk in attendance. Many of the spirit nagas resented him for this and plotted to wrest control of the Kronikl from him.
Krrzaalt wielded an enchanted heavy mace and heavy crossbow. He owned a cloak of resistance, bracers of armor, and two scrolls of cure moderate wounds.
