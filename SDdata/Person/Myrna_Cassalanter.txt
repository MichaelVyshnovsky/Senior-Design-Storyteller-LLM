'''Myrna Cassalanter''' was a [[Waterdeep|Waterdhavian]] noble of the [[Cassalanter]] family in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
She had a beautiful and carefully groomed appearance, and possessed a sizable fortune.

==Personality==
Extremely talented in gathering and spreading gossip from other Waterdhavians, she was frequently sought after and even hired to collect information and spread lies.

==Possessions==
Myrna's residence was located in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] on the south side of [[Ivory Street]] between [[Flint Street]] and [[Torch Lane]].
[[File:WavesilverVilla-1372.png|thumb|right|270px|''Myrna Cassalanter's residence (labeled <code>$78</code>) ''circa'' 1372 DR.'']]

==Relationships==
After having been rejected by [[Regnet Amcathra]] in favor of [[Galinda Raventree Amcathra]], Myrna spent much of her time exacting revenge against the couple.
