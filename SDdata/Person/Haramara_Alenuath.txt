'''Haramara Alenuath''' was the founder of the [[sun elf|gold elf]] House [[Alenuath]].

==Possessions==
Haramara was said to have slain the [[red dragon]] [[Skarra]] with just one [[arrow]] from her non-magical [[bow]] (''[[Haramara's bow]]''), which become an item of great significance to the house.
