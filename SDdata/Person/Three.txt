Three was a goblin child who lived in the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.
She wore a simple leather vest and short pants as well as some simple, animal-bone jewelry.
In the , a group of adventurers and their bear tried to sneak into the Shattered Sanctum. The intruders were caught and imprisoned in the dungeons of the Shattered Sanctum. Three and her friend One spent a wonderful time torturing the bear and throwing rocks at it.
