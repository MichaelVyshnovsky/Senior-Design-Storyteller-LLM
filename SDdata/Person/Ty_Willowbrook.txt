'''Ty Willowbrook''' was a reporter employed by [[Baldur's Mouth]] and a frequent customer of the [[Elfsong Tavern]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{quote|''Templed Turned Slaughterhouse in Shock Horror Priest Murder!'' ...no, no, no, BREVITY, Ty! Sheesh. ''Stabbed and Stabbed Again!'' ...that's not so bad. But not good enough. All right, it's Ty time; a drink at the Elfsong will sharpen my wits.|Ty Willowbrook}}

==Description==
Ty Willowbrook was a [[human]] man with a dark skin tone, green eyes, and short, curly, dark brown hair.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Ty Willowbrook worked at Baldur's Mouth's offices in [[Heapside]] of the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]]. He regularly struggled with finding words and inspiration, especially for catchy headlines like ''Open Hand Loses its Grip!'', ''Savage Butchery of Revered Priest!'', ''Templed Turned Slaughterhouse in Shock Horror Priest Murder!'' and ''Stabbed and Stabbed Again!''
