'''Ghellest''' was a derelict [[human]] living in [[Downshadow]] in [[1479 DR]].

==History==
In 1479 DR, she was wandering when she was captured by [[lizardfolk]] followers of [[Sess'innek]] who wanted to use her as a sacrifice to summon an avatar of their god.
