'''Sylsinssath Sultlue''' was a [[yuan-ti pureblood]] and member of the [[Sultlue]] family of [[Waterdeep]].

==History==
Once part of [[House Eselemas]] in [[Lushpool]], Sylsinssath was forced to flee [[Lapaliiya]] during the midâ€“13{{th}} century [[DR]] by his cousin and rival. Sylsinssath settled in the City of Splendors where he remade his fortune. He slumbered within [[Sultlue Vault]] in the [[Waterdeep/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]] as of the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, and was attended by [[tainted one]]s and other members of the Sultlue family.
