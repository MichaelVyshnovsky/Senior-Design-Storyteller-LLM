'''Blasko Halamar''' was a stout [[warrior|ex-warrior]] and stable owner who owned [[Halamar's Horses]] in [[Beliard]] around [[1370 DR]].

==Description==
The stable master was stout with a long white beard.

==Personality==
Blasko enjoyed telling stories about adventures, [[orc]] battles, and treasure; his favorite tale was about riches buried by wealthy ladies fleeing the fall of [[Netheril]].
