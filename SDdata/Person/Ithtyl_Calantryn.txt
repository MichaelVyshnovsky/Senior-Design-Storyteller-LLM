'''Ithtyl Calantryn''' was a [[wizard]] and wench in [[Baldur's Gate]] in [[1359 DR]].

==Activities==
Ithtyl worked as a serving wench at the [[Three Old Kegs]] inn in Baldur's Gate. She tutored her fellow servers, [[Katheera]] and [[Nathbaera]], in [[magic]]. Ithtyl was always ready to defend herself and the inn.

==Personality==
Ithtyl was devoted to her employer [[Nantrin Bellowglyn]]. Ithtyl was a skilled sorceress from [[Tethyr]] specialized in [[levitate|levitation]] spells
