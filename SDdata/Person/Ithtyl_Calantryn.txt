Ithtyl Calantryn was a wizard and wench in Baldurs Gate in 1359 DR.
Ithtyl worked as a serving wench at the Three Old Kegs inn in Baldurs Gate. She tutored her fellow servers, Katheera and Nathbaera, in magic. Ithtyl was always ready to defend herself and the inn.
Ithtyl was devoted to her employer Nantrin Bellowglyn. Ithtyl was a skilled sorceress from Tethyr specialized in levitation spells
