Maelos Karanok was the figurehead ruler of Luthcheq in Chessenta in 1357 DR and 1372 DR.
Maelos saw magic in every corner and knew only one way to deal with it destruction.
By 1372 DR, Maelos was nearly senile, understanding little about House Karanoks plans.
