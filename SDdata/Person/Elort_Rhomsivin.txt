Stormlord '''Elort Rhomsivin''' was the leader of the [[House of Winds]], a temple dedicated to [[Talos]], located in [[Westgate]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Elort was the leader of Talos' faithful in Westgate. His congregation within the temple consisted of three other priests and four followers. Elort was a specialty priest of Talos known as a [[Stormlord (priest)|Stormlord]].
