Vasos Flameslayer was a dwarven warrior.
Vasos was a stocky, muscular dwarf, with a brown beard and matching mustache. He dressed in plate mail and wore a spiked helmet.
He was a particularly skilled fighter, noted for his great agility.
Vasos owned a multitude of magical items, including a girdle of frost giant strength, a ring of protection, an enchanted pole arm, and three potions of fire breath.
He was descended from a long line of warriors.
Vasos was committed to the craft of fighting, practicing his techniques with regularity, He was particularly fond of going on missions with Worden Ironfist, the hero of his clan.
