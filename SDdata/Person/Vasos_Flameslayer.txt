'''Vasos Flameslayer''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] warrior.

==Description==
Vasos was a stocky, muscular dwarf, with a brown beard and matching mustache. He dressed in [[plate mail]] and wore a spiked [[helmet]].

==Abilities==
He was a particularly skilled fighter, noted for his great agility.

==Possessions==
Vasos owned a multitude of [[magic]]al items, including a ''[[girdle of giant strength|girdle of frost giant strength]]'', a ''[[ring of protection]]'', an enchanted [[pole arm]], and three ''[[potion of fire breath|potions of fire breath]]''.

==History==
He was descended from a long line of warriors.

==Activites==
Vasos was committed to the craft of fighting, practicing his techniques with regularity,{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1991|609|Udall Granitecrusher}} He was particularly fond of going on missions with [[Worden Ironfist]], the hero of his clan.
