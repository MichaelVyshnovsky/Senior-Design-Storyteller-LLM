'''Emric''' was the welcoming innkeeper of [[The Swaying Bough]] in [[Elventree]] around [[1367 DR]].

==Personality==
Emeric was a warm-hearted individual who welcomed all to the inn. His establishment was a popular spot for locals to eat, drink an socialize.
