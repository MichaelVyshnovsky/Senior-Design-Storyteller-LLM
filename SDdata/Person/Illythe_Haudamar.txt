'''Illythe Haudamar''' was a shopkeeper inhabiting [[Yeshpek]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p168">

==Personality==
Illythe was a shrewd judge of character and could usually spot when someone was being deceptive.<ref name=EotSS-p168"/>

==Description==
She was an aging woman as of the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century DR but her singing voice was still beautiful. She had a soft spot for young suitors.<ref name=EotSS-p168"/>

==Activities==
Illythe owned a festhall called the [[Dancer's Blushing Mask]]. Without an heir, and realizing she could not take her earned coin with her to the grave, Illythe invested in many local adventuring companies and businesses. She was beloved by many within Yeshpek who would come to her aid in an instant.<ref name=EotSS-p168"/>
