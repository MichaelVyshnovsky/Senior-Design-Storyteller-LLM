Brainburn was a flameskull member of the Twisted Rune in Undermountain in 1375 DR.
In 1375 DR, his masters sent Brainburn and his legion, composed of baneguard and dread, to investigate the message a dying Halaster Blackcloak had sent to adventurers. Brainburns strategy was to capture all the adventurers called and use a detect thoughts spell on them.
