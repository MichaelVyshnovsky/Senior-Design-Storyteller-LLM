Ander was a clerk who worked at Barthens Provisions in the late 15 century DR.
Ander worked for Elmar Barthen, and would mostly load and unload wagons full of goods with fellow clerk, Thistle. When Elmar was not in the trading post, the clerks would meet the needs of the traders and potential customers.
The young clerk worked for Elmar Barthen circa 1491 DR.
