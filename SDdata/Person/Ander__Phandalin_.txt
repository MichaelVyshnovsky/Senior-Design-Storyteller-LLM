'''Ander''' was a clerk who worked at [[Barthen's Provisions]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Ander worked for [[Elmar Barthen]], and would mostly load and unload wagons full of goods with fellow clerk, [[Thistle (clerk)|Thistle]]. When Elmar was not in the trading post, the clerks would meet the needs of the traders and potential customers.

==History==
The young clerk worked for Elmar Barthen ''circa'' [[1491 DR]].{{Phandelver-time}}
