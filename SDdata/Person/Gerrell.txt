'''Gerrell''' was one of the [[Blades of the Old Man]] in the fortress of [[Sentinelspire (fortress)|Sentinelspire]]. He was a part of the team accompanying [[Sauk]] to look for [[Kheil]] and secure the ''[[Erael'len]]'' in the Northern [[Shalhoond]] in [[Tarsakh]] of {{yearlink|1374}}, conspiring to kill the [[Chereth|Old Man of the Mountain]].{{cite book/Sentinelspire||4}} He was a westerner, with round eyes and pale skin;{{cite book/Sentinelspire||3}} he helped [[Sauk]] ambush [[Berun]].{{cite book/Sentinelspire||12}}
