'''Fendrick Pestle''' was a [[deep gnome]] in [[Blingdenstone]].

==History==
In [[1479 DR]], Fendrick was in charge of supplies in the city and was concerned about the not-yet-reestablished merchant routes.

==Personality==
Fendrick had a pessimistic and grim attitude.
