Daffyd was a stonemason who lived in Gracklstugh. He believed he was a Morndin of Clan Ironstar.
Daffyd had red beard braided in dwarven style.
Daffyd had inherited an Ironstar mace.
Daffyd was the son of merchants from Hammergate. He left his family when he was able to use an Ironstar mace his father had bought to an old dwarf, as Daffyd believe he was one of the fabled Morndin. He went to the north were he became the apprentice of the stonemason Ryordin Hammerfist. He later accompanied the twin dwarves Gamlin and Farrik to an unlucky expedition to Araumycos to get rock gourds, were he meet another Morndin, Rook.
Daffyd claimed he was the true creator of the motedisc, and that Ryordin only merit was to stole the idea from him.
