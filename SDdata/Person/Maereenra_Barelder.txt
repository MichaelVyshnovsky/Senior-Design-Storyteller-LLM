'''Maereenra Barelder''' was a young [[Cormyr]]ean noblewoman of the [[Barelder]] family in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Her parents were [[Narnathra Barelder]] and [[Varkander Heldruth]], and had her sister [[Braeryl Barelder|Braeryl]]. Her name was a old popular Barelder name for girls.
