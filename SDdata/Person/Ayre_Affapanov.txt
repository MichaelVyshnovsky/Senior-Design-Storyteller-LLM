Zora '''Ayre Affapanov''' was a girl in [[Mulmaster]] in [[1491 DR]].

==Rekationships==
Ayre was the daughter of Lord [[Serge Affapanov]], a noble predicted to become one of the [[Blades (Mulmaster)|Blade]]s allied with the [[Lords' Alliance]].

She spent most of her time with other noble scions: [[Elred Korsinchev]], [[Orym Illustriana]], and [[Liticia Gos]].

==History==
In 1491 DR, Ayre and some of her friends went missing. An adventuring party was hired to find them.{{Cite adventurers league/Breath of the Yellow Rose|14}} She was at last found in the [[Yellow Breath Chapter House]] under [[Thylius Caramitru]]'s influence.{{Cite adventurers league/Breath of the Yellow Rose|25}}
