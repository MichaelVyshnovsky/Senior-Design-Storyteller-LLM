'''Magpie Davys''' was a member of [[the Guild]] from the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, the Guild reinstated tethered knife fights to its members delight. The fights were held until the first blood. During the [[Absolute crisis]] ravaging Baldur's Gate, newcomer Magpie Davys was scheduled to fight [[Hans Feather|Hans "Leather" Feather]]. Bursar [[Uktar (The Guild)|Uktar]] collected bets and dispensed winnings.
