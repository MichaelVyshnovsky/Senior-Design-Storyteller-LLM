Kizzlok was a Maztican drow. He was one of the Ancient Ones.
In 1361 DR, during the Night of Wailing, Kizzlok was one of the drow sent to take Darien to Mount Zatal. During the fighting with the Golden Legion, Kizzlok was one of the few survivors. He was with Darien and the Ancestor during the meeting to discuss the next strategy to kill Erixitl.
