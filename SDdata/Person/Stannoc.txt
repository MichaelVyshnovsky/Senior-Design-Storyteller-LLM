'''Old Stannoc''' was a [[halfling]] in [[Waterdeep]] in [[1479 DR]].

==Activities==
Stannoc spent all his time inside the [[Yawning Portal]] inn. He was a gambler and odds-maker, speculating on various aspects of adventurers' [[Undermountain]] careers. He knew a lot about the dungeon, including some forgotten secrets.

==Personality==
Stannoc was a very able judge of character and often with only a small conversation with an adventurer Stannoc understood if they would live or die in the dungeon.
