'''Vimal Craumerdaun''' was a [[human]] male  and the head accountant for the [[Craumerdaun]] family in [[Eshpurta]], [[Amn]], in early [[1370 DR|1370 DR, the Year of the Tankard]]. Due to the treatment of [[arcane spellcaster]]s in Amn, Vimal kept his [[wizard]]ly skills a closely guarded secret.
