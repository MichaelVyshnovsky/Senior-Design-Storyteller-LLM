Shadha was a holy slayer living in Hawa in 1367 DR.
Against her will, Shadha served as an assassin of the Dark Wave for the Cult of Istishia. She was under the control of Faizahs Waters of devotion.
Shadha was the sister of her fellow Dark Wave members Sahlah and Setara bint Aasim.
