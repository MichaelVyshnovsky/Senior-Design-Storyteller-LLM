'''Atsuko''' was a [[yakuza]] in the town of [[Aru]] in [[Wa]].

==Personality==
Atsuko was a very paranoid individual. She always questioned people who followed her.

==Activities==
Atsuko was one of the best accomplices of [[Naga (yakuza)|Naga]]. She was a yakuza collector of medium rank.
