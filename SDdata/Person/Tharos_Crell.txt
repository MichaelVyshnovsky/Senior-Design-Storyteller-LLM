Tharos Crell was the leader of a tribe within the Great Dale during the late -10 century DR.
Thaross son, Thargaun Crell, assumed leadership of the tribe upon his death in . Thargaun would later subvert all of the surrounding tribes and call the newly formed nation Tharos in honor of his father. He would also become the first in a line of Nentyarchs in the Crell Dynasty that would rule Narfell for several centuries.
