Salvanas di Riyos was an elven sailor who could be found philandering in the Copper Coronet of Athkatla in the late 14 century DR.
Salvanas was a pale-skinned elf who wore green-colored clothing.
Salvanas accosted attractive women who frequented the tavern with flattery and attempts at seduction, however, in reality, his behavior hid insecurity and fear of actual intimacy.
In the , Salvanas di Riyos frequented the Copper Coronet in Athkatlas River District slums. During that time he have had at least one encounter showing unwanted attention to the aspiring halfling paladin Mazzy Fentan, referring to her as his "little chestnut." Another frequent visitor of the Coronet, Nalia deArnise, was well aware of Salvanas and eagerly turned him down, not letting him shower her with unwanted attention.
