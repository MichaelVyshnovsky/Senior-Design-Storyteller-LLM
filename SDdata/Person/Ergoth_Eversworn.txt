Ergoth Eversworn was a dwarf cleric in the late 14 century DR.
Ergoth had short white hair and goatee. He wore a suit of scale mail under a white and red surcoat, a shield, and carried a mace with exaggerated flanges.
In the  during the Elven Crusade, Ergoth joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp on the outskirts of Myth Drannor. He served as a guide for priests there.
