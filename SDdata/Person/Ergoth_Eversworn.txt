'''Ergoth Eversworn''' was a [[dwarf]] [[cleric]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==Description==
Ergoth had short white hair and goatee. He wore a suit of [[scale mail]] under a white and red [[surcoat]], a [[shield]], and carried a [[mace]] with exaggerated flanges.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}} during the [[Elven Crusade]], Ergoth joined the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|fortified camp]] on the outskirts of [[Myth Drannor]]. He served as a guide for priests there.
