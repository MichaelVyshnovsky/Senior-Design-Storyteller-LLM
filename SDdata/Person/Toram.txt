Toram was a half-elf Harper agent who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.
Toram was passionate by nature, and usually acted or spoke without thinking, in contrast to his sisters personality.
Toram was the half-brother of Theryis.
After the death of Cymril, Toram became the leader of the Harpers cell in Neverwinter.
As of 1479 DR, Toram was one of the only two official Harper agents operating in Neverwinter. The other one was his sister.
In 1479 DR, Toram may have aided of group of adventurers who were searching for information about the "Lost Heir" of Alagondar.
