'''Blind Ippsley''' was a merchant and vendor that lived in [[Helm's Hold]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
During the [[Siege of Helm's Hold]] in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Ippsley set up shop in the [[Old Dirty Dwarf]] inn. He offered a variety of useful items that benefited the [[adventurer|adventuring types]] frequenting the inn while trying to help Helm's Hold with his horrific [[devil]] situation.
