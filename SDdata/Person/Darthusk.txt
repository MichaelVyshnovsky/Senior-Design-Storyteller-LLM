Darthusk was a guard in service of Mourngrym Amcathra in the mid-late 14 century DR.
He was open about his mistrust of magic.
In , while sparring with Mourngrym Amcathra, he noticed that the Lord of Shadowdale had grown distracted. When the Lord finally reacted, he recounted a memory suddenly flaring up. Darthusk soon dropped the subject.
