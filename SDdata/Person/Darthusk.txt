'''Darthusk''' was a guard in service of [[Mourngrym Amcathra]] in the mid-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was open about his mistrust of [[magic]].

==History==
In {{yearlink|1372}}, while sparring with Mourngrym Amcathra, he noticed that the Lord of [[Shadowdale]] had grown distracted. When the Lord finally reacted, he recounted a memory suddenly flaring up. Darthusk soon dropped the subject.
