'''Yelada''' was an inhabitant of [[Shadowdale]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
By {{yearlink|1479}}, Yelada and a group of [[elves]] worked together to keep [[Storm Silverhand]]'s farm from being completely overrun with wild greenery, despite the latter's abandonment of the property.
