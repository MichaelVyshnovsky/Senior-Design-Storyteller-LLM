'''Beyab al-Rashid''' was a [[holy slayer]] who lived in [[Liham]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Beyab and his brothers were members of a merchant house in Liham. They used this as a front to mark a [[wikipedia:en:Caravanserai|caravanserai]] for robbery.

==Relationships==
Beyab was brother to [[Ramas al-Rashid]] and [[Harun al-Rashid]].
