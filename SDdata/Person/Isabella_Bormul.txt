Lady '''Isabella Bormul''' was a [[silver]] merchant and a member of the noble [[patriar]] [[Bormul]] family of [[Baldur's Gate]], active sometime in the 14{{th}} or 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Lady Isabella Bormul was a supporter and patron of the [[Counting House]] in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of Baldur's Gate. She made significant donations to the bank, including seven cartfuls of [[Amn]]ian silver. Her donations earned Lady Bormul a place in the Counting House's ''Record of the Honored'' sometime before the {{Yearlink|1492}}.
