Sunbright Steelshanks was a Rengarth barbarian of circa , who caught the eye of the Netherese wizards Candlemas and Sysquemalyn.
The blond-haired barbarian was slender and quick with corded muscles. With his bright hair and strong legs, the barbarian was given the name of Sunbright Steelshanks by his mother Monkberry on his nameday.
When traveling, Sunbright dressed in a worn linen shirt, leather and iron-studded boots, and a brown goatskin jerkin. Over his shoulders, he wore a longbow, a bedroll, and his sword, which was called Harvester of Blood.
