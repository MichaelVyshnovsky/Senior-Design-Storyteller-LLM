'''Aesgareth''' was a planewalking [[cambion]] who was once trapped inside [[Watcher's Keep]] in [[Amn]].

Eventually, he met [[Gorion's Ward]] and tried to bargain. However, Gorion's Ward killed him in order to collect the items needed to exit the keep.

==Possessions==
Won in a game against his relative, Duke [[Rowan Darkwood]], Aesgareth possessed a special [[deck of many things]] made by a [[genasi]] wizard.
