'''Branoch''' was a shopkeeper of the [[Bruenghor Clan]] of the [[Sunset Mountains]].
{{quote
|Never will you see such fine [[steel]] as you will in this shop.
|Branoch
}}

==Description==
The dwarf wore a light-purple jerkin, blue shorts, runic bracers, and woolly boots. He had a braided black beard, a mustache, pale skin covered in tattoos, and mid-length black hair.

He sold several [[weapon]]s, both [[magic]]al and ordinary. He claimed that he had more than enough weapons for everyone. Branoch also sold a variety of [[armor]], [[potion]]s, and other goods.

==History==
In [[1374 DR]], he sold and bought items among a dwarven mining camp.
