'''Isela''' was an old [[witch]] dwelling in the ruined [[Talfirc]] city of [[Talis (city)|Talis]] in the [[Reaching Woods]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
She had dark eyes and wrinkled skin.

==Abilities==
She was capable of [[magic]].<ref>

==History==
She remained in Talis to await the child wizard of the [[Talfirc prophecy]].<ref>

When the [[Fellowship of the Dreaming Dragon]] passed through Talis in [[1364 DR]], she told them about the Talfirc prophecy and gave the mage [[Morhion Gen'dahar]] a magical ring set with a violet stone, and a book, ''[[On the Nature of Shadows]]''.<ref> She was thought to be defeated by Shadowhounds, but no one could say for sure.<ref>
