Ohlmer Cormaeril was the head of the Cormaeril noble house in Cormyr in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Although he appeared to be a respectable nobleman, he engaged in kidnapping for slavery, smuggling, and trade with pirates. He had been known to mistreat young female slaves.
He was found slain in his bed in the , after Gaspar Cormaeril assumed control of the house for himself.
