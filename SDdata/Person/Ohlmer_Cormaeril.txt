'''Ohlmer Cormaeril''' was the head of the [[Cormaeril]] noble house in [[Cormyr]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Although he appeared to be a respectable nobleman, he engaged in kidnapping for [[slavery]], smuggling, and trade with pirates. He had been known to mistreat young female slaves.

==History==
He was found slain in his bed in the {{yearlinkName|1369}}, after [[Gaspar Cormaeril]] assumed control of the house for himself.
