'''Alak Faerzhind''' was the Master of Apportation of the school of [[Alteration]] in [[Sshamath]] in the year [[1370 DR]].

==Activities==
Alak was a senior member of the school of Alteration who served directly the Master of Alteration and member of the [[Conclave of Sshamath]], [[Shurdriira Helviiryn]]. His position included the control of all magical and non-magical entry into the city, a position that gave him great influence in Sshamath. Alak served as the primary emissary of the Conclave when the dealing with [[Darkhold]] and over the years he forged a strong working partnership with [[Sememmon]] and [[Ashemmi]].
