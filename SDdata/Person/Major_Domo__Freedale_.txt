Major Domo was the name by which the manservant of the wizard Netheril in Freedale was known during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He was either a majordomo, or his name was in fact Major Domo.
He was armored in leather armor and wielded a long sword.
His job was being a manservant.
He worked for Netheril, a local wizard.
During the mid-to-late 14 century DR, the individual referred to as Major Domo was a manservant at around the same time that Taran Goldenstar was kidnapped. Netheril, the wizard, may have called upon him to remove adventurers he felt werent showing him proper respect.
