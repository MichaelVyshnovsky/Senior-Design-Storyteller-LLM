'''Gwennath''', the''' Bishop of the Black Blades''', was a female [[human]] [[cleric]] of [[Tymora]]. She was in the service of the royalty of [[Cormyr]] and a member of the [[Black Blades]].

Gwennath wore blue sapphire-coloured robes.
