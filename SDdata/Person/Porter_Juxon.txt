Porter Juxon was a human commoner from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Porter was a human man with light skin, gray eyes, and black curly hair. His black mustache and mutton chops were properly groomed and shaped.
In the , during the threat of the Cult of the Absolute, Porter could often be seen arguing with hin friend, Bliggerillo, over the Baldurs Mouth gazettes reliability and its claims that the walls of the Gate would protect its citizens. Bliggerillo showed no confidence in the broadsheets propaganda, unlike Porter. The human believed that the broadsheet would never lie to the entire city.
