Geth Stormlight was a sage of Waterdeep, who worked in the Great Library of the Font of Knowledge during the mid-to-late 15 century DR.
Geth was an elderly man with grey eyes and white hair. He wore comfortable clothes of good quality, and spoke with a slight wheeze. Geth had a gentle personality, and was known as a reputable man among the citizens of Waterdeep.
Before working for the temple of Oghma, Geth was a tutor for the children of various Waterdhavian noble families, including House Moonstar.
