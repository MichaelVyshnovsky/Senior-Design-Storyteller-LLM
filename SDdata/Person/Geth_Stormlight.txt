'''Geth Stormlight''' was a sage of [[Waterdeep]], who worked in the Great Library of the [[Font of Knowledge]] during the mid-to-late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Heirloom|6-7}}

==Description==
Geth was an elderly man with grey eyes and white hair. He wore comfortable clothes of good quality, and spoke with a slight wheeze. Geth had a gentle personality, and was known as a reputable man among the citizens of Waterdeep.

==History==
Before working for the temple of [[Oghma]], Geth was a tutor for the children of various [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble families]], including [[Moonstar (family)|House Moonstar]].
