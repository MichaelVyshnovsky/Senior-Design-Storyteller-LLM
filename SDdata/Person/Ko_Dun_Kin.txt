Ko Dun Kin was a Shou merchant in Tu Lung in 1358 DR.
Ko Dun Kin was a merchant, representative of the mercantile Ko clan in the town of Pe Nok in Tu Lung. In 1358 DR, he welcomed his cousin Ko La Ko and an adventuring party hired by Ko Ho San. He helped them with their journey to Hsin-to, at the mouth of the Fenghsintzu River. Possibly they were attacked by Dragon Claw followers trying to scare Ko Dun Kin.
