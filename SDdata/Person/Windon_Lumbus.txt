'''Windon Lumbus''' was a [[halfling]] boy in the [[Moonsea]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Windon lived with his family at the [[Lumbus]] farm, near the village of [[The Stop]].
 
==History==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], a group of [[goblin]]s wearing strange robes attacked the Lumbus farm, killing all the Lumbuses while they slept. Windon however managed to escape and hid himself in the barn among the surviving goats.{{Cite adventurers league/Quelling the Horde|9-10}}
 
==Personality==
Windon trusted halflings and [[human]]s but was shy with other races. He knew much about goblins, as his uncle had told him many tales about them.
