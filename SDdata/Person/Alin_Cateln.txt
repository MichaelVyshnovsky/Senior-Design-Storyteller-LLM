Alin Cateln was a human bard and a brief member of the adventuring group, the Moor Runners.
Alin wore a light tunic, an indigo-dyed vest and leather breeches.He carried and played a harp, and used a rapier.
Alin always had to sing for his supper, rides and things he needed. He enjoyed collecting stories.
Alin felt his magic was useless in a fight, especially against dragons.
*Sound Burst
*Invisibility
*Dancing lights
*Prestidigitation
Alin was originally from Tilverton and was sent away by his father for failing at an academy in Cormyr.
