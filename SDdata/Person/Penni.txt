'''Penni''' was a wealthy inhabitants of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Penni was accounted with the [[elf|leven]] [[Lady Whitburn (elf)|Lady Whitburn]]. Penni was known for wearing fanciful hats she purchased in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of the Gate. During the [[Absolute crisis]], Lady Whitburn and her husband traveled to the Lower City, searching for the hat shop frequented by Penni..
