'''Calabastasingavor''' was a [[blue dragon]] who lived in [[Threskel]]. He was a servant of [[Alasklerbanbastos]].

==History==
Calabastasingavor took part on Alasklerbanbastos's invasion to [[Soolabax]], but was killed by [[Tchazzar]] at the start of the campaign.
