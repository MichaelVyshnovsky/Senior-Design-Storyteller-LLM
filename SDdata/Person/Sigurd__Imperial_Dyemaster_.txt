'''Sigurd''' was the Imperial Dyemaster and the only manufacturer of [[Drake General dye]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Drake General [[dye]] was created exclusively by Sigurd and his apprentice [[Aurora (apprentice)|Aurora]]. This red and gold dye was considered to be extremely rare and valuable and encountered in the [[Western Heartlands]], [[West Faerûn]], and the city of [[Baldur's Gate]].
