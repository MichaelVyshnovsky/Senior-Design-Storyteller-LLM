'''Chuldan Helmstar''' was a fence of stolen goods inhabiting [[Waterdeep]], City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Chuldan operated out of his own warehouse, which was simply called [[Helmstar Warehouse]], located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] on [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]]. He dealt mostly in statues and carvings.
