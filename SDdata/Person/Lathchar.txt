Lathchar was an agent of the Lords of Waterdeep inhabiting the City of Splendors during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Lathchar was actually a thief who attempted to swindle unsuspecting adventurers wandering through the city into finding various objects of value. He would often attack such adventurers with other sellswords to obtain the objects.
He wore an amulet of proof against detection and location and wielded a sword of dancing and wand of magic missiles.
