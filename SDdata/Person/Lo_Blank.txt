Lo Blank was a smuggler and an archer who worked with the infamous Guild in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Lo had a light olive skin and chestnut hair. She wore a light ruffled tunic and was armed with a simple bow.
In the , Lo Blank worked for a gruff hin smuggler named Big Huido, transporting weapons, drugs, and firewine into Baldurs Gate. The shipments were well defended by Lo and the rest of the crew and all were released into the hands of the members of the Guild only.
