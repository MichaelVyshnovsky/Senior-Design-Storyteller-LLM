Dethaera Matchlass was a participant in a ritual of the mid-late 14 century DR.
At the end of her ritual, she had gained knowledge of so many spells it hurt.
In , Dethaera was a participant in a Magefire ritual, which unfortunately coincided with the moment when Mystra noticed that Elminster Aumar had become trapped in Avernus. In her distress, amongst other effects, Dethaeras gentle floating became a soaring, and she gained knowledge of spell after spell, in a painful manner.
