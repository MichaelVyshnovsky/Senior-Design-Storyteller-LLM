Cerandis Greenward was a head scout for the Iliyanbruen outpost of New Sharandar.
She rode a deer mount.
Around the , Cerandis led a team of scouts to excavate the ruins of Sharandar in the Neverwinter Wood. The group came under attack by a band of trolls, who abducted several of Cerandiss scouts and planned to eat them. Cerandis enlisted the aid of the Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge from nearby Neverwinter to rescue her missing scouts.
Later, Cerandis sought to recruit adventurers to defeat the fey Master of the Hunt whose exploits in the Realm of Malabog was inadvertently driving Malabogs army into the territory of New Sharandar, threatening to overrun the small outpost.
