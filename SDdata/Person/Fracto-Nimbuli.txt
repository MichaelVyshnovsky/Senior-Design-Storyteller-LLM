'''Fracto-Nimbuli''' was a [[cloud giant]] ally of [[Yrkhetep]] in [[Chondath]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Personality==
Fracto-Nimbuli was a greedy and vain individual.

==Activities==
Usually Fracto-Nimbuli was in his main room with three spotted [[lion]]s as pets.

==History==
In 1358 DR, Fracto-Nimbuli agreed to help the [[arcanaloth]] Yrkhetep in exchange for gold and power. He transported a portion of Yrkhetep's troops—including the [[Skykillers]], the [[Dark Watch]], and others—to the [[Five Crown]] inn in [[Arrabar]] using his [[cloud castle|flying castle]] in order to battle the combined forces of the [[Sisterhood of the Oaks]], [[Company of the Singing Dawn|the Lightblades, the Sunshafts]], and the [[Windriders]].
