'''Perantris Vasco''' was a musician and a member of a cell of the [[Cult of Reborn Flame]] that operated out of [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]], in [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|33}}

==Description==
Perantris was a skinny [[human]] male with with large eyes and little to none facial hair.

==History==
In 1479 DR, Perantris was recruited by [[Bazamarl]] to join his cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame. He was in charge of providing music when the cult met.

When a band of [[adventurer]]s that were sent by [[Halagothra|Halagothra the Healer]] to Sambral to destroy the cell of the cult operating there, killed both Bazalmarl and the [[Firebird (elemental)|Firebird]],{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|45}} Perantris fled in panic, avoiding being killed by the adventurers.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|40}}
