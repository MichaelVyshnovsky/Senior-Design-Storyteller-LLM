Sundarth was a Zhentarim mage of the mid-14 century DR.
Manshoon always considered Sundarth more ambitious than thoughtful. Sundarth was excited to report to him.
Sundarth was a mage.
Sundarth formed part of a squad of Zhentarim mages.
Other mages in the same squad to which Sundarth belonged were Chsalbreian and Arluth.
In , Sundarth reported to Manshoon, while the latter was in the middle of torturing Simron, that a group of mages seeking spellfire in Sembia was attacked, first by Harpers and then by Cult of the Dragon forces. He named Arluth and Chsalbreian as casualties before Manshoon stopped him.
