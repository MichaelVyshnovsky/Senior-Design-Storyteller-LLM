'''Spiderculler''' was an opportunistic [[hook horror]] that roamed the [[Spiderhaunt Wood]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==History==
The hook horror was seen prowling about the [[Nylerian's lair|lair]] of the [[fang dragon]] [[Nylerian]], who had the services of a number of [[ettercap]]s and [[spider]]s.
