'''Osric''' was a [[half-elf]] and member of [[Zhentil Keep]]'s [[wizard]]'s guild in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.

==History==
He lived in [[Darkhold]] before coming to Zhentil Keep.

==Rumors & Legends==
Osric was actually a ''[[polymorph]]ed'' [[osyluth]] who served as a spy for Lord [[Orgauth]]. He was tasked with monitoring the [[Red Wizard]]s who had infiltrated the guild.
