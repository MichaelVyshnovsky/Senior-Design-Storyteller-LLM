'''Duethaea Lauraunfeir''' ({{pronounce|doo|eth|AY|ah|_|lore|AWN|feer}}) was a [[Chosen of Mystra]] of the [[Delimbiyr Vale]] in the 9{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
She was tall and very skinny, more hard-bitten than beautiful, and often dressed in a rigid leather greatcoat that reached her ankles.

==Personality==
She was spirited and difficult, but gregarious enough to mingle. She also liked very strong drink.

==Abilities==
Duethaea was strong in [[the Art]].

==Possessions==
She wore two enchanted [[bracer]]s that launched a number of [[spell]]s.

==Relationships==
Her family saw her as difficult. She had been trained by [[Elminster Aumar]].

==Activities==
She liked to mingle in taverns all around the Savage North.

==History==
Duethaea met her match in the [[Battle of Blades Afire]], where she attacked heedlessly and was ultimately mortally wounded by the demonic foes.{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/MMP|26|2}}
