'''Ithana Redstave''' was an ambitious [[Zhentarim]] wizard in [[Scardale]] in [[1368 DR]].

==Activities==
Ithana lead the [[Zhent]] garrison in the city. She was busy with plotting the conquest of Scardale for the Black Network by trying to make [[Myrian Beechwood]] a puppet.

==History==
Around 1368 DR, Ithana was trying to frame the [[Hillsfar]] garrison for some murders.
