Merony was a rogue guard who worked at the Szarr Palace of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Merony was a gołd dwarven woman with an olive skin tone and a dark chestnut eyes. She kept her face clean-shaven and wore a cowl atop of her armor. Merony was armed with a light crossbow.
In the , Merony was one of three enthralled sentries who stood guard of the entrance to the massive Szarr Palace – home of vampire lord Cazador Szarr located in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate. On the day of the accession ritual, the palaces chamberlain Antwun Dufay‎ ordered no visitor nor delivery to be allowed an entrance, promising a "reward" from Cazador upon the rituals completion.
