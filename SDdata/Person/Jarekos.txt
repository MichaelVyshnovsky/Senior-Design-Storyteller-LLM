'''Jarekos''' was an adventurer in [[Maztica]].

==History==
Jarekos was among the first to see the arrival of [[Cordell]] and his forces in [[1361 DR]]. Under the influx of foreigners, she opened a stall in her village, inside the land owned by Lord [[Drakosa]]. Jarekos supported all the newcomers.

==Personality==
Jarekos believed that the arrival of the [[Golden Legion]] had changed the lives of all in positive ways. She appreciated the attitude of the newcomers towards women.
