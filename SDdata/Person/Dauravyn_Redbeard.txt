==Activities==
Dauravyn was an adventurer until his retirement. He spent his later years managing [[The Way Inn]], which was located near [[Dragonspear Castle]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1363}}, his inn was menaced by the [[Hordes of Dragonspear]], so he joined the [[Daggerford]] army. It was later rebuilt thanks to the sponsorship of several merchant families based in [[Waterdeep]].
