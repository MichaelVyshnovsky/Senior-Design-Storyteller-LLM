Natali Dormetsk was a warrior of Sossal during the Rage of Dragons of 1373 DR.
Natali was a commander in the contingent led by Madislak Pemsk during the war against the Ice Witch Iyraclea and the white dragon dracolich Zethrindor. He was chosen alongside Stival Chergoba to join Madislak in the duel against Zethrindor.
