Maferil was a mage rumored to have been studying the history of the Burning Dawn during the late 15 century DR.
Maferil was notorious for writing everything down, and had a secret library in the ruins of Illusk.
Maferil had an apprentice, Hommet Shaw, but sent him to Neverwinter.
Around 1490 DR, Maferil was murdered according to Soronil Noonshadow, this was presumably by the Arcane Brotherhood.
