'''Nathyn Powerl''' was a cult leader inhabiting the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Nathyn led a group of followers of [[Loviatar]], the Maiden of Pain. He planned to blackmail one such follower, Lady [[Marie Phaulkon]], to gain her family's money and influence to further is personal goals.
