Baerhar Surtlan was a shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Baerhar owned a shop called Surtlans Metalwares that specialized in selling metalwares. His shop was located on River Street in the Trades Ward. Baerhar was also the public spokesman for the Guild of Trusted Pewterers and Casters.
