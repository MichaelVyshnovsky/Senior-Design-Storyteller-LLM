'''Incus''' was a male [[osyluth]] that was a [[fiend patron]] for [[warlock]]s in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationship==
Incus was the patron of a warlock heir of the [[Toril thirteen]] called [[Nasmos]].

==History==
In [[1486 DR]], the ghostly [[Bryseis Kakistos]] assassinated his protege Nasmos.
