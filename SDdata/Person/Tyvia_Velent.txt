Tyvia Velent was a mistress of the Mulman spice merchant Silas Billmore in the late 14 century DR.
Tyvia was Silass traveling companion. The couple was also accompanied by the merchants half-orcish bodyguard named Marcus Aboy.
At some point before the late 14 century DR, Silas came across a Thayvian slave. Her name was Tyvia Velent. Silas purchased her and granted her freedom. Subsequently, the two became lovers. Tyvia accompanied Silas on his business trips, including when trading spices with the Merchant House of Mossbridges in the Vast. Circa the , Tyvia Velent, her lover Silas Billmore, and his bodyguard, were guests of the Golden Goblet Inn in Mossbridges. The trio showed little interest in conversing with the locals nor paid any attention to traveling adventurers.
