'''"Bigmouth" Thimsen''' was a [[dwarf|dwarvish]] commoner from the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Look - I don't want your wares, I don't want to donate to anything, I'm just chatting with my friend. Excuse yourself.''|"Bigmouth" Thimsen}}

==Description==
Thimsen was a [[gold dwarf]] with dark skin and wavy black hair. He had blue eyes, large bulbous nose and a clean-shaven face.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, as the [[Cult of the Absolute]] loomed over the city of Baldur's Gate, "Bigmouth" Thimsen could often be seen gossiping in the Lower City with his easily impressed [[gnome|gnomish]] friend [[Margolenn]].
