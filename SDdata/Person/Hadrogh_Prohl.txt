'''Hadrogh Prohl''' was an elderly cane-wielding [[half-drow]] merchant of [[Mantol-Derith]] dealing in slaves.

==History==
Hadrogh was approached by [[Xandra Shobalar]] to purchase prey for the [[Blooding]].

==Physical description==
Hadrogh had white hair and a weathered face and wore a patch over his left eye. 

==Equipment==
He carried a wooden cane ornamented with [[gem]]s and gilding, and wore a silk tunic and an embroidered cape with a [[diamond]] neck clasp.
