'''Arzastra''' was a [[half-ogre]] innkeeper and tavern owner in [[Xantharl's Keep]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==Activities==
Arzastra managed the [[Falling Orc]] inn and tavern.

==History==
in the late 1480s or early 1490s DR Arzastra took as waiters the poor [[shield dwarf]] [[Worvil Forkbeard|Larg]] (in truth a wanted criminal from [[Icewind Dale]]).
