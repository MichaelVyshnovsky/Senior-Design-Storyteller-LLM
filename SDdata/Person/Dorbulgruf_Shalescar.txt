'''Dorbulgruf Shalescar''' ({{Pronounce|DOR|bull|gruf|_|SHAYL|scar|refs=}}) was the speaker of [[Bremen]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was an even-tempered individual who always welcomed those that visited his town.

==History==
Originally from [[Ironmaster]], Dorbulgruf moved to Bremen after an accident in the mines made him realized he needed to move above ground.

After Bremen was partially destroyed in a flood, he was unrelenting in his efforts to help with reconstruction efforts. After earning the town's respect and their gratitude, Dorbulgruf was nominated as its speaker.
