Andarphisk Hawkspike was a businessman in Suzail.
Andarsphisk was one of the two surviving leaders of Thurbrand and Arley, Wendra of the Willing Whips, and Splendors of the Shining Sea Importations, which was situated in the poor quarters of Suzail.
Around 1480 DR, Andarphisk and his partner Drounan Harbrand were paid by Lady Dawningdown to go to the remote and secret prison stronghold of Castle Irlingstar in the Thunder Peaks on the eastern border of Cormyr.
