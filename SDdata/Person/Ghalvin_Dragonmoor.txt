'''Ghalvik Dragonmoor''' was a [[half-elf]] member of the [[Emerald Enclave]] in [[Goldenfields]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==History==
In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Ghalvik was captured by the minions of Chief [[Guh]] and taken to [[Grudd Haug]]. He wanted to return to Goldenfields to report the situation to the abbot, [[Ellardin Darovik]].
