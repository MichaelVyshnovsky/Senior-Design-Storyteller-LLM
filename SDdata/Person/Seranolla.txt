Seranolla the Whisperer was a forest gnome priestess of Mielikki and an agent of the Emerald Enclave in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.
Circa 1486 DR, Seranolla was in Elventree, where she possibly hired some adventurers to help Elanil Elassidil with some drow arriving on the surface.
Seranolla was possibly involved in the case of dracoliches and ghost ships roaming the Moonsea in the . She said that the Enclave was concerned about strange changes in the weather.
Seranolla was sister of Serinia and Yavendel.
