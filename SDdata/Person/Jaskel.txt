'''Jaskel''' was a warrior for the [[Cult of the Dragon]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
[[Thoadrin]] and [[Curthryn]] were aware of his name.

==History==
In late {{yearlink|1357}}, [[Thoadrin]] led a force of [[Cult of the Dragon]] warriors to harry [[Orthil Voldovan]]'s caravan, aiming to capture one of her passengers, [[Shandril Shessair]]. After one of their ambushes, his underling Curthryn related that Jaskel was one of the dead.
