Random Riellor, or Random the Mad, was a necromancer who wandered the entire breadth of Faerûn.
Brilliant and capable, Random was an accomplished spellcaster whose personality seemed to vary on a daily basis.
Random wandered the Realms in search of magic that would further his research. He was the only non-Halruaan wizard known to crack the mystery of southern magic through the use of his unique spell, read southern magic..
Among other magical items, Random owned a ring of teleportation which he used frequently during his travels. He kept a skyship, fully outfitted with a spelljamming helm, in orbit high above Faerûn.
