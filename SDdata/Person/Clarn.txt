Clarn was a servant of the Oldbridle family in the late 15 century DR.
He had a bright smile.
Clarn served the Oldbridle family as a bullyblade.
He was an old servant of the Oldbridle family, known to Arclath Delcastle by face and name.
Clarn rode with other servants of the Oldbridle family to intercept Arclath Delcastles coach in late Mirtul of the , in the aftermath of the massacre during the Council of the Dragon in Castle Obarskyr. He extended an invitation for a meeting at Arkanon Nalanders home, for Arclath to go alone and then left Arclath believed treason would be concocted in such a meeting.
