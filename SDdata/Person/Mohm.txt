'''Mohm''' was a [[hobgoblin]] in the late-15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Giant Diplomacy|7}}

==Personality==
Unlike the men that served under him, Mohm unwilling to retreat from or surrender to an enemy no matter the intimidated. He was the kind of man that fought to the death.

==Possessions==
Mohm was known to possess a well-made [[greatsword]] that had the holy symbol of the [[giant pantheon|giant deity]] [[Annam All-Father]] emblazoned on it.{{Cite adventurers league/Giant Diplomacy|8}}

==History==
In the late-15{{th}} century DR, Mohm served as a captain over some of the [[goblin]] forces of the [[ettin]] [[Gralm]] in the [[Forgotten Forest]].
