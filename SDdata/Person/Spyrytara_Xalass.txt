High Whipmistress '''Spyrytara Xalass''' was the high priestess of [[Painbliss Hall]], a temple dedicated to [[Loviatar]], in [[Westgate]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Spyrytara was the leader of Loviatar's faithful in Westgate. Her congregation within the temple consisted of 27 other priestesses and 34 followers.
