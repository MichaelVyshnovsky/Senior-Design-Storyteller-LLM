'''Reetha''' was a bowyer in the [[Dessarin Valley]] during the [[Elemental Evil]] crisis.

==History==
Reetha hailed from [[Westbridge]]. In [[1491 DR]] she tried to avoid a remnant cell of the [[Cult of Black Earth]] that tried to gain control of her city.
