Rehorustep II was the fourteenth pharaoh of Mulhorand.
Rehorustep II was born in 1210 DR and he ascended to the throne of pharaoh of Mulhorand in 1248 DR.
His reign lasted until 1320 DR, when he died on a hunting expedition in the Eastern Shaar.
