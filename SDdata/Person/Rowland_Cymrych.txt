'''Rowland Cymrych''', known as "'''the Boy King'''" was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] for a brief time in the mid&ndash;7{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] and the son of his predecessor, High King [[Alec Cymrych I|Alec I]]. His successor was his uncle, [[Samuel Cymrych|Samuel]].

==History==
Rowland assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|640}}, following the death of his father. He was fourteen years old.

He ruled for only two years before dying of [[disease|illness]] in the {{YearlinkName|642}}.
