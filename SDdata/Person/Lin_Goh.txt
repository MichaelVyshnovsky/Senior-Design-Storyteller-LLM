'''Lin Goh''' was the clanmaster of the [[Panthers of Xi]] [[ninja]] clan in [[Xi]], [[Koryo]], around [[1357 DR]].

Lin Goh was the wielder of the ''[[black kumade]]'', an enchanted [[javelin]].
