'''Reinhar III''' was a king of the [[Arkaiun]]s of [[Dambrath]].

==Relationships==
The man who would rule as Reinhar III was the only son of [[Reinhar II]] before him. He had at least one sister and two sons.

==History==
Reinhar III was born in the [[547 DR|Year of the Simpering Courtier, 547 DR]]. He took the throne of his father in [[591 DR]].

His reign ended violently in [[600 DR]] when he and both of his sons were killed by [[giant]]s while on a hunting trip in the [[Gnollwatch Mountains]].<ref name=tGHotR-p92> This triggered a short war of succession. At the end of it, his [[Reinhar IV|nephew]] succeeded him as king.<ref name=tGHotR-p92>
