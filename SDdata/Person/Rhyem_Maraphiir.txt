'''Rhyem Maraphiir''' was a [[moon elf]] and the father of the [[wizard]] [[Ashemmi]].{{Cite dragon/357/Volo's Guide: Renegades of Darkhold|74}}

==Relatives==
Rhyem had two children with the [[sun elf]] [[Chelryn Sarynnryllar]]: [[Ashemmi]] (born Shemithil Maraphiir) and [[Malphys Maraphiir]].

==History==
Soon after Ashemmi's birth in [[Silverymoon]], the family moved to live with the [[wood elf|wood elves]] in the [[Neverwinter Wood]], and were taken in by the [[Ilbaereth]] house.
