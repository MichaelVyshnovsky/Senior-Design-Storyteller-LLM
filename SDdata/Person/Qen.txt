Warrior Qen was a githyanki guard from Crèche Yllek, on Toril during the late 15 century DR.
Qen was a gith with gaunt body and leathery yellow skin. He had long brown hair and he was adorned in a set of heavy githyanki armor and armed with a gith greatsword.
Warrior Qen, along with other gith of what would become Crèche Yllek, attacked and claimed the Rosymorn Monastery of Lathander in the Western Heartlands of West Faerûn sometime before the . Soon after, the function of Crèche was changed as the githyanki war against the mind flayers in Faerûn shifted in balance. Qen stood guard in the Crèches hatchery and showed stern determination to his duty.
