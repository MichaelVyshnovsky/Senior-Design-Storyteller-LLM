'''Kevan Cymrych''' was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the early-to-mid 9{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|33}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] and the first son of his predecessor, High King [[Byron Cymrych I|Byron I]]. His successor was his second son, [[Dolan Cymrych]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|818}}, High King Byron I fell into a [[magic]] slumber, and Kevan stepped in to rule while his father was incapacitated. This short regency lasted until [[819 DR|the following year]].

Kevan became the true High King in the {{YearlinkName|826}}, after the death of his father. He ruled for twenty-four years before he died of natural causes in the {{YearlinkName|850}}.
