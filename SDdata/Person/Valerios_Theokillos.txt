Valerios Theokillos was the Tharchion of Pyarados in Thay and a retired adventurer in 1357 DR.
Valerios was a success adventurer in his youth its said that he slew also an avatar of one of the Mulhorand god-king now Valerios was retired and appointed tharchion. Valerios as tharchion had participation in all outfitting shop in the city and thank to it he had a close eye to strangers and adventurers in his city.
Usually he taxed the adventures back in city with a big treasure of some of the most prized objects but many Red Wizards knowing his hobby arrived to reclaim for themselves the objects.
