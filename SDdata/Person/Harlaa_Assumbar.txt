Lady '''Harlaa Assumbar''' was a [[priest]] in service to [[Siamorphe]], the Divine Light, within the [[Chapel and Chalice of the Divine Right]] in [[Waterdeep]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Her famous good looks drew many young noblemen to attend services within the Chapel since she proclaimed that she would not marry anyone who was not fully devoted to Siamorphe.

==Activities==
Lady Harlaa was one of three senior priests who oversaw the activities of the lesser priests and acolytes within the Chapel.

==Relationships==
She was the daughter of Lord [[Laeros Assumbar|Laeros]] and Lady [[Kerri Assumbar|Kerri]]. Harlaa was also the niece of First High Lady Scion of Siamorphe [[Belkerri Gulderhorn]] who headed the Chapel and Chalice of the Divine Right.
