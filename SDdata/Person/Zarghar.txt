'''Zarghar''' was a playwright of some renown who authored a "grand play" called ''[[And So They Met the Gargoyle]]'' sometime before the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1370}}, "grand play" gained popularity in [[Athkatla]] of the [[Lands of Intrigue]]. The famous [[Athkatla/Temple District|Temple District]] theater [[Crown Aflame]] added Zarghar's ''Gargoyle'' to its repertoire to the delight of theatergoers of Athkatla.
