Zarghar was a playwright of some renown who authored a "grand play" called And So They Met the Gargoyle sometime before the late 14 century DR.
As of the , "grand play" gained popularity in Athkatla of the Lands of Intrigue. The famous Temple District theater Crown Aflame added Zarghars Gargoyle to its repertoire to the delight of theatergoers of Athkatla.
