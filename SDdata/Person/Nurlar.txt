'''Nurlar''' ({{Pronounce|NURR|larr|refs=}}) was a merchant in [[Ordulin]] and [[Scardale]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Nurlar controlled a network of merchant activities all through [[interior Faer√ªn]]. He moved only between his citadel in Ordulin and his palace in Scardale.

==History==
Nurlar started with only two ships inherited from his father around [[1345 DR]] and step-by-step he created his empire. Around [[1356 DR]], he bought his palace in Scardale.

==Personality==
Nurlar was a sophisticated man who disliked sporting activities. He had a pathological fear of being lost in the wilderness and preferred to remained close to his palace.

==Rumors==
Rumors said that Nurlar greatly feared thieves and used many traps to protect his treasure. Rumors also said he wanted to marry an [[elf]] maiden he had imprisoned in Scardale.
