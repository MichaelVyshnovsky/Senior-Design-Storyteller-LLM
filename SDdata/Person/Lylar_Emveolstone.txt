Lylar Emveolstone was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Lylar was the patriarch of the Emveolstone noble family of Waterdeep. He lived within Emveolstone Villa on the Street of the Singing Dolphin in Waterdeeps Sea Ward.
His son and heir was Lylar Emveolstone II.
