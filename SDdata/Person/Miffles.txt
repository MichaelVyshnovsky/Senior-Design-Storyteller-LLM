Miffles was a pet terrier that belonged to Lady Bantakent, a merchant from the city of Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.
In the 1490s DR, the Bantakent manse in Neverwinter was being robbed by Edgin Darvis and Holga Kilgore of Targos. Edgins daughter Kira, while invisible terrorized the Lord and the Lady of the house as a distraction. Kira pretended to be a poltergeist and terrorized the household by smashing plates and floating priceless vases. Little Miffles was among the floating objects carried by Kira. The dog enjoyed the time of his doggy life while his mistress screamed in terror.
