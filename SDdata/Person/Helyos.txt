'''Helyos''' was a mercenary in [[Chessenta]].

==History==
Helyos was one of the best fighters in Chessenta. Once he nearly won the throne of [[Mourktar]] during a military tournament.

He also led the [[Renegades]], a military unit of [[Akanax]], the town of soldiers, who had deserted with dreams of gold and glory.
