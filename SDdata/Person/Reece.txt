'''Reece''' was a [[halfling]] girl that lived in [[Phlan]] that worked at [[Madame Freona's Tea Kettle]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. She was one of five daughter's the restaurant's owner, Madame [[Freona]].{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|6}}

==Description==
Reece had ginger curly hair. She was not quite yet considered an adult as of the 1480s [[DR]].{{ToD-time}}

==Relationships==
Reece's other sisters included [[Blaizette]],{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|23}} [[Briez]],{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|11}} [[Grelinda]],{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|17}} and [[Whittlee]].{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|27}}
