'''Kriv''' was one of the few [[dragonborn]] [[wizard]]s of [[Tymanther]]. 

==Description==
Kriv had yellow eyes and bronze scales flecked with black.

==History==
In [[1479 DR]], Kriv was one of the wizards [[Vanquisher]] [[Tarhun]] sought to study the [[dragonspawn]]-controlling [[orb]]s created by [[Nala]].
