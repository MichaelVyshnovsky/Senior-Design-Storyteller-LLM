Ulumpha was a paladin member of the Dead Eyes, a criminal group from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate that named itself for the grim trophies they collected from their enemies in the late 15 century DR.
Ulumpha was a half-orcish woman with handsome features, brown skin, and long brown hair. She was adorned in a simple breastplate and wielded a warhammer in a fight.
In the , the Dead Eyes set out to perform a job that would end up setting the gang against the Guild and its leader, Ninefingers. The Guild did not take competition lightly and sent thugs to take care of the Dead Eyes. The band survived and relocated to a new lair. Unbeknownst to Aideen Ni Chuirc, the gangs leader, one of her men was a traitor, informing the Guild of the lair and the hidden stashs location. The traitor bard instructed Ninefingerss men to strike at the hideout quickly and hard. It was unknown if Ulumpha and the Dead Eyes survived an attack from the Guild or other eager adventurers of Baldurs Gate.
