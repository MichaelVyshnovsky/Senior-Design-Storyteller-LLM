'''Sherrill''' was a Dark Breaker of [[Umberlee]], a member of the Bitch Queen's [[Church of Umberlee|clergy]] under Flood Tide [[Allandra Grey]] of the [[Water Queen's House]] in [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Umberlee hears us and rejoices! Sing! Sing for the Wavemother!''|Dark Breaker Sherrill}}

==Description==
Sherrill was a [[human]] woman with dark skin, dark brown curly hair and watery blue eyes. Her face was decorated with metallic-colored golden and aquamarine [[tattoo]]s.

==Posessions==
Sherrill's Dark Breaker uniform was a set of skin-tight heavy [[chainmail]] dress. The armor was decorated with golden trim and had decorated metallic pauldrons. Her weapon of choice was a [[polearm]].

==History==
''Circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the [[Absolute crisis]], Dark Breaker Sherrill was among the church's members who performed the faith's ceremonies and sang hymns in praise of the Bitch Queen. She was alongside Flood Tide Allandra Grey and other priestesses during [[Holli Dylford]]'s last rites.
