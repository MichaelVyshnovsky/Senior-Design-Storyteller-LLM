'''Jadron the Fang''' was the [[ogre]] leader of [[The Teeth (mercenaries)|the Teeth]] mercenary company in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Jadron was famed for his brutality and cruelty, and was feared and respected by his enemies and his own warriors.

==Relationships==
He won many enemies and no allies in his blood-soaked career, and earned a personal vendetta from [[Nightshade (elf)|Nightshade]] of the [[Silent Rain]].

His son [[Rarnok Jadronson|Rarnok]] led some of his forces for him.
