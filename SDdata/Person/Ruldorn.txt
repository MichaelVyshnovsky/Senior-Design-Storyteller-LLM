Ruldorn "the Storm Ranger" was a guide, a Harper and a community leader within the city of Everlund. Ruldorns home was hidden a bit west of city, though he could usually be found frequenting the Battered Hat when not guiding others on various trails. Ruldorn has earned his nickname for his ability to navigate in even the worst of storms.
