'''Ruldorn "the Storm Ranger"''' was a guide, a [[Harper]] and a community leader within the city of [[Everlund]]. Ruldorn's home was hidden a bit west of city, though he could usually be found frequenting the [[Battered Hat]] when not guiding others on various trails. Ruldorn has earned his nickname for his ability to navigate in even the worst of storms.

==Appearance==
A thin, giant of a man, Ruldorn stood almost {{SI|7|ft}} tall, and weighed just over {{SI|220|lb}}.
