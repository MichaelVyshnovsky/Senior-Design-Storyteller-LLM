Briney Valesaris was a half-elf buccaneer who worked on a ship known as the Cambacks.
He joined every battle he could find, always siding against the attackers, and pursued Thayvian ships even into the Pirate Isles.
Briney had stolen his ship from the Thayan merchant-mage Haldracon. Hed since destroyed and sunk many Thayvian ships.
