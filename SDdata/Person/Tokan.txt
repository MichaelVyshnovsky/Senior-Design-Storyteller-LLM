'''Chief Tokan''' was the leader of the [[dwarven]] [[Orothiar Clan]] in [[Bloodstone Barony]] as of the {{YearlinkName|1357}}.
 
==Activities==
Tokan normally spoke of ancient events like they'd happened yesterday.

==Possessions==
He carried with him an [[Item enchantment|enchanted]] ''[[dwarven thrower]]'' [[hammer]] that was revered by his clan.

==History==
In 1357 DR, Tokan and his tribe met the adventuring party of [[Gareth Dragonsbane]].

Later in the {{YearlinkName|1359}} Tokan and his people had joined the [[Alliance of Belt Watchers]] alongside a group of local [[halfling]]s and [[centaur]]s.
