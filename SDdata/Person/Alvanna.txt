'''Dawnbringer Alvanna''' was a [[priest]] in service of the [[Church of Lathander]] of [[Athkatla]]'s [[Athkatla/Temple District|Temple District]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Lathander may be prone to the vagaries of youth, but he has other gifts. To serve the Morninglord is to strive for vitality and joy... what is wrong with that?''|Dawnbringer Alvanna}}

==Description==
Dawnbringer Alvanna had tanned skin and light brown hair. He preferred to wear purple garb.

==Possessions==
She was equipped with a set of [[chain mail]] and armed with a simple [[quarterstaff]].

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Dawnbringer Alvanna could be found outside the [[Temple of Lathander (Athkatla)|Temple of Lathander]], greeting approaching supplicants. She was a devoted worshipper who was ready to discuss her deity and defend his infamous transgressions of youth. Alvanna denounced all who felt pride and praised hopefulness, creativity, and optimism and believed the cycle of renewal to be her church's most praised virtue.
