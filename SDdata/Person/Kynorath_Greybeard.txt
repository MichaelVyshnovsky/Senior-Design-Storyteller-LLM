Kynorath Greybeard was a scholar and historian who lived in the city of Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.
Kynorath valued history, especially that of his home city, and did what he could to preserve it for others.
He was affiliated with the House of Knowledge, the temple of Oghma in Neverwinter.
The eruption of Mount Hotenow in the  was especially disastrous for the city of Neverwinter. Many Waukeenar shrines in the Tower District were destroyed, and along with them relics that had been gathered by the citys merchants.
Decades after the cataclysmic event, following the Siege of Neverwinter in the , Kynorath led a number of treasure hunts in the Tower District to recover these lost relics. He sent these to the House of Knowledge for preservation.
