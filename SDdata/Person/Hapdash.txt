Hapdash was a kobold slinger in Undermountain in 1479 DR.
Hapdash was in charge of creating alchemical products for the army of the mad half-elf mercenary Zarr.
Hapdash was very zealous in his work. He had lost faith in Zarr and only wanted to make more alchemical experiments. Hapdash attacked anyone who tried to touch his laboratory .
