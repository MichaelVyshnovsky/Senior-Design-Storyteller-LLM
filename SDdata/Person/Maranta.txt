Maranta was a mute cleric in Daggerford around 1370 DR.
Maranta was the cleric of a unidentified nature goodness. However, a powerful curse robbed from her her voice. She couldnt regain it even with the help of Khelben Arunsun, Alustriel, and two others of the Seven Sisters. Alustriel supposed the curse was the work of Malar.
She lived in a house in the southeast section of Daggerford in between Water Street and Kaoth Alley near the River Gate.
