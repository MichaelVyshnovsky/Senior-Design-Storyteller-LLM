Olpara Mindle was the wife of Corkaury Mindle. She briefly played host to Jivex while the latter was accompanying Taegan Nightwind in Lyrabar during Greengrass in , though the faerie dragon was quite upsetting. A halfling, she was plump, little, and had white, curled hair.
Per her husband, she liked to be woken up with griddlecakes slathered with butter and cherry syrup. During Greengrass of , she put tulips up in every room as observance of the spring festival.
