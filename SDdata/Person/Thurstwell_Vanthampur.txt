'''Thurstwell Vanthampur''' ({{Pronounce|THURST|well|_|VAN|tham|per|refs=}}) was the eldest son of [[Thalamra Vanthampur]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Thurstwell was born to Thalamra Vanthampur and her first husband in the 1450s DR.

By the {{YearlinkName|1492}},{{BG-DiA-time}} Thurstwell commanded several [[imp]]s, which he used to spy upon his brothers.
