'''Cleuvus''' was a swordsman of the [[Zhentarim]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
At the time of his death, he was lost in thought, contemplating how swordsmasters were only loved by [[coinlass]]es.

==Relationships==
He was part of a squad that included [[Othrogh]], a [[half-orc]], amongst others.

==History==
In {{yearlink|1357}}, Cleuvus was part of a group that tried to ambush [[Shandril Shessair]] in the [[Hullack Forest]]. He was ambushed and slain by [[Rathan Thentraver]], who then fought the rest of the squad.
