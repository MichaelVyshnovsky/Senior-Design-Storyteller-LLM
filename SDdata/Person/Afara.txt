'''Afara''' was a Griefguard in the [[House of Grief]] temple that hid the [[Cloister of Sombre Embrace]] in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Please, do not delay Contentment is within your grasp - just step inside, with an open mind.''|Griefguard Afara}}

==Description==
Afara a pale-skinned [[elf]] with straight raven-black hair and black eyes. She wore [[leather armor]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the [[Absolute crisis]], Afara served the [[Viconia DeVir|Mother Superior]] as one of her Griefguard at the gates of the House of Grief. Afara, along with the entirety of the cloister may have confronted its member [[Shadowheart]] were she to turn away from [[Shar]].
