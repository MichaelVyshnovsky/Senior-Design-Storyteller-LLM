Lord '''Aulauthar Orbryn''' was a mage of [[Cormanthyr]] working in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In {{yearlink|261}} he helped raise the ''[[mythal]]'' of [[Myth Drannor]]:{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|354, 356|Epilogue}} he attempted to contribute one of the minor powers of the effect. Unfortunately, he became the first victim of [[Uldreiyn Starym]], who slew him and attempted to hide in the place where his consciousness was{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|363, 365|Epilogue}} and thus he was unable to add powers to it.
