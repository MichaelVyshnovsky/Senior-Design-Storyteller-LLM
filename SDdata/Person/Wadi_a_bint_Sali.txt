Wadia bint Sali was an gnome shair within the Palace of the Enlightened Throne in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.
Wadia was an assistant of the Grand Vizier, Alyana al-Azzazi.
Her familiar was a fire gen named Ladok.
