'''Tacy Sands''' was a potter that lived in [[Baldur's Gate]] in [[1482 DR]].

==Description==
The potter lived in [[Rivington]], and produced bowls for [[Felogyr's Fireworks]]. Tacy made the bowls exactly to [[Avery Sonshal]]'s specifications.
