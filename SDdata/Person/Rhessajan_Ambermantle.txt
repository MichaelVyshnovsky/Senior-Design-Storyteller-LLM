'''Lady Rhessajan Ambermantle''', nicknamed '''Old Vixen''', was the ruler of [[Scornubel]] in [[1366 DR]] and she was generally loved and respected by the city's population. She had control over the militia, which was provided by the [[Red Shields]] mercenary company.
