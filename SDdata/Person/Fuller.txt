'''Fuller''' was one of the four [[Watchers of Candlekeep|Watchers]] of [[Candlekeep]], as of the {{YearlinkName|1368}}.

==Abilities==
He was particularly fond of [[mead]] and known to over-indulge during celebrations in the Watchers' barracks.

==Possessions==
Among his possessions, Fuller owned an [[Item enchantment|enchanted]] dagger that once belonged to his father. According to Fuller it was once used to kill a [[hobgoblin]] in a single strike.

==Relationships==
Fuller served alongside his fellow Watchers [[Hull]], the Keeper of the Portal, and two other individuals.
