'''Cerith Gell''' was a [[human]] inhabitant of [[Eveningstar]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}

==Description==
She had light brown hair parted into two small braids, brown eyes, and a light skin tone. She wore a short brown dress, white bodice, and leather [[corset]] with her leggings and boots.
