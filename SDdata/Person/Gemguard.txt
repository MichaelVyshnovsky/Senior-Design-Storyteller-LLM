'''Gemguard''' was a [[water weird]] guardian that remained in the ruins of the [[Onaglym]] in [[Myth Drannor]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==History==
The [[elemental]] was bound to the House of Gems, and remained there in a flooded pit while the [[Red Wizards]] of [[Droic Orgul]] took over the building.
