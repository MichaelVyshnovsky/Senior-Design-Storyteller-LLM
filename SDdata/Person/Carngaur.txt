Carngaur was a caravan guard in the Sword Coast during the mid-14 century DR.
He was one of Orthil Voldovans men.
During Orthil Voldovans caravan run from Scornubel to Waterdeep in late , Carngaur was one of the caravan guards. During an ambush by the Cult of the Dragon, he was struck with a lance, and later died a poisoned lance was suspected.
