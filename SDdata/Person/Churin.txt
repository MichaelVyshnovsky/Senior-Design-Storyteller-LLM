'''Churin''' was the warehouse manager of [[Gaspar]] the fishmonger in [[Easthaven]] within the [[Ten Towns]] of [[Icewind Dale]].

==Description==
The manager dressed in yellowish clothing.

==Activities==
Churin owned a warehouse in the village and was paid by Gaspar to store [[knucklehead trout]] and other emergency supplies.

==History==
During [[Eleint]], [[1281 DR]], Churin anticipated a shipment of supplies to aid Easthaven in the coming winter for Gaspar from [[Ilmus Gallaway]], but the caravan from [[Caer-Dineval]] was ambushed by an [[ogre]] raider and his [[orc]] henchmen.
