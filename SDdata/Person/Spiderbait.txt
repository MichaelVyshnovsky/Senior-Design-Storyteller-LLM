Spiderbait was a goblin living in the Silken Paths during the late 1480s or early 1490s DR.
He was a thrill-seeking individual. Spiderbait was a relatively sociable denizen of the Underdark.
The goblin was the inseparable friend and partner of Yuk Yuk. They called themselves the Web Runners.
He offered to help and guide strangers through the Underdark if they paid Yuk Yuk.
