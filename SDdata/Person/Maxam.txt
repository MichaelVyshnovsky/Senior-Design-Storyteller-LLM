Maxam was the consort of Storm Silverhand in the mid-14 century DR.
Storm Silverhand considered Maxams intelligence and kindness to rival her own, for whom adventuring was in the blood.
Maxam was an adventurer.
He was Storms man.
Maxam was an orphan. After they got together, Maxam went to live with Storm Silverhand in Shadowdale, where he remained for a decade, though they were adventurers throughout that time. He perished in the summer of , during a battle with tanarri at a gate opened by illithids.
