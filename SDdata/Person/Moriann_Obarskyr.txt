King '''Moriann Obarskyr''', born in the [[147 DR|Year of the Iron Colossus, 147 DR]], was the 14{{th}} [[Obarskyr]] monarch and king of [[Cormyr]]. He took the throne in the [[169 DR|Year of the Weary Warrior, 169 DR]] after the death of his father [[Irbruin Obarskyr|Irbruin]] and older brother [[Faerlthind Obarskyr|Faerlthind]].

Both of his heirs perished in the [[Goblin Wars (200 DR)|Goblin Wars]] of [[198 DR]] to [[200 DR]] against the [[goblin]]s from [[Hlundadim]], while he himself perished in the victory feast in the [[201 DR|Year of the Student, 201 DR]].{{Cite dragon/407/Blades of Kings|30-31}}
