Illusk
Tharbas was a golem assigned to guard the lake surrounding Khalusk.
Tharbas was a golem constructed from goliath bodyparts.
Tharbas was loyal to Khalusk and took his role seriously. It was his job to pass judgement on potential visitors. Enough coin might convince Tharbas to allow visitors to use one of his boats.
Tharbas could summon undead crows to attack any unauthorized visitors.
Tharbas maintained a collection of various boats and rafts that he used to ferry passengers to and from Khalusk.
