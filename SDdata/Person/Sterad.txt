Sterad was a warrior that lived in Vaasa during the late 14 century DR. He joined the famous expedition led by Vala Thorsdotter, descendant of Bodvar the Black, to seek out the whereabouts of Melegaunt Tanthul in the west during the early 1370s DR.
He was a stealthy man and capable of sneaking up elven warriors, even within their own territory.
The Vaasan expedition ended when Vala and her fellow Vaasans arrived at the Greypeak Mountains on Nightal 20 in the . Raven remained with the group during their initial encounter with elves of the Tomb Guard, an attack from a phaerimm behind the Sharn Wall, and onward as they entered into the city of Evereska.
