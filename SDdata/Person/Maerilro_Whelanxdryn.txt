Maerilro Whelanxdryn was a businessman of Elturel who operated Whelanxdryns of Elturel in the 15 century DR.
Maerilro was the latest of the Whelanxdryns to run their scabbardmaking business. Under his influence the business grew in size, and he began making sizable investments in Elturel, Baldurs Gate, and Iriaebor, as well as becoming an important landlord in those places.
