Rugglar Tossarim was a cooper inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Rugglar was the Guildmaster for the Coopers Guild during the early 1370s DR. He worked out of the Coopers Rest, their headquarters located on Pressbow Lane in Waterdeeps Dock Ward.
