'''Sabryn''' was a minor [[Zhentarim]] [[wizard]] in [[High Dale]].

==History==
In [[1358 DR]], Sabryn was a member of the secret [[Zhent]] occupation army in High Dale. He was killed discreetly by [[Elminster Aumar]] during a patrol duty.
