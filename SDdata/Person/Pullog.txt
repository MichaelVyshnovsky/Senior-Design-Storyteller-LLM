'''Pullog''' was a [[desert dwarf]] chieftain in [[Maztica]].

==History==
Pullog ruled over a village far to the south. In [[1361 DR]], after the [[Night of Wailing]], he was one of the chieftains gathered by [[Luskag]] to discuss the matter of the [[goblinoid]] armies from [[Nexal]].
