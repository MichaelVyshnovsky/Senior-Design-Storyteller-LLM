'''Jhavaerra Erbrand''' was a member of the [[Soft Claws]] in [[Neverwinter]] in [[1374 DR]]. She was a scout working for the [[Moonstone Mask]], who used her position to establish useful contacts in far cities and collected information about the [[Cult of the Dragon]].
