'''Imlon "the Touched"''' was the second [[Obarskyr]] monarch and king of [[Cormyr]]. He was crowned in [[55 DR|Year of the Caustic Blood, 55 DR]] and ruled for two years before his death in [[57 DR|Year of Lamp-lit Nights, 57 DR]].

==Relatives==
Imlon's father was the legendary [[Faerlthann Obarskyr]]. Imlon also had three younger brothers [[Embrus Obarskyr|Emrus]], [[Eskruis Obarskyr|Eskruis]] and [[Rhiiman the Glorious|Rhiiman]]; and a son, [[Bryndar Obarskyr|Bryndar]].
