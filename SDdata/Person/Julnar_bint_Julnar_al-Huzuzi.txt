'''Julnar bint Julnar al-Huzuzi''' supervised [[Huzuz]]'s Foreign Bureau in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

== Activities ==
Julnar reported to Ambassador [[Jiraad]], but she directly managed the bureaus of local relations, [[Zakhara]]n relations, and distant relations. She was a competent ambassador and bureaucrat.
