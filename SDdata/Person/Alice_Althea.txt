Alice Althea was a woman of means and a client of Sorcerous Sundries in the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Alice was a wood elven woman with fair skin tone with greenish tints. She had light short bob hair and was dressed in dark green finery.
In the , Alice found herself contemplating a purchase of a freshly reanimated zombie from Sorcerous Sundries. The undead was created by the shops in-house duergar necromancer Cordrane the Recent. However, Alice was unsure if the price of a zombie servant was worth it.
