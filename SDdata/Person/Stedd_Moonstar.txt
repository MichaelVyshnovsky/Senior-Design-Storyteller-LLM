'''Stedd Moonstar''' was the Lord of the [[Moonstar (family)|Moonstar]]  [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble family]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Gilding a Noble|44}}

{{quote|''While once just a pretty face among the lads, Lord Moonstar has bloomed over the winter months into a strapping young stag, and is sure catch the eye of many of Waterdeep's most eligible women.''|
Extract from a ''[[North Wind]]'' article.}}

==Description==
Stedd was a young lord with dark hair who liked to dress in fine clothes.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Gilding a Noble|6}}

==Personality==
Stedd Moonstar received the burden of leading his family at a particularly young age.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Heirloom|6}} Superstitious, he believed his family to be particularly unlucky, but refuted rumors about it being cursed.

==Activities==
Stedd Moonstar tried to rebuild its house to his former glory, by expanding its business activities and re-introducing his house to the nobility. With this goal in mind he organized the [[End of Winter Gala]], which he intended to be the best of the season.
