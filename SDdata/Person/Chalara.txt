Chalara was a news hawker who worked for the Baldurs Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Chalara was a very pale-skinned human child with mousy-blonde hair and brown eyes.
In the , during the Absolute crisis, Chalara peddled Baldurs Mouth Gazette in front of the Blushing Mermaid tavern in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate.
