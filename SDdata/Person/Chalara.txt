'''Chalara''' was a news hawker who worked for the ''[[Baldur's Mouth]] Gazette'' in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|''Good day, guv'nor, I gots a copy of the Bladder's Mouth Gazette fresh off the presses for you - good price, too!''|Chalara}}

==Description==
Chalara was a very pale-skinned [[human]] child with mousy-blonde hair and brown eyes.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the [[Absolute crisis]], Chalara peddled ''Baldur's Mouth Gazette'' in front of the [[Blushing Mermaid]] tavern in the Lower City of Baldur's Gate.
