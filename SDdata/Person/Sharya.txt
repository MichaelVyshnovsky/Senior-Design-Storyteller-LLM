'''Sharya''' was an armor merchant from [[Luskan]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. 

==History==
Around [[1487 DR]], Sharya had a stall in the Luskan market district next to [[Oraphelia Mayne|Burkins]], where she encountered the [[Champion of the Burning Dawn]].
