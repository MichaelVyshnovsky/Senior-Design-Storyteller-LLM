'''Lyzandra Calderos''', or '''Lyzzie''', was the third-in-command of the [[Cult of the Eternal Flame]] at the [[Temple of the Eternal Flame]] in [[Tyar-Besil]] during the [[Elemental Evil]] crisis.

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], Lyzzie was with four [[magmin]]s until some adventurers infiltrated the temple and confronted and defeated her.
