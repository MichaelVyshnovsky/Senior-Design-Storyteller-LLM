'''Takiyah''' was a [[Shadow Druid]] who lived in the [[Cloak Wood]] forest during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=BG">

==Personality==
He believed that most people who merely appreciated nature without taking up arms to protect it from intruders were naive and deserved to be "purged" from [[Faerûn]].<ref name=BG" />

==Relationships==
He was a contemporary of the druid [[Faldorn]], both of whom served under [[archdruid]] [[Amarande]].<ref name=BG" />
