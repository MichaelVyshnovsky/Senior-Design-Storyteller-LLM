'''Hsin Fu Chan''' was a sage of [[Shou Lung]] who wrote the ''[[Book of Change]]'', which detailed a system of divination through the use of trigrams.

Legend told that Hsin Fu Chan discerned the trigrams from patterns in the back of a great lizard as it slept.
