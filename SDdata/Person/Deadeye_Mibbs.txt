Fist "Deadeye" Mibbs was a member of the Flaming Fist and who served the city of Baldurs Gate in late 15 century DR.
"Deadeye" was a human man with light skin, light brown eyes eyes, and brown wavy hair. He wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist – a set of leathers adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia, typical for crossbowmen of the organization.
In the , "Deadeye" Mibbs was stationed just outside one of the gates between the Lower City of Baldurs Gate and its more affluent Upper City. His and the fellow Fists job was stopping rabble from entering the Upper City, allowing only those with much coin through. The Fists managed a slow-moving queue of rich and poor – city folk trying to return home or just find safety behind the second set of walls.
