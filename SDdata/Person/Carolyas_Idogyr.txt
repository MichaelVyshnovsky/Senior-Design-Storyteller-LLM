'''Carolyas Idogyr''' was a [[half-elf]] [[sorcerer]] of the [[Idogyr]] clan from [[Waterdeep]].

==Description==
Idogyr had wild curly red hair and flashing green eyes.

==Personality==
The half-elf was a wild, magic hurler.

==Relationships==
Carolyas Idogyr was the niece of [[Gamalon Idogyr]]. She was a  member of the [[Gray Hands]].
