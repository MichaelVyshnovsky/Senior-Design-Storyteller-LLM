'''Rumar''' was a well-to-do man who lived in [[Athkatla]] during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He was a regular patron of the [[Copper Coronet]] tavern located in the [[Athkatla/River District|River District]] of Athkatla. He made regular use of the tavern's "back rooms" in order to continue an affair with his lover [[Priss]].

==Relationships==
Rumar was married to a woman named [[Tiana]] while simultaneously maintaining relationship with Priss.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, Tiana caught wind of Rumar's dalliances and went to the Coronet to confront him over his affair.
