Gauntlet Dain was a solider of the Flaming Fist of that served during the late 15 century DR.
She was a determined individual who sprung into action during emergencies.
Her preferred weapon was a flanged mace.
Dain was present at the Waukeens Rest inn when it was set ablaze by cultists of the Absolute, the . She tried to help clear rubble away, to find a way past the flaming wreckage, but was largely unsuccessful.
