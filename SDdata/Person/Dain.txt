Gauntlet '''Dain''' was a solider of the [[Flaming Fist]] of that served during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Can't you see the inn is burning?! Help or get out of the way!|Guantlet Dain.}}

==Personality==
She was a determined individual who sprung into action during emergencies.

==Possessions==
Her preferred weapon was a flanged [[mace]].

==History==
Dain was present at the [[Waukeen's Rest]] inn when it was set ablaze by [[Cult of the Absolute|cultists]] of [[the Absolute]], the {{YearlinkName|1492}}. She tried to help clear rubble away, to find a way past the flaming wreckage, but was largely unsuccessful.
