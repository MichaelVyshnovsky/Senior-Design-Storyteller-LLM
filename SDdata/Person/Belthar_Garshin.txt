'''Belthar Garshin''' was a [[paladin]] of [[Torm]] that lived in [[Impiltur]] during the 8{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|729}}, the [[Battle of Horned Heads]] broke out in Impiltur between the nation's forces and the dark army of [[Soneillon]]. During the battle, [[Argosh|Argosh the Scaled]], the [[half-fiend]] spawn of the [[demon lord]] [[Eltab]] and consort of Soneillon.
