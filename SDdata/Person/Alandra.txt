Alandra was a member of the Cult of the Dragon in 1489 DR.
Alandra was a Cult member who joined the reformed faction under Severin Silrajin. She was the main cultist delegate to the great dragon Claugiyliamatar of Kryptgarden Forest.
She managed to establish an alliance with the green dragons primary agent, Garbul, also acquiring the Green Dragons Mask. However, in the end, both Garbul and Alandra were killed by an adventuring party hired to defeat Old Gnawbones.
