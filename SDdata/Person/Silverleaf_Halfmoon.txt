Silverleaf Halfmoon was an adventuring elf in the Dalelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He was blond and rugged-looking.
Silverleaf was a competent warrior, and wielded wizardly abilities as well.
Of note, he carried a bow and a quarterstaff.
He was an adventurer.
Silverleaf was from the same tribe as Taran Goldenstar.
In Freedale, he was part of an adventuring party with Beldar, Delvar Ironfist, Morganth, Slinker, and Lordan.
Silverleaf Halfmoon was part of an adventuring group in Freedale that may have rescued Taran Goldenstar, recovered the orb of dragonkind, faced Gunter Weiss in Harrow Hill, and perhaps explored Azazabus dungeon under Mount Dread.
