'''Garrick''' was a [[gnome]] and [[cleric]] of [[Tymora]] inhabiting the city of [[Daggerford]] in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He lived in a small house located in central Daggerford on the northern perimeter of the drill field.{{Cite interactive atlas|3990 Daggerford}} Garrick distanced himself from [[Fairfortune Hall]] and planned to test Tymora's blessings by traveling the countryside.
