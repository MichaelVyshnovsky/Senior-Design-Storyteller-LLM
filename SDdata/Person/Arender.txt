'''Arender''' was a legendary [[gnome]] [[architect]] and builder who lived in [[Tarmalune]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]].{{Cite dragon/372/Backdrop: Tarmalune|55}}

==History==
Arender was the architect who designed and built almost all the manors of [[Tarmalune/Arendermore|Arendermore]], one of the wards of Tarmalune, a long time before the [[Spellplague]].

His descendants became the most skilled builders in the history of Tarmalune.
