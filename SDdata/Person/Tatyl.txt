'''Tatyl''' was a [[transmuter]] and one of three minor [[wizards]] stationed at the [[Hinnar's Red Wizard enclave|enclave]] run by the young [[Red Wizard]] [[Hinnar]].

==Description==
Tatyl's body bore a magical tattoo

==Abilities==
Tatyl was tough, dexterous, and intelligent and was trained in [[alchemist|alchemy]], spellcraft, and the scribing of scrolls.

==Possessions==
Tatyl carried a [[dagger]] and a [[Light crossbow|small crossbow]] of superb craftsmanship.

Tatyl's spellbook contained 23 different spells.

==Activities==
Tatyl spent most of the day's hours in the compound's main barracks scribing spells with other wizards.
