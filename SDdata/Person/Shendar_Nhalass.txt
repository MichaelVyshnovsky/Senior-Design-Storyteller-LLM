'''Shendar Nhalass''' was the sixth [[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Nhalass Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Shendar inherited the throne when his father, [[Darthen Nhalass I|Darthen I]], died in {{Yearlink|-749}}. He ruled until his death in {{Yearlink|-700}}.

==Relationships==
His firstborn son and heir was [[Darthen Nhalass II|Darthen II]].
