'''Shurlash''' was the most powerful of the [[Northern Wizards]] in [[Unther]] in [[1357 DR]], and was that group's leader in [[1375 DR]].

==Personality==
Shurlash was a man of honor and honesty but he never saw the evil inside other people, believing all were good inside.
