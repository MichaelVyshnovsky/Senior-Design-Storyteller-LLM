Sauruki was a member of the Cult of the Crushing Wave at the Scarlet Moon Hall.
In battle, Sauruki dressed in a chain shirt and fought with quarterstaff.
During the Elemental Evil crisis in 1491 DR, Sauruki was sent by his masters to infiltrate the Cult of the Eternal Flames camp in order to spy on their Rite of the Wicker Giant. Around this time, Sauruki joined a group of druids known as Comnall, Murcadh, and Ragnad.
