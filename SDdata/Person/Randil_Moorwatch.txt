Randil Moorwatch was a mercenary in the 1370s DR.
In 1374 DR, Randil and his Blue Griffon Company were hired by the Sembian lord Borstag Duncastle to participate in the Cormanthor War. However, later, Randil and his men passed into the command of Miklos Selkirk.
Randil and his men intercepted Ilsevele Miritar and Fflar Starbrow Melruth, who were on a diplomatic mission, and questioned them.
