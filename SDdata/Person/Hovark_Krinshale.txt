'''Hovark Krinshale''' was a [[half-orc]] [[monk]] member of the [[Shadow Thieves]] in [[Skullport]] in [[1379 DR]].

==History==
In 1379 DR, Hovark was tasked with the mission to find and kill the slaver [[Lanador]], a rival of the [[Xanathar]], who had also betrayed the Shadow Thieves in [[1369 DR]]. Posing as a Skullport fisherman, Hovark tried to infiltrate the [[Unyielding Sword]] mercenaries working for Lanador but was captured.{{Cite dungeon/206/Eyes on the Ball}}  

==Personality==
Hovark would do anything for success in his mission, even working momentarily for his target.
