Henrietta Hhune was the daughter of Hurlbut Hhune of the Hhune patriar family of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
In the , Henrietta Hhune became romantically involved with handsome Manip Leythim of the Flaming Fist. Despite their tryst, Henrietta was already betrothed to Derque Rillyn of the Rillyn patriar family, a man of a notoriously poor attitude. Henriettas father, Hurlbut, organized Leythim to be beaten up by a group of thugs to scare away the manips affections and secure Henriettas highborn marriage. Leythim accepted the physical threat and the marriage, being afraid of any blowback against Henrietta.
