'''Lhorar Gildeggh''' was a guildmaster inhabiting [[Waterdeep]], City of Splendors, in the mid-to-late 13{{th}} century [[DR]]. He was the guildmaster of the [[Order of Master Shipwrights]].<ref>

==Activities==
In the {{YearlinkName|1262}}, Lhorar Gildeggh was one of two guildmasters to survive the brutal [[Guildwars]] that occurred in Waterdeep several years after [[Ahghairon]]'s death. Lhorar and his fellow surviving guildmaster, [[Ehlemm Zoar]], declare themselves the "Two Lords Magister" of the city.
