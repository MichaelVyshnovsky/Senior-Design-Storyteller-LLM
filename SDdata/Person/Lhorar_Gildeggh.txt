Lhorar Gildeggh was a guildmaster inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, in the mid-to-late 13 century DR. He was the guildmaster of the Order of Master Shipwrights.
In the , Lhorar Gildeggh was one of two guildmasters to survive the brutal Guildwars that occurred in Waterdeep several years after Ahghairons death. Lhorar and his fellow surviving guildmaster, Ehlemm Zoar, declare themselves the "Two Lords Magister" of the city.
