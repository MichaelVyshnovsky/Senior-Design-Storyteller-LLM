Ruldel was a Cormyte soldier in the mid-14 century DR, part of the Eastern Stonelands Company.
He had a hard face and a gray moustache.
He was eager to fight the Zhents.
Ruldel was part of the guard on Thundarlun in late  when Shandril Shessairs party arrived at the town. While she was being interrogated as a traveler, the guard was attacked by Zhentilar forces in pursuit of the girl Ruldel was quickly slain.
