'''Ravos Calantar''' was a member of the [[Calantar]] family, a noble house of [[Cormyr]].

==History==
Following the execution of his aunt and matriarch of his family, [[Calantar (Lady)|Lady Calantar]], Ravos came to hate the [[Obarskyr]]s with a passion. By [[1372 DR]], the Cormyrean rulers expected that he was likely to rebel against them.

==Personality==
Ravos was bored with life and sought to perform great deeds.

He enjoyed hunting both [[stag]]s and [[bandit]]s.
