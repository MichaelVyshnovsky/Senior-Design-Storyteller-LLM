'''Naia Bonebreaker''' was the wife of butcher [[Durto Bonebreaker]], who helped him and his sibling operate the [[Two Brothers' Butchery]] in [[Ravens Bluff]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Naia wore her dark brown hair braids and had sparkling green eyes. She stood {{SI|3'4|ft-in|long=yes}} tall and weighed around {{SI|95|lb|k|long=yes}}.

==Personality==
She was a rather quiet and hardworking individual.

==Abilities==
Naia was proficient in fighting with [[handaxe]]s. Because of her upbringing she also had some experience with mining, tracking things underground, and appraising objects.

==Possessions==
Naia typically dressed in green clothes that complemented her eyes.

==Activities==
At the Two Brothers' Butchery, Naia was typically managing the storefront and dealing with most customers. Once a week, when her husband was not working at the Two Brothers' Butchery, Naia would go with him to the local [[The Dancing Bear Inn|Dancing Bear Inn]].{{Cite polyhedron/59/The Living City: The Two Brothers' Butchery|8}}

==History==
Naia was born into a family of miners. Her later marriage to Durto made her long for the days of being underground, causing her to enjoy strolling the icehouse caverns of the butchery despite her sometimes being frightened within them.
