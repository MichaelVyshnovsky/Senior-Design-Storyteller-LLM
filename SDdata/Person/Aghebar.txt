Aghebar was an agent of the Zhentarim during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He was a subordinate of the Head of Caravans Meer, both before and after the rise of Fzoul Chembryl within the Black Network. Aghebar had a number of low-level Zhentarim merchants who reported directly to him.
