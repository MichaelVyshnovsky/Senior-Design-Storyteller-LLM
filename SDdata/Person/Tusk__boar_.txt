'''Tusk''' was a boar that lived in the [[Emerald Grove]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|*Grunts*}}

==Personality==
Tusk was a rather impatient boar, bordering on boorish even.

==Activities==
He enjoyed laying out and basking in the warmth of sunlight.

==History==
Tusk became upset when Master [[Halsin]] went missing from the Emerald Grove in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}. The kindly [[archdruid]] had promised to bring a mated boar for Tusk as a companion.
