'''Gurk''' was a [[warlock]] that devoted his life to [[the Absolute]], living among the Absolute'sfaithful within the [[Shattered Sanctum]] for a time during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Like other fanatics of the [[cult of the Absolute]], Gurk was branded with a [[mark of the Absolute|distinct brand]] by High Priestess [[Gut]].

==History==
Gurk was among the [[goblin]]s at the Shattered Sanctum celebrating their successful raid of the [[Waukeen's Rest]] inn, some time in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
