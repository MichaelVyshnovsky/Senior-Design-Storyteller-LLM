Xeno Mirrormane was a cleric of Cyric.
Xenos face was covered with wrinkles and he had curly silver-white hair. He wore long purple robes and a deaths-head ring on one finger.
Xeno murdered his brother Maskul Mirrormane.
Xeno turned 60 in 1368 DR.
Xeno kept a dirk in the sleeve of his robe.
