Fenkenkabradon Ishkhanak was a dragonborn of Tymanther and the patriarch of Clan Fenkenkabradon in 1487 DR.
Ishkhanak had dull silver scales and many scars on his body, the most prominent a bright line that bisected the left side of his snout.
Ishkhanak was the elder brother of Fenkenkabradon Dokaan.
Ishkhanak expended most of his youth hunting dragons in the Smoking Mountains.
In 1487 DR, Ishkhanak allied with Verthisathurgiesh Anala and Yrjixtilex Vardhira to put Yrjixtilex Kallan as the interim Vanquisher, in preparation for the First Tymanther-Unther War and to outmaneuver his rivals, Clan Kepeshkmolik.
