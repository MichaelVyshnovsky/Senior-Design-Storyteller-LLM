Kishiria was a human warrior who worked for the Red Wizards of Thay in the southern part of the Sea of Fallen Stars.
She had sharp facial features dotted with freckles, and curly, orange hair, cropped short. She wore a black cloak, and was dressed in a blue shirt underneath her armor.
In addition to her breastplate, Kishiria owned a large claymore sword.
Stories circulated about Kishirias prowess with her claymore. Notably, she was known to have beheaded three men in a single stroke in battle.
