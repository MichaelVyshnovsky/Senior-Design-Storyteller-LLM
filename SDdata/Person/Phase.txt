Phase was an agent of the Zhentarim, temporarily allied with The Guild of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Phase was a light-skinned rock gnome with long brown hair and hazel eyes. He wore a monks garb, dyed in greens and yellows â€“ the colors of the Zhentarim, and was armed with daggers.
In the , Nine-Fingers Keene of The Guild forged a temporary alliance with the Zhentarim during the strife that was brought on by the war with the Cult of the Absolute. Phase was among the Zhentish mercenaries hired by Nine-Fingers to hold the Guilds territories being claimed by the man known as the Stone Lord. Phase disliked Nine-Fingers looking down on the Zhentarim hirelings and was ready to join Roah Moonglow and Friol in their attempts to usurp the Guild for the Zhentarim. It was unrecorded if the Zhents takeover was successful nor whether Phase survived the guild war.
