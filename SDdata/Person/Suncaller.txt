'''Suncaller''' was a [[lizardfolk]] chieftain of the [[Scaly Death]] tribe in the [[Mere of Dead Men]].

==History==
Just before the start of the [[Tyranny of Dragons]], Suncaller was killed by the [[bullywug]] chieftain [[Pharblex Spattergoo]].
