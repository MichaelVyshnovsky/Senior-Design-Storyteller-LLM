Mizri Mizzrym was the matron mother of House Mizzrym in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
She was considered comely but perpetually wore an ugly scowl.
Mizri was the matron mother of her house by 1361 DR and through 1372 DR.
Circa 1486 DR, she dominated the citys slave trade.
She had two twin daughters Greyanna and Sabal. She favored Greyanna, the eldest, until Greyannas death in 1372 DR at the hands of her brother Pharaun.
