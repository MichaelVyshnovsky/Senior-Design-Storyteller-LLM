'''Arn Widdens''' was an owner of the [[Cart and Coin]] caravan outfitter in [[Triboar]] in the [[Dessarin Valley]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Arn was married to [[Syreen Widdens]] and together they owned the store and had four young children.
