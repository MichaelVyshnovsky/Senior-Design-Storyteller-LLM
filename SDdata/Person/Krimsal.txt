Krimsal was a Lord of Cormyr and a serial killer of the late 15 century DR.
During the Open Feast at Castle Obarskyr in Mirtul 26 of the , Krimsal was discussed by Jhalikoe, and an envoy from Milvarune. Jhalikoe insisted that Krimsal, while responsible for much murder and mutilation, was no worse than other nobles, just more open about it. At the time of that conversation, Lord Krimsal was in hiding, after his murders the previous winter.
His exploits were known as far as Milvarune.
