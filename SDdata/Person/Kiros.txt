Chief '''Kiros''' was the leader of the [[centaur]]s of [[Guard (village)|Guard]] in [[Bloodstone Barony]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He had a grizzled, slow-talking, but powerful personality. He was always terribly formal and precise. 

==Activities==
Kiros was a kind of sage about history, warfare, and nature. He served as the leader of Guard's Council of Elders, which included War Chief [[Valon]] and Elder [[Shinar]].

==History==
By [[1358 DR]], Kiros had led his village in joining the [[Alliance of Belt Watchers]] with alongside the [[halfling]]s of [[Waukeshire]] and the [[dwarves]] of [[Clan Orothiar]]. That year, Kiros and his fellows met [[Gareth Dragonsbane]]'s party.
