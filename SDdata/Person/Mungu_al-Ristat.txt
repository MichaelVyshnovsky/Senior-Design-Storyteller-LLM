'''Mungu al-Ristat''' was a [[dwarf]] and Chief ''[[Midani dictionary|Wadi]]'' in [[Fahhas]] in the [[Pantheist League]] of [[interior Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Personality==
Mungu was stressed and overworked.

==Activities==
He performed each of the caliph's wishes exactly as they were worded. He did anything within his power to avoid the need to bring questions to the caliph.

==Abilities==
Mungu was a skilled [[askar]].
