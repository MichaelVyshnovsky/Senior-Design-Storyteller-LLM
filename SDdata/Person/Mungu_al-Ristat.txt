Mungu al-Ristat was a dwarf and Chief Wadi in Fahhas in the Pantheist League of interior Zakhara in 1367 DR.
Mungu was stressed and overworked.
He performed each of the caliphs wishes exactly as they were worded. He did anything within his power to avoid the need to bring questions to the caliph.
Mungu was a skilled askar.
