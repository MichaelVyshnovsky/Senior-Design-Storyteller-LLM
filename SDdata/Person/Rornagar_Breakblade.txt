Rornagar Breakblade was a member of the Bloodshields Band adventuring party in the late 15 century DR.
He had keen eyes.
As an adventurer with the Bloodshields Band, he mostly kept watch at the rearguard.
Rornagar Breakblade was a member of the Bloodshields Band his leader was Gaerond.
While looking for Elminster Aumar in Shadowdale during the early spring of the , the leader of the Bloodshields Band, Gaerond, called on Rornagars talent at spotting to determine where their guide, Thal, had disappeared to after showing them the way to Elminsters cave.
The whole band was slain by doppelgangers, led by a fake Elminster, after they conversed with him briefly.
