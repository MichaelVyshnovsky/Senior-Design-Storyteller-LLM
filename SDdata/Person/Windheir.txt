Duke '''Windheir''' was the most prominent member of the [[avoral]]s and one of the [[Talisid and the Five Companions|Companions]] of the Celestial Lion [[Talisid]] of [[Elysium]]. According to the [[World Tree cosmology]], Windheir resided with Talisid and the other [[guardinal]] [[Celestial paragon|paragons]], in the [[House of Nature]].

==Description==
Windheir was the wisest and most powerful of all avorals.

==Abilities==
The duke shared all powers common to avorals, including the ability to shoot forth [[lightning bolt]]s.

==Relationships==
Winheir had a daughter, named [[Morningwind]], who served as the guardinal ambassador of the town of [[Release-From-Care]] on [[Amoria]] to the [[gate-town]] of [[Ecstasy]] in the [[Outlands]].

==History==
By the mid- to late-14{{th}} century [[DR]], Duke Windheir had retired and been replaced by Lady [[Zvestra]] and later by Dutchess [[Sathia]].
