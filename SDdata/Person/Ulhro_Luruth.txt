'''Ulhro Luruth''' was a shopkeeper in [[Red Larch]] and a member of the [[Believers]] during the [[Elemental Evil]] crisis in [[1491 DR]].

==Personality==
Ulhro knew that he was prohibited to talk about the Believers to strangers, but he could be easily tricked.

==Activities==
Ulhro ran a tannery shop in the village.
