'''Dracil''' was a [[dwarf]] who lived within the [[Forest of Amtar]] in [[Dambrath]].

==Activities==
Dracil was the leader of the nefarious adventuring group known as [[Dracil's Raiders]]. His group preyed upon smaller caravans who traveled the [[Trader's Way]] to the east of the great forest.

==Possessions==
Dracil owned a [[short sword of speed]]. This sword was a sentient weapon that allowed him to fly for up to one hour per day.
