'''Drexa''' was the chieftain of the [[Blackmaw]] [[lizardfolk]] tribe at the [[Fane of the Eye]] under [[Tyar-Besil]] and a member of the [[Cult of the Crushing Wave]] during the [[Elemental Evil]] crisis.

==Personality==
The [[lizard king]] believed that he should be one of the leaders of the Crushing Wave. He dreamed of overthrowing [[Gar Shatterkeel]] one day.

==Relationships==
Drexa led his tribe in the worship of [[Olhydra]] with the counsel of the shaman [[Sskesh]].

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], Drexa and his tribe were at the Fane of the Eye until some adventurers infiltrated the Fane and confronted and defeated him and his minions.
