Sabbagh was a human clericIt is unclear exactly which deity Sabbagh worships. The hammer symbol indicates that it could be Tyr or Gond, but it is not explicitly stated. from Ravens Bluff who enjoyed giving small hammers to children.
Clad from top to toe in chainmail, adorned with a hooded white tabard with blue trim, he had light skin and blonde hair.
Aside from his bag of hammers and chainmail, Sabbagh carried a large hammer and a magical shield.
Sabbagh originally hailed from Ravens Bluff, but took to the road after becoming frustrated with the Lord Mayors intervention in the matters of his church. He was fond of children, and handed out small replicas of his own hammer to any he met.
