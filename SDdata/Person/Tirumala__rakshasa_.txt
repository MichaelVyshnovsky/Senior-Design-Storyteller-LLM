Tirumala was a rakshasa who founded the city of Tirumala in the Beastlands and ruled as its rajah through the late 15 century DR.
Tirumala constructed their eponymous city upon the ruins of an old Durpari settlement, and declared it to be a holy site.
By the , one of Tirumalas servants was a fellow rakshasa named Devang, who had become a trusted vizier to the rajah of Estagund and helped to push Tirumalas interests.
