'''Jinara al-Munsif''' was a princess of [[Hudid]] in the [[Pantheist League]] of [[interior Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Personality==
She despised the strict nature of the League of the Pantheon. Unlike the rest of her family, she had no problem lying to get what she wanted.


==History==
Jinara was born in [[Huzuz]]. Upon reaching maturity she declared her independence and left her family to find her own way.

She lived in [[Tajar]] within the [[Pearl Cities]] region of [[west Zakhara]] in {{Yearlink|1367}}, where she managed a trading firm.{{AQ-time}} She did not use her family name in order to remain anonymous, instead choosing to go by the name "Jina".

==Relationships==
Jinara was the daughter of Caliph [[Sajah al-Munsif]]. She had a single sister named [[Inara al-Munsif|Inara]].
