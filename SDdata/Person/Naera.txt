'''Naera''' was an inhabitant of [[Shadowdale]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Possessions==
She received a chain of some sorts from [[Torm (adventurer)|Torm]].

==Relationships==
She was pursuing Torm, of the [[Knights of Myth Drannor]]. [[Rold]], a guard, was her uncle.

==History==
One night in the {{YearlinkName|1357}}, Torm was called away from her bed to guard [[Shandril Shessair]]. He gave her a chain as an apology.

==Rumors & Legends==
[[Jhessail Silvertree]] once teased Torm that Naera was his bride-to-be.{{cite book/Spellfire/Paperback 1988|193|10}}{{cite book/Spellfire/Paperback 2005||11}}
