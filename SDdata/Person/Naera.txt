Naera was an inhabitant of Shadowdale in the mid-14 century DR.
She received a chain of some sorts from Torm.
She was pursuing Torm, of the Knights of Myth Drannor. Rold, a guard, was her uncle.
One night in the , Torm was called away from her bed to guard Shandril Shessair. He gave her a chain as an apology.
Jhessail Silvertree once teased Torm that Naera was his bride-to-be.
