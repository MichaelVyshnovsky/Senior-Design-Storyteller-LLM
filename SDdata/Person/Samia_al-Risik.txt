Imam Samia al-Risik was a High Priest of the Mosque of Zann the Wise in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.
Samia was the second of Imam Renn min Zann, leading services in the mosque since his duties kept him at the Golden Mosque much of the time.
She was an expert linguist, being completely fluent in 27 languages. She was extremely interested in learning Drow and Affa, and was willing to pay for the chance to learn from a fluent speaker.
