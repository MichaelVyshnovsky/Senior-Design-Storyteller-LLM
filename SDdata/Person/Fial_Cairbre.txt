'''Fial Cairbre''' was a [[human]] [[proxy]] and alleged son of the [[Celtic pantheon|Celtic]] and [[Faerûnian pantheon|Faerûnian]] [[power]] known as [[Oghma]].

==Personality==
Fial was a very eloquent and persuasive speaker.

==Abilities==
His skills in [[bard]]ing with a [[harp]] were quite exceptional, able to quiet anyone who heard them. He was even capable of [[raise dead|raising the dead]] and killing the living through use of his barding skills.

==Activities==
Fial spent much of his time wandering across the [[plane]]s, though he occasionally visited the [[Prime Material plane|Prime Material]] world of [[Toril]].

==Relationships==
[[Arawn]] felt no ill will towards the deaths and resurrections that Fial performed, as the man made sure to keep the balance of such things equal.
