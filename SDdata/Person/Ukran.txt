==Home==
Ukran lived in a blue two-story house on [[Magician's Row]]  It was the second of five houses. Written on the wall above the door were the [[Thorass alphabet|Thorass letters]] for "[[Thay]]".

==Description==
Ukran had a [[crow]] [[familiar]], smoked Dragonfire [[tobacco]], and was primarily a [[tea]] drinker.

==History==
''Circa'' [[1357 DR]], the [[City Guard (Ravens Bluff)|City Guard]] serving Regent of the Exchequer [[Vernon Condor]] considered Ukran the Great for the theft of two minor [[magic item]]s of historical importance from the city vaults, after suspicions and [[divination]]s tracked the thieves to Magician's Row. However, Ukran was innocent.
