'''Karst''' was a ferryman in [[Phlan]] in [[1489 DR]].

==Personality==
Karst was an old individual. He continued his work only because he knew nothing else to do.

==History==
Karst managed the ferry that transported people to [[Sokol Keep]] for forty years.{{Cite adventurers league/Secrets of Sokol Keep|10}}
