'''Hurc''' was a confident [[kobold]] who lived in the [[Silver Marches]] in the late-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, Hurc participated in an attack on the village of [[Hilltop]] with a small band of his fellow kobolds. During the attack he got separated from the main group and barricaded himself in the kitchen of the [[Bubbling Cauldron]] tavern, taking the chef [[Mara (chef)|Mara]] as hostage.
