Thurlock () was a wizard in Hillsfar in 1357 DR.
As a skilled diviner, Thurlock revealed the hidden for the wealthy citizens of Hillsfar. She was in constant search of more powerful divination-related magic items.
Rumors said that Thurlocks treasure vault was full of powerful magic and scrolls containing both current gossip and old legends. However, it was protected by magical creatures and traps.
Thurlock was assisted by the half-elf cleric of Deneir, Thondar Glimmershield.
