'''Keonid''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and very prolific [[spell]] inventor who lived during the [[Golden Age]].

==History==
Keonid developed nine spells during their life, most of which were focused on magic that manipulated the mind. The spells they invented in the 20{{th}} century [[NY]] were ''[[geas]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1958|NY}}, ''[[confusion]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1959|NY}},  ''[[friends]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1969|NY}}, ''[[emotion]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1976|NY}}, ''[[fear]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1984|NY}}, and ''[[forget]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1992|NY}}.

The spells they invented in the 21{{th}} century NY were ''[[charm person]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2004|NY}}, ''[[mass charm]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2009|NY}}, and finally, ''[[suggestion]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2010|NY}}.
