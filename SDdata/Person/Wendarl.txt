'''Wendarl''' was an old monk of [[Candlekeep]].

==Activities==
Wendarl was a superb calligrapher in Candlekeep.

==History==
In [[1487 DR]], he was killed by the [[shadovar]] agent [[Maerandor]], disguised as the head cook.
