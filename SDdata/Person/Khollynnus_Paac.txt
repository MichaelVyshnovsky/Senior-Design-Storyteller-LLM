'''Khollynnus Paac''' was a textile merchant and the leader of the largest [[Cowled Wizards]] cell in [[Amn]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Khollynnus had skin of [[olive]] hue and strawberry blonde hair.

==Abilities==
She was proficient in ''[[conjuration]]'' magic and had access to such powerful [[spell]]s as ''[[Abi-Dalzim's horrid wilting]]'', ''[[chain lightning]]'', ''[[circle of death]]'', ''[[pierce magic]]'', ''[[ruby ray of reversal]]'', ''[[spell turning]]'', ''[[maze]]'', ''[[simulacrum]]'', and many others.

==History==
Khollynnus led the first cell of the Cowled Wizards in Amn. Her group's secret base was hidden underneath the [[Jann Lane]].
