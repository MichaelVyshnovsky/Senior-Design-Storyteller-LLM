Khollynnus Paac was a textile merchant and the leader of the largest Cowled Wizards cell in Amn in the late 14 century DR.
Khollynnus had skin of olive hue and strawberry blonde hair.
She was proficient in conjuration magic and had access to such powerful spells as Abi-Dalzims horrid wilting, chain lightning, circle of death, pierce magic, ruby ray of reversal, spell turning, maze, simulacrum, and many others.
Khollynnus led the first cell of the Cowled Wizards in Amn. Her groups secret base was hidden underneath the Jann Lane.
