'''Lady Terese Marliir''' was a member of the noble [[Marliir]] family of [[Cormyr]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

In the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Lady Terese attended King [[Azoun Obarskyr IV]]'s birthday feast, hosted by Lord [[Partic Thistle]] at [[Thistleflame Keep]]. She was accompanied there by Lord [[Raynaar Marliir]].
