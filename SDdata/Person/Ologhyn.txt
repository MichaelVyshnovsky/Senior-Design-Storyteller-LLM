'''Ologhyn of Voonlar''' was a [[wizard]] and a member of the [[Zhentarim]]. He served as a mageling to [[Ruatheene]] and was based in a [[Zhentilar]] camp in the valley of [[Arntethyl]] by the river [[Tesh]].

Ologhyn was known for his collection of rare and strange wands.
