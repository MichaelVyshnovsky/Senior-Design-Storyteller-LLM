'''Dwyn the Still''' was an [[elf]] [[monk]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==Description==
Dwyn had green eyes and a close-shaven scalp. She wore a leafy green outfit.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}} during the [[Elven Crusade]], Dwyn joined the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|fortified camp]] outside the ruins of [[Myth Drannor]]. She provided training to monks at the camp.
