'''Urmas''' was a [[stone giant]] librarian living in [[Gravenhollow]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Urmas was the busiest of the librarians there.

==Activities==
Urmas regularly kept in touch with all [[giant]] communities in the [[Underdark]] using a vast network of magically augmented subterranean beasts used as messengers. One of his contacts was Stonekeeper [[Hgraam]] in [[Gracklstugh]]. He was also in constant contact with all stone giant mystics and some [[xorn]] and [[galeb duhr]] servants.

==Personality==
Urmas was a patient and polite individual on surface. However he disliked people or things that disturbed his work.
