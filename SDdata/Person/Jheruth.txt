Jheruth was a githyanki child from Crèche Yllek on Toril in the late 15 century DR.
Ghaan had short red hair and was dressed in leather pants and bracers of githyanki design.
Githyanki of what would become Crèche Yllek, attacked and claimed the Rosymorn Monastery of Lathander in the Western Heartlands sometime in the late 15 century DR. Jheruth was hatched in the Crèche and, along with other gith children, attended military and combat training around the , during the Absolute crisis.
