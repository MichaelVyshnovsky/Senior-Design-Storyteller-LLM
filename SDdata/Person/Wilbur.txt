'''Wilbur''' was a Griefguard in the [[House of Grief]] temple that hid the [[Cloister of Sombre Embrace]] in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Take your time, look around. Only you can decide when it is time for healing.''|Griefguard Wilbur}}

==Description==
Wilbur was a [[human]] man with a fair skin tone brown hair, short beard, and blue eyes. He wore a [[leather armor]] set.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the [[Absolute crisis]], Wilbur served the [[Viconia DeVir|Mother Superior]] as one of her Griefguard inside the House of Grief. Wilbur, along with the entirety of the cloister may have confronted its member [[Shadowheart]] were she to turn away from [[Shar]].
