'''Badiat al-Farif''' was a member of the court of [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Gem of Zakhara|70}}

==Activities==
Concerned because the [[Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir|Grand Caliph]] was unable to produce a male heir, she inquired about the issue at the [[Mosque of Zann the Wise]], tasking them to try to find a solution to the problem. Members of the [[Readers]] spent many months searching through texts, hoping to experience an insightful vision that would shed some light on the problem.
