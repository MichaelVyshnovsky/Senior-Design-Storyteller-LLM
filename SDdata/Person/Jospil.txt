'''Jospil''' was a soldier of the [[Flaming Fist]] who lived in [[Baldur's Gate]] during the midâ€”14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was known to slip into bouts of depression when faced with seemingly overwhelming circumstances. He was receptive to words of self-determination and an earnest call to action.

==Possessions==
Jospil was in the possession of an ''[[item enchantment|enchanted]] [[Brevin's quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]'' that had several owners over the years, and was stored for at least some time in [[Candlekeep]].

==Activities==
For at least some time Jospil was stationed at the [[High Hall]] in the [[Baldur's Gate/Upper City|Upper City]].

==History==
Jospil became increasingly distressed when the [[War of the Shining Crusade]] broke out on the [[Sword Coast]] in [[Uktar]] of the {{yearlinkName|1368}}.
