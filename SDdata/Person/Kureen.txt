Lady Kureen was a noblewoman from the city of Samargol, Samarach, circa 1374 DR.
Circa the , Lady Kureen offered patronage and protection to several merchants who operated in cities and towns of Samarach. One of the traders she patronized operated in the hamlet of Nimbre. In 1374 DR, the villagers of Nimbre were accused of being associated and corrupted by the much-hated yuan-ti and the Samarach Patrol was rounding up the accused to stand trial in Samargol. Lady Kureens merchant, however, remained unharassed, thanks to the noblewomans influence.
