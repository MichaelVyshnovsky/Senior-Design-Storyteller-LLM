'''Utuhegel''' was the chief priestess of [[Ishtar]] in [[Unthalass]] in [[Unther]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
She believed that the clergy of Ishtar must fight [[Gilgeam]] and was gathering like-minded priestess in order to start an attack.
