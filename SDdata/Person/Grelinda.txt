Grelinda was a young woman that lived in Phlan and worked at Madame Freonas Tea Kettle during the late 15 century DR. She was one of Madame Freonas five daughters.
She had short-cropped hair that was the greenish color that copper turned to over an extended period of time.
Grelindas other sisters included Blaizette, Briez, Reece, and Whittlee.
