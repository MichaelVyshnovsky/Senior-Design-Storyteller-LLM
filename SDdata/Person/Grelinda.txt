'''Grelinda''' was a young woman that lived in [[Phlan]] and worked at [[Madame Freona's Tea Kettle]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. She was one of Madame [[Freona]]'s five daughters.{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|17}}

{{Quote|Stupid boring work.|Grelinda, when clearing plates at the Tea Kettle.}}

==Description==
She had short-cropped hair that was the greenish color that [[copper]] turned to over an extended period of time.

==Relationships==
Grelinda's other sisters included [[Blaizette]],{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|23}} [[Briez]],{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|11}} [[Reece]],{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|6}} and [[Whittlee]].{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|27}}
