'''Seszgar Huntcrown''' was a heir of House [[Huntcrown]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
When he was decapitated, his features got frozen in terror. His usual face was a sneer.{{cite book/Elminster Must Die/MMP|229|22}}

==Personality==
He was generally known as a sneering braggart.{{cite book/Elminster Must Die/MMP|261|24}}

==Abilities==
Seszgar was purportedly confident in his fencing skills.

==Relationships==
[[Marlin Stormserpent]] hated him until the day he died. Seszgar was the heir of House [[Huntcrown]].

==History==
In his childhood, the Huntcrown heir had bullied a young Marlin Stormserpent, who bore a grudge against him to the end.

In {{yearlink|1479}}, Seszgar was slain by the [[Blueflame ghost]]s [[Relve Langral]] and [[Treth Halonter]], sent by Marlin Stormserpent against him.{{cite book/Elminster Must Die/MMP|233-236|22}} They carried out his head in a bundle, which they presented to Marlin Stormserpent after the fact.

==Rumors & Legends==
While [[Ganrahast]] and [[Vainrence]] were briefly informed that Seszgar Huncrown was plotting with [[Rothglar Illance]] and [[Harmond Hawklin]] to unleash a "ball of [[Spellplague]]" in the [[Council of the Dragon]] of {{yearlink|1479}}, this was a plot by Marlin Stormserpent.{{cite book/Elminster Must Die/MMP|84-85|8}}
