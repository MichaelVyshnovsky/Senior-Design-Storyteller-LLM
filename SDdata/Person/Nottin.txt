'''Nottin''' was a [[hedge wizard]] who lived on the [[Sword Coast North]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|It's a shame to see good [[material component|magical components]] go to waste...}}

==History==
While [[Helm's Hold]] was [[Siege of Helm's Hold|under siege]], ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Nottin set himself up at a small camp located just outside of city on the outskirts of [[Watcher's Forest]], joininh the likes of the ranger [[Dassar Truebow]], the merchant named [[Kella|Kella the Fair-Dealing]], and [[Meryth Heartwood]]. He aided the [[Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge]] with the sabotage of [[material component]]s used by the [[Ashmadai]] [[conjurer]]s to complete their [[devil]]-summoning [[ritual]]s.
