Mreskan Haundle was the seneschal of Eskorn, Laerakond, as of 1479 DR.
Mreskan was an old but kind man.
When King Ornan Roaryl was traveling across the kingdom, Mreskan was left in charge of the rule of Eskorn. He used the Seeing Stones to communicate with the king whenever was needed.
