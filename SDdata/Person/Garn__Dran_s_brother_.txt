'''Garn''' was a treasure hunter in th [[Moonwood]].

==History==
In [[1484 DR]], Garn, his twin [[Dran]], and [[Lisstra]] followed [[Karrik]] to explore the ruins of [[Methegrist]].

==Relationship==
Garn was the twin brother of Dran. Like his twin, Garn was totally loyal to Karrik.
   
==Personality==
Garn was the quiet one of the twins, but in truth he always had no idea about what to do.
