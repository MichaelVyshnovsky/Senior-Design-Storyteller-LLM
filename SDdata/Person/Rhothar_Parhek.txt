King '''Rhothar Parhek''' was the third [[Amn]]ian monarch of the [[Parhek]] dynasty.

==Relationships==
Rhothar was the third son of King [[Malek Parhek]] and brother of King [[Derek Parhek|Derek]]. His only heir was [[Dhanar Parhek|Dhanar]].

==History==
During his reign, the realm began to suffer from the intrigues of powerful merchant families as they challenged the royal authority.
