Kepeshkmolik Narghon was a dragonborn of Tymanther and the patriarch of Clan Kepeshkmolik in 1486 DR. He was the father of Kepeshkmolik Uadjit, and a direct descendant of Shasphur.
Narghon had tarnished brass, green and dark scales
Narghon always resented Mehen for having spurned his daughter Uadjit.
In 1486 DR, Narghon was investigating a series of murders in the Catacombs of Djerad Thymar.
