'''Hildreth Highhammer''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] adventurer from [[Easthaven]] within the [[Ten Towns]] of [[Icewind Dale]].

==Description==
The dark-haired dwarf woman dressed in a blue [[wikipedia:tunic|tunic]] and brown [[leather armor|leathers]].

==Possessions==
Even when drinking at [[Grisella]]'s [[Winter's Cradle Tavern]], Hildreth carried an [[axe]].

==History==
In [[Eleint]], [[1281 DR]], Hildreth joined [[Hrothgar]]'s expedition to aid [[Kuldahar]] in the [[Spine of the World]].

Hildreth was killed when [[frost giant]]s attacked the expedition in [[Kuldahar Pass]].
