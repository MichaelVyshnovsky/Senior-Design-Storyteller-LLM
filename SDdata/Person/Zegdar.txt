'''Zegdar''' was a [[minotaur]] chieftain and an ally of the [[Cult of the Eternal Flame]] at the [[Fane of the Eye]] under [[Tyar-Besil]] during the [[Elemental Evil]] crisis.

==Relationships==
Zegdar led the [[Emberhorn]] tribe.

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], [[Vanifer]] sent [[Wyx]], a [[nothic]] fire cultist, to Zegdar. Wyx convinced Zegdar to ally with the Eternal Flame. However, some adventurers infiltrated the Fane and confronted and defeated him.
