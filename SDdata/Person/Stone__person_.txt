Scout Stone was a member of the Neverwinter Guard who helped reclaim the city from violent forces during the late 15 century DR.
She did work for Hubert Windcastle, an aide to Lord Dagult Neverember.
Following the Siege of Neverwinter, in the , Stone was stationed in the Blacklake District of the city. While there she worked with some human adventurers (directed her way by Hubert) to clear away the barricades built by the violent political faction known as the Nashers to bar Neverwintan citizens from leaving their homes.
After helping the districts evacuation, Stone called for the removal of some Nasher spellcasters who were specifically starting fires within that area of Neverwinter. Finally, she made sure the adventurers recovered some deeds to property that the Nashers stole from Lord Neverember himself.
