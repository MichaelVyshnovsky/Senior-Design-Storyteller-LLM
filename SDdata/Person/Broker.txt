'''Broker''' was an officer in the [[Golden Legion]] and first captain of the Legion's cavalry. 

==History==
Broker first led the cavalry but in [[1361 DR]] he was badly injured in a skirmish against the pirate [[Akbet-Khrul]], forcing him to retire.<br />
