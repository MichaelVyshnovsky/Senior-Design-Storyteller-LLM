Aszerra Untlimmer was a priestess of Shaundakul, the Rider of the Winds, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
She was overweight and had a motherly air about her.
Aszerra lived in Ordulin in northern Sembia. She was one of only a handful of known priests of Shaundakul following the end of the Time of Troubles in the .
