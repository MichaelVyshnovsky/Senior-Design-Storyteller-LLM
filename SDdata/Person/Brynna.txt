Brynna was a young woman from the Western Heartlands who joined the Cult of the Absolute during the late 15 century DR.
Brynna and her companion Andrick were followers of the True Soul Edowin. They were wholly devoted to the man who they viewed as some form of chosen.
In the , Brynna, Andrick and Edowin were tasked with fighting a group of "fugitives" of the Absolute, individuals that were imprisoned aboard a mind flayer Nautiloid that crashed near the River Chionthar. Unbeknownst to Brynna and Andrick, their true goal was to retrieve a mysterious artifact for the Absolute.
Unfortunately for the trio, Edowin was mortally wounded by an owlbear and left to die in the forest.
