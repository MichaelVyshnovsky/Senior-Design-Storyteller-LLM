X. Scuttermoore was an inhabitant of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Scuttermoore owned a wrinkly pet pug dog.
During the Absolute crisis of , the Guild of Baldurs Gate targeted X. Scuttermoore, presumably as part of the Guilds protection racket or unpaid debts. When Scuttermoore began running low on coin, the Guild claimed their deposit box key from the Counting House however, that did not stop harassment and demands of payment by the agents of the Guild.
