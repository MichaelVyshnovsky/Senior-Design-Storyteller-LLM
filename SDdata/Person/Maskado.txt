Maskado was a mysterious individual who lived in Neverwinter in the 14 century DR.
Maskado was a blue-skinned and tall humanoid. Because of his features, it was speculated that he maybe was a member of the Arcane.
Maskado was the owner of Maskados Maps & Legends, a bookstore located in the City Core district. He usually dealt with adventurers and bought from them information and maps, usually paying much less than the price he would later sell such things.
