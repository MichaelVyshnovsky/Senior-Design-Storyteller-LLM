'''Deval''' was a guard in [[Freedale]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Possessions==
Deval was armored in [[chain mail]] and wielded a [[short sword]].

==Activities==
He guarded the entrance to the [[Tower of the High Wizard Netheril]].

==Relationships==
He worked for [[Netheril (person)|Netheril]], a local wizard, alongside his fellow [[Fortu]].

==History==
During the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], Deval guarded the tower at around the same time that [[Taran Goldenstar]] was kidnapped.
