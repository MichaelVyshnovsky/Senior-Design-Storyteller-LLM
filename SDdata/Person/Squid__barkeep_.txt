'''Squid''' was a [[half-elf|half-elven]] barkeep at the [[Stable Quiver]].

==Description==
Squid's namesake was a [[squid]]-shaped birthmark on his face.

==Relationships==
Squid was acquainted with [[Oppal DeScart]], a [[doppelganger]] agent of [[Acquisitions Incorporated]], and was aware of her disregard of the law. He did, however, recognize that she would never commit murder.
