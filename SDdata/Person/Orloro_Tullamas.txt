'''Orloro Tullamas''' was a plump merchant inhabiting [[Tulmon]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p165-166">

==Personality==
Orloro had a commanding voice and enjoyed chuckling.<ref name=EotSS-p165-166"/>

==Activities==
His store, [[Tullamas Sales]] on [[Shandlas Street]], focused on the sale of dry goods, carrying items from "gloves to grain and candles to carts."<ref name=EotSS-p165-166"/>

==Relationships==
Orloro was the nemesis of [[Horomel Ibrin]], believing the younger merchants of Tulmon should focus on their own profits and steer clear of larger politics.<ref name=EotSS-p165-166"/>
