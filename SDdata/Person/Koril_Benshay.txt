'''Koril Benshay''' was a member of the [[Freedom Riders]] of [[Daggerdale]] in [[1369 DR]].

He was a merchant and a good friend of fellow captain [[Ariton Delmis]] and when Koril was captured just before the decisive battle in [[Dagger Falls]], Ariton tasked adventurers he had recruited to free [[Randal Morn]] to also enter [[Zhent]] headquarters and liberate Koril.
