'''Damien''' was the son of [[Gaspar|Gaspar the Fishmonger]] in [[Easthaven]] within the [[Ten Towns]] of [[Icewind Dale]].

==Description==
The young boy dressed in brown clothing.

==History==
In [[Eleint]], [[1281 DR]], against his father's wishes, Damien decided to fish for [[knucklehead trout]] on the shores of [[Lac Dinneshere]], but was attacked by a group of [[goblin]]s.
