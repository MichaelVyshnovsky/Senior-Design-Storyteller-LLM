'''Boldor Steelshield''' was a member of the [[Council of Elders (Everlund)|Council of Elders]] of [[Everlund]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==Activities==
Boldor represented the merchants inside the Council. He was prone to hyperbole and accepted bribes.
