'''Cireka''' was a [[githyanki]] general who came at some point into the possession of the [[Silver Sword of Gith|silver sword]].

[[Ygorl]], a [[slaad]], wished to take this sword from her, leading to a war between the two. She was eventually slain by the [[red dragon]], [[Caminus]].<ref>
