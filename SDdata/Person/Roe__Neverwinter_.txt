Roe was a male human Watch lieutenant of the city of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North in the 1370s DR.
Roe was the lieutenant of the Watch.
Roe was Neverwinter Watchs lieutenant. He served the Watch with a great loyalty. If the Kalach-Cha sided with the watch, Roe would eventually die in a fire at the city guardhouse made by Moire.
