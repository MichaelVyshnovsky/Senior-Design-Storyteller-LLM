'''Merrywile''' was a [[halfling]] in [[Elturel]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relations==
Merrywile and her husband [[Pilster Pebblehuck]] had three children.

==History==
When the whole city of Elturel was transported to [[Avernus]], first layer of the [[Nine Hells]], in the {{YearlinkName|1494}}, Merrywile and her family hid in their bakery's basement. When their food started to run out, Pilster ventured into the streets to look for supplies.
