Merrywile was a halfling in Elturel in the late 15 century DR.
Merrywile and her husband Pilster Pebblehuck had three children.
When the whole city of Elturel was transported to Avernus, first layer of the Nine Hells, in the , Merrywile and her family hid in their bakerys basement. When their food started to run out, Pilster ventured into the streets to look for supplies.
