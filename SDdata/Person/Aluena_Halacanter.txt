Aluena Halacanter was a sorceress and a member of the Harpers who led the community of Serpents Cowl in the late mid–to–late 14 century DR.
She was known for both her beauty and the influence she wielded in her area of the Western Heartlands.
Aluena trained pegasi in her Heartwing Estate, which was located north of the crossing of Boareskyr Bridge. She employed staff members who to teach people how to mount the winged horses.
She aided the adventurers Barim Stagwinter and Theskul Mirroreye with overseeing the tented community sprang up around Boareskyr Bridge in the mid–1360s.
Expectantly, Aluena was acquainted with Elminster, and was at one point owed a favor by the wizened sage.
Some time in the mid–14 century DR, the adventuring company known as the Seekers found one of Aluenas errant pegasi and returned it to her estate. She accompanied the group to look for the steeds missing rider, and found the young mans body in the nest of some giant spiders.
After her adventurer allies left the settlement that former around Boareskyr Bridge, Aluena shifted her priorities. As of the , Aluena was doing all she could to protect her estate at Serpents Cowl from the growing threat of yuan-ti from the Forest of Wyrms.
