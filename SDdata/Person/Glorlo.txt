'''Glorlo''' was the cleric of the [[dwarven]] [[Orothiar Clan]] in the [[Barony of Bloodstone]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Instead of speaking, Glorlo chanted and for him events that happened only a week ago were like legends of ancient times.

==History==
In 1357 DR, Glorlo and his tribe met [[Gareth Dragonsbane]]'s party.
