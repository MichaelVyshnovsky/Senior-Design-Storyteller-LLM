Tulasker was an inhabitant in Scornubel during the mid-14 century DR, working at the Stormy Tankard.
He had shrewd eyes.
He greeted visitors who entered the Stormy Tankard from a side entrance.
He knew of Marlel.
In late , Marlel was very briefly detained by Tulasker at the entrance of the Stormy Tankard inn while bringing Shandril Shessair and Narm Tamaraith to Pharaulee.
