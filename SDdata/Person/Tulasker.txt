'''Tulasker''' was an inhabitant in [[Scornubel]] during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]], working at [[the Stormy Tankard]].

==Description==
He had shrewd eyes.

==Activities==
He greeted visitors who entered [[the Stormy Tankard]] from a side entrance.

==Relationships==
He knew of [[Marlel]].

==History==
In late {{yearlink|1357}}, Marlel was very briefly detained by Tulasker at the entrance of the Stormy Tankard inn while bringing [[Shandril Shessair]] and [[Narm Tamaraith]] to [[Pharaulee]].
