Watchful Skull Tynnos Argrim was the founder of the Flames of the Dark Sun and the head priest of the Cyricist Mountain of Skulls temple in Amn during the late 14 century DR.
He was a wholly vicious man that held little to no loyalty for others, even within the greater church of Cyric.
