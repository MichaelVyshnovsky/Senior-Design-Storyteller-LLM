'''Adhan el Ilmater''' was a [[human]] man and a priest of [[Ilmater]]. He was the leader of the [[House of the Broken God]] temple in [[Keltar]], [[Calimshan]], ''circa'' [[1370 DR]].

Adhan was a kind and wise man, who often worked directly with his patients. He was gray-haired and balding, but usually smiling.
