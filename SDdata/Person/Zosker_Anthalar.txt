'''Zosker Anthalar''' was a sage in [[Escalant]], [[Thay]]. 

==Personality==
Anthalar believed there was a great need for strong and brave companions to fight evil during the Year of the Shadows.

==History==
In [[1358 DR]], as part of a speech to the city's council, Zosker said:

{{Quote|''A hero? someone too foolish or stubborn to be a coward. Thank the gods we have 'em, though - these days, they're needed all too often.'' |[[Ed Greenwood]], ''[[The Code of the Harpers]]''}}
