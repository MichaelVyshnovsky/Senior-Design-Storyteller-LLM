'''Rathal Evanara''' was the high priest of the [[Temple of Corellon Larethian]] in [[Leuthilspar]] on the island of [[Evermeet]].

==History==
Around [[1369 DR]], Evanara was responsible for running the temple and maintaining the building and its grounds with the help of two acolytes.
