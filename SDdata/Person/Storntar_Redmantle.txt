'''Thaeram Duskwinter''' was a [[War Wizard]] of [[Cormyr]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In {{yearlink|1373}}, after becoming a spy for [[Caladnei]], the [[Royal Magician of Cormyr]], the [[Waterdhavian]] [[thief]] [[Narnra Shalace]]'s first assignment was to investigate a few [[War Wizards]] suspected of untoward activities, amongst which a traitor was suspected. Storntar Redmantle was one of these suspect wizards.
