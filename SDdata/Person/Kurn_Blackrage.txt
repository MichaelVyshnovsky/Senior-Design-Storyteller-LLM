'''Kurn Blackrage''' was a [[blackguard]] of [[Bane]] involved in the [[Zhent]] invasion of [[Shadowdale]] in the [[1375 DR|Year of Risen Elfkin, 1375 DR]].

==History==
Kurn was stationed as the supreme commander of the Banite troops in [[Castle Krag]].
