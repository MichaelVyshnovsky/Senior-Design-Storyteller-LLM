'''Grace Hardstone''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] resident of [[Phandalin]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/In Volo's Wake|15}}

==Relationships==
She was friends with fellow teenagers [[Alana Hallwinter]], [[Nedd Angelite]], and [[Sasha Flint]].{{Cite adventurers league/In Volo's Wake|16}}

==History==
When she was a teenager in the 1490s DR, she and two dwarven friends&mdash;Nedd and Sasha&mdash;became skeptical of some of the tall tales written by [[Volothamp Geddarm]] in the newly-published ''[[Volo's Guide to Monsters (in-universe)|Volo's Guide to Monsters]]''.{{Cite adventurers league/In Volo's Wake|17}} The three teens decided to travel to the [[Crooked Forest]] to investigate, where they captured by [[gnoll]]s. Soon, their friend Alana arrived in the woods to search for them, but was captured as well. The harrowing ordeal left Grace and her friends in shock and barely able to speak.
