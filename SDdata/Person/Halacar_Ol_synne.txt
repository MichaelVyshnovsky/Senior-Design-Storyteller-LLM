'''Halacar Olósynne''' was the fifth king of the [[Olósynne Dynasty]] who ruled the nation of [[Aglarond]].

==Activities==
Halacar inherited the throne when his mother, [[Ulae Olósynne]], died in the {{YearlinkName|1257}}. He only ruled for a few years until he was slain by agents of [[Thay]] in the {{YearlinkName|1260}}.

==Relationships==
Halacar's younger sister was [[Ilione Olósynne|Ilione]].
