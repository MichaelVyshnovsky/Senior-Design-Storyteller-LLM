'''Father Alby''' was a [[priest]] of [[Kelemvor]] who watched over the [[Temple of Kelemvor (Baldur's Gate)|temple]] in [[Baldur's Gate]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. He was assisted by a younger [[cleric]], [[Nerys Kathon]].{{Cite comic/Shadows of the Vampire 1|11}}

==Activities==
As a faithful of Kelemvor, Alby offered ''[[coin of Kelemvor|coins]]'' as a form of compensation to those who performed favors for the Baldurian [[church of Kelemvor|church]].

==Description==
Alby was a reasonable and determined man capable of sensible compromise. He did whatever he had to in order to maintain and protect his temple.

==History==
Some time in the 1480s DR, he hired the [[Heroes of Baldur's Gate]] to investigate some goings-on at his temple.{{Cite comic/Shadows of the Vampire 1|13}}

In [[1492 DR]], the priest joined the [[Cult of the Absolute]].{{Cite comic/Mindbreaker 1}}
