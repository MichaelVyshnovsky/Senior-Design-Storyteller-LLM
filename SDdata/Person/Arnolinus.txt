'''Arnolius''' was an armorsmith and merchant who lived in [[Athkatla]] during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{Quote|Gah! Must Arnolinus being disturbed at every opportunity?! What is it you being wanting?|Arnolius, to his patrons.}}

==Personality==
He spoke in broken [[Common]] and had the peculiar affectation of referring to himself in the third person. He was proud of the name "Arnolius" – a fine dwarven name.

==Activities==
Arnolius operated his smithy in the [[Waukeen's Promenade]] marketplace in the [[Athkatla/Center District|Center District]] of Athkatla, sharing the workshop with a renown fletcher named [[Perter]]. He offered a great variety of [[enchanted]] [[shield]]s painted in a variety of different colors.
