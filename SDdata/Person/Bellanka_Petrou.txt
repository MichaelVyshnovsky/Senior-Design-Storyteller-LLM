'''Bellanka Petrou''' was a short-lived member of a cell of the [[Cult of Reborn Flame]] that operated out of [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]], in [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|33}}

==Description==
Bellanka was a teenager [[human]] female with long hair that fell in ringlets.

==History==
In 1479 DR, Bellanka was recruited by [[Bazamarl]] to join his cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame. Because of her brief time in the cult, she was not a worshiper of the [[primordial]] [[Achazar]].

Two days later, a band of [[adventurer]]s that were sent by [[Halagothra|Halagothra the Healer]] to Sambral to destroy the cell of the cult operating there, killed both Bazalmarl and the [[Firebird (elemental)|Firebird]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|45}} Bellanka fled in panic, avoiding being killed by the adventurers.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|40}}
