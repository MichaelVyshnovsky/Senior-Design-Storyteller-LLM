'''Sharaera Starym''' was an inhabitant of [[Myth Drannor]] of the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''The Mythal must fall, and Elminster must be destroyed!''|Sharaera Starym}}


==Description==
Her age was only betrayed by the streaks of white and gray at her temples. During the moonrise after the laying of the ''[[Mythal]]'', in {{yearlink|261}}, she wore jeweled slippers, a low-cut bejeweled gown, and diamonds in the shape of twin falling dragons on her breast.

==History==
In {{yearlink|261}}, after contemplating the remains of [[Uldreiyn Starym]], she vowed vengeance upon [[Elminster Aumar]] and the destruction of the ''Mythal''.
