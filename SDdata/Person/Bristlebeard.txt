'''Bristlebeard''' was a [[dire boar]] inhabiting the [[Ashenwood]] in [[Rashemen]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Bristlebeard had patches of bright red hair.

==Activities==
The dire boar was ''[[Awaken|awakened]]'' by the [[telthor]]s of the forest and paid them heed. He aided any [[Rashemaar]] lost within the Ashenwood.
