Sethel was a scribe and librarian of Freedale in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He was friendly.
His local knowledge was solid, and he could find more information if needed, including the weaknesses of monsters, for instance.
Sethel was the proprietor of Freedales library.
He maintained the library and sometimes did research on commission.
He was friends with local adventurer Morganth the Mysterious.
Morganth and Sethel studied under the same master and Sethel became a historian afterwards. He later opened a library in Freedale.
