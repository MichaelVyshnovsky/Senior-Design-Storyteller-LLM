Slumgullion was a hin commoner, displaced by The Absolute, and as a refugee, sought shelter in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Slumgullion was a lightfoot halfling with a pale complexion, blonde hair, and light brown eyes.
In the , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldurs Gate, displacing countless refugees in the process along the way. Slumgullion fled from the Absolutists and found shelter within the city walls. Slumgullion left behind all of his money and had to beg for food scraps at the back of the Elfsong tavern in the Lower City, alongside other poor refugees.
