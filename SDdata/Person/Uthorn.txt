Uthorn was a doppelganger and a baron of the city of Calaunt in the Vast in 1358 DR.
Together with Wenszrin, a fellow baron and doppelganger, Uthorn commanded the citys standing army, The Teeth of Calaunt, with all warcaptains reporting to the two barons. The pair also commanded all mercenary forces the city hired to supplement its military.
Uthorn and Wenszrin also served as spies and scouts for the Merchant Dukes of Calaunt. However, they were under the magical control of Duke Iritar the Dark.
Uthorn was supplied with a variety of magical items for protection.
Uthorn normally adopted the appearance of a plain, veteran warrior.
