'''Uthorn''' was a [[doppelganger]] and a [[baron]] of the city of [[Calaunt]] in [[the Vast]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Activities==
Together with [[Wenszrin]], a fellow baron and doppelganger, Uthorn commanded the city's standing army, [[The Teeth of Calaunt]], with all warcaptains reporting to the two barons. The pair also commanded all mercenary forces the city hired to supplement its military.

Uthorn and Wenszrin also served as spies and scouts for the [[Merchant Duke]]s of Calaunt. However, they were under the magical control of Duke [[Iritar|Iritar the Dark]].

==Possessions==
Uthorn was supplied with a variety of [[magical item]]s for protection.

==Description==
Uthorn normally adopted the appearance of a plain, veteran warrior.
