'''Thibauneau''' was a skilled [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] who taught at the [[Alchemical Seminary]] in [[Eileanar]] until its destruction in {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}.

==Activities==
Thibauneau was an expert in the area of elemental [[air]] and the [[transmutation]] of similar substances, such as the conversion of normal air to [[poison]]ous gases. She taught the subject at the Seminary under [[Quintus]], from the most basic principles to the most advanced applications.
