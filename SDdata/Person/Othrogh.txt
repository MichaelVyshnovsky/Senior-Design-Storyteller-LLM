Othrogh was a Zhentilar warrior of the mid-14 century DR.
He was not great with metaphor and hyperbole.
He was highly confident in his ability to smell people approaching.
The rest of his squad liked to listen to his captain, Cleuvus, berate Othrogh.
Othrogh was part of a Zhentilar squad in late , which was sent to Hullack Forest to capture Shandril Shessair. While trying to be stealthy, they were ambushed and slain by Rathan Thentraver and Torm of the Knights of Myth Drannor.
