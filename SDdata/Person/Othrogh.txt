'''Othrogh''' was a [[Zhentilar]] warrior of the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was not great with metaphor and hyperbole.

==Abilities==
He was highly confident in his ability to smell people approaching.

==Relationships==
The rest of his squad liked to listen to his captain, [[Cleuvus]], berate Othrogh.

==History==
Othrogh was part of a [[Zhentilar]] squad in late {{yearlink|1357}}, which was sent to [[Hullack Forest]] to capture [[Shandril Shessair]]. While trying to be stealthy, they were ambushed and slain by [[Rathan Thentraver]] and [[Torm (adventurer)|Torm]] of the [[Knights of Myth Drannor]].
