Voruld was a member of the Purple Dragons in Castle Obarskyr during the late 15 century DR.
The man was head and shoulders taller than Mirt, just as wide, and all muscle.
He held the rank of telsword in the Purple Dragons.
Voruld came to the aid of Castle Obarskyrs senior hostler, Neld, after the Council of the Dragon in , when Mirt the Moneylender, one of the Lords of Waterdeep from the 14 century DR, suddenly appeared and demanded that Neld ready a closed coach for one of the gates. The hostler got agitated, and Mirt was resolute Voruld decided to fight the outlander. Mirt took him down almost immediately.
