'''Khamtar Raofal''' was a ship captain from [[Caer Callidyrr]] and the regional agent of the [[Moonstars]] for the [[Moonshae Isles]], [[Lantan]] and [[Ruathym]] in the [[1370 DR|Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR]].

==History==
Originally from Callidyrr, Khamtar was captain of the ''[[Raven's Eye (ship)|Raven's Eye]]'', a ship that sailed the [[Sword Coast]] from Lantan to [[Luskan]]. At one point he was recruited into the Moonstars.
