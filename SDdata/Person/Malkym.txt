Malkym was a member of the Bloodshields Band in the late 15 century DR.
He carried a weapon that required peace-bonding.
He was a member of the Bloodshields Band.
Other adventurers in the band were Flamdar, Rornagar Breakblade and Gaerond.
Malkym was a member of the Bloodshields Band when they headed for Shadowdale looking for the sage Elminster Aumar. Instead of Elminster, they came across doppelgangers, which pretending to be Elminster, lured him into an ambush and killed him.
