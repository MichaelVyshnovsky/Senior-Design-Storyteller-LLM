'''Malkym''' was a member of the [[Bloodshields Band]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Possessions==
He carried a weapon that required peace-bonding.

==Activities==
He was a member of the [[Bloodshields Band]].

==Relationships==
Other adventurers in the band were [[Flamdar]], [[Rornagar Breakblade]] and [[Gaerond]].

==History==
Malkym was a member of the Bloodshields Band when they headed for [[Shadowdale]] looking for the sage [[Elminster Aumar]]. Instead of Elminster, they came across [[doppelganger]]s, which pretending to be Elminster, lured him into an ambush and killed him.
