Maaril (, he was among the many powerful mages Khelben Arunsun brought together to cleanse part of the High Moor and restore Faertelmiir, a lost city of Miyeritar.
He lived in Waterdeep as an eccentric mage until he disappeared after the Spellplague of 1385 DR.
Just before disappearing, Maaril entrusted the custody of the Dragonstaff to the gold dragon Aurinax.
