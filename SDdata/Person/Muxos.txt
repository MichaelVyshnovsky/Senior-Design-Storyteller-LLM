'''Muxos''' was a [[transmuter]] and one of two lesser [[wizards]] stationed at the [[Hinnar's Red Wizard enclave|enclave]] run by the young [[Red Wizard]] [[Hinnar]].

==Description==
Muxos bore a magical tattoo on his body
==Abilities==
Muxos was tough, dexterous, and intelligent and was trained in [[alchemist|alchemy]] and spellcraft. He also knew how to brew [[potion]]s and to scribe scrolls.

==Possessions==
Muxos carried a [[dagger]] and a [[Light crossbow|small crossbow]], both of master craftsmanship, and he usually had a [[Cure light wounds|healing]] [[potion]] on his person.

His spellbook contained 31 different spells.

==Activities==
Muxos spent most of the day's hours in the compound's main barracks scribing spells with other wizards and brewing potions, including [[potion of love|''potions of love'']].
