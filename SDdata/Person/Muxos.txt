Muxos was a transmuter and one of two lesser wizards stationed at the enclave run by the young Red Wizard Hinnar.
Muxos bore a magical tattoo on his body
Muxos was tough, dexterous, and intelligent and was trained in alchemy and spellcraft. He also knew how to brew potions and to scribe scrolls.
Muxos carried a dagger and a small crossbow, both of master craftsmanship, and he usually had a healing potion on his person.
His spellbook contained 31 different spells.
Muxos spent most of the days hours in the compounds main barracks scribing spells with other wizards and brewing potions, including potions of love.
