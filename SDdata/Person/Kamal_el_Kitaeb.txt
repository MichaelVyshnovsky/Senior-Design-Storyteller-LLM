Kamal el Kitaeb was a druzir of Calimport, the owner of a library, and a secret affiliate of the Harpers.
Kamal el Kitaeb was a half-elf mage and sage.
Kamal owned a private lending library and consortium of sages called the Bards Wisdom in Yaraf Drudach of Quill Ward in Calimport.
As a druzir, Kamal would have been officially responsible for the management of his drudach.
While operating the business of the Bards Wisdom, Kamal also was secretly connected to the Harpers. He saw this as his actual calling the operation of his business was just a front.
Both Kamals father and his grandfather had also had connections with the Harpers, and Kamal was happy to follow in their footsteps.
