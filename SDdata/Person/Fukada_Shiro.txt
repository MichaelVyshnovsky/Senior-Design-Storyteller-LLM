'''Fukada Shiro''' was a [[samurai]] lord and ally of the [[Hiro clan]] in [[Kozakura]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Fukada owned the [[shoen]] surrounding the [[Gobo (village)|Gobo]] village. He directly controlled his land. He had at his disposal a company of thirty-six samurai and sixty [[bushi]]. Shiro was often called to Hiro castle and in those cases it was his son [[Fukada Takauji]] who managed the place.

==Relationship==
Shiro was the head of a very small samurai clan, the Fukada family, allied with the [[Hiro clan]].
