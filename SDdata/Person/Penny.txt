Penny was an apprentice alchemist who worked in the city of Neverwinter during the late 15 century DR.
Penny had a sister named Piniwin who was also learning the trade of an alchemist. They worked under a woman named Hazel, who focused her efforts on studying the Blacklake sludge that appeared in the Blacklake District of their home city.
Following the siege of Neverwinter in the , Penny went with Hazel and Penny to the Blacklake District to study the sludge. Later, Penny sought out adventurers to help Hazel fight off the sludge-covered zombies that were being summoned by Therzt, the Chosen of Ghaunadaur.
