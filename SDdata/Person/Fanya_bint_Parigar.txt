'''Fanya bint Parigar''' was in charge of the [[Grand University of Huzuz]] located in the [[Pilgrims' District]] of the City of Delights in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Gem of Zakhara|61}}

==Activities==
Fanya was a priest of [[Zann]]. She was a proficient teacher who knew several languages, including [[Shou|Shou Lin]] from the distant northeast.

She dreamed of the day that her school would be the grandest in all of Huzuz, though in reality she had a lot of work to do to accomplish such a feat.
