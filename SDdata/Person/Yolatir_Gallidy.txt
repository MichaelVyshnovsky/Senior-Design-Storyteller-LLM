Lady '''Yolatir Gallidy''' was the ruler of [[Two Stars]] as of {{yearlink|1372}}. She ruled with a light touch.

==Personality==
In her opinion, just about anything was okay so long as no one got hurt. She was stubbornly good-hearted,
==Activities==
In {{yearlink|1372}}, she was struggling with an incursion of the [[Shadowmasters]] of [[Telflamm]]. She taxed only wares entering the town. She continued to rule the town in {{yearlink|1373}}.
==Relationships==
Lady Yolatir Gallidy was the latest in a long line of Gallidys, which started with [[Prasso Gallidy]], the town's founder, legendary in 1372. Her cousin, [[Shanton Gallidy]], had also created the largest temple to [[Shaundakul]] that year.{{cite book/Lady of Poison|48|6}}
