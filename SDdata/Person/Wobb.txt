Wobb was a Bhaalist doppelganger member of the Murder Tribunal in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
His his dwrven guise, Wobb appeared to be an affluent, man with brown curly hair. When the disguise was dropped, Wobb looked like the rest of his pale kin.
In the , Wobb and several other doppelgangers accompanied a Bhaalist murderer Dolor to assassinate and mutilate the famed Figaro Pennygood, the proprietor of the Facemakers Boutique, targeted by the church. In case unwanted eyes visited the store, Wobb played the role of an impatient customer who was in line to see the fabulous Figaro. Wobb was presumed killed by a group of adventurers known as the Absolute Adversaries who were investigating the series of murderers perpetrated by the Murder Tribunal.
