Cyrilla, nicknamed "Loose Lips" was one of the employees of Lady Jannaths Estate in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Circa the , Chamberlain Mercer, the man in charge of Lady Firelia Jannaths staff, dismissed all servants from the mansion on the mistresss orders. Mercer placed Cyrilla, the linen washer, Furbleston, Bailey, Jooli, Phubbs, and Chef Marjoram on temporary leave with the pay of half-wages and allowance to seek temporary employment somewhere else. The chamberlain issued a statement that this dismissal was done at no imputation of malfeasance and would be ended upon notice.
She worked hard scrubbing, polishing, and cleaning for Lady Jannath, hoping to eventually make the mistresss private maid. However, all the hard work was unnoticed.
