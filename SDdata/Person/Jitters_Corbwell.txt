Jitters Corbwell was an addict from the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Jitters was a pale gnome with blond hair and blue eyes. She wore green frock finery atop a brown very and a ruffled tunic.
In the , Jitters Corbwell found herself addicted to an illicit substance found in Baldurs Gate called silkroot. She found a dealer in the Lower City, in an alley near the Basilisk Gate. The brightly acid-color-scaled dragonborn was a member of the infamous Guild, but instead of silkroot, he peddled aloe ointment, as simpletons could never know the difference between the two substances.
