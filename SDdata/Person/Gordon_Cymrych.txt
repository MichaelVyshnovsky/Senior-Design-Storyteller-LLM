Gordon Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid&ndash6 century DR.
He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the first son of his predecessor, High King Colin. His successor was his daughter, Miranda Cymrych.
Gordon assumed the throne of the High King in the , after slaying his own father, who had become infected with lycanthropy.
In the thirteenth year of his reign, Anhaern Rhyllgallohyr, Captain of the Sisters of Synnoria on Gwynneth, died and returned to life as the "Shee" (short for "Banshee Rider").
