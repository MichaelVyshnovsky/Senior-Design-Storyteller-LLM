Chynna Crytrapper was a priestess who represented the Church of Lliira in Crimmor during the mid-to-late 14 century DR
Chynna was a woman with white-blonde hair, known for her impish behavior. She tended to the faithful of Lliira in the Theater of Joy, that was dedicated to the deity from dawn at night.
Chynna belonged to one of the major noble families of Crimmor. As such, she had several influential relations

* She was the favorite cousin of Corlyn Braen, the city Mayor.
