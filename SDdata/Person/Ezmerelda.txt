Ezmerelda was the aunt of Rab Bitnez, the garlic-obsessed wizard from Liams Hold, who authored the 101 Garlic Recipes cookbook.
Ezmerelda always claimed that Rab Bitnezs mother laid down in a garlic patch to give birth to him. It was believed that this resulted in the gnomes life-long obsession with the pungent plant.
