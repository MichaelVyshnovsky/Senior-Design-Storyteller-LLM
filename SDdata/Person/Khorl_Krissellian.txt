'''Khorl Krissellian''' was a [[sun elf]] [[sorcerer]] and farseer, and a member of the [[College of Divination]] in the city of [[Sshamath]] in the year [[1377 DR]].

==History==
Khorl was four centuries old by the late 14th century [[DR]], and had lived for a long time in the [[Underdark]].

In [[1377 DR]], he was the senior member of the College of Divination sent to join the combined force of Sshamath wizards and priests of [[Eilistraee]] that planned to attack the main temple of [[Kiaransalee]].
