Bill was a dwarvish pirate from the isle of Brynnlaw in the Sea of Swords in the late 14 century DR.
Bill was a male dwarf with dark hair and a horned helmet. He wore armor and carried a large shield, armed with a simple blade.
In the , Bill sailed the Bleedin Mary and could be found drinking in the Vulgar Monkey dock-side tavern alongside the rest of the ships crew. He cheerily made terrible sea-related puns and made fun of landlubbers.
