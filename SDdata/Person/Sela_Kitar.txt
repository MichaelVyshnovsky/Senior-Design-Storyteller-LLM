'''Sela Kitar''' was an inhabitant of [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Sela had the immense responsibility of coordinating all of the activity within the warehouses of the [[House of Al-Danafi]]. She was a capable administrator who rose to the challenge.
