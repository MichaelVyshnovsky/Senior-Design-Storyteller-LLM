==Activities==
He was apprenticed to Manshoon and performed a number of important duties and tasks for him. Manshoon expected him to be up to date on his conversations with [[Sarhthor]], implying Beluard was in both their confidences.

==History==
Beluard accompanied Manshoon late in the {{yearlinkName|1357}}, to an encampment when the latter got ready to capture [[Shandril Shessair]]. Before Manshoon set off to fight her, he ordered his apprentice to arrange shortages of [[pork]] and [[sugar]] in [[Sembia]].
