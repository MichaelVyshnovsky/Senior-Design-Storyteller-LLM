Falar was a agent of the Knights of the Shield who operated throughout the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.
Falar was an underling of Kaddrus, a cambion who served lady Lutecia Hhune of Baldurs Gate.
In the , Falar and two other agents, Nulra Blacksaddle and Zaroud alRyshal, were tasked with aiding Kaddrus on a specific mission. While Kaddrus disguised himself as a human, the group were to seek out and bring back the missing Shield of the Hidden Lord.
