'''Falar''' was a agent of the [[Knights of the Shield]] who operated throughout the [[Sword Coast]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Falar was an underling of [[Kaddrus]], a [[cambion]] who served lady [[Lutecia Hhune]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].

==History==
In the {{yearlinkName|1492}},{{BG-DiA-time}} Falar and two other agents, [[Nulra Blacksaddle]] and [[Zaroud al'Ryshal]], were tasked with aiding Kaddrus on a specific mission. While Kaddrus disguised himself as a [[human]], the group were to seek out and bring back the missing ''[[Shield of the Hidden Lord]]''.
