'''Callabus''' was a [[goblin]] [[wizard]] who attacked the [[Ten Towns]] for the [[Legion of the Chimera]].

==Personality==
The goblin loved to weaken his enemies with powerful spells like ''[[fireball]]''.

==Possessions==
In battle, Callabus used his ''[[filthy goblin staff]]''.

==History==
In [[1312 DR]], Callabus lead an attack on the [[Icewind Dale]] community of [[Targos]]. During the battle in the [[Palisade (Targos)|palisade]], the goblin wizard was slain along with his companion [[Ghotrag]].
