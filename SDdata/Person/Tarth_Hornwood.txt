'''Tarth Hornwood''' was an adventuring [[wizard]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|2|Realmslore}}

==Relationships==
He was a student of [[Nerndel]]. Later, he was further tutored by [[Nimra Ninehands]].

==Possessions==
He acquired a magical staff from Nerndel, and looted the ''[[Ring of Murbrand]]'' from Myth Drannor,{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|227-228|15}}

==History==
Before {{yearlink|1360}}, Tarth Hornwood had been the chosen heir-in-art of Nerndel in [[Amphail]], who gave him [[Nimra Ninehands|his staff]]. With the staff, Tarth took to adventuring, exploring tombs in the [[Dragonreach]], and plundering other places too, including [[Myth Drannor]], where he acquired the ''ring of Murbrand''. At some point, he was tricked by Sarlin.

In late [[Mirtul]] of 1360 DR,{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|228-231|15}}

He soon after completed the rite with Elminster, who revealed afterwards that he knew he'd traded away the ring for a fake staff. Teleporting his staff to Tarth, Elminster left him, and Tarth resolved to complete the ritual for real. He fainted after the destruction of his staff,{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|232-234|15}}
