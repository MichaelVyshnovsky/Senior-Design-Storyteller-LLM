Falten was an employee of Baldurs Gates Elfsong Tavern in the late 15 century DR.
Falten was a human man with olive skin tone, brown eyes, and blond hair and beard. When at the inn, he wore a simple workman garb and an apron.
In the , Falten spent his days begrudgingly mopping floors, collecting dirty dishes, and serving the guests of the Elfsong Tavern while complaining that the job was beneath his education level. He was under the employ of Alan Alyth.
