'''Victor Tikhomirov''' was a [[Raumviran]] who lived in [[Almorel]] on the [[Lake of Mists]] in the [[Hordelands]] in the {{YearlinkName|1357}}.

==Activities==
Victor managed a small community of flour mills simply referred to as "the Mills." The Mills were located outside of Almorel's [[East Gate (Almorel)|East Gate]] along a small unnamed tributary of the Lake of Mists.
