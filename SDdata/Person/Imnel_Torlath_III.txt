King '''Imnel Torlath III''' "Longbeard" was an [[Amn]]ian monarch of the [[Torlath]] dynasty, known as the third Wizard-King of Amn.

==Relationships==
Imnel was the first son of of King [[Brinnaq Torlath II|Brinnaq II]], and his own second son was [[Jhanos Torlath II|Jhanos II]].

==History==
In [[760 DR]], following the destruction of the [[Ring of Eyes]] by [[Tashara]] of the Seven Skulls, Amn expanded into the former territory of [[Cortryn]] and achieved the eastern borders that would last for centuries to come.
