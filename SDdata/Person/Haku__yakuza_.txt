Haku was a yakuza assassin in the town of Aru in Wa.
In battle, Haku used a chain and knife.
In 1358 DR, Haku was ordered to kill Toshikazu Wada, the commander of the city Home Guard. Waiting for the right time to scratch Toshikazu Wada with a poisoned needle, Haku observed Wada during a meeting with an adventuring party hired by the daimyo Benju Matsutomo.
