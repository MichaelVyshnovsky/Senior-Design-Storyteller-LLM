Forold was a Purple Dragon Knight, part of Azoun IVs retinue in the mid-14 century DR.
Forolds voice was low.
He served in Azoun IVs personal retinue.
In Azouns personal retinue he served together with others including Daervin and Regrar.
Forold was part of Azouns retinue in late , during the kings visit to Tessaril Winter in Eveningstar after the departure of Shandril Shessair. While burying Daervin after the attack of a malaugrym, Forold related something that a contact of theirs, Delmar, had said about the events in Eveningstar prior to their arrival. He also had suspicions of his own regarding Tessaril.
