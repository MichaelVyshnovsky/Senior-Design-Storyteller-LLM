'''Sirhan min Hajama''' was the Imam of the [[Midnight Mosque]] in [[Liham]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Personality==
Sirhan was a cheerful individual.

==Activities==
He desperately wanted to retrieve a relic rumored to be the heart of the Lion of Suja. He believed that successfully retrieving the relic would bring him enough prosperity to fend off attacks from [[holy slayer]]s.
