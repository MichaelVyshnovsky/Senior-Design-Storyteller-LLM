'''Nimoar''', "the Reaver"

==Possessions==
Nimoar owned a heavy magic [[steel]] [[shield]] known as his [[Nimoar's blazing bulwark|''blazing bulwark'']]. This spiked ''[[flaming]]'' shield was carried into battle during the [[First Trollwar]] of the {{YearlinkName|932}}, where it served well against the [[Regeneration|regenerating]] [[Troll|giant]]s.{{Cite web/Mintiper's Chapbook/Part 10}}

==History==
Nimoar was involved in the [[Orcfastings War]], in which the [[orc]]s from the [[Sword Mountains]] attacked and besieged Nimoar's Hold. Nimoar survived the war, but died of old age in [[936 DR]].
