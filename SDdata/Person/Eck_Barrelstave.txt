'''Eck Barrelstave''' was a [[dwarven]] inhabitant of [[Almorel]] in the [[Hordelands]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Eck served as the guildmaster of the [[Moneylender's Guild]] of the city. He ran the guild from a fortified compound in the south central portion of the city.
