'''One-Eyed Jukk''' was a fence of stolen goods inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
One-Eyed Jukk worked out of the [[Bloody Fist]] [[tavern]] located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] where [[Presper Street]] and [[Shrimp Alley]] joined [[Snail Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5308a}} He dealt exclusively in stolen weapons, [[armor]], and fine metalwork.
