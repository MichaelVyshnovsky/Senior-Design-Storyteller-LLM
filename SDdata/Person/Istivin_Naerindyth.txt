'''Istivin Naerindyth''' was a [[Zhentarim skymage]] in [[1375 DR]].

==Activities==
Itsivin was a [[Zhent]] wizard who was part of the [[Zhentarim Occupation Army]] that controlled [[Shadowdale]] in 1375. His main duty was to patrol the entrance of the town of [[Shadowdale (town)|Shadowdale]].
