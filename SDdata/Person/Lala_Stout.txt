'''Lala Stout''' was a [[Lightfoot halfling|halfling]] burglar that lived in [[Baldur's Gate]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. She was a regular patron of the [[Elfsong Tavern]] located in the [[Eastway (Baldur's Gate)|Eastway]] district of the city.

==Description==
Lala had a particularly noticeable scar on her face.
