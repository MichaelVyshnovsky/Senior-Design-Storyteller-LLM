'''Luma Drapan''' was a [[human]] inhabitant of [[Eveningstar]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}
{{Quote
|My brother tried to fight the Drow armed with nothing but a [[pitchfork]] and they … and they …
|Cerith
}}
==Description==
She had light skin, long blonde hair, and green eyes. She wore a plain gray dress with black leggings.

==History==
{{Quote
|The villagers here fought bravely against the Drow — but they are not warriors …
|Purple Dragon
}}
Luma lost her brother during the first battle of the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar]]. She could be seen with a [[Purple Dragon]] grieving at his grave.
