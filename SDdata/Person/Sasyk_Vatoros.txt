Sasyk Vatoros was a yuan-ti pureblood wizard of House Eselemas.
Sasyk was secretly trading magical secrets to rivals of House SeSehen. He was forced to flee his home in Lushpool when he was discovered. Eselemaa assassins caught up to him in Hlondeth and slew him for his treachery.
Sasyks bodyguard was an ophidian barbarian named Vassk.
