'''Inrath Mirandor III''' was one of the first kings of [[Impiltur]], the 14{{th}} ruler of the kingdom's [[Mirandor Dynasty]]. He ruled for over thirty years in the early-to-mid–2{{nd}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Inrath was born the first son to [[Baranth I]]. He had two sons―[[Inrath Mirandor IV|Inrath IV]] and [[Pendarn Mirandor|Pendarn]]―with his first wife, who eventually succumbed to the plague. Inrath had one other son, [[Doraven Mirandor|Doraven]], born to another woman.

==History==
Inrath III ruled until the {{YearlinkName|157}}, when he was succeeded by his eldest son Inrath IV.
