Jalless was a deep gnome healer in Blingdenstone.
Jalless in 1479 DR was one of the healers and the most outspoken critic of Kargien Dissengulp.
Jalless disliked Kargien because she believed he wanted the crown despite not being of royal descent.
Jalless always worked with fellow healer Krell.
