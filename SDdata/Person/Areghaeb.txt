'''Areghaeb''' was the [[animal companion]] of the [[Tashlutan]] bounty hunter named [[Inshula sar Mashewe]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Areghaeb was a large pitch-black wild jungle [[cat]].

==History==
