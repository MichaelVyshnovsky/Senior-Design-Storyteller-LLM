'''Ephren''' was a soldier of the [[Flaming Fist]] that fought in the army of [[Baldur's Gate]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|He Of The Unsleeping Eyes. Grant me the might to carry this burden. Grant me the faith to face darkness without fear.|Ephren, praying over the body of a fallen soldier.}}

==Relationships==
She was particularly close with her fellow soldier [[Jacek]], whom she convinced to join the Flaming Fist.

==History==
Ephren was among the contingent of Flaming Fist soldiers present at [[Waukeen's Rest]] when the inn was raided by [[goblin]] and [[drow]] [[Cult of the Absolute|cultists]] of [[the Absolute]] in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}. Even though the inn was set aflame and Duke [[Ulder Ravengard|Ravengard]] was abducted, Ephren could not bring herself to take action, stricken by grief at the death of Jacek.
