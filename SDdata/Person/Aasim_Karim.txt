'''Aasim Karim''' was a [[Zakharan (ethnicity)|Zakharan]] [[desert rider]] and head of the [[House of Al-Karim]] living in the desert outside of [[Hiyal]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1993|84|[[Alia al-Karim]]}}

==Relationships==
Aasim had two children, his daughter [[Alia al-Karim|Alia]] and son [[Jamal al-Karim|Jamal]].

==Activites==
Aasim's daughter Alia brought news of an unprovoked attack against her brother that resulted in his death. The wealthy merchant who perpetrated the attack, a man from Hiyal named [[Farid]], incurred the wrath of the entire family as a result.
