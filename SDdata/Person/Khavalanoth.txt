Khavalanoth was a juvenile green dragon dwelling in Waterdeep circa 1372 DR.
He was the offspring of ChloracridaraCity of Splendors Waterdeep lists Khavalanoths parents as "Chloracidara of Mhiilamniir" which appears to be a misspelling of Chloracridara. and Grimnoshtadrano, the Riddling Dragon of the High Forest.
He served as the mount and guardian of Maaril, the Dragon Mage, as the price of a pact between him and Grimnoshtadrano.
