Sahlah was a holy slayer living in Hawa in 1367 DR.
Against her will, Sahlah served as an assassin of the Dark Wave for the Cult of Istishia. She was under the control of Faizahs Waters of devotion.
Sahlah was the sister of her fellow Dark Wave members Shadha and Setara bint Aasim.
