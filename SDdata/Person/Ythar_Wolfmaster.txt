'''Ythar Wolfmaster''' was a [[Rashemaar berserkers|Rashemaar berserker]] inhabiting [[Immilmar]] in [[Rashemen]] in the {{YearlinkName|1367}}.

==Activities==
Ythar was the leader of the [[Owlbear lodge]] based out of the [[Owlbear Berserker Lodge]] in Immilmar. Ythar and his followers enjoyed taunting strangers with the hopes of engaging them in fights, drinking contests, or displays of weapon prowess. Anyone skillful enough to impress him during one of these contest was invited to feast with Ythar.
