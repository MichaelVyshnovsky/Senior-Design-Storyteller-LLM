Alec II of the Cymrych Dynasty was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early-to-mid 8 century DR.
He was the second son of High King Ernest Cymrych and the younger brother of his predecessor, High King Carthus. His successor was his first son, Pendar Cymrych.
