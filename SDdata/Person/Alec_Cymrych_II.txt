'''Alec II''' of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the early-to-mid 8{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|33}}

==Relationships==
He was the second son of High King [[Ernest Cymrych]] and the younger brother of his predecessor, High King [[Carthus Cymrych|Carthus]]. His successor was his first son, [[Pendar Cymrych]].

==History==
Alec II assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|731}}, following the death of his father and the abdication of his brother.

He ruled for seven years before being dying of [[disease|plague]] in the {{YearlinkName|738}}.
