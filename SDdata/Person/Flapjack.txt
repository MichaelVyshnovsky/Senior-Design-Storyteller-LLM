Flapjack was a flumph sailor and adventurer who traveled Wildspace in the late 15 century DR.
Flapjack was an average flumph in appearance. It wore a fashionable seafarers hat backwards.
When Flapjack left Moondancer, it kept in touch with the crew and called for a ballista barrage in times of need.
Flapjack joined the crew of the spelljammer called Moondancer to sail the stars in the late 15 century DR. After some time, it developed an interest in sightseeing and began accompanying adventurers away from the ships safety.
