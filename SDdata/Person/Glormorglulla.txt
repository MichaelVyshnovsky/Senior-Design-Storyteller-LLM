Glormorglulla was a beholder of the 15 century DR.
Once, Glormorglulla was defeated and frozen by Elminster and Dove Falconhand.
In the , the Srinshee summoned it to Myth Drannor in order to create chaos among the Sembian mercenaries and bound Glormorglulla to that city. There, however, Glormorglulla met Dove again and tried to have its vengeance but Dove managed to destroy the beholders mind just before dying herself.
