==Activities==
In the late 1350s DR, she served as an acolyte at the [[House of Plenty (Maskyr's Eye)|House of Plenty]] temple under [[Lharathuel Hesmyr]]. She was available to provide healing to the farmers.

==Description==
She was young and relatively well-to-do.
