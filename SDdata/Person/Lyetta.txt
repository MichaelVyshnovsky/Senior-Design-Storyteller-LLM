Lyetta was a young slave in Mulmaster in 1373 DR.
Lyetta was bought as a slave by the Zhentarim, who placed upon her body a magic tattoo. However, she managed to escape into the citys sewers, despite not knowing the dangers of that place. Lyetta entered a room where a displacer beast menaced her.
