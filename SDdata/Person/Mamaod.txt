'''Mamaod''' was a [[djinni]] and inhabitant of the [[Palace of the Enlightened Throne]] in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Gem of Zakhara|86}}

==Activities==
Gifted to the Grand Vizier, [[Alyana al-Azzazi]], by [[Jiraad]], Mamaod guarded her within her personal quarters.

==Relationships==
Mamaod swore to serve Alyana for 100 years plus one day.
