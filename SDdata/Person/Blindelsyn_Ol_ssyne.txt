'''Blindelsyn Olósynne''' was the appointed leader of [[Relkath's Foot]] in [[Aglarond]] from at most {{yearlink|1372}}{{cite book/Unapproachable East|109-110}} to at least {{yearlink|1375}}.{{cite book/Stardeep|131}} She spent most of her time overseeing the functioning of the town from the [[Relkath's Foot#Royal Hall|Royal Hall]]. She was the sister of Prince [[Branelwyr]]; both of them were direct, yet distant descendants of [[Brindor Olóssyne]].
