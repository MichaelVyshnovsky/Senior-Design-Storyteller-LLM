'''Bannor Karralo''' was a barkeep inhabiting the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Bannor worked in a small tavern in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] called the [[Wizards' Rest]]. The tavern closed when his mother died. He lived in a small house on [[Coach Street]] in the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] near the [[Troll Wall]] that he attempted to repair for his future wife.
