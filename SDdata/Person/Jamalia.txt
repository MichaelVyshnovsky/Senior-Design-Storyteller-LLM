Jamalia was a gnoll official and Chief Scribe of the Regent of Hafayah within the Free Cities of Zakhara in .
Outwardly Jamalia appeared to be simple and honest. Yet she was quite cunning and able to skillfully manipulate groups against each other.
Jamalia was rare among the palace officials in that she was quite close with Regent Uqban and had retained her position for many years. Anyone seeking to curry favor with the Regent typically went through Jamalia for this very reason.
Jamalia was descended from a tribe of gnolls living in the desert.
