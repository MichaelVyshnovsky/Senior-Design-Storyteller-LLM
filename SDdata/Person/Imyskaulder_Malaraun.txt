'''Imyskaulder Malaraun''' was a skilled [[wizard]] inhabiting [[Yeshpek]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p169">

==Description==
His impressive beard was only surpassed by his extravagant taste in lavish robes.<ref name=EotSS-p169"/>

==Personality==
Imyskaulder was a kind old man who often gave gifts to children and strangers, even when no one was looking.<ref name=EotSS-p169"/>

==Activities==
Imyskaulder served as a close advisor to the [[khaldomaun]] [[Ilbaerth Malklyn]].<ref name=EotSS-p169"/>
