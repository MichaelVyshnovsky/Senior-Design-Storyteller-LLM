'''Halamar''' was a [[mage]] specializing in [[fire magic]] and a member of the [[Blue Wyverns]] [[adventuring company]] of [[Ravens Bluff]] in the [[Vast]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite digital book/Prince of Ravens/Kindle|2498|8}}

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Halamar and the Blue Wyverns helped [[Jack Ravenwild]] recover the [[Sarkonagael]] tome from the ancient [[dwarven]] city of [[Sarbreen]] beneath [[Ravens Bluff]].

==Description==
Halamar had red hair and a "W"-shaped goatee.{{Cite digital book/Prince of Ravens/Kindle|2494|8}}
