'''Zobeida bint Maneira''' was a [[Sorcerer (kit)|sorcerer]] within the [[Palace of the Enlightened Throne]] in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Gem of Zakhara|84}}

==Activities==
Zobeida served as an assistant of the Grand Vizier, [[Alyana al-Azzazi]]. She was particular skilled with warding magic.
