Nagamat was a dragon general of Tiamat during the Time of Dragons.
In , Nagamat was in charge of the siege of the aearee kingdom of Shara, destroying its hatcheries and claiming the realm as his own.
He was soon drawn into religious conflicts between the factions of the draconic gods, and in , he was killed at the hands of followers of Xymor. His death sparked the Dragonfall War, a conflict between the followers of Bahamut and Tiamat, which persisted for thousands of years.
