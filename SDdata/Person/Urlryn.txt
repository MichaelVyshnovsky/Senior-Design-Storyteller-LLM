Urlryn was a drow of Menzoberranzan in the late 14 century DR and one of Houndaer TuinTarl and Syrzans rebel group.
Urlryn once trained at Melee-Magthere under Ryld Argith and was proficient enough that Ryld remembered his name many years later.
During the Silence of Lolth in the , Urlryn joined Houndaers group desiring to start a slave revolt. The plot was discovered by Ryld Argith and Pharaun Mizzrym. When they escaped from the conspirators building, Urlryn and his fellows hunted them, but Urlryn was killed by his former master.
