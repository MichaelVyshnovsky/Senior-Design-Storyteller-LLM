Derf was a businessman and tattoo artist inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He owned a shop called Derfs Skin Art specializing in body art such as tattoos and piercings.
Derfs apprentices were Sniden Elkslodge and Zarebor.
