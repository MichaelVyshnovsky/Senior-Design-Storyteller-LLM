Gelfuril was a shopkeeper inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Gelfuril owned a shop named after himself simply called Gelfuril the Trader located at the intersection of Sambril Lane and Smugglers Run in Waterdeeps Dock Ward. He operated his shop similar to a pawn shop and even allowed bartering.
He was a priest of Tymora before he retired to open his shop.
