'''Gelfuril''' was a shopkeeper inhabiting the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Gelfuril owned a shop named after himself simply called [[Gelfuril the Trader]] located at the intersection of [[Sambril Lane]] and [[Smuggler's Run]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. He operated his shop similar to a pawn shop and even allowed bartering.<ref>

==History==
He was a [[priest]] of [[Tymora]] before he retired to open his shop.
