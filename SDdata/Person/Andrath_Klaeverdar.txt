'''Andrath Klaeverdar''' was a mage of the [[Klaeverdar]] clan in the late mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
By {{yearlink|1370}}, he had a number of spells of his own devising and enjoyed spellcasting for itself. He was a likely candidate for leadership of the clan.
