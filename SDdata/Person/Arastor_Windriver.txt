Arastor Windriver was the middle son of [[Sabrast Windriver]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Per his father, [[Sabrast Windriver]], Arastor was turning out to be the most important man in stonemasonry within the city of [[Westgate]] as of {{yearlink|1356}}.

In a revel on {{yearlink|1356}},{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|2|Realmslore}} Sabrast Windriver explained he wasn't sending his middle son any money.
