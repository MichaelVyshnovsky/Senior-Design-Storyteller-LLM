Hyrriet was a Fist of Bane who served the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 century DR.
Volgu was a human woman with a light skin tone and hark hair. She wore a chainmail adorned with Banes holy symbols. He was armed with a large wooden shield and a mace.
In the , the Cult of Bane joined forces with the Cult of the Absolute, led by the Chosen of Bane, Lord Enver Gortash. Fist of Bane Hyrriet was a part of the Cults operations that targeted the countless refugees that flooded Baldurs Gate. Her branch of the Cult assembled donations and toys rigged with blackpowder explosives in the Felogyrs Fireworks shops third floor. Presumably, Hyrriet perished during or shortly before the Cult attacked Baldurs Gate.
