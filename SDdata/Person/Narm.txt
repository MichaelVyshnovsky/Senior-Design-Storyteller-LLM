'''Narm''' was a large [[half-orc]] warrior and the leader of the [[Blue Wyverns]] [[adventuring company]] of [[Ravens Bluff]] in the [[Vast]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite digital book/Prince of Ravens/Kindle|2517|8}}

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Narm agreed that the Blue Wyverns would help [[Jack Ravenwild]] recover a tome of [[shadow magic]], the [[Sarkonagael]], from [[Sarbreen]] in exchange for 500 [[currency|gold pieces]] per member of the company.
