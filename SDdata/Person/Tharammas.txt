'''Tharammas''' was a red wizard, infamous for the strength of his ''[[nightmare (spell)|nightmare]]'' spell.

==Abilities==
Tharammas was known by [[the Simbul]] to have perfected a stronger version of the ''[[nightmare (spell)|nightmare]]'' spell.

==History==
In {{yearlink|1372}}, the Simbul sought him out to kill him.

==Rumors & Legends==
In {{yearlink|1372}}, The Simbul woke up from a nightmare and found herself wounded. She assumed it was a spell of nightmares from [[Tharammas]].
