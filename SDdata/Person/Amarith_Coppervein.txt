Amarith Coppervein was a dwarf in the Underdark in the late 15 century DR.
Amarith was a member of the Emerald Enclave and one of the students of Morista Malkin in Gauntlgrym. Circa 1486 DR, she went alone into Underdark, where she created her own personal zoo.
Amarith was on good terms with Sladis Vadir, a fellow student of Morista living in Mantol-Derith.
