Haransau Olimer was a trader in blandreths of the mid-14 century DR.
He was fat, with facial pimples and baggy eyes.
At the time of his death, he was carrying a number of blandreths in a wagon, labeled "Haransau Olimers Best Blandreths".
He traded in blandreths.
He had an assistant.
Haransau Olimer had joined the caravan led by Orthil Voldovan from Scornubel to Waterdeep, the same one where Shandril Shessair had joined in the caravan, he was assassinated and impersonated by Marlel.
