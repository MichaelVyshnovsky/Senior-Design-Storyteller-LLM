Maerilee Goodfellow was a maid for Mirt in the mid-14 century DR.
Maerilees back was criscrossed with white and purple scars.
Maerilee became a maid for Mirt in Waterdeep.
At some point prior to , Maerilee Goodfellow was a pirates slave in the Nelanther isles. After catching Mirts eye, he paid for her release, whereupon she became his maid. On 1357, she briefly showed her back scars to Paraster Montheir on Aspers command.
Asper convinced Paraster Montheir that Maerilees scars were Mirts doing, and that hed lashed his maid over a debt.
