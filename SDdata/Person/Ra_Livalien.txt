'''Ra Livalien''' was an [[elf|elven]] solicitor that lived in [[Silverymoon]] in the late mid-to-late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. 

==History==
In [[1379 DR]],{{Cite dungeon/206/Eyes on the Ball|1}} while visiting friends in [[Waterdeep]] kidnapped and [[slavery|enslaved]] by [[Lanador]]. During his disappearance a 500 [[gold piece]] reward was put out for his safe return.
