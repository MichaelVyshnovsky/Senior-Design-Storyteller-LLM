'''Rilyanna Saerfynn''' was a young noblewoman of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Rilyanna's parents died when she was young and she lived at the family manor with her brother. The dark [[Angel (astral)|angel]] [[Sathariel]] visited her when she was a child and she agreed to serve [[Asmodeus]].

In the {{yearlinkName|1480}}, Rilyanna attempted to finish the ritual of the [[First Flensing]] to bring Asmodeus to [[Waterdeep]] but was killed by [[Quessahn Uthraebor]].

==Relationships==
Together with her brother [[Callak Saerfynn|Callak]], she was the last of her family.

Rilyanna was rumored to have had incestuous relations with her brother.
