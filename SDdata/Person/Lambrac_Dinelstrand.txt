'''Lambrac Dinelstrand''' was a [[Lantanna]] master smith.

After being exiled from [[Lantan]], Lambrac came to lead the town of [[Lhazantal]] in [[Lapaliiya]] by around [[1373 DR]]. This town was holy to [[Gond]] and known for its iron mines and metalworking.
