Cromwell was one of the best blacksmiths of Athkatla in Amn. His forge was located to the south-east of the Docks District.
Cromwell was a master blacksmith, proficient in crafting many types of magical armor, as well as a multitude of weapons. Among . He did not have an apprentice to help him at his forge, so would often ask the customer themselves to provide assistance. If the customer wished to have a unique magical piece of equipment made from parts, they would often have to stay a few days, manning Cromwells forge.
He could make plate mail out of animal carapaces, such as ankheg plate mail.
Among many of his incredible crafts, he was known for having assembled the following artifacts
* The long sword, The Equalizer
* A holy githyanki silver sword
* The warhammer Crom Faeyr
* The shortbow of Gesen
* The halberd, Wave
* The mace of disruption
* Red dragon scale
* Shadow dragon scale
As well as his forge and dwelling, Cromwell owned, and donned, an enchanted +2 warhammer and +3 chain mail. He also wore an open-faced helmet for protection.
