'''Smolyn''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and prolific [[spell]] inventor who developed spells that affected one's vision.

==History==
Smolyn invented eight spells during their life. The spells they developed were ''[[blindness]]'' and ''[[foresight]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1895|NY}}, ''[[true seeing]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1901|NY}}, ''[[blur]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1917|NY}}, ''[[veil]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1925|NY}}, ''[[eyebite]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1935|NY}}, ''[[false vision]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1940|NY}}, and finally, ''[[mirror image]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1943|NY}}.
