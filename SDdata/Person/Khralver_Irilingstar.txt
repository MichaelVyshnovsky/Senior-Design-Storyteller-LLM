Khralver Irilingstar was an official from Luskan, active circa 1374 DR.
Khralver was a human man with pale skin and long brown hair.
In the , the city of Luskan attempted to take over the town of Port Llast, enrolling the aid of the Prioress, a powerful cleric of Umberlee, the Bitch Queen. After she completed her part of the deal, the Luskanites backed out of the deal, causing the Prioress wrath, trapping Luskans ships in Port Llast. Khralver Irilingstar was sent to negotiate with the scorned woman, but he met his doom in the Drowning chamber of the Priory of the Depths, where the priestess performed her Umberlant rituals.
