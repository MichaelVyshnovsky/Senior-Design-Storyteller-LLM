Hsi Yog was a Tuigan shugenja and member of the Grand Army of the Tuigan during the mid 14 century DR.
Hsi used his clerical powers to aid the soldiers of the army under Prince Hubadai Khahans command.
The following spells were available to Hsi deflection, cure light wounds, snake charm, hold person, levitate, snake barrier, detect lie, and possess.
Hsi owned a pair of bracers of defense and a magical tetsubo.
