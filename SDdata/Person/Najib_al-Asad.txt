'''Najib bin Kamal al-Asad''' was Sheikh of the [[House of Asad]] tribe in the [[High Desert]] of [[west Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}} He was considered by some to be the most powerful leader of all the desert tribes.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p36-37">

==Description==
Najib was a lean and hardened [[desert rider]].<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p36-37"/>

==Activities==
Nabil travelled to [[Tajar]] once a year for trade.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p36-37"/>

==Relationships==
Najib's wife, [[Alia al-Jamal umm Nabil]], also served as his chief vizier. Najib cared so much for Alia that he willingly chose not to take other wives, though it was well within his right to do so. Najib's eldest son was [[Nabil bin Najib]].<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p36-37"/>
