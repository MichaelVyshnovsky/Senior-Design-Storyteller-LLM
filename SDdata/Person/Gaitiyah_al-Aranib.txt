'''Gaitiyah al-Aranib''' was an employee of the [[Merchant's Home]] in [[Huzuz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
[[Tun Hayal]], the owner of the Merchant's Home, trusted Gaitiyah with managing the affairs of the lavish [[wikipedia:en:caravanserai|carvanserai]].

She was able to house several caravans under the same roof without them bickering, which was no small feat considering the boisterous nature of most caravan-masters.
