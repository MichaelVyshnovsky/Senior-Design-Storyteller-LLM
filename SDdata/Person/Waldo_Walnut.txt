Waldo Walnut was a patron of the Elfsong Tavern from the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Waldo was a well-dressed male rock gnome with a light skin tone, dark brown eyes, and short curly brown hair.
In the , Waldo Walnut was a frequent customer of the Elfsong Tavern in the Eastway neighborhood of Baldurs Gate. He attended the "Laff Riot" show at the Elfong, a stand-up comedy performance hosted by Harvard Willoughby in Elfsongs side room with a stage. Waldo hoped the show would entertain him, as he heard that Harvard was a peculiar man and had a "smashing monocle."
