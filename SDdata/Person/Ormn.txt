Ormn was a bear that dwelled at the Emerald Grove along with his master, Archdruid Halsin.
He had the bear form of low self-esteem and did not view himself as particularly strong.
In the , Halsin departed the Emerald Grove for the nearby Shattered Sanctum to investigate a strange happenings at Moonrise Towers that saw the implanting of mind flayer tadpoles into the eyes of humanoid creatures and their conversion to the mysterious Cult of the Absolute. 
Ormn was despondent over his masters departure. He spent all his time catching fish at a small stretch of beach near the grove, unable to pick up Halsins scent despite the return of those belonging to his traveling companions.
