Roge Applewink was a large halfling farmer in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.
Roge regularly sold his products to Reyill Werned.
His partnership with Reyill had been very good for Roges businesses over the years.
Circa 1486 DR, Roge was drinking with his halfling friends and Reyill at the Plodding Plow, in the village of the Stop.
Roge was friends with Narder Ulthub and Guylian Stundter. He had good dealings with Reyill Werned.
