'''Elmarr Cardellith''' was a merchant lord in [[Sembia]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was quite ruthless and cruel. According to his wife, he was somewhat sexist, not considering her fit for conversing in many topics.

==Possessions==
He was quite rich.

==History==
Elmarr Cardellith was married to Noumea for some time between {{yearlink|1364}} and {{yearlink|1373}}. During the span of time in which they were married, she bore him four children. Later, he attempted to have her killed; failing that, he paid her to stay away and changed churches in the meantime. He got their marriage annuled at his new church, and then remarried.
