'''Sevenhaunt''' was the [[Rengarth]] father of [[Sunbright Steelshanks]] ''circa'' {{Yearlink|-700}}.

==Personality==
The [[shaman]] was a powerful leader of the [[Raven Tribe]].

==Possessions==
Sevenhaunt carried the [[greatsword]] [[Harvester of Blood]] into battle.

==Relations==
Sevenhaunt and [[Monkberry]] sired the [[fighter]] [[Sunbright Steelshanks]].

==History==
Later in the Shadowed Age, Sevenhaunt became ill with a wasting disease and died.
