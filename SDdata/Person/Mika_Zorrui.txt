Mika Zorrui was a famous kabuki playwright from Semmishi in Wa.
Zorrui was known for his emotional tales of courage on the battlefield and honor in the home. His plays tended to be enjoyed by citizens from all social classes.
* The Wise Carp, a tale of a talking fish who brings peace to two brothers in conflict.
* The Vulgar Maiden, a story of a scheming geishas downfall.
