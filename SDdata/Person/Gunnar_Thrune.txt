'''Gunnar Thrune''' was a [[Tymora]]n patriarch of the [[Lady's Hall]] in [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Gunnar was exceptionally boisterous, hearty, happy, and loud.

==Abilities==
Thrune was most often found motivating gamblers and bettors either verbally or via a slap on the black. During crises, he was known to encourage bets on such events.

==History==
Thrune was originally from the remote island known as [[Gundarlun]]. In [[1482 DR]], Gunnar led the other priests of the Tymoran temple known as the Lady's Hall.
