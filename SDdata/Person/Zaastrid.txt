'''Zaastrid''' was a [[fire giant]]ess that lived during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. She was noteworthy for keeping a [[troll]] as her slave.

Like many of her kind, Zaastrid sought fragments of the ''[[vonindod]]'' in the years after the [[War of the Silver Marches]] of [[1485 DR]].
