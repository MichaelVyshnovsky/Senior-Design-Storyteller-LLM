Teldin Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr in the mid-to-late 13 century DR. Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.
He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty and was the son of his predecessor, King Vance. His eventual successor was his second son, Rieger.
