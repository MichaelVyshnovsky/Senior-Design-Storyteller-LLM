'''Teldin Carrathal''' was High King of [[Callidyrr]] in the mid-to-late 13{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|33}} Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]], he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the [[Ffolk]] outside of Callidyrr.

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Carrathal Dynasty]] and was the son of his predecessor, [[Vance Carrathal|King Vance]]. His eventual successor was his second son, [[Rieger Carrathal|Rieger]].

==History==
Teldin assumed the throne in the {{YearlinkName|1266}}, after the death of his father. He ruled for only five years before he died of [[heartstop]] in the {{YearlinkName|1271}}.
