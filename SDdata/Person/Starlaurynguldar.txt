'''Starlaurynguldar''' was a [[dragon aging|wyrm]] [[black dragon]]. He slew the [[Magister]] [[Ohland Grethgar]] in {{yearlink|1282}}, but died of his wounds in the same day.

==Possessions==
The dragon possessed an antimagic amulet as of his final fight.

==History==
Starlaurynguldar was known to have slain [[Ohland Grethgar]], the [[Magister]], in {{yearlink|1282}}; his victory was due to an antimagic amulet of sorts. However, Ohland's power was enough to destroy the amulet, and the dragon perished of his wounds not long after the fight.
