'''Devryn Stoutt''' was a follower of [[Selûne]] who lived and died in the Realms, presumably in the [[Sword Coast]] region of the [[Western Heartlands]].{{Speculation}} The records of Devrynn's death was included among other obituaries in the chapel records found within an [[Temple of Jergal (Sword Coast)|ancient crypt]] of [[Jergal]] located near the [[River Chionthar]].

While the exact circumstances of Devryn's death were unknown, Devryn was known to have succumbed to the effects of fever.
