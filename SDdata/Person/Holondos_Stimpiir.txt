'''Holondos Stimpiir''' was the high priest of [[Tyr]] for [[the Waiting]] in [[Phlan]] around [[1367 DR]].

==Activities==
Holondos oversaw six other priests within the temple during a time when the worship of Tyr in Phlan was on the rise.
