Sheikh '''Ibrin''' ruled the [[House of Hotek]] tribe in the [[Haunted Lands]] of [[interior Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p44">

==Personality==
Ibrin was a natural leader whose charisma attracted many followers to his cause.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p44"/>

==Activities==
Ibrin was obsessed with destroying the city of [[Hiyal]] and the corruption and wickedness that he believed it stood for. The source of this obsession was his [[kahin]] [[Amene]].<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p44"/>

==Relationships==
Ibrin's wife died shortly after Amene came into his life. His son [[Jisaron]] gave Amene a wide berth.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p44"/>
