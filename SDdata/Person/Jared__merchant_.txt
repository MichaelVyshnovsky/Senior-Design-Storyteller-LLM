Jared was a merchant from the Sword Coast, active circa 1368 DR.
Jared had an uppity and abrasive personality.
The merchant owned a pair of boots of the north.
In the , Jared somehow ended up on a river crossing the west from the town of Nashkel, pursued by a hungry polar bear. The merchant was desperate to find aid against the beast and offered gold and his pair of boots of the north as the reward.
