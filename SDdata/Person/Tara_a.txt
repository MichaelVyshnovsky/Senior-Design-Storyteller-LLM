'''Tara'a''' was a [[tasked genie|tasked]] [[artist genie]] working within the [[Palace of the Enlightened Throne]] in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Gem of Zakhara|90}}

==Activities==
Tara's artistic specialization revolved around cooking. She prepared many fine meals for the residents of the [[Royal Harim of Huzuz|Royal Harim]], including the [[Grand Caliph]].
