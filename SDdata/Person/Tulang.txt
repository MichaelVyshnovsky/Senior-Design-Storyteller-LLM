'''Tulang''' was a [[monk]] of the Ku Nien school of the [[Path of Enlightenment]] in [[Laothan]], [[Malatra]]

==History==
According to legend, Tulang shifted a hill and made a spring at [[Thiam]], making it a holy site and among the oldest known to the [[Seng]]. By [[1357 DR]], a Ku Nien monastery stood there.
