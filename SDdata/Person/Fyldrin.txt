'''Fyldrin''', who was also called '''Fyldrin of the Eleven Chairs''', was a [[Rashemaar berserkers|Rashemaar berserker]] inhabiting the town of [[Mulsantir]] in [[Rashemen]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He led a small brotherhood of buccaneers who executed attacks on the coast of [[Thay]].

==Relationships==
Fyldrin was the son of one of Rashemen's previous [[Iron Lord]]s, [[Thydrim Yvarrg]].
