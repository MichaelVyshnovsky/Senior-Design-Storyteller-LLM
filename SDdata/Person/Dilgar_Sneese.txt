Dilgar Sneese was a worshiper of Umberlee who prayed at the Water Queens House in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Dilgar was a light-skinned rock gnome woman with light brown hair and dark brown eyes. She wore a simple cloth jerkin and a blue tunic.
Circa the , during the Absolute crisis, Dilgar Sneese frequented the Water Queens House, uttering prayers to her goddess. She was fearful of messing up the prayers and sought to come in for a single devotion and promptly leave.
