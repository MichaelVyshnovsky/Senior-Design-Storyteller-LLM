Sul was a warrior and cultist of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.
Sul had yellow-orange skin decorated with blue warpaint. He wore a suit of crudely-made plate armor that featured vicious spiked pauldrons and bracers decorated with bones.
His preferred weapon was a two-handed battleaxe.
In the , the cult of the Absolute launched a terrible raid on the Waukeens Rest inn. It was unknown if Sul joined in on the assault, but he was most definitely present at the great feast held afterward at the Shattered Sanctum.
