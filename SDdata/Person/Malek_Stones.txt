Malek Stones was a reporter employed by Baldurs Mouth in the late 15 century DR.
Malek was a beardless male shield dwarf with pudgy features, brown eyes, and short curly brown hair.
In the , Malek Stones worked on investigative scoop articles for the Baldurs Mouth Gazette. During the Absolute crisis, the irate dwarf could be found working on his pieces within Baldurs Mouths offices in Heapside of the Lower City.
