'''Scavenge''' was a hungry [[otyugh]] that foraged in the the ruins of [[Myth Drannor]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==History==
The fellow lived in the remains of the [[Dlabraddath]] neighborhood, looking for things to eat while the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] worked to reclaim the city.
