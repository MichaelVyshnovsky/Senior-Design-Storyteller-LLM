'''Elzarr of the Crimson Eye''' was the [[zulkir]] of [[divination]] during the early 12{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/277/Bazaar of the Bizarre: Soargar's Legacy}}

==History==
In the year [[1187 DR]], Elzarr was presented with the [[Item enchantment|magical]] [[scimitar]] ''[[Ashram]]'' by [[Sorros]]. The long-lost blade of [[Mulhorand]] had been lost in the [[Giantspire Mountains]] by [[Imbrar I|King Imbrar I]] some sixty years prior.
