Noora bint Amon was a beloved Zakharan hakima inhabiting a small village in Zakhara around 1367 DR.
Noora was a beautiful woman but was equally famous for her wisdom.
Noora would help good-natured travelers or adventurers if their cause was just.
Noora wielded a jambiya and owned a ring of djinni summoning, a ring of sustenance, dust of appearance, a bag of bones, a candle of propitiousness, and an amulet of perpetual youth.
