'''Quen Montgar''' was a [[horse]] trader and owner of [[Montgar's Horses]] in [[Procampur]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite document/Procampur City Expansion Packet|3}}

Quen's eldest son was [[Arim Montgar]], who ran [[Montgar's Horse Outlet]] in the [[Port District (Procampur)|Port District]].
