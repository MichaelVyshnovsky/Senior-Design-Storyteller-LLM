'''Tula Woodsbridge''' was one of the four staff of [[The Rapids]] inn in [[Teshwave]] in [[1367 DR]], working as a barmaid.

She secretly was also a [[Harper]] and was always in search of trusted adventurers to deliver her messages to the other Harpers.
