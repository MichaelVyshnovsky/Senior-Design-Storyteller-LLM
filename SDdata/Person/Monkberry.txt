'''Monkberry''' was the [[Rengarth]] mother of [[Sunbright Steelshanks]] ''circa'' {{Yearlink|-700}}.

==Description==
The Rengarth woman used her hair to make [[bowstring]]s.

==Personality==
The strong-willed [[human]] female was an outcast in the [[Raven Tribe]] because she only bore one child.

==History==
Monkberry raised Sunbright after the death of her husband, [[Sevenhaunt]], and granted him his father's [[greatsword]], [[Harvester of Blood]].
