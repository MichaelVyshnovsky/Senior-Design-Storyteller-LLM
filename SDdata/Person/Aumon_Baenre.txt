'''Aumon Baenre''' was the elder boy of [[matron mother]] [[Quenthel Baenre]] circa [[1484 DR]]. He was in his first year at [[Tier Breche]]. He was secretly approached by the [[Jaezred Chaulssin]], who coerced him into eventually revolting against his mother.
