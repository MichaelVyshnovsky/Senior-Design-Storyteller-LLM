Calkontor was a mage in Zhentil Keep. He became envious of the appointment of Harlshoon as first Lord of Zhentil Keep, and died in an attempt to poison him.
Calkontors son was Chess.
In 1258 DR, Calkontor, who was envious of Harlshoons appointment as a Lord, poisoned him, and then taunted him. Harlshoon was able to throw a chair at Calkontor and throw him out of a window to his death, although Harlshoon succumbed to the poison thereafter.
