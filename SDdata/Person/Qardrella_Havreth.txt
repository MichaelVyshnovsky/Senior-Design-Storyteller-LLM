'''Qardrella Havreth''' was a minstrel of some renown, active in the early 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Qardrella was known for a ballad she published in the {{YearlinkName|1400}}. The song ''[[Down Another Tankard]]'' was about joys of clouding one's head with alcohol.
