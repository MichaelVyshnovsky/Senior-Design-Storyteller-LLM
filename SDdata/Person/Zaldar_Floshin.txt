==Personality==
Zaldar was said to be a rather brooding individual.

==Activities==
Zaldar was the captain of the ''[[Kelpie's Kiss]]''. He had a [[sprite]] as first mate and a [[Harper]] agent as his ship's bosun.

==History==


''Circa'' [[1485 DR]], Zaldar was hired by the Harpers to travel in search of the ship [[morkoth (ship)|''Morkoth'']] and its hostage King [[Hekaton]].
