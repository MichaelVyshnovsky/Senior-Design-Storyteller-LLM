Vuzlyn Freth was a drow in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 century DR and a member of House Freth. He was one of Houndaer TuinTarl and Syrzans rebel group.
During the Silence of Lolth in the , Vuzlyn joined Houndaers group desiring to start a slave revolt. Vuzlyn and Alton Vandree were, among others, summoned to Pharaun Mizzrym and Ryld Argith, as might be some of the males they needed to find.
