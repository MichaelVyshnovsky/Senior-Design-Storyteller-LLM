'''Jhorrgel''', who was nicknamed '''the Black''', was the master of the [[Old Lodge]] in [[Thasunta]] in [[Rashemen]] in the {{YearlinkName|1373}}.

==Activities==
Jhorrgel was a renowned [[Rashemaar berserkers|Rashemaar berserker]] who had mastered the fighting styles of three other berserker lodges before joining the Old Lodge. Given his fierce reputation, Jhorrgel was surprisingly perceptive and patient.
