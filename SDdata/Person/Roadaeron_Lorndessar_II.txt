'''Roadaeron Lorndessar II''' was a king of [[Westgate]] in the late 12{{th}} century [[DR]], reigning over [[1191 DR]] and [[1192 DR]].{{Cite web enhancement/Westgate Dynasties}}

A member of the [[Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty]], he was born in the {{YearlinkName|1154}} and was the first child of King [[Blaervaer Lorndessar|Blaervaer the Reluctant]].
