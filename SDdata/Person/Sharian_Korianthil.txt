Sharian Korianthil was a windrider of House Korianthil in Evermeet in 1367 DR.
Sharian was the head of the isles eagle-riders. He was known for his near-suicidal tactics, acrobatics maneuvers, and use of exotic weapons. Sharian was also known to indulge in sylvan celebrations, drinking contests, passionate affairs, and numerous brawls, especially against sun elves.
Despite his reputation, Sharian was a powerful warrior loyal to Queen Amlaruil Moonflower.
