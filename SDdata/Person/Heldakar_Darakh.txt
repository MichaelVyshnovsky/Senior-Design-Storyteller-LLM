Heldakar Darakh was the second Nentyarch of the Darakh Dynasty to rule Narfell.
Heldakar assumed the title of Nentyarch upon the death of the previous ruler, Jesthren Darakh, in . He ruled until .
Heldakar was a half-fiend son of a human woman of the Darakh line and the demon lord Orcus. His mother was sacrificed to Orcus immediately after his birth, beginning a tradition of matriarchal sacrifice that would continue for the rest of the rulers throughout the Darakh lineage.
