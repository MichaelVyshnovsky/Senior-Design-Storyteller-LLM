'''Rathene Fel''' was a bandit in [[Phlan]] in [[1489 DR]].

==Personality==
Rathene believed that her comrades had abandoned her and for the right price she would betray them. For this, she was bitter and lashed out at others.

==History==
Rathene was a [[half-orc]] mercenary who joined the [[Gray Patriots]] bandit group after they freed her from a labor camp near Phlan. However, she was captured by [[Knights of the Black Fist]] and sentenced to [[Grimshackle Jail]]. Rathene believed her comrades had abandoned her, but on the contrary a jailer was bribed to free her.{{Cite adventurers league/Outlaws of the Iron Route|9-10}} However, an adventuring party there to interrogate her recaptured her.{{Cite adventurers league/Outlaws of the Iron Route|12}}

Around [[1492 DR]] Rathene was a bandit leader that help in the reclamation of Phlan.{{Cite adventurers league/Reclamation of Phlan|68}}
