'''Hassana Alim al-Gaib''' was the Emira of [[Dihliz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Ruined Kingdoms/Campaign Guide|16}}

==Activities==
Hassana was granted the position of Emira based upon the recommendation of the padishah of [[Afyal]]. She was appointed to her position by the [[Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir|Grand Caliph]] himself for a provisional period of five years.

Always planning for retirement, Hassana spent much of her time hoarding her riches. 

==History==
Before becoming the ruler of Dihliz, Hassana was a simple merchant from Afyal.

==Relationships==
Hassana had four husbands.{{cite book/Ruined Kingdoms/Campaign Guide|17}}
