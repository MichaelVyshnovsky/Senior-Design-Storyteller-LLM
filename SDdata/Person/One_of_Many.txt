One of Many was a creature of pure distilled malice, once imprisoned within the Death Gods Vault in shadow Mulsantir until the late 14 century DR.
One of Many appeared as a floating horned wraith-like mass of swirling shadows with massive humanoid hands. Its conscience consisted of a multitude of evil individuals, murderers, killers, and the criminally insane. These evil souls were trapped inside the Death Gods Vault crematoriums incinerator in which their lives ended. The spirits were gradually driven mad over several decades by their imprisonment and their unending malice and hatred for the world.
One of Many radiated a palpable aura of power and malice.
By some unknown means, One of Many came into possession of the First Imaskarcana, which it carried with it into the jungles of Samarach.

Driven by a hunger to consume sentient beings or to sow death and violence upon them, eventually, One of Many manifested in the jungles of the Samarlogh in the hidden kingdom of Samarach on the Chultan Peninsula.
