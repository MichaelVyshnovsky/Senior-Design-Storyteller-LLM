'''Phoenix Anvil''' was a member of [[Acquisitions Incorporated]]'s [["B" Team]] franchise.

==Personality==
Phoenix was said to be a soft-spoken individual, to the point that he was rarely known to speak. His primary motivation as part of the "B" team was earning coin, having spent so much of his life without any, and had an obsessive dedication to their assets and accounts. However, he was fiercely loyal to the team, due in part to considering his teammates as assets.

==Abilities==
Being a devout follower of the goddess [[Waukeen]], Phoenix was capable of casting the [[cleric]] [[spell]]s ''[[bane (spell)|bane]]'', ''[[cure wounds]]'', ''[[guidance]]'', ''[[guiding bolt]]'', ''[[hold person]]'', ''[[light]]'', ''[[mending]]'', ''[[sacred flame]]'', and ''[[spiritual weapon]]''.

Besides spellcasting, he was capable of causing his shield to flare with divine light once per day, blinding creatures within {{SI|30|ft|long=yes}} of him for a whole minute.

==Possessions==
Phoenix was known to possess a set of [[chainmail]] armor, some form of [[shield]], and a [[warhammer]].

==Activities==
Being a member of the "B" Team, Phoenix was known travel around under the guise of a musician. Having the role of hoardperson within the team, he spent much of his time storing the loot from their adventures and managing their resources.

==Relationships==
Phoenix was not known to have warmed up to any member of the "B" Team except for [[Oak Truestrike]].

==History==
Phoenix grew up on the streets as an urchin [[fighter]]. His path in life eventually turned around when he one day acquired a job sweeping the steps of a temple of Waukeen.
