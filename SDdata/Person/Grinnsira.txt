'''Grinnsira''' was a [[red dragon]] of the [[Stonelands]] in [[Cormyr]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Abilities==
Grinnsira was of particular magical skill, outclassing some of the greatest dragons.

==Activities==
The dragon was secretly the mastermind behind a number of border-raiding parties in the Stonelands.

==Relationships==
She had made pacts with two [[chimera]]e, promising them food and treasure in return for service.

==History==
Grinnsira was one of the red dragons in the [[Goblin Marches]]. As of {{yearlink|1367}}, she was working on organizing the local [[kobold]]s into an army, being opposed in her schemes by the [[silver dragon]] [[Fi Lendicol]].

==Rumors & Legends==
Grinnsira claimed to have visited a site holy to dragons somewhere in [[Anauroch]].
