'''Torogar Steelfist''' was a [[minotaur]] who served as bodyguard to [[Arkhan|Arkhan the Cruel]].

==Personality==
Torogar was known to be savage. He followed Arkhan to all of his missions.

==Abilities==
A fierce and tireless warrior, Torogar was skilled with the use of double [[scimitar]]s, which he could wield in a mindless rage. He also dealt devastating attacks with his horns.

==Possessions==
In addition to his ''[[belt of fire giant strength]]'' and his ''[[gauntlets of flaming fury]]'', Torogar also rode a [[Devil's Ride]].

==Activities==
Torogar's duties as bodyguard led him to follow Arkhan to his periodic pilgrimages to a monument dedicated to [[Tiamat]] near [[Arkhan's Tower]] in [[Avernus]]. He also followed Arkhan to battle whenever necessary.
