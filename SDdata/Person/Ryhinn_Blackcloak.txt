Ryhinn Blackcloak, Hurler of Thunderbolts was a mage from the Dragon Reach region during the mid-14 century DR.
In the end months of the year 1357 DR, Ryhinn and Narve Dwarfkin assembled a band of 20 mercenaries and assembled in the Sembian city of Selgaunt. They ventured out to investigate the lost elven ruins of Myth Drannor.
