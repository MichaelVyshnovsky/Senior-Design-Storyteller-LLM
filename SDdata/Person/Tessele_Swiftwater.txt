'''Tessele Swiftwater''' was the owner of the [[Dagger's Rest]] Inn during the mid-to-late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Heirloom|6-8}}

==Description==
Tessele was a tall woman with long brown hair, green eyes, and a warm and friendly smile. She was considered middle-aged ''circa'' [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Woolmen's Restless Tomb|4}}

==Personality==
Tessele was a retired [[rogue]], who made a point of helping younger [[adventurer]]s who frequented her establishment.
