'''Ulraen Caulbor''' was a launderer inhabiting [[Waterdeep]], City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Ulraen was main point of contact for the [[Launderers' Guild]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He worked within the guild's headquarters at the [[House of Cleanliness]] on [[Slipstone Street]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] where he held the title of "Soap Master."
