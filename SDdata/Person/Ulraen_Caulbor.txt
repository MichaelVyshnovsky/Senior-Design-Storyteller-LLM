Ulraen Caulbor was a launderer inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Ulraen was main point of contact for the Launderers Guild during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. He worked within the guilds headquarters at the House of Cleanliness on Slipstone Street in Waterdeeps Trades Ward where he held the title of "Soap Master."
