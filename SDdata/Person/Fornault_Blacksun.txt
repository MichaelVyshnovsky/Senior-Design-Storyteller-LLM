'''Fornault Blacksun''' was a [[cleric]] of [[Cyric]] in [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Fornault had a pointed chin and beady eyes. He was always smirking.

==Activities==
Fornault was the High Priest within [[Twilight Hall (Zhentil Keep)|Twilight Hall]], a temple dedicated to Cyric that was consturcted within the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] following the city's destruction in the {{YearlinkName|1368}}. He enjoyed the challenge of rebuilding Cyric's faith in Zhentil Keep. Fornault was served by sixteen underpriests and lay brethren.
