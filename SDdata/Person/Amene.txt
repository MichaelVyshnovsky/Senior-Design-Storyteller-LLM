'''Amene''' was a [[kahin]] and member of the [[House of Hotek]] tribe in the [[Haunted Lands]] of [[interior Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p44">

==Activities==
Amene served as Sheikh [[Ibrin]]'s vizier. She convinced him of the erroneous ways of his past when he traded with [[Hiyal]], convincing him of the need to destroy the city. She was always at the sheik's side offering advice and comfort.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p44"/>

==Relationships==
Many whispered that Amene would one day become Ibrin's wife. Ibrin's first wife died shortly after Amene became part of the tribe.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p44"/>
