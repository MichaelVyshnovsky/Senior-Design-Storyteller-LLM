'''Kamo Ti''' was a shopkeeper in [[Shou Lung]].

==Activities==
Kamo Ti lived in the city of [[Pasar]], the largest city in the [[Shao Mountains]]. He sold fish, often spoiled. However as brother-in- law of the city's magistrate the militia never acted against him.
