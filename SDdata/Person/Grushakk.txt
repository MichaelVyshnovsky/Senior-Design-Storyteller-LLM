Grushakk was a powerful glabrezu under the command of Sarya Dlardrageth in 1374 DR. 
During the Battle of the Cwm, Grushakk was the leader of the demons allied with the feyri. Sarya ordered to him to kill the enemy commanders. Grushakk engaged with Araevin Teshurr, Ilsevele Miritar, Grayth Holmfast, and Maresa Rost, but was quickly brought down and defeated.
