'''Oryk Kalain''' was a [[Zhentarim|Zhentish]] merchant and agent in [[Thentia]] in [[1367 DR]].

==Activities==
Oryk operated a business as a perfume and spice merchant, and lived at the [[Zhentish Merchant Quarters]] with fellow trader and spy [[Abrix Myll]]. Together, they were charged with overseeing the hoard of 16,000 [[gp]] that the Zhentarim heisted from the local temple of [[Waukeen]] in [[1345 DR]].
