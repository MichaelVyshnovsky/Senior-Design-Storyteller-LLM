'''Nusrah bin Kerim''' was a the leader of the [[Winged Bulls]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|21}}

==Decription==
Nusrah's skin was dark brown. He had curly gray hair that was thinning. All of his teeth were decayed. Nusrah wore a tiny beard that all of the male followers within the Winged Bulls imitated to make their allegiance clear.{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Cardsheets}}

==Activities==
Nusrah was merely the puppet of the true leader of the Winged Bulls, [[Aida al-Irrahman]].

==Relationships==
Nusrah was the grand nephew of the [[Marwan al-Jabal al-Hiyali|Shadow Caliph]].
