Nusrah bin Kerim was a the leader of the Winged Bulls in 1367 DR.
Nusrahs skin was dark brown. He had curly gray hair that was thinning. All of his teeth were decayed. Nusrah wore a tiny beard that all of the male followers within the Winged Bulls imitated to make their allegiance clear.
Nusrah was merely the puppet of the true leader of the Winged Bulls, Aida al-Irrahman.
Nusrah was the grand nephew of the Shadow Caliph.
