'''Krenmore Toth''' was a [[gnome]] [[artificer]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==Description==
Krenmore had pale pinkish skin and bright red hair, short on top and a beard with a chin curtain and forked braids. He wore a long [[robe]] and chipped [[breastplate]] for protection.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}} during the [[Elven Crusade]], Krenmore joined the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|fortified camp]] on the outskirts of [[Myth Drannor]]. He served as a trainer for other artificiers who passed by.
