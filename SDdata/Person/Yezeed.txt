Qadi Yezeed was a dwarf priest living in Sams Bandar on Jazirat al-Qraidis in the Crowded Sea in 1367 DR.
Yezeed spoke so slowly that it often infuriated others.
Yezeed was aware of every single bloodline and feud that went on in the village of Sams Bandar. He attended court meetings to share in the latest gossip.
