High Revelmistress Chlanna Asjros was the high priestess of the Palace of Holy Festivals in Selgaunt in 1358 DR.
Chlanna was the leader of Lliiras faithful in Selgaunt. Her congregation within the temple consisted of 39 other priests and 87 followers.
During the Time of Troubles, Chlanna was taken as a lover by Lliira while the goddess was in mortal form.
Chlanna was murdered by followers of Loviatar, causing the order of the Scarlet Mummers to hunt agents of the Maiden of Pain for vengeance.
