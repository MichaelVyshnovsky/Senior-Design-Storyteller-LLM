Erna was a guardswoman who worked for the Zhentarim during the late 15 century DR.
Erna wore a suit of leather armor and a helmet that masked her visage. She had black tattoos that covered portions of her face, around her eyes and chin.
Her preferred weapon was a simple longbow.
In the , Erna and her fellow guard Yerle accompanied Roah Moonglow during her dealings with the cult of the Absolute at the Shattered Sanctum. The Zhentarim traded smokepowder for slaves that were taken captive by cult raiders. The duo watched over a chamber in the sanctum that housed over a dozen barrels of the highly-combustible smokepowder.
