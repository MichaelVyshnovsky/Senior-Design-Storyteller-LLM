'''Chaspra''' was a skilled [[enchanter]] who lived in [[Selgaunt]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Description==
Chaspra's outward beauty cloaked her manipulative nature.

==Activities==
Her favorite pastime was using her considerable powers to make men fall in love with her.
