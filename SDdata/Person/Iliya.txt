'''Iliya''' was a beautiful woman known to inhabit the oases of the deserts of the interior of [[Interior Zakhara|Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Iliya's beauty was shocking to anyone who spied her swimming in an oasis. When not swimming naked, she dressed in a robe made from golden feathers.

==Activities==
Iliya was in fact a [[polymorph]]ed [[simurgh]] who wished to be married as soon as possible.
