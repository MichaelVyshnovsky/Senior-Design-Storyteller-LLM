'''Irunteld''' was a [[gelugon]] living in [[Mulmaster]] in [[1373 DR]].

==Activities==
Irunteld laired in Mulmaster under direct orders from its master. It commanded a [[greater basilisk]], some [[barbazu]], and some [[osyluth]]s. Irunteld also had an agreement with the [[beholder]] [[Nelac]] and two [[oni mage]]s.
