'''Purnia''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]]<ref group="note" name="note1"/> remembered for the creation of one spell.

==History==
Purnia lived during [[Netheril]]'s [[Golden Age of Netheril|Golden Age]]. She or he invented the spell ''[[fool's gold]]'', then consistently called ''Purnia's fools' gold'', in [[-1694 DR|2165 NY (−1694 DR)]].<ref>

All other facts about Purnia's life (including her or his gender) were lost to history.<ref group="note" name="note2">''[[Arcane Age]]'' sources contain only information about Purnia related to the ''fools' gold'' spell.
