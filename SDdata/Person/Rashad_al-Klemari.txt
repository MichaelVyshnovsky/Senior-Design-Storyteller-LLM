Rashad al-Klemari was an elven merchant who lived in Huzuz in 1367 DR.
He was not a member of the House of Al-Klemari merchant family.
Rashad owned a small shop that faced the plaza housing the mosques of all the great gods of Zakhara. He sold religious items to the many pilgrims who visited the area.
Rashad was a religious man who worshiped all of the Zakharan gods. He attended services at the Golden Mosque twice a day.
