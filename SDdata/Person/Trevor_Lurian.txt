Procampur, the Vast
Lord Trevor Lurian was a boy noble of House Lurian in the city of Procampur in the Vast in the late 14 century DR.
He was a relative of Lord Consul Povalo Lurian, the head of the family.While several members of House Lurian are known, most of their relationships are unknown Trevor is most likely intended to be a son of Povalo and Jasmine Lurian, or of Stautin Lurian.
He was aged 11 in 1370 DR.
