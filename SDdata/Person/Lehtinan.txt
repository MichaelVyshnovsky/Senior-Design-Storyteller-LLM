Lehtinan was the owner of the Copper Coronet tavern in the city of Athkatla as of the .
He was a thoroughly despicable and extremely prideful individual.
Lehtinan was involved in many criminal dealings, primarily prostitution, slavery, and gambling on slave pit-fights.
Among his belongings, Lehtinan was in the possession of the enchanted bastard sword Kondar.
While Lehtinan retained ownership of the Copper Coronet, he left the day–to–to operations to the barkeep Bernard, who was in fact a semi-retired Harper sympathizer.
