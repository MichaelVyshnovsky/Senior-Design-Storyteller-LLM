Gaerd was a magic instructor in the mid-late 8 century DR. He was Alustriels second teacher.
He had some manner of crystal ball.
He became Alustriels instructor by . She believed him to be a Harper. Her uncle Thamator believed him to be a master of magic.
In Uktar 12 of , Gaerd was scrying on Alustriel while she fought off Irlar, finally killing him she went to him afterwards, to ask him how to join the harpers. A tenday before, he had taught her a spell akin to shocking grasp and recommended she keep her knowledge of it secret.
