'''Horlo''' was a caravan guard in the [[Sword Coast]] during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Horlo was one of [[Orthil Voldovan]]'s guards during the voyage from [[Scornubel]] to [[Waterdeep]] in late {{yearlink|1357}}. He was slain during the first ambush that struck the caravan after leaving Scornubel; Orthil found out about his death while dictating a task for him.
