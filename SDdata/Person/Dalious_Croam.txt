King '''Dalious Croam''', who was also called the '''Errant King''', was the third and final King of the [[Croam Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Dalious ruled from the {{YearlinkName|135}}, to the {{YearlinkName|137}}, when he abdicated the throne in favor of the first of many [[Croamarkh]]s who would rule Westgate for over a century. 

==Relationships==
Dalious was the third son of King [[Sorentah Croam]].
