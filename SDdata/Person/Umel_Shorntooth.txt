'''Umel Shorntooth''' was a retired adventurer who lived in the "Royal Keep" of [[Mistshaws]] in [[Eskorn]], [[Laerakond]].

==Activities==
In [[1479 DR]], Umel was searching for adventurers to accompany him, despite his advanced age, in an expedition to the ruins of [[Savanfast]], which he had visited when he was young.
