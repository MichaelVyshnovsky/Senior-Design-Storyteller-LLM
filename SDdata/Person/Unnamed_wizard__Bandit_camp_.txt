These '''unnamed wizards''' were [[human]] spellcasters in the [[Bandit camp (Mere of Dead Men)|bandit camp]] near the [[Mere of Dead Men]] on the [[Sword Coast North]].

==History==
These wizards were employed by the [[Unnamed bandit leader|bandit leader]]. The female wizard would attack on sight, while the male was waiting for the leader's orders.
