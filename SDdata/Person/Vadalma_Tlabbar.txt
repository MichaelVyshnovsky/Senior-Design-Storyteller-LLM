Vadalma Tlabbar was the drow matron mother of House Faen Tlabbar, the fourth house of Menzoberranzan, in the 14 and 15 centuries DR.
She was fanatically devoted to Lolth.
Vadalma ascended to the position of matron mother in the . In that year, Nimor Imphraezl of the Jaezred Chaulssin killed both matron mother Ghenni Tlabbar and her favorite daughter, Silzet Tlabbar. Vadalma was the fifth daughter of Ghennitiroth Tlabbar and thus ascended to matron mother position. She was still in charge as of 1484 DR.
