'''Baram''' was one of the five [[High Captains (Luskan)|High Captains]] of the city of [[Luskan]]. Along with the others he was an experienced adventurer and a former pirate.<ref name="VoloN-p111" >

==Description==
Baram was oldest of the High Captains in [[1361 DR]] and considered the most conciliatory.
