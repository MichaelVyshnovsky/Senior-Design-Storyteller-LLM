'''Bann Alagondar''' was a member of the [[Alagondar]] royal family and the king of Neverwinter in the late years of the 14th century DR.

==Relationships==
Bann was the son of [[Nasher Alagondar]]. He had a younger half-brother, [[Vers Never]].

==History==
After the death of Nasher Alagondar, Bann succeeded his father as the second king of Neverwinter. He founded the Alagondar royal family.
