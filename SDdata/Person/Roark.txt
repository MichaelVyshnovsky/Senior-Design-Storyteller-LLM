Roark was a tavernkeeper and the owner-proprietor of the Uphill Climb in Caer-Dineval during the late 15 century DR.
He was a middle-aged man as of the late 1480s DR, with a weathered and rough sort of handsomeness.
Roark was known for buying only the finest libations for his tavern, favoring the good food of Bryn Shander and the best honey wine made in Good Mead.
When the Everlasting Rime spread across Icewind Dale, around the , Roarks tavern out of both beer and liquor and its business suffered.
When the Knights of the Black Sword took over Caer-Dinevals keep, Roark nudged adventurers in their direction in the hopes they would uncover its mysterious goings-on.
