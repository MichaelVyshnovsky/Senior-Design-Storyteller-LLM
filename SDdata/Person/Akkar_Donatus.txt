Father Akkar Donatus was a priest of Ilmater residing in the city of Zazesspur in 1370 DR.In chaper 9 of the novel, War in Tethyr, Zaranda Star visits the chapter house located at the House of Tears and speaks to the archpriest and chief cleric there, who is unnamed. If the two characters are the same, then the book would describe Akkar as tall and gaunt, with sad, gray eyes, and fringes of gray hair around a doorknob-shaped head.
Daily, Father Akkar led the roughly 60 clerics and 200 laypersons in sacred rituals at the House of Tears, a temple to Ilmater and local chapter house for the Order of Ilmaters Brethren.
