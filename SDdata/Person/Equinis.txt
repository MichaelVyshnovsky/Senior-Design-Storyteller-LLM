Equinis was an eladrin ritualist in Waterdeep in 1479 DR.
In 1479 DR, Equinis went into Undermountain in search of magical knowledge. However, he was captured by lizardfolk followers of Sessinnek who wanted to use him as a sacrifice to summon an avatar of their god.
Equinis had a great knowledge of rituals and arcane lore.
