'''Equinis''' was an [[eladrin]] ritualist in [[Waterdeep]] in [[1479 DR]].

==History==
In 1479 DR, Equinis went into [[Undermountain]] in search of magical knowledge. However, he was captured by [[lizardfolk]] followers of [[Sess'innek]] who wanted to use him as a sacrifice to summon an avatar of their god.

==Skills==
Equinis had a great knowledge of rituals and arcane lore.
