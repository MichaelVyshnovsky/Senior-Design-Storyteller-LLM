'''Hiwa Itzusune''' was the admiral of [[Kozakura]]'s Imperial Navy in [[1357 DR]].

==History==
Itzusune was a member of the [[Niwa clan]] of [[Miyama Province]]. He married into the [[Takegai]] court nobility and was appointed as an admiral for his political connections. Despite this, Itzusune had some skills in seafaring and fleet tactics.
