Sirvinhandra was a green dragon (great wyrm) who, on 22 Mirtul 1373 DR, emerged from a lair in the Dun Hills to attack the Abbey of the Just Hammer, causing devastation, before being killed by Lord High Justiciar High Avenger Deren Eriach.
