'''Darndreth Goldsword''' was a nobleman of [[Cormyr]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
He was the youngest [[Goldsword]].

==History==
Darndreth Goldsword was a participant in the [[Rightful Conspiracy]], an attempt to murder [[Alusair Obarskyr]] and her mother, [[Filfaeril Obarskyr]], in {{yearlink|1373}}. He was delayed by [[Noumea Cardellith]].
