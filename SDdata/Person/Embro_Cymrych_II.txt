Embro Cymrych II was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early-to-mid 7 century DR.
He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Embro I. His successor was his first son, Alec. His second son, Samuel, would also go on to become High King after the death of Alec and of Alecs son.
