'''Suldag the Boar''' was the appointed lord of [[Tyrluk]] in [[Cormyr]].
  
==Personality==
Suldag was a good-oriented fighter of passable merit and incredible girth.

==Rumors==
Rumors said that his nickname, "the Boar", derived from his hunting abilities or his eating habits.
