'''Thumir Aingahuth''' was the proprietor of the [[Waterdhavian]] store [[Nueth's Fine Nets]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], following the demise of its previous owner.

==Description==
Thumir was rudely described by others as having a very [[rat]]-like face.

==Personality==
This man was alert of his surroundings and had a sharp, sarcastic wit.
