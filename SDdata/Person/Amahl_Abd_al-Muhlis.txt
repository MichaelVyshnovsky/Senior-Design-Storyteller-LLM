'''Amahl Abd al-Muhlis''' was a [[mamluk]] who lived in the [[Cities of the Pantheon|Pantheist]] city of [[I'tiraf]] in [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|21}}

==Activities==
Commander Amahl was the leader of the [[Devoted Mamluk Society]], and led them against conflicts with the [[Exalted Mamluk Society|Exalted]] for dominance as the region's finest mamluk organization. 

Amahl also held the title of Most Respected Warrior.
