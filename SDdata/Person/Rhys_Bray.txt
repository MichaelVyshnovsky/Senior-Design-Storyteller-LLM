Rhys Bray was a human Purple Dragon knight in the town of Eveningstar in the 15 century DR.
Rhys was a mountain of a man with a powerful square jaw and short brown hair and green eyes.
In the 15 century DR, knight Rhys Bray did business with the adventurers from the world of Eberron, who set out to investigate the nearby ruins of the Haunted Halls. He offered the adventurers access to some mundane weapons, ammunition, and crafting supplies.
