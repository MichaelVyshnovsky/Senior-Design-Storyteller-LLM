Eralt Gavin was the leader of the Friends of Blacklake Association of Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.
As the leader of the Friends of Blacklake Association, Eralts goal was to clean and restore the Blacklake to its former glory. He was a regular of the Beached Leviathan, and usually helped out Harrag when the old bartender needed a hand in his business.
