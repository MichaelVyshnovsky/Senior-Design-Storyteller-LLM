'''Eralt Gavin''' was the leader of the [[Friends of Blacklake Association]] of [[Neverwinter]] in the late years of the 15{{Th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dungeon/193/Shards of Selûne|28-29}}

==Activities==
As the leader of the Friends of Blacklake Association, Eralt's goal was to clean and restore the [[Blacklake]] to its former glory. He was a regular of the [[Beached Leviathan]], and usually helped out [[Harrag]] when the old bartender needed a hand in his business.
