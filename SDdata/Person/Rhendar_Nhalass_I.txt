'''Rhendar Nhalass I''' was the ninth [[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Nhalass Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Rhendar I reigned from {{Yearlink|-662}} to {{Yearlink|-633}}, inheriting the throne upon the death of his father [[Umathar Nhalass V|Umathar V]].

==Relationships==
His firstborn son and heir was [[Darthen Nhalass III|Darthen III]].
