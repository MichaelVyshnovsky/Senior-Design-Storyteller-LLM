King Esmel Torlath II was an early Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty who had a tragic reign.
Esmel was the first son of King Corl Torlath I, and Marcos Vymmar was his wifes father. Esmel III was his second son. His brother Prince Ereskas bore the family blade Namarra north to found Mirabar.
