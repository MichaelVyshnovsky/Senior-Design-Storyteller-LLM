'''Farnath Ilistar IV''' was the eighth King of the [[Ilistar Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Farnath ruled as Westgate's King from the {{YearlinkName|783}}, until his death in the {{YearlinkName|834}}. He was suspected of killing his brother, King [[Thartryn Ilistar II|Thartryn II]], to take the throne.

==Relationships==
His second son who would succeed him to the throne was [[Pendernath Ilistar|Pendernath]].
