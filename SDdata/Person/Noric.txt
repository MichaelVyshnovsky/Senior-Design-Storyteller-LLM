Deputy Noric was one of the deputies of Bron Bourstag of Voonlar in the Moonsea circa 1373 DR.
Noric was an extremely quarrelsome person, with that quality perfectly matched by his laziness.
Deputy Noric was one of the oldest assistants of Voonlars cruel Bron, who by the  was among the citys Deputies. Circa that year, Noric was often charged with guarding the Locktowers entrance. Deputy Noric survived the invasion of Voonlar but the summoned fiends of the Order of the Crippled Fang of 1373 DR.
