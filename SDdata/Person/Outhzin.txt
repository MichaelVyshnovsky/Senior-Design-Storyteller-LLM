Outhzin was one of the goblins that followed Lord High Chief Ghistpok within the mines of Dekanter. He held a position of importance among his fellow tribespeople.
Outhzins appearance differed slightly that most other goblins from Dekanter. His eyes were a bit more red and his fingers unusually longer than most.
In the , Outhzin and his warriors led a group of humans and their former tribesman Sheemzher to Chief Ghistpok for an audience at their camp in the central chamber of Dekanter. After their meeting, Outhzin and the outsiders fought against a group of the Beast Lords mongrelfolk, monstrosities that were formed from goblins from his very own tribe.
