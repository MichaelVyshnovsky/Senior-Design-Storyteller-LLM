'''Vayloss Talanthil III''', who was also called '''the Younger''' and who was originally named '''Jharath''', was the sixth [[Sorcerer]]-[[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Talanthil Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Vayloss III inherited the throne when his father, [[Vayloss Talanthil II]], died in {{Yearlink|-404}}. His name was changed from Jharath to Vayloss at this point. He reigned until {{Yearlink|-370}}.

==Relationships==
Vayloss III had one older brother. His son and heir was [[Mornarth Talanthil I|Mornarth I]].
