Chalathra Nyndra was a high priestess of Loviatar, the Maiden of Pain, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
She had dark eyes and raven hair. Chalathra was a thin woman.
Her particular tastes and craving for pain was legendary even among the clergy of Loviatar.
Chalathra was high priestess within the House of Spires temple, holding the esteemed title of Queen of Torment. She used an ancient draconic spell, combined with a special mixture she created called the Milk of the Maiden, to heal herself by immersion in a pool.
