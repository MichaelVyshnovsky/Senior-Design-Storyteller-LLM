'''Imnezzar''' was a [[Zakharan (ethnicity)|Zakharan]] inhabitant of [[Sokkar]] before the demise of the city.

==History==
Much to his eventual regret, Imnezzar was once the lover of Princess [[Ophidia as-Sokkari|Ophidia]]. When she grew tired of his affections the princess slew him, earning her an eternal curse from Imnezzar's powerful father.
