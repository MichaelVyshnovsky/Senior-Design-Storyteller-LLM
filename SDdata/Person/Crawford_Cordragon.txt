'''Crawford Cordragon''', known as the friendly dragon, was a [[dragon]] from the [[Whamite Isles]]. With his cordial nature and power of flight, Crawford Bucket was open to allying with any side in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
