'''Catalan Bree''' was a [[human]] male [[cleric]] of [[Selûne]].

In [[1367 DR|the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR]], he led the small temple of [[Moonwater]] in the town of [[Ylraphon]] in [[the Vast]], with four subordinate clerics.
