Cate Quirkilious was a daughter of Thomas C. Quirkilious, the dragonborn proprietor of the Chromatic Scale music shop in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
In the , Cate departed Baldurs Gate for Calimshan. The city of Calimport dazzled the woman and its music tickled her ears. There, she encountered a curious snakeskin musical instrument called slitherhorn. In her letter to Thomas, she promised to bring the instrument home and play it for him.
