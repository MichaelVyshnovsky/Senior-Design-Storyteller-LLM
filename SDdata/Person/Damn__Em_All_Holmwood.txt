'''Damn 'Em All Holmwood''' was a [[duergar]] from the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Holmwood wore a simple set of [[chainmail]] [[armor]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Damn 'Em All Holmwood, along with other duergar, [[giant spider]]s, and [[drow]], was slaughtered in the basement cave under the home of [[Bloomridge Park]] groundskeeper, [[Lavernica]]. Holmwood's body was left charred and blackened by flames near the mutilated body of [[Incontinentia Figgin]], who was turned into a grim effigy by [[Orin the Red]] and her [[Murder Tribunal]] of [[Bhaal]].
