Damn Em All Holmwood was a duergar from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Holmwood wore a simple set of chainmail armor.
In the , Damn Em All Holmwood, along with other duergar, giant spiders, and drow, was slaughtered in the basement cave under the home of Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica. Holmwoods body was left charred and blackened by flames near the mutilated body of Incontinentia Figgin, who was turned into a grim effigy by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal.
