'''Brinna Brightsong''' was a [[halfling]] [[bard]] who lived and performed in [[Triel]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
She had a prominent nose, hazel eyes, and light blonde hair.

==Activities==
She was known for performing some rather bawdy and ultimately controversial songs.

==History==
Brinna met her untimely end in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}. She was killed while performing at a tavern in Triel, by a [[Cult of the Absolute|cultist of the Absolute]]. She was among the dozen individuals plucked from the [[Fugue Plane]], chosen by the entity [[Withers]] to aid in his campaign against the [[Dead Three]].
