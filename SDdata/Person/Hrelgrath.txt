'''Hrelgrath''' was a [[Shar]]ran faithful during the mid-8{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was a bald man with purple and black robes.{{cite book/Elminster Ascending/The Temptation of Elminster|692|16}}

==Possessions==
He looted a plethora of magic from [[Karsus' Keep]].

==Relationships==
Hrelgrath was one of [[Elryn]]'s dreadspells.

==Abilities==
As a dreadspell, he wielded borrowed arcane power.

==History==
Hrelgrath was one of the dreadspells sent with [[Elryn]] to find and slay a [[Chosen of Mystra]] near [[Westgate]] two tendays into the Chaos of [[the Art]] in the {{yearlink|797}}. During the first leg of the trip, he had [[Uldus Blackram]] lead the way, while holding a knife to the man's back. During the first leg of their trip, Uldus Blackram was killed by [[Ilbryn Starym]], an [[elven]] [[wizard]] they chanced upon.{{cite book/Elminster Ascending/The Temptation of Elminster|696-698|17}}

Once they found the ruin, Karsus' Keep, Hrelgrath helped loot it of valuables.{{cite book/Elminster Ascending/The Temptation of Elminster|725-726|19}} Once their looted magic failed against Azuth, despite bringing down the ruin, they fled.{{cite book/Elminster Ascending/The Temptation of Elminster|730-731|19}}

When Dread Sister [[Klalaera]] appeared and herded them back against Azuth, they were stopped partway by [[Umbregard]] and [[Starsunder]], two mages that appeared suddenly. During the battle, one of Starsunder's spells destroyed him and [[Vaelam]] at the same time it blocked their spells.
