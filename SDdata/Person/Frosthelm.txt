'''Frosthelm''' was a king of [[Sundabar]] during in 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
He was the son of [[Helm Dwarf-Friend]] and become the king of Sundabar after him. Frosthelm had a son that became the next king of the city, [[Firehelm]].
