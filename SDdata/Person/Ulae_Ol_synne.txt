'''Ulae Olósynne''' was the first Mage-Queen of the [[Olósynne Dynasty]] who ruled the nation of [[Aglarond]].

==Activities==
Ulae shared the rule of Aglarond jointly with her twin sister [[Thara Olósynne]]. They inherited the throne upon the death of their father, King [[Philaspur Olósynne|Philaspur]].

==Relationships==
Ulae gave birth to two children–her firstborn son [[Halacar Olósynne|Halacar]] and daughter [[Ilione Olósynne|Ilione]].
