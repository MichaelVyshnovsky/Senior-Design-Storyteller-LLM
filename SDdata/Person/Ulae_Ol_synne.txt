Ulae Olósynne was the first Mage-Queen of the Olósynne Dynasty who ruled the nation of Aglarond.
Ulae shared the rule of Aglarond jointly with her twin sister Thara Olósynne. They inherited the throne upon the death of their father, King Philaspur.
Ulae gave birth to two children–her firstborn son Halacar and daughter Ilione.
