Samuel Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid&ndash7 century DR.
He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the uncle of his predecessor, High King Rowland the Boy King. His elder brother, Alec I, had been Rowlands predecessor and his father, Embro II, had been High King before that. Samuels successor was his first son, Warren II.
