'''Lord Paeral''' was an inhabitant of [[Cormanthyr]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]], and the head of House [[Paeral]].

==Appearance==
He was scarred, and kept his sword at the ready even during meetings. His voice had the weight of command.

==History==
He participated in a covert meeting in support of the [[Coronal]] [[Eltargrim]]. There, he was inquisitive as to who was guarding the Coronal.
