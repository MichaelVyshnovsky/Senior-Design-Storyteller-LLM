Major Cargunn was the proprietor of the well-stocked Major Cargunns Military and Equipment Shoppe in Eshpurta, Amn, in the Year of the Shield, 1367 DR. He was a retired officer of the local Shield Militia but rumors said he had certain "expertise" that kept him involved with Militia operations.
