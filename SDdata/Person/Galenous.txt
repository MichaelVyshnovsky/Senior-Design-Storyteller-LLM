'''Galenous the Bard''' was a [[bard]] in the 13{{th}} century [[DR]]. They were most well known for having written [[Battle of Urml (poem)|a poem]] about the [[Battle of Urml]]. A hundred years following the historic battle, the bard's ballad continued to be taught within [[Urml (Turmish)|Urml's]] [[Temple of Tyr (Urml)|Temple of Tyr]].
