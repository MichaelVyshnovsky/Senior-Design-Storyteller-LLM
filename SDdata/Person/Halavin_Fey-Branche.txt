'''Halavin Fey-Branche''' was the [[Matron Mother]] of [[House Fey-Branche]] in the early 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
The fall of [[House Do'Urden]] in the {{yearlinkName|1338}}, led to the ascension of House Fey-Branche from the ninth house of [[Menzoberranzan]] to the eighth, granting Halavin a seat on the ruling council.<ref>
