'''Jhartos Illance''' was a young nobleman who lived in the [[Cormyrean Embassy]] in [[Calimport]].

==Relationships==
Jhartos was the fourth son of the [[Illance]] noble family and the younger cousin of [[Mallik Illance|Mallik]], third in line to his father's position. He enjoyed the decadence of Calimport but did not have the business sense of his cousin.
