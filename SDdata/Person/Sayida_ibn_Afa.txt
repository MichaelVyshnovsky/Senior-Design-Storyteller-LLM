}}'''Sayida ibn Afa''' was a [[rawun]] who lived within the fortress of [[Sarahin]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|16}}

==Personality==
Contrary to those with whom she lived, Sayida despised killing.

==Activities==
Sayida was well-connected within Sarahin, serving as confidant to both the [[Marwan al-Jabal al-Hiyali|Shadow Caliph]] and the leader of the [[Loyalists]], [[Yusuf bin Ayyub]].

She remained confident that Yusuf would appoint her as leader of the Loyalists upon Marwan's death.

==Relationships==
Sayida was the niece of the Shadow Caliph.

==Possessions==
Sayida owned a ''[[dagger of quickness]]'' and a ''[[qanun of quiet]]''.
