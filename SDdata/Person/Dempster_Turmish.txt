Dempster Turmish was a young nobleman of Alaghôn whod operated as a mercenary captain, selling his mercenaries services to the highest bidders. After years of building up his forces, he used his mercenary army to attack two rival nobles of Alaghôn. After a short but bloody civil war, Dempster emerged unopposed as ruler of the proud city and named himself mayor in the .
He quickly embarked on an expansionary military campaign, seizing control of the lands surrounding Alaghôn all the way to the lowlands outside of Hlondeth.
However, despite repeated attempts he was unable to conquer the city, and died in his sleep in the , while preparing for yet another invasion.
