Sashara Keenspear was a famous genasi mercenary who operated as a "kraken hunter" out of Westgate in the late 15 century DR.
She was known to take an interest in the movements of the city of Xxiphu.
It was rumored that curious adventurers could find her in the private rooms of the Shell Queen or inspecting the ships in Westgates harbor.
She wielded a glowing,  spear inscribed with runes.
Sashara worked and took meals with Yu Kai, a fellow famous kraken hunter.
As of the , Sashara was regarded as perhaps the only so-called "kraken hunter" in Westgate who might have actually slain a kraken.
