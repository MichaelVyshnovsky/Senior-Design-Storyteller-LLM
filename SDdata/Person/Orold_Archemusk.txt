'''Orold Archemusk''' was a wizard of the 12th and 13th centuries [[DR]].

==History==
Around [[1100 DR]], he wrote ''[[Trends in Approaches to the Art]]''. He once visited [[Thay]] at the start of that nation.

He was greatly admired by surviving [[Netherese arcanist]]s and [[magelord]]s of [[Athalantar]].

He died around {{YearlinkName|1287}}.
