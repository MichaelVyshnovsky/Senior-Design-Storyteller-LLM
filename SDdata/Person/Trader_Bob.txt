'''Trader Bob''' was a [[human]] adventurer-turned-merchant. His shops were notable for being more expensive than the norm.

==Description==
Bob had long, brown hair, light skin, and wore a purple tunic with green pants.

==Possessions==
On his person, Trader Bob carried three pieces of magical equipment: a suit of [[plate mail]], a shield, and a [[battleaxe]].

==History==
Trader Bob began his career as an adventurer at a young age, and decided to retire early as well. He went into business as an equipment merchant, and his shops could be found all over [[Toril]], even far from civilization.
