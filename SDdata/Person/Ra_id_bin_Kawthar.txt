Raid bin Kawthar was a sea elf living in Chaos Bay near Hawa in 1367 DR.
Raid was friendly and highly intelligent.
On occasion, he served as a navigator for ships entering Chaos Bay, assisting their captains to safely pass through the deadly coral maze lurking beneath the waves.
Raid used a magical spear or a normal short sword during combat.
Raid was the son of the leader of the sea elves inhabiting the coral maze of Chaos Bay.
