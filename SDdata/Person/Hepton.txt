'''Hepton''' was a [[human]] male and general of the [[Zhentilar]] army during the [[Time of Troubles]] in [[1358 DR]].

He was with the [[god]] [[Bane]] when he planned the invasion of [[Tantras]]. Hepton, fearing for the lives of his soldiers, tried to argue with Bane, but was silenced by the god.{{cite book/Tantras|176}}
