'''Kryll''' was a female [[necromancer]] who was a one-time companion of [[Dorn Il-Khan]] until she betrayed him to [[Luskan]]'s guards. 

Eventually, with the help of [[Gorion's Ward]], Dorn found Kryll near [[Nashkel]] and killed her in revenge.
