High Lady '''Jasmine Lurian''' was a noble of House [[Lurian]] in the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite document/Procampur City Expansion Packet|9}}{{Cite web/House Lurian}}

==Activities==
As head of House Lurian, he was involved in the family's [[gold]]-smithing business.{{Cite document/Procampur City Expansion Packet|6}}

==Relationships==
Her husband was Lord Consul [[Povalo Lurian]], the head of the family, and they had some children.While several other members of House Lurian are known, their relationships are unknown, though some are most likely intended to be the children of Povalo and Jasmine.

==History==
She was the High Lady by [[1370 DR]], when she was aged 52.
