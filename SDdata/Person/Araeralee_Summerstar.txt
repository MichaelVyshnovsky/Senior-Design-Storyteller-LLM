'''Araeralee Summerstar''' was an apprentice [[wizard]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
She was a compassionate woman.

==Activities==
Araeralee was a student of [[Blackstaff Academy]] within [[Blackstaff Tower]] in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] of Waterdeep in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.

==History==
She was an orphan who grew up in Blackstaff Tower.
