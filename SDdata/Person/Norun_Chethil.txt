'''Norun Chethil''' was a monk cook of [[Candlekeep]].

==History==
Chethil was the most able cook of the monastery.

In [[1487 DR]], the [[shadovar]] [[Maerandor]] decided to use him as his disguise to infiltrate the monastery. Then Maerandor broke Chethil's neck, but before before he died he read all his mind.
