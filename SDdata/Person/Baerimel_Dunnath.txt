'''Baerimel Dunnath''' was a wizard in [[Thentia]] in {{yearlink|1367}} and in {{yearlink|1373}}.{{cite book/The Rite}} She was deputy of [[Sureene Aumratha]], of the [[House of the Moon]].

==Description==
As of {{yearlink|1373}}, Jannatha and Baerimel were petite and impishly pretty.{{cite book/The Rite}}

==Activities==
Together with her sister [[Jannatha Goldenshield]], they used to play tricks on people for fun, such as pretending they were waifs and wandering into adventurers' camps to see what sort of treatment they got, as of {{yearlink|1367}}.

==Relationships==
She was the sister of Jannatha Goldenshield, and the cousin of [[Sinylla Zoranyian]].

==History==
Baerimel Dunnath helped [[Taegan Nightwind]] by casting lie-detection spells on the other mages aiding the effort to decrypt [[Sammaster]]'s notes on the [[Rage of Dragons]] in {{yearlink|1373}}. She also helped in the final battle against [[Phourkyn One-Eye]]'s impersonator.
