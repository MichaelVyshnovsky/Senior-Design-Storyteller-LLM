Alia al-Karim was a half-elven desert rider and member of the House of Al-Karim living in the desert outside of Hiyal around 1367 DR.
Due to an unprovoked attack on her clan, Alia and the rest of her family swore vengeance on a merchant from Hiyal named Farid.
Alia wielded an enchanted longsword and a jambiya. She wore leather armor and a daraq.
Alia was the daughter of the House of Al-Karims leader, Aasim Karim. Her brother Jamal al-Karim was slain by a merchant named Farid.
