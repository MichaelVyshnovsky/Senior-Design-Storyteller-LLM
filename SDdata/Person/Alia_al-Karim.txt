'''Alia al-Karim''' was a [[half-elven]] [[desert rider]] and member of the [[House of Al-Karim]] living in the desert outside of [[Hiyal]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1993|84|[[Alia al-Karim]]}}

==Activites==
Due to an unprovoked attack on her clan, Alia and the rest of her family swore vengeance on a merchant from Hiyal named [[Farid]].

==Possessions==
Alia wielded an [[flame tongue|enchanted longsword]] and a ''[[jambiya]]''. She wore [[leather armor]] and a ''[[daraq]]''.

==Relationships==
Alia was the daughter of the House of Al-Karim's leader, [[Aasim Karim]]. Her brother [[Jamal al-Karim]] was slain by a merchant named [[Farid]].
