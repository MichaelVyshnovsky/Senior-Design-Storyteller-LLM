Orem Arvaeyn was a kanqat of the Vale Guard that protected the city of Evereska and the Evereska Vale during the late 14 century DR.
He had a severe look about him and spoke in the serious manner befitting a guardsman or soldier.
Orem possessed some wisdom during his years and made a habit of offering thoughtful insight to his friends.
He was on good terms with Galaeron Nihmedu, a patrol leader of the Evereskan Tomb Guard. The pair had graduated together at Evereskas Academy of Arms.
