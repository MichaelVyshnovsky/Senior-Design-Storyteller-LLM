Olin was an ex-adventurer in Daggerford around the . As an adventurer he had particularly bad luck.
Olins ill fortune changed when he and his fellows discovered a large amount of money. He decided to retire in the first place he came across, the town of Daggerford.
He lived in a house in the southeast section of Daggerford at the intersection of Water Street and Kaoth Alley near the River Gate.
