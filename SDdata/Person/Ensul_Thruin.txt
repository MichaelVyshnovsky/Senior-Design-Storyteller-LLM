'''Ensul Thruin''' was a scholar and [[arcanist]] of ancient [[Netheril]]. He spent much of his career studying the fundamental relationship between [[the Weave]] and the natural world.

Among his other accomplishments, Thruin was the inventor of the [[spell]], ''[[Ensul's soultheft]]''.
