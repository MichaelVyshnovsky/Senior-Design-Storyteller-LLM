'''Lemere Soleilon II''' was the third King of the [[Soleilon Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Lemere II reigned from {{Yearlink|-74}} until his death in {{Yearlink|-68}}. He inherited the throne from his father King [[Lemere Soleilon I|Lemere I]]. 

==Relationships==
Lemere II married Lady [[Lharida Ithal]] of [[Tethyr]]. His firstborn son and heir was [[Ryndarth Soleilon I|Ryndarth I]].
