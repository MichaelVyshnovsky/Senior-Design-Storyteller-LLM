Murrai was the second-in-command of the Sand Kings rebel group in Anauroch in 1479 DR.
Murrai was a Bedine mercenary who decided to join the Sand Kings. In time, he became the second-in-command of the Sand Kings.
In 1479 DR, the Sand Kings discovered that the shadovar were on the verge of gaining control of the Spiral Gate, a crucial portal nexus. So they tried to sabotage the shadovars efforts.
Murrai was a mercenary at heart, fighting for the love of conflict itself. He was rash and impulsive.
