'''Temprith Ilistar''' was the sixth King of the [[Ilistar Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Temprith became the next King of Westgate by seizing the throne from his nephew [[Farnath Ilistar III|Farnath III]] upon the death of King [[Farnath Ilistar II|Farnath II]] in the {{YearlinkName|756}}. He would only rule for a few years until he was overthrown and killed by his cousin [[Thartryn Ilistar II|Thartryn II]] in the {{YearlinkName|759}}.
