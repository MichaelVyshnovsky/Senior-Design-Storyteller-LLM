Ulthar Orgolath, who was also called Ulthar the Darkhorn, was the second Nentyarch of Narfell during the reign of the Orgolath Dynasty.
Ulthar aided his older twin brother Ulthas in slaying Belevan Crell and overthrowing the Crell Dynasty of Narfell in . As he was slightly younger than his twin, Ulthar stepped aside and allowed his older brother to claim the title of Nentyarch and become the first ruler of the Orgolath Dynasty. Ulthar succeeded his brother as Nentyarch upon Ulthas death in . He would rule for many years until .
Ulthars mother, Ayanna Orgolath, was impregnated by the demon lord Grazzt, resulting in his half-fiend demonic heritage. His firstborn son was a tiefling named Vargaun who would inherit Ulthars title upon his death in -578 DR.
