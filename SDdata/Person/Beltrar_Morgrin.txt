'''Beltrar Morgrin''' was a [[War Wizard]] of [[Cormyr]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], and a traitor to the Realm.

==History==
When the retinue of the [[Royal Magician of Cormyr]], [[Caladnei]] raided the [[Marsember]] meeting of the [[Rightful Conspiracy]] in early spring of {{yearlink|1373}}, Caladnei observed that Beltrar Morgrin was among the traitors and had hid downcellar. She directed [[Thoaburr]] to him. Though Beltrar was to be taken quietly, he was not to see morning alive.
