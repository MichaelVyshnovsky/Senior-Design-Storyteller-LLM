Maggie Terrens, sister to Marcus Terrens, was a child who lived in the town of Moonhaven in the late 15 century DR.
Sometime before the Moonhaven was attacked and ruined by the goblin raiders of the Cult of the Absolute in the , Maggie was among the four children who had gone missing from Moonhaven.
