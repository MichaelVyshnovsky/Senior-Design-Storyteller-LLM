'''Ottad yn Malik el Ostak''' was the younger brother of [[Basad el Ostak]] and the head of the thieves' guild in [[Memnon]] for [[el Kahmir]] family in [[1370 DR]]. 

==Personality==
Ottad was young and power-hungry, which spymaster [[Nureh el Pesarkhal]] used to her advantage.
