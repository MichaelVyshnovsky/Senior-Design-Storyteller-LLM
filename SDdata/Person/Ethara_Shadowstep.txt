'''Ethara Shadowstep''' was the [[elf|elven]] captain of the [[Tethyr]]ian merchant [[caravel]] ''[[Moon's Wake]]'' in the late mid-to-late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. 

==History==
In [[1379 DR]],{{Cite dungeon/206/Eyes on the Ball|1}} while in [[Waterdeep]] Ethara was kidnapped and [[slavery|enslaved]] by [[Lanador]]. Thereafter, a reward of 100 [[gold piece]]s was put up for any information regarding her whereabouts.
