Sims Winters was the son of Beartrid Winters and Kalas Winters, who worked at the Wolfs Pelt Inn in the Icewind Dale community of Targos circa 1369 DR.
Sims was a fiery 13-year-old boy with short-cut black hair and his familys big brown eyes. He was  tall and weighed .
Sims looked out for his family, especially his mother and sister. He jumped to their defense at a hint of insult or confrontation, trying to prove his manliness to his father.
Sims always carried a dagger gifted to him by his father Kalas for his 13th birthday.
Around Tarsakh in 1369 DR, Sims lived with his family in the Ten Towns.
