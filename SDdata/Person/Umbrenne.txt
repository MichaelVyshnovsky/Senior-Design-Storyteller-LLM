Umbrenne was a Talonite preacher from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Umbrenne was a midle-aged in appearance wood elf with sickly green skin, long straight graying hair, and brown eyes. He was dressed in simple burlap priestly robe.
In the , Umbrenne could be found preaching to commoners and refugees in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate, not far from the Basilisk Gate. He spent his days evangelizing over the cycle of life and death through pestilence as taught by the Mother of all Plagues.
