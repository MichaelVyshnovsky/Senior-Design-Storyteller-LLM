'''Grabber Forth''' was a [[deep gnome]] [[archmage]] in service to the [[yugoloth]] [[Maram il Vass]] in the [[Deep Wastes]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/It's All in the Blood|17}}

==Activities==
Grabber worked for Maram il Vass. He was custodian of the private rooms of the yugoloth, together with a [[shield guardian]].
