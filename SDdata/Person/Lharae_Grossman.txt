Lharae Grossman, who was nicknamed the Lithe, was a thief and inhabitant of Tilverton in the mid-14 century DR.
Lharae was quite beautiful and witty.
Lharae was a member of the Rogues of Tilverton, though most of her time with the group was spent infiltrating the Cormyrean garrison and the local clergy of Gond in order to gather valuable information.
Her father, Artur Grossman, was the leader of the Rogues.
In the , her father Artur had disappeared and was presumed dead. She took up leadership of the guild from that point.
