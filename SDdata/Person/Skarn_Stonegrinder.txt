'''Skarn Stonegrinder''' was a [[dwarf]] sage in [[1358 DR]]. His expertise was in geology, mining, and metal smithing. Nobody knew why he lived among the men of [[Impiltur]]. His fees were a percentage of the operations he advised. His treasure was hidden somewhere in the [[Earthfast Mountains]].
