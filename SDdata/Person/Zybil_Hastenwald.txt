Zybil Hastenwald was a duergar from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Zybil wore a simple set of chainmail armor.
In the , Zybil Hastenwald, along with other duergar, giant spiders, and drow, was slaughtered in the basement caver under the home of Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica. Zybils body was left broken near the mutilated body of Incontinentia Figgin, who was turned into a grim effigy by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal.
