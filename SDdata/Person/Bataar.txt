'''Bataar''' was [[Ulaan]]'s sister, and a servant in the [[Fortress of the Old Man]].{{cite book/Sentinelspire|18}} She helped wash and clothe [[Lewan]] when he was brought in by [[Sauk]] after [[Tarsakh 16]] of {{yearlink|1374}}.{{cite book/Sentinelspire|13}} On the night of [[Tarsakh 25]], Ulaan believed her sister had been killed in the fighting that broke out throughout the fortress, but her death was never confirmed.{{cite book/Sentinelspire|40}}
