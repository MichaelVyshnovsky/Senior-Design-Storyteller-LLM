Ensril Surlpar was one of the younger adults and heirs in the Surlpar family&mdasha wealthy and powerful clan that held one of the twenty Onsruur (lords council) seats in the city of Khôltar, south Faerûn, circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR.
Ensril had the shock of blond-to-white hair that was a characteristic of the Surlpars.
He was one of the Iron City elite and had developed an expensive taste in clothing. He relished fine wines and beautiful women.
Ensril Surlpar was the owner of a filthy, old blurdren (food counter) called Surlpars Stews that had a prime location. He cared nothing for the business except that it turned a nice profit by keeping prices low and quality at a bare minimum.
