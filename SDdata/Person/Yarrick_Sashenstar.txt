Yarrick Sashenstar was a Ser patron of the arts from the Sashenstar patriar family of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
During the Absolute crisis of the , Ser Yarrick Sashenstar was invited to attend the art show titled "Strange Tides Self-Portraits from the Sword Coast," hosted by Lady Jannath in her Lower City estate. Yarrick RSVPd to attend but insisted on bringing his very young triplets along, despite Lady Jannath politely reminding him that her manse didnt offer attendant nursemaids for the guests.
