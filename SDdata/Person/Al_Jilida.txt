'''Al Jilida''' was a ruthless, powerful schemer, and the local head of the slaver ring posing as the [[Sandvoyagers' Guild]]. He was in league with the guildhall at the [[Oasis of the White Palm]].{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|14}}

==Activities==
Anyone who wished to become part of a Sandvoyager caravan would have to deal with this man.
