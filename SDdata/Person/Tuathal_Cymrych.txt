'''Tuathal Cymrych''' was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] at the end of the 5{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] and the first son of his predecessor, High King [[Tristram Cymrych|Tristram]]. His successor was his son, [[Carrig Cymrych III]].

==History==
Tuathal assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|483}}, after the death of his father.

He ruled for sixteen years before he was assassinated in the {{YearlinkName|499}}.
