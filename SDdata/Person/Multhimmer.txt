'''Multhimmer''' was a [[merchant]], importer and shopkeep who ran a [[Multhimmer the Merchant's shop|general store]] in [[Ashabenford]] in 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He would buy just about any kind of item He did occasionally deal in stolen goods, but was very careful not to bring in stolen items from the local Mistran.
