Aihonen was a great hero who killed the white dragon Icasaracht.
When in battle, Aihonen used his sword the Blade of Aihonen and equipped a shield which bore the symbol of Tempus.
Over the years, Aihonen fought with the Uthgardt barbarian tribes of the North. During this time, the sea spirit Elisia fell in love with him.
Around 1181 DR, Aihonen fought the last of the great white dragon matriarchs of the Spine of the World on banks of Lac Dinneshere.
During the battle, Aihonen fell before the might of Icasaracht, but with his dying breath, he pierce her heart with the Blade of Aihonen.
