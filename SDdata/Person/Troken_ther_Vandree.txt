Trokenther Vandree was the matron mother of House Vandree in Menzoberranzan around 1358 DR.
The drow elf matron was known for being inefficient in challenging other houses, such as House Barrison DelArmgo.
Trokenther was killed by her daughter Fiirnelther Vandree in 1388 DR.
