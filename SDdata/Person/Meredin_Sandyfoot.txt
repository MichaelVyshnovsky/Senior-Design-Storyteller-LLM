==Description==
Bt the time he headed the guild, Meredin Sandyfoot was fifty winters old who had a cheerful positive personality.

==Possessions==
Meredin wore a set of [[enchanted]] ''+3'' [[leather armor|leathers]], a ''[[cloak of displacement]]'', and a ''[[ring of invisibility]]''. He was armed with a magic ''+1 [[luck blade]]''.

==History==
As the leader of Bral's thieves' guild, Meredin controlled fences and smuggling in the Middle City, never resorting to bloodshed and always resolving conflicts via rewards and bribery.
