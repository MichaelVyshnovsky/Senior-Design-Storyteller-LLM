'''Marbone the Cosh''' was a roguish [[halfling]] [[adventurer]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}
{{Quote
|We're wasting our time. Everyone knows there's better loot in the [[Stonelands]]!

==Description==
Marbone had short brown hair and dressed in leathers.

==History==
[[File:Ball and Chain adventurers.jpg|300px|thumb|''Marbone and company at the tavern.'']]
In between adventures, he patronized the [[Ball and Chain Tavern]] on the docks of the [[Wheloon Prison]] alongside his fellows [[Irwynne]], [[Gorli]], and [[Bash (adventurer)|Bash]]. He was seen with his [[dagger]] in the table while advocating for adventures in the [[Stonelands]].
