Marbone the Cosh was a roguish halfling adventurer in the late 15 century DR.
Marbone had short brown hair and dressed in leathers.
300px|thumb|Marbone and company at the tavern.
In between adventures, he patronized the Ball and Chain Tavern on the docks of the Wheloon Prison alongside his fellows Irwynne, Gorli, and Bash. He was seen with his dagger in the table while advocating for adventures in the Stonelands.
