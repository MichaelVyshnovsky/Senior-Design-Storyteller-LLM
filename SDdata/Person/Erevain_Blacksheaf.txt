'''Erevain Blacksheaf''' was an [[elf|elven]] adventurer from [[Evereska]] who journeyed to the [[Icewind Dale]] town of [[Easthaven]].

==Description==
The gold-haired elf was of slender build and dressed in [[leather armor|hunting leathers]].

==Personality==
Erevain was a kind elf who felt a great desire to see all [[Faer√ªn]] and not take the [[Retreat]] to [[Evermeet]].

==Possessions==
He wielded an [[Erevain's broad sword|enchanted broad sword]] that was originally forged by the [[Greycloaks]] of [[Evereska]].

==Relationships==
He had a cousin named [[Xan]], who himself was a member of the Greycloaks.

==History==
In [[Eleint]] of the {{yearlinkName|1281}}, Erevain stayed at the [[Snowdrift Inn]] after traveling from [[Kuldahar]] in the [[Spine of the World]]. He was killed by [[spider]]s in [[Dragon's Eye]] later that year.
