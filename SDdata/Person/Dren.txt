Dren was one of the Blades of the Old Man in the fortress of Sentinelspire. He was a part of the team accompanying Sauk to look for Kheil and secure the Eraellen in the Northern Shalhoond in Tarsakh of , conspiring to kill the Old Man of the Mountain. Kerlis, Valmir and Dren stayed behind to watch Berun and Lewan when Sauk decided to hunt down some yaqubi, expecting them to be carrying gold. Berun disabled them and fled, though Sauks party caught up to them by the next morning.
