Qualleth was a red-haired ranger in Nesmé in 1484 DR.
Just after the start of the War of the Silver Marches, Qualleth was appointed leader of the towns rangers in preparation for the imminent troll attack. He was greatly depressed by the Darkening and believed that only a miracle could save his hometown.
