Darreth was a prisoner at the Temple of Elemental Evil under Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.
Darreth lived in Womford in the Dessarin Valley. In 1491 DR, he was captured by elemental cultists who took him to the altar of the Temple of Elemental Evil in order to use him as a sacrifice. However, some adventurers infiltrated the temple and freed him.
