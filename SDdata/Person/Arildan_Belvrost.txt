'''Arildan Belvrost''' was the patriarch of the [[Belvrost]] clan of mages in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Abilities==
He was known to have the ability to enchant daggers so that they struck on their own.

==History==
As of {{yearlink|1370}}, he was the patriarch of the [[Belvrost]] family.

==Possessions==
He dwelt in the family manor at [[Sundstone]], in the hamlet of [[Baernwood]].

==Activities==
He was known to pen [[scroll]]s and enchant [[dagger]]s to strike on their own.
