'''Fayiz Mikali''' was a crewman on the pirate vessel ''[[Leap for the Throat]]'' in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
As a [[Elemental mage|sea mage]], Fayiz made good use of his powers while raiding merchant vessels sailing on [[Zakharan seas|Suq Bay]]. Fayiz was also in charge of the other [[wizard]]s onboard the ship.

==Possessions==
Fayiz owned a special ''[[mirror of mental prowess]]'' that [[Almari bint-Kafira]] used to communicate with Prince [[Tannous al-Assad]] back in [[Huzuz]].
