Rossina was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Rossina was a forest gnome with a pale skin tone, icy blue eyes, and long straight brown hair.
In the , Rossina and several other citizens of Baldurs Gate were randomly attacked by a gang sahuagin who emerged from the Gray Harbor as Rossina was strolling along the shore. Serendipitously, a band of adventurers known as the Absolute Adversaries were nearby as the attack commenced.
