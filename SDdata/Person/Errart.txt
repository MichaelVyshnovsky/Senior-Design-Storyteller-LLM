'''Errart''' was a guard at Castle [[Felmorel]] in the mid–8{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Abilities==
He could wield a sword.

==Relationships==
He was a servant of Lord [[Esbre Felmorel]]. As a guard, his fellows included [[Glavyn]], [[Feiryn]],{{cite book/Elminster Ascending/The Temptation of Elminster|550-551|3}} and [[Bretchimus]].{{cite book/Elminster Ascending/The Temptation of Elminster|563|3}} To Lady [[Nasmaerae Felmorel]], he was an old, trustworthy man.

==History==
After the god [[Azuth]] put Lady Nasmaerae Felmorel under warning for trying to plunder the mind of one of her guests, [[Elminster Aumar|Wanlorn]] in early spring of the {{yearlinkName|761}}, Lord Esbre Felmorel, her husband, came to her with three men-at-arms, and forced her to cast the spell she'd been using to enthrall him again, which Azuth had warned would result in her being forever shorn of her magic. One of those guards was Errart.
