Lorris Niss was a half-elf acolyte at Candlekeep on the Sword Coast in the late 15 century DR.
Lorris was middle aged.
In the , Loris was reading The Price of Beauty with her friend, a fellow acolyte named Falthrax Loderr, when he went missing. Lorris grew worried when she hadnt seen him in two days. Encountering some curious adventurers, she explained what had happened and enlisted their help.
