==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Brega was overseeing the investigation of the [[skinner murders]], a series of killings that saw citizens of Athkatla being murdered and having their skin removed.

At that time he was also leading the local manhunt for the nefarious murderer [[Neb]].
