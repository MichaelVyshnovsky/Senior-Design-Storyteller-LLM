'''Myrkyr''' was a [[cleric]] of [[Lathander]] of the [[House of the Morning]] temple in the village of [[Eveningstar]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Myrkyr was in charge of hiring [[adventurer]]s to help serve as guards.
