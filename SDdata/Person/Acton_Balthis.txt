'''Pip''' was the butler at the [[Balthis Estate]] in the City of Coin, [[Athkatla]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Brigands in my home, is it? Bah, where are the guards?! Guards!!''|Acton Balthis}}

==Description==
Lord Balthis was a [[human]] male who dressed in turquoise finery.

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Lord Acton Balthis served as the Vice-Regent for the [[Council of Six (Amn)|Council of Six]], despite not having a seat on the council. However, Acton acted on its behalf and was the de facto ruler when the Council of Six was not in session. Lord Balthis could often be found in his [[Balthis Estate|estate]] in Athkatla's [[Athkatla/Bridge District|Bridge District]]. His peace was protected by the loyal [[hin]] butler named [[Pip]] and home defended by armored bodyguards.
