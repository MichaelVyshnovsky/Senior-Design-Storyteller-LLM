Jelvus Grinch was the "First Citizen" of Neverwinter in the mid&ndash15 century DR, meaning he was the de facto leader of the ruined town and of the Neverwinter Guard following the Ruining.
Through his toughness, courage, and strength of will, Jelvus Grinch rose to become the nominal leader of the people eking out a living in the ruins of Neverwinter following the citys destruction. He was known not to shy away from any challenge, but so too could recognize when he had no leverage in a confrontation.
In his capacity as a capable leader of the ruined city of Neverwinter, Jelvus Grinch met and made allies of Artemis Entreri and Drizzt DoUrden. He had also met Reginald Roundshield (as "Bonnego Battleaxe") later in life.
After Mount Hotenow exploded in the , and destroyed his native city, Jelvus came to be recognized among the survivors as a leader, and was referred to by many as the "first citizen of Neverwinter".
In the , Jelvus allied with the Shadovar representative Herzgo Alegni against the Thayans.
Following the arrival of Dagult Neverember in Neverwinter, Jelvus was sidelined, although he was offered a modicum of respect by the new regime in recognition that many citizens still looked to him for guidance. As of the , he still commanded a few of the Guards manning The Wall.
In the , an elderly Jelvus accompanied Lord Dagult Neverember and General Sabine to a meeting with Bruenor Battlehammer and Emerus Warcrown in Neverwinter to discuss the reclamation of Gauntlgrym from the drow.
