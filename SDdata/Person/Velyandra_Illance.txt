'''Velyandra Illance''' was a noblewoman of the [[Illance]] family of [[Cormyr]] of the mid-to-late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
She was married to [[Daeclander Illance]], and had borne him his heir, [[Rothglar Illance]]. Word around court was that he had eight siblings.
