Baziel was a powerful pit fiend in the late 14 century DR.
Baziel was often summoned by Brennus Tanthul. By 1374 DR, he had entered into service to Mephistopheles.
In the , Brennus summoned Baziel to have more information about Kesson Rel. However, because of Baziels recent change in loyalties, it was was Mephistophles himself that answered the summons.
