'''Kuldus Droum''' was a male [[human]] [[werebear]] from the [[Moonwood]] and was a [[cleric]] for the tribe of the [[People of the Black Blood]] living there.

==Description==
Kuldus was a strong, large, and quiet man. He had been a werebear since his birth. Kuldus possessed the physical strength necessary to challenge [[Jarthon Drael]], but he recognized a lack of leadership within himself.

==Possessions==
Kuldus was usually equipped with his [[heavy mace]], [[crossbow]], and [[hide armor]] and a large shield for protection.
