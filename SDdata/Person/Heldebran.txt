'''Heldebran''' was a [[human]] [[wizard]] of [[Athalantar]] and the last surviving apprentice of the [[magelord]]s after [[Elminster Aumar]]'s insurrection. 

==History==
The wizard followed Elminster into [[Cormanthyr]] seeking revenge but was killed by [[elf|elves]] in [[241 DR]].
