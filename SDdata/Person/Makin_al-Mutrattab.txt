Chancellor '''Makin al-Mutrattab'''<ref group="note">Makin's last name on page 41 of the Golden Huzuz booklet is shown as "al-Mutttsysn", but this is believed to be a typo. was an inhabitant of [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]], and a member of the royal family.{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Golden Huzuz|24}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Golden Huzuz|43-44}}

==Personallity==
Chancellor Makin possessed a strong sense of right and wrong.

==Activities==
Makin was in charge of the [[Footmen]], the city watch of Huzuz.

==History==
During the adventuring days of Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir, before he became the [[Grand Caliph]], Makin always accompanied his cousin on daring expeditions. The two were quite close from childhood through adulthood.
