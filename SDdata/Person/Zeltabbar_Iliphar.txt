'''Zeltabbar Iliphar''' was a [[human]] [[sage]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Zeltabbar's areas of expertise as a sage included [[:Category:Human languages|human languages]], legends, and folklore; and, to a lesser degree, the [[planes of existence]]. He understood a great number of human languages, as well as [[Dwarvish]] and [[Elvish]].

==Abilities==
Zeltabbar was highly intelligent, but completely [[deaf]]. Nevertheless, he could read lips without error and understood many human languages, as well as [[Dwarvish]] and [[Elvish]]. He was able to speak, but regularly wrote his replies on slate boards, and he had many to hand in his office.

==Residence==
He lived in a [[Zeltabbar Iliphar's residence|row house]] on [[Nephranter's Street]] on the northwest side of the [[Court of the White Bull]], located in the southern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]]. His office lay on the ground floor.{{Cite interactive atlas|Dead Trades Ward}}

==Relationships==
He had two daughters, described as lovely, who helped him manage his library.
