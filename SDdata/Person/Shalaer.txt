Shalaer was once an able half-elf bard who became a spectral harpist and a caretaker for the Harpers in 1368 DR.
Shalaer took care of the House of the Harp in Elventree. She acted as a counselor and information broker for visiting Harpers, loving to sing and chat with them. She always tried to rally the spirits of despairing or depressed Harpers.
