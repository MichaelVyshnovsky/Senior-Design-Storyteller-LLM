'''Lehaya''' was a [[Air genasi|windsoul genasi]] and the Steward of the Sky of the [[Four Stewards]] of [[Akan没l]] in [[1479 DR]].

==Appearance==
Lehaya was a genasi with silver skin and blue ''szuldar'' lines.

==Activities==
As the Steward of the Sky, Lehaya was in charge with administering the laws of Akan没l. 

Lehaya avoided using her influence to punish her political enemies, offering leniency to those that could gave her some political advantage. Since such individuals were usually nobles, Lehaya balanced things by frequently pardoning commoners brought before her for minor crimes minor offenses. Because of her famous "mercy", she was given the nickname "Mother Law."

Lehaya used her fame to hide her extracurricular interest: studying the [[Virushead]],{{Cite dragon/377/Explore Airspur|65}} 

==History==
In 1479 DR, as Queen [[Arathane]] opposed the experimentation with the Virushead, and fearing the other Stewards will not support her, Lehaya sent her [[wizard]]s to study the Virushead in secret. 

When the threat of a war between Akan没l and [[Tymanther]] became inminent later that year, Lehaya was the only one of the Stewards who didn't wanted to battle against the [[dragonborn]]. However, she did not oppose her fellow Stewards when they decided to sent the army to help [[Tchazzar]] against the Tymantherans.
