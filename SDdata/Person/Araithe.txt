Araithe was a prince of Sarifal on the island of Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles in the .
He handled most of the daily governmental functions of running Sarifal.
Araithe was the son of High Lady Ordalf, the ruler of Sarifal. There was a possibility of an arranged marriage between the prince and Princess Tarilyn Kendrick of Callidyrr. Though Araithe showed no interest in a romantic relationship, he understand how such a union would strengthen both Sarifal and Callidyrr against the many dangers they faced.
