'''Pyricus''' was an [[elemental]] prince in service to the [[primordial]] [[Maram|Maram of the Great Spear]]. In the {{YearlinkName|1480}}, he was freed from the [[Anchor of Chaos]] and tried to use subjugated barbarians from [[the Ride]] to fully destroy the anchor and free his master but was defeated by a band of [[adventurer]]s hired by Lord Protector [[Anivar Daoran]] of [[Phlan]].{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|50}}
