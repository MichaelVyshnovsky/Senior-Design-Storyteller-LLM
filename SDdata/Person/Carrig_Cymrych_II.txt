Carrig Cymrych II was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the early-to-mid 5 century DR.
He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty, and was named in honor of Carrig I, son of the dynastys legendary founder, Cymrych Hugh. Carrig II was the first son of his predecessor, High King Lann. His successor was his own first son, Kemble Cymrych.
