'''Carrig Cymrych II''' was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the early-to-mid 5{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]], and was named in honor of [[Carrig Cymrych I|Carrig I]], son of the dynasty's legendary founder, [[Cymrych Hugh]]. Carrig II was the first son of his predecessor, High King [[Lann Cymrych|Lann]]. His successor was his own first son, [[Kemble Cymrych]].

==History==
Carrig II assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|420}}, after the death of his father.

He ruled for eleven years until he died of [[disease|plague]] in the {{YearlinkName|431}}.
