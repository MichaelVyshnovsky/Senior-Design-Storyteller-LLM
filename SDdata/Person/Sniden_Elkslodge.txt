Sniden Elkslodge was an old apprentice inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Though he was fifteen years his elder, Sniden was apprenticed to Derf and worked in Derfs Skin Art learning the trade of decorating a persons body with tattoos and piercings. He gained notoriety for tattooing map on a dwarfs back once.
