Xaemar Datharathi was a member of the Datharathi chaka of Vaelan in Durpar and a plangent.
While an extremely calculating person, he was lampooned in underground plays for his overweening confidence. According to family gossip, he never drank, was celibate, and got on average half a nights sleep per night. He sported a number of crystal prostheses.
Xaemar played a minor role during the meeting that Warian Datharathi attended in , as the nominally presiding member of the meeting.
Xaemar perished after the sealing of Pandorym in Tarsakh 19 of that year, alongside all of the senior members of the Datharathi.
