'''Saril''' was a saint of the [[Church of Ilmater]]. They were the patron and namesake of a priory nestled between [[Calimshan]] and [[Tethyr]].

==History==
A later descendant of Saril, the [[Hamash el Saril|Revered Father Hamash]], would be in charge of [[St. Saril's Priory]].
