'''Kelan''' was a guard in [[Shadowdale]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He had a sharp voice, but could speak softly.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1357}}, Kelan was amongst the guards with whom [[Rold]] palavered in the morning after [[Culthar]] revealed himself to be an agent of the [[Cult of the Dragon]]. He had some concerns.
