Jaylee Clammath was part-owner, cook, and hostess at the Three Elves inn in Voonlar circa the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR.
__NOTOC__
She was tall and quite overweight.
Jaylee was a cheerful person who liked to joke and laugh.
She was an excellent cook.
The Three Elves was a very popular inn throughout the Moonsea region and its owner/operators were highly regarded.
