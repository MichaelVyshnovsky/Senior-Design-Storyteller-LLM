'''Niofu Sangto''' was a hero of [[Koryo]].

Niofu Sangto defeated the [[Kozakura]]n navy in the [[Battle of Wui Eyong]] in the year Koryo 812 and possessed the ''[[sitting dog]]'', a magical chalice of uncertain powers, and was the last known have to used it. Niofu Sangto also owned a fortress that stood on the coast somewhere to the east of the city of [[Tu Pe]]. Both the fortress and the ''dog'' were lost before [[1357 DR]].<ref group="note">The date "Koryo 812" is unknown in familiar calendar systems.
