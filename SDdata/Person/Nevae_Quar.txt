Nevae Quar was a half-elven ranger and a member of the Sisters of the Blood Moon in the early 14 century DR.
Nevae was a gifted archer, and a talented ranger.
As a ranger, Nevae was clad in her leather armor and carried a shortbow.
Nevae Quar was still a young woman circa the , when she met and swiftly befriended two rock gnome sisters Hylei and Kylei Shadowhand. The trio spent some time traveling together until one fateful night when they came across a site of a goblin ambush in a forest outside Silverymoon. Three fellow adventurers Sferia Ranul, Oaglata, and Nyzeil Nighthammer were fighting for their lives, and the trio of friends rushed to help. With all the women dispatching bandits with natural ease, battling in synch, Sferia became enamored with the new allies battle prowess and convinced them to join her troupe - Sisters of the Blood Moon.
In 1310 DR, Sferia Ranul received a letter&mdasha plea for help from the town of Targos in Icewind Dale. Despite the promise of gold and fame, Sferia led her Sisters to the northern city, answering its pleas for help.
