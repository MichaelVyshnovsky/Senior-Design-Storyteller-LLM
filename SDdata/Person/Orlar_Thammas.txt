'''Orlar Thammas''' was an [[Illuskan]] [[wizard]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name="CoSW-p30>

==Activities==
Orlar was a member of the [[Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors]] where he served as Speaker of the Order. He worked out of the [[Tower of the Order]] located on the [[Street of Bells]] in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]].
