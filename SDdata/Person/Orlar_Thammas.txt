Orlar Thammas was an Illuskan wizard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Orlar was a member of the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors where he served as Speaker of the Order. He worked out of the Tower of the Order located on the Street of Bells in the Castle Ward.
