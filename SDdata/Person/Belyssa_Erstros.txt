'''Belyssa Erstros''' was a whaler from [[Tarmalune]] and the captain of the ship ''[[Devastating]]'' in [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Quench the Fire of the Raging God|13}}

==Description==
Belyssa was described as an amazon of a woman.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Quench the Fire of the Raging God|16}}

She was ratter infamous for her attraction to males smaller than her, such as [[dwarves]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Pyrophobia|15}}

==Activities==
Belyssa was a whaler operating out of Tarmalune. In 1479 DR, she obtained a good fortune and became an influential person across the [[Windrise Ports]]. Thanks to her newfound fortune, she became the captain of her own ship, the ''Devastating''.
