Lady '''Kaitlin Tindall Bloodhawk''', First Lady Knight of the Red Standard, was the leader of the adventuring band (and later mercenary company) the Company of the Red Falcon,<ref group="note">This organization is mentioned in the same source separately to the later but similarly named Order of the Red Falcon. It is possible that the latter subsumed, or was named in honor of, the former. and eventually head of the [[Order of the Red Falcon]] and a High Priestess of the Red Knight.<ref>

==History==
During the [[Time of Troubles]], Lady Bloodhawk became the avatar for the Red Knight and led an adventuring band to [[Tethyr]] and routed an invading horde of monsters from the region. After this she and her companions gained much fame and gratitude from the Tethyrian people.
