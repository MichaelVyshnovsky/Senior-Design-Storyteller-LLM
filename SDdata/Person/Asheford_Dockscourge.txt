'''Asheford Dockscourge''' was the master of a hunting lodge in [[Skullport]] in [[1370 DR]].

==Personality==
Asheford was a noble whose devotion to [[Malar]] was surpassed only by his thirst for hunts. He decided who could join the lodge.
