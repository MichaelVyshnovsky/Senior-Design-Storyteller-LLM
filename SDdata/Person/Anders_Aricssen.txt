Anders Aricssen was a Northman barbarian and rogue living in the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast in the late 14 century DR.
In the , along with Jack Ravenwild, Anders broke into House Kuldath in order to steal a set of five perfect rubies purchased by the Brothers Kuldath from jeweler Shorlock Revahl. He also accompanied Jack and the dwarf Tharzon in an attempt to find the tomb of master distiller Cedrizarun by following Zandria and her band of adventurers.
