'''Anders Aricssen''' was a [[Northman]] [[barbarian]] and [[rogue]] living in the city of [[Ravens Bluff]] in [[the Vast]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, along with [[Jack Ravenwild]], Anders broke into [[House Kuldath]] in order to steal a set of five perfect [[ruby|rubies]] purchased by the [[House Kuldath|Brothers Kuldath]] from jeweler [[Shorlock Revahl]]. He also accompanied Jack and the [[dwarf]] [[Tharzon]] in an attempt to find the tomb of master distiller [[Cedrizarun]] by following [[Zandria]] and her band of adventurers.
