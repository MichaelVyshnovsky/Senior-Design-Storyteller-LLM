Curthryn was a brigand working for the Cult of the Dragon in the mid-14 century DR.
Curthryn was working for Thoadrin.
In late , Curthryn was part of a series of ambushes on Orthil Voldovans caravan, seeking to capture Shandril Shessair when he reported to Thoadrin, he recounted in the aftermath that, of their numbers, Jaskel and likely Murbryn had perished in the first ambush.
In a later ambush, there were only two survivors among Thoadrins forces. Curthryn wasnt amongst them.
