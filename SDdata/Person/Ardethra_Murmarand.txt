'''Ardethra Murmarand''' was a [[cleric]] of [[Chauntea]] with the rank of Watchful that resided in the [[Bounty of the Goddess (Voonlar)|Bounty of the Goddess]] temple in [[Voonlar]].

==Abilities==
Watchful Sister Ardethra was a horticulturist who specialized in herbalism for both humans and farm animals.
