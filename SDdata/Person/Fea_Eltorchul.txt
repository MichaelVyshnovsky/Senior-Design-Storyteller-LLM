Fea Eltorchul was a young wizard and noblewoman inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Fea worked within the Eltorchul Academy in Waterdeeps Sea Ward where she helped run the school.
She was a member of the Eltorchul noble family. Her father was Thesp Eltorchul and her older brother was Oth Eltorchul.
