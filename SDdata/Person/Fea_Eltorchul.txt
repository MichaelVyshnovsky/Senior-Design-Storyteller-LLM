'''Fea Eltorchul''' was a young [[wizard]] and [[noble|noblewoman]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Fea worked within the [[Eltorchul Academy]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] where she helped run the school.

==Relationships==
She was a member of the [[Eltorchul]] noble family. Her father was [[Thesp Eltorchul]] and her older brother was [[Oth Eltorchul]].
