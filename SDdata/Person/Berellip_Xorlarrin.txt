Berellip Xorlarrin was a drow priestess of House Xorlarrin in Menzoberranzan in the 15 century DR.
Berellip wore black robes with elaborate designs and spider-shaped charms.
She was brash, loud, and stubborn.
Berellip accompanied the expedition led by Ravel Xorlarrin to Gauntlgrym in the .
But Berellip was killed by Artemis in the , after she imprisoned him and Dahlia in QXorlarrin and allowed them to be tortured.
Berellip was the daughter of Matron Mother Zeerith QXorlarrin.
