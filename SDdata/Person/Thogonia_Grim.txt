'''Thogonia Grim''' was a [[human]] woman and a priestess of [[Umberlee]] in the city of [[Tsurlagol]] in [[the Vast]].

By [[1367 DR]], Thogonia Grim was high priestess of [[Battering Wave]] temple of Umberlee in Tsurlagol, where she was served by six priests of fanatical beliefs. She was still in charge by [[1370 DR]].
