'''Jarathine''' was a [[moon elf]] [[psychometabolicist]] from the hidden city of [[Evereska]] in the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Possessions==
Jarathine wore a powerful ''[[ring of protection]]'' and owned a ''[[decanter of endless water]].''

==History==
Jarathine left her home in Evereska sometime before the mid–14{{th}} century DR to explore the world of [[Toril]]. She was known to use her powerful psychic abilities to help others and join adventuring groups as their healer on quests against forces of evil. Jarathine was willing to tutor others in the [[psionic]] discipline of [[psychometabolism]].
