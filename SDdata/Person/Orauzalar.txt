Orauzalar was a famous wizard and scholar of the 14 century DR, who from the nation of Impiltur . He was considered the foremost authority on Impiluturs long and storied history.
While Red Wizard activities were tolerated throughout the realm by Queen-regent Sambryl and the Lords of Imphras II, they were secretly opposed by Orauzalar. 
He openly criticized the council as they allowed the formation of Thayan enclaves in the cities of Dilpur, Ilmwatch and Lyrabar. He believed that the council acquiesced to the demands of the cities merchants and filled their own personal coffers in the process.
