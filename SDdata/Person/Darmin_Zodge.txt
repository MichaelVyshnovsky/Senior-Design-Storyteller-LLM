Captain Darmin Zodge was an officer of the Flaming Fist who was stationed in the city of Baldurs Gate during the late 15 century DR.
While he presented himself as an earnest, dutiful soldier, Zodge was actually a self-centered and opportunistic individual. Despite his lack of concern for others, he did genuinely care for the welfare of the soldiers under his command.
When Grand Duke Ravengard disappeared along with the city of Elturel, in the , Zodge was tasked with handling the great many refugees that swarmed to Baldurs Gate. He saw the crisis as an opportunity to take charge of the Flaming Fist army.
