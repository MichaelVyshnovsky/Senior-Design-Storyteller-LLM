Bel Morgahl was a captain of the Neverwinter Guard who served her city during the late 15 century DR. She led the guards stationed at one of the citys districts other than Blacklake.
She was rather tall and burly for a dragonborn.
Captain Morgahl was a corrupt member of the City Guard, and had little patience for festidious guards that followed the letter of the law to each exact detail.
She wielded a longsword whenever she engaged in combat.
At some point during or before the , Captain Morgahl began harassing Rubalta, the head of the Bahamutan temple in Neverwinter. She then got into something of a confrontation with a fellow guardsman, a dwarf cleric of Marthammor Duin.
