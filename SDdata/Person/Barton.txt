'''Barton''' was an innkeeper who lived in the [[Ten Towns]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. He was the proprietor of the [[Hooked Knucklehead]] inn within the walled town of [[Bryn Shander]].

==Personality==
Barton respected the privacy of his patrons and did not involve himself in their business.
