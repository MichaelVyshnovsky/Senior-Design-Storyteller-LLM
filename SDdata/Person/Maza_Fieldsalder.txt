'''Maza Fieldsalder''' was a [[blacksmith]] who ran the [[Smithy (Phandalin)|Smithy]] in [[Phandalin]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Maza was a hard-worker, and had both the smithing talents and powerful muscles to prove it.

==Relationships==
She had been trained as an apprentice by master smith [[Alger Frakk]].

Maza was generally well-liked among the folk of Phandalin, although some were wary of her. Those included jealous smiths who were said to covet her job.

==History==
Maza became the sole proprietor of the Phandalin Smithy after the death of her master, Alger Frakk, in the {{YearlinkName|1495}}.{{AI-time}} Soon, rumors began to swirl that she had killed Alger. While the truth remained unclear, these rumors appeared to be predominantly spread by those who already disliked Maza.
