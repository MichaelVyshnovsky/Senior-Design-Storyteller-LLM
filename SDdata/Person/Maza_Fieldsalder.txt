Maza Fieldsalder was a blacksmith who ran the Smithy in Phandalin in the late 15 century DR.
Maza was a hard-worker, and had both the smithing talents and powerful muscles to prove it.
She had been trained as an apprentice by master smith Alger Frakk.
Maza was generally well-liked among the folk of Phandalin, although some were wary of her. Those included jealous smiths who were said to covet her job.
Maza became the sole proprietor of the Phandalin Smithy after the death of her master, Alger Frakk, in the . Soon, rumors began to swirl that she had killed Alger. While the truth remained unclear, these rumors appeared to be predominantly spread by those who already disliked Maza.
