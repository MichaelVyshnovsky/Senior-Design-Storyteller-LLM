Proctiv was a Netherese arcanist, prolific spell inventor, and master of variation.
Proctiv was an extraordinary mage who developed the second most powerful spell in the Empire of Netheril, Proctivs breach crystal sphere (which was tied with Mavins worldweave in terms of power).
