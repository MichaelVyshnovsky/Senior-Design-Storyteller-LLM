'''Dhaerivus''' was a [[human]] [[wizard]] with dark eyes and a thin white face.

==Description==
The mage wore a dark [[tabard]] with glowing mystic signs, and carried a [[staff]] adorned with [[gem]]s and a crystal sphere at one end. He had many rings on his fingers. 

==History==
Dhaerivus was once turned into a [[toad]] at a [[magefair]].
