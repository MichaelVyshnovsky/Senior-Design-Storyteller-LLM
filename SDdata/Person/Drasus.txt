Drasus was an Iron Throne agent that operated in the Sword Coast region of Faerûn during the mid–14 century DR.
Drasus had great confidence in the combat prowess of himself and his allies. He also had a lively sense of humor and felt great enthusiasm for violence.
He wore a suit of chain mail armor and wielded a +1 morning star in battle.
Drasus was the leader of a band that included three other individuals, a fellow warrior by the name of Genthore, and two mages, Kysus and Rezden.
In the , Drasus and his group were tasked by Rieltar Anchev with escorting Davaeorn to the Cloak Wood mine and defending its entrance from intruders.
Some time later they encountered Abdel Adrian and his companions, hoping to defeat the young Bhaalspawn after so many Throne-hired bounty hunters had failed.
