'''Ulbaereg Bloodhand''' was a [[warrior]] who lived during the 5{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
In the {{YearlinkName|472}}, Ulbaereg Bloodhand conquered the various tribes that settled and farmed the land that would one day become the foundation for [[Waterdeep]]. He organized the tribes into the [[Bloodhand tribe]].
