'''Telgerd Belvrost''' was a member of the [[Belvrost]] clan of [[mage]]s.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1370}}, he was one of the best-known and most influential members of the [[Belvrost]] clan in [[Reddansyr]]. Besides being the town wizard, he was a toymaker, the owner of the [[Belled Boot]].
