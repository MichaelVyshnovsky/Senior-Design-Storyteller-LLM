'''Joy''' was an [[aasimar]] dancer in [[Neverwinter]] in the 1370s [[DR]].

==Activities==
She was a dancer who took pride in bringing happiness and light to the people.

==History==
Joy was brought up by priests of [[Lathander]], together with her twin sister, [[Light of Heavens]].

In [[1374 DR]], she was in the Merchant's District in Neverwinter. Joy may have been recruited by the [[Kalach-Cha]] to go to [[Crossroad Keep]] to work for [[Sal]], the tavern-keeper at the Keep.
