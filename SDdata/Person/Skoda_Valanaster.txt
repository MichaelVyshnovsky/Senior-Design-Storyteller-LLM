Skoda Valanaster, nicknamed Bookwyrm, was the First Reader of Candlekeep for a time during the late 15 century DR.
An experienced spellcaster, Skoda had access to a variety of spells up to and including 6 level.
As First Reader, Skoda dedicated their efforts to continually increase the number of tomes and other articles of lore that were counted among Candlekeeps ever-expanding collection.
First Reader Skoda served under Janussi the Keeper of the Tomes, and was aided by the efforts of the eight Great Keepers of the Avowed.
