Saul () was a halfling in Mulmaster in 1491 DR.
The halfling was an inquisitive, talkative, and knowledgeable individual. He liked telling strangers all about Mulmaster.
Saul was a servant at Astrid Sajs house and hoped to enter into the citys Blade elite.
In 1491 DR, he was one of the main servants at Astrid Sajs great party in her villa.
