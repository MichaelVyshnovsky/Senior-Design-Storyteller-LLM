'''Soares''' was a thug in [[Phlan]] in [[1489 DR]].

==Personality==
Soares was a charming and sure-of-himself individual. He indulged in compliments if they gave him an advantage over others. He was afraid to deceive his new masters however.

==History==
Soares was a thug and a mercenary in Phlan until he was recruited by the [[Cult of the Dragon]]. One of his first missions in 1489 DR was to silence an adventuring party hired to recover some stolen books but he was defeated and killed.{{Cite adventurers league/The Scroll Thief|13-14}}
