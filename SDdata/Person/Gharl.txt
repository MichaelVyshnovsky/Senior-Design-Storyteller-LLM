Gharl was a human man and the War Lord of Nimoars Hold (which would later become Waterdeep).
Gharl was a standoffish man, but was determined and loyal to his people, viewing it as his duty to protect the people of Nimoars Hold at any cost.
He was known to possess a javelin of lightning.
Gharl spent much of his life engaged in warfare. He obtained the position in 936 DR when War Lord Nimoar the Reaver died. Previously, Gharl had been Nimoars best general.
In the , Gharl ordered his men to light the fields outside of the Trollwall on fire to halt the advance of trolls. Meanwhile, he used his javelin of lightning to hold a number of trolls at bay.
