'''Morthass Orgolath''', who was also called '''the Cloven''', was a [[tiefling]] [[Nentyarch]] of the [[Orgolath Dynasty]] of [[Narfell]].

==Activities==
Morthass ruled as Nentyarch from {{Yearlink|-475}} to {{Yearlink|-434}}.

==Relationships==
Morthass was the secondborn son of [[Horthaun Orgolath|Horthaun]].
