Akbet-Khrul was a pirate lord of the Pirate Isles.
In 1361 DR, Akbet-Khruls fleet plagued the seas near Amn until the Council of six hired the Golden Legion to defeat the pirates.
Cordell and his men fought Akbet-Khruls pirates in a great battle and were victorious.
Halloran found the body of Akbet-Khrul himself with multiple arrows in him after the battle.
