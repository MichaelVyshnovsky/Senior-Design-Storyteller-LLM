'''Lord Artificer Khotan''' was the Emperor of Lower [[Imaskar]] during the Middle Kingdoms period. During his rule, the ancestors of the [[Mulan]] people were captured and enslaved.{{cite book/The Grand History of the Realms|18}}

==History==
On Khotan's orders as Lord Artificer of Imaskar, artificers created the two [[gate]]s that would be used to abduct the ancestors of the [[Mulan]], searching for a people who had no grasp of [[the Art]] to enslave. A spell was used in order to keep their prayers from reaching their gods.{{cite book/The Grand History of the Realms|26}}
