Grisbane the Great was a renowned nobleman and a knight from Baldurs Gate on the Sword Coast who lived sometime before the late 14 century DR.
During his glory days, Grisbane the Great wore an enchanted belt that, with time, was passed along to his grandson, Sir Griswald. In the , Griswald wore his grandfathers belt to the siege of Castle Exeltis in attempts to rescue his beloved "princess" Soliana.
