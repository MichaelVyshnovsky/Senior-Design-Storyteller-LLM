'''Grisbane the Great''' was a renowned nobleman and a knight from [[Baldur's Gate]] on the [[Sword Coast]] who lived sometime before the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
During his glory days, Grisbane the Great wore an [[enchanted]] [[Griswald's belt|belt]] that, with time, was passed along to his grandson, Sir [[Griswald]]. In the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, Griswald wore his grandfather's belt to the siege of [[Castle Exeltis]] in attempts to rescue his beloved "princess" [[Soliana]].
