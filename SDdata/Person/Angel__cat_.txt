'''Angel''' was a [[cat]] and the pet of the young [[elven]] girl [[Petrine]].

{{Quote|My cat's really an angel though, and she protects me when bad things happen. Or at least she did. I can't find her now though.|Petrine, speaking about Angel.}}

==Description==
While Angel was a male cat, both Petrine always referred to it as "she", presumably believing Angel was a female.

==Abilities==
According to Petrine, Angel had the ability to somehow protect her from harm.

==Possessions==
Angel the cat had a favorite trinket that it carried around, a specific [[angel skin ring]].

==History==
In the {{yearlinkName|1368}}, Angel accompanied Petrine when she ran away from home. The stubborn cat went back however in order to retrieve its favorite ring. Petrine was distraught without her pet and protector.
