'''Kiro''' was an aged [[shukenja]] in [[Kozakura]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Kiro took care of the [[Shrine of Obun the Traveler]]. He was a talkative and cheerful man but suffered some infirmity due to old age.
