'''Jaster Thul''' was a guild spokesman inhabiting [[Waterdeep]], City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Jaster was the spokesman for the [[Guild of Watermen]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
His father [[Zzundar Thul|Zzundar]] was the head of the Guild of Watermen.
