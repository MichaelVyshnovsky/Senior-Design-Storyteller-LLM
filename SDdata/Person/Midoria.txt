'''Midoria''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] who taught at the [[Academy of Mentalist Study]] in [[Eileanar]] shortly before its destruction in {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}.

==Activities==
Midoria was extremely skilled at magical information gathering, and taught it at the Academy under Dean [[Zucchi]].
