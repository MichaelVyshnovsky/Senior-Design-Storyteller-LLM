Sharp-eye '''Eef''' was a [[goblin]] [[cult of the Absolute|cultist]] and devotee [[the Absolute]] who lived in the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Watch y'step down by the pens - worgies been getting fat on your kind lately.|Eef.}}

==Description==
He wore simple clothing accented with a fashionable green neck-wrap and leather-and-bone [[bracer]]s. He also wore a crude, leather skullcap and an amulet bearing the holy symbol of the Absolute.

He was among the goblin cutlists branded with the ''[[mark of the Absolute]]''.

==Possessions==
In battle, Eef wielded a simple wooden [[bow]].
