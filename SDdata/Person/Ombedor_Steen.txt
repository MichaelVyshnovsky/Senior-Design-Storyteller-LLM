'''Ombedor Steen''' was a [[human]] man of the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]], who lived ''circa'' [[1358 DR]].

He was a [[wizard]] and a former adventurer who been elevated to the city's nobility. As an old man, he studied history, researched esoteric details of magical theory, and developed new [[spell]]s.
