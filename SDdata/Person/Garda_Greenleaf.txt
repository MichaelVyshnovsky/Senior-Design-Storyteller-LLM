Garda Greenleaf was a scribe of Mantors Library in Phlan in 1489 DR.
The librarian was a middle-aged man.
The scribe was a senior librarian of Mantors Library with a flawless record of attendance.
Garda was known to dress in plain grey robes while working in the library.
In 1489 DR, Garda was killed in the Archives room of Mantors Library by fellow scribe Ellison Berenger, a secret Cult of the Dragon member, in order of acquire a book about Ren o the Blade.
