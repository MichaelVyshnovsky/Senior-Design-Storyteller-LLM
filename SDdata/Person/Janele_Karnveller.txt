'''Janele Karnveller''' was a rancher and owner of the [[Happy Horse Ranch]] in [[Triboar]] in the [[Dessarin Valley]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
A widow, she was the matriarch of the Karnveller family. She had three grown sons: [[Aldo Karnveller|Aldo]], [[Hingo Karnveller|Hingo]], and [[Rasko Karnveller]].

==Personality==
She was feisty and would rather deal with [[horse]]s than people.
