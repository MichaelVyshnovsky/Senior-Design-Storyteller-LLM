'''Iphimedra''' was a member of the [[Peerless Champions]] of [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]], as of [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|13-14}}

==Description==
Iphimedra was a female [[doppleganger]].

==Relationships==
Iphimedra was the wife of [[Myrelas]].

==Activities==
Iphimedra was a member of the Peerless Champions, a troupe of "bravos" (blademasters) vigilantes that patrolled the city of Sambral.

==History==
In 1479 DR, Iphimedra was kidnapped by the [[Cult of Reborn Flame]] to be a sacrifice to the [[primordial]] [[Achazar]]. Her lover Myrelas joined forces with a band of [[adventurer]]s to rescue her and to stop the cult for good.

In [[1480 DR]], after having defeated the cult, Myrelas and Iphimedra married.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|45}}
