Iphimedra was a member of the Peerless Champions of Sambral, one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond, as of 1479 DR.
Iphimedra was a female doppleganger.
Iphimedra was the wife of Myrelas.
Iphimedra was a member of the Peerless Champions, a troupe of "bravos" (blademasters) vigilantes that patrolled the city of Sambral.
In 1479 DR, Iphimedra was kidnapped by the Cult of Reborn Flame to be a sacrifice to the primordial Achazar. Her lover Myrelas joined forces with a band of adventurers to rescue her and to stop the cult for good.
In 1480 DR, after having defeated the cult, Myrelas and Iphimedra married.
