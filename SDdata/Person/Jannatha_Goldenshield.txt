Jannatha Goldenshield was a wizard in Thentia in  and in . She was deputy of Sureene Aumratha, of the House of the Moon.
As of , Jannatha and Baerimel were petite and impishly pretty.
Together with her sister Baerimel Dunnath, they used to play tricks on people for fun, such as pretending they were waifs and wandering into adventurers camps to see what sort of treatment they got, as of 1367 DR.
She was the sister of Baerimel Dunnath, and the cousin of Sinylla Zoranyian.
Jannatha Goldenshield helped Taegan Nightwind by casting lie-detection spells on the other mages aiding the effort to decrypt Sammasters notes on the Rage of Dragons in 1373 DR. She also helped in the final battle against Phourkyn One-Eyes impersonator.
