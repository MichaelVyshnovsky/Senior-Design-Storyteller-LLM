Garias was a member of the Zhentarim that operated within the organizations hideout hidden beneath Waukeens Rest inn during the late 15 century DR.
He liked colorful, pretty birds and kind of wanted a picture of one.
Garias served as a greeter of sorts for his fellow Zhentarim agents, granting access to those given permission by their leader Zarys.
He was among those present in the hidden Zhentarim outpost when it was set to be destroyed in the . The group was going to cave-in the caverns in which they operated, following the abduction of Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard from Waukeens Rest right above them.
