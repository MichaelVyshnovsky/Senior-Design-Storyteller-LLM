'''Merlin Fastfoot''' was a [[halfling]] [[rogue]] in [[Cormyr]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}
{{quote
|T'was Friday morn when we set sail,<br/>
and we were not far from the land,<br/>

with a comb and a glass in her hand.
|Merlin, singing ''[[The Mermaid]]''}}
==Description==
Merlin had sandy blond hair and green eyes.

==History==
Merlin was encamped at the ruins of [[Beckside Castle]] on the [[High Road (Cormyr)|High Road]] around the time of the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar]] and [[Shadovar invasion of Wheloon]]. He passed the time with the [[bard]] [[Lunasa Eveningwood]], singing songs together including ''[[The Mermaid]]'', ''[[The Crafty Maid's Policy]]'', and ''[[Here's a Health to the Company]]''.
