Looxil was a conjurer who once served Umberlee, the Sea Queen, years before the 14 century DR.
Looxil committed a grave mistake of betraying Umberlee. Years later, the conjurer was assassinated by Dami Darn, a rogue to exacted the Sea Queens revenge. As the reward for Looxils slaying, Dami was gifted an enchanted set of leather armor called the Armor of Deep Night.
