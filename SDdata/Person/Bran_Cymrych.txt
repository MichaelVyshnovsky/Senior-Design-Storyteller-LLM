'''Bran Cymrych''' was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the mid-to-late 4{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]], and was the second son of his predecessor, High King [[Cameron Cymrych|Cameron the Wise]]. His successor was his first son, [[Conall Cymrych]].

==History==
Bran assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|370}}, after the death of his father.{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|26}}

Bran ruled for eight years before dying of [[disease|illness]] in the {{YearlinkName|378}}.
