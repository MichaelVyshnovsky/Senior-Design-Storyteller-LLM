'''Destus Gurn''' was a member of the [[Baldur's Gate]] branch of the [[Iron Throne]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He served as the assistant Chief Accountant for all operations in the [[Sword Coast]].

==History==
During the [[iron crisis]] of [[1368 DR]], Destus was set to intercept [[Tar (emissary)|Emissary Tar]] during her visit to the Iron Throne's [[Iron Throne headquarters|headquarters]] in Baldur's Gate.
