'''Najeeya bint Siddiq''' was a skilled [[elemental mage|flame mage]], and the first wife of [[Jamal al-Yindannim]] for 20 years, who inhabited [[Krak al-Niraan]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Najeeya was an exotic beauty.

==Activities==
Though she spent much of her time in drug-induced stupors thanks to her addiction to ''[[dreambliss]]'', Najeeya was a dangerous flame mage when active. She helped educate new recruits of the [[Brotherhood of the True Flame]] who were accepted into the keep.

==Possessions==
Najeeya wore a ''[[sash of fire resistance]]''. She also carried a ''[[potion of fire breath]]'' and several scrolls of the ''[[sunfire]]'' and ''[[fire track]]'' spells.
