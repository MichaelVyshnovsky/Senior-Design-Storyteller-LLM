'''Salitre''' was a [[trap]]-making [[halfling]] imprisoned in [[Wheloon Prison]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}
{{Quote
|Watch your step. Hah!<br/>No! The traps, no one gets by the traps!
|Salitre, on encountering Cormyrean forces
}}
==Description==
Salitre had short brown hair and pale skin. He wore a suit of [[leather armor]], and habitually used a [[crossbow]] and traps.

==Personality==
He was rather paranoid and obsessed with his traps.

==History==
{{Quote
|… Nobody gets by my traps …
|Salitre's last words
}}
Salitre was condemned to Wheloon Prison for regular criminal activities, not [[Sharran]] worship. He lurked in the prison and built his traps, and resisted both the [[Shadovar invasion of Wheloon]] and the [[Cormyr]]ean forces that fought for the prison.
