==History==
Torsin deLancie led the Waterdhavian troops within the [[Coalition]] during its [[War of the Shining Crusade|war]] with [[Caelar Argent]]'s [[Shining Crusade]] in the {{YearlinkName|1368}}. During the war, deLancie sought a number of ways to give an edge to Coalition forces, including poisoning the crusade's water supply and planting explosives beneath [[Dragonspear Castle]].
