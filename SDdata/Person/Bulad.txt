'''Bulad''', also called '''Bulad the Steelmaker''', was a renowned weaponsmith inhabiting in [[Hiyal]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Bulad was considered to be one of the finest weaponsmiths throughout all of [[Zakhara]]. He specialized in smithing [[longsword]]s, [[broadsword]]s, [[cutlass]]es, and of course [[scimitar]]s.

Bulad was found dead with a [[dagger]] buried in his neck, leaving his daughter to continue the family heritage. Though the magistrate declared his death accidental, his daughter was convinced someone connected with powerful friends was to blame.

==Relationships==
His daughter, [[Fuladayah bint Bulad]], followed in his footsteps.
