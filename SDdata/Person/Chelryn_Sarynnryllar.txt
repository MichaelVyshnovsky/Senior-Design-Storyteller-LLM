Chelryn Sarynnryllar was a sun elf and the mother of the wizard Ashemmi.
Chelryn had two children with the moon elf Rhyem Maraphiir Ashemmi (born Shemithil Maraphiir) and Malphys Maraphiir.
Soon after Ashemmis birth in Silverymoon, the family moved to live with the wood elves in the Neverwinter Wood, and were taken in by the Ilbaereth house.
