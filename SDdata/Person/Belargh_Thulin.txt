'''Belargh Thulin''' was a male [[wizard]] who was one of the [[magelord]]s of [[Athalantar]] and was killed by [[Elminster Aumar]] early in his insurrection.

==Personality==
He was a minor magelord, known for devoting his time to romancing all women who came within range of his eye (and his magical compulsions) in rural [[Ambletrees]].

==History==
He was killed by Elminster in [[234 DR]] when he, in the feminine form of Elmara, escaped from Athalantar.
