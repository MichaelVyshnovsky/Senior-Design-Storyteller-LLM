Mikki Kori was a Dark Breaker of Umberlee, a member of the Bitch Queens clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queens House in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Mikki Kori was a human woman with unkempt strawberry blonde hair and a tan skin tone. She had watery blue eyes and sharp facial features.
Koris Dark Breaker uniform was a set of skin-tight heavy chainmail dress. The armor was decorated with golden trim and had decorated metallic pauldrons. Her weapon of choice was a polearm.
Circa the , during the Absolute crisis, Dark Breaker Mikki Kori was among the churchs members who performed the faiths ceremonies and sang hymns in praise of the Bitch Queen. She was alongside Flood Tide Allandra Grey and other priestesses during Holli Dylfords last rites.
