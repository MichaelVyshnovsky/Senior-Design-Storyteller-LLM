'''Mikki Kori''' was a Dark Breaker of [[Umberlee]], a member of the Bitch Queen's [[Church of Umberlee|clergy]] under Flood Tide [[Allandra Grey]] of the [[Water Queen's House]] in [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Oh, Umberlee, hear us! Umberlee, bring your daughter home! May we all breathe deep of the Wavemother's waters!''|Dark Breaker Avgarioletta}}

==Description==
Mikki Kori was a [[human]] woman with unkempt strawberry blonde hair and a tan skin tone. She had watery blue eyes and sharp facial features.

==Posessions==
Kori's Dark Breaker uniform was a set of skin-tight heavy [[chainmail]] dress. The armor was decorated with golden trim and had decorated metallic pauldrons. Her weapon of choice was a [[polearm]].

==History==
''Circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the [[Absolute crisis]], Dark Breaker Mikki Kori was among the church's members who performed the faith's ceremonies and sang hymns in praise of the Bitch Queen. She was alongside Flood Tide Allandra Grey and other priestesses during [[Holli Dylford]]'s last rites.
