'''Ortolar''' was the 37th [[Obarskyr]] monarch and a king of [[Cormyr]]. He took the throne after murdering his nephew [[Sargrannon Obarskyr|King Sargrannon]], who was only a youth. His reign, however, was short. Within a year, he was disposed of by [[Gorauna]].
