'''Aria Whucknolls''' was a [[human]] follower of [[Oghma]] and a librarian serving at the [[Font of Knowledge]] temple in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], working alongside her sister [[Cera Whucknolls|Cera]]. She was fluent in many [[demihuman]] [[language]]s.
