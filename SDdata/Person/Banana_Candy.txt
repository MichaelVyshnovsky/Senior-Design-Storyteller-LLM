'''Banana Candy''' was a well-trained [[hadrosaurus]] who competed in the [[dinosaur races]] of [[Port Nyanzaru]] in [[Chult]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
As of the start of the [[death curse]],{{ToA-time}} Banana Candy was a favorite at the races, with bookies giving it 3:1 odds of winning.{{Cite adventurers league/A Day at the Races|20}} and may have competed in the first leg of that event in which racers drove hadrosaur-pulled [[chariot]]s.{{Cite adventurers league/A Day at the Races|7}}
