'''Gwynneth''', known as '''the First Mother''', was a leader of the first [[Talfir]] people to settle in the [[Moonshae Isles]]. The [[Gwynneth|southern island]] on which they made their home was named in her honor.{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes}}

==Relationships==
She was the second daughter of the Archduke of [[Ebenfar]], [[Markus Randol]].

Her consort was [[Taylor]], and with him she had a son, [[Deric]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|140}}, she led Talfiric refugees fleeing  persecution by the [[Shadowking]] of Ebenfar. They travelled across the [[Sea of Swords]] to the Moonshae Isles, where they established the first [[human]] settlement there. She died the [[141 DR|next year]] of [[consumption]].
