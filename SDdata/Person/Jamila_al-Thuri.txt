'''Jamila al-Thuri''' was the [[dwarf|dwarven]] matriarch of the [[House of Al-Thuria]] in  [[Huzuz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Though elderly, Jamila ruled her family with iron resolve. She spent most of her time within the family estates in the [[Clothiers' District]].
