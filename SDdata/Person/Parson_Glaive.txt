'''Parson Glaive''' was a [[dwarf]] priest of [[Moradin]] of [[Citadel Felbarr]] in the mid-to-late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Parson was appointed as Steward-in-Waiting of Citadel Felbarr in the {{YearlinkName|1463}}.

He was a decisive person during the [[War of the Silver Marches]]. In the {{YearlinkName|1484}}, Parson confirmed to King [[Emerus Warcrown]] that young [[Reginald Roundshield]] was in fact the legendary [[Bruenor Battlehammer]] reborn.

He was made King Parson Glaive of Citadel Felbarr in the {{YearlinkName|1486}} when King Emerus Warcrown abdicated his throne and title to join Bruenor Battlehammer on the march to reclaim [[Gauntlgrym]].
