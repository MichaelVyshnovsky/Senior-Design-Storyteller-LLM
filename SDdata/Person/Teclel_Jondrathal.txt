Teclel Jondrathal was a skilled tailor for hire from the city of Neverwinter, active in the late 15 century DR.
By the late 15 century DR, Teclel was known as an extraordinarily indulgent man who created tailored clothes of the same luxurious quality. Despite his skill, Teclels love for lavish lifestyle prohibited him from opening his own business. Instead of running a shop, Teclel offered his services to protectors and adventurers from Neverwinter.
