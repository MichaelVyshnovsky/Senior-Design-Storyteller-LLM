'''Naeryndroth''' was an [[Dragon aging|adult]] [[black dragon]] and one of the "overdragons" of [[Melabrauth]] in [[Laerakond]] as of [[1479 DR]].

==Personality==
Naeryndroth was cunning and able to deceive many foes with his acting skills.

==Equipment== 
Naeryndroth had a cache of [[magic item]]s that granted him many [[illusion]] and [[enchantment]] [[spell]]s.
