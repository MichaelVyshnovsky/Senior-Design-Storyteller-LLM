'''Trebbe''' was an [[archwizard]] and one of the most prolific spell creators in [[Netheril]].

==Abilities==
He was a specialist in magic that protected arcanists, enabling spell duels to last much longer.

==History==
Trebbe was born in {{Yearlink|-2292}}. He established [[Aquessir|Shadowtop Borough]], a Netherese enclave for magical research in {{Yearlink|-2267}}. It was here he created the ''[[Crown of Horns]]'', an evil artifact. 

===Death===
In {{Yearlink|-2237}}, a spellcasting accident triggered while Trebbe was working on the crown led to his death.
