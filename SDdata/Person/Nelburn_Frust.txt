Nelburn Frust was an inhabitant of the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Nelburn was a dark-skinned gold dwarf man with a clean-shaved face, black hair, and blue eyes.
In the , Nelburn Frust procured a spell or an incantation of dubious authenticity, presumably from the nearby Devils Fee magic shop. Regardless of the spell and the promises of luck it could deliver, Nelburn struggled greatly with recitation.
