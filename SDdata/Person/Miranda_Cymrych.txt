'''Miranda "the Fair" Cymrych''' was High Queen of the [[Moonshae Isles]] at the turn of the 7{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
She was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] and the daughter of her predecessor, High King [[Gordon Cymrych|Gordon]]. Her successor was her first son, [[Embro Cymrych I]].

==History==
Miranda assumed the throne of the High Queen in the {{YearlinkName|569}}, after the death of her father.

She ruled for forty years before dying of natural causes in the {{YearlinkName|609}}.
