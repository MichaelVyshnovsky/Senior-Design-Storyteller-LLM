Lady Cyrtue was a festhall owner inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Lady Cyrtue owned the Hanging Lantern located in Waterdeeps Dock Ward in a small neighborhood bordered by Ship Street to the west, Pressbow Lane to the east, and Oar Alley to the south.
In reality, Lady Cyrtue was one of many identities for a wicked doppleganger named Hlaavin.
