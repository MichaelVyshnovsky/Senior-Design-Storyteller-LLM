Lady '''Cyrtue''' was a [[festhall]] owner inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Lady Cyrtue owned the [[Hanging Lantern]] located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] in a small neighborhood bordered by [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]] to the west, [[Pressbow Lane]] to the east, and [[Oar Alley]] to the south.{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5310}}

==Rumors & Legends==
In reality, Lady Cyrtue was one of many identities for a wicked [[doppleganger]] named [[Hlaavin]].
