'''Halemar''' was a weaponsmith and armorer, and proprietor of [[Halemar's Fine Blades]] in the city of [[Tantras]] in [[the Vast]].

During the [[Time of Troubles]] of [[1358 DR]], when Tantras expected war with the forces of [[Zhentil Keep]], Halemar was kept busy crafting war materiel for the [[Guard (Tantras)|city's army]].
