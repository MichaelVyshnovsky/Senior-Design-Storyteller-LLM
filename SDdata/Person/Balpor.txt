'''Balpor''' was a [[Shadovar]] soldier and scout in a reconnaissance unit that served Prince [[Escanor Tanthul]] of [[Thultanthar]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Balpor's unit led was led by the young Shadovar officer [[Parth Gal]]. He served alongside [[Carlig (Shadovar)|Carlig]], [[Elar]], and eight others.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, Balpor and Parth Gal's other scouts ventured into the [[Irithlium]] of [[Myth Drannor]] to gain information about [[phaerimm]] activity within the ruins. Balpor was among the first of the Shadovar to die. The only remains left after his death was his head and a single arm.
