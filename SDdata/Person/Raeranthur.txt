'''Raeranthur''' was an [[elven]] mage prior to the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==Legacy==
A cloak made by him was left in the [[Vault of the Ages]]. Per the [[Srinshee]], the cloak had the power to banish blights from trees.
