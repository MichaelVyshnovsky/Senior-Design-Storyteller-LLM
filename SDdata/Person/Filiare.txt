Filiare (Pronounce|Fill|ee|AIR|refs=
The middle-aged human was quite jovial and Filiare had greying dark hair and a mustache.
Using the inn as a base of his operations, Filiare sold used weapons and found work for down-on-their-luck mercenaries. It was through this latter business that Filiare became a rival of Blazidon One-Eye.
