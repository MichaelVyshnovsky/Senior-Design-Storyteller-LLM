'''Nilsa Dendrar''' was the oldest child of the Dendrar family of [[Phandalin]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Her father was a woodcarver, called [[Thel Dendrar|Thel]], and her mother was called [[Mirna Dendrar|Mirna]]. She had a younger brother, [[Nars Dendrar|Nars]].

==History==
In [[1491 DR]],{{Phandelver-time}} the [[Redbrands]] caused much trouble in her home village, and slew her father, Thel. The remaining members of her family were kidnapped, and taken to [[Tresendar Manor]], where they were to be sold as slaves.
