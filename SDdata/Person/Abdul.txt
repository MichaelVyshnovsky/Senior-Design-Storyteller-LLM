'''Abdul''' was a [[daolani]] inhabiting the deserts near the [[Free Cities of Zakhara|Free City]] of [[Muluk]] in {{Yearlink|1367}}.{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
He stood {{SI|1|ft|long=yes}} tall and was nearly as wide. His skin was tanned and he had stringy dark hair. Abdul usually only wore a small loincloth so he could display the folds of his ample belly.

==Personality==
Abdul was cruel, dumb, and crude. However, he was fanatically loyal to his master.

==Relationships==
Abdul served as the [[familiar]] of the [[sha'ir]] [[Shafazal]].
