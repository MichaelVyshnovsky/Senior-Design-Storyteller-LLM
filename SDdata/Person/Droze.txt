'''Droze''' was a fisherman and later the innkeeper of [[The Mermaid's Purse]] in [[Corth]], [[Aglarond]], in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Droze was forced to change his occupation from a fisher to an innkeeper after suffering an accidental injury. He was known to often entertain guests at The Mermaid's Purse inn, telling the story of rescuing a [[mermaid]] and being rewarded with three wishes from her king.
