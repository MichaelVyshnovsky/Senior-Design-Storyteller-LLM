Dalida al-Jazarijja was a member of the House of Hanif tribe within the Haunted Lands in 1367 DR.
Dalida was kidnapped by Jamal al-Yindannim because of her great beauty. She was taken to the fortress of Krak al-Niraan and held captive and subjected to Jamals lustful advances.
Dalidas father, Adil al-Jazarijja, was Sheikh of their tribe. Her sister Aisha was also kidnapped.
