'''Dalida al-Jazarijja''' was a member of the [[House of Hanif]] tribe within the [[Haunted Lands]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Dalida was kidnapped by [[Jamal al-Yindannim]] because of her great beauty. She was taken to the fortress of [[Krak al-Niraan]] and held captive and subjected to Jamal's lustful advances.

==Relationships==
Dalida's father, [[Adil al-Jazarijja]], was Sheikh of their tribe. Her sister [[Aisha al-Jazarijja|Aisha]] was also kidnapped.
