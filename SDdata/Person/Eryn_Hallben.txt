'''Eryn Hallben''' was a [[human]] inhabitant of [[Eveningstar]] during the mid-1480s [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}

==Description==
She had pale skin, and long blonde hair. She wore a white and dark gray dress with dark brown leggings.

==History==
{{Quote
|We use the bonfires to keep the Drow away. They hate the light.
|Eryn
}}
During [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar]], Eryn contributed to the defenses by building and maintaining the large bonfires around the village.
