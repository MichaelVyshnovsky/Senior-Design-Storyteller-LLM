'''Alfwin''' was a [[Shadovar]] [[sorcerer]] serving under the [[Netherese]] emissary [[Herzgo Alegni]] in the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
He was part of Herzgo's retinue when, in the {{YearlinkName|1463}}, the Shadovar ambushed [[Dahlia Sin'felle]] and [[Drizzt Do'Urden]] in [[Gauntlgrym]].
