Wythyndle was a blind half-elf bard of the 10 century DR known for receiving a holy book from Milil.
Wythyndle received the "Book that Sings" from the Lord of All Songs while singing his praises, alone, on an unnamed mountaintop in the eastern Cloven Mountains.
After receiving the book, Wythindle was carried by Milil down to the House of Alastrin to bring news of this holy gift. He lived there until his death in the winter of the , at which point the book magically disappeared from the temple.
