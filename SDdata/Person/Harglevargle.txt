Harglevargle was the ettin chieftain of the Clotskull orc tribe in the 15 century DR.
Once Harglevargle was the bodyguard of the orc chieftain Prook, who had lead his tribe to occupy some levels of the subterranean Illefarn. However, around 1484 DR, Prook died during a dungeon collapse. Thank to the lessons learned from Prook, Harglevargle managed to become chieftain,
Like all ettins, Harglevargle had two personalities, Hargle and Vargle. Hargle was more serious and narrow-minded, while Vargle was curious and imaginative. However, they both enjoyed smashing stuff, counting coins, and busting heads together.
