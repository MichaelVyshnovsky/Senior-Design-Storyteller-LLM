Korial Quintara was a captain in Maztica.
Quintara was a quiet old man.
In battle, Korial wielded a longsword.
Quintara was the captain of the Helms Choice, a small cog sailing from Helmsport to other places in Maztica. In 1362 DR, he transported the adventuring party hired by Drakosa to find the Cloak of One Plume, Ironspike, and Hasoctl.
