'''Korial Quintara''' was a captain in [[Maztica]].

==Personality==
Quintara was a quiet old man.

==Possessions==
In battle, Korial wielded a [[longsword]].

==History==
Quintara was the captain of the ''[[Helm's Choice]]'', a small [[cog]] sailing from [[Helmsport]] to other places in Maztica. In [[1362 DR]], he transported the adventuring party hired by [[Drakosa]] to find the [[Cloak of One Plume]], [[Ironspike]], and [[Hasoctl]].
