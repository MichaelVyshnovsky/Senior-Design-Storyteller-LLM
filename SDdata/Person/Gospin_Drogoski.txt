'''Gospin Drogoski''' was a [[half-elven]] farmer who lived in [[Almorel]] in [[1367 DR]].

==Activities==
Gospin worked on his farm which was located just northwest of Almorel along a thin piece of land that jutted into the [[Lake of Mists]].
