Ryndarth Soleilon II, who was also called Ryndarth the Younger and the Masque King, was the fifth and final King of the Soleilon Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate.
Ryndarth IIs reign began with the death of his father, King Ryndarth I, in . However, unbeknownst to the public at the time, a doppelganger killed Ryndarth II shortly before the death of his father and masqueraded as the prince. The doppleganger ruled Westgate as its king for nearly a year until it was ousted by the mulan Ashtukzu I. 
Ryndarth II died before siring any heirs. The Soleilon line of Kings died with him.
