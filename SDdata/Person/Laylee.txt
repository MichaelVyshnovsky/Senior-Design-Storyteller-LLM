Laylee was a kobold from the Dragons Rest cloister in the late 15 century DR.
Laylee served as a helper for Myla, a kobold tinkerer.
Laylee had an inquisitive nature and a flair for tools and building.
Laylee once handled Mylas alchemical fire and caused an incident in the cloister kitchen. Myla prohibited her from using it again.
Circa the , Laylee shared with three other kobolds a small monastic cell in the Dragons Rest cloister cut into the cliff face. Each of the four slept on individual hammocks. Just like all the other kobolds, she worked at night because of their sunlight sensitivity.
