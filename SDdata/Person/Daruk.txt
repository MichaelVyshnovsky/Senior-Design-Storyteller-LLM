Daruk was a human bard of the late 15 century DR and a friend of the Ikemmu merchant Tatigan Carrlock. He was said to be beloved by Shar.
In the , Daruk traveled with a caravan from Ikemmu to Rashemen. Along the way, Daruk helped defeat bandit and winter wolf attacks.
Daruk had a special connection to Shar and was able to channel some of her power. He was able to infuse some of Shars power into the shadar-kai Ashok to help him defeat a winter wolf attack.
Daruk had a cocky demeanor and often annoyed those around him.
