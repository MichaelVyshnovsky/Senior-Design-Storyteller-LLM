Quelindritar, also known as Wardancer,Though referred to as a male in The Rite, Wardancer is listed as a female in the roll call and in The Ruin. The latter sources are considered to take precedence. was a bronze dragon who had lived in the Moonsea her entire life, and one of Karasendrieths rogues.
On Kythorn 6 of , she was one of the proponents of the theory that it was the Zhentarim in Thentia that summoned the chasme that slew Sinylla Zoranyian while disrupting the efforts of the mages of Thentia to decrypt Sammasters notes, and she suggested acting immediately, irrespective of any confirmation. She eventually relented at the request of Sinyllas kin.
During the final battle against Sammaster on Nightal 7 of that same year, Quelindritar was one the Talons that were sent to attack Sammasters citadel. She was one of the few Talons who survived the battle.
