'''Badiyah al-Sa'id al-Sahim''' was an [[elf]] and a skilled [[flame mage]] living in the [[Free Cities of Zakhara|Free City]] of [[Qadib]] in [[north Zakhara]] in {{Yearlink|1367}}.{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
In addition to running the [[University of Flame Mages]], Badiyah was also a member of the Sultan's court.

==Rumors & Legends==
Badiyah was rumored to be a secret member of the [[Brotherhood of the True Flame]].
