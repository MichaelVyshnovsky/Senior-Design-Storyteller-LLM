'''Nellaura Orbrynsar''' was a renegade [[Netherese]] sorceress.

==Legacy==
Her crumbling bones were in a tomb within the [[Orsraun Mountains]] as of {{yearlink|1370}}. [[Azuth]] directed [[Talatha Vaerovree]] there, where she recovered a [[girdle of fire]].
