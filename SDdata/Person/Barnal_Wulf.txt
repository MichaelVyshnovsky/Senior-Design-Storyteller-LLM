'''Barnal Wulf''' was a [[human]] resident of [[Eveningstar]] in [[Cormyr]] during the mid-1480s [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}

==Description==
Barnal had shoulder-length light brown hair, a noticeable scar on his face, and wore a long gray and brown robe.

==History==
While living in Eveningstar during the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar|drow attacks]], Barnal noted that the animals of the [[King's Forest]] were greatly disturbed and it had become incredibly dangerous to go into the once-safe forest.
