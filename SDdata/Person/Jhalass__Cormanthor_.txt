'''Jhalass''' was an [[elven]] inhabitant of [[Cormanthyr]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==History==
She was a maid to the [[Evendusk]] family of [[Cormanthyr]]. According to the matriarch of the house, [[Duilya Evendusk]], she was much younger and prettier than herself.
