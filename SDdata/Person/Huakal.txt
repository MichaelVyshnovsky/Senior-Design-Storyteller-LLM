'''Huakal''' was a former [[Eagle Knights (Maztica)|Eagle Knight]] at [[Kultaka]].

==History==
Huakal was a valorous Eagle Knight until he retired because of old age. Around [[1351 DR]], he bought the young slave [[Erixitl]].

In [[1361 DR]], after an incident between Erixitl and his son, he decided to sell her to [[Kachin]], a priest of Qotal from [[Payit]].
