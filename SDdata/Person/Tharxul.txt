'''Tharxul''' was a [[beholder]] attendant of [[Fzoul Chembryl]] in the 1370s [[DR]].

In [[1374 DR]], during the [[Cormanthor War]], he introduced the castellan [[Scyllua Darkhope]] to Fzoul's private room.
