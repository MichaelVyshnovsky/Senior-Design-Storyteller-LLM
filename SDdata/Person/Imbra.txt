'''Imbra''' was a member of the [[Twisted Rune]] around late [[1487 DR]].

==History==
Imbra was a wizard, spy, and thief in service to Runemaster [[Calathlarra]]. She reported to her master about the fighting in the [[Dragon Rampant]] club in [[Suzail]].
