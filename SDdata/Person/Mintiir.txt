Mintiir was a famed jeweler and member of a cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame that operated out of Imdolphyn in 1480 DR.
Mintiir was the husband of the popular genasi singer Tiilra Nonintarr.
In 1480 DR, Mintiir was a member of a cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame operating out of Imdolphyn. He allowed his wife to be taken by members of the cult, to be used as a sacrifice to awaken the primordial Achazar. Mintiir was eventually killed by a group of adventurers and other powerful individuals from the Windrise Ports that wanted to destroy the cult for good.
