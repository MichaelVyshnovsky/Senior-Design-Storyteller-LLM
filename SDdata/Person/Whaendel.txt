'''Whaendel''' was a butcher in the mid–8{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Whaendel brought a bag of money to purchase [[South Spire]] and the other landholdings of [[Thessamel Arunder]] about two [[tenday]]s into the Chaos of [[the Art]] in the {{yearlink|767}}, together with [[Hulder Phelbellow]] and others.
