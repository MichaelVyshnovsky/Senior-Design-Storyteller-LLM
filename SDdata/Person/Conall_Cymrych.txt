'''Conall Cymrych''' was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the late 4{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]], and was the first son of his predecessor, High King [[Bran Cymrych|Bran]]. His successor was his own first son, [[Lann Cymrych]].

==History==
Conall assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|378}}, after the death of his father.

He ruled for eleven years before dying in a [[crossbow]] accident in the {{YearlinkName|389}}.
