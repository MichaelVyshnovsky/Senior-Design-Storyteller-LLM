As butler and personal valet of Lord Victoro Cassalanter, Willifort was usually at the side of his master. He always want knew all that happened inside the mansion and survey all the guests in order to hide connection with Asmodeus cult.

In 1492 DR, the Cassalanter family may have managed to acquire the Stone of Golorr. If so, Willifort, supported by some devils, tried to stop some adventurers that wanted retrieve the stone from the Cassalanter mausoleum inside the City of the Dead. He later went back with the stone to the Cassalanter villa.
