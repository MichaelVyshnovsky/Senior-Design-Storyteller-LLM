'''Ulburto''' was a man who lived in a small village on an isle near [[Phlan]] in the [[Moonsea]] region in [[1489 DR]].

==Activities==
He ran the village's smokehouse. He knew the village's dark secret.

==Personality==
Ulburto was a violent and dangerous man, waiting for time when he could use violence. He was suspicious of all strangers but liked the stories of the village's crones.{{Cite adventurers league/Shadows over the Moonsea|19}}
