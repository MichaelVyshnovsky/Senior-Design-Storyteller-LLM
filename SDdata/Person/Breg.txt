Sharp-eye Breg was a goblin scout and member of the Cult of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.
He had blotchy yellow-green skin and light brown hair he kept brushed over to one side. He dressed in simple cloth clothes, leather gauntlets, and pieces of salvaged metal armor.
Breg was intimated by Crusher, a mighty goblin warrior and novice of the Absolute.
In the , Breg was among the goblin cultists celebrating the successful raid of the Waukeens Rest inn at the Shattered Sanctum.
