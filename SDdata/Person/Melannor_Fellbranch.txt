Melannor Fellbranch () was a groundskeeper and member of the Emerald Enclave in Waterdeep in 1492 DR.
Melannor was the groundskeeper of Phaulkonmere, a compound located one block south of Kolat Towers that was owned by the Tarm and Phaulkon noble families. He also directed the Emerald Enclave agents in the city with Jeryth Phaulkon, a Chosen of Mielikki.
Melannor obtained information from magical conversations with the animals of the city.
Melannor was friendly but humorless. He liked pigeons and cats and always used them as couriers for his faction.
Melannor was brother to Talisolvanar Fellbranch.
