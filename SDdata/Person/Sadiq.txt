'''Sadiq''', also called '''Sadiq the Smokey''' was an [[Efreet pashas|efreeti pasha]] who lived in [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Sadiq served in Zakhara as a representative of the Sultan of the Efreet, [[Marrake al-Sidan al-Hariq ben Lazan]]. He controlled his own stronghold, complete with a staff of other efreet and noble efreet as well as a contingent of slaves of various races.
