==Description==
Zahara was a nimble and quick-witted woman.

==History==
In the mid-14{{th}} century DR, Zahara the Shark's ships made a living by protecting merchant ships traveling across the Sword Coast and raiding pirate ships as well as an occasional port in [[Tethyr]], always keeping chaos and destruction to the minimum.
