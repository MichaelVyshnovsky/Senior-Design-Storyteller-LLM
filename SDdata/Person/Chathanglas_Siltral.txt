Lord '''Chathanglas Siltral''' was an elven lord, dead by the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==History==
He styled himself the Lord of the Rivers and Bays; his territory was later annexed by [[Cormyr]].

==Legacy==
His pectoral was kept in the [[Vault of Ages]] as of {{yearlink|241}}.
