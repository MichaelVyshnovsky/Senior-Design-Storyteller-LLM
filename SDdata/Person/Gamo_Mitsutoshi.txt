'''Gamo Mitsutoshi''' was a retainer of [[Funada clan]] in [[Nakamaru]] around [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Mitsutoshi regularly spitting in public. He had traveled a lot and known things from far places.

==History==
Mitsutoshi and his brothers may be involved in a plot of [[Funada Toyoo]] that can end with their death at hands of [[Mashida Kowa]] and [[Oka Sanai]]

==Personality==
Mitsutoshi was a weapon fetichist. He was rude and arrogant, little loved by people he met.
