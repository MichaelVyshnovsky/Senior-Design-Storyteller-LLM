'''Rehorustep I''' was the thirteenth [[pharaoh]] of [[Mulhorand]].

==History==
Rehorustep I was born in [[1066 DR]]. He ascended to the throne of pharaoh of Mulhorand in [[1098 DR]] and his reign lasted until [[1248 DR]] when he was killed by [[sahuagin]] during a sea voyage.
