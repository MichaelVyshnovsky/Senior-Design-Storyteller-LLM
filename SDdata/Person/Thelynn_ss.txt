Thelynnss was a drow captain, who acted under the orders of the Bhaalspawn, Sendai.
The drow captain fought with enchanted scimitars and wore an amulet of cheetah speed, as well carrying a healing potion.
Thelynnss was appointed by Sendai to guard her compound. She was found in the barracks of Sendais enclave, and commanded several drow fighters. The captain may have been killed by Gorions Wards party, upon intruding into the barracks. She was also in possession of the drow guards key, which opened many areas of the complex.
