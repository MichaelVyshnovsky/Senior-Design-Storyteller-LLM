Setara bint Farid was the human female captain of the ship Spray, operating in the Crowded Sea around 1367 DR.
Setara was a muscular, brown-haired woman, who wore padded armor.
She was more interested in gaining fame, rather than fortune. Setara tended to get into more trouble than she could handle.
Setara owned a ring of swimming, as well as a magical cutlass.
Setara hailed from the city of Gana. She once insulted the marid Nabil, leading to a personal feud.
