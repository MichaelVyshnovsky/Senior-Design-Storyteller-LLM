Lord '''Hezom''' was a [[human]] cleric of [[Helm]] and lord of the settlement of [[Espar]] in [[Cormyr]] as of [[1368 DR]]. He maintained a shrine to Helm in the settlement and treated the sick and wounded there. Prior to this, he was a full-time priest of Helm.
