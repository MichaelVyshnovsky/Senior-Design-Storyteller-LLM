'''Shevaril Starcloak''' was a female [[half-elf]] [[abjurer]] from [[Dagger Falls]]. She served as an advisor to [[Randal Morn]] on [[wizard]]ing matters.

==Abilities==
As an experienced abjurer, Shevaril was well-versed in a number of protective [[spell]]s that could be put to use.

==Activities==
Shevaril Starcloak worked as an advisor to the lord of [[Daggerdale]], Randal Morn, offering advice on matters relating to [[magic]].

==History==
In [[1372 DR]], Shevaril was in the employ of Randal Morn as his advisor. She advised him on wizard matters during the [[drow]] raids in [[Marpenoth]] of that year.
