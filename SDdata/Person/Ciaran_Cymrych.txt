Ciaran Cymrych was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 8 century DR.
He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty and the son of his predecessor, High King Declain. His successor was his cousin, Ardan Cymrych.
Ciaran assumed the throne of the High King in the , after poisoning his father.
He ruled for eleven years before he was killed by an angry mob during the Festival of the Spring Equinox in the .
