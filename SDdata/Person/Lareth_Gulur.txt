'''Lareth Gulur''' was a sword captain and a member of the [[Purple Dragons]]. 

==Activities==
He was a veteran of the [[Tuigan War]]. He witnessed [[Ammanadas Silversword]] stab [[Ensibal Threen]] in the throat and quickly subdued Ammanadas for the crime.
