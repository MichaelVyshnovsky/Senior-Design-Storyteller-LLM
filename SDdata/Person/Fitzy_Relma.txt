'''Fitzy Relma''' was an Untaken of [[Umberlee]], a member of the Bitch Queen's [[Church of Umberlee|clergy]] under Flood Tide [[Allandra Grey]] of the [[Water Queen's House]] in [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Can't chat. These offerings need to be stowed before the next tide or there'll be Allandra to pay.''|Untaken Fitzy Relma}}

==Description==
Fitzy Relma was a fair-skinned [[human]] woman with white hair she wore in bob, decorated with an intricate metal headdress. She wore metallic [[silver]] eye-liner that countoured her light brown eyes and her face bore metallic blue markings of paint or [[tattoo]]s.

==Posessions==
Fitzy's Untaken uniform was a revealing [[chainmail]] dress with light pauldrons and decorative braces. Her weapon of choice was a simple [[sickle]].

==History==
''Circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the [[Absolute crisis]], Untaken Fitzy Relma was placed in charge of stowing tithes on behalf of Allandra Grey. She worked diligently, fearful of the Flood Tide's wrath.
