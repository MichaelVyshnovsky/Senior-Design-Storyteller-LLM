'''Meggin''' ({{Pronounce|MAY|ginn|refs={{Cite adventurers league/Dock Ward Double Cross|12}}

==Description==
Meggin was a woman in her mid-twenties sporting fiery red hair.{{Cite adventurers league/Dock Ward Double Cross|9}} She ran the [[Ship's Prow]] in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==History==
Meggin won the inn during a high-stakes game of [[Three-Dragon Ante]]. She ran it for a couple years until the [[drow]] [[Xzand're Hsyl'ran]] killed her ''circa'' the {{yearlinkName|1492}} and proceed to impersonate her using a ''[[hat of disguise]]''.
