'''Maerclorn Wintersun''' was the [[Wintersun]] heir in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He frequented ''[[The Bold Blazon]]''.

==Relationships==
Maerclorn was a rival of [[Arclath Delcastle]], and the son of the elder Lord Wintersun.

==History==
In late [[Mirtul]] of {{yearlink|1472}}, Arclath Delcastle was a rival of Merclorn, who was a regular at ''[[The Bold Blazon]]''.
