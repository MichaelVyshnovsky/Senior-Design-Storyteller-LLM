'''Xuchallit''' was a [[roper]] that lived in and controlled part of the [[Undermoor]] region of the [[Underdark]], beneath the [[Dungeon of the Hark]], in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
This roper operated as an agent of the [[Beast Lord]] and was on a long-term assignment for them when living in the Undermoor.
