Latro Abiectus was one of the Cult of the Dragon members stationed in the Tower of Bones, Polyandrium of Myth Drannor in 1369 DR.
In 1369 DR, Latro Abiectus was under the command of Ryngoth, the High Priest of Velsharoon who commanded the cults excavation of the Polyandrium of Myth Drannor. Latro had a gambling problem as he wrote an IOU to the cults female necromancer owing her two hundred gold coins.
