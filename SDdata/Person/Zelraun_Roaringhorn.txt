'''Zelraun Roaringhorn''' was a [[wizard]] member of the [[Harper]]s in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Despite being in his 50s in the late 1480s DR, Zelraun appeared to be only about 30 years old. He maintained his youthful look by means of ''[[Potion of longevity|potions of longevity]]''.

==Personality==
Zelraun always maintained an attitude of self-assurance and pride that derived from his family. Accustomed to nobility and wealth, he used the money of his family on fine living. He loved to gamble.

==Possession==
He always had with him a [[shield guardian]].

==History==
Zelraun hailed from the noble [[Roaringhorn (Waterdeep)|Roaringhorn]] of [[Waterdeep]] and trained as a wizard.

''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], Zelraun was the Harper delegate to King [[Bruenor Battlehammer]] of [[Gauntlgrym]] to discuss the madness spreading in the [[Underdark]].

Zelraun was the Harper delegate sent to offer their support to Duchess [[Morwen Daggerford]] when [[werewolves]] from [[Barovia]] were terrorizing the lands around the [[Misty Forest]] in the {{YearlinkName|1491}}.<ref>
