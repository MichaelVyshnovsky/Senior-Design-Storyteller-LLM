'''Matron Maglanna''' was a matron in [[Castle Obarskyr]] in [[Suzail]], [[Cormyr]]. She was responsible for the entire floor on which [[Castle Obarskyr#Places of interest|Satharwood Hall]] was situated. She was doughty and dependable.
