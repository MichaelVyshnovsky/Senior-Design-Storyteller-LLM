Darsh Nyach was a salesman and peer of Baldurian society.
Darsh was a well-known merchant of canvas used for making sails. Even though he was a member of the Parliament of Peers, he lived outside the Upper City.
He had a son called Omdarsh.
In 1482 DR, Rilsa Rael wished to abduct a member of Darshs family, and settled on kidnapping his son.
