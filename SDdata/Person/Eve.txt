'''Eve''' was an elderly widow from [[Phlan]] that spent her time in idle gossip and daydreaming about the lives of tohers ''circa'' the 1480s [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|23}}{{ToD-time}}

==Relationships==
Eve and her friend [[Esma]] met weekly at [[Madame Freona's Tea Kettle]]. They spent their time people-watching and endlessly chattering on about the possible lives they led.

==Possessions==
Either Eve or Esma owned a magical, yet somewhat boring, amulet that was [[item enchantment|enchanted]] to stay clean and not become tarnished.
