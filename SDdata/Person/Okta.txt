'''Okta''' was an elderly [[tiefling]] that lived in [[Elturel]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. Like other Elturian tieflings, she was forced from her home following [[the Descent]] of the city into the [[Nine Hells]] in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.

{{Quote|Best to fill your belly now — while we still can.}}

==Personality==
She had a generous nature and spent her time caring for fellow tieflings during their trek across the [[Sword Coast]]

==Activities==
Okta served as the cook for the tieflings exiled from Elturel, making for them her very own specialty: [[gruel]].

==History==
She formed a makeshift kitchen in the Hollows of the [[Emerald Grove]] when her group took shelter there after being harried by [[goblin]] raiders.
