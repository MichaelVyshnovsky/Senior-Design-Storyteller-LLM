Meryl Vyrmoth was the halfling cook of Qilué Veladorn at the Promenade of the Dark Maiden.
Meryl had been Qilués cook for decades and knew well all the inhabitants of the Promenade.
In 1379 DR, he spoke with Cavatina Xarann, expressing his worries about Qilués mental health, because the lady had acted very strangely in recent times. However, soon after, Cavatina understood that Meryl had been replaced by a dretch and raised the alarm.
Later the real Meryl was found unharmed.
