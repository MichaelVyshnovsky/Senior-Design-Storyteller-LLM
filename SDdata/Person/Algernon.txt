Algernon was a wealthy paunchy man who became a target for Pheirkas, a dwarven neer-do-well in the late 14 century DR.
In the , Algernon was staying in one of the most expensive rooms of Feldeposts Inn in the town of Beregost. The man traveled with his enchanted cloak, the item that caught Pheirkass eye. She sought a competent thief to acquit the magic item without shedding Algernons blood, offering gold and payment.
