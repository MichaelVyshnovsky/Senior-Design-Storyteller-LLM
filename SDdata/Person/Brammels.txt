Brammels was a Cashguard Basher in the employ of the Counting House of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Brammels was a human man with olive skin, a bushy well-kept brown beard, and short hair. As a Cashguard Basher, he wore a set of uniform plate mail armor and a helmet. Brammels was armed with a two-handed war hammer.
In the , Cashguard Basher Brammels was tasked with guarding and patrolling the private docks that belonged to the Counting House in the Lower City, alongside a number of fellow Cashguards and the citys steel watchers.
