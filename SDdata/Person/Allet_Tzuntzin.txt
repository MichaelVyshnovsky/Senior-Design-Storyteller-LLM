'''Allet Tzuntzin''' was a tavern owner in [[Waterdeep]] around [[1367 DR]].

==Activities==
Allet co-managed the [[Misty Beard]] tavern in the [[North Ward of Waterdeep]] together with her sister [[Vindara Tzuntzin]]. 

==History==
Allet and Vindara managed a tavern in [[Elturel]] before settling in Waterdeep.

==Personality==
Allet had an iron will masked by her soft speech. She was totally loyal to her sister. Allet planned to marry within a [[Nobility of Waterdeep|noble house]] or create her own half-elven clan when she could gather enough [[magic]].

==Possessions==
She was a skilled [[wizard]] who enjoyed wielding a variety of [[wand]]s including a ''[[wand of enemy detection]]'', ''[[wand of frost]]'', ''[[wand of magic missiles]]'', ''[[wand of misplaced objects]]'', and a ''[[wand of negation]]''.
