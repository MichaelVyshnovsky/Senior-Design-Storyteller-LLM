'''Lord Kalthas''' was a nobleman of [[Zhentil Keep]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
At the time of the meeting with [[Manshoon]], he was wearing a fur-trimmed doublet and was styled with a moustache, which was of sand color.

==Personality==
During his meeting with Manshoon in {{yearlink|1357}}, he showed himself as boastful and confident.

==Activities==
He was the general of the armies of [[Zhentil Keep]] north of the [[Moonsea]] by the mid-14{{th}} century DR.

==History==
During a meeting of the High Lords of Zhentil Keep with Manshoon in [[Mirtul 21]] of the {{YearlinkName|1357}},{{cite book/The Forgotten Realms Atlas|105}} Lord Kalthas boasted of the strength of Zhentil Keep, and announced the defeat of [[Arkhigoul]], a lich, arguing it would mean they could relax their forces in [[Thar]]. The meeting was interrupted by the sudden departure of Manshoon.

==Rumors & Legends==
Per agents of the [[High Imperceptor of Bane]] relating those events, he was a fool that started telling all grandly nearly from the moment the meeting started.{{cite book/Spellfire/Paperback 1988|158|9}}{{cite book/Spellfire/Paperback 2005||9}}
