'''Deljasaa Rammathor''', whose nickname was '''Lady Wind''', was a [[rogue]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]], City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
She had a reputation for being a shrewd negotiator.

==Activities==
Deljasaa was the guildmaster for the [[Saddlers' and Harness-makers' Guild]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. ==History==
Once a skilled jockey, Deljasaa switched from racing [[horse]]s to breeding them following several dangerous accidents.
