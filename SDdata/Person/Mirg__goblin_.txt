Sharp-eye '''Mirg''' was a [[goblin]] scout from the [[Sword Coast]] and a member of the camp aligned with [[the Absolute]] at the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Bloody arm is killin' me. 'Ere's to the best sedative there is â€“ BOOZE.|Mirg, during goblin festivities.}}

==Activities==
Mirg enjoyed her drink just as much as any other goblin, if not more so.

==Possessions==
Mirg had an impeccable sense of fashion, wearing alluring [[makeup]] around her eyes and decorating her [[leather]] rags with elongated feathers, bones, and animal claws.

==History==
She was among the raiders that celebrated her camp's raid on [[Waukeen's Rest]] in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}. She ditched her guard duties, and failed to replace her fellow cultist [[Nozk]].
