'''Umathar Nhalass VI''', who was also nicknamed '''the Grim''', was the eleventh [[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Nhalass Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Umathar VI reigned for only a few years from {{Yearlink|-610}} to {{Yearlink|-605}}. He inherited the throne from his father [[Darthen Nhalass III|Darthen III]].
