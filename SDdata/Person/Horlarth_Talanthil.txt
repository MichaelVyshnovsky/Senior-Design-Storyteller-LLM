'''Horlarth Talanthil''' was the third [[Sorcerer]]-[[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Talanthil Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Horlarth reigned from {{Yearlink|-499}} to his death in {{Yearlink|-460}}, inheriting the throne when his father [[Corlarth Talanthil|Corlarth]] die.

==Relationships==
He was the third son of Corlarth. Horlarth's firstborn son and heir was [[Nayloss Talanthil|Nayloss "Fireyes"]].
