'''Elraghona Selember''' was a retired adventurer who lived in [[Amphail (village)|Amphail]] in [[northwest Faerûn]], where she operated a ranch, providing mounts for travelers along the [[Long Road]]. She distributed these horses to nearby places such as the [[Sleeping Dragon]] in [[Rassalantar]]. {{Cite dragon/128/Welcome to Waterdeep}}
