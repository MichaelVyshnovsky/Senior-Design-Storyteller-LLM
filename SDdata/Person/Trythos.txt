'''Trythos''' was one of the leaders of [[New Waterdeep]] in [[1370 DR]].
  
==History==
Trythos simply went to [[Maztica]] in order to find a new land. He led a group who settled the small colony of [[Trythosford]] inside the New Waterdeep territory. As the most honorable man among the leaders of New Waterdeep, Trythos was chosen as Open Lord of all settlements.
