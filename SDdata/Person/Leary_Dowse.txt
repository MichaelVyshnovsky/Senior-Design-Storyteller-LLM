Leary Dowse was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Leary was a human woman with a fair complexion and short, dark, curly hair. She wore a simple dark jerkin and a button-down tunic, with an oversized hat.
In the , Leary Dowse was attracted by a curbside preacher of Talona, Umbrenne, spewing out his beliefs of the cycle of life and death being carried out through pestilence. Leary thought his beliefs to be nothing but madness.
