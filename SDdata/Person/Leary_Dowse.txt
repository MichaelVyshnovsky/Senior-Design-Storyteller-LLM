'''Leary Dowse''' was an inhabitant of the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''There's always some weird god or another they want to piss about with. Madness.''|Leary Dowse}}

==Description==
Leary was a [[human]] woman with a fair complexion and short, dark, curly hair. She wore a simple dark jerkin and a button-down tunic, with an oversized hat.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Leary Dowse was attracted by a curbside preacher of [[Talona]], [[Umbrenne]], spewing out his beliefs of the cycle of life and death being carried out through pestilence. Leary thought his beliefs to be nothing but madness.
