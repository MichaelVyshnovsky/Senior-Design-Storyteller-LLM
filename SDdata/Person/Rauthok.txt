'''Rauthok''' was a hero of the ancient wars between [[Narfell]] and [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Rauthok's name was heard as far south as [[Unther]] and [[Mulhorand]], though the specific heroic deeds he performed during the wars did not survive the ages.
