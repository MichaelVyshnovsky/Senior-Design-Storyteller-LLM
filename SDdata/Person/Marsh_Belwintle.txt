Marsh Belwintle was the Slave Lord of Zhentil Keep in 1357 DR and an important member of the Zhentarim.
He was involved a little in the hunt for Shandril Shessair when she attacked Zhentil Keep.
He was a member of the Council of Lords and supported Manshoon. Marsh died during Cyrics assault on the city.
His estate was located in the central section of Zhentil Keeps Inner Quarter at the intersection of Elephstron Way and Flostrens Memory.
