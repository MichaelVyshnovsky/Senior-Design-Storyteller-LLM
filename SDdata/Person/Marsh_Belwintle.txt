'''Marsh Belwintle''' was the Slave Lord of [[Zhentil Keep]] in [[1357 DR]] and an important member of the [[Zhentarim]].

He was involved a little in the hunt for [[Shandril Shessair]] when she attacked Zhentil Keep.
He was a member of the [[Council of Lords (Zhentil Keep)|Council of Lords]] and supported [[Manshoon]]. Marsh died during [[Cyric]]'s assault on the city.

His estate was located in the central section of Zhentil Keep's [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]] at the intersection of [[Elephstron Way]] and [[Flostren's Memory]].
