Galdrin Bressar was a retired lumberjack who was the Axe-Mayor of Quaervarr in 1484 DR.
After Highharvestide of 1484 DR, a supposed phantom started to appear in the village. Galdrin did not believe in a supernatural apparition but admitted that his villagers were very scared.
