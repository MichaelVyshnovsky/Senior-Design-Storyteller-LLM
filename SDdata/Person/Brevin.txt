Brevin was a gnome who lived in Baldurs Gate during the late 15 century DR.
Brevin lived in Baldurs Gate and he was determined to become a member of Baldurs Gates City Watch, but he was constantly confronted by prejudices regarding his race.
In the late 15 century, Brevin got wind of a "training camp" organized by Enver Gortash that supposedly could help him make his way into the Watch. Brevin enrolled in it, but he did not get what he was looking for. Instead, his brain was extracted for experiments or usage in the Steel Watch project.
In the , Brevins brain was put into a brain jar named Closed Mind that could be found in the Necrotic Laboratory of the Mind Flayer Colony beneath Moonrise Towers.
