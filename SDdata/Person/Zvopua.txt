Zvopua was a foulwing involved in the Zhent invasion of Shadowdale in 1375 DR.
Zvopua was the loyal mount of the high ranking member of Zhentarim Eregul the Freestave like his master Zvopua was part of the Zhentarim Occupation Army in Shadowdale in 1375. Eregul and Zvopua were escorting a Zhent supply caravan when they were attacked by some adventurers and Eregul and Zvopua were killed.
