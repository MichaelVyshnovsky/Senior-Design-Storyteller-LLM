'''Argrad Stonefist''' was a [[dwarf]] jeweler active sometime before the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Argrad Stonefist was a fine crafter. One of the dwarf's pieces was recovered in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century DR in the mountains of the [[Vast]], among the treasures found on the [[Griffon Peak]]. This item was a thick [[gold]]en [[ring]] encrusted with five heart-shaped [[emerald]]s. Argrad's mark was engraved on the band's inner surface and it was valued at 1,500 [[gold]] [[coin|pieces]].
