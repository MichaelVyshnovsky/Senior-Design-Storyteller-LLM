Argrad Stonefist was a dwarf jeweler active sometime before the late 14 century DR.
Argrad Stonefist was a fine crafter. One of the dwarfs pieces was recovered in the mid-to-late 14 century DR in the mountains of the Vast, among the treasures found on the Griffon Peak. This item was a thick golden ring encrusted with five heart-shaped emeralds. Argrads mark was engraved on the bands inner surface and it was valued at 1,500 gold pieces.
