==Description==
Daeranatha was an old lady as of the {{YearlinkName|1367}}. She preferred to wear [[silk]] robes of bright orange coloration.

==Activities==
In addition to ruling the Golden City, Daeranatha was a [[high priestess]] of [[Waukeen]]. She became somewhat disillusioned after her goddess' disappearance following the [[Time of Troubles]]. She ruled Suldolphor for nearly a century, but her health begun to fade quickly with the loss of her [[spell]]s following Waukeen's silence.
