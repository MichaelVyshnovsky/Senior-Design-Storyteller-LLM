'''Ikaron''' was a [[tiefling]] from [[Elturel]] that was forced out of the city along with others of his kind, following [[the Descent]] of the city into the [[Nine Hells]] in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.

{{Quote|We've known enough grief this tenday, traveler. Don't be the cause of more.|Ikaron, to on of the [[True Soul]]s.}}

==Possessions==
He wore a suit of battered metal armor and carried a [[greataxe]].

==Activities==
Ikaron served as a protector of his people and was readily willing to put anyone down that dared harm them.

==History==
After Elturel was pulled down into [[Avernus]] and subsequently returned to the [[Western Heartlands|Heartlands]] all of the tieflings in the city were exiled and forced to fend for themselves. Ikaron was among those that followed the former [[Hellriders|Hellrider]] [[Zevlor]] that eventually settled at the [[Emerald Grove]] for a time.
