'''Aswiyah al-Muftahir''' was the caliph of [[Muluk]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Personality==
Aswiyah was a crafty and cunning individual, just like her ancestors. She preferred to be addressed as "Caliph", as opposed to the traditional feminine title of "Calipha."

==Activities==
The Caliph exercised her political skills during her reign by successfully negotiating alliances with each of the [[Free Cities of Zakhara]].

==Relationships==
Aswiyah was the eldest daughter of the previous caliph, [[Ibrahim al-Muftahir]]. She was the mother of princess [[Hadra bint Aswiyah]].
