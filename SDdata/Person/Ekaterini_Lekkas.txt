'''Ekaterini Lekkas''' was a noble and member of a cell of the [[Cult of Reborn Flame]] that operated out of [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]], in [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|33}}

==Description==
Ekaterini was a young [[human]] female with a strong chin and sunken eyes.

==History==
In 1479 DR, Ekaterini was a member of [[Bazamarl]]'s cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame. She joined as an act of rebellion against her family.

When a band of [[adventurer]]s that were sent by [[Halagothra|Halagothra the Healer]] to Sambral to destroy the cell of the cult operating there, killed both Bazalmarl and the [[Firebird (elemental)|Firebird]],{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|45}} Ekaterini fled in panic, avoiding being killed by the adventurers.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|40}}
