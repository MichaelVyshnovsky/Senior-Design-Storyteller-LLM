Mlartlar was an elven inhabitant of Cormanthyr in the 3 century DR.
Mlartlar was the herald for Coronal Eltargrim. However, in , while referencing the latest assassination attempts against himself, Eltargrim explained that Mlartlar thought himself a better swordsman than the Coronal, leading to the implication that hed made one such attempt himself.
