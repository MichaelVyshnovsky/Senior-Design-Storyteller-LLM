The dwarf Durnam Goblinkicker was the owner of the Laughing Goblin Inn in Phlan during the 15 century DR. 
For a while Durnam was mostly absent from his inn, leaving the day-to-day operations to his employee Imizael. He was forced to take a more hands-on leadership after Imizaels departure during the events of the Tyranny of Dragons, which left him quite grumpy.
When his city was taken over by Vorgansharax circa the , Durnam was one of the few business owners who secretly rebelled against the dragon.
