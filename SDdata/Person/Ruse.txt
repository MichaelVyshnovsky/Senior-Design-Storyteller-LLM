'''Ruse''' was a bandit [[berserker]] in the [[Moonsea]] in [[1489 DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Outlaws of the Iron Route|11}}

==History==
Ruse was one of the two lieutenants of the [[Gray Patriots]] bandit group under [[Sahnd Krulek]]. During a meeting with another bandit group, the [[Claws of Tiamat]], an adventuring party hired to clear the [[Iron Route]] of outlaws caused havoc. Ruse was killed in fighting.{{Cite adventurers league/Outlaws of the Iron Route|20}}
