'''Reinhar VI''' was a king of the [[Arkaiun]]s of [[Dambrath]].

==Relationships==
The man who would rule as Reinhar VI was the third son of King [[Reinhar IV]]. He had at least four sons.

==History==
Reinhar VI was born in the [[610 DR|Year of the Spellfire, 610 DR]]. He took his brother [[Reinhar V]]'s throne and name upon the latter's death in [[647 DR]]. Reinhar VI died in [[691 DR]], passing the throne to his [[Reinhar VII|fourth son]].
