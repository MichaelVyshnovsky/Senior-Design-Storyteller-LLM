'''Sarvyn Eorn''', who was also called '''the Old''', was the first King of the [[Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Sarvyn came into his throne by widespread acclamation of the general public in the {{YearlinkName|1018}}, after King [[Tarnhart Campion II|Tarnhart II]] died without an heir. Sarvyn ruled for many years until {{YearlinkName|1111}}.

==Relationships==
His grandnephew, [[Roadaeron Lorndessar]], would succeed him to the throne.
