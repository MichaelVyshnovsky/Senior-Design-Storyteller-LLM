'''Morok Ulgren''' was an [[orc]] blacksmith in the [[Kingdom of Many-Arrows]] in [[1484 DR]].

==Activities==
Morok was the most highly skilled orcish blacksmith in the entirety of [[Many-Arrows]], making him the most prominent trade figure in the kingdom. He was trained by [[dwarf]] blacksmiths in [[Sundabar]], and stood as a living testament to the growth that could come from peace between the orcs and other races. He was a staunch advocate of [[Obould XVII]] and [[Lorgru]]'s plans for continued peace, and provided their personal guard with [[iron]] weapons and armor.{{Cite dragon/429/History Check: Dark Arrow Keep}}
