Oth Eltorchul was a young wizard and noble inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Oth worked within the Eltorchul Academy in Waterdeeps Sea Ward until a tragic accident in the , damaged his mind. Oth was attempting to create a dream sphere using the Mhaorkiira Hadryad when the accident occurred.
His father was Thesp Eltorchul and his younger sister was Fea Eltorchul. Oth was the heir apparent of the Eltorchul noble family before his accident.
