Rylatarralahtyma, also known as simply Rylatar, was a red dragon dwelling in the Forest of Wyrms in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Rylatar was around 80 feet (24 meters) long. Her scales were crimson-colored and her wings were a deep indigo color.
Rylatar claimed to not be fond of anything,
Rylatar had a silver ring in the shape of a winged dragon eating its own tail, which had the power to change her shape into the form of a human or elf.
