'''Salida''' ({{Pronounce|sah|LEE|dah|refs=}}) was a [[yuan-ti pureblood]] posing as a [[human]] guide in [[Port Nyanzaru]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. She was in fact a spy for [[Ras Nsi]] who relayed information about expeditions that ventured south into the jungles of [[Chult]].

==Appearance==
She was {{SI|5'4"|'"}} tall and weighed {{SI|120|lbs|k|long=yes}}. Salida appeared physically fit and maintained the appearance of an experienced traveler.

Due to her [[yuan-ti]] heritage, areas of her skin appeared as scales.

==Personality==
Salida was adept as creating vivid and fantastic lies to obscure her identity as a spy. She was particularly witty and adroit.

==Possessions==
Salida possessed a ''[[sending stone]]'' that she used to communicate with Ras Nsi.
