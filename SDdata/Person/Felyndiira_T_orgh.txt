Felyndiira Torgh was the head of the College of Illusion and one of the members of the Conclave of Sshamath in the city of Sshamath in the year 1372 DR.
In 1377 DR, Qarlynd Melarn told the priestess of Eilistraee Miverra that Felyndiira was a slippery and oily individual.
Rumors said Felyndiira worshiped Lolth in secret.
In 1379 DR, she supported Guldor Zauviirs proposal to promote Tlar Mizzrynturl, Master of Baeqeshel Magic.
