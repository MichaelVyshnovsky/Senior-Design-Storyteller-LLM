Alai Aivenmore was one of the eight Great Readers of the Avowed that served Candlekeep during the late 15 century DR.
Great Reader Aivenmore was an academic authority on deities and the nature of divinity.
As one of the great Great Readers, Alai was partly responsible for the daily operations of Candlekeep. 
She was a devout follower of Oghma, the God of Knowledge.
