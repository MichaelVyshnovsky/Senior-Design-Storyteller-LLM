'''King Andilber Obarskyr''' was the 49th monarch of [[Cormyr]]. He gained the crown when his cousin [[Jarissra Obarskyr|Queen Jarissra]] abdicated in his favour.

Andilber was the eldest son of [[Arathra Obarskyr|Queen Arathra]] and had a brother named [[Anglond]]. Andilber died without an heir.<ref>
