Mayella took Muzlim with her when she went out with her father, the merchant [[Barthelm Meadowbrock]], to the [[Bold Bard]] tavern on [[Eleint 16]] of the {{YearlinkName|1367}}. However, Muzlim growled and snapped at Mayor [[Tobald]] and Mayella chided the dog. Later, Muzlim was frightened by [[Dovo]] the blacksmith's assistant when he came to pester Mayella, when Dovo and Rolf brawled, and Mayella carried the fearful dog out under her arm.

==Personality==
Although yappy, Muzlim turned out to be a very good judge of character.
