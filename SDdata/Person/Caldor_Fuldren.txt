Caldor Fuldren was a law enforcement officer in the town of Voonlar sometime during the term of Bron Buorstag Hlammythyl, i.e., from about 1355 DR until at least 1372 DR.
Caldor was cruel and capricious, but fanatically dedicated to the Bron. He was known to attack, verbally and sometimes physically without warning, anyone who appeared to be speaking or working against the Brons plans for the city. He was often erratic in battle but would usually listen to and follow the orders of Andrus Kriivor, the de facto leader of the deputies.
Caldor was a specialist with the short sword.
He wore chain mail and carried a shield,
Caldor was trained by the Zhentilar and chosen for the position of deputy.
