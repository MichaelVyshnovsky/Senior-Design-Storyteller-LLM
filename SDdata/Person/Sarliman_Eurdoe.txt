Sarliman Eurdoe was an inhabitant of the city of Wheloon in Cormyr and a local entrepreneur.
As a "hothands", or entrepreneur, Sarliman imported the rosecork tree from Prespur to his native land for its fire-resistant properties and value in building. The trees went on to flourish by the southern tributaries of the Wyvernflow.
