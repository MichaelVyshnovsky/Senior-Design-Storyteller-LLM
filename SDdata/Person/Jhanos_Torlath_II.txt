King '''Jhanos Torlath II''' "the Ancient" was an exceptionally long-lasting [[Amn]]ian monarch of the [[Torlath]] dynasty, known as the fourth Wizard-King of Amn.

==Relationships==
Jhanos was the second son of of King [[Imnel Torlath III|Imnel III]], and his own fourth son was [[Rhadik Torlath II|Rhadik II]].
