'''Kessla''' was a beautiful [[half-elf]] retired minstrel who ran the [[Red Rock]] inn in [[Dagger Falls]].

==Activities==
She was widely believed to be an agent of the [[Harpers]] who served under [[Storm Silverhand]] of [[Shadowdale]].
