Alasitra was an abjurer and one of three minor wizards stationed at the enclave run by the young Red Wizard Hinnar.
Alasitras body bore a magical tattoo
Alasitra was tough, dexterous, and intelligent and was trained in alchemy, spellcraft, and the scribing of scrolls.
Alasitra carried a dagger and a small crossbow of superb craftsmanship.
Alasitras spellbook contained 23 different spells.
Alasitra spent most of the days hours in the compounds main barracks scribing spells with other wizards.
