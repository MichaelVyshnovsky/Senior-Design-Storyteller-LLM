Maelgot was a member of the reformed Cult of the Dragon under Severin Silrajin.
While working as a guard, Maelgot usually kept around 20 gp on his person.
Maelgot was a cultist and, alongside Sorvic, one of the bodyguards of Talis Kasterel, at his masters side in the Cults occupied hunting lodge in the Greypeak Mountains.
