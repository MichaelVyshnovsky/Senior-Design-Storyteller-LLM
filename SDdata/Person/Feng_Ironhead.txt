Feng Ironhead was a half-orc shopkeeper in Red Larch during the Elemental Evil crisis.
Feng was a genius in his field he could pick what looked to be an ordinary weapon or piece of armor that in truth was extremely durable. However, he was a bad businessmen, often selling good items for cheap prices, but he didnt care.
Feng ran a shop in the village.
Feng was a sellsword and caravan guard until 1489 DR when he settled in Red Larch, opening his shop.
