'''Bennik Vandyrmir''' was an employee of [[Sa'Sani]]'s [[Mercantile House]] that operated in the village of [[West Harbor]] in the late  14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Bennik was a serious man with short blonde hair, pale skin, blue eyes, and chiseled face.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, Bennik was one of the local merchants who operated on the [[Sword Coast North]]. When merchant princess Sa'Sani expanded her trading coaster, Bennik Vandyrmir was among her hires. The man was put in charge of the Mercantile House's trading post in a remote swamp village of West Harbor, famous for its main export â€“ [[Harvest Mead]]. Despite working for Sa'Sani, Bennik never offered any of his stock for free, not even to Sa'Sani's hirelings nor business partners.
