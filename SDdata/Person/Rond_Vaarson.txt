'''Rond Vaarson''' was the Great Chief of the [[Elk tribe]] of the [[Uthgardt]] in the years following the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==Personality==
Chief Rond was an old warrior who had seen many battles throughout his years. He had fought and slew so many by the end of his life, he lost all feelings of bloodlust or thirst for warfare.

==History==
During his final years, circa the {{YearlinkName|1485}}, many young warriors vied for position to be named the next chieftain of the Elk tribe.
