'''Pe Nii-Qwoh''' was an officer in the army of [[Shou Lung]].

==History==
Pe was the adjutant of General [[Batu Min Ho]] in the provincial [[Army of Chukei]].

In [[1359]], Pe followed his general on a mission ordered by Emperor [[Kai Tsao Shou Chin]] to defeat the [[Grand Army of the Tuigan]].

At the end of the war, devastated by the loss of his family, Batu left the service of the Emperor but, with the Emperor's permission, made Pe the heir to both his and his wife properties and ranks.
