Earl Ravenak was the leader of the Hairheads, one of the violent factions active in the city of Zazesspur during the Tethyrian Interregnum period.While confirmed to be a true minor noble, Ravenaks earldom is never stated.
Earl Ravenak spent a large amount of his time preaching through a speaking-tube from the streets of Zazesspur a message that all non-humans should be driven from Tethyr.
Earl Ravenak was hated by Baron Hardisty and Zaranda Star alike, and his Hairheads were opposed to another major faction in Zazesspur, the Social Justice League, which consisted primarily of halflings.
In the , when Zaranda Star visited Zazesspur, she heard Ravenak give a speech on one occasion and also encountered several of his fanatical followers.
