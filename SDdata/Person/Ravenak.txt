Earl '''Ravenak''' was the leader of the [[Hairheads]], one of the violent factions active in the city of [[Zazesspur]] during the [[Tethyrian Interregnum]] period.While confirmed to be a true minor noble, Ravenak's earldom is never stated.
{{Quote|[[Gnome|Gnomish]] blood shall spurt under the knife!|An excerpt from one of Earl Ravenak's speeches}}
{{TOC limit|3}}
==Activities==
Earl Ravenak spent a large amount of his time preaching through a [[speaking-tube]] from the streets of Zazesspur a message that all non-[[human]]s should be driven from [[Tethyr]].

Earl Ravenak was hated by [[Baron Hardisty]] and [[Zaranda Star]] alike, and his Hairheads were opposed to another major faction in Zazesspur, the [[Social Justice League]], which consisted primarily of [[halfling]]s.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1366}}, when Zaranda Star visited Zazesspur, she heard Ravenak give a speech on one occasion and also encountered several of his fanatical followers.
