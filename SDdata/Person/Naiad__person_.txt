Naiad was one of the students of the Neverwinter Academy in 1372 DR during the Wailing Death epidemic.
Naiad adored Aribeth de Tylmarande and was impressed with the paladins skills and stealth with which she was searching for the Wailing Death cure.
Naiad was one of the graduating class of the Neverwinter Academy in the , alongside the Hero of Neverwinter. Shortly before the graduation commenced the Academy was attacked by the cult that sought to destroy Neverwinter.
