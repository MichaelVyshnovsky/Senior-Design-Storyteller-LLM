Rharvuld Hethcanter was a renowned furniture maker from Immersea and the father of Paresper Hethcanter, active in the late 15 century DR.
Rharvulds superb skills made him a highly sought-after furniture maker by the late 15 century DR. His wardrobes were all the rage among wealthy noblewomen of Suzail, the capital of Cormyr. Rharvuld Hethcanter had some undisclosed connection to the inquisitive War Wizards, but the furniture maker kept mum on the subject.
