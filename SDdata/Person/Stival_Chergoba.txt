Stival Chergoba was a warrior of Sossal during the Rage of Dragons of 1373 DR.
Stival was a commander in the contingent led by Madislak Pemsk during the war against the Ice Witch Iyraclea and the white dragon dracolich Zethrindor. He was chosen alongside Natali Dormetsk to join Madislak in the duel against Zethrindor.
