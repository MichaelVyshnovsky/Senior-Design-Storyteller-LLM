Shehstek was a lizardfolk from the Vast Swamp in Cormyr in 1479 DR.
In 1479 DR, Shehstek was one of the lizardfolk that came through a portal in the Vast Swamp and arrived in Undermountain. He was sent to explore a portion of the dungeon and was captured by the vampire Dayan. Shehstek was left in his cell to die.
