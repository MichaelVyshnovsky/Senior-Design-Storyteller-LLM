'''Nytus the Shipwright''' was a sailor who retired to become a shipwright in [[Veltalar|Velprintalar]] (later Veltalar), [[Aglarond]]. He made a reputation for creating quality fishing vessels and large merchant ships that extended across the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]]. Other shipbuilders would approach him for advice or commissions.
