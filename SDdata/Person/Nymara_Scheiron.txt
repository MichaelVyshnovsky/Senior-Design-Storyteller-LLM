'''Nymara Scheiron''', more commonly known as '''Kitten''', was a [[Lords of Waterdeep|Masked Lord of Waterdeep]], ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1372}}. Few people knew her full name.

==Description==
Kitten had brown hair, usually unkempt, and a full, strong figure. She was fond of dressing up in rather revealing outfits, decorated in [[makeup]] and scented with strong [[perfume]]s.

==Personality==
She was known for her pragmatic disposition but particularly fierce temper. She refused any gift of wealthy or charitable donation offered to her by others.

==Activities==
Kitten often posed as a tavern dancer in the [[Dock Ward of Waterdeep|Dock Ward]]. She was quite skilled at stealthily stalking other individuals through the streets of [[Waterdeep]], and was quite knowledgeable about both the city's skyline and its sewer system.

==History==
During her adventuring days, Kitten was known to have survived a few delves into the [[House of Stone]].{{Cite dragon/273/The New Adventures of Volo: The House of Stone|76}} At some point she even fought one-on-one against the son of the god [[Bane]], [[Iyachtu Xvim]].

She was recruited to serve as Masked Lord by [[Mirt]] and [[Durnan]] in the 1340s DR. 

==Residence==
Kitten rarely stayed in one place. She had a number of residences around the city.

==Relationships==
Kitten was one of the "Like-Minded Lords" as of the {{YearlinkName|1364}}, along with [[Mirt]], [[Khelben]], [[Durnan]], [[Larissa Neathal|Larissa]], and [[Brian|Brian the "Swordcaster"]], who frequently met up to enjoy each other's company.
