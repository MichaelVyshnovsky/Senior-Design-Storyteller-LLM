'''Mornarth Talanthil I''' was the seventh [[Sorcerer]]-[[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Talanthil Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Mornarth I reigned from {{Yearlink|-370}} to {{Yearlink|-323}}, inheriting the throne when is father, [[Vayloss Talanthil III|Vayloss III]], died.

==Relationships==
His son and sole heir was [[Tallos Talanthil I|Tallos I]].
