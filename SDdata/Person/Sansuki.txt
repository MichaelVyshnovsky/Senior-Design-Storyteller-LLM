Sansuki was a fighter member of the Shadow Thieves in Athkatla in the late 14 century DR.
Sansuki was a pale-skinned human male who was dressed in gray garb and simple thieves leathers.
In the , the criminal underbelly of Athkatla erupted in an all-out guild wars between the Shadow Thieves and a vampire coven under Bodhi. Sansuki was one of many thieves killed in the war. He was pursued by vampires led by Del across the night streets of Athkatlas Scepter District. Once Del coughed up with the man, Sansukis fate was sealed.
