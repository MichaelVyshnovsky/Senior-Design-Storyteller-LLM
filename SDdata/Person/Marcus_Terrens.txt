'''Marcus Terrens''', brother to [[Maggie Terrens]], was a child who lived in the town of [[Moonhaven]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Sometime before the Moonhaven was attacked and ruined by the [[goblin]] raiders of the [[Cult of the Absolute]] in the {{YearlinkName|1491}}, Marcus was among the four children who had gone missing. Their names: Marcus Terrens, Maggie Terrens, [[Mathen Deetch]], and [[Rochelle Kirk]]. The missing children's notice was still hanging on a wall as the village laid in ruins in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
