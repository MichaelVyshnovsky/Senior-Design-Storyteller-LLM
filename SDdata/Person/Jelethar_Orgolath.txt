'''Jelethar Orgolath''', who was also called '''the Blackscale''', was a [[tiefling]] [[Nentyarch]] of the [[Orgolath Dynasty]] of [[Narfell]].

==Activities==
Jelethar's reign as Nentyarch was brief, lasting only from {{Yearlink|-482}} to {{Yearlink|-475}}. He disappeared while on an expedition to the [[Sunrise Mountains]] and was presumed dead.
