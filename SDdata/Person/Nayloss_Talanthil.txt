'''Nayloss Talanthil''', who was also called '''Fireyes''', was the fourth [[Sorcerer]]-[[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Talanthil Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Nayloss inherited the throne when his father, [[Horlarth Talanthil]], died in {{Yearlink|-460}}. He ruled until {{Yearlink|-408}}.

==Relationships==
Nayloss' son and heir was [[Vayloss Talanthil II|Vayloss II]].
