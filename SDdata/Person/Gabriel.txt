'''Gabriel''' was a [[solar]] in the service of [[Bahamut]].

==History==
In [[1357 DR]], Gabriel was captured by a group of [[balor]]s in the [[Layers of the Abyss]] ruled by [[Orcus]]. He was tortured by them until eventually he was freed by [[Gareth Dragonsbane]]'s party.
