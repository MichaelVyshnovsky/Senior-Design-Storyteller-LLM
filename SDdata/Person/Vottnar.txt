Guardian '''Vottnar''' was the [[cleric]] of The Watcher, in charge of the [[Temple of Helm (Athkatla)|Temple of Helm]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Welcome to the temple of Helm, my Lord. Helm is the watcher and the guardian, protector of all who require his benevolent presence. Be safe, my Lord, and be welcome.''|Guardian Vottnar}}

==Description==
Vottnar was a [[plate armor]]-clad [[human]] male with pale skin. He wore an open-faced horned [[helmet]] and was armed with a [[mace]] and a [[shield]].

==History==
In the late 14{{th}} century DR, Guardian Vottnar could be found running a small temple dedicated to [[Helm]] in [[Athkatla]]'s [[Athkatla/Bridge District|Bridge District]]. He offered services of divine magic and magic [[potion]]s in exchange for a donation to the [[Church of Helm]]. Vottnar also had a small selection of magic ''scrolls of protection'' and wooden [[stake]]s.
