Warren Cymrych II was High King of the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 7 century DR.
He was a member of the Cymrych Dynasty, and had been named in honor of the first Cymrych monarch. He was the first son of his predecessor, High King Samuel, and his successor was his own first son, Tobert.
