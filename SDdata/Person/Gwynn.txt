Gwynn was a Netherese arcanist and inventor.
Gwynn was a prominent mage who specialized in inventive spells.
In , Gwynn invented the spell feign death, which was originally known as Gwynns feign death. About a hundred years later, the arcanist invented another spell, vampiric touch, which was also originally named after Gwynn, and was known as Gwynns vampirism.
Circa , Gwynns name was shouted along with those of other prominent arcanists by Sysquemalyn when she and Candlemas tried to escape the Nine Hells.
