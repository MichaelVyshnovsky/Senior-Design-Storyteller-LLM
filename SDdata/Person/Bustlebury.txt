Bustlebury was a middle class inhabitant of the city of Baldurs Gate in late 15 century DR.
Bustlebury was a hin man with bronze skin, dark brown eyes, and short red hair. The halfling wore good quality clothes.
In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldurs Gate, the gates between the Lower City of Baldurs Gate and its more affluent Upper City were closed, monitored by the Flaming Fist mercenaries and allowing only the wealthy and current residents of the Upper City through. Bustlebury was among the Baldurians who sought passage to the Upper City, suffering through the long lines and rude Fists.
