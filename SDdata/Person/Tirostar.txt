Tirostar, who referred to himself by the title Dark Imperceptor, was a specialty priest and one of the seven High Hands in the Risen Cult of Bane in the mid-14 century DR.Dragon #362 provides an exact date for the events of Halls of the High King, which did not give an exact date within itself.
