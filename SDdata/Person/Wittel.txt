'''Wittel''' was a teamster in [[Hillsfar]] in the [[Moonsea]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Harried in Hillsfar|25}}

==Activities==
Wittel was a teamster who worked transferring goods from the farms around Hillsfar to the city.

==History==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], his wife [[Dottee]] announced that she was pregnant and they decided to go for a trip in the [[Cormanthor]] forest. There, a family of [[jackalwere]]s ambushed them: Wittel was left unconscious and Dottee taken.
