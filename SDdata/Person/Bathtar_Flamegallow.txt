'''Bathtar Flamegallow''' was a [[War Wizard]] of [[Cormyr]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Per [[Narnra Shalace]], then a spy, he was jovial and could amuse with his jokes.{{cite book/Elminster's Daughter/MMP|313|18}}

==Description==
Narnra described him as trim-bearded.

==Activities==
He liked to spend time at [[The Downed Falcon]], a tavern. Narnra Shalace determined he had little interest in other than making toy ships float.

==History==
In {{yearlink|1373}}, the [[Waterdhavian]] [[thief]] Narnra Shalace's first assignment as a spy for the [[Royal Magician of Cormyr]], [[Caladnei]], was to eavesdrop on a number of the [[War Wizards]], including Bathtar Flamegallow. During her investigation, she found him at least once in the bathroom.
