Shiv Dahlia was a wildheart barbarian thug who opposed the Guild crime gang of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Dahlia was a human woman with a light skin tone and strawberry-red hair. She wielded a great club in battle.
In the , during the Absolute crisis, Farlin led a gang of Guild members in dealing with the Stone Lord and his men. The new kingpin secretly served the Absolute and was trying to remove the Guilds influence from across the Gate. Farlins group faced a band of the Stone Lords men led by the tiefling Cairos, including Shiv Dahlia. They clashed under the Wyrms Crossing bridge in Rivington over a shipment of supplies. However, the meeting devolved into a battle and either squads fates remained uncertain.
