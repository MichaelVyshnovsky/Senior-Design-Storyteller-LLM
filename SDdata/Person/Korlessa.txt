'''Korlessa''' was a [[dragonborn]] [[bard]] that lived in [[Baldur's Gate]] some time during or after the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite MTG|CLB|280|Korlessa, Scale Singer|[[Jesper Ejsing]]}}<ref group="note" name="Korlessa">Information about Korlessa included in the prompt given to the artist was shared on [https://web.archive.org/web/20230125141721/https://www.muddycolors.com/2022/06/korlessa-scale-singer/ their blog]

{{Quote|Would you like a song of wing-wind and fire, or one of gentle heartbeats atop a golden hoard?|Korlesaa, Scale Singer.}}

==Abilities==
Korlessa had the powers of a [[spellsinger]], unique [[spellcaster]]s that accessed their magic by means of song or dance. Her music attracted small, spectral [[dragon]]s wherever she played.<ref group="note" name="Korlessa" />
