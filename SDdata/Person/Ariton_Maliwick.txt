Ariton Maliwick was the Grand Druid of the Gulthmere Forest in the Vilhon Reach, active in the 3 century DR.
In the , Ariton Maliwick opposed the establishment of two Chondathan logging towns – Timindar and Orbech. The grand druid issued a stern warning to the ruling family of Chondath – the Illistine Dynasty, to stop despoiling Satyrwoods natural beauty or face the wrath of its inhabitants. The warning was ignored, resulting in the disaster seven years later -  known as the Crushed Helm Massacre.
