'''Yturn''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and [[spell]] inventor who lived during the [[Age of Discovery]].

==Description==
This arcanist focused on spells which manipulated how long one could stay in the air.

==History==
Yturn developed three spells during their life, each one year after the other. The spells they developed were ''levitate'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2699|NY}}, ''[[jump]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2700|NY}}, and finally, ''[[feather fall]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2701|NY}}.
