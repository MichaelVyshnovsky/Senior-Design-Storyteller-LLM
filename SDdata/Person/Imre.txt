'''Imre''' was a lesser-known [[Imaskari]] [[Artificer (Imaskari)|artificer]] [[wizard]].{{Cite web/theedverse/2020-01-13 - Imre of Imaskar}}

==Description==
Imre was a brown-haired man of unremarkable looks, often wearing ankle-length [[robe]]s.

==Personality==
Imre delighted in [[spell]] innovation and crafting, putting little stock in political importance, and was a teacher to many.

==Abilities==
Imre was one of the earliest specialists in the school of [[transmutation]] in the record.

==Possessions==
Imre bore a satchel that hovered around shoulder-level. In the satchel, he carried [[tea]]-making equipment, tools, a ''dereth'' (the Imaskari word for a clipboard), [[scroll]]s, [[ink]]s, and [[quill]]s.

==History==
Between {{yearlink|-7979}} and {{yearlink|-7975}}, Imre was involved in the raising of [[Inupras]].
