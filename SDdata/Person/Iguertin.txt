'''Iguertin''' was a [[Dog People]] guide in [[1362 DR]].

==History==
Iguertin's brother once went to [[Michaca]] and told about its wealth to Iguertin. In 1362 DR, [[Sozarro]] hired Iguertin as guide to locate the [[City of Gold]].
