Shal was a succubus posing as the female duergar consort of Deepking Horgar Steelshadow V in Gracklstugh in the late 15 century DR.
Shal was loyal to the demon lord Grazzt, whom she served.
Her only mission was to hide the madness that had infected the Deepking circa 1486 DR. She may have been exposed by an adventuring party that had escaped Velkynvelve.
