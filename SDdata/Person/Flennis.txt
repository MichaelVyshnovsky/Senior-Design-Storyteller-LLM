'''Flennis''' was a high-ranking member of a [[Dead Three]] cult in [[Baldur's Gate]] in {{YearlinkName|1494}}.

==Activities==
During the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], Flennis was the highest-ranking follower of [[Myrkul]] inside the cult in Baldur's Gate. She was busy creating new [[zombie]] minions for the cult.
