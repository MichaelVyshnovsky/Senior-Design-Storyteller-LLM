Wulf was a dutiful hireling guard who worked for Baldurs Mouth in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Wulf was a human man with bronze skin, gray eyes, and dark hair.
Wulf was equipped with a simple chainmail uniform of Baldurs Mouths guards and armed with a non-magical halberd.
In the , Wulf was employed by Baldurs Mouth and its editor-in-chief, Ettvard Needle. He was tasked with guarding the broadsheets main entrance from unwanted visitors and critics of the gazettes editorial direction.
