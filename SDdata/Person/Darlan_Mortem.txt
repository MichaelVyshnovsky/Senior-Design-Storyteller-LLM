'''Darlan Morten''' was a male [[half-elf]] [[shadow adept]] who was one of the members of the [[Shadow Council]] of the [[Shadow Thieves]].

Darlan was an obscure manipulator of the [[Shadow Weave]] who used his position to increase his collection of magic items. His duty was to control the guild's operations between [[Baldur's Gate]] and [[Amn]].
