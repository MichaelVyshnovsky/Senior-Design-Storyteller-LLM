'''Thammar Saddusk''' was a very friendly wine merchant in [[High Dale]].

==History==
When in [[1358 DR]] a secret [[Zhent]] occupation army occupied the dale, Saddusk was a member of the [[Council of the High Dale]]. Saddusk was apparently interested only in the good of the dale.
