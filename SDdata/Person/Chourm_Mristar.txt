Chourm Mristar was a monk from the Monastery of the Yellow Rose in Damara in the late 15th century DR.
Chourm was a good friend of Lana, and was one of the people that Lana trusted most.
Chourm lived in the village of Tomrav, acting as the monasterys envoy.
