'''Two-Penny Tam''' was a performer from [[Mulsantir]] in [[Rashemen]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Tam was a cheery individual and oftentimes a slovenly mess, possibly due to his alcohol consumption. He possessed a meticulously brushed and curled red mustache.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, Two-Penny Tam, along with his fellow actor, [[Clive the Dandy]], could often be found drinking heavily before their so-called performances at [[the Sloop]] in Mulsantir.
