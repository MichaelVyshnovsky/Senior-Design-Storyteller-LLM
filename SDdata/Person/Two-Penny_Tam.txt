Two-Penny Tam was a performer from Mulsantir in Rashemen during the late 14 century DR.
Tam was a cheery individual and oftentimes a slovenly mess, possibly due to his alcohol consumption. He possessed a meticulously brushed and curled red mustache.
As of the , Two-Penny Tam, along with his fellow actor, Clive the Dandy, could often be found drinking heavily before their so-called performances at the Sloop in Mulsantir.
