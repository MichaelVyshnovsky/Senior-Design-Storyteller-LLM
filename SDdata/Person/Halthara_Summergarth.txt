Halthara Summergarth was a halfling woman of the city of Procampur in the Vast.
Around 1368 DR, Summergarth was a ship-owner and a maker of barrels and crates, and conducted her trade from Summergarth Facilitations in Procampur. She was also an active Harper agent, though she remained based in Procampur.
