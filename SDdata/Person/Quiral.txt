'''Quiral''' was a [[human]] blacksmith and [[adventurer|ex-adventurer]] who lived in [[Espar]], [[Cormyr]] as of [[1368 DR]]. 

==Activities==
The blacksmith was known for creating high quality [[sword]]s, although his methods were slow and careful. He treated swordmaking as a hobby rather than a business.
