Garauder was a pit fiend in the late 14 century DR slain by the Simbul.
He had hatreds and schemes in place at the time of his death.
In the , Garauder was one of the pit fiends sent to strike at the Simbul when she assaulted Avernus in search of Elminster Aumar. He was the last survivor after the creatures summoned by the Simbuls blood ring spell struck out to defend her however, when he attempted to make a suicide attack on her, he was eaten by a dragon summoned by the blood ring that he had missed before.
