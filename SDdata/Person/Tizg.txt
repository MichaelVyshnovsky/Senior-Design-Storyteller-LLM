Tracker '''Tizg''' was a member of [[cult of the Absolute]] who dwelled in the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Smell that meat sizzlin'? Can't tell if it's the brandin' or the party - makin' me bloody 'ungry either way.|Nrog.}}

==Description==
He had yellow-orown skin and his face was decorated with blue paint.

==Possessions==
Tizg wore crude leather armor adorned with animal bones, along with an amulet featuring the [[holy symbol]] of [[the Absolute]]. He carried with him a simple [[bow]].
