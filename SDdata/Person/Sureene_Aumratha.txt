As of 1373 DR, Sureene Aumratha was a middle-aged woman, tall, handsome, with hair dyed moon blond.
Sureene was a shrewd businesswoman, who wisely invested the offerings in the temple in shipbuilding and caravans. She was said to be the wealthiest woman in the city.
She had two deputies, Jannatha Goldenshield and Baerimel Dunnath, both trained by Firefingers.
Sureene aided Taegan Nightwind by casting lie-detection spells on the other mages aiding the effort to decrypt Sammasters notes on the Rage of Dragons in 1373 DR. She also kept Rilitar Shadow-water comfortable and cared for in his final moments.
