'''Tolgar Anuvien''' was a veteran member of the [[Company of Crazed Venturers]] who went on to found the abbey of [[Goldenfields]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite web enhancement/Environs of Waterdeep|9}}

==Personality==
Tolgar had a quick wit and rather dry sense of humor. He believed he was truly doing the work of [[Chauntea]], the goddess whom he venerates, by founding and running the temple-complex of Goldenfields.

==Relationships==
Tolgar was counted among the friends of [[Malchor Harpell]].{{Cite polyhedron/58/The Everwinking Eye: Adventures in Maskyr's Eye|25}}

He was considered an ally of the [[Harpers]]. In face they hid away some of his vast wealth in the [[Tower of Twilight]], on the eastern side of the [[Neverwinter Wood]].

==Possessions==
Tolgar wielded a ''+3 [[mace]]'', that he has dubbed ''[[Chauntea's Fire and Sword]]'' that possessed the ability to unleash ''[[fireball]]s'' and ''[[blade barrier]]s'' once per day. He also carried dual [[ring of spell storing|rings of spell storing]].

==History==
Tolgar Anuvien became a very wealthy man after his many years of adventuring. Tolgar gained great amounts of wealth that he has stored at his house in [[Waterdeep]], the [[Dripping Dagger]] inn, and the homes of several friends, including the [[Piergeron's Palace|palace]] of [[Piergeiron the Paladinson|Piergeiron]], [[Lords of Waterdeep|Open Lord]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Rumors & Legends==
The 15{{th}} century [[paladin]] of Chauntea [[Kharissa Anuvien]] claimed that she was a descendent of the famous [[church of Chauntea|Chauntean]] adventurer.
