'''Nagda''' was a [[half-orc]] [[barbarian]] who lived in [[Neverwinter]] in the late years of the 15{{Th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Nagda had little patience for anyone that wasn't a warrior.

==Activities==
Nagda was a weaponsmith who owned an stand in the market square of the [[Protector's Enclave]]. She sold [[weapon]]s of her own make.

==History==
Nagda was a member of the [[Uthgardt]] [[Black Wolf Tribe]]. After her tribe was attacked by [[plaguechanged]] monsters, she was the only survivor. She tried to join the [[Gray Wolf Tribe]], but they rejected her due to her being a half-orc. After wandering in [[Neverwinter Wood]] for a time, she went to Neverwinter and became a weaponsmith.
