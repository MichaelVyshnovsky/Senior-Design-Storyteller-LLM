'''Larch deJun''' ({{Pronounce|LARCH|_|duh|SHOON|refs={{Cite adventurers league/City of Danger|40}}}}) was a young stable hand in [[Mulmaster]].

==Personality==
Larch was a boy with a good heart and happy with his life. He dreaded becoming a [[paladin]] of [[Tyr]].

==Relationships==
Larch was a stablehand under [[Elize]], a paladin of Tyr. He was the brother of [[Nelvon deJun]], a [[Brotherhood of the Cloak]] member.

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], at a great party in the [[Astrid Saj]] villa, Larch was kidnapped by the [[Kron]] family, who wanted revenge against his brother.{{Cite adventurers league/City of Danger|7-8}} However, Nelvon hired an adventuring party who happened to be present and they eventually saved Larch.{{Cite adventurers league/City of Danger|11}}
