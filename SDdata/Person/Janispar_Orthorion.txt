Janispar Orthorion was a noble elf of Evereska and a member of the citys fighting force known as the Swords.
In the , Janispar fought alongside Lord Dureth and the other Swords against the phaerimm at the old outpost of Rocnest. They banded together with the human archmage Khelben Arunsun, managing to survive the conflict and return to Evereska alive.
