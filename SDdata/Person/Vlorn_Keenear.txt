Vlorn Keenear was a sage who was extremely knowledgeable about halfling folklore and human politics of the Sword Coast. He was especially astute when it came to the nobility of Waterdeep.
Vlorn was known to frequently make sarcastic remarks in jest, even when working with a client.
Vlorn arrived in Waterdeep sometime around 1368 DR.
Vlorn spent much of his time at the Grinning Lion.
