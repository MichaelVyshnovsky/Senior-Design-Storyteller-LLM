Xrqnkvzk was the leader of an avolakia tribe in Undermountain in 1375 DR.
Xrqnkvzk led a tribe of avolakia worshipers of Ghaunadaur that had founded the Wormbarrow community. They offered refuge to adventurers using drow disguises in exchange for corpses as payment. They used the corpses to animate them as zombies.
