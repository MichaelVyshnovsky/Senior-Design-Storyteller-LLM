'''Xrq'nkvzk''' was the leader of an [[avolakia]] tribe in [[Undermountain]] in [[1375 DR]].

==Activities==
Xrq'nkvzk led a tribe of avolakia worshipers of [[Ghaunadaur]] that had founded the [[Wormbarrow]] community. They offered refuge to adventurers using [[drow]] disguises in exchange for corpses as payment. They used the corpses to animate them as [[zombie]]s.
