Lord '''Hardivyper''' was a nobleman from [[Cormyr]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
He attended the [[Council of the Dragon]] in late [[Mirtul]] of the {{yearlinkName|1479}}, where he was unfortunately among those that died after steel was drawn on King [[Foril Obarskyr I]].
