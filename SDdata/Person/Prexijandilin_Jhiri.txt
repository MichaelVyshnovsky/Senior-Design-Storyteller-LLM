'''Prexijandilin Jhiri''' was a [[dragonborn]] warrior member of [[Clan Prexijandilin]], and a member of the [[Lance Defenders]] in [[1479 DR]]. 

==Description==
Jhiri had crimson eyes and umber scales.

==History==
In 1479 DR, Jhiri was in charge of a detachment of Lance Defenders during the [[invasion of Tymanther]] by the [[ash giants]].
