'''Moala Adrell''' was one of the [[Stalwart Lions]] at [[Crosswinds Keep]] as of {{yearlink|1372}}.

==Description==
She was pale, lithe, and fire-haired, often keeping her hair tied back, which highlighted her cheekbones and ears. She stood at 5' 10", and weighed {{SI|135|pounds|k|long=yes}}.

==Possessions==
Her bedchambers, shared with [[Bran Oakenshire]], was furnished with elven items from across the planes.{{Cite web/Astral Encounters/Crosswinds Keep Upper Floor and Catacombs}}

==Activities==
Moala was the party member who most often handled diplomacy for [[Crosswinds Keep]]. She was also the spiritual center for the keep, and viewed every battle as a blessing from [[Chauntea]].

==Relationships==
Bran Oakenshire and her had both confessed their love for each other.

==History==
One of the Stalwart Lions, she hid her feelings for Brak Oakenshire for many years during their time as adventurers in [[Faer√ªn]]; when he confessed his feelings for her, she revealed her own rather than let him go. She received a vision from [[Chauntea]] shortly after, where she was prompted to build a safe haven in the [[Astral Plane]]. Together with the rest of the [[Stalwart Lions]], she helped found Crosswinds Keep, her leadership becoming central to the effort.
