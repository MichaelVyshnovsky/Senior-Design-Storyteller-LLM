'''Andrathath''' was a [[mage]].

==Possessions==
His mask, ''[[Andrathath's Mask]]'', was named after him.

==History==
He was known to have used all the powers of ''Andrathath's Mask'', and to have been the only user to ever do so. He was found dead and spiritless at [[Dlardrageth Keep]] at some point.

==Rumors & Legends==
Given the loss of his spirit, it was assumed the mask may have stored it.
