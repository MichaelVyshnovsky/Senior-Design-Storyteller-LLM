'''Kamshir Strongtalon''' was a [[human]] [[barbarian]] [[priest]] from [[north Faerûn]] active in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Possessions==
Kamshir was often protected by his [[hide armor]] and his enchanted ''+2'' [[battleaxe]] from the dangers of combat, and by his ''[[ring of warmth]]'' from the harsh northern climate. He also possessed an enchanted item called ''[[Murlynd's spoon]]''.

==History==
Kamshir Strongtalon was an experienced cleric of one of the barbarian tribes of the far north. He served his people well as a battle counselor and strategist for many years of his long life. His invaluable advice came from performing spirit quests and being guided by his faith.
