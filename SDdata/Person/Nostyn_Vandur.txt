Nostyn Vandur was a War Wizard of Cormyr.
In 1480 DR, Nostyn arrived with two ironlord bodyguards at Castle Irlingstar, just after Arclath Delcastle and Amarune Whitewave. He claimed to be sent to lead the investigation into the murder of seneschal Marthin Avathnar but lord steward Gelnur Farland mistrusted him.
He together with Duth Gulkanun and Imbrult Longclaws tried to stop Gelnur, Lord Delcastle and Amarune but in the fighting was killed.
