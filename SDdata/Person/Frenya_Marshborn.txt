Frenya Marshborn was an archer from the village of West Harbor in the Mere of Dead Men circa 1374 DR.
She was an energetic young woman with pale skin and brown hair. She had an intense stare as she was challenging those around her. Frenya was confident in her skills.
Frenya Marshborn was one of the villagers who lived in the rebuilt walled village of West Harbor circa the . When the town and its brewery became threatened by a duo of black dragons, Frenya began training in using a bow to protect her swamp home.
