'''Frenya Marshborn''' was an archer from the village of [[West Harbor]] in the [[Mere of Dead Men]] ''circa'' 1374 [[DR]].

{{quote|''Did you see that? A bullseye!''|Frenya Marshborn}}

==Description==
She was an energetic young woman with pale skin and brown hair. She had an intense stare as she was challenging those around her. Frenya was confident in her skills.

==History==
Frenya Marshborn was one of the villagers who lived in the rebuilt walled village of West Harbor ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1374}}. When the town and its brewery became threatened by a duo of [[black dragon]]s, Frenya began training in using a [[bow]] to protect her swamp home.
