'''Dyltharra''' was a [[half-elf]] high priestess of the temple of [[Selûne]] in [[Velprintalar]], [[Aglarond]], around [[1368 DR]]. She led six other priestesses at the temple and helped to maintain contact between [[the Simbul]], the [[Royal Council of Aglarond]], and the [[Harpers]].
