'''Xolkin Alassandar''' was the leader of the [[Seven Snakes]] mercenary group in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Xolkin and his six fellow mercenaries usually did "dirty work" for the [[Zhentarim]], in particular [[Nelkin Danniker]].

==History==
In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Xolkin plotted with the fellow Zhent [[Kella Darkhope]] to occupy the village of [[Nightstone]]. However, a [[cloud giant]] bombardment ruined Kella's plan. Xolkin and his men arrived to find the settlement ruined and infested by [[goblin]]s.

==Relationship==
Xolkin was in love with Kella Darkhope.
