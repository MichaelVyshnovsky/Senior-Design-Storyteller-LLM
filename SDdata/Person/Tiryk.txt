'''Tiryk''' was a popular [[dinosaur]]-racer in [[Port Nyanzaru]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Tiryk was the son of the merchant prince [[Ekene-Afa]] and the painter [[Kura (Chultan)|Kura]]. He was named in honor of the [[River Tiryki]].

Tiryk had a twin brother [[Soshen]], who was a member of the city guard.
