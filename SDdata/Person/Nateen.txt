Fist Nateen was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Nateen had a human man with tanned skin tone, red hair, and beard. He wore a set of chainmail armor as part of the Flaming Fist uniform, decorated with the organizations emblem on a tabard. He was armed with a halberd and was ready to use it to punctuate his stern and cruel attitude.
In the , Fist Nateen was tasked with patrolling the Lower City, near the Basilisk Gate. He and his fellow patrolmen had no qualms with shutting protesters up with force.
