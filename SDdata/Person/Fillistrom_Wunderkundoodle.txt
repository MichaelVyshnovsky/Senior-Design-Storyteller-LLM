'''Fillistrom Wunderkundoodle''' was a healer in [[Phlan]] in [[1489 DR]].

==Personality==
Fillistrom liked taking his time to do things but was a very competent healer.

==History==
Fillistrom was a healer with a shop in Phlan. He was consulted by [[Aleyd Burral]] of the [[Knights of the Black Fist]] about a rumored [[dracolich]] or [[ghost ship]]'s attack on a nearby village but found no definitive answers about the assailants.{{Cite adventurers league/Shadows over the Moonsea|10}}
