Llewan Aspenwold was a priestess of Mystra in the Harrowdale region of the Dalelands around the 14 century DR.
Llewan had a great fascination with magic tricks
In the , Llewan opened the House of Mystra in Harrowdale. However, she made very few converts and most Dalesfolk simply ignored her. Llewan wondered to do something great to arrite attention.
In the , Llewan died within her temple as it was destroyed by the blue fires of the Spellplague. Her temple would go on to be left abandoned, leaving the restless spirit of Llewan to haunt it as a ghost.
