Shestandeliath Ereshkin was a dragonborn warrior member of Clan Shestandeliath, and a rookie member of the Lance Defenders in 1487 DR.
Ereshkin was the cousin of Parvida and Zaroshni.
In 1487 DR, Ereshkin was a rookie member of the Lance Defenders. She was one of the disciples of Verthisathurgiesh Arjhani. She was also one of the first converts to the new faith of Enlil, after being convinced by Dumuzi.
