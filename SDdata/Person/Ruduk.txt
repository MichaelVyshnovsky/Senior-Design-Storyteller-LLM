'''Ruduk''' was a [[Red Wizard of Thay]] agent in [[Messemprar]] in [[Unther]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Ruduk was sent by the [[zulkir]] [[Lauzoril]] to destabilize the country in order that one day [[Thay]] could conquer it. He had great autonomy in his plans.

==Personality==
Ruduk did not truly care for the future of Unther, only to advance his position inside the Red Wizards.
