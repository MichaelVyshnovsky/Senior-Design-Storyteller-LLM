'''Nandel Greenward''' was an [[Illuskan]] [[wizard]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] and [[Undermountain]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Nandel was a student of [[Arcane magic|arcane studies]] at [[Halaster's Heirs]], a school of magic established [[Trobriand]] and [[Muiral]] who were apt past pupils of [[Halaster Blackcloak]].
