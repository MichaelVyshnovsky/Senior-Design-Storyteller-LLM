Kaaltar was a cloud giant who resided in Lyn Armaal during the late 15 century DR.
Kaaltar was  tall and weighed , equivalent in size to a muscular human male.
Kaaltar was the son of the cloud giant Countess Sansuri.
He had a twin sister named Alastrah. During their younger years the twins were given very little attention by their mother, but checked in on by their uncle Thullen, every so often.
