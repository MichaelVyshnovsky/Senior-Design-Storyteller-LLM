'''Klav Martilmur''' was the pastry chef for the [[Elfsong Tavern]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. They worked alongside a sous chef [[Azar Valsheem|Azar]], under the joyful [[Strongheart halfling|halfling]] [[Chenna Fatrabbit]].

Klav was an unsighted individual. One of the dishes they specialized in baking was bread pudding served with rich syrup.
