Stimk was a bugbear who was associated with the cult of the Absolute during the late 15 century DR.
His preferred weapon was a one-handed morningstar.
In the , cultists of the Absolute launched a terrible raid on the nearby Waukeens Rest inn. Afterwards, the cultists retired to the Shattered Sanctum temple to drink, carouse and otherwise celebrate their victory. Stimk was among the raiders who remained passed out, completely drunk, along the upper reaches of the temples courtyard.
