Grimham was a svirfneblin who was captured by the Khoroshen tribe of lizardfolk and prevented from returning to the Underdark. He was tasked with preparing the Khoroshens food, and took the prisoner Chaver Tegarech to the nearby Baeniff Tor on his raft. Grimham possessed a magically enhanced kukri.
