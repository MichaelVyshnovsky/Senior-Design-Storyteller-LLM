Dartheema was a young woman inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Tzarrakyn of the Company of Crazed Venturers married a merchants daughter named Dartheema. Dartheema perished in childbirth less than a year after Tzarrakyn died while facing a beholder. The child was named Tzarrakyn the Younger in honor of his father.
