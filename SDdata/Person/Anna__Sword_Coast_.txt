Anna was an individual lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.
Anna was in love with the fisher named Sy. The two dreamt of moving to Baldurs Gate together, a goal that helped them endure hard times during their relationship.
As of the , Anna wrote a letter to Sy, telling her partner the time was right for them to move to the big city. Anna planned to meet Sy near the "hill overlooking the old bridge", carrying only whatever he could upon his person.
Unfortuantely the two would never meet. Sy was killed when a mind flayer nautiloid came crashing down upon the shore of the River Chionthar, right where Sy and other fishers were working.
