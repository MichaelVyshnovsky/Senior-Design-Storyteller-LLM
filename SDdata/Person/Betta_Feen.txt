'''Betta Feen''' was a [[human]] barmaid at the [[Ball and Chain Tavern]] on the docks of the [[Wheloon Prison]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}

==Description==
Betta had coppery red hair in two braided pigtails. She wore a short shoulderless white dress with a dark gray corset and [[sandal]]s while working.
