King Goldander Blackenrock was the king of a svirfneblin community that lived in the region of the Underdark beneath Tethyr.
The king was in the possession of a light gem that allowed entrance into the lair of the silver dragon Adalon.
He also owned a unique mace called Skullcrusher.
In the , Blackenrocks community dug too deep into Toril and they encountered a balor demon. King Goldander sought to have the fiend destroyed and use a stoneshape scroll in order to seal the chasm.
