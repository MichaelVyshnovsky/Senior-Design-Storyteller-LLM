Eltragar was a mercenary in the late mid-14 century DR.
He was dark-skinned.
He was thought of as a warrior.
Together with Baergrim and Mheriyam, Baergrim was part of the Blades.
In , Eltragar, Mhaeriyam, and Baergrim were escorting Mhulker in Undermountain. Mhulker was seeking a particular woman to eat her brain he found her chained and gagged in an altar, but it turned out to be a trap, which Baergrim spotted by noticing that the womans gag covered her nose too. After Halaster Blackcloaks trap slew Mhulker and the ancient wizard revealed himself, the mercenaries fled.
