'''Tethirmin''' was the Archdruid of [[Mosstone]] in [[Tethyr]] in [[1358 DR]]. He was friend of the [[Company of Eight]] like most in that city.

Tethirmin also tutored [[Arkaneus Silvermane]], who succeeded him as Archdruid.
