Lucklady '''Demetria Fortunato''' was the High Priest of [[Tymora]] in [[Tymora's Holy Hall of Good Fortune and Casino]] in the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] of [[Zhentil Keep]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.

==Personality==
Demetria was very outgoing and possessed a keen intelligence, though she suffered from a short attention span.

==Activities==
Demetria oversaw the entire casino and temple. She was assisted by 24 other [[cleric]]s and assistants.
