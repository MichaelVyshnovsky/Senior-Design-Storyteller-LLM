Maastik was a drow priestess of Ghaunadaur who lived in the Underdark.
Maastik had a mane of white hair that contrasted with her night-black skin. She wore a black robe with orange and purple details.
She owned a wand of fear, as well as a cloak of protection.
As part of Ghaunadaurs clergy, Maastik could control slimes and similar creatures that fell under her gods portfolio. She often took part in live sacrifices in honor of Ghaunadaur.
