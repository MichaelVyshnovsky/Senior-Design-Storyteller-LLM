'''Maastik''' was a [[drow]] [[cleric|priestess]] of [[Ghaunadaur]] who lived in the [[Underdark]].

==Description==
Maastik had a mane of white hair that contrasted with her night-black skin. She wore a black robe with orange and purple details.

==Possessions==
She owned a ''[[wand of fear]]'', as well as a ''[[cloak of protection]]''.

==Activities==
As part of Ghaunadaur's clergy, Maastik could control [[slime]]s and similar creatures that fell under her god's portfolio. She often took part in live sacrifices in honor of Ghaunadaur.
