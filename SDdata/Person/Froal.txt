Froal was the first ambassador of Neverwinter circa 1358 DR.
Froal dressed in a long, dark blue robe that displayed the emblem of Neverwinter.
In 1358 DR, the ambassador was captured by members of the Kraken Society, who had also captured the ambassador of Mirabar, Wimeg. The Krakens bound the small man to an iron set of rings in a garden wall, but he was later saved by the Heroes of Ascore.
