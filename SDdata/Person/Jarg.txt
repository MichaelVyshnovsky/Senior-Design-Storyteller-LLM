Jarg was a mercenary of the Zhentarim that operated out of their hideout within caves deep beneath the Risen Road on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.
He was exceptionally confident and even cocky about his skill as an archer.
Jarg wielded a pair of hand crossbows, one in either hand.
When the Zhentarim hideout was ordered to be demolished in the , Jarg was among those setting up explosives.
