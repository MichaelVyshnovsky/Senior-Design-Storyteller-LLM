'''Meldrethe Klaeverdar''' was a member of the [[Klaeverdar]] family in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
She liked to "ride the storms", that is, flying into storms and hurling ''[[lightning bolt]]s'' while protected by spells of her own devising. She was also known to enjoy the act of spellcasting, and to have devised a number of bespoke spells.

==Relationships==
Meldrethe Klaeverdar of Summerfields was a possible successor to [[Gueladdeth Klaeverdar]] as of {{yearlink|1370}}.
