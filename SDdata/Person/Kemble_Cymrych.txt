'''Kemble "the Great Father" Cymrych''' was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the mid&ndash;5{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] and the first son of his predecessor, High King [[Carrig Cymrych II|Carrig II]]. His successor was his own first son, [[Tristram Cymrych]].

==History==
Kemble assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|431}}, after the death of his father.

In the [[467 DR|thirty-sixth year of his reign]], a large group of [[Tethyrian]] immigrants arrived in the Moonshae Isles.{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|26}} Kemble welcomed them,  and they settled among the [[Ffolk]]. This introduced [[Faerûnian pantheon|mainland religion]] to the Ffolk as well as [[Faerûn]]ian methods of architecture and engineering for building strong defensive fortifications.<ref name="Dra.362-p26 />"

Kemble ruled for fifty-one years before dying of natural causes in the {{YearlinkName|482}}.
