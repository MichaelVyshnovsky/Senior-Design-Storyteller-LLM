'''Myrelas the Gallant''' was the leader of the [[Peerless Champions]] of [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]], as of [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|13-14}}

==Description==
Myrelas was a male [[doppleganger]]. He was flamboyant and well mannered, and liked to be finely dressed at all occasions. He was noble, valiant, and chivalrous to a fault, even in situations where his life was at a stake.

Myrelas wore a [[leather]] [[cuirass]] and a large plumed hat. He also carried an extravagant [[rapier]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Pyrophobia|7}}

==Relationships==
Myrelas was the husband of [[Iphimedra]].

==Activities==
Myrelas was the leader of the Peerless Champions, a troupe of "bravos" (blademasters) or vigilantes that patrolled the city of Sambral.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|13-14}}

Myrelas was the self-proclaimed rival of [[Davien Mecatoris]].

==History==
In 1479 DR, Iphimedra was kidnapped by the [[Cult of Reborn Flame]] to be a sacrifice to the [[primordial]] [[Achazar]]. Myrelas joined forces with a band of [[adventurer]]s to rescue her and to stop the cult for good.

After that, Myrelas joined the adventurers and helped them in their fights against the cult in [[Harglast]] and [[Ramekho]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Pyrophobia|4-5}}

In [[1480 DR]], they faced [[Inar Venenellin|Pyrultimus]], but were defeated and Pyrultimus cursed them with madness. A few weeks later, they went to [[Tarmalune]] seeking help from [[Halagothra|Halagothra the Healer]], who was unable to help them.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Pyrophobia|21}}

After having being cured, Myrelas joined forces again with the adventurers to defeat Pyrultimus, this time for good.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Quench the Fire of the Raging God|40-48}}

After having defeated the cult, Myrelas and Iphimedra married.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|45}}
