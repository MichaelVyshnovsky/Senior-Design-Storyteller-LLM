'''Elénaril''' was an [[elven]] princess of [[Myth Drannor]] during the 8{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
She was married to [[Ederan Nharimlur]] who was the [[High Mage (Silverymoon)|High Mage]] of [[Silverymoon]] at the time. She had one daughter with him named [[Amaara Nharimlur|Amaara]], who would succeed her father as High Mage.
