'''Mordoc Torsilley''' was the captain of the elite [[Company of the White Dragon]] of the [[City Guard (Waterdeep)|City Guard]] of [[Waterdeep]] during the [[Time of Troubles]] in [[1358 DR]].

When the hordes of [[Myrkul]] invaded the city, he was ordered by [[Piergeiron]] to enlist the help of [[Khelben Arunsun]] and fight the undead.{{cite book/Waterdeep|328}}
