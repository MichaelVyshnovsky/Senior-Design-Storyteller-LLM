'''Anom''' was an [[elf]], and a [[Nentyar hunter]] in the service of the [[Nentyarch]] in {{yearlink|1373}}. He dressed all in brown cloth, and carried a staff of dark wood. Though the Nentyarch had assigned him to accompany [[Marrec]] and [[Elowen]]'s party in their quest to confront the [[Rotting Man]],{{cite book/Lady of Poison|127|13}} [[Fallon]]'s betrayal forced the party to depart posthaste, without him.{{cite book/Lady of Poison|135-136|14}}
