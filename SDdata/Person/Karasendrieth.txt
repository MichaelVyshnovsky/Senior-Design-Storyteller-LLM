'''Karasendrieth''' was a female adult [[song dragon]] who lived in [[Impiltur]] around [[1374 DR]].

==Description==
As a song dragon, Kara was able to take the form of a beautiful, willowy, silver-haired [[human]]. In her draconic form, she was a slender [[dragon]] with silvery blue scales.

==Abilities==
Karasendrieth could cast ''[[suppress breath weapon]]'', ''[[Snilloc's snowball swarm]]'',{{cite book/The Rage|54|3}} ''[[flame dagger]]''{{cite book/The Rage|57|3}} and ''[[remove paralysis]]''{{cite book/The Rage|58|3}}. She could also almost certainly employ ''[[mirror image]]''{{cite book/The Rage|134|9}} and a spell that softened the scales of other wyrms, almost certainly ''[[scale weakening]]''.{{cite book/The Rage|135|9}}. She also had a spell that closely matched ''[[scintillating sphere]]''.{{cite book/The Rage|200|13}} She knew a spell to hide her belongings from observation,{{cite book/The Rage|243|17}} and almost certainly ''[[shield]]''{{cite book/The Rage|244|17}} as well as ''[[fireball]]''{{cite book/The Rage|246|17}} and likely ''[[wall of fog]]''.{{cite book/The Rage|247|17}} One of her spells, when cast underwater, caused ice to expand on the enemy; the spell may have been ''[[ice storm]]''.{{cite book/The Rage|311|20}} Another spell Kara had cast was ''[[darkvision]]''.{{cite book/The Rage|92|1}} One of her spells used a sonic effect to shatter the ground, possibly ''[[shatterfloor]]''.{{cite book/The Rage|106|5}} She also knew a spell akin to ''[[knock]]'',{{cite book/The Rite|110|5}} one for thunderbolts, almost certainly ''[[lightning bolt]]'', and another that created obstacles made of ice, possibly ''[[wall of ice]]''.{{cite book/The Rite|191|8}} She also knew a spell akin to ''[[unseen servant]]''{{cite book/The Rite|250|11}} and eventually learned ''[[abate dracorage]]''.{{cite book/The Rite|252|11}}

Karasendrieth could cast a compulsion to force a creature to follow a simple command.{{cite book/The Rite|67|3}} Karasendrieth could cast a spell that injured foes through sound. She also had a spell that cloaked her in a protective aura of light.{{cite book/The Rite|68|3}} At one time she cast ''[[invisibility]]'',{{cite book/The Ruin|201|9}} ''[[magic missile]]'',{{cite book/The Ruin|271-272|12}} and finally, alongside a sonic attack, a spell resembling ''[[solid fog]]''.{{cite book/The Ruin|270|12}}

==Relationships==
She and [[Dorn Graybrook]] became lovers.

==History==
Karasendrieth was born in the forests of [[Cormanthor]] around [[1273 DR]]. She spend her first century traveling across the north to spread her songs and to help any innocents she found in her travels.

She participated in the effort to rid [[Faerûn]] of [[Sammaster]]'s [[Rage of Dragons]] during [[1373 DR]].
