Minda Obarskyr (born Minda Bleth) was the sister of Mondar Bleth and settled on the land that would become known as Suzail in 15 DR. She had a secret relationship with Ondeth Obarskyr after his wife had left him that was eventually exposed.
Minda had very dark hair and a pretty face. She was quite tall.
Minda had a brother, Mondar Bleth, who was killed by elves in 16 DR. Her nephew was Mondars youngest son, Arphoind Bleth.
Minda came to the new settlement on the future site of Suzail in 15 DR. She later married Ondeth.
