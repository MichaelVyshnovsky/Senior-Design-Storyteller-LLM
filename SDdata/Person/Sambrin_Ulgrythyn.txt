'''Sambrin Ulgrythyn''' was a sage of the city of [[Sammaresh]] in [[Lapaliiya]] in the 14th century [[DR]], holding the title of "Lord Sage".

Sambrin wrote a book titled ''[[The View from Stormwind Hill]]'', which was published in [[1341 DR]].{{Cite web/The Candlekeep Collection}}
