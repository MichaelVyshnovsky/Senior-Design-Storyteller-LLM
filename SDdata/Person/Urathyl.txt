Urathyl was the Councilor of Zhentil Keeps Council of Lords in 1368 DR.
Urathyl was loyal to Manshoon and the Zhentarim but also supported the Church of Bane.
Urathyls family profited from festhall and vine trade. He was appointed to the Council under Manshoon and proved very capable in his role. Urathyl did all the amministrative works of the Council. Urathyl seldom  "creative solutions" to resolve troubles, like creating false documents. Urathyl supervised all the Councils meetings.
Urathyl collected monthly fees from all council members within the High Hall. The fees were based upon the individual wealth of each council member.
His estate was located in the western section of Zhentil Keeps Inner Quarter just off of Imperceptor Street.
