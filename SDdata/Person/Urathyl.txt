'''Urathyl''' was the Councilor of [[Zhentil Keep]]'s [[Council of Lords (Zhentil Keep)|Council of Lords]] in [[1368 DR]].

==Personality==
Urathyl was loyal to Manshoon and the [[Zhentarim]] but also supported the [[Church of Bane]].

==History==
Urathyl's family profited from festhall and vine trade. He was appointed to the Council under [[Manshoon]] and proved very capable in his role. Urathyl did all the amministrative works of the Council. Urathyl seldom  "creative solutions" to resolve troubles, like creating false documents. Urathyl supervised all the Council's meetings.

Urathyl collected monthly fees from all council members within the [[High Hall (Zhentil Keep)|High Hall]]. The fees were based upon the individual wealth of each council member.

His estate was located in the western section of Zhentil Keep's [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]] just off of [[Imperceptor Street]].
