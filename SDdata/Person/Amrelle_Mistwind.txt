Lady Amrelle Mistwind was a human female.
Amrelle was stunningly beautiful and had dark swirling hair that matched her eyes. A gem-studded wyvern tattoo graced her upper thigh. She was not in the habit of wearing undergarments.
Amrelle was involved in the Rightful Conspiracy against the crown, and a member of the very private noble house, Mistwind.
