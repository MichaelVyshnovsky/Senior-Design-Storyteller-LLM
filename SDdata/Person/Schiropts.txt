'''Schiropts''' was a tavern owner of the [[Bat's Roost]] in [[Skullport]].

==Description==
The [[wizard]] was a pale skinned man.

==History==
Around [[1366 DR]], Schiropts won the Bat's Roost tavern after his [[bat]] [[familiar]] won a fighting tournament.
