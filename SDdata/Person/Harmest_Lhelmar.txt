'''Harmest Lhelmar''' was an animal trainer who lived in the independent [[Moonsea]] city of [[Hillsfar]] during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Services==
Harmest was available on retainer, or could be hired to assist with taming an individual animal. The cost for these services was 40 [[gp]] per month, or 3 gp per individually tamed beast.
