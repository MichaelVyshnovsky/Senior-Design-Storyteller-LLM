'''Gracie''' was a server who worked in the City of Coin, [[Athkatla]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Whatever the Thumb tells you about me, don't believe a word.''|Renfeld}}

==Description==
Gracie was a flirtations and good-looking [[human]] woman with light blonde hair.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Gracie was employed by [[The Thumb]], the innkeeper of the [[Sea's Bounty Tavern]]. The establishment was located in Athkatla's [[Athkatla/Wave District|Wave District]]. One of notable patron whom Gracie served was the notorious [[Red Wizards of Thay]] named [[Edwin Odesseiron]].
