Gracie was a server who worked in the City of Coin, Athkatla in the late 14 century DR.
Gracie was a flirtations and good-looking human woman with light blonde hair.
In the , Gracie was employed by The Thumb, the innkeeper of the Seas Bounty Tavern. The establishment was located in Athkatlas Wave District. One of notable patron whom Gracie served was the notorious Red Wizards of Thay named Edwin Odesseiron.
