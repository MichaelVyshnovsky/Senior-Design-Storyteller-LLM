Ben, or Ben the Brewer was a herbalist living on the outskirts of Luskan.
While working as healer for the farmers of Luskan, Ben was called by Meg to heal the poisoned Dahlia Sinfelle, injured by a poisoned attack from Beniago of Ship Kurth.
