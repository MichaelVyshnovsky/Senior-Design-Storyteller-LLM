'''Ben''', or '''Ben the Brewer''' was a herbalist living on the outskirts of [[Luskan]].

==History==
While working as healer for the farmers of Luskan, Ben was called by [[Meg]] to heal the poisoned [[Dahlia Sin'felle]], injured by a poisoned attack from [[Beniago]] of [[Ship Kurth]].
