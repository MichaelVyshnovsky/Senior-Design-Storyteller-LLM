Elliot Graves was a Harper and the servant and close confident of Loene in Waterdeep.
Graves was a skilled soldier and chef.
In 1361 DR, Graves was living and working in Loenes townhouse in the Castle Ward near Castle Waterdeep. After Arilyn Moonblade showed up in tow with an injured Danilo Thann, Graves was targeted and killed by the Harper Assassin.
