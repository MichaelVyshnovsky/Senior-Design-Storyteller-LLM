Vildo was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Sometime before the , Vildo followed his uncles example and joined the Flaming Fist of Baldurs Gate. When the Absolute crisis struck the Gate, Vildo received his long-awaited promotion to the rank of Gauntlet, of which he eagerly informed his now-retired uncle via a letter. The nephew and his uncle were to meet at the Singing Lute in the Lower City to celebrate Vildos promotion and watch the scenic sunset over the Seatower of Balduran. Vildo promised to get the "old man" back to his home before the uncles bedtime.
