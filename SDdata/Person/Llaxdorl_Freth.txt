Llaxdorl Freth was a drow wizard of House Freth inside Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.
As a cousin of Erelal Freth, Llaxdorl was the gatekeeper to Spiderwatch Keep, the main Freth headquarters around the Year of Three Ships Sailing, 1492 DR, in the Maze Level together with some guards. He reported directly to Rilna Freth.
