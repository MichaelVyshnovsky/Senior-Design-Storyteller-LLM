'''Kurzen Brewhammer''' was a [[dwarven]] warrior and [[adventurer]] and a member of the [[Blue Wyverns]] [[adventuring company]] of [[Ravens Bluff]] in the [[Vast]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite digital book/Prince of Ravens/Kindle|2833|8}}

When not adventuring, Kurzen helped his father, [[Tharzon Brewhammer]], run the [[Smoke Wyrm]] [[tavern]].{{Cite digital book/Prince of Ravens/Kindle|1322|5}} Kurzen was knowledgeable about the ancient dwarven ruins of [[Sarbreen]] beneath Ravens Bluff, knowledge that proved invaluable during the Blue Wyverns' mission to retrieve the [[Sarkonagael]], a tome of [[shadow magic]].{{Cite digital book/Prince of Ravens/Kindle|2552|8}}
