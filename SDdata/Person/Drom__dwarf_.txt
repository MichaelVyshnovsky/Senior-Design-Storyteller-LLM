Drom was a dwarf fighter worshiper of Helm and a member of Order of the Gauntlet in 1489 DR.
Drom believed that you could fix problems with a big hammer.
He was the Gauntlets delegate to help a hired adventuring party to defeat the great dragon Claugiyliamatar in Kryptgarden Forest. The objective of Droms order was to destroy the Cult of the Dragons contingent without warning the green dragon and to destroy all undead raised by Thayan necromancers.
