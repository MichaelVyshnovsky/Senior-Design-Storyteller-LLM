Baen Chalgash was a wererat and a high-ranking member of the Dead Rats gang in Neverwinter.
The wererat was the brother and lieutenant of Varin Chalgash.
Baen was involved in the kidnap of the Nashers leader Gothwan Cooper, but was killed by an adventurer sent to free Gothwan.
