'''Scarback''' was a young [[tyrannosaurus]] who competed in the [[dinosaur races]] of [[Port Nyanzaru]] in [[Chult]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
As of the start of the [[death curse]],{{ToA-time}} the young Scarback was on the verge of retirement. Going into its purported final race, bookies were giving it only 1:2 odds of winning.{{Cite adventurers league/A Day at the Races|20}}
