Mannock was the Captain of the Watch of the local militia in the village of Deadsnows, as well as a member of the towns council, during the late 1300s DR. He served Lady Arletha Icespear as she oversaw justice for lawbreakers, whether their crimes were petty or more severe.
Captain Mannock was outfitted with a masterwork breastplate and equipped with a +1 magical longsword.
