Naurorin was one of the three war-leaders of the Tornskulls tribe of orcs that lived within the Dead Orc Pass during the 14 century DR.
Naurorin was supremely fast in battle and as such, led the tribes raids during daytime hours.
Naurorin enjoyed a strong rivalry with his fellow war-leader Koront.
