'''Naurorin''' was one of the three war-leaders of the [[Tornskulls]] tribe of [[orc]]s that lived within the [[Dead Orc Pass]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Abilities==
Naurorin was supremely fast in battle and as such, led the tribe's raids during daytime hours.

==Relationships==
Naurorin enjoyed a strong rivalry with his fellow war-leader [[Koront]].

==History==
Naurorin, Koront and [[Baerzel]] came into power after the death of the tribe's previous leader, [[Glorlarkh|Chief Glorlarkh]] died around the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.
