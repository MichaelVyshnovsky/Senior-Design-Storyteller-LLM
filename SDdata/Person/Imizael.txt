'''Imizael''' was a innkeeper in [[Phlan]] during the [[Tyranny of Dragons]].

==Personality==
Imizael was an efficient tavern-keeper, always attentive to patrons' needs. However, she did not like to talk with customers, responding to polite questions and directing all customers wanting speak with her to the waiter, [[Markoth]].{{Cite adventurers league/The Courting of Fire|9-10}}

==History==
Imizael was born to a mother obsessed with [[elf|elves]], even giving her a clearly elven name, despite the fact that they were completely [[human]].

Imizael managed the [[Laughing Goblin Inn]] in Phlan, helped by Markoth.{{Cite adventurers league/Tyranny in Phlan|7}}
