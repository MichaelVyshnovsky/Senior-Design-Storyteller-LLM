Klarynda Ballasdreia, nicknamed The Lady of Masks, was a successful perfume merchant who resided in Athkatlas Temple District in the late 14 century DR.
Klarynda Ballasdreia was a well-known socialite, breaker of hearts, and a schemer of Athkatlas high society.
