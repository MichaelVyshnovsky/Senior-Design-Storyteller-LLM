'''Marlus Eirontalar''' was a [[noble]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]], City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Marlus was the patriarch of the [[Eirontalar]] [[Nobility of Waterdeep|noble family]] of Waterdeep. He lived within [[Eirontalar Villa]] on [[Diamond Street]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]].{{Cite web enhancement/Noble Houses of Waterdeep|3}}

==Relationships==
His consort was [[Chalan Eirontalar]] and his eldest son and heir was [[Silas Eirontalar|Silas]].
