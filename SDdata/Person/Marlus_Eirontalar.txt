Marlus Eirontalar was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Marlus was the patriarch of the Eirontalar noble family of Waterdeep. He lived within Eirontalar Villa on Diamond Street in Waterdeeps Sea Ward.
His consort was Chalan Eirontalar and his eldest son and heir was Silas.
