Savahn was a high-ranking monk of the Monastery of the Yellow Rose in the Bloodstone Lands in 1484 DR.
Savahn assisted Perrywinkle Shin. In 1484 DR, Savahn observed Perrywinkle guiding Jarlaxle and the two copper dragon sisters Tazmikella and Ilnezhara to the presence of the immote grandmaster Kane and the awakening of the grandmaster.
