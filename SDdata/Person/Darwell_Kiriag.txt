'''Darwell Kiriag''' was a descendant of [[Dunman Kiriag]] and the proprietor of [[The Lonesome Tankard Inn and Tavern]] in the town of [[Eveningstar]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
In the late 15{{th}} century DR, Darwell was a stout middle-aged [[human]] man with a light skin tone, brown hair, and well-trimmed facial hair.

==History==
In the 15{{th}} century DR, Darwell Kiriag could be seen welcoming guests and adventurers who set out to investigate the nearby ruins of the [[Haunted Halls]].
