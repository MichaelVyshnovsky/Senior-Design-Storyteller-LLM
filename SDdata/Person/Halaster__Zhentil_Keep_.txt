Lord '''Halaster''' was a a lesser noble of [[Zhentil Keep]] and a puppet member of its Council of Lords in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=RoZK-p64>

==History==
As a young man, Halaster was an able [[Zhentilar]] commander and fought many battles against bandits and the [[goblinoid]]s of [[Thar]].

He was one of the few lords to survive the [[Fall of Zhentil Keep]] in 1368 DR.

==Activities==
By [[1369 DR]], he owned many buildings in the Foreign Quarter and had interests near [[Tesh]].

On the Council of Lords, Halaster usually supported [[Manshoon]] and the [[Zhentarim]] in exchange for Zhentish assistance in [[Teshendale]]. He was a skilled politician and was usually chosen as envoy to nearby cities.

Halaster's estate was located in the north section of Zhentil Keep's [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]] on the [[Street of the Gods]].
