Lord Halaster was a a lesser noble of Zhentil Keep and a puppet member of its Council of Lords in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
As a young man, Halaster was an able Zhentilar commander and fought many battles against bandits and the goblinoids of Thar.
He was one of the few lords to survive the Fall of Zhentil Keep in 1368 DR.
By 1369 DR, he owned many buildings in the Foreign Quarter and had interests near Tesh.
On the Council of Lords, Halaster usually supported Manshoon and the Zhentarim in exchange for Zhentish assistance in Teshendale. He was a skilled politician and was usually chosen as envoy to nearby cities.
Halasters estate was located in the north section of Zhentil Keeps Inner Quarter on the Street of the Gods.
