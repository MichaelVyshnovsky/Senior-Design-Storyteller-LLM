'''Antola Pilark''' was a [[halfling]] [[Druid|Great Druid]] and an ally of House [[Kendrick]] in the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|4}}

==Activities==
Antola led troops from House Kendrick against the spreading evil of the [[goblin]] king [[Gark]]'s minions within the [[Dernall Forest]] near [[Caer Callidyrr]].
