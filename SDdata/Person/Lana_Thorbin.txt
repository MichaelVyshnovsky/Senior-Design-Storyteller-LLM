'''Lana Thorbin''' was a novice [[human]] [[wizard]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1992|65|Lana Thorbin}}

==Description==
Lana Thorbin was often complemented on her beauty and intelligence, which gave her confidence at an early age. She used her looks and her smarts to manipulate infatuated men into aiding her and she simply outsmarted the rest. Regardless of that manipulative streak, she always made effort to be a good person.

==Possessions==
Lana was armed with a simple [[dagger]].

==History==
She discovered her talent with the arcane at an early age and chose to pursue a career as a [[wizard]]. She discarded all other attachments to concentrate on the studies.
