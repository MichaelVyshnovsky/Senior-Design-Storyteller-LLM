'''Ahmad al-Rahib''' was a [[moralist]] and member of the [[League Conclave]] in [[I'tiraf]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Originally from [[Hilm]], Ahmad was elected to lead the group of delegates from that city to represent them within the League Conclave. Ahmad and his fellow delegates ensured Hilm's best interests were heard at court.
