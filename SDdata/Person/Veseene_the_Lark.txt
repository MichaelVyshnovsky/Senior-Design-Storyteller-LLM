'''Veseene the Lark''' was a songwriter who produced the ballad "[[Two Sisters]]" in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In [[1360 DR]], Veseene presented her work "Two Sisters" to High Moonmistress [[Dhauna Myritar]] of [[Moonshadow Hall]] in [[Yhaunn]].
