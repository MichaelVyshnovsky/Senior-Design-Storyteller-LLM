Varin Chalgash was a wererat thug and a high-ranking member of the Dead Rats gang in Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.
Varin was an elite defender of the Dead Rats lair in the Blacklake Sewers beneath Blacklake District.
Varin was much larger and bulkier than regular wererats, probably having come from a larger non-human race. Varin wore ornate golden-and-teal-hued armor.
In the , Varin and four regular wererats defended a passage into their sewer lair against an adventurer sent to free Gothwan Cooper, a Nashers thief whom the Dead Rats had kidnapped. Varin was slain in the confrontation.
