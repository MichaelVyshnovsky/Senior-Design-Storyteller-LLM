Lady '''Polaris''' was the [[archwizard]] leader of [[Delia]] in the [[Netheril|Netherese Empire]] during the [[Shadowed Age]].

==Description==
The [[Netherese]] female was attractive with golden skin and snow white hair.  However as Shadowed Age reached its cresendo her once thin and defined frame mutated to obesity.<ref>[https://ia800701.us.archive.org/10/items/netheril-empire-of-magic/Netheril%20Empire%20of%20Magic.pdf Netheril Empire of Magic]

==Relationships==
The [[mentalist]] enjoyed bossing around her [[wikipedia:steward (office)|steward]], [[Candlemas]], and her [[wikipedia:chamberlain (office)|chamberlain]], [[Sysquemalyn]].

==History==
''Circa'' {{Yearlink|-700}}, Lady Polaris was concerned with a blight known as rust appearing on the wheat crops of her people.

Around {{Yearlink|-339}}, Lady Polaris succumbed to fear and perished in her castle during the [[Karsus's Folly|End of the End]].
