Lady Polaris was the archwizard leader of Delia in the Netherese Empire during the Shadowed Age.
The Netherese female was attractive with golden skin and snow white hair.  However as Shadowed Age reached its cresendo her once thin and defined frame mutated to obesity.[https//ia800701.us.archive.org/10/items/netheril-empire-of-magic/Netheril%20Empire%20of%20Magic.pdf Netheril Empire of Magic]
The mentalist enjoyed bossing around her steward, Candlemas, and her chamberlain, Sysquemalyn.
Circa , Lady Polaris was concerned with a blight known as rust appearing on the wheat crops of her people.
