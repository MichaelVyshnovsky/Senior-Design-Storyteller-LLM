Primate Oumrant Tsulkide was the head of the temple fortress known as the Tower of Swift Death.
As a Primate, Oumrants role was to oversee the ongoings of fortress under his command, the Tower of Swift Death in Thay. Within, the Primates murderers and priests were trained in the tower, and often sent on missions to aid the Red Wizards of Thay.
In , the temple under Oumrant Tsulkides care converted from the worship of Bhaal, to that of Cyric.
