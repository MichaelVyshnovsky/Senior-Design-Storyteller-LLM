'''Udall Granitecrusher''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] warrior.

==Description==
A heavily muscled man, Udall had a short, brown beard with no mustache. He clad himself in [[chainmail]] and leather, and wore a metal [[helmet]].

==Abilities==
He was noted for the force with which he could swing a weapon.

==Possessions==
Aside from his mundane suit of chain mail, Udall owned several pieces of [[magic]]al equipment: a ''[[shield|shield +4]]'', an ''[[Axe of Hurling|axe of hurling +5]]'', a pair of ''[[gauntlets of ogre power]]'', a ''[[ring of fire resistance]]'', a ''[[gem of seeing]]'', and a ''[[bag of holding]]''.

==Activites==
Udall was fond of adventuring, and spent most of his spare time practicing combat with his friend [[Vasos Flameslayer]].
