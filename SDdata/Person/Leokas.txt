'''Ranger Leokas''' was a mercenary who worked for [[Company Yargo]] aboard the ''[[Buccaneer's Tear]]'' during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
When the ''Buccaneer's Tear'' soared up to [[Pirates' Skyhold]] above the [[Sea of Swords]] in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Leokas was among the first mercenaries to establish a beachhead on the [[earthmote]]. He worked with a [[Hero of Sleeping Dragon Bridge|hired adventurer]] to eradicate some of the local [[lizardfolk]] that threatened the expedition from the nearby [[Vilebog]] swamp the swamp.
