Iyrindyl was a Sharran faithful during the mid-8 century DR.
He was a bald man with purple and black robes. Also, he was the youngest of his peers.
As a dreadspells, he wielded borrowed arcane power.
While the Sharran woodsman Uldus Blackram was leading the Sharran dreadspells to ruins within the Dead Place during the Chaos of the Art of the , with a noose tied to his throat, they came across a hostile elven mage, Ilbryn Starym. Iyrindyl was struck down by a tree that fell due to the wild magic raised during the ensuing spell-battle Femter Deldrannus, a fellow Dreadspell, confirmed he was dead after the fight.
