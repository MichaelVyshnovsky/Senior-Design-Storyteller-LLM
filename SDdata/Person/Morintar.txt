Lady '''Morintar''' was the leading priestess of [[Lliira]] in the [[Vilhon Reach]] in [[1370 DR]].
 
==History==
Morintar gained prominence when, just after the [[Time of Troubles]], she managed to convert a number of wealthy merchants.
