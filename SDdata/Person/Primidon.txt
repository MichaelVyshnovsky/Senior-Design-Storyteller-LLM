'''Primidon''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and inventor of [[fire]]-based [[spell]]s who lived during the [[Golden Age of Netheril]].

==History==
The fire mage developed five spells during their life. The spells they invented were ''[[flaming sphere]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1892|NY}}, ''[[flame arrow]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1900|NY}}, ''[[incendiary cloud]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1912|NY}}, ''[[pyrotechnics]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1918|NY}}, and lastly, ''[[burning hands]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1950|NY}}.
