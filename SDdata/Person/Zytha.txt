Zytha, who was also called the Lame because of an injury suffered in battle a decade ago, was a Rashemaar stablemaster inhabiting Immilmar in Rashemen in the .
Zythas stable was located just south of the Huhrongs Citadel near the center of Immilmar. He cared for horses at a rate of 1 cp per night. Zytha also sold sturdy Rashemaar ponies that were better suited for Rashemens harsh climate for 150 gp each.
