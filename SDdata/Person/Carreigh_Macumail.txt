Carreigh Macumail was one of the few humans in Evermeet in 1367 DR.
Born in the Moonshae Isles, Carreigh was for a long time a pirate in the Sea of Fallen Stars, first under Captain Delvar Corzon and afterward with his own ship, the Mist-Walker.
After relocating to Evermeet, he patrolled the Sword Coast under the orders of Queen Amlaruil Moonflower.
Carreigh was a good man with a good sense of humor. Most people underestimated his intelligence but Carreigh learned not to care. He was a skilled sailor and warrior.
Carreigh was a close friend of the elf druid Aerilaya and thanks to her he also became friends with the queen.
