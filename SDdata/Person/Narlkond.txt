Narlkond was a Sharran priest during the mid–8 century DR.
He had noticeable ritual scarring on his forearms, and when in ritual, he wore a mantle of shards between his legs, drawing blood with every step.
He was a senior priest at the House of Holy Night.
Dread Sister Avroana wasnt as confident as Narlkond was in his scheme.
In early spring of the , Narlkond had magically sent for many other priests of Shar after trapping an avatar of Mystra—Dasumia, the Queen of Galadorna—and discovering that through mass ritual human sacrifice, he could entrap the avatar and siphon away its power to grant his own priests the spells of wizards. They agreed to supply him with Sharran faithful.
