Selphiras Elvenstar was a squire from the city of Ravens Bluff in the late 14 century DR.
In Marpenoth of the , Leyland - Nightstalker wedding took place. Dame Jenilynn Leyland, a Knight of the Right Hand of Tyr, and Sir Sarren Nightstalker, a Knight of the Griffon, were united in a social gala that was reported on the pages of the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter. Squire Selphiras Elvenstar was one of the brides attendants during the ceremony. It was reported that the wedding was scheduled for an earlier date, but Jenilynn Leyland was delayed due to her mission to the Beastlands and being transformed into a Unicorn.
