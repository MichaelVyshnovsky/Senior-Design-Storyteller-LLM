'''Tam''' was a junior [[priestess]] of [[Selûne]] at [[Moonshadow Hall]] in [[Yhaunn]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
As an inexperienced and young priestess, Tam was quite unnerved by the events at Moonshadow Hall, and rather easily cowed and led by more authoritative people.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1373}}, Tam assisted the senior priestess [[Velsinore]] in her duties at Moonshadow Hall.
