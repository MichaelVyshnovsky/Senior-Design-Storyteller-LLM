Swift Elyan was a halfling adventurer in the Dalelands.
Swift was an expert in traps and locks. She was a member of the adventuring party Byars Seven until most of its members went missing during an adventure at Semberholme.
In 1479 DR, Swift was with her friend Dorring Brightaxe until they separated after an argument. She decided to accompany the caravan of Nyssa Seebenstern.
