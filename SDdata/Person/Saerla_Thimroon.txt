[[Saerla Thimroon]] was a sage of [[Innarlith]] in the late mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
She was older than [[Talatha Vaerovree]].

==Personality==
She grew to hate and fear Talatha's talent.

==Abilities==
By her own estimation, she was weaker magically than Talatha Vaerovree.

==Activities==
She collected information and cast a few minor spells of charlatanry. Her areas of knowledge included Talatha Vaerovree, another local sorceress.

==Relationships==
She was happy to undermine Talatha Vaerovree.
