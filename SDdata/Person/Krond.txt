'''Krond''' was a [[half-orc]] [[eldritch knight]] of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He had grey skin and a black ponytail tied with a blue hair tie. He had a long scar that ran across his face. He was dressed in leathers with blue details, and had a blue [[scarf (clothing)|scarf]] attached to it. He carried a book attached to his belt.

==Abilities==
As an experienced eldritch knight, Krond had learned how to perform an [[arcane charge]].

==Possessions==
Krond carried a bonded [[warhammer]] that he had named [[Unity (warhammer)|Unity]].

==History==
Krond was born to an academic [[human]] [[wizard]]ess and a vicious chieftain of an [[orc]]ish tribe that her mother was researching. As such he knew of both magic and warfare since a young age. Though he started his training as a warrior, after his mother died in a [[goblin]] raid he started focusing more on arcane arts.
