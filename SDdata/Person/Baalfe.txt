Baalfe was a hireling guard who worked for Baldurs Mouth in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Baalfe was a human woman with light skin, dark brown eyes, and greying brown hair she wore gathered in a loose bun. Like her fellow guards, Baalfe wore chainmail armor, carried a simple shield, and was armed with a mace.
In the , Baalfe was employed by Baldurs Mouth and its editor-in-chief, Ettvard Needle. She was tasked with guarding the broadsheets main entrance from unwanted visitors and critics of the gazettes editorial direction. Despite Baalfes disdain for the "writer types," she still preferred working in front of the Baldurs Mouth to guarding the rancid fish stench-ridden docks.
