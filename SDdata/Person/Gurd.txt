Brawler '''Gurd''' was a  [[goblin]] [[cult of the Absolute|cultist]] who lived in the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He wore a set of crude [[leather armor]], a simple loincloth, and [[bracer]]s adored with pointed bones. His face was covered in a leather mask under a cloth good.

==Personality==
Gurd was among the few cultists of [[the Absolute]] at the Shattered Sanctum who remained focused on their duties and was not easily distracted.

==Possessions==
He carried with him a savage-looking goblin [[scimitar]].

==Activities==
Gurd was among the Absolute's warriors who was ready to assault the [[Emerald Grove]] enclave, once [[True Soul]] [[Minthara Baenre]] gave the order.
