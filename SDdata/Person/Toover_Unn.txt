Toover Unn was a wererat and a member of the Dead Rats gang in Neverwinter.
Toover was a wererat merchant of the Dead Rats, living in the sewers of the Protectors Enclave. An adventurer met him during a mission and asked why he was not hostile like his fellows. Toover explained that he was a well-known coward. Instead, he offered to do business with the adventurer.
