'''Nuri al-Zaratani''' was an [[amiq rasol]] living on a [[zaratan]] in the waters of the [[Great Sea]] near the [[Corsair Domains]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Nuri was 6 ft. (1.8 m) tall.

==Activities==
Nuri was the self-proclaimed Caliph of an island formed from the exposed shell of a floating zaratan. He defended his claims against [[sahuagin]] living in the area.
