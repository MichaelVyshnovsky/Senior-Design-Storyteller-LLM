'''Alukk Dannath''' was a [[human]] man of the city of [[Yartar]] on the [[Savage Frontier]] in [[northwest Faerûn]].

==Description==
Dannath was a short, red-haired man with a bristly beard. He used two thick [[monocle]]s to see.

He was a proprietor of [[Dannath's Pickles, Nuts, & Foods]], which sold foods for travellers in the North. He ran the store with the aid of this three strong, quiet daughters. One of his daughters was adopted, but all three were skilled [[fighter]]s and [[Harpers]].
