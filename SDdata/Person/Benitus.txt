Brother '''Benitus''' was a male [[human]] and a [[Cleric]] of [[Helm]]. He was a prelate of the [[Golden Fortress]] in [[Eshpurta]], [[Amn]], He, along with younger priests of each faith, oversaw the chapels to [[Helm]], [[Tempus]], [[Torm]], and [[Tyr]] that existed within the Golden Fortress.
