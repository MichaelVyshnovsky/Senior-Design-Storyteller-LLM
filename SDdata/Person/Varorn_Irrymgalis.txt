Varorn Irrymgalis was an elf steward in Myth Drannor.
Varorn was the Steward of the Southern Gate of the city.
In 1487 DR, Storm Silverhand, Arclath Delcastle, and Amarune Whitewave were conducted in front of Varorn in order to decide if they could enter in the city. Storm tried to argue with him. In the end, it was Fflar Starbrow Melruth himself who decided to admit them.
Varorn was the son of Orblyn Irrymgalis.
