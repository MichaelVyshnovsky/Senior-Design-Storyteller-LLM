Bael An Ashok was the proprietor of the Grand Emporium in Neverwinter during the late 15 century DR.
Bael spent much of his time offering advice to adventurers that were new to the city. He aided the efforts of Lord Neverember, helping facilitate novice adventurers with the accompaniment of traveling companions.
Circa the , Bael became in some secret matter regarding the tieflings of the city. To this end he began recruiting tiefling adventurers to complete some specific missions..
