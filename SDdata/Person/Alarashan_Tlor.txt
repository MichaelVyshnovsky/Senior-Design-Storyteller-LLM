'''Alarashan Tlor''' was a male [[wizard]] who was one of the [[magelord]]s of [[Athalantar]] and was killed in [[Elminster Aumar]]'s insurrection. 

==History==
He was one of the more rationally minded magelords and agreed with his master [[Ithboltar]] to fight together but died in the spell-battle in [[Ithboltar]]'s tower.
