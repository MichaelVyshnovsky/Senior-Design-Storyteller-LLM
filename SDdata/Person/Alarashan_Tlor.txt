Alarashan Tlor was a male wizard who was one of the magelords of Athalantar and was killed in Elminster Aumars insurrection. 
He was one of the more rationally minded magelords and agreed with his master Ithboltar to fight together but died in the spell-battle in Ithboltars tower.
