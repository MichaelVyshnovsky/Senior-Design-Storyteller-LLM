Enhanen was a mercenary recruiter in Arrabar.
Enhanen usually recruited from his trusted veterans team, but newcomers were also welcome. Thanks to a proven team of trusted messengers, he could gather 100-200 recruits in two days.
During his youth, he often traveled with his company in search of glory. His views changed over the course of his life to where he saw it as just a job.
In 1358 DR he together with the Company of the Singing Dawn and the Llandrydds Steel faced the undead army of the mad cleric of Tempus Theodoric.
