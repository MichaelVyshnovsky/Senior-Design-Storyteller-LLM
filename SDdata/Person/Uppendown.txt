Uppendown was a rock gnome baker living in Gnomengarde in the late 15 century DR.
Along with Panana, Uppendown was responsible for prepping bread dough made from Gnomengardes distinctive green mushrooms. His role was to shape the dough into loaves before baking. He took his work very seriously, and worked like an artist in deep concentration with his tongue sticking out at an angle as he sculpted the dough.
Like all the other gnomes in Gnomengarde, Uppendown had some training as a wizard in addition to his innate abilities. In combat, he preferred to attack with spells like ray of frost and magic missile.
