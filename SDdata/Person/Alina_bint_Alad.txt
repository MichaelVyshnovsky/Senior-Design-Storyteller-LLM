'''Alina bint Alad''' was a member of the royal family of [[Medina al-Afyal]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Personality==
Alina was intense and passionate, especially regarding her study of [[magic]].

==Relationships==
Since her older brother [[Alakbar bin Alad bin Alaq|Alakbar]] was lost at sea and presumed dead, Alina was the only surviving heir of the Padishah [[Alad bin Alaq bin Alonka|Alad]] and likely the next ruler of the island kingdom of [[Afyal]].
