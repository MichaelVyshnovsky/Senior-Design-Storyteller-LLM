'''Manthuran Glaezel''' was the head of the [[Glaezel]] family of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Manthuran came to lead the prominent and long-standing Glaezel family some years before the late 14{{th}} century DR. Under his control, the family came to own [[Glaezel's Dock]] in the [[Baldur's Gate/Seatower|Seatower]] district of the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]].
