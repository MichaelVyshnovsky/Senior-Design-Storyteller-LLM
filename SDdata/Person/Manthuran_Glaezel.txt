Manthuran Glaezel was the head of the Glaezel family of Baldurs Gate in the late 14 century DR.
Manthuran came to lead the prominent and long-standing Glaezel family some years before the late 14 century DR. Under his control, the family came to own Glaezels Dock in the Seatower district of the Lower City.
