Odd Valros was a coffinmaker from the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
By the , Odd Valross services were in high demand due to the Absolute Crisis. The coffin makers specialty were deluxe hardwood coffins that, according to the crafter, were thrice as durable as other types of wood. Odd Valross coffins were said to preserve the bodies for longer than the competitors and protected the dearly departed from vermin such as rats and maggots. To promote the business, Odd Valros penned The Superior Durability of Hardwood.
