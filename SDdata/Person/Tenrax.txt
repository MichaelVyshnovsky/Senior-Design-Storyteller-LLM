Tenrax was the seldom-seen leader of the Nugha tribes in Tu Lung in 1357 DR.
Tenrax was the kio ton mu (witchdoctor) leader of the horsemen of Esang Yi in the hill country south of Chunming in Ausa Province. He performed ancient rites and possessed ancient and dangerous knowledge. Tenrax spoke for the Nugha at the court of Wai.
