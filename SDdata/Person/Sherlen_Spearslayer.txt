Sherlen Spearslayer was the leader of Daggerfords militia during the midâ€“14 century DR.
She possessed a very austere demeanor.
Sherlen was an adventurer who settled down in Daggerford with her husband. When she was pregnant, her husband went to adventure and never returned. After her child grew, Sherlen joined the local militia, rising through the ranks until she became the commander. She delighted in the good works of the militia and was full of wise advice.
Her home was located in north Daggerford on Fields Lane near the Farmers Gate. 
Sherlen entered combat while wielding a +2 spear along with a common shield, while additionally wearing a suit of +2 chainmail.
Sherlen additionally always wore boots of levitation and a ring of jumping.
