Ammanadas Silversword was a member of the Silversword noble house of Cormyr. In 1369 DR, he stabbed Ensibal Threen, a War Wizard, in the throat for believing him to be a supporter of Vangerdahasts proposed regency. He was quickly subdued by Lareth Gulur, a passing Purple Dragon.
