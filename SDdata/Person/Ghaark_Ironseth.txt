Lord Ghaark Ironseth was a human of Amnwater, Amn, around 1368 DR, where he led the Ironseth family, owners of about half of the land around Amnwater. 
Lord Ironseth was known as a noisy and insensitive man. 
Ghaarks third son was the blacksmith Togar Ironseth.
