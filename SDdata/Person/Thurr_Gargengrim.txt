'''Thurr Gargengrim''' was a [[dwarf]] businessman in the [[Monastery of the Precipice]] in [[1479 DR]].

==Activities==
The [[Gargengrim]] clan supplied the Monastery with all they needed in exchange for a percentage of the profits from distributing the monastery's whiskey. Thurr was the clan delegate to lead the caravans to and from the monastery.
