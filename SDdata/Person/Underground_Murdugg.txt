'''Underground Murdugg''' was a [[giant spider]] from the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Murdugg, along other giant spiders, [[drow]], and [[duergar]], all were slaughtered in the home of [[Bloomridge Park]] groundskeeper, [[Lavernica]]. The murder spree was performed by [[Orin the Red]] and her [[Murder Tribunal]] of [[Bhaal]]. It left Murdugg's body scorched my magical flames.
