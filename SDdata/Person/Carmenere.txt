'''Carmenere''' was an [[earthsoul genasi]] and a priestess of [[Selûne]].  She was the niece of Queen [[Arathane]] and was formerly a close friend of the [[rogue]] [[Riltana]].  Carmenere aided [[Demascus]] in destroying the [[Cult of the Elder Elemental Eye]].  She later took a post as a diplomat to [[High Imaskar]].{{Cite digital book/Spinner of Lies|530}}	

==Possessions==
Carmenere used a [[mace]] as her primary weapon.

==Description==
Carmenere had light brown skin with gold szuldar.
