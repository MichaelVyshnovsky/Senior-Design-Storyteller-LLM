Telliam was a human mercenary who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.
Telliam was a slender woman with dark hair.
Telliam was one of the Mintarn mercenaries hired by the New Neverwinter organization to protect Neverwinter. She was an aide to Lord Dagult Neverember.
Around 1479 DR, Telliam contacted a group of adventurers to help her solve a series of mysterious murders in Neverwinter.
She was one of the few Neverwinter Guards who survived the aboleths attack.
