==Description==

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, Uramyl joined the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|forward camp]] outside the ruins of [[Myth Drannor]]. She worked there at a field kitchen, feeding and watering the expedition.
