'''Astrid Saj''' ({{Pronounce|ASS|trid|_|SAW|refs={{Cite adventurers league/City of Danger|40}}}}) was a wealthy merchant in [[Mulmaster]] in [[1491 DR]].

==History==
Astrid was part of a merchant house that hoped to enter into the city's Blade elite. In order to gain useful contacts, she hosted a great party at her villa in 1491 DR.
