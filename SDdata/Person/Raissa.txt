'''Raissa''' was a defected former member of the [[Order of the Gilded Eye]] in [[Luskan]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.

==History==
Raissa, having recently defected from the Order of the Gilded Eye, sought asylum in the guildhall of the [[Order of the Burning Dawn]] in Luskan. She was later smuggled out of the city with the aid of members of that order.
