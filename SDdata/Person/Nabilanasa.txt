Nabilanasa was an elven ranger and guide who traveled the deserts of Zakhara around 1367 DR.
Though she seemed taciturn and kept to herself, Nabilanasa could be trusted.
As of 1367 DR, Nabilanasa had been a guide for 50 years. In addition to guiding travelers and caravans, she also tracked criminals.
She wore a ring of chameleon power and armor of the desert evening, and wielded a great scimitar that was a desert blade.
