'''Nabilanasa''' was an [[elven]] [[ranger]] and guide who traveled the deserts of [[Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1993|196|[[Nabilanasa]]}}

==Personality==
Though she seemed taciturn and kept to herself, Nabilanasa could be trusted.

==Activites==
As of 1367 DR, Nabilanasa had been a guide for 50 years. In addition to guiding travelers and caravans, she also tracked criminals.

==Possessions==
She wore a ''[[ring of chameleon power]]'' and ''[[armor of the desert evening]]'', and wielded a [[great scimitar]] that was a ''[[sun blade|desert blade]]''.
