'''Jan Buckman''' was a young beginner [[mage]], son of the village Mayor [[Lazlo Buckman]], from [[West Harbor]] in the [[Mere of Dead Men]] ''circa'' 1374 [[DR]].

{{quote|''Uh, deals? D-did I say deals? Just my - imagination is all, deals. Master Tarmas says I have quite the imagination! So does my friend [[Sheydann Zinn]]. You will find Master Tarmas, won't you?''|Jan Buckman}}

==Description==
Jan was was a nervous young man who found himself unable to properly concentrate of his magic studying and his well-worn ''[[spellbook]]''. He was a shy and somewhat awkward individual.

==History==
Jan Buckman studied under the village mage, Master [[Tarmas]] ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1374}}. Jan looked up to his father and was impressed by the Mayor's leadership and the deals he'd made to keep the village safe. When the town and its brewery became threatened by a duo of [[black dragon]]s, Tarmas was captured by the wyrms, leaving the young apprentice worried for the master's fate and West Harbor's safety.
