Jan Buckman was a young beginner mage, son of the village Mayor Lazlo Buckman, from West Harbor in the Mere of Dead Men circa 1374 DR.
Jan was was a nervous young man who found himself unable to properly concentrate of his magic studying and his well-worn spellbook. He was a shy and somewhat awkward individual.
Jan Buckman studied under the village mage, Master Tarmas circa the . Jan looked up to his father and was impressed by the Mayors leadership and the deals hed made to keep the village safe.
