'''Stannor Thistlehair''' was a [[lightfoot halfling]] carpenter in [[Red Larch]] during the [[Elemental Evil]] crisis.

==Activities==
Stannor worked as a carpenter at [[Waelvur's Wagonworks]].

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], Stannor noticed his boss [[Ilmeth Waelvur]] and some others sometimes disappear into a hidden tunnel.
