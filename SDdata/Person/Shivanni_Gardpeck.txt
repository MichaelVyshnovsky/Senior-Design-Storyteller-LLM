'''Shivanni Gardpeck''' was the proprietor of the [[Cutlass (inn)|Cutlass]] inn in [[Luskan]]. She was also an informant for the [[drow]] band [[Bregan D'aerthe]].

==Relationships==
Shivanni Gardpeck claimed to be the great-great-great-grandniece of [[Arumn Gardpeck]], who owned the Cutlass during [[Wulfgar]]'s stint there as a bouncer.
