'''Umathar Nhalass II''' was the second [[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Nhalass Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Umathar II inherited the throne when his father, [[Umathar Nhalass I|Umathar I]], died in {{Yearlink|-867}}. He ruled until his death in {{Yearlink|-821}}.

==Relationships==
His firstborn son and heir was [[Umathar Nhalass III|Umathar III]].
