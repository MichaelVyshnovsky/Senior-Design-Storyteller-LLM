'''Honthreena Ravensgar''' was a noblewoman in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Appearance==
She wore many bejeweled rings.

==History==
Honthreena Ravensgar was one of the guests of Lady [[Joysil Ambrur]] in the mid-to-late spring of {{yearlink|1373}}. She was extremely dismissive of the state of [[Cormyr]], and hinted to [[Azoun Obarskyr V]], then an infant of two years of age, being likely to die before his coronation.
