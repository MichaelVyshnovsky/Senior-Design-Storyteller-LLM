Naeros Karanok was the son of the ruler of Luthcheq in Chessenta in 1357 DR.
Naeros usually wandered the city with his retinue, watching people run away.
Naeros believed that, thanks to the power of his family, he could do anything he wanted and nobody could kill him. He was cruel and arrogant. He liked disfiguring his victims before burning them.
Naeros had been badly spoiled by his father and his grandfather.
