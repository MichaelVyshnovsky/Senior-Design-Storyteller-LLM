'''Elbert Oldbridle''' was a nobleman from [[Cormyr]], who became head of the noble house [[Oldbridle]] from {{yearlink|1479}}.

==Relationships==
He was the heir of [[Olgarth Oldbridle]].

==History==
After the death of Olgarth Oldbridle in the {{yearlinkName|1479}}, his heir, Elbert, received a missive from him by his servant [[Osbur]], a warning about Sembian intrigue in Cormyr. He had Osbur transmit the warning to [[Traevyn Illance]].

Later, he had people seek out [[Arclath Delcastle]], inviting him to a meeting at [[Arkanon Nalander]]'s. They caught up to him while he was on his coach.{{cite book/Bury Elminster Deep|274|29}}
