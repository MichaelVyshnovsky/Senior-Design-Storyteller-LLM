'''Clack''' was a  [[goblin]] warrior and [[Cult of the Absolute|cultist]] of [[the Absolute]] who protected the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|If yer close enough to talk with me, then yet too damn close.|Clack.}}

==Description==
She had orange skin and kept her head bald.

==Personality==
Unlike many of her fellow goblin cultists, Clack had a no-nonsense attitude.

==Possessions==
Clack wore a suit of crude [[plate armor]] and wielded a [[battleaxe]] in combat.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Clack remained in the Shattered Sanctum while other members of the cult celebrated their successful raid on the [[Waukeen's Rest]] inn.
