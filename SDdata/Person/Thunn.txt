'''Thunn''', who was also called '''the Usurper''', was the second King of the [[Mulan Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Thunn betrayed his uncle, King [[Ashtukzu I]], and claimed the throne for himself in {{Yearlink|-8}}. He ruled until {{Yearlink|4}} when his cousin, [[Ashtukzu II]], reclaimed his birthright.
