'''Amaranthae Auglamyr''' was an elf of House [[Auglamyr]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==Appearance==
During a revel in {{yearlink|241}}, they wore high boots. They were tall for elves.

==Relationships==
She was terrified of her cousin, Symrustar.

==History==
In {{yearlink|241}}, she was a confidante for [[Symrustar Auglamyr]].

During a revel, she helped Symrustar find some time alone with [[Elminster]] by conversing with [[Earynspieir Ongluth]].{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|199-202|10}} However, she revealed later to the [[Srinshee]] and Earynspieir she believed her cousin meant Elminster harm.{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|213-214|11}}

She became concerned about her cousin after the latter disappeared.{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|278|14}}
