King Rhor Torlath "the Lackey" was an unfortunate Amnian monarch of the Torlath dynasty.
Rhor was the bastard son of King Imnel IV.
Rhor was installed on the throne in the , following the assassinations of King Imnel IV and King Niskam. Rhor was intended to be a puppet used by the Braethar merchant house, but their plans fell apart when he was in turn assassinated by their rivals, the Villax merchant house. They installed the distantly-related Dornak Torlath as a puppet.
