Merkul was a priest of Bane from the Dark God Reformed, tempe of the Dark Lord in Voonlar circa 1373 DR.
In the , Merkul descended into the catacombs under Voonlar, home of banelich named Shrazagul, imprisoned there by the Church of Bane mani years prior. With Banes death and return, wards that restarted the banelich faded and the temple of the Dark God Reformed attempted to restore the ancient prion. Merkul was slain by Shrazaguls undead, leaving behind his enchanted +1 plate mail, adorned with Banes holy symbols and regalia.
