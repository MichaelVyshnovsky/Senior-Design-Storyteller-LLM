[[High Phantasmage]] '''Meleghost Zoaster''' was the ruling governor of [[Samarach]] as of the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.

==Activities==
Originally from [[Nimbral]], Meleghost left his homeland to rule Samarach. Rumors abounded that he had no intention of returning home. Some believed he was under the influence of a sect of [[Cyricist]]s who settled in Samarach following the [[Time of Troubles]].
