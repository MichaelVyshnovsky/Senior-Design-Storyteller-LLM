'''Taraj Hurlymm''' was a male [[wizard]] who was one of the [[magelord]]s of [[Athalantar]] and was killed by [[Elminster Aumar]]. 

==Activities==
Taraj's hobby was taking beast-shape and stalking men and women as if they were prey.

==History==
He took over governance of outlying [[Dalniir]] for the magelords, pressing the [[elf|elves]]. Thus they asked Elminster to kill Taraj. However, Taraj summoned his fellow magelord [[Kadeln Olothstar]] was eventually slain by Elminster.
