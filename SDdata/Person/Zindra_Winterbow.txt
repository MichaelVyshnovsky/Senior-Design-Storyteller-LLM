'''Zindra Winterbow''' was a guide in [[Dessarin Valley]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==Activities==
Zindra was a guide and scout member of the [[Emerald Enclave]]. Often she went to drink at the [[Triboar Arms]] tavern.
