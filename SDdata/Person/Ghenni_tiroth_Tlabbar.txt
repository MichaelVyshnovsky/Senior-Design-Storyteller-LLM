'''Ghenni'tiroth Tlabbar''' was the [[drow]] [[matron mother]] of [[House Faen Tlabbar]] the fourth house of [[Menzoberranzan]].
==Description==
Unlike most drow, Ghenni was a tall and very slim. She had snow white hair and ebon skin.

==Personality==
Ghenni'tiroth was one of the most devoted followers of Lolth in Menzoberranzan.

==Possessions==
In battle, Ghenni'tiroth used a [[dagger]] called [[Scrag-tooth]], which was taken by K'yorl Odran upon her death. Within the Faen Tlabbar compound, Ghenni used a [[platinum]] sacrificial platter known as the [[Faen Tlabbar communing plate]].

==History==
In [[1358 DR]], Ghenni prayed to [[Lolth]] to protect her house from the advances of [[House Oblodra]]

===Death===
Near the end of the [[Time of Troubles]], [[K'yorl Odran]] entered the chapel of House Faen Tlabbar and used psychic energy to rip the heart from Ghenni'tiroth and placed it on the sacred plate. At her death, Ghenni'tiroth was over 640 years old.
