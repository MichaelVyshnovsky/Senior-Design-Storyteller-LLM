Orran was a senior Doomguide and priest of Kelemvor who lived in Neverwinter as of the late 15 century DR.
As a member of the Eternal Order of Kelemvor, Orrans mission was to preserve Neverdeath Graveyard and cleanse it of undead. 
He was a contemporary and associate of Doomguide Volahk.
In the early goings of the events known as the Tyranny of Dragons, circa the , Orrin began investigating a group of Red Wizards that were exhuming bodies in the Neverdeath Graveyard. He worked with adventurers to clear the holy graveyards of undead abominations and cleanse the lands by means of Kelemvors influence.
