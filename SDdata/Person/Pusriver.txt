'''Pusriver''' was a [[green hag]] of the [[Bloodroot Coven]] in [[Twilight Marsh]] near [[Phlan]] in [[1489 DR]].

==Personality==
Pusriver was the kindest of her coven, with the job of attracting people. She was also very forgetful, often asking the same question more than once.{{Cite adventurers league/Raiders of the Twilight Marsh|10-11}}
