'''Crag Ungart''' was a [[shield dwarf]] [[giant-killer|giantslayer]] who roamed around the [[Savage Frontier]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]],

==Personality==
Crag had a very keen and strategic mind, making him quite an efficient leader. However, he was rather detached from those he worked with.

==Relationships==
Crag was a member of the giantslayer organization [[Hook, Line, and Sinker (organization)|Hook, Line, and Sinker]].
