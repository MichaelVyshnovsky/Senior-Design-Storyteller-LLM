'''Quartermaster Marcellus''' was a soldier who served in the [[Neverwinter Guard]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Template:Cite game/Neverwinter}}
{{Quote|The orcs of the [[Many-Arrows|Many-Arrows Tribe]] are threatening to overwhelm this area of the city. The Neverwinter Guard could use your help in driving them back!|Lysson}}

==Possessions==
He wore a suit of [[chain mail]] armor and a tabard.

==History==
After the [[siege of Neverwinter]] in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Marcellus was stationed in the city's [[Neverwinter/River District|Tower District]]. He provided equipment to his fellow soldiers and sold supplies to adventurers acting on behalf of Captain [[Janros]] to aid in the effort to take back the area from the occupying force of the [[Many-Arrows]] [[orc]]s.
