'''Thartryn Ilistar III''' was the eleventh King of the [[Ilistar Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]]. He was also the last Ilistar King of Westgate.

==Activities==
Thartryn III began his rule when his father, King [[Halcoranth Ilistar|Halcoranth]], died in the {{YearlinkName|886}}. He only reigned for a few years before succumbing to plague, along with the remainder of the Ilistar heirs, in the {{YearlinkName|890}}.
