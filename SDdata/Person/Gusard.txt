Gusard was a rawun and the youngest son of Alia bint Hazir and Sultan Yusef al-Wadi of Gana in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara.
Gusard was a natural entertainer and enjoyed joking around.
He had a mole under his right eye in the shape of a butterfly.
As with all of his siblings, Gusards parentage was kept secret from the population of Gana. He was raised in a monastery high in Al-Yabki Mountains.
