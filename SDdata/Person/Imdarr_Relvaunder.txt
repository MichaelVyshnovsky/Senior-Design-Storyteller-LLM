Imdarr Relvaunder was a priest of Tempus in Red Larch and secretly a member of the Order of the Gauntlet during the Elemental Evil crisis.
Imdarr was a stern man, and keenly interested in news, rumors, and visitors.
Imdarr, along with Lymmura Auldarhk, took care of the Allfaiths Shrine. Sometimes, he acted for the Gauntlet, sending reports and passing messages
In 1491 DR, Imdarr was involved in the disappearance of a diplomatic caravan near the city. The Order asked him to support some adventurers hired to solve the matter.
