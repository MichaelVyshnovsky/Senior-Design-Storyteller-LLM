'''Ederan Nharimlur''', who was also nicknamed '''Catseye''', was the third [[High Mage (Silverymoon)|High Mage]] of [[Silverymoon]].

==Activities==
Ederan served as High Mage from the {{YearlinkName|719}}, to the {{YearlinkName|784}}, when he died of old age.

==Relationships==
He married the [[elven]] princess [[Elénaril]] of [[Myth Drannor]]. His [[half-elf]] daughter [[Amaara Nharimlur|Amaara]] would succeed him as High Mage.
