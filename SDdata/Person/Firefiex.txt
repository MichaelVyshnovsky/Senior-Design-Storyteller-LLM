'''Firefiex''' was a [[Dambraii]] [[horse]] that was the mount of Baron [[Montar Kaspar]] of the city of [[Kaspar]] in [[Ulgarth]] around [[1367 DR]]. Firefiex was reputed to be the swiftest steed in the kingdom.
