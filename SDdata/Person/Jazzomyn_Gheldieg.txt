Jazzomyn Gheldieg was a member of the Gheldieg family and head of the Eshpurta Gheldiegs, which gave her a seat on the Shield Council as of early 1370 DR, the Year of the Tankard.. She was the niece of Curkon Gheldieg (a former Namarch of the Council of Six) and the cousin of Lady Vyma (Gheldieg) Tanislove, both of Athkatla. Her ambition was to get her family to increase their share of the art and collectables market.
