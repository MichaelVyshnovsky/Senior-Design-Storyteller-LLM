'''Vhloar''' was the second king of the [[sahuagin]] kingdom of [[Aleaxtis]].

==History==
In the year {{YearlinkConvert|-443|TS}}, Vhloar succeeded the first king of Aleaxtis, [[T'Raam]]. His reign would go on for over 468 years, lasting until the year {{YearlinkConvert|25|TS}}.
