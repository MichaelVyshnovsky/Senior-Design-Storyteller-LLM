'''Khaizuran''' was a [[tasked genie|tasked]] [[warmonger genie]] who served the [[Brotherhood of the True Flame]] in the fortress of [[Krak al-Niraan]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Simply put, Khaizuran was the tactical genius behind all of the raids sent forth from the keep. He also coordinated an elaborate system of supply lines bringing goods into the keep from all corners.

==History==
Khaizuran led a savage attack against the [[Free Cities of Zakhara|Free City]] of [[Liham]] years ago.

==Possessions==
Khaizuran wielded a ''[[sword of wounding|scimitar of wounding]]'' called ''[[Tears of Eternity]]''.
