Ravaedrin was a Zhentarim who survived combat with The Simbul at some point prior to , only to be summoned by her on that year.
He knew of a method to convert flames to acid.
He had wands.
At some point in the decades prior to , Ravaedrin fought The Simbul and survived unknown to him, shed thrust some of her blood into his body, where it had lodged, making him vulnerable to her blood ring spell later on. She used the spell in , calling him to her service in Avernus. She later ended the summon, returning him to wherever he had been.
