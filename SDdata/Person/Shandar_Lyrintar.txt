Lady Joyworker Shandar Lyrintar was the High Priestess of the Hall of Laughter, a temple devoted to Lliira, in Melvaunt around 1367 DR.
Shandar stood  tall and had red hair. She was quite beautiful by most standards and had a lovely singing voice which she used when the Hall of Laughter hosted revelries every fortnight.
Shandar oversaw seven other clergy within the temple.
Shandar worshiped in Hillsfars church of Lliira before coming to Melvaunt.
