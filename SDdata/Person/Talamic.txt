'''Talamic''' was a [[bard]] of the 1360s [[DR]].

Talamic sang at the [[Griffin's Claw]] in the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]] in the [[1362 DR|Year of the Helm, 1362 DR]]. One of Talamic's songs was the ''[[Lay of the Purple Dragons]]''.
