Elan TanorThal was a drow wizard of House TanorThal in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.
Elan was a student of the magical academy on the Dweomercore level. 
House TanorThal once ruled a successful slaving ring in Skullport until the Xanathars Thieves Guild destroyed them.
In 1492 DR, Elan was at the Dweomercore studying magic.
Elans objective was to gain enough magical power to conquer Skullport for his family. He was a typical treacherous drow at first he appeared friendly to people, only to betray them later.
