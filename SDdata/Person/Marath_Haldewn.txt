Marath Haldewn was the leader of a circle of Chauntean druids active on the Moonshae Isles during the middle of the 14 century DR.
Marath was the last worshiper of Chauntea to watch over Earthmothers Weapons, a book containing several spells unique to her faith. She protected the book while her order passed knowledge of its spells by oral tradition.
During the , Marath was attacked and brutally murdered by a force of Malarite berserkers who found her hidden tree-home. While she managed to dispatch half of her assailants, she was ultimately overpowered and offered as a sacrifice to the Beastlord.
