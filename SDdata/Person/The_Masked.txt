'''The Masked''' was one of the Seven Wizards of [[Myth Drannor]].

==Description==
They constantly wore ''[[Andrathath's Mask]]''.

==Personality==
She offered little explanation for her desire to remain masked all the time, claiming only a desire for privacy.

==Abilities==
They were one of the Seven Wizards of [[Myth Drannor]].

==Possessions==
The Masked got their name from their constant use of '''''[[Andrathath's Mask]]'''''.

==Activities==
They served the city with dignity and grace.

==History==
The Masked received the mask from [[Elminster Aumar]] a short time after he took it from [[Llombaerth Starym]], and became one of the Seven Wizards of [[Myth Drannor]].{{cite book/The Fall of Myth Drannor|18}}

==Rumors & Legends==
Some doubted her motives and character as of {{yearlink|650}}.
