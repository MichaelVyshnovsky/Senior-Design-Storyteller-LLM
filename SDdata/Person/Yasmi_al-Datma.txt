'''Yasmi al-Datma''' was one of many wives of the [[Grand Caliph]] [[Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir]] in [[Huzuz]].

==Description==
Yasmi was a slim, elven woman with wide, sky blue eyes.

==Personality==
Yasmi was quiet but intelligent.

==Abilities==
She possessed skills in [[sorcery]] and had the ability to play the [[dulcimer]].

==Activities==
When Khalil was occupied at night with his other wives, Yasmi and Tanya would often escape the palace by using Tanya's ''[[teleport]]'' [[spell]].

==Relationships==
Yasmi was married to the Grand Caliph in [[1362 DR]]. Yasmi was quickly befriended by [[Tanya bint Perijan]] who realized the importance of having a sorcerer in the harem.

==History==
Before she married the Grand Caliph, Yasmi was a respected member of the [[Alyana al-Azzazi|Grand Vizier]]'s palace security force.
