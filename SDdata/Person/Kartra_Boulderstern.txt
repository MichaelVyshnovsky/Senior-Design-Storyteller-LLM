Kartra Boulderstern was a dwarf laborer in Elturel in the late 15 century DR.
After Elturel was transported to Avernus, first layer of the Nine Hells, in the , many quakes struck the city and three dwarves—Kartra Boulderstern, Strovin Ironfist, and Velkora Ashenwell—became trapped in the rubble of a collapsed building. If they were lucky, they may have been rescued by passing adventurers.
