'''Khalil''' was a [[desert rider]] and grandson of Sheikh [[Ibrin]] of the [[House of Hotek]] tribe in the [[Haunted Lands]] of [[interior Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p44">

==Relationships==
Khalil was a dutiful son who obeyed both is father, [[Jisaron]], and grandfather. He made sure to stay out of the way of Jisaron's vizier [[Amene]]<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p44"/>
