'''Parcilla''' was an employee of [[Kalas Winters]], who worked as a waitress at the [[Wolf's Pelt Inn]] in the [[Icewind Dale]] community of [[Targos]] ''circa'' {{YearlinkName|1369}}.

==Description==
Parcilla was a 20-year-old woman with short blonde hair and green eyes. She was {{SI|5'|'"|long=yes}} tall and weighed {{SI|107|lb|k|long=yes}}.

==Personality==
Parcilla did not perform her duties wellâ€”her thoughts was often elsewhere. She felt she deserved a better lot in her miserable life than being stuck waiting tables. The guests got the impression that she looked down on them with an air of superiority.

Parcilla acted coldly toward and often snapped back at anyone who tried to flirt with her.

==Possessions==
Parcilla wore a small [[gold]] [[ring]] on her left hand that was a ''[[ring of protection]] +1''. It was an engagement ring given to her by her fiance and she felt it kept her safe. She also carried a kitchen [[knife]] with her in case of trouble.

==History==
At some point in her past, Parcilla was engaged, but her fiance went missing in [[Maer Dualdon]] after a sudden storm.

Around [[Tarsakh]] in 1369 DR, Parcilla worked as a waitress in Wolf's Pelt Inn.
