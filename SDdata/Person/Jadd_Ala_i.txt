Jadd Alai was a human shair living in Madinat al-Mumin in the Steaming Isles in 1367 DR.
Jadd was the closest advisor to his wife and leader of Madinat al-Mumin, Jaddat Jamila. He generally preferred a cautious approach to solving problems.
Jadd was married to Jaddat Jamila.
