'''Akila Abd al-Amin''' was a caravan guard captain from [[Hiyal]] in [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Akila was a small, wiry woman.

==Activities==
She commanded a group of [[mamluk]] guards called the "[[Faithful Mamluk Society|Faithful]]." The Faithful was made up of three 10-man platoons.
