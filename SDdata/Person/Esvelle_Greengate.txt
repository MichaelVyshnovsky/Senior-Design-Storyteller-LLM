Esvelle Greengate was a merchant in Ylraphon and the head of the local council of merchants in .
Though she had hard eyes, Esvelle Greengate was described as a plump, harmless dumpling of a woman. The night of Hammer 23, she was wearing a quilted dressing gown and a nightcap. She had graying, curly hair.
Around her home, she had equipment for pressing fish and rendering it into oil.
On Hammer 23, Dorn Graybrook woke her up to inform of the signs of a Rage of Dragons. She sent an apprentice to rouse the other members of the local council of merchants.
