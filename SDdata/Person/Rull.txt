Rull was a Bhaalist doppelganger member of the Murder Tribunal in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
His his elvish guise, Rull appeared to be a man with light skin, green eyes, and black hair. When the disguise was dropped, Rull looked like the rest of his pale kin.
In the , Rull and several other doppelgangers accompanied a Bhaalist murderer Dolor to assassinate and mutilate the Figaro Pennygood, the proprietor of the Facemakers Boutique targeted by the church. In case unwanted eyes visited the store, Rull played the role of a crossbow-wielding hired guard. Rull was presumed killed by a group of adventurers known as the Absolute Adversaries who were investigating the series of murderers perpetrated by the Murder Tribunal.
