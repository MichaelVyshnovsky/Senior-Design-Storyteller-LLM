'''Captain Orlberl''' was a [[human]] [[Purple Dragons]] officer who was deployed in [[Tilver's Gap]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Around [[1358 DR]], Orlberl commanded a cavalry patrol on the roads near [[Tilverton]], alongside other squads commanded by Captain [[Pripyn]] and Captain [[Thardock]].
