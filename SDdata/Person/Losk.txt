'''Losk''' was a powerful [[hagspawn]] inhabiting northern [[Rashemen]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Losk ruled over the [[Fortress of the Half-Demon]] in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}. A savage band of brigands under his control helped enforce his influence in the region.
