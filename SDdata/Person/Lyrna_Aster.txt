Lyrna Aster was a halfling rogue in the late 14 century DR.
Lyrna had tanned skin, brown eyes, and dark brown hair tied back in a bun. She wore a suit of patchwork leather armor and had a pair of fancy daggers.
In the , Lyrna joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their base camp at the ruins of Myth Drannor. She served as a trainer for prospective sneaks and thieves at the camp.
