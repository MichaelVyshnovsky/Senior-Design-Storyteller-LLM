'''Iraska''', who was known to her [[derro]] subjects as the '''Slime King''' was a [[yuan-ti pureblood]] who ruled a clan of derro in the [[Underdark]].

==Description==
With the exception of canine teeth which looked like fangs, Iraska appeared as an elderly [[human]] female.{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|2828}}

==Abilities==
Iraska learned to open portals to the [[Far Realm]], an ability which ingratiated her to the Far Realm obsessed derro.{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|3270}}

==History==
Iraska was born into a small tribe of yuan-ti in the jungles of Southern [[Faer没n]].  Because she was pureblood, she was nearly human in appearance and she was sent to the human city of [[Delzimmer]] as a spy.{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|3172}}

Decades before [[1479 DR]] she was abducted by [[derro]] of the [[Underdark]] and [[slavery|enslaved]].  She manipulated her way to the top of the derro hierarchy and eventually became their leader, adopting the honorary title of Slime King.{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|3319}}  In [[1362 DR]] she ordered the derro to attack and enslave her former tribe out of spite.{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|3222}}

In 1479 DR her great-great-grandaughter, [[Zaltys Serrat]] and Zaltys' cousin [[Julen Serrat|Julen]] journeyed to the derro settlement with the intent of freeing Zaltys' birth clan.  Iraska revealed Zaltys' true nature as a yuan-ti pureblood{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|2827}} and attempted to poison Julen.  Julen and Zaltys attacked Iraska and she was pushed through a Far Realm portal by Zaltys' guard [[Krailash]] who had followed Zaltys' trail to the derro village.{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|3605}}
