'''Amon abd al-Wajib''' was a [[mamluk]] of the [[Dutiful Mamluk Society]] in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}} He was originally from [[Qudra]].

==Description==
The markings of the Dutiful were tattooed on Amon's face.

==Activities==
Amon was permanently assigned as a guard for [[Alara bint Kira]]'s family. He was very protective of his ward, especially from would-be suitors.

==Possessions==
Amon wore [[Leather armor|padded leather armor]] and a blue-dyed kilt.
