Merin Falle was a swift and hard-working hin cook of the Yawning Portal in the late 14 century DR.
In Kythorn of the , Merin was present at the inn along with her fellow cook Frinli Hinkille and the foul-tempered chef. A gaggle of drow assassins emerged from the Yawning Portals entrance to Undermountain and staged a slaughter of the gathered adventurers. Even though the attackers were repelled back into Undermountain, Merins worries over a fellow cook under-spiceing dishes started to seem not as dramatic as before.
