'''Yuraka''' was a [[wu jen]] who lived in the city of [[Singye]] in [[Koryo]] around [[1357 DR]].

==Possessions==
Yuraka was the owner of the last known scroll case of the ''[[Ky Trencha Ukang]]'', a powerful magical [[staff]].
