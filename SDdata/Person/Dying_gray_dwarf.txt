This '''dying gray dwarf''' was a male [[duergar]] who attacked the village of [[West Harbor]] on the [[Sword Coast North]] in [[1374 DR]].

==History==
This dwarf was one of the attackers of West Harbor. Despite being wounded, he managed to kill several villagers, calling them weak. The Kalach-Cha had the option of healing him or slaying the duergar personally. In any case, the duergar died.<ref>
