'''Basil Willowbrook''' was a [[halfling]] innkeeper in [[Skullport]].

==History==
Once an [[adventurer]] in the [[Shining South]], Basil settled in the [[Lower Heart of Skullport|Lower Heart]] district of Skullport, where he opened the [[Bindle's Blade]] inn. Around [[1370 DR]], his inn was one of the best-priced quality establishments in the city.
