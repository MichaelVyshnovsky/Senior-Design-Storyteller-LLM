Basil Willowbrook was a halfling innkeeper in Skullport.
Once an adventurer in the Shining South, Basil settled in the Lower Heart district of Skullport, where he opened the Bindles Blade inn. Around 1370 DR, his inn was one of the best-priced quality establishments in the city.
