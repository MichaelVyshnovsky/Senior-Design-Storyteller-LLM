'''Aubaudameira Dree''', usually known as '''Alais''', was a female [[elf]] who was the herald of the [[Coronal]] [[Eltargrim]] of [[Cormanthyr]].

==History==
Alais was a prominent member of [[Dree House]]. However, she was mentally enslaved by her fiancé, [[Glarald Starym]], who used her in an attempt to kill the coronal. Glarald's plot failed and Alais was freed and became the new herald of the coronal, becoming one of his most trusted servants. Alais also lend her life-force for the raising of the ''[[mythal]]'' in [[261 DR]].{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|4-6}}

In later years Alais joined the [[Harpers in Twilight|Harpers at Twilight]] and fought well in the [[Weeping War]].
Alais was one of the commander in a decisive battle against the [[Army of Darkness]] in [[Ches 4]]-5 of [[712 DR]] when thank to the [[Chosen of Mystra]] 
[[Elminster]] and [[Khelben Arunsun]] the Army suffered a defeat at [[Elven Wood]]

Alais was one of the few Harpers to survive alongside [[Elminster]], [[Khelben Arunsun]] and [[Meil Araeln]]. Alais was at the [[Gathering of the Gods]] in the [[720 DR]] together with her fellow Harpers.

==Relationship==
Alais studied magic under one of the founders of the [[Circle of Flames]], [[Symkalr Vandiir]]. She was a close ally of [[Mentor Wintercloak]]
