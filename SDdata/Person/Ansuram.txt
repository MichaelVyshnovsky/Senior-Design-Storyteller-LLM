'''Ansuram''' was the signature found on a Letter of Marque by [[Raidon Kane]] on the body of a dead [[Red Wizard]] of [[Thay]] in {{yearlink|1375}}. Ansuram's signature was in a writ of marque authorizing raids up and down the [[River Umber]], even unto the edges of [[Aglarond]].{{cite book/Stardeep|90|8}}
