'''Ernest Cymrych''' was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] at the turn of the 8{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|33}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] and the first son of his predecessor, High King [[Tobert Cymrych|Tobert]]. His successor was his own first son, [[Carthus Cymrych]]. His second son, [[Alec Cymrych II|Alec II]], then ruled as High King after Carthus abdicated.

==History==
Ernest assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|679}}, following the death of his father.

He ruled for fifty-two years before being dying of natural causes in the {{YearlinkName|731}}.
