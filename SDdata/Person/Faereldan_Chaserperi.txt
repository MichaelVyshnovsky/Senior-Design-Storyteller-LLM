Faereldan Chaserperi was the patriarch of the Chaserperi clan of mages as of the late mid-14 century DR.
He lived with his wife in a small manor on Skellin Street, Athkatla. Their home had a walled garden on the back.
As of the , Faereldan was driven to black rages because of his failing memory.
Though he used to verbally spar with his wife, shed grown much more supportive of him in his dotage, writing on his hand the names of people he had business with during meetings, and sketching their faces, too.
