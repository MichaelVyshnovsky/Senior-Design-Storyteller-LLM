King Highstead was the ruler of the Onusclan tribe of orcs from the Orsraun Mountains in the late 14 century DR.
King Highsteads reign was in full power circa the . Under his rule, Onusclan orcs raided neighboring territories of the southern Ancient Ax goblin clan and the eastern Tattered Cloth kobolds. Due to regular raids, populations of all three factions of the Orsrauns were kept in check and away from becoming a problem for Turmish. However, King Highsteads orcs trespassed in the Orbrekh, the territory of a group of giants calling themselves the community of the high brows, the giants decimated Onusclan.
