'''Vaal''' was a [[cloud giant]] count sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==History==
Vaal was one of the [[giant]] lords at the time of the shattering of the [[Ordning (social structure)|ordning]].

In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR Vaal was one of the giant lords arrived in [[Maelstrom (fortress)|Maelstrom]] in search of news about princess Serissa's plans for the future.

==Personality==
Vaal was susceptible to bribes
