Reagan Splint was an inhabitants of the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Reagan was a large and muscular half-orcish woman. She had gray skin and fur and styles her hair into a red mohawk.
In the , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldurs Gate, displacing countless refugees in the process along the way and provoking fear and paranoia inside the city walls. Reagan was outraged by the number of public executions that took place on the plaza outside of the Basilisk Gate. She wrote to the Baldurs Mouth Gazette about the issue every tenday and every tenday, the broadsheet ignored the half-orcs calls. During that time, Reagan spent some time renting lodgings at Nortales Hostel until an incident that had her removed from the reestablishment.
