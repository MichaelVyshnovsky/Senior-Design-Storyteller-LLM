Baoeknuk was a bugbear that lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.
While he made quite the formidable mercenary, Baoeknuk was not known for skills as a conversationalist.
In the , Baoeknuk was hired by the forces of Dror Ragzlin and the cult of the Absolute. He aided in the protection of their makeshift fortress referred to as the Shattered Sanctum.
