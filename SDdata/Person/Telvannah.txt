Telvannah was the White Worm high shaman in Vaasa in 1479 DR. 
Telvannah could summon the Spirit of the Remorhaz, gaining great control over heat and fire.
Telvannah was the wife of chieftain Tor-Rem. She had a tundra yeti as a spirit companion.
