'''Malik the Cruel''' was an Iron Consul of [[Bane]] who served the [[Cult of the Absolute]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Malik was a [[human]] man with a light skin tone. He wore a hellish-looking [[plate mail]] and a horned mask, reminiscent of a [[devil]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, the [[Cult of Bane]] joined forces with the [[Cult of the Absolute]], led by the [[Chosen]] of Bane, Lord [[Enver Gortash]]. Iron Consul Malik the Cruel was placed in the [[Steel Watch Foundry]], guarding its [[Gondian]] slaves in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].
