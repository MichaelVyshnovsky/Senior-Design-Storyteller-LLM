'''Zobia Shrinsha''' was a tavern owner who inhabited the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
She was a shy and quiet woman whose demeanor reminded her patrons of a little girl.

==Activities==
She owned [[A Maiden's Tears]] that was located on a small unnamed alley that branched south off of [[Lion Street]] in the northeastern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Relationships==
Zobia maintained close relationships both with the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]] and the [[Lords of Waterdeep]].
