'''Emycharianatris''', also known as '''The Jewel-Born Empress''', was a [[dragon]] ruler of [[Abeir]], in the centuries before the [[Spellplague]].

==History==
Emycharianatris was the owner of [[Khorsaya]], before the [[dragonborn]] earned her freedom.
