'''Yashiira''' was promising young [[wizard]] inhabiting the city of [[Yhep]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p170">

==Personality==
She was quite rambunctious and wild, and often a strain on her master [[Zalazel Harauntan]].<ref name=EotSS-p170"/>

==Activities==
Originally from the local [[Talu-min]] tribe, Yashiira came to Yhep to apprentice with the skilled wizard Zalazel.<ref name=EotSS-p170"/>
