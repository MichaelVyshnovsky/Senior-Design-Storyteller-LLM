'''Bethany Carrathal''' was High Queen of [[Callidyrr]] in the early-to-mid 12{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|33}} Like her predecessors, although he held the title of High Queen of the [[Moonshae Isles]], she was considered to be merely a figurehead by the [[Ffolk]] outside of Callidyrr.

==Relationships==
She was a member of the [[Carrathal Dynasty]], and was the first daughter of her predecessor, King [[Edwin Carrathal I|Edwin I]]. Her first son and eventual successor was [[Edwin Carrathal II|Edwin II]].

==History==
Bethany assumed the throne in the {{YearlinkName|1127}}, following the death of her father. Her reign lasted only six years before she died of illness in the {{YearlinkName|1133}}.
