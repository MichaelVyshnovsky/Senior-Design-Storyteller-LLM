Lord '''Calantar''' was a nobleman of [[Cormyr]], and a head of House [[Calantar]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Per the Marchioness of [[Immerdusk]], [[Storm Silverhand]], Lord Calantar was quiet.

==Possessions==
He carried a [[blueflame item]] in the form of a belt buckle. He also carried a weapon.

==Relationships==
He was the head of House [[Calantar]].

==History==
Lord Calantar used an enspelled belt buckle to call a [[blueflame ghost]] down on the crowd at [[The Bold Blazon]] during the [[Festival of Handras]], to everyone's surprise; the ghost cut down several specific nobles in the crowd. He was quickly beset by [[War Wizards of Cormyr]] and [[Storm Silverhand]], the Marchioness [[Immerdusk]], who managed to cleanly slice off his hand and take his belt buckle.
