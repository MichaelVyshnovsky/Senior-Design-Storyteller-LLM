'''Buldos Durinbold''' was a [[noble]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]], City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Buldos was the patriarch of the [[Durinbold]] [[Nobility of Waterdeep|noble family]] of Waterdeep. He lived within [[Durinbold Villa]] on [[Vhezoar Street]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]].{{Cite web enhancement/Noble Houses of Waterdeep|3}}
