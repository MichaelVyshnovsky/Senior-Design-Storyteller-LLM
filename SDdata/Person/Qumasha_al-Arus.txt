'''Qumasha al-Arus''' was a skilled [[elemental mage|flame mage]] inhabiting [[Krak al-Niraan]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Personality==
Qumasha was a strong woman who was very aggressive in the pursuit of her goals.

==Description==
Qumasha's innocent beauty was magnified by her athletic frame.

==Activities==
Though she was the second wife of [[Jamal al-Yindannim]], Qumasha outwardly praised Jamal's first wife, [[Najeeya bint Siddiq]]. However, Qumasha secretly drugged Najeeya with ''[[dreambliss]]'' to foster her addiction to the drug and keep her out of the picture.

Qumasha also helped train new flame mages who joined the ranks of the [[Brotherhood of the True Flame]].

==Possessions==
Qumasha was quite skilled at throwing daggers and carried several with her at all times. Each of these daggers was coated with ''dreambliss''. She owned a ''[[sash of fire resistance]]'', a ''[[ring of shocking grasp]]'', and a ''[[potion of extra healing]]''. She also carried several ''[[smoke sphere]]s''.
