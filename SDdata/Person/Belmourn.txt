'''Belmoun''' was a cook in [[Yûlash]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Death on the Wall|13}}

==Activities==
Belmoun, together with [[Jamrry]] and [[Otiben]], managed the chickens at the [[Red Plumes]] camp in Yûlash.
 
==History==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], during the visit of [[Torin Nomerthal]] to the city, Belmourn may have helped to overthrow the First Lord.{{Cite adventurers league/Death on the Wall|15}}

==Personality==
Thank to his cousin, Belmourn was sympathetic to non-[[human]]s and helped them if he could.

==Relationship==
Belmourn was cousin to the [[half-elf]] [[Constance Goodroot]].
