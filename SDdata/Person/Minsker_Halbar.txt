'''Minsker Halbar''' was a [[human]] male [[wizard]] from [[Calaunt]] in [[the Vast]]. He was the proprietor of [[Halbar's Bookshop]] around [[1358 DR]].

He was an old and quiet man, but one of the most accomplished wizards among Calaunt's oppressed mages.
