Karzon Kul was a ruffian dwarf from Loudwater in 1479 DR.
Karzons head was shaved, and he had tattoos across his body.
Karzon Kul was a local ruffian who usually hanged out at the Fishers Friend Pub in Loudwater, and had a reputation for starting fights with other patrons.
Karzon was an ally of the Lady of Shadows, leader of the crime gang in Loudwater in the late 15th century.
