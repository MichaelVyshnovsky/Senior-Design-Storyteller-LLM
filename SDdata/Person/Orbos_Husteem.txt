'''Orbos Husteem''' was a [[Chondathan]] human and the patriarch of the [[House Husteem|Husteem]] noble house of [[Waterdeep]]. 

==Personality==
He was well aware of his brother [[Orlpar Husteem|Orlpar's]] illegal activity and attempted to keep him in check.

==Relationships==
Orbos's younger brother was [[Orlpar Husteem]].
