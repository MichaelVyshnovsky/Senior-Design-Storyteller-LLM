Oshalla was a sahuagin priestess who lived in the Realms during the late 15 century DR.
She spoke both the common tongue and the language of her people.
Over time Oshalla cultivated a collection of various items she found in the depths of the oceans.
At some point in her life, Oshalla was exiled from her undersea kingdom for plotting the assassination of its monarch.
As of the , she was staying at the Elfsong Tavern in the city of Baldurs Gate. She stayed at the establishments largest guest room on the second floor, decorating it with various trinkets and seascape paintings reminiscent of her home.
