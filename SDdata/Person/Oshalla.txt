'''Oshalla''' was a [[sahuagin]] priestess who lived in the Realms during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Abilities==
She spoke both the [[common]] tongue and [[Sahuagin langue|the language of her people]].

==Possessions==
Over time Oshalla cultivated a collection of various items she found in the depths of the oceans.

==History==
At some point in her life, Oshalla was exiled from her undersea kingdom for plotting the assassination of its monarch.

As of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, she was staying at the [[Elfsong Tavern]] in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]]. She stayed at the establishment's largest guest room on the second floor, decorating it with various trinkets and seascape paintings reminiscent of her home.
