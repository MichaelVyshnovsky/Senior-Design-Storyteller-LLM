Calliope was a cheetah that was a part of the Jack Mooney & Sons Circus of Ravens Bluff in the mid-14 century DR.
Calliope was a fast and nimble  long feline. Like other cheetahs, Calliope had unmatched speed and dangerous rending claws on all for appendages. She was a trained circus animal.
In the mid-14 century DR, Calliope could often be found in the company of her master and the circus owner Jack Mooney, a ranger of fine skill. Alongside his other cats, Raynock the tiger, and Stray the leopard, Calliope served as rangers animal companion and defender.
