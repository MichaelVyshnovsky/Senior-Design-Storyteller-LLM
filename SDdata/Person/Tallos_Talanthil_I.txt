'''Tallos Talanthil I''', who was also called '''the Windlord''', was the eighth [[Sorcerer]]-[[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Talanthil Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Tallos inherited the throne when his father, [[Mornarth Talanthil I]], died in {{Yearlink|-323}}. He ruled until {{Yearlink|-286}}.

==Relationships==
His grandson and heir was [[Tallos Talanthil II|Tallos II]].
