Lady '''Akaglossa Tornglara''' was an inhabitant of [[Cormanthor]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]]. She was involved in the raising of the ''[[Mythal]]''.

==History==
In {{yearlink|241}}, during a party at [[Satyrdance Pool]], she and a number of other ladies shared concerns about the future of Cormanthor; in particular, she believed it might be destroyed within her lifetime. They then agreed to try and nudge their husbands towards stabilizing the city.{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|278-281|14}}

In {{yearlink|261}}, during the laying of the ''Mythal'', Alaglossa Tornglara was briefly frightened by the negative reaction of her servant, [[Nlaea]], who appeared to come to harm by the ''Mythal'''s power.{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|360, 370, 375-376|Epilogue}}  However, she ultimately was a contributor to the energy that the ''Mythal'' required.{{cite book/Cormanthyr: Empire of the Elves|143}}

==Rumors==
Alaglossa Tornglara was said to ''scry'' distant cities to discover new fashions.
