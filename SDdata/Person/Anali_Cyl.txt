Anali Cyl, the daughter of a wealthy Cyl family, was betrothed to a merchant family scion of Waterdeep. With the wars advent, Anali was to be shipped away to the City of Splendors. The young woman was outraged over her familys actions. She was rather die defending her home than be wed against her will. Analis parents sneered at their daughters words until she grabbed the family heirloom falchion, called Decision off the wall, and stormed away.
Anali Cyl joined the defending forces of Neverwinter and, over the course of the war, became a renowned General. She wielded Decision in every battle since leaving her family estate behind.
