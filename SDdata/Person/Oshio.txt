'''Oshio''' was a [[kensai]] master in [[Kozakura]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Oshio lived and managed his school in [[Tamanokuni]], housed and sponsored by [[Yukio]], a rice merchant. One of his students was Yukio's son.
