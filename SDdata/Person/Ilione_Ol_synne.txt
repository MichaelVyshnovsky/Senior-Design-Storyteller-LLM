'''Ilione Olósynne''' was the third Mage-Queen of the [[Olósynne Dynasty]] who ruled the nation of [[Aglarond]].

==Activities==
Ilione reigned from the {{YearlinkName|1260}}, until the {{YearlinkName|1320}}. She inherited the throne where her brother, [[Halacar Olósynne]], was killed by agents from [[Thay]]. She died of [[Great Plague of the Inner Sea|plague]] in {{Yearlink|1320}}.

==Relationships==
Ilione's mother was [[Ulae Olósynne]].

The [[Simbul]] was known to be Ilione's apprentice.
