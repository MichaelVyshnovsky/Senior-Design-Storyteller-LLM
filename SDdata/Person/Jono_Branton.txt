During the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar]] in the mid-1480s [[DR]],{{DDO-time}} Jono and [[Delethir Mistveil]] attended the injured [[Purple Dragons]] who needed treatment at the [[Eveningstar War Hospital]], under the guidance of [[Hurim Jaskyr]]. Unfortunately, the patients were victims of [[werewolf]] bites, making the situation far worse.
