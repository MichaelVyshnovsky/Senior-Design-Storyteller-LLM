Durn Stoneborn was a passenger aboard the Vigilant in 1374 DR.
Durn was a solemn, heavily armed, and armored duergar. He was not keen on conversing and uttered not a word during the voyage on the Vigilant. Durn used his trusty longsword in battle.
In the  , Durn Stoneborn was among the passengers aboard Captain Lastri Kassirehs Vigilant that traveled from the Sword Coast to the nation of Samarach on the Chultan Peninsula. Several passengers were onboard the ship, including renowned writer Volothamp Geddarm. The voyage was cursed with bad luck and culminated in a deadly storm. The Vigilants shipwreck was washed ashore on Omgars Teeth, not far from the city of Samargol. Durn was among several survivors of the crash and faced a scavenging band of Batiri goblins on the beach shortly after the disaster.
