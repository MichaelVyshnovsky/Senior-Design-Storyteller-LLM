Jarvy was a halfling who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.
Jarvys humor was purposefully low-brow, meant for cheap laughs. When he told his tales, he did so in a bombastic way, as he believed that was the way bards told their stories.
Jatvy was a merchant who owned an stand in the market square of the Protectors Enclave. He sold pies and vegetables. He also dreamed to be a bard, and so he made a few gold by telling tales and gossiping.
