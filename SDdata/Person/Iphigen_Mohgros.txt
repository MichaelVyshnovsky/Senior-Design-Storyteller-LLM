'''Iphigen Mohgros''' was a theologian and a writer, who authored ''[[Judge of the Damned]]'' sometime in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Iphigen penned his [[book]] on the strange occurrences of mortals who reached apotheosis, their mortal lives and divine existence, including the Lords of Death [[Kelemvor]],  sometime after the {{YearlinkName|1368}} and before the {{YearlinkName|1374}}. The book remained in circulation as late as the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, with a copy stored in the library of the [[Stormshore Tabernacle]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].
