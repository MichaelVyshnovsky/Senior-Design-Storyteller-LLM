'''Storstil''' was a caravan drover in the [[Sword Coast]] during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Storstil was known to grimace at [[Narbuth]]'s ramblings. He was sour a sour man.

==Description==
Storstil often wore a distinctive tunic, dun in color with red trim. The man was slim.

==Possessions==
He owned a pipe and smokeweed.

==History==
Storstil worked as a caravan drover in [[Orthil Voldovan]]'s caravan. He was part of the caravan when they headed from [[Scornubel]] to [[Waterdeep]] in late {{yearlink|1357}}, and were joined by [[Shandril Shessair]].

While relieving himself and trying to get a smoke in, Storstil was slain by an unknown assailant who reduced his head to ash with an intense flame.

==Rumors & Legends==
[[Thorst]], another caravan guard, was inclined to believe Shandril was his slayer.{{cite book/Hand of Fire/TPB 2002|89|9}} [[Narbuth]], yet another fellow, thought [[Narm Tamaraith]] was instead.{{cite book/Hand of Fire/TPB 2002|103|7}}
