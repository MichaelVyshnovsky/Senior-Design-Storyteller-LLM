'''Durang Head-taker''' was an [[orc]] chieftain inhabiting [[Tarkhaldale]], the Lost Dale, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Durang was strong and especially crafty.

==Activities==
Durang organized the orcs in the dale into a single force operating out of a fortress within the [[Desertsmouth Mountains]] called [[Swordcrag]].
