'''Mustah Abd al-Nasik''' was a [[mamluk]] who lived in [[Kadarasto]] in east [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|21}}

==Activities==
Mustah was the commander of the [[Devout Mamluk Society|Devout]]. The protection of Prince [[Nari ibn Aman]] was his prime concern.
