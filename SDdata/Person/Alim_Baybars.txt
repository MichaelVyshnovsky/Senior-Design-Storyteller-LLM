'''Alim Baybars''' lived on the island of [[Kaff]] in the [[Djinni's Claws]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Appearance==
Alim's face and body were ruined from constant exposure to humid air, dark magic, and his own obsessive pursuit of power.

==Activities==
Alim was a rare Zakharan in that he willingly departed the Land of Fate in search of powerful magic to give him mastery over the dead.

==History==
Alim left Zakhara at a young age in search of dreadful magic. He returned to Zakhara centuries later to the island of Kaff where he could practice his dark art in secrecy.

==Skills==
Alim was skilled in the use of the sword and [[Necromancy|necromantic magic]].

==Possessions==
Alim owned a ''[[ring of mind shielding]]'', a ''[[wand of fire]]'', a ''[[robe of protection|caftan of protection]] '', a ''[[crystal ball]]'', and a ''[[ring of regeneration]]''.

==Relationships==
Alim had one daughter, [[Melisende Baybars]], who wished to kill him.
