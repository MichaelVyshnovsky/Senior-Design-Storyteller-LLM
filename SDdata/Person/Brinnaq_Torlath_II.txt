King '''Brinnaq Torlath II''' was an [[Amn]]ian monarch of the [[Torlath]] dynasty, known as the second Wizard-King of Amn.

==Relationships==
Brinnaq was the first son of of King [[Jhanos Torlath I|Jhanos I]], and his own first son was [[Imnel Torlath III|Imnel III]].
