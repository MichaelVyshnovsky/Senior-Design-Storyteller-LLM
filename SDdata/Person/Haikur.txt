Haikur was a bushi from Kara-Tur.
He clad himself in blue do-maru armor, with white-and-red kote and a kabuto helm. He had a long, black mustache.
Haikur carried several weapons, including a katana, a nunchaku, and a daikyu for ranged combat.
Haikur was born in a small village in a hilly region of Kara-Tur. He grew up to hate the increasing presence of evil in his lands, becoming skilled in both martial arts and armored combat.
In addition to being an accomplished adventurer, Haikur was a renowned poet.
