Penelope Ringwarble was a writer who lived and published her work sometime circa the late 14 century DR.
Penelopes book – Adopters of Culture, was an anthropological work that voiced her opinion on the culture, or lack thereof, among the half-orcish people of Faerûn. The book was published sometime in or before the  and found among the possessions of the inhabitants of Baldurs Gate.
Penelopes cousin, Joyce Ringwarble, did not share Penelopes favorable view of half-orcs. She showed disdain and desire to sterilize them in her work titled Tusky Grins.
