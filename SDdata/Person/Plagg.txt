Warrior Plagg was a devoted cultist of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.
He wore a suit of goblin-made plate mail armor and bore a greataxe in battle.
In the , Plagg and other members of the Absolutes cult raided the nearby inn known as Waukeens Rest. There they killed many Flaming Fist soldiers, and made off with some captives they marched off towards Moonrise Towers.
After the raid, Plagg, Nik-Nuk, and Crub played a favored game among the Absolutes goblin followers fantasize how they would torture and kill someone.
