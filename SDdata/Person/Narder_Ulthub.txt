Narder Ulthub was a halfling farmer in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.
Narder regularly sold his products to Reyill Werned. He was a troublemaker who liked provoking reactions.
His partnership with Reyill was very good for Narders business over the years.
Circa 1486 DR, Narder was drinking with his halfling friends and Reyill at the Plodding Plow, in the village of The Stop.
Narder was a friend of Roge Applewink and Guylian Stundter. He had good dealings with Reyill Werned.
