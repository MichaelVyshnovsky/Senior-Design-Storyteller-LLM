'''Kadresh Olonrae''' was a [[drow]] [[cleric]] of [[Vhaeraun]] of the city of [[Rilauven]] in the [[Underdark]] in the early 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He sold many pieces of magical [[armor]], [[weapon]]s, and trinkets. Kadresh also sold a variety of [[potion]]s, particularly those that enhanced the strength of those who consumed them.

==History==
He was part of the coup that overthrew Rilauven's existing government, and helped elevate [[Malavon Despana]] to power. He managed to amass a horde of enchanted items left from battles in the region. He sold these while at Malavon's camp in the Underdark in the {{Yearlink|1312}}.

==Personality==
Kadresh was passionate about opposing [[Lolth]] and her matriarchal societies. He was happy to be a part of the [[church of Vhaeraun]] and held a great deal of power.
