'''Waertin Nanszrai''' was a [[half-elf]] [[priest]] of [[Shaundakul]], the Rider of the Winds, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was an old man who wore spectacles.

==Activities==
Waertin lived in [[Elmwood]] on the southern shore of the [[Moonsea (lake)|Moonsea]]. He was one of only a handful of known priests of Shaundakul following the end of the [[Time of Troubles]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.
