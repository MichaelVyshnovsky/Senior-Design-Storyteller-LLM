Waertin Nanszrai was a half-elf priest of Shaundakul, the Rider of the Winds, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He was an old man who wore spectacles.
Waertin lived in Elmwood on the southern shore of the Moonsea. He was one of only a handful of known priests of Shaundakul following the end of the Time of Troubles in the .
