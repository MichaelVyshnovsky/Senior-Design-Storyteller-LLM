Fist '''Murphy''' was a member of the [[Flaming Fist]] mercenary company from the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|''It- it was just a purse, mate. Bloody Hells, you make Blaze Portyr look like a softie.''|Fist Murphy}}

==Description==
Murphy had a fair complexion and pale blue eyes. He wore a set of [[chain armor]] as part of her Flaming Fist uniform and was armed with a simple [[spear]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Fist Murphy was stationed in the [[Basilisk Gate]]'s barracks and prison, serving under Blaze [[Liara Portyr]] and [[Manip Seyama]]. During the war with [[The Absolute]], Fist Murphy was cause stealing a purse of [[gold]] from the evidence locker in the Basilisk Gate. Murphy swore to Blaze Liara Portyr that he made a mistake and would never do such a things again. As a punishment, Portyr ordered Murphy to stand balancing on one foot until she believed his promise. Murphy was trying his best to do just that.
