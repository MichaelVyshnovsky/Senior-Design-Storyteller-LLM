Estele Greymantle was a human woman and a priestess of Eldath. She was a noble lady in the village of Highbank Forest in the Vast around 1370 DR.
She was a retired adventurer and one of the wealthiest of the petty lords in the area. She devoted herself to maintaining and regrowing woodlands in her domain.
