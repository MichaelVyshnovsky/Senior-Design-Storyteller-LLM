Pellanonia Auvryndar was a drow member of House Auvryndar in the drow settlement in the Sargauth Level in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.
Pellanonia was a priestess of Lolth and a daughter of Matron Mother Vlonwelv Auvryndar.
In 1492 DR, Pellanonia was engaged in a consensual act of torture together with her lover Ghirith Nhilran.
Pellanonia was very proud of her heritage and to save her life could divulge precious information to her assailants.
Pellanonia was in love with her fellow female Ghirith.
Pellanonia and Ghirith liked to use their free time torturing each other.
