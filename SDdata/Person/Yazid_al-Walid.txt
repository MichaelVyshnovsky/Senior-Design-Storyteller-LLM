'''Yazid al-Walid''' was a [[moralist]] and member of the [[League Conclave]] in [[I'tiraf]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Originally from [[Hudid]], Yazid was elected to lead the group of delegates from that city to represent them within the League Conclave. Yazid and his fellow delegates ensured Hudid's best interests were heard at court.
