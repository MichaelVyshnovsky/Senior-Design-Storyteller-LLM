'''Tolmen''' was a [[transmuter]] and one of five novice [[wizards]] stationed at the [[Hinnar's Red Wizard enclave|enclave]] run by the young [[Red Wizard]] [[Hinnar]].

==Description==
Tolmen's body bore a magical tattoo

==Abilities==
Tolmen was tough, dexterous, and intelligent and was trained in [[alchemist|alchemy]], spellcraft, and the scribing of scrolls.

==Possessions==
Tolmen carried a [[dagger]] and a [[Light crossbow|small crossbow]].

Tolmen's spellbook contained nineteen different spells.

==Activities==
Tolmen alternated with the other five novice wizards in running the compound's sales booth with two other companions. When sales were slow, Tolmen might offer assistance to the wizards responsible for crafting the magic goods that were for sale.
