Tolmen was a transmuter and one of five novice wizards stationed at the enclave run by the young Red Wizard Hinnar.
Tolmens body bore a magical tattoo
Tolmen was tough, dexterous, and intelligent and was trained in alchemy, spellcraft, and the scribing of scrolls.
Tolmen carried a dagger and a small crossbow.
Tolmens spellbook contained nineteen different spells.
Tolmen alternated with the other five novice wizards in running the compounds sales booth with two other companions. When sales were slow, Tolmen might offer assistance to the wizards responsible for crafting the magic goods that were for sale.
