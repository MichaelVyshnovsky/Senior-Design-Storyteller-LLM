'''Jerome of Melvaunt''' was a shopkeeper in [[Phlan]] in [[1340 DR]].

==Activities==
Jerome had a [[Jerome's of Melvaunt|grocer's shop]] located in the residential district. He also bought items, even stolen goods. Jerome had a knack for procuring requested items within a few days.

==History==
Jerome was a long-time native of Melvaunt until his larcenous activities forced him to flee.
