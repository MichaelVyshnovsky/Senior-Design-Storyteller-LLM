'''Chaless''', also called '''the Cruel''', was a priestess of [[Garagos]] and head of the [[House of Steel]] temple in [[Westgate]] in [[1368 DR]].

==Activities==
Chaless' congregation of faithful consisted of a group of outlaws, murderers, and [[half-orc]] savages.
