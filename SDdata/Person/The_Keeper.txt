'''The Keeper''' was a [[banelar naga]] involved in the [[Zhent]] invasion of [[Shadowdale]] in [[1375 DR]].

==History==
The Keeper was a banelar evoked by clerics of [[Bane]] in order to act as prison warden of the [[Twisted Tower of Ashaba]]. He was eventually defeated by adventures.
