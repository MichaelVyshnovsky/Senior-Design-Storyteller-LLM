Jhalanessa Brithlar was a cleric of Chauntea with the rank of Watchful Sister who resided in the Bounty of the Goddess temple in Voonlar around 1372 DR.
__NOTOC__
She had a bit of a wild spirit that led to mischievousness and deep, passionate feelings about many things.
Watchful Sister Jhalanessa was a horticulturist who specialized in herbalism for both humans and farm animals.
