Skarn Brassaxe was a dwarven commander that committed his forces to the Army of the North, in order to combat the threat of the Shadovar during the late 14 century DR.
In Mirtul of the , Skarn, his dwarves, and the rest of the Army of the North teleported from the Forest of Wyrms to the Shaeradim, just outside the Shadovar-crafted shadowshell.
