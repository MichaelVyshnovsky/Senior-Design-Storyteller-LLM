'''Guld''' was a young [[Zhentilar]] swordsman in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
To his superiors, he was too quick to wag his tongue.

==Abilities==
He helped make traps, and acted as muscle within his squad.

==Activities==
He was a young [[Zhentilar]] warrior.

==Relationships==
He worked together with [[Alorth Bloodshoulder]], and under swordmaster [[Bluth]].

==History==
Guld was part of a group under the control of swordmaster Bluth, sent to ambush and capture [[Shandril Shessair]] during her trip through [[Hullack Forest]] in late {{yearlink|1357}} on the orders of [[Manshoon]]. He built a concealed spiked pit trap as part of the ambush.
