'''Peraphon Thond''' was a [[Cormyr]]ean of the Merchant [[Thond|House Thond]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. By [[1356 DR]], they had moved their jewelry business to the [[Moonsea]] region. While Peraphon would purchase gems from the trading hub of Hillsfar, they were later resold in the [[Sword Coast]].
