Adjam el Drakhon was syl-pasha of Calimport during the Third age of Calimshan.
Syl-Pasha Adjams son was one of the sultans of Calimport.
In , the famous wizard Ali el Samesaj recued Adjams granddaughter from kidnappers. As a reward, Ali was made court wizard of Adjams son.
During the entirety of Adjams reign, the empire was at war with the beholders of the Alimir Hive, led by Qyraaptir the Bloodeye.
