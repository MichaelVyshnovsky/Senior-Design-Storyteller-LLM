'''Adjam el Drakhon''' was [[syl-pasha]] of [[Calimport]] during the [[Third age of Calimshan]].

==Relationships==
Syl-Pasha Adjam's son was one of the [[sultan]]s of Calimport.

==History==
In {{Yearlink|-1161}}, the famous [[wizard]] [[Ali el Samesaj]] recued Adjam's granddaughter from kidnappers. As a reward, Ali was made court wizard of Adjam's son.

During the entirety of Adjam's reign, the empire was at war with the [[beholder]]s of the [[Alimir Hive]], led by [[Qyraaptir|Qyraaptir the Bloodeye]].
