'''Casarial''' was a female [[black dragon]], youngest daughter of [[Hesior]] and granddaughter of the great [[Thauglorimorgorus]]. Her mate was [[Mirantol]] and she was the mother of [[Kreston]].  

== Description ==
She had a scar over her left eye given to her by her grandsire for being impetuous.
