==Activities==
Usamah was both the [[nakhuda]] and [[rubban]] of his sailing vessel. This was a source of great pride for him.

==Possessions==
Usamah owned ''[[leather armor]] +2'', a ''[[figurine of wondrous power]] (marble elephant)'', and a ''[[scroll of protection from fire]]''.
