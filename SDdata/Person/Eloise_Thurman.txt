Eloise Thurman was an inhabitant of the Brampton neighborhood of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
In the , Eloise Thurman attempted to report suspicious housecalls late at night among the neighbors as well as people failing to make offerings to Umberlee, claiming that these activities were proof of the Cult of the Absolutes activity. Eloise made the reports to Liara Portyrs garrison, where they were dismissed.
