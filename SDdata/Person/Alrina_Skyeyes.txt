Alrina Skyeyes was a wizard sailing aboard the Osprey during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
She stood  tall. She had soft brown eyes and many men found her attractive.
Alrina served as ships wizard aboard the Osprey. Captain Raider McAllister rescued her father when she was much younger, and the two were friends for many years. She loved life at sea but often thought of adventuring on the land in order to acquire more magic items.
Alrina carried a cloak of protection, wand of lightning bolts, decanter of endless water, ring of water elemental control, and several scrolls and potions.
