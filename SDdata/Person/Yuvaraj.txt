'''Yuvaraj''' was the last [[Lord Artificer]] of [[Imaskar]].

==Relationships==
He rode a [[copper dragon]] into battle.

==Possessions==
He wore the ''First [[Imaskarcana]]'' as his crown, while wielding a fabled tome of the same set of artifacts, probably the ''[[Third Imaskarcana|Third]]''.

==History==
During the battle the final confrontation between the [[Imaskari]] and the [[Mulan]] and their deities in {{Yearlink|-2488}}, he fell in battle with the deity [[Horus-Re|Horus]] at the palace of [[Inupras]].
