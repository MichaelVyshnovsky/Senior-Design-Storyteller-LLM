Huedyn was a half-elven druid inhabiting the Dernall Forest in Callidyrr in the Moonshae Isles in the .
Huedyn was part of a group of druids under Great Druid Antola Pilark who were allied with House Kendricks forces against the dark fey infesting the Dernall Forest. Huedryn was actually a planted spy for the dark fey who was trying to expose the druids and forces of Callidyrr to attack.
