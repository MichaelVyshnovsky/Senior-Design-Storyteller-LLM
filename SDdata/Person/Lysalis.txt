}}'''Lysalis''' was a female [[fey'ri]] [[wizard]] in service to [[Kaanyr Vhok]], the leader of the [[Scoured Legion]] at the time of the Legion's assault on [[Sundabar]].

==History==
During the attack in [[1385 DR]], Lysalis helped Vhok to enter unseen into the town but was later killed fighting some [[dwarf|dwarven]] guards. She was secretly replaced by a [[half-dragon]] named [[Myshik Morueme]].
