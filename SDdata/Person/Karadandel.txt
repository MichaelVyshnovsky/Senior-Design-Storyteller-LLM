'''Karadandel''' was a fence and information broker who operated out of [[Tarmalune]] as of [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Pyrophobia|15}}

==Description==
Karadandel was a [[tiefling]] woman with long honey-blonde hair and mischievous blue eyes.

==Activities==
Karadandel frequented darren (gambling clubs), where she sold her services to the best bidder. She was one of the few citizens in Tarmalune who knew the truth about the [[Achazar|Raging Flame]] amid the city.
