Karadandel was a fence and information broker who operated out of Tarmalune as of 1479 DR.
Karadandel was a tiefling woman with long honey-blonde hair and mischievous blue eyes.
Karadandel frequented darren (gambling clubs), where she sold her services to the best bidder. She was one of the few citizens in Tarmalune who knew the truth about the Raging Flame amid the city.
