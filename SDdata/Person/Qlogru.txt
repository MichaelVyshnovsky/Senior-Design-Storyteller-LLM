==Abilities==
In addition to typical illithid abilities, they were a powerful arcane spellcaster, and were served by various creatures including [[water elemental]]s and [[skeleton]]s.

==History==
Qlogru lurked near the [[drow]] city of [[Sschindylryn]] in the [[Underdark]] around the time of their [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar|invasion of Eveningstar]].
