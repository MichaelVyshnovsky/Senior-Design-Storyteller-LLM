'''Zetch'r'r''' was a [[githyanki]] [[blackguard]] and warlord in the city of [[Tu'narath]] in the [[Astral Plane]].

==Description==
==Personality==
Zetch'r'r was a blackguard, but he was also an honorable githyanki who kept his word and valued the teachings of [[Gith]]. He believed githyanki and githzerai should reunite and become a single people again.

==Activities==
