Zetchrr was a githyanki blackguard and warlord in the city of Tunarath in the Astral Plane.
Zetchrr was a blackguard, but he was also an honorable githyanki who kept his word and valued the teachings of Gith. He believed githyanki and githzerai should reunite and become a single people again.
Zetchrr was a warlord in Tunarath and, in secret, he was a sponsor of the Shasal Khou.
