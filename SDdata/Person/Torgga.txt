Torgga was a duergar looter who was active on the Sword Coast region during the late 15 century DR. For a time, she operated under the command of Gimblebock.
She had dark skin and white hair that she wore back in a braid.
Torgga wore a suit of padded armor and carried with her a shortbow.
In the , Torgga and the rest of Gimblebocks gang ventured into an ancient crypt near the banks of the River Chionthar. She was among the more battle-hardened group that delved deepter into the crypt, while Gimblebock and a few of his lackeys laid claim to an alien vessel that crashed nearby.
