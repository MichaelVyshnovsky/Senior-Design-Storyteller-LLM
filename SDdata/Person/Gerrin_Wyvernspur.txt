'''Lord Gerrin Wyvernspur''' was a member of the [[Wyvernspur (house)|Wyvernspur]] noble house of [[Cormyr]].

==Background==
Gerrin fought against the [[red dragon]]s in the attack on [[Suzail]] in [[1018 DR]], and subsequently pursued [[Thauglor]], who badly burned him, although Thauglor was defeated.
