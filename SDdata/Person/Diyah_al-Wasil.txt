'''Diyah al-Wasil''' was an inhabitant of [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Diyah had oily skin that marred his complexion. Despite looking like a scoundrel, he was completely loyal to Huzuz and the [[Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir|Grand Caliph]].

==Personality==
Diyah's competency was complimented by his thoroughness, making him a perfect tax collector.

==Activities==
Diyah was responsible for the collection of taxes and inspecting caravans passing through the [[Pilgrims' Gate]] in Huzuz.
