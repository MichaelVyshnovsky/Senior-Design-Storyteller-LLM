'''Garwin''' was a mage in [[Phlan]] in [[1340 DR]].

==History==
Along with his [[ogre]] bodyguard, [[Buldwar]], Garwin was instructed by the [[Tyranthraxus|Boss]] to go to the [[Podol Plaza]] market to examine, bid on, and eventually acquire a [[magic item]] - a [[wand of fear]].
