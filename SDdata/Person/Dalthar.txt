Dalthar was a cultist of the Absolute that ventured along with her comrades into the ruins of Grymforge in the late 15 century DR.
She was a dwarf of few words and extremely wary of outsiders that came near her groups operations.
Dalthar preferred to wield a crossbow when called to battle. Like others of her order, she wore an amulet bearing the unholy symbol of the Absolute.
