'''Dalthar''' was a [[Cult of the Absolute|cultist]] of [[the Absolute]] that ventured along with her comrades into the ruins of [[Grymforge]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
She was a [[duergar|dwarf]] of few words and extremely wary of outsiders that came near her group's operations.

==Possessions==
Dalthar preferred to wield a [[crossbow]] when called to battle. Like others of her order, she wore an amulet bearing the unholy symbol of the Absolute.

==History==
She was among the [[duergar]] tasked by [[True Soul]] [[Nere]] with uncovering an entrance to a long-lost [[Shar]]ran temple within Grymforge in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
