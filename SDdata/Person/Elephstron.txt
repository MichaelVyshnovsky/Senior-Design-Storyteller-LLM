'''Elephstron''' was the leader of a group of twelve merchants who purchased the fortified stronghold that would later be known as [[Zhentil Keep]].<ref name=FRA-p121">

==History==
Elephstron and his group purchased the stronghold from [[Orlephar Flostren]]. Elephstron later perished at the hand of [[Zhentar]], a [[wizard]] whom he invited to help govern the quickly growing city. Zhentar also died in the battle.<ref name=FRA-p121"/>
