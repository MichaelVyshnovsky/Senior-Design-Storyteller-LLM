Rhiiman Obarskyr was the brother of the first king of Cormyr, Faerlthann Obarskyr.
Rhiimans father was Ondeth Obarskyr and his mother was Suzara Obarskyr. His older brother was the first king of Cormyr, Faerlthann Obarskyr.
Rhiiman came to the land that would become known as Suzail with his parents and his older brother in 6 DR.
