Dickon Sharken was a skilled mercenary who lived in the city of Pyarados in Thay during the mid-14 century DR. He nearly reported observations of his varied encounters to the regions tharchion, Valerios Theokillos.
Dickon was known as a particularly vicious, even sadistic, individual, traits that led to high recommendations from those who retained his services. He followed directions exceptionally well and carried his own in combat.
The cruel mercenary traveled to Thay when he was a teenager, and has traveled through the Sunrise Mountains a number of times by the year 1357 DR.
