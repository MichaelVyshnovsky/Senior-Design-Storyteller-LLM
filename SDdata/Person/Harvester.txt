}}The '''Harvester''', or '''He Who Harvests for Hunger''', was a sinister [[yak-man]] who lived beneath [[Huzuz]] in the [[Lair of the Unspeakable]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Gem of Zakhara|77}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Golden Huzuz|51}}

==Activities==
The Harvester hunted human prey on the streets of Golden Huzuz. He performed his hunts weekly accompanied by a single [[dao|earthservant]]. His victims were returned to the Lair of the Unspeakable deep underground where they were kept locked in cages
