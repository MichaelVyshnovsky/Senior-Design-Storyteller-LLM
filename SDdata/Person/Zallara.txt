Zallara was a drow priestess of Eilistraee.
Zallara had black skin and very long, flowing white hair.
Like other followers of Eilistraee, Zallara was good-natured and wanted to aid her race in peacefully returning to the surface of Toril.
Zallara owned a blessed magical scimitar.
She participated in moonlit rituals such as High Hunt and Circle of Song.
