High Priestess '''Josura Hlammel''' was a worshiper of [[Lathander]] inhabiting [[Scornubel]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Josura was the leader of the Morninglord's faithful within the [[Healing House of Lathander]]. She was assisted by 12 other priests and 42 followers.
