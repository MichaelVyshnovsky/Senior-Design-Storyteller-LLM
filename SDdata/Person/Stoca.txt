'''Stoca''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and [[spell]] inventor who lived during the [[Golden Age]].

==History==
Stoca developed six spells in total, all within an eight-year period. The spells they invented were ''[[shape change]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2061|NY}}, ''[[plant growth]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2062|NY}}, ''[[animal growth]]'' and ''[[fly]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2065|NY}}, ''[[water breathing]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2067|NY}}, and lastly, ''[[enervation]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2069|NY}}.
