'''Olhin Shalut''' was a tavern owner inhabiting the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Olhin was a cocky old man.

==Activities==
He owned [[the Ship's Wheel]] tavern located in the western section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] on the north side of [[Diamond Street]] where it joined [[Westwall Street]] near the [[West Gate]].

Olhin used to be an [[adventurer]]. He wisely invested his money to make him wealthy upon his retirement from the adventurer's life.

==Possessions==
He owned a slew of [[magic item]]s including a ''[[ring of blinking]]'', ''[[ring of protection]]'', ''[[rod of lordly might]]'', ''[[iron bands of Bilarro]]'', a ''[[Sword of quickness|shortsword of quickness]]'', and several more.
