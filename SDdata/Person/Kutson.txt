'''Kutson''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and [[spell]] inventor who lived during the [[Golden Age]] and [[Age of Discovery]].

==History==
This arcanist developed three spells during their life. The spells they invented were ''[[programmed illusion]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2618|NY}}, ''[[antipathy/sympathy]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2645|NY}}, and finally, ''[[mind blank]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2685|NY}}.
