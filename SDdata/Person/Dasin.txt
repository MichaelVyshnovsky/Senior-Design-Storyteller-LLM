Dasin was an aquatic elf Dukar of the Order of Kupavi in Myth Nantar.
Dasin was a young wizard-in-training in Faenasuor when she had a dream where she was summoned to Myth Nantar. She followed the dreams suggestion and went to the city. She joined the Dukars and the paragons decided to kept an eye over her.
