Booyagh '''Shekt''' was a [[spellcaster]] of the [[cult of the Absolute]] who lived in the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Shekt had orange-brown skin and green hair she wore swept over to one side. She wore a simple cloth tunic, decorated with feathers and animals claws.

==Personality==
Unlike her fellow [[goblin]] cultists, Shekt was very outgoing and friendly to outsiders who came into the cult's fortress.

==Possessions==
She carried with her a large [[shaman]]istic staff decorated with the spine of some unknown creature.
