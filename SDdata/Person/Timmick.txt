'''Timmick''' was a child who lived in the town of [[Moonhaven]] sometime during or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
At some point, Timmick accidentally swallowed a bottle [[cork]]. The Town's healer encouraged the boy to pass the cork naturally, and gave him a stern talking to.
