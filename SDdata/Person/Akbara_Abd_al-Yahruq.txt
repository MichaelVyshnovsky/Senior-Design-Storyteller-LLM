'''Akbara Abd al-Yahruq''' was a [[mamluk]] inhabiting [[Talab]] in [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|21}}

==Activities==
Akbara was the leader of the [[Parched Mamluk Society]] and held the title of Most Respected Warrior. 

She ensured that the supply of fresh water for the city of Talab was never compromised.
