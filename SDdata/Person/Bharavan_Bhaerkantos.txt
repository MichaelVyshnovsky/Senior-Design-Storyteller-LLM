'''Bharavan Bhaerkantos''' was a merchant and member of the [[Thousandheads Trading Coster]].

==Activities==
Bharavan was the founder and leader of the Thousandheads Trading Coster. He worked out of his base of operations in his stronghold located just east of [[Riatavin]].

==History==
In his youth Bharavan was an adventurer. In [[1370 DR]] he was a member of the [[Merchant's Chosen]] and one of the leaders of the rebellion against [[Amn]].
