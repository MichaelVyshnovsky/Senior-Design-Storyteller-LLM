'''Praven''' was a student of [[Neverwinter Academy]] in [[1374 DR]].

==Biography==
Praven was a student of Neverwinter Academy, and he liked to amuse ladies. When [[Qara]] caused trouble at the Academy, Praven went after her. When Qara and the [[Kalach-Cha]] came out of the [[Sunken Flagon]], Praven would confront them and attack.
