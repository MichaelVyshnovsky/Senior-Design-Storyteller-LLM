Luan was a news hawker who worked for the Baldurs Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
At the age of six, Luan was a pale-skinned human child with icy-blue eyes and hazel hair. The boy stuttered, was timid and very unsure of himself.
In the , during the Absolute crisis, Luan could often be found peddling the Baldurs Mouth Gazette on the plaza outside of the Basilisk Gate in the Lower City.
