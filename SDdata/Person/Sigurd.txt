'''Sigurd''' was a [[shield dwarf]] man of the dwarven city of [[Earthfast]] of the [[Earthfast Mountains]] of [[north Faer没n]], in the [[Earthroot]] region of the [[Underdark]].

==History==
Sigurd was a warrior of Earthfast and an ally of the [[Harpers]].

He made a number of journeys to [[Rashemen]].

On his travels, Sigurd spent a great deal of time with the [[Rashemaar]] and their [[Berserker (Rashemi)|berserker]]s, making many friends among them

Sigurd wrote a report on Rashemen for the [[scribe]] [[Lhaeo Rhindaun]]. Lhaeo used it as part of his report on the [[Unapproachable East]], sent to Lady [[Alustriel Silverhand]] of [[Silverymoon]] in the [[1368 DR|Year of the Banner, 1368 DR]].

==Personality==
Like many dwarves, Sigurd despised the water and hated swimming, and grew seasick even on Lake Ashane.

==Possessions==
Sigurd wielded an axe in battle.
