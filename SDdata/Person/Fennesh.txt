Fennesh was a captain of the city guard of Westgate in the late 14 century DR.
Fennesh was a stout and extremely muscular dwarf of intimidating physical presence. He wore a set of splint mail in the uniform colors of the city guard. His brown beard was decoratively braided, and a bald spot shone from the back of the dwarfs head.
He was a brash and impatient man who valued his time and hated small talk and questions.
By the , Captain Fennesh grew increasingly frustrated with the overwhelming bureaucratic paperwork he and his city guard were forced to fill. He thought forms and reports took away time that would be better used for arresting criminals.
