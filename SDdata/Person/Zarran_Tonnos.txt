High Moonmistress Zarran Tonnos was a Selûnite priest and the leader of Moonmaidens Hall in Murann of Amn during the 8 century DR.
In the , during her service at Moonmaidens Hall, Zarran was informed of the charges laid against the New Moon Pact by High Moonmistress Brima Chintamn of Bright Ladys Tower in distant Chancelgaunt. Believing that the Pact had indeed fallen to the heresy of believing Shar and Selûne the same being, Zarran supported the charges and condoned the extermination of the Pact.
