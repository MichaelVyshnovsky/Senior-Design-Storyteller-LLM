'''Kallicl''' was a priest of [[Zaltec]] in [[Nexal]], [[Maztica]].

==History==
In [[1361 DR]], he was the right-hand man of high priest [[Hoxitl]]. He conspired with him to kill [[Erixitl]].

During the [[Viperhands]] attack at the start of the [[Night of Wailing]], he reported to Hoxitl on the situation of the battle.
