'''Imrar Roaryl''' was the Crown Prince of [[Eskorn]], [[Laerakond]], as of [[1479 DR]]. He was a member of the  [[Clan Roaryl|Roaryl]] dynasty.

==Personality==
Imrar was a hotheaded, proud, and capable warrior who underestimated his younger siblings.

==Background==
As of 1479 DR, Prince Imrar was the heir of Eskorn's throne.

==Relationships==
Crown Prince Imrar was the older son of King [[Ornan Roaryl]], and the elder brother of [[Flandarr Roaryl|Flandarr]] and [[Dlorna Roaryl]].
