'''Aerix Vokototh''' ({{Pronounce|AER|ix|VOH|kuh|toth|refs={{Template:Cite book/Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden|12}}}}) was a young [[Reghedmen]] of the [[Tribe of the Wolf]] who lived in [[Icewind Dale]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Template:Cite book/Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden|211}}

==Abilities==
Due to the circumstances of his birth, Aerix was not harmed by extreme cold as much as other were.

==Relationships==
The young boy was the son of his clan's chieftain.

==History==
Aerix was born on the day of [[Midwinter]], an occasion that was blessed by the Frostmaiden [[Auril]].

In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Aerix's camp was assaulted by the Wolf King [[Isarr Kronenstrom]] and his loyal Wolf Tribe barbarians. Aerix's father and many of his clansmen were slain in the raid, though the young boy managed to survive for some time.
