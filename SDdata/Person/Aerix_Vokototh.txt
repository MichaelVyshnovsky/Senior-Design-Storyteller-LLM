Aerix Vokototh () was a young Reghedmen of the Tribe of the Wolf who lived in Icewind Dale during the late 15 century DR.
Due to the circumstances of his birth, Aerix was not harmed by extreme cold as much as other were.
The young boy was the son of his clans chieftain.
Aerix was born on the day of Midwinter, an occasion that was blessed by the Frostmaiden Auril.
In the , Aerixs camp was assaulted by the Wolf King Isarr Kronenstrom and his loyal Wolf Tribe barbarians. Aerixs father and many of his clansmen were slain in the raid, though the young boy managed to survive for some time.
