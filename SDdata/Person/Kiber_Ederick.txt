Kiber Ederick was a paladin of Tempus inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Kiber performed his daily morning prayers in the House of Heroes.
During his adventuring days Kiber owned a set of enchanted plate mail, an enchanted flame tongue bastard sword, a ring of water walking, and a bag of holding.
