Private Mather was a soldier of the Neverwinter Guard during the late 15 century DR.
In the , Private Mather was injured by one of the Many-Arrows orcs while making a delivery in Neverwinters Tower District. He was on his way to deliver a chest of components to Mtari Printal of the Seven Suns Trading Coster at her shop in Protectors Enclave, but then he took an arrow in the knee. He beseeched an adventurer to aid him.
