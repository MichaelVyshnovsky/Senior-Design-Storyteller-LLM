Private '''Mather''' was a soldier of the [[Neverwinter Guard]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{Quote|I'm running behind schedule!|Private Mather, not running anywhere}}

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Private Mather was injured by one of the [[Many-Arrows]] [[orc]]s while making a delivery in Neverwinter's [[Tower District]].{{NWDistricts}} He was on his way to deliver a chest of components to [[M'tari Prin'tal]] of the [[Seven Suns Trading Coster]] at her [[Professions Supply|shop]] in [[Neverwinter/Protector's Enclave|Protector's Enclave]], but then he took an [[arrow]] in the knee. He beseeched an adventurer to aid him.
