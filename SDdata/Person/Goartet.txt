'''Goartet''' was a [[thief]] and shopkeeper inhabiting [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was quite lazy.

==Activities==
Goartet was one of the owners of [[Reliable Guides]], a shop that hired out guides to take adventuresome individuals through the ruins of Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the {{YearlinkName|1368}}.

His shop was located on the [[Throat (road)|Throat]] outside of the [[Throat Gate]] of the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]]. 

==Relationships==
His brother, and co-owner of Reliable Guides, was [[Jenzar]].
