Torgrave Needlebaum was the seneschal of Hillsfar in 1491 DR.
Torgrave was the citys seneschal and Head of Household. He also kept the cleanest house of the city. Torgrave knew all gossip in the city and was often consulted for this knowledge.
In 1491 DR, Torgrave attended the First Lords Midsummer Masquerade ballet.
He possibly hired as servers an adventuring party (who had been hired by Elanil Elassidil to infiltrate the masquerade without Torgraves knowledge).
During the visit of the court to Yulash the Harpers discovered his true work and possibly ordered to Harpers-affiliated adventurers to capture him alive.
Torgrave was proud of his clean house. He was friendly and outgoing, living his dream. Torgrave always took care of others problems.
