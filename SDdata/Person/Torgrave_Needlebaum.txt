'''Torgrave Needlebaum''' was the seneschal of [[Hillsfar]] in [[1491 DR]].
 
==Activities==
Torgrave was the city's seneschal and Head of Household. He also kept the cleanest house of the city. Torgrave knew all gossip in the city and was often consulted for this knowledge.{{Cite adventurers league/Death on the Wall|25}}
 
==History==
In [[1491 DR]], Torgrave attended the First Lord's [[Midsummer Masquerade]] ballet.{{Cite adventurers league/Hillsfar Reclaimed|9}}

He possibly hired as servers an adventuring party (who had been hired by [[Elanil Elassidil]] to infiltrate the masquerade without Torgrave's knowledge).{{Cite adventurers league/Hillsfar Reclaimed|12}}

During the visit of the court to [[Yulash]] the [[Harpers]] discovered his true work and possibly ordered to Harpers-affiliated adventurers to capture him alive.{{Cite adventurers league/Death on the Wall|15}}

==Personality==
Torgrave was proud of his clean house. He was friendly and outgoing, living his dream. Torgrave always took care of others' problems.
