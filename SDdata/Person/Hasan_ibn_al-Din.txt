'''Hasan ibn al-Din''' was a [[human]] [[corsair]] living on the various lands of the [[Great Sea]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Hasan was a young and inexperienced [[rubban]] whom [[Fate]] deemed worthy of elevating. He had a passion for adventure and was willing to learn from the more experienced sailors working under his command.

==Possessions==
Hasan owned a ''[[great scimitar]] +2'', ''[[leather armor]] +3'', a ''[[potion of fire resistance]]'', and a ''[[scroll of protection from possession]]''.
