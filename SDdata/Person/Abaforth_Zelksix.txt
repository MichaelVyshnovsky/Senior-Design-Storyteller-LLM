Abaforth Zelksix was a duergar from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Abaforth wore a simple set of chainmail armor.
In the , Abaforth Zelksix, along with other duergar, giant spiders, and drow, was slaughtered in the basement caver under the home of Bloomridge Park groundskeeper, Lavernica. Abaforts body was left broken near the mutilated body of Incontinentia Figgin, who was turned into a grim effigy by Orin the Red and her Murder Tribunal of Bhaal.
