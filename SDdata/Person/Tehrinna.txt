'''Tehrinna''' was the proprietor of [[The Lake Spirits]], a bathhouse and inn located in the [[Amn]]ian city of [[Amnwater]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Tehrinna had flame red hair and a sweet nature. Despite being only {{SI|4'6"|ft-in}} tall, she was muscular enough to expel most troublemakers from her inn.
