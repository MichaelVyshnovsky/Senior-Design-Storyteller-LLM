Tehrinna was the proprietor of The Lake Spirits, a bathhouse and inn located in the Amnian city of Amnwater during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Tehrinna had flame red hair and a sweet nature. Despite being only  tall, she was muscular enough to expel most troublemakers from her inn.
