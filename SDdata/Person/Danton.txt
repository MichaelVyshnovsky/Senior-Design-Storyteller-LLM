Danton was a tiefling druid and a peacock breeder who lived and died in the Western Heartlands during the late 15 century DR.
Danton was a tiefling man with garish blue skin, long blue hair, and short horns. He had glowing green eyes.
In the late 15 century DR, Danton was a refined druid who attempted to escape approaching cultists of the Absolute whist in the shape of one of the peacocks he cared for. This attempt was in vain and ended in Dantons demise in the . Shortly after, he ended up among the dozen individuals plucked from the Fugue Plane, chosen by the entity Withers to aid in his campaign against the Dead Three. Like other "hirelings," Danton forgot his living tongue and could only communicate through Withers words.
