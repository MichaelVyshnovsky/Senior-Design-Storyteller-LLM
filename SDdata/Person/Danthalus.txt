'''Danthalus''' was a [[Highknight]] of [[Cormyr]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Danthalus was a [[highknight]].

==Relationships==
During the raid in {{yearlink|1479}} on [[Tethgard]], Danthalus was part of the team including [[Ilvellund Nordroun]], [[Hondryn]], and [[Merlar]].

==History==
Danthalus was part of the raid on Tethgard led by [[Kelgantor]], looking for the fugitive wizard [[Elminster Aumar]], who had recently stolen the ''[[Royal Gorget of Battle]]'' from the Royal Palace in [[Cormyr]].
