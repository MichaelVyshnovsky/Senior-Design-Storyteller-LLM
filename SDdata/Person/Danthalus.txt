Danthalus was a Highknight of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.
Danthalus was a highknight.
During the raid in  on Tethgard, Danthalus was part of the team including Ilvellund Nordroun, Hondryn, and Merlar.
Danthalus was part of the raid on Tethgard led by Kelgantor, looking for the fugitive wizard Elminster Aumar, who had recently stolen the Royal Gorget of Battle from the Royal Palace in Cormyr.
