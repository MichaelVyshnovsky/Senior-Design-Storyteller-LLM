Aerlyse was a half-drow psion and a member of Bregan Daerthe as of the late 15 century DR.
Only those with intelligence and charm could impress Aerlyse.
Aerlyse was a student of Kimmuriel Oblodra, the powerful psionic second-in-command of Bregan Daerthe.
In the , Aerlyse was sent along with other Bregan Daerthe operatives to secure an encampment in the ruins of Castle Never, and from there to explore the crypts and spy on the city of Neverwinter. In addition, Aerlyse had been given a special mission from Kimmuriel to spy on the captain of the Bregan Daerthe camp, Xalbyn, and did so dutifully.
