'''Yaelyn Mitraliir''' was a [[wood elven]] blacksmith for hire from the city of [[Neverwinter]], active in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Yaelyn Mitraliir was thought to be different from her wood elven kin because she took on a smithing vocation, but she found a fascination with refining of [[metal]]s and highlighting its natural beauty. Becoming a virtuoso blacksmith, Yaelyn Mitraliir made a living by doing work for hire in the city of [[Neverwinter]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
