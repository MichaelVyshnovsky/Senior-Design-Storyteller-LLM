'''Bhuraelea''' was an [[elven]] [[sorceress]] of [[Cormanthyr]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Bhuraelea was selected to protect [[Mythanthor]] by the [[Srinshee]] in {{yearlink|241}} after the death of the [[human]] [[armathor]], [[Elminster Aumar]]. While guarding his tower, she was ambushed and felled by a number of elven mages bent on slaying her charge.{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|16}}
