'''Naloc''' was the high priest of [[Zaltec]] at [[Kultaka]] and one of the closest advisors of the Kultakan's ruler [[Takamal]].

==History==
In [[1361 DR]], he was with Takamal during the battle against the [[Golden Legion]], when, in order to turn the destiny of the conflict, the [[mage]] [[Darien]] [[levitate]]d to them and killed first Naloc and then Takamal.
