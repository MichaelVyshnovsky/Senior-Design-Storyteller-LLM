Nuthos Eagleshield was a noble inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Nuthos was the patriarch of the Eagleshield noble family of Waterdeep. He lived within Eagleshield Villa on Feather Street in Waterdeeps Sea Ward.
Nuthos consort was Glenn Eagleshield and his eldest daughter and heir was Arundel.
