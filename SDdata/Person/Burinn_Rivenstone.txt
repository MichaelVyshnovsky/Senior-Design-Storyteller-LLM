Clanlaird '''Burinn Rivenstone''' was the [[rock gnome]] ruler of the town of [[Zashuma]] in [[Lapaliiya]] in [[1373 DR]]. Zashuma was the Lapaliiyan town devoted to [[Garl Glittergold]] and a farming community renowned for wines and olives.
