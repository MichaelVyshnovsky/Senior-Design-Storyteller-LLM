'''Maresta Rhanbuck''' was an apprentice [[wizard]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
She was a [[half-elf]] with [[moon elf]] blood.

==Activities==
Maresta was a student of [[Blackstaff Academy]] within [[Blackstaff Tower]] in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] of Waterdeep in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}. She adopted the role of a motherly figure among the other students, and helped [[Laeral Silverhand]] run the daily operations of the tower.
