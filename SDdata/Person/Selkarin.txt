King Selkarin was the ruler of Estagund in the Shining South during the 11 century DR.
Selkarin led an invasion of Durpar and Veldorn in the . Both nations resisted his advances so Selkarin turned his attention to Dambrath. His fleet attacked Dambraths southern coast but were beaten back with severity, resulting in Selkarins death.
Selkarin was a descendant of King Bonrial. As Selkarin died without producing any offspring, his brother Seltarir took the throne upon his death.
