'''Rymel''' was a [[bard]] adventurer, member of the [[Company of the Bright Spear]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He had a short beard.{{cite book/Spellfire/Paperback 2005||2}}

==Personality==
He took setbacks in stride{{cite book/Spellfire/Paperback 2005||3}}

==Abilities==
His skill at telling tales was great, and he commanded much attention at taverns for it.

==Possessions==
He was known to have owned a lute, a sword, and a dagger.

==Relationships==
He was a member of the Company of the Bright Spear.

==History==
At [[the Rising Moon (inn)|the Rising Moon]] in [[Mirtul 6]]{{cite book/Spellfire/Paperback 1988|25-26|2}}

The following morning, after a discussion in which Rymel had said more than anyone but [[Thail]], [[Burlane]] changed the course for the entire company, heading for the [[Semberflow]] along the way to [[Myth Drannor]].{{cite book/Spellfire/Paperback 1988|29-33|2}}

The following morning on [[Mirtul 12]],{{cite book/Spellfire/Paperback 1988|32-38|2}}

In his final combat at the [[Gates of Doom]] when they were ambushed on [[Mirtul 20]],{{cite book/Spellfire/Paperback 1988|49-52|3}} Delg confirmed his death later that year.
