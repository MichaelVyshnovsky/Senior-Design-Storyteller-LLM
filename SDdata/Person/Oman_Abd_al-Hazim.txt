'''Oman Abd al-Hazim''' was a [[mamluk]] general of the [[Respected Mamluk Society|Respected]] who lived in [[Muluk]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|21}}

==Activities==
A skilled warrior in his own right, Oman also commanded 1,500 mamluks of the Respected, including 500 cavalry, for the city of Muluk.
