'''Kruts Marpin''' was a [[human]] inhabitant of [[Eveningstar]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}

==Description==
Kruts had tanned skin, and kept a small black moustache and a shaved head.

==History==
{{Quote
|We'll make it through this. Us Evenors are tougher than we look.
|Kruts
}}
During the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar]], Kruts could be seen fishing on the [[Starwater River]]. He seemed relatively unconcerned with the ongoing [[drow]] aggression against his town.
