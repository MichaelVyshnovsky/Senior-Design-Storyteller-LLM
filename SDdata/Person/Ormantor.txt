Ormantor was one of the bodyguards for Marlin Stormserpent in the late 15 century DR.
He was immense and very muscular.
He usually said nothing, but thought his lords caginess was kind of stupid.
He worked as a bodyguard for Marlin Stormserpent.
His fellow bodyguard was Gaskur.
By , Ormantor had served Marlin Stormserpent for six or seven seasons.
Ormantor accompanied Marlin Stormserpent on his way to the Dragonskull Chamber in Castle Obarskyr, when the latter wanted to try out the power of the Blueflame items.
