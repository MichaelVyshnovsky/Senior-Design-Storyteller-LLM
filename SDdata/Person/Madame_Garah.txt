'''Madame Garah''' was a businessperson who ran a boarding house in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Madame Garah owned a [[Madame Garah's Boarding House|boarding house]] named after herself. Her establishment was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]] on the south side of [[Slop Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}
