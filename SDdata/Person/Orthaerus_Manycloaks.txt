'''Orthaerus Manycloaks''' was a [[wizard]] who lived in the [[Wildwoods]] mansion near [[Waymoot]] in [[1367 DR]].

==History==
Orthaerus was slain during a spell-duel by the wizard Endarthar.

==Activities==
As a [[ghost]] who retained full spellcasting abilities, Orthaerus tormented [[Endarthar]] relentlessly. Any visitors to Wildwoods who showed hostility toward Endarthar were aided by Orthaerus.
