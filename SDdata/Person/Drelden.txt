'''Drelden''' was an innkeeper in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
As an innkeeper, Drelden threw out everyone who drew steel in his home.

==History==
In {{yearlink|241}}, Drelden was the innkeeper of the [[Herald's Horn]] near [[Cormyr]]. He had built the place himself, per his reckoning.
