'''Malathon''' was a [[half-orc]] [[cleric]] of [[Bane]] who helped in the attack on [[Shadowdale]].

==Abilities==
His [[cleric]] domains were [[Destruction domain|Destruction]] and [[Evil domain|Evil]].

==Possessions==
The Dark Doom wore ''+3 [[full plate]]'' that provided him with resistance to [[spell]]s in battle, and wielded an enchanted ''+2 [[morningstar]]''. For additional protection, Malathon carried a ''[[ring of counterspells]]'' and an ''[[amulet of proof against petrification]]''.

==History==
During the [[Zhent]] occupation of [[Shadowdale]] in [[Hammer]], [[1375 DR]], High Priest Malathon was charged with rebuilding the temple of Bane in [[Castle Krag]].
