Zabardan Barpar was a shipwright inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Zabardan was the public spokesperson for the Order of Master Shipwrights circa 1357 DR. He worked out of the Shipwrights House, which was their headquarters on the corner of Asterils Way and Dock Street in the Dock Ward.
