'''Flammos Galdekund''' was a stooped, pox-marked stout merchant, married to [[Aglarra Galdekund]]. The couple lived in [[Waterdeep]] until, in [[1369 DR]], [[Alaphondar]] assumed Flammos's identity and appearance.
