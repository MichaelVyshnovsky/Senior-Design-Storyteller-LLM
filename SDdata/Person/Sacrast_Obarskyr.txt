'''Sacrast '''was the ninth [[Obarskyr]] monarch and a king of [[Cormyr]]. He was an illegitimate child officially adopted by [[Imbre Obarskyr|King Imbre]] a year before the latter's death. The first sixteen years of his reign was ruled by a [[Regency of the Four Barons]] until he came of age in [[110 DR|Year of the Quirt, 110 DR]]. He ruled for 13 years until his death in [[123 DR|Year of the Icy Axe, 123 DR]].
