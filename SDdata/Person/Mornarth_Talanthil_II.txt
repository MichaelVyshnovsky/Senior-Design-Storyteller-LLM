'''Mornarth Talanthil II''' was an [[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Talanthil Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Mornarth II inherited the throne when his father, [[Tallos Talanthil II|Tallos II]], died in {{Yearlink|-250}}. He ruled until {{Yearlink|-215}} when he died of natural causes.

==Relationships==
Mornarth II died without an heir, resulting in the throne passing to his brother [[Tallos Talanthil III|Dhalarth]] who was renamed Tallos III.
