'''Meralaine''' was a young [[human]] [[necromancer]] from [[Luthcheq]].

==History==
After [[Tchazzar]] lifted restrictions on [[mage]]s, Meralaine joined the [[Chessenta]]n army during the war with [[Threskel]]. She angered Tchazzar, who had a strong dislike for the [[undead]], when she raised the dead during a battle.

After the war she joined the [[Brotherhood of the Griffon]] and fell in love with the [[wizard]] [[Oraxes]].

==Description==
Meralaine was dimunitive and had a snub nose.
