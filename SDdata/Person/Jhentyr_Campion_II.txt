'''Jhentyr Campion II''' was the fourth King of the [[Campion Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Jhentyr II inherited the throne upon the death of his father, [[Tarnhart Campion I|Tarnhart I]], in the {{YearlinkName|993}}. He ruled until the {{YearlinkName|1002}}.

==Relationships==
His firstborn son and heir was [[Tarnhart Campion II|Tarnhart II]].
