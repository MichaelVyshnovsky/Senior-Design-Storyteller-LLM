Sapper '''Moke''' was a [[goblin]] [[cult of the Absolute|cultist]] of the [[Shattered Sanctum]] who served [[the Absolute]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Moke had orange brown skin, facial [[tattoo]]s, and dressed in simple leathers and rags.

==Activities==
As a "sapper", Moke's duties involved strapping a massive [[bomb]] atop their back and running headlong into any obstacle or structure that required immediate demolition.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Moke was among the Absolute's cultists who remained poised to assail the [[Emerald Grove]], as soon as [[Minthara Baenre]] gave the order.
