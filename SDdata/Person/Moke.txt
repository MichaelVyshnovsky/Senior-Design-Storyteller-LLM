Sapper Moke was a goblin cultist of the Shattered Sanctum who served the Absolute during the late 15 century DR.
Moke had orange brown skin, facial tattoos, and dressed in simple leathers and rags.
As a "sapper", Mokes duties involved strapping a massive bomb atop their back and running headlong into any obstacle or structure that required immediate demolition.
As of the , Moke was among the Absolutes cultists who remained poised to assail the Emerald Grove, as soon as Minthara Baenre gave the order.
