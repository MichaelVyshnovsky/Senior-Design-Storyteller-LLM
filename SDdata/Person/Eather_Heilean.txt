'''Eather Heilean''' is a [[High Harpers|High Harper]], who was recruited by the organization at a young age. She has recruited many members to the [[Harpers]] that are loyal to [[Twilight Hall]] first and foremost. Eather has been also been an agent who has dealt with members who have defected to the [[Moonstars]].
