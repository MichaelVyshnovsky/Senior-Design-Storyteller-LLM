'''Brace Lenalice''', who usually went by '''Brace the Bold''', was a [[gnome]] [[bard]] who lived in [[Westgate]].

==Description==
Brace had a huge nose and large ears.{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|910}}

==Abilities==
Brace was an accomplished swordsman and trained in the [[blade dancer|bladedance]] technique at the [[Timeless Blade]].{{Cite digital book/Eye of Justice|1223}}  He was also able to infuse his words with magic to heal.

==Possessions==
Brace carried a [[magic|magical]] [[rapier]] that he was able to split into two blades.{{Cite digital book/Eye of Justice|1185}}

==History==
In [[1480 DR]] Brace met [[Myrin Darkdance]] and talked his way into her employ.{{Cite digital book/Eye of Justice|972}}  Brace had an eternally sunny disposition and was something of a ladies man but he was also a bit of a bumbler.  He wasn't a traditional bard but could effectively heal the wounded by shouting insults at them.{{Cite digital book/Eye of Justice|3430}}  Brace was killed by a crusader of [[Hoar]] named [[Vaelis]] while guarding a wounded [[Kalen Dren]].{{Cite digital book/Eye of Justice|5065}}
