'''Twilitha Daysbreak''' was a [[half-elf]] [[proxy]] of the [[Faerûnian pantheon|Faerûnian]] [[power]] known as [[Mystra]].

==Personality==
Rejecting what she believed to be the chaotic nature of [[elves]], Twilitha believed that [[spellcraft]] was the only thing that gave reason to living. She took comfort in various laws and rituals of [[magic]]. A rather lonely woman, not given to dealing with crowds.

==Activities==
Twilitha strove to promote the development of magical societies, believing they were the only answer to the questions of life. She was willing to do most anything to achieve this goal, even pushing through her dislike of large crowds.
