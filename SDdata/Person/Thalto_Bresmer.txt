'''Thalto Bresmer''' was a traveling merchant based in [[Telflamm]], [[Thesk]]. He covered in the [[Dragon Reach]] area, including the [[Moonsea]] and the [[Dalelands]].{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks archive|30}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 24: An Inn For Adventurers}}

==Abilities==
Thalto was an expert in fine furniture, ornate hardware for doors (locks, handles, and hinges), and [[hardstones]] in quantity, used in wall decorations and wood furniture inlays.

==History==
He was known to stay at the [[Whistling Wizard]] inn when passing through [[Voonlar]]. He called it "The most comfortable bad inn it has ever been my pleasure to stay in&mdash;quiet if shabby comfort, privacy if one desires it, and much entertainment if one does not."
