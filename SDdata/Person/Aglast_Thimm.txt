Aglast Thimm was a wizard and a member of the Zhentarim. He served as a mageling to Ruatheene and was based in a Zhentilar camp in the valley of Arntethyl by the river Tesh.
Previously, Aglast acted as a poisoner for the Zhents in various Sea of Fallen Stars cities until his face became too known.
