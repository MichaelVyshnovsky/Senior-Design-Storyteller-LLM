'''Hafren Ember''' was an [[elf]] [[sorceress]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==Description==
Hafren had backswept red-brown hair and pale skin. She wore a bright green and yellow [[robe]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, Hafren joined the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|fortified camp]] outside the ruins of [[Myth Drannor]]. She provided training and guidance to aspiring sorcerers in the camp.
