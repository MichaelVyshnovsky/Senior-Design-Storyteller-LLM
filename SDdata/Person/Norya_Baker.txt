'''Norya Baker''' was a Dark Breaker of [[Umberlee]], a member of the Bitch Queen's [[Church of Umberlee|clergy]] under Flood Tide [[Allandra Grey]] of the [[Water Queen's House]] in [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Norya was a dark-skinned [[human]] woman with white hair she wore in a mohawk. She wore metallic [[gold]]en eye-liner that accentuated her light brown eyes and her face bore metallic blue markings of paint or [[tattoo]]s. Golden tattoo decorated a side of her neck.

==Posessions==
Norya's Dark Breaker garb was a decorated [[chainmail]] dress with pauldrons. Her weapon of choice was a simple [[trident]].

==Relationships==
Norya was in love with a fellow priestess – Dark Breaker [[Vilmina]]. Norya carried a [[conch]] – a gift from her lover, as well as letter proclaiming the day when the gift was made her live's finest hour.

==History==
''Circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the [[Absolute crisis]], Norya Baker stood guard of Allandra Grey's study on the Water Queen's House's second floor.
