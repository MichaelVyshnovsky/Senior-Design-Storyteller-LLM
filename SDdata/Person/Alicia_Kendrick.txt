Alicia Kendrick was the High Queen of the Moonshae Isles.
Born in 1346 DR, Princess Alicia was the eldest daughter of High King Tristan Kendrick and the druid Robyn Kendrick. Groomed during her early years by the kingdoms archmage Keane, Alicia became an adventurer in 1365 DR.
During the , Alicia was briefly granted the powers of an avatar by the Earthmother, in order to defeat the dracolich Gotha, a servant of Talos who attacked the Moonshae Isles.
She died unexpectedly in the , and was widely believed to have been poisoned by agents of Lionel Carrathal. Her death prompted a series of insurgencies in Callidyrr, which were swiftly quelled by the Callidyrr dragoons.
