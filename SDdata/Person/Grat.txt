'''Grat the Trader''' was a [[goblin]] merchant and member of the the [[Cult of the Absolute]] who sold his wares in the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He had yellow-green skin and long, brown hair he wore back. He decorated his skin with two lines of blue face paint.

==Activities==
Grat's wares were all items he and other cultists of the Absolute had taken from others during their raids.

==Possessions==
Among the goods Grat had for sale were a ''[[doom axe]]'', the ''[[boots of aid and comfort]]'', a [[Flaming Fist]] [[kite shield]], the ''[[Whispering Promise]]'', several [[item enchantment|enchanted]] weapons, and various pieces goblin-made weaponry.
