Varri Vanthampur was an unwanted member of the Vanthampur family of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Varri Vanthampur perished in the , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldurs Gate. Varris tombstone was commissioned to be carved by Candulhallows Tombstones in the Lower City. The freshly-carved grave marker was stored in Candulhallows shops main room, just left of the main entrance. It read "Varri Vanthampur. Unwanted in life, welcomed in death."
