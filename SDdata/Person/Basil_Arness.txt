Basil Arness was a high-ranking officer of Muktar in the Turmish in 1358 DR.
Basil was a member of the Champions of Muktar. He liked befriending his victims before killing them.
In 1358 DR, Basil and his fellow Champions arranged a trap inside Fort Blood planned by the self-styled king of Muktar, Hetep, to eliminate an adventuring party well-known in Golconda.
Later, he took part in the Battle of Wilma Pass.
Basil enjoyed killed supposed "friends", believing this made the killing more poignant.
