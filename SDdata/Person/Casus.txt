'''Casus''' was a [[minotaur]] warlord.

==History==
In [[1377 DR]], Lord Casus was captured by the Crone of Kiaransalee and taken to [[V'elddrinnsshar]], where he was tortured and turned into an [[undead]] by [[Cabrath Nelinderra]].
