'''Huang''' was [[Raidon Kane]]'s flatmate in {{yearlink|1375}}. He was quiet, but he smelled horrible.{{cite book/Stardeep|31-32|4}} Raidon Kane found him dead and dismembered by the [[Nine Golden Swords]] in his apartment after recovering his family sword from [[Chun (Telflamm)|Chun]]. He promptly left the city.{{cite book/Stardeep|40|4}}
