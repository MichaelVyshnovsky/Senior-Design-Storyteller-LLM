Canumbrax was a shadurakul (shadow canomorph) bounty hunter in 1374 DR.
Circa 1374 DR, Canumbrax was hired by Lord Hulraven Irlingstar to help an adventuring party rescue his wife Nael, whod been kidnapped by some agents of the Jaezred Chaulssin for unknown reasons. Canumbraxs duty was to lead the adventures into the Plane of Shadow.
