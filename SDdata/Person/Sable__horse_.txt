'''Sable''' was a [[horse]] belonging to the [[Moor Eagle]] clan of [[Vaasa]] in the mid-to-late 13{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Sometime after saving the Moor Eagle tribe and building the [[Granite Tower]] for them in the {{YearlinkName|1269}}, [[Melegaunt Tanthul]] met and became quite fond of Sable{{Cite dragon/281/Rogues Gallery: Heroes of the Summoning|81}} His fondness for the horse was such that [[Vala Thorsdotter]] was familiar with Sable despite the horse having lived in the time of her great-grandfather, [[Bodvar]].{{Cite dragon/281/Rogues Gallery: Heroes of the Summoning|83}} he was saddened to see the horse was not with them, and had to be reminded that she had died more than 80 years prior.
