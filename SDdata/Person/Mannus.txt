Mannus was a house guard for the noble Seasilver family of Marsember during the late 15 century DR.
He was a rather tall and gangly fellow.
When the city of Marsember was invaded by the Shadovar, on Uktar 2 of the , Mannus failed to heed the warning of the servant Odelia, leaving Lady Arietta alone with two shades. He and his fellow guard Suther were under strict orders not to yet the young noblewoman leave her familys estate in the city. They both failed in their duties.
