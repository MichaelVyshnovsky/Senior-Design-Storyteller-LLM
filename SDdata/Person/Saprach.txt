'''Saprach''' was wine-seller who lived in the city of [[Tantras]] in [[the Vast]] around [[1358 DR]].

Saprach was the proprietor of [[Saprach's Fine Wines]], but also had a side-line in thieving and fencing stolen goods, and specialized in the discreet disposal of dead bodies.
