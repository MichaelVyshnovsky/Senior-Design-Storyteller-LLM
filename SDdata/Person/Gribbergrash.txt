'''Gribbergrash''' was an [[orog]] mercenary and chieftain of the Eye Killers company, who led his contingent of orog soldiers in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He was based in [[Wyvernwatch Castle]] in service to Lord [[Vorik Aris]] of [[Cormyr]].

==Personality==
Gribbergrash was a foul and mean fellow, who got his way by force and bullying.

==Possessions==
He carried a ''+2 [[battleaxe]]'' and wore [[plate mail]], and had a hidden stash containing a ''[[potion of extra-healing]]'', a ''+3 [[dagger]]'', 400 [[ep]], and a valuable [[diamond]]. He enjoyed collecting trophies, including a shrunken [[elf]] head and a stuffed {{SIAdj|3|foot|long=yes|adj=long}} [[lizard]].

==Activities==
In the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, he served Lord Aris as leader of the Eye Killers, commanding three orog officers and sixty veteran orog warriors at [[Wyvernwatch Castle]].
