Lord '''Serge Affapanov''' was a noble in [[Mulmaster]] in [[1491 DR]].

==Relationships==
Serge was the father of [[Ayre Affapanov]]. He was allied with the [[Lords' Alliance]].

==Activities==
Serge was a Mulmaster noble involved in the gemstone trade from [[Damara]] and the [[Bloodstone Lands]].

==History==
In 1489 DR, his wife died and he was left alone with his daughter.{{Cite adventurers league/Breath of the Yellow Rose|14}} 

In 1491 DR, Serge was one of the nobles predicted to become one of the [[Blades (Mulmaster)|Blades]] at the next election meeting. However, during one of his trips to Damara, Ayre disappeared. The Lords' Alliance asked an adventuring party hired to find [[Liticia Gos]] to also find Ayre.{{Cite adventurers league/Breath of the Yellow Rose|7}} The adventurers found Liticia in the [[Yellow Breath Chapter House]] under [[Thylius Caramitru]]'s influence.{{Cite adventurers league/Breath of the Yellow Rose|25}}
