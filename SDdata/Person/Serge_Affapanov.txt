Lord Serge Affapanov was a noble in Mulmaster in 1491 DR.
Serge was the father of Ayre Affapanov. He was allied with the Lords Alliance.
Serge was a Mulmaster noble involved in the gemstone trade from Damara and the Bloodstone Lands.
In 1489 DR, his wife died and he was left alone with his daughter. 
In 1491 DR, Serge was one of the nobles predicted to become one of the Blades at the next election meeting. However, during one of his trips to Damara, Ayre disappeared. The Lords Alliance asked an adventuring party hired to find Liticia Gos to also find Ayre. The adventurers found Liticia in the Yellow Breath Chapter House under Thylius Caramitrus influence.
