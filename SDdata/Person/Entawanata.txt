Chief '''Entawanata''' was the hereditary ruler of [[Lheshayl]] in the [[Shining Plains]] in [[1370 DR]].
 
==Activities==
Despite not being as competent as his predecessor, Entawanata proved himself a skilled diplomat. He was always the delegate to the [[Hill of Memories]].
