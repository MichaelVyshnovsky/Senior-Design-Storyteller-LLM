StreeaValsharess Jashnil Zolond was the high priestess of Lolth in the city of Sshamath in the year 1379 DR.
In 1379 DR Jashnil Zolond was choose as the new StreeaValsharess after the demise of Ilharess Laele Zauviir. Under the orders of Tlar Mizzrynturl, she summoned the quasit demon Glizn, who was previously bonded to Piri, to ask about Qarlynd Melarn.
