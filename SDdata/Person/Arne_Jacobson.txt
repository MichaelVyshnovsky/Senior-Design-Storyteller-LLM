'''Arne Jacobson''' was a commoner from the [[Rivington]] district of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Arne Jacobson was a [[human]] man with a pale skin tone and a shaved head, hidden underneath a commoner's hat. His skin and commoner's garb were often dirty.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, [[Belron Strongstep]], [[gold dwarvish]] commoner, could often be seen conversing with Arne Jacobson on the streets of Rivington, discussing the [[Cult of the Absolute]] and the refugee crisis that affected the [[Outer City]].
