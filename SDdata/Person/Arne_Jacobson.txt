Arne Jacobson was a commoner from the Rivington district of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Arne Jacobson was a human man with a pale skin tone and a shaved head, hidden underneath a commoners hat. His skin and commoners garb were often dirty.
In the , Belron Strongstep, gold dwarvish commoner, could often be seen conversing with Arne Jacobson on the streets of Rivington, discussing the Cult of the Absolute and the refugee crisis that affected the Outer City.
