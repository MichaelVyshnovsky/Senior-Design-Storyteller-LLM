'''Moarinskoar Irlingstar''' was a noble of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Irlingstar]].

==Activities==
He lived within [[Irlingstar Villa]] located on [[Sul Street]] in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==History==
Moarinskoar decided to make his own way in life and left the comforts of his family villa to sail the [[Sword Coast]] as a trader. He eventually turned to piracy.

==Rumors & Legends==
It was whispered that Moarinskoar sailed his ship into undeath as a [[wight]] commanding a crew of [[zombie]]s aboard the [[ghost ship]] ''Moonwind''. He would accost anyone sailing to Waterdeep asking them to deliver a message to his mother saying he was not ready to come back home yet.
