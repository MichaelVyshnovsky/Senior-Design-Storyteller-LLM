'''Jhassilm Onespear''' was a bandit lord in the [[Stonelands]] near [[Cormyr]] in the early 14th century [[DR]].

Jhassilm held a citadel in the Stonelands, before it was notably stormed by the famed mercenary [[Brandon]] some time before [[1356 DR]].
