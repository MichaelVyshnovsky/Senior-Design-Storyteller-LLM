'''Simon Thrithyn''' was the innkeeper of the [[House of Good Sprits]] In the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Simon was a rather calm and timid man, but a dependable fighter.

==Relationships==
Simon was a member of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Vintners', Distillers', & Brewers' Guild]].
