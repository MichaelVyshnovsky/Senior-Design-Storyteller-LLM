Sakrattars Irithyl was second Coronal of Cormanthyr from  to , the youngest great-grandson of Kahvoerm Irithyl, first Coronal. He became the ruler of the empire after his fathers death and the unsuccessful blade draw of Divisav Irithyl, Kahvoerms first son. His reign ended with his death as he sacrificed himself to create a blood dragon that slew a horde of drow that had occupied the former realm of Uvaeren and the Twisted Tower.
