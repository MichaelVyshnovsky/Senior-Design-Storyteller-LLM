'''Malvin Draga''' was a [[human]] rogue and fighter on the [[Council of Elders (Everlund)|Council of Elders]] in the city of [[Everlund]], appointed as the "Keeper of the Bridges", as of [[1372 DR]]. He had a scheming personality and aimed to gain wealth by controlling customs in the city through corruption of the city watch.
