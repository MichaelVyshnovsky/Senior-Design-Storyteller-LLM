'''Mahmud Ben Aziz''' was a [[half-elf]] living on [[Masud Jazayir]] in the [[Crowded Sea]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}} He ruled a small town called [[Bandar al-Sa'adat]].

==Activities==
Mahmud ruled with a relaxed grip. Some thought his decisions were corrupt, but he knew how to keep the important individuals of the town happy. Mahmud approached the law as a business and sold it to those who paid.

He created posts to deal with the daily affairs and ease his schedule.

==Relationships==

Mahmud was the older brother of [[Karim Ben Aziz]] and father of [[Halima Aziz]], both of whom lived in Banda al-Sa'adat.
