'''Unamundass''' "The Butcher" was a king of [[Westgate]].

==History==
Unamundass was known for consorting only with female courtiers. He was killed by a plate enspelled with ''[[Irithra's spelltouch]]'' so as to deliver a ''[[fireball]]'' to the first man to touch it.
