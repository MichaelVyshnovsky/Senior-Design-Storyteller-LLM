Falael was a laborer, packer, and a delivery woman of the Baldurs Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Falael was a young half wood elven woman with a light skin tone, short curly light brown hair, and brown eyes. She often wore a simple laborers jacket over a tunic.
In the , during the Absolute crisis, Falael was employed by Baldurs Mouth and could be found outside the broadsheet publishers office counting papers packed for delivery. Counting in several hundreds was a challenge, and miscounting meant she had to start over.
