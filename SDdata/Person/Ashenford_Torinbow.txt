Ashenford Torinbow was a bard and a member of the Elder Circle of the Emerald Enclave and a follower of Silvanus.
Early in his life, he was a Harper in Shadowdale in the Dalelands, until the , when he was captured and tortured by the Zhentarim. After being rescued, Ashenford left the Harpers and lived for a time in Waterdeep as a bard before relocating to the Vilhon Reach where he joined the Emerald Enclave.
Because of the chaos caused by the Spellplague, which had altered natures ecosystems and driven Cindermoon, another member of the Elder Circle, mad, the Elder Circle was stagnating in the late 15 century. However, in the , the boy prophet of Lathander, Stedd Whitehorn, arrived and successfully cured Cindermoon.
