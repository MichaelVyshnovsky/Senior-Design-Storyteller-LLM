'''Akhenauben Bolphur''' was a famous crafter inhabiting [[Yeshpek]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p168">

==Personality==
He was straight to the point and did not haggle with customers. The price he set was final.<ref name=EotSS-p168"/>

==Activities==
Akhenauben was known for his work with [[silver]], specifically decorated bowls and flagons that were personalized for each individual customer. He was also a spokesmen for Yeshpek's crafters, representing them in matters of local law and trade.<ref name=EotSS-p168"/>
