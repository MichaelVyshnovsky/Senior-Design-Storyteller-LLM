Akhenauben Bolphur was a famous crafter inhabiting Yeshpek along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He was straight to the point and did not haggle with customers. The price he set was final.
Akhenauben was known for his work with silver, specifically decorated bowls and flagons that were personalized for each individual customer. He was also a spokesmen for Yeshpeks crafters, representing them in matters of local law and trade.
