Kaestra Karanok was the most powerful cleric of Entropy (in truth Tiamat) in Luthcheq in Chessenta in 1372 DR.
Kaestra was the nominal head of all religious activities of House Karanok.
Early in the Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR, the priestess of Loviatar Ythnel Duumin and survivors of the defeated Mage Society rebels escaped to Mordulkin went back to Luthcheq to sabotage the witchweed supplies to pave the way for an invasion they did that and slew Kaestra as well, on Midwinter.
Kaestra was daughter of Jaerios Karanok and twin sister of Saestra Karanok.
