Nathra was an elf mage of the early 11 century DR.
She owned Thrauntspire.
She was an orphan. She married Phanturgost, and gave birth to Ieiridauna Amalree.
In her time, Nathra had traveled widely in the lands of men before settling down with Phanturgost.
She died in early autumn of , after inviting eight sorcerers from lands south of Calimshan with whom she and her husband had been exploring a Netherese ruin. The sorcerers slew the two of them swiftly, but encountered difficulty slaying their daughter.
