Haerlit Thomm was a fence of stolen goods inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Haerlit worked out of a tavern called Felzouns Folly located in Waterdeeps Trades Ward on Salabar Street. His point of contact there was the taverns owner, Felzoun Thar. Haerlit specialized in furniture and other distinctive furnishings, keeping most of his goods within Thomm Warehouse on Sambril Lane in the Dock Ward.
