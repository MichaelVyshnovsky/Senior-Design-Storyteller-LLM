'''Vrushnom''' was an unusual [[illithid]] [[cleric]] of [[Shar]].

Vrushnom lived in the secret Shar-worshiping enclave of [[LoobliShar]] in the [[Middledark|Middle]] [[Darklands]] of the [[Underdark]]. However, also Vrushnom acted spied on the city for the illithids of [[Oryndoll]].
