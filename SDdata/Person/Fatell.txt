Fatell was a member of the Cult of the Eternal Flame in Mulmaster.
Fatell was a lumber merchant and a member of the Brotherhood of the Cloak and also a fire cultist in Mulmaster. In 1491 DR, a fire destroyed his firewood shop. He and a few fellow surviving fire cultists were investigating the ruins of the shop when they were surprised by some adventurers hired by Cora Peytir, who uncovered and defeated Fatell.
Fatell, as a lumber merchant, had dealings with Cora Peytir.
Fatell was very cunning and had a very good knowledge about the inner workings of Mulmasters political structure.
