'''Quellena''' was a [[warlock]] looter who operated on the [[Sword Coast]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. For a time, she worked for the [[gnome]] crew leader [[Gimblebock]].

==Description==
She had light brown hair and an intricate pattern of tattoos that adorned her face and neck.

==Abilities==
The warlock fought with a [[quarterstaff]] and utilized a variety of [[arcane]] [[spell]]s.

==Relationships==
Quellena was a target of romantic affections from a person named [[Alys]]. She carried a note from the admirer on her person during the time she worked with Gimblebock.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Quelenna and the other looters were trying to find their way into an [[Temple of Jergal (Sword Coast)|ancinet crypt]]. Quelenna took the high ground during her guard duties, looking over the crypt's main entrances.
