'''Garth''' was a member of the Delimbiyr family inhabiting the city of [[Daggerford]] in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
His house was located along [[Rogar Alley]] in western Daggerford near the [[Caravan Gate]].{{Cite interactive atlas|3990 Daggerford}} His family sent him out into the world to become an [[adventurer]] in order to keep him from drinking away all of the profits of their wine cellars at the [[River Shining Tavern]].
