Sister '''Palonya''' was a priest of [[Jergal]] in [[Waterdeep]] in [[1479 DR]].

==History==
In 1479 DR, Sister Palonya arrived at the [[Yawning Portal]] inn with Brother [[Sepulcher (priest)|Sepulcher]]. Palonya went inside Undermountain to replace the previous priest of Jergal assigned to catalog the bodies inside some particular Undermountain tombs, while Sepulcher waited.
