Sister Palonya was a priest of Jergal in Waterdeep in 1479 DR.
In 1479 DR, Sister Palonya arrived at the Yawning Portal inn with Brother Sepulcher. Palonya went inside Undermountain to replace the previous priest of Jergal assigned to catalog the bodies inside some particular Undermountain tombs, while Sepulcher waited.
