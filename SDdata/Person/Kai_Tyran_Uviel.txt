Kai Tyran Uviel was a bandit in the Moonsea in 1489 DR.
Kai was a member of the Gray Patriots bandit group under Sahnd Krulek. During a meeting with another bandit group, the Claws of Tiamat, he acted as sentry at the meeting of the two leaders of the outlaws. However, an adventuring party hired to clear the Iron Route of outlaws killed him.
