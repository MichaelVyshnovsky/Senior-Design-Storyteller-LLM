'''Snuggleguts''' was a kitten gifted to [[Imraea]] by the thief [[Torm (adventurer)|Torm]] in the {{yearlinkName|1357}}.

==Description==
The kitten was silver and black, with glistening black eyes.

==History==
In early [[Flamerule]] of the {{yearlinkName|1357}}, [[Torm (adventurer)|Torm]] gifted Snuggleguts, then a kitten, to a young kid named Imraea, alongside nine [[gold piece]]s and instructions to find it a good name, for Snuggleguts was only its kitten name.
