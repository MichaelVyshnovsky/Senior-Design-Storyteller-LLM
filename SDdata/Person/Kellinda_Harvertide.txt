'''Kellinda Harvertide''' was a [[halfling]] [[cleric]] of [[Yondalla]] at the [[Hearthcircle of Yondalla|Hearthcircle]] in the village of [[Shining Falls (village)|Shining Falls]] in the [[Delimbiyr Vale]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Living in a cottage near the grove, Kellinda and her two young acolytes provided magical healing for donations.
