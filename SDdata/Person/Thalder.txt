Thalder was a member of the Cult of the Dragon in 1489 DR.
Thalder was a Cult member of the traditionalist faction. He was the main cultist delegate to the great dragon Claugiyliamatar of Kryptgarden Forest. He was killed during one of the skirmishes with an adventuring party hired to defeat Old Gnawbones.
