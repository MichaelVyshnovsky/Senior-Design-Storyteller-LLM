'''Thalder''' was a member of the [[Cult of the Dragon]] in [[1489 DR]].

==History== 
Thalder was a Cult member of the traditionalist faction. He was the main cultist delegate to the great dragon [[Claugiyliamatar]] of [[Kryptgarden Forest]].{{Cite adventurers league/Corruption in Kryptgarden|26}} He was killed during one of the skirmishes with an adventuring party hired to defeat Old Gnawbones.{{Cite adventurers league/Corruption in Kryptgarden|50}}
