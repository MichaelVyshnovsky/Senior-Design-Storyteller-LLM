'''Dus Abd al-Dawwar''' was the admiral and commander of the [[Wanderers Mamluk Society]] in [[Qudra]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Dus spent most of his time trying to bring at least half of Qudra's fleet of ships up to working order through constant repairs.
