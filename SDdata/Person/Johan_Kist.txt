Johan Kist was a talented Red Wizards of Thay and the inventor of the Nomads rings, active some years before the late 14 century DR.
Johan Kist was still a young and promising Red Wizard when he created the first of the Nomads rings. His apprentice attempted to claim the invention after stealing the ring. The thief was quickly apprehended by Johan and in retaliation, he created a spell that caused its targets flesh to peel away in small strips. As of the , Johan Kist kept the torturous spell to himself, never sharing its magic formulas.
