'''Shaal''' was a [[human]] female [[cleric]] of [[Azuth]] in [[Saerloon]], [[Sembia]], around [[1370 DR]].

Shaal was married to [[Telornym]], a priest of [[Mystra]]. They lived in a house in Saerloon owned by [[Khelben Arunsun]] but rented to Telornym for thirty years.
