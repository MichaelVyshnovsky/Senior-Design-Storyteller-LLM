'''Mamoon Klesir''' was a [[Zakharan (ethnicity)|Zakharan]] inhabitant of [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in {{Yearlink|1367}}.{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Mamoon lived within Huzuz's [[Huzuz/Craft District|Craft District]] in the [[Huzuz/North Quarter|North Quarter]]. He ran the prestigious school simply called [[Mamoon Klesir's University]] that only admitted Huzuz's nobility for students.
