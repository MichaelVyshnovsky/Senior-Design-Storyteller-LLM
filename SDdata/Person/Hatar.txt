Hatar was a sorcerer who served in the court of Tajar in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in .
Hatar discovered that Riqqiyah was actually Sultan Kori al-Zafiri, Tajars previous ruler. He intended to use this information to his advantage.
Hatar was originally from Itiraf across the Golden Gulf.
He was served by a loyal dao who would perform any command given by Hatar.
