Lady '''Cilivren Doedance''' was an inhabitant of [[Cormanthor]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]]. She was the head of a major house.

==Personality==
She was more polite and reserved than her friends. She also had no taste for [[tripleshroom sherry]].

==Powers==
Her personal [[mantle|Cormathan spellmantle]] held ''[[feather fall]]'', ''[[web]]'', ''[[burning hands]]'', ''[[ESP]]'', ''[[protection from normal missiles]]'', ''[[lightning bolt]]'', ''[[dimension door]]'', ''[[ice storm]]'', ''[[major creation]]'' and ''[[cone of cold]]''.

==History==
In {{yearlink|241}}, during a party at [[Satyrdance Pool]], she and a number of other ladies shared concerns about the future of Cormanthor; in particular, she believed it might be destroyed within her lifetime. They then agreed to try and nudge their husbands towards stabilizing the city.
