'''Klarv''' was a Night Blade who resided in the ruined [[Temple of Bhaal (Undercity)|Temple of Bhaal]] in the [[Undercity]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Klarv was a brown-haired and darker-skinned [[human]] woman armed with a [[crossbow]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the [[Absolute crisis]], Night Blade Elda Klarv was among the [[Cultists of Bhaal]] led by [[Cirian]] on a mission of venerating their evil [[deity]] through slaughtering of innocents in the [[Bloomridge Park]] of the [[Lower City]]. It was unknown if the slaughter was commenced or if the Bhaalists were stopped and slain by the [[Absolute Adversaries]].
