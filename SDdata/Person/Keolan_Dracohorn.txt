'''Keolan Dracohorn''' was the founder of the [[Dracohorn]] noble house of [[Cormyr]].

==History==
He found a dead young [[blue dragon]] on the site that would become known as [[Soldier's Green]], and claimed to have killed it, which was the origin of the family name.
