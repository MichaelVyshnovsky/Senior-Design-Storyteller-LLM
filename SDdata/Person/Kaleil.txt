Kaleil was a druid of Maidens Glade near the Mere of Dead Men on the Sword Coast North.
In , Kaleil and the animals dwelling in Maidens Glade were corrupted and driven mad by a sinister presence in the Mere of Dead Men. He shapechanged himself into a bear, but was not able to change back. Traveling with the druid Elanee, the Kalach-Cha encountered Kaleil in Maidens Glade and had no choice but to kill him.
