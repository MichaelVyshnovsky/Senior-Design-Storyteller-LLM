Galoomp the Bookkeeper was a book merchant from the City of Coin, Athkatla, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Galoomp was a portly man with fair skin.
Galoomps book stall could be found in the grand market known as Waukeens Promenade in Athkatlas Center District in the . Among more mundane tomes, Galoomp carried a selection of mages spell scrolls.
