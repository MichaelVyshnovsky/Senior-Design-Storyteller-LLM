Malask Huntinghorn was the heir of the Huntinghorn family and a royal guard in Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.
He formed part of Alusair Obarskyrs retinue in .
He was one of Alusairs bedmates.
Malask Huntinghorn was in Alusairs retinue when she was dismissed by Elminster Aumar one night in early spring of the , when he took Royal Magician Caladnei to meet with Vangerdahast the old mage refused to take Alusair, thinking this a matter that did not call for her. She began to vent her frustrations on a sapling he told her not to, foolishly, and she demanded he spar with her, attacking him savagely. She broke his nose after that, their fighting eventually devolved into rutting.
