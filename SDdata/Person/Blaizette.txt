Blaizette was a halfling waitress in Phlan during the late 15 century DR.
Blaizette worked as waitress for her mother at the Madame Freonas Tea Kettle.
She was the daughter of Madame Freona, the Tea Kettles owner and proprietor. Grelinda, Reece, and Whittlee.
In the 1480s DR, she was briefly interrogated by Buhrell Caah about a missing dangerous magic item.
