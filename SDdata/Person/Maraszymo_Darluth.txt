Maraszymo Darluth was a woodcutter who worked around Secomber in the 15 century DR.
Maraszymo cut wood from the High Forest, and was one of the most reliable and affordable suppliers of the raw lumber. He was linked by merchants map to Samrils Fine Blades also in Secomber, who used the duskwood and blueleaf timber to make wooden handles for weapons such as longswords.
