Haverish Hallefon was a Purple Dragon and later innkeeper of the Bubbling Bulwark in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Haverish was an old friend of Aleka Ravenheart, who he had shared a regiment with. Because of his history with the Dragons, his tavern was very popular with the soldiers, who saw him as one of their own.
In the , while operating the Bulwark he provided information that helped Aleka solve a murder mystery at Thistleflame Keep.
