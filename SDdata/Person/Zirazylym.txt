'''Zirazylym''' was an adult [[bronze dragon]] in [[Daggerford]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==Activities==
Zirazylym was normally [[polymorph]]ed as a [[half-elf]]. Usually she spent her time at the [[Happy Cow]] tavern. She liked observing the [[Hardcheese]] family.
