Fiirnelther Vandree was the matron mother of House Vandree in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 century DR.
She and her house were considered even by drow standards to be vicious and vengeful, as well as ambitious.
She killed her mother and previous matron mother Trokenther Vandree in the . Under Fiinelthers guidance, the Vandree rose quickly to become the eighth house and thus a member of the Ruling Council.
She was still in power by around 1486 DR.
