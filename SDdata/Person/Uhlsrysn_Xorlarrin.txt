'''Uhlsrysn Xorlarrin''' was a member of [[House Xorlarrin]] and a master of [[Sorcere]] in [[1479 DR]].

==Personality==
The [[drow]] had a prickly pride, always feeling himself inferior to his brother and he was easily provoked.

==Abilities==
Uhlsrysn was a talented transmuter, known especially for his transmutations of [[goblin]] slaves into terrible monsters.

==History==
Uhlsrysn was the secondboy of House Xorlarrin, but because the elderboy was a more talented wizard, Uhlsrysn was chosen to remain as an instructor at Sorcere.
