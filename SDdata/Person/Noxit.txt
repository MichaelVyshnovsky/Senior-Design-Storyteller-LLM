'''Noxit''' was a [[quasit]] who served the [[fey'ri]] [[conjurer]] [[Laelran Aelorothi]]. He aided in his deceptions when he joined the [[Arcane Brotherhood]] of [[Luskan]] ''circa'' [[1370 DR]].

Noxit could adopt the shape of a [[bat]] or [[giant centipede]].
