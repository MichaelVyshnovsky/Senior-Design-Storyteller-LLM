'''Hovor Seawind''' was the [[half-elf|half-elven]] high captain and [[Aglarondan griffonrider]] commander of the [[Simbulmyn]], the Simbul's personal guard. 

==History==
Around [[1374 DR]], Hovor was master of the [[Palace of the Simbul]] in the Simbul's absence.
