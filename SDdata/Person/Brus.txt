'''Brus''' was a commoner child messenger and a nephew of the [[Shadow Thief]] named [[Gaelan Bayle]] from [[Athkatla]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Ye're a [[wizard]] or somethin', right? Well... me boys tell me there's this feller named [[Madeen]] in the [[Scepter District|Government District]]... he's workin' fer the [[Cowled Wizards]] and offering some job. I could takes ye to the edge of the slums if ye wants to check it out.''|Brus}}

==Description==
Brus was an affable young boy with brown skin and light brown hair.

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1369}} helped his uncle as a messenger and informant who led a group of Athkatlan youths, gathering rumors and kept an ear close to the gourd on various profitable opportunities around the City of Coin. Young Brus might've helped [[Abdel Adrian]], serving as a guide and pointing him towards various well-paying individuals in Athkatla when Gaelan Bayle, along with the Shadow Thieves offered to sell him information that would help rescue his sister [[Imoen]].
