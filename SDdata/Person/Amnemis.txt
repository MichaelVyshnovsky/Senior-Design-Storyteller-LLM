'''Amnemis''' was a [[black dragon]] member of the [[Cult of the Dragon]]. 

==Relationships==
She was the familiar of the [[half-dragon]] [[Vargo Kent]] but when she became too big, Vargo choose a new familiar and Amnemis became his cohort. In truth, Amnemis was very devoted to Vargo and was really jealous of other familiars.

==Activities==
The [[dragon]] resided in the [[Well of Dragons]] with her master.
