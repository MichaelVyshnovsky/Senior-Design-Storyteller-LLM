Suther was a house guard for the noble Seasilver family of Marsember during the late 15 century DR.
He was not particularly well-spoken, and became disquieted when confronted by his superiors.
He served under the house guard Mannus, and their superior, Sergeant Carlton.
When the city of Marsember was invaded by the Shadovar, on Uktar 2 of the , Suther and Mannus failed to heed the warning of the servant Odelia, allowing two shades to infiltrate the bedchambers of Lady Arietta Seasilver. They were ordered to not allow the young noblewoman to leave the familys estate, but failed in their efforts.
