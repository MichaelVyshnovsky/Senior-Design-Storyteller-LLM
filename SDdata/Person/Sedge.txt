'''Sedge''' was a [[halfling]] [[bard]] in [[Neverwinter]].

==Activities==
Around [[1479 DR]], Sedge made his living as a fireside companion for hire, bodyguard, and teller of tales. One of his preferred spots was the [[Moonstone Mask]] inn in the [[Protector's Enclave]].
