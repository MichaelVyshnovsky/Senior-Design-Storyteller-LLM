'''Farim''' was a [[human]] [[mamluk]] who lived in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Farim was a handsome man.

==Activities==
He was a member of the [[Valiant Mamluk Society]], but served as the bodyguard of the [[sha'ir]] [[Qoha'dar]] and was rarely seen apart from her. He disliked senseless violence, so only drew his weapon as a last resort.

==History==
During a past adventure, Farim was accidentally splashed with a magical oil that caused Qoha'dar to fall in love with him.

==Relationships==
Though the magical oil wore off long ago, Qoha'dar remained infatuated with Farim.

==Possessions==
Farim owned a set of ''[[armor of the desert evening]]'' and a [[gold]]-hilted [[scimitar]].
