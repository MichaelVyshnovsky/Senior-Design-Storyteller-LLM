'''Norik Tormsson''' was a merchant that sold his wares on the [[Sword Coast North]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Possessions==
He offered a variety of wares for sale, including several types of magical potions, [[gold|gold bar]]s, and preconstructed kits that provided an advantage to [[adventurer]]s or other outdoorspeople to utilize their skills while out in the wild, exploring dungeons, breaking into secure locations, or dealing with matters of an [[arcane]] or religious nature.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Norrik had set up shop at the entrance to the [[Cragmire Crypts]] alongside the [[Harpers]] agent [[Garl Gnarlclaw]].
