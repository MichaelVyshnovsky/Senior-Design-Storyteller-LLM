'''Sudai Calin''' lived within the [[Tower of Adnan al-Raqi]] in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Gem of Zakhara|62}}

==Activities==
Sudai was a [[Dwarf|dwarven]] [[sha'ir]] and instructor for [[Adnan al-Raqi]]'s students. He also spent time learning the higher powers of the sha'ir with Adnan and the other instructors.
