Sudai Calin lived within the Tower of Adnan al-Raqi in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.
Sudai was a dwarven shair and instructor for Adnan al-Raqis students. He also spent time learning the higher powers of the shair with Adnan and the other instructors.
