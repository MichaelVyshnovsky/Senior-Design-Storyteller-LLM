Ahmad was a Semphari warrior who inhabited the Hordelands during the mid 14 century DR.
Ahmad was educated and from a well-established family in Semphar. He was a handsome man who was fiercely protective of his half-sister.
His sword, dagger and battleaxe were enchanted. He also wore enchanted brigandine armor. Ahmad carried a potion of climbing, ring of elemental command, a rope of climbing, and a scroll of protection from lycanthropes.
Ahmad had a half-sister named Yasmine.
