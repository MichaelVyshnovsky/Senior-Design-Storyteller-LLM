==Abilities==
Along with [[Razor (cat)|Razor]], Grimalkin was one of almost a hundred cats in Benalaius's possession. They served him as companions, guardians, and agents, and were good judges of character. He could communicate silently with them.

==History==
By occupying Benelaius's lap after midnight on [[Eleint 20]] of the {{YearlinkName|1367}}, Grimalkin was one of a number of cats present at the meeting of townsfolk he called to reveal the murderer of [[Dovo]] and [[Grodoveth]], as well as an [[Iron Throne]] spy ring.
