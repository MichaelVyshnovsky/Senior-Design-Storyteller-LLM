'''Bladuid''' was an elder [[gold elf]] [[Elven high magic|high mage]] from [[Evermeet]] that served as the aide to Lord [[Laerm Ryence]], co-commander of the [[Swift Cavalry]] as of the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Like other gold elves, Bladuid had a particular disdain for [[human]]s and [[half-elves]].

==History==
Bladuid attended to Lord Ryence during the campaign against the [[phaerimm]] in the {{YearlinkName|1371}}.
