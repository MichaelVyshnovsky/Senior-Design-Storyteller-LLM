'''Ysmyrl'da Starym''' was a clan archmage of the [[elven]] [[Starym]] clan, one of the twelve founding houses of [[Cormanthyr]], and mother of the house's famous missing heir, [[Josidiah Starym]]. She was niece by marriage to [[Taleisin Starym]], one of the clan elders. She was a [[moon elf]], a minority in the clan.
