'''Flospin Ralmathun''' was a caravan master operating out of the city of [[Delzimmer]] ''circa'' the [[1373 DR|Year of Rogue Dragons, 1373 DR]].

==Abilities==
Flospin was well respected for his ability to manage caravans successfully, and folks listened to his advice.
