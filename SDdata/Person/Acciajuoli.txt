'''Acciajuoli''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and co-owner of [[Transgate Industries]], who lived in [[Karsus Enclave]] until its destruction in {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}.

==Activities==
After the death of her parents [[Ocelot (Netherese)|Ocelot]] and [[Ytartia]], Acciajuoli operated Transgate Industries alongside [[Shander]] and [[Thamer]]. The three siblings managed to do extremely well and greatly increased the success of the company.
