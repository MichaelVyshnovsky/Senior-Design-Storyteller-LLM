'''Barunga Foestone''' was a [[frost giant]] in the late 15th century [[DR]].

==History==
Barunga was part of the frost giant tribe that allied with [[drow]] and [[orc]]s during the [[Darkening]]. She even took a drow husband.
In [[1485 DR]], during the [[War of the Silver Marches]], Barunga was part of a troop that was attacked by [[Luruar]] forces on the counterattack. In the fighting, her drow husband was killed by an arrow.
