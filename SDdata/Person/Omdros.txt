'''Omdros''' was a [[Thayan]] [[half-orc]] [[mage]] stationed at [[Surcross]] in [[Thay]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
As a worshiper of [[Yurtrus]], Omdros was drawn to the powers of misery, [[disease]], and [[death domain|death]]. In battle, Omdros had no fear of dying.

==Possessions==
Omdros wore a white [[robe]] and carried a black [[quarterstaff]].

==Relationships==
Omdros served [[Dempharis Sibront]], a renegade [[Red Wizard]] and self-proclaimed "[[zulkir]] in exile" who opposed [[Szass Tam]].

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Omdros served as the leader of a group of Sibront's followers who had infiltrated the Thayan garrison at Surcross. They plotted to subvert the schemes of Szass Tam and his underling, [[Valindra Shadowmantle]].
