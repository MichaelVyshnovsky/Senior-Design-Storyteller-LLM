Half&ndashblack dragon (troll)
Graxxygak () was a half-dragon troll in the Twilight Marsh near Phlan in the 1480s DR.
Graxxygak was the half-dragon son of Throstulgrael in the Twilight Marsh. He was his fathers last line of defense until he was confronted and killed by an adventuring party sent to recover some items from his fathers lair.
