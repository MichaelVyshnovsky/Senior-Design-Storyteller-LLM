'''Garn''' was a blacksmith in [[Bryn Shander]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==Activities==
Garn was the blacksmith of the [[Blackiron Blades]], known for the cheapest blades of the [[Ten Towns]], that was managed by his sister [[Elza]].
