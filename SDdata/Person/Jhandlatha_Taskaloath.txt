Jhandlatha Taskaloath was the co-owner of the Swinging Sword inn in Red Larch with her sister, Peieyrie Taskaloath.
The wizard was elderly and earthy.
Jhandlatha inherited unknown magical items from her father.
She and her sister loved to hear tales of adventure and pranks. If in danger, Jhandlatha would defend herself with fireball and lightning bolt spells.
Taskaloath was originally from Luskan, where her father was a wizard who opposed and was eventually killed by the Arcane Brotherhood.
