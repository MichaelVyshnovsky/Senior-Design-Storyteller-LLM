'''Hu''' was a [[tiger hengeyokai|tiger]] [[hengeyokai]] adventurer in [[Shou Lung]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]]. 

==Activities==
Hu wandered the land, sometimes defending the weak if their cause captured his interest. He usually acted during the night, stalking the jungles in silence. 

==Personality==
Hu was a silent and subtly menacing individual. He didn't like people much, but would aid them. He almost always remained in the form of a huge tiger.

==Description==
Hu was a huge [[tiger]]. In [[human]] form, he was massive and stood nearly 6 feet 3 inches (1.98 meters) tall, but he moved quietly and confidently.

==Possessions==
He was adept in a two-handed [[sword]] and a [[longbow]].

==Relationship==
Around [[1357 DR]], Hu was sometimes the companion of the [[cat hengeyokai|cat]] hengeyokai [[YuYa Mao]].
