'''Evaine''' was a [[wizard]] in [[Phlan]].

{{quote|''I never rush into battle, ever. If that [[sword]]-swinging lout wants to hurl himself into a trap, that's up to him, but it's not my style.''|Evaine}}

==Relationships==
Evaine was an acquaintance of [[Shal Desanea]].

==History==
Evaine lived in a small tower near the city of Phlan. Her duty was to protect the city from the evil of the ''[[pool of radiance|pool of darkness]]''. She lived with her [[cat]] familiar [[Gamaliel]].
