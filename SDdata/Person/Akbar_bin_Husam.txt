Akbar bin Husam was a Zakharan sorcerer visiting Hiyal in Zakhara around 1367 DR.
Akbars uncle, a wealthy merchant named Farid, was involved in a dispute with an Al-Badia named Jamal al-Karim that resulted in Jamals death. Akbar traveled to Hiyal to support his uncle during the looming blood feud with Jamals tribe.
Aside from his spellbook, Akbar owned a ring of protection +2.
