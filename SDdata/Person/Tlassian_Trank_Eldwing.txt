'''Tlassian Trank Eldwing''' was a [[dragonborn]] of [[Tymanther]] and a member of [[Clan Tlassian]] in [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Death before Dishonor|5}}

==Description==
Trank had a jagged scar across the top of his head from a wound that he took in the service of his clan.

==Personality==
Trank was considered an impulsive individual.

==History==
Some years before 1479 DR, Trank was severely wounded while on duty. As a reward for his services, he was given his own shop in the [[Market Floor (Djerad Thymar)|Market Floor]] of [[Djerad Thymar]]. He sold both mundane and magical arms and armor, and his works were considered masterpieces by the people of Djerad Thymar.
