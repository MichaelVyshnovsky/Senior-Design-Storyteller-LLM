Prellyn TehKinrellz was a drow member of House TehKinrellz in Ched Nasad in the late 14 century DR.
Prellyn was the main enforcer of the Matron TehKinrellz in the ruins of Ched Nasad. She acted as an overseer to Qarlynd Melarn, also using him for sex.
In the , Qarlyn subtly learned from Prellyn information about a medallion of Eilistraee he had found while scavenging in the rubble.
