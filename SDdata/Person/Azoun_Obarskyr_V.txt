Azoun Rhigaerd Palaghard Duar Obarskyr V
He was the son of former Crown Princess Tanalasta Obarskyr and Rowen Cormaeril, a scout in the army of Cormyr.
In the , Azoun was just an orphaned babe, and Regent Alusair Obarskyr and Dowager Queen Filfaeril Obarskyr were ruling Cormyr until he came of age. Azoun was cared for by Alaphondar, Royal Sage of Cormyr and lover of Filfaeril. The utmost care was given to keep Azouns whereabouts a secret, as there were many ambitious malcontents who would wish to do him harm.
When he finally came of age in the , at the insistence of the royal court, he was crowned king.
The following year, Azoun had to deal with the effects of the Spellplague and the death/disappearance of a third of his elite War Wizards.
He managed to fulfill his royal duties, however, and married Nalara Marliir, siring two children, Emvar and Foril in 1409 DR. Emvar, however, was later murdered in 1437 DR by Sembians, along with Forils wife, Jemra.
Two years later, in 1439 DR, Cormyr entered into a two-year-long war with Sembia, now a vassal state of Netheril.
Finally, a peace treaty was made and Daerlun seceded from Cormyr. shortly before his death in the .
