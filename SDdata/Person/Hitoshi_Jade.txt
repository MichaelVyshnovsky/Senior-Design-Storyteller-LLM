'''Hitoshi Jade''' was a sailor based in [[Baldur's Gate]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. They worked for the [[Irlentree]] [[patriar]] family, aboard the merchant ship the ''[[Golden Gull]]''.

Hitoshi was a patron of the [[Low Lantern]], located in the Gate's [[Lower City]].
