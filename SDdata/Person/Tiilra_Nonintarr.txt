'''Tiilra Nonintarr''' was a singer who lived in [[Imdolphyn]] in [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Quench the Fire of the Raging God|16}}

==Relationships==
She was the wife of [[Miintir]], a famed jeweler in Imdolphyn.

==Activities==
Tiilra was a talented singer, popular across the [[Windrise Ports]]. Many considered her a "social beast" whose fame and wit could be more devastating than any blade. She was married to Miintir, a wealthy jeweler who supplied many of the rich in Imdolphyn.

==History==
In [[1480 DR]], Tiilra, alongside other renowned individuals of Imdolphyn, was kidnapped by the [[Cult of Reborn Flame]], who intended to use them as a sacrifice in a [[ritual]] to awaken the [[primordial]] [[Achazar]]. She may have been saved by a band of [[adventurer]]s.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Quench the Fire of the Raging God|40-48}}
