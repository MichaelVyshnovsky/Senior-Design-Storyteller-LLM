'''Yrudis Gregan''' was a wealthy businessman  in [[Sundabar]] in [[1373 DR]].

==Description==
Yrudis was an obese man.

==History==
In 1373 DR, Yrudis was searching for a dress for a present to his daughter. While disguised, [[Aliisza]] saw that he harshly treated [[Lizel|a girl]] in [[Velsin]]'s employ.
