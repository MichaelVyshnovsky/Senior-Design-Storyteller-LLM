Araag Dyrr was a member of House Agrach Dyrr of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR.
Araag graduated from Sorcere at an unknown time.
During the siege of Dyrrs compound for their betrayal of Menzoberranzan during the Siege of Menzoberranzan, Araag was called back to his house together with all other Dyrr wizards to help raise magical defenses.
