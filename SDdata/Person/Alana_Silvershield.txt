Alana Silvershield was a noblewoman from the wealthy Silvershield family living in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century.
Alana was the younger daughter of Torlin Silvershield and Evelyn Silvershield.
Alana was born in the Silvershield family and lived in the Silvershield estate in Baldurs Gate. In the , Alana was seven and she was living in the estates nursery with her brother Entar.
