'''Callidyrr Hugh'''  was King of [[Corwell]] in the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the late 2{{nd}} century [[DR]] and the founder of the [[Hugh Dynasty]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}} He was the namesake for the Kingdom of [[Callidyrr]] and for its capital, [[Caer Callidyrr]].

==Relationships==
Callidyrr was a son of [[Ebenfar]] nobility. His first son, [[Cymrych Hugh]], would go on to become the legendary first High King of the Moonshae Isles.

==History==
Callidyrr claimed the throne of Corwell in the {{YearlinkName|177}} after unifying the [[Ffolk]] kingdoms that had been engaged in civil war since the death of King [[Deric]] and the end of the [[First Dynasty of the Moonshaes]] in the {{YearlinkName|171}}.{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|25}}

He ruled for sixteen years before he was slain by [[troll]]s in the {{YearlinkName|193}}. Eight years later, his son would name the land of Callidyrr in his honor.
