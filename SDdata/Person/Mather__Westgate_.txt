Mather, who was nicknamed Wyrmshroud, was a wizard and the third King to rule the city-state of Westgate.
Mather reigned from  until . He was slain by a trio of black dragons.
Mather was the apprentice of King Saldrinar. Since the King Saldrinar did not have any heirs of his own, he decreed that Mather would inherit the throne upon his death.
