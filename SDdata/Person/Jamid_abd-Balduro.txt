'''Jamid abd-Balduro''' was an [[elf|elven]] [[Fighter|warrior]] who traveled across the deserts of [[Zakhara]] with her father's caravan around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Jamid was a muscular woman with very long, black hair. She had tall cheekbones, dark lips, and an earring in her right ear. She wore a golden helmet and red [[Dragon scale mail|dragonscale armor]].

==Possessions==
In addition to her dragonscale armor, Jamid owned a ''[[dragon slayer sword|scimitar of wyrm-slaying]]''.

==History==
Jamid fell in love with a [[human]] caravan worker. When her father, [[Balduro]], discovered the romance, he killed the man out of anger. Fearful for her life, Jamid killed her own bodyguard and fled the caravan.
