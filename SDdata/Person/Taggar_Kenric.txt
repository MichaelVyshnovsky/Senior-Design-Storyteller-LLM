Constal '''Taggar Kenric''' was a military officer of the [[Marsember Watch]] who served [[Cormyr]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
In addition to being a dedicated soldier, Taggar was a devoted follower of the deity [[Helm]].

==Relationships==
He came from a long line of [[Helm]]ite worshipers who dedicated themselves to routing out the corrupt behavior that was so prevalent in the Watch. He had a son named [[Kleef Kenric|Kleef]], who served as Helm's [[chosen]] during the events of the [[Second Sundering]].
