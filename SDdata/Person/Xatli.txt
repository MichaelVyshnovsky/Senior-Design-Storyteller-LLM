'''Xatli''' was a priest of [[Qotal]] in [[Nexal]].

==History==
In [[1361 DR]], he was one of the survivors after the [[Night of Wailing]].

Xatli was the eldest of the surviving priests and became their unofficial leader. He acted as advisor to [[Erixitl]] during the march.
