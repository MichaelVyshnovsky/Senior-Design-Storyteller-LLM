'''Dell McCourt''' was a [[dwarf]] [[soldier]] of the [[Dagult Neverember]] army in [[Neverwinter]].

==History==
Dell recruited an [[adventurer]] just arrived in the city to collect arrows from the dead in a skirmish with the [[undead]] of [[Valindra Shadowmantle]] and delivered them to the archers commanded by private [[Hawthidon]].
