'''Auldo Chaserperi''' was a member of the [[Chaserperi]] clan of [[mage]]s of the late mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Rumors & Legends==
He was said to have found a [[lich]] in the cellar of a home he bought in [[Waterdeep]] around the {{YearlinkName|1310}}, and to have come to an arrangement with it, which included him bringing the lich dead bodies. In exchange, the [[undead]] helped protect his properties around the city.
