Luvon Greencloak (Pronounce|LOO|vahn|refs=
He was in 1374 DR among the many powerful mages Arunsun brought together in order to cleanse part of the High Moor and restore Faertelmiir, a lost city of Miyeritar.
Despite his sun elf heritage, Luvon believed in the Moonstars ideal of unity of humans and elves.
