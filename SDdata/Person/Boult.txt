Boult was a dwarf who was imprisoned for treason in Tethyr during the mid-15 century DR.
Some time during or before the , Boult was imprisoned in Vankila Slab for "treason", or as a fellow prisoner described it interfering with the business of some nobleman. While in prison, Boult was slain by the Vankila Slabs ogres that served as its prison guards.
Boults identity was assumed by the disgraced royal guardsmen Amhar, convicted perpetrator of the Childrens Massacre, and the villainous murderer who was hated throughout all of Tethyr.
