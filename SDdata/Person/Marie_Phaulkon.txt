Lady Marie Phaulkon was a merchant inhabiting the city of Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Marie was a thrill-seeker who secretly worshiped Loviatar, performing painful rituals in honor of the goddess with several of her lovers. Her cult leader, Nathyn Powerl, planned to use her family connections to gain money and influence by blackmailing her.
She was the eldest daughter of Lord Bronson and Lady Cera.
