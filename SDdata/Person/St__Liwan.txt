St. Liwan was a saint of the Church of Ilmater and the patron of the House of St. Liwans Blessing.
St. Liwans teachings focused on food and diet and the peaceful effect they had on people. His followers would not only feed the hungry they insisted on providing the best food possible as a means of bringing satisfaction from want. Liwan believed that even war could be eradicated if all people everywhere were well-fed.
