'''Misikin''' was a [[feng huang]] member of the [[Celestial Bureaucracy]].

==Activities==
Misikin was one of the officers of the [[Qui sub-committee]] that governed the affairs of the animal population of the [[Fochu Peninsula]] under orders of the [[Ministry of Animal Affairs]] of the Celestial Bureaucracy. Misikin oversaw the feathered animals. She commanded 30 giant [[eagle]]s and 10 [[human]] slaves, of which 10 eagles were always on patrol. She interacted directly with intruders and asked for tribute to her.

==History==
In [[1358 DR]], one of her human slaves was the body of [[Setsu Iki]].
