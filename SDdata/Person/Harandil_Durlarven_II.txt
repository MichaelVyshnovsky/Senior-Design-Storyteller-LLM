'''Harandil Durlarven II''' was the second king of the [[Durlarven Dynasty]] to rule over [[Impiltur]].

==Activities==
Harandil II inherited the throne from his father [[Harandil Durlarven I]] in the {{YearlinkName|537}}. He reigned until his death in the {{YearlinkName|562}}

==Relationships==
He was the first son and heir of Harandil I and [[Tayissa Mirandor]]. Harandil II's only son and heir was [[Thorodil Durlarven|Thorodil "the Fierce"]].
