Medrash was a musician and entertainer, part of the Circus of the Last Days troupe in the late 15 century DR.
Medrash was a dragonborn with a red dragon ancestry. He had flaming red eyes and wore a fanciful bards garb. He was a proficient lyre player.
As a red dragonborn, Medrash could breathe out a burst of flames. He also could cast the faerie fire spell.
