Gabrielle was a wizard-in-training of Bloodstone Village in 1357 DR.
Gabrielle grew up in Bloodstone Village dreaming of becoming a wizard.
In 1357 DR, she joined Gareth Dragonsbanes party as she wanted to study under the partys wizard, Emelyn the Gray.
In 1359 DR, Gabrielle was the apprentice of Emelyn and a member of the Twilight Riders.
Gabrielle was fond of older men. She dreamed of becoming a wizard, and wanted to become an apprentice to one in order to learn. Gabrielle was chaste and shy but romantic.
Gabrielle was the daughter of the villages bakers, Benjamin and Anna. She fell in love with Emelyn.
