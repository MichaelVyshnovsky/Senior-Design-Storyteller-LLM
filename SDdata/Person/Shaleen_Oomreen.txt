Shaleen "Talonkiss" Oomreen was a human woman of the city of Calaunt in the Vast. She was a priestess of Loviatar.
She was the High Priestess, or "Whipmistress", of the House of Scarlet Hooks temple by 1358 DR, a position which she still held by 1370 DR. She had twenty-four subordinate priests and 114 lay-followers.
