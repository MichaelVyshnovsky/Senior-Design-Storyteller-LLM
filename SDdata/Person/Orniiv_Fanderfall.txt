Orniiv Fandarfall, also called the Eclipse, was a male human cleric of Mask. He was a member of the Shadow Council of the Shadow Thieves.
Orniiv was responsible for the guilds operation in Baldurs Gate. He was the perfect example of the guilds thieves always cold and inscrutable. His nickname came from a tattoo of an eclipse on his left cheek.
