'''Markoth Hasselpond''', usually known as '''Fat Mar''', was a waiter in [[Phlan]] during the [[Tyranny of Dragons]].{{Cite adventurers league/Secrets of Sokol Keep|7}}

==Description==
The waiter was a rotund man who sweated profusely and spoke breathlessly.{{Cite adventurers league/The Courting of Fire|10}}

==Personality==
Markoth had a sharp tongue and a foul mouth. He disliked his nickname but not the fact that everyone knew him. He was the principal fount of gossip inside the city, and usually took a liking to strangers who inquired about his real name.

==Relationships==
While the Laughing Goblin was owned by the [[dwarf]] [[Durnam Goblinkicker]], Markoth worked under [[Imizael]].{{Cite adventurers league/The Courting of Fire|9}}

==History==
Markoth worked at the [[Laughing Goblin Inn]] in [[Phlan]], helping the tavern owner Imizael. In 3 [[Marpenoth]] [[1489 DR]], he became involved in the [[Cult of the Dragon]]'s coup in the city, as the tavern became the headquarters of the forces fighting against the cultists.{{Cite adventurers league/Tyranny in Phlan|7}} Tragically, Fat Mar was slain by an [[ettin]] under the command of [[Farvnik|Farvnik the Venomous]] when [[Vorgansharax]] attacked the city.{{Cite adventurers league/Tyranny in Phlan|9}}
