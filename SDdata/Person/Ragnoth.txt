He was a cold and methodical individual.
He stood  tall and slender. He almost always wore black robes. 
Circa 1369 DR, Ragnoth was in command of the Zhentarim and Zhentilar occupying Snowmantle.
He carried a wand of polymorphing and rod of alertness.
