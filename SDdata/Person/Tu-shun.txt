Tu-shun was a sohei and also a zussho in Kozakura in 1357 DR.
Tu-shun administrated a shoen of the Konjo school near the Itsuki village. He led 77 sohei and was at the time in a dispute over ownership of the village with the samurai Sakai Kazuhiko. Tu-shun had captured the eastern end of Itsuki.
