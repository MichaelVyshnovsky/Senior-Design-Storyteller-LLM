'''Shalrin Meraedos''' was the Gentleman Keeper of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Solemn Order of Recognized Furriers and Woolmen]] and the proprietor of [[Meraedos Fine Furs]] in the [[North Ward of Waterdeep|North Ward]] in the mid&ndash;14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He always wore a fur half-cloak, under which he concealed a pair of blades.

==Personality==
Shalrin was known as a careful, soft-spoken, and highly observant man.

==Possessions==
His favored weapon was a pair of ''[[sword of dancing|swords of dancing]]'' that would fly back to him when called. He was also known to use other [[magic]]al protection.

==Activities==
As Gentleman Keeper, Shalrin served as the Order's public contact.

==Rumors & Legends==
According to local rumor, Shalrin had secretly embarked on a lucrative [[adventurer|adventuring]] career in [[Undermountain]]. He was reluctant to speak on the matter.
