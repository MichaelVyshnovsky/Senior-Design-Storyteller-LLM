'''Duldinbold Alarkyn''' was a [[gnome]] [[illusionist]] and high priest of [[Leira]], the Lady of the Mists, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Duldinbold was the Illusionmaster, or high priest, within the [[Mistkeep]] temple on [[Prespur]] in the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]]. He was rumored to possess the knowledge of how to enchant tabards and masks with battle [[magic]] granted by Leira.
