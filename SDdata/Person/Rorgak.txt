Rorgak was a tanarukk and the chief commander under Kaanyr Vhok of the Scoured Legion in the late 14 century DR.
A massive tanarukk, he was red-scaled and tusked.
Rorgak served Vhok as his envoy and his personal bodyguard.
Rorgak assisted his master in preparing the legion for war against Menzoberranzan in the . He also commanded part of the tanarukk troops in the field during the Siege of Menzoberranzan.
Rorgak also assisted Kaanyr in the attack on Sundabar and remained in control of the troops when Kaanyr and his command staff infiltrated the city and afterward left for the planes.
