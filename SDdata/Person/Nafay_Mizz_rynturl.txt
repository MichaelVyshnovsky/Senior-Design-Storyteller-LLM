Nafay Mizzrynturl was a drow traitor priestess of Guallidurth in 1378 DR.
Nafay was a noble of House Mizzrynturl of the city of Guallidurth but secretly also a traitor priestess of Vhaeraun.
She arranged a meeting with a mute wild elf who knew about a very ancient temple to Lolth on the surface. There, however, she was attacked and killed by her old childhood friend Tlar Mizzrynturl, under orders of the Velkyn Velve, the assassin branch of the new Temple of the Black Mother.
