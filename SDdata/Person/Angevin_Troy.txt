'''Angevin Troy''' was a patron of the [[Blushing Mermaid]] in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Angevin was an aged [[human]] man with a pale skin tone and a graying head of hair and mustache. He wore a simple leather jerkin.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the time [[Captain Grisly]] was replaced by the [[green hag]] [[Auntie Ethel]] as the owner of the Blushing Mermaid, Angevin could often be found passed out and drunk on the tavern's second floor.
