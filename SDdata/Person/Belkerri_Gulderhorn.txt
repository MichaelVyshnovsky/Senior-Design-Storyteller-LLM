First High Lady Scion of Siamorphe '''Belkerri Gulderhorn''' was the high priestess of [[Siamorphe]] within the [[Chapel and Chalice of the Divine Right]] in [[Waterdeep]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
In addition to her duties as high priestess, which included helping to resurrect Siamorphe's worship in Waterdeep following the [[Time of Troubles]], Lady Belkerri was the matriarch of the [[Gulderhorn]] noble family.

==History==
Belkerri fled [[Tethyr]] with her family during the [[Ten Black Days of Eleint]]. She secured a place in Waterdeep for her family by marrying her daughter to Lord [[Laeros Assumbar]].

==Relationships==
Her children were Lord [[Vrom Gulderhorn]], who worked with her in the Chapel, and Lady [[Kerri Assumbar]], who was the matriarch of the [[Assumbar]] noble family.
