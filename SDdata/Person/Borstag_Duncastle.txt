Borstag Duncastle was a merchant prince and an official of Ordulin in Sembia in the late 14 century DR.
Borstag served as a representative of Sembia in matters involving industry, settlement, and trade in the Dalelands and the Moonsea.
On Kythorn 4 in the , Borstag was in Hillsfar where he, together with First Lord Maalthiir, dealt with Sarya Dlardrageth.
Later in the Cormanthor War, before Flamerule 3, he was assassinated by the disguised daemonfey Teryani Ealoeth, who cut out and took his eyes.
