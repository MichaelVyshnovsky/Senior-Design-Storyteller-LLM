Vokkrzyr Rualfren was a cleric of Vhaeraun.
He was the leader of the drow settlement in Ultoksamrin,
Despite this background, Vokkrzyr Rualfren advocated cooperation with surface elves in their fight against Lolth and her priestesses, in this case against the temple city of Guallidurth. While his invitation and willingness for such cooperation was widely known, it wasnt known how much success he had with recruitment efforts.
