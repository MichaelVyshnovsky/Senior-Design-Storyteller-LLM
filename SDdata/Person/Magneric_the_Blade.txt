'''Magneric the Blade''' was a [[human]] [[barbarian]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==Description==
Magneric had a prominent scar across his face and a head of ashy brown hair, including a large thick beard. He wore an outfit made from [[Hide armor|hides]], teeth, and bits of carapace from various beasts, and carried a spiked [[greatclub]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}} during the [[Elven Crusade]], Magneric joined the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|fortified camp]] on the outskirts of [[Myth Drannor]]. He served as a trainer for fellow warriors who passed through the camp.
