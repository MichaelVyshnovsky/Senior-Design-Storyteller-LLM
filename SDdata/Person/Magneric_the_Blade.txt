Magneric the Blade was a human barbarian in the late 14 century DR.
Magneric had a prominent scar across his face and a head of ashy brown hair, including a large thick beard. He wore an outfit made from hides, teeth, and bits of carapace from various beasts, and carried a spiked greatclub.
In the  during the Elven Crusade, Magneric joined the Elves of Cormanthor at their fortified camp on the outskirts of Myth Drannor. He served as a trainer for fellow warriors who passed through the camp.
