Indra was an enormous wolf kept by the Zhentarim mercenaries that operated within an underground hideout found deep beneath the Waukeens Rest inn on the Sword Coast for a period during the late 15 century DR.
She seemed somewhat scared of the dark, or at least unnatural darkness.
Indra loyally protected her person Vol.
