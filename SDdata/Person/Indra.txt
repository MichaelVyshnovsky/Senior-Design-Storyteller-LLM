'''Indra''' was an enormous [[wolf]] kept by the [[Zhentarim]] mercenaries that operated within an [[Zhentarim hideout (Risen Road)|underground hideout]] found deep beneath the [[Waukeen's Rest]] inn on the [[Sword Coast]] for a period during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''The dark smells wrong. Not night-dark. Waiting-dark.''|Indra}}

==Personality==
She seemed somewhat scared of the dark, or at least unnatural darkness.

==Relationships==
Indra loyally protected her person [[Vol]].

==History==
The wolf Indra accompanied Vol as she helped prepare the Zhentarim hideout for evacuation and eventual demolition in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
