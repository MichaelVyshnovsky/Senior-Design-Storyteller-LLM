'''Veperyd''' was one of the [[fire giant]] lieutenants of Queen [[Hledh Hellspawn]], who ruled the city of [[Maerimydra]] in the [[Deep Wastes]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/It's All in the Blood|35}}

==History==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], Veperyd was in charge of the security of [[Coliseum (Maerimydra)|Coliseum]] with four [[bar-lgura]] guards until an adventuring party hired by [[Solom Ned'razak]] to collect samples of fiendish fire giant blood confronted and defeated him and his minions.{{Cite adventurers league/It's All in the Blood|33}}

==Personality==
Veperyd enjoyed the power of his position and did anything to keep control of the fighting in the Coliseum.
