Fukawa was a mysterious sage in Shou Lung.
Fukawa spoke all the time, about all kinds of subjects. He was a kind of clown but had truly great knowledge. He loved to eat and drink.
Fukawa was a sage living in Mount Fukawa in Chu Yuan province. He was quite famous in the area.
In 1358 DR, an adventuring party hired by the Emperor to find General Goyat Nagumo met Fukawa, who insisted on accompanying them on the adventure.
