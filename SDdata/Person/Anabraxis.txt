'''Anabraxis the Black Talon''' was a runescribed [[dracolich]] revered by the [[Cult of the Dragon]] as their ultimate authority around the [[1479 DR|Year of the Ageless One]]. 

==Activities==
She had various lairs in [[Lorosfyr]] in the [[Underdark]], from were she could travel to the [[Shadowfell]] at will through its bottomless abyss, and she commanded a tribe of [[Death giant]]s that lived in the deepest regions of the chasm.
