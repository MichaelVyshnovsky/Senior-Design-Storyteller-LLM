'''Irphiina''' was a [[shade]] [[human]] who worked for [[Telamont Tanthul|Lord Telamont]] of the [[Shadovar]].

==Description==
Appearing human, Irphiina had dusky gray skin, a dour expression, and shadows that seemed to cling to her skin. She wore crimson and purple robes that were both elaborate and exotic.
