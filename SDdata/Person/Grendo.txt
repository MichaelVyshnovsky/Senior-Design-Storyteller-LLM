'''Grendo''' was a tavern server at the [[Bargewright Inn]] during the 1490s DR.

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], he became involved in a plot by [[Inglor Brathren]] to discredit both [[Chalaska Muruin]] and [[Nalaskur Thaelond]].
