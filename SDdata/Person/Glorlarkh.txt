'''Chief Glorlarkh''' was the former ruler of the [[Tornskulls]] [[orc]]s, that dwelled within [[Dead Orc Pass]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was known as a ferocious chieftain and war-leader.

==Relationships==
The chief was survived by his wife and mate [[Baerzel]], who was known for her exceptional wisdom.

==History==
Glorlarkh died sometime around the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, after he was caught within a cavern when it collapsed.
