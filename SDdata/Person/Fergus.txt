==Activities==
At least for a while, Fergus was stationed at [[High Hall]] in the [[Baldur's Gate/Temples District|Temples District]] of the [[Upper City]].

==Relationships==
Fergus had a paramour he referred to has "his lady", and was wholly in love with her.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, Fergus received a letter from his love saying she would come home to Baldur's Gate very soon. He planned on obtaining an ''angel skin ring'' to offer his love upon her return to the city.
