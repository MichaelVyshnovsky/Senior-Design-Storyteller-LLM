'''Gorthrimmon''' was a member of the [[Cult of the Dragon]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In {{yearlink|1357}}, while deployed to [[Orthil Voldovan]]'s caravan from [[Scornubel]] to [[Waterdeep]] to shadow [[Shandril Shessair]], Holvan and Brasker were under orders from Gorthrimmon to take Shandril alive.
