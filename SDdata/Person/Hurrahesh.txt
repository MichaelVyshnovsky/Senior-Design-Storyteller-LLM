'''Hurrahesh''' was a notorious [[winter wolf]] inhabiting the [[High Country (Rashemen)|High Country]] of [[Rashemen]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Hurrahesh was easily recognized by his distinct green eyes.

==Activities==
He was known to work with the local [[goblin]] and [[troll]] tribes.
