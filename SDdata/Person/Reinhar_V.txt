Reinhar V was a king of the Arkaiuns of Dambrath.
The man who would rule as Reinhar V was the second son of King Reinhar IV. He also had a younger brother, who would succeed him as king.
Reinhar V was born in the Year of the Kindly Lich, 606 DR.
When a bloating plague killed his father in 617 DR, along with ten percent of the population of Dambrath, Reinhar V took the throne.
At his death in 647 DR, his brother succeeded him as Reinhar VI.
