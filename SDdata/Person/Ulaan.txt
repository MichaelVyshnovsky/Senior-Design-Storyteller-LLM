'''Ulaan''' was and a servant in the [[Fortress of the Old Man]].{{cite book/Sentinelspire||18}} She helped wash and clothe [[Lewan]] when he was brought in by [[Sauk]] after [[Tarsakh 16]] of {{yearlink|1374}},{{cite book/Sentinelspire||13}} and eventually became close with him.
==Appearance==
To Lewan, she was close to his age, maybe a bit older. She had slanted eyes, honey-wax-colored skin, and long raven-eye hair.
==Powers==
As one of the personal Blades of Lady Talieth, Ulaan was trained in fighting.{{cite book/Sentinelspire||35}}
==History==
Ulaan was sold to Lady [[Talieth]] in [[Almorel]] around {{yearlink|1364}}. Her mother had been a servant of a merchant trading along the [[Golden Way]].{{cite book/Sentinelspire||21}}
Ulaan began to get closer to Lewan on the night of [[Tarsakh 19]], during the assassins' vigil; she feigned fear to get into his room, and then seduced him,{{cite book/Sentinelspire||19}} on the orders of Lady Talieth, who wanted to motivate him to stay in Sentinelspire.{{cite book/Sentinelspire||37}}
