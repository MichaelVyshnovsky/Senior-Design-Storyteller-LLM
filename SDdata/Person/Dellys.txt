Dellys was a news hawker who worked for the Baldurs Mouth Gazette in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Dellys was a dark-skinned human boy with brown eyes. He wore a quality leather jerkin and a hat while selling the broadsheets.
In the , during the Absolute crisis, Dellys could often be found peddling the Baldurs Mouth Gazette across the street from Beehive Goods and Wares in the Lower City alongside Pharnilla, with whom Dellys took breaks. When he was interrupted whilst on a break, Dellys claimed to be a member of the Gazetteers Guild and stated he had rights and whatever business the person had would have to wait.
