Bexendral was a gold dragon and a prince of Dawnfire during the Dawn Age around .
He was huge.
The prince was known to cast at least one spell that hurled a shower of sparks, which then ignited and exploded. He was also capable of casting at least one spell that teleported it, most likely teleportation.
Bexendral was able to hover in place with some difficulty on his own power.
Bexendral was known to make a study of magic.
He was the heir of Orchtrien, the king of Dawnfire.
Around , Bexendral aided the forces of Rhespen Ash, an elven commander under his reign, after they were ambushed by rebels, saving their lives. triggering  their later schemes.
