'''Xarann A'Daragon''' was an assassin spy of [[House Freth]] in [[Undermountain]] in the early 1490s [[DR]].

==Activities==
In [[1492 DR]],{{Waterdeep-time}} Xarann was ordered by his masters to infiltrate [[Muiral's Gauntlet]], headquarters of their enemy [[House Auvryndar]], and try to kill the princess [[Vlonwelv Auvryndar]]. He was posing as an Auvryndar assassin. He also tried to free his accomplice [[Tazirahc Oussar]].

==Relationship==
Xarann had the loyalty of [[Sornnozz]], who betrayed the Auvryndar for him, and of Tazirahc.
