Xarann ADaragon was an assassin spy of House Freth in Undermountain in the early 1490s DR.
In 1492 DR, Xarann was ordered by his masters to infiltrate Muirals Gauntlet, headquarters of their enemy House Auvryndar, and try to kill the princess Vlonwelv Auvryndar. He was posing as an Auvryndar assassin. He also tried to free his accomplice Tazirahc Oussar.
Xarann had the loyalty of Sornnozz, who betrayed the Auvryndar for him, and of Tazirahc.
