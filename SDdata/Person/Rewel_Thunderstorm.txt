High Morninglord '''Rewel Thunderstorm''' was the High Priest of [[Lathander]] in [[Morningdawn Hall]] in the village of [[Shadowdale (town)|Shadowdale]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Rewel was the leader of Lathander's faithful in Shadowdale. He was assisted by seven other priests and eleven followers.
