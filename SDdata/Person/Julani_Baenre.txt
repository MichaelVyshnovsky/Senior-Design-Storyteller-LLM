'''Julani Baenre''' was a [[drow]] [[wizard]] of [[House Baenre]] in [[Menzoberranzan]] and the Master of [[Evocation]] at [[Sorcere]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Along with the other mages of House Baenre, Julani aided [[Gromph Baenre]] in attacking [[Nimor Imphraezl]] through a [[scrying]] crystal during the [[Siege of Menzoberranzan]] in the {{YearlinkName|1373}}. However his ''[[magic missile]]'' spell was reflected back on Julani, killing him.
