Aradoness '''Angharad Odaeyns''' was an [[elven]] priestess and healer that lived in [[Evermeet]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Your concern for your friend is touching. Bear in mind that it is just that concern that will keep his spirit in his body.|Angharad to [[Khelben Arunsun]].}}

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1371}}, Angharad was dispatched by the [[Island Council]] to [[Blackstaff Tower]] in [[Waterdeep]] via its ''[[elfgate]]'', in order to tend the injured [[Gervas Imesfor]]. The High Lord of [[Evereska]] sought aid from his friend [[Khelben Arunsun]] in the city, after finding himself unable to ''planewalk'' back to his home city.

After the safety of the High Lord was ensured by Khelben and his allies, the priestess gated back to Evermeet to report what she learned aboutt he goingson at the elven LastHome.
