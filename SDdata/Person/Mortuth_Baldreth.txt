'''Mortuth Baldreth''' was a traveling merchant that dealt in wagons, wagon wheels, cart wheels, lanterns, and large tools such as [[pick]]s, woodcutting [[axe]]s, and shovels.

==Abilities==
Mortuth was extremely strong and a specialist with the [[longsword]].

==Possessions==
His possessions included his wares and other business property. He wore [[splint mail]] and carried a shield when he felt the need to protect himself.

==Activities==
Mortuth was an agent of the [[Zhentarim]] that traveled to [[Voonlar]] as part of his itinerary. He gathered intelligence and kept an eye on Bron [[Buorstag Hlammythyl]] and his deputies,{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 2: The Bron of Voonlar}}
