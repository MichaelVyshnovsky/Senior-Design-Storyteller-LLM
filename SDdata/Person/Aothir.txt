Aothir was a dwarf who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.
Aothir was a portly, red-bearded dwarf.
Aothir was an armorer who owned a stand in the market square of the Protectors Enclave. He sold armors made by his friends from Sundabar, his hometown.
Aothir proclaimed himself to be an heir of Delzoun, and in his younger years he traveled the North in search of fabled Gauntlgrym. However, at some point he retired from his quest and opened an armory in Neverwinter.
