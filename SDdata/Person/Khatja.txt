Khatja was a half-orc muscle of the adventuring company - the Ladys Lament from Waterdeep, active circa 1310 DR.
Unlike her mate, Khatja was a chaotic woman who venerated the ferocity of barbarians. Khatja wielded her giant two-handed broadsword in battle.
Several years before the , Khatja, along with her mate Geldin, joined the Ladys Lament and quickly gained respect for their strength. The couple rarely talked about their past, but one thing was known about them - they have been ostracised by both humans and orcs alike.
In 1310 DR, the Ladys Lament, along with many other mercenaries, traveled from Luskan to the besieged city of Targos in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.
