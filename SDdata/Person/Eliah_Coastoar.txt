'''Eliah Coastoar''' was a seller in the [[Moonsea]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/No Foolish Matter|11}}

==Activities==
Eliah worked at [[Doctor Jubal's Carnival of Curiosities]] as a seller of [[wine]] and [[pie]]s.
 
==History==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], Eliah noticed that [[Jubal]] had brought four new attractions to the carnival: [[Madame Darkness]], a mirror maze, dancing rats, and some fools.
