'''Nabil al-Karim''' was a [[Zakharan (ethnicity)|Zakharan]] [[sha'ir]] and member of the [[House of Al-Karim]] in [[Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1993|255|[[Nabil al-Karim]]}}

==Activities==
Nabil and his [[djinnling]] [[Faruk]] were hopeful that a peaceful solution could be reached to end the blood feud between his tribe and a wealthy merchant from [[Hiyal]] named [[Farid]].

==Possessions==
Nabil owned a ''[[wand of magic missiles]]'' and wielded a [[dagger]] and [[quarterstaff]].
