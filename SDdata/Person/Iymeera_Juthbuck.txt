'''Iymeera Juthbuck''' was a [[War Wizard]] of [[Cormyr]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In {{yearlink|1373}}, the first assignment that the [[Royal Magician of Cormyr]], [[Caladnei]], assigned to the [[Waterdhavian]] [[thief]] [[Narnra Shalace]], her newest spy, was to tail numerous [[War Wizards]], Iymeera Juthbuck among them.

==Rumors & Legends==
[[Narnra Shalace]] came across rumors that she was a bit of a wildcat where it came to strong adventurers.{{cite book/Elminster's Daughter/MMP|313|18}}
