Iymeera Juthbuck was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.
In , the first assignment that the Royal Magician of Cormyr, Caladnei, assigned to the Waterdhavian thief Narnra Shalace, her newest spy, was to tail numerous War Wizards, Iymeera Juthbuck among them.
Narnra Shalace came across rumors that she was a bit of a wildcat where it came to strong adventurers.
