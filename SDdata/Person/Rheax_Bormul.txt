'''Rheax Bormul ''' was a [[rogue]] and one of the members of the [[Shadow Council]] of the [[Shadow Thieves]].

Rheax was the son of a ruined noble family and he enjoyed causing the decline of members of his former class. His job was to control the guild's operations in [[Amn]].
