'''Hayanalof Brezen''' was a member of the [[Red Wizards of Thay]] who operated within the [[Thay]]an enclave in [[Mulmaster]].

==Activities==
Hatanalof was tasked with the duty to ensure the profitability of Mulmaster enclave, and to help advance [[Thay]]'s plans for the region. He would protect his secrets with his life.

==History==
In 1373 DR Hatanalof together with conjumaster [[Ahryn Firefinger]] devised a plot to gain control of the local [[Zhentarim]] selling to them a group of slaves magically altered.
