Hayanalof Brezen was a member of the Red Wizards of Thay who operated within the Thayan enclave in Mulmaster.
Hatanalof was tasked with the duty to ensure the profitability of Mulmaster enclave, and to help advance Thays plans for the region. He would protect his secrets with his life.
In 1373 DR Hatanalof together with conjumaster Ahryn Firefinger devised a plot to gain control of the local Zhentarim selling to them a group of slaves magically altered.
