Lady '''Lharida Ithal''' was a noble from [[Tethyr]] who married into the [[Soleilon Dynasty]] of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Lady Lharida ruled Westgate as Regent when her husband died in {{Yearlink|-68}}. She ruled until she died from poisoning in {{Yearlink|-55}}. 

==Relationships==
Lady Lharida married King [[Lemere Soleilon II]]. Their son was [[Ryndarth Soleilon I]].
