Grinna Brightbell was a halfling innkeeper and cook in the Quivering Forest near Phlan in 1489 DR.
Grinna was spunky, opinionated, and spoke her mind. She complemented what Hupe lacked. She was very popular in the community and anyone that liked her cooking could have a place in her heart.
Grinna ran the Leaky Bucket inn with her husband Hupe Brightbell in the Kabels Hills community in the Quivering Forest just near Phlan. She was an amazing cook.
