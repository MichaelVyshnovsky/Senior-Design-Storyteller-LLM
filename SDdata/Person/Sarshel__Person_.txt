'''Sarshel''' was an apprentice at [[Blackstaff Tower]]. On 28 Uktar [[1374 DR]], Sarshel was involved in the defence of the tower against [[Kemarn Darkthrush]], casting a wall of ice to prevent his summoned [[nishruu]] from entering a hole in the tower's wall.
