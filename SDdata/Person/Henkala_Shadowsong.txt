Henkala Shadowsong was a deep gnome shopkeeper in Blingdenstone.
Henkala escaped from Blingdenstone in 1371 DR.
Circa 1439 DR, she went with the first expedition led by Kargien Dissengulp back to the ruins of the city and started reconstruction. 
In 1479 DR, she was concerned about the Ogremochs Bane.
