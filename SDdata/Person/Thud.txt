'''Thud''' was the [[minotaur]] innkeep of [[The Inlet]] in [[Thentia]] in [[1367 DR]].

==Personality==
Thud was bookish and well-read, and extended his tastes to his inn. If a patron was too loud or rowdy, he would forcibly educate them on politeness, then pummel them senseless.
