The '''"Keeper of the Old Faith"''' was the title of the senior [[Sage-Priest]], the official historian, of the [[Church of Ilmater]] in the late 13th century [[DR]].

The Keeper of the Old Faith succeeded the previous senior Sage-Priest, the [[Spontaer]], after his sacrifice ''circa'' [[1265 DR]].
