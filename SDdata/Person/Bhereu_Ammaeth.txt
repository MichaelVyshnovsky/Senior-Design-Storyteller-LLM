Duke Bhereu Ammaeth was the Lord High Marshal of Cormyr. He was bald. He resided in High Horn.
In 1369 DR Bhereu was on a hunt with King Azoun IV, Baron Thomdor and Aunadar Bleth when he was poisoned by a magical abraxus in the abraxus affair, putting him in a coma-like state. He died soon after this, vomiting black viscous bile.
Bhereu was the brother of Baron Thomdor and the cousin of former King of Cormyr, Azoun IV.
