'''Cork''' was a [[warrior]] and [[adventurer]] inhabiting the city of [[Daggerford]] in the [[Western Heartlands]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
His home was located in the northwest corner of Daggerford along [[Horse Way]].{{Cite interactive atlas|3990 Daggerford}} 

==Relationships==
He was friends with [[Maelwyn]] and often went adventuring with him. He was in a romantic relationship with [[Aswansea]] and wished to marry her, but the [[elf]] resisted his proposal because he was a "short-lived" [[human]].
