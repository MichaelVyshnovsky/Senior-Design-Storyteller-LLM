'''Farid al-Nisr''' was a [[Harper]] wizard in [[1372 DR]].
  
==History==
Around [[1369 DR]], Farid was sent to [[New Waterdeep]] in [[Maztica]] by [[High Harpers|High Harper]] [[Khelben Arunsun]] together with his Harper fiancee Lady [[Syllia Adarbrent]] and fellow Harper [[Orist Moonbow]]. There he was chosen as one of the new [[Lords of the Borderlands]] for his integrity and his connections.

Around [[1371 DR]], Farid and Syllia were back in [[Waterdeep]] and they impetuously married before going back to Maztica.

==Relationship==
Known to few, Farid was the son of [[Fahd yn Sarsor al Nisr]], a First Yrshelem of the [[Rundeen]].
 
==Personality==
Farid hated slavery.
