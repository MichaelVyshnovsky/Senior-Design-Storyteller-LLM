'''Fovem''' was a [[thief]] and a seller of black market goods from the city of [[Athkatla]] in [[Amn]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Might ye wish to take a look at some wares o' mine? I can offer them at a mighty fine price... Come, take a look.''|Fovem}}

==Description==
He was a [[human]] man who worse tan and brown thieves garb and [[Leather armor|leathers]].

==Possessions==
Fovem offered items at reduced prices. His stock included several types of weapons and armor, including dangerous ''[[potions of explosions]]''.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Fovem peddled his illicit goods in Athkatla's [[Athkatla/Wave District|Wave District]], just outside the [[Galvarey Estate]], secretly a [[Harper]] safehouse. He also offered information on the city and its goings-on for a price of 50 [[gold]] [[coin|pieces]]. Fovem was not affiliated with the [[Shadow Thieves]] of Athkatla nor the new emergent mysterious rival guild; however, he claimed that the Grandmaster of the Shadow Thieves sat on the [[Council of Six (Amn)|Council of Six]] and [[Renal the Bloodscalp]] ran the guild's daily activities.
