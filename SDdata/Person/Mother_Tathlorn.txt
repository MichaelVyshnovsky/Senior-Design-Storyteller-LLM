'''Mother Tathlorn''' was a businessperson inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
She owned a [[festhall]] and spa called [[Mother Tathlorn's House of Pleasure and Healing]] located on [[Gem Street]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]].

==Relationships==
She was a close friend of [[Regnet Amcathra]].
