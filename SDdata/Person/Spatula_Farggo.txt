'''Spatula Farggo''' was an employee of [[Baldur's Gate]]'s [[Elfsong Tavern]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Farggo was a dusky-skinned [[gold dwarf]] with a clean-shaven face and blue eyes.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Spatula Farggo worked in the Elfsong Tavern's kitchens. When the city began being flooded by refugees of the encroaching army of [[the Absolute]], the dwarf began helping out the beggars in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]], by bringing them kitchen scraps and leftovers.
