Spatula Farggo was an employee of Baldurs Gates Elfsong Tavern in the late 15 century DR.
Farggo was a dusky-skinned gold dwarf with a clean-shaven face and blue eyes.
In the , Spatula Farggo worked in the Elfsong Taverns kitchens. When the city began being flooded by refugees of the encroaching army of the Absolute, the dwarf began helping out the beggars in the Lower City, by bringing them kitchen scraps and leftovers.
