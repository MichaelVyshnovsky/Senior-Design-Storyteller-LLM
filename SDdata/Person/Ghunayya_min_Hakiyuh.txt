'''Ghunayya min Hakiyuh''' was a high priestess of [[Hakiyah]] of the [[Mosque of Hakiyah the Honest]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Gem of Zakhara|67-69}}

==Activities==
Ghunayya was a member of the [[holy slayer]] organization called the [[Soft Whisper]]. The basement of the mosque was their headquarters. Ghunayya was loyal to the [[Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir|Grand Caliph]].

==Rumors and Legends==
It was rumored that if there was a threat to the Grand Caliph's life, the [[Alyana al-Azzazi|Grand Vizier]] came to the mosque to pray. The threat to the Grand Caliph was eliminated shortly thereafter.
