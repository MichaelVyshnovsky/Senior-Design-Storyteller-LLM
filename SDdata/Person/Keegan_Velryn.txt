Keegan Velryn was a man who lived in Targos during the late 15 century DR.
He was described as being rather handsome.
Keegan was married to the rugged outdoorsman Garret Velryn. 
In the , Garret Velryn went missing while leading an expedition across Kelvins Cairn. Over the next few days, Keegan became increasingly worried, and eventually hired some adventurers to uncover his whereabouts.
