'''Kilti''' was a young [[Eagle Knights (Maztica)|Eagle Knight]] of [[Nexal]].

==History==
In [[1361 DR]], he was one of the survivors after the [[Night of Wailing]]. He was among the leaders of the survivors and discussed the menace of the [[Viperhands]] monsters.
