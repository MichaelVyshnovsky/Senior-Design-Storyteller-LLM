'''Haither Stonehand''' was the general of [[Daggerford]]'s militia during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Haither considered all her soldiers to be more friends and family than subordinates, gaining their absolute loyalty. She was honest but not well versed in diplomacy.{{Cite web/Siege of Dragonspear: Field Report|25}} 

Haither's mother died during the [[Second Dragonspear War]], in the {{YearlinkName|1363}}.{{Cite web/Siege of Dragonspear: Field Report|24}}

==History==
The masons of [[Clan Stonehand]] lived in the town of Daggerford for generations before Haither.

General Stonehand led the [[Army of Daggerford|Daggerford troops]] during the [[War of the Shining Crusade]] in the {{YearlinkName|1368}}.
