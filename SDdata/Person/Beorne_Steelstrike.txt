'''Beorne Steelstrike''' was a caravan master who lived in [[Icewind Dale]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{Quote|''"I've seen some things that would curl your beard here in [[Icewind Pass]]."''|Beorne, to adventurers}}

==Description==
He had the fair skin of a [[shield dwarf]] and a long, gray beard.

==Personality==
Beorne was extremely knowledgeable about the [[Ten Towns]], and had a pointed opinion about each of its settlements.{{Template:Cite book/Legacy of the Crystal Shard/Campaign Guide|12}}

==Possessions==
He owned a [[pick axe]] with a grip made of ''[[truesilver]]''.

==History==
At some point, Beorne spent some time in the great [[the North|northern]] city of [[Mirabar]].{{Template:Cite book/Legacy of the Crystal Shard/Campaign Guide|23}}

Beorne had a great amount of experience living in Icewind Dale, having tried his hand at a number of different professions. At one point he tried panning for gold on the banks of the [[Shaengarne]] river, near [[Bremen]].{{Template:Cite book/Legacy of the Crystal Shard/Campaign Guide|33}} In fact, he was quite knowledgeable about mining, specifically with regards to the mineral [[chardalyn]].

In the {{YearlinkName|1489}}, he sought out some adventurers along the [[Icewind Pass]] to help mine or scavenge any chardalyn they could find.
