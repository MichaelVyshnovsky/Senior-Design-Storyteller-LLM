'''Carthus Cymrych''' was a High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the early-to-mid 8{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|33}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] and the first son of his predecessor, High King [[Ernest Cymrych|Ernest]]. His successor was his younger brother, [[Alec Cymrych II|Alec II]].

==History==
Carthus assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|731}}, following the death of his father. He then immediately abdicated the throne to his brother, Alec II.

Carthus died of natural causes fourteen years later in the {{YearlinkName|745}}, by which time his nephew, [[Pendar Cymrych|Pendar]], had become High King following Alec II's death.
