Lady Harriana Hawkwinter was a noble member of the Waterdhavian Hawkwinter family and a knight in service of the Order of the Gauntlet during the late 15 century DR.
She was a follower of the god Helm.
Some time around the , Lady Hawkwinter and her squire rescued a group of children that had been trapped in the wreckage of a building destroyed by a band of stone giants. They sought out individuals to care for the children while they pursued the giants.
