'''Rundeth Hobyltar''' was the only tailor and dyer of [[High Dale]].

==Personality==
He was cynical and laconic.

==History==
Rundeth was in his youth a warrior on [[Sembia]]'s payroll before he settled in High Dale. When in [[1358 DR]] a secret [[Zhent]] occupation army occupied the dale, Rundeth was a member of the [[Council of the High Dale]].
