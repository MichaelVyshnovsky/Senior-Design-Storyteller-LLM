'''Frait the Ardent''' was an [[elf]] [[favored soul]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==Description==
Frait had gray skin, gray eyes, and short dark gray hair. She wore a sleeveless [[dress]] of sorts, which was entirely black and gray.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}} during the [[Elven Crusade]], Frait joined the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|fortified camp]] outside the ruins of [[Myth Drannor]]. She provided training to prospective favored souls at the camp.
