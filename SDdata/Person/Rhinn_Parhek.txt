'''Rhinn Parhek''' was an [[Amn]]ian son of the [[Parhek]] dynasty in the late 13{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Rhinn was the eldest son of Prince [[Malek Parhek (prince)|Malek the Coinmage]], and the twin of [[Savra Parhek|Savra]]. Their mother died in childbirth, and they had a younger half-brother [[Parav Parhek|Parav]] by their stepmother.

==History==
Rhinn grew up in [[Waterdeep]] as part of the exiled Parhek family. They were located by his cousin Prince [[Rohav Steelsar|Rohav]], and Rhinn was killed in a staged tavern brawl in [[1305 DR]] by his agents.
