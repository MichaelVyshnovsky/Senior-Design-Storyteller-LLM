Crown Prince Emvar Obarskyr was the older brother of King Foril Obarskyr I but was killed before he could ascend the throne of Cormyr.
His parents were King Azoun V and Queen Nalara. Emvar had two bastard children, Halance and Erzoured,
Relatively little is noted of Crown Prince Emvars life. His was apparently a life of responsibility, having been groomed for rulership. He did enjoy at least two romantic affairs, however, one with a woman named Laendra Shatterhawk, whose son was adopted by House Crownsilver, and another with a merchants daughter from Dhedluk named Solatha Boldtree, who was made a Countess and invited to live in the Royal Palace in Suzail.
Emvar was assassinated in the  by Sembians south of the Vast Swamp scant months before the birth of his second son, leaving his brother Foril as the new heir to the throne of Cormyr.
