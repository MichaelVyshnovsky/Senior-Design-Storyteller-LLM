'''Ghaurin''' was [[dwarf]] [[warrior]] of [[Citadel Adbar]] during ages past.

==Activities==
Ghaurin was considered a great hero among the dwarves of Citadel Adbar. His [[Ghaurin's Hold|mansion]] once sat upon a trade road that connected Citadel Adbar with [[the Fork]].
