'''Nelve Harssad''' was an inhabitant of [[Tsurlagol]], a traveler and an author.

Nelve was the author of ''[[My Journeys Around the Sea of Fallen Stars]]'', a book detailing their travels on the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]]. It was written in [[1332 DR|the Year of the Sword and Stars, 1332 DR]].
