Boss Zurga was a goblin warrior who joined the cult of the Absolute in late 15 century DR.
Zurga had brown skin. She wore a crude set of plate armor, complete with a metal helmet adorned with feathers.
She had a high opinion of herself and her fighting capabilities, but was less confident about the goblins she led. She thought them to be a lot of lazy drunks.
As of the the , Zurga remained stationed at the Shattered Sanctum.
