Boss '''Zurga''' was a [[goblin]] warrior who joined the [[cult of the Absolute|cult]] of [[the Absolute]] in late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|I'm as hard as they come, an' I'm itchin' for another fight.|Boss Zurga.}}

==Description==
Zurga had brown skin. She wore a crude set of [[plate armor]], complete with a metal [[helmet]] adorned with feathers.

==Personality==
She had a high opinion of herself and her fighting capabilities, but was less confident about the goblins she led. She thought them to be a lot of lazy drunks.

==History==
As of the the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Zurga remained stationed at the [[Shattered Sanctum]].
