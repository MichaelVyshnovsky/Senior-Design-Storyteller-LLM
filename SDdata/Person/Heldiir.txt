'''Heldiir''' was a [[Zhentarim]] mage of the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was only old enough to master his nerves.

==Personality==
He was somewhat skittish.

==History==
In late {{yearlink|1357}}, [[Manshoon]] ordered Heldiir to lead a group of twenty mages in keeping a magical watch over [[Shadowdale]], with the purpose of ensuring [[Elminster]] and other notables did not undertake secret movements.
