Aragath Taltar was an evil lich-like undead in Turmish in 1370 DR.
Aragath was a priest of Talos in the smugglers city of Jathrins Jump. He was planning to become undead when his temple was destroyed by followers of Lathander. Somehow he returned from death as a wraith-like lich leading some stone golems.
Around 1310 DR, a thief recovered Aragaths diary and sold it to the Uluuthin family, who died later during an adventure.
Around 1370 DR, Aragath killed an adventurer.
