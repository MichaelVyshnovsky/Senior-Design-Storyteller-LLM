Adnon Kareese was a male drow and a member of a band of four renegades including Donnia Soldou, Kaerlic Suun Wett, and Tosun Armgo.
Adnon belonged to House Khareese of Ched Nasad. He was once a scout, thief, and assassin there, but a failed assassination attempt forced him to leave the Underdark.
He was killed in battle in 1370 DR when Drizzt DoUrden vaulted behind him and sliced his head off.
He was Donnia Soldous lover.
