==Description==
Easton had light skin and short red-brown hair. He wore a long tunic and carried a [[quarterstaff]].

==History==
During the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar]] in the mid-1480s [[DR]],{{DDO-time}} Easton tried to get heroic individuals such as [[adventurer]]s to rescue Eveningstar residents from being stranded in the [[King's Forest]], which was a battlefield with the [[drow]] of [[Sschindylryn]].
