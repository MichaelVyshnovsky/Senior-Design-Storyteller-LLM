William was the mayor of the halfling town of  Waukeshire in Bloodstone Barony in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He had a waxed moustache and wore a waistcoat with a silver chain.
William liked to make long, formal speeches.
In 1357 DR, with the beginning of Zhengyi the Witch-Kings uncontested rule over Bloodstone Pass, William formed the Alliance of Belt Watchers along with Tokan, leader of the dwarves of Clan Orothiar, and ultimately with Chief Kiros of the centaurs.
In 1358 DR, William and his people met Gareth Dragonsbanes party.
