Us was an intellect devourer that was found aboard a nautiloid in the 15 century DR.

Us was an aberration that appeared to be friendly to others affected by its illithid creators.
When Us telepathically communicated with another, they talked in a soft voice.
In the late 15 century DR, Us was aboard a nautiloid that was under attack by githyanki. During the calamity, Us found its way into the head of the high elf Myrnath. Us wished to be free and attempted to recruit a "friend" to help them get to the helm of the nautiloid.
