'''Us''' was an [[intellect devourer]] that was found aboard a [[nautiloid]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Do you not hear it? We will not survive here. We are needed to navigate - we are needed to leave this realm.|Us' cryptic message.}}

==Description==
Us weighed {{SI|33|lb|k}}.

==Personality==
Us was an [[aberration]] that appeared to be friendly to others affected by its [[illithid]] creators.

==Relationships==
When Us telepathically communicated with another, they talked in a soft voice.

==History==
In the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], Us was aboard a nautiloid that was under attack by [[githyanki]]. During the calamity, Us found its way into the head of the [[high elf]] [[Myrnath]]. Us wished to be free and attempted to recruit a "friend" to help them get to the helm of the nautiloid.
