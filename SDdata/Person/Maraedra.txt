Maraedra, also known as "Mother Maraedra", was the keeper of a brothel called The Willing Smile in Suzail, Cormyr, in the late 15 century DR.
Rather than alluring, she was motherly.
She was acquainted and on good terms with Elminster Aumar.
Maraedra allowed Elminster and his relative, the coinlass Amarune Whitewave, to enter the Willing Smile in the , providing them with a table where they could converse.
