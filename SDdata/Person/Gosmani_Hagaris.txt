'''Gosmani Hagaris''' was a [[human]] man of the town of [[Hlintar]] in [[the Vast]] in [[1367 DR]]. 

He was a [[druid]] of [[Chauntea]] and led a small temple devoted to the Grain Goddess in Hlintar.
