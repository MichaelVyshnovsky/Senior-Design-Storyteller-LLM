'''Slub''' was a [[hill giant]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==History==
In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Slub was one of the minions of Chief [[Guh]] in [[Grudd Haug]]. He lived in a very dirty room together with his pet [[otyugh]].
