'''Valia''' was a woman in [[Luskan]] in the late 15{{th}} century. 

==History==
[[Taddock|Bosun Taddock]] ordered the punishment of Valia for forgery. She had attempted to enter the city under false documentation in a hurry to procure an apothecary for her sick son.
