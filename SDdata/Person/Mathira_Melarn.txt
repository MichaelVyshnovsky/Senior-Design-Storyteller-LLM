'''Mathira Melarn''' was an [[Eilistraee]]an [[sword dancer]] from [[Ched Nasad]] who wielded the [[Crescent Blade]].

==History==
Mathira Melarn was a member of the Ched Nasadan [[House Melarn (Ched Nasad)|House Melarn]]. There was a broken bust of Mathira in House Melarn's great hall. At some point, she joined the worshipers of Eilistraee and became a [[sword dancer]]. She wielded the [[Crescent Blade]], a sword said to be created by Eilistraee for the purpose of destroying [[Lolth]].

Mathira was last seen in [[Harrowdale]], making her way to [[Black Feather Bridge]].
