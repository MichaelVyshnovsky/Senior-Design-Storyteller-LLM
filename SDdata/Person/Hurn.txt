'''Hurn''' was a [[dwarf]] knight in [[Phlan]] in [[1489 DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Secrets of Sokol Keep|10}}

==Personality==
Hurn was an old and unfriendly individual, only wanting to reach his retirement day. Hurn was not corrupt but did not refuse a bribe either.

==History==
Hurn was a member of the [[Knights of the Black Fist]]. He was in charge of a guardpost at one entrance to [[Sokol Keep]].{{Cite adventurers league/Breath of the Yellow Rose|9}}
