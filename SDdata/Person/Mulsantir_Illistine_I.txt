'''Mulsantir Illistine''' was the first King of the [[Illistine Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Mulsantir was a mercenary with relations to the noble [[Illistine]] family in [[Chondath]]. In the {{YearlinkName|429}}, he attacked the city of Westgate and slew the pirate king holding power, claiming the throne for himself. He was recognized as King Mulsantir I and reigned until the {{YearlinkName|440}}. 

==Relationships==
His firstborn son and heir was [[Mulsantir Illistine II|Mulsantir II]].
