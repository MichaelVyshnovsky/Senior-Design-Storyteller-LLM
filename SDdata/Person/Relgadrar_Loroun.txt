Lord '''Relgadrar Loroun''' was an noble of the [[Loroun]] family in [[Cormyr]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, [[Manshoon]] tried to enslave his mind and, despite Relgadrar having powerful [[magic ]]in his defense, Manshoon managed at last to enslave him.

==Relationships==
Relgadrar considered [[Melder Crownrood]] to be one of his greatest rivals.
