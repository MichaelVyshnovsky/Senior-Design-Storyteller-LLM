'''Senior Fury Barasta Cleeith''' was a wealthy noblewoman of [[Lurraxol|House Lurraxol]] who lived in [[Athkatla]] during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]] She a high-ranking [[Waukeenar]] [[priest]]ess at [[Goldenspires]] as well as a member of the [[Knights of the Shield]] as of the {{YearlinkName|1370}}.

==Activities==
She invested much effort in trading with [[Maztica]], via her estate in [[New Amn]].

Her other concern was undermining the nomination of her rival [[Daerea Ethgil]], a fellow priestess at Goldspires, for membership in the Knights.

==Relationships==
Barasta was the daughter of the [[Amn]]ian noble Lord Lurraxol and the wife of fellow noble [[Lord Cleeith]].

While she served at Goldpsires, Barasta became the oldest and most trusted confidant of Holycoin [[Tharundar Olhem]], the High Priest of the entire [[Faerûn]]ian church of Waukeen.

==History==
Barasta became a senior priestess of [[Waukeen]] at the abbey of Goldspires outside [[Athkatla]].

Following Maztica's disastrous war with [[sahuagin]], Lady Barasta redoubled her efforts investing in the region.
