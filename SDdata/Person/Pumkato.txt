Pumkato was a svirfneblin of Blingdenstone.
The deep gnome had gray eyes.
Pumkato wielded a pickaxe in combat and had stones which could summon an earth elemental.  
In 1357 DR, Pumkato was among a deep gnome patrol who stumbled upon Catti-brie, who used Agathas mask to disguise herself as a drow female. With the help of Seldig, he stopped the attack after recognizing Guenhwyvar, whom he played with when Drizzt DoUrden lived in Blingdenstone
Later that year, he become a burrow warden.
