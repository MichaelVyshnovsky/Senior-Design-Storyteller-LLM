'''Kansif''' was a [[half-orcish]] [[wizard]]-[[evoker]], a worshiper of [[the Absolute]], and a guide, stationed in the [[Moonrise Towers]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|[[True Soul]]. An honor.|Kansif}}

==Description==
Kansif was a half-orcish man with a strong body and a hide of muddy gray. He had short brown hair and wore a set of [[enchanted]] ''[[thermoarcanic gloves]]''.

==Abilities==
As a mage, Kansif specialized in fire magic, dispensing ''[[firebolt]]s'' and ''[[fireball]]s''. He also wielded the ''[[cloud of daggers]]'' and ''[[cure wounds]]'' [[spell]]s.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Kansif was among those who joined the new religion that sprang in the [[Western Heartlands]]. He was stationed within the Moonrise Towers – the fortress controlled by the Absolute's warlord – [[Ketheric Thorm]]. Kansif was among the loyal [[Cult of the Absolute]]'s guides that helped pilgrims reach the Moonrise Towers amidst the deadly [[shadow-cursed lands]].
