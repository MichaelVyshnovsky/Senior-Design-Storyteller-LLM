Kansif was a half-orcish wizard-evoker, a worshiper of the Absolute, and a guide, stationed in the Moonrise Towers during the late 15 century DR.
Kansif was a half-orcish man with a strong body and a hide of muddy gray. He had short brown hair and wore a set of enchanted thermoarcanic gloves.
As a mage, Kansif specialized in fire magic, dispensing firebolts and fireballs. He also wielded the cloud of daggers and cure wounds spells.
In the , Kansif was among those who joined the new religion that sprang in the Western Heartlands. He was stationed within the Moonrise Towers – the fortress controlled by the Absolutes warlord – Ketheric Thorm. Kansif was among the loyal Cult of the Absolutes guides that helped pilgrims reach the Moonrise Towers amidst the deadly shadow-cursed lands.
