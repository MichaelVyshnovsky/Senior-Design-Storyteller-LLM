'''Ardet Peytir''' was a young socialite in [[Mulmaster]].{{Cite adventurers league/Black Heart of Vengeance|5}} 

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], Ardet was one of the main opponents of the [[Liberators]] led by [[Aleyd Burral]], saying that Mulmaster did not need a war against [[Phlan]] and could use those men in better ways. For this he was kidnapped, and his mansion was destroyed by Aleyd and her brother [[Wylan Burral]], who was secretly a member of the [[Cult of the Black Earth]].{{Cite adventurers league/Black Heart of Vengeance|5}} He was eventually saved by an adventuring party hired by his cousin, [[Blades (Mulmaster)|Blade]] [[Cora Peytir]], when she uncovered the truth and defeated Wylan and his minions.{{Cite adventurers league/Black Heart of Vengeance|24}}
