'''Mharane Featherbone''' was an [[elf|elven]] member of the [[Griffon Cavalry]] in the late mid-to-late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. 

==Activities==
Mharane worked as both an animal trainer and breeder for the Griffon Calvary.

==History==
In [[1379 DR]],{{Cite dungeon/206/Eyes on the Ball|1}} Mharane was one of many Waterdhavians kidnapped and [[slavery|enslaved]] by [[Lanador]].
