'''Glavel''' was a monk at the [[Monastery of the Yellow Rose]].

==Activities==
Glavel was a monk in the Monastery of the Yellow Rose and a member of the Order of the Chisel in the [[Disciples of St. Sollars the Twice-Martyred]]. His duty was to act as a brewer and cook for both the monastery and the Guest House. Secretly, he was also a member of the [[Cult of the Howling Hatred|Cult of Howling Hatred]].

==History==
Glavel was one of the cultists who infiltrated the monastery in [[1491 DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Eye of the Tempest|8}} When the adventurers arrived at the monastery, they confronted Glavel.

==Relationship==
Glavel was the lover of [[Olara]], a fellow cultist.

==Personality==
Glavel at heart was a selfish sociopath. He joined the Howling Hatred only because they encouraged his hobbies. Glavel was a very good liar and would quickly change sides if his life was in danger.
