'''Chulu Thall''' was a merchant of [[Ithmong]].<ref group="note">It is unknown which [[Ithmong (disambiguation)|Ithmong]] Chulu hails from: [[Ithmong (Lapaliiya)|Ithmong]] in [[Lapaliiya]], or Ithmong in  [[Tethyr]], later renamed [[Darromar]].

Chulu somehow acquired the ''[[Book of Bats]]'' and sold it to the [[mage]] [[Khondall Sszundar]].The date of this is unknown, only being sometime between the ''Book of Bats'''s appearance in c. [[1147 DR]] and its last disappearance c. [[1357 DR]].
