Chulu Thall was a merchant of Ithmong.It is unknown which Ithmong Chulu hails from Ithmong in Lapaliiya, or Ithmong in  Tethyr, later renamed Darromar.
Chulu somehow acquired the Book of Bats and sold it to the mage Khondall Sszundar.The date of this is unknown, only being sometime between the Book of Batss appearance in c. 1147 DR and its last disappearance c. 1357 DR.
