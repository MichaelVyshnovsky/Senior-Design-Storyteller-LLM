'''Woarsten Nanther''' was the Lord of [[Nanther|House Nanther]] and one of the seven councilors that ruled [[Melvaunt]] in [[1372 DR]].

==History==
In 1372 DR, Woarsten's son [[Oreal Nanther]] went on adventures with some friends and disappeared. Lord Nanther hired an adventuring party to find him.

==Relationship==
The main enemies of House Nanther were Lord [[Dornig Leiyraghon]] and the Lord of the Keys [[Halmuth Bruil]].
