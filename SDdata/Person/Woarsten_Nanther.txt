Woarsten Nanther was the Lord of House Nanther and one of the seven councilors that ruled Melvaunt in 1372 DR.
In 1372 DR, Woarstens son Oreal Nanther went on adventures with some friends and disappeared. Lord Nanther hired an adventuring party to find him.
The main enemies of House Nanther were Lord Dornig Leiyraghon and the Lord of the Keys Halmuth Bruil.
