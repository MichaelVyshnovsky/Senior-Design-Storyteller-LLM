Bertrand Carrathal was High King of Callidyrr in the latter half of the 11 century DR. Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the Moonshae Isles, he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the Ffolk outside of Callidyrr.
He was a member of the Carrathal Dynasty via his marriage to Yolanda Carrathal. The pair had multiple children, and Bertrands eventual successor was their third son, Edwin.
