'''Tarnhart Campion I''' was the third King of the [[Campion Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Tarnhart ruled from the {{YearlinkName|954}}, until his death in the {{YearlinkName|993}}. His reign would be the longest of the Campion Dynasty. Tarnhart I inherited the throne upon the death of his uncle [[Jhentyr Campion I|Jhentyr I]].

==Relationships==
He had two sons. His second son, [[Jhentyr Campion II|Jhentyr II]] would succeed him on the throne.
