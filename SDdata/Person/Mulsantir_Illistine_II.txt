'''Mulsantir Illistine II''' was the second King of the [[Illistine Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Mulsantir II inherited the throne when his father, King [[Mulsantir Illistine I|Mulsantir I]], died in the {{YearlinkName|440}}. He ruled until the {{YearlinkName|452}}, when he was slain by a [[wizard]] of the [[Shoon Imperium]] called [[Myntharan|Myntharan "the Magus"]] who claimed the throne and ended the [[Illistine]] bloodline in Westgate.
