Wynn Freshwater was a street vendor from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in late 15 century DR.
Wynn was a high elven woman with fair sun-kissed skin, blue eyes, and straight red hair. While working, Wynn wore a simple white blouse and a brown leather corset.
In the , Wynn Freshwater could often be found manning her produce stall in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate, on a moot near The Glitter Gala jewelry shop. Unlike many fellow citizens of the Gate, Wynn thought articles published by the Baldurs Mouth to be nothing but fluff pieces penned to uplift the new Lord Gortash. She did not approve of the new army of steel watchers, thinking them a menace.
