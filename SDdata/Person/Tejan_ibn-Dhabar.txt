'''Tejan ibn-Dhabar''' was a skilled [[sha'ir]] who traveled the [[elemental plane]]s.{{Cite dungeon/60/Nemesis|57}}

==History==
Tejan eventually transformed himself into a [[water elemental]] when visiting the [[Elemental Plane of Water]].

==Possessions==
He carried the wicked ''[[vorpal|vorpal scimitar]]'' ''[[Starzad]]'' for a time. Tejan sent the sword to a friend in [[Sigil]] before his transformation.
