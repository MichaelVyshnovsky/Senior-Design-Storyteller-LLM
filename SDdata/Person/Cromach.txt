Cromach was a successful blacksmith inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He lived in a house in the southeast section of Daggerford located along Water Street near the River Gate. He enjoyed spending coin on lavish accessories to decorate his home and keep it warm in the winter. 
He owned Cromachs Smithy located in central Daggerford on Rogar Alley and the High Road.
