'''Cromach''' was a successful blacksmith inhabiting the city of [[Daggerford]] in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He lived in a house in the southeast section of Daggerford located along [[Water Street]] near the [[River Gate]].{{Cite interactive atlas|3990 Daggerford}} He enjoyed spending coin on lavish accessories to decorate his home and keep it warm in the winter. 

He owned [[Cromach's Smithy]] located in central Daggerford on [[Rogar Alley]] and the [[High Road (Daggerford)|High Road]].
