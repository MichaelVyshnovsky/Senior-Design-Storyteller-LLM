Fyodor was a monosyllabic bugbear entertainer, part of the Circus of the Last Days troupe in the late 15 century DR.
Fyodor was a tall and muscular bugbear with brown hide and red hair. He wore a colorful jesters collar.
In the , the Circus of the Last Days returned from the Feywild and opened its gates to the inhabitants of the Rivington district in Baldurs Gate. Fyodor was present at the circus when it was revealed that the Cult of the Absolute had murdered one of the troupe, the clown called Dribbles, and replaced him with a murderous doppelganger.
