'''Peuter Marsk''' was the master of [[House Marsk]] and the elected Despot of [[Melvaunt]] in [[1374 DR]].

==History==
Peuter sponsored many of the city's improvement projects, including the troubled sewer project in order to gain the population's appreciation.

==History==
Peuter's son [[Elaint Marsk]] went on adventures with some friends and disappeared. Lord [[Woarsten Nanther]] hired an adventuring party to find the noble scions.

==Personality==
Peuter was worried by the relationship between Elaint and [[Kara Calaudra]].
