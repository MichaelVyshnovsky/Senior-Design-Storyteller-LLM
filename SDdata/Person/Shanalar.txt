'''Shanalar''' was an [[human]] female inhabitant of [[Zhentil Keep]] in [[1358 DR]] who was secretly opposed to the [[Zhentarim]].

==Personality==
Shanalar had a fierce temper.

==Activities==
In addition to researching new [[spell]]s, Shanalar enjoyed riding [[griffon]]s with a degree of skill.
