'''Quavylene Oblodra''' was the First Priestess of [[House Oblodra]] and daughter of [[Matron Mother]] [[K'yorl Odran]] in [[Menzoberranzan]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Quavylene was the target of her mother's psychic tortures because she could not measure up to K'yorl's extreme expectations. Years of abuse transformed Quavylene into a broken shell who trembled at her mother's glance.

==Powers==
Like all in her house, Quavylene had good [[psionics|psychic powers]].

==History==
Quavylene once conspired to kill Matron Mother K'yorl but thanks to K'yorl's ability to read minds, she was discovered and punished terribly.

==Relationships==
In the {{YearlinkName|1118}}, she shared a bed with [[Arathis Hune]] of [[Bregan D'aerthe]].
