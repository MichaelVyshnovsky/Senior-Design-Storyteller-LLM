'''Maheer yn Kadar el Saddith''' was a traveling merchant and master instrument maker who traveled the Realms in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Maheer was a portly fellow with bronze skin.

==Personality==
As befitting his career as a merchant, Maheer was a particularly friendly individual.

==Abilities==
While he had to proficiency with weapon smithing, Maheer was skilled in the art of crafting musical instruments, particularly [[horn|musical horns]]. He was even known to have been able to work with and further enhance ''[[Horn of Valhalla|horns of Valhalla]]''.

==Activities==
Traveling across the lands, Maheer primarily dealt in mundane and [[Item enchantment|enchanted]] weapons. He specifically had numerous weapons made from [[iron]] found in the [[Storm Horns]] of [[Cormyr]].

==Relationships==
He was acquainted with the expert [[Athkatla]]n blacksmith [[Cromwell]].

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, Maheer had established himself at the open marketplace of [[Waukeen's Promenade]] in Athkatla. Previously, he was selling his wares and abilities in the far-eastern nation of [[Rashemen]].
