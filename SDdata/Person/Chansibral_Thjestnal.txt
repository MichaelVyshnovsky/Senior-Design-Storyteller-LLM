High Priest Gondsman '''Chansibral Thjestnal''' was ranking priest of the  [[High House of the Wonderful Wheel]], a temple dedicated to [[Gond]], in [[Ordulin]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Activities==
Chansibral was the leader of Gond's faithful in Ordulin. His congregation within the temple consisted of 22 other priests and 38 followers.
