Kiirma Blackmane was the cloakmaster of Riatavin for the Shadow Thieves. She was also a senior agent of the Moonstars (TelTeukiira) in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.
Kiirma posed as an ordinary wealthy widow and businesswoman.
She was an accomplished thief and a highly trained wizard. She was a former apprentice of Khelben Arunsun.
Kiirma Blackmane owned several taverns, boarding houses, and festhalls in the town of Riatavin which gave her income as well as being a front for more shady activities. She kept her spellcasting abilities secret by being very discreet and using her ring of translocation which allowed her to choose the point of origin for any spells she cast, up to 30 ft (9.1 m) away from her.
Kiirma was the cloakmaster of Riatavin in the Shadow Thieves, an organized crime syndicate which in 1370 DR had a monopoly on the criminal activity in the town. Unbeknownst to them, she was also a senior agent for the TelTeukiira.
As a former apprentice of Khelben Arunsun, she was loyal to him and his organization, passing necessary information about the Shadow Thieves to the TelTeukiira. However, she did not reciprocate she never relayed news of the Moonstars to the Shade Council.
Kiirma commanded the loyalty of all her guildmasters except for Rekx Tihler.
Kiirma was a former apprentice of Khelben Arunsun, and his choice to make her a senior agent of the TelTeukiira was questioned by the other senior agents because she was a high-ranking member of the Shadow Thieves.
