Prince SarkonIss was a drider that dwelled in the drow city of beneath Rothé Valley during the late 15 century DR.
When personally engaged in battle, the prince used his natural ability to spin webs to immobilize his foes, while assailing them with waves of dark energy.
SarkonIss undertook a drow led, slave-capturing expedition that targeted villages and farmers in Rothé Valley during the . The drow forces enjoyed success for some time, until their operation was put to an end by an adventurer from the surface city of Neverwinter. The prince was slain that year at the hands of that same adventurer.
