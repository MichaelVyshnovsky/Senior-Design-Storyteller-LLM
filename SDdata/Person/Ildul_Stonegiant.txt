'''Ildul Stonegiant''' was an inhabitant of [[Dhedluk]] in [[Cormyr]] around [[1367 DR]].

==Activities==
Ildul served the town's ruler, [[Thiombar]], as herald. He worked out of the [[Blushing Maiden Inn]].
