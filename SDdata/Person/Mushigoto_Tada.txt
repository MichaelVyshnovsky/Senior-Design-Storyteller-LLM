Mushigoto Tada was a samurai of Fochu Peninsula in Wa. 
Mushigoto Tada was the shoya of the village of Sokijan in Fochu Peninsula. 
In 1358 DR, Tada was worried about a bajang living near the village. So when Yusuke Ting went back to the village with Osari Minhiro and a foreign adventuring party, he asked the adventurers to kill the bajang.
