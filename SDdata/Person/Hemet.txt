'''Hemet''' was a [[dwarf]] who participated in a street brawl in the year {{yearlink|1396}}, within [[New Sarshell]]. He and a number of other street toughs picked a fight with the [[half-elf]] [[monk]] [[Raidon Kane]], from which the latter fled.
