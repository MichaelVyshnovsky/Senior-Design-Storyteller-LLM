Dorhum was a dwarf fighter that volunteered to join the to-be Heroes of Waterdeep circa 1358 DR.
The fighter donned chain mail and a dwarven helmet. He fought with an axe, and brought rations and a potion of healing with him.
The young dwarf first resided with his trouble-stricken clan. When a group of adventurers ventured down to slay Xanathar, Dorhum volunteered to join them, and search out his clans captive prince, Keirgar.
