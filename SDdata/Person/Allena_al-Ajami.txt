Allena al-Ajami was a foreign ajami and the Chief Vizier to the Caliph of the Free City of Utaqa in north Zakhara in .
Given that she was a "foreign barbarian" herself, Allena usually dealt with situations in Utaqa related to foreigners. Most importantly, she was responsible for paying Utaqas massive mercenary barbarian force.
Allena fled to Zakhara to put some distance between herself and powerful enemies.
