Minghee Graywind was a human who worked at the Lionshield Coster trading post in Phandalin during the late 15 century DR. She was the daughter of the stores owner, Linene Graywind.
Minghee was an outgoing, excitable, and restless young woman who dreamt of traveling and of learning to use magic. Above all, she wished to make a name for herself and to see the world before she settled down.
She had a knack for business and was good with math.
Although she worked at the Lionshield Coster for her mother, Minghee did not want to. She was quick to share that she wanted to do something different with her life.
As of the , Minghee worked at her mothers store reluctantly, and was acutely aware that her mother expected her to take over the family business within a few years. Hoping to travel and to find something more exciting to do with her life, Minghee was quick to seek out other opportunities that would afford her the chance to leave Phandalin.
