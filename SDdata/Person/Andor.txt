'''Andor''' was a member of the criminal underworld of [[Scornubel]]. He was replaced by a shapeshifter in {{yearlink|1357}}.

==Relationships==
Either Andor or a shapeshifter taking his identity were people [[Tornar]] wanted to see dead in late {{yearlink|1357}}.

==History==
Andor's body was found tangled up in the nets of [[Ornrim]] in mid-to-late 1357 [[DR]], a fact known to few other than [[Belgon Bradasker]]. The latter related unto [[Tornar]] that Andor had been replaced by a shapeshifter.
