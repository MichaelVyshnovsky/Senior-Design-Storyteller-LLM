'''Aerendyl Tassarion''' was an inhabitant of [[Cormanthyr]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Aerendyl took part in the hunt for [[Elminster]] in {{yearlink|241}}, where he was killed. His father, Lord Tassarion, named him as a reason for his conspiracy against [[Coronal]] [[Eltargrim]].
