'''Daven Jr''' was a child born in [[Baldur's Gate]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
The babe was born to a mother [[Flora]], and [[Daven]], after whom he was named.

==History==
Daven Junior was born in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}. Unfortunately, Daven Senior never heard news of his son's birth, as the courier bearing a letter from his mother was slain along the [[Risen Road]] shortly afterwards.
