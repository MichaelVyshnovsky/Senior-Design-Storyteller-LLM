Thabbys was a necromancer and one of seven High Hands in the Risen Cult of Bane in the mid-14 century DR.Dragon #362 provides an exact date for the events of Halls of the High King, which did not give an exact date within itself.
Thabbys took great delight in researching all manner of necromantic magic.
Thabbys was to possess a wide variety of necromantic powers.The book states that a player could use the accessory Lords of Darkness (1998 to detail the many powers and subservient creatures she possessed.
Thabbys was known to be served by a number of bonebats, which she used largely as messengers.
Unlike some members, Thabbyss dedication to the Risen Cult of Bane was rooted solely in their ability to provide her the necessary material components and opportunities to further her goals of becoming more adept in necromancy.
