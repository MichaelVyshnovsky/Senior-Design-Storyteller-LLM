Lord '''Segar Eeyescee''' was the leader of the [[Nubaedfyd]] family of [[Murann]] in [[Amn]] ''circa'' [[1370 DR]].

A former seafarer and ship's captain, he was gruff-natured and hard-bitten, complete with a peg leg.
