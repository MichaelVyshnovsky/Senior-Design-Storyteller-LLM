Opara Rendril was a human woman of the city of Procampur in the Vast.
Around 1358 DR, Rendril was a well-known Procampan master metal-smith who crafted high-quality longswords and helms, while her bracelets and belt-daggers were famous around the Inner Sea. By 1368 DR, Rendril was the owner of Rendrils Forge, and also an active Harper agent, though she remained based in Procampur.
