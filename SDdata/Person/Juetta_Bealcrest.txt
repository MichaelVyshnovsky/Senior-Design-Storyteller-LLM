Juetta Baelcrest was a brewmistress and the proprietor of the Old Dirty Dwarf inn and tavern in the city of Helms Hold during the late 15 century DR.
Juetta directed the Old Dirty Dwarf to cater to newcomers to Helms Hold. While she welcomed spellscarred individuals into her establishment, some of her staff went against her wishes and discouraged their patronage.
In addition to running the inn and tavern, Juetta regularly tended the taproom bar herself.
Ever the intrepid entrepreneur, Juetta managed to keep the inn open and running during the Siege of Helms Hold in the .
She stepped down as innkeeper  of the Old Dirty Dwarf some time during or before the , when it was taken over by Kharissa Anuvien and renamed the Venturers Rest.
