'''Baerzel''' was one of the three war-leaders of the tribe of [[orc]]s known as the [[Tornskulls]] that dwelled within [[Dead Orc Pass]]. Despite her leadership role, she had no want to rule over the tribe nor manipulate or guide any future chiefs.

==Personality==
War-leader Baerzel was known for her wisdom and cunning intelligence. She was quite adept at formulating strategies for battle that proved ensure her tribe's continued success.

She was ardent about strengthening her tribe, and sought new endeavors for their betterment.

==Relationships==
Baerzel was the mate of the tribe's previous leader, [[Glorlarkh|Chief Glorlarkh]], before his untimely death. 

She often played the role of mediator when working with her fellow war-leaders, [[Koront]] and [[Naurorin]]. 

Baerzel favored [[Anya (Tornskulls orc)|Anya]], the chief scout serving under [[Vaug the Strong]].
