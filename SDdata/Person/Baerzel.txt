Baerzel was one of the three war-leaders of the tribe of orcs known as the Tornskulls that dwelled within Dead Orc Pass. Despite her leadership role, she had no want to rule over the tribe nor manipulate or guide any future chiefs.
War-leader Baerzel was known for her wisdom and cunning intelligence. She was quite adept at formulating strategies for battle that proved ensure her tribes continued success.
She was ardent about strengthening her tribe, and sought new endeavors for their betterment.
Baerzel was the mate of the tribes previous leader, Chief Glorlarkh, before his untimely death. 
She often played the role of mediator when working with her fellow war-leaders, Koront and Naurorin. 
Baerzel favored Anya, the chief scout serving under Vaug the Strong.
