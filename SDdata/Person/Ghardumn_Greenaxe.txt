Ghardumn Greenaxe was a swordsman from Cormyr.
Ghardumn was the captain of the adventuring company known as the Dragonclaw Clan.
The warrior wore a mighty five-headed hydra helm.
By Eleint, 1281 DR, Ghardumn was last seen entering the Marsh of Tun in Cormyr and had not returned. His hydra helm was left on display in the Temple of Tempus in Easthaven.
