Rondrand was a highknight of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.
During his last raid, he was in the same group as Shuldroon and Athlar.
Rondrand was one of the highknights that took part in the raid on Elminster Aumars refuge at Tethgard in the Hullack Forest after the old sage had stolen the Royal Gorget of Battle, on Mirtul 5 of the . He was not named among the known survivors afterwards.
