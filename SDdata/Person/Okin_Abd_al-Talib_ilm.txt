Okin Abd al-Talibilm was the general and commander of the Studious Mamluk Society in Qudra in 1367 DR.
Okins strengths catered to research and tactics for the Studious.
Okin was crippled in battle in his past. Though fully healed, he since lost his taste for warfare and focused on other aspects.
