'''Shastin''' was a [[Netherese]] [[priestess]] of [[Mystryl]].

==Description==
The [[neutral good]] female [[human]] was a powerful priestess of the Lady of Mysteries.

==Relationships==
Shastin was considered a formidable foe. Even the ruthless [[arcanist]], [[Jarm]], who was her nemesis and was known for his incredible success at [[spell]] duels, did not have the courage to assassinate her.
