'''Tibold Hillmover''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] [[cleric]].

==Description==
He had tan skin, a brown beard with no mustache, and wore a metal helmet.

==Personality==
Like his peers, Tibold enjoyed combat, but he also understood the value of religion. He was a ferocious combatant, but otherwise calm and soft-spoken.

==Abilities==
Tibold was skilled in the healing arts.

==Possessions==
He owned a suit of [[plate mail]] and a large [[shield]], as well as several [[magic]]al items, including a ''[[staff of curing]]'', a ''[[rod of smiting]]'', a ''[[girdle of dwarvenkind]]'', and a ''hammer +3''.

==Relationships==
Tibold was a cousin of [[Worden Ironfist]], the clan hero.

==Activites==
Together with [[Udall Granitecrusher]] and [[Vasos Flameslayer]], Tibold was part of an adventuring band led by Worden Ironfist. On one mission, they sought to eliminate a tribe of [[giant]]s who encroached on their lands.{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1991|607|Worden Ironfist}}
