Bugthimble was a grave robbing kobold in the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Bugthimble was a kobold with pale orange scales and makeshift armor made of leather, horns, and fangs.
Bugthimble wielded the enchanted scimitar called Kurwins Cauteriser.
In the , Bugthimble led a small gang of kobold gave robbers to dig around the graves and coffins of the Lower City Graveyard of Baldurs Gate, including Antunio Bongles final resting place. Bugthimble operated outside of the other criminal organizations of the Gate and was intimidated by the Guild and its notorious leader, Nine-Fingers Keene.
