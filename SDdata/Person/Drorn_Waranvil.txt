'''Drorn Waranvil''' was a [[dwarf]] that had served in both the [[Iron Guard]] of [[Citadel Adbar]] and the [[Citadel Guards]] of [[Citadel Felbarr]].

==Possessions==
In battle, Drorn wielded a [[warhammer]].

==History==
Drorn fought in the orc wars for over two centuries, where he lost a foot to an [[orc]]. He also helped [[Emerus]] in liberating Citadel Felbarr twice, and aided in retaking [[Gauntlgrym]] before retiring, and chronicling his experiences for the benefit of younger dwarves.
