Velkora Ashenwell was a dwarf mason and acolyte of Moradin in Elturel in the late 15 century DR.
After Elturel was transported to Avernus, first layer of the Nine Hells, in the , many quakes struck the city and three dwarves—Velkora Ashenwell, Kartra Boulderstern, and Strovin Ironfist—became trapped in the rubble of a collapsed building. If they were lucky, they may have been rescued by passing adventurers.
