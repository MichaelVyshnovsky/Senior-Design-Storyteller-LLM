Wenta was a daughter of a brewer who was abducted and enslaved by the Cult of the Dragon in 1369 DR.
Wenta was a friendly large young woman.
In 1369 DR, Wenta was one of the unfortunate townies captured by the Cult of the Dragons slavers. Alongside Tren, Jorsh, and Ristim, the villagers were taken to the Cults slave pens in the ruined Darnarests tower.
