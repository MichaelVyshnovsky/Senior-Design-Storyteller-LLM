'''Wenta''' was a daughter of a brewer who was abducted and enslaved by the [[Cult of the Dragon]] in [[1369 DR]].

==Description==
Wenta was a friendly large young woman.

==History==
In 1369 DR, Wenta was one of the unfortunate townies captured by the Cult of the Dragon's slavers. Alongside [[Tren (person)|Tren]], [[Jorsh]], and [[Ristim]], the villagers were taken to the Cult's slave pens in the ruined [[Darnarest's tower]].
