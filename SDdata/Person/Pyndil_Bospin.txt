'''Pyndil Bospin''' was an [[elf]] [[sage]] who specialized in ''[[portal]]s'' and the [[planes]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}
{{quote
|The planar fabric in the old city has been weakened by frequent magic use. When the Mythal began to act erratically after the city's fall, portals to other places started to appear and disappear unpredictably.
|Pyndil}}
==Description==
Pyndil had long silvery hair and brown eyes. He wore an ornate green set of robes.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, Pyndil was a senior member of the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|forward camp]] outside of [[Myth Drannor]]. He provided expertise on the subject of the planar instability caused by the ''mythal'', which caused a number of unusual hazards and threats inside the ruins.
