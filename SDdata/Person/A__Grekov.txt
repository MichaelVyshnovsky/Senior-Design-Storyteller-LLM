'''A. Grekov''' was a [[Raumviran]] innkeeper who lived in [[Almorel]] on the [[Lake of Mists]] in the [[Hordelands]] in the {{YearlinkName|1357}}.

==Activities==
Grekov owned and operated the [[Inn of the Crooked Staff]]. His [[inn]] was located near the center of town next to the [[Raumkremyl]].
