Jamala Abd al-Raffa was a mamluk inhabiting the League of the Pantheon in Zakhara in 1367 DR.
Jamala was the leader of the Exalted Mamluk Society and named Most Respected Warrior. She was a moralist who shared the views of the Pantheon that all of Zakhara should be more civilized and law-abiding.
