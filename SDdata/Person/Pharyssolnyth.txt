'''Pharyssolnyth''' was a female [[wyrm]] [[silver dragon]] of the 16th century before [[Dalereckoning]]. She was the author of the [[Tablets of Pharyssolnyth]].

==History==
Pharyssolnyth dwelled in the elven city of [[Yrlaphon]], among the eastern woodlands of [[Cormanthor]], in its first century after its founding in {{Yearlink|-1535}} (prior to {{Yearlink|-1435}}).

There she wrote the Tablets of Pharyssolnyth, which detailed how [[dragon]]s learned and handled [[magic]].

Pharyssolnyth was deceased well before [[1373 DR]].
