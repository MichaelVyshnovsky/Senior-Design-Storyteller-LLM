'''Dalmar''' was a [[human]] abbot and [[cleric]] of [[Chauntea]] who led the [[monk]]s of the [[Green Lady's Keep]]<ref group="note">The 3rd edition sourcebook, [[Unapproachable East (sourcebook)|Unapproachable East]] states the "Green Lady's Keep" was the name of the abbey where monks made famous ale on page 111, but in [[Spellbound]], the 2nd edition boxed set, the name of the temple is not given., a temple to Chauntea in [[Veltalar|Velprintalar]], [[Aglarond]], around [[1368 DR]].

==Personality==
The abbot enjoyed making and drinking the strong ale brewed in the abbey.
