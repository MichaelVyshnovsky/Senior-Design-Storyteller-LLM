Dalmar was a human abbot and cleric of Chauntea who led the monks of the Green Ladys KeepThe 3rd edition sourcebook, Unapproachable East states the "Green Ladys Keep" was the name of the abbey where monks made famous ale on page 111, but in Spellbound, the 2nd edition boxed set, the name of the temple is not given., a temple to Chauntea in Velprintalar, Aglarond, around 1368 DR.
The abbot enjoyed making and drinking the strong ale brewed in the abbey.
