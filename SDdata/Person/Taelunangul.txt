Taelunangul was the chieftain and matriarch of the Batiri Shattered Spear tribe in Chult circa 1374 DR.

In the , Taelunanguls tribe came across the shipwreck of the Vigilant, a vessel that belonged to SaSanis trade company. The Batiri attacked the survivors, including the infamous author Volothamp Geddarm. Some of the survivors ended up captured by Taelunanguls goblins as well as some of the ships precious cargo. Cannibalistic Batiri consumed several survivors, with only Vigilants Second Mate surviving long enough to be rescued by a group of mercenaries hired by SaSani. Chieftain Taelunangul was killed in the process, unwilling to negotiate.
