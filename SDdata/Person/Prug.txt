'''Prug''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and [[spell]] inventor who lived during the [[Golden Age]].

==History==
Prug developed six spells during their life. The spells they invented were ''[[domination]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1985|NY}}, ''[[hold monster]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|1995|NY}}, ''[[repulsion]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2044|NY}}, ''[[hold person]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2054|NY}}, ''[[charm plant]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2114|NY}}, and ''[[hold undead]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2188|NY}}.
