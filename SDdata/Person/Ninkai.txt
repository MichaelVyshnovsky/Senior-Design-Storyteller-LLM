'''Ninkai''' was a head priest in [[Kozakura]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Ninkai administrated the [[Temple of Purification]], one of the major shrines of the [[Kanchan]] school.

==Personality==
Ninkai was a serious individual and a very brilliant orator, giving very inspired sermons.
