Villynaty was a sahuagin prince in the Sea of Swords.
The rebel prince owned the blade of an enchanted halberd, the Wave.
Villynaty lived in the City of Caverns. At one point, he started a civil war in order to dethrone King Ixilthetocal. Eventually Gorions Ward arrived in the City of Caverns and Villynaty met them, asking them to kill Ixilthetocal.
