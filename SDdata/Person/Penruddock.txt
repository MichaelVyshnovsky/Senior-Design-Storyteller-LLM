'''Penruddock''' was a [[Netherese]] [[priest]] who was a librarian at the [[Library of Netheril]] in the [[enclave]] of [[Eileanar]] until its destruction in {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}.

==Activities==
Penruddock worked alongside [[Vincent (Netherese)|Vincent]] and [[Linden]] to catalogue the texts of the library, and help visitors find them on demand.
