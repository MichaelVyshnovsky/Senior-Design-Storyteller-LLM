'''Noprum''' was a [[duergar]] merchant in [[Gracklstugh]] in [[1484 DR]].

==Activities==
Noprum traded weapons between Gracklstugh and [[Blingdenstone]].
  
==History==
In 1484, traveling to Blingdenstone just after the start of the [[War of the Silver Marches]], a group of [[drow]] attacked and slaughtered Noprum's party without reason. Only Noprum managed to escape and arrived near the [[deep gnome]] city.
