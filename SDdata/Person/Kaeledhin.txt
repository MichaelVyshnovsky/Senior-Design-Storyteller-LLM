'''Kaeledhin''' was the recipient of one of [[Ithraides]]'s ''[[telkiira]]''. It was taken by [[Sarya Dlardrageth]] who, over five thousand years later, concealed it on the body of [[Philaerin]] after he was killed, and was subsequently taken up by [[Araevin Teshurr]].
