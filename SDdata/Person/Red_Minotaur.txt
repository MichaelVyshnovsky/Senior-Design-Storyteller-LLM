The Red Minotaur was a minotaur gladiator who fought in the Arena of Hillsfar.
The minotaur was said to be a tough opponent, and attacked in a repeating pattern. He would attack to the left, then ram his enemies, then follow up with an attack to the right. The minotaur kept his name a secret from everyone.
The Red Minotaur wielded an enchanted greataxe - the Red Ruin.
The Red Minotaur fought in the arena circa 1358 DR.
