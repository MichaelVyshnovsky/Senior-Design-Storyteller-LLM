Jazzt Dyrr was a member of House Agrach Dyrr of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR.
Jazzt was small and scarred.
Jazzt was the second cousin of Matron Mother Yasraena Dyrr. During the Silence of Lolth, when the duergar and tanarukk armies marched against Menzoberranzan, he was ordered by his matron mother to escort the disguised Nimor Imphraezl to Rhazzts Dilemma.
