Shams al-Ezai was a half-elven sorcerer inhabiting Qudra in north Zakhara in .
Shams worked as a qadi in the slave market. He ensured that the slaves were treated properly and that no free men or women were there against their will. He was assisted by a pair of hakimas whose ability to read the truth in others was put to use.
He also served the Studious Mamluk Society by providing magical assistance when needed.
