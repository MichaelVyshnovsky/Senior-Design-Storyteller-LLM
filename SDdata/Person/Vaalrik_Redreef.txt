'''Vaalrik Redreef''' was a sea captain in [[Luskan]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==Activities==
Vaalrik a captain in the employ of the [[High Captains of Luskan]].

==History==
In the late 1480s or early 1490s [[DR]], Vaalrik was hired to accompany an adventuring party to [[Svardborg]].
