Amori DeSpri was a resurrected noble in Undermountain in 1479 DR.
In her past, Amori was a noble in Waterdeep. She was buried in Undermountain after her death, together with her husband Julain DeSpri.
In 1479 DR, the vampire Dayan using a strange resurrection machine he had found resurrected Amori. However, because she returned to life thanks to the life forces of kobolds and rage drakes, Amoris mind was temporarily addled and she often displayed kobold and rage drake mannerisms. Amori disliked her new life and wanted to return to her death with her Julain.
