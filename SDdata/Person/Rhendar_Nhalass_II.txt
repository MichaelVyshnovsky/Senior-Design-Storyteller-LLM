'''Rhendar Nhalass II''' was the twelfth [[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Nhalass Dynasty]] to rule the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Rhendar II inherited the throne when his older brother, [[Umathar Nhalass VI|Umathar VI]], died. His reign was short indeed as he was slain by [[demon]]s of [[Narfell]] during his first year as Arkhan in {{Yearlink|-605}}.

==Relationships==
Rhendar II was the third son of [[Darthen Nhalass III]]. His eldest brother died with their father while invading Narfell. His next oldest brother was Umathar VI, and his younger brothers were [[Tharren Nhalass|Tharren]] and [[Sharath Nhalass|Sharath]].
