Hanamoto Genji was an evil shugenja in Kozakura in 1357 DR.
Hanamoto managed the Red Fog cult, a heretical branch of the Konjo school based in Daidake on Tenmei island. He easily manipulated the shugodai Suzuki Motosune, a Red Fog convert.
