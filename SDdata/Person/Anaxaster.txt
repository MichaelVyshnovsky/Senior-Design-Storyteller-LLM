Anaxaster was a blue dragon who lived in the ruined city of Ascore during the 15 century DR.
Anaxaster made its lair within one of the wrecked, dwarven stone ships that dotted the landscape.
Anaxaster was born to the ancient blue dragon Iymrith.
Anaxaster had a playful relationship with its sibling Chezzaran. They spent their time playfully fighting in the skies above their mothers domain.
