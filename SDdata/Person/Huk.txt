'''Huk''' was a [[goblin]] warrior that served [[Dror Ragzlin]] and the [[Cult of the Absolute]] in the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
He followed Dror Ragzlin's every word and did not like others interrupting the boss' speeches.

==History==
Huk was present when the boss Dror Ragzlin attempted to [[Speak with dead|speak with]] a dead [[mind flayer]] brought into their sanctuary in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}. The mighty [[hobgoblin]] leader was attempting to extract information crucial to [[the Absolute]] and her plans for the [[Sword Coast]].
