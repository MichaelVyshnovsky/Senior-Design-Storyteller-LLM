'''Harandil Durlaven I''' was the first King of the [[Durlarven Dynasty]] to rule over [[Impiltur]].

==Activities==
Harandil defeated a massive horde of [[orc]]s from [[Vastar]] in the {{YearlinkName|512}}, at the [[Battle of Bloody Reeds]], earning him a favorable reputation that would eventually lead to his rise to the title of king by acclamation. He reigned until his death in the {{YearlinkName|537}}.

==Relationships==
He married the daughter of King [[Sharaun Mirandor]], Princess [[Tayissa Mirandor|Tayissa]]. Harandil's son and heir was [[Harandil Durlarven II|Harandil II]].
