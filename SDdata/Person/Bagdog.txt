'''Bagdog''' was a Fist of [[Bane]] who served the [[Cult of the Absolute]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Bagdog was a dark muddy-green-skinned [[half-orc]]ish man with long brown hair. He wore a set of cultist [[chain mail]]s adorned with Bane's black hand and was armed with a simple [[shield]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, the [[Cult of Bane]] joined forces with the [[Cult of the Absolute]], led by the [[Chosen]] of Bane, Lord [[Enver Gortash]]. Fist of Bane Bagdog was placed in the [[Steel Watch Foundry]], guarding its [[Gondian]] slaves in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].
