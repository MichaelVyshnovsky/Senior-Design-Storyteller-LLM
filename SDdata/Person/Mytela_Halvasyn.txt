Lady Mytela Halvasyn was a half-elf noble with considerable influence in the city of Velprintalar. Her house, a palace-like structure, Mytela Halvasyns house, later became the base of operations for her descendants, who operated the Society of the Verdant Arrow, an anti-human organization.
