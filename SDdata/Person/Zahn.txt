'''Zahn''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and prolific [[spell]] inventor who lived during the [[Age of Discovery]].

==History==
The mentalist developed seven spells, one per year. Many of their spells revolved around the senses. The spells they invented were ''[[clairaudience]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2931|NY}}, ''[[mirage arcana]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2932|NY}}, ''[[clairvoyance]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2933|NY}}, ''[[find familiar]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2934|NY}}, ''[[ESP]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2935|NY}}, ''[[locate object]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2936|NY}}, and finally, ''[[vision]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2937|NY}}.
