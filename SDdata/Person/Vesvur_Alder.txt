Vesvur Alder was an agent of the Harpers who operated out of Mulmaster. 
The bard frequently reported his findings within the city to Chesslyn Onaubra
Alders wife was Dela, the owner of the Travelers Cloak Inn which they ran together. She had no knowledge of his second life as one of "Those Who Harp".
