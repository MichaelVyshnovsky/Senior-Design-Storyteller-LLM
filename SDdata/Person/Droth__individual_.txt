Droth was a mercenary formerly in service to the Cult of the Howling Hatred at Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.
In 1491 DR, Droth was hired by the Howling Hatred. However, during a skirmish with the Cult of the Black Earth, he was injured and captured. He remained catatonic in the Temple of the Black Earths jail until some adventurers defeated the cultist jailers.
