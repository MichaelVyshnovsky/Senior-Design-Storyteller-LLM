Ensul '''Thruin''' was a [[Netherese]] scholar during the height of the ancient empire of [[Netheril]].

==Personality==
He was a quiet man.

==History==
Thruin studied the relationship between the [[magic]] of the [[Weave]] and the natural world. His tower was destroyed by a jealous rival.
