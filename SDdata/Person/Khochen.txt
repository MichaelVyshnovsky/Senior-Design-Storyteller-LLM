'''Khochen''' was a young [[Tuigan]] thief and [[Harper]] agent.

==Activities==
Khochen posed as an itinerant merchant.{{Cite dragon/430/The Harpers of Waterdeep}}

==History==
In [[1486 DR]], Khochen and her fellow [[Vescaras Ammakyl|Vescaras]] were on a mission to Westgate.

Afterward, they went to [[Waterdeep]] to make a report to [[Dahl Peredur]] and [[Tam Zawad]].

Later, she and Dahl talked about their personal problems.

She and Vescaras were chosen to help, and surveil, [[Mehen]] in his research into the disappearance [[Farideh]], whom Vescaras believed to be a [[shadovar]] agent.

==Personality==
Khochen was a very manipulative individual. She befriended all people but manipulated their friendship for the mission's goal.{{Cite dragon/430/The Harpers of Waterdeep}}
