'''Emthrara Undril''' was a female [[human]] [[Harper]] who worked as a dancer at [[The Laughing Lass]] tavern in [[Suzail]] to maintain her cover.

==Appearance==
Emthrara was beautiful with long blonde hair.

==Background==
Emthrara once defeated and took apart a [[clockwork horror]], so she was called by [[Laspeera Inthré]] to help analyze the remains of the [[abraxus]] during the [[abraxus affair]] in [[1369 DR]].

==Possessions==
Emthrara wore a silver locket around her neck that contained a tiny silver harp, the sign of a Harper.

==Personality==
Emthrara was proud of the fact that she had met [[Elminster]] and he paid her a compliment about her dancing.
