Dhairn was a drow judicator of Selvetarm.
Dhairn was part of a group of drow exiled from Eryndlyn for worshiping Selvetarm as a deity on its own instead of a servant of Lolth.
In 1375 DR, Dhairn went to recruit a large group of driders living in the Underdark under the Misty Forest for membership in the Selvetargtlin.
