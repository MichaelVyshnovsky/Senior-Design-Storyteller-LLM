'''Fydor''' was a [[fire giant]] and a lieutenant of Queen [[Hledh Hellspawn]], who ruled the city of [[Maerimydra]] in the [[Deep Wastes]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/The Occupation of Szith Morcane|20}}

==History==
Fydor was one of the commanders of the fire giant army in the attack on [[Szith Morcane]] ''circa''. He managed to capture [[Solom Ned'razak]], the [[drow]] archmage leader of the settlement, but some time after he was confronted and defeated by an adventuring party hired by [[Elanil Elassidil]].
