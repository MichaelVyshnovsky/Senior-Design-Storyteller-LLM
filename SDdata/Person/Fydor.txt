Fydor was a fire giant and a lieutenant of Queen Hledh Hellspawn, who ruled the city of Maerimydra in the Deep Wastes in the late 15 century DR.
Fydor was one of the commanders of the fire giant army in the attack on Szith Morcane circa. He managed to capture Solom Nedrazak, the drow archmage leader of the settlement, but some time after he was confronted and defeated by an adventuring party hired by Elanil Elassidil.
