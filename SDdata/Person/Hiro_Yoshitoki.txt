Hiro Yoshitoki was a samurai retainer of the Niwa clan and shugodai of Okahira in Kozakura in 1357 DR.
Yoshitoki lived in Okahira, where he had his office. He formally led 123 samurai and 1,038 bushi. Yoshitoki spent most of his time at the bakufu capital.
Yoshitoki was a drab and colorless individual.
