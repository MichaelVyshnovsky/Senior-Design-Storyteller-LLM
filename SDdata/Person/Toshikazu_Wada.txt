'''Toshikazu Wada''' was a [[samurai]] commander in the town of [[Aru]] in [[Wa]].

==Personality==
Toshikazu was a easy-going individual, always friendly and outgoing. However he knew when times need he to be serious. He had a very good dealing with his [[daimyo]].

==Activities==
Toshizaku was the commander of the [[Home Guard]] of Aru and the direct subordinate of [[Yuji Takaesu]].
Often he went to hunt with his daimyo [[Benju Matsutomo]].

==History==
Around [[1358 DR]], his daimyo Benju hired an adventuring party in order to unveil a menace against pilgrims. During a courtesy meeting with the adventurers Toshizaku was targeted by a [[yakuza]] assassin.
