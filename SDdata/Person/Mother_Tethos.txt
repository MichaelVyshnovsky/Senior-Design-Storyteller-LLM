'''Mother Tethos''' was a doctor and midwife who lived in the village of [[Eveningstar]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Mother Tethos owned a shop specializing in herbs, cordials, and gentle cures. Her shop was located in the center of Eveningstar.
