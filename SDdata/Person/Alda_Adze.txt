Alda Adze was a dwarven tavernkeeper who co-operated the Ball and Chain Tavern on the docks of the Wheloon Prison in the late 15 century DR.
Alda had dark brown hair and wore a simple white and gray dress while working.
Alda joined her husband Trosken Adze in operating the Ball and Chain after the opening of the prison, which served as the Wheloon docks sole spot of relaxation. She was not pleased with the name.
