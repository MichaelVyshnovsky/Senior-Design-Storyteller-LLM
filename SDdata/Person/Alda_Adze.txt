'''Alda Adze''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] tavernkeeper who co-operated the [[Ball and Chain Tavern]] on the docks of the [[Wheloon Prison]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}
{{Quote
|The tavern name was my husband's idea. And no, I don't think it's funny.
|Alda Adze, not amused
}}
==Description==
Alda had dark brown hair and wore a simple white and gray dress while working.

==History==
Alda joined her husband [[Trosken Adze]] in operating the Ball and Chain after the opening of the prison, which served as the Wheloon dock's sole spot of relaxation. She was not pleased with the name.
