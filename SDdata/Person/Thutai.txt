Thutai was a Red Wizard of Thay overseer in the Doomvault dungeon in 1486 DR.
Thutai cared little for his underlings. If a fight went bad for him, he always tried to escape.
Thutai was a Red Wizard assigned to the secret complex of Doomvault under Thaymount. As one of the most loyal and fanatical followers of Szass Tam, he was tasked with the custody of the Chosen of Yurtrus Bandagh in the deepest levels of the dungeon. Heroes from Daggerford fought him in 1486 DR and Thutai tried to escape.
