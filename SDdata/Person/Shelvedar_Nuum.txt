Shelvedar Nuum was a half-elven merchant inhabiting the town of Mulsantir in Rashemen during the late 14 century DR.
Though he posed as a merchant from Thesk, Shelvedar was actually a Shadowmaster spy who had safely infiltrated Rashemen.
