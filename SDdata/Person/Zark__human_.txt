Zark was a scribe inhabiting the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 century DR following the citys destruction in the .
Zark was a small rat-faced man.
He job was salvaging records and documents from the ruins of the city. He reported anything of interested directly to the Zhentarim. Zark was not happy with his lot in life, and enjoyed tormenting anyone who came to him in search of information.
