'''Ithaqull''' was a very large [[beholder]] member of the [[Zhentarim]] in [[1358 DR]].

==History==
Ithaqull and many other beholders was with [[Manshoon]] when the wizard perceived the start of the [[Time of Troubles]] and whispered to Manshoon that the event would offer opportunities.
