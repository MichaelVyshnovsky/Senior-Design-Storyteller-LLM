Aasim was a cleric who dedicated himself to the dogma of the Iron Throne leader and Bhaalspawn Sarevok during the midâˆ’14 century DR.
Unbeknownst to the Iron Throne, Aasim and his companions Gardush, Naaman, Diyab, Alai, along with their leader Zhalimar Cloudwulfe, sought to advance Sarevoks agenda rather than that of the mercantile organization.
Aasim wore a suit of +1 enchanted studded leather armor and a +1 mace. He also possessed a single wand of the heavens.
In the , Aasim and his allies were sent by the Iron Throne to the city of Baldurs Gate to advise Thaldorn in his negotiations with Emissary Tar and the citys Council of Four. Just prior to when that meeting was supposed to take place, the group was thwarted by Sarevoks half-brother, Abdel Adrian.
