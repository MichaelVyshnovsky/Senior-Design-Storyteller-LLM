Festina was the mother of Chkkch and an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Ma Festina was a human woman with a light skin tone, light brown eyes, and long, wavy blond hair.
In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldurs Gate, Festina brought her daughter, Chkkch, to a performance of some showy magics by three spellslingers, on the Murls Rest plaza in front of Sorcerous Sundries. The mother was delighted by her daughters joy seeing the magical show.
