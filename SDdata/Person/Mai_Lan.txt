'''Mai Lan''' was a [[Shou]] [[human]] woman and the wife of [[Merquam Pang]], one of the most prosperous merchants of [[Koryo]] around [[1357 DR]]. She was beautiful and he was intensely jealous of her, and kept her cloistered in his mansion in [[Xi Hulang]].
