'''Myrnath''' was a [[high elf]] that was abducted by a [[nautiloid]] in the 1490s DR.

==Description==
Myrnath had white-yellow skin and weighed {{SI|110.2|lb|k}}. He wore blue [[trousers]] and brown leather [[boots]].

==History==
Some time during the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Myrnath was taken aboard a nautiloid. Myrnath's brain was used to give birth to the [[intellect devourer]] known as [[Us]].
