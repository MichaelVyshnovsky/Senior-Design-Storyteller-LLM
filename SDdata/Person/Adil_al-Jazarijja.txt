'''Adil al-Jazarijja''' was a member of the [[House of Hanif]] tribe within the [[Haunted Lands]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Adil was Sheikh of a smaller branch of the tribe near the [[Weeping Desert]]. His camp was attacked and put to the torch by raiders from [[Krak al-Niraan]]. Adil's daughters, [[Dalida al-Jazarijja|Dalida]] and [[Aisha al-Jazarijja|Aisha]] were kidnapped during the raid and taken back to the castle.
