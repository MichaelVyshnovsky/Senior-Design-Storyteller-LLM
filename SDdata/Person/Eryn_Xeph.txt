Eryn Xeph (
She had a rather slender form, with pale skin, blue eyes, and silver hair. Eryn had the distinctive characteristic of having an extremely low tolerance for physical pain.
Eryn had a fervent interest in philosophy.
Eryn desired to attain true power over the Prime Material plane, and believed her best chances were to join the inner circle of Zariels cult.
In the , Eryn unwittingly offered herself as a sacrifice to the cult of Zariel in the sewers of Baldurs Gate, believing she was being accepted as a full-fledged member.
