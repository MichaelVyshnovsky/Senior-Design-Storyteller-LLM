'''Jaemas Xorlarrin''' was a [[drow]] [[wizard]] of [[House Xorlarrin]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Jaemas was a competent Xorlarrin wizard. During the [[Siege of Menzoberranzan]], he was called to help the blind archmage [[Gromph Baenre]] to perform an eye transplant from a prisoner who was a member of [[House Agrach Dyrr]].

In the {{YearlinkName|1485}}, Jaemas and his cousin and fellow Sorcere master [[Faelas Xorlarrin]] accompanied the [[demon]] army led by [[Marilith (individual)|Marilith]] and [[Nalfeshnee (individual)|Nalfeshnee]]. They witnessed the defeat of the two [[Demon lord|demon lords]] at the hands of [[Drizzt Do'Urden]] and [[Jarlaxle]] urged them to report the news to Matron Mother [[Zeerith Q'Xorlarrin]].

In the {{YearlinkName|1486}}, Jaemas was chosen to join the reformed [[House Do'Urden]] together with his cousins [[Saribel Xorlarrin]] and [[Ravel Xorlarrin]], but not Faelas.
 
==Relationship==
Ravel was one of the best friend of Jaemas. Often he partnered with Faelas.
