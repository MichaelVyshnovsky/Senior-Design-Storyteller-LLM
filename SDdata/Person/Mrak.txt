Devout Mrak was a cleric of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.
Mrak had yellow-brown skin and light brown hair he kept sheared on the sides.
He wore a crude leather cuirass, bracers, and a simple loin cloth.
In the , the cult of the Absolute launched a terrible raid on the Waukeens Rest inn. After the assault, Mrak hoped to receive the mark of the Absolute from High Priestess Gut.
