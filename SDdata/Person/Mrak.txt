Devout '''Mrak''' was a [[cleric]] of [[the Absolute]] who dwelled within the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|You 'ere to get branded too? Priestess reckons we should be marked up before the next raid. Make sure whoever we're stickin' it to knows we're doin' it for the Absolute.|Devout Mrak.}}

==Description==
Mrak had yellow-brown skin and light brown hair he kept sheared on the sides.

==Possessions==
He wore a crude [[leather]] cuirass, [[bracer]]s, and a simple loin cloth.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, the [[cult of the Absolute]] launched a terrible raid on the [[Waukeen's Rest]] inn. After the assault, Mrak hoped to receive the ''[[mark of the Absolute]]'' from High Priestess [[Gut]].
