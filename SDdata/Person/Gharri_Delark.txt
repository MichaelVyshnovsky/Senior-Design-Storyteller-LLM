Gharri Delark was a member of the daemonfey in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.
Gharri had won the grudging respect of Baron Xhalth.
Gharri was an adventurer member of the Company of the Jaded Heart. They went to explore the ruin of Hellgate Keep under orders of the Arcane Brotherhood. However, the daemonfey killed most of them. Gharri and the surviving members pledged their loyalty to the feyri.
