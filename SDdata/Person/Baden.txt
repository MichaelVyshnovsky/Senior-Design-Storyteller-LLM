'''Baden''' was a [[Netherese]] [[archwizard]] and a scholar who worked in [[Karsus Enclave]].

==Activities==
The archwizard was the Dean of Teachers at the [[University of Definitive Archwizardry]], the most reputable school of the enclave. As well as his duties as dean, Baden taught [[variation]] [[spell]]s to the students of the university.
