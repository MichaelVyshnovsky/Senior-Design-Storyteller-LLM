'''Margolenn''' was a [[gnome|gnomish]] commoner from the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Wow, not seen someone like you before - not even in the gazette illustrations!''|Margolenn}}

==Description==
Margolenn was a fair-skinned [[rock gnome]] with brown eyes and hair. He wore a blue tunic of quality tailoring and a skullcap.
