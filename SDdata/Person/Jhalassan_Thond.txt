'''Jhalassan Thond''' was a glassblower and shopkeeper inhabiting the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
She owned a glass shop located on [[Sleepers' Walk]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] called [[Thond Glass and Glazing]]. Jhalassan was also the main point of contact for the [[Guild of Glassblowers, Glaziers, & Speculum-makers]].
