'''Totoq''' was a grizzled [[Jaguar Knights]] of [[Nexal]].

==History==
In [[1361 DR]], he was one of the survivors after the [[Night of Wailing]]. He was among the leaders of the survivors and discussed the threat of the [[Viperhands]] monsters.
