'''Lharave''' ({{Pronounce|lah|rah|VAY|refs=}}) was a [[ranger]] and [[swanmay]] in the [[Dalelands]] area in [[1357 DR]].
  
==Activities==
Lharave lived in the wilds of [[Daggerdale]]'s western reaches, and the eastern slopes of the [[Thunder Peaks]] to the south of Daggerdale. She preferred to avoid associating herself with [[human]]s but helped people in danger.

==Relationship==
Lharave was an ally of the [[Harpers]].
