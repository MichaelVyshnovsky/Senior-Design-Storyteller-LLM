Chymist Norffe was an alchemist and cultist of Bane who served the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 century DR.
Norffe was a human man who wore dark hooded Baneite robes.
In the , the Cult of Bane joined forces with the Cult of the Absolute, led by the Chosen of Bane, Lord Enver Gortash. Chymist Norffe was a part of the Cults operations that targeted the countless refugees that flooded Baldurs Gate. His branch of the Cult assembled donations and toys rigged with smokepowder explosives in the Felogyrs Fireworks shops third floor. Presumably, Norffe perished during or shortly before the Cult attacked Baldurs Gate.
