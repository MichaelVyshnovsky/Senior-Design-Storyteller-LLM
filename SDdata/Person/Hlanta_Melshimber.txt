Lady '''Hlanta Melshimber''' was a [[noble]] and practitioner of [[magic]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name="CoSW-p30>

==Activities==
She lived within the [[Melshimber Villa|family villa]] located in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] on northwest corner of the [[Street of Whispers]] and [[Grimwald's Way]].

==Relationships==
Lady Hlanta was the matriarch of the [[Melshimber]] noble family of Waterdeep. Her heir and eldest son was [[Khallos Melshimber|Khallos]].
