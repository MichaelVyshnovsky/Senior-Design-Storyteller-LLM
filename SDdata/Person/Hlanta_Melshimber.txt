Lady Hlanta Melshimber was a noble and practitioner of magic inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
She lived within the family villa located in the Sea Ward on northwest corner of the Street of Whispers and Grimwalds Way.
Lady Hlanta was the matriarch of the Melshimber noble family of Waterdeep. Her heir and eldest son was Khallos.
