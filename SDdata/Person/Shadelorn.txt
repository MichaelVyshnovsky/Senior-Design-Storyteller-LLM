Shadelorn was a Netherese archwizard who lived during the Shadowed Age.
The eager Shadelorn inhabited Shadowtop Borough, a enclave that held a friendly community of arcanists and archwizards. Shadelorn achieved much during his life, and even accomplished feats that several other archwizards were unable to achieve.
