Radatha was the leader of the Fire Knives cell in Tilverton in 1357 DR.
Radatha led a group of Fire Knives that escaped to Tilverton. After the cell refuged in Westgate was destroyed by Aliass party, in 1357 Radatha agreed to join a New Alliance with Tyranthraxus, the Zhentarim, priests of Moander and one Red Wizards of Thay in order to imbue in an adventurer the Azure Bonds. Radathas goal was kill the King Azoun Obarskyr IV. Meantime his minions managed to capture the runaway princess Alusair Obarskyr in order to lure Azoun IV in Tilverton. At last however Radathas plan backfired and he was killed by Alusair.
