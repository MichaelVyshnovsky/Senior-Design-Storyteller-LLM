Valantajar was a rakshasa who worked as a private investigator in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.
By the , Valantajar went by the name Vincent Trench and managed the Tigers Eye, a detective business on Trollskull Alley in the North Ward. He could discover any secret in the city in exchange for gold or a service to him.
Valantajar had lived for many years in Waterdeep, changing identities as necessary. He had grown accustomed to living among mortals and&mdashto his own astonishment&mdashwas rather fond of the city and its inhabitants.
