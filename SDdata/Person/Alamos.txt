'''Alamos''' was a famous [[Netherese]] [[bard]] and playwright who wrote many famous theatrical works during the final years of [[Netheril]].

==Activities==
She specialized in long, depressing stories about [[adventurer]]s falling from grace. They were extremely popular, and were regularly performed by the [[League of Noted Singers and Performers]].
