'''Fraeyer Illance''' was a member of the [[House Illance|Illance]] noble family of [[Cormyr]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Fraeyer was tall, slender, and soft-spoken. He was described as darkly handsome. He spoke softly.

==Personality==
Fraeyer was cold-hearted and unscrupulous.

==Activities==
Fraeyer was one of the younger but more well-traveled members of his family. Prominent in [[1372 DR]], he was the one most actively engaged in promoting Illance interests, following the motto "Freedom from all laws, and a spur to all profits, daggers for our foes, and a pox on all lawmakers and keepers."
