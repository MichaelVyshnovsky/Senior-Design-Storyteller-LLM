'''Colis Goldsfall''' was the proprietor of the [[Badger's Den]] tavern in [[Trailstone]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He ran the business with his two daughters, [[Jehan Goldsfall|Jehan]] and [[Marila Goldsfall|Marila]].

One of Goldsfall's legs was a [[peg leg]].
