'''Ramenhorus III''' was the twelfth [[pharaoh]] of [[Mulhorand]].

{{Quote|''An item of great utility is one to be cherished into eternity.''|An inscription on the tomb of Ramenhorus III in the [[Great Vale]]<ref>}}

==History==
Ramenhorus III was born in [[941 DR]]. He ascended to the throne of pharaoh of Mulhorand in [[955 DR]].

Ramenhorus III's reign lasted until [[1098 DR]], when he died fighting the [[Red Wizards]] of [[Thay]] as they made their first attempt to invade Mulhorand.<ref>
