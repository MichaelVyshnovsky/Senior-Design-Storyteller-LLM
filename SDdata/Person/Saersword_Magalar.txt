'''Saersword Magalar''' was a notable [[thief]] and swindler inhabiting [[Yeshpek]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p168">

==Personality==
Saersword had a silver tongue and usually able to manipulate others with ease.<ref name=EotSS-p168"/>

==Description==
A childhood disease left half of Saersword's face pockmarked, otherwise he would be considered a handsome youth. He had long hair.<ref name=EotSS-p168"/>

==Activities==
Saersword employed children to be his spies and could call on a gang of toughs to back him up if necessary.<ref name=EotSS-p168"/>
