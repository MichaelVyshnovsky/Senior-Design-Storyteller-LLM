Tholtz Daggerdark was a sea-captain in Trackless Sea sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.
Tholtz was one of the mad devotee thralls of Slarkrethel. He commanded the ship Morkoth. He believed himself to be in constant telepathic link with the kraken, but in truth he only received rare and fragmented messages from his master.
In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR the storm giant king Hekaton was captured by the Morkoths crew, posing as Lords Alliance agents. Slarkrethel ordered Tholtz to remained at sea until this matter was resolved.
