'''Ophurkh''' was a [[quasit]] servant of [[Baphomet]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1494}}, Ophurkh was one of the many [[demon]]s in [[Avernus]] when [[Elturel]] fell into the [[Nine Hells]]. He was one of the first to enter the fallen city, acting as scout. Ophurkh managed to infiltrate the Cathedral to [[Lathander]] and there waiting the arrival of more demons.
