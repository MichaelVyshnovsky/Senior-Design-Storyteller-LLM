Tralmir '''Numac''' was the leader of a tribe of horsemen calling themselves the [[Mir (tribe)|Mir]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Numac was a skilled horseman whose savagery in battle was renowned. A dozen loyal guards followed him at all times. Numac and his fellow horsemen battled both the [[Zhentarim]] and [[Purple Dragons]] of [[Cormyr]] within their territory around the [[Marsh of Tun]] in the [[Tunlands]]. His title of "tralmir" was hereditary.
