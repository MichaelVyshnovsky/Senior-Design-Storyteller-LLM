Elonia Starre was an illusionist, as well as the owner and proprietor of Elonias Beauty Shoppe, in the city of Ravens Bluff during the late 14 century DR. She was a skilled beautician who offered her predominantly female clientele a number of services including skin and nail care, hair styling, shaves, tattoos, and magical, illusory beauty enhancements.
Elonia had a daughter, Salena, with whom she shared a residence above the salon.
In addition to her skill as a businesswoman and beautician, Elonia was known for her exceptional beauty.
