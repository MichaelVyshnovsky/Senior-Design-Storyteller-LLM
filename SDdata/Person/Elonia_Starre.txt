'''Elonia Starre''' was an [[illusionist]], as well as the owner and proprietor of [[Elonia's Beauty Shoppe]], in the city of [[Ravens Bluff]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. She was a skilled beautician who offered her predominantly female clientele a number of services including skin and nail care, hair styling, shaves, tattoos, and [[magic]]al, illusory beauty enhancements.

Elonia had a daughter, [[Salena Starre|Salena]], with whom she shared a residence above the salon.

==Description==
In addition to her skill as a businesswoman and beautician, Elonia was known for her exceptional beauty.
