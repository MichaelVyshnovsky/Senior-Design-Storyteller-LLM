Ulamyth Quantor served as the High Priest, or High Torturelord, of Loviatar within the Tower of Pain Exalted in Zhentil Keep in 1358 DR.
High Torturelord Quantor supervised 14 fellow priests of the Maiden of Pain. He preached to a congregation of 26 followers.
Following the destruction of most of Zhentil Keep in the , Ulamyth moved Loviatars church to the newly erected Palace of Sweet Pain located on the southern shore of the River Tesh in the Foreign Quarter.
