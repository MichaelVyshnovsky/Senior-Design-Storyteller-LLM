Procampur, the Vast
Lady Sarah Lurian was a noble of House Lurian in the city of Procampur in the Vast in the late 14 century DR.
She was a relative of Lord Consul Povalo Lurian, the head of the family.While several members of House Lurian are known, most of their relationships are unknown Sarah is most likely intended to be a daughter of Povalo and Jasmine Lurian.
She was aged 18 in 1370 DR.
