'''Heen''' was a Cashguard Artillerist in the employ of the [[Counting House]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Heen was a [[human]] man with an olive skin tone and brown eyes. As a Cashguard, he wore a set of uniform [[breastplate]] [[armor]] and a [[helmet]]. Heen was armed with a two-handed [[heavy crossbow]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Cashguard Artillerist Heen was tasked with guarding and patrolling the private docks that belonged to the Counting House in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]], alongside a number of fellow Cashguards and the city's [[steel watcher]]s.
