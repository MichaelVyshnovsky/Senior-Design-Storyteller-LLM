Heen was a Cashguard Artillerist in the employ of the Counting House of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Heen was a human man with an olive skin tone and brown eyes. As a Cashguard, he wore a set of uniform breastplate armor and a helmet. Heen was armed with a two-handed heavy crossbow.
In the , Cashguard Artillerist Heen was tasked with guarding and patrolling the private docks that belonged to the Counting House in the Lower City, alongside a number of fellow Cashguards and the citys steel watchers.
