Rylla was the Half-drow battlemistress of the Promenade of the Dark Maiden.
Rylla was the main military leader of the Church of Eilistraee.
In 1379 DR, she summoned the Darksong Knight Cavatina Xarann for a meeting. Rylla was the first that Cavatina alerted when she discovered that the cook Meryl had been replaced by a dretch. Rylla led the Protectors to attempt to catch the infiltrator. 
Later Rylla discovered that Qilué had killed the druid Horaldin tried to stop her Lady believing her in thrall of Wendonai. However Qilué scaring that she would ruin her plot to kill Lolth was forced to kill also Rylla.
