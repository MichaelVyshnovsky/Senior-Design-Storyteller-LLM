'''Captain Rulathorn''' ({{Pronounce|Roo|LATH|on|refs= commander of the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|city watch]] of [[Waterdeep]]. A fair, but tough man, Rulathorn is more than willing to arrest the same person time and again until they cease wrongdoings within the city. Rulathorn reports directly to Open Lord [[Piergeiron the Paladinson|Piergeiron]].

==Description==
Captain Rulathorn hair was long and black, his face was decorated with a black mustache. He was a warrior of significant expertise in subtle reconnaissance and thorough knowledge of the people of Waterdeep.

==Possessions==

Rulathorn's armed with the ''[[red sword|red sword]]'', which is somewhat famous within Waterdeep. He is also known to use an [[iron]] ''[[horn of Valhalla]]'' to summon reinforcements.

==History==
In [[1371 DR]]{{DDTS}}, on the 17{{th}} and 18{{th}} of Eleint, Captain Rulathorn was present at the wedding ceremony between the [[Open Lord]] of [[Waterdeep]] [[Piergeiron the Paladinson]] and [[Eidola]] of [[Neverwinter]]. The event was swept away by a whirlwind of sinister plots around the wedding. When the Open Lord discovered the looming danger, Rulathorn was one of the people Piergeiron trusted to investigate the Master Mariners' Guild, the shapeshifters, and the eventual abduction of the bride.
