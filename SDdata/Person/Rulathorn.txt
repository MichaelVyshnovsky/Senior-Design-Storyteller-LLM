Captain Rulathorn (, on the 17 and 18 of Eleint, Captain Rulathorn was present at the wedding ceremony between the Open Lord of Waterdeep Piergeiron the Paladinson and Eidola of Neverwinter. The event was swept away by a whirlwind of sinister plots around the wedding. When the Open Lord discovered the looming danger, Rulathorn was one of the people Piergeiron trusted to investigate the Master Mariners Guild, the shapeshifters, and the eventual abduction of the bride.
