'''Ghondeth''' was a skilled [[wizard]] inhabiting [[Westgate]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Personality==
Gondeth was a calm and shy individual.

==Activities==
Gondeth sold magical [[scoll]]s and cast [[spell]]s for a fee, but he never went adventuring.
