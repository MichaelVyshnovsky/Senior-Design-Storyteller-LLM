Anton Marivaldi was a pirate from Turmish in the Sea of Fallen Stars in the late 15 century DR.
Anton originated from Sapra in Turmish but after some crime he went into exile and became a pirate captain.
Wanting the reward of a bounty, Anton tried to capture the young Chosen of Lathander Stedd Whitehorn in the , during the Second Sundering but in the end he chose to help him in his mission.
