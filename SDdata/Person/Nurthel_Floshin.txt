Nurthel Floshin was a feyri who served the daemonfey leader Sarya Dlardrageth as her spymaster.
Nurthel had only one eye, and it was green.
Nurthel wore golden mail.
In 1374 DR, he was responsible for stealing a shard of the Gatekeepers Crystal from Tower Reilloch. He
Nurthel was killed on Tarsakh 11, 1374 DR, in a white tower in the Elven Court area of the forest of Cormanthor when Araevin Teshurr used the magic he had learned from the Nightstar to invert Nurthels magical protections. Araevin stabbed Nurthel through the throat with his own sword, stating that his death was a revenge for the killing of Grayth Holmfast.
