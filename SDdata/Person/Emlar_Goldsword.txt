'''Emlar Goldsword''' was a [[rogue]] [[fighter]] and a member of House [[Goldsword]] of [[Cormyr]].{{Cite dragon annual/2000|66}}

==Description==
Elmar was a bald man.<ref>

==History==
He was loyal to Cormyr but not to the [[Obarskyr]]s.<ref> In fact, he cleaved to [[Sembia]] and hated and feared the Obarksyrs. He preferred rule by a merchant council, but was too cowardly and too wise to actively rebel.
