'''Melann Brandish''' was a [[cleric]] of [[Chauntea]] at the [[Bounty of the Goddess (Archenbridge)|Bounty of the Goddess]] temple in [[Archendale]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Melann had long dark hair, tied away from her face. She wore a [[leather]] [[wikipedia:en:Jerkin (garment)|jerkin]] and a brown [[cloak]] for protection while traveling. She was never found without her wooden [[holy symbol]] to Chauntea around her neck on an amulet.

==Relationships==
Melann had a single sibling, her brother [[Whitlock Brandish]].

==History==
''Circa'' {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Melann and her brother went on a quest to try and lift a deadly curse that was cast on their family by [[Chare'en]].
