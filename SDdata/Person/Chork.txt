'''Chork''' was a [[half-orc]] guard in [[Melvaunt]] in [[1373 DR]].

==History==
In 1373 DR, [[Corwyn Jaffe]] hired Chork and his pack of guard dogs to keep watch over a group of slaves that Corwyn had acquired.
