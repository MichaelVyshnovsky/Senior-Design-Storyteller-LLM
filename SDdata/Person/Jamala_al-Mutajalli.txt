'''Jamala al-Mutajalli''' was a [[janni]] of the [[House of Sihr]] within the [[High Desert]] of [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39">

==Personality==
Jamala's blood ran hot, urging her to seek adventure whenever possible.

==Skills==
Her ability to ''[[shapechange]]'' allowed Jamala to travel with non-janni in disguise. She was also an accomplished vizier.

==Relationships==
Jamala was the eldest daughter of Amir [[Bouladin al-Mutajalli]].
