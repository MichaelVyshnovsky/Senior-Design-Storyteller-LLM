Viis Dyrr was a member of House Agrach Dyrr of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR.
Viis graduated from Sorcere at unknown time.
During the siege of Dyrrs compound for their betrayal of Menzoberranzan during the Siege of Menzoberranzan, Viis was called back to his house together with all Dyrrs other wizards to help raise magical defenses.
