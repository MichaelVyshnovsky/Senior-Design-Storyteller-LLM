'''Myrthos Shyllantham''' was a member of the [[Harpers]] who petitioned [[Alustriel Silverhand|High Lady Alustriel Silverhand]] to restore the [[Fochlucan]] [[bard]] college. 

==History==
Around [[1370 DR]], Shyllantham saw the rise in influence of the [[New Olamn]] bards' college in [[Waterdeep]] and wanted to spread the knowledge to Silverymoon.
