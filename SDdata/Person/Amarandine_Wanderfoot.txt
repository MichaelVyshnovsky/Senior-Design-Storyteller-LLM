Adventurer
Amarandine Wanderfoot was a retired adventurer who served as the Chief Speaker of Helms Hold during the late 15 century DR.
At the time of her political career, Amarandine was described as rather matronly.
She maintained a good relationship with the citys Holy Watcher, the Helmite head of the citys Cathedral of Helm.
Having been an adventurer in her younger years, Amarandine enjoyed success as an elected politician later in life. By the  she had been elected as Chief Speaker of the city council in Helms Hold.
