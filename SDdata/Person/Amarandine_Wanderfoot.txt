'''Amarandine Wanderfoot''' was a retired adventurer who served as the Chief Speaker of [[Helm's Hold]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=SCAG-p85">

==Description==
At the time of her political career, Amarandine was described as rather matronly.<ref name=SCAG-p85" />

==Relationships==
She maintained a good relationship with the city's Holy Watcher, the [[Helmite]] head of the city's [[Cathedral of Helm]].<ref name=SCAG-p85" />

==History==
Having been an adventurer in her younger years, Amarandine enjoyed success as an elected politician later in life. By the {{YearlinkName|1489}} she had been elected as Chief Speaker of the city council in Helm's Hold.<ref name=SCAG-p85" />
