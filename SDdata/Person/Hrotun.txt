'''Hrotun''' was the very first [[frost giant]] [[skald]].

==Relationships==
Hrotun was the son of [[Ottar]], making him one of the second generation of giants to live on [[Toril]]. He had a brother named [[Hjurgen]], and he was the uncle of the great hero [[Hjurnur Wyrmrever]].

==Works==
Hrotun composed the ''[[Saga of the Dragon Queller]]'' about Hjurnur Wyrmrever, his nephew.
