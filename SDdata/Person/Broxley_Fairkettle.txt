'''Broxley Fairkettle''' was a [[strongheart halfling]] representative of the [[Fellowship of Innkeepers]] in [[Waterdeep]] in the early 1490s [[DR]].

==History==
In [[1492 DR]],{{WDH-time}} Broxley possibly went to check the new owners of the [[Trollskull Manor]], trying hard to recruit them into his guild and offering advice.

==Personality==
Broxley always lamented the difficulty of being the father of nine children and believed that if all followed the Guild's rules his life would become little easier.
