'''Chaul''' was an [[annis|annis hag]] who inhabited and ruled the village of [[Citadel Rashemar]] in [[Rashemen]].

==Activities==
She lived in the lower levels of the ruined keep in the former fortress and had a hatred for the [[hathran]], having killed two of them who attacked her. She trained her [[ogre]] and [[goblin]] followers in the art of [[magic]]. She associated with the half-orc [[Durakh Haan]].
