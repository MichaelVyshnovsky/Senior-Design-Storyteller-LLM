'''Eaerlraun Shadowlyn''' was the [[Harpers|Harper]] master of [[Moongleam Tower]] in [[Everlund]]. 

==History==
In [[1372 DR]], Eaerlraun held the title of Moonlord and was sympathetic to the [[Moonstars|Moonstar]] cause.
He was the one that kept alive the Harpers' traditions after the [[Spellplague]], focusing in fighting the [[shadovar]].{{Cite dragon/367/Epic Faerûn}}
