Eaerlraun Shadowlyn was the Harper master of Moongleam Tower in Everlund. 
In 1372 DR, Eaerlraun held the title of Moonlord and was sympathetic to the Moonstar cause.
He was the one that kept alive the Harpers traditions after the Spellplague, focusing in fighting the shadovar.
