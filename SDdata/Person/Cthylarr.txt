'''Cthylarr''', also known as '''Cthylarr the Illithilich''', was an [[undead]] [[mind flayer]] from [[Cyrog]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Cthylarr was an [[illithilich]] of epic power.

==History==
When [[Orcus]] arrived in Cyrog in the {{YearlinkName|1486}}, Cthylarr ended up serving him. The illithilich travelled with its patron to [[Castle Never]], where it sought the [[phylactery]] of the [[dracolich]] [[Azharzel]].
