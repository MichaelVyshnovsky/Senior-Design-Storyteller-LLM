Gwen was a willowy female soldier who traveled with Salen and Jensena.
Gwen was a short slender brunette.
Slim and sexy, Gwen used her feminine wiles to trick and charm her opponents.
In battle, Gwen armed herself with a sword, a few daggers, a throwing axe and dressed in chain mail.
On the 19th of Flamerule, 1340 DR, Gwen drank at the Laughing Goblin Inn with her companions and tried to trick Ren o the Blade out of a bagful of coins.
On the night of the 28th of Flamerule 1340 DR, Gwen was killed while fighting a troll outside the city walls of Phlan.
