'''Yingdipata''' was a warrior in the [[Pasocada Basin]] in [[Maztica]] and a member of the hunter's fraternity.

==History==
In [[1362 DR]], Yingdipata was chosen to act as security chief during the welcoming of [[Sozarro]]'s expedition.
