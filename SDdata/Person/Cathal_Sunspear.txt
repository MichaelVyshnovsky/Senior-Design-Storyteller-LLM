Cathal Sunspear was the counter clerk for the Auroras Realms Shop, Waterdeep Way Catalogue Counter in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
She was the daughter of a wealthy family of Tethyr,
She moved to Waterdeep and was working for Auroras by the mid-1360s DR.
As well as her work at the shop, Cathal was an investor. She paid close attention to the backroom deals of the citys noble families and guilds.
She was middle-aged.
She was calm and cultured.
