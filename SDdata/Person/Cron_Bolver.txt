'''Cron Bolver''' was a member of the [[Knights of the Black Fist]] in [[Phlan]] in [[1489 DR]].

==Personality==
Bolver had a lot of power in the city and made sure that all knew this. He took pleasure from his job and often accepted bribes. Bolver had a sharp wit and a sharper tongue.

==History==
Bolver was one of the Black Fist lieutenants in charge of the [[Stojanow Gate]].{{Cite adventurers league/The Courting of Fire|9}}
After the [[Vorgansharax]]'s coup Bolver became one of the [[Tears of Virulence]], maintaining all his power. He was displaced with a patrol to capture Madame [[Freona]] but was defeated and killed by an adventuring party hired by the resistance to permit the escape of Freona.{{Cite adventurers league/Escape from Phlan|12-13}}

==Activities==
Bolver usually stayed at a desk in the Black Fist barracks to answer to citizens' petitions.{{Cite adventurers league/The Courting of Fire|9}}
