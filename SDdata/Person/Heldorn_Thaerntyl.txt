'''Heldorn "Goldsun" Thaerntyl''' was a [[human]] [[fighter]], [[wizard]], [[thief]] and a member of the [[Council of Sparkling Stones]] in [[Mirabar]]. 

==Description==
The adventurer gained his nickname because he had long curly blond hair, and he used two [[sun blade]]s in combat.
