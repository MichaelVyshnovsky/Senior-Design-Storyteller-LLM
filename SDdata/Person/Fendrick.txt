Fendrick was a cook in Freedale in the Dalelands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Though nice, he was fastidious about the look and feel of his restaurant, oft appearing to care more about his food than himself.
He ran Fendricks Fine Foods, a local eatery where he was the head cook.
He was said to be the best cook in , an area that encompassed much of the Dalelands.
He employed an undercook called Boris, and a serving girl called Clara.
