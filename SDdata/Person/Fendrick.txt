'''Fendrick''' was a cook in [[Freedale]] in the [[Dalelands]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Though nice, he was fastidious about the look and feel of his restaurant, oft appearing to care more about his food than himself.

==Activities==
He ran [[Fendrick's Fine Foods]], a local eatery where he was the head cook.

==Abilities==
He was said to be the best cook in {{SI|100|miles|k|long=yes}}, an area that encompassed much of the Dalelands.

==Relationships==
He employed an undercook called [[Boris (Freedale)|Boris]], and a serving girl called [[Clara (Freedale)|Clara]].

==History==
Fendrick was an inhabitant of Freedale in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Reputation==
His "Chicken Surprise" drew interested eaters from tens of miles around.
