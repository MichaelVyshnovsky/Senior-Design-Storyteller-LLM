'''Baar Tworgeir''' was a carpenter living in [[Hillsfar]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Shackles of Blood|10}}

==History==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], Baar was hired by [[Deirk Erlich]] to repair the vacant [[Tinfellow Farm]]. He suspected the farm was stolen but knew that Deirk's brother was [[Haakon Erlich]], an officer of the [[Red Plumes]], and was scared of reprisals if he disagreed.
