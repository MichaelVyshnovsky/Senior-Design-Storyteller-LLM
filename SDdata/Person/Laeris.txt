Laeris was a Painbearer of Ilmater and a member of the adventuring company - the Ladys Lament from Waterdeep, active circa 1310 DR.
Laeris was a contemplative and quiet man who rarely talked and asked for very little. He used a mace, a shield, and a sling in battle.
Sometime before the , Laeris joined the Ladys Lament, becoming the teams spiritual guiding force. He complimented the groups leader - Sheris Lyricists charisma with his wisdom.
In 1310 DR, the Ladys Lament, along with many other mercenaries, traveled from Luskan to the besieged city of Targos in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.
