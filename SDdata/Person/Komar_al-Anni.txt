'''Komar al-Anni''' was a historical inhabitant of [[Zakhara]].

==History==
Komar bravely sacrificed his life to save a group of pilgrim's from a group of bloodthirsty [[jann]]. After his death, the [[Shrine of the Brave]] was erected along the [[Pilgrims' Route]] to honor his memory.

==Rumors and Legends==
Komar's spirit was said to send useful visions to warriors who stayed at the shrine, offering valuable advice on future battles.
