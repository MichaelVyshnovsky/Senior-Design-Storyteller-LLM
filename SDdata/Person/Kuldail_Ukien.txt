==Description==
Kuldail had sandy-blonde hair and pale skin. He wore a gray tunic and dark leggings, and favored a [[bow]].

==Personality==
Under the influence of [[Halsaime's staff]], Kuldail's personality was devoted to destroying all possible opposition to the concept of nature.

==History==
Kuldail Ukien and his [[giant spider]] companion [[Skitter]] defended the [[Druid's Deep]] for the [[druid]] [[Halsaime]], who had used [[Halsaime's staff|his staff]] to subvert the local druids, rangers, and wild creatures. He was joined in this service by the fierce druid [[Juhni]].
