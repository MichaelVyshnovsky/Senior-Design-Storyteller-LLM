Travis Ormaster was the steward of Lord Partic Thistle, and served him at Thistleflame Keep around 1369 DR.
He was quick-witted and clever, with a brusque manner and sharp wits.
As the steward of his lord, Travis was entrusted with Lord Thistles signet ring.
Travis had a variety of simple arcane spells which he used to perform his administrative work for Lord Thistle.
As steward, Travis was responsible for a wide variety of issues at Thistleflame. He managed Lord Thistles schedule, handled the staff, administered the finances and ensured the manors stores were kept stocked. Because of his position, he was well aware of the gossip and activities of his staff.
Travis was friends with Lord Thistles messenger Kaleb Fleetwing, but jealous rivals with the gardener Berke Glorata.
