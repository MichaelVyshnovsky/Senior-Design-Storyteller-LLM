'''Shield''' was a white [[horse]] ridden by [[Shandril Shessair]] on her journey away from [[Shadowdale]] in {{yearlink|1357}}.

==Description==
Shield had a white coat.

==Personality==
It snorted and shook its head while [[Florin Falconhand]] talked to [[Shandril Shessair]].

==Relationships==
Florin was aware of its name before Shandril rode it.

==History==
In the {{yearlinkName|1357}}, when Shandril Shessair parted ways with the [[Knights of Myth Drannor]] for the last time, right before heading to the [[the Rising Moon (inn)|Rising Moon]], she was riding a horse whose name was known to Florin Falconhand.
