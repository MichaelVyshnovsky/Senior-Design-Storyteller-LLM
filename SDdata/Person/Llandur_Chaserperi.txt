'''Llandur Chaserperi''' was an entrepreneurial member of the [[Chaserperi]] family in the late mid-14{{th}} century.

==Activities==
He was a fur trader who owned a warehouse and sponsored small merchants. He was also among the best known members of the [[Chaserperi]].
