Geremis Dyrr was an old member of House Agrach Dyrr of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR.
Geremis graduated from Sorcere at an unknown time and became an apprentice of the Lichdrow Dyrr.
During the siege of Dyrrs compound for their betrayal of Menzoberranzan during the Siege of Menzoberranzan, Geremis, bald and aging, was called back to his house together with all of Dyrrs other wizards to help raise magical defenses.
