King '''Hygaarth Croam''', who was also called the '''Pious''', was the first King of the [[Croam Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Hygaarth was a devout [[priest]] of [[Ilmater]] in Westgate during a vicious plague that swept through the city. After his heroic work to thwart the spread of the plague, Hygaarth was elevated to the position of King after the previous ruler, King [[Kergaard Twinblade]], fell to the plague in the {{YearlinkName|75}}, without leaving any heirs. 

==Relationships==
His firstborn son and heir was [[Sorentah Croam|Sorentah]].
