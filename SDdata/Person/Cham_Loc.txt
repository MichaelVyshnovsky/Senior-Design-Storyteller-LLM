'''Cham Loc''' was an evil spirit of the [[Hordelands]] who once fought with [[Furo|Furo the Mighty]], [[Padhrasattva|Padhrasattva of Knowledge]].

In [[1359 DR]], [[Koja]] of [[Khazari]], follower of Furo, named his gray-and-yellow [[pony]] Cham Loc, owing to its tendency to bite.
