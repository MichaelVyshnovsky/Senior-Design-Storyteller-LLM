Ander was a commoner who resided in the village of Imnesvale in the Umar Hills of Amn in the late 14 century DR.
Ander was a human man with long hair and light skin who wore olive-colored commoners garb.
In the , the village of Imnesvale faced a slew of disappearances and locals did not hesitate to blame the legendary Umar Witch. Ander, along with his wife Johanna, spent much of his free time in the Umar Inn but avoided interacting with strangers out of fear.
