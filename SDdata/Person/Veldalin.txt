'''Veldalin''', who was nicknamed '''the Dark''', was a [[necromancer]] of note inhabiting [[Saerloon]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Veldalin was a well-known expert in the art of [[necromancy]]. All of his servants and guardians were rare and bizarre forms of [[undead]].
