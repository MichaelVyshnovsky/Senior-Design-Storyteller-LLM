Brawler '''Rot''' was a [[goblin]] member of the [[cult of the Absolute]] who dwelled within the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Wass holdin' up the game? Owlbear ain't dead is it?|Brawler Rot.}}

==Description==
Rot had orange skin and long brown hair, that went down to his feet. He wore a [[leather]] cuirass and crude [[bracer]]s adorned with bones.

==Possessions==
His preferred weapon was a crudely-made goblin [[scimitar]].

==History==
Rot was among the goblins that celebrated at the [[Shattered Sanctum]] the cult's successful raid on [[Waukeen's Rest]], in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
