'''Alia al-Sadid''' was a [[half-elven]] [[kahin]] and member of the [[House of Thawr]] tribe in the [[High Desert]] of [[west Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p41">

==Activities==
Disturbed by her [[Ali al-Sadid|father's]] obsession with his vision of sweeping through the [[Pearl Cities]], Alia confronted the tribe's elders about the possibility of new leadership with the best interests of the tribe at heart. This suggestion was received with mixed emotions. Alia even offered to do this task herself and vowed to lead the tribe back to safety deeper in the desert.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p41"/>

==Relationships==
Alia was the only surviving child of her father.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p41"/>
