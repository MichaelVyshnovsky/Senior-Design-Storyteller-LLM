Beldar the Brave was an adventuring fighter in the mid-late 14 century DR.
Beldar had a moustache.
While not heroically so, Beldar was notably strong.
Of note, Beldar wielded a long sword and shield, wearing a chainmail.
He was an adventurer.
Thran was his childhood friend Beldar often helped in defense of the town.
The adventuring party he was a part of in Freedale included him alongside Delvar Ironfist, Silverleaf Halfmoon, Morganth, Slinker, and Lordan.
In his childhood, Beldar had been friends with Thran, later a local militiaman.
Beldar the Brave was part of an adventuring group in Freedale that may have rescued Taran Goldenstar, recovered the Orb of dragonkind, faced Gunter Weiss in Harrow Hill, and perhaps explored Azazabus dungeon under Mount Dread.
