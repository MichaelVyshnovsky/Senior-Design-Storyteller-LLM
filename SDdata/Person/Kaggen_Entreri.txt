'''Kaggen Entreri''' was a young [[Warlock Knight]] in [[Vaasa]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dungeon/177/Realmslore: Vaasa|79}} 

==History==
In [[1480 DR]], Kaggen and his platoon were sent north of the [[Lugsaas Chain]] mountains to [[Frohm's Rest]] in order to investigate a relic of the Witch-King [[Zhengyi]].

==Personality==
Kaggen was a natural leader and a forceful swordsman.

==Relationship==
Unknown to everyone, including himself, Kaggen was the last heir of the [[Dragonsbane]]s, being a secret son of [[Brianne Dragonsbane]].
