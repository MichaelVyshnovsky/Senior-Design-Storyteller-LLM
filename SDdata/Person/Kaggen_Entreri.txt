Kaggen Entreri was a young Warlock Knight in Vaasa in the late 15 century DR. 
In 1480 DR, Kaggen and his platoon were sent north of the Lugsaas Chain mountains to Frohms Rest in order to investigate a relic of the Witch-King Zhengyi.
Kaggen was a natural leader and a forceful swordsman.
Unknown to everyone, including himself, Kaggen was the last heir of the Dragonsbanes, being a secret son of Brianne Dragonsbane.
