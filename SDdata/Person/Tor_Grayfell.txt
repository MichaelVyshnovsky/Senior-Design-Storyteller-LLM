'''Tor Grayfell''' was a carver in [[Skullport]].

==History==
Tor went to Skullport to forget about his bad past and start a new life.
Around [[1370 DR]], he was an able carver.

During the early 1490s [[DR]] his brain was eaten by a [[mind flayer]] still at large in Skullport.
