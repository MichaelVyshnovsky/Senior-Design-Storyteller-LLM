Duchess '''Merelda Marliir''' was a member of the [[Marliir]] noble family around [[1370 DR]]. She was the wife of Duke [[Raynaar Marliir]] and mother to [[Dauneth Marliir]].

==Description==
She was a stumpy little woman who lacked court etiquette.
