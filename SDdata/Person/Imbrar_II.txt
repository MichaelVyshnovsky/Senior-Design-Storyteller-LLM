Imbrar II was the last king of Impiltur.
He was the second nephew of Soarimbrar the Younger (who was a descendant of Imphras IIs fourth son), through his older sister.
Before his coronation, the teenage Imbrar would travel under the alias of "Sarshel of Laviguer". During this time his identity was kept hidden and his life protected by the Knights of Imphras II.
After reaching age 16, he was crowned on Marpenoth 24, the . He took the throne from the aged Queen-Regent Sambryl, who herself gained power when Imbrars uncle was killed just before his coronation.
