Panana was a rock gnome baker living in Gnomengarde in the late 15 century DR.
Along with Uppendown, Panana was responsible for prepping bread dough made from Gnomengardes distinctive green mushrooms. Her role was to mix and knead the dough using a rolling contraption, which required her to stand atop her workbench in order to operate.
Like all the other gnomes in Gnomengarde, Panana had some training as a wizard in addition to her innate abilities. In combat, she preferred to attack with spells like ray of frost and magic missile.
