Morndin Gloomstorm was the high cleric of Laduguer in Gracklstugh in 1370 DR
Morndin was born to Clan Bukbukken, as the son of Kildor and blood of Balgor, in Shimmerglooms Run in Mithral Hall. Morndin lived under the shadow dragon Shimmerglooms rule until the shield dwarves of Clan Battlehammer reclaimed their ancestral home. He was one of the few surviving members of his clan to manage to escape to Gracklstugh. There, Morndin become high priest of the local temple of Laduguer.
