'''Velstrode the Venturer''' was a notable [[adventurer]] and successful merchant who lived in the city of [[Waterdeep]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Velstrode was a rather tall and bearded man, who traveled around with defensive [[magic]]s on his person.

==Activities==
Velstrode ran an equipment shop out of [[House of Velstrode|his home]] in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] of Waterdeep.
