'''Hlintas Urtel''' was a vigilante inhabiting [[Waterdeep]], City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Hlintas was a member of the secretive group called the [[Red Sashes]]. He could be found most evenings within the [[Sleeping Snake]] [[tavern]] on [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]].
