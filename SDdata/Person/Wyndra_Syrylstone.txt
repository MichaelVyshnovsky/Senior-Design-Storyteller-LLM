Wyndra Syrylstone was a human woman of the city of Calaunt in the Vast. She was a priestess of Selûne.
She was the High Priestess, or "Moon Mistress", of the Moonsilver House temple by 1358 DR, a position which she still held by 1370 DR. She had twenty-two subordinate priests and 229 lay-followers.
