Lzeth, mostly known as Bitter Breath, was a cornugon warlord in Avernus in the late 15 century DR.
Lzeth was obsessed with vengeance and was very ambitious.
Once Lzeth was a powerful pit fiend allied with Zariel. However, to gain more power, he started plotting with Bel but was discovered by Zariel, who demoted him to a cornugon. After some time, Lzeth became involved in a new plot to depose Zariel and was again caught. This time, the archdevil broke Lzeths wings and cursed him so he could no longer enter into deals or sign contacts, and his voice became only smoke. Thus Lzeth became Bitter Breath.
