'''Batu''', who was nicknamed '''the Lame''', was a civilized nomad who lived in [[Almorel]] on the [[Lake of Mists]] in the [[Hordelands]] in the {{YearlinkName|1357}}.

==Activities==
Batu lived in a large dwelling located just outside of northeast Almorel. He earned a living by offering his services as a skilled guide for the region.
