'''Barglun Blackbutter''' was the founder of the [[Blackbutter Inn]] in [[Red Larch]].

==Personality==
Barglun was a fat and jovial individual who was fondly remembered as a local legend in the village.

==History==
Around [[1358 DR]], Barglum was slain by a pack of [[wolves]].
