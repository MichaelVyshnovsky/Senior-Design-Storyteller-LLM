Ildryn Thallin was a male wizard who was one of the magelords of Athalantar and was killed in Elminster Aumars insurrection. 
He was a sly and manipulative magelord. He agreed with his master Ithboltar to fight together but died in the spell-battle in Ithboltars tower.
