Kung Pu Mok was the High Priest of Shou Lung in 1357 DR and 1358 DR
Kung Pu Mok plotted to replace the Emperor with a theocracy. He also sought to marry Kai Tsao Shou Chins third daughter, Princess Pai. In order to fulfill his dream, he forged ties with Black Chung Tao. He had an uneasy truce with Minister of Faith Yui Min Li.
In 1358 DR, Kung Pu Mok was busy with a tour of the monasteries in Wa Kan.
Kung Pu Mok was a vain and stubborn individual.
