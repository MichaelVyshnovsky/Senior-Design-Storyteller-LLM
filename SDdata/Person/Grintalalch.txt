'''Grintalalch the Red''' was an enigmatic [[spriggan]] merchant from [[Deepearth]] in [[1479 DR]].{{Cite dungeon/177/Realmslore: Vaasa|79}}

==Activities==
Gintalalch managed a merchant company in Deepearth. Often, however, he did business in [[Fugue (settlement)|Fugue]] in [[Vaasa]], where he managed the movement of illicit goods. Some said he had a link to the [[Assassins Guild of the Galenas]].
