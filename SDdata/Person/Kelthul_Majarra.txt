Lord '''Kelthul Majarra''' was a [[Tethyrian]] [[bard]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He served as president of the [[New Olamn]] bardic college.

==Relationships==
Kelthul was the patriarch of the [[Majarra]] [[Nobility of Waterdeep|noble family of Waterdeep]] as of the {{YearlinkName|1372}}. His consort was Lady [[Regan Majarra|Regan]]. His two eldest sons were [[Axor Majarra|Axor]] and [[Kehlann Majarra|Kehlann]].
