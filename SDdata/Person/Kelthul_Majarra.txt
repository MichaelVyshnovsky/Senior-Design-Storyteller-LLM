Lord Kelthul Majarra was a Tethyrian bard inhabiting Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He served as president of the New Olamn bardic college.
Kelthul was the patriarch of the Majarra noble family of Waterdeep as of the . His consort was Lady Regan. His two eldest sons were Axor and Kehlann.
