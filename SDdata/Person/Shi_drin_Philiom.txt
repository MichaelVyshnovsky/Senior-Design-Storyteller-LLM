Shidrin Philiom was a fanatic priest of Ghaunadaur in Llurth Dreir.
Shidrin was a member of House Philiom in Llurth Dreir and the second-in-command of Molvayass group.
In 1379 DR, he questioned Kâras about Molvayass disappearance but Kâras said he was killed by a priest of House Abbylan. Shidrin didnt question any further, happy to become the new Eater of Filth of the House.
