Rheligaun Darakh, who was also called the Horned, was the sixth and final Nentyarch of the Darakh Dynasty to rule Narfell. He was referred to as the Hidden Nentyarch.
Rheligaun overthrew and slew his cousin Ilithkar Darakh to take the Crown of Narfell and become Nentyarch in . He ruled until  when Narfell collapsed during the Great Conflagration. Rheligaun disappeared during this time and was assumed dead.
Rheligaun was the half-fiend offspring of a human woman and the demon lord Fraz-Urbluu.
