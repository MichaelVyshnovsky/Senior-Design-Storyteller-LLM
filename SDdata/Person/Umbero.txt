'''Umbero''' was an adventurer with the [[Brightstar Sash]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EDmmp-p275-c17/>

==Activities==
He adventured with the [[Brightstar Sash]] company.<ref name=EDmmp-p275-c17/>

==History==
Umbero was a part of the Brightstar Sash at the same time as the [[sorceress]] [[Caladnei]]. At least one of their forays yielded them about six thousand [[gold piece]]s, for which he thanked [[Tymora]].<ref name=EDmmp-p275-c17/>

He later perished as they escaped a [[wyvern]].<ref name=EDmmp-p278-c17/>
