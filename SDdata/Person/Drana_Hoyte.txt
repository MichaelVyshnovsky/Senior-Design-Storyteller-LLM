Drana Hoyte was a visitor of the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Drana was a lightfoot halfling woman with blue eyes, pale skin, and dark brown hair.
In the , Filson Vago and his hin friend, Drana Hoyte, were visiting the city of Baldurs Gate during the looming threat of the Cult of the Absolute. Drana was convinced she knew how to navigate the Lower City, and was unwilling to accept that they were lost. Filson, on the other hand, was very aware of their situation but still needed help finding a way in the busy warrens of the Lower City.
