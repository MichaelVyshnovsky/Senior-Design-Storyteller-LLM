High Priestess '''Aummerglau Tistrin''', who was also called '''the Sun of Ordulin''' because of her legendary beauty, was the ranking priest of the [[House of Revels]], a temple dedicated to [[Sune]], in [[Ordulin]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Aummerglau was the leader of Sune's faithful in Ordulin. Her congregation within the temple consisted of 29 other priestesses and 56 followers.
