'''Dawn''' was a popular [[elven]] seamstress in [[Westgate]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Dawn owned [[Dawn's Dress Shop]] located on [[East Market Street]] in the south section of Westgate. She was well known for her beautiful dresses and gowns and quick turnaround time, making her very popular with some of Westgate's finest ladies.
