'''Pir'Oront Tlabbar''' was the weapon master of [[House Faen Tlabbar]] the fourth house of [[Menzoberranzan]].
==History==
Around [[1358 DR]], Pir'Oront was the elderboy of [[Matron mother|Matron]] [[Ghenni'tiroth Tlabbar]] and was chosen also as weapon master.
