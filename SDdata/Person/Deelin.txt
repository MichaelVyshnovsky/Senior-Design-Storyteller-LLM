Deelin was a dwarf wizard who lived in Neverwinter during the late 14 century DR.
Like almost all the other students of the Neverwinter Academy, Deelin was fond of Lady Aribeth and was grateful that she didnt blame all the wizards during the spreading of the Wailing Death and other events in Neverwinter.
During the graduation ceremony in 1372 DR, Deelin was chatting with other students, Kiki and Fonlim.
Deelin was a student of the Neverwinter Academy and finished her studies in the . Deelin was one of the eight students, among whom was the future Hero of Neverwinter, present at the graduation ceremony of that year. During the ceremony led by Lady Aribeth, the Academy was attacked and she fought against the mysterious mages in the Graduation Chamber. It is not known if Deelin survived the attack.
