Korienna Redarm was a human female bandit leader in southern Amn around the .
Korienna was carried Mordemar, a magic longsword that was particularly effective against undead creatures. It was a gift from her grandfather.
Korienna was originally an officer, as indicated by her full plate armor and shield, but deserted the Council Army and chose a lawless life. She led a small band of about a dozen well-equipped deserters and they marauded up and down the South Road between Eshpurta and Hillfort Keshla, and perhaps beyond, robbing travelers for their horses and other valuables.
