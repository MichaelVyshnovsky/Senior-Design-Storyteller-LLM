Orion Goodbeard was an inhabitant of Baldurs Gate who died some years before the late 15 century DR.
Orion Goodbeard died sometime before the  and was buried in the Lower City Graveyard, next to the Grand Mausoleum, sharing a grave with Alexer Goodbeard. The two were known of their explorations of the Shadowfell. Their shred gravestone read "Adventurers Alexer and Orion Goodbeard - Intrepid explorers of the Plane of Shadows." The Goodbeard grave may had been accidentally desecrated, and their bodies risen as a zombies by Nina Dortmell, a talented child with a gift, who was trying to resurrect her recently deceased brother.
