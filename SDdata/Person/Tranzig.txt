Tranzig was an Iron Throne operative during the time of the iron crisis of 1368 DR. He acted as messenger between Tazok and Mulahey.
Being the messenger between Mulahey and Tazok, Tranzig often traveled between the Nashkel mine to either Peldvale or Larswood in the Wood of Sharp Teeth. His superiors paid him well for his discretion.
The mage owned one of the ring of the princes and carried with him a wand of magic missiles.
Circa the , he petrified the cleric Branwen during a caravan raid.
