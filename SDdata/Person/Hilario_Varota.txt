Hilario Varota was a middle class inhabitant of the city of Baldurs Gate in late 15 century DR.
Hilario was a male rock gnome light brown wavy curls and amber eyes. He wore a fine quality doublet of bright colors.
In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldurs Gate, Hilario Varota could be found in the Lower City, gossiping with his dwarvish friend, Megthanet. Megthanet expressed her disgust with the food at a local eatery, the Elfsong tavern, and the two discussed the death of Duke Belynne Stelmane. Hilario was adamant that only the locals knew knew the story of her death, and not the Baldurs Mouth Gazette.
