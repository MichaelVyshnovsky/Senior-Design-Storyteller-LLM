Julain DeSpri was a ghost noble in Undermountain in 1479 DR.
In his past, Julain was a magistrate in Waterdeep. He was buried in Undermountain after his death with his wife Amori DeSpri.
In 1479 DR, the vampire Dayan resurrected Julain as a ghost during an experiment. Julain wandered alone inside his tomb.
He was concerned about Amori but urged her to continue to live without him protecting their family.
