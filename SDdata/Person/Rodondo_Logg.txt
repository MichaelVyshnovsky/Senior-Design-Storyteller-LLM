Rodond Logg was an inhabitant of Baldurs Gates Lower City in the late 15 century DR.
Rodondo was a light-skinned half wood elf who kept his head shaven. He wore simple brown tunic, trousers, and a green hat.
In the , during the Absolute crisis, Rodondo Logg frequented The Singing Lute alongside his significant other, a fellow half-elf man named Fuschia Vineshoot. Rodondo hated the Lute whilst his darling loved the place and was clueless of Rodonds true feelings.
