Laele Zauviir, most knew as StreeaValsharess Zauviir, was the high priestess of Lolth in the city of Sshamath in the year 1370 DR.
In 1379 DR StreeaValsharess together with her nephew Guldor Zauviir and Tlar Mizzrynturl devised a scheme to gain more power in the Conclave of Sshamath but failed.
Later they discussed about why their plot failed. Tlar killed Guldor and vanished and the blame for the killing fell to StreeaValsharess.
After Tlar killed also StreeaValsharess and took control of her temple.
