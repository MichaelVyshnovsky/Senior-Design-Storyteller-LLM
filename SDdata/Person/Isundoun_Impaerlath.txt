'''Isundoun Impaerlath''' was a [[dwarven]] blacksmith inhabiting [[Saerloon]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Isundoun's claim to fame was the special enchanted ''[[Axe of Hurling|axes of hurling]]'' that he forged. He was a well-known [[adventurer]] in his earlier days before he fired up his forge.
