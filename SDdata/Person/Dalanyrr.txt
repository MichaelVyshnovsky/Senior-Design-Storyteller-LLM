'''Dalanyrr''' was a [[dragon aging|young]] [[silver dragon]] that dwelled in [[the North]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
She respected the sacred spirit mounds of the [[Uthgardt]] barbarian tribes, and would not plunder them if requested.

==Activities==
Dalanyrr would offer her services to help [[adventurer]]s, if they provided her some type of payment.
