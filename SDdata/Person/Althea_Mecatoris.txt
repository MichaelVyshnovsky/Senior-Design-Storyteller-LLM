Lady '''Althea Mecatoris''' was the matriarch of [[House Mecatoris]] and one of the merchant lords of [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]], as of [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|8}}

==Description==
Althea was a tall, thin human female with steel-colored hair.

==Relationships==
Althea was the mother of [[Davien Mecatoris]].

==History==
In 1479 DR, Althea was one of the merchant lords, the rulers of Sambral.

That same year, Althea helped a band of [[adventurer]]s to destroy a cell of the [[Cult of Reborn Flame]] that operated in Sambral.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|45}}

In [[1480 DR]], Althea allowed the adventurers to use one of her merchant ships to sail to [[Imdolphyn]] when the true identity of [[Inar Venenellin|Pyrultimus]] was discovered, in order to put an end to the cult for good.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Quench the Fire of the Raging God|6}}
