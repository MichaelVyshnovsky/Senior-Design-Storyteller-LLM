Zark was a kobold from the Dragons Rest cloister in the late 15 century DR.
Zark was characterized by rudeness and he took pleasure in hurling vivid insults. His favorites were "Eat my sword, bugbear breath!" and "Your father was a gas spore!"
Circa the , Zark shared with three other kobolds a small monastic cell in the Dragons Rest cloister cut into the cliff face. Each of the four slept on individual hammocks. Just like all the other kobolds, he worked at night because of their sunlight sensitivity.
