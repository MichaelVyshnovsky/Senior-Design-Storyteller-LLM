'''Narle Shieldbiter''' was an [[orog]] chieftain of the [[Burning Banner]] tribe in [[Thar]] and a mercenary in the [[Moonsea]] in [[1489 DR]].

==Personality==
Narle was persuaded by Sharrak's words to help the [[Cult of the Dragon]] and believed that his tribe was chosen to serve the soon-to-be [[dragon]] kings. They changed their artwork of war to honor dragons.{{Cite adventurers league/Outlaws of the Iron Route|23}}
Narle's model was [[Obould]] of [[Many-Arrows]] and he dream to one day rivaled the feats of that great king but in truth he not have the demeanor or discipline for it. He was also vain and greedy.{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|16}}

==History==
Narle was hired by [[Sharrak]], a cultist spy who had infiltrated the [[Claws of Tiamat]] bandit group. Sharrak instructed Narle to wait for a signal to attack and destroy a meeting between the Claws and another bandit group, the [[Gray Patriots]]. Narle led the attack, but in the fighting was defeated by an adventuring party hired to clear the [[Iron Route]] of outlaws.{{Cite adventurers league/Outlaws of the Iron Route|23}} However he survived and allied with the [[stone giant]] shaman [[Kranum]] (unknown to anyone possessed by [[Tyranthraxus]]) in order to entered together at the service of new ruler of [[Phlan]] [[green dragon]] [[Vorgansharax]]. However he was confronted by an adventuring party hired to stop the Vorgansharax's cultists and defeated.{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|13,16,21}} 

Narle survived again however and around [[1492 DR]] was one of the leading lieutenant of [[Vorgansharax]] in [[Phlan]] but was killed for good by some adventurers during the reclamation of the city.{{Cite adventurers league/Reclamation of Phlan|77-78}}
