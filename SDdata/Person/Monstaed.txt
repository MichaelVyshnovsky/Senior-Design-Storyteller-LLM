Lady Monstaed was a wealthy woman of Yhaunn in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
By 1373 DR, Lady Monstaed was an elderly woman.
Lady Monstaed was a widow, and inherited a number of properties from her husband. Around Flamerule of the , she was secretly replaced by the Thultantharan priestess of Shar Variance Amatick using her magic ring, and Keph Thingoleirs assault on Lyraene, allowing her to modify Lyraenes memories of the events and keep Keph from further trouble.
After Variance was revealed and driven from Yhaunn on the new moon of Eleasis, it remained to be seen what had become of the real Lady Monstaed.
