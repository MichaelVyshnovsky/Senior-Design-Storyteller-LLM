'''Derrnog''' was a [[warrior]] and ruler of [[hill giant]]s in the [[Plain of Standing Stones]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Derrnog stood over {{SI|20|ft|long=yes}} tall.

==Personality==
Derrnog was observed by [[Ibn Al'Arif]] as being surprisingly intelligent.

==History==
During his time as ruler the hill giants in the Plain of Standing Stones were frequently waging a concentrated war against the local [[hobgoblin]]s, as well as attacks on the occasional [[fomorian]].
