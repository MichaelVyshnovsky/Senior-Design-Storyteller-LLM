Cassius was a dire wolf that resided in the Astral Plane and was summoned by a unique figurine of wondrous power carved in a likeness of a wolf.
Cassius was bound to a figurine of wondrous power that had the wolfs name inscribed on its side. In order to summon Cassius, the owner of its figurine needed to rub the magic item. This could be one once per day.
Most of Cassiuss history remained a mystery. One of the wolfs companion was a druid from Cormanthor, Branon Oakheart. The duo was attacked a group of fiends and Cassius lured the creatures away from Branon, allowing him to escape.
