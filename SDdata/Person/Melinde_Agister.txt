'''Melinde Agister''' was a [[spirit binder]] of the [[Church of Amaunator]], who, for a time, operated out of [[The Lonesome Tankard Inn and Tavern]] in the town of [[Eveningstar]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Melinde was a [[human]] woman with light skin and red braided hair. She wore a set of Amaunatoran [[chain armor]], decorated with the deity's ''[[holy symbol]]''.

==History==
In the 15{{th}} century DR, Melinde Agister stayed at the Lonesome Tankard in Eveningster, offering her church's aid to the off-world adventurers who set out to investigate the nearby ruins of the [[Haunted Halls]].
