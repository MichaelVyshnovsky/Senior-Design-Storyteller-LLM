Melinde Agister was a spirit binder of the Church of Amaunator, who, for a time, operated out of The Lonesome Tankard Inn and Tavern in the town of Eveningstar in the 15 century DR.
Melinde was a human woman with light skin and red braided hair. She wore a set of Amaunatoran chain armor, decorated with the deitys holy symbol.
In the 15 century DR, Melinde Agister stayed at the Lonesome Tankard in Eveningster, offering her churchs aid to the off-world adventurers who set out to investigate the nearby ruins of the Haunted Halls.
