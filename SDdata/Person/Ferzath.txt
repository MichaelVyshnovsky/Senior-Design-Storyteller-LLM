Ferzath was a black dragon and one of the Princes of Murghôm in 1479 DR.
Ferzath was one of the princes of Murghôm who participated in Brimstones xorvintaal.
He was one of the dragons Alasklerbanbastos sought out to attack Luthcheq and destroy the Brotherhood of the Griffon after the dracolich and Brimstone discovered Aoth Fezim attempts to disrupt the xorvintaal.
During the Battle of Luthcheq, Ferzath was killed by the gold dragon Chelnadatilar.
