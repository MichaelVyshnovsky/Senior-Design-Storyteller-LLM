Churirajachi Versengethor was a dragonborn member of Clan Churirajachi, and the Vanquisher of Tymanther in the period of 1467 to 1477 DR.
Versengethor was the father of Churirajachi Kerashna.
Versengethor was the predecessor of Tarhun. His tenure as a Vanquisher was considered the most militaristic in the history of Tymanther so far.
