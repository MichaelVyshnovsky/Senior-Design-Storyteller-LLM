Kaulgetharr Drell was the Master of Beasts for the king of Cormyr in the mid-14 century DR.
He had strong, hairy hands, and kept his beard styled with a goatee. His eyes were dark, and his mien was cruel.
He did not show much respect to War Wizards of Cormyr.
He could use spells to make a trapper follow him around.
He kept control over at least two panthers and a trapper.
Kaulgetharr Drell came across Bolifar Geldert in the hours prior to his disappearance in Alturiak , while he was on his way to a clear room.
