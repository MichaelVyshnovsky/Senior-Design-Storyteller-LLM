'''Kaulgetharr Drell''' was the Master of Beasts for the king of [[Cormyr]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He had strong, hairy hands, and kept his beard styled with a goatee. His eyes were dark, and his mien was cruel.

==Personality==
He did not show much respect to [[War Wizard]]s of [[Cormyr]].

==Abilities==
He could use spells to make a [[trapper]] follow him around.

==Possessions==
He kept control over at least two [[panther]]s and a [[trapper]].

==History==
Kaulgetharr Drell came across [[Bolifar Geldert]] in the hours prior to his disappearance in [[Alturiak]] {{yearlink|1365}}{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|2|Realmslore}}, while he was on his way to a clear room.{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|87|5}}
