Sefer al-Raq was an inhabitant of the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.
As a priest of Kossuth and staunch member of the Brotherhood of the True Flame, Sefer was tasked with recruiting potential members for the Brotherhood from the efreeti city.
Sefer was rumored to be the lover of Jamina al-Mubarak, the Brotherhoods ambassador in the City of Brass.
