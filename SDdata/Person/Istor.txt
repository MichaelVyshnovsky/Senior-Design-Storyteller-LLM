Istor was a wolf kept by the Zhentarim operating within the organizations hideout found within a massive cave found beneath the Waukeens Rest inn during the late 15 century DR.
Istor was set to guard an pathway in the rear of the Zhentarim hideout that led down into the depths of the Underdark.Istors keen sense of smell made suspicious of the illusory wall that obscured the entrance to this pathway, though as a wolf he did not fully comprehend the discrepancy between his senses.
The massive wolf was tasked with guard duty as the Zhentarim planned to demolish their hideout in the , to avoid detection by the Flaming Fist.
