Benryn was a man that lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.
He was married to a woman named Mirileth, whom he loved very much.
In the , Benryn and Mirileth spent some time at the Waukeens Rest inn, which was found on the Risen Road west of Elturel. The two were carrying with them a dowry for Mirileths sisters wedding a simple ring of gold.
Unfortunately, the couples stay was interrupted, when goblin raiders from the Cult of the Absolute assailed the inn and set it aflame. Benryn remained trapped in the inns upper story, while Mirileth, who remained outside, was not so fortunate.
