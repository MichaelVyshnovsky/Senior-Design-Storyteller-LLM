'''Phredd''' was a [[giant fire beetle]] who lived in [[Marigold's Menagerie]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Phredd was quite a popular attraction among visitors to Marigold's Menagerie, especially children. The latter of whom delighted in watching the beast light up.
