'''Sepur''' was the tenth [[High Mage (Silverymoon)|High Mage]] of [[Silverymoon]].

==Activities==
Sepur briefly held the position of High Mage from the {{YearlinkName|1230}}, to the {{YearlinkName|1232}}, before abandoning his position.

He was slain by an unknown assailant after he left Silverymoon.

==History==
Sepur was the apprentice of High Mage [[Orjalun]] who later appointed him as High Mage upon his retirement.
