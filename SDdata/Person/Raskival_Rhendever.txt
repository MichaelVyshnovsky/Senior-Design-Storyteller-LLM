'''Raskival Rhendever''' was a merchant of [[Suzail]] in the mid-15{{th}} century [[DR]].If it is assumed Arclath Delcastle is between 19 and 39 years old, the time in which Raskival Rhendever was using the house must fall between 1460 and 1440 DR, which is the mid-15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
The man was "crabbed".

==Description==
To [[Arclath Delcastle]]'s recollection, the man was hunched, and walked with two canes.

==History==
In Arclath Delcastle's youth, Raskival Rhendever had owned a house later used by [[Larak Dardulkyn]].
