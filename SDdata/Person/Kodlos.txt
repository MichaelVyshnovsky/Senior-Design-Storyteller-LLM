'''Kodlos''' was a [[Sembia]]n who was alive around [[1188 DR]].

He led the discussions with King [[Pryntaler Obarskyr]] in an attempt to acquire the city of [[Marsember]]. The others in his group were [[Homfast]], Lady [[Threnka]], [[Bennesey]] and [[Jollitha Par]].
