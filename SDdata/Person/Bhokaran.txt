Bhokaran was a priest of Haku in Huzuz in 1367 DR.
Bhokaran was an athletic young man.
Keen intelligence helped Bhokaran rise quickly within the priesthood of Haku.
He served as the spiritual advisor for Prince Tannous al-Assad.
Bhokaran had a close relationship with Prince Tannous and High Priest Gholam ibn Jubair.
