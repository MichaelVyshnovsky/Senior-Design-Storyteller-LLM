'''Melder Rythtin of the Healing Hand''' was a priest of [[Ilmater]] and leader of the [[House of the Broken God]] temple in [[Keltar]], [[Calimshan]], ''circa'' [[1369 DR]].

Melder was renowned for his accurate diagnoses and miraculous treatments of those afflicted with suffering.
