'''Thloram Flambaertyn''' was an adventurer of the [[Brightstar Sash]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was an older man, with a tufted beard.

==Personality==
He was easy on the jokes.

==Abilities==
He made an amazing [[hotspice stew]].

==Activities==
He was an adventurer.

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Brightstar Sash]].

==History==
Thloram was already a member of the Brightstar Sash when they recruited the [[sorceress]] [[Caladnei]], and he cautioned against letting her join too swiftly, as she was young. She was ultimately allowed to join, and during one of their forays together, the Sash recovered a treasure of at least 6,000 [[gp]].{{cite book/Elminster's Daughter/MMP|277|17}}

When [[Vonda]] and [[Rimardo]] attempted to rob a nobleman's house at [[Waterdeep]] only to be captured and slain, Thloram sough out Caladnei and warned her to escape. In their getaway, Thloram had to fight a large, enraged mob.

Thloram Flambaertyn died after a fall in the [[Great Rift]] at some point before {{yearlink|1373}},{{cite book/Elminster's Daughter/MMP|282-283|17}}
