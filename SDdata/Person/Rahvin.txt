'''Rahvin''' was a mercenary who operated out of the nation of [[Sembia]] in the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was a skilled archer and was known to employ ''[[arrow of detonation|arrows of detonation]]'' in combat.

==Relationships==
Rahvin's mercenary companions included the [[ogre]] [[Gorf]], two fellow warriors [[Carston (mercenary)|Carston]] and [[Haseo]], the [[mage]] [[Shaldrissa]], and a [[cleric]] named [[Wudei]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, Rahvin and his allies were dispatched by the Sembian [[Iron Throne]] to hunt down and kill [[Sarevok Anchev]], who had caused the collapse of their western branch in [[Baldur's Gate]].

Rahvin's group tracked him down to the subterranean [[Temple of Bhaal (Undercity)|Temple of Bhaal]] in the [[Undercity]]. There they encountered [[Abdel Adrian]] and his adventuring party, and attempted to claim the bounty placed upon his head.
