Imbrult Longclaws was a curse-ridden War Wizard of Cormyr.
During a mission against some outlaws he was cursed by Manshoon riding a black dragon.
In 1480 DR, Longclaws went to Castle Irlingstar with Nostyn Vandur. He tried to stop Lord Steward Gelnur Farland, Arclath Delcastle, and Amarune Whitewave from opening the cells.
