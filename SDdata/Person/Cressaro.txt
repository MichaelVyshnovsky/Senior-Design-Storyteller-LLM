Cressaro was the cloud giant castellan of Lyn Armaal sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.
Cressaro was a young giant in the late 1480s or early 1490s DR. He was the castellan, consort and personal bodyguard of Countess Sansuri.
His normal duty was overseeing the correct flying of Lyn Armaal.
Cressaro followed the orders of Sansuri without doubts, deeply loyal to her.
