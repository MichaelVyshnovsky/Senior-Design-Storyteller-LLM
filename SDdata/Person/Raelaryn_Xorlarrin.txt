Raelaryn Xorlarrin was an elder of the Council of Spiders.
Raelaryn invited his cousin, Hoshtar Xorlarrin, to meet with the Council in the compound of the fallen House SruneLett in Narbondellyn. During the meeting, he and the other elder present were assassinated by a member of the Jaezred Chaulssin to start a civil war.
