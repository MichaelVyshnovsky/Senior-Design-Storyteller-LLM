Haeleeya Hanadroum  was a shopkeeper in Red Larch and an Emerald Enclave supporter during the Elemental Evil crisis of 1491 DR.
Since they saved her life, Haeleeya always supported the Enclave, and she shared with them whatever interesting secret talk she heard in the bathhouse.
Haeleeya managed the village bathhouse in Red Larch.
In her youth, Haeleeya became lost in the wilderness but was saved by an Emerald Enclave ranger. She later moved from Amn to Red Larch.
