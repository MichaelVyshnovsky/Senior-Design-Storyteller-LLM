'''Sharandra''' was a princess of southern [[Faerûn]] in the late 8{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Rumors & Legends==
According to the sage [[Tabarast]] of the [[Moonshorn Tower]], Princess Sharandra had woken up to find that nine [[tressym]] had birthed four kittens each on top of her one night. While he wondered how she slept through that, he considered it one of the signs of the Gods in the {{yearlinkName|761}}.
