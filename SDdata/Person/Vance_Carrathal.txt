'''Vance Carrathal''' was High King of [[Callidyrr]] in the midâ€“13{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|33}} Like his predecessors, although he held the title of High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]], he was considered to be merely a figurehead by the [[Ffolk]] outside of Callidyrr.

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Carrathal Dynasty]] and was the first son of his predecessor, [[Cedric Carrathal|King Cedric]]. His eventual successor was his own son, [[Teldin Carrathal|Teldin]].

==History==
Vance assumed the throne in the {{YearlinkName|1243}}, after the death of his father. He ruled for twenty-three years before he died of natural causes in the {{YearlinkName|1266}}.
