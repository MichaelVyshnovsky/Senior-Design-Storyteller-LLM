'''Cork Renford''' was a [[human]] [[fighter]] and [[adventurer]] of the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He had a wanderlust and a desire for excitement, and he was on the lookout for adventure.

==Activities==
Cork aimed to join a promising [[adventuring party]] or find employment as a bodyguard for a traveler. Hence, he was often found in taverns and inns while looking for work or a hook. He would undertake any mission or adventure that promised him excitement.

==Possessions==
Among Cork's adventuring gear, he wore a [[Helmet|helm]], a [[Leather armor|leather]] [[breastplate]], and an [[Item enchantment|enchanted]] ''+1 [[chain mail]]'', and wielded a ''[[Sword of quickness|shortsword of quickness]]''.
