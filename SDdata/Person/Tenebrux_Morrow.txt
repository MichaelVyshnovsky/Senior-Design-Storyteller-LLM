'''Tenebrux Morrow''' was an author who lived in the city of [[Waterdeep]]. She wrote herself into a series of pulp novels, entitled ''[[The True and Impossible Adventures of Tenebrux Morrow]]'', about the the captains' adventures aboard her planes-hopping vessel the ''[[New Bride]]''.

==Activities==
While the fictional Captain Morrow was a daring adventurer, who met iconic characters across the planes, the real Tenebrux Morrow was a mild-mannered person who rarely left her room at the [[Yawning Portal]] inn. She traded in her new manuscripts for meals and writing supplies.

==History==
Her most famous works were published sometime during or before the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
