Karad was a member of the Zhentarim that operated out of their hideout in some caves on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.
He appeared to have a particularly strong dislike of the Flaming Fist, especially those that accepted Zhentarim bribes but still threatened their business.
Karad carried with him a shortbow that he wielded with some skill.
When Zarys ordered the demolition of their hideout in the , Karad was among those gathering their goods and setting up explosives for demolition of the caves.
