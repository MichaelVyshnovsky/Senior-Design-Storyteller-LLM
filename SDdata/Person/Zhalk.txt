Commander '''Zhalk''' was a [[cambion]] in the service of [[Zariel]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Zhalk was a medium-sized [[fiend]] with large wings. He wore heavy armor.

==Abilities==
Zhalk was skilled with [[sword]]s and was resistant to many types of damage.

==History==
In the 1490s DR, Zhalk and a squadron of [[imp]]s and cambions raided a [[nautiloid]] that was flying through [[Avernus]]. He attempted to slay the [[mind flayer]] that was piloting it.
