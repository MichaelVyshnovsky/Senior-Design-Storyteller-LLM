Commander Zhalk was a cambion in the service of Zariel in the late 15 century DR.
Zhalk was a medium-sized fiend with large wings. He wore heavy armor.
Zhalk was skilled with swords and was resistant to many types of damage.
In the 1490s DR, Zhalk and a squadron of imps and cambions raided a nautiloid that was flying through Avernus. He attempted to slay the mind flayer that was piloting it.
