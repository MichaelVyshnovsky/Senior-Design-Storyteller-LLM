High Scrivener Melendilar Huantilar was the high priest of the Hallowed House of Higher Achievement, a temple dedicated to Deneir, in Selgaunt in 1358 DR.
Melendilar was the leader of Deneirs faithful in Selgaunt. His congregation within the temple consisted of 18 other priests and 21 followers.
