Jardwim was the leader of the Gray Hands adventuring company who helped guard the city of Waterdeep.
He had a stocky, muscular build and shoulder length black hair. His skin was a grayish hue.
He was a follower of Mielikki. He was ruthless and indomitable. Jardwim felt that resistance should be met by lethal force and his concentration was legendary.
Jardwim had two blink dogs. As leader of the Gray Hands, he reported to Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun.
