'''Quarlo''' was the innkeeper at the [[Winsome Repose]] in [[Zazesspur]] in [[1366 DR]].

==Description==
Quarlo was a small man, wiry and thin. He had dark hair and beady eyes. He wore an [[apron]].

==History==
When the civic guard of Zazesspur came to arrest&mdash;for a third time&mdash;[[Zaranda Star]], who had been staying at Quarlo's inn, he notified the guards that she had left the city.
