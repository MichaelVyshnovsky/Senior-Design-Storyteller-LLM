Quarlo was the innkeeper at the Winsome Repose in Zazesspur in 1366 DR.
Quarlo was a small man, wiry and thin. He had dark hair and beady eyes. He wore an apron.
When the civic guard of Zazesspur came to arrest&mdashfor a third time&mdashZaranda Star, who had been staying at Quarlos inn, he notified the guards that she had left the city.
