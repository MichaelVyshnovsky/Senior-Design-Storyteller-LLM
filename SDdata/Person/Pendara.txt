'''Pendara the Slim''' was a skilled [[wizard]] and [[adventurer]] inhabiting [[Urmlaspyr]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Description==
Pendara was quite attractive and slender.

==Activities==
Pendara once rode an untamed [[griffon]] and crashed in front of her house on [[Windsummer Street]] in the {{YearlinkName|1352}}.
