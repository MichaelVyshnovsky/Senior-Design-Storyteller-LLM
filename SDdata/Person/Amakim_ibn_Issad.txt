'''Amakim ibn Issad''' was the first king of the great city of [[Muluk]] in the [[Free Cities of Zakhara|Free Cities]] of [[North Zakhara]].

==History==
Amakim founded Muluk during the fifth century [[DR]]. As of [[1367 DR]],{{AQ-time}} his blood line had ruled Muluk for over 900 years.
