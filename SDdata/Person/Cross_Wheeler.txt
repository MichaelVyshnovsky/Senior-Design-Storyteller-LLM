'''Cross Wheeler''' was the innkeeper of [[The Way Inn]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==History==
in the late 1480s or early 1490s DR Cross retired from his work as ranger and became the innkeeper of The Way Inn.

==Relationship==
Cross had many friends among the [[wood elves]] of the [[Misty Forest]].
