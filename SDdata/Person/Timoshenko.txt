'''Timoshenko''' was a [[human]] [[fighter]] and the [[Grandfather of Assassins]], the leader of the [[Citadel of Assassins]], in [[Damara]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He was previously a commander of its army.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1357}}, Timoshenko was the commander of the bandit army when they attacked [[Bloodstone Village]].

He rose to power after [[Kane]] defeated the [[Unnamed Grandfather of Assassins|last Grandfather]]. Backed by the [[archmage]] [[Knellict]], Timoshenko surpassed [[Banak]] in power, a once mighty priest of [[Orcus]], who was the other candidate for leader of the Citadel.

==Personality==
Although Timoshenko was a mighty warrior he was thoughtful, conservative, and took advice from Knellict often.

==Activities==
Timoshenko played the political face of the organization and let his lieutenants carry out the dirty work.<ref>
