Marten was a toll collector who lived and worked on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.
He had gray-white hair that he worn nealy brushed back.
Marten was killed in the , when goblin cultists of the Absolute raided the tollhouse at which he worked. Marten, Vance, and their fellows were abandoned by the tollhouses guards. They attempted to flee and hide, but were ultimately cut down.
