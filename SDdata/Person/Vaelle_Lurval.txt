'''Vaelle Lurval''' ({{Pronounce|vay|ELLE|_|lur|VAWL|refs=}}) was a florist and a follower of [[Asmodeus]] in [[Waterdeep]] during the early 1490s [[DR]].
 
==Activities==
Vaelle was a member of the cult led by [[Victoro Cassalanter]].

==History==
In [[1492 DR]],{{WDH-time}} if the [[Cassalanter]]s managed to obtain the ''[[Stone of Golorr]]'', Vaelle, her boyfriend [[Holiver Tornrudder]] and three other cultists protected the Stone inside the Cassalanter mausoleum in the [[City of the Dead (Waterdeep)|City of the Dead]]. However two of the cultists, under the secret orders of Victoro Cassalanter, killed Holiver and the other cultist and left Vaelle for dead.

==Relationship==
Vaelle was the girlfriend of Holiver, who'd convinced her to join the Asmodeus cult.
