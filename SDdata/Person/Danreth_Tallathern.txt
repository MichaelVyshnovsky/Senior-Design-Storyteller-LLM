Danreth Tallathern was a key member of the Seekers of the DawnÂ in Myth Drannor in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.
Danreth was an adventurer and diplomat in Westgate until his faith in Lathander directed him to Myth Drannor, where he joined the Seekers.
Danreth acted as liaison between the Seekers and their guests, discreetly evaluating them. He was aided by three beautiful adventuresses, Shandel Iribar, Jathalaera Sire, and Arleera Landreth.
Danreth was very capable in reading peoples expressions and knew very well any trick or stratagems that adventurers or shady folk tried.
Danreth had a wild talent ESP power.
