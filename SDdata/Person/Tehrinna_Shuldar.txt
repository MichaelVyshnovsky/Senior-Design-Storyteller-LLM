'''Tehrinna the Towering''' was the proprietor of the [[Tymoran Trails]] inn of [[Crimmor]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|30}}

==Description==
Tehrinna was a red-haired woman of forceful character and striking good looks. She was a former adventurer known for her wealth and for her sense of humor.
She owed her nickname to her great stature, being about {{SI|7|feet}} tall.

==Relationships==
Tehrinna was the lover of [[Mikaal Krimmevol]], head of one of the ''grand families'' of Crimmor.
