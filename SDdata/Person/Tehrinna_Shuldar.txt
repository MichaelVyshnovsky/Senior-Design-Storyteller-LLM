Tehrinna the Towering was the proprietor of the Tymoran Trails inn of Crimmor during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Tehrinna was a red-haired woman of forceful character and striking good looks. She was a former adventurer known for her wealth and for her sense of humor.
She owed her nickname to her great stature, being about  tall.
Tehrinna was the lover of Mikaal Krimmevol, head of one of the grand families of Crimmor.
