Farnath Ilistar was the first King of the Ilistar Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate.
Farnath was a skilled wizard under the tutelage of the Handweaver. After Nessmara, the lamia noble who killed King Lyonarth, was revealed and slain in a battle to the death that also claimed Lyonarth, Farnath was named King in the .
His firstborn son and heir was Belhendar "the Stout".
