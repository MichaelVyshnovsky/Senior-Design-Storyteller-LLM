'''Farnath Ilistar''' was the first King of the [[Ilistar Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Farnath was a skilled [[wizard]] under the tutelage of the [[Handweaver]]. After [[Nessmara]], the [[lamia noble]] who killed King [[Lyonarth]], was revealed and slain in a battle to the death that also claimed Lyonarth, Farnath was named King in the {{YearlinkName|616}}.

==Relationships==
His firstborn son and heir was [[Belhendar Ilistar|Belhendar "the Stout"]].
