'''Garrod Phipps''' was a [[human]] resident of [[Eveningstar]] in [[Cormyr]] during the mid-1480s [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}

==Description==
Garrod had light skin and medium-length dark brown hair, tied back. His outfit included an extensive belt of [[pouch]]es.

==History==
{{Quote
|I saw [[Elminster]]! As big as life and looking just like the story-books say.
|Garrod
}}
Around the time of the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar]], Garrod worked as a craftsman in Eveningstar. He was quite thrilled to have seen the famous [[Elminster]], despite the circumstances.
