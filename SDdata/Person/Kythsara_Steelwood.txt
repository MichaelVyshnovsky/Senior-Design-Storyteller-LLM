Mage Arcane '''Kythsara Steelwood''', who was nicknamed the '''Silent''', was a powerful [[wizard]] inhabiting [[Saerloon]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Kythsara was a devout follower of [[Mystra]] and one of several leaders of the [[Tower of Mystery]] temple in Saerloon.
