'''Kawthar bint Husn umm Ra'id''' was a [[aquatic elf|sea elf]] living in [[Chaos Bay]] near [[Hawa]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Kawthar ruled the sea elves inhabiting Chaos Bay with her husband.

==Possessions==
She owned a set of [[bracers of defense]] and carried a magical [[jambiya]].

==Relationships==
Kawthar was the mother of [[Ra'id bin Kawthar]].
