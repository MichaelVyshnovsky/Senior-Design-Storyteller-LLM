'''Ganather''' was a [[wizard]] active in [[Secomber]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/417/Eye on the Realms: The Sword of Spells}}

==History==
''Circa'' the {{yearlinkName|1454}}, Ganather volunteered to act as a lure to help [[Elminster]] capture the [[Sword of Savras]]. The capture succeeded, but Ganather unfortunately lost their life in the process.
