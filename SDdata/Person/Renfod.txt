'''Renfod''' was a [[cleric]] and a Loremaster Accordant of the [[Order of Blue Flame]].

==Appearance==
He had short, graying black hair and dark ebony skin. He had cataracts that dulled his brown eyes. He wore pale blue robes.

==History==
In [[1479 DR]], he was assistant to Accordant [[Vraith]].
