==History==
Errick Crivens perished sometime before the fall of Reithwin and it being consumed by the [[Shadow-cursed Lands]] before the {{YearlinkName|1374}}. He was buried in Reithwin's graveyard behind the [[House of Healing]]. His tombstone read: "Here rests Errick Crivens, sole inheritor of the Crivens family. May the [[god]]s forgive him." Errick's grave marker remained undamaged as of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
