'''Tluth Melarn''' was a [[drow]] in [[Menzoberranzan]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He was one of [[Houndaer Tuin'Tarl]] and [[Syrzan]]'s rebel group.<ref group="note">Since [[House Melarn]] was not formed until [[1383 DR]], it's not clear what relation Tluth Melarn has to House Melarn.

==Abilities==
He was highly skilled at massage and was favored by [[Waerva Baenre]].

==History==
During the [[Silence of Lolth]] in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, Tluth joined Houndaer's group desiring to start a slave revolt Tluth and [[Alton Vandree]] acted as bodyguards of the magical disguised Syrzan at one meeting of [[goblinoid]] slaves.
