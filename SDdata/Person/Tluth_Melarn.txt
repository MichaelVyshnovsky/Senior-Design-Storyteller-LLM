Tluth Melarn was a drow in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 century DR. He was one of Houndaer TuinTarl and Syrzans rebel group.Since House Melarn was not formed until 1383 DR, its not clear what relation Tluth Melarn has to House Melarn.
He was highly skilled at massage and was favored by Waerva Baenre.
During the Silence of Lolth in the , Tluth joined Houndaers group desiring to start a slave revolt Tluth and Alton Vandree acted as bodyguards of the magical disguised Syrzan at one meeting of goblinoid slaves.
