'''Korr of Ilmater''' was a knight of [[Ilmater]] circa [[1363 DR]].

==History==
Korr led a company of fifty fellow knights devoted to Ilmater, called simply the [[Knights of Ilmater]]. They came to [[Daggerford]]'s side and joined its alliance during the [[Second Dragonspear War]]. 

==Personality==
Korr was practically manic about his faith. He was brave and reckless to the point of being suicidal.
