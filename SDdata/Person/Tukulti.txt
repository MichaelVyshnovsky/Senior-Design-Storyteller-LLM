Tukulti was the ruler of Firetrees in Unther in 1357 DR.
Tukulti was a supporter of Gilgeam. He wanted to become the right hand of the king but he had no chance.
Tukulti was an idiot who believed that the rebellion was a minor problem. He envied his fellow Great Lords with good reasons.
In 1358 DR, Tukulti was one of the nobles who were on Gilgeams army when Tiamat attacked Unthalass. Tukulti was killed when a rebel soldier impaled him with a lance.
