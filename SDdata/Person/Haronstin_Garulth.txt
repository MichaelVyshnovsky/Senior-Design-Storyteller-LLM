Haronstin "Leatherjaws" Garulth was a cleric of Chauntea with the rank of Close One that resided in the Bounty of the Goddess temple in Voonlar.
__NOTOC__
Haronstin was calm, casual, and carefree.
Leatherjaws was able to consume copious quantities of alcohol without the usual adverse effects.
He drove carts and sold seeds for the Bounty.
