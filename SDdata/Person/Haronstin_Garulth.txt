'''Haronstin "Leatherjaws" Garulth''' was a [[cleric]] of [[Chauntea]] with the rank of Close One that resided in the [[Bounty of the Goddess (Voonlar)|Bounty of the Goddess]] temple in [[Voonlar]].
__NOTOC__
==Personality==
Haronstin was calm, casual, and carefree.

==Abilities==
Leatherjaws was able to consume copious quantities of alcohol without the usual adverse effects.

==Activities==
He drove carts and sold seeds for the Bounty.
