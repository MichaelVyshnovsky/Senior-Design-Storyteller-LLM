Sharp-eye '''Zami''' was a  [[goblin]] member of the [[cult of the Absolute]] who lived in the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Thought [[Tiefling|their]] horns would be bigger. What's the use ifya can't even drink from 'em?|Sharp-eye Zami}}

==Description==
She had yellow skin decorated with red face paint. She wore her hair back in sets of braids.

==Personality==
Zami was among the few goblins of the Shattered Sanctum who focused on her duties to the cult, rather than carousing and fighting for fun.

==Possessions==
The goblin lookout wore a suit of cloth-and-[[leather]] armor and wielded a crude wooden [[bow]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Zami remained Shattered Sanctum while other cultists feasted and drank to celebrate their deadly raid on [[Waukeen's Rest]].
