'''Laric''' was the Captain of the [[Bloodriders]], the personal guard of [[Grunnarch|Grunnarch the Red]].

==History==
In [[1345 DR]], Laric accompanied Grunnarch the Red to [[Oman's Isle]] to the council before attacking the [[Ffolk]] cities in the southern cities of the [[Moonshae Isles]].
