'''Hornclaw''', who was nicknamed '''the Gray''', was a legendary [[owlbear]] inhabiting the [[Ashenwood]] in [[Rashemen]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Rumors & Legends==
Hornclaw was rumored to be over one hundred years old and larger than most [[giant]]s.
