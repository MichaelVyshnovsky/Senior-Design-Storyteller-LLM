Bryon Kendrick was a member of the Kendrick family, the king of Corwell and father to Tristan Kendrick.
King Bryon was a strong man with dark hair and long black beard streaked gray.
Adding to Bryons despair, he was disappointed in the actions of his son, Prince Tristan.
Around 1329 DR, By 1345 DR, Bryon powerful presence had descended into a weakened state of discord.
