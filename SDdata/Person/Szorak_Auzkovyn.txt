Szorak Auzkovyn was a drow priest of Vhaeraun in 1375 DR.
Szorak was a priest from Clan Auzkovyn who joined Malvags plot.
Szorak tried to attack Eilistraees shrine in the Forest of Lethyr, but after infiltrating the shrine and subduing a priestess he was killed by behind.
Szorak was the brother of Seyll Auzkovyn.
