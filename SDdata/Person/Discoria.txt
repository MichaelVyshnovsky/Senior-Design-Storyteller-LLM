Fist '''Discoria''' was a member of the [[Flaming Fist]] mercenary company from the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Discoria was a [[human]] woman with a light skin tone, and brown eyes. She wore a Fist uniform [[helmet]] with her platinum pair streaming from under it. She also wore a set of [[chainmail]] armor decorated with a [[tabard]] embroidered with the Flaming Fist insignia. Fist Discoria wielded a [[polearm]] and defense herself with a massive [[shield]] when on duty.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Fist Discoria was tasked with patrolling the Lower City, along the [[Wendserpent Street]], and near the [[Manor Gate]], accompanied by a group of fellow Fists and a [[steel watcher]].
