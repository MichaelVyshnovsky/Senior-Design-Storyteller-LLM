'''Thorm''' was a [[dwarf]] merchant of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
He partnered with [[Tatigan Carrlock]] and another merchant in the {{YearlinkName|1480}}, to form a caravan that journeyed to [[Rashemen]]. Unknown to Tatigan, Thorm was secretly working with bandits who attacked the caravan along the [[Golden Way]].{{Cite digital book/The Darker Road|2437}}

Throm was killed in a battle with his former comrades when the [[Witches of Rashemen|witch]] [[Ilvani]] and [[Daruk]] the [[bard]] used [[illusion]] [[magic]] to hide a crevasse into which Thorm fell.{{Cite digital book/The Darker Road|3918}}
