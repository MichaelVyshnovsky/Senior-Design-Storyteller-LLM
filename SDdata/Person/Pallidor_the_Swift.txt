'''Pallidor the Swift''' was a [[wood elf]] storyteller from the [[Wood of Sharp Teeth]].

==Personality==
Palidor was considered one of the more venerable elves of his community, and his stories were held in high esteem. It took him some time to build trust and share his stories with outsiders.
