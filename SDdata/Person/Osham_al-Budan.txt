'''Osham al-Budan''' was a [[moralist]] and member of the [[League Conclave]] in [[I'tiraf]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Originally from I'tiraf, Osham was elected to lead the group of delegates from that city to represent them within the League Conclave. Osham and his fellow delegates ensured I'tiraf's best interests were heard at court.
