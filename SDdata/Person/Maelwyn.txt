Maelwyn was a cavalier inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Maelwyn had decent luck working as an adventurer, but preferred the solitude of court life in Daggerford. 
His father was a respected veteran of the militia.
