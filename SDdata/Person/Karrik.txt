Karrik was a warrior and treasure hunter in the Moonwood circa the late 15 century DR.
Karrik was gruff, dour, and greedy. He disliked taking unnecessary risks.
In 1484 DR, Karrik and his companions Lisstra, Dran, and Garn decided to explore the ruins of Methegrist.
