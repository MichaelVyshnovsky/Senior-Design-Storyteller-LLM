'''Karrik''' was a warrior and treasure hunter in the [[Moonwood]] ''circa'' the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
   
==Personality==
Karrik was gruff, dour, and greedy. He disliked taking unnecessary risks.

==History==
In [[1484 DR]], Karrik and his companions [[Lisstra]], [[Dran]], and [[Garn (Dran's brother)|Garn]] decided to explore the ruins of [[Methegrist]].
