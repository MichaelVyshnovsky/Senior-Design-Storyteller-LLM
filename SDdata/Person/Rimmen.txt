Rimmen of the Scarlet Eye was a magelord who carried himself with the attitude of those who practiced the same magic in Athalantar that is, one of supremacy through magical might. He particularly enjoyed showing off with his arcane skill to intimidate others.
Among Rimmens possessions were a amulet of natural armor, a pair of bracers of armor +3, a dusty rose ioun stone, a pair of gloves of dexterity +2, a headband of intellect, two pearls of power, a ring of counterspells, and a +1 shortsword.
In addition, he was known to possess a scroll of clairaudience/clairvoyance, a scroll of false life, a scroll of minor creation, a potion of cure moderate wounds, and two potions of cure light wounds.
