'''Plod Graveltoe''' was a [[deep gnome]] in [[Mulmaster]].

==History==
Plod led a svirfneblin expedition into the Mulmaster sewers.{{Cite adventurers league/The Sword of Selfaril|17}} He and his men were attacked by some [[black pudding]]s but could have been saved by some adventurers working for [[Selfaril Uoumdolphin]].

==Relationship==
Plod had good dealings with the [[Emerald Enclave]].
