'''Dalabrindar''' was a [[Netherese]] [[wizard]]. He sacrificed himself as part of one of [[Karsus]]'s experiments.
{{quote|...Know, then, that touching the speculum that evoked this image of me, while speaking the word ''Dalabrindar'', will heal all hurts. This power can be called upon as often as desired for so long as this rune remains unbroken, and can so serve anyone who speaks thus. The word is the name of the wizard who died so that this spell might live, truly, he has served us well, and...|Message left in Karsus' Keep by Karsus, last activated in the {{yearlinkName|767}}}}

==Relationships==
He was a servant of Karsus.

==History==
One of the runes at [[Karsus' Keep]] was triggered by the word "Dalabrindar", which according to Karsus was the name of the wizard who sacrificed his life for that rune to function.
