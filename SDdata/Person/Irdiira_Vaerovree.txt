'''Irdiira Vaerovree''' was an inhabitant of [[Yallasch]] and a parent to [[Talatha Vaerovree]].

==History==
Their daughter was taken in a pirate raid, in which [[Mirt]] participated.

==Rumors & Legends==
Their daughter, Talatha Vaerovree, believed they'd died in the pirate raid where she was taken.
