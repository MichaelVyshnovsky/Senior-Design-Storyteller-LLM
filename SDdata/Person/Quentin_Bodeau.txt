[[Pasha]] '''Quentin Bodeau''' was the most recent leader of [[Pook|House Pook]], a thieves guild in [[Calimport]]. He started as a [[thief]] and became master of the house after the death of [[Pook]] and the disappearance of [[Regis]].

The house's wizard, [[LaValle]], considered him a good man and a competent master.
