Ondar was a farmer in the village of Toralth in Tethyr in 1370 DR.
In 1370 DR, he lost six head of cattle to some gnolls.
Soon after, on Uktar 10, Ondar was invited by his best friend Tadaz to the Blue Badger Inn in order to honor Tadazs son Tural heading off to join Tethyrs new army at Riatavin.
