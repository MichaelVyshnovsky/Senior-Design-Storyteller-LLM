==Relationships==
Keptus Hunzrin was a cousin of Matron Mother [[Shakti Hunzrin]] and the 15{{th}} century DR master of [[Melee-Magthere]], [[Syr'daun Hunzrin]]. Syr'daun's relationship with Keptus allowed the master of Melee-Magthere to lobby with Matron Mother for attacking lower and weaker Houses of Menzoberranzan.

==History==
Keptus Hunzrin claimed the title of the House Weapon Master sometime during or before the rule of Matron Mother Shakti Hunzrin that began in {{YearlinkName|1412}}.
