'''Kori al-Zafiri''' was a sultan of [[Tajar]] in the [[Pearl Cities]] of [[west Zakhara]].<ref name=LoF.AGtZ-p37"><ref name=LoF.AGtZ-p92">

==History==
Kori formed an alliance with the [[House of Dhi'b]] to rebel against the sultan of Tajar, a position which he then claimed for himself.<ref name=LoF.AGtZ-p37"/> Kori sent his son to plead their case to the [[Grand Caliph]] in [[Huzuz]] who sent investigators to determine the situation in Tajar. Kori's predecessor was taken back to Huzuz in chains and Kori was given the position of Sultan, or Sheikh as he preffered, of Tajar.<ref name=LoF.AGtZ-p92"/>

==Relationships==
Kori's son, [[Ali al-Hadd]], was the sultan of Tajar in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name=LoF.AGtZ-p37"/><ref name=LoF.AGtZ-p92"/>
