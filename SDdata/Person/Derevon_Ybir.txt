==Description==
Derevon had dark skin and black hair tied back.

==History==
{{Quote
|You must listen. There's a gap two hands wide in the southern barricade!
|Derevon
}}
During the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar]] in the mid-1480s [[DR]],{{DDO-time}} Derevon tried to get the attention and assistance of the priest [[Anazar Narroun]] regarding the town's barricades. However, he was snubbed in favor of the crisis emerging in the [[Eveningstar War Hospital]] with [[Peron Uthe]], as shared by [[Meena Follakin]] and [[Eolynn Arva]].
