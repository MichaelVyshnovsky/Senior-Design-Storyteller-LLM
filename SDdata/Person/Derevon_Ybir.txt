Derevon Ybir was a human resident of Eveningstar in Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.
Derevon had dark skin and black hair tied back.
During the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar in the mid-1480s DR, Derevon tried to get the attention and assistance of the priest Anazar Narroun regarding the towns barricades. However, he was snubbed in favor of the crisis emerging in the Eveningstar War Hospital with Peron Uthe, as shared by Meena Follakin and Eolynn Arva.
