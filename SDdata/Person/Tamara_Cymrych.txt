'''Tamara Cymrych''' was High Queen of the [[Moonshae Isles]] for a brief time in the mid-to-late 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
She was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] and the first daughter of the dynastyâ€™s legendary namesake, [[Cymrych Hugh]]. Her mother was his second wife.

She was the sister of both her immediate predecessor, High King [[Warren Cymrych I|Warren I]], and of her successor, [[Carrig Cymrych I|Carrig I]].

==History==
Tamara assumed the throne of the High Queen in [[Callidyrr]] during the {{YearlinkName|259}}, after the death of her brother, High King Warren I. At the time, [[Cedric Riker]] ruled in her family's ancestral seat of [[Corwell]] as its regent, and would do so for her entire reign.

She ruled for only four years before dying of [[poison]] in the {{YearlinkName|263}}.{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|26}}
