'''Evemerus''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] who worked at the [[Netheril Mint]] in [[Eileanar]] until its destruction in {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}.

==Activities==
Alongside [[Elgiva]], Evemerus led the Mint's team of [[wizard]]s in producing the magical anti-counterfeiting prismatic effects on the coinage.
