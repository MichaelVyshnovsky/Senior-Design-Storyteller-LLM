'''Tot Jansen''' was a child of the numerous [[gnome]] [[Jansen]] family in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Ye're really, really ugly.''|Tot Jansen}}

==Description==
Just like his sister, [[Tat Jansen|Tat]], Tot had fair skin and light brown hair.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Tat and Tot lived in [[Athkatla]]'s [[Athkatla/River District|River District]] slums in the Jansen's family home, along with [[Ma Jansen]], [[Kylie Jansen]], [[Jan Jansen]], and Uncle [[Gerhardt Jansen|Gerhardt]]. Tot had a foul attitude, calling guests ugly to their face.
