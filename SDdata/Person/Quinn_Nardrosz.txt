Quinn Nardrosz was a retired ranger in Jalanthar sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.
Quinn was the head of the council that ruled the village. He was also a member of the Emerald Enclave.
Quinn was a famed ranger, known for a fierce battle against an Uthgardt barbarian of the Red Tiger Tribe. Quinn lost his left ear but defeated the enemy.
In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Quinn retired in Jalanthar but remained an active member of the Emerald Enclave.
