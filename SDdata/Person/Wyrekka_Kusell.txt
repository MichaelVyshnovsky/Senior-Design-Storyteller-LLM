'''Wyrekka Kusell''' was a bard famous in the eastern [[Sea of Fallen Stars]] and the regional agent of the [[Moonstars]] for [[Thesk]], [[Impiltur]], [[Narfell]] and [[Great Dale]] in the [[1370 DR|Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR]].

==History==
Wyrekka made her home in [[Telflamm]] when she wasn't wandering Thesk, Impiltur, and the Great Dale.
