Larikal Dyrr was a member of House Agrach Dyrr of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR.
Larikal was the third daughter of matron mother Yasraena Dyrr.
During the siege at Dyrrs compound for their betrayal of Menzoberranzan during the Siege of Menzoberranzan, Larikal happened across the archmage Gromph Baenre, who had infiltrated the compound in order to destroy the Lichdrow Dyrrs phylactery. Gromph, mistaking her for her mother, immediately killed her. After Gromph used her disguise to better infiltrate the Dyrr house temple
