Garagai was a frost giant who lived in an ice lodge on the tundra of Icewind Dale during the late 15 century DR.
While he did not hold the title of jarl, Garagai was well-respected among his kin. He regularly invited other frost giants to his home for good company.
Garagai wielded a greataxe.
He had two winter wolf allies, Koran and Kanan, and an awakened mammoth companion named Norsu.
Around the , Garagai was slain by a group of adventurers.
Garagais animal allies began a campaign of terror and revenge on the settlement of Dougans Hole in the year that followed. The wolves lured residents of the town to the giants abandoned lodge, where they were killed in an act of vengeance by Norsu.
