Strogue Sstar was a dwarf ruler in the Frozenfar.
Unlike most dwarves, Strogue had no fear of magic.
In battle, Sstar wore magical armor.
Around 1358 DR, Strogue Sstar ruled the isolated city of Ironmaster. In 1366 DR, Sstar still ruled in the dwarven city.
