Gharwin Umbryl was the proprietor of the Smiling Satyr tavern outside Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the late 15 century DR.
Gharwin was running the Smiling Satyr by the late 1480s DR. He was sympathetic to the Harpers and allowed the tavern to be used for their teleportation network.
He was a descendant of Arvyn Umbryl, who ran the Smiling Satyr in the mid-to-late 1300s DR.
