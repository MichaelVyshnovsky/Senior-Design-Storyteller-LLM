Alamarayne was a beauty, and she had a pure laugh, described as "like liquid moonlight" in 1367 DR.
Alamarayne had employed divinations to determine the capabilities of the Zhentilar forces occupying Elmwood, ascertaining that they may have divinatory powers of their own. When fighting the Zhents, she employed such spells as hold person and dismissal.
Alamarayne did not flaunt her beauty. She had a mischievous streak. Per Will Turnstone, she was merry and even coquettish, though she could be as cheery as a mass grave sometimes.
