'''Aya Glenmiir''' was an elven wizard in [[Phlan]] during the [[Tyranny of Dragons]].

==Personality==
Aya was a brilliant individual, well versed in the [[Weave]]. She understood the allure and mystery that surrounded the elven race and had no scruples against using it for her vantage. Similarly she used her natural beauty.

==History==
Aya was a 300-year-old elf who was a scholar at [[Mantor's Library]] in Phlan. In [[1489 DR]] a book propriety of the Library was stolen from her house and she was interrogated by some adventurers hired to recover it{{Cite adventurers league/The Scroll Thief|9}}
Later during the [[Cult of the Dragon]]'s coup in the city, she and others were captured by the cultists led by [[Farnvik]]. Freed by some adventurers, Aya wanted to escape the city with the help of [[Glevith]], a member of the [[Welcomer]] crime syndicate whom she knew.{{Cite adventurers league/Tyranny in Phlan|10-11}} After refugee from Phlan arrived in [[Mulmaster]] Aya become one of the top advisor of resurrected [[Aleyd Burral]].{{Cite adventurers league/Breath of the Yellow Rose|9}}
