J Orchard was a Gondian artificer from the city of Baldurs Gate, who died some years before the late 15 century DR.
J Orchard died sometime before the  and was buried in the Lower City Graveyard, next to the Grand Mausoleum. The artificers gravestone had a single inscription "J Orchard. Artificer of Gond, crafter of odd sockets." Js grave may had been accidentally desecrated, and their body animated as a zombie by Nina Dortmell, a talented child with a gift, who was trying to resurrect her recently deceased brother.
