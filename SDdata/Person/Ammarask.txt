Ammarask was a mage who challenged the Simbul prior to . She won, and Ammarask perished.
In , Elminster Aumar judged that the Simbuls slaying of mages, whether from Thay or not, had become excessive, and the feats of her power were tilting the balance of power around Aglarond. Amongst other mages slain, he counted the spellhunter Ammarask.
