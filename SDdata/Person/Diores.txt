'''Diores the Charming''' was a musician of some renown who lived sometime during or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Some said that Diores once used nothing but a trusty old [[flute]] to lure a nest of [[harpies]] to their death. This story was known to the people of the [[Western Heartlands]] as of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
