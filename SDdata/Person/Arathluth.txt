'''Arathluth''' was a [[malaugrym]] of the 14{{th}} and 15{{th}} centuries [[DR]].{{Cite dungeon/206/Ecology of the Malaugrym|1-6}}

==Abilities==
Unable to fly in air, he disliked winged shapes; having no magic power of his own, he collected [[magic item]]s to empower him.

==Activities==
''Circa'' [[1372 DR]], he was believed to reside in [[Amn]] and was impersonating a [[human]] merchant. Known aliases included '''"Ulstult"''' and '''"Imbar"'''.

By [[1479 DR]], Arathluth was on [[Sword Coast]] posing as a merchant or a mercenary and had added to his known aliases '''"Gahnek"''' and '''"Napid"'''.{{Cite dungeon/206/Ecology of the Malaugrym|1-6}}
