'''Maea Dulgussir''' was a [[wizard]] in the village of [[Eveningstar]] in the kingdom of [[Cormyr]]. She owned and operated [[The Low Lantern (Eveningstar)|the Low Lantern]], a tavern, dance hall and theater.

She kept her magical skills concealed, and few if any knew that she could work magic.
