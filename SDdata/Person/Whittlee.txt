'''Whittlee''' was a young woman of [[Phlan]] that worked as a server at [[Madame Freona's Tea Kettle]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. She was one of five daughter's the restaurant's owner and proprietress, Madame [[Freona]].{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|27}}

==Description==
She had stark white hair that she kept tied back in a braid.

==Relationships==
Whittlee's other sisters included [[Blaizette]],{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|23}} [[Briez]],{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|11}} [[Grelinda]],{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|17}} and [[Reece]].{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|6}}
