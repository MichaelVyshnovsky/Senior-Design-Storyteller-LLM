Whittlee was a young woman of Phlan that worked as a server at Madame Freonas Tea Kettle during the late 15 century DR. She was one of five daughters the restaurants owner and proprietress, Madame Freona.
She had stark white hair that she kept tied back in a braid.
Whittlees other sisters included Blaizette, Briez, Grelinda, and Reece.
