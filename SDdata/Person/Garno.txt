Garno was a vengeful slayer of clergy of Loviatar who lived some time before the late 14 century DR.
Some years before the , Garnos family was put to the torch by malicious members of the Church of Loviatar. Driven to disillusionment, Garno swore to bring vengeance on the evil clergy. He claimed many Loviatan lives thanks to wielding Arbanes Sword of Agility. It was believed that a deity of chaos helped the sword to reach Garnos hand, reveling in havoc caused by the vengeful man.
