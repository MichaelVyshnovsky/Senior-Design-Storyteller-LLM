'''Envir Sykensdottir''' was a member of the [[Uthgardt]] [[Gray Wolf tribe]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Envir was the daughter of [[Syken Nightblaze]], former Great Chief of the Gray Wolf tribe.

==History==
Sometime around the {{YearlinkName|1485}}, Great Chief Nightblaze was slain by [[adventurer]]s that hailed from the city of [[Neverwinter]]. Envir planned her revenge and sought to assault the city and regain the tribal authority that was taken from her father.
