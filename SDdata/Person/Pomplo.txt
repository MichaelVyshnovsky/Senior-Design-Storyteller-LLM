'''Pomplo the Elder''' was an epic poet.

==Description==
Pomplo was credited with writing the epic poem known as ''[[Neverwinter Nights (poem)|Neverwinter Nights]]''. Pomplo's work of literature was even converted into a play in the village of [[Farr Windward]]. It was often performed by three actors whilst balancing bowls of [[kumquat]]s on their heads.
