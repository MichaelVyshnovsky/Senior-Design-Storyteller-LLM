'''Rrestel''' was a [[wizard]] from [[Athkatla]] in [[Amn]], of the 13th century [[DR]].

Rrestel accepted [[Glaragama Dunrohiyr]] as an apprentice, following the death of [[Sharabirt]], Glaragama's former instructor.
