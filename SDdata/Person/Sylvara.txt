Sylvara was a wood elf scout who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.
Sylvara was the twin sister of Ayala.
Sylvara had a magic sword.
Alongside her twin sister, Sylvara led a band of elves that operated out in the Blacklake District. As of 1479 DR, the sisters goal was to become stronger so they could join a band of Bregan Daerthe that was operating near Castle Never.
