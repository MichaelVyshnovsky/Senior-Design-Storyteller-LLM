Lhaera was an inhabitant of the Dalelands in the mid-14 century DR.
As of , she wasnt young anymore.
She was a kindly host.
She wore patched clothes and owned at least one cauldron. Rathan Thentraver had blessed her house in the name of Tymora.
Her daughters name was Imraea.
She was visited by Rathan and Torm of the Knights of Myth Drannor one day in early Flamerule of , to bring her daughter a kitten, Snuggleguts. She had been healed by one of them recently, almost certainly by Rathan specifically.
