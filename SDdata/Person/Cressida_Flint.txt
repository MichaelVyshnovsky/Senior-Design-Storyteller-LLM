Cressida Flint was an inhabitant of the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Cressida was a human woman with a tan skin tone, straight and long dark brown hair, and dark brown eyes. She wore a colorful striped orange and red tunic.
In the , Cressida Flint could often be found pacing down the streets of the Lower City, consumed by her thoughts. She reacted hostilely to anyone who dared to interfere with her busy day. She was one of the customers interested in the Honest Vendors not-so-magical protective trinkets.
