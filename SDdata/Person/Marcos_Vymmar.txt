'''Marcos Vymmar''' was a man of the [[Vymmar]] family who served as regent for the [[Amn]]ian [[Torlath]] dynasty in the 7{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Marcos was the father of King [[Esmel Torlath II|Esmel II]]'s wife, and grandfather of [[Esmel Torlath III|Esmel III]].

==History==
Marcos' daughter and eldest grandson disappeared in [[600 DR]] when the realm of [[Minsorran]] was destroyed. His son-in-law King Esmel II died of grief the following year, resulting in the young son Esmel III taking the throne. Marcos acted as regent for his grandson until the {{YearlinkName|614}}.
