Kovor Gemetsk was the Patriarch of the Temple of the Dawn in Heliogabalus. He was also Pavel Shemovs mentor.
He was a stooped old man, with a bald, spotted pate.
Kovor was Pavel Shemovs mentor.
On Kythorn 25, Kovor allowed Pavel Shemov into the palace to make the case that Brimstone could wake Gareth Dragonsbane from his curse.
