'''Kovor Gemetsk''' was the Patriarch of the [[Temple of the Dawn]] in [[Heliogabalus]]. He was also [[Pavel Shemov]]'s mentor.{{cite book/The Rite}}

==Description==
He was a stooped old man, with a bald, spotted pate.

==Relationships==
Kovor was Pavel Shemov's mentor.

==History==
On [[Kythorn 25]], Kovor allowed Pavel Shemov into the palace to make the case that [[Brimstone (drake)|Brimstone]] could wake [[Gareth Dragonsbane]] from his curse.
