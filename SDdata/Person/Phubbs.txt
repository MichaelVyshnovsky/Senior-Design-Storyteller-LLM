Phubbs was one of the employees of Lady Jannaths Estate in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Circa the , Chamberlain Mercer, the man in charge of Lady Firelia Jannaths staff, dismissed all servants from the mansion on the mistresss orders. Mercer placed Phubbs, who was assigned to be a guest greeter, Furbleston, Bailey, Cyrilla, Jooli, and Chef Marjoram on temporary leave with the pay of half-wages and allowance to seek temporary employment somewhere else. The chamberlain issued a statement that this dismissal was done at no imputation of malfeasance and would be ended upon notice.
