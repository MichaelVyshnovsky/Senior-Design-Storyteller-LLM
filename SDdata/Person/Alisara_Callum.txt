Alisara Callum was the Chief Speaker of Helms Hold circa 1479 DR.
She wore a purple tabard emblazoned with a white dragon sigil.
Alisara was well liked within Helms Hold, helping inspire loyalty among its residents and granting a degree of legitimacy to its local government.
She was once a soldier in the Purple Dragons of Cormyr.
Alisara was accepting of spellscarred refugees.
