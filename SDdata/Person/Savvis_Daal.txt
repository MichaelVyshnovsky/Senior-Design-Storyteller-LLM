'''Savvis Daal''' was a [[Thayan knight]] who served her nation within their [[Thayan enclave (Proskur)|enclave]] in the city of [[Proskur]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

Savvis was charged with overseeing the subterranean cellars that held the enclave's slaves.
