'''Safsaf''' was a [[vishap]] living in the [[Furrowed Mountains]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Personality==
Safsaf was a coward, preferring to observe travelers in her land well in advance of encountering them.

==Activities==
The [[holy slayer]]s of the [[Everlasting]] bribed Safsaf to report any travelers found within her territory.
