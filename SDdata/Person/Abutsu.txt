Abutsu was a villager of Fochu Peninsula in Wa. 
Abutsu was a widow of Seinin village.
In 1358 DR, Abutsu saw the death of Toshi by the Soldiers of Iron kabukimono under order of a figure in yellow.
Afterward, she met Osari Minhiro and a foreign adventuring party at the villages shrine. Abutsu told Minhiro the truth about the killing of his ex-wife.
