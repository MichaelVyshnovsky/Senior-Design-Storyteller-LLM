'''Abutsu''' was a villager of [[Fochu Peninsula]] in [[Wa]]. 

==History==
Abutsu was a widow of [[Seinin]] village.

In [[1358 DR]], Abutsu saw the death of [[Toshi]] by the [[Soldiers of Iron]] [[kabukimono]] under order of [[Za-Jikku|a figure in yellow]].

Afterward, she met [[Osari Minhiro]] and a foreign adventuring party at the village's shrine. Abutsu told Minhiro the truth about the killing of his ex-wife.
