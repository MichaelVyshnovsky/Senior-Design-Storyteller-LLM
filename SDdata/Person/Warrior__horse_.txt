'''Warrior''' was a horse, owned by [[Narm Tamaraith]] shortly in {{yearlink|1357}}.

==Personality==
When he was brushed, he nickered appreciatively.

==History==
Warrior was given to [[Narm Tamaraith]] not long before he arrived at [[the Rising Moon (inn)|the Rising Moon]], after his marriage to [[Shandril Shessair]].

The horse was slain not long after, when its owner was ambushed by [[Shargrailar]].
