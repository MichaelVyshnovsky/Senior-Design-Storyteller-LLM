'''Neira''' was a female [[druid]] [[bounty hunter]] who tried to collect the bounty on the head of [[Gorion's Ward]] in [[Nashkel]].

==Description==
The druid could cast a handful of [[spell]]s, with ''[[rigid thinking]]'' being the most powerful. She had little remorse for for slaying her bounties.
