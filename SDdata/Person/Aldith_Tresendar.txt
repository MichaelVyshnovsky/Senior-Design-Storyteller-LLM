'''Aldith Tresendar''', also known as the '''Black Hawk''', was a [[knight]] of great repute in the 10{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Possessions==
The knight owned [[Tresendar Manor]], a large house to the east of [[Phandalin]]. He was also in possession of the enchanted [[longsword]] ''[[Talon (Phandalin)|Talon]]''.

==History==
When Phandalin was raided in the {{YearlinkName|951}}, Aldith descended into the caverns beneath his home to fight off the [[orc]]s. Unfortunately, he died in this encounter, and his longsword was lost in the cavern complex.
