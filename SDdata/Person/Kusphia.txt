Kusphia was a member of the reformed Cult of the Dragon under Severin Silrajin.
In battle, Kusphia wore leather armor and fought with a scimitar.
Around 1489 DR, the dragonclaw was a cultist and head assistant of Talis Kasterel, at her masters side in the Cults occupied hunting lodge in the Greypeak Mountains. She was devoted to Talis to the point of dying for her.
