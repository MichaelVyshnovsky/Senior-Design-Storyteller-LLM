==History==
Brunnt Hallow died sometime before the fall of Reithwin and its consumption by the [[Shadow-cursed Lands]], before the {{Yearlink|1374}}. Brunnt was buried in Reithwin's graveyard behind the [[House of Healing]], at the hospital's northwestern walls. His tombstone read: "Here lies Brunnt Hallow, man of few words." The grave marker remained undamaged and legible as of the {{Yearlink|1492}}.
