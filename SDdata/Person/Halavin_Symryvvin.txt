Halavin Symryvvin was a drow of Menzoberranzan and a member of House Symryvvin in the late 14 century DR. She was a novice priestess of Lolth at Arach-Tinilith.
She was known for being fond of wearing jewelry.
In the , she joined Drisinil Barrison DelArmgos cabal against headmistess Quenthel Baenre and was killed with all the other conspirators.
