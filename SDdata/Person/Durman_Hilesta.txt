'''Durman Hilesta''' is the carpenter and bouncer of [[The Old Skull Inn]] in [[Shadowdale]].


==Death==
In Hammer of [[1375 DR]] Durman was killed during a brawl in the taproom of the Old Skull Inn during the [[Zhent]] Occupation of that same year.
