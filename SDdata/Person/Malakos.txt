'''Malakos''' was a notable [[wizard]] inhabiting the city of [[Yhep]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p170">

==Activities==
He made [[potion]]s for amorous merchants and [[contraceptive]]s for the heirs of Yhep's wealthy families.<ref name=EotSS-p170"/>

Malakos was secretly a devout follower of [[Iyachtu Xvim]] and a member of the [[Twisted Rune]] who monitored events in Yhep.<ref name=EotSS-p170"/>
