Messanth was a bronze dragon turned shadow dragon in Szith Morcane in the late 15 century DR.
The Archmage of Szith Morcane, Solom Nedrazak, at one point captured the bronze dragon Messanth and imprisoned him inside a pocket of Shadowfell. The shadow energies changed Messanth into a shadow dragon that attacked anything he saw.
In the , the Order of the Gauntlet asked adventurers affiliated with them to find Messanth.
Messanth had good dealings with the Order of the Gauntlet.
