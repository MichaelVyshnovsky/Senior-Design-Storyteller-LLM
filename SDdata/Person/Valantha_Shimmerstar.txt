'''Valantha Shimmerstar''' was a [[wizard]] of note inhabiting [[Suzail]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Valantha was a member of the [[War Wizards]]. She was well known among her peers for her  beautiful singing voice and the mischief she caused at social gatherings.<ref>[[Eric Haddock]] (1994).
