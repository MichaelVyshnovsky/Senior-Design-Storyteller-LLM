Nogami Sado was a samurai retainer of the Niwa clan who commanded Awa in Kozakura in 1357 DR.
He led ten samurai and thirty bushi at Awa village, controlling the shoen, despite the fact that the land was administrated directly from nearby Tamanokuni.
