Sir '''Dylan Lionshand''', titled the First Champion of Torm, was a [[paladin]] of [[Torm]] of the mid–14th century [[DR]].

By [[1369 DR]], Dylan led the [[Order of the Golden Lion]], and was still in this position in [[1374 DR]].
