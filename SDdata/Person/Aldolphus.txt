Aldolphus the Stout () was a sage in Arabel in Cormyr in 1357 DR.
Aldolphus loved astronomical studies but his primary field was the physical sciences the identification and usage of minerals, woods, and plants.
Aldolphus fled Arabel during the Time of Troubles and settled in Urmlaspyr in Sembia.
Aldolphus was a stout, joyous man who didnt have the pomposity same of his fellow sages.
Aldolphus was a good friend of King Azoun Obarskyr IV, who always visited the sage when traveling in Arabel. Aldolphus was particularly fond of the meals they shared together.
