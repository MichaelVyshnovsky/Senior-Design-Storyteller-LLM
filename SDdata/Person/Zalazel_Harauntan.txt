'''Zalazel Harauntan''' was a skilled [[wizard]] inhabiting the city of [[Yhep]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p170">

==Activities==
As the most skilled practitioner of the [[magic|Art]] in town, she used her skills to protect both Yhep and its valuable oyster beds. She also tutored a handful of students in the use of magic. She owned a large house in town, but actually spent most of her time within a large stilt house positioned directly over the oyster beds on the Lake of Steam.<ref name=EotSS-p170"/>

==Relationships==
Zalazel was a member of Yhep's ruling family. Her most promising student was a wild tribeswoman named [[Yashiira]].<ref name=EotSS-p170"/>
