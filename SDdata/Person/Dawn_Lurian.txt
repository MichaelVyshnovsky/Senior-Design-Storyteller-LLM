Lord '''Dawn Lurian''' was an infant noble of House [[Lurian]] in the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite document/Procampur City Expansion Packet|9}}{{Cite web/House Lurian}}

==Relationships==
She was the twin brother of [[Richard Lurian]].While several members of House Lurian are known, most of their relationships are unknown; Richard and Dawn may be the children of any of the adult members. They are presumed to be twins because they are listed together and have the same age, though this may be a coincidence.

==History==
She was aged 18 months in [[1370 DR]].
