Birta Grougg was a dockworker from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Birta was a large female half-orc with grayish hide and dark hair gathered in twin buns.
In the , as the Cult of the Absolute menaced the city of Baldurs Gate, Birta Grougg worked in the Gates docks alongside Yves. They were known to borrow wine bottles from the shipments they worked on and, during breaks, sip the alcohol while pretending to be fanciful sommeliers, claimed that they detected notes, smells, and flavors of various foul things in the wine.
