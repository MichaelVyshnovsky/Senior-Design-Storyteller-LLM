'''Katrice Ansar''' was a [[human]] [[hexer]] who oversaw the headquarters of [[Thay]]'s interests in the [[Shadowfell]] city of [[Evernight]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Abilities==
She practiced a combination of [[arcane]] and [[Primal magic|primal]] spellcasting.

==Activities==
She led a small force of Thayans who operated out of [[Thayan outpost (Evernight)|an outpost]] in the south of the city, and in turn she served [[Valindra Shadowmantle]].
