Balant Mornlight was a halfling fighter adventurer of infamous ill luck, active in the 14 century DR.
Balant hailed from a tiny hamlet just outside the town of Whitehorn in the White Peaks of the Moonsea North. Ever since he was a child, Balant was prone to legendarily bad luck. He accidentally burned his hamlet to the ground while still a kid, ensuring it disappeared from the 14 century maps. His adventuring career was mostly uneventful. Balants most grievous wound came from him once running headfirst into a tree while fleeing from his paramours scorned partner. Another adventure of note was when Balant Mornlight traveled with a caravan through a desert. The halflings bad luck was so immense, the caravan faced a freak thunderstorm tuned flood. The caravans destruction ended Balants employment with it. By the time the halfling fighter reached the age of 51, he was believed to be a jinx by most.
Sometime in the 14 century DR, Balant Mornlight was among ambitions adventurers who was interested in being sent to a mysterious dungeon by an unnamed sorceress to retrieve the equally mysterious magical orb from its depths.
