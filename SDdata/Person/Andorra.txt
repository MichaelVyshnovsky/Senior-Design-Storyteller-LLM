'''Andorra of the Many Veils''', or the '''Gazelle''', was a wildly popular dancer living in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Combined with her impressive dancing skills, Andorra's beauty, genuine smile, and friendly demeanor ensured she always had a captive audience of amorous nobles.

==Activities==
Andorra danced at the restaurants and coffee shops of Huzuz's ''[[Midani dictionary|suqs]]''. Her call to fame was an enchanting display involving flamboyant sheer silk veils that disappeared during her performance.

==Rumors & Legends==
Unknown to all who watched her perform, Andorra was an elite [[holy slayer|agent]] of the [[Soft Whisper]]. She used her position to gather intelligence from naive young nobles and reported directly to the [[Rahmah al-Zahir|Grandmother]] of the Soft Whisper.

==Possessions==
Andorra owned a magical ''[[jambiya]]'', ''[[pipes of sounding]]'', and an assortment of assassination tools.
