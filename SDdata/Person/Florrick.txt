Councilor Florrick was a city official from Baldurs Gate that served the great metropolis of the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.
Florrick spoke with grace and authority, simultaneously wielding flattery and generosity to elicit aid from others.
Florrick held Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard in high esteem, and believed him to be the key to the citys salvation during its most dire times.
Councilor Florrick was present at Waukeens Rest when it was assaulted by drow and goblin raiders belonging to the cult of the Absolute in the . She was trapped on the second floor of the inn as fires raged through the structure.
