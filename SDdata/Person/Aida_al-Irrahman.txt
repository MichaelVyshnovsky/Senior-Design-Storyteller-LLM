Aida al-Irrahman was a the true leader of the Winged Bulls in 1367 DR.
Aidas cunning knew no bounds. She was a master of disguise and often paraded around as a man. She was also a superb orator.
Aida had pale skin and dark, curly hair. She had dark brown eyes. Her fingers and limbs were long and slender.
The Shadow Caliph recognized Aidas potential to be his successor upon his eventual death. Because of this he had her operating far away from the intrigue and danger of Sarahin.
Aid worked in the city of Qudra, building the power of the Everlasting there and in other great cities across Zakhara through spreading propaganda.
Aida owned a pair of bracers of defense, chime of opening, dagger of life stealing, jellaba of protection, and a ring of human influence.
