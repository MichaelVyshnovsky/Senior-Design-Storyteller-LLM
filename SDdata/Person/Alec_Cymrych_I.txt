'''Alec I''' of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the early-to-mid 7{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He was the first son of High King [[Embro Cymrych II|Embro II]] and the elder brother of [[Samuel Cymrych]]. His successor was his young son, [[Rowland Cymrych]].

==History==
Alec I assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|632}}, following the death of his father.

He ruled for only eight years before being mauled to death by a [[bear]] while hunting in the {{YearlinkName|640}}.
