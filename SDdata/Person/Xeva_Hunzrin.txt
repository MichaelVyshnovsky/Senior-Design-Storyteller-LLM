Xeva Hunzrin was the House Wizard of House Hunzrin, one of the ruling drow Houses of Menzoberranzan in the Northdark, in the late 15 century DR.
Xeva was a drow woman with obsidian-black skin and hair dyed a loud red color to match her red eyes.
Xeva Hunzrin claimed the title of the House Wizard sometime during or before the rule of Matron Mother Shakti Hunzrin, which began in . despite Houses Hunzrin and Baenre being bitter rivals.
