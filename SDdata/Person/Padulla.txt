Padulla was an Untaken of Umberlee, a member of the Bitch Queens clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queens House in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Padulla was a tan-skinned human woman with red hair and green eyes. Her skin was freckled and a single scar cut through the womans left cheek. Her red hair was gathered in a severe bun. Her garb was a simple jerkin and a tunic, as well as a cooks apron.
Circa the , during the Absolute crisis, Untaken Padulla was serving in the Water Queens Houses kitchens. After the death of Holli Dylford, a member of the church, Untaken Padulla and Untaken Zastreva worked on a funeral feast to the served in the drowned priestess honor.
