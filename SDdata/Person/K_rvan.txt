Dark Master Krvan was a devoted high priest of Cyric and the leader of the Dark Redoubt during the late 14 century DR.
During his tenure as head of the Dark Redoubt, the temples clergy and its congregation underwent significant transformation. He instituted a doctrine of spiritual discipline and instituted strict military training for all its members.
Dark Master Krvan became the leader of the Dark Redoubt in the , after the temples former head priest was slain by Watchful Skull Tynnos Argrim of the Mountain of Skulls. Argim then hand-selected Krvan for the role, in order to maintain his control over the Dark Redoubt.
