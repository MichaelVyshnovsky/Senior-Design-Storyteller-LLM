'''Rousselet''' was a [[Netherese]] shipwright who helped oversee [[Gallery Steamers]] in [[Eileanar]] in [[Netheril]] until its destruction in {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}.

==Activities==
Rousselet served [[Stuckley]] as a high-ranking supervisor on the construction of [[flying ship]]s used in [[Netheril]]. He worked alongside [[Flue]], and had [[Apulee]], [[Fourcrow]], [[Gau]], and [[Bruck]] directly managing the work crews.
