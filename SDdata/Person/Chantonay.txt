Chantonay was a Netherese priest who served as an Underchief at Constabulary Branch One in Eileanar before its destruction in .
Chantonay aided Chief Constable Zacharia and organized the constables of the branch alongside Flamininus and Chanteclaire. She was responsible for protecting the building via spells and quasimagical items, and had the jail cells protected with extraordinary wards thanks to calling in a friend, resulting in Branch One becoming the most impregnable constabulary of the three.
