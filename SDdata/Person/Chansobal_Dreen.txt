'''Chansobal Dreen''' (known by locals as the '''Chanter''') was a High Morninglord of [[Lathander]] who led the [[Morningmist Hall]] in [[Marsember]], [[Cormyr]].

==Activities==
He oversaw sixteen priests and twelve other staff, and was strongly opposed to piracy, often sending out ships against them. He was also an investor in new ventures.
