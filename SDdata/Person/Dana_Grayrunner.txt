Dana Grayrunner was a Rashemaar craftsman and shopkeeper inhabiting Immilmar in Rashemen in the .
Dana owned the Sleighs and Skis shop where she crafted a fine assortment of sleighs, skis, and sleds. She often worked with the renowned weaponsmith Tholli Ironweaver who made the runners for her sleds. Danas item were sometimes commissioned by Wychlaran for enchantment. Her shop was located in the center of Immilmar near the Huhrongs Citadel.
