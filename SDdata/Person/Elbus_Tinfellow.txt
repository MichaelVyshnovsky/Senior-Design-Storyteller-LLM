Elbus Tinfellow was a halfling living in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.
Elbus was the patriarch of the Tinfellows, a large family of farmers in a valley near Hillsfar. Circa 1486 DR, some corrupt Red Plumes led by Captain Haakon Erlich and the sorcerer Breex Vandermast attacked Elbuss farm, capturing and killing all present. Elbus was captured and destined to go to the Hillsfar Arena.
Elbus and his family were allies of the Harpers.
