'''Elbus Tinfellow''' was a [[halfling]] living in the [[Moonsea]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Shackles of Blood|13}}

==History==
Elbus was the patriarch of the [[Tinfellow]]s, a large family of farmers in a valley near [[Hillsfar]]. ''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], some corrupt [[Red Plumes]] led by Captain [[Haakon Erlich]] and the [[sorcerer]] [[Breex Vandermast]] attacked Elbus's farm, capturing and killing all present. Elbus was captured and destined to go to the [[Hillsfar Arena]].{{Cite adventurers league/Shackles of Blood|23}}

==Relationship==
Elbus and his family were allies of the [[Harpers]].{{Cite adventurers league/Shackles of Blood|8}}
