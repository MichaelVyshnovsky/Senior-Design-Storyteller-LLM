'''Toscudlo''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]], master of [[illusion]]s, and [[spell]] inventor who lived during the [[Golden Age]].

==History==
This [[mentalist]] developed seven spells in total. The spells they invented were ''[[illusionary wall]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2309|NY}}, ''[[hallucinatory terrain]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2315|NY}}, ''[[project image]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2338|NY}}, ''[[advanced illusion]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2359|NY}}, ''[[demand]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2384|NY}}, ''[[permanent illusion]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2385|NY}}, and finally, ''[[mass invisibility]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|2396|NY}}.
