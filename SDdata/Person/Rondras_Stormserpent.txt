Rondras Stormserpent was a heir of House Stormserpent in the early mid-15 century DR.
He and Marlin Stormserpent hated each other a lot. He was a heir of House Stormserpent.
Rondras Stormserpent was one of the four Stormserpent heirs in the 1430s DR. When Nethglas Stormserpent stole the Wyverntongue Chalice, he told his siblings, before deciding hed prefer no one knew it and swearing them to secrecy on pain of death. After Nethglas killed his brother Elgrym Stormserpent for sharing this information with Nael Rowanmantle, only two of his siblings were left alive knowing of the prank.
When in , Emvar Obarskyr was slain in the Vast Swamp close to Sembia, Nethglas Stormserpent was killed with him. While his body was being brought to the house, Marlin Stormserpent quietly poisoned Rondras, killing him and becoming the only heir.
