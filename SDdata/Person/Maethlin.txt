'''Maethlin''' was an apothecary who lived in the village of [[Eveningstar]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/157/Next Stop: Eveningstar|65}}

==Activities==
In addition to providing herbs and ointments, Maethlin secretly made poisons. He was also a spy for the [[Zhentarim]].
