Olivessa Untapoor was a cooper who lived in the town of Good Mead during the late 15 century DR.
Olivessa was a skilled cask-maker, a skill that was quite important to the towns honey wine industry.
The Untapoor family was deeply involved in Good Meads history, having been among the original settlers who traveled to Icewind Dale from Chult. That being said, she lived along and had no immediate family members of her own.
She had a political rival in Shandar Froth, a dwarf outlaw who was being manipulated by the Zhentarim.
Olivessa was urged to campaign as speaker of Good Mead, following the death of Speaker Kendrick Rielsbarrow, around the . While she was reluctant to serve, Olivessa was among the most favored candidates.
