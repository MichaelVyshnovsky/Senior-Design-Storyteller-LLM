Ashtukzu I, who was also called the Mulan, was the first King of the Mulan Dynasty who ruled the city-state of Westgate.
Ashtukzu was a mulan worshiper of Gilgeam who once lived in Unther. He was sent by the God-King to conquer and absorb the city-state of Westgate. He accomplished this in  when he took the city and ousted the doppleganger posing as King Ryndarth Soleilon II. However, once he had established a firm grip on the city as King he declared Westgate to be independent of Unther.
