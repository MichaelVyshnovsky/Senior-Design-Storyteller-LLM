Krull was a tortle cleric of Tiamat.
Krull had his entire shell carved with runes of prayers to Tiamat in Draconic.
Krull wielded a +1 maul, and carried five potions of healing and two soul coins in a bag.
The death-priest conducted numerous experiments in Arkhans Tower in Avernus in an attempt to control the Hand of Vecna wielded by Arkhan. He was interested in obtaining artifacts and creatures that might help with his experiments, such as an orb of dragonkind or a unicorn.
In the , after conquering the tower that served as Krulls laboratory, Arkhan left a complement of dragons and undead servants under Krulls command to garrison the tower while he went to a nearby monument to commune with Tiamat.
