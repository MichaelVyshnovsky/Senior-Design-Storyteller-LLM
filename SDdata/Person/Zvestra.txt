Lady '''Zvestra''' was the leader of the [[avoral]]s and one of the [[Talisid and the Five Companions|Companions]] of the Celestial Lion [[Talisid]] of [[Elysium]]. According to the [[World Tree cosmology]], Zvestra resided with Talisid and the other [[guardinal]] [[Celestial paragon|paragons]], in the [[House of Nature]].

==Abilities==
Beyond the powers common to all avorals, Lady Zvestra was trained as a [[wizard]] and a [[cleric]].

==History==
Zvestra was the replacement for Duke [[Windheir]], who retired from the Five Companions in the mid- to late-14{{th}} century [[DR]].
