Opal Starlight was a half-elven scout of Ravens Bluff, active in the late 14 century DR.
Opal Starlight lived in the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast, but little was recorded of her life however, the womans name graced the pages of the locally popular broadsheet – the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter. Jacinth Moonspring, the Society Editor, connected Opal to a notorious womanizer named Don Juan Montery, who, apart from her, also entangled and frolicked with several other notable women within just a single month pock-faced Vedina Stareye‎, a lovely paladin of Tyr, Yvonne Chantel, and the fellow Knight of the Golden Rooster named Ladyhawke.
