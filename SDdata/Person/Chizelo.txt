Chizelo was a young man living in Easthaven during the 11 century DR.
The red haired man liked to wear grey and green tunics.
Chizelo was an nervous young boy who had a hard time holding his bladder.
Like many kids in the small community, Chizelo was interested in all the strangers coming into the Ten Towns.
Chizelo was friends with a few other local children, like Ahmadora, Dillsandra, and Jeffy.
In Eleint, 1281 DR, Chizelo and a few other children met a group of mercenaries who decided to head with Hrothgar on his expedition to Kuldahar.
