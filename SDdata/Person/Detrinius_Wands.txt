Detrinius Wands was a Faerunian lich in Tu Lung in 1358 DR.
Detrinius Wands was a lich from Faerun who decided to go to Kara-Tur, believing it was a land without clerics. However, he was trapped by a curse in a maze in Tu Lungs Underdark.
Detrinius went mad from solitude. He was desperate for company. If he met someone, Detrinius tried by all means not to remain alone anymore. He also wanted news about the outside world.
