'''Alstigar''', who was nicknamed '''the Silent''', was an inhabitant of [[Tilverton]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Description==
Alstigar was bald and had a beard.

==Activities==
Alstigar served as the head cook at the [[Whispering Witch]] [[inn]]. However, he was secretly a [[bard]] and agent of the [[Harpers]].
