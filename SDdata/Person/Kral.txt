Kral was a bard and Harpers agent in Phlan in 1489 DR.
The bard was little paranoid and preferred to be underestimated by his enemies. Despite this, he was a joking and personable individual.
In 1489 DR, after Vorgansharaxs coup, Kral helped the Lord Sage of Phlan to unveil the mysteries of Denlors Tower. Publicly, he acted as the Lords servant.
