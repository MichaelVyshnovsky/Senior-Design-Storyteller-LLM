Alton Vandree was a drow cobbler in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 century DR. He was a member of House Vandree and one of Houndaer TuinTarl and Syrzans rebel group.
During the Silence of Lolth in the , Alton joined Houndaers group desiring to start a slave revolt. Vuzlyn Freth and Alton Vandree were, among others, summoned to Pharaun Mizzrym and Ryld Argith as they might be some of the males they needed to find.
