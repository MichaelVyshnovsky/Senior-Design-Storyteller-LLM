'''Kuang Ming Ling''' was a [[shukenja]]-in-training in [[Shou Lung]] around [[1357 DR]].

==History==
Kuang Ming was the daughter of a wealthy merchant from [[Linshung]] who, against her father's wishes, decided to train as a shukenja. Around 1357 DR, she decided to go on pilgrimage to a far-away monastery. Her father sent with her two [[korobokuru]] retainers to protect the girl. 

==Personality==
Kuang Ming was a romantic but headstrong woman.

==Relationships==
Kuang Ming traveled with two korobokuru retainers of her father [[Hsiao Nan Tzu Wen]] and [[Ling Yu Pao]].
