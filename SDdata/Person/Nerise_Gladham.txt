'''Nerise Gladham''' was a prisoner at the [[Fane of the Eye]] under [[Tyar-Besil]] during the [[Elemental Evil]] crisis.

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], Nerise was captured by [[Cult of the Howling Hatred|air cultists]], who took her to a jail at the Fane until some adventurers infiltrated the temple and freed her.
