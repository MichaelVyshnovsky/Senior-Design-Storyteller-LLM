Golordin Maerthringul was a skilled warrior inhabiting Suldolphor in the far eastern reaches of Calimshan in south Faerûn during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He was middle-aged as of the .
Golordin was one of two High Swords who led the Flame Guard—an elite group of warriors who protected Suldolphors ruling olanger.
He served as a mercenary for many years in Tethyr and other places.
Golordin wore an enchanted suit of armor and wielded a flame tongue sword.
