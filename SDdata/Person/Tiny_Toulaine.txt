'''Tiny Toulaine''' was a chef who worked in the kitchens of the [[Revel's End]] prison during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was a heavily built man.

==Personality==
In contrast to his perhaps imposing appearance, Tiny was a good-humored and cheerful individual.

==Possessions==
In times of needed, Tiny could wield his mighty [[saucepan]] the same way another man would wield a more traditional weapon.
