Miirphys Irithyl was fourth Coronal of Cormanthyr, having led the city for hundreds of years from  to . He was a revered priest of Sehanine Moonbow and was remarkable as the only moon elf to rule the empire as Coronal.
He did lots of work sending ships into Realmspace to prevent the incursions of  Netherese spelljammers from expanding into his domain. 
Unlike other coronals, Miirphys only raised the Rulers Blade once, during the blade-ritual, and never again.
Coronal Miirphys was known for for raising the Crescent Court, a temple of his patron deity Sehanine Moonbow, in the . 
He was also known for having imprisoned the Trio Nefarious, a band of nycaloths. These fiends returned to the realm centuries later, causing the downfall of his empire in the 8 century DR. 
Lastly, Miirphys was involved in the binding the demon-consorting elves of House Dlardrageth to their villa in Myth Drannor.
It was rumored that Coronal Miirphys returned to the Crescent Court after his death, in the form of either a ghost, baelnorn, or even as one of the elven Reverend Ones.
