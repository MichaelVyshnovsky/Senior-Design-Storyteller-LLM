'''Ahmad al-Mudill''' was a [[Zakharan (ethnicity)|Zakharan]] [[flame mage]] who served as a spy and recruit for the [[Brotherhood of the True Flame]] in [[Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1993|201|[[Ahmad al-Mudill]]}}

==Personality==
Ahmad was ruthless in his goal to climb the ranks of the Brotherhood. He would remove any obstacle barring him from attaining a higher ranking.

==Possessions==
Ahmad owned a ''[[wand of fire]]''.
