'''Tresh Lanngolyn''' was a weaver inhabiting [[Waterdeep]], the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Tresh was the guildmaster for the [[Most Excellent Order of Weavers and Dyers]] ''circa'' [[1357 DR]]. She worked out of the [[House of Textiles]], which was their headquarters on [[Nethpranter's Street]] in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]].

==Relationships==
Tresh was also the matriarch of the [[Lanngolyn]] [[Nobility of Waterdeep|noble family]]. Her consort was [[Ormaes Lanngolyn|Ormaes "Seamaster" Lanngolyn]] and their eldest daughter, [[Tryssia Lanngolyn|Tryssia]], was the designated heir.
