'''Kerit Darkrider''' was a [[Sembite]] [[adventurer]] and member of the [[Gold Coins]] company in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Kerit attended King [[Azoun IV]]'s birthday party at [[Thistleflame Keep]] in [[Suzail]] alongside his companion [[Garit Strongarm]]. They stayed the night at the Keep and were suspected of being involved in Lord [[Partic Thistle]]'s apparent murder, but proved innocent.
