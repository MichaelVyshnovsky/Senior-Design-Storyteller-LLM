'''Oreasha''' was an [[erinyes]] that served [[archdevil]] [[Bel|Lord Bel]], ''circa'' the 15{{th}} century [[DR]] on [[Toril]].{{Cite comic/Infernal Tides 4|2-4}}

==Description==
As a high-ranking [[devil]] and an agent of the once-ruler of [[Avernus]], Oreasha commanded great authority over her lessers in the infernal hierarchy.

==Personality==
[[File:Krydle Oreasha.png|thumb|right|250px|''Oreasha lays out the terms of her pact with Krydle.'']]
Oreasha was confident and direct in her actions, not allowing subordinates to slow down or impede her retrieval of what she desired.

She held a particular hatred for Archduke [[Zariel]], who had supplanted Bel as the Lord of Avernus.

==Possessions==
Oreasha wielded a single blade and barbed whip when she decided to personally engage in combat.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}},{{BG-DiA-time}} Oreasha entered into an [[infernal contract]] with the mortal [[Krydle]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].{{Cite comic/Infernal Tides 4|15-16}} As Bel had taken great interest in the [[half-elf]] [[rogue]],{{Cite comic/Infernal Tides 5|20}}

She reappeared after Krydle was infected with a [[mind flayer]] tadpole and in danger of undergoing [[ceremorphosis]]. She removed the tadpole with infernal magic.{{fact}}
