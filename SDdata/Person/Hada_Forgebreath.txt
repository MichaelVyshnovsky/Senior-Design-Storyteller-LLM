Hada Forgebreath was a dwarf smith in Mulmaster.
Hada was a metalsmith of some renown and one of the few dwarves in Mulmaster.
In the past, Hada had some dealings with Furgis Boltsmelter, and knew of the power of his mind-reading amulet, Arameshas Wisdom.
Around 1491 DR, Nada directed Furgis and his adventurer bodyguards to Oster Ironhand at Osters Stables after being pursued by Cult of the Crushing Wave cultists.
