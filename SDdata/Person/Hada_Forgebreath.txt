'''Hada Forgebreath''' was a [[dwarf]] smith in [[Mulmaster]].

==Activities==
Hada was a metalsmith of some renown and one of the few dwarves in Mulmaster.

==Relationship==
In the past, Hada had some dealings with [[Furgis Boltsmelter]], and knew of the power of his mind-reading amulet, ''[[Aramesha's Wisdom]]''.

==History==
Around [[1491 DR]], Nada directed Furgis and his adventurer bodyguards to [[Oster Ironhand]] at [[Oster's Stables]] after being pursued by [[Cult of the Crushing Wave]] cultists.
