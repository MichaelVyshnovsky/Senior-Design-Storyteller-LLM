'''Nanny Plunk''' was a [[green hag]] living in the [[Darklake]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], Plunk taunted travelers and lure them into trouble, though sometimes she bargained with powerful visitors, giving them her knowledge of the Darklake instead.
