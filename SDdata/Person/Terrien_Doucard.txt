Terrien Doucard was a half-elven soldier of the Knights in Silver who replaced Besnell as leader during the Battle of Keepers Dale.
Terrien had shining brown hair which shimmered even after the knight fought and killed on the battlefield.
The knight was fiercely faithful to Lady Alustrial, Besnell, and the rest of the Knights in Silver. In fact, he was in love with Alustriel but was wise enough not to act on those feelings.
Terriens father was an elf, a cousin of Besnell. His mother was the second daughter of one of Silverymoons wealthiest families.
