'''Jassur Dragonwood''' was a nobleman of [[Cormyr]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
According to a knowledgeable master of intrigues, Jassur was really nowhere near as smart as he thought.

==History==
In the lead-up to the [[Council of the Dragon]] in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Jassur was mind-controlled by the evil [[archmage]] [[Manshoon]], just as [[Rorskryn Mreldrake]] was denounced as a traitor.{{cite book/Bury Elminster Deep/MMP|84|9}} After the Council degenerated into violence, Jassur Dragonwood was killed.
