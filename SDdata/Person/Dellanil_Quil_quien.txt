'''Dellanil Quil'quien''' was an [[elf]] king of [[Shilmista]] and one of the forest's legendary heroes. His writings were contained in [[book of Dellanil Quil'quien|an ancient tome]].

His son was [[Gil Telleman]], and his descendants were [[Galladel]] and [[Elbereth]].
