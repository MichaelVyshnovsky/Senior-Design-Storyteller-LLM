Nebuseddar was a fighter in Unther and one of the best champions in the service of Gilgeam in 1357 DR.
In 1357 DR, under the orders of his god-king, Nebuseddar went to participate in the tournament held in Mourktar in Threskel to choose the new king. He managed to finish among the tournaments finalists.
Gilgeam powered Nebuseddar with his divine powers.
