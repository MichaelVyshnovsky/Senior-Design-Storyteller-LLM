Lien Hua Liang was a young samurai in Shou Lung in 1357 DR. 
Lien was waiting to marry. However, her father usually assigned very impossible tasks to all her suitor so she could not be separated from him.
Lien possessed all the classical virtues for a Shou maiden, being beautiful, virtuous, respectful to her parents, obedient, devout and talented.
Lien Hua was the only daughter of Lord Chen of the illustrious Lien Clan of Linshung. The uncle of her father was Minister of Public Works under the previous Emperor and remained well considered.
