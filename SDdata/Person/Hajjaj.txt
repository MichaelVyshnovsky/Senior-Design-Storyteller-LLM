Hajjaj, also called Hajjaj the Intemperate was an efreet pasha who lived in Zakhara in 1367 DR.
Hajjaj served in Zakhara as a representative of the Sultan of the Efreet, Marrake al-Sidan al-Hariq ben Lazan. He controlled his own stronghold, complete with a staff of other efreet and noble efreet as well as a contingent of slaves of various races.
