'''Satiir Thione-Hhune''' ({{pronounce|sah|TEER|thee|AWN|hewn}}) was a noblewoman of the [[Hhune]] [[patriar family]] of [[Baldur's Gate]], and a member of the [[Knights of the Shield]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Even in her 70s, Satiir maintained a fit physique and the distinct presence of Baldurian aristocracy.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Lady Satiir was kidnapped by agents the Baldurian [[Vanthampur]] family. They held her hostage in their [[Vanthampur Villa|estate]] in the [[Upper City]] in an effort to find information the missing ''[[Shield of the Hidden Lord]]''.
