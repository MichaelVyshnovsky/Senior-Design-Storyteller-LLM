'''Maern''' was a [[Ffolk]] moneylender inhabiting the town of [[Aithelar]] in [[Callidyrr]] on the [[Moonshae Isles]] in {{YearlinkName|1345}}.

==Activities==
Maern provided banking and moneychanging services and lent money. His establishment was located near the center of Aithelar at the intersection of [[Finn's Alley]] and [[Aerynn's Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}
