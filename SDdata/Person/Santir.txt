Santir was a monk of elven ancestry at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose.
Santir loved to talk and to gossip, and he was always interested in a good story. He knew he could not be an adventurer himself, liking commodity, but he enjoyed tales of adventurers from books and stories. However, Santir was a man of his word and if pressed he would promise to keep quiet the subjects of guests research.
Santir was a monk at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose and a member of the Order of the Scroll of the Disciples of St. Sollars the Twice-Martyred. His duty was to act as a librarian. Often he was assigned to help guests in their research.
In 1491 DR Santir was possibly involved in an investigation about a strange storm originated from the monastery. Some time later Santir and some others monks was kidnapped by cultist of Cult of the Howling Hatred in order to use them in a new plot to access to the elemental node. In a room of the nodes complex under the monastery thank to the elementary forces Santir was keep in custody by two shadow demons. Later he met an adventuring party hired by Grandmaster Perrywinkle Shin to found the missing monks. The adventurers managed freed him from the demons
