Zora '''Tiega Gos''' ({{Pronounce|ZOR|uh|_|TEE|eg|uh|_|GAHSS|refs={{Cite adventurers league/Flames of Kythorn|29}}}}) was a noble in [[Mulmaster]] of the [[Gos]] family.

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], for her birthday, Faltyn commissioned his nephew [[Andrik Gos]] to create a painting of her and hired [[Marim Tem]] as a musician, and hosted a party at the popular dance hall the [[Wave and Wink]].{{Cite adventurers league/Flames of Kythorn|11-12}}

==Personality==
Tiega was down to earth and friendly, in total opposition to her husband. She did not care for status. Tiega's interests were art, knowledge, and living life to its fullest, despite these not being typical characteristics of the Gos family.

==Relations==
Tiega was the wife of [[Faltyn Gos]].
