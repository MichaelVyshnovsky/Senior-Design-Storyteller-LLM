Texter the Paladin was a Masked Lord of Waterdeep.
Texter had a powerful build, ruddy face, and a thick brown mustache.
Texter had little love for the city and spent much of his time in the wilderness battling monsters. He disliked intrigue and politics, especially among the clerics in the Hall of Justice.
When in the city, Texter dwelt in the Guard Barracks.
Texter had a horse named Galadin.
