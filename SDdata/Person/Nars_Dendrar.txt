'''Nars Dendrar''' was the youngest member of the Dendrar family in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
His father was a woodcarver, called [[Thel Dendrar|Thel]], and his mother was called [[Mirna Dendrar|Mirna]]. He had an older sister, [[Nilsa Dendrar|Nilsa]].

==History==
''Circa'' [[1491 DR]],{{Phandelver-time}} the local brigands, known as the [[Redbrands]] killed his father. He, his mother, and his sister were taken to the cellar of [[Tresendar Manor]], where they were to be sold as slaves.
