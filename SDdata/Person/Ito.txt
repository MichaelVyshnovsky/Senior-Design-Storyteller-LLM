Ito was a Flaming Fist soldier who served the city of Baldurs Gate during the late 15 century DR.
He did not think highly of Wyll, the adventurer known as Blade of Frontiers. Ito believe Wyll was not trustworthy and no real hero.
Ito was among the Flaming Fist soldiers stationed at or nearby the Waukeens Rest when it was raided by the cult of the Absolute in the . He along with other soldiers, including Gauntlet Yeva, gave their best efforts to rescue Councilor Florrick from the inn after it was set ablaze.
