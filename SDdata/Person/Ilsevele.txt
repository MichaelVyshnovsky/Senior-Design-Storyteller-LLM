Lord Ilsevele was the master of Tower Aerilpe. To Kiril Duskmourns recollection in , he had shown sympathy to the Keepers of the Cerulean Sign in the past she thought to inform him that the nilshai had decided to reveal themselves as agents of the Abolethic Sovereignty. His tower was deserted as of that year, with the still rotting bodies of some of the guards around it.
