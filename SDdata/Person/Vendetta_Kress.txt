'''Vendetta Kress''' was a [[tiefling]] businesswoman who lived in [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Usually she sold [[wine]] and spirits for the [[Oathoon]] [[Nobility of Baldur's Gate|patriar]] family. She had a web of contacts in the city, including every tavern owner in the city. 

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1494}}, Vendetta was abducted while walking in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] by [[Dead Three]] cultists. During her captivity, she was interrogated about her masters' security measures. During that time she witnessed the interrogation and eventual death of [[Effinax Zalbor]] and overheard rumors about a possible means of escape.
