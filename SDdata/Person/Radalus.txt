'''Radalus''' was a merchant in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In late {{yearlink|1357}}, [[Mirt]] remarked on a [[Malaugrym]] wearing the shape of the [[Luskanite]] [[Bronor]] that he disliked his [[gold piece]]s, and that he'd most certainly gotten them from Radalus.
