'''Delvon Ludwig''' was a [[priest]] of [[Torm]] who lived in the [[Sword Coast North]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
While in the [[Ten Towns]], Delvon befriended the fisherman-turned-acolyte [[Sirac]] of [[Suzail]].

==History==
Delvon came from the city of [[Neverwinter]] and moved to [[Bryn Shander]] some time around the {{YearlinkName|1485}}. He took up residence within the [[House of the Triad (temple)|House of the Triad]].
