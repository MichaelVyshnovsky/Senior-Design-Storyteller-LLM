In battle, Gardush wore a suite of plate mail armor and wielded a long sword and shield.
In the , Gardush and his companions were sent to Baldurs Gate by the Iron Throne to act as advisors to Thaldorn, to aid in his negotiations with Emissary Tar and the Baldurian Council of Four.
