'''Buirin Thalshond''' was a merchant from the city of [[Tantras]] in [[the Vast]] in the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite polyhedron/88/The Everwinking Eye: Treasures of the Vast, Part One|13}}

Speaking of treasure tales in the Vast in the [[1357 DR|Year of the Prince, 1357 DR]], Buirin remarked "Treasure? Aye, lots of it in the Vast, lad; why didn't ye ask earlier? Oh… I see; ye wanted to live a while, first.|Buirin on treasure tales."
