Rilrae Zolond was member of House Zolond and a novice priestess of Arach-Tinilith in 1372 DR.
In 1372 DR, Rilrae was a novice priestess at Arach-Tinilith. She was possessed by one of the demons sent by Gromph Baenre to kill his sister Mistress Quenthel Baenre, profiting off the Silence of Lolth. However, Quenthel noticed Rilraes the strange behavior in time and immediately killed her, thwarting the assassination attempt.
