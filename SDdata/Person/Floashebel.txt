'''Floashebel''' was an early knight of [[Neverwinter]] and the third of the Nine knights who served Lord [[Halueth Never]]. They were the basis for the [[Neverwinter Nine]].

==History==
She never bore a child, but was honored as the mother of the newborn Neverwinter. A statue of Floashebel and her sword stood guard in Halueth's tomb.
