'''Tosker Wainwright''' was an inhabitant of [[Triboar]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==Activities==
Tosker managed the long-standing [[Wainwright's Wagon]] shop. However, he was more interested in profit than quality and had no skill in carpentry.
