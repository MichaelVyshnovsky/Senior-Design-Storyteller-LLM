Trantolox was an nalfeshnee of the Abyss that ventured into the Nine Hells in the late 15 century DR.
In 1494 DR, under orders of Yeenoghu, Trantolox led a group of demons inside the Scab searching for the Bleeding Citadel. He found it and hoped to claim the Sword of Zariel for his demon lord but had problems opening the doors of the Citadel.
Trantolox was a treacherous and devious demon.
