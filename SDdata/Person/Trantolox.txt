'''Trantolox''' was an [[nalfeshnee]] of the [[Abyss]] that ventured into the [[Nine Hells]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In [[1494 DR]],{{BG-DiA-time}} under orders of [[Yeenoghu]], Trantolox led a group of [[demon]]s inside the [[Scab]] searching for the [[Bleeding Citadel]]. He found it and hoped to claim the [[Sword of Zariel]] for his [[demon lord]] but had problems opening the doors of the Citadel.

==Personality==
Trantolox was a treacherous and devious demon.
