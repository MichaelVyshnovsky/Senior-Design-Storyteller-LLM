'''Stormshield''' was a herald in [[Triel]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was a tall, gaunt, worn man.

==Activities==
He often remained at [[Duskview House]], to be a witness to any hirings of mercenaries.

==History==
Stormshield was present in {{yearlink|1357}} when [[Shandril Shessair]]'s caravan stopped at [[Triel]].
