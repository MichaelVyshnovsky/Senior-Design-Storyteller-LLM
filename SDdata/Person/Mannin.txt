'''Mannin the Stout''' was a mercenary in [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Mannin was one of the field officers of the [[Mercenaries' Guild of Westgate]]. He was responsible for discipline and training, together with [[Losifan Urdo]]. Mannin was a pragmatical and harsh commander.

==History==
In his youth, Mannin was a duelist. He joined the Guild as a recruit but impressed [[Palla]] so much that she hired him permanently.
