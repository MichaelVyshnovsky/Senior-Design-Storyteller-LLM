Fist Eostre was a mage member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Eostre was a human woman with long curly dark hair, black eyes, and a pale complexion. She wore a wizardly robes uniform of the Flaming Fist and a circlet, and carried a simple quarterstaff.
In the , Fist Eostre was stationed in the Basilisk Gates barracks and prison, serving under Blaze Liara Portyr and Manip Seyama. She was tasked with cataloguing requisitioned produce, which was always, to her frustration, "organized by a rabid worg."
