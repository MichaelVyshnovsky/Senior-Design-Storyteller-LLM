'''Chalinthra''' was a powerful [[drow]] mage living in [[Ust Natha]].

==Activities==
She participated in [[Sondal]]'s [[Ust Natha tavern]] duel arena, in mage battles.

== Rumors ==
It was rumored that, in [[1369 DR]], she wished to take revenge on a duelist who defeated a male drow who was favored by her house. This defeated duelist was her favorite male, [[Lasaonar]].
