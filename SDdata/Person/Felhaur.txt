'''Felhaur''' was a fishmonger inhabiting the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite web/theedverse/2021-09-21 - Felhaur's Fine Fish}}

==Activities==
Felhuar owned a fishmongery called [[Felhaur's Fine Fish]] located on [[Spices Street]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Relationships==
He had six children who all helped run the store.
