'''Hino Sadamoto''' was the head of [[Hino clan]] in [[Nakamaru]] around [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Sadamoto was often drunk, sleeping until late in morning. A life of dissolution and evil living had reduced him to a hollow husk.
