'''Hesione''' was a popular tailor who lived in [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]], as of [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|7}}

==Description==
Hesione was a lovely [[human]] female. When she converted to the worship of [[Sune]], she dyed her hair red to honor the [[god]]dess.

==History==
At some point before 1479 DR, Hesione converted to the worship of Sune.
