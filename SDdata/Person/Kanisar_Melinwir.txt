Kanisar Melinwir was a Captain who served in the region of the Vilhon Reach sometime in or before the 14 century DR.
Kanisar made his opinion of the nation of Sespech known in a quote recorded by Adamar Session, Bard of Starmantle. Kanisar stated that there are only two things of importance to remember about Sespech, how close ones enemies are, and ones loyalty to Baron Thuragar Foesmasher.
