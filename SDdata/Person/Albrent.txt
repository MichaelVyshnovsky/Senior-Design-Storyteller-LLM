'''Albrent''' was a [[ghost]] who haunted [[the Riverman]] [[inn]] and [[tavern]] in [[Blackfeather Bridge (village)|Blackfeather Bridge]] in [[Featherdale]].

==Activities==
The ghost protected the inn fire and thieves and occasionally offered advice to those in need.

==History==
Albrent was a members of the [[Harpers]] when he was alive.
