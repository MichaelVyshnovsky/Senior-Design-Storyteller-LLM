'''Hanyar al-Muhif''' was a [[mercenary barbarian]] inhabiting [[Talab]] in the [[Pantheist League]] of [[interior Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}} 

==Personality==
Canny and secretive, Hanyar preferred to carefully analyze situations from the background.

==History==
Hanyar was born in [[Al-Sayaj Mountains]] and raised among the [[Zakharan pantheon|unenlightened]] hill tribes native to the mountains. He later found the way of [[Pantheist Enlightenment]].

==Activities==
Hanyar served Caliph [[Kia al-Sadid]] as his personal emissary and conduit to the hill tribes of the mountains.
