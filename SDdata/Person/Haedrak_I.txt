King Haedrak I of the House of Ithal-Strohm-Bormul was the fourth king of the Lions Dynasty of Tethyr.
Haedrak was a first cousin to Kortal, the previous king. He had at least five sons.
Haedrak was born in 988 DR, took the throne from his cousin upon the latters death in 1022 DR, and passed the throne to this fifth son Coram on his own death in 1059 DR.
