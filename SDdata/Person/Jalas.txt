'''Jalas''' was a female [[efreeti]] inhabiting [[Hiyal]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Jalas served as the personal servant of Prince [[Omar bin Alurah]], a skilled [[sha'ir]] and member of the royal family of Hiyal.
