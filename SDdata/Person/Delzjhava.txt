'''Delzjhava''' was a [[Netherese]] [[archwizard]].

==Description==
Delzjhava inhabited the frontier of [[Netheril]].

==History==
In {{YearlinkConvert|2693|NY}}, another [[arcanist]], [[Decampus]], wandered Netheril. Upon reaching the frontier, the two engaged in a spellbattle. Delzjhava won the battle and Decampus was slain.
