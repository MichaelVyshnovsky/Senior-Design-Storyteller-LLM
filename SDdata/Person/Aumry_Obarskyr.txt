Aumry Obarskyr was the Lord of Shadowdale from 1300 DR to 1339 DR. His last name, Obarskyr of Cormyr, was known only to a few individuals. He was the husband of Syluné Silverhand. He ruled Shadowdale peacefully until his assassination by Zhentarim agents in 1339 DR. His assassins were captured and killed by Jyordhan, but Jyordhan was himself a hidden Zhentarim agent.
Aumry was laid to rest in Aumrys Tomb, on Watchers Knoll in Shadowdale. His wife eventually died in 1356 DR.
Aumry had a magical staff It was later replaced by Esvele Greycastle in 1374 DR in an attempt to destroy Syluné, who at that time was a spectral harpist and would remain so only as long as her husbands possessions remained uninterred.
