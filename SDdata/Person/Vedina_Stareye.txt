Vedina Stareye was an inhabitant of Ravens Bluff, active in the late 14 century DR.
Vedina was described as a woman with a "face of a pock-marked road and a voice of like fingernails on slate."
Vedina Stareye was a dock frequenter or a dock worker who lived in the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast in the late 14 century DR. Little was recorded of her life however, the womans name graced the pages of the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter broadsheet. Jacinth "Jackie" Moonspring, the Society Editor, connected Vedina to a notorious womanizer named Don Juan Montery, who, apart from her, also entangled with several other notable women within just a single month the half-elven scout named Opal Starlight, a handsome paladin of Tyr, Yvonne Chantel, and the fellow Knight of the Golden Rooster named Ladyhawke.
