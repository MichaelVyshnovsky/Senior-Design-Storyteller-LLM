'''Lashaera Thindol''' was a female [[human]] [[cleric]] of [[Tymora]] the Luckbringer in [[Tantras]] in [[the Vast]] around [[1358 DR]].

Lashaera was high priestess of the [[House of Hope]] temple to Tymora at the time. She led five priests and eight lay-followers.
