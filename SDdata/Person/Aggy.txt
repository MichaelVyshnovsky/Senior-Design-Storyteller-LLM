Sharp-eye Aggy was a raider and cultist of the Absolute who served at the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.
Aggy had green skin upon which he slathered blue war paint. He dressed in simple cloth and leather clothes, along with a small scrap metal breastplate.
He carried within him a crude wooden bow.
In the , Aggy and other cultist raiders launch a full on assault against the Waukeens Rest inn. They stole loads of riches, food, and alcohol from the establishment and set it ablaze after they left.
Back at the Shattered Sanctum, the goblins and their allies held a great party to celebrate their victory.
