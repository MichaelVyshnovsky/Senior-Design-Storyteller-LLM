Nalmissra possessed some inherent resistances that were typically found in demonic beings, such as natural regeneration.
Her kiss could charm others and even drain them of their life force.
Nalmissra and her companions served as one of the three groups connected to the fifth level of Watchers Keep, which held the final seal that kept away the being known as the Imprisoned One. The group was summoned to the keep when the heart key was used to unlock the seal.
Her allies included her fellow demons Xei Win Toh, Ytossi, and the Huntress, along with the drow Ameralis Zauviir and an unnamed hive mother.
