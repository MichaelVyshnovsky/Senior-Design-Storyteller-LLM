'''Bindle "Eye-nock" Tempertuppin''' was the inventor of ''[[Tempertuppin's Ever-Thrower]]s'', active sometime before the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''You'll be sorry when a giant puts YOUR ass in a sling.''|Bindle Tempertuppin}}

==History==
Bindle created his [[enchanted]] [[sling]]s sometime before the {{YearlinkName|1372}}. The muses came to the man after he witnessed a [[fire giant]] using a giant-sized sling, big enough to launch a [[donkey]] 300 paces. However, some questioned the truthfulness of Bindle's story.
