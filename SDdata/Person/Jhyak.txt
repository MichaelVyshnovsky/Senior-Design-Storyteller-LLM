'''"Bold Jhyak"''' was a very old, retired adventurer who owned a [[Bold Jhyak's|bar by the same name]] in [[Teshburl]] in [[1370 DR]].

Jhyak hired a couple mercenaries to tell stories of their battle scars to patrons, which increased his business in an otherwise sea-focused town.
