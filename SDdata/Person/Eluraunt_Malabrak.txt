'''Eluraunt Malabrak''' was a [[Shadovar]] [[arcanist]] of [[Thultanthar]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Eluraunt was one of the few Shadovar to survive the fall of Thultanthar in the {{YearlinkName|1487}}.

He came to serve [[the Three (mages)|the Three]]. In [[Tarsakh]] of the {{yearlinkName|1488}}, he was observed from afar to be in the [[Oldspires]] mansion in [[Cormyr]] when [[Tabra]], on her crusade against all surviving Shadovar arcanists, found him and killed Eluraunt.
