Mlanduver Tassio was a traveling merchant who operated around Innarlith in the late mid-14 century DR.
He was a traveling merchant.
At some point after the death of Onlagar Blurnwood, Mlanduver broke into his house looking for magic to sell, only to be removed by Talatha Vaerovree, his apprentice who still worked there. By , Mlanduver had hired a number of magelings to attack Talatha Vaerovree.
