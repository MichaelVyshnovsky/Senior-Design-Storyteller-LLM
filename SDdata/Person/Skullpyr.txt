'''Skullpyr''' was a [[flameskull]] under the orders of the [[beholder]] [[Vaxall]].{{Cite dungeon/69/Sleep of Ages|76}}

==Relationships==
Skullpyr was one of the main minions of Vaxall. Its main task was to protect [[Gauntlet (battle horror)|Gauntlet]] and it was always near the [[helmed horror]].

==History==
Skullpyr was crafted by priests of [[Bane]] from the skull of the last Court Sorcerer of [[Mintar]], [[Pyrax|Pyrax of the Ebon Skull]].

In [[1370 DR]], Skullpyr accompanied Gauntlet and his troop in trying to stop the awakening of the [[Knights of the Crescent Moon]], considering them a threat for its master,

==Powers==
Skullpyr could cast some [[spell]]s, such as ''[[magic missile]]'', ''[[flame strike]]'', and ''[[ice storm]]'', and always had active ''[[spell reflection]]''. It also could spew fire from its mouth.
