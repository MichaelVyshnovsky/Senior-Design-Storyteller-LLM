Takegai Yodori was a wandering shugenja of the noble Takegai clan in Kozakura in 1357 DR.
Yodori acted as a courier for Takegai Uezami, the head of the council of state and a high-ranking member of the Takegai clan. Yodori traveled all Kozakura to deliver messages to Uezamis allies.
Yodori was totally devoted to his clan. He was an ambitious, energetic, and serious individual.
