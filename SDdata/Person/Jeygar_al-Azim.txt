Prince Jeygar al-Azim was the favored heir of the Sultan of Sikak in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara in 1367 DR. 
Jeygar was quite the dashing young gnome.
Jeygar spent his time heading the fishing fleet that frequently visited Huzuz. He was said to be a favorite guest at the Grand Caliphs court.
Prince Jeygar was the son of Sultan Magar al-Azim. He had many brothers and sisters.
