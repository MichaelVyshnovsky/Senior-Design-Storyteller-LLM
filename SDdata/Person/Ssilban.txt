'''Ssilban The Silent''' was a notorious [[thief]] from [[Theymarsh‎‎]] of the [[Border Kingdoms]], active in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Ssilban of Theymarsh spent time on the [[Sword Coast]] sometime before the {{YearlinkName|1351}}. During that time he made enemies, including [[Baelam]], a [[wizard]]/thief of [[Myth Drannor]].
