'''Tharkus Gromm''' was a member of the ruling triumvirate in [[Fireshear]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Along with the other members of the ruling triumvirate, Tharkus was known to accept bribes, exchanging ore and coin for more and better supplies, whose distribution they controlled.

==History==
In the late 1480s or early 1490s [[DR]], sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]], Tharkus was the leader of of the local branch of [[Hammaver House]], a merchant company based in [[Mirabar]]. Triumvirate members served until they died or resigned.

Tharkus was one of the leaders of the town when it was attacked by a marauding party of twenty [[frost giant]]s, who arrived in a frost giant [[greatship]].
