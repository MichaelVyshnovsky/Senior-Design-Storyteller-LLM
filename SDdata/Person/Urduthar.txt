Urduthar was a shadowslain cleric of Shar involved in the Zhent invasion of Shadowdale in the Year of Risen Elfkin, 1375 DR.
Urduthar was a cleric of Shar that chose to become undead to rise in rank within the Church of Shar and so he become the second-in-command of Esvele Greycastle.
He was in the lair of the ally lich Alokkair in 1375 DR, performing shadow rituals to destroy the Weave in the Shadowdale area.
