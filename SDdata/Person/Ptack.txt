'''Ptack''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and [[spell]] inventor who lived during the [[Shadowed Age]].

==History==
Ptack developed five spells during their life, all within a two year period. The spells they invented were ''[[crystalbrittle]]'' and ''[[knock]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|3356|NY}}, and ''[[secret page]]'', ''[[nondetection]]'', and ''[[wizard lock]]'' (which was used to upgrade the quality of a lock) in {{YearlinkConvert|3357|NY}}.
