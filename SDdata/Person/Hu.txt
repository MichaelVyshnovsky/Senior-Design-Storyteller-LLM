Hu was a sea captain in 1358 DR.
Hu was a Wanese captain in service to the mercantile Ko clan, commanding the Victorious Morning ship.
In 1358 DR, he was ordered to take Ko La Ko and an adventuring party hired by Ko Ho San to Hsin-to, at the mouth of the Fenghsintzu River in Tu Lung.
Hu was a hot-tempered individual. A long time living outside his country made him even forget his true Wa-an name.
