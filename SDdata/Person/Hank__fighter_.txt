Hank the Double-Bitted was a high-ranking officer of Muktar in Turmish in 1358 DR.
Hank was the leader of the Champions of Muktar. His weapon of choice was axes, hence his nickname.
Hank was a veteran fighter of the wars in Turmish.
In 1358 DR, Hank and his fellow Champions arranged a trap inside Fort Blood, planned by the self-styled king of Muktar, Hetep, to eliminate an adventuring party well-known in Golconda.
Later, he led the Muktar army in the Battle of Wilma Pass.
It said that once Hank had killed alone a black dragon.
