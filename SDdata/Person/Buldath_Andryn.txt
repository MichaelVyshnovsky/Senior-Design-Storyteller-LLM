'''Buldath Andryn''' was a taciturn and secretive mage who lived in [[Scornubel]]. He bought the remains of monsters and prepared them into magical reagents, to sell across the [[Sword Coast]].

==Relationship==
Buldath had a [[pseudodragon]] familiar and a [[wraith]] guardian.
