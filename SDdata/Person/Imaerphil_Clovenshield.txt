Imaerphil Clovenshield was a female human who led Maalthiirs guard in Hillsfar.
As an experienced fighter, Imaerphil could hold her own in most battles. She was also versed in the skills of a rogue and could operate quietly behind the scenes, if need be.
Imaerphil Clovenshield led Maalthiirs 32 elite guards and helped protect the First Lord against any threats.
By 1372 DR, Imaerphil had risen to the position of leader of Maalthiirs guard. This duty meant Imaerphil was personally responsible for protecting the First Lord of Hillsfar.
