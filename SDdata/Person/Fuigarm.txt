Besides being a skilled fighter, Fuigarm was possessed of heroic strength. He also knew a special sign language, known only to him and to his master. When he protected someone on the streets, the odds of pickpockets were considerably reduced.
Fuigarm carried an exceedingly powerful magical scimitar, a magical dagger of superior strength, and a silk shirt of protection +1 that provided incredible defense against missiles. He also carried a silver medallion that marked him as a resident of Bralizzar.
Circa 1356 DR, Fuigarm may have been directed by Aman to escort adventurers in his employ around Bralizzar.
