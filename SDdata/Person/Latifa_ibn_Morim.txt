'''Latifa ibn Morim''' was a [[half-elf]] [[Elemental mage|sand mage]] within the [[Palace of the Enlightened Throne]] in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Gem of Zakhara|84}}

==Activities==
Latifa served as one of the [[Alyana al-Azzazi|Grand Vizier's]] assistants. Her prime responsibility was security within the palace guard.
