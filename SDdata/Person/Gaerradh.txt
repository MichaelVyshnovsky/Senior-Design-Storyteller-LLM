Gaerradh was an elf ranger and scout in the High Forest
Gaerradh had long russet hair tied back in a single braid.
The ranger carried a bow and two curved axes.
The elf was responsible for keeping watch over the northern marches of the wood.
Gaerradhs companion was Sheeril, a large and powerful wolf. By 1479 DR, Gaerradh was married to Methrammar Aerasumé, High Marshal of the Argent Legion of Luruar.
