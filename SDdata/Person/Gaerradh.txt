}}'''Gaerradh''' was an [[elf]] [[ranger]] and scout in the [[High Forest]]

==Description==
Gaerradh had long russet hair tied back in a single braid.

==Possessions==
The ranger carried a [[bow]] and two curved [[axe]]s.

==Activities==
The elf was responsible for keeping watch over the northern marches of the wood.

==Relationships==
Gaerradh's companion was [[Sheeril]], a large and powerful wolf. By [[1479 DR]], Gaerradh was married to [[Methrammar Aerasumé]], High Marshal of the [[Argent Legion]] of [[Luruar]].
