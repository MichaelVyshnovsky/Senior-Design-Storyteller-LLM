Teghun was a bugbear who allied with the cult of the Absolute during the late 15 century DR.
He wielded a morningstar in battle.
In the , the forces of the Absolute launched a vicious on the nearby Waukeens Rest situated on the Risen Road. After the assault, the goblinoid cultists retired to the Shattered Sanctum to celebrate their victory with a grand feast.
