Calabrista, or Great Lady Calabrista, was the director of a finishing school in the mid-14 century DR.
In some occassions she could appear to be triple-bosomed. During her meeting with Elminster, she wore many petticoats with gauze and gems, and many of her clothes had lace.
She liked to talk up her students, but some people she talked to got the impression that she was talking them up as though they were wares.
She ran a finishing school for wealthy dames, the lessons of which included magic. She prided on her ability to draw out their talent for magic.
