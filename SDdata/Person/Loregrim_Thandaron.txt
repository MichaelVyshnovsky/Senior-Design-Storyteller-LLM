'''Loregrim Thandaron''' was an elderly, yet powerful [[human]] [[druid]] of [[Silvanus]] who lived in the [[Misty Forest]] around the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
There were a variety of [[ranger]]s in the Misty Forest that Loregrim habitually worked alongside. He also had two young, novice druids under his wing that acted as assistants.

==History==
Prior to settling down in the Misty Forest, Loregrim had been a citizen of the city of [[Neverwinter]].
