Lady '''Larissa Thongolir''' was a member of the [[Thongolir]] noble family of [[Waterdeep]] in the early 1370s [[DR]].{{Cite web enhancement/Noble Houses of Waterdeep|7-8}}

She was also an active member of the [[Confluence]], a secret society of [[steel dragon]]s and dragon-blooded humans and their patrons, who battled the influence of the [[Unseen]] and other shapechangers in Waterdeep. She was an authority figure within the organization around [[1374 DR]].
