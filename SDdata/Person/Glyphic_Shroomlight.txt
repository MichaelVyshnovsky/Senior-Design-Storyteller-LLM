'''Glyphic Shroomlight''' was a young [[deep gnome]] acolyte of [[Segojan Earthcaller]] in [[Blingdenstone]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Glyphic was very nervous because he knew his position needed an older and more experienced priest.

==History==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], Glyphic was assigned to run the recently reconsecrated [[Ruby in the Rough]] temple.
