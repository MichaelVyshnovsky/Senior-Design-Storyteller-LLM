Tashkla was a priestess of Eilistraee.
Tashkla was one of the Protectors of the Song in the Promenade of the Dark Maiden.
In 1377 DR, she was one of the six Protectors chosen for an assault led by Cavatina Xarann on the main temple of Kiaransalee.
In 1379 DR Tashkla was killed by an ooze during the attack of the Ghaunadaurs followers to the Promenade but Cavatina hoped to resurrect her later.
