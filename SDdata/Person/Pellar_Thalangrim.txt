'''Pellar Thalangrim''' was a [[human]] [[cleric]] of [[Selûne]] the Moonmaiden in [[Tantras]] in [[the Vast]] in the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

Around [[1358 DR]], Pellar was high priest of the [[House of Moonlight]] temple to Selûne in Tantras. He led nine priests and sixteen lay-followers.
