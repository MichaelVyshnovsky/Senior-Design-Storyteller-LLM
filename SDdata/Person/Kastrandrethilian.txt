Kastrandrethilian was a silver dragon paladin of Xymor (Bahamut) and a member of the Talons of Justice.
Kastrandrethilians lair was located somewhere in the western Galena Mountains.
As of 1374 DR, Kastrandrethilian was one of the few known members of the Talons.
