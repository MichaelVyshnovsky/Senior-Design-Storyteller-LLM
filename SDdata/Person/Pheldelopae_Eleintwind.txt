Pheldelopae Eleintwind was a human chambermaid at the Yawning Portal inn in Waterdeep in the late 14 century DR.
Circa 1375 DR, she worked alongside Neetha Darra, Hendra Stornshar, and Yamra Stelkyn in doing the everyday cleaning of the inn. They were hardworking and dedicated to the owner Durnans family.
She was attractive and strong.
