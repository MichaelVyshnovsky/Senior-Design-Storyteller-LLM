'''Commarth''' was the commander of the border armies of [[Sembia]] but he was also one of the Wearers of the Purple of the [[Cult of the Dragon]].

==History==
As leader of the Cult he was involved in the hunt for [[Shandril Shessair]] in [[1357 DR]], and was in his position in [[1369 DR]].
