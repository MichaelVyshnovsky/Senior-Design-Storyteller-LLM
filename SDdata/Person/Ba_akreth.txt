Baakreth was a githyanki youth from Crèche Yllek, on Toril during the late 15 century DR.
Baakreth had a yellow spotted leathery skin typical for her people and blonde hair.
Youth Baakreth, along with other gith of what would become Crèche Yllek, was brought to the recently claimed Rosymorn Monastery of Lathander in the Western Heartlands sometime before the . Soon after, the function of the crèche was changed as the githyanki war against the mind flayers in Faerûn shifted in balance, while Baakreth was still a teenager and not fully trained. She continued her military education along with other youths of the Crèche under instructor Savarsh Kethk.
