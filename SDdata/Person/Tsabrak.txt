Tsabrak was a drider in Menzoberranzan in the late 14 century DR. He was one of Houndaer TuinTarl and Syrzans rebel group.
Tsabrak was part of a drider colony living in tunnels near Menzoberranzan.
During the Silence of Lolth in the , Tsabrak and others driders joined Houndaers group in the hopes of a better future for their fellows.
