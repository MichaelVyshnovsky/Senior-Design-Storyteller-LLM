'''Celadae''' was a [[human]] [[cleric]] of [[Torm]] who left her temple to fight for her god after receiving a divine vision.

==Description==
Underneath the helm she wore in battle, Celadae had shoulder-length brown hair. She had light brown eyes and light skin.

==Possessions==
Celadae wore a suit of [[plate mail]], complete with helm, [[tabard]], and [[shield]]. Her weapon of choice was a magical [[mace]].

==Activities==
Upon turning 20 years of age, Celadae received a vision from Torm, calling upon her to venture into the world as a warrior-priest.
