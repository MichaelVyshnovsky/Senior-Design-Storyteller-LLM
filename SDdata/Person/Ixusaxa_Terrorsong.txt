'''Ixusaxa Terronsong''' was a high-ranking member of the [[Cult of the Dragon]] in [[Phlan]] during the [[Tyranny of Dragons]].

==Personality==
Ixusaxa's main objective was the power to command true dragons, if someone can offer to her something related to it, she can betray her current masters, otherwise she followed loyally her duty, hoping to rise the rank. Ixusaxa considered all being with a sorter life than her just garbage{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|10}}

==History==
Ixusaxa hailed from [[Myth Drannor]] but was made outcast at early age; she decided to join the Cult after seeing the devastation unlashed by a [[red dragon]]{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|10}}
Alongside [[Farnvik]], Ixusaxa was one of the main leaders of the cultists that took over Phlan in [[1489 DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Tyranny in Phlan|10}}

When it was reported that the resistance's leaders had likely tried to escape the city through the sewers, thanks to some adventurers, she decided to solve the matter herself. With her two [[wyvern]]s and [[shield guardian]], she managed to ambush the escaping group. However, in the fighting, the adventurers with the support of [[Ector Brahms]] managed to kill her and her minions.{{Cite adventurers league/Tyranny in Phlan|27}} Somehow she survived and was ordered by [[Vorgansharax]] to led a contingent of cultist outside the city to search for the restored [[Pool of Radiance (novel)|Pool of Radiance]] but was again defeated by some adventurers.{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|7}}
