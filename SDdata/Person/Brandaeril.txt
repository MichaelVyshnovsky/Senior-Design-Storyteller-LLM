Brandaeril was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.
He was older than Glathra Barcantle.
When the Hall of Justice became filled with deadly smoke on the day of the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the , Glathra Barcantle coordinated the effort to counter that poisonous smoke. Her announcement about the cancellation of the Council on that day was questioned by Brandaeril when she explained it meant a traitor among the ranks of the War Wizards, he reluctantly agreed with her orders.
