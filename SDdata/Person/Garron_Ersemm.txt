Master Garron Ersemm was a skilled horseman and horse breaker and captain of the cavalry under the control of the Countess of Calimmon, Lady Kyrin Hawkwinter. He was also her fiancé, having come with her from Waterdeep during the Reclamation Wars.
Garron was almost perpetually happy and was well-loved by the young horse soldiers of Tethyrs cavalry.
A worshiper of Torm, Garron joined the Loyal Order of Innocents after its creation by Queen Zaranda in 1369 DR. Garron spent time at least four times a year at Fortress Faithful a Tyran keep.
