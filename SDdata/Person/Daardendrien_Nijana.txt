'''Daardendrien Nijana''' was a [[dragonborn]] warrior member of [[Clan Daardendrien]], and a member of the [[Lance Defenders]] of [[Djerad Kethendi]] in [[1487 DR]].

==Abilities==
She was capable of breathing [[acid]].

==Relationships==
Nijana was the daughter of [[Daardendrien Turan]] and the sister of [[Daardendrien Medrash|Medrash]].<ref group="note">See Notes.

==History==
Nijana was one of the survivors of the Kethendan forward regiment of the Lance Defenders, 

On [[Hammer 8]] of 1487 DR, Nijana led a group of survivors of that battle to safety, after they freed themselves from [[chain]]s, taking advantage of a storm that interrupted [[Gilgeam]]'s speech on the eve of the battle of [[Djerad Thymar]].
