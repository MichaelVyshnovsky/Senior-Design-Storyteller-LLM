High Radiance '''Durneth Seafarer''' was the leader of the temple known the [[Tower of the Morning]].

==Activities==
Durneth was involved in recruiting leading clerics along with [[Daelegoth Orndeir]].

==History==
Seafarer led the clerics of the Tower of the Morning from at least [[1369 DR|1369]] to [[1374 DR]]. In {{YearlinkName|1374}}, Durneth proclaimed edicts that gave credit to the [[Risen Sun heresy]].
