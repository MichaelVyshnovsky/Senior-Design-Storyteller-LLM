'''Rider of the Evening Breeze''', or simply '''Evening Breeze''' to close friends, was an [[androsphinx]] inhabiting the [[Haunted Lands]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Evening Breeze was known to approach travelers passing through the Haunted Lands, requesting hospitality through the [[salt bond|bond of salt]]. Evening Breeze was gravely insulted by any refusals. In exchange for hospitality the androsphinx warned travelers of local dangers and offered his protection for the evening.
