Flo Minsydel was an inhabitant of Baldurs Gates Lower City in the late 15 century DR.
Flo was a human woman with a fair skin tone and shortly-cut blonde hair. She wore a frilled white blouse and a corset.
In the , during the Absolute crisis, Flo Minsydel frequented The Singing Lute tavern overlooking the Gray Harbor and the Counting House. The unfriendly woman could be found sitting at one of the outside tables, sharp declining company and conversation from strangers.
