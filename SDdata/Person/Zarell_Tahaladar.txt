'''Zarell Tahaladar''' was a [[drow]] scroll merchant in [[Mantol-Derith]].

==Personality==
Zarell was an elegant drow with a passion for books. She read and kept more volumes than she sold.

==History==
Around [[1479 DR]], Zarell sold scrolls and books in Mantol-Derith. She wrote the scrolls herself using [[paper]] from [[Tawar Kroul]]. Zarell also had a vast network of contacts to buy and sell rare books.
