Jenzar was a warrior and shopkeeper inhabiting Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Like his brother, he was quite lazy.
Jenzar was one of the owners of Reliable Guides, a shop that hired out guides to take adventuresome individuals through the ruins of Zhentil Keep following the citys destruction in the .
His shop was located on the Throat outside of the Throat Gate of the Foreign Quarter. 
His brother, and co-owner of Reliable Guides, was Goartet.
