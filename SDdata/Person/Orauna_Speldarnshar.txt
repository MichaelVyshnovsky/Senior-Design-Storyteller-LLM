'''Orauna Speldarnshar''' was a [[wizard]] and a member of the [[Zhentarim]]. She served as a mageling to [[Ruatheene]] and was based in a [[Zhentilar]] camp in the valley of [[Arntethyl]] by the river [[Tesh]].

Orauna was a former [[Calimshan|Calishite]] dancing girl. She hated men and tried to kill them at every occasion.
