Nouméa Cardellith (maiden name Nouméa Fairbright) was a Sembian wizard and an apprentice of Elminster Aumar in the mid–14 century DR.
During her encounter with Elminster, she was wearing, almost certainly as part of a complete attire, golden slippers with spike heels.
She was practical and not reticent to respond to absurd situations.

She carried early on a wand of ever-searing flames.
