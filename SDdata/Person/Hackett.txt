'''Hackett''' was a [[Calishite]] [[human]] journeyman laborer who worked on the [[Sword Coast]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
On [[Uktar 2]] of the {{YearlinkName|1371}},<ref group="note">While this year is not explicitly stated, it is inferred from the date given in the "Dark Journal" and the "Moonhaven Logbook". Presumably, Ilyn Toth wrote each document over the course of the same time. Hackett passed through the sleepy village of [[Moonhaven]]. His activities were observed by the exiled [[Red Wizard]] [[Ilyn Toth]].
