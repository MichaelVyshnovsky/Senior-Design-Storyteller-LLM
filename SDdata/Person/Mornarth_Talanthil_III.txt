'''Mornarth Talanthil III''' was an [[Arkhan (title)|Arkhan]] of the [[Talanthil Dynasty]] who ruled the nation of [[Raumathar]].

==Activities==
Mornarth III inherited the throne when his father, [[Tallos Talanthil III|Tallos III]], died in {{Yearlink|-199}}. He reigned until {{Yearlink|-168}}.

==Relationships==
Mornarth III's had two sons with [[Tallos Talanthil III|Tallos IV]] being the eldest and [[Vayloss Talanthil III|Vayloss IV]] being the youngest.
