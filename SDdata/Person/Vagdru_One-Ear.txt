'''Vagdru One-Ear''' was the owner of the [[Fallen Tower]] tavern in [[Neverwinter]] in the late 15{{Th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Vagdru had only one ear, hence his nickname.{{Cite dungeon/193/Shards of Selûne|38}}

==Activities==
As the owner of the Fallen Tower tavern, Vagdru's job was to sell food and drinks to his fellow [[orc]]s and other patrons, and to manage the tavern. Vagdru was known for his homemade [[beer]], which only orcs found palatable.{{Cite dungeon/193/Shards of Selûne|39}}

==History==
When a tribe of orcs from [[Many-Arrows]] invaded the [[River District (Neverwinter)|River District]] of [[Neverwinter]] in the 1470s DR, they discovered the Fallen Tower and reopened the tavern, with one of them, Vagdru One-Ear, taking the role of bartender and food-seller by [[1479 DR]].
