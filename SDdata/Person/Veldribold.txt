'''Veldribold''' was a [[mage]] of [[Calimport]] during the early 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. In {{yearlink|1322}}, he slew the [[Magister]] [[Phelandern Ghemelt]] over his home in the [[Shining Sea]] by tearing him apart. He was then slain moments later by [[Ulraen]] of [[Eltabbar]].
