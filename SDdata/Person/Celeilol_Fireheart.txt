'''Celeilol Fireheart''' was an [[elf]] lord of [[Leuthilspar]] on [[Evermeet]]. He commanded a company of two hundred and fifty elves in Leuthilspar.

==History==
Fireheart was killed in the first rush of the [[Battle of the Cwm]] on [[Ches 27]], [[1374 DR]].
