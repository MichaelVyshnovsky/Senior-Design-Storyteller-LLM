'''Flamininus''' was a [[Netherese]] [[priest]] who served as an Underchief at [[Constabulary Branch One]] in [[Eileanar]] before its destruction in {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}.

==Activities==
Flamininus aided Chief Constable [[Zacharia]] at the constabulary and organized the constables of the branch alongside [[Chantonay]] and [[Chanteclaire]].
