Qazim al-Satir was skilled wind mage inhabiting Qudra in north Zakhara in .
Qazim was a trusted advisor the the court of Qudra, though he was not technically associated with them. He spent most of his time within his manor, but always answered a summons from the court.
Qazim played a pivotal role during the Battle of Hawa by saving most of Qudras fleet from destruction at the hands of the corsairs.
