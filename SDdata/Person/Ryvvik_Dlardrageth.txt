Ryvvik Dlardrageth was the third-in-command of the daemonfey in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.
Ryvvik was the youngest and only surviving child of Sarya Dlardrageth. His father was a vrock.
Ryvvik oversaw all intelligence-gathering operations.
Sarya killed Ryvvik around 1371 DR.
Ryvvik wanted always to know others thoughts and feelings. He enjoyed using his powers to inflict emotional, mental, or psychic pain on others. Ryvvik usually used telepathy and spoke very little.
