Velvorn was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.
He was on the older side.
He numbered amongst Caladneis closer associates by .
One night in the early spring of 1373 DR, after the raid on the Rightful Conspiracy in Marsember, the Royal Magician of Cormyr Caladnei was visited by Elminster Aumar to request her presence elsewhere. One of the people who arrived to protect her was Velvorn. He was dismissed by Caladnei, after some deliberation.
