'''Bennesey''' (known as '''Old Bennesey''') was a [[Sembia]]n who was alive around [[1188 DR]]. He participated in the discussions with King [[Pryntaler Obarskyr]] in an attempt to acquire the city of [[Marsember]]. The others in his group were [[Kodlos]] [[Homfast]], Lady [[Threnka]] and [[Jollitha Par]].
