'''Harin al-Hayif''' was the caliph of [[Liham]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Personality==
Harin was a shy and timid individual who enjoyed writing poetry.

==Activities==
Harin lived in the [[Palace of Sly Ravens]] located within the city near the banks of [[Al-Sari River]].
