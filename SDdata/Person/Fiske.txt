'''Fiske''' was a house guard who worked for the noble [[Seasilver]] family during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was described as having thick lips and a double chin.

==History==
When the city of [[Marsember]] was under siege by the [[Shadovar]], on [[Uktar 2]] of the {{yearlinkName|1486}}, Fiske was threated under pain of arrow to open the gates of the Seasilver city manse. Lady [[Arietta Seasilver]] then slipped out of her family's home to aid the beleaguered [[Kleef Kenric]] as he fought off a number of [[shade]]s.
