Rorskryn Mreldrake was a former War Wizard of Cormyr in Suzail.
He was one of Kelgantors "wolves".
Around 1479 DR Rorskryn was one of the War Wizards sent under Kelgantor for a raid on Tethgard where the fugitive wizard Elminster Aumar was after stealing the Royal Gorget of Battle.
After the battle, he was among the known survivors.
Later his superior Andram Rendarth recruited Rorskryn to check if the Elminster remained into the Royal Palace of Suzail. Elminster quickly countered with an enchanted wand of paralysis, which froze two war wizards, missing Rorskryn.
Some time later Rorskryn was ethralled by Manshoon. Together with understeward Corleth Fentable, Rorskryn was summoned by Manshoon to the shop of Immaero Sraunter to receive his orders.
Later Alusair Obarskyr revealed to king Foril Obarskyr I the treason of Mreldrake however Corleth was in the room and so Manshoon accomplished the fact.
Under his masters compulsion Rorskryn went thought a gate to Marsember, where he polymorphed himself in a young female called Mythandra that want sail to Turmish, without any memory of his life as Rorskryn.
In 1480 DR he went back to Suzail to bargain an agreement with a mysterious cabal of renegade shadovar. 
The cabal forced Mreldrake to kill from distance many others War Wizards using a new kind of magic he had discovered.
At last he was uncovered as the killer inside Castle Irlingstar by Elminsters party and Jarlin Flamtarge, one of the Manshoons bodies.
At last his cabal protectors were killed by Elminster and Mreldrake died.
