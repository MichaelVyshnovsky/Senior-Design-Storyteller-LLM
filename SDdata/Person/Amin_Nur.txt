Nur, or Amin Nur, was an official of Dihliz in 1367 DR.
Amin Nur was a keenly intelligent individual who possessed an eye for intrigue. As his name suggested, he was a trusted ally of Emira Hassana Alim al-Gaib.
Amin Nur headed the Ministry of Secrets, an appointment granted to him by the Emira after years of loyal service.
Amin Nur was an unenlightened Zakharan from the Haunted Lands.
