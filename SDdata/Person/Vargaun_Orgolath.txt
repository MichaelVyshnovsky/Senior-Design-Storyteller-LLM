'''Vargaun Orgolath''' was the first [[tiefling]] [[Nentyarch]] of the [[Orgolath Dynasty]] of [[Narfell]].

==Activities==
Vargaun reigned from {{Yearlink|-578}} until {{Yearlink|-535}}. He was the third Nentyarch during the Orgolath Dynasty.

==Relationships==
His father, [[Ulthar Orgolath|Ulthar the Darkhorn]], was Nentyarch before him. Vargaun was Ulthar's firstborn son.
