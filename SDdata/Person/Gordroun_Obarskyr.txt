'''Gordroun '''was the 26th [[Obarskyr]] monarch and was listed among the kings of [[Cormyr]] although he never actually reigned. He was crowned posthumously by his younger brother [[Keldroun Obarskyr|King Keldroun]]. Gordroun was known for killing his traitorous stepbrother and stepfather, who had murdered his older brother and then king [[Torst Obarskyr|Torst]].
