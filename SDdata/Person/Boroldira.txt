'''Boroldira''' was the daughter of [[Borold]] and [[Nalitheen]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|2|Realmslore}}

==Relationships==
Both she and her sister, [[Naleetha]] became orphaned two summers prior to {{yearlink|1321}}, to be raised by their mother alone, Nalitheen. They got acquainted with [[Mirt the Merciless]] in that year.

==History==
Either she, her sister, or both survived into {{yearlink|1372}}.{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|106|6}}
