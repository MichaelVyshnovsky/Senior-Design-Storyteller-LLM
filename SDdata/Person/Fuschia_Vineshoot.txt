'''Fuschia Vineshoot''' was an inhabitant of [[Baldur's Gate]]'s [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{Quote|''You'll have to excuse us. My darling and I are enjoying the view of the city.''|Fuschia Vineshoot}}

==Description==
Fuschia was a pale-skinned [[Half-elf|half]]–[[high elf]] with sharp facial features and short golden curls.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the [[Absolute crisis]], Fuschia Vineshoot frequented [[The Singing Lute]] alongside his significant other, a fellow half-elf man, [[Rodondo Logg]]. Rodondo hated the Lute whilst his darling loved the place and was clueless of Rodondo's true feelings.
