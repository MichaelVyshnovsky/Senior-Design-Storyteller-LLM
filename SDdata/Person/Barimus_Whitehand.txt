Barimus Whitehand was a law enforcement officer in the town of Voonlar sometime during the term of Bron Buorstag Hlammythyl, i.e., from about 1355 DR until at least 1372 DR.
He did not pursue violence for violences sake, but liked the peace and orderly calm that resulted from force applied appropriately.
He was proficient with a greatsword, but had no other significant abilities.
He wore chain mail
Barimus joined in a fight on the Brons side one night and was offered the position of deputy on the spot. At some point in his career he received some training from the Zhentilar.
