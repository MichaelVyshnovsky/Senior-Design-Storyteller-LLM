'''Glorming''' was a male [[orc]] chieftain of the tribes that dwelled in the [[Earthfast Mountains]] in [[the Vast]].

He was a hero to the local orcs, and died fighting in the pass that was named for him—[[Glorming Pass]]—some time before [[1370 DR]].
