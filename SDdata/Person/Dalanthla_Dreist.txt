'''Dalanthla Dreist''' was one of the leaders of the [[Kardryn]] in [[Yeshpek]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p167">

==Personality==
She was known for her fiery temper.<ref name=EotSS-p167"/>

==Activities==
Dalanthla earned her fame as an [[adventurer]] where her skill at throwing [[dagger]]s, or whatever else was within easy reach, preceded her.<ref name=EotSS-p167"/>
