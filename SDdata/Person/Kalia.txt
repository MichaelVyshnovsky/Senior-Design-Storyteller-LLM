Kalia was a high-ranking officer of Muktar in the Turmish in 1358 DR.
Kalia was a member of the Champions of Muktar. She was also a high priestess of Myrkul.
In 1358 DR Kalia and his fellow Champions arranged the trap inside Fort Blood planned by the self-styled king of Muktar, Hetep, to eliminate an adventuring party well-known in Golconda.
Later she commanded her brigade in the Battle of Wilma Pass.
Kalia liked showing her face to the sacrificed victims she made to her god.
