'''Cordrane the Recent''' was a [[necromancer]] in the employ of [[Sorcerous Sundries]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Look, I'm sure there's somebody else who can help you - I need to work while the flesh is fresh.''|Cordrane the Recent}}

==Description==
Cordrane was a [[gray dwarf]] with charcoal skin, shaved head, and a snow-white beard.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Cordrane was employed by [[mage]] [[Lorroakan]] on the first floor of Sorcerous Sundries in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]]. He offered his necromantic skills to customers, selling reanimated creatures like [[zombie]]s. [[Alice Althea]], a wealthy Baldurian, was among Cordrane's customers. She was contemplating purchasing a newly created zombie of Cordrane's making.
