'''Myrtle''' was an innkeeper in [[Bryn Shander]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==Activities==
Myrtle managed the [[Geldenstag's Rest]], one of the oldest inns of the city. She considered her business to know everyone else's business.
