'''Nkonzi Maru''' was a [[ranger]] from [[Katashaka]] and, with her husband [[Kwanza Maru|Kwanza]], joint leader of the [[Anaconda (organization)|Anaconda]] mercenary company and warrior society in the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Together with her husband Kwanza, she commanded their operations in the [[Heartlands]] of [[Faerûn]] in the early 1360s DR. She was an intelligent commander who hated losing people in her corps.

==Abilities==
She was a veteran wilderness ranger and specialized in the use of a spear. Nkonzi had several [[animal companion]]s, with a [[tiger|white tiger]] and a pair of [[hawk]]s.

==Possessions==
She fought with a ''+3 [[spear]] of [[Axe of Hurling|hurling]]'' and wore a ''[[Ring of chameleon power|necklace of chameleon power]]'' and ''[[bracers of defense]]''.
