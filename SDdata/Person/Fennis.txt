'''Fennis''' was a [[tasked genie|tasked]] [[winemaker genie]] who served the [[Brotherhood of the True Flame]] in the fortress of [[Krak al-Niraan]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
When he wasn't lounging in a drunken stupor, Fennis spent his time harvesting [[grape]]s and making wine for the keep. Fennis was assisted by a trio of [[copper automaton]]s in his work.

==Possessions==
Fennis had three ''[[potion of plant control|potions of plant control]]'' that he kept hidden within an empty wine urn.
