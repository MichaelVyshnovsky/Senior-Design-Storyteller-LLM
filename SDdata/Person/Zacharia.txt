Zacharia was a Netherese fighter who served as the Chief Constable at Constabulary Branch One in Eileanar before its destruction in .
Zacharia led the constables of the branch and liaised with Karsus and his assistants. She was aided by the Underchiefs Chantonay, Chanteclaire, and Flamininus, who managed the other constables directly.
