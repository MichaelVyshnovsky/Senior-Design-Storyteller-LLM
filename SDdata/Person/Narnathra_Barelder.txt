'''Lady Narnathra Barelder''' was a [[Cormyr]]ean noblewoman and the head of the [[Barelder]] family in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Narnathra wore a short haircut suitable for wearing a [[helmet]], and only wore gowns at weddings.

==Activities==
Lady Narnathra practised with two [[sword]]s daily, and used clever and shrewd investments in the rebuilding of [[Sembia]] to enrich the family.

==Relationships==
Narnathra was the eldest of Lord [[Montror Barelder|Montror]] and Lady [[Asmrae Barelder|Asmrae]]'s three children. She married [[Varkander Heldruth]], a lowborn seacaptain who she loved dearly, and had two children, [[Maereenra Barelder|Maereenra]] and [[Braeryl Barelder|Braeryl]].

Two of her closest friends were Lady [[Laeyalane Tantorn]] and Lady [[Cathone Ravenhill|Cathone "Cat" Ravenhill]], who were also her lovers.

==History==
Narnathra was raised by her mother to be a sensible and wise ruler, and became head of House Barelder in [[1479 DR]] when her father Montror Barelder was slain.
