'''Yolanda Carrathal''' was High Queen of [[Callidyrr]] in the mid-to-late 11{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|33}} Like her predecessors, although she held the title of High Queen of the [[Moonshae Isles]], she was considered to be merely a figurehead by the [[Ffolk]] outside of Callidyrr.

==Relationships==
She was a member of the [[Carrathal Dynasty]] and grandniece to King [[Alec Carrathal III|Alec III]].

She and her husband, [[Bertrand Carrathal|Bertrand]], had multiple children. Their third son would go on to rule as [[Edwin Carrathal I|Edwin I]].

==History==
Yolanda assumed the throne in the {{YearlinkName|1052}}, following the overthrow of her predecessor, King [[Bayle Carrathal]].

Her reign lasted for thirty-seven years before she died of [[disease|illness]] in the {{YearlinkName|1089}}. She was succeeded by her husband, who ruled for six years before he was in turn succeeded by their son, Edwin I.
