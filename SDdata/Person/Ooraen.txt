Ooraen was a young member of House Agrach Dyrr of Menzoberranzan in 1373 DR.
Ooraen graduated from Sorcere in 1372 DR.
During the siege of Dyrrs compound for their betrayal of Menzoberranzan during the Siege of Menzoberranzan, Ooraen was called back to his house together with all of Dyrrs other wizards to help raise magical defenses.
