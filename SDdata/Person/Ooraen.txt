==History==
Ooraen graduated from [[Sorcere]] in [[1372 DR]].

During the siege of Dyrr's compound for their betrayal of [[Menzoberranzan]] during the [[Siege of Menzoberranzan]], Ooraen was called back to his house together with all of Dyrr's other wizards to help raise magical defenses.
