Olodan was a member of the Shadow Druids who infiltrated the Emerald Grove during the late 15 century DR.
Olodan had white hair worn in dreadlocks, and tattoos all across her body and face. She wore a simple leather harness and loincloth.
While in the Emerald Grove, Olodan took on the form of a large, gray rat.
While spying upon the druids of the grove, Olodan worked alongside two others of her order, Pinta and Wren.
As of the , Olodans cover in the Emerald Grove was still secure. She closely followed her subordinate, Kagha, during the time that she temporarily gained control over the grove, in Master Halsins absence.
