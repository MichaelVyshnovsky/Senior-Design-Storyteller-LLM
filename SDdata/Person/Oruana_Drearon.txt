Oruana Drearon was a member of the Sharran monastic order of the Dark Moon and its representative in Westgates Dark Ladys Promise temple in the late 15 century DR.
Oruana was a woman of pale skin and long obsidian hair. She had fierce lavender eyes and was wrapped in dark-colored robe.
Circa the , Oruana Drearon was one of the members of the church of Shar who served High Priestess Velisha and was her personal aide, responsible for the temples maintenance and well-being.
