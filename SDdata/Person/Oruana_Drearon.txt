'''Oruana Drearon''' was a member of the [[Sharran]] [[monastic order]] of the [[Dark Moon]] and its representative in [[Westgate]]'s [[Dark Lady's Promise]] temple in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''The Dark Lady sees all that happens in shadows and darkness, woman. And as each and every one of us has some hint of darkness in our hearts, the Lady of Loss is mistress to us all - none can escape her unwavering gaze. We can hide nothing from the Dark Lady.''|Oruana Drearon}}

==Description==
Oruana was a woman of pale skin and long obsidian hair. She had fierce lavender eyes and was wrapped in dark-colored [[robe]].

==History==
''Circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, Oruana Drearon was one of the members of the church of Shar who served High Priestess [[Velisha]] and was her personal aide, responsible for the temple's maintenance and well-being.
