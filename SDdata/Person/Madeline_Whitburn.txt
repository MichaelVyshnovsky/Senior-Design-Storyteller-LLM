Lady Madeline Whitburn was the wife of Lord "Whit" Whitburn, a member of the Parliament of Peers, and a member of a patriar family of Baldurs Gate in the 15 century DR.
Lady Madeline was a mature human woman with light skin with gray eyes.
In the , Lord "Whit" Whitburn faced with the lack of "stiffness" in the bedrooms and Madeline purchased a remedy through her butler, Blathers. However, Rigid-Quik Cordial proved to be ineffective when mixed with a glass of Lords evening sherry. Lady Whitburn wrote an angry letter of complaint regarding the cordial and demanded her money back or an alternative solution to the soft problem of her husbands.
When the Absolute crisis hit Baldurs Gate, the citys new Lord, Enver Gortash, secretly behind the strife, was given names of eight members of the Parliament of Peers who were susceptible to influence. According to the Black Hand of Loyaltys research, Lady Madeline Whitburn was one of the nobles who would easily fall in line with the cult via bribery, blackmail, or direct threats.
