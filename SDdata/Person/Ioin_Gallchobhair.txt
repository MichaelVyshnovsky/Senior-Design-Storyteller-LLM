'''Ioin Gallchobhair''' was a [[bandit]] who operated out on the [[Sword Coast]] and [[Amn]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
He was aided in his banditry by his fellows Billy and Dribben. They were fond of demanding "tolls" for those who passed through the land they claimed.

Ioin later took up with a crew from [[Athkatla]], led by [[Tarnor (bandit)|Tarnor the Hatchetman]].

==Possessions==
Ioin wielded a unique [[throwing axe]] called ''[[Hangard's axe]]''.

==History==
Ioin and his gang were among the many bandit groups that plagued the Sword Coast during the [[iron crisis]] in the {{YearlinkName|1368}}. During the crisis, they eked out a territory in an area north of [[Nashkel]].

==Rumors & Legends==
In the cemetery near the [[Temple of Helm (Nashkel)|temple]] of [[Helm]] in Nashkel, there was a gravestone inscribed with the name "Ioin O Gallchobhair", at the same time Ioin the bandit was alive and well.
