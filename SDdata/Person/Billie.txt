Billie was a pet mammoth that belonged to a son of one of the well-off families of the Living City, Ravens Bluff, in the late 14 century DR.
Billie had a distinct orange birthmark on its abdomen. The mammoth loved receiving belly rubs and eating fruits and nuts. It was forbidden to feed Billie any apple cider.
Billies disappearance was announced in the Marpenoth issue of the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter in the . The owners parents placed a classifieds ad in the broadsheet, stating that the mammoth had been recently seen near the tent city just outside the city gates of Ravens Bluff. They offered a grand reward to anyone who could return the stray creature to its owners.
