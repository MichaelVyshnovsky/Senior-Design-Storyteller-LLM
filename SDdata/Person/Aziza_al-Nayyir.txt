'''Aziza al-Nayyir''' was a [[half-elven]] [[Mystic (Zakhara)|mystic]] and a fabled [[Dome Dancers|Dome Dancer]] in [[Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1993|209|[[Aziza al-Nayyir]]}}

==Activities==
Aziza worshiped [[Haku]] with fervent dance routines that aided her desert kin and spread the worship of her god. Her base of operations was the [[Desert Mosque]].

==Possessions==
As with all Dome Dancers, Aziza wielded a [[scimitar]] in each hand. One of her swords was enchanted.
