'''Nureh yr Catahra el Pesarkhal''', an exceptional spymaster of the [[Memnon]] underworld, was the wife of the crime lord [[Nasim el Pesarkhal|Nasim yn Feroz el Pesarkhal]] and sister-in-law to the [[Ralan el Pesarkhal|syl-pasha]] in [[1370 DR]]. 

==Description==
She was a beautiful [[half-elf|half-elven]] woman with jet black hair.

==Personality==
Nureh utilized many guises&mdash;[[bard]], barmaid, and [[jhasina]] to name a few&mdash;and many agents. The syl-pasha considered her the most skilled spymaster he had ever known.
