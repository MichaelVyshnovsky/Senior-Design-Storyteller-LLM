'''Karim Ben Aziz''' was a [[half-elf]] living on [[Masud Jazayir]] in the [[Crowded Sea]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Karim served as the captain of the marketplace guards in [[Bandar al-Sa'adat]]. All of the guardsmen serving under him were also close family members.

==Relationships==
Karim was the younger brother of [[Mahmud Ben Aziz]].
