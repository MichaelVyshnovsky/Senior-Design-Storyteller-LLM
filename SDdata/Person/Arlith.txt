'''Arlith''' was a [[halfling]] [[adventurer]] living in [[Ravens Bluff]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. She was a member of an adventuring group known as the [[Blue Wyverns]].{{Cite digital book/Prince of Ravens/Kindle|2577|8}}

==Abilities==
In a fight, she preferred to use ranged weapons rather than melee attacks and was proficient in the use of the [[crossbow]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Arlith participated in the company's foray into the ancient [[dwarven]] city of [[Sarbreen]] to recover the [[Sarkonagael]], a tome of [[shadow magic]].

==Description==
Arlith had reddish hair.{{Cite digital book/Prince of Ravens/Kindle|2494}}
