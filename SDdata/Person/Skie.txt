'''Skie''' was a young barmaid in the village of [[Toralth]] in [[Tethyr]] in [[1370 DR]].

==Activities==
Skie worked at the [[Blue Badger Inn]], a tavern situated in Toralth, a village in northeastern Tethyr. She worked there to help her starving family. During the day, Skie helped on her family's farm.
