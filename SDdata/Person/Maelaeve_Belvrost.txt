Maelaeve Belvrost was the matriarch of the Belvrost clan of mages as of the late 14 century DR.
Maelaeve raised the best brownflower mushrooms anywhere, and knew her way around medicines. She was a local source for slitherstill.
She dwelt in the family manor at Sundstone, in the hamlet of Baernwood.
