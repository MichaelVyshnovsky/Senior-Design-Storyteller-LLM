Lord Briarbroke was a nobleman from Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.
He attended the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the , where he was unfortunately among those that died after steel was drawn on King Foril Obarskyr I. His body was later taken to the Chapel of the Valiant and identified.
One of the War Wizards, Welwyn Tracegar, characterized his death as "not much loss".
