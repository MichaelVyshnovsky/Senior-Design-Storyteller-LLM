Gorli was a dwarven adventurer in the late 15 century DR.
Gorli had a large twin-forked braided black beard and a full head of hair. He wore a suit of scale mail with a white and red tabard over it.
300px|thumb|Gorli and friends at the tavern.
In between adventures, he patronized the Ball and Chain Tavern on the docks of the Wheloon Prison alongside his fellows Irwynne, Bash, and Marbone the Cosh. He could be seen with a large goblet of drink while grumbling about his companions.
