'''Gorli''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] [[adventurer]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}

==Description==
Gorli had a large twin-forked braided black beard and a full head of hair. He wore a suit of [[scale mail]] with a white and red [[tabard]] over it.

==History==
[[File:Ball and Chain adventurers.jpg|300px|thumb|''Gorli and friends at the tavern.'']]
{{Quote
|I told 'em to stop, but did they listen? No, they just blundered in like a bunch of ninnies!
|''Gorli, grumbling to himself''
}}
In between adventures, he patronized the [[Ball and Chain Tavern]] on the docks of the [[Wheloon Prison]] alongside his fellows [[Irwynne]], [[Bash (adventurer)|Bash]], and [[Marbone the Cosh]]. He could be seen with a large [[goblet]] of drink while grumbling about his companions.
