'''Aldurl Nael''' was a [[Waterdhavian]] [[brass]] merchant in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the early spring of the {{YearlinkName|1373}}, Nael was in debt to the moneylender [[Caethur]]; at a meeting with five other debtors, he protested that the business climate had been terrible, and thus was not able to make the required payment. He was soon killed by the moneylender.
