Kameros was an overworked Flaming Fist cook stationed in the Basilisk Gate barracks of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Kameros was a crimson-skinned tiefling with stubby pointy horns and a big hair of curly brown hair.
In the , during the Absolute crisis, Kameros slaved in the kittens of the Basilisk Gate barracks in the Eastway district, feeding a seeming endless number of ravenous Flaming Fist mercenaries. The tiefling worked hard and could barely keep up with the jobs demands.
