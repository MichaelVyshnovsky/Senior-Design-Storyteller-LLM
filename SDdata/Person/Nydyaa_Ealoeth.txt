Nydyaa Ealoeth was one of the leaders of the daemonfey in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.
Nydyaas consort was Myrlaer Ealoeth.
Nydyaa was the lord and matriarch of House Ealoth. She was in charge of internal security, scrying in search of traitors or infiltrators. She reported to Baron Xhalth, despite the fact that she hated his stupidity.
