Torbryn Unker was a skilled hin armorer for hire from the city of Neverwinter, active in the late 15 century DR.
By the late 15, Torbryn Unker was known as a miracle worker of some skill. Even though she was not the best among her peers, she compensated for it with being energetic, loud, and excited to take on paying work. She was often offering her services to adventurers and defenders of Neverwinter.
