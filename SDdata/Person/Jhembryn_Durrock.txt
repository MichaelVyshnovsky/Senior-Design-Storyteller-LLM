'''Jhembryn Durrock''', or simply '''Durrock''', was a male [[human]] [[assassin]] and a worshiper of [[Bane]] before and during the [[Time of Troubles]] in [[1358 DR]].

==History==
Durrock was an elite assassin in service to the church of Bane based in [[Scardale]] during the Time of Troubles. He was tasked by his god with the mission to capture the [[wizard]] [[Midnight]] but in end he was killed.{{cite book/Tantras|4-6}}
