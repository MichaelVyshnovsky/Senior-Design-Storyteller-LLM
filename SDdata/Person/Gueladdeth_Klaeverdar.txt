'''Gueladdeth Klaeverdar''' was the matriarch of the [[Klaeverdar]] family of [[Elturel]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
She bred [[goat]]s that she trained to never ate anything containing paper, or anything at all indoors.

==Relationships==
Her most likely successors as of {{yearlink|1370}} were [[Meldrethe Klaeverdar]] and [[Andrath Klaeverdar]].

==Possessions==
She had six magically trained [[wolves]], and dwelt in the ruins of Oldturret at [[Oldturret Farm]], where she had stashed numerous spellbooks.

==Rumors & Legends==
Gueladdeth was said to [[scry]] for items up and down the [[Sword Coast]], which she retrieved by ''[[teleport]]ing''. She was also said to transform disobedient family members into goats.
