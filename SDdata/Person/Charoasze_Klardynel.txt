'''Charoasze Klardynel''' was a noblewoman of the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Charoasze Klardynel was a guest of Lady Joysil Ambrur for at least one day in the early spring of {{yearlink|1373}}, on the same afternoon where their host retired to her chambers prior to a conference with [[Harnrim Starangh]] and his comrades.
