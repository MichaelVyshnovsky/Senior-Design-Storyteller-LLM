Llandrydd Wyvernheart was a mercenary leader in Chondath in 1358 DR.
Llandrydd led the Llandrydds Steel company. In 1358 DR, he joined the Company of the Singing Dawn and Enhanens men to investigate rumors of a legion following the tactics of the old Chessentan Empire.
He faced the undead army led by the mad cleric of Tempus Theodoric.
Llandrydd was a man of authority. He was known for his speed in battle and his lethal sting, from which derived his epithet.
