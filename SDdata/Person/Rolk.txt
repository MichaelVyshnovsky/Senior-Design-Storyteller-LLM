Rolk was a dwarf merchant in Phlan in 1489 DR.
Rolk was a gregarious individual who recently had a change of fortune. He took his new job very seriously.
He was an acquaintance of Gilfron Milon.
In 1489 DR, after Vorgansharaxs coup, he became the main agent of the local Merchants Guild. Rolk hired an adventuring party to recover a book from the lair of the black dragon Throstulgrael, which the dragon had raided from a Guilds caravan.
