'''Gamaliel''' was the [[familiar]] of the [[wizard]] [[Evaine]].

==Description==
He was a big cat resembling a [[saber-toothed tiger]], but he could turn into a tall [[human]] warrior.

== History ==
Gamaliel lived with Evaine in a small tower near the city of [[Phlan]].
