'''Olfsvenn''' was a [[Northlander]] inhabiting the island of [[Norland]] in the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|11}}

==Activities==
Given the age of his father in {{Yearlink|1479}}, Olfsvenn was in a good position to be the next Jarl of Norland. However, the [[Storm Maiden]]'s rise to power posed a substantial threat to his ascension.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|11-12}} 

Olfsvenn was killed while battling the Storm Maiden sometime before the {{YearlinkName|1489}}.

==Relationships==
Olfsvenn's grandfather was Jarl [[Rault|Rault the Wise]]. He was the son of [[Olfgaut]], the Jarl's son.
