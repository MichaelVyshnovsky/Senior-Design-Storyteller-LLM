Asahi was a member of the ninja Kata clan in the town of Aru in Wa.
Asahi was an expect tactician, always taking the fewest risks.
Asahi led one of the ninja teams sent by the daimyo Yoshi Yoyogi to recover an ancient weapon wielded by descendants of a disgraced samurai of his land in order to stop a prophecy about the end of his rule. Asahis mission in Aru was to recover the blade Oil Seller and kill Fusae and Cachiko.
