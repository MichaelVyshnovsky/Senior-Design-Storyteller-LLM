'''Kaddrus''' was a [[cambion]] agent of the [[Knights of the Shield]] who acted on behalf of the organization during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He served as the personal attendant to [[Lutecia Hhune]], who held great trust in his abilities.

==Abilities==
One tactic of his included ''[[alter self|masquerading himself]]'' as an innocent bystander in order to get near to his quarry.

==Relationships==
Kaddrus was served by three particularly evil [[human]]s, [[Falar]], [[Zaroud al'Ryshal]], and [[Nulra Blacksaddle]].

==History==
When the ''[[Shield of the Hidden Lord]]'' was taken from the [[Hhune]] family crypt, in the {{yearlinkName|1492}},{{BG-DiA-time}} Kaddrus and his compatriots were tasked with its retrieval.
