Slow-Boat Drubella was a Seawind of Umberlee, a member of the Bitch Queens clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queens House in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Drubella was a human woman with short dark brown hair, gathered in a tight bun, and a tan skin tone. She had dark eyes and bore a collection of aquamarine tattoos that framed her face from the forehead to her neck, distracting the observers gaze from scars on the womans nose and right cheek.
Drubellas Seawind uniform was a set of skin-tight ceremonial chainmail dress with light pauldrons and decorative bracers. Her weapon of choice was heavy crossbow.
Circa the , during the Absolute crisis, Seawind Slow-Boat Drubella was among the churchs members who performed the ceremonies and sang hymns in praise of the Bitch Queen. She was alongside Flood Tide Allandra Grey and other priestesses during Holli Dylfords last rites. The recently perished priestess died while swimming in Baldurs Gates Gray Harbor. Drubella believed that death to be a blessing of the Birth Queen, whose embrace was the end goal of all the faithful.
