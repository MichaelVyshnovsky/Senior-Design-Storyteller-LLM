'''Oorok''' was an exceptionally brutal [[ogre]] who commanded others of his kind within the [[Hordes of Dragonspear (army)|Dragonspear horde]], in the {{YearlinkName|1363}}.

==Description==
What Oorok lacked in intelligence and courage he made up for in size and severity.

==History==
During the [[Second Dragonspear War]], Oorok commanded the ogres that were part of the division known as [[Hordes of Dragonspear (army)#Organization|The Storm]]. They fought in the final battle just outside of [[Dragonspear Castle]].
