Ugris was a thief inhabiting Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Ugris sold maps of the ruins of Zhentil Keep following the citys destruction in the . The maps sold for 5 gp a piece a Ugris did not claim any responsibility for loss of life, limb, or property as a result of following one of his maps.
His shop was located on the Throat outside of the Throat Gate of the Foreign Quarter.
