Thoroun was a weapons master and senior member of the Hands of Thay in the late 14 century DR. He was known to operate within the Inner Sea, working a campaign against the Harpers activity within the region.
Thoroun was a stocky man, who bore many scars from a life of combat and martial prowess.
The agent of Thay was known to wear gray leather armor and wielded the scimitar known as Ashram for a time, after it was in the possession of a fellow member of the Hands, Daryn Mellas.
In Uktar 1370 DR, he killed a group of ruffians in a bar within the city of Proskur, just west of Cormyr. After this incident he left town and headed eastward.
