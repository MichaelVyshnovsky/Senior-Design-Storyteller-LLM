'''Choin''', or '''Choin the Blue-Nosed''', was the [[bakemono]] ruler of [[Panjuis]] in [[1359 DR]].

==Description==
As his name implied, Choin possessed a large blue nose centered on a bulbous head. He was scrawny even for a bakemono.

==Activities==
Choin ruled over several hundred bakemono within Panjuis.
