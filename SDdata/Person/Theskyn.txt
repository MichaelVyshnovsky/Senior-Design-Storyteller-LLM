Theskyn was the court mage of Athalantar and an old friend and trusted servant of the Stag King Uthgrael Aumar.
He tutored prince Cauln Aumar and many magelings sent to Hastarl by the outlying knights of the realm. He died soon after Uthgrael in 217 DR, poisoned by Belaur Aumars agents.
