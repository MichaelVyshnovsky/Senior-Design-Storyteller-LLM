Drisinil Barrison DelArmgo was a novice priestess of Lolth at Arach-Tinilith in Menzoberranzan and a member of House Barrison DelArmgo in the late 14 century DR.
Drisinil was described as tall. She had a sharp nose and green eyes.
In the , she snuck out of Tier Breche and was punished for it by being forced to cut her own little fingers off. in order to have her bring her fellow conspirators to an unused classroom in Arach-Tinilith, where she summoned hordes of spiders that slayed them. During the coup, Drisinil killed her aunt and instructor Molvayas Barrison DelArmgo, claiming that Molvayas would have betrayed them to Quenthel. As punishment for the plot, Drisinil was allowed to live but her remaining fingers and tongue were cut off.
