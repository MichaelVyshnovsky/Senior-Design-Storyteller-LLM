King Roderin, or Roderin the Bastard, was the 19th Obarskyr monarch, crowned the king of Cormyr after the reign of Gantharla in the Year of the Soaring Stars, 261 DR.
He succeeded her despite his illegitimacy due to the support of the Silver families and Baerauble. Unfortunately, he did not have an heir when he died and was succeeded by his mothers cousin Thargreve.
His mother was Gantharla the First Queen, daughter of Boldovar the Mad.
