Sledge Pettifeet was a member of the Guild from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Sledge appeared to be a middle-aged halfling woman with light skin, brown eyes, and dirty blonde hair. She wore an archers leathers dyed in the Guilds green colors and carried a bow.
In the , Sledge Pettifeet was tasked with standing guard at the secret entrance into Baldurs Gates Undercellar, where the Guilds Guildhall was hidden. Whilst a half-orcish enforcer Tusgront manned the main entrance, Sledge was ready to dispatch any unwanted guest from a distance.
