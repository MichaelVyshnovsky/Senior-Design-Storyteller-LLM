'''Enitryx''' was a [[black dragon]] and a member of the [[Cult of the Dragon]]. 

==Relationships==
He was the familiar of the [[half-dragon]] [[Vargo Kent]] after [[Amnemis]] became too big and became Vargo's cohort instead.

==Activities==
The [[dragon]] resided in the [[Well of Dragons]] with his master.
