'''Naglarn''' was a farmer of [[Skuldaskar]] in the mid-3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was scornful of prospectors.

==History==
In {{yearlink|240}}, he was present at the [[Herald's Horn]] tavern the night that [[Elminster Aumar]] purchased a ''[[wyrmtongue scepter]]'' from [[Surgath Ilder]], whom Naglarn needled earlier about his job not being honest work.
