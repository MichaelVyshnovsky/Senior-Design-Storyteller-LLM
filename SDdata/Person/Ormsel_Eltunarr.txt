'''Ormsel Eltunarr''' was a member of the [[Harpers]] in the [[Eastern Heartlands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He was a caravan-master who specialized in running fast [[wagon]]s. He was based in [[Mulhessen]], but his activities ranged throughout [[Sembia]] and into the [[Dalelands]].

==Relationships==
He was a friend of [[Mestrel Hawkmantle]], a fellow Harper.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1357}}, Ormsel assisted Mestrel Hawkmantle by communicating the identities of several [[Zhentarim]] agents discovered by the latter to Harpers in [[Shadowdale]].
