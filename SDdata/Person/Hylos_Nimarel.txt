'''Hylos Nimarel''' was an [[elf|elven]] hunter who lived in [[Cormyr]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{Quote
|There are no [[Purple Dragon Knights]] to protect you in the [[Storm Horns]] mountains. You'll have to rely on your own wits and skills if you travel there.
|Hylos
}}
==Description==
Hylos had light skin, blue-green eyes, and short white hair.

==History==
Around the time of the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar]] in the mid-1480s [[DR]],{{DDO-time}} Hylos visited Eveningstar with his [[cart]] and [[ox]]en. He took note of the interest in the [[Storm Horns]] mountains by [[adventurer]]s and [[Purple Dragons]] at the time.
