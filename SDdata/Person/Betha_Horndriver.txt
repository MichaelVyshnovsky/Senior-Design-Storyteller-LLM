'''Betha Horndriver''' was a skilled young woman who performed music in [[Yhaunn]] in [[Sembia]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In [[Eleasis]] of the {{YearlinkName|1373}}, Betha appeared at a high-society party hosted by [[Ammanas Aumleagarr]] at his estate in Yhaunn, where she played the [[harp]] for the attendees. Among others, she briefly met Moonmistress-Designate [[Feena Archwood]] of [[Moonshadow Hall]].
