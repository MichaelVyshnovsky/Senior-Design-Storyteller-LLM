'''Mother''' was a monstrous spawn of [[Graz'zt]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Assault on Maerimydra|33}}
  
==History==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], Graz'zt entrusted Mother to the [[derro]] of [[Ravagestone]]. There, she ate derro, [[myconid]]s, and fungus, growing enormous.

Later, Mother decided to join her father in [[Maerimydra]], and she arrived during the allied assault on the city. Mother and her derro minions inflicted heavy losses on the invading troops.{{Cite adventurers league/Assault on Maerimydra|29}} At last she was killed by an adventuring party.

==Relationships==
Mother was the daughter of the [[demon lord]] Graz'zt and of an unknown, unspeakable creature.

==Abilities==
Mother could spawn various [[fiend]]s, including [[succubi]], [[vrock]]s, and [[bar-lgura]]s.
