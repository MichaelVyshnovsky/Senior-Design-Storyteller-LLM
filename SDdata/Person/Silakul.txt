Silakul was a member of the Cult of the Dragon in Cormanthor.
Silakul was the leader of the Cult cell that assisted Dretchroyaster.
In 1479 DR, the lair of Dretchroyaster was attacked by some adventurers and they defeated Silakul.
A cunning and twisted individual, Silakuls goal was to unlock the true power of a dracolich and use it to increase the Cults power over the Dalelands.
