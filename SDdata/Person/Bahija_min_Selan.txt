Bahija min Selan was a pragmatist and a member of the royal court of Rogostos khedive, Samia al-Said, in 1367 DR.
Bahija was known to be very hedonistic and vain.
Bahija acted in khedive Samias court as the representative of all priests in the city of Rogosto.
