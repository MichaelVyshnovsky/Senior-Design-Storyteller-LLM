'''Semaj''' was a [[conjurer]] and one of the most trusted allies of [[Sarevok Anchev]] who fought with [[Gorion's Ward]] in the [[Undercity]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].

His access to the spell "[[Tattoo of power|Tattoo of Power]]" suggests he has spent some time in Zakhara.
