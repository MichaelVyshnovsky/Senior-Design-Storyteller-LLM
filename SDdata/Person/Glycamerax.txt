'''Glycamerax''' was a [[white dragon]] living near the [[Well of Dragons]].

==History==
When the [[Cult of the Dragon]] attempted to summon [[Tiamat]] into the world at the Well of Dragons, Glycamerax was one of the [[chromatic dragon]] heralds sent to ensure the coming of the Dragon Queen.
