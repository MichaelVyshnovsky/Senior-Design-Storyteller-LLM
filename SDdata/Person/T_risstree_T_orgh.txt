Trisstree Torgh was a drow warrior and priestess of Lolth in Menzoberranzan and an instructor at Arach-Tinilith in the late 14 century DR.
She was a good warrior, always wielding a falchion.
In the , Trisstree was an instructor at Arach-Tinilith, having risen in power despite her commoner origins. She approached Drisinil Barrison DelArmgo and her fellow conspirators to offer her help in overthrowing Mistress Quenthel Baenre but Drisinil did not trust Trisstree and killed her.
