Skedmoor was a butcher of the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR.
Butcher Skedmoor sold several kinds of meat—beef, lamb, fish, and poultry, and so on. While he had his own shop, he delivered to inns and taverns in town by wagon. He had a good reputation for fresh meat.
Skedmoor and his men delivered a load of meat to Lukas Spoondrift of the Sheaf of Wheat inn on Eleint 18 in the  this was a special order to cater for the Merchants Guild meeting the next day. While there, having heard Lukas blame Rolf the roofer for the murder of Dovo, Skedmoor explained to Jasper the origin of Lukass enmity for Rolf, namely a feud over a collapsed roof.
