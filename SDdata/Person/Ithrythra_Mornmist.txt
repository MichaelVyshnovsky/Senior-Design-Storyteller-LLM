'''Ithrythra Mornmist''' was an inhabitant of [[Myth Drannor]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==Appearance==
During her meeting with her friends at [[Satyrdance Pool]], she wore spike-heeled, thigh-high silver boots, a lacy gown, and an undergown. Her hair had a sideswirl around a hand lyre, the symbol of her house.

==Abilities==
She helped weave a spell to turn wine into water.

==Activities==
She grew [[mushroom]]s in her cellar.

==History==
After [[Elminster Aumar]] was made armathor, Ithrythra shared her concerns for the stability of [[Cormanthyr]] with her friends at [[Satyrdance Pool]]. They agreed to try and nudge their husbands towards keeping Cormanthor together.{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|278-281|14}}

In {{yearlink|261}}, she attended to her husband, [[Nelaeryn Mornmist]], when he was affected by the laying of the ''[[mythal]]'', and then struck by the effects of the fighting during its laying.{{cite book/Elminster in Myth Drannor|359-360, 369, 375|Epilogue}}
