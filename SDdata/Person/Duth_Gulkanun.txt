Duth Gulkanun was a War Wizard of Cormyr.
In 1480 DR, Gulkanun went to Castle Irlingstar with Nostyn Vandur. He tried to stop Lord Steward Gelnur Farland, Arclath Delcastle, and Amarune Whitewave from opening the cells.
Later he agreed to form a partnership with them in order to uncover the killer that plagued the castle.
