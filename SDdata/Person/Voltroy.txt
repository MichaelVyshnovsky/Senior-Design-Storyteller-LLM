'''Sir Voltroy''' was a member of the [[Church of Lathander]] from the city of [[Ravens Bluff]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Sir Voltroy was tasked with healing of young Lord [[Lorien Darkarrow]]'s [[acid]] burns and bite wounds left by a [[dlack dragon]]. Voltroy arranged fellow [[cleric]] to cast the ''[[regeneration]]'' spell on Lord Darkarrow on a request from [[Mayor Thoden]]. Sir Voltroy's name was mentioned in the [[Uktar]]-[[Nightal]] issue of ''[[Ravens Bluff Trumpeter]]'', {{YearlinkName|1370}}{{LCRB}}.
