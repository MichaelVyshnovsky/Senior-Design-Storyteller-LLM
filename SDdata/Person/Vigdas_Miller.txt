'''Vigdas Miller''' was a widow miller living near [[Hillsfar]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Shackles of Blood|10}}

==History==
Vigdas lived with her husband, a miller, until he died ''circa'' [[1484 DR]].

''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], she saw [[Red Plumes]] in the [[Tinfellow Farm]] one night the day after the [[halfling]] family had disappeared.

==Relationship==
Vigdas was friends with her neighbors the [[Tinfellow]] family.
