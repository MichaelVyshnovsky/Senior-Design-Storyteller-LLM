Harriet Uldinath was a human blacksmith of the town of Triboar in the Dessarin Valley in the late 15 century DR. She was the owner of the Uldinaths Arms smithy.
She sold fine weapons that were stamped with the Uldinath family glyph, which meant they typically sold for 25% more than the typical price.
