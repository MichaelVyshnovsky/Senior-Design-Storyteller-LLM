'''Harriet Uldinath''' was a [[human]] blacksmith of the town of [[Triboar]] in the [[Dessarin Valley]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. She was the owner of the [[Uldinath's Arms]] smithy.

==Activities==
She sold fine weapons that were stamped with the Uldinath family glyph, which meant they typically sold for 25% more than the typical price.
