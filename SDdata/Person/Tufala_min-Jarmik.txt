'''Tufala min-Jarmik''' was a [[Half-elf|half-elven]] [[ethoist]] priest of [[Jarmik]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Tufala supervised the ten priests of the [[Mosque of the Thirsty Jann]] along the [[Pilgrims' Route]].

All of the priests of the mosque helped passing travelers and pilgrims by offering food and a drink from their special well.
