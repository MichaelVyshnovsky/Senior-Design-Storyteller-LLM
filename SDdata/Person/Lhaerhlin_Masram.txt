'''Lhaerhlin Masram''' was an innkeep who inhabited the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Lhaerlin was tall and well-built.

==Activities==
He owned and ran [[the Raging Lion (Waterdeep)|the Raging Lion]] [[inn]] located on [[Lion Street]] in the northeastern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Possessions==
He always wore his ''[[ring of spell turning]]'' and ''[[wand of lightning|ring of lightning]]''.
