Derio was a clergyman and low-ranking member of the Order of the Gauntlet from Port Nyanzaru in Chult in the late 15 century DR. 
Deiro worked under Alastar Bol.
During the events of the death curse around 1490 DR Viplo, a priest of Azuth who had been driven mad by a piece of a dreamers amulet, captured Deiro and attempted to sacrifice him to the amulet.
