'''Briost Elthauryn''' was a male [[wizard]] who was one of the most powerful [[magelord]]s of [[Athalantar]].

==History==
Briost was one of the most talented members of the magelords. He often acted as an envoy of the magelords in Athalantar and the lands around.

He was killed in [[Elminster Aumar]]'s insurrection.
