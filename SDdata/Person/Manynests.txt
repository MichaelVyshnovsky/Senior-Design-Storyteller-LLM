'''Manynests''' was a [[finch]] that served as a messenger for Lord [[Erlan Duirsar]] of [[Evereska]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Abilities==
The excitable bird could communicate with Evereskan [[elves]] in the language they referred to as "peeptalk".

==History==
Manynests proved himself a brave and trusted ally as he delivered messages between elven sentries and Lord Duirsar when the [[phaerimm]] threatened Evereska in the {{YearlinkName|1371}}.
