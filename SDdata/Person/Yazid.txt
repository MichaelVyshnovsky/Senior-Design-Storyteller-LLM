Yazid was a skilled desert rider and member of the House of Hanif tribe in the Haunted Lands of interior Zakhara around 1367 DR.
Though short, Yazid was extremely strong. He inherited his fathers sharp senses and features.
Dissatisfied with the state of the tribe, Yazid believe they could build their own enlightened city were they not tethered to the court in Huzuz.
Yazid was sympathetic to the House of Hoteks quarrel with Hiyal and its Sultana.
Yazid was lost in a sand storm and separated from his tribe for five days. He spent that time among the jann who divulged wondrous secrets of the future.
Yazid was one of two sons born to Sheikh Kaldhun bin Hanif. Muawiya was his half-brother. Yazid was conceived on the same day as his brother and born later on the same day as his brother.
