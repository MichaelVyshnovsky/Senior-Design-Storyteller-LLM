'''Xana''' was a [[cleric]] of [[Iyachtu Xvim]] in [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Xana was the leader of the cult of Xvim in Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the {{YearlinkName|1368}}. She was a priest of [[Bane]] before Zhentil Keep's fall.
