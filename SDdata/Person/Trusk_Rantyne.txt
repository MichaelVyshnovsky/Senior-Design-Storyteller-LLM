'''Trusk Rantyne''' was the Gate Lord of [[Mreyelundar]] in [[1479 DR]].

==Background==
Trusk was a jeweler and the nominal lord of Mreyelundar. However, he had no real power because the city's true ruler was a [[thief]] named [[Narnorn]].
