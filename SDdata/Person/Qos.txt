Qos was the most powerful of the dukar in Myth Nantar.
Qos was one of the greatest mysteries of Serôs. He lived inside the Dukar Reef inside Myth Nantar and only his students and his fellow paragons had met him. Qos had total control over the Great Barrier and was the only paragon capable of teaching the peacekeepers ways.
Qos was a key player in the Twelfth Serôs War.
