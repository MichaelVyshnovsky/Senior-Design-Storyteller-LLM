Fist '''Smithwynn''' was a member of the [[Flaming Fist]] mercenary company from the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|''Don't go looking for trouble.''|Fist Smithwynn}}

==Description==
Smithwynn was a [[human]] man with a clean-shaven face, blue eyes, and a light skin tone. He wore a set of [[chainmail]] armor as a part of her Flaming Fist uniform decorated with a [[tabard]] that bore the company's banner, and was armed with a [[spear]] and a [[shield]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Fist Smithwynn was stationed within the barracks of the [[Heapside]] district of Baldur's Gate, where he, a [[steel watcher]], and several other Flaming Fists guarded a small dungeon prison.
