'''Trisk Adamantelpiece''' was the [[svirfneblin]] commander of the reformed [[Burrow Wardens]] of [[Blingdenstone]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. They were trained for this purpose by the [[ghost]] of former Burrow Warden [[Jadger]], who considered Trisk to be his brightest student.
