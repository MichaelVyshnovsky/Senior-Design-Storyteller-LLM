Fender Stouthammer is a young dwarf of the Stouthammer clan. He was among those that followed Torgar when he left Mirabar for Mithral Hall. He was captured by Tosun Armgo in a sneak attack with a poisoned blade.Ibid. p. 730-1 He managed to warn refugees from Nesmé of an ambush before being skewered by a sharpened branch.
 He is later healed by a Cleric of Lolth, Kaerlic Suun Wett. Tosun and Kaerlic present him to Obould Many-Arrows as a gift.Ibid. p. 1015-6 He returns home in the company of Drizzt DoUrden.Ibid. p. 1038
