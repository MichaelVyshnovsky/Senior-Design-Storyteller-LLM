Crown Prince Foril Obarskyr was part of the ruling family of Cormyr and son of Azoun IV and Filfaeril Selzair. He was born on Mirtul 4 1332 DR, but was assassinated on Uktar 11, 1334 DR by the Fire Knives at the age of two.
Foril Obarskyr I, his grandnephew, was honored with his name.
