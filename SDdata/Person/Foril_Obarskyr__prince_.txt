'''Crown Prince Foril Obarskyr''' was part of the ruling family of [[Cormyr]] and son of [[Azoun IV]] and [[Filfaeril Selzair]]. He was born on Mirtul 4 [[1332 DR]], but was assassinated on Uktar 11, [[1334 DR]] by the [[Fire Knives]] at the age of two.{{Cite dragon annual/2000/After the Dragon: The Kingdom of Cormyr Today|61}}

[[Foril Obarskyr I]], his grandnephew, was honored with his name.
