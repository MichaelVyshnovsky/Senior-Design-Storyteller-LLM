'''Roben''' was the head monk in [[Kuda]] in [[Kozakura]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Roben managed a small monastery in Kuda attached to the [[Shrine of Niwa Ozuchi]]. He led twenty monks.

==Personality==
Roben always tried to find peaceful solutions.
