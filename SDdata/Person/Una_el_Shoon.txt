Una el Shoon was a Calishite woman who was the mother of Akkabar Shoon the Younger, Queen Arhymeria Ithal of Tethyr, and Amahl Shoon I. She was the daughter-in-law of Akkabar Shoon the Elder, being married to his oldest son.
