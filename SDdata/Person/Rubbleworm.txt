'''Rubbleworm''' was a hungry [[carrion crawler]] that resided in the [[Underdark]] beneath the ruins of [[Myth Drannor]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==History==
The monster laired in an Underdark cavern with [[demon]]s and other creatures beneath the ruined city, passed by the [[drow]] captain [[Niziira Xiltun]] during a raid.
