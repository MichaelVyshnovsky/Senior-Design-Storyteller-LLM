Chiltriss Kilsek was the leader of a group of driders.
Chiltriss was once a priestess of Lolth of House Kilsek before she was cursed to become a drider.
In time, she became the leader of a large group of driders living in the Underdark under the Misty Forest.
In 1375 DR, Chiltriss and her fellows were recruited into the Selvetargtlin by the judicator Dhairn.
