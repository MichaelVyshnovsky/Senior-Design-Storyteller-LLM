'''Manjusri''' was an inhabitant of [[Manass]] in [[Khazari]].

==Activities==
Manjusri served as the personal adviser of [[Sanjar al-Mulk]], the governor of Manass.

==History==
Manjusri was originally a dong chang from the [[Katakoro Shan]] mountains.
