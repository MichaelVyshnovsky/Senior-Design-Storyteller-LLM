Philaerin was a moon elf and the Eldest of the Circle of Reilloch Domayr. He resided in Tower Reilloch on the island of Evermeet.
Despite Philaerins age, his face was smooth and unlined.
Philaerin was slain in early 1374 DR when Tower Reilloch was attacked. He died protecting a vault in the Tower known as Nandiyerrons Armory.
