Patrick was an adventurer that lived and died in Amn during the midâ€“14 century DR. He was turned to an undead shadow and forced to serve the Shade Lord.
Patrick adventured with a group that included the intrepid Mazzy Fentan. The two were companions as well as lovers, and cared deeply for one another.
In his undead existence, Patrick wore the suit of armor called Darkmail and carried the Duskblade halberd.
In the , Patrick and his comrades were hired by Minister Lloyd of Imnesvale. They set out discover the whereabouts of Imnesvales missing protector Merella, and investigate murders that afflicted the town.
Their quest took them to the nearby abandoned temple to confront the evil Shade Lord that dwelled within. Most of the group were slain by this being and his growing army and transformed into shadows themselves. The shadow of Patrick was recruited to serve as the personal guard for the Shade Lord himself.
