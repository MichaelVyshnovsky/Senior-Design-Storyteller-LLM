Barger was a bald clerk and member of the town watch in Nesmé in 1484 DR.
Just after the start of the War of the Silver Marches, Barger was appointed leader of the town watch after all the high-ranking officers died in the fighting. He was very nervous, having never fought a real battle in all his life.
