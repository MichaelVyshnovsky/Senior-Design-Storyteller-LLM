'''Brassons''' was a rogue [[neogi]] restaurateur and owner of the [[Ocean's Wake]] bar on [[Dragon Rock]] in the [[Tears of Selûne]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
A former slave trader, he escaped from a [[mindspider]] around [[1362 DR]] and became a follower of [[Torm]].

==Abilities==
He was a magic-user able to cast spells like a priest. 

He was a good source of information.

==Description==
Brassons was {{SI|3|feet|long=yes}} tall and very ugly, but exceptionally mild-mannered for a neogi.
