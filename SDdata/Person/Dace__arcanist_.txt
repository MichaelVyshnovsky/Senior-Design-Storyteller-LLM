Dace was a Netherese arcanist who developed several language-based spells.
This mentalist was devoted to the goddess of magic, Mystryl. He considered revering Mystryl to be the most important thing to him.
The archwizards of Netheril saw Dace as not much more than an arcanist who should have been a priest. As many arcanists at the time considered themselves to be just below the gods, they frowned upon Daces reverence.
Dace was born in . In , Dace came up with the spell that would become known as comprehend languages. After developing ventriloquism and tongues, the arcanist invented the taunt spell. Dace attempted to discover a means of magical life extension and sought out potions to do so. However, archwizards of Netheril would plot to end Daces life as they didnt share his utmost devotion to Mystryl.
