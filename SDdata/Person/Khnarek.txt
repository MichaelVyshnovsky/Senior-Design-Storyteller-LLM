'''Khnarek''' was a senior member of the [[Unseen]] in [[Waterdeep]] in [[1370 DR]].

==Personality==
The [[doppelganger]] was a reluctant ally of [[Hlaavin]], often acting autonomously, such as when it forged an alliance with [[Priamon Rakesk]] of the [[Twisted Rune]].
