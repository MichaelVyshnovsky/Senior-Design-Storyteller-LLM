Alataz Thrindol was a merchant from the city of Telflamm who once resided in the small town of Ravilars Cloak in Turmish.
Alataz was a grim-looking man with a shaved head. The merchant was renowned for nerves of steel and sharp blades that discouraged his competitors from crossing the man.
Before settling in the Ravilars Cloak, Alataz Thrindol had a rich career and many travels under his belt. Alataz spent about a dozen years at the Cloak before embarking on another journey.
Circa the , Ravilars Cloaks inhabitants discovered a bag of emeralds valued at more than 686,000 gold coins. The discovery was made in a drained cesspit near Alataz Thrindols house. The locals quickly assumed that the treasure belonged to the wealthy merchant whos been gone from his home in the Clock for seven years at the time of discovery.
Alatazs fate remained unknown as his home was claimed by Rastar of the Blades, a factor from Calimshan.
