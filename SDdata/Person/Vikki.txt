Vikki was a Cashguard Basher in the employ of the Counting House of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Vikki was a human woman with olive skin and long straight dark hair. As a Cashguard Basher, she wore a set of uniform plate mail armor and a helmet. Vikki was armed with a two-handed war hammer.
In the , Cashguard Basher Vikki was tasked with guarding and patrolling the private docks that belonged to the Counting House in the Lower City, alongside a number of fellow Cashguards and the citys steel watchers.
