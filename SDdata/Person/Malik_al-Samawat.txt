'''Malik al-Samawat''' was a [[noble djinni]] who lived atop the highest peak on the island of [[Al-Zira]] in the [[Djinni's Claws]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Personality==
Malik was incredibly haughty and easily distracted.

==Activities==
Malik enjoyed relaxing in a field of golden clouds within his home.
