Weilus was one of the Blades of the Old Man as of . On the night of Tarsakh 25, they became aware of an intruder in Sentinelspire. While they were looking out for the intruder, they came across Lewan and Ulaan. On his orders, Dayul and Turan tried to get them to return to their room however, this led to a fight. He took Lewans hammer during the fight, but was killed by swarming killoren not long after.
