'''Maklin Mucklar''' was a [[gnome]] [[alchemist]] in [[Waterdeep]] in [[1479 DR]].

==Activities==
Maklin sold his alchemical products inside the [[Yawning Portal]] inn with the permission of [[Durnan]].

==History==
In 1479 DR, a male human arrived at the Yawning Portal accusing Maklin of selling to him years ago in [[Baldur's Gate]] a poisoned healing potion that caused the death of his companion.

==Relationship==
Durnan really appreciated the kickbacks that Maklin gave him.

==Personality==
Maklin was a distant and aloof individual. Many considered him quite odd but Durnan believed him an honest guy.
