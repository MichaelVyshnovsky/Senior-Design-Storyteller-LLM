'''Ashtara''' was an [[asabi]] merchant and slaver in the revived [[Netheril|Netherese]] flying city of [[Undrentide]].

==History==
Ashtara was exploring the ruins of Undrentide when he met [[Drogan Droganson's apprentice]]. He tried to enslave him.
He died during the subsequent fall of the city.
