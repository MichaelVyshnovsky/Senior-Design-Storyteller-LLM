Cragh was a Netherese arcanist of the Golden Age of Netheril who was born deaf and developed the spell deafness.
Unable to speak, he communicated entirely via sign language, only saying a few syllables when required for spellcasting.
The skilled archwizard and mentalist was able to cast the majority of his spells without requiring verbal components, apart from those of very high power.
As well as being well respected by archwizards of Netheril, Cragh gained the respect of the dwarves that dwelt in Ascore. They named him "dwarf-friend" and he went to visit them in their stronghold quite often.
