'''Radeel''' was an expatriate from the land of [[Calimshan]], living in the City of Coin, [[Athkatla]], in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''I do so be missing my homeland... one day I am swearing to Calimshan I shall return. Yes, one day.''|Radeel}}

==Description==
Radeel was a pale-skinned man with brown hair.

==History==
Belmin Gergas frequented the the grand market in Athkatla's [[Athkatla/Center District|Center District]] – [[Waukeen's Promenade]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}. He was not a native of Amn and did not know Athkatla well, sometimes getting lost and asking for directions to his lodgings.
