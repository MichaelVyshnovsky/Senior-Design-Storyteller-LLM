Ulviirala Rilynt was the matron mother of House Rilynttar of Ched Nasad.
The priestess of Lolth did not take the full name of her house as her surname, but only part of.
In 1370 DR, Ulviirala had a seat on Ched Nasads ruling council as the eighth matron mother.
