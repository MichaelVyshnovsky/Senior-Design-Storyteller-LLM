}}The '''Guardian''', or '''He Who Guards Those That Sleep''', was a [[Yak-folk|yak-man]] inhabiting a secret complex beneath [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Golden Huzuz|51}}

==Activities==
The Guardian was tasked with watching over the helpless bodies of both the [[Watcher]] and the [[Unspeakable]] while they remained in possession of their victims. He was assisted by a pair of [[earthservant]]s.
