'''Olarra Idlewist''' was a [[Harper]] agent who, for a time, operated out of [[The Lonesome Tankard Inn and Tavern]] in the town of [[Eveningstar]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Olarra was a [[human]] woman with dusky skin, white eyes, and white hair. She wore her ''[[Harper pin]]'' and carried a [[lute]].

==History==
In the 15{{th}} century DR, Olarra Idlewist posed as a [[minstrel]] patron of the Lonesome Tankard in Eveningster, offering Harpers' aide to the off-world adventurers who set out to investigate the nearby ruins of the [[Haunted Halls]].
