Almeryl al-Shap was a halfling pragmatist and wandering traveler in Zakhara around 1367 DR.
Almeryl was an outgoing individual.
Almeryl enjoyed traveling the Land of Fate and discovering new gods and goddesses to worship. He prayed to each and every one that he knew of on a daily basis.
He wore padded armor and wielded a long-handled mace. He had quite an impressive collection of holy symbols.
