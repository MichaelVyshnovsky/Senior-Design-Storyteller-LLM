'''Almeryl al-Shap''' was a [[halfling]] [[pragmatist]] and wandering traveler in [[Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1993|374|[[Almeryl al-Shap]]}}

==Personality==
Almeryl was an outgoing individual.

==Activities==
Almeryl enjoyed traveling the Land of Fate and discovering new gods and goddesses to worship. He prayed to each and every one that he knew of on a daily basis.

==Possessions==
He wore [[padded armor]] and wielded a long-handled [[mace]]. He had quite an impressive collection of [[holy symbol]]s.
