Secondhand Dunlop was a merchant operating in the Blacklake District of Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.
Dunlop sold supplies—possibly secondhand—to people entering the dangerous, ruined district. He had a stall near the entrance to the district in the Tatters area.
In the , Dunlop provided a few potions of minor healing to adventurers working for Private Hawthidon Faalwun to retrieve the stolen Crown of Neverwinter. He hoped theyd get back at the Nashers for running honest merchants out of the district.
