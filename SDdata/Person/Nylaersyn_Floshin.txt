Nylaersyn Floshin was a member of House Dlardrageth that infiltrated the Unseen in Waterdeep by the Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR.
Nylaersyn was a feyri member of House Dlardrageth. Some time before 1372 DR she posed as a doppelganger named Synyl and managed to infiltrate the Unseen, a spy network in Waterdeep.
Nylaersyn as Synyl was known to have posed as these individuals
 Biafyndar Loceath A male gold elf initiate of Corellon Larethian who frequented the Pantheon Temple of the Seldarine.
 Hrangor Splitstone A male human of Illuskan descent who worked as a fletcher at Riautars Weaponry.
 Tymaara Moonsmile A female half-elf of moon elf blood who worked as a waitress at the Elfstone Tavern.
