'''Bezelar Mujarif''' was a well-known [[psionicist]] living in [[Calimshan]] in the late 1350s [[DR]].

He described a tangent of psionic combat as feeling "like a gallstone in the mind" and full [[contact]] as "like a tiger has unsheathed its claws in your head".
