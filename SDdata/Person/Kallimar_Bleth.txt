'''Kallimar Bleth''' was a member of the [[Bleth]] noble house of [[Cormyr]].

==Description==
Kallimar was a large man with dark hair.

==Personality==
Kallimar was similar to his ancestor [[Mondar Bleth]], both crude and bad-tempered.
