'''Ghunduilith Leiyraghon''' was the Lord Chancellor, which included duties as chairman and treasurer, for the [[Council of Lords (Melvaunt)|Council of Lords]] in [[Melvaunt]] around [[1367 DR]] and [[1373]].
==Personality==
The Lord Chancellor was a wise and cautious individual.

==Description==
Ghunduilith was an old bearded man.
