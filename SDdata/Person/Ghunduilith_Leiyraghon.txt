Ghunduilith Leiyraghon was the Lord Chancellor, which included duties as chairman and treasurer, for the Council of Lords in Melvaunt around 1367 DR and 1373.
The Lord Chancellor was a wise and cautious individual.
Ghunduilith was an old bearded man.
