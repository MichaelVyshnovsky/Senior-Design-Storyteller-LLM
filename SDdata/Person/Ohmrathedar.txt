'''Ohmrathedar the Thoughtful''' was a [[bronze dragon]] who lived in [[Waterdeep]] ''circa'' the [[1374 DR|Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR]].

==Activities==
Ohmrathedar was one of roughly a dozen dragons who lived in hiding in Waterdeep. He was an ally of [[Taraunramorlamurla]], a [[song dragon]] who acted as an intermediary between the dragons and human society. Ohmrathedar was known to fight on Taraunramorlamurla's behalf when necessary.
