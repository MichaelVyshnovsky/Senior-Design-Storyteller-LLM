Alexander Rainforest was an inhabitant of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Alexander Rainforest perished in the , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldurs Gate. Rainforests tombstone was commissioned to be carved by Candulhallows Tombstones in the Lower City. The grave marker was stored in Candulhallows workshop. It read "Alexander Rainforest. Meticulous in life, mellifluous in death."
