'''Stong''' was a wealthy merchant from the [[Sembia]]n city of [[Selgaunt]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He dealt primarily in fine clothing, garments and leather products. Stong was particularly famous in the years around the [[Time of Troubles]].
