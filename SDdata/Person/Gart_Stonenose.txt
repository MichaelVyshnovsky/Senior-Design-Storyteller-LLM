Gart Stonenose was a dwarven shopkeeper of Freedale in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The dwarf had only one eye.
Gart was very grumpy, except among friends.
He was a capable warrior, but had some difficulties due to the loss of an eye.
As the proprietor of Freedales General Store, he had just about one of everything.
He was the owner and shopkeeper of the general store in Freedale. He doubled prices for people he disliked.
He was friends with local adventurer Delvar Ironfist.
Once an adventurer, Gart had to retire from adventuring after losing an eye. He had since become the shopkeeper for the General Store in Freedale.
