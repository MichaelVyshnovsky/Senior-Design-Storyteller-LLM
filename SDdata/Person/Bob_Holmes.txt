'''Capt'n Bob Holmes''' was a [[human]] pirate from the isle of [[Brynnlaw]] in the [[Sea of Swords]] who sailed the ''[[Bleedin' Mary]]'' vessel in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Meat and grog, innkeep! By Umberlee's teat, where is my meat and grog!?''|Bob Holmes}}

==Description==
Holmes was a boisterous man with a mean competitive streak, especially when alcohol was involved. He gaunt in appearance.

==Relationships==
The Captain of the ''Bleedin' Mary'' had no love for pirate lord [[Desharik]] of Brynnlaw. He hated the high fees enforced on the captains, like those of a "stuffed-[[tunic]] harbormaster," in Bob's own words.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Capt'n Bob Holmes and the crew of the ''Bleedin' Mary'' could be found drinking in the [[Vulgar Monkey]] dock-side tavern.
