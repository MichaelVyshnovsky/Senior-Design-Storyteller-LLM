Captn Bob Holmes was a human pirate from the isle of Brynnlaw in the Sea of Swords who sailed the Bleedin Mary vessel in the late 14 century DR.
Holmes was a boisterous man with a mean competitive streak, especially when alcohol was involved. He gaunt in appearance.
The Captain of the Bleedin Mary had no love for pirate lord Desharik of Brynnlaw. He hated the high fees enforced on the captains, like those of a "stuffed-tunic harbormaster," in Bobs own words.
In the , Captn Bob Holmes and the crew of the Bleedin Mary could be found drinking in the Vulgar Monkey dock-side tavern.
