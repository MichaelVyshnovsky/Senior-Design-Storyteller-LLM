'''Wyancott''' was the leader of an extended family of foresters living in the [[Forest of Tethir]] in [[1366 DR]].

==Description==
In 1366, Wyancott was a middle-aged man. He had faint-colored hair and narrow features.

==Activities==
Wyancott led his group in a journey from southern Tethir along the [[Trade Way]] over the [[Starspire Mountains]] to [[Zazesspur]], intending to trade animal skins and bark used for tanning and vegetables used for the manufacture of [[dye]]s.

==History==
In the spring of the {{YearlinkName|1366}}, Wyancott and his wagons were set upon by bandits. Thankfully, they were rescued by [[Star Protective Services]].
