'''Bouladin al-Mutajalli'''<ref group="note">The alternate spelling of Bouldadin al-Mutajialli was found on page 34 of the source. was the Amir of the [[jann]] of the [[House of Sihr]] within the [[High Desert]] of [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Personality==
Bouladin was an honest and noble janni, but did not hesitate to strike against those who opposed him or threatened the House of Sihr.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39"/>

==Relationships==
A longtime ally of [[Tajar]] and [[Ali al-Hadd|Sheikh Ali al-Hadd]], Bouladin aided the great city during times of need. Bouladin also made sure a janni representative from the House of Sihr appeared before the [[Grand Caliph]] to pledge their support to him as well.

Bouladin's oldest daughter was named [[Jamala al-Mutajalli|Jamala]]. 

==Activities==
Bouladin had to spend much of his time and effort keeping the [[ghul-kin]] and [[great ghul]]s of the [[Pit of the Ghuls]] in check.
