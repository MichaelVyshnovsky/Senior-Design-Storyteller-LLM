'''Blaermar''' of [[Athkatla]] was a mage of the late 3{{rd}} century [[DR]]. He challenged [[Adriyana Voudsarr]] for the position of the [[Magister]]; during their duel, though he lost, his flames laid waste to much of the [[Neverwinter Wood]].
