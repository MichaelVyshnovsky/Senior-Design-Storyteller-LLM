'''Lady Threnka''' was a [[Sembia]]n who was alive around [[1188 DR]].

She participated in the discussions with King [[Pryntaler Obarskyr]] in an attempt to acquire the city of [[Marsember]]. The others in her group were [[Kodlos]], [[Homfast]], [[Bennesey]], and [[Jollitha Par]].
