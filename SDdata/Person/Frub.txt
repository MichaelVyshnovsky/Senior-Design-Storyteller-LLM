Frub was a kobold from the Dragons Rest cloister in the late 15 century DR.
Frub had limitless energy and relished asking questions about the activities of the cloisters inhabitants.
Circa the , Frub shared with three other kobolds a small monastic cell in the Dragons Rest cloister cut into the cliff face. Each of the four slept on individual hammocks. Just like all the other kobolds, he worked at night because of their sunlight sensitivity.
