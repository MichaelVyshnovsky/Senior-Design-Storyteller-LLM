Zhessae was a member of the Zhentarim in Mulmaster in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.
Zhessae was a follower of Fzoul Chembryl.
As a trusted member of Fzouls faction, Zhessae was peripherally involved in the hunt for Shandril Shessair.
