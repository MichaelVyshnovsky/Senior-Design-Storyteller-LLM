Brandagaeris was a hero who was deceased in or before the 8 century DR.
He was tall, tanned, and strong.
His choice in romantic partners was at the very least quite peculiar.
He had been a hero of some renown.
By the , Brandagaeris had had a relationship with Saeraede Lyonora, an apparently unique Netherese ghost-like experiment, and fed her his own lifeforce for three seasons. However, shed drained him completely by the end.
Brandagaeris had come to love Saeraede Lyonora, and fed her willingly.
