'''Langreth Ironhorn''' was a steward of King [[Foril Obarskyr I]] of [[Cormyr]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was gruff.

==Relationships==
The sight of the [[ghost]] of Princess [[Alusair Obarskyr]] only made him chuckle, strongly implying he had seen it before.

==Abilities==
He knew first aid, most likely.

==Appearance==
He was a very old, tall and girthy man, who carried himself around in two sticks.

==History==
In the {{yearlinkName|1479}}, Langreth was part of a meeting called by King [[Foril Obarskyr I]] about the toxic smoke in the [[Castle Obarskyr/Hall of Justice|Hall of Justice]], where the [[Council of the Dragon]] was supposed to have been held that day; at least one [[War Wizard]] had to have been a traitor, to teleport inside the materials to create the poisonous vapors. During the converse, Queen [[Alusair Obarskyr]] entered, causing a fellow in their converse, the [[Lady of Graces]] [[Jalessa Windstone]], to faint;{{cite book/Bury Elminster Deep|46-48|5}}
