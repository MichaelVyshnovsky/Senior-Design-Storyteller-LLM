'''Duramagar''' was a caravan guard in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was exultant at times.

==Description==
Duramagar had a ragged beard.

==Possessions==
The guard wielded a [[waraxe]].

==History==
Duramagar was a member of [[Orthil Voldovan]]'s caravan during the trip from [[Scornubel]] to [[Waterdeep]] in the late {{yearlink|1357}}. He drew attention to himself while riding to [[Face Crag]] during the first part of the trip, where he stood on his saddle, and waved a waraxe while hooting energetically.
