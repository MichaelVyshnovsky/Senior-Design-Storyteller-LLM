Duramagar was a caravan guard in the mid-14 century DR.
He was exultant at times.
Duramagar had a ragged beard.
The guard wielded a waraxe.
Duramagar was a member of Orthil Voldovans caravan during the trip from Scornubel to Waterdeep in the late . He drew attention to himself while riding to Face Crag during the first part of the trip, where he stood on his saddle, and waved a waraxe while hooting energetically.
