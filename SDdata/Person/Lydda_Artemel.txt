Lydda Artemel was a noblewoman of the Waterdhavian noble house Artemel during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
She lived within Artemel Villa located at the corner of Sul Street and Vondil Street in the northeast section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward.
Lydda was the consort of Artemels patriach, Bresnoss Artemel. Ord was her eldest son.
