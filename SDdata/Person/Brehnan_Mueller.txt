==Relationships==
Brehnan was married for a time before his wife passed away ''circa'' the late 1340s [[DR]]. He raised their daughter [[Annalee]] as a single parent for nearly a decade at least.

==History==
During the [[Time of Troubles]], in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}, Brenhan discovered a young [[magic-user]] by the name of [[Midnight]] alone and unconscious during a terrific storm. He took the young woman to his home and cared for her over two weeks' time.
