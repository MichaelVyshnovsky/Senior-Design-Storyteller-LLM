'''Old Meg''' was a [[ghost]] reputed to haunt a gorge near [[Eveningstar]].

==History==
A [[Highknight]] of [[Cormyr]] brought up the myth of Old Meg while burying the remains of a comrade, [[Daervin]], in {{yearlink|1357}}.

==Rumors & Legends==
People in [[Eveningstar]] swore the gorge was haunted by the ghost of a local witch, identified as "Old Meg" by the locals.
