Aranah al-Mursal was a moralist priest inhabiting Talab in the Pantheist League of interior Zakhara in 1367 DR. 
Aranah was scholarly and quiet.
Aranah was Caliph Kia al-Sadids chief assistant. She served as his scribe, record-keeper and librarian. Her duties included oversight of all trade documentation of both goods and slaves, as well as inspecting outpost forts. She often visited Hilm on official business to represent Talab.
