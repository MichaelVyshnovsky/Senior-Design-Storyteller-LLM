'''Ikert''' was a [[halfling]] merchant from the City of Coin, [[Athkatla]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Good business to you, my Lady... Ikert bids you welcome to my humble establishment. You are new to the docks, yes? Might I ask your name?''|Ikert}}

==Description==
Ikert was a hin male who liked to dress in black leathers and carried a [[short sword]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Ikert ran his merchant business from a stall in Athkatla's [[Athkatla/Wave District|Wave District]]. His humble stock included some mundane weapons and armor with an occasional common magic [[potion]]. He politely tried to convince clients of the finest quality of his wares, despite poor selection and prevalent thievery in the district.
