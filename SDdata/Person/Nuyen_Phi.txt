Nuyen Phi was the prince of Sayan in Malatra.
Phi believed himself a great general. He was an able speaker. Phi was an arrogant and supremely self-confident individual.
Around 1357 DR, Phi was the leading member of the Nuyen House of Laothan, which controlled most of the military and the lands from the Tu Lung borders to Nalamh. Phi usually ignored the edicts of King Thok Lian.
