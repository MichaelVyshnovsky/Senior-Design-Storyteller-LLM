'''Nuyen Phi''' was the prince of [[Sayan]] in [[Malatra]].

==Personality==
Phi believed himself a great general. He was an able speaker. Phi was an arrogant and supremely self-confident individual.

==History== 
Around [[1357 DR]], Phi was the leading member of the [[Nuyen House]] of [[Laothan]], which controlled most of the military and the lands from the [[T'u Lung]] borders to [[Nalamh]]. Phi usually ignored the edicts of King [[Thok Lian]].
