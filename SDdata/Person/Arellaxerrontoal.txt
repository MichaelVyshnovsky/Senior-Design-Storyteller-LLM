Arellaxerrontoal was an bronze dragon living in Forest of Tethir and the regional agent of the Moonstars for Amn and Tethyr in the Year of the Tankard, 1370 DR.
Arellaxerrontoal was a bronze dragon living in Forest of Tethir with her mate in the past at one point the blue dragon great wyrm Iryklathagra killed her mate. For a long time she was consumed by revenge. Arellaxerrontoals work for the Moonstars alleviated her pains.
Arellaxerrontoal believe that the unity of humanity and elves that the Moonstars hope to achieve can bring the demise of all evil dragons, like the killer of their mate.
