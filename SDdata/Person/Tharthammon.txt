'''Tharthammon''' was a [[pit fiend]] of [[Avernus]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]] who was slain by [[Nergal (archdevil)|Nergal]].

==Personality==
He was grim and close-mouthed.

==Description==
He was unusually portly and large, even among his fellow fiends.

==Relationships==
He was frightening enough that even the dukes did not talk much to him when he gave them a dark look.

==History==
As of the {{yearlinkName|1372}}, [[Nergal]] had slain Thartammon, and would use his shape to avoid discovery if needed.
