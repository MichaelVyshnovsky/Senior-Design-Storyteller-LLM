Tharthammon was a pit fiend of Avernus in the late 14 century DR who was slain by Nergal.
He was grim and close-mouthed.
He was unusually portly and large, even among his fellow fiends.
He was frightening enough that even the dukes did not talk much to him when he gave them a dark look.
As of the , Nergal had slain Thartammon, and would use his shape to avoid discovery if needed.
