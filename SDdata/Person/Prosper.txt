'''Prosper''' was a [[tiefling]] [[warlock]] and [[adventurer]] on the [[Sword Coast]].

==Personality==
Prosper's motivation for adventuring was to amass wealth and power.

==Possessions==
The focal point of his powers was his Book of Shadows, which contained his greatest secrets and that was protected from prying eyes by several gruesome [[curse]]s.

==Relationships==
He was part of an adventuring troupe with the [[elf|elven]] [[paladin]] [[Galea]], the [[human]] [[wizard]] [[Sefris]], and the [[dragonborn]] [[barbarian]] [[Vrondiss]].

==History==
Prosper claimed to have gained his warlock powers after winning a bet with an ancient and powerful being that he would not name.
