'''Haedyll''' was a [[halfling]] [[cleric]] of [[Eldath]] from the [[Thornwood]].

==Description==
Haedyll had bright, blue eyes, brown hair, and a pug nose. He dressed in white clothes, and wore a golden necklace with red gemstones.

==Personality==
He was known as a calm and harmonious person.

==Possessions==
Haedyll owned a pair of ''[[boots of levitation]]''.

==Relationships==
He had at least one brother.The description on Haedyll's card says "he and a brother traveled to Silverymoon", which implies he had additional siblings.

==History==
While traversing the [[Evermoors]] en route to [[Silverymoon]], Haedyll and his brother came under attack. His brother was struck by an arrow and died, and Haedyll tried desperately to scrounge up enough gold to pay for both his ''[[resurrection (spell)|resurrection]]'', and their return home.
