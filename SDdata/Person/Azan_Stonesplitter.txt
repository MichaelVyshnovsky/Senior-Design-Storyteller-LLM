'''Azan Stonesplitter''' was the owner and proprietor of [[Ironhand's Arms]], a weapon shop in [[Highcastle]], in the late 1400s [[DR]]. His store offered quality weapons at fair prices for the local Dalesmen and travelers who often passed through on the road between [[Cormyr]] and [[Sembia]]. Azan was also a capable blacksmith and could forge excellent weapons for patrons so requested.
