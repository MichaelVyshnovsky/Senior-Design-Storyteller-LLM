'''Roadaeron Lorndessar''' was the second and fourth King of the [[Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty]] who ruled the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Roadaeron became King in the {{YearlinkName|1111}}, when his granduncle King [[Sarvyn Eorn|Sarvyn "the Old"]] died. He ruled until the {{YearlinkName|1117}}, before his throne was usurped by his Mage Royal [[Alzurth]]. Roadaeron lived in exile for over 40 years before reclaiming his rightful throne right before his death in the {{YearlinkName|1162}}.

==Relationships==
He had seven children during his life. His third son [[Blaervaer Lorndessar|Blaervaer]] would rule as King.
