Onlagar Blurnwood was a mage until the late mid-14 century DR.
Onlagar Blurnwood was old and crippled.
As a master, though testy, he cared a lot for his apprentice he was quite protective a father. He delighted in teaching Talatha about anything.
With his apprentices help, he defeated Indolrere Moravan.
Onlagar had a somewhat ramshackle house, which slowly fell apart after his death. Nonetheless, there were objects in it that interested people passing by, such as Mlanduvver Tassio.
Onlagar was once an adventuring companion of Mirt. The latter delivered unto him Talatha Vaerovree, whom he raised.
