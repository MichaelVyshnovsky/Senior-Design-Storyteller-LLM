'''Perijan al-Danaf''' was the driving force behind the wealthy [[House of Al-Danafi]] merchant family in [[Huzuz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/City of Delights/Golden Huzuz|45}}

==Personality==
Perijan was shy and soft-spoken around others.

==Activities==
Despite her calm demeanor, Perijan was a financial genius. Though she would never brag about her accomplishments, she single-handedly built the wealthiest merchant house in all of [[Zakhara]]. She was a wise investor for any profits from her [[Khwaja al-Danaf|husband's]] many sea voyages.

==Relationships==
Perijan's daughter, [[Tanya bint Perijan|Tanya]], was a wife to [[Grand Caliph]] [[Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir]]. Perijan remained close with her daughter after her wedding, allowing the family intimate access to the Grand Caliph.
