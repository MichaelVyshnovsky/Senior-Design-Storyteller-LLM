Rugan was a transporter for a branch of the Zhentarim that operated on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR.
He was an unscrupulous individual who was more than willing to steal from his fellow Zhentarim.
Rugan operated out of the hidden Zhentarim outpost located in a cavern beneath the Waukeens Rest inn.
He recruited the younger Zarys and taught her the rules of the organization before she emerged as the leader of their group.
In the , Rugan and his companions were tasked with transporting a strongbox containing some unknown item to Baldurs Gate and delivering it to an unknown wealthy individual in the city. When traveling along the Risen Road near Moonhaven, Rugan and his men were attacked by a band of savage gnolls and forced to take refuge within a nearby cave and fight for their lives.
