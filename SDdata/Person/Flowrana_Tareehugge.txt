Flowrana Tareehugge was a nature-loving elf wizard active in the 14 century DR.
Flowrana Tareehugge was an avid lover of nature and its lush greenery. She often neglected her companions needs in favor of the plant life.
Flowranas most noteworthy possession was her wand of wonder that she inherited.
Flowrana Tareehugge was a lover of nature since an early age. Eventually, she decided to commit her life to nurturing and protecting of plants. After she received her wand of wonder as a part of her inheritance, she started actively using the wands powers in her lifes work as the natures protector.
