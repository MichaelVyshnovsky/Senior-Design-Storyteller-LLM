'''Flowrana Tareehugge''' was a nature-loving [[elf]] [[wizard]] active in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1992|307|Flowrana Tareehugge}}

==Personality==
Flowrana Tareehugge was an avid lover of nature and its lush greenery. She often neglected her companions' needs in favor of the plant life.

==Possessions==
Flowrana's most noteworthy possession was her ''[[wand of wonder]]'' that she inherited.

==History==
Flowrana Tareehugge was a lover of nature since an early age. Eventually, she decided to commit her life to nurturing and protecting of plants. After she received her ''wand of wonder'' as a part of her inheritance, she started actively using the ''wand's'' powers in her life's work as the nature's protector.
