Cora was a servant at the Journeys Rest in 1369 DR.
Along with Maris, Cora worked at the Journeys Rest inn when it reopened in the , and they lived together in one of the upstairs rooms. They tended to the common room and the customers during the day, while the owners Ryanna and Moria worked during the night. They had no idea that their mistresses were actually a pair of predatory vampires who fed on the customers, and were shocked when a party of adventurers drove them off after being attacked one night.
