'''Shorg''' was a [[hobgoblin]] living near [[Julkoun]] around [[1485 DR]].

==Possessions==
Dressed in [[chainmail]], Shorg fought with a [[scimitar]], [[morning star]], and [[javelin]]s.

==History==
Around [[1486 DR]], Shorg was the leader of the [[goblinoid]]s who invaded the village of Julkoun under the suggestion of one of the [[Red Wizards of Thay]]. Later, he was defeated by an adventuring party.
