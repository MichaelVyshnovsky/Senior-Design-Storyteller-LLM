Tohkkal Burrwarden was a rock gnome shopkeeper in Skullport.
Tohkkal was the more practical of the Burrwarden twins. It was her organizational skills that kept the shop open. Tohkkal cared deeply for Tykkyl, but was concerned about his inspiration.
Tohkkal typically wore an apron. She possessed a pair of bracers of protection, a short sword with a +2 enchantment against creatures considered vermin, and a ring of telekinesis that could lift up to  of weight.
Tohkkal, along with her twin brother Tykkyl Burrwarden, managed the shop Clockwork Wonders.
