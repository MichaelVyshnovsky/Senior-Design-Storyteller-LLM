Bibeek was a musician inhabitant of Baldurs Gate who died some years before the late 15 century DR.
Bibeek died sometime before the  and was buried in the Lower City Graveyard. His gravestone was etched with a praise to Oghma, and the musicians songs. The inscription read "Bibeek, favoured of Oghma. Your songs live on in the streets and in our hearts." The musicians grave may had been accidentally desecrated, and the body risen as a zombie by Nina Dortmell, a talented child with a gift, who was trying to resurrect her recently deceased brother.
