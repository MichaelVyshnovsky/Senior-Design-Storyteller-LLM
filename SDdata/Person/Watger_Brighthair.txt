==History==
Watger was guided in the ways of priesthood by [[Salyndra Shaern]] and worked alongside the priestess to foster peace between the [[human]]s and [[elves]] of Neverwinter. He also taught her much about the wisdom of humans.

==Activities==
Watger was known to offer free teaching sessions in the House of Knowledge to anyone who was willing to learn about the Oghma faith.
