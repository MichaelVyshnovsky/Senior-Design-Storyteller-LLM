The '''Unwanted One''' was a [[hengeyokai]] (badger) priest in [[Kozakura]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
The Unwanted One took care of the [[Shrine of the Dark Ones]]. Rumors said that it was a center for forbidden services.
