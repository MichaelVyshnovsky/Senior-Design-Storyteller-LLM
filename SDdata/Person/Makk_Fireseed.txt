Makk Fireseed was an elven priest of Rillifane Rallathil.
He convinced his followers that the deitys favorite creature was the Duathamper canary, a type of canary that nested in the butternut trees on the River Duathamper. The canarys yellow feathers were symbolic of the sun, and its green throat represented the leaves of trees.
