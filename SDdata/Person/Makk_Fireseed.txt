'''Makk Fireseed''' was an [[elf|elven]] priest of [[Rillifane Rallathil]].

==Activities==
He convinced his followers that the deity's favorite creature was the [[Duathamper canary]], a type of canary that nested in the butternut trees on the [[Duathamper|River Duathamper]]. The canary's yellow feathers were symbolic of the sun, and its green throat represented the leaves of trees.<ref>
