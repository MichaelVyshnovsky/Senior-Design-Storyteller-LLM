'''Pugney''' was the proprietor of the [[Mithrest Inn]] in the City of Coin, [[Athkatla]], in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Good tidings to you, young wayfarer. Welcome to the Mithrest Inn. How might I be of assistance?''|Pugney}}

==Description==
Pugney was a portly bald man with fair skin.

==History==
Pugney's exclusive inn was located in the grand market named [[Waukeen's Promenade]] in Athkatla's [[Athkatla/Center District|Center District]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}. Pugney shared the latest news from Athkatla to his customers but rarely gossiped. His inn was a calm and quiet establishment and he liked to keep it that way.
