Heldorm was a member of the Cult of the Black Earth at the Temple of the Black Earth in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.
Heldorm was an unbalanced individual. He normally spoke with himself and tittered at a series of black jests he only partially shared aloud.
In 1491 DR, Heldorm and three fellow cultists acted as jailers in the temples jail until some adventurers infiltrated the temple and confronted and defeated them.
