'''Orvanath''' was a [[lich]] and a [[necromancer]]. In [[1266 DR]], he trapped the [[Bright Banner Brandished]] adventuring company, including the wizard [[Ashemmi]], in a subterranean crypt north of [[Mirabar]], where he killed them in their entirety, except for Ashemmi who escaped by using a ''[[teleport]]'' scroll.{{Cite dragon/357/Volo's Guide: Renegades of Darkhold|74}}

Orvanath joined the [[Arcane Brotherhood]] in [[Luskan]].{{Cite dragon/357/Volo's Guide: Renegades of Darkhold|75}}
