'''Calathlarra''' was a Runemaster of the [[Twisted Rune]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Calthlarra was one of the most cold and emotionless leaders of the Twisted Rune.

==History==
She was one of the [[archmage]]s who competed for possession of the [[Lost Spell]] during a [[spellstorm]] in [[Tarsakh]] of the {{yearlinkName|1488}}. Calathlarra tried to kill [[Tabra]] but was ultimately killed by her in the fight.
