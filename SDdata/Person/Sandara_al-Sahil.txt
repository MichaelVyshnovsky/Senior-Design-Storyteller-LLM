'''Sandara al-Sahil''' was a [[Tasked genie|tasked]] [[administrator genie]] who was in charge of supervising the [[North Center]] of [[Huzuz]].

== Description ==
Sandara was a lovely genie who weighed well over 500 pounds (over 227 kg).

== Activities ==
Sandara's prime charge was monitoring and assuring the steady flow of caravans entering the [[City of Delights]] through the North Center.
