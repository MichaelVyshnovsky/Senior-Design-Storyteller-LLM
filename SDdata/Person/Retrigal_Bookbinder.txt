Retrigal Bookbinder was a scholar who lived in Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.
In the , Retrigal ventured into Neverdeath graveyard to investigate Lord Neverembers claims of inheritance to the throne of Neverwinter. While parsing over some old records, Retrigal was assaulted by the risen undead and forced to seek the aid of a nearby human adventurer at Doomguides Watch.
He eventually found the tomb of Vers Never, but it was guarded by a particularly powerful undead guardian.
