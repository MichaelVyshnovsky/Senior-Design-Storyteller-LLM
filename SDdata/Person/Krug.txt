'''Krug''' was a [[goblin]] devotee of [[the Absolute]] who lived in the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He wore the attire commonly worn by goblin [[booyagh]]s and [[warlock]]s.

==History==
Krug was among the [[Cult of the Absolute|cultists of the Absolute]] who raided [[Waukeen's Rest]] in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}. During the assault, he fell and cut his neck on a [[dagger]]. He subsequently died from his wounds.
