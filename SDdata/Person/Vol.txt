'''Vol''' was a member of the [[Zhentarim]] that operating within [[Zhentarim hideout (Risen Road)|secluded caves]] hidden beneath the [[Risen Road]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Nothing to see here. Move along.}}

==Personality==
She had a no-nonsense attitude much like her superior [[Zarys]].

==Relationships==
Vol was often accompanied by the massive wolf [[Indra]].

==History==
When Zarys ordered the Zhentarim hideout to be demolished in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Vol was among those members that helped set out explosives and gather all their loot.
