Gerak was a hunter in Sembia.
Gerak lived in the small village of Fairelm in shadovar-dominated Sembia with his wife Elle.
He was conscripted into some of the Shadovars wars against Cormyr but always went back.
In 1484 DR, Elle announced to Gerak that she had finally become pregnant. Therefore he decided to go to hunt for his growing family.
When he went back Gerak found all Fairelms inhabitants slaughtered and Elle with a curse upon her. It was the works of Sayeed and Zeeahd.
Later arrived to help Vasen Cale and his squad and Orsin.
After witnessing the corruption and the death of Elle, Gerak decided to join Vasens party hoping to have vengeance.
