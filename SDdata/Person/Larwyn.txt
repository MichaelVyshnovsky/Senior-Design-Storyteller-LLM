Larwyn was a maid at Thistleflame Manor and servant of Lord Partic Thistle circa 1369 DR.
Larwyn was reliable and sensible, and took her duties very seriously.
Larwyn was one of Thistleflames maids, alongside Tami, and was responsible for maintaining the upper two floors of the Manor. She was extremely good at her job, and remembered every detail of the Manor.
Larwyn had a good friendship with Moorefield the butler, as well as a mild rivalry with him as they competed to see who could serve Lord Thistle more efficiently. There was a rumor in the household that they were lovers.
