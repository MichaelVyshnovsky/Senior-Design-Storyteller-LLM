'''Velorna Jalaneth''' was a [[bard]] and writer of ballads who lived in the early-mid 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1328}}, Jalaneth published the ballad, ''[[Friends I Weep For You]]''. The poem spoke of friends standing together from life until death, including their experiences, such as "gathering trouble as farmers reap [[turnip]]s".
