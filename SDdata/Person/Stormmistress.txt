'''Stormmistress''' was the High Priestess of [[Umberlee]] in the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] of [[Zhentil Keep]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.

==Activities==
Stormmistress was in charge of a newly erected temple called [[the Jagged Rocks]]. She was assisted by six other priestesses and helpers.
