'''Chantlarn Iyrmerr''' was a male [[wizard]] who was one of the [[magelord]]s of [[Athalantar]] and was killed in [[Elminster Aumar]]'s insurrection.

==History==
In [[240 DR]], he was forcibly summoned to a spell-battle in [[Ithboltar]]'s tower in [[Athalgard]] and died there.
