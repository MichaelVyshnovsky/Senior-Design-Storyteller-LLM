'''Evari Ruyost''' was a [[Raumviran]] inhabitant of [[Almorel]] in the [[Hordelands]] in [[1375 DR]].{{Cite dragon/349/The Horde: Barbarians of the Endless Waste}}

==Activities==
Evari was one of three Keepers of the city watch. As such she was afforded a seat on the mayor's council and helped advise him on how to govern the city. Like all Keepers, Evari was expected to name someone to assume her duties in the event of her death.
