Tanatha of the Glittering Blades was the Chief Vizier of Mahabba in the Pantheist League of interior Zakhara in 1367 DR. 
As Chief Vizier, Tanatha served as the caliphs most trusted advisor.
Tanatha was a skilled farisa who served on several campaigns with Caliph Mamum al-Sahnan.
Tanatha was briefly married to Mamum and bore him three sons. Two of their sons perished in combat, but the third trained to become a moralist in Itiraf.
