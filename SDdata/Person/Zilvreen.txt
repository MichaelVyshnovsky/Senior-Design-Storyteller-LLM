Zilvreen was one of the Wearers of Purple in the Cult of the Dragon, their master thief member of the council of their Sembian cell. He usually resided in Saerloon.
Zilvreen trusted Maenoth Silversword as much as he trusted anyone. Malark Himbruel seemed to resent and mistrust him.
Zilvreen was one of the leaders who ordered the hunt for Shandril Shessair in 1357 DR. He was later in favor of abandoning the search for spellfire.The scene is ommited from the 2005 rewrite.
By , Zilvreen may have suspected Maenoths true allegiance and taken steps to rid himself of the Harper.
