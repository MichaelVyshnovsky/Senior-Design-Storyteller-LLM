'''Nyella''' was the [[spirit]] of a deceased [[dog|canine]] inhabiting the village of [[Tinnir]] in [[Rashemen]] in the {{YearlinkName|1373}}.

==Activities==
Nyella protected the local [[inn]] called the [[Lakeside Inn]]. She was fiercely territorial and often barked at strangers. Nyella was quite fond of the inn's owner, [[Vrulla]].

==History==
Nyella belonged to the man who constructed the Lakeside Inn during the mid 13{{th}} century [[DR]].
