}}'''Donnia Soldou''' was a [[drow]] [[assassin]] and a member of a band of four, including [[Ad'non Kareese]], [[Kaer'lic Suun Wett]] and [[Tos'un Armgo]].

==Description==
Donnia had long silver hair which she wore across the right half of her face covering her right eye. She dressed in clothes of deep gray and black, and had a cowl.

==Relationships==
Donnia was the lover of Ad'non.

==History==
She was originally from [[Ched Nasad]].

===Death===
In [[1370 DR]], Donnia was killed by [[Drizzt Do'Urden]] while trying to escape from him by levitating down a precipice; Drizzt shot her with her own [[hand crossbow]], paralyzing her until the levitation spell wore off and she plummeted to her death.
