'''Nerys Dawnhelm''' was an [[elf]] [[paladin]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==Description==
Nerys had light skin, gray eyes, and neatly-styled short black hair. She wore a suit of [[half plate]] armor and carried an engraved [[longsword]] and [[shield]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, Nerys joined the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|base camp]] at the ruins of [[Myth Drannor]]. She served as a trainer for other paladins at the camp.
