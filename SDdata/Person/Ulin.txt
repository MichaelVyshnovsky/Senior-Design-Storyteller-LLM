'''Ulin''' was a [[priest]] and [[philosopher]].

Ulin studied [[Wikipedia:Epistemology|epistemology]] and presented a theory that stated that things and people that one did not observe did not actually exist.

In [[1362 DR|the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR]], at the [[temple of Deneir (Procampur)|temple of Deneir]] in the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]], [[Koja]] fancied that if he ignored his secretary [[Foxe]] then he would disappear, in accordance with Brother Ulin's theory.
