Mintassan the Magnificent (also known as the Sage or the Mad) was a planewalker and sage of the planes who lived in the city of Westgate.  His sigil was a stylized sideview of a four-legged beast that vaguely resembled the letter M.
Whimsical and impulsive, Mintassan loved adventuring.
He was known to have several magical items, including a staff of the undead.
Mintassans townhouse was located in the southern section of Westgate on a branch of Southgate Market Street.  The sign out front read "Mintassans Mysteries Curios from Very Faraway Places". It was known to be heavily warded.
He was romantically linked with Alias.
