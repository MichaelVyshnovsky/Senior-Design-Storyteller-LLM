'''Dauna Lorastur''' was the High Lord of [[Marrauk]] in [[1479 DR]].

==Background==
Dauna was a calm person, well-liked by her subjects. However, as of 1479 DR, she grew defiant of the [[green dragon]] [[Orlarrakh]], making her three young sons fear for her safety.
