'''Nadaar''' was a [[dragonborn]] [[paladin]] and an [[adventurer]].

{{Quote|Easy now, everyone. I'll take care of this.|Nadaar{{Cite MTG|AFR|168|You Come to the Gnoll Camp|[[Billy Christian]]}}}}

==Personality==
Nadaar was bold and confident, and he liked to explore ancient ruins. And while he enjoyed when his party was caught in some precarious situation, he never let his enjoyment to blunt his alertness.{{Cite web/The Legends of Adventures in the Forgotten Realms}}

==Activities==
As a paladin, Nadaar's role was to serve as a shield to protect his friends, a role he happily accepted.

==Relationships==
Nadaar was part of an adventuring troupe with the [[bard]] [[Ellywick Tumblestrum]], the [[wizard]] [[Hama Pashar]], and the [[ranger]] [[Varis]], who explored ancient ruins across the Realms.{{Cite MTG|AFR|213|You Find a Cursed Idol|[[Sidharth Chaturvedi]]}}
