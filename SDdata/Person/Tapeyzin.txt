'''Chief Tapeyzin''' was the founder of the [[Maztica]]n city of [[Michaca]].

He recognized that the abundance of [[gold]] in the area was causing men to become greedy and try to obtain personal wealth, and instead decided to use the gold to build a city that would serve as a tribute to the [[:Category:Maztican pantheon|gods]].
