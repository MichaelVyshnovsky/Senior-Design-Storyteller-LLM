'''Dog Perry''' was the assassin in the employ of the thieves guild of [[Quentin Bodeau]] in [[1364]]. He was a brutal and savage man. When [[Artemis Entreri]] came back to [[Calimport]], Perry against all reasons thought to gain great glory by killing the elder assassin. Unfortunately for Perry, Enteri was more deadly than him and Enteri slew Dog Perry.
