Dymia was a diviner and one of five novice wizards stationed at the enclave run by the young Red Wizard Hinnar.
Dymias body bore a magical tattoo
Dymia was tough, dexterous, and intelligent and was trained in alchemy, spellcraft, and the scribing of scrolls.
Dymia carried a dagger and a small crossbow.
Dymias spellbook contained nineteen different spells.
Dymia alternated with the other five novice wizards in running the compounds sales booth with two other companions. When sales were slow, Dymia might offer assistance to the wizards responsible for crafting the magic goods that were for sale.
