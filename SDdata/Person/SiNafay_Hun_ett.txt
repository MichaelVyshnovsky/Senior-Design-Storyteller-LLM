SiNafay Hunett was the Matron Mother of House Hunett in Menzoberranzan in the early 14 century DR.
Thinking House DoUrden posed a threat, she attacked it with the help of Bregan Daerthe in the . But with Jarlaxles betrayal, her house was defeated and punished by the ruling council of Menzoberranzan.
After that, Matron Hunett became a member of House DoUrden under a new name, Shinayne DoUrden.
She was later tricked and sacrificed to Lolth by Matron Malice DoUrden for the Zin-carla ritual to resurrect Zaknafein in the .
She was particularly diminutive, even by drow standards, standing just over four feet tall.
