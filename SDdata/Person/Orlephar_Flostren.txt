'''Orlephar Flostren''' was a native of [[Chancelgaunt]] who fortified the location that would eventually become [[Zhentil Keep]].<ref name=FRA-p120-121">

==History==
Orlephar turned the lawless, and often raided, caravan camp that would be Zhentil Keep into a fortified stronghold. Flostren later sold his stronghold to twelve merchants.<ref name=FRA-p120-121"/>
