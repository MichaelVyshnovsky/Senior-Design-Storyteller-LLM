'''Kame-Renn''' was a [[lung wang|sea dragon]] member of the [[Celestial Bureaucracy]].

==Activities==
Kame-Renn was one of the officers of the [[Qui sub-committee]] that governed the affairs of the animal population of the [[Fochu Peninsula]] under orders of the [[Ministry of Animal Affairs]] of the Celestial Bureaucracy.
Kame-Renn oversaw about the scaly animals of the water. He ruled Qui during the [[Season of the Turtle]]. He governed directing 30 giant crabs, of which 10 always on patrol. He delegated most of the affairs to his lieutenants.
