Lord Reinlake was a nobleman of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.
He wore a monocle.
He was acquainted with Lord Parespur Bloodbright.
Lord Reinlake was a guest to the Open Feast in Mirtul 26 of the  held at Castle Obarksyr, where he briefly conversed with Lord Parespur Bloodbright and praised the beauty of the lords escort, the mask dancer and coinlass Amarune Whitewave.
