'''Markovel Stonestorm''' was a maker of [[harp]]s and owner of [[Stonestorm's Harpery]] in the city of [[Silverymoon]] in the [[Silver Marches]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. Around [[1372 DR]], Markovel was a regular client of [[Chesmyr Morrowynd]], a maker of harp strings, and they met to do business in [[Swordsfall House]].{{Cite web/My Slice of Silverymoon/Part 2}}
