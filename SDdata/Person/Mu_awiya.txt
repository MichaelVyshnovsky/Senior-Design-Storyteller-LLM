Muawiya was the eldest son of Sheikh Kaldhun bin Hanif of the House of Hanif tribe in the Haunted Lands of interior Zakhara around 1367 DR.
Muawiya was tall and gaunt.
He was a calm man.
Muawiya believed his father and grandfathers support of the Grand Caliph, by serving as his guards in the desert, was the proper path for the tribe.
Muawiyas had a half-brother named Yazid. Muawiya was conceived later on the same day as his brother and born before his brother on the same day.
