'''Mu'awiya''' was the eldest son of Sheikh [[Kaldhun bin Hanif]] of the [[House of Hanif]] tribe in the [[Haunted Lands]] of [[interior Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p43">

==Description==
Mu'awiya was tall and gaunt.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p43"/>

==Personality==
He was a calm man.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p43"/>

==Activities==
Mu'awiya believed his father and grandfather's support of the [[Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir|Grand Caliph]], by serving as his guards in the desert, was the proper path for the tribe.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p43"/>

==Relationships==
Mu'awiya's had a half-brother named [[Yazid]].<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p43"/> Mu'awiya was conceived later on the same day as his brother and born before his brother on the same day.
