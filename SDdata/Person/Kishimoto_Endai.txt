'''Kishimoto Endai''' was a [[samurai]] retainer of the [[Niwa clan]] and [[jito]] of [[Kan-oji]] in [[Kozakura]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
In Kan-oji, he led a company of three samurai and ten [[bushi]]. He was at the time busy with emergencies. An infestation of evil [[hengeyokai]], [[goblin]]s, and [[bakemono]] plagued the village. Kishimoto had begun to be suspicious about it.

==Personality==
Kishimoto was not a good administrator and was not loved by the [[yakusho]].
