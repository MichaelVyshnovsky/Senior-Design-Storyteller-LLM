Carbury was a Netherese arcanist and prolific spell inventor who specialized in the school of illusion, particularly with phantasms.
Despite his ingenuity, Carbury was thoroughly chaotic, fierce, and evil.
The evil inventor was born in Spiel in . He took full advantage of the enclaves universities, and became a proficient arcanist who took a great interest in illusion magic. The chaotic Carbury did not pass quietly. In , he attempted to overtake the entire conclave of Spiel in order to usurp its archwizards power. In order to achieve this, he created several illusions to make others believe he was the legendary sarrukh lord, the Terraseer. However, Carbury was found out by an adventuring group, and he was summarily executed.
