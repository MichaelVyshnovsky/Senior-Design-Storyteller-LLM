Umyatin was the first Lord Artificer of Imaskar, and the founder of Inupras.
In , Umyatin founded the city of Inupras, capital of the Imaskari empire, and gave himself the title of Lord Artificer.
In the spring of the , Ususi Manaallin found two Imaskari murals in a ruin on the border between Veldorn and Estagund. The murals depicted, to her judgement, two different emperors of Imaskar, Umyatin and Omanond. The figure she believed to represent Umyatin was surrounded by two demonic figures one of a dragon with a lions head, and one of a midnight-black centaur with a demons horn and a vicious look of glee in his eyes. Umyatin was reaching out to the latter, which was labeled "Mizar".
One of Ususi Manaallins offensive spells was known as the decomposition of Umyatin.
