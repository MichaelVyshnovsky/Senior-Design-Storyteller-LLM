May Winfield was an inhabitant of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
May Winfield was a wife and a mother of twelve, beloved by all. She died in the , shortly before the armies of the Cult of the Absolute attacked the city of Baldurs Gate. Mays tombstone was commissioned to be carved by Candulhallows Tombstones in the Lower City. Her freshly-carved grave marker read "May Winfield. Mother of twelve, wife of one. Beloved by all." The completed headstone was stored in Candulhallows shop main room, just past the main entrance, to the left.
