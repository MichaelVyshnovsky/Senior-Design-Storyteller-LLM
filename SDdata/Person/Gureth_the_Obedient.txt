'''Gureth the Obedient''' was an [[elf]] [[warlock]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-MoMDtime}}

==Description==
Gureth had pale orange hair and dark green eyes. He wore an ornate leaf-styled green [[robe]] and could be seen floating off the ground in a ring of flickering energy.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}} during the [[Elven Crusade]], Gureth joined the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|fortified camp]] on the outskirts of [[Myth Drannor]]. He served as a trainer for other warlocks who passed by.
