'''Anndoquat'''<ref group="note" name="note1">Anndoquat's name is spelled 'Andoquat' on the back cover of The Great Khan Game, but is spelled as Anndoquat on his game card.  was a sardar from the city of [[Al-Ubaid]] in the [[Whamite Isles]]. There, he had a group of personal bodyguards. Anndoquat represented Al-Ubaid in a series of wild conflicts between various nations and pirates of the Whamite Isles
