Sato Kitashi was a female samurai in Kozakura in 1357 DR.
Kitashi born from Sato Mikio, the shugo-daimyo of the Hoichi Province. In youth Kitashi was an active adventurer and during this time she befriend the li lung dragons of the nearby mountains. The dragons proved to be powerful allies in the struggle that oppose the Sato clan, supporter of the shikken Takenaka Okawa, to the sohei army of Namikami Temple in Yokozu Province, supporter of Hojo Kawakubo.
