'''Kendrin Feldarr''' was a merchant during the [[Elemental Evil]] crisis.

==Activitivies==
Kendrin usually sold exotic animals all around the [[Sword Coast North]].

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], during a stop at the [[Bargewright Inn]], he became involved in a plot by [[Inglor Brathren]] to discredit both [[Chalaska Muruin]] and [[Nalaskur Thaelond]].
