Shem was a gambler. In , he played Three-Dragon Ante against Yasha and Warian Datharathi somewhere north of Cathyr in Dambrath the last hand was interrupted by Bui the Hog, who chose that moment to pick a fight with Warian. He did not take part in the ensuing fight when Warian finished the battle, victorious, Shem did not contest him taking the accumulated pot.
