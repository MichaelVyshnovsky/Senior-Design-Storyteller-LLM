Faheed was a veteran adventurer who lived in Amkethran circa the .
After a long time adventuring, Faheed and his companion MajiraFaheed and Majira may be a play on Khalid and Jaheira, two other veteran adventurers. moved to Amkethran to deal with the powerful monk Balthazar. They bought a key to Balthazars monastery from local smugglers and aimed to kill the Bhaalspawn for all the trouble he had brought to the village.
