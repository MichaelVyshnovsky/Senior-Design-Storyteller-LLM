Dernim Lossit was a member of the Sembian High Council in Sembia in the late 14 century DR.
Dernim was a compromising individual by nature.
Dernim Lossit was the Highspeaker of the Council, the person that acted as mediator during the councils debate.
In the , after the death of Kendrick Selkirk, Dernim Lossit summoned the Council on Marpenoth 10 in order to elect the new Overmaster. The two main candidates were Countess Mirabeta Selkirk and Endren Corrinthal of Saerb.
