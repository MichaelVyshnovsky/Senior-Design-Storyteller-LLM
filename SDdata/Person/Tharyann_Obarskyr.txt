'''Tharyann "the Elder"''' born in the [[157 DR|Year of the Resounding Call, 157 DR]], was the 15th [[Obarskyr]] monarch and a king of [[Cormyr]].

==Relatives==
Tharyann was the tenth child and third son of [[Irbruin Obarskyr|King Irbruin]].

His second son, [[Boldovar Obarskyr|Boldovar]] was his lovechild.

==History==
Tharyann was known for putting down the first rebellion of [[Arabel]].
