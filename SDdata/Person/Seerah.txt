Seerah was a ranger
She had pale skin and blonde hair. She wore worn leather armor with a short sword at her hip and a crossbow on her back.
She did not speak much, and when she did it was often to curse.
Seerah was killed in 1479 DR when Duvan threw a dagger coated with a paralyzing poison into her thigh.
