'''Elsa''' was a barmaid who worked at the [[Stonehill Inn]].

==Description==
The barmaid was said to gossip often.

==History==
''Circa'' [[1491 DR]],{{Phandelver-time}} she was employed by [[Toblen Stonehill]]. She knew that [[Daran Edermath]] of [[Edermath Orchard]] was a former adventurer, and may have passed this information on to new patrons.
