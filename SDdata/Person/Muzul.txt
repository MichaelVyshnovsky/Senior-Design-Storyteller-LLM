Devout '''Muzul''' was a novice priestess of [[the Absolute]] that resided with the goddess' other [[Cult of the Absolute|cultists]] at the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Look ''this''. That's god's mark.|Muzul.}}

==Description==
She was blessed with the [[mark of the Absolute]], given to her by Priestess [[Gut]].

==History==
Muzul was present at the [[Shattered Sanctum]] in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, when the leaders of the Shattered Sanctum went searching for individuals present aboard a [[mind flayer]] [[nautiloid]] that came crashing near their fortress along the shore of the [[River Chionthar]].
