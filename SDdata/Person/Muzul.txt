Devout Muzul was a novice priestess of the Absolute that resided with the goddess other cultists at the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.
She was blessed with the mark of the Absolute, given to her by Priestess Gut.
Muzul was present at the Shattered Sanctum in the , when the leaders of the Shattered Sanctum went searching for individuals present aboard a mind flayer nautiloid that came crashing near their fortress along the shore of the River Chionthar.
