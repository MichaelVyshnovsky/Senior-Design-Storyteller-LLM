'''Jollitha Par''' was a [[Sembia]]n who was alive around [[1188 DR]].

He participated in discussions with King [[Pryntaler Obarskyr]] in an attempt to acquire the city of [[Marsember]]. The others in his group were [[Kodlos]], [[Homfast]], Lady [[Threnka]], and [[Bennesey]].
