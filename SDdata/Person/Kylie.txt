Kylie was a lightfoot halfling rogue of the adventuring company - the Ladys Lament from Waterdeep, active circa 1310 DR.
Kylie was a cheerful halfling who used daggers and shortbow in battle.
Several years before the , Kylie joined the Ladys Lament and quickly befriended his comrades. The halfling wanted to put a smile on the faces of his new friends via jokes and pranks. He understood well that "misery likes company."
In 1310 DR, the Ladys Lament, along with many other mercenaries, traveled from Luskan to the besieged city of Targos in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.
