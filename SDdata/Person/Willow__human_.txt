Willow was a child customer of Felogyrs Fireworks, enamored with fireworks and their scent, in the late 15 century DR.
Willow was a human child with long white dreadlocked hair, hazel eyes, and chestnut skin. She wore a commoners tunic.
In the , Willow frequented Felogyrs Fireworks, being lured in by the seductive smell of smokepowder. When she had pocket coin, Willow purchased fireworks to launch outside with fellow kids. Felogyrs Fireworkss proprietor, Avery Sonshal, sold the child the fireworks, but not before making sure she remembered the safety rules and after reciting Felogyrs Fireworkss legal disclaimer of not accepting any "liability for singed nails, toasted eyelashes, loss of limbs, scalded torsos, or blown off heads."
