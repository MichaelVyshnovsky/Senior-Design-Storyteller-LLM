'''Surrey''' was an apprentice blacksmith who lived in the town of [[Beregost]] during the midâˆ’14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
For a time, Surrey learned his craft under the tutelage of the famous smith, [[Taerom Fuiruim]].

==History==
When the [[iron crisis]] struck the [[Sword Coast]], in the {{yearlinkName|1368}}, Surrey left Beregost in search of better prospects. He traveled north to the [[Friendly Arm]] inn, en route to the great metropolis of [[Waterdeep]].
