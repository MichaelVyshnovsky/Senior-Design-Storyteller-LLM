'''Elaerue Estspirit''' was a [[half-elf]] woman and an inhabitant of the city of [[Wheloon]] in [[Cormyr]].

Elaerue was a [[Heralds (local)|local member]] of the [[Heralds of Faerûn]], holding the office and title of "Wheloon". In addition to her heraldry and other duties, she served Lord [[Sarp Redbeard]] as his assistant, clerk, and record-keeper.

She held this position by [[1357 DR]]
