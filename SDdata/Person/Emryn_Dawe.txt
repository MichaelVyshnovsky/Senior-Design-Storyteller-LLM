'''Emryn Dawe''' was a [[Harper]] based in [[Luskan]] in [[1485 DR]].

==History==
She was contacted by a fellow Harper named [[Aarun]] from the [[Ten Towns]]. He required [[silver]] weapons to use against a [[wererat]]. She managed to send the weapons to him with a caravan.
