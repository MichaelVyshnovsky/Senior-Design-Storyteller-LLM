Prince Anir al-Walad al-Hadi was a member of the royal family of the Free City of Qadib in north Zakhara in .
Anir was a skilled faris and spent most of his time hawking and hunting. Contrary to most of Qadibs royalty, Anir was decidedly nonmagical. This greatly disturbed the court and they urged the aging Sultan to sire more children with the hopes that they would possess some skill with magic.
Prince Anir was the sole heir of Sultan Kamal al-Hadi.
