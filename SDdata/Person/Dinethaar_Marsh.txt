'''Dinethaar Marsh''' was a well-respected chef who worked for Duke [[Ulder Ravengard]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|I trained in Duke Ravengard's kitchens under Chef Dinethaar Marsh. He was an efficient teacher. I have the burns to prove it.|Chef Marjoram}}

==History==
Chef Dinethaar Marsh was the instructor of Chef [[Marjoram (chef)|Marjoram]], who later would become the chef of [[Lady Jannath's Estate]] in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of the Gate. Later in life, Chef Marjoram would say that Dinethaar was an efficient but tough teacher.
