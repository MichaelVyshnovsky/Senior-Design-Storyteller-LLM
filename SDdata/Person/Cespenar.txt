Cespenar was an imp who used to serve Bhaal as a "butler". He appeared to have vanished from existence with that deitys death, but reappeared in the Pocket Plane, which Gorions Ward and party unconsciously created to protect themselves within Bhaals domain.
Cespenar was able to upgrade and create various enchanted items from the right ingredients. Some of the items Cespenar could improve included
* Angurvadal
* Carsomyr
* Firetooth
* Flail of Ages
* Ixils Nail
* Keryvian (Foebane)
* The Ravager
