'''Thoss Balein''' was Master of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Solemn Order of Recognized Furriers and Woolmen]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was an extraordinarily conservative, uncompromising, and ruthless guildmaster.

==Activities==
As Master of his guild, Thoss implemented a series of policy changes. These included refusing to accept new members for the entirety of his tenure, and encouraging wealthy guild members to buy out their less-moneyed peers from the organization.

==History==
Thoss was born in the {{YearlinkName|1297}}, and was elected Master of the Solemn Order in the {{YearlinkName|1338}} when his predecessor died. Under his leadership, the Order's membership decreased by 25% while revenues grew by 40%.
