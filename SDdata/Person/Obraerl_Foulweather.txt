'''Obraerl Foulweather''' was a noble of [[Cormyr]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. He was the head of House [[Foulweather]].

==Description==
He was an aging man.

==Relationships==
He was the head of House [[Foulweather]].

==History==
During the [[Council of the Dragon]] in late [[Mirtul]] of the {{yearlinkName|1479}}, Obraerl Foulweather was one of the proponents of a noble council that would wield power in behalf of, and instead of, the throne.

Later, as the council was assaulted by a [[blueflame ghost]], he was amongst those hewn down. His death was later confirmed.{{cite book/Bury Elminster Deep|94|10}}

==Reputation==
Per the [[War Wizard of Cormyr]] [[Welwyn Tracegar]], his death wasn't much loss.{{cite book/Bury Elminster Deep|138|15}}
