Solonus Canathil was a cleric of Mielikki living in Elventree in the late 15 century DR.
The priest was careful and preferred conflicts to end peacefully.
Canathil was a member of the Order of the Gauntlet.
Circa 1486 DR, Solonus asked for aid from his fellow members of Order of the Gauntlet to protect the summer solstice festival in Elventree.
