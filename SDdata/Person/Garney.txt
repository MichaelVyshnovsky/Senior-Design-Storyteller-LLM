Garney was a member of the Golden Legion.
Garney took part in the Maztican expedition.
He was one of the two guards at the entrance to Nalteconas room in Nexal when Erixitl and Halloran tried to free the Nexalan ruler. During the fighting, Halloran killed Garney.
