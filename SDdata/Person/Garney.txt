'''Garney''' was a member of the [[Golden Legion]].

==History==
Garney took part in the [[Maztica]]n expedition.

He was one of the two guards at the entrance to [[Naltecona]]'s room in [[Nexal]] when [[Erixitl]] and [[Halloran]] tried to free the Nexalan ruler. During the fighting, Halloran killed Garney.
