'''Zzutam''' was an influential [[ice para-elemental]] whose actions directly affected the [[Fall of Zhentil Keep]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.

==History==
During the month of [[Tarsakh]] in {{Yearlink|1369}}, Zzutam reasserted his control over a group of [[frost giant]]s who had besieged Zhentil Keep. Zzutam retreated with the frost giants to the north as the weather began to warm.
