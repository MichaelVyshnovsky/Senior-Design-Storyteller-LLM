Dillsandra was a young girl living in Easthaven during the 11th century DR.
The dark haired girl liked to wear grey dresses.
Dillsandra was an inquisitive young lady who was interested in the adventuring garb of strangers.
Like many kids in the small community, Dillsandra enjoyed learning about the wild creatures of the Frozenfar.
Dillsandra was friends with a few other local children, like Jeffy, Chizelo, and Ahmadora.
In Eleint, 1281 DR, Dillsandra and a few other children met a group of mercenaries who decided to head with Hrothgar on his expedition to Kuldahar.
