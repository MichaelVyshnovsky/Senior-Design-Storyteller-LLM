Cassius (Pronounce|CASS|ee|uss|refs= 
As of 1370 DR, Cassius had short gray hair kept closely shaved. He had bright blue eyes and a rather slender build.
While being a skilled diplomat, Cassius military ability and leadership skills were legendary throughout the Ten Towns.
Cassius never put away the thiefs ways he learned as child, but he nevertheless became a respected and capable leader.
Cassius was originally from Luskan and grew up there as a street urchin.
