'''Darthym''' was a [[War Wizard]] of [[Cormyr]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He prided in speaking softly, pleasantly, and parsimoniously.

==Activities==
As of {{yearlink|1373}}, Darthym was a member of the retinue guarding [[Vangerdahast]]'s sanctum near [[Mouth o' Gargoyles]].

==Relationship==
He reported to [[Huldyl Rauthur]].

==History==
One day after spring began in {{yearlink|1373}}, there was an attack on the sanctum of the retired [[Royal Magician]] of [[Cormyr]], Vangerdahast, directed by [[Harnrim Starangh]]. In the aftermath, Darthym reported the damage to Huldyl Rauthur, his superior, somewhat losing his composure.
