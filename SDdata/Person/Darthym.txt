Darthym was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.
He prided in speaking softly, pleasantly, and parsimoniously.
As of , Darthym was a member of the retinue guarding Vangerdahasts sanctum near Mouth o Gargoyles.
He reported to Huldyl Rauthur.
One day after spring began in , there was an attack on the sanctum of the retired Royal Magician of Cormyr, Vangerdahast, directed by Harnrim Starangh. In the aftermath, Darthym reported the damage to Huldyl Rauthur, his superior, somewhat losing his composure.
