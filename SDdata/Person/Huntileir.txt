'''Huntileir''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] weaponsmith in [[Tilverton]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Hunteleir was {{SI|3|ft|long=yes}} tall and 3 feet wide. He possessed extraordinarily thick muscles, particularly the biceps. He had a thin white beard around the edge of his jaw.

==Possessions==
He always carried at least four [[throwing axe]]s, which he was very proficient with.

==Activities==
Around [[1358 DR]], Huntileir ran a weapons shop in Tilverton, making and selling [[axe]]s, [[sword]]s, and a variety of other implements.
