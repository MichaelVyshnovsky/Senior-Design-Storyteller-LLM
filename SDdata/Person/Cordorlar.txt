'''Cordorlar''' was an [[arcanist]] of late [[Netheril]].

==Activities==
Cordorlar had conducted some experiments with the blood of [[dragon]]s.

==Rumors & Legends==
Their dragonblood experiments were part of what the magical historian [[Tabarast]] had investigated at the [[Moonshorn Tower]] together with his colleague [[Beldrune]]; both were rather skeptical of such fare.
