==Personality==
Benji was a fervent supporter of the Emerald Enclave, believing them to be stalwart allies of the natural world of [[Faer√ªn]]. According to him, they were the best people to bring about balance between nature and the civilized world.

Benji had two living parents, and at least one sibling, a sister named [[Abigail]] who served at the [[High House of Wonders]] in [[Baldur's Gate]].

At some point, Benji's sister wrote him [[On the Subject of Gond and Progress|a letter]] asking him to visit her in the city. She also reminded him of their father's upcoming birthday.
