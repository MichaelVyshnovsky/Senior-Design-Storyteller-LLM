'''Uton''' was a [[crusader]] of [[Selûne]] and a member of the [[Knights of the Half Moon]] at the [[House of the Moon]] temple in [[Waterdeep]] in the mid–14th{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite comic/Lunatics|7-9}}<ref group="note">While Uton and the others are not mentioned to be crusaders or members of the Order of the Half Moon in "Lunatics", the Order invented in the later ''Powers & Pantheons'' is undoubtedly based on these characters in the comic.

==History==
One night during the [[Time of Troubles]] of [[1358 DR]], while a purported [[avatar]] of the goddess resided at the House of the Moon, Uton and a fellow Knight called out [[Onyx the Invincible|Onyx]] and [[Timoth]] as they infiltrated the temple in robes stolen from the Knights standing guard at the entrance. Timoth claimed they were new converts; Uton queried the poor fit of their robes, but Timoth argued fitted robes were in short supply and they made a speedy departure. Uton remained suspicious, and alerted High Priestess [[Naneatha Suaril]]. Onyx and Timoth were caught and divested of their robes, and Naneatha left [[hell hound]]s to deal with them instead.
