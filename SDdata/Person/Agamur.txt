Agamur was a pit fiend in the late 14 century DR slain by the Simbul.
In the , Agamur was one of the pit fiends under Geryon when he came after the Simbul, who was scouring Avernus in search of Elminster Aumar. Together with Perstur, he was sent directly to follow a cloud of blue fire, in which the Simbul was hiding all of the pit fiends close to it were crushed later, when a mountain crag was magically thrown on top of them all.
