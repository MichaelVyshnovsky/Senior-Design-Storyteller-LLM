Kalil yi Tulmon was a pirate in service of the Rundeen in 1370 DR.
Kalil usually acted as first mate on pirate ships of the Rundeen fleet out of Almraiven, Suldophor, and Theymarsh. He was loyal first and foremost to the Rundeen, sometimes over the ships captain. Kalil was also a cruel men, deceiving  sailors to convince them to surrender and afterward cruelly killing them in front of their fellows. Sometimes he was specifically chosen to "teach a lesson" to a particular merchant.
