Lord Naerh was a member of the Council of Lords in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR before that citys desttruction.
Naerh was an imbecile who obeyed any order from Lord Chess, who in turn was a puppet for higher powers within Zhentil Keep. He was frightened of the Zhentarim since Lord Chess was appointed as watchlord of the Council in the .
His estate was located in the central section of Zhentil Keeps Inner Quarter at the intersection of Flostrens Memory and Elephstron Way.
