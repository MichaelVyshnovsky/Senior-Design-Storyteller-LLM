Master '''Ormil''' was a skilled [[wizard]] inhabiting [[Saerloon]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Ormil was a devout follower of [[Azuth]], the Lord of Spells. He was a ranking member within the [[House of the High One]], a temple dedicated to Azuth found in Saerloon.
