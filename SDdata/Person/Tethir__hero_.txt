'''Tethir the Dragonslayer''' was an [[Elf|elven]] hero and the first elven dragonslayer.

==Relationships==
Tethir was related to the elven hero [[Keltormir (elf)|Keltormir]].

==History==
Tethir sacrificed his own life to slay two [[Dragon aging|ancient]] [[red dragon]]s living in [[the Ridge]], allowing most of his tribe to escape to the southern reaches of the forest.
