Montgomery was a family man commoner inhabitant of the Rivington neighborhood of Baldurs Gate  in the late 15 century DR.
Montgomery was a human man with dark brown skin, brown eyes, and black hair of fine curls. He wore a well-trimmed beard and a commoners jerkin atop a blue tunic.
In the , Montgomery took his son to see the famed Dribbles the clown of the Circus of the Last Days. In lieu of recently surging murders and violent crimes in Baldurs Gate, the circus assigned the Principal Sniffer ghoul named Benji to sniff out blood on every guest entering the circus. The ghoul was supervised by Klaus, the Assistant Sniffer, who allowed patrons through the locked gates. Montgomery and his son both were clean, the boy was terrified of the blood-covered jester ghoul and refused to enter the circus.
