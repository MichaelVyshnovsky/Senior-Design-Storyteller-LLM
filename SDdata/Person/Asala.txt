'''Asala''' was a priestess of [[Kiaransalee]] in [[Szith Morcane]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
 
==Activities==
Asala was a young priestess of Kiaransalee under [[Velasta T'sarran]].

==History==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], after the [[fire giant]]s occupied the outpost, Asala's lover was killed by a group of [[hell hound]]s and [[azer]]s while they tried to escape. Asala wanted vengeance.{{Cite adventurers league/Szith Morcane Unbound|8-9}}
