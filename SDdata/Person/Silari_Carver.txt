'''Silari Carver''' was the mayor of the [[halfling]] city of [[Fasruil]] in [[Luiren]] in [[1367 DR]].

==Activities==
In addition to being the mayor of Fasruil, Silari was a wealthy farmer and landowner.

==History==
Silari served as a priestess of [[Yondalla]] in her past, but she left the clergy to focus on dealing with her land and duties as mayor.
