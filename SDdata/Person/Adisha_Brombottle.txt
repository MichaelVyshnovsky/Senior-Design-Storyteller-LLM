'''Adisha Brombottle''' was a [[halfling]] merchant at the [[Monastery of the Yellow Rose]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Adisha was depressed, bored, and very often drunk. She believed, out of paranoia, strangers to be agents for the [[Great Worm]] of the glacier intending to destroy the barrier that kept the storm at bay.

==History==
Adisha was one of the guests at the monastery in [[1491 DR]] with her two teamsters.
