Borigon Hillsafar was the king of Clan Hillsafar in Vaasa in the late 15 century DR.
In , the Warlock Knights of Vaasa invaded Hillsafar Hall. Borigon assumed command, taking on the mantle of king, and repelled the invaders. Afterward, he welcomed inside the Hall a handful of neighboring clans displaced during the war, and with great reluctance, ordered the gates to be closed and sealed. As of , the gates were still closed.
Borigon was the third son of Garumbelly Hillsafar.
