'''Tersonm''' was a powerful [[wizard]] inhabiting [[Westgate]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Description==
Tersonm was cold and formal.

==Activities==
Tersonm sold [[potion]]s and [[scroll]]s and tutored students on occasion if the price was right. He enjoyed placing magical traps on his possessions.
