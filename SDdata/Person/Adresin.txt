'''Adresin''' was a knight of the [[Golden Star]] who had joined [[Seiveril Miritar]]'s crusade.

In [[1374 DR]], during the [[Cormanthor War]], he acted as Seiveril's herald and adjutant on the field of battle.

Later, he died fighting alone at the [[Semberholme]] border.
