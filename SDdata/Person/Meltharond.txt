Meltharond was a once-powerful wizard-ruler
At some point in their relationship, Meltharond was considered to be Arveiaturaces master. The white dragon served as his aerial steed, and he instructed her in the arcane arts. They became close friends, lovers, and in fact, Meltharond was the only being that Arveiaturace ever truly confided to.
Meltharond was once the ruler of Ice Peak, a frigid island south of the Sea of Moving Ice.
