Rorrina Stoneshaft was a shield dwarf cleric of Abbathor that Artor Morlin, the vampire lord of Waterdeep, turned into one of his spawn. Circa the , she dwelled in the abandoned dwarven settlement of Melairrin in the depths of Mount Sar in the Sword Mountains just north of Waterdeep.
Rorrina was the daul (daughter) of Tuvala of Clan Stoneshaft.
Rorrina was the source of many reports of a vampire lurking in the depths of the Sword Mountains.
