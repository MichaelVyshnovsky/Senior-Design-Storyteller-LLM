'''Rorrina Stoneshaft''' was a [[shield dwarf]] [[cleric]] of [[Abbathor]] that [[Artor Morlin]], the [[vampire]] lord of [[Waterdeep]], turned into one of his [[vampire spawn|spawn]]. ''Circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, she dwelled in the abandoned dwarven settlement of [[Melairrin]] in the depths of [[Mount Sar]] in the [[Sword Mountains]] just north of Waterdeep.

==Relationships==
Rorrina was the [[Dwarvish dictionary#D|daul]] (daughter) of [[Tuvala Stoneshaft|Tuvala]] of [[Clan Stoneshaft]].

==Rumors & Legends==
Rorrina was the source of many reports of a vampire lurking in the depths of the Sword Mountains.
