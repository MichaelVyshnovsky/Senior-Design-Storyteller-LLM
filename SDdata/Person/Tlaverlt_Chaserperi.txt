'''Tlaverlt Chaserperi''' was a member of the [[Chaserperi]] clan of [[wizard]]s, and a likely candidate to become the patriarch of the clan in the late mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He was a shipmaker, and also produced [[olive oil]].

==Relationships==
The clan matriarch, [[Chancereene Chaserperi]], was considering him to replace [[Faereldan Chaserperi]] by the {{YearlinkName|1370}}.
