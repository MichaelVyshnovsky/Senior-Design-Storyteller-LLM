'''Saldrinar''', who was nicknamed '''of the Seven Spells''', was a [[Netherese]] [[wizard]] and the first [[human]] King to rule the city-state of [[Westgate]].

==Activities==
Saldrinar claimed the throne for himself when he slew the previous King, a [[topaz dragon]] named [[Kisonraathiisar]] in {{Yearlink|-349}}. He ruled until his death in {{Yearlink|-307}}.

==Relationships==
His apprentice, [[Mather (Westgate)|Mather "Wyrmshroud"]] would inherit the throne upon his death.
