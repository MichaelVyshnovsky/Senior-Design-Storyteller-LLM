'''Elthond Olósynne''' was the third King of the [[Olósynne Dynasty]] who ruled the nation of [[Aglarond]].

==Activities==
Elthond's short reign lasted from the {{YearlinkName|1189}}, to the {{YearlinkName|1194}}. He inherited the throne upon the death of his father, King [[Althon Olósynne|Althon]].

==Relationships==
His younger brother was [[Philaspur Olósynne|Philaspur]].
