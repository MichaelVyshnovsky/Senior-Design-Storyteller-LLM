'''Rose''' was a worker at the [[Herald's Horn]] in the mid-3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|240}}, she was serving at the [[Herald's Horn]] tavern the night that [[Elminster Aumar]] purchased a ''[[wyrmtongue scepter]]'' from [[Surgath Ilder]]. She dropped a plate of hot [[tarts]] on her own toes at the sight of the wealth being traded in the tavern.
