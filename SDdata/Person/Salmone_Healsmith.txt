Salmone Healsmith was a priest of Eldath, and an adventurer for hire.
Salmone had tan skin and shoulder-length dark hair. He dressed in all-green clothing.
He owned a necklace of prayer beads, a cloak of protection +3, and an amulet of power.
Born in Scardale, Salmone made a name for himself as a highly capable cleric for hire. However, his lacking discernment about the character of his employers eventually earned him a bad reputation. He was named "Salmone-for-anyones-hire", and fell into ruin.
