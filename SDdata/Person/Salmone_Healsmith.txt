'''Salmone Healsmith''' was a [[cleric|priest]] of [[Eldath]], and an adventurer for hire.

==Description==
Salmone had tan skin and shoulder-length dark hair. He dressed in all-green clothing.
==Possessions==
He owned a ''[[necklace of prayer beads]]'', a ''[[cloak of protection]] +3'', and an ''[[amulet of power]]''.

==History==
Born in [[Scardale]], Salmone made a name for himself as a highly capable cleric for hire. However, his lacking discernment about the character of his employers eventually earned him a bad reputation. He was named "Salmone-for-anyone's-hire", and fell into ruin.
