'''Antara o' Bones''' was a [[Shaaran]] [[human]] [[cleric]] of [[Kelemvor]] in [[Lapaliiya]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. As of the {{YearlinkName|1373}}, she was the ruler of [[Sheirlantar]], the Lapaliiyan city dedicated to Kelemvor, holding the title of Keeper of Crypts, having been appointed by the Overking of [[Lapaliiya]].
