Broadshield was a outlaw in Cormyr.
He led the notorious outlaw group known as Broadshields Beasts, a gang notorious for kidnapping nobles.
In 1480 DR, Broadshield was instructed by Manshoon to capture Arclath Delcastle and Amarune Whitewave and deliver them to Alorglauvenemaus but the two managed to escape from the ambush and arrive at Castle Irlingstar.
