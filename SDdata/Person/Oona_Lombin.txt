==Description==
Oona was a gray-skinned woman with gray eyes and wavy white hair in a short mullet cut. She wore a simple brown and white dress.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, Oona joined the [[Elves of Cormanthor]] at their [[Myth Drannor outskirts camp|forward camp]] at the outskirts of [[Myth Drannor]], alongside their [[drow]], [[human]], and [[dwarf]] allies. Oona quickly set up a simple shop with a small [[alchemy]] set in her [[tent]] where she sold all sorts of basic supplies and gear to the soldiers, [[pilgrim]]s, and [[adventurer]]s who passed through the camp.
