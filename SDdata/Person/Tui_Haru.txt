'''Tui Haru''' was a librarian in [[Chu' Yuan]] province in [[Shou Lung]].

==Activities==
Tui Haru lived in the city of [[Pasar]], the largest city in the [[Shao Mountains]]. She was the head librarian of the city's public library.
