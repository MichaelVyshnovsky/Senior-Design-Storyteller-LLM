'''Grellin Stoutaxe''' was a [[dwarf]] bosun's mate aboard the ''[[Osprey (ship)|Osprey]]'' during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He stood {{SI|4'1|'"|c}} tall.

==Activities==
Grellin was in charge of the ship's cargo and supplies. His photographic memory aided him considerably with this.

==Possessions==
Grellin carried an [[enchanted]] [[battle axe]] and [[shield]] and three ''[[Potion of water breathing|potions of water breathing]]''. He wore [[chainmail]] for [[armor]].
