Grellin Stoutaxe was a dwarf bosuns mate aboard the Osprey during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He stood  tall.
Grellin was in charge of the ships cargo and supplies. His photographic memory aided him considerably with this.
Grellin carried an enchanted battle axe and shield and three potions of water breathing. He wore chainmail for armor.
