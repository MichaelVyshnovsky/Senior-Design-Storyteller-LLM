'''Chadal''' was a young [[thief]] in [[Neverwinter]] in [[1479 DR]].

==Activities==
Chadal was one the [[Little Rats]], a gang of children affiliated with the [[Dead Rats]] gang who acted as spies and messengers.

==History==
In 1479 DR, she was on her way to meet her boss [[Darla]] to relay the location of [[Elden Vargas]]' base when Chadal was attacked and burned. Thanks to an adventuring party, she was cured and told them the crucial information.
