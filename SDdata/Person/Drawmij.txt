DrawmijThe creator of Drawmij can be found by reading his name backwards. was a great mage from Oerth He was known by a few inhabitants of the Realms, and his spells were used throughout.
The Oerthly wizards known spells in the Realms were Drawmijs instant summons and Drawmijs marvelous shield.
In the 14 century DR, Khelben Arunsun thought of the "great Drawmij of Oerth" when imagining a spell that was made to penetrate magic fog. In the same century, Drawmij visited Ravens Bluff, where he told stories to the public.
