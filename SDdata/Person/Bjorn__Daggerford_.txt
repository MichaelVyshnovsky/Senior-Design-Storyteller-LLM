Bjorn was a landlord inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He owned a large compound that he rented out to destitute tenants. He used hired muscle to collect rent when it was due. 
Bjorn was originally a noble from Waterdeep.
