Fist Olloa was a member of the Flaming Fist mercenary company from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Olloa had a tanned complexion with black hair gathered in a bun. She wore a set of leather armor as part of her Flaming Fist uniform and was armed with a deadly heavy crossbow. Olloa had a strict and somewhat cruel personality.
In the , Fist Olloa was tasked with patrolling the Lower City, near the Basilisk Gate. She and her fellow patrolmen had no qualms with shutting protesters up with force.
