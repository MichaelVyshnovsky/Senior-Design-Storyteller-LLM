'''Arulf "the Swordmerchant"''' was a [[weapon]]s-dealer who lived in [[Battledale]] during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]]. Arulf offered high quality [[steel]] weapons of all sorts within the city of [[Essembra]].

Due to Arulf's nickname, it was believed they had a fine selection of [[blade]]s and [[sword]]s.
