VilKenna was a female barbarian from the Sky Pony tribe of Uthgardt barbarians. In 1374 DR she traveled to Nesmé on the edge of the Evermoors to join others from her tribe who had been hired by First Speaker Tessarin Alaurin to defend the city against troll and giant attack.
