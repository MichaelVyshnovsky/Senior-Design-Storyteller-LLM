Bene was a commoner human from Athkatla, the City of Coin, in Amn who lived in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Bene met his demise in the . He, along with several other men, was captured by a drow druid named PaiNa who built a massive spider nest in ancient tombs underneath Athkatlas Grave District. Bene and other peasants were consumed from the inside by PaiNas spiders.
