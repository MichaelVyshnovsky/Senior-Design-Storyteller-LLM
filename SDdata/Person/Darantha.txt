Darantha was the personal maid of Lady Marantine Delcastle in the late 15 century DR.
Darantha was a head maid in Delcastle Manor.
Darantha served as the personal maid of Marantine Delcastle.
As of Mirtul in the , it was an established ritual in Delcastle Manor that anyone of the House should introduce themselves to Marantine upon entering, or to Darantha, if Marantine would not want to be disturbed.
