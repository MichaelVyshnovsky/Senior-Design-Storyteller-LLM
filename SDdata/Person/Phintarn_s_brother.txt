The brother of Lord Phintarn "Redblade" of Llorkh in the Savage Frontier owned the Worried Wyvern, the only inn in the town in the 1350s DR.
Not long after Phintarn was murdered in a Zhentarim takeover shortly before the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR,The date of this unknown. Waterdeep and the North (dated 1357 DR) only says this was "recently", implying sometime in 1357 DR or shortly before. his brother died mysteriously one night, and his inn was razed the very next day.
