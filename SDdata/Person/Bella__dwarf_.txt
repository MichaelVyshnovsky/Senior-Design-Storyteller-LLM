'''Bella''' was a member of the [[Flaming Fist]] who served the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Fist Bella was a fair-skinned [[gold dwarf]] woman with light hazel eyes and hair of the same color, tied in a bushy ponytail. She kept her face clean-shaven. Bella wore a uniform of the Flaming Fist: a set of [[leather armor|leathers]], and a pair of leather [[gauntlets]]. Her chest was adorned with the Flaming Fist insignia. Bella wielded a [[heavy crossbow]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Fist Bella was stationed in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of Baldur's Gate, within the [[Wyrm's Crossing]] fortress.
