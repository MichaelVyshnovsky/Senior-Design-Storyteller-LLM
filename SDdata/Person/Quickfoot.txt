Private Quickfoot was a halfling soldier of the Neverwinter Guard during the late 15 century DR.
Quickfoot served under Captain Janros, working alongside other specialized guardsmen such as Scout Gaelen and Private Mather.
Following the Siege of Neverwinter, in the , Quickfoot and his fellow guards were stationed in the Tower District of the city, near the Cloak Tower. Janros sent an adventurer working with the Guard to meet with Quickfoot so Quickfoot could show them the way to Scout Gaelen.
