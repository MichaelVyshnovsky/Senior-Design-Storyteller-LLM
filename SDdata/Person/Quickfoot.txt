Private '''Quickfoot''' was a [[halfling]] soldier of the [[Neverwinter Guard]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{quote|Keep your weapon handy. These orcs are treacherous!|Private Quickfoot}}

==Relationships==
Quickfoot served under [[Janros|Captain Janros]], working alongside other specialized guardsmen such as [[Gaelen|Scout Gaelen]] and [[Mather|Private Mather]].

==History==
Following the [[Siege of Neverwinter]], in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Quickfoot and his fellow guards were stationed in the [[Tower District]] of the city, near the [[Cloak Tower]]. Janros sent an adventurer working with the Guard to meet with Quickfoot so Quickfoot could show them the way to Scout [[Gaelen]].
