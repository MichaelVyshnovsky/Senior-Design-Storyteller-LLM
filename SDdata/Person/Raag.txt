'''Raag''' was a member of the [[Reghedmen]] [[barbarian]] tribe of the [[Tribe of the Wolf]] in the 13{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Raag lived some years before the {{YearlinkName|1281}}. His son, [[Angaar]], was among those who joined the barbarian King [[Wylfdene]] of the [[Tribe of the Great Wyrm]] in his crusade agains the [[Ten Towns]] of [[Icewind Dale]].
