High Moonmistress '''Dhauna Myritar''' was the high [[priestess]] of [[Selûne]] at [[Moonshadow Hall]], in the city of [[Yhaunn]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century. 

==Description==
She had tan skin, dark hair, and dark gray eyes.
