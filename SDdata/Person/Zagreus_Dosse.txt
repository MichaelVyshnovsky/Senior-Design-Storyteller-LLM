'''Zagreus Dosse''' was a wandering traveler and a member of a cell of the [[Cult of Reborn Flame]] that operated out of [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]], in [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|33}}

==Description==
Zagreus was considered a very good-looking [[human]] male.

==History==
In 1479 DR, Zagreus was recruited by [[Bazamarl]] to join his cell of the Cult of Reborn Flame. Zagreus joined because he was looking to give some semblance of purpose to his life.

When a band of [[adventurer]]s that were sent by [[Halagothra|Halagothra the Healer]] to Sambral to destroy the cell of the cult operating there killed both Bazalmarl and the [[Firebird (elemental)|Firebird]],{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|45}} Zagreus fled in panic, avoiding being killed by the adventurers.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|40}}
