Kaylaar Allun was a Chosen of Mystra in the late 15 century DR.
She dressed in a dark blue, hooded robe, trimmed with yellow. Kaylaar had a dark complexion, with brown hair and piercing, blue eyes.
Kaylaar was an exceptionally powerful wizard. Her patron Mystra granted her the ability to open time gates.
She was instructed by Mystra to aid powerful adventurers by temporarily transporting them back in time.
