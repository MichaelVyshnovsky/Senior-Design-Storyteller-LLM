'''Tau'am bint Nisr''' was a skilled [[hakima]] inhabiting [[Qudra]] in [[north Zakhara]] in {{Yearlink|1367}}.{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
As a hakima, Tau'am used her ability to see the truth in others within the [[Qudra/Slave market|slave market]] of Qudra to make sure that none of the slaves were actually free men or women or ensorcelled against their will.

==Relationships==
Tau'am's twin sister, [[Tum bint Nisr|Tum]], was also a hakima and worked beside her.
