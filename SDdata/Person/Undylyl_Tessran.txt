'''Undylyl Tessran''' was a [[human]] woman of the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]].

She was a [[wizard]] and an ambitious young adventuress. Around [[1358 DR]], she was looking for adventuring bands to join or hire her. She was one of the more notable Procampan mages of the time.
