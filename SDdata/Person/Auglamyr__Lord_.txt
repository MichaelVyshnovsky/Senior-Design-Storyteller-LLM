'''Lord Auglamyr''' was an inhabitant of [[Cormanthyr]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]], and the head of House [[Auglamyr]].

==Reputation==
He was said to have an unspeakably foul and violent disposition, which his daughter, [[Symrustar Auglamyr]], may have inherited.
