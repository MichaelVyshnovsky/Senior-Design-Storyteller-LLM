Lok was the leader of the Eagle Knights in Payit in 1361 DR.
Lok was one of the most powerful men in Ulatos. He was always close to the ruler Caxal during times of war.
In 1361 DR, soon after the arrival of the Golden Legions ships, Lok was called by Caxal to a war council with the leader of the Jaguar Knights, Gultec, to discuss how to fight the strangers.
