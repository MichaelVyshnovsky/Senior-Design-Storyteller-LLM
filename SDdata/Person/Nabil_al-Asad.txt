Nabil bin Najib was the heir to the House of Asad tribe in the High Desert of west Zakhara in 1367 DR.
Nabil seemed to have inherited his mothers wisdom and his fathers strength. He was intelligent, friendly, and trusting.
As of 1367 DR, Nabil began recently assuming his fathers duty of taking a yearly trip to Tajar to trade.
Nabil was the eldest son of the tribes leader, Najib bin Kamal al-Asad, and his wife, Alia al-Jamal umm Nabil.
