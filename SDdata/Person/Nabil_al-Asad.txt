'''Nabil bin Najib''' was the heir to the [[House of Asad]] tribe in the [[High Desert]] of [[west Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p37">

==Personality==
Nabil seemed to have inherited his mother's wisdom and his father's strength. He was intelligent, friendly, and trusting.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p37"/>

==Activities==
As of 1367 DR, Nabil began recently assuming his father's duty of taking a yearly trip to [[Tajar]] to trade.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p37"/>

==Relationships==
Nabil was the eldest son of the tribe's leader, [[Najib bin Kamal al-Asad]], and his wife, [[Alia al-Jamal umm Nabil]].<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p37"/>
