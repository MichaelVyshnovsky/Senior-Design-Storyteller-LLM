According to just about everyone who met him, Pritchard was exceedingly pompous and overbearing.

Pritchards father fought in both the First and Second Dragonspear Wars.
He had an extensive family who lived all throughout the Sword Coast. He was cousins with a cleric who had a similar yet different surname, Glint Gardnersonson.
