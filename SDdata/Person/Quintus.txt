'''Quintus''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] who oversaw the [[Alchemical Seminary]] in [[Eileanar]] shortly before its destruction in {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}.

==Personality==
Quintus was a kindly old man.

==Activities==
Quintus was an expert [[transmuter]] and had learned the techniques for turning [[lead]] to [[gold]]. He spent his time managing the Seminary and its roster of teachers, including [[Hakewill]], [[Cujas]], [[Thibauneau]], [[Fries (transmuter)|Fries]], [[Delacroix]], and [[Colombier]]. He was extremely good at running the school, and had turned it into the foremost institution in the [[enclave]] regarding [[alchemy]] and the related magical fields.
