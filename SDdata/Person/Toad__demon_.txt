'''Toad''' was a [[hezrou]] servant of the [[demon lord]] [[Graz'zt]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
  
==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1486}}, Graz'zt sent Toad to serve the [[cambion]] siblings [[Gattash]] and [[Rashara]], who had gained control of a [[kuo-toa]] tribe in the [[Deep Wastes]], by the [[Waydown River]]. However, Gattash and Rashara bullied Toad.{{Cite adventurers league/The Waydown|20}} He was ordered to remain in his lair until the cambions needed him.

==Personality==
Toad was very stupid but believed himself to be smart. He knew that the cambions were bullying him and wanted to rebel but was alone and knew he needed allies first.{{Cite adventurers league/The Waydown|22}}
