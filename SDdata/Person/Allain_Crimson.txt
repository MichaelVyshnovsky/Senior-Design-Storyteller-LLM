'''Allain Crimson''' was a [[human]] [[proxy]] of the [[Vedic pantheon|Vedic]] [[power]] known as [[Ushas]].

==Activities==
Allain spent most of his time operating the temple of Ushas that stood in the [[divine realm]] of [[Morninglory]].

==Rumors & Legends==
Rumors claimed that Allain had wed [[Aurora Brightday]], a proxy of the [[Faerûnian pantheon|Faerûnian]] power [[Lathander]], who likewise spent her time in Morninglory operating a temple to her power. One rumor stated that she was even pregnant with his child.
