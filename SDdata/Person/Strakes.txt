Strakes was a domestic servant in the house of Duke Alaric Hembreon in the city of Zazesspur.
Strakes combed his thin black hair over the balding spot of his head. He had long features and a button nose.
Strakes wore a uniform in the dukes official colors, white and blue.
Strakes commonly bore a sad look on his face. He had a way of speaking in which his lips  barely moved.
In the , when adventuress Zaranda Star had an audience with Duke Hembreon to discuss her confiscated goods, they were interrupted by Strakes, who announced that the dukes daughter, Tatrina had been stealing money from her fathers purse to donate to the cause of the All Friends, who followed Ao.
