Errde Blackskull was the duergar commander of the Stone Guards in Gracklstugh in the late 15 century DR.
Errdes main concern was the growing corruption in the citys governments. Errde knew even her guards were ineffective in uncovering it.
Errde concentrated her efforts on capturing the derro courier Droki, who she believed was a crucial pawn in a conspiracy of the Council of Savants.
