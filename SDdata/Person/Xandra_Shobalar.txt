Xandra Shobalar was a drow of House Shobalar and the third daughter of Hinkutesnat Alar Shobalar. She was the houses Mistress of Magic, tasked with training others in the house about wizardry. She trained the young Liriel Baenre.
Xandras mother was Hinkutesnat Alar Shobalar and she had ten sisters. Five of these sisters were wizards.
Xandra became deeply envious of Liriels magical ability and plotted to kill her, travelling to Mantol-Derith in order to buy an enslaved wizard, Tresk Mulander, to pit against her in the Blooding.
