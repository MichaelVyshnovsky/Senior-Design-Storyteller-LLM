'''Shael''' was an [[elf]] who operated a clothing shop in [[Leuthilspar]] on the island of [[Evermeet]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Shael created [[cloak]]s, gloves, hats, shoes, and tunics for which he charged very little, but he usually dealt with wealthy clients. Many of Shael's clients invited him to their estates and provided him with the things he needed to live in luxury, such as Queen [[Amlaruil Moonflower]] and the [[Council of Matrons]]. 

==History==
By [[1367 DR]], Shael was a retired [[windrider]] and [[adventurer]].
