Ahmarreld was a once-renowned adventuring sorcerer who lived a long time before the 14 century DR.
Ahmarrelds local renown was mostly forgotten by the  14 century DR. However, his name was immortalized as the feminine version of his name, Ahmarreldra, gained popularity. Eventually, it became commonly shortened to Reldra.
