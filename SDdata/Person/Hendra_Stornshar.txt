'''Hendra Stornshar''' was a [[human]] chambermaid at the [[Yawning Portal]] inn in [[Waterdeep]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
''Circa'' [[1375 DR]], she worked alongside [[Neetha Darra]], [[Pheldelopae Eleintwind]], and [[Yamra Stelkyn]] in doing the everyday cleaning of the inn. They were hardworking and dedicated to the owner [[Durnan]]'s family.

==Description==
She was attractive and strong.
