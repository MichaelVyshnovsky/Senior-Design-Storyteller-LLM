'''Randal Whytstone''' was a [[paladin]] of [[Tyr]], and the captain of the [[Chapel of Resounding Justice]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
As the captain of the chapel, Randal led four other resident guards in defense of the temple. He led a simple life, serving his deity in honorable duty.

==History==
As an orphan child, Randal was raised by the members of the [[Tyrran]] temple in [[Selgaunt]].
