'''Nikolai Nikopolis''' was a member of the [[Unclean (Zakhara)|Unclean]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|19-20}}

==Personality==
Nikolai was a repugnant pig. As a favored student, he felt he could get away with anything, including pestering the low-ranking [[holy slayer]]s, guards, and [[faris]]an who lived in [[Sarahin]].

==Description==
Nikolai was a tall man with pale skin. His dirty blond hair contrasted sharply with those of his [[Zakharan (ethnicity)|zakharan]] neighbors. His eyes were deep blue, appearing violet at times.

==History==
Fascinated by [[genie]]s, Nicolai traveled far across [[Faerûn]] to [[Zakhara]] in order to learn the mystical ways of the [[sha'ir]].

==Activities==
Nikolai was the lead apprentice of [[Akim al-Kalaas]].
