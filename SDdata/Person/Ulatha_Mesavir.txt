Ulatha Mesavir was an aquatic elf prince in the Moonsea in 1491 DR.
Ulatha was captured by the marel Shelavassa of the Cult of the Crushing Wave, who planned to use him as a sacrifice. A patrol led by Musanna went to rescue him. However, Shelavassa was confronted and defeated by an adventuring party hired to destroy the cultist group for their bandit activities near River Lis and Ulatha was freed.
