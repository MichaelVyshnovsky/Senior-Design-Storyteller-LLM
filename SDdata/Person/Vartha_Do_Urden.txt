'''Vartha Do'Urden''' was the mother and predecessor of [[Malice Do'Urden]], [[matron mother]] of [[House Do'Urden]].

==History==
Vartha Do'Urden was the matron mother of House Do'Urden when she suffered a sudden death in the 10{{th}} century [[Dalereckoning|DR]]. Her daughter Malice subsequently became matron mother, even though she was less than 100 years old at the time.

==Relationships==
Vartha was the mother of Malice Do'Urden and thus the grandmother of Malice's children: [[Briza Do'Urden|Briza]], [[Nalfein Do'Urden|Nalfein]], [[Dinin Do'Urden|Dinin]], [[Vierna Do'Urden|Vierna]], [[Maya Do'Urden|Maya]], and [[Drizzt Do'Urden]].
