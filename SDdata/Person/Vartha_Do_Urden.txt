Vartha DoUrden was the mother and predecessor of Malice DoUrden, matron mother of House DoUrden.
Vartha DoUrden was the matron mother of House DoUrden when she suffered a sudden death in the 10 century DR. Her daughter Malice subsequently became matron mother, even though she was less than 100 years old at the time.
Vartha was the mother of Malice DoUrden and thus the grandmother of Malices children Briza, Nalfein, Dinin, Vierna, Maya, and Drizzt DoUrden.
