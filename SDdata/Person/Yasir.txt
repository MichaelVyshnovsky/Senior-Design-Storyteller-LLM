'''Yasir''', who was nicknamed '''the Snake''' behind his back, was a bandit and member of the [[Desert Wind]] during the mid–14th century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Yasir served as one of Sheik [[Ar-Rabi]]'s lieutenants. He commanded fellow bandits, raiding local tribes on the [[Plains of Purple Dust]] and southern [[Murghôm]].

==Relationships==
Though he was loyal to his half-brother Ar-Rabi, Yasir harbored personal ambitions as well.
