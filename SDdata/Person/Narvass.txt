Narvass was a devoted member of the cult of the Absolute who lived on the Sword Coast during the late 15 century DR. Along with the human Kagran, Narvass was one of the few non-goblinoid cultists granted permission to stay within the Shattered Sanctum.
Like many of the Absolutes other devotees, Narvass wore an amulet bearing the Absolutes holy symbol.
He also carried a crossbow strapped to his back.
