'''Corwyn Jaffe''' was an ex-pirate who ran the [[Devil's Fire Tavern]] in [[Melvaunt]], an establishment he purchased from a merchant in [[1372 DR]]. Corwyn had not completely left his old life behind however, continuing to serve as the first mate on a pirate ship.
