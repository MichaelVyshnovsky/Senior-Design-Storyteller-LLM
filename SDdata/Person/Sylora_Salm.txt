'''Sylora Salm''' was a [[sorceress]] of [[Thay]] who served [[Szass Tam]].

==Relationships==
Salm was [[Dahlia Sin'felle|Dahlia Sin'felle's]] rival. 

==History==
She tricked Dahlia in [[Gauntlgrym]] which ended in the release of the fire primordial.

===Death===
Sylora died at the hands of the [[mummy]] [[Jestry Rallevin|Jestry]] after being defeated by the trio [[Drizzt Do'Urden]], Dahlia, and [[Artemis Entreri]] in the {{YearlinkName|1463}}.
