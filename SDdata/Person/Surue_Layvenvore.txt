'''Surue Layvenvore''', nicknamed '''Ladylore''', was a priest of the [[church of Oghma|church]] of [[Oghma]], called a Namer, based in the city of [[Saerloon]] in [[Sembia]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. By around [[1480 DR]], Surue had gained great prosperity from work in an arts or entertainment industry.
