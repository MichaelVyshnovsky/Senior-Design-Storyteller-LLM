'''Oolitikana''' was an inhabitant of [[Mitzlato]] in the [[Pasocada Basin]] in [[Maztica]].

==Abilities==
Oolitikana knew many languages: those of the [[Dog People]], [[desert dwarf|desert dwarves]], [[Poscadar elf|Poscadar elves]], [[Metahel]], [[Nahopaca]], and the [[Short Ones]].

==History==
In [[1362 DR]], he acted as a translator to [[Sozarro]]'s expedition.
