'''Keldor Hannith''' was a [[human]] man of the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]], who lived ''circa'' [[1358 DR]].

He was a [[wizard]] who sold [[scroll]]s and [[potion]]s, and performed spellcasting services for a fee. Hannith was described as a quiet and careful man, but was one of the more notable Procampan mages of his day.
