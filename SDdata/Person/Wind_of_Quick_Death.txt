The '''Wind of Quick Death''' was a pirate who sailed the [[Zakharan seas|Golden Gulf]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
The Wind of Quick Death attacked merchant ships traveling along the [[Southeastern Sea Route]] until he was successfully defeated by the Admirality of [[Huzuz]].

==History==
Aided by a pair of [[djinn]], ships from Huzuz attacked and killed the Wind of Quick Death upon the open waters. His head was returned to Huzuz and the [[Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir|Grand Caliph]] rewarded the captains involved with a magical [[cutlass]].
