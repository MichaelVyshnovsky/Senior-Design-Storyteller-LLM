Garrant was an officer in the Golden Legion and captain of the Legions swordsmen. 
Despite being one of the main officers, Cordell never consulted him in making strategic decisions.
During the Viperhands assault at the start of the Night of Wailing, Garrant led the legionaries in repelling the enemy, while Cordell, developed a strategy in order to escape the enemys siege.
Garrant did not survive the battle.
