'''Choris Steelsar''' was an unfortunate child of the exiled [[Amn]]ian [[Parhek]] dynasty.

==Relationships==
Choris was the child of Princess [[Rasa Parhek]] [[Dornar Steelsar]], and was the eldest sibling of [[Rosa Steelsar|Rosa]], [[Rohav Steelsar|Rohav]], and [[Rhothar Steelsar|Rhothar]].

==History==
Choris was born in [[Silverymoon]], and was only 10 years old in the {{YearlinkName|1297}} when the family was attacked by assassins from Amn. Dornar and Choris fought to the death to allow the others to escape, with only Rasa and Rhothar fleeing while Rohav was kidnapped and Rosa was killed.
