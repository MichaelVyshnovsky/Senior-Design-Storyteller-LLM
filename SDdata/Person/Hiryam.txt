'''Hiryam''' was a shopkeeper inhabiting the city of [[Waterdeep]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Hiryam owned a cloth goods shop simply named after himself called [[Hiryam's Cloth Goods]] that was located along [[Burnt Wagon Way]], in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] in the City of Splendors.
