}}Lord '''Kythyss''' was the ruler of the coastal town of [[Kythyss]] in [[Callidyrr]] in the {{YearlinkName|1345}}.

==Activities==
Lord Kythyss maintained an ongoing quarrel with his neighbor to the north, Lord [[Llewellyn (person)|Llewellyn]] of rival town [[Llewellyn]]. Both lords argued over who had rights to fish the fertile waters of [[Llewellyn Harbor]]. The matter was even brought before the High King.
