'''Altheia''' was a young [[Courtesan (prostitute)|courtesan]] from the [[Athkatla/Wave District|Wave District]] of [[Athkatla]] in [[Amn]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Altheia was a beautiful young woman with blonde hair and dressed in blue garments.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Altheia, still a very young woman, found herself in financial strain. Her upper class family faced hardships after the death of Altheia's father and her mother was unable to provide, leaving Altheia desperate for [[coin]] and selling herself in the Wave District of Athkatla at night.
