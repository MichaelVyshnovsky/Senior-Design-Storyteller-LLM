Hellenhild was a frost giant jarl sometime after the War of the Silver Marches.
Hellenhild was one of the giant lords at the time of the shattering of the ordning.
In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Hellenhild was one of the giant lords who had arrived in Maelstrom in search of news about Princess Serissas plans for the future.
Hellenhild respected battle prowess above all.
