'''Atham One-Eye''' was a blacksmith from [[Suzail]], the capital of [[Cormyr]], active in the early 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Atham One-Eye was the proprietor of his smith shop, located to the south of Suzail's [[The Market (Suzail)|Market]], on the bank of the [[Dragonmere]]. By the {{YearlinkName|1356}}, Atham was out of business and his smithy was taken over by [[the Purple Scar]] festhall.
