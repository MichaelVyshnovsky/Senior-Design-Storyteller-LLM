King '''Rhadik Torlath II''' "the Sleeping King" was a long-lived [[Amn]]ian monarch of the [[Torlath]] dynasty, known as the fifth Wizard-King of Amn.

==Relationships==
Rhadik was the fourth son of of King [[Jhanos Torlath II|Jhanos II]], and his sole son was [[Imnel Torlath IV|Imnel IV]].
