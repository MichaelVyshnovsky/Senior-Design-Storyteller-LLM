'''Mace''' was a [[half-orc]] cleric of [[Bane]] in [[Phlan]] in [[1340 DR]].

==History==
Mace led a group of [[orc]]s that had occupied the [[temple of II-Mater]] until an adventuring party hired by [[Dirtan]] killed him and his minions.
