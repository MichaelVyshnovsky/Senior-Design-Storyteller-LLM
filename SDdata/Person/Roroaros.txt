'''Roroaros''' was an [[Dragon aging|elder]] [[black dragon]] and one of the "overdragons" of [[Melabrauth]] in [[Laerakond]] as of [[1479 DR]].

==Personality==
Roroaros was cruel and enjoyed killing those who had defied him. He was clever enough to consult with [[Melauthaur]] before killing his enemies.

==Activities==
In 1479 DR, Roroaros was in charge to rule and protect a coastal region of Melabrauth.
