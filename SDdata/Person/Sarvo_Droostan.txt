'''Sarvo Droostan''', also known as "'''Old Sarvo'''", was the tavern master of the [[Copper Coronet]], a tavern in the [[River District (Athkatla)|river district]] of [[Athkatla]], around the year [[1370 DR]].

==Description==
Sarvo was about as fat as the [[cask]]s of cheap [[ale]] and [[wine]] he kept in the cellar of the Coronet.
