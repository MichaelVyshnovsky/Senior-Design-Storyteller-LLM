'''Nimrud''' was a powerful member of the [[Northern Wizards]] in [[Unther]] in [[1357 DR]].

==Personality==
Nimrud was quite pessimistic, believing that [[Gilgeam]] would kill all of them. The only option was to overthrow him before he did.
