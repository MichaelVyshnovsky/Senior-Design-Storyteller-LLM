'''Belnak Vikom''' was a [[dragonborn]] noble and a member of [[Clan Belnak]]. He was Clan Belnak's representative in [[Ruinspoke]] in [[1480 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Hand of Darkness|6}}

==Activities==
In [[1479 DR]], Vikom was under the tutelage of Lord [[Jalt Tuanek]].

In 1480 DR, when  servants of the [[lamia]] Queen [[Sisay]] tried to kill Lord Tuanek, Lord Vikom hired some [[adventurer]]s to dealt with the monsters.
