'''Lana Mara''' was a high-ranking officer of [[Muktar]] in the [[Turmish]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Activities==
Lana was a member of the [[Champions of Muktar]].

==History==
Lana was the latest, and possible greatest, apprentice of the famed illusionist [[Yhelva|Yhelva the Wicked]].
In [[1358 DR]] Lana and his fellow Champions arranged the trap inside [[Fort Blood]] planned by the self-styled king of [[Muktar]], [[Yrkhetep|Hetep]], to eliminate an adventuring party well-known in [[Golconda]].
Later she took part in the [[Battle of Wilma Pass]].
