Lana Mara was a high-ranking officer of Muktar in the Turmish in 1358 DR.
Lana was a member of the Champions of Muktar.
Lana was the latest, and possible greatest, apprentice of the famed illusionist Yhelva the Wicked.
In 1358 DR Lana and his fellow Champions arranged the trap inside Fort Blood planned by the self-styled king of Muktar, Hetep, to eliminate an adventuring party well-known in Golconda.
Later she took part in the Battle of Wilma Pass.
