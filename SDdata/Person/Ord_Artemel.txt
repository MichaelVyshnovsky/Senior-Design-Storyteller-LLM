Ord Artemel was a noble of the Waterdhavian noble house Artemel during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
He lived within Artemel Villa located at the corner of Sul Street and Vondil Street in the northeast section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward.
Ord was the eldest son and heir of his father, Bresnoss Artemel, and his mother, Lydda, who was his fathers consort.
