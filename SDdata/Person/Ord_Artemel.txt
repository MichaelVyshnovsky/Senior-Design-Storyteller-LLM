'''Ord Artemel''' was a noble of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Artemel]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He lived within [[Artemel Villa]] located at the corner of [[Sul Street]] and [[Vondil Street]] in the northeast section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Relationships==
Ord was the eldest son and heir of his father, [[Bresnoss Artemel]], and his mother, [[Lydda Artemel|Lydda]], who was his father's consort.
