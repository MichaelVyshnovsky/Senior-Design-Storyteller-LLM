Yusef was a faris and the eldest son of Alia bint Hazir and Sultan Yusef al-Wadi of Gana in the Pearl Cities region of west Zakhara.
Yusef was headstrong and typically saw things in black in white.
He had a mole under his right eye in the shape of a butterfly.
As with all of his siblings, Yusefs parentage was kept secret from the population of Gana. Yusef was raised in a monastery high in Al-Yabki Mountains.
