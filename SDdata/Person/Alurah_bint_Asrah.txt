'''Alurah bint Asrah''' was the Sultana of [[Hiyal]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Land of Fate/Adventurer's Guide to Zakhara|60-63}}

==Personality==
She was considered a wise a fair ruler by some, while others thought of her as a dark-hearted schemer.

==Appearance==
Alurah was in her 60s in 1367 and in a poor state of health.

==History==
Alurah's husband, then the sultan, died suddenly in [[1362 DR]] during a dinner. She was able to convince her allies in [[Huzuz]], Prince [[Tannous al-Assad]] chief among them, that she alone could quell the chaos that ensued after her husband's death.
