'''Crystyn''' was a barmaid at the [[Bubbling Bulwark]] in [[1369 DR]].

==Activities==
She worked as a servant at the Bulwark, alongside [[Sara]], [[Heather (barmaid)|Heather]], [[Caryn]], [[Raewyn]], and [[Annalise]]. She was best friends with Annalise.
