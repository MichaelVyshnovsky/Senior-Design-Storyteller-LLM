'''Ojin Voninsdottir''' was the great chief of the [[Black Raven tribe]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Chief Ojin was known to have a rather cold and uncaring personality.

==Possessions==
Among her possessions, Ojin had a [[helmet]] made from the skull of an [[orc]].
