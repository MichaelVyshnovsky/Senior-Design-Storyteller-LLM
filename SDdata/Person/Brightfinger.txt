Brightfinger was a Netherese arcanist that was known for his development of light-based spells.
This variator was interested in spells that had more practical than destructive uses. He was astonished that the archwizards of Netheril didnt feel the same way.
Brightfinger was born in . Growing up with a priest, he sought to develop utility spells that were commonly used by clerics. In , Brightfinger developed the light spell. Five years later, he created the light-based spell that was known as Brightfingers dancers, though it eventually became known as dancing lights. His peers commended his skill, and Brightfinger started to develop a version that would suddenly explode once someone approached it, though it is unknown if he ever completed its formulation.
