'''Viln Tirin''' was a [[drow]] gang leader and cultist in [[Menzoberranzan]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
Viln led the [[Bandersnatches]], a gang of young [[demon]]-worshiping drow in [[West Wall]] district. They hoped to spread more chaos in the city in the wake of [[Demogorgon]]'s rampage ''circa'' [[1486 DR]].

==Relationship==
Viln and her gangsters had a [[quasit]] advisor.
