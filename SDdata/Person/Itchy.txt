Itchy was one of two crawling claw undead pets kept by Lucretious, the proprietress of of the Circus of the Last Days in the late 15 century DR.
Itchy was an amputated reanimated hand of a humanoid creature. It had pale skin, and its fingers were decorated with many rings.
Like other crawling claws, Itchy had the usual strengths and vulnerabilities of an undead creature. It was unaffected by poison and possessed a blindsight.
In the , Itchy and another fellow crawling claw named Scratchy could be found in Lucretiouss tent at the back end of the area in Rivington of Baldurs Gate, where the Circus of the Last Days performed for a short period of time, before moving on across the planes.
