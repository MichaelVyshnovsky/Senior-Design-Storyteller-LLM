Ayala was a wood elf warrior who lived in Neverwinter in the late years of the 15 century DR.
Ayala was the twin sister of Sylvara.
Ayala had a magic sword.
Alongside her twin sister, Ayala led a band of elves that operated out in the Blacklake District. As of 1479 DR, the sisters goal was to become stronger so they could join a band of Bregan Daerthe that was operating near Castle Never.
