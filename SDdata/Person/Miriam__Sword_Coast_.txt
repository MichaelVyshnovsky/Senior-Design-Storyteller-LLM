Miriam was a woman from the Sword Coast who fell in love with the man named Edmund who became a warlock.
At some point in her life, Miriam became indebted to Maggie Two-Fingers. Somehow, Maggie learned of Miriams name and began harassing her for the money. Miriams lover Edmund took decisive action. He agreed to an infernal pact with a cambion to gain warlock powers and joined a mercenary group to earn money, and pay back Miriams debt.
