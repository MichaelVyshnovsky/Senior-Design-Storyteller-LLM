Setara min Jisan was a priest of the Golden Mosque in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.
As the Keeper of the Ceremonies, Setara was responsible for all of the services held within the Golden Mosque. She was also in charge of the acolytes assigned to the House of the Loregiver.
