'''Rhieldra Zoldaftel''' was a sorcerer who lived in [[Crimmor]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|29}} She was the unmarried daughter of the infamous [[wagon]] maker [[Zan Zoldaftel]].

==Activities==
Rhieldra was the business manager of [[Zoldaftel Wagons]]. She placed magical [[dweomer]]s on the wagons built by her father to prevent counterfeits.
