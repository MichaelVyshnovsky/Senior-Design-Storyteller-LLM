'''Loravyre Impkith''' was a [[tiefling]] [[wizard]] who inhabited [[Fort Precipice]] in the [[Chasm]] within the [[Ebon Downs]] in [[1484 DR]].

==History==
She recruited a tiefling adventurer to recover some strange items in possession of some [[Ashmadai]]-related [[devils]] in the Chasm.
