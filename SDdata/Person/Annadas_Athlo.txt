'''Annadas Athlo''' was a blind [[monk]] and a playwright who followed teachings of [[Ilmater]], The Broken God sometime before the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Annadas Athlo's most well known work was the "grand play" called ''[[The Shattered Throne Tumbles]]''. By the late 14{{th}} century DR, his play was a part of [[The Crown Aflame]]'s repertoire – famous theater inside a repurposed temple of [[Azuth]] that stood in the [[Athkatla/Temple District|Temple District]] of [[Athkatla]] in [[Amn]].
