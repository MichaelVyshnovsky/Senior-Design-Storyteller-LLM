'''Burdan Hlathiman''' was an official in [[Scornubel]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
As one of the Lord High Advisors, Burdan aided Lady [[Rhessajan Ambermantle]] with regards to profit and trade for the city.

==History==
Burdan was a trader and caravan master before turning toward politics.
