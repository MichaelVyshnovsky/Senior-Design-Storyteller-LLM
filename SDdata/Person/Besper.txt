'''Besper''' was a [[djinni]] inhabiting the [[Haunted Lands]] of [[Zakhara]] in {{Yearlink|1367}}.{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
He was {{SI|10|ft|long=yes}} tall.

==Personality==
Besper was friendly and charming. He had a reputation for being a ladies man back on his home on the [[Elemental Plane of Air]].

==Relationships==
Besper answered the summons of Sheikh [[Kaldhun bin Hanif]] through a ''[[ring of genie summoning]]'' that the Sheikh carried.
