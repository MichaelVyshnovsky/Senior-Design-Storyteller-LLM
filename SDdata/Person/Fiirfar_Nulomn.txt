'''Fiirfar Nulomn''' was a [[human]] [[magic-user]] and a member of the [[adventuring company]] known as The [[Four]]. He was hanged as an assassin in [[Tethyr]] on the day before [[Higharvestide]] in [[1369 DR]] following the siege of [[Myratma]], along with fellow members [[Telorn]] and [[Diloule|Diloule "Lanternhand"]].
