Derezdreene was an older actress and a companion who worked at the Dryads Dreams of Mosstone in Tethyr.
Derezdreene was an aged tall matronly woman with white hair. She carried herself with pose and sophistication worthy of a queen.
Derezdreene was one of the female escorts who worked at Dryads Dreams in the late 14 century DR, along with several men and women who offered their services and companionship at the only festhall in Mosstone. She was renowned for her acting and was often hired exercise this talent by performing a role of someones visiting mother, a noblewoman, or, on occasion, an exiled monarch of a faraway land.
