'''Yintras Bedelmrin''' was a mage during the 8{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He wore patchy robes.

==Personality==
Yintras was quite devout.

==Abilities==
The places where he dwelt had wards that could sear off limbs with a touch.

==Relationships==
He dwelt alone.

==History==
In the {{yearlinkName|761}}, Yintras Bedelmrin was alone at [[Yintras' tower|his tower]], praying to [[Azuth]] for guidance on who to pass his Art on to. He received a sign from the god, who magically armored him and forewarned him about a danger coming to him. At that point, [[Saeraede Lyonora|the Slayer]], an enigmatic entity, flowed into his tower room and towards him. With Azuth's boon, Yintras was able to repel it, and the entity fled his tower.
