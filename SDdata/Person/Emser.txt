'''Emser''' was a mysterious [[gnome]] inhabiting the city of [[Daggerford]] in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
He owned a small house located at the intersection of [[Duke's Way]] and the [[High Road (Daggerford)|High Road]] across the street from the [[Grand Playhouse]].{{Cite interactive atlas|3990 Daggerford}} Emser was believed to be an [[illusionist]] of unparalled skill. Most gave him a wide berth when they saw him around Daggerford.
