Babyjowls was a member of the Guild from the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
In the , the Guild reinstated tethered knife fights to its members delight. The fights were held until the first blood. Babyjowls and Three-Piece had at least one fight and were scheduled for a rematch sometime during the Absolute crisis in Baldurs Gate. Bursar Uktar collected bets and dispensed winnings.
