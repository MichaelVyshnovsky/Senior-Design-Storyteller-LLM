'''Orina''' was a librarian of the [[book cache]] in [[Luskan]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Orina had mousy brown hair, and wore long blue robes.

==History==
Some time around [[1490 DR]], Orina encountered the [[Champion of the Burning Dawn]].
