Madame Darkness was a fortune teller in the Moonsea in the late 15 century DR.
Madame Darkness was the fortune teller in Doctor Jubals Carnival of Curiosities. Her visions were always vague but after twilight her responses had a hint of menace.
Madame Darkness escaped the Underdark circa 1486 DR and joined the Carnival. She sensed something strange from the new mirror maze attraction.
