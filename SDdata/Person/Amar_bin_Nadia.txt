'''Amar bin Nadia''' was a member of the [[House of Nasr]] tribe in the [[High Desert]] of [[west Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39">

==Personality==
Amar was bitter and cynical because of the attention and praise heaped upon his sister.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39"/>

==Activities==
Overshadowed by his older sister, Amar chose a different path and studied the ways of the [[Elemental mage|sand mage]].<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39"/>

==Relationships==
Amar was the youngest child of Sheikh [[Nadia umm Fadela]]. His older sister was [[Fadela Bloodmane]].<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39"/>
