Marsalas Endrivin was a stormlord of Talos at the deitys only sanctioned temple, located at the base of the semi-active volcano Mount Ugruth, around 1370 DR. His realm of influence, which he referred to as the "Stormlands", included the city of Hlondeth and the lands between the Aphrunn and the Orsraun Mountains in the Vilhon Reach.
Stormlord Endrivin was known for his ruthless and vindictive personality.
Being well-equipped for protection, Endrivin was known for wearing bracers of defense and a +3 ring of protection. His preferred weapons of choice were a +2 morningstar or similarly enchanted javelins.
