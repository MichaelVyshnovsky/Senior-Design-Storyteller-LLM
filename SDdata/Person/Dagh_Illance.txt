Dagh Illance was a knight and a member of the Illance noble family in Cormyr who rode with Alusair Obarskyr.
He was hot tempered and battle-hungry,
In the Year of the Gauntlet, 1369 DR, Alusair and her knights were operating in the Stonelands, pursuing a bold orc band that had raided a caravan east of Eveningstar in six days of pursuit, theyd killed a hydra, a "fire lizard", and three chimeras. They were on a high meadow north of Startop Crag when they sighted the orcs Dagh was the first to hurl a spear and the knights impetuously pursued the orcs into an ambush. Their dark naga master hurled a lightning bolt and Dagh and a few others were killed, before Alusair rallied her forces to defeat their foes. She muttered "good riddance to Illance".
