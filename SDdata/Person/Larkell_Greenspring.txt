Larkell Greenspring was a mercenary in Zhent-occupied Daggerdale in 1367 DR.
In 1367 DR, Larkell Greenspring and Trover escorted Constable Trens envoy Marrakt to meet a just-arrived-in-town adventuring party.
Larkell was a seasoned veteran, not easily intimidated by others.
