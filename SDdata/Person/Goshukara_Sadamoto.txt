'''Goshukara Sadamoto''' was a member of [[Goshukara clan]] in [[Nakamaru]] around [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
Sadamoto supported his brother [[Goshukara Akinari]] in his plots, acting as wild card. He always traveled armed in public believing that the [[Funada clan]] would attack him in an instant

==History==
Sadamoto may tried to ruin [[Funada Mitsuhide]] by ruining a peace meeting between Mitsuhide and the [[Big Head]] yakuza gang. 
In 1357 born [[Goshukara Tora]], the third son of Sadamoto and he held a great party

==Personality==
Sadamoto was a man of impulse, not good in plots or schemes. He was, like his brother, concerned for the success and survival of the family and was especially desirous for a restoration of its fortunes.
