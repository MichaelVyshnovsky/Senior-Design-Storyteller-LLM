'''Safia al-Gurab Sakir''' was a member of the [[Unclean (Zakhara)|Unclean]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|19-20}}

==Personality==
Safia was demure and loyally obeyed every command of her master. She was nervous compared to her sister [[Mara al-Gurab Sakir]].

==Decription==
Safia had shiny black hair and dark eyes.

==Activities==
Safia served as one of [[Akim al-Kalaas]]'s apprentices, learning the mystical ways of the [[sha'ir]].
