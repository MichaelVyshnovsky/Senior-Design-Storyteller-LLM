Safia al-Gurab Sakir was a member of the Unclean in 1367 DR.
Safia was demure and loyally obeyed every command of her master. She was nervous compared to her sister Mara al-Gurab Sakir.
Safia had shiny black hair and dark eyes.
Safia served as one of Akim al-Kalaass apprentices, learning the mystical ways of the shair.
