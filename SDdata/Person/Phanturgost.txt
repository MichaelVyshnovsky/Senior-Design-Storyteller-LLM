Phanturgost Amalree was a human wizard of the early 11 century DR.
He was adventuresome.
He owned Thrauntspire.
He married Nathra, and fathered Ieiridauna Amalree.
In his time, Phanturgost made his name devising warding spells. Still in his youth, he fathered Ieiridauna Amalree with the elf Nathra.
He died in early autumn of , after inviting eight sorcerers from lands south of Calimshan with whom he and his wife had been exploring a Netherese ruin. The sorcerers slew the two of them swiftly, but encountered difficulty slaying their daughter.
