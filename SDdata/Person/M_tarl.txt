Mtarl was a drow lieutenant within Jarlaxles Bregan Daerthe.
In 1358 DR, while traveling through the deadly streets of Menzoberranzan seeking valuable information to help the organizations cause, Jarlaxle and Mtarl encountered Gromph Baenre.
To have a private conversation with Jarlaxle, Gromph transported Mtarl to an extra-dimensional realm.In the novel Siege of Darkness, Gromph takes Jarlaxle into an extra-dimensional pocket and destroys a zombie servant with fire. While there is no concrete evidence this zombie is Mtarl, the question is still presented for the reader.
