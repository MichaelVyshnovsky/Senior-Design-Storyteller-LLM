Stendak was a middle-ranking cleric of Tiamat living in Unther in 1374 DR.
Stendak was a celebrant at the Altar of Scales temple in Unthalass. He often used his skills to act as an intermediary, being liked by all inside the temple.
Stendak was a manipulative and opportunistic individual. He believed that deception and manipulation were his contribution to his goddesss plans. He liked playing both sides in a fight.
