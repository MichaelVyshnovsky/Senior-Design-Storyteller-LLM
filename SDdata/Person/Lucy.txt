Lucy was an adolescent pet wyvern in the City of Coin, Athkatla in Amn in the 14 century DR.
She was a wyvern roughly the size of a humanoid. She was a somewhat haughty creature who ignored customers of the shop.
By the , Lucy was a pet of Ribald Barterman, the proprietor of the Adventurer Mart in Athkatlas Waukeens Promenade. She could be found flying about the shops spacious back section with open floors and bookshelves.
