[[Lucy]] was an adolescent pet [[wyvern]] in the City of Coin, [[Athkatla]] in [[Amn]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
She was a wyvern roughly the size of a [[humanoid]]. She was a somewhat haughty creature who ignored customers of the shop.

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, Lucy was a pet of [[Ribald Barterman]], the proprietor of the [[Adventurer Mart]] in Athkatla's [[Waukeen's Promenade]]. She could be found flying about the shop's spacious back section with open floors and bookshelves.
