Daren Timbide was a general of the Banite army stationed at Mintar in 1372 DR.
A fervent worshiper of Bane, Daren was known for the speed with which he rose through the ranks of that evil army. He spent most of his time inspiring his troops with rousing speeches, practicing his martial skills, and worshiping Bane at a secret shrine he called the "Thunder Room".
A classically handsome man, Daren had long blonde hair and intense blue eyes.
Daren Timbide wore +2 full plate armor while wielding a +1 human bane longsword and a +1 heavy steel shield.
