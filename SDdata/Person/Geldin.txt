Geldin was a half-orc muscle of the adventuring company - the Ladys Lament from Waterdeep, active circa 1310 DR.
Unlike his mate, Geldin respected law and order. He usually wore a set of leather armors, and was armed with a short sword and a club.
Several years before the , Geldin, along with his mate Khatja, joined the Ladys Lament and quickly gained respect for their strength. The couple rarely talked about their past, but one thing was known about them - they have been ostracised by both humans and orcs alike.
In 1310 DR, the Ladys Lament, along with many other mercenaries, traveled from Luskan to the besieged city of Targos in the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.
