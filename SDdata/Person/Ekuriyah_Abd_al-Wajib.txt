'''Ekuriyah Abd al-Wajib''' was the general of the [[Dutiful Mamluk Society]] in [[Qudra]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
She served as the active voice for the Dutiful on the [[Mamluk Council]] because [[Hatit Abd al-Wajib]]'s duties as emir prevented him from having a say on council matters.
