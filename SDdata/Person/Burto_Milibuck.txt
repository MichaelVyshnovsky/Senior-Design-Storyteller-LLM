'''Burto Milibuck''' was an insane [[halfling]] [[thief]] in [[Yûlash]] in [[1357 DR]].

==History==
In 1357 DR, Burto was scavenging in the ruins of the city when he saw the [[avatar]] of [[Moander]] that escaped from Yûlash. From that day, he saw vegetable monsters everywhere.
