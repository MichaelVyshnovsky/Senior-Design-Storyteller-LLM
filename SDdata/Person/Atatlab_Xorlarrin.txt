'''Atatlab Xorlarrin''' was a warrior of [[House Xorlarrin]] of [[Menzoberranzan]] in [[1372 DR]].

Atatlab was one of the sentries patrolling the house compound during the [[Silence of Lolth]]. [[Matron Mother]] [[Zeerith Q'Xorlarrin]] knew him by name.
