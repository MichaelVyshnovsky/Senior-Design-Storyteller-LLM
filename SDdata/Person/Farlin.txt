Farlin was a member of The Guild crime gang in Baldurs Gate and an Envoy of Nine-Fingers to the Stone Lord in the late 15 century DR.
Farlin was a rock gnome woman with a pale skin tone and green eyes. She wore a leather helmet and leather armor dyed in emerald-greenâ€”the Guilds telltale color. She was armed with two daggers a mundane one, and an enchanted Gleamdance dagger.
In the , during the Absolute crisis, Farlin led a gang of Guild members in dealing with the dreaded Stone Lord and his men. The new kingpin secretly served the Absolute and was trying to remove the Guilds influence from across the Gate. Farlins group faced a band of the Stone Lords men led by the tiefling Cairos, under the Wyrms Crossing bridge in Rivington, hoping to seize their illegal goods haul. However, the meeting devolved into a battle. Farlins and her squads fate remained uncertain, whether they were killed by the gangsters or survived and returned safely to the Guilds safehouse.
