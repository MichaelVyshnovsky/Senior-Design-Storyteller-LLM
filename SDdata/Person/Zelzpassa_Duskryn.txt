'''Zelzpassa Duskryn''' was a [[drow]] [[rogue]] and [[cleric]] of [[Lolth]] and a member of [[House Duskryn]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

She was the leader of House Duskryn's permanent garrison in the ruin of [[Blingdenstone]] around [[1372 DR]].
