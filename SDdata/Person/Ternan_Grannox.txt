'''Ternan Grannox''' was the Lord-Mayor of [[Darromar]], the capital of [[Tethyr]]. 

==Relationships==
Ternan was the only child of councilor [[Valsin Grannox]] and was the nephew of Count [[Malcor Grannox]]. The Lord-Mayor was married to Consort [[Brenna Grannox]].
