Arlen was the captain of the guard at Caer Corwell and the teacher of Prince Tristan Kendrick.
Arlen was a gruff old war veteran.
While training Prince Kendrick, Arlen had the youth run across the moors for exercise.
Arlen died in 1345 DR while fighting a group of firbolgs in Llaryth Forest.
