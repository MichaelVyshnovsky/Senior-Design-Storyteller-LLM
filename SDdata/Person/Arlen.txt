'''Arlen''' was the captain of the guard at [[Caer Corwell]] and the teacher of [[Tristan Kendrick|Prince Tristan Kendrick]].

==Description==
Arlen was a gruff old war veteran.

==Personality==
While training Prince Kendrick, Arlen had the youth run across the moors for exercise.

==History==
===Death===
Arlen died in [[1345 DR]] while fighting a group of [[firbolg]]s in [[Llaryth Forest]].
