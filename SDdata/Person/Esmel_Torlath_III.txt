King '''Esmel Torlath III''', originally named '''Dharik Torlath''', was an early ruler of [[Amn]] of the [[Torlath]] dynasty in the 7{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
Esmel was the second son of King [[Esmel Torlath II]], and [[Marcos Vymmar]] was his maternal grandfather. [[Corl Torlath II|Corl II]] was his only son.

==History==
Dharik's mother and elder brother disappeared in the {{YearlinkName|600}} when the realm of [[Minsorran]] was destroyed. His father died of grief the following year, putting him on the throne at age 16 with the regnal name Esmel III. He was guided through a long regency by his mother's father, Marcos Vymmar, which ended in the {{YearlinkName|614}}. From then on, Esmel reigned until his death in the {{YearlinkName|656}}.
