King Esmel Torlath III, originally named Dharik Torlath, was an early ruler of Amn of the Torlath dynasty in the 7 century DR.
Esmel was the second son of King Esmel Torlath II, and Marcos Vymmar was his maternal grandfather. Corl II was his only son.
