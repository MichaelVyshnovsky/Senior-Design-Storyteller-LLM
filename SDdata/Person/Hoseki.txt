Hoseki was a disguised sohei follower of the dai-oni in Kozakura in 1357 DR.
Hoseki was a sohei of the Black Temple. In 1357 DR, Hoseki posed as a farmer living in Myudo village, a village under the dai-onis control, to spy on the inhabitants. He noticed some adventurers whod arrived at the village and asked to sleep in his house for night. In the night, Hoseki led his men in trying to kill them but was defeated.
