'''Prindentle Carh''' was a [[diviner]] within the [[Red Wizards]] of [[Thay]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

He served as the [[autharch]] of the city of [[Nethentir]]. He carried out his duties under the authority of the weak-willed [[Tharchion]] [[Hezass Nymar]].
