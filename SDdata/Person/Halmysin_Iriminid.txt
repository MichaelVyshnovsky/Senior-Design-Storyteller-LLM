'''Halmysin Iriminid''' was an [[elf]] of [[Eveningstar]] in [[Cormyr]] during the mid-1480s [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}

==Description==
She had long auburn hair tied back in a large ponytail, gray eyes, and a pale skin tone. She wore a fashionable long black dress and long gloves.

==History==
{{Quote
|Eveningstar used to be such a quiet little village! How I miss those days.
|Halmysin
}}
Halmysin lived in Eveningstar during the time of the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar|Sschindylryn invasion]], and strongly preferred the times before rampaging arsonous [[drow]].
