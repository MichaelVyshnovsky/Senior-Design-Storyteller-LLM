Stonemason '''Gergerann''' was a [[duergar]] craftsman of [[Clan Flameshade]] that took up residence in [[Grymforge]] for a time during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Clan Flameshade were hired by the [[Cult of the Absolute]]'s Sergeant [[Thrinn]] to help [[Nere]] with the excavation of Grymforge with the aim of staking a claim on the [[Adamantine Forge]]. Gergerann helped [[Kith (duergar)|Kith]] in his studies of the ruined architecture of the underground fortress.
