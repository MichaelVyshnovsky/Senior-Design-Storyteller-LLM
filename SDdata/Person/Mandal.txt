'''Mandal''' was a taverner in [[Ylraphon]]. He was also a [[wererat]] who captured [[Karasendrieth]].{{cite book/The Rage||1}}

==Description==
He was a gaunt man with spiky, grizzled hair, and shifty eyes.

==Activities==
Mandal was a taverner.

==History==
On {{yearlink|1373}}, on [[Hammer 16]], Mandal captured the [[song dragon]] Karasendrieth in her human form. She was later rescued by [[Dorn Graybrook]]'s party: they slew all of the wererats, including Mandal.
