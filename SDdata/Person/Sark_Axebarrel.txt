'''Sark Axebarrel''' was a [[deep gnome]] guard in [[Blingdenstone]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
The cynical Sark commanded a patrol at the entrance of the closed [[Rockblight]] section of the city. His reports told of the presence of a [[medusa]] inside.
