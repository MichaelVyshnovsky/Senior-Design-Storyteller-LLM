'''Carrig Cymrych I''' was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the mid-to-late 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] and the second son of the dynastyâ€™s legendary namesake, [[Cymrych Hugh]]. He was the brother of both of his immediate predecessors: High King [[Warren Cymrych I|Warren I]] followed by High Queen [[Tamara Cymrych|Tamara]]. Carrig I's successor was his first son, [[Gwylloch Cymrych]], while his second son, [[Gorham Cymrych|Gorham]], would become High King after Gwylloch.

==History==
Carrig I assumed the throne of the High King in [[Callidyrr]] during the {{YearlinkName|263}}, after the [[poison]]ing of his sister, High Queen Tamara. At the time, [[Cedric Riker]] ruled in the family's ancestral seat of [[Corwell]] as its regent, and would do so until [[272 DR|the ninth year of his reign]].

Carrig I ruled for twenty-four years before dying in battle against [[Northmen]] invaders from  [[Tuern]] and [[Gundarlun]] in the {{YearlinkName|287}}.{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|26}}
