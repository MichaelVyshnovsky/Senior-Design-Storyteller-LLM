Booyagh '''Gnat''' was a [[goblin]] [[spellcaster]] and a member of the [[Cult of the Absolute]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|[[True Soul]], eh? Figured [[the Absolute]] had standards.

==Personality==
He was notably rude to people outside the Cult of the Absolute.

==History==
Gnat was present when the True Soul [[Dror Ragzlin]] attempted to [[Speak with dead|commune]] with a dead [[mind flayer]] brought into the [[Shattered Sanctum]] in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
