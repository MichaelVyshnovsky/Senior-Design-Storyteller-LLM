'''Ukharim Grimshackle''' was a [[halfling]] jailer at [[Grimshackle Jail]] in [[Phlan]] in [[1489 DR]].

==Personality==
Ukharim and his brother were only interested in money. He was filthy and bedraggled like his brother.

==Activities==
Ukharim and his brother [[Bhevek Grimshackle]] opened Grimshackle Jail, where they took criminals whom they ransomed to the highest bidder.{{Cite adventurers league/Outlaws of the Iron Route|9}}

==History==
In 1489 DR, Ukharim permitted, for some money, an adventuring party to interrogate [[Gray Patriots]]'s member [[Rathene Fel]]. Afterward, however, in an attempted prison break, the corrupt jailer [[Iorg Brokenjaw]] took him hostage, but the adventurers resolved the situation.{{Cite adventurers league/Outlaws of the Iron Route|12}}
