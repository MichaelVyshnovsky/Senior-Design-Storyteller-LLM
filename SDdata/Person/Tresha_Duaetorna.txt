'''Tresha Duaetorna''' held the office of "officer of the holies" in [[Tulmon]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p166">

==Description==
Tresha was rotund and motherly.<ref name=EotSS-p166"/>

==Activities==
She encouraged the sultan's policy of personal unassisted worship among Tulmon's citizens, which helped many of the city's smaller shrines and chapels flourish.<ref name=EotSS-p166"/>

==History==
Tresha was originally an [[adventurer]] from [[Waterdeep]] who worshiped [[Ao]] at one point in her life.<ref name=EotSS-p166"/>
