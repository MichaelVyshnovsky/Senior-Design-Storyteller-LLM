'''Halston''' was a cooper in [[Immerford]] in [[Cormyr]].

==History==
In [[1480 DR]], Halston and other wealthy citizens went to protest to Lord [[Lothan Durncaskyn]] about an unseen thief and murderer. He was persuaded by Durncaskyn's speech.
