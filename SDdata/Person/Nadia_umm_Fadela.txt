Sheikh Nadia umm Fadela was the leader of the House of Nasr tribe in the High Desert of west Zakhara around 1367 DR. As of 1367 DR, she had led the tribe for twenty years.
Nadia was a strong independent woman with an impressive force of will.
Nadia protected the Desert Mosque from an ajami raid.
Nadia married twice but outlived both husbands. Her daughter was Fadela.
