Sheikh '''Nadia umm Fadela''' was the leader of the [[House of Nasr]] tribe in the [[High Desert]] of [[west Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}} As of 1367 DR, she had led the tribe for twenty years.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39">

==Personality==
Nadia was a strong independent woman with an impressive force of will.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39"/>

==Activities==
Nadia protected the [[Desert Mosque]] from an [[ajami]] raid.<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39"/>

==Relationships==
Nadia married twice but outlived both husbands. Her daughter was [[Fadela Bloodmane|Fadela]].<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39"/>
