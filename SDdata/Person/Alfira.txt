'''Alfira''' was a [[tiefling]] [[bard]] who lived among a group of her kin that was exiled from [[Elturel]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|''"Words of mine will turn to ash<br />When you call the last light down"''|Lyrics from ''[[The Weeping Dawn]]''}}

==Personality==
Despite her talent with music, Alfira could be discouraged quite readily.

==Abilities==
She was a very talented songwriter and singer.

==Relationships==
While her teacher [[Lihala]] was rather hard on her, she was the person who gifted Alfira her [[lute]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Lihala was killed by a pack of [[gnoll]]s while playing music. Alfira stopped playing music for some time, until she began composition of ''[[the Weeping Dawn]]'', dedicated to Lihala's memory.
