'''Fossil''' was a remnant of an [[Angel (astral)|angel]] in service to the [[deity|god]] [[Cyric]]. In [[1479 DR]], he existed in the form of a sentient mask.{{Cite digital book/Spinner of Lies|1054}} He recalled the spirit of [[Madri]] from the dead and manipulated her into plotting against the [[Deva (aasimar)|deva]] [[Demascus]].<ref>{{Cite digital book/Spinner of Lies|}}

==Description==
Fossil appeared to be a plain mask. As an angel, he was 8 feet (2.4 meters) tall and had black wings.{{Cite digital book/Spinner of Lies|4121}}
