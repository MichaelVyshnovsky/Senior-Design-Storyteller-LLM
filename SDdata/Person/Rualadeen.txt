Rualadeen was a Netherese fighter and weaponsmith who owned Dundadens Armory in Karsus Enclave until its destruction in .
Rualadeen was considered the best weaponsmith in Karsus, and had a special talent for making products that resisted rust, corrosion, and magical damage. He employed Sleda and Cheron to help meet the demand for his blades, but made sure to be involved in every weapon made so as to ensure the quality.
