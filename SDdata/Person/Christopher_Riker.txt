'''Christopher Riker''', also known as '''Christopher Koart''',<ref group="note">It is not clear if the use of both "Riker" and "Koart" in ''[[Dragon Magazine]]'' [[Dragon magazine 362|Issue #362]]: "Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes" is intentional or the result of a mistake. was regent of [[Corwell]] during the reign of [[Cymrych Hugh]], the first High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]], in the first half of the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He had a son, [[Cedric Riker]], who would go on to succeed him as regent of Corwell.

==History==
Christopher was a trusted champion of High King Cymrych Hugh.{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|26}} he appointed Christopher as regent in Corwell to steward the rule of the [[Hugh Dynasty]].

When High King Cymrych ultimately succumbed to his injuries in the {{YearlinkName|250}}, he was succeeded by his thirteen year old son, [[Warren Cymrych|Warren]], who established the [[Cymrych Dynasty]] of [[Callidyrr]]. With such a weak heir to the throne of High King, the lords of the [[Ffolk]] began to squabble amongst each other. At this time, Christopher's regency ended and he was succeeded by his own son, Cedric.

Christopher passed away six years later in the {{YearlinkName|256}}.
