Korona Caramitru was a minor noble in Mulmaster in 1491 DR.
Korona was a dour person to the point of being puritanical. Zora Korona had no time for frivolity.
She knew her husband and her son had telepathic powers.
Korona was the wife of Julios Caramitru, a minor noble working in the citys bureaucracy.
In 1491 DR, she was interrogated about her son Thylius Caramitru.
