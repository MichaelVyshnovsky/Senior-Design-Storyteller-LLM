Wulgreda was a prisoner of the Cult of the Black Earth at the Temple of the Black Earth in Tyar-Besil during the Elemental Evil crisis.
In 1491 DR, Wulgreda was prospecting the hills near the Sacred Stone Monastery when she was captured by Black Earth cultists. She remained in the Temple of the Black Earths jail until some adventurers defeated the jailer cultists and freed her.
