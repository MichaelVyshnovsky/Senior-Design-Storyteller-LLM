Worden Ironfist was a dwarven warrior.
Worden was a muscular dwarf, with tan skin and a brown beard with no mustache.
He was highly regarded by his clan, who revered him as a hero.
Worden wore a great helm and a magical suit of field plate armor. He owned a horn of Valhalla, a hammer +3 dwarven thrower, and a ring of earth elemental command.
He was a cousin of Tibold Hillmover.
Worden was the leader of an adventuring band consisting of Udall Granitecrusher, Vasos Flameslayer, and his cousin Tibold. On one mission, the band sought to eliminate a tribe of giants who encroached on their lands.
