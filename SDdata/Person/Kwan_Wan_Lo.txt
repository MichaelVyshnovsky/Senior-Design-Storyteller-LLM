'''Kwan Wan Lo''' was a [[kensai]] participant at the [[Komite]] in [[Shou Lung]].

==History==
Kwan Wan Lo was an aged and revered kensai who had devoted all his life to martial arts. Around [[1328 DR]], he attained the title of grandmaster of the elegant and ancient style of [[Norayu]]. In [[1358 DR]], Kwan Wan Lo went to participate at the Komite, fulfilling a lifelong dream.
