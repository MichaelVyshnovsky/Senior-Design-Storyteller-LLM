'''Iraun Thelder''' was a one-eyed mercenary at the [[Swinging Sword]] tavern in [[Red Larch]] and secretly a member of the [[Cult of the Howling Hatred]] during the [[Elemental Evil]] crisis.

==Activities==
Iraun worked as a guard, and stayed at the Swinging Sword tavern when he had no work. He reported to fellow cultists all that happened during his work.

==History==
In [[1491 DR]], Iraun was at the Swinging Sword tavern when some adventurers were hired to find a diplomatic caravan that had gone missing near the city. They probably uncovered him.
