'''Artreth Obarskyr''', a son of King [[Ortolar Obarskyr|Ortolar]], he was the 39th [[Obarskyr]] monarch and one of the three "boy kings" of [[Cormyr]] while [[Rathdar Orlenthar]] ruled as regent. He died in the [[Purplethroat Plague]].
