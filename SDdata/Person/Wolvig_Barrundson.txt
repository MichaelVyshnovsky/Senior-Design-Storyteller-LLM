'''Wolvig Barrundson''' was a Chieftain within the [[Reghedmen]] [[Tribe of the Bear]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Some time around the {{YearlinkName|1485}}, Wolvig fell under the influence of the being known as the [[Ice Witch]]. Unfortunately, this alliance led to Wolvig's eventual demise.

After his death, [[Günvald Halraggson]] remained as the only Chieftain of the Tribe of the Bear and became the new Bear King.
