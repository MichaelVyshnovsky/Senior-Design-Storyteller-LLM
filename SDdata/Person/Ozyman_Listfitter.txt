'''Ozyman Listfitter''' was a [[halfling]] [[druid]] and a member of the [[adventuring company]] known as [[Halfling Inc.]]

==Abilities==
As a druid, Ozyman often chose as his "mammal form" to take the shape of a [[human]], appearing as a hulking [[Northern]] [[barbarian]].
