'''Delacrae Gentleblossom''' was a sage and shopkeeper in [[Phlan]] in [[1489 DR]].

==History==
Delacrae ran a shop in Phlan called [[All Questions Answered]], where she used her sage knowledge to answer customers' questions. She was consulted about a suspect [[dracolich]] or [[ghost ship]]'s attack but she had no answers in this case.{{Cite adventurers league/Shadows over the Moonsea|9}}
