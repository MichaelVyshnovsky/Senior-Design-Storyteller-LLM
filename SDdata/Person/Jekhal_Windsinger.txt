'''Jekhal Windsinger''' was a [[priest]] of [[Torm]] that served as the nawab of [[Orpher]] in the nation of [[Durpar]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was a strong leader and served his people well by diversifying their economic investments.

==Activities==
In addition to his role as leader of Orpher, Jekhal was the head of the [[Trollslayers]], an adventuring company that acted as one of the city's merchant chakas.

==Relationships==
Unfortunately for Jekhal, many of his fellow Trollslayers did not agree with the way he ran the city or the company.
