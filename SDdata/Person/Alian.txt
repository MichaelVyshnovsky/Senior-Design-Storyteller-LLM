Alian was a dwarf innkeep inhabiting the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Alian was the owner of the Dragonback Inn. The inn catered mostly to Daggerfords farmers, who spent months at a time tending their crops. 
Alian enjoyed the life of an adventurer before retiring and buying the Dragonback Inn.
Alian lived on the second floor of the Dragonback Inn with his girlfriend and a pet dire wolf hybrid named Sheeba.
