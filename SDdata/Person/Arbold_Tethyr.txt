Arbold Tethyr was a wainwright and businessman who lived in the village of Eveningstar during the late 14 century DR.
He was quite fat and greedy.
Arbold ran the Old Boot, a wagon repair and harness shop that was located near the center of Eveningstar across the street from the Market Square. He also owned Tethyr Hardware and Tethyrs Court, but let his three daughters run the store while his wife ran the rooming house.
