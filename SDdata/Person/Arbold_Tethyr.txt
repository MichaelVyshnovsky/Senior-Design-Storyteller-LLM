'''Arbold Tethyr''' was a wainwright and businessman who lived in the village of [[Eveningstar]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
He was quite fat and greedy.

==Activities==
Arbold ran the [[Old Boot]], a wagon repair and harness shop that was located near the center of Eveningstar across the street from the [[Market Square (Eveningstar)|Market Square]]. He also owned [[Tethyr Hardware]] and [[Tethyr's Court]], but let his three daughters run the store while his wife ran the rooming house.
