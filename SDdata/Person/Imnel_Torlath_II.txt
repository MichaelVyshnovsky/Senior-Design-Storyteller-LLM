King '''Imnel Torlath II''' was an early ruler of [[Amn]] of the [[Torlath]] dynasty.

==Relationships==
Imnel was the first son of King [[Rhadik Torlath I|Rhadik I]], and the uncle of [[Corl Torlath II|Corl II]].
