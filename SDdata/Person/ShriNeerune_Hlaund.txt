ShriNeerune Hlaund was the matron mother of House Hlaund of Ched Nasad.
ShriNeerune was a competent priestess of Lolth, but was the weakest on the council.
In 1370 DR, ShriNeerune had a seat on Ched Nasads ruling council as the twelfth and final matron mother.
