Tracker Kirz was a member of the cult of the Absolute who dwelled within the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.
Kirz had mottled green and yellow skin. She wore a crude leather harness, loincloth, and a cloth-and-leather hooded mask.
She carried with her a crude wooden bow.
Kirz held great respect for her fellow cultist Crusher.
In the , Kirz was among the Absolutes raiders celebrating their successful raid on the Waukeens Rest inn.
