Murbryn was a warrior for the Cult of the Dragon in the mid-14 century DR.
Thoadrin and Curthryn were aware of his name.
In late , Thoadrin led a force of Cult of the Dragon warriors to harry Orthil Voldovans caravan, aiming to capture one of her passengers, Shandril Shessair. During one of their ambushes, his underling Curthryn related that Murbryn may have been one of the dead.
