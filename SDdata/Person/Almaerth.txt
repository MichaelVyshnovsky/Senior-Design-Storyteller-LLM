'''Almaerth''' was a merchant from [[Calaunt]] in [[the Vast]], who dealt in rugs and textiles.

Around [[1370 DR]], Almaerth was known to be linked to the [[Viper Ring]], a secret society based in [[Ravens Bluff]].
