Beulla Sandfoot was a criminal who worked against the infamous Guild in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
She was a hin woman with a fair skin tone, blue eyes, and blonde hair. She wore simple garb consisting of a navy blue jacket, brown trousers, and a light blouse.
In the , Beulla could be found with her co-conspirator, the half-orc Fyrd Nuugh in the Lower City near one of the docks. Their meeting place was a seaside gazebo, where they discussed the Guild and Nine-Fingers Keene while pretending to simply discuss the weather if unwelcome listeners were near.
