'''Bilmam Amziz''' was a renowned shipbuilder and [[architect]] who lived in [[Imdolphyn]] as of [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Quench the Fire of the Raging God|15}}

==Activities==
Bilmam was a respected shipbuilder across the [[Windrise Ports]], and was considered the last word in Imdolphian architectural design. He understood the basics of [[magic]] and used such knowledge to augment his craft.

As of 1479 DR, he had nearly a dozen ongoing projects in Imdolphyn.
