Hondryn was a War Wizard of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.
His voice was unfriendly, and thin.
He had a personality much like that of Shuldroon, being overconfident, trigger-happy, and zealous.
He was a war Wizar of Cormyr who often worked with Shuldroon.
He was a toady to Shuldroon, and one of Kelgantors "wolves".
Hondryn was one of the War Wizards sent under Kelgantor for a raid on Tethgard where the fugitive wizard Elminster Aumar was after stealing the Royal Gorget of Battle.
After the battle, he wasnt named among the known survivors.
