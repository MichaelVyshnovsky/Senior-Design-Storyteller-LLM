'''Hondryn''' was a [[War Wizard]] of [[Cormyr]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|''We can end your weariness forever, old man.''|Hondryn, to [[Elminster Aumar]], in the {{yearlinkName|1479}}}}

==Description==
His voice was unfriendly, and thin.

==Personality==
He had a personality much like that of [[Shuldroon]], being overconfident, trigger-happy, and zealous.

==Activities==
He was a [[war Wizar]] of [[Cormyr]] who often worked with Shuldroon.

==Relationships==
He was a toady to Shuldroon, and one of [[Kelgantor]]'s "wolves".

==History==
Hondryn was one of the War Wizards sent under [[Kelgantor]] for a raid on [[Tethgard]] where the fugitive wizard [[Elminster Aumar]] was after stealing the ''[[Royal Gorget of Battle]]''.{{cite book/Elminster Must Die/MMP|33|3}}

After the battle, he wasn't named among the known survivors.
