Ghunayya of the Rose Petal was a human privateer captain living on Masud Jazayir in the Crowded Sea in 1367 DR.
Ghunayya captained a ship known as either the Djinnis Flower or the Floating Rose. She targeted Zakharan ships owned by her employers rivals, stealing the goods and returning them to Bandar al-Saadat where she kept half of the profits.
She was respected among the other inhabitants of Masud Jazayir for spreading the wealth from her ventures.
