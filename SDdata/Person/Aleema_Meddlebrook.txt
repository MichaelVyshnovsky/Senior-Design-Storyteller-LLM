Aleema Meddlebrook was a herbalist from Neverwinter who traveled along the High Road between Neverwinter and Luskan during the late 15 century DR. 
Aleema had deep tanned skin and dark brown hair.
Aleema was old friends with Miranda, the occult supplier in Luskan.
Some time around the , Aleema joined the merchants caravan of Tollin and his crew going from Neverwinter to Luskan, accompanied by members of the Order of the Burning Dawn. The trip was somewhat of a disaster, as several members of their group went missing, and members of the caravans group were attacked by mercenaries, an assault that was set up by the treacherous Martumal.
