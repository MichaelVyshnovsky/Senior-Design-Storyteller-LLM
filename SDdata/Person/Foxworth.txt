'''Foxworth''' was a moneylender in [[Freedale]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He always claimed to be a busy man, and wanted little to do with other people beyond his business.

==Description==
He was short, and looked slimy, with a high-pitched voice.

==Activities==
Foxworth was a moneylender and moneychanger operating out of his house in Freedale.

==Relationships==
He lived alone.

==Possessions==
He hid his money trove somewhere in his house where no one would ever find it.
