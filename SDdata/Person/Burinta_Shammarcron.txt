'''Burinta Shammarcron''' served as an Overcaptain in the [[City Guard of Saerloon|city guard]] of [[Saerloon]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Activities==
Burinta was one of two appointed Overcaptains in the city. She aided Lady [[Merelith]] with running the guard.
