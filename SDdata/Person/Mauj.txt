'''Mauj''' was a [[reef giant]] living on the various lands of the [[Great Sea]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Mauj was short for his race standing {{SI|15|ft}} tall. He had the look of a terrifying monster which contradicted his intelligent and wise nature.

==Activities==
Mauj worked as an enforcer for the moneylenders of [[Gana]]. He preferred to solve problems without bloodshed.
