Loki was an old gluttonous tomcat that belonged to Firtha Kerdos of Targos in Icewind Dale in the early 14 century DR.
Loki was one of the many cats that lived with the elderly foul-tempered blind woman Firtha Kerdos in the dock district of the town of Targos. Shortly before the town became menaced by goblin raids orchestrated by the Legion of the Chimera, circa Tarsakh of the , Loki went missing. The tomcat had made his way into one of the warehouses of the Gallaway Trading Coster where he went hunting for mice. The cat consumed over a dozen rodents, twice his weight, and died of overeating. According to his mistress Firtha, he died happy.
