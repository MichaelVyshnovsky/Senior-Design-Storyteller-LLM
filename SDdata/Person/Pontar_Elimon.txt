'''Pontar Elimon''' was a sage in [[Arrabar]] around the year [[1370 DR]]. He was known for his tedious, and overly cautious personality.

==Activities==
Pontar sold information to anyone who paid him. He was protected by the [[Zhentarim]] and in exchange he acted as a spy for them in the area. Despite exceptional circumstances, he has cheated death on more than a few occasions.

==Possessions==
Among Pontar's possessions were a ''[[cloak of the bat]]'', and rings of ''[[ring of mind shielding|mind shielding]]'' and ''[[ring of shooting stars|shooting stars]]''.
