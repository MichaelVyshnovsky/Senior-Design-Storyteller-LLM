'''Yulia Strauss''' was a [[human]] armorer for hire from the city of [[Neverwinter]], active in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Yulia was an over-confident virtuoso craftswoman and armorer with a serious demeanor and strong work ethic. Even though she was confident to a fault, her skills and and demeanor ensured her continuous employment. Yulia made a living by selling her armorer's skills to those who could afford them, including adventurers of Neverwinter in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
