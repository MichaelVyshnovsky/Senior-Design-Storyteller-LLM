Princess Sikayah was the eldest daughter of Caliph Hava al-Gatil of Halwa in the Cities of the Heart region of interior Zakhara.
Sikayah was the eldest of triplets. Her sisters were Rajiyah, and Mahabbiyah. They were nearing maturity as of .
The Caliphs Grand Vizier, Zarad, wished to marry her to bolster a future claim on the throne.
