Matron '''Merrigold Hlath''' was the ''de facto'' leader of the noble [[patriar]] [[Hlath (family)|Hlath]] family of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Lord [[Orthagon Hlath]] was already an old man who was the leader of the family in name only. Merrigold was making all financial and social discussions for the family in Orthagon's place.
