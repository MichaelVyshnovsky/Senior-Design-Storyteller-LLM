'''Latovenomer''' was a [[green dragon]] who lived within the [[Great Swamp of Rethild]] in western [[Dambrath]].

==Activities==
Latovenomer had a lair hidden deep beneath the waters of the swamp. He slept for decades at a time before waking to wreak havoc upon the surrounding land.
