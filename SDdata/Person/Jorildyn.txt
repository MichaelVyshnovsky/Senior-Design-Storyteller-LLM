Jorildyn was a half-elf battlemage and a member of the Circle of Reilloch Domayr.
The half-elf had a grey-streaked beard.
In Alturiak 1374 DR, Jorildyn survived the attack on the Tower Reilloch. After fighting alongside elves on many occasions, Jorildyn joined up with Seiveril Miritar in traveling to Faerûn to combat the daemonfey invasion.
In the final battle against the daemonfey at the Vale of Lost Voices Jorildyn tried to kill Malkizid himself but was instead killed by the fallen solar.
