'''Eth Junster''' was a male [[wizard]] who was one of the [[magelord]]s of [[Athalantar]] and was killed by [[Elminster Aumar]].

==History==
Eth was a young promising magelord. However, he was sent to [[Narthil]] in [[234 DR]] to quell a revolt started by Elminster. Eth was one of the first magelords killed by the young wizard.
