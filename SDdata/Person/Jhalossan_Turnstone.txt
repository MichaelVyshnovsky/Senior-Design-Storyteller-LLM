Jhalossan Turnstone was a plumber inhabiting Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Jhalossan worked for the Cellarers & Plumbers Guild during the mid-to-late 14 century DR where he served as the guilds main point of contact. He worked out of the Old Guildhall–their headquarters in the Dock Ward–as well as Turnstone Plumbing and Pipefitting on Belnimbras Street.
His father, Hilithiimm Turnstone, was the guildmaster for the Cellarers & Plumbers Guild.
