Najib al-Samsi was the ruler of Sams Bandar on Jazirat al-Qraidis in the Crowded Sea in 1367 DR.
Najib was a skinny, balding man who had heavily weathered skin from time spent outdoors.
Najib held court once a week to spend time gossiping or to settle any new disputes. The court was attended by fellow captains and friends.
