Tyran the Outlaw was a bandit leader in Teshendale in 1368 DR.
Tyran was a descendant of an old Teshwave family.
Around 1368 DR, he gathered a small army and started to attack Zhentarim caravans with the ultimate goal of freeing his dale. The Zhents put a bounty on his head.
