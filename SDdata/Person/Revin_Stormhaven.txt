'''Revin Stormhaven''' was a captain of the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]] in [[Waterdeep]] in [[1379 DR]].

==History==
In 1379 DR, Revin was investigating the kidnapping of some [[elves]] by the [[Unyielding Sword]] mercenaries hired by the slaver [[Lanador]]. He decided to hire an adventuring party that was casually involved.{{Cite dungeon/206/Eyes on the Ball}}  

==Personality==
Revin had worked with many adventurers and knew well how to motivate them.
