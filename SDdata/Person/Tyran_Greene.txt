'''Tyran Greene''' was a councilor within the [[Dale Council]] in [[High Dale]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He was also an experienced veteran in battle, having served in the [[Pegasus Archery Company]] before his tenure as an official of [[Highcastle]]. In addition, Tyran maintained farmland in the lower slopes of the dale.
