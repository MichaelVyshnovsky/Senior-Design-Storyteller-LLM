Booyagh '''Benta''' was a [[Cult of the Absolute|cultist]] of [[the Absolute]] who served her goddess in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Rhaal tuuch! Grab the meat while it's fresh, mate!''|Booyagh Benta}}

==Possessions==
She wore the crudely-crafted [[leather]]-and-cloth straps, and signature headdress that were often worn by [[goblin]] spellcasters.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Booyagh Benta remained among the Absolute's devotees who were readied to assail the [[Emerald Grove]], under the command of their general, [[True Soul]] [[Minthara Baenre]].
