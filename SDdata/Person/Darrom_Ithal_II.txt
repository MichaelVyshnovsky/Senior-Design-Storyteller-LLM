'''Darrom Ithal II''' was the first crown prince of the kingdom of [[Tethyr]].

==Relationships==
Darrom was the son of King [[Darrom Ithal]] and Queen [[Saraala Ithal|Saraala]]. He was the older brother of [[Silvam Ithal]] and had a younger sister as well.

==History==
Prince Darrom was born shortly after the marriage of his parents in {{Yearlink|-211}}. Sadly, the kingdom's first prince and heir to the throne of the [[Ithal Dynasty]] died of a fever in {{Yearlink|-206}}, while not even six years old.
