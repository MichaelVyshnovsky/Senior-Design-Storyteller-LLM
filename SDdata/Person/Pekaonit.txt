Pekaonit was a korobokuru chieftain in the Northern Wastes of Kara-Tur.
Pekaonit inherited his position as chieftain of the village of Karoah but thanks to his violent nature he defeated or drove off all challengers. He led his fellows in constant fighting with other korobukuru tribes, humans, and humanoids in his belief in the survival of the fittest.
