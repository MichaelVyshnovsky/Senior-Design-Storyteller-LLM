Vokarr the Eyebiter was an orc war chief who died sometime before the winter of the .
Exceptional aggression, fury, and the ability to let out rallying battle cries made Vokarr quite dangerous on the battlefield.
Vokarr wielded a greataxe and a spear.
Vokarr was able to command the respect of hunters from the Many-Arrows tribe.
Slain by dwarves sometime prior to 1489 DR, Vokarrs spirit came to be tied to an orc stone that made its way to Icewind Dale. This spirit had to obey the commands of one who was attuned to the stone.
