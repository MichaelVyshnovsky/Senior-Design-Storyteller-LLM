Robyn Kendrick was the ward of the king of Caer Corwell and later High Queen and the Great Druid of the Earthmother in the Moonshae Isles in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Robyn was a beautiful young lady with bright green eyes and thick black hair.
Unlike most ladies on Corwell, Robyn wore a rust-colored cape over her dark green leggings.
Robyn was friends with the halfling Pawldo and felt a strong connection to Tristan Kendrick.
