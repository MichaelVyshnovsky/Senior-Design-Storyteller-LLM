Asavir was an infamous legendary pirate from the Nelanther Isles and the namesake of Asavirs Channel, active in the early 11 century DR.
Asavir commanded a fleet of over a dozen vessels.
In the , Captain Asavir made history by capturing trade cargoes from twelve Amnian merchant families, claiming a seasons worth of commodities from over one hundred ships. This feat was considered the most impressive pirate operation that had ever taken place along the Sword Coast.
Several years later, Asavir and his entire fleet were lost in a deadly storm west of the Nelanther Isles in the Sea of Swords.
