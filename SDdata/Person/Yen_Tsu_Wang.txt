==Activities==
Yen Tsu was the primary confidant and advisor to her husband, [[Li Sung Ming]], the magistrate of Cham Fau.

==Personality==
Yen Tsu was opinionated and clear thinking with a sterling wit and a spirited nature.

==Relationship==
Yen Tsu was the wife of the magistrate of Cham Fau, who truly loved her. She was the older sister of both [[Ju Huo Wang]] and [[An Ching Wang]].
