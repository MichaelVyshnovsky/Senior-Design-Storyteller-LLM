A '''civilar''' was a member of the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]] of [[Waterdeep]], holding a rank equivalent to a lieutenant or captain.{{Cite comic/The Gathering}}

==Activities==
They typically patrolled inside the walls of Waterdeep in groups of four, with two [[Generic City Watch patrolperson (Waterdeep)|patrolpersons]], one [[Generic City Watch sergeant (Waterdeep)|sergeant]], and one captain or lieutenant.

==Possessions==
''Circa'' [[1357 DR]], civilars of the City Watch typically had the standard City Watch kit of [[leather armor|leather]] and [[chain mail|chain armor]], [[short sword]]s, [[dagger]]s, and sturdy rods like [[club]]s.

''Circa'' [[1372 DR]], in addition to their uniform, civilars were outfitted in a ''+1 [[chain shirt]]'', with a steel [[light shield]], and carried a ''+1 [[short sword]]'' and masterwork [[club]]. They also possessed a ''[[potion]] of [[cure light wounds]]'' and an ''[[amulet of the watch]]''.
