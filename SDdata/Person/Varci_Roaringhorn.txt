==Relationships==
While in Baldur's gate, Varci was under the care of Lord Priest [[Tremain Belde'ar]]. He was friends with Tremain's son, [[Casson Belde'ar|Casson]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, Varci and Casson snuck [[Water Queen's House]] to cause some mischief for the [[priest]]esses of [[Umberlee]]. Unfortunately, they were caught and Casson was killed for the transgression. His body was being kept by the the head priestess, [[Jalantha Mistmyr]].

Troubled over the death of his friend, Varci went searching throughout the streets of Baldur's Gate for help to have him ''[[Resurrection (spell)|resurrected]]''.
