'''Othmar bin Jaqal''' was a [[sha'ir]] who lived in [[Mahabba]] of the [[Cities of the Pantheon|Pantheist League]] in [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Relationships==
Othmar bound an [[efreeti]] named [[Zet]] into his service. He belittled Zet at any opportunity and generally treated him poorly. 

==Activities==
In addition to being the caliph's trusted judge, Othmar was also his chief spymaster.

Othmar was known for burning heretics who opposed [[Gods of the Pantheon|the Pantheon]].

==Rumors & Legends==
Othmar was suspected of being affiliated with the [[Brotherhood of the True Flame]]. In truth he was a devoted Pantheist and wanted only to cleanse heresy with purifying flame.
