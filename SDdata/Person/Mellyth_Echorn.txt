Elder star Mellyth Echorn was a cleric of Corellon Larethian and the highest ranking priest of the deity in Evermeet after Seiveril Miritar.
Strongly conservative, Mellyth was nominated to replace Seiveril Miritar on the Council of Evermeet by Selsharra Durothil.
