'''Laranthan''' was a brigand working for the [[Cult of the Dragon]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
He was [[Thoadrin]]'s best warrior.

==History==
In late {{yearlink|1357}}, Laranthan was part of Thoadrin's forces during their harrying of [[Orthil Voldovan]]'s caravan in search of [[Shandril Shessair]], attempting to capture her for her [[spellfire]]. His duties included scouting ahead,{{cite book/Hand of Fire/TPB 2002|208-209|13}}

After the ambush went wrong, he survived, and joined [[Thoadrin]] in their trip towards [[Waterdeep]] afterwards.{{cite book/Hand of Fire/TPB 2002|254-255|15}}
