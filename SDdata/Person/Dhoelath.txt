'''Dhoelath''' was an [[elven high mage]] who lived in his tower, [[Dhoelath's Tower]], in [[Leuthilspar]] on [[Evermeet]].

==Personality==
The [[wizard]] was reclusive, but had been known to take on an apprentice.

==History==
Around [[1367 DR]], Dhoelath was an important defender of Leuthilspar.
