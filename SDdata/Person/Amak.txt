'''Amak''' was an [[efreeti]] guard in the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]] in [[1373 DR]].

==History==
Amak was a guard at a mine run by [[Hafiz al-Milhab]] in the Elemental Plane of Fire, where efreet put enslaved [[azer]]s to work. In 1373 DR, the mine was attacked by [[Kaanyr Vhok]] and his allies, who had forged an agreement with azer leaders. Kaanyr questioned Amak about the mine's defenses and in exchange for information spared his life.
