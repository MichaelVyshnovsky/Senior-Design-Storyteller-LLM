'''Brakkal''' was a follower of [[Maglubiyet]] who lived on the [[Sword Coast]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|Praise his ever-bleedin' axe, praise his throne of flamin' iron. Praise Maglubiyet!|Brakkal.}}

==Description==
He decorated his face with brown face paint. He wore a simple cloth tunic, the collar of which was decorated with large feathers.

==Personality==
Brakkal's faith in his god was strong, and he refused to be swayed by other religions, no matter their growing popularity. He believed that gods had the duty to keep their followers in line, and keep them from only giving in to their baser instincts.

==History==
Brakkal was a follower of Maglubiyet since he was very young.

In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Brakkal encountered [[Cult of the Absolute|cultists]] of [[the Absolute]], but refused to join the growing movement. For his obstinance, he was taken captive and imprisoned within the [[Shattered Sanctum]].
