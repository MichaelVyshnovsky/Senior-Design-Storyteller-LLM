'''Bez''' was a [[goblin]] [[warrior]] and follower of [[the Absolute]] that lived in the [[Shattered Sanctum]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|If you're headin' down tot he spiders, give 'em a tickle.|Bez.}}

==Personality==
She had a particular fondness and interest in the [[giant spider]]s that [[Dror Ragzlin]] kept in the depths of the Shattered Sanctum.

==History==
Bez was present at the [[Shattered Sanctum]] in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, when the leaders of the Shattered Sanctum began searching for individuals aboard a crashed [[nautiloid]] they believed were [[True Soul]]s of their goddess.
