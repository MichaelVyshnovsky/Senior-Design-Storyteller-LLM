'''Shan''' was a [[Netherese]] [[arcanist]] and [[spell]] inventor who lived during the [[Shadowed Age]].

==History==
Shan developed three spells of the [[inventive]] during their life: ''[[web|Shan's web]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|3205|NY}}, ''[[grease|Shan's tallow]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|3215|NY}}, and finally, ''[[wall of force|Shan's forcewall]]'' in {{YearlinkConvert|3235|NY}}.
