Lord Whitburn was the husband of Lady Whitburn, and a member of a patriar family of Baldurs Gate in the 15 century DR.
Lord Whitburn was a fair-skinned bespectacled human man with light chestnut hair and brown eyes. He wore quality firebird-embroidered suits of teal finery.
The elfin woman known as Lady Whitburn married into the Whitburn patriar family sometime before the late 15 century DR.
