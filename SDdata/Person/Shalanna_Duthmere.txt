'''Shalanna Duthmere''' was an innkeep inhabiting the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
She had pale skin and had a generally "worn" appearance.

==Activities==
Shalanna owned [[the Splintered Stair]], a decent [[inn]] located on [[Gut Alley]] in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Relationships==
She was a widow with six daughters.
