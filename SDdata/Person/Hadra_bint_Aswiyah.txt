Hadra bint Aswiyah was a princess of of Muluk in 1367 DR.
In addition to being the royal heir of Muluk, Hadra was an accomplished shair who held the rank of Sorcerer-General in Muluks army. She led successful attacks against both Umara and Qadib during her time in the army.
She commanded the cavalry of Muluk during battles.
Hadra was the only daughter of Muluks Caliph, Aswiyah al-Muftahir.
