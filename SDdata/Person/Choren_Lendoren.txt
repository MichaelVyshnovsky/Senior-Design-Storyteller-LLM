Choren Lendoren was a tavern owner of the Dizzy Drake in Skullport.
Around 1370 DR, Choren started to drink with his customers. The reason was simple Nhyris DHothek had made his lair in his basement and forced Choren to assist him, by luring some of his patrons down to the lich. He obeyed out of fear.
