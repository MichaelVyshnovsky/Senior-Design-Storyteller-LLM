Togar Ironseth was a human blacksmith in Amnwater, Amn, around 1368 DR, and a member of the Ironseth family, owners of about half of the land around Amnwater. 
The large dark skinned smith learned his trade from a dwarven blacksmith.
Togars father was Lord Ghaark Ironseth. Togar was best friends with Lord Jhanos Yhliivast.
