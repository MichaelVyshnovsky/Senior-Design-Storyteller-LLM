'''Dalfred Noakes''' was a [[halfling]] innkeeper in [[Menzoberranzan]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
''Circa'' [[1486 DR]], Dalfred ran the [[Narbondel's Shadow]], an inn and the "finest rooming house in Menzoberranzan" in [[Eastmyr]] district. He also opposed the [[drow]] government by smuggling goods and hiding wanted people, after an altercation with a young noble of [[House Hunzrin]] left him greatly scarred.

==Relationship==
Dalfred adopted the [[half-drow]] [[Shinzi]] and used her as one of his spies.
