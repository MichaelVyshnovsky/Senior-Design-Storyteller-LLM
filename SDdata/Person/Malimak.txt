Malimak was a whimsical mage who lived some years before the late 14 century DR.
Malimak was known for creating a wand of wonder to be presented as a prank gift to his rival. It was unknown if anything harmful came out of it, but Malimak quickly relocated soon after the wand of wonder was gifted.
