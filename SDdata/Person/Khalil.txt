Khalil was a half-elf shair living on the various lands of the Great Sea in 1367 DR.
Khalil owned a ship that was in a poor state of repair. Along with his rubban and crew, he sailed to dangerous destinations.
Khalil carried a jambiya +2, various magical scrolls, a ring of mind shielding, a ring of water walking, a scarab of deception, and a pearl of the sirines.
Khalils bound gen was a maridan.
