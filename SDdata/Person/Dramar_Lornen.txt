Dramar Lornen was a collector from the "Royal Keep" of Ironhar in Eskorn, Laerakond.
Dramar was obsessed with the Dawn Titans Spire, and, as of 1479 DR, she had hired many adventurers and agents in all of Laerakond to investigate any clues that would allow Dramar to gain entry to the Spire.
