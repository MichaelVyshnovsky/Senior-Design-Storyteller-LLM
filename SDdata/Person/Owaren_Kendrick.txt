'''Owaren Kendrick''' was the youngest son of High King [[Derid Kendrick]] of the [[Moonshae Isles]] in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|13}}

==Activities==
Owaren was kidnapped by Lady [[Erliza Daressin]] and taken to the island of [[Snowdown]] were he was held as a hostage to ensure that High King Derid did not try to wrest control of Snowdown from her and her [[Amn]]ian allies. He was held within [[Caer Westphal]].
