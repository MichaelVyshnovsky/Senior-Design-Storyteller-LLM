'''Dannal Harftevor''' was a [[half-elf]] male cavalry officer of the [[Amn]]ian [[Council Army]] in [[Eshpurta]], in the early {{YearlinkName|1370}}.

==History==
Originally from [[Tethyr]], Dannal joined the Council Army Cavalry and rose to the rank of lieutenant. Due to the treatment of [[arcane spellcaster]]s in Amn, Dannal kept his [[wizard]]ly skills a closely guarded secret.
