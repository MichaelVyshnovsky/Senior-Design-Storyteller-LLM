Zelderan Guthel was a grocer inhabiting Waterdeep, the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Zelderan was the guildmaster for the Council of Farmer-Grocerscirca 1357 DR. He held the title of "Master Provisioner" within the guild and worked out of the Market Hall, which was their headquarters on Traders Way in the Castle Ward.
