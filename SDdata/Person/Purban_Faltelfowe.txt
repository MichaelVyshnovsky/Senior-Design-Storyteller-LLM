'''Purban Faltelfowe''' was a member of the [[Shadowmasters of Telflamm|Shadowmasters]] thieves' guild during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

While the guild primarily operated out of the city of [[Telflamm]], they maintained operations in a number of other cities in the east. Faltelfowe led the chapter located within the [[Thay]]an city of [[Escalant]] ''circa'' [[1372 DR]].
