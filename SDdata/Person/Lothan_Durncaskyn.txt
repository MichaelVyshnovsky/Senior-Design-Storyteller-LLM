Lothan Durncaskyn was a lord of Cormyr.
Lothan was the lord of Immerford in the Thunder Peaks on the eastern border of Cormyr. He was also the liaison between Castle Irlingstar and the king.
Lothan was a veteran of the Purple Dragons that had often proved his loyalty and his ability..
In 1480 DR, Lothan was informed of the death of seneschal Marthin Avathnar inside Castle Irlingstar but he had no War Wizards to send as reinforcements. Plus, he had three other troubles in his land a group of outlaws preying upon caravans, an unseen thief, and a possible crazy wizard in Hullack Forest.
Some time later, Mirt and the tax collector Rensharra Ironstave asked for his help and protection.
At last, Storm Silverhand and some of her new Harper agents delivered to Lord Durncaskyn all he needed to clear the region of it problems.
Lothan had the absolute trust of King Foril and of Crown Prince Irvel..
