Murkolk Szolt  was the main duergar delegate in Mantol-Derith in 1372 DR.
Murkolk become the representative of Gracklstugh in 1371 DR after his predecessor Gradoc Rockhand was thrown into the Darklake for his use of a thought medallion.
Murkolk had a poker face, slept only four hours a night and hid his emotions very well.
