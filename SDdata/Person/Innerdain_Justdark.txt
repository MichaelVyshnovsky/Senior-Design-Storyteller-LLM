'''Innerdain Justdark''' was a [[Half-dragon|half-silver dragon]] [[paladin]] of [[Bahamut]] and a member of the [[Talons of Justice]].

==History==
In [[1374 DR]], Innerdain was the first half-dragon to be accepted into the ranks of the Talons. This event allowed other half-dragons to become members of the order.
