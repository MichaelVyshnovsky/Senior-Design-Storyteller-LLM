'''Questele''' was a [[moon elf]] [[mage]] and member of the [[Moonstars]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1486}}, Questele's duty was to protect the young [[Chosen]] of [[Lathander]], [[Stedd Whitehorn]]. However, she was killed by the reaver captain [[Anton Marivaldi]], who tried to collect the bounty on Stedd.
