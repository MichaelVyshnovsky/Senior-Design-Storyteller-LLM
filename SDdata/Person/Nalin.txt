'''Nalin''' was a priest of [[Helm]] who lived in the town of [[Nashkel]] during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Template:Cite game/Baldur's Gate}}

==Personality==
Nalin was a very understanding man. He interpreted Helm's dogma as justice being an ideal that could be found through penance rather than punishment.

==Activities==
He served at the town's local [[Temple of Helm (Nashkel)|temple]], offering healing services and certain divine items for sale.

==Possessions==
Nalin wore a suit of [[plate mail]] armor and carried a ''+1 [[item enchantment|enchanted]] [[mace]]''.

==Relationships==
He thought highly of [[Brage|Captain Brage]], the leader of the town's guardsmen.
