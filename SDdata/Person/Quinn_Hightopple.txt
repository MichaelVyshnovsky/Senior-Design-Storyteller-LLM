'''Quinn Hightopple''' was a [[halfling]] [[warrior]] who lived in the region of the [[Sword Coast North]] near [[Phandalin]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality== 
Quinn valued his friendships and exhibited great loyalty to his friends.

He had an indomitable spirit, believing that no challenge could prove too difficult for him to beat.

==Abilities==
The upbeat halfling had great pride in his skill as a hand-to-hand combatant. He preferred a move he dubbed the "Halfling Hello", that involved him punching a larger person in the groin.
