Shert was one of the horses that belonged to the noble Pharaboldi family of Arrabar circa 1373 DR.
Shert was a bigger-sized steed with a bay coat, a reddish-brown color.
Shert, along with other Pharaboldi horses were cared for by Turcan, the familys stablemaster. In the , Shert was the favorite mount of Pharaboldis young boarish scion Denrick Pharaboldi. He rode the horse on Tarsakh 10, during the visit from the unwilling object of his affections - Emriana Matrell. The couple planned on taking a horseback ride that culminated in a picnic.
