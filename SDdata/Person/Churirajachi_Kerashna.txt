Churirajachi Kerashna was a dragonborn member of Clan Churirajachi, and a member of the Lance Defenders in 1487 DR.
Kerashna was the daughter of Versengethor. Her giant bat was named Ishaniki.
Kerashna was one of the Lance Defenders that survived the siege of the advance military operations during the First Tymanther-Unther War. During this operation, she teamed up with Amurri.
