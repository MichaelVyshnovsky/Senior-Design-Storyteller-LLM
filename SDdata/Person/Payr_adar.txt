Payradar was a puppet member of Zhentil Keeps Council of Lords in 1368 DR.
Payradar hailed from Chessenta and arrived in Zhentil Keep with a caravan full of exotic women at his service. Quickly, he started a very lucrative festhall and with mutual vote Payradar was elected to the Council. Secretly he was a member of the Zhentarim.
He was one of the few lords to survive the 1368 DR assault on the Keep by Cyrics monstrous army
Payradars estate was located in the north section of Zhentil Keeps Inner Quarter.
