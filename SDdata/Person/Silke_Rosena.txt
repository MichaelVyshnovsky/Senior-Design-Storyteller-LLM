Silke Rosena was a talented musician and entertainer living in Beregost as of the late 1360s DR.
She was a musician in Beregost but she usually used her talents to manipulate others into doing her will.
Silke had a lover and "fiance" in her fellow entertainer and bard, Garrick.
At one point in the , she tricked Glayde, Faltis, and Tessilan in a deal that favored herself over them. They realized she had cheated them and began searching for her throughout the city. While she and Garrick stayed at Feldeposts Inn, she hoped to hire some adventurers to deal with the men she had cheated.
