'''Urphaer''' was an old [[orc]] commander in [[Many-Arrows]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
 
==Activities==
Urphaer was the lieutenant of King [[Obould XVII]] and the leader of the sentries at the pass between Many-Arrows and [[Luruar]].
 
==History==
Just after the start of the [[Darkening]] in [[1484 DR]], Urphaer agreed to welcome into the kingdom the [[half-orc]] [[Rhupp]] and the adventuring party that escorted him in hopes of stopping the war.
