Gorrunk was a bugbear raider during the Tyranny of Dragons.
Dressed in hide armor, Gorrunk fought with a morning star and shield in battle.
Gorrunk became leader of the Foulwater goblin tribe. In 1489 DR, he led them in a series of raids on farms near Phlan under the orders from a a mysterious patron, who suggested they use the lair of a black dragon recently killed by adventurers as a base. Gorrunk captured many prisoners.
