Phaeryl was a Netherese sorceress.
To Shandril Shessairs knowledge, she bred dragons.
Phaeryl created Haunted House in Eveningstar, presumably during the times of Netheril.
Lord Tessaril Winter told Shandril in  that Phaeryl was the creator of the Haunted House in Eveningstar after the latter explored it for a bit.
