Hinnia Fernshadow was a member of the Elves of Cormanthor in the late 14 century DR.
She had long brown hair and pale eyes. She wore a chain shirt and carried an elaborate staff.
In the , Hinnia joined the Elves of Cormanthor to help reclaim Myth Drannor at their camp near the city. She spent her time coordinating different efforts in the ruins, largely focused on rescuing the lost Eilistraees Wanderers and counter-attacking the Red Wizards of Droic Orgul.
