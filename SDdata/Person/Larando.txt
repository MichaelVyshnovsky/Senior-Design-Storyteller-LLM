==Relationships==
He was an adventurer, in a group that also included [[Klargathan Srior]] and [[Ardelnar Trethran]]; Ardelnar in particular was a friend of his.

==History==
In the {{yearlinkName|760}}, Larando was part of a group of three different parties that allied together and adventured into [[Myth Drannor]], with seven mages amongst them, only to find themselves exterminated by [[baatezu]]. Larando himself was caught by several [[barbazu]], who tore his limbs off with their weapons, and he was ultimately decapitated by an [[erinyes]]. His companions ran on.
