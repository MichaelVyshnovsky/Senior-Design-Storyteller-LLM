Devout Cry was a lesser priestess of the Absolute that lived in the cults fortress known as the Shattered Sanctum during the late 15 century DR.
She had a more calm and almost understanding demeanor, which stood in stark contrast to the other goblin cultists.
Cry was present at Dror Ragzlins ritual, when the hobgoblin warlord. attempted to interrogate a dead mind flayer brought back to the Shattered Sanctum in the . The aberrant creature was aboard a nautiloid ship that crashed near the River Chionthar, a vessel that carried an item of great importance to the Absolute.
