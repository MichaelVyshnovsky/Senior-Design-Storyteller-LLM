Lord '''Gilbert Irlentree''' was a [[patriar]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] sometime in or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Gilbert Irlentree's likeness was immortalized in a series of portraits. He was depicted as a middle-aged [[human]] man with fair skin, dark eyes, graying beard and a large important-looking hat. He was adorned in dark finery and wore a large ruffled collar.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, a portrait of Gilbert Irlentree was kept in the attic of the [[Jannath estate]] of Baldur's Gate. That year, it became possessed by wailing [[poltergeist]]s and tortured spirits.
