Jasrya Ilviiri was a tall female feyri who served Sarya Dlardrageth. Her skin was covered with diamond-shaped scales and she had marilith heritage. She was given control of the houses of Ilviiri, Aelorothi and Dhaorothi against the wood elves of the High Forest, along with Saryas son, Xhalph.
