'''Jasrya Ilviiri''' was a tall female [[fey'ri]] who served [[Sarya Dlardrageth]]. Her skin was covered with diamond-shaped scales and she had [[marilith]] heritage. She was given control of the houses of [[House Ilviiri|Ilviiri]], [[House Aelorothi|Aelorothi]] and [[House Dhaorothi|Dhaorothi]] against the [[wood elf|wood elves]] of the [[High Forest]], along with Sarya's son, [[Xhalph]].
