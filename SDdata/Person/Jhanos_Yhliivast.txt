'''Lord Jhanos Yhliivast''' was a male [[human]] who, as of 1368 DR, was the head of the [[Yhliivast]] family and the most powerful man in [[Amnwater]], [[Amn]]. He was the proprietor of [[The Gilded Spur]] inn and his lover (and hostess at the inn) was [[Krigar]].
