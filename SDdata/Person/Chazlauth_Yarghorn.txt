'''Chazlauth Yarghorn''' was a wizard in [[Waterdeep]] sometime after the [[War of the Silver Marches]].

==Activities==
Chazlauth was a kind of dragon expert. Chazlauth lived in a house in the [[North Ward of Waterdeep]] with a [[silver dragon]] [[wyrmling]] companion, [[Irizzorl]].

==History==
In the late 1480s or early 1490s [[DR]], he suggested to an adventuring party working to stop a [[giant]] uprising to go to speak with [[Claugiyliamatar|Old Gnawbone]] in [[Kryptgarden Forest]].
