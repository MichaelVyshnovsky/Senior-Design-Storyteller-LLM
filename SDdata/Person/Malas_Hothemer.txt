'''Malas Hothemer''' was a [[noble]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]], the City of Splendors, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Malas was a portly fellow.

==Activities==
Given his immense girth, any time he left [[Hothemer Villa]] Malas had to be carted around Waterdeep in a portage chair drawn by a pair of [[ogre]]s.

==Relationships==
He was the patriarch of the [[Hothemer]] [[nobility of Waterdeep|noble family]].
