Orox was a beholder ally of the Orthodox Church of Bane in 1357 DR.
Orox was a beholder supporting the High lmperceptor of Bane of Mulmaster.
In 1357 DR, hidden in a crate, Orox went to Zhentil Keep to help Inquisitor Ginali to kill the non-Orthodox Fzoul Chembryl.
Orox tried to kill Fzoul while the latter was busy with the adventuring party possessing the Azure Bonds but was defeated.
On Uktar 18 of 1369 DR, Mulmaster was attacked by a band of beholders led by Fzoul Chembryl, Desmonda, and Manxam, killing Orox and over half the followers of Cyric in the city.
