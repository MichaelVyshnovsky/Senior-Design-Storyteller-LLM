Ikitipsa was an evil bent priestess in the Pasocada Basin in Maztica.
In 1359 DR, Ikitipsa sent a farastu onto the Sun Trail to stop the Gilded Priest Koyisqatapi, in order to bring down the Azuposi nation.
In 1362 DR, the adventurers of Sozarros expedition defeated the farastu. Later, she conspired with some evil Azuposi and a tribe of ogres to kill the adventurers.
