'''Nightshade''' was the [[elf|elven]] leader of the [[Silent Rain]] mercenary company.

==Description==
Nightshade was reputed to be as beautiful as she was deadly, though her true identity remained a secret.

==Relationships==
She had a personal hatred of [[Jadron the Fang]] and [[The Teeth (mercenaries)|the Teeth]] ogre mercenaries that he led.
