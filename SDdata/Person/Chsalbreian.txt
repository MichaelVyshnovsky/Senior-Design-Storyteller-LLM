Chsalbreian was a Zhentarim mage of the mid-14 century DR.
Chsalbreian was a mage.
Chsalbreian formed part of a squad of Zhentarim mages.
Other mages in the same squad to which Chsalbreian belonged were Arluth and Sundarth.
In , Sundarth reported to Manshoon that a group of mages seeking spellfire in Sembia was attacked, first by Harpers and then by Cult of the Dragon forces. He named Chsalbreian as one of the casualties.
