'''Majira''' was a veteran adventurer who lived in [[Amkethran]] circa the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.

==Description==
She was one to speak up much to those she did not know.

==History==
After a long time adventuring, Majira and Faheed<ref group="note">Faheed and Majira may be a play on [[Khalid]] and [[Jaheira]], two other veteran adventurers. moved to Amkethran to deal with the individual known as [[Balthazar]]. They bought a key to the monk's lair from local smugglers as part of a plan to kill the [[Bhaalspawn]] as he had caused too much chaos in Amkethran for their liking.
