'''Sirule Kalishenko''' was a [[half-elven]] chamberlain inhabiting [[Almorel]] on the [[Lake of Mists]] in the [[Hordelands]] in the {{YearlinkName|1357}}.

==History==
Sirule's heritage was of [[elven]] and [[Raumviran]] descent.

==Activities==
As chamberlain of the [[House of the Dragomen]], Sirule was tasked with meeting the needs of the dignified foreign officials, and the occasional respected caravan-master, who lived there.
