'''Zeerith Q'Xorlarrin''' was the [[matron mother]] of [[House Xorlarrin]] of [[Menzoberranzan]]. and remained in that position as of [[1484 DR]], when House Xorlarrin moved to the city of [[Q'Xorlarrin]].

==Personality==
She was known for her diplomatic skills and other houses often called upon her to mediate in disputes.

==History==
Zeerith moved her house from Menzoberranzan to the new city of Q'Xorlarrin in the {{YearlinkName|1484}}. Her family was deeply involved with the [[Darkening]].

==Relationships==
Zeerith's brother was the mage and later [[lich]] [[Kharza-kzad Xorlarrin]]. Her house patron was the mage [[Horroodissomoth Xorlarrin]] until his death during the [[Spellplague]].

Zeerith had a number of children, including [[Brack'thal Xorlarrin|Brack'thal]] and [[Ravel Xorlarrin]]. Brack'thal was both brother and father to Ravel after Zeerith took his seed while he was semi-comatose.
