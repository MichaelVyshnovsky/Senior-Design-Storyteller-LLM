'''Rathandar''' was a [[War Wizard]] of some rank in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Rathandar had assigned [[Huldyl Rauthur]] to his post at the [[Harbortower]] of [[Marsember]], and reminded him not to try any powerful castings, nor to bring any women to the post, sometime before {{yearlink|1373}}.
