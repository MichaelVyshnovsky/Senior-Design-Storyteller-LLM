'''Gorham Cymrych''' was High King of the [[Moonshae Isles]] at the turn of the 4{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|32}}

==Relationships==
He was a member of the [[Cymrych Dynasty]]  and the grandson of the dynastyâ€™s legendary namesake, [[Cymrych Hugh]]. He was the brother of his predecessor, High King [[Gwylloch Cymrych|Gwylloch]], and his father ruled before that as High King [[Carrig Cymrych I|Carrig I]]. Gorham's successor was his nephew, [[Markus Cymrych]].

==History==
Gorham assumed the throne of the High King in the {{YearlinkName|299}}, after Gwylloch and his first son, [[Ketheryll Cymrych|Ketheryll]], killed each other and their retainers at the [[Castle of Skulls]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|26}}

He ruled for twenty-three years before Gwylloch's second son, Markus, slew him and took the throne in the {{YearlinkName|322}}.
