'''Tolemius Worthywart''' was a [[halfling]] resident of the Realms who lived during the late 14{{th}} or 15{{th}} centuries [[DR]].

{{Quote|Weren't nothing lost, Conn. It's not Nan's lucky gnashers, is it? It sank.|Tomelius, regarding the fate of Luiren.}}

==Personality==
He believed that the halfling ancestral home of [[Luiren]] had sunk beneath the [[Great Sea]].

==History==
While traveling through the [[Fireshear Mountains]], Tomelius and his brother encountered a wandering [[dragonborn]]. The dragonborn was a writer, and recorded the discussion they had regarding the fate of Luiren following the [[Spellplague]].
