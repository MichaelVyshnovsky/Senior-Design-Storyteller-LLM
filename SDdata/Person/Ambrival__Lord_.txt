Lord Ambrival was a nobleman from Cormyr. He was the head of the Ambrival family in the late 15 century DR.
Lord Ambrival was the head of the Ambrival family.
Lord Ambrival was one of the guests at the Council of the Dragon held in the Hall of Justice in Castle Obarskyr during late Mirtul in the . After the meeting degenerated into violence, the Lord was slain by a blueflame ghost that arrived unexpected. She slashed his throat before he could even turn to face her he died within a minute.
After his death, his body among others was taken to the Chapel of the Valiant and recognized his death was confirmed.
