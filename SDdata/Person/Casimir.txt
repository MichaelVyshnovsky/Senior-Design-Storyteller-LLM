Casimir was an evil human wizard.
He had very pale skin, long, white hair, and a matching full beard. Casimir clad himself in a voluminous, high-collared blue robe, over which he wore a cloak made from red fabric and fur. He also wore a red hat ornamented with a horned skull.
In his old age, Casimir grew to become a bitter person.
Casimir owned a necklace of adaptation, a mirror of mental prowess, and a robe of scintillating colors, as well as a ring of protection +5.
As a young wizard, Casimir was of a goodly disposition. After suffering years of defeat and witnessing rampant corruption, he grew to become evil.
Casimir spent most of his time creating evil magical items in his laboratory. He frequently used his mirror of mental prowess to travel to other planes.
