'''Raegl Halgohar''' was a young lord in [[Cormyr]].

==History==
In [[1480 DR]], he decided to join the council of lords gathered by [[Melder Crownrood]] under the orders of [[Manshoon]]. There, Raegl asked about the assassination of [[Rensharra Ironstave]].
