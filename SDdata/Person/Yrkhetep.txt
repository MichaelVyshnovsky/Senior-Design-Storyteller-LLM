'''Yrkhetep''' was an [[arcanaloth]] who tried to conquer [[Chondath]] and [[Turmish]] as part of a plot to usurp the Oinoloth [[Anthraxus]]. He was defeated by a group of adventurers secretly being manipulated by Anthraxus.
