'''Hesken-P'aj Symryvvin''' was the matron of the 18th house of [[Menzoberranzan]] and the most powerful priestess of [[Lolth]] in the city.

==Personality==
The matron and her family was sometimes referred to as "the eyes of Lolth".

==History==
Hesken-P'aj was chosen by the Spider Queen to declare her godly will to the twenty most powerful matrons in the year of [[1361 DR]] after a [[yochlol]] announced that peace should be among the houses.
