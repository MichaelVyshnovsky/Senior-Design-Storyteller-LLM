Hesken-Paj Symryvvin was the matron of the 18th house of Menzoberranzan and the most powerful priestess of Lolth in the city.
The matron and her family was sometimes referred to as "the eyes of Lolth".
Hesken-Paj was chosen by the Spider Queen to declare her godly will to the twenty most powerful matrons in the year of 1361 DR after a yochlol announced that peace should be among the houses.
