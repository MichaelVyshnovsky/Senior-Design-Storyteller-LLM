'''Jedren Hiller''' was a the [[Bailiff of the Wide|Bailiff]] of [[the Wide]] in [[Baldur's Gate]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was a wholly corrupt man who fully expected to be offered bribes, which he readily accepted.

==Activities==
While he carried out his normal duties as bailiff,{{Template:Cite book/Murder in Baldur's Gate/Campaign Guide|12}} Hiller gave preferential market space to nobles of the [[Upper City]], and those who offered more generous inducements.
