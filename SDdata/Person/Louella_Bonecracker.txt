Louella Bonecracker was a burly dwarven bouncer at the Bubbling Bulwark in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. She was responsible for guarding the inner private door which led into Thistleflame Keep, while Dwak guarded the main public door.
Louella rather liked Annalise, one of the barmaids.
