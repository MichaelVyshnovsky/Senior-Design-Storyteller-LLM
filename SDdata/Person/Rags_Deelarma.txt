Rags Deelarma was a begger and a refugee from the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Deelarma was a human woman with a light skin tone and blue eyes. She kept her brown hair cut short.
In the , an army assembled by the Cult of the Absolute moved from the Moonrise Towers in the Chionthar Valley to menace the city of Baldurs Gate, displacing countless refugees in the process along the way. Rags Deelarma was one of the refugees forced from her home. She found shelter in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate at Nortales Hostel. To survive, she begged for coin in front of the Counting House.
