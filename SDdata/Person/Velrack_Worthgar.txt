'''Velrack Worthgar''', also known as '''the Roaring Giant''', was a wealthy investor and retired sea captain from [[Athkatla]] as of the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
By the late 14{{th}} century DR, Velrack Worthgar was long retired and was profiting from an active investing business. He financed privateering ships that targeted vessels on behalf of those who had coin and could afford the illicit activity.
