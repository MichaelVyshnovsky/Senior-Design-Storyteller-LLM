'''Ba'sula Baenre''' was a young member of [[House Baenre]] in [[1486 DR]].

==Activities==
Ba'sula served under [[Sos'Umptu Baenre]] at the [[Fane of the Goddess]] temple.
 
==History==
In 1486 DR, chosen as member of the reformed [[House Do'Urden]], Ba'sula went to inform [[Kiriy Xorlarrin]] about an attack from [[House Hunzrin]] troops. However, she saw Kiriy plotting with members of [[House Melarn (Menzoberranzan)|House Melarn]]. Kiriy killed the young priestess, using a magic that caused great suffering, to impress her new allies.
