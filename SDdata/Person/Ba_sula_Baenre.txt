Basula Baenre was a young member of House Baenre in 1486 DR.
Basula served under SosUmptu Baenre at the Fane of the Goddess temple.
In 1486 DR, chosen as member of the reformed House DoUrden, Basula went to inform Kiriy Xorlarrin about an attack from House Hunzrin troops. However, she saw Kiriy plotting with members of House Melarn. Kiriy killed the young priestess, using a magic that caused great suffering, to impress her new allies.
