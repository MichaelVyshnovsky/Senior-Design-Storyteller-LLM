Lady '''Ialantha Rumithamal''' was the cousin of Queen [[Amlaruil Moonflower]] and was the former leader of the fishing town of [[Nimlith]] on the island of [[Evermeet]]. Before this, she pursued a career as a [[unicorn]]-rider.
