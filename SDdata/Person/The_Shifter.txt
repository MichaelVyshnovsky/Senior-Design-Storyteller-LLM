The Shifter was a shade wizard and member of the Bounty Hunters of Cavus Dun in the mid-to-late 15 century DR.
She was the wizard of the Cavus Dun squad tasked by Effron with finding Dahlia Sinfelle and Drizzt DoUrden in the . The Shifter survived the first skirmish and later attacked the pair of elves again with her fellows and a larger squad of reinforcements in Gauntlgrym. However, the intervention of drow of House Xorlarrin caused the defeat of the Shadovar.
