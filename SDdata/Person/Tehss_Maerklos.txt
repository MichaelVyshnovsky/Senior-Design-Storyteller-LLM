Tehss Maerklos was an Illuskan wizard inhabiting Waterdeep and Undermountain during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Tehss was a student of arcane studies at Halasters Heirs, a school of magic established Trobriand and Muiral who were apt past pupils of Halaster Blackcloak. 
She was a member of the Maerklos noble family of Waterdeep of which she was the heiress.
