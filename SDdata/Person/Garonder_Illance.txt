'''Garonder Illance''' was an old noble in [[Cormyr]] and the lord of [[House Illance]].

==History==
In [[1487 DR]], Garonder gathered with other nobles to discuss the threats facing the kingdom, among them there were also [[Mirt]] and [[Manshoon]].
