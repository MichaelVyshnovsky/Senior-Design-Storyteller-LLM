Slog was a customer of the Blushing Mermaid in the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Slog was a large half-orcish man with a muddy-green hide and dark brown hair.
In the , during the time Captain Grisly was replaced by the green hag Auntie Ethel as the owner of Blushing Mermaid, Slog was often seen drinking on the taverns second floor.
