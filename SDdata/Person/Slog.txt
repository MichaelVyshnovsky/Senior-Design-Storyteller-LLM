'''Slog''' was a customer of the [[Blushing Mermaid]] in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|The hells is the Captain? Why didn't she stop them freaks?|Slog}}

==Description==
Slog was a large [[half-orcish]] man with a muddy-green hide and dark brown hair.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the time [[Captain Grisly]] was replaced by the [[green hag]] [[Auntie Ethel]] as the owner of Blushing Mermaid, Slog was often seen drinking on the tavern's second floor.
