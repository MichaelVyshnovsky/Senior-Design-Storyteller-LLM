'''Aquilan Greatspan''' was the leader of the [[avariel]] inhabiting the [[Aerie of the Snow Eagles]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
An avariel winged elf, Aquilan Greatspan had porcelain white skin, with tinges of blue and faint silver. His eyes were large and expressive.
