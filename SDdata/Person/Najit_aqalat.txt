'''Najit'aqalat''' was a bound [[marid]] who served as harbormaster of [[Hawa]] within the [[Corsair Domains]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Abilities==
Najit'aqalat possesed all of the impressive abilities common to marids.

==Activities==
Najit'aqalat collected a small fee from every ship entering the harbor of [[Chaos Bay]].

==Relationships==
Bound in service by the [[sha'ir]] [[Inayah bint In'am]], his contract was renegotiated every 101 days. Most marids distasted dealing with mortals, but Najit enjoyed the verbal sparring with Inayah.
