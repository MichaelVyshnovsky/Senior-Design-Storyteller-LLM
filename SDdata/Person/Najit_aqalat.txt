Najitaqalat was a bound marid who served as harbormaster of Hawa within the Corsair Domains in 1367 DR.
Najitaqalat possesed all of the impressive abilities common to marids.
Najitaqalat collected a small fee from every ship entering the harbor of Chaos Bay.
Bound in service by the shair Inayah bint Inam, his contract was renegotiated every 101 days. Most marids distasted dealing with mortals, but Najit enjoyed the verbal sparring with Inayah.
