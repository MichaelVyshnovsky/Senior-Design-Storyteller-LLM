'''Nazrym''' was a minor [[cleric]] of [[Bane]] involved in the [[Zhent]] invasion of [[Shadowdale]] in the [[1375 DR|Year of Risen Elfkin, 1375 DR]].

==History==
His main task was under order of the high priest [[Malathon]] to oversee the [[Castle Krag]] reconstruction work on the barbican.
