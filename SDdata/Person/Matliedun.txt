Matliedun was a cambion priest in the Undying Temple in Maerimydra in the Deep Wastes in the late 15 century DR.
Matliedun was responsible for the temple security but under the demon madness of the late 1480s DR he was distracted with gambling.
As of 1486 DR, Matliedun was the master of the Undying Temple and served under Squallocks, the vrock advisor to Grazzt.
When the allied factions attacked Maerimydra, Matliedun led a patrol of drow warriors to reinforce the defenses if the alarm sounded.
Matliedun was a son of the demon lord Grazzt and one of his concubines.
