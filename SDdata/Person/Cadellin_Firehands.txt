==Activities==
''Circa'' {{Yearlink|1358}}, Cadellin was the Lady's Mage at the [[Tower of Mystery]] temple in Saerloon. He was a devout follower of [[Mystra]] and served as the leader of the temple.

==History==
On [[Uktar 29]] of the {{yearlinkName|1374}}, it was suspected that Cadellin and his fellows from the temples of Mystra and of [[Azuth]] used ''[[teleportation circle]]'' to transport the Saerloon army to just outside the rebel city of [[Selgaunt]].
