'''Halgondas''' was a mage known to the [[Srinshee]] in the 3{{rd}} century [[DR]].

==Legacy==
The [[Overmantle of Halgondas]], almost certainly named after them, was in the [[Vault of Ages]] until {{yearlink|241}}, when [[Ilimitar]], the high court mage, destroyed it.
