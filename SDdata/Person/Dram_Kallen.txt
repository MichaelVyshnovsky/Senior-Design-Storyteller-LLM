'''Dram Kallen''' is an [[alchemist]] and inventor of the [[Silver Stake gas bomb]], a device used by the [[Silver Stake]] to subdue and capture [[lycanthropes]]. He is also a founding member of the Silver Stake organization itself. He was born in [[Baldur's Gate]] and has lived there his entire life.{{Cite web/The Search for the Circle of Vehlarr/Part Three}}
