Cefrey was a spellcaster and looter who was active on the Sword Coast region during the late 15 century DR. For a time, she worked under the employ of Gimblebock.
She had long dark hair, wore a simple traveling robe, and carried a quarterstaff.
In the , Cefrey and her fellow looters had been trying to break into a crypt for a few days. They had made some progress, and had encountered skeletons. Not wanting to go home empty-handed yet again, she was determined to get further in.
