Lunasa Eveningwood was an elf bard in Cormyr during the late 15 century DR.
Lunasa had bright orange backswept hair and gray eyes.
Lunasa camped at the ruins of Beckside Castle on the High Road around the time of the Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar. She sang with her companion Merlin Fastfoot, including Heres a Health to the Company, The Mermaid, and The Crafty Maids Policy.
