'''Lunasa Eveningwood''' was an [[elf]] [[bard]] in [[Cormyr]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}
{{quote
|Kind friends and companions, come join me in rhyme,<br/>
come lift up your voices in chorus with mine.<br/>
Let us drink and be merry, all grief to refrain,<br/>
for we may and might never all meet here again.
|Lunasa, singing ''[[Here's a Health to the Company]]''}}
==Description==
Lunasa had bright orange backswept hair and gray eyes.

==History==
Lunasa camped at the ruins of [[Beckside Castle]] on the [[High Road (Cormyr)|High Road]] around the time of the [[Sschindylryn invasion of Eveningstar]]. She sang with her companion [[Merlin Fastfoot]], including ''[[Here's a Health to the Company]]'', ''[[The Mermaid]]'', and ''[[The Crafty Maid's Policy]]''.
