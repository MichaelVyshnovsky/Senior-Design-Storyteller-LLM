Ibron was an illusionist and one of five novice wizards stationed at the enclave run by the young Red Wizard Hinnar.
Ibrons body bore a magical tattoo
Ibron was tough, dexterous, and intelligent and was trained in alchemy, spellcraft, and the scribing of scrolls.
Ibron carried a dagger and a small crossbow.
Ibrons spellbook contained nineteen different spells.
Ibron alternated with the other five novice wizards in running the compounds sales booth with two other companions. When sales were slow, Ibron might offer assistance to the wizards responsible for crafting the magic goods that were for sale.
