'''Sapphiraktar''' was an ancient male [[blue dragon|blue]] [[dracolich]] and a member of the [[Twisted Rune]].

==Description==
Out of vanity, the dracolich maintained his [[dragon]] appearance through magic. 

==Personality==
Preparing to kill [[Jymahna]] for spying, Sapphiraktar taught dragon magic to [[Rhangaun]].

==Activities==
Sapphiraktar made his cavern home in the  ruined city of [[Teshyll]] west of [[Alatorin]] in [[Calimshan]].

==History==
Around [[1072 DR]], Sapphiraktar became a dracolich.
