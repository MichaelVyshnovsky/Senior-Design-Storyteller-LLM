Salene Freig was a broadsheet delivery clerk employed by Baldurs Mouth in the late 15 century DR.
Salene was a human woman with a light tan complexion. The woman had long, dark brown hair, and eyes of the same color. She wore simple garb of a common delivery woman.
In the , Salene worked on managing the delivery of freshly printed Baldurs Mouth Gazette bundles. During the Absolute crisis, she could be often be found within Baldurs Mouths offices in Heapside of the Lower City.
