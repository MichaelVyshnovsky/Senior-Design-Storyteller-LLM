'''Abbey Fargo''' was a soldier in the [[Neverwinter Guard]] who served during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Template:Cite game/Neverwinter}}

==Relationships==
She had a sister named [[Zoey Fargo|Zoey]], who was also a member of [[Neverwinter]]'s guard force.

==History==
Following the [[Siege of Neverwinter]] in the {{yearlinkName|1479}}, Abbey was stationed with her sister in the city's [[Neverwinter/Blacklake District|Blacklake district]].

Abbey was part of a force that stormed the [[Tower of Solace]] in order to rescue [[Dorothea Linkletter]] and stop the evil [[wizard]] [[Rhazzad]]. She fought alongside Zoey and their fellow guardsmen: [[Sergeant Knox]], [[Dell McCourt]], [[Hawthidon Faalwun]], and Dorothea's husband [[Josef Linkletter|Josef]].
