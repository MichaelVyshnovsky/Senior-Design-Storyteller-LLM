'''Peter''' was an adventuring [[fighter]]-[[cleric]] from the city of [[Waterdeep]] in the midâˆ’14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Abilities==
Peter was trained in handling [[sword]]s of all sizes and had access to several divine spells, such as ''[[rigid thinking]]'', ''[[hold person]]'', ''[[spiritual hammer]]'', and ''[[entangle]]''.

==Possessions==
The adventurer was protected by a suit of [[chainmail]], a horned [[helmet]], a [[medium shield]], and was armed with an [[enchanted]] ''+1'' [[morning star]]. He also carried a bottle of ''[[oil of fiery burning]]'' and a vial of ''[[potion of explosions]]''.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, Peter's adventuring party, led by [[Kirian]] visited the area east of [[Beregost]] known as [[Mutamin's Garden]]. There, Peter, Kirian, [[Lindin]], and [[Baerin]] antagonized travelers, fellow adventurers, and "rustic locals," provoking them into combat.
