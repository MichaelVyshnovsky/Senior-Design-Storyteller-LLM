'''Ophinshtalajiir Sepideh''' was a [[dragonborn]] member of [[Clan Ophinshtalajiir]], and a field commander of the [[Lance Defenders]] in [[1486 DR]].

==Description==
Sepideh had silver scales.

==Relationships==
Sepideh was the mother of [[Ophinshtalajiir Rahdia|Rahdia]].

==History==
In her youth, Sepideh was a close friend of [[Mehen]], [[Kepeshkmolik Uadjit|Uadjit]] and [[Verthisathurgiesh Arjhani|Arjhani]] during their service in the Lance Defenders.

In 1486 DR, Sepideh was a field commander of the Lance Defenders, and Clan Ophinshtalajiir's  candidate for the elections to chose the new [[Vanquisher]], that were expected to be celebrated in [[1487 DR]].

Sepideh was killed by a [[maurezhi]] servant of the [[deity|god]]-king [[Gilgeam]], while investigating a series of murders in the [[Catacombs (Djerad Thymar)|Catacombs]] of [[Djerad Thymar]].
