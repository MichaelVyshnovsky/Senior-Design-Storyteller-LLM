Crypt King was an angry undead specter that dwelt within one of the tombs of Athkatlas Grave District in the late 14 century DR.
The creature wore plate armor and an open-faced helmet while wielding an enchanted sword called Namarra.
As of the , Crypt King was a restless and vengeful undead being, woken from his slumber by some unknown individuals. Crypt King was ready to slay anyone unwise enough to either the crypt. It gained an upper hand in battle as it radiated an aura of powerful horror that could rattle and render weak-willed creatures defenseless agains the specter.
