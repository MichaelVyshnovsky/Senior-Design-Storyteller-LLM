'''Brathwen'''  was a [[duergar]] slaver and a [[cult of the Absolute|cultist of the Absolute]] who dwelled [[Grymforge]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{Quote|'Less you're here to kick some stuffs lake-side, I suggest you bugger off.|Brathwen.}}

==Description==
He had dark gray skin and white hair and beard. He wore black padded clothing lined with silver and an [[amulet]] bearing the symbol of [[the Absolute]].

==Possessions==
Brathwen wielded a [[warhammer]] in combat.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Brathwen, [[Viss]] and other duergars of their clan aligned themselves with the cult of the Absolute. After the cult slaughtered and enslaved a community of [[deep gnome]]s, Brathen and Viss were assigned the unfortunate task of dumping their corpses into the waters of the [[Darklake]].
