Untharym Frostanar was the high priest of Darkwave Hall, a temple dedicated to Umberlee, in Urmlaspyr in 1358 DR.
Untharym was the leader of Umberlees faithful in Urmlaspyr. His congregation within the temple consisted of seven specialty priests called waveservants and four followers.
