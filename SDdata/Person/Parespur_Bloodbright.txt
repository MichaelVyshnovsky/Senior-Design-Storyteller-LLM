Lord '''Parespur Bloodbright''' a noble in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Parespur hired the [[coinlass]] [[Amarune Whitewave]] to be his escort for the [[Open Feast]] held in [[Castle Obarskyr]], [[Suzail]], [[Cormyr]], in the {{yearlinkName|1479}}, being quite fastidious about the fact he wanted to be seen with her downright wrapped around him. He also dragged her off from at least one conversation when her interest turned to eavesdropping.{{cite book/Elminster Must Die/MMP|70-71|6}}
