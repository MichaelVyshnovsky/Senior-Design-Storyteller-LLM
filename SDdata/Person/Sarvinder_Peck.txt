'''Sarvinder Peck''' was a groundskeeper and head groom who lived in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. He worked for the [[Vanthampur]] family at their [[Vanthampur Villa|villa]] in the city's [[Temples District]].

He was described as being rather "salty" in demeanor.
