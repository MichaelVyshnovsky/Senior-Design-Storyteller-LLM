'''Unhath''' was a [[marilith]] who was a personal attendant to the [[demon lord]] [[Graz'zt]].

She was a cruel creature who ruled Graz'zt's [[Argent Palace]] when he was away with an iron hand, along with her cohort, [[Reluhantis]].
