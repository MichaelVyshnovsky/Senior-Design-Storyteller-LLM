'''Glyria''' was a Death's Head of [[Bhaal]] who resided in the ruined [[Temple of Bhaal (Undercity)|Temple of Bhaal]] in the [[Undercity]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Gore on the wall... pulp on the hands... head all gone... yes, yes, I killed him. I killed them all.''|Death's Head of Bhaal Glyria}}

==Description==
Glyria was a rusted metal and [[leather]]-clad [[human]] who hid his facial featured under a cowl – uniform of the [[Bhaalist]]s. He had dark brown eyes and his upper body was covered in [[tattoo]]s of designs and skulls.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, during the [[Absolute crisis]], Glyria killed and slaughtered his victims in service of [[Bhaal]], the Lord of Murder and his [[Chosen]] – [[Orin the Red]]. His desire to kill was an overwhelming bloodthirst. Thorough servitude to Orin, Death's Head of Bhaal Glyria supported the [[Cult of the Absolute]] that threatened the city of Baldur's Gate.
