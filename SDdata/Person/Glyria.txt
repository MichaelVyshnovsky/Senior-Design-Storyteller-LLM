Glyria was a Deaths Head of Bhaal who resided in the ruined Temple of Bhaal in the Undercity of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Glyria was a rusted metal and leather-clad human who hid his facial featured under a cowl – uniform of the Bhaalists. He had dark brown eyes and his upper body was covered in tattoos of designs and skulls.
In the , during the Absolute crisis, Glyria killed and slaughtered his victims in service of Bhaal, the Lord of Murder and his Chosen – Orin the Red. His desire to kill was an overwhelming bloodthirst. Thorough servitude to Orin, Deaths Head of Bhaal Glyria supported the Cult of the Absolute that threatened the city of Baldurs Gate.
