'''Helmur Faerrad''' was a jeweler and fine pastry maker in [[Immerford]] in [[Cormyr]].

==History==
In [[1480 DR]], Helmur and other wealthy citizens went to protest to Lord [[Lothan Durncaskyn]] about an unseen thief and murderer.
