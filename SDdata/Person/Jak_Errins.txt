'''Jak Errins''' was a male [[human]] and a [[Battleguard]] of [[Tempus]] with the title of Trusted Sword. He was the seneschal of the [[Towers of the Vengeful Hand]] in [[Eshpurta]], [[Amn]], Jak was getting on in years and two underpriests, Battlelady [[Peris Whallnsor]] and Lance of the Lord [[Niskam Tresdaap]], were contending for his position.
