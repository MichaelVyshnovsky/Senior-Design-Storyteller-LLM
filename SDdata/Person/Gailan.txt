Gailan was the commander of the dwarven Orothiar Clan in Bloodstone Barony in 1357 DR.
Gailan always saw a military solution to all kinds of problems.
In 1357 DR, Gailan and his tribe met Gareth Dragonsbanes party.
In 1359 DR, Gailan was a key member of the Alliance of Belt Watchers with local halflings and centaurs.
