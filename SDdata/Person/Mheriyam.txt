Mheriyam was a mercenary in the late 14 century DR.
Mheriyam was a nervous person.
Her leather armor was patched and worn.
She carried at least two daggers.
She was thought of as a thief. She wielded her two daggers at the same time.
Together with Baergrim and Eltragar, Mheriyam was part of the Blades.
In the , Eltragar, Mhaeriyam, and Baergrim were escorting the half-illithid wizard Mhulker in Undermountain. Mhulker was seeking a particular woman in order to eat her brain he found her chained and gagged at an altar, but it turned out to be a trap, which Baergrim spotted by noticing that the womans gag covered her nose too, meaning she couldnt breathe. After Halaster Blackcloaks trap slew Mhulker and the ancient wizard revealed himself, the mercenaries fled.
