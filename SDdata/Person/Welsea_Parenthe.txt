Welsea Parenthe was a vampire sorcerer and Zhentarim agent in Phlan in 1489 DR.
Welsea normally stuck to the shadows, but when forced by circumstances to go into the open, she tried to conceal her features. She spoke clearly, carefully, and quietly, never raising her voice. Welsea never discussed her undead condition.
Welsea one of the Zhent delegates in the resistance against Vorgansharax. She helped the Lord Sage of Phlan to send adventurers to explore Yarashs pyramid.
