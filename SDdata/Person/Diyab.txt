Diyab was a divine spellcaster and devotee of the Bhaalspawn Sarevok who lived in the Realms during the midâˆ’14 century DR.
Diyab worked under Zhalimar Cloudwulfe alongside his fellow agents Aasim, Alai, Gardush, and Naaman. Together they sought to advance Sarevoks plans and goals over those of the Iron Throne.
He wielded a +1 warhammer and carried with him a number of potions and oils.
In the , Diyab and his allies were sent by the Iron Throne to the city of Baldurs Gate to advise Thaldorn in his negotiations with Emissary Tar, who represented the Baldurian Council of Four. Right before the meeting, the Diyab and the others were defeated by Abdel Adrian and his companions.
