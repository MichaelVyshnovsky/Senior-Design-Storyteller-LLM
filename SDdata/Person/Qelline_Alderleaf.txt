Qelline Alderleaf was a halfling farmer that lived in Phandalin.
The farmer was very knowledgeable about the ongoings of the village she lived in. She was also said to be wise and practical. Qelline was kind enough to let adventurers stay in the hayloft of her home free of charge.
She had a young adventurous son known as Carp. Qelline was old friends with the druid, Reidoth.
