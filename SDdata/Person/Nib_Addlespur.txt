'''Nib Addlespur''' was a [[spellcaster (class)|spellcaster]] who lived in the region of the [[Sword Coast North]] around [[Phandalin]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
Nib was not one to do something the simple way, preferring complicated schemes to basic plans.

She was an avid gambler, and often found herself betting on a "sure thing".

==Possessions==
Wherever she went, Nib carried with her a deck of ''[[Three-Dragon Ante]]'' cards.

==Relationships==
Nib adored [[bird]]s, and even had a pet [[goldfinch]] called Lil.
