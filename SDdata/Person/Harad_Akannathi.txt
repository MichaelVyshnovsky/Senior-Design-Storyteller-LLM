'''Harad Cloudstrider Akannathi''' was a [[warrior (5e)|warrior]] of the [[Akannathi]] tribe of [[goliath]]s that dwelled within [[Skytower Shelter]] in the [[Spine of the World]] mountains during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Relationships==
He was married to the Akannathi chieftain [[Arn Akannathi|Arn Rockfist]]. They lived together in Skytower with Harad's elderly mother [[Kaniaka Akannathi]], and a pet [[crag cat]].
