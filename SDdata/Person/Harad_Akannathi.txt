Harad Cloudstrider Akannathi was a warrior of the Akannathi tribe of goliaths that dwelled within Skytower Shelter in the Spine of the World mountains during the late 15 century DR.
He was married to the Akannathi chieftain Arn Rockfist. They lived together in Skytower with Harads elderly mother Kaniaka Akannathi, and a pet crag cat.
