'''Cvaal Daoran''' was a member of the [[Zhentarim]] and a leader of the [[Brothers of the Black Fist]] that went on to lead a hostile takeover of [[Phlan]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. He emerged as the city-state's first lord protector, starting a dynasty that would last for over a hundred years.

==Relationships==
During his tenure as the leader of the Brothers of the Black Fist, Cvaal's reported directly to [[Kandar Milinal]], Lord of the [[Citadel of the Raven]].

Cvaal was the founder of [[House Daoran]] the noble family of Phlan that ruled over the city for over a century. He was succeeded by his son  [[Talaric Daoran|Talaric]], and grandson [[Anivar Daoran|Anivar]], neither of whom lived to the family's original patriarch.{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|65}}

==History==
Cvaal was a member of the Zhentarim from before the [[Time of Troubles]] . After it, he became Hatemaster and captain of the Brothers of the Black Fist at the [[Citadel of the Raven]], devoting himself to the god [[Bane]],{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|55}}

After the return of Bane, Cvaal commanded a [[Zhentilar]] army against Phlan in the {{yearlinkName|1375}},{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|52}} The Brothers of the Black Fist came to be known as the Knights of the Black Fist and defended the city from the aggressive expansion of the returned [[Netherese Empire]].

In the {{yearlinkName|1383}}, the [[Shadowbane War]] was raging across the [[Moonsea]] region. Lord Cvaal entered into an alliance with the [[dark fey]] of the [[Quivering Forest]], ensuring the protection of his people and their city in exchange for respecting the borders of their forest home.{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|81}} As [[Fzoul Chembryl]] and the Zhentarim entered into a twisted agreement with the aberrant [[phaerimm]], spurning the [[Shadovar]] of Netheril, Lord Protector Cvaal single-handedly slew one of the [[Princes of Shade]].{{Cite web/Phlan during the Tyranny of Dragons}}.
