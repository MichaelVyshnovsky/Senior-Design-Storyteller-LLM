Vonda was an adventurer with the Brightstar Sash in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
She was buxom.
Through her stay in Tharnadar Edge, she wore a tight bodice.
She adventured with the Brightstar Sash company.
Vonda was a part of the Brightstar Sash already when they recruited the sorceress Caladnei. At least one of their forays yielded them about six thousand gold pieces.
She died when she tried to rob a noble together with her fellow adventurer Rimardo they were captured, and in turn tortured to death to get the location of the rest of their companions.
