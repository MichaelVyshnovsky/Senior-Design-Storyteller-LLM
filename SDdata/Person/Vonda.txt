'''Vonda''' was an adventurer with the [[Brightstar Sash]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EDmmp-p274_5-c17/>

==Description==
She was buxom.<ref name=EDmmp-p274_5-c17/>

==Possessions==
Through her stay in [[Tharnadar Edge]], she wore a tight bodice.<ref name=EDmmp-p274_5-c17/>

==Activities==
She adventured with the [[Brightstar Sash]] company.<ref name=EDmmp-p274_5-c17/>

==History==
Vonda was a part of the Brightstar Sash already when they recruited the [[sorceress]] [[Caladnei]]. At least one of their forays yielded them about six thousand gold pieces.<ref name=EDmmp-p274_5-c17/>

She died when she tried to rob a noble together with her fellow adventurer [[Rimardo]]; they were captured, and in turn tortured to death to get the location of the rest of their companions.<ref name=EDmmp-p274_5-c17/>
