He was rather young.
Baerengard was a highknight of Cormyr.
Baerengard was a highknight of Cormyr at the time when Eskrel Starbridge was appointed head of the organization. He accompanied Starbridge in his personal search for Elminster Aumar, a fugitive wizard, in Shadowdale, where he was part of his forces when doppelgangers attempted an ambush.
