'''Markos Silverhammer''' was a [[dwarf]] who lived in the Kingdom of [[Siremun]] in the [[Hordelands]] in [[1367 DR]].{{Cite dragon/349/The Horde: Barbarians of the Endless Waste}}

==History==
Markos was the king of Siremun in 1367 DR.

==Relationships==
Markos was a direct descendant of the dwarf who initially discovered the [[Firepeaks]].
