Valdick was a Netherese archwizard and prolific spell inventor who lived during the Golden Age.
The archwizard was a keen wildspace traveler, and enjoyed travelling to different crystal spheres. However, Valdick didnt like the time it took to get from one sphere to another. Thus, he worked on formulating spells to make this method of travel much more convenient.
