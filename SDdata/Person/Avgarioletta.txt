Avgarioletta was a Dark Breaker of Umberlee, a member of the Bitch Queens clergy under Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queens House in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Avgarioletta was a human woman with strawberry blonde hair and a tan skin tone. She had watery blue eyes and had several tiny moles on her face, including a beauty mark under her heft eye.
Avgariolettas Dark Breaker uniform was a set of skin-tight heavy chainmail dress. The armor was decorated with golden trim and had decorated metallic pauldrons. Her weapon of choice was heavy crossbow.
Circa the , during the Absolute crisis, Dark Breaker Avgarioletta was among the churchs members who performed the faiths ceremonies and sang hymns in praise of the Bitch Queen. She was alongside Flood Tide Allandra Grey and other priestesses during Holli Dylfords last rites.
