Syal Amandol was a member of Zhentil Keeps Zhentil Keep in 1368 DR.
Syal was the youngest Lord on the Council. His father, Rorst Amandon, was the Zhentilar battle lord and a ferocious opponent of Manshoon, but died of poisoning in 1334 DR. In fear for himself and his family, Syal pledged himself to the Zhentarims suggestions. His family controlled the trade of iron and weapons.
