Sir Gwydion the Quick, known as the Champion Reborn, was a paladin of Torm of the 14th century DR.
He was born in Suzail. along with Gwydion and his protege Rinda.
Gwydions task of escorting Rinda the Scribe, bearer of the Cyrinishad, was considered by the church of Torm to be part of fulfilling the Debt of Persecution aspect of the churchs Penance of Duty.
At some point, Gwydion was a member of the Order of the Golden Lion.
