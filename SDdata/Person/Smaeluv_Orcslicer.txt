==Relationships==
Smaeluv adventured along with the [[dwarven]] warrior [[Mencar Pebblecrusher]], the [[thief]] [[Brennan Risling]], as well as [[Sorcerous Amon]] and his [[imp]] [[familiar]] [[Pooky]].

He enjoyed Mencar's derision of others but could barely tolerate the imp Pooky, as it referred to him as "Smelly".

==Possessions==
Smaeluv wielded a ''[[curse]]d +3 [[greatsword]]''.

==History==
At some point in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, he and his companions stayed at the [[Den of the Seven Vales]] in [[Athkatla]].
