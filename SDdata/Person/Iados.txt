Iados was a tiefling warrior and a companion of the dragonborn paladin Thava. He was an old friend of the monk Kwan Shang-Li and joined forces with him to defeat Caelynna and recover several rare tomes belonging to the Standing Tree Monastery.	
Iados had dark red skin, large horns that he could use as a weapon, and a tail. He had shoulder length hair.
Iados used a sword and shield.
