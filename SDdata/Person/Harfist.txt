'''Deputy Harfist''' was one of the deputies of [[Bron]] [[Bourstag]] of [[Voonlar]] in the [[Moonsea]] ''circa'' [[1373 DR]].

==Personality==
Harfist was a mountain of a man who loomed over his peers from almost {{SI|7'|'"|long=yes}}. He was armed with a massive two-handed [[axe]], strong enough to cleave an [[oak]] three in half with a a single strike.

==History==
Harfist owned the deputies of Bron Bourstag in the city's [[Locktower]] by the {{YearlinkName|1373}}. The same year, Voonlar fell under an onslaught of a [[Cyricist]] [[Order of the Crippled Fang]]. The cultists unleashed a force of summoned [[fiend]]s of the city. Despite casualties, Deputy Harfist survived.
