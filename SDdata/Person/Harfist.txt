Deputy Harfist was one of the deputies of Bron Bourstag of Voonlar in the Moonsea circa 1373 DR.
Harfist was a mountain of a man who loomed over his peers from almost . He was armed with a massive two-handed axe, strong enough to cleave an oak three in half with a a single strike.
Harfist owned the deputies of Bron Bourstag in the citys Locktower by the . The same year, Voonlar fell under an onslaught of a Cyricist Order of the Crippled Fang. The cultists unleashed a force of summoned fiends of the city. Despite casualties, Deputy Harfist survived.
