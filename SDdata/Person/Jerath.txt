Jerath was a blind githyanki knight in Stardock in the early 1490s DR.
Once Jerath saved the life of Ashtyrranthor when she was a wyrmling. During the battles against the illithids of the Seadeeps inside the Crystal Labyrinth, a ulitharid blinded Jerath before it died. However, Ashtyrranthor cared for him during his recovery.
Jerath was usually inside the infirmary in Stardock.
Jerath was a good friend of Ashtyrranthor.
