King '''Dornak Torlath''' "the Puppet King" was the final [[Amn]]ian monarch of the [[Torlath]] dynasty.

==Relationships==
Dornak was the distant descendant of King [[Rhadik Torlath II|Rhadik II]].

==History==
Dornak usurped the throne of Amn in the {{YearlinkName|1095}}, following the rapid assassinations of King [[Imnel Torlath IV|Imnel IV]], King [[Niskam Torlath|Niskam]], and King [[Rhor Torlath|Rhor]]. He won the crown with the assistance of the [[Villax]] merchant house, but was himself quickly assassinated as well, putting an end to the Torlath dynasty after 635 years. The same year, the mounting chaos was halted by [[Erdhinn Wavegold I|Erdhinn]] of the [[Wavegold]] family, distant descendants of [[Jamyll Torlath]].
