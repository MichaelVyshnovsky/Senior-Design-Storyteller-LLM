'''Andemel Graeven''' was a [[Cormyrean]] noble in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Personality==
He was quite given to joking about his fellow nobles.

==Possessions==
His home had a garden, and he owned at least one shop.

==Relationships==
He was a friend of [[Sabrast Windriver]]. He had not stopped shuddering for a tenday after the first time he met [[Elminster Aumar]],{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|2|Realmslore}}

==History==
During a revel in {{yearlink|1356}}, Andemel talked to Sabrast Windriver and [[Raurild Sarpath]] about his shop. He also considered propositioning a lady in a particularly revealing dress; Sabrast warned him the lady was actually Elminster. He also became a witness to [[Immult Murauvyn]] trying to collect back taxes on Sabrast Windriver.
