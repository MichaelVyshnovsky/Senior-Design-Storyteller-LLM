'''Bastian Thermandar''' was the second-in-command of the [[Cult of the Eternal Flame]] at the [[Temple of the Eternal Flame]] in [[Tyar-Besil]] in the late 1490s DR.

==Personality==
Bastian was a very paranoid individual, not trusting anyone.

==History==
Bastian learned much of his [[elemental magic]] from Vanifer herself. In [[1491 DR]], Bastian was responsible for guarding the northern half of the temple.
