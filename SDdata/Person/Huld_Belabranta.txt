'''Huld Belabranta''' was a [[noble]] inhabiting [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name="CoSW-p30>

==Activities==
He lived within [[Belabranta Villa]] located in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] at the intersection of the [[Rough Road]] and [[Mendever Street]].

Huld was a rarity among the [[wizard]]s of Waterdeep in that he was one of a small handful of individuals who were not affiliated with the [[Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors]].

==Relationships==
He was the patriarch of the [[Belabranta]] noble family of Waterdeep.{{Cite web enhancement/Noble Houses of Waterdeep|8}} Since some years prior his death, it was noted that his character had somewhat changed, from a cheerful and generous person to a darker, amoral and greedy wizard.
