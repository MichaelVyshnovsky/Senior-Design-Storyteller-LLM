Imraea was a kid, and an inhabitant of [[the Dalelands]] in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
She was small and dark-haired.

==Personality==
She was a whirlwind, but quite polite.

==Relationships==
She was [[Lhaera]]'s daughter.

==History==
During early [[Mirtul]] of {{yearlink|1357}},{{cite book/Forgotten Realms Atlas|105}} Imraea was visited by [[Torm (adventurer)|Torm]] and [[Rathan Thentraver]], who brought her a kitten, then named [[Snuggleguts]], and nine [[gold piece]]s for kitten-related expenses in the future.
