Imraea was a kid, and an inhabitant of the Dalelands in the mid-14 century DR.
She was small and dark-haired.
She was a whirlwind, but quite polite.
She was Lhaeras daughter.
During early Mirtul of , Imraea was visited by Torm and Rathan Thentraver, who brought her a kitten, then named Snuggleguts, and nine gold pieces for kitten-related expenses in the future.
