Reiner Trall was a male human ranger of Daggerdale and a captain of the Freedom Riders in the 1360s DR.
In 1367 DR, Captain Reiner Trall led a patrol of men searching for Zhentish agents in Daggerdale and trying to prevent a Zhent caravan from reaching Dagger Falls.
In battle, Reiner carried a bastard sword, handaxe, light crossbow, and spear.
