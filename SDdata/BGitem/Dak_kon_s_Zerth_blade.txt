Dakkons Zerth blade was a unique katana that belonged to the famous githzerai named Dakkon from the planar city of Sigil.
Dakkons Zerth blade had a strong +2 enchantment and increased the wielders defenses. Additionally, when the sword was wielded by a user of arcane magic, it expanded their mind allowing for memoization of four additional spells per day.
Dakkons Zerth blade accompanied its famous master on a journey alongside a mysterious individual only known as the Nameless One. Subsequently, Dakkon was killed by a powerful entity. While it was unknown how a Dakkons Zerth blade appeared on Toril, it came into the possession of a merchant named Deidre, a member of Auroras Magic Emporium, during the 14 century DR. Sometime prior to the  she began selling her wares in the Adventurer Mart of Athkatla.
