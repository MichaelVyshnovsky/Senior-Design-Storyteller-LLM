Heavy crossbows of searing were enchanted and marvelously engineered crossbows marked with the signs of the Church of Gond.
These wonders of tinkering possessed a +1 enchantment that helped the missiles strike true. Additionally, these heavy crossbows could use any type of bolt that, before being launched, were covered in oil. As the bolts were fired, they were lit, dealing additional flame damage to the targets. The weapon itself was also enchanted to remain well oiled and lubricated at all times.
