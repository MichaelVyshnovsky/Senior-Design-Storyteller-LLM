The red potion was a rather unique draught that was offered for sale by a traveling merchant during the midâ€“14 century DR.
The actual solution was rather peculiar in appearance.
The imbiber of this potion experienced a number of changes to their physicality and mental faculties. They were struck down with extreme weakness, to the point where they could only carry a few pounds on their person. The potion also left them with very poor judgement and lack of common sense.
The primary benefit of the red potion was that it enhanced a persons intellect, to a degree that was beyond genius-level. It also offered them significant protection from magical spells and spell-like effects.
Both of the potions effects lasted for a full 24 hours.
