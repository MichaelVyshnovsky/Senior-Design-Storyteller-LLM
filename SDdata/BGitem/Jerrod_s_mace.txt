Jerrods mace was a unique enchanted bludgeon from the town of Easthaven in Icewind Dale.
The mace possessed a +2 enchantment that guided the wielders aim towards the enemy and landed deadlier strikes. It was specifically ensorcelled to be devastating when used against demons and devils.
As its named suggested, Jerrods mace once belonged to Jerrod, a historic individual. The barbarian shaman of Tempus wielded his mace against the powerful devil known as Belhifet ages before the 13 century DR. The hero sacrificed his life in services of town of Easthaven in Icewind Dale.
In the , Jerrods mace, along with several other magic items from Icewind Dale, ended up in the hands of the merchant named Joluv who traveled from Icewind Dale to the City of Coin, Athkatla, to sell the items to wealthy Amnian adventurers.
