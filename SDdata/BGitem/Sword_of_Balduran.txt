The Sword of Balduran was the magical longsword wielded by the legendary explorer Balduran.
This +2 longsword bestowed upon its wielder partial protection from magical spells and abilities.
Baldurs sword, along with his shield and armor, were stolen from a museum in the city of Baldurs Gate. At some point it came into the inventory of Deidre, a merchant from Auroras Magic Emporium, who did business from the Adventurer Mart located in Amnian the city of Athkatla.
