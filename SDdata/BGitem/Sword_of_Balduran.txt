The '''''Sword of Balduran''''' was the [[magic]]al [[longsword]] wielded by the legendary explorer [[Balduran]].

==Powers==
This ''+2 longsword'' bestowed upon its wielder partial protection from magical [[spell]]s and abilities.

==History==
Baldur's sword, along with his [[Shield of Balduran|shield]] and [[Plate of Balduran|armor]], were stolen from a museum in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]]. At some point it came into the inventory of [[Deidre]], a merchant from ''[[Aurora's Magic Emporium]]'', who did business from the [[Adventurer Mart]] located in [[Amn]]ian the city of [[Athkatla]].
