'''''Acid arrows''''', also known as '''arrows of acid''', were [[enchanted]] [[arrow]] projectile, commonly used throughout the [[Sword Coast]] and the [[Moonsea]] regions.

==Powers==
These magic projectiles pierced their targets and upon an impact released a splash of alchemical acid.

==Notable Users==
*[[Herab]], the captain of a pirate vessel, the ''[[Barracuda (ship)|Barracuda]]'', allied with the [[Zhentarim]] ''circa'' [[1372 DR]].
