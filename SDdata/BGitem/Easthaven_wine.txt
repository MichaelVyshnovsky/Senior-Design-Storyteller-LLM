Easthaven wine was an unnamed type of wine that was fermented locally in the town of Easthaven in Icewind Dale as early as the late 13 century DR.
This wine was made by the inhabitants of Easthaven, not professional wine-makers. Its ingredients were a mystery and the wine itself had a slight fishy scent.
Circa the , Easthaven wine was available for purchase from Pomab Akazmhir, the proprietor of Pomabs Emporium in Easthaven. The local drunk named Old Jed was an avid consumer of the concoction.
