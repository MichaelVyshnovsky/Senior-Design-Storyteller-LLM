'''Easthaven wine''' was an unnamed type of [[wine]] that was fermented locally in the town of [[Easthaven]] in [[Icewind Dale]] as early as the late 13{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
This wine was made by the inhabitants of Easthaven, not professional wine-makers. Its ingredients were a mystery and the wine itself had a slight [[fish]]y scent.

==History==
''Circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1281}}, Easthaven wine was available for purchase from [[Pomab Ak'azmhir]], the proprietor of [[Pomab's Emporium]] in Easthaven. The local drunk named [[Old Jed]] was an avid consumer of the concoction.
