Gauntlets of extraordinary specialization were a variety of enchanted gauntlets that greatly improved the fighting prowess of its wearer.
These gauntlets allowed an individual to strike with greater accuracy, speed, and power in combat.
Gauntlets of extraordinary specialization were exceedingly rare, and highly sought after by warriors throughout the Realms.
The marilith known as Ytossi owned a pair of these gauntlets.
