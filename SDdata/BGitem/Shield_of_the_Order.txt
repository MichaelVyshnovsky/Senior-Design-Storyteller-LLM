The Shield of the Order was a unique enchanted shield associated withe the Order of the Radiant Heart.
The shield held a powerful +5 enchantment and bolstered the inherent mental and physical defenses of its wielder.
The shield once belonged to the brave knight of the Radiant Heart, Peragath the Valiant. He carried it during his final battle on Toril, against a horde of tanarri that were invading the prime via a portal linked to the astral plane. It was lost for quite some time when Peragath lept through the portal in pursuit of the demonic forces.
By the , the shield came into the possession of the fire giant Yaga-Shura, who carried it with him during the siege of Saradush.
