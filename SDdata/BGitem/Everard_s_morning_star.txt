Easthaven
Everards morning star was a weapon that was once owned by the famous priest that lived in Easthaven during the late 13 century DR.
Everards morning star possessed a +2 enchantment.
When it struck an arcane spellcaster there was a chance they would forget one of their previously memorized spells.
Some time after Everard saved the Ten Towns in the , his namesake morning star came into the possession of the merchant Joluv.
