Clerics staves were enchanted staves crafted and ensorcelled to be used by clerics.
These staves were simple weapons that radiated powerful magic. Their simple design suggested the staves were created to be used by modest priests or clerics. Clerics staves were enchanted with a potent +3 enchantment that made the staff a deadly weapon and guided the wielders hand towards the target.
