'''''White dragon scale''''' was a variety of [[armor]] made from the [[Dragon scale mail|scales]] of a [[white dragon]].<ref group="note">Despite being made from the dragon's ''scales'' the armor was considered [[splint mail]] armor.

==Powers==
This armor offered the wearer protection from ice and cold energy, such as was emitted by white dragon's [[dragon breath|breath weapon]]. They were also granted the power to emit a ''[[cone of cold|blast of frost and ice]]'', up to three times per day.

==History==
The blacksmith [[Conlan]] of [[Kuldahar]] knew how to craft this type of armor, as did the [[imp]] [[Cespenar]] from the [[Throne of Blood]].
