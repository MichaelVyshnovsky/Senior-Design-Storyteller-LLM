Knaves robes were common enchanted robes worn by many wizards across Faerûn in the 14 century DR.
Knaves robes were defensive robes often used by shady wizards and thieves who dabbled in the Art. The robes enchantments shielded the wearer from poisons, blades, and death spells.
*Elan Garaq a rakshasa trader and Baeloth Barrityls right-hand from the Black Pits of Underdark.
*Yago a wizard from Baldurs Gate in the mid−14 century DR.
*Zordral a witch hunter wizard from Nashkels carnival in 1368 DR.
