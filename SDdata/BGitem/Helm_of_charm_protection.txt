A '''''helm of charm protection''''' was a type of [[magic]]al [[helmet|helm]] that protected its wearer from mind-altering [[spell]]s and effects.

==Powers==
As the name suggests, this variety of helm shielded a person's mind from spells and abilities that fell under the ''[[charm]]'' classification. It did not however offer any protection against spells or effects that influenced the wearer's emotions.

==Reputation==
These helmets were often utilized by high-end mercenary companies to ensure the performance of their members.

==Notable Owners==
One of these helmets was worn by the bandit [[Draug Fea]].

The [[Athkatla]]n slavemaster [[Olaf Rassmussen]] also wore this type of helm.
