A '''''Solar Aegis''''' was a variety of [[medium shield]] created for and carried by the most righteous warriors of the Realms.

==Description==
A ''Solar Aegis'' was at least partially layered with [[silver]] that never tarnished and always shone with radiance, despite the circumstances of use or battle. They featured intricate engravings and were inlaid with brilliant gems, such as [[sapphire]]s.

==Powers==
The shield offered great protection to its wielder from the forces of [[evil]], bestowing upon them greater vitality and protection from both mundane and magical flames.

==History==
These shields most often forged and [[item enchantment|enchanted]] for [[paladin]]s belonging to fighting orders of the Realms' morally upstanding faiths.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Caelar Argent]], the Shining Lady who led a [[Shining Crusade|crusade]] across the [[Sword Coast]] in the {{yearlinkName|1368}}.
