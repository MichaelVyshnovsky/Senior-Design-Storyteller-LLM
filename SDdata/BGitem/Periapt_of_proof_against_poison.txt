The '''''periapt of proof against poison''''' was a type of [[magic]]al amulet that granted protective attributes and curative abilities to its wearer. They were found in the continent of [[Kara-Tur]]. [[Eunuch]]s who served as food-tasters for emperors would commonly wear these articles of jewelry.

==Description==
The periapt was a large [[gem]]stone, hanging from a [[gold]] chain. Post-[[Second Sundering]] versions of the amulet consisted of a black gem pendant hanging from a [[silver]] chain.

==Powers==
This amulet completely protected its wearer from magical and mundane [[poison]]s.

==Notable Users==
* [[Gathlarue]] had an amulet rendering her immune to all poisons in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].
