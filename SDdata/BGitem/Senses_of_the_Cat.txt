Senses of the Cat, also known as boots of avoidance were enchanted boots that increased the wearers defense against ranged weapons.
The enchantments of Senses of the Cat allowed the wearer to sense incoming missiles and helped them to avoid harm from them.
Boots of avoidance were originally created for an instructor of some archery academy after they were tired of falling victim of countless "accidents" at he hands of the students.

*Meklin a halfling associate of Neera from Baldurs Gate.
