The '''''Armor of Deep Night''''' was a [[magic]]al suit of [[leather armor]], bestowed unto [[Toril]] by the [[deity]] [[Umberlee]].

==Description==
The armor was made from black [[leather]] and fastened with [[ebony]] [[rivet]]s.

==History==
The ''Armor of Deep Night'' was a gift from Umberlee to the [[rogue]] [[Dami Darn]], after she killed the [[conjurer]] [[Looxil]], who had betrayed the Bitch Queen.

In the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, the ''Armor of Deep Night'' was available for purchase from [[Ribald Barterman]]'s [[Adventurer Mart]] in [[Athkatla]].
