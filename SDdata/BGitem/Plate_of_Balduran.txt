==History==
Balduran's suit of armor was originally uncovered by a group of adventurers in the [[Sword Coast]] and sold off for a small fortune. Several copies were made to accommodate the demands of [[Faer√ªn]]ian collectors and historians. 

A copy of this armor was sold by [[Neurik]], who manned the Temple of Tyr in [[Beorunna's Well]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

It was believed the original eventually wound up in the possession of a museum in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]], where it was subsequently stolen from, along with the explorer's [[Sword of Balduran|sword]] and [[Shield of Balduran|shield]]. At some point the original came into the inventory of [[Deidre]], a merchant from ''[[Aurora's Magic Emporium]]'', who did business from the [[Adventurer Mart]] located in [[Amn]]ian the city of [[Athkatla]] around the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.
