A '''''girdle of fortitude''''' was a [[magic]]al [[belt (clothing)|belt]] that improved the wearer's hardiness and vitality for a limited duration.

==Powers==
Once per day, the wearer of a ''girdle of fortitude'' could gain the benefits of its powers for eight hours.

==Notable Owners==
[[Violos|King Violos]] wore one of these girdles during his later years when leading his people in battle against a group of [[troll]]s.

A ''girdle of fortitude'' was owned by [[Gaal]], the leader of a cult dedicated to the  [[Unseeing Eye]] that operated within [[Athkatla/Sewers|the sewers]] of [[Athkatla]].
