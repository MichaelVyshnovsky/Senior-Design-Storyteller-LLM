Boroks Fist was an enchanted warhammer that once was wielded by a dwarven hero, Borok Thundercracker, long before the 14 century DR.
Boroks Fist possessed a +2 enchantment that guided the wielders strikes towards their targets and dealt increased damage. Additionally, upon each strike, Boroks Fist discharged a burst of electricity.
Boroks Fists first known owner was Borok Thundercracker, who led his people in battles against giants, or "tall vermin", as he called them. It was believed Boroks Fist was blessed. Boroks elvish allies jokingly called the hammer his "little iron fist." Circa the , Boroks Fist was available for purchase from Ribald Bartermans Adventurer Mart in Athkatla, the City of Coin.
