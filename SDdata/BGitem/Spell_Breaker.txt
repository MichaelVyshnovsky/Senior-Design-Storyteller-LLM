Spell Breaker was an enchanted long sword that greatly aided those who fought against arcane spellcasters.
The sword featured a silver hilt decorated with translucent blue gemstones.
Spell Breaker possessed a +2 enchantment and had a chance to dispel any magical effects on any person or creature it struck.
Additionally it offered its wielder significant protections against spells and spell-like effects. This effect was amplified for those martial warriors and paladins who hunted down mages.
By the , the sword was lost somewhere in the Western Heartlands, not far from the Boareskyr Bridge.
