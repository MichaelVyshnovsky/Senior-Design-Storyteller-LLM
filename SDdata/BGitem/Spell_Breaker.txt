'''''Spell Breaker''''' was an [[Item enchantment|enchanted]] [[long sword]] that greatly aided those who fought against [[arcane spellcaster]]s.

==Description==
The [[sword]] featured a [[silver]] hilt decorated with translucent blue [[gemstone]]s.

==Powers==
''Spell Breaker'' possessed a ''+2 enchantment'' and had a chance to ''[[dispel magic|dispel]]'' any magical effects on any person or creature it struck.

Additionally it offered its wielder significant protections against [[spell]]s and spell-like effects. This effect was amplified for those [[wizard slayer|martial warriors]] and [[inquisitor|paladins]] who hunted down [[mage]]s.

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, the sword was lost somewhere in the [[Western Heartlands]], not far from the [[Boareskyr Bridge]].
