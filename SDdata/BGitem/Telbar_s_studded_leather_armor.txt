Telbars studded leather armor was a set of enchanted studded leathers that one belonged to a individual named Telbar.
Telbars studded leather armor possessed a +2 enchantment.
Telbar famously wore this set of armor but it was not known why or how it left his hands. In 1368 DR, the armor belonged to Sendai Argrim, an aggressive Amnish nationalist who dwelled to the south of Nashkel.
