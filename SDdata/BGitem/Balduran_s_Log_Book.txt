Baldurans Log Book was the incomplete logbook of Balduran, detailing his journey to Anchorome and back.
Following his ill-fated voyage, it was left in the wreck of his ship, the Wandering Eye, on Werewolf Island. It was damaged by time and the elements.
In the , Mendas in Ulgoths Beard hired Gorions Ward to go to Werewolf Island and retrieve the log book, claiming he wished to study it.
