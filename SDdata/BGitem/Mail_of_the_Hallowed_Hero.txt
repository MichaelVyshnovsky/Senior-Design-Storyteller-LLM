The Mail of the Hallowed Hero was an enchanted suit of chainmail armor associated with the Argent family of Waterdeep.
It was a suit of elven craftsmanship, having been made by those who lived in the Misty Forest.
The Mail of the Hallowed Hero possessed a +3 enchantment. It granted its wearer a boon against evil foes and rendered them immune to both poison and disease.
The armor was bestowed upon Olen Argent when he aided the elves of the Misty Forest by hunting down a murdering band of Malarite cultists.
