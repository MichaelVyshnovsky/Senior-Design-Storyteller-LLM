An adventurers robe was a type of magical garment worn by those arcane spellcasters who traveled across the Realms.
These robes offered a spellcaster greater protection than mundane clothing.
One variety of of adventurers robes crafted circa the  protected its wearer from blunt-trauma weapons. It offered further protection against polymorphic magic and petrification effects, such as that caused by the gaze of a basilisk.
Yet another type of adventurers robes merely mitigated any physical harm that was inflicted upon the wearer.
It was said that these robes originated from designs made by the famous Vangerdahast, Court Wizard of Cormyr. Their creation made him quite popular among spellcasting circles.
