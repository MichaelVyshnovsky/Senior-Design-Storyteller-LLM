Edventars Gift was a unique ring of free action.
The wearer of the ring was immune to effects, objects or other impediments that limited or hampered their movement. Similarly, their movements could not be enhanced by in any way, such as by certain spells.
This ring was named after Edventar, a scavenger that played no small part in the defeat of the "Pirate Queen" Yenandra Hazmcri. He was gifted his namesake ring by a group of aquatic elves he counted among his friends.
