'''''Boneblade''''' was a ''+4 [[magic]]al [[dagger]]'' that, while simple in design, had a powerful [[Item enchantment|enchantment]]. It had a blade of [[steel]] and a handle carved from the tooth of a [[black dragon|black]] [[dragon]].

==History==
The ''boneblade'' was forged centuries before the [[Time of Troubles]] of [[1358 DR]] by a [[duergar]] smith, and tempered in his blood. As of the year [[1369 DR]], it was in the possession of a [[priest]]ess of [[Lolth]] in the subterranean city of [[Ust Natha]].
