The Answerer was an enchanted longsword that had to power to weaken the defenses of its wielders foes.
It possessed a very unique design that made it difficult for the wielder to make a strong start in combat.
To counteract the drawbacks of its form, the Answerer possessed the unique power to weaken the physical defense and magical resistances of any creature it struck. This effect became more intense with each additional strike that landed.
As of the , the sword was owned by Jamis Tombelthen, a general of the Tethyrian army.
