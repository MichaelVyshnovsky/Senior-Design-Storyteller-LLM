The '''''Jansen AdventureWear''''', or the '''''Jansen-Brand Metal-Repellent AdventureWear''''', was a unique set of [[armor]] that was invented and worn by the [[gnome]] inventor [[Jan Jansen]].

==Description==
The ''AdventureWear'' was made from a flexible yet strong material that was not native to [[Amn]].<ref group="note">While the material of the armor is not explicitly stated, it is classified as [[leather armor]] Its appearance was objectively stylish.

It could be cleaned by dry-clean methods only.

==Powers==
The armor protected Jan from projectiles and partially limited the physical harm inflicted upon him. It did not restrict his [[Arcane spellcaster|spellcasting]] abilities in any way.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, the patent for the ''AdventureWear'' was still pending.
