Vhailors helm was the helmet that was worn by Vhailor, one of the Mercykillers of Sigil.
The helm granted the wearer the ability to create an illusory copy of their person once per day.
Some time before the , Vhailors helm wound up on the planet of Toril. It came into possession of the merchant Deidre, who sold her wares in the Amnian city of Athkatla.
