'''''Vhailor's helm''''' was the [[helmet]] that was worn by [[Vhailor]], one of the [[Mercykillers]] of [[Sigil]].

==Powers==
The helm granted the wearer the ability to ''[[simulacrum|create an illusory copy of their person]]'' once per day.

==History==
Some time before the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, ''Vhailor's helm'' wound up on the planet of [[Toril]]. It came into possession of the merchant [[Deidre]], who sold her wares in the [[Amn]]ian city of [[Athkatla]].
