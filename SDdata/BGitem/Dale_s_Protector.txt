Dales Protector was the name given to a set of enchanted bracers that were given to the archers of the Great Dale under direction of the realms king.
These bracers greatly increased the accuracy of the wearer with ranged weapons.
When the King of the Great Dale arranged a meeting with one of his more dangerous political rivals, he requested the aid of his nations most skilled archers in addition to his usual guard. As they expected, opposing soldiers ambushed the king and his retinue, but were shot down in a hail of arrows before they could reach the monarch.
