Templar was one of the twelve moons of Garden in Realmspace.
Templar was a small,  discworld with a diameter of . Like most of Gardens moons, Templar was larger than its parent.
The moon served as an outpost for a society of one hundred wizards of all schools of magic, who dedicated themselves to studying the secrets of Garden. They were able to survive on their small flatworld by the sale of magic items.
