The Star-That-Shines-By-Day was as celestial body seen from the continent of Mazticas Valley of Nexal.
In the , the Star-That-Shines-By-Day shone brightly in the skies over the great empire of Nexal. It grew in brilliance over the course of a tenday, and this strange occurrence was believed to be a dire omen by the clerics Atl-Ollin of Calor, Caracatl of Tezca, and Hoxitl of Zaltec. Whether gods or mystical means caused the Star-That-Shines-By-Days brilliance, it was, indeed, soon followed by the fateful arrival of the Golden Legion to the True World, and eventual destruction of Nexal and return of the Iridescent Ancestor of the Gods, Qotal.
