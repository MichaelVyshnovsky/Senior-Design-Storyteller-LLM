'''Ironpiece''' was one of the "worldlets" that existed in the same [[crystal sphere]] as the [[Rock of Bral]].{{Rock of Bral location}}

It was inhabited by [[gnome]]s and had at least one lake, called Lake Crashsplash. The worldlet was also, at least partially, covered in forests.
