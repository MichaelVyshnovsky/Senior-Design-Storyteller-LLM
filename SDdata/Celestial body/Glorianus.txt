'''Glorianus''' was one of the twelve moons of [[Garden]] in [[Realmspace]].

==Description==
Glorianus was a very strange celestial object shaped like a crescent about {{SI|3|mi|k|long=yes}} by {{SI|2|mi|k|long=yes}}. The moon was covered with a soft, green grass.

==Inhabitants==
The moon was inhabited by a single [[human]], a [[druid]] named [[Griffon Broadleaf]].
