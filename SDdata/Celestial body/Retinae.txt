Retinae was one of the twelve moons of Garden in Realmspace.
Like most of Gardens moons, spherical Retinae was larger than its parent, with a diameter of . It received its name because of a large crater that appeared like the pupil of an eye from a distance.
Within the "pupil" grew a species of fungus that was useful in the casting of spells and brewing of potions related to vision.
