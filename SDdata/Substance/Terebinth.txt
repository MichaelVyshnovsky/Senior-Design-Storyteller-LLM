Terebinth, or turpentine was a resin distillate used in industry.
Terebinth acted as a solvent and distinctive, reeking smell.
* Terebinth was widely used by artisans in the Realms for a variety of tasks, chief among them being the removal of paint.
* Enamel was made through a combination of terebinth, silex, and pigments.
* Varnish was made with terebinth and used to give fine wooden furniture and tools a protective finish.
* Noxious, volatile substances such as terebinth be poured on or near an ankheg to induce a state of sleep.
* Oil of fumbling could only be removed with a solvent such as alcohol and terebinth.
