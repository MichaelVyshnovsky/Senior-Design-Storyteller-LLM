Chromium was an uncommon metal found in the Realms.
Chromium was extremely shiny, similar to metals given everbright treatment.
The Elemental Store of Karsus Enclave sold pure elemental chromium for 5 gp per .
Other, strange planes were known to have chrome.
Chrome could be used to decorate objects such as shields.
Chromium dragons were a particularly cruel type of ferrous dragon associated with the metal, with bright, shining, silvery scales.
