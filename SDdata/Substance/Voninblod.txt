'''''Voninblod''''' (meaning "'''titan blood'''" in [[Giant language|Jotun]]<ref name= "FOR7-p28-29">), was a type of [[magic]]al substance created by [[giant]]s in the days of [[Ostoria]].

==Description==
This substance was blue in coloration.

==History==
Around the late-15{{th}} century [[DR]], ancient containers of ''voninblod'' were occasionally found around [[Bryn Shander]], [[Cold Run]], and [[Lonelywood]].{{Cite web/Neverwinter Developer Blog/Relics of Ostoria}}

==Usages==
''Voninblod'' was highly valued for use in [[item enchantment|enchanting items]], such as ''[[relic armor]]s''.
