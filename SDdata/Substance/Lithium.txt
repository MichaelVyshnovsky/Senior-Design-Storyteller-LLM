'''Lithium''' was an elemental material found in the [[Realms]].

==Availability==
In {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}, the [[Elemental Store]] of [[Karsus Enclave]] sold pure elemental lithium bricks for 6 [[sp]] per {{SI|1|ounce|long=yes}}.

==Usage==
* Lithium was used in the production of explosive materials by the ancient [[Netherese]].
