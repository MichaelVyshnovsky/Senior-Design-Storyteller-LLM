'''Atharstone''', or '''athar-stone''', was a heavy type of rock found in [[asteroid]]s and in a few mountain ranges of select [[crystal sphere]]s.

==Properties==
Atharstone was noted to be both heavier and more durable than [[lead]].

==Applications==
The durability of atharstone made it suitable for constructing ''[[dead box]]es''.
