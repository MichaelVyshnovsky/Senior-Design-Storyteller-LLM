'''Propolis''' was a [[wax]]y, [[resin]]ous substance produced by [[bee]]s.

==Availability==
A variety of propolis was made by the bees of [[Chult]].

==Usage==
Propolis was used to make a variety of [[varnish]] used for sealing and finishing wood, fabric, leather, and metal, preserving the material from hot, humid conditions such as in Chult.
