'''Opium''' was a type of [[drug]] that was derived from the [[poppy]] flower.There is no licensed D&D source that clearly states the connection between opium and poppies. The closest thing we have is ''[[Murder in Baldur's Gate]]'' mentioning "poppy dreams" in the gambling den Oasis.

{{Quote|[[Spelljammer|Ships that sail between the stars]]? Next ye will spin tales of a [[giff|talking hippopotamus that walks on two legs]] and carries a [[bow]]. I asked ye to research facts about other worlds, not spend a week in an opium den.|[[Elminster]], [[Volo's Guide to Monsters (in-universe)|''Volo's Guide to Monsters'']]}}

==History==
In [[1357 DR]], opium dens, establishments where the drug was sold and smoked, were quite common in the hedonistic city of [[Semmishi]].

In [[1482 DR]], in the [[Baldur's Gate/Little Calimshan|Little Calimshan]] district of [[Baldur's Gate]] one could partake in opium at the gambling den [[Oasis]].

==Notable Opium Products==
[[Laudanum]] was a tincture of opium and alcohol.{{Cite dungeon/20/The Ship of Night|13}}
