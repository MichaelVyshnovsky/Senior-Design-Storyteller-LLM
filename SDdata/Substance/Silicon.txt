Silicon was an elemental substance found in the Realms.
In , the Elemental Store of Karsus Enclave sold pure elemental powdered silicon for 3 gp per .
* Quartz was made up of silicon dioxide, as were its variants rose quartz, amethyst, cairngorm, citrine, iris agate, morion, and chalcedony.
* Silicon was used by the Netherese in building construction.
* Bronze alloys frequently included silicon impurities, which lowered the melting temperature.
