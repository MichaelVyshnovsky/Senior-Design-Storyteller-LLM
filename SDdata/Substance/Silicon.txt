'''Silicon''' was an elemental substance found in the [[Realms]].

==Availability==
In {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}, the [[Elemental Store]] of [[Karsus Enclave]] sold pure elemental powdered silicon for 3 [[gp]] per {{SI|1|ounce|long=yes}}.

===Occurrences===
* [[Quartz]] was made up of silicon dioxide, as were its variants [[rose quartz]], [[amethyst]], [[cairngorm]], [[citrine]], [[iris agate]], [[morion (gem)|morion]], and [[chalcedony]].{{Cite dragon/248/Crystal Confusion|48}}

==Usages==
* Silicon was used by the [[Netherese]] in building construction.
* [[Bronze]] alloys frequently included silicon impurities, which lowered the melting temperature.{{Cite dragon/123/Fire for Effect!|35}}
