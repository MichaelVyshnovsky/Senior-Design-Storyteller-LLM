'''Velveteen''' was a type of fabric found in the Realms, often used in the crafting of clothing.

==Usage==
Articles of clothing velveteen was used in crafting included armwraps, headdresses, hoods, jackets, pants, [[sash]]es, and shirts.

==Notable Users==
The [[wizard]] [[Jazrac]] owned [[robe]]s that were made of velveteen.<ref>
