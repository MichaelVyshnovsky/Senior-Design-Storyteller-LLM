'''Gum arabic''' was a plant [[resin]] from the [[acacia]] tree, used for mundane, alchemical, and magical purposes.

==Availability==
[[Aurora's Emporium]] sold gum arabic for 5 [[sp]] per {{SI|6|oz|long=yes}}, intended for [[wizard]]s to use in their spells and laboratory experiments.

==Usage==
Gum arabic was widely used to make baked goods, [[candy]], [[adhesive]]s, and more esoteric [[alchemical]] recipes. It also found some use in medicine, such as repairing damaged tissue.{{Cite dragon/13/How Heavy Is My Giant|7}}

===Spell Components===
Gum arabic was a [[material component]] in several [[spell]]s:
* ''[[Invisibility]]'' and ''[[mass invisibility]]'' used an eyelash encased in gum arabic.
* ''[[Otiluke's resilient sphere]]'' and ''[[Otiluke's telekinetic sphere]]'' used a hemisphere of gum arabic and a hemisphere of [[gemstone]].{{Cite dragon/67/From the Sorcerer's Scroll|57}}
* ''[[Polymorph any object]]'' used gum arabic, [[mercury]], and smoke as material components.
* ''[[Sequester]]'' used gum arabic, a [[basilisk]] eyelash, and [[whitewash]].{{Cite dragon/68/From the Sorcerer's Scroll|56}}
