Maple sap, was tree sap produced by maple trees.
This substance had a sticky consistency and was edible.
* Bolden Coarsca, a merchant in the Seven Suns Coster Market, sold jars of maple sap at a price of 54 copper pieces.
* Maple sap could be refined to produce a type of sugar.
* Some ingested maple sap as a drink. Urban worshipers of Silvanus often sold it as such in an attempt to attract followers to the church.
* Some used maple sap as an ingredient for creating potions of accuracy.
* In the Sword Coast, this sap was often used as a sweetener.
Just a small amount of this substance could make a dohwar drunk.
