'''Cobalt''' was an uncommon metal found in the [[Realms]].

==Availability==
In {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}, the [[Elemental Store]] of [[Karsus Enclave]] sold pure elemental cobalt for 6 [[gp]] per {{SI|1|ounce|long=yes}}.

===Occurrences===
* Deep-sea mineral nodules could contain concentrations of various metals, including cobalt.{{Cite dragon/190/Deep Beneath the Waves|17}}
* Cobalt was obtained from [[wikipedia:en:Skutterudite|skutterudite]], a hard gray ore.{{Cite dragon/152/In a Cavern, In a Canyon...|23}}
* Cobalt could be found as an impurity in [[marble]], giving it a blue coloration.

==Usages==
* Cobalt powder could be used as a pigment for coloring [[glass]].
