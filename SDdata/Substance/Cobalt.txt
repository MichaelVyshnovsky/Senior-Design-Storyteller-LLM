Cobalt was an uncommon metal found in the Realms.
In , the Elemental Store of Karsus Enclave sold pure elemental cobalt for 6 gp per .
* Deep-sea mineral nodules could contain concentrations of various metals, including cobalt.
* Cobalt was obtained from skutterudite, a hard gray ore.
* Cobalt could be found as an impurity in marble, giving it a blue coloration.
* Cobalt powder could be used as a pigment for coloring glass.
