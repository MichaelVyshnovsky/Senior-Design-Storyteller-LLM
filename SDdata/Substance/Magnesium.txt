Magnesium was a volatile metal found in the Realms.
Magnesium was a very light substance and weighed only  per .
In , the Elemental Store of Karsus Enclave sold pure elemental bricks of magnesium for 3 gp per .
Magnesium was found in dolomite ore. More pure deposits were considered extremely dangerous to encounter while mining, similar to digging into rock oil.
* The ancient Netherese used magnesium in the production of explosives.
* Magnesium strips were used to provide a clear, bright, white light when torch or lamplight wouldnt do.
* The sparkler and flare spells used strips of magnesium as their material components.
* The flare spell could use powdered magnesium as a component for metamagic.
* Magnesium-rich lobster shells could be used as fertilizer.
