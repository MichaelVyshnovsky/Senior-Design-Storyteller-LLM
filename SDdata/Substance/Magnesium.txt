'''Magnesium''' was a volatile metal found in the [[Realms]].

==Properties==
Magnesium was a very light substance and weighed only {{SI|110|pound|k|long=yes}} per {{SI|1|cubic foot|l|long=yes}}.

==Availability==
In {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}, the [[Elemental Store]] of [[Karsus Enclave]] sold pure elemental bricks of magnesium for 3 [[gp]] per {{SI|1|ounce|long=yes}}.

===Occurrence===
Magnesium was found in [[Wikipedia:Dolomite|dolomite]] ore.{{Cite dragon/13/How Heavy Is My Giant|6}} More pure deposits were considered extremely dangerous to encounter while mining, similar to digging into [[rock oil]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Building the Pyre|15}}

==Usage==
* The ancient [[Netherese]] used magnesium in the production of explosives.
* Magnesium strips were used to provide a clear, bright, white light when [[torch]] or [[lamp]]light wouldn't do.
* The ''[[sparkler]]'' and ''[[flare (2nd level)|flare]]'' spells used strips of magnesium as their [[material component]]s.{{Cite dragon/227/Arcane Lore: And the Rocket's Red Glare|94}}
* The ''[[flare]]'' spell could use powdered magnesium as a component for [[metamagic]].
* Magnesium-rich [[lobster]] shells could be used as fertilizer.{{Cite web/theedverse/2020-02-10 - Port Kir in the 15th century DR}}
