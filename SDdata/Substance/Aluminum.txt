'''Aluminum''' was an elemental material found in the [[Realms]].

==Properties==
Aluminum was a silvery-white metal, appearing very similar to [[platinum]]. It was extremely light for a metal, and in its pure form weighed {{SI|170|pound|k|long=yes}} per {{SI|1|cubic foot|l|long=yes}}.{{Cite dragon/13/How Heavy Is My Giant|6}}

==Availability==
In {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}, the [[Elemental Store]] of [[Karsus Enclave]] sold pure elemental aluminum for 3 [[gp]] per {{SI|1|ounce|long=yes}}.

===Occurrences===
[[Spinel]] [[gem]]s were made up of crystalline [[manganese]] oxide and aluminum.{{Cite dragon/248/Crystal Confusion|49}}
