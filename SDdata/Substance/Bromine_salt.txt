Bromine salts or bromides were an uncommon mineral salt used in a variety of trades.
* Bromine salts were refined from seawater alongside regular salt production.
* Alkaline lakes in Zakharas deserts such as the Pit of the Ghuls were a source of alkaline salts, bromine salts, and table salt, but were often too remote and dangerous to exploit.
* Auroras Emporium sold bromine salts for 1 gp per .
* Bromine salts were used in wizards laboratories for various processes.
* Airy water could use a small handful of bromine salts as a material component.
* Otilukes bubbling buoyancy used a small cork and a pinch of bromine salt for its material components.
