Lanolin, also known as wool wax,
On the planet of Toril, knowledge of lanolins existence and how to extract it from the coats of sheep dated as far back as the days of the Netherese Empire.
In the late 15 century DR, lanolin was quite common in the Icewind Dale area. Being found in such settlements as Bryn Shander and Lonelywood.
* This substance was known and used in many of the cold and temperate regions of Toril.
* Lanolin was commonly applied to materials to protect them from water and prevent rust.
