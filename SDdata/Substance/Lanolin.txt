'''Lanolin''', also known as '''wool wax''',{{Cite web/theedverse/2021-02-13 - Beekeeping and Wax in the Realms}}

==History==
On the planet of [[Toril]], knowledge of lanolin's existence and how to extract it from the coats of sheep dated as far back as the days of the [[Netherese Empire]].<ref>

In the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], lanolin was quite common in the [[Icewind Dale]] area. Being found in such settlements as [[Bryn Shander]] and [[Lonelywood]].

==Usages==
* This substance was known and used in many of the cold and temperate regions of Toril.
* Lanolin was commonly applied to materials to protect them from water and prevent rust.
