Soda ash was a substance used for a variety of purposes, and included the mineral salt natron.
Soda ash could be bought from Auroras Emporium for 3 sp per , intended for use in a wizards laboratory.
* The animate fire spell used some soda ash, charcoal, and sulfur.
* The vitriolic sphere spell could be neutralized by soda ash, charcoal, lye, or water.
* A carbonate of soda such as natron was used in mummification as a preservative.
