'''Oil of vitriol''' was a strong [[acid]] distilled from [[green vitriol]].

==Properties==
The substance was highly corrosive and dangerous to handle. It had an oily, clear appearance.{{Cite organized play/LC/In the Hall of the Mountain Mage|5}}

==Usages==
* The acid was used in all manner of trades, such as the [[dye]]ing and preparation of some [[fabric]]s, and turning [[cashmere]] [[wool]] into cashmere yarn.
* Oil of vitriol was used to dissolve [[tin]] for coating [[steel]], making [[tinplate]].
* It could be distilled with [[alum]] and [[nitre]] to create the stronger acid [[aqua fortis]].
