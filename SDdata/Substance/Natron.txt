'''Natron''' was a mineral salt used for a variety of purposes, and a type of [[soda ash]].

==Availability==
Natron was harvested from mud at the bottom of the sea.

==Usages==
* Natron could be used as a [[soap]].
* Natron was an ingredient in [[glass]]making.
* Natron was used to turn [[wool]]len [[fleece]] into [[felt]].
* Natron was an ingredient of an ancient [[Calim Empire|Calim]] potion that would instil long-term sleep or paralysis.
* Sea mud natron was used in ancient Calim to perform [[Mummy|mummification]], as it dried out the flesh.
