'''''Dungeon'' issue 63''' was released in January/February of 1997. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Huzza's Goblin O'War==
: ''By [[Paul Culotta]], illustrated by [[Stephen Schwartz]].'' '''p. 30-33'''
[[File:Huzzas-Goblin-OWar.png|thumb|left|100px]]
A short encounter with a most unusual boarding party. Module for 4-7 PC's of levels 4-6.

==Blood and Fire==
: ''By [[John Baichtal]], illustrated by [[Tony DiTerlizzi]], Cartography by [[Stephen Daniele]].'' '''p. 34-53, 57'''
[[File:Blood-and-Fire.png|thumb|left|100px]]
The Land of Fate is known for its hot sands and fiery passions. You must survive both to restore order to the city. An ''[[Al-Qadim]]'' module for 5-7 PC's of levels 7-9.
