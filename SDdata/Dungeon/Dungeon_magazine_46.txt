'''''Dungeon'' issue 46''' was released in March/April of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Floating Rock==
: ''By [[Steve Kurtz]], illustrated by [[David O. Miller]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 24-26'''
Meet the strangest band of pirates on the seas. Meet their ship, too. A 3-page Side Trek ''[[Al-Qadim]]'' adventure for a party of 5th to 9th level PCs. The PC's ship is attacked by a unique group of [[bugbear]] pirates on the high seas. {{Dusup|46/Floating Rock}}

==The Iron Orb of the Duergar==
{{Canon|adap}}
: ''By [[Peter Aberg]], illustrated by [[David O. Miller]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 50-75'''
The Northmen are on a rampage, and only the [[duergar]] know why. Discover the secret of the iron orb. A 22-page AD&D adventure for a party of 6-8 characters totaling 70-100 levels. The PCs are asked or hired to end a devastating series of raids by Northmen who possess a powerful artifact. This adventure can be adapted to the ''Forgotten Realms'' setting, with the adventure location replaced with [[Icewind Dale]] or [[Damara]]. {{Dusup|46/The Iron Orb of the Duergar}}
