'''''Dungeon'' issue 61''' was released in September/October of 1996. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Storm Season==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 61/Storm Season}}
: ''By [[Paul Culotta]], illustrated by [[Tony DiTerlizzi]], Cartography by [[Michael Scott]].'' '''p. 28-43'''
Bizarre thunderstorms in the city of [[Arabel]] are only the beginning. Who is killing all the city's mages? Module for 6 PC's of levels 7-12.
<br/><br/><br/>

==To Save a Forest==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 61/To Save a Forest}}
: ''By [[Dovjoser Anderson]], illustrated by [[Tony DiTerlizzi]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 44-58'''
[[File:To save a forest.png|thumb|left|301x301px]]
Only you can prevent forest curses! Module for 4-6 [[good]]- and [[neutral]]-aligned characters of levels 4-6 (24 or more total levels).
