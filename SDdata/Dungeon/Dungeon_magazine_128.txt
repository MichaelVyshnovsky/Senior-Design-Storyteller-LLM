==Vampires of Waterdeep: The Fireplace Level==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 128/Vampires of Waterdeep: The Fireplace Level}}
: ''By [[Eric L. Boyd]], illustrated by [[Chad du Lac]], [[Mark Sinclair]], and [[Gala Ferriere]], Cartography by [[Rob Lazzaretti]].'' '''pp. 72&ndash;93'''
As the party faces an ancient foe who has never been defeated, their hunt threatens to unleash the buried ghosts of a forgotten feud between two of [[Waterdeep]]'s powerful merchant houses.  Part three of Vampires of Waterdeep. Module for 4 PC's of level 14, scaleable for levels 12&ndash;16.{{Dusup|128/Vampires of Waterdeep: The Fireplace Level}}
