'''''Dungeon'' issue 181''' was released in August of 2010 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Campaign Workshop: Hero’s Battle: Elminster==
: ''By [[Chris Sims]], illustrated by [[Alex Aparin]].'' '''p. 38-42'''
[[Elminster]] has been driven mad by the [[spellplague]] and the death of his beloved [[Simbul]], and in his rage and anguish, he’s laying waste to the countryside. Someone needs to stop him, but it won’t be easy. A dangerous encounter for high paragon or low epic characters.

==[[Eye on the Realms]]: The Circle of Fangs==
: ''By [[Ed Greenwood]], illustrated by [[William O'Connor]].'' '''p. 74-76'''
The ''[[circle of fangs]]'' is an enigmatic magic device of unknown origin. Its purpose is clear, but who made it? {{Dusup|181/Eye on the Realms: The Circle of Fangs}}
