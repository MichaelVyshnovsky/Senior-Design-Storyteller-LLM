'''''Dungeon'' issue 45''' was released in January/February of 1994. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==An Artist's Errand==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 45/An Artist's Errand}}
: ''By [[Steve Kurtz]], illustrated by [[L. A. Williams]].'' '''p. 8&ndash;31'''
A [[reigar]] hires adventurers to retrieve a stolen necklace. This adventure is set in [[Pirtelspace]] and is suitable for 4&ndash;6 player characters of levels 6&ndash;8.{{Dusup|45/An Artist's Errand}}

==All Things Nice==
: ''By [[John Baichtal]], illustrated by [[Ken Kosowicz]], cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 32&ndash;33,49'''
Some scoundrels can rob you with a smile. This Side Trek adventure is set on the [[Sword Coast]] and is for a party of 4&ndash;6 player characters of levels 2&ndash;4 (about 15 totals levels).{{Dusup|45/All Things Nice}}
