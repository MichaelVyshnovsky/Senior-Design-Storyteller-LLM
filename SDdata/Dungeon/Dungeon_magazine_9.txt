'''''Dungeon'' issue 9''' was released in January/February of 1988. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==The Golden Bowl of Ashu H'San==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 9/The Golden Bowl of Ashu H'San}}
: ''By [[Rick Swan]], Illustrations by [[Richard Bennett]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 35&ndash;43'''
An angry spirit can even steal the rain. This adventure is intended for 3-5 ''[[Oriental Adventures]]'' characters of 2nd-4th levels. {{Dusup|9/The Golden Bowl of Ashu H'San}}
