'''''Dungeon'' issue 144''' was released in March of 2007. It contained the following article related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==The Muster of Morach Tor==
: ''By [[Russell Brown]], illustrated by [[UDON]], Cartography by [[Robert Lazzaretti]].'' '''p. 16-26'''
This adventure is set in the [[Evermoors]] and involves a failed attempt by the town of [[Nesmé]] to hire [[lizardfolk]] mercenaries to push back attacks from [[troll]]s and [[giant]]s. Module for 4 PC's of levels 4. {{Dusup|144/The Muster of Morach Tor}}
