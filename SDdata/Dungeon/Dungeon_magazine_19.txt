'''''Dungeon'' issue 19''' was released in September/October of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==The Serpent's Tooth==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 19/The Serpent's Tooth}}
: ''By [[Nigel Findley]], illustrated by [[Valerie Valusek]], cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 17-31'''
Someone would like you to spy on a tavern&mdash;but not just any tavern. Module for 4-8 PC's of levels 3-6. {{Dusup|19/The Serpent's Tooth}}

==House of Cards==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 19/House of Cards}}

An adventure centering on a ''[[deck of many things]]''. Module for 4-6 PC's of levels 9-12. {{Dusup|19/House of Cards}}
