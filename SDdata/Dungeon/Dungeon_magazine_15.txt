'''''Dungeon'' issue 15''' was released in January/February of 1989. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==The Dragon's Gift==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 15/The Dragon's Gift}}
: ''By [[Thomas M. Kane]], illustrated by [[Stanley Fukuoka]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 24-29'''
The adventurer's are dispatched to answer a dragon's request, but things are not always as simple as they seem. ''[[Oriental Adventures]]'' module for 2-8 PC's of levels 2-7. {{Dusup|15/The Dragon's Gift}}

==The Glass House==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 15/The Glass House}}
: ''By [[Wolfgang Baur]], illustrated by [[George Barr]], Cartography by [[Diesel]]..'' '''p. 31-38'''
Slain by evil forces who destroyed his home, a Harper seeks revenge from beyond the grave. A module for 2-8 PC's of levels 4-6. {{Dusup|15/The Glass House}}
