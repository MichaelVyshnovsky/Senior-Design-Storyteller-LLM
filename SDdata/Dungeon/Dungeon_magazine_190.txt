'''''Dungeon'' issue 190''' was released in May of 2011 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==[[Eye on the Realms]]: The Wild Lords==
: ''By [[Ed Greenwood]], illustrated by [[Patrick McEvoy]].'' '''p. 1-3'''
Of old, the nobles of [[Waterdeep]] took haughty pride in their bloodlines and birthrights. Few could marry into the ranks of the nobles without being swallowed up in the manners and trappings of nobility, and nobles shunned those who refused to act “higherborn than the common unwashed,” as one broadsheet put it. {{Dusup|174/Eye on the Realms: The Way of Lost Power}}
