'''''Dungeon'' issue 34''' was released in March/April of 1992. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Euphoria Horrors==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 34/Euphoria Horrors}}
: ''By [[Alan Grimes]], illustrated by [[Steve Schwartz]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 8-20'''
Can you find a little boy's missing friend? The description he gives you is very strange. Module for 4-6 PC's of levels 1-2. {{Dusup|34/Euphoria Horrors}}

==On Wings of Darkness==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 34/On Wings of Darkness}}
: ''By [[Craig Barrett]], illustrated by [[Ken Widing]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 54-71'''
Summer in [[Calimshan]] brings a "simple" monster hunt in the cool mountain air. What could be easier? Module for 4-6 PC's of levels 5-8. Recommended PC mix is two to four warriors, a wizard, and a cleric or druid. {{Dusup|34/On Wings of Darkness}}
