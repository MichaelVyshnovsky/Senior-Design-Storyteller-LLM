'''''Dungeon'' issue 73''' was released in March/April of 1999. It contained the following article related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Mere of Dead Men: Eye of Myrkul==
: ''By [[Eric L. Boyd]], illustrated by [[Stephen Daniele]], Cartography by [[Stephen Daniele]].'' '''p. 22-53'''
This adventure is the final part of the "[[Mere of Dead Men]]" series started in [[Dungeon magazine 69|''Dungeon'' #69]] and involves retrieving the bones of a [[dracolich]] to save a [[paladin]]. The adventure is related to the [[Ring of Myrkul]] and involves an encounter with the [[Sentinel of Uthtower|sentinel]] of [[Uthtower]].{{Dusup|73/Eye of Myrkul}}

{{Quote|''(AD&D FORGOTTEN REALMS Adventure, character levels 6-8; 56 total levels). Retrieve the bones of a dracolich to save the life of a paladin. The epic conclusion of the [[Mere of Dead Men]] series!''}}
