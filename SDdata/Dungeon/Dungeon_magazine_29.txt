'''''Dungeon'' issue 29''' was released in May/June of 1991. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Nymph's Reward==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 29/Nymph's Reward}}
: ''By [[Jeff Fairbourn]], illustrated by [[Kevin Ward]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 8–24'''
First impressions are sometimes dead wrong.  Module for 5–7 PC's of levels 4–6. {{Dusup|29/Nymph's Reward}}

==Ex Libris==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 29/Ex Libris}}
: ''By [[Randy Maxwell]], illustrated by [[Tom Baxa]].'' '''p. 26–45'''
A dungeon that offers a truly moving experience. Module for 4–7 PC's of levels 5–8. {{Dusup|29/Ex Libris}}

=='Til Death Do Us Part==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 29/'Til Death Do Us Part}}
: ''By [[J. Mark Bicking]], illustrated by [[Terry Dykstra]].'' '''p. 50–57'''
They were joined for better or for worse—mostly for worse. Module for 5–6 PC's of levels 8–10. {{Dusup|29/'Til Death Do Us Part}}
