'''''Dungeon'' issue 210''' was released in January of 2013 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==The Vulture's Feast ==
: ''By [[Brian Cortijo]] and [[Christopher Perkins]], illustrated by [[Kieran Yanner]], Cartography by [[Mike Schley]].'' '''p. 2–20'''
{{Quote|''An [[Aleta Vanmarwin|evil warlord]] has prepared a feast for her new [[Zhentarim]] allies, and she has a [[Kimach Taias|gift]] for them as well. The [[Harpers]] need your help to break this unholy alliance. A D&D ''Forgotten Realms'' adventure for characters of levels 6–8.''{{Dusup|210/The Vulture's Feast}}}}
