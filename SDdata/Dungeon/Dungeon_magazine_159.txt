'''''Dungeon'' issue 159''' was released in October of 2008 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Menace of the Icy Spire==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 159/Menace of the Icy Spire}}
: ''By [[M. Sean Molley|Sean Molley]], illustrated by [[Rob Alexander]], [[Ben Wootten]] and [[Ron Lemen]], Cartography by [[Kyle Hunter]].'' '''p. 4-22'''
New to [[Loudwater]], the heroes learn of a tower locked in ice in the nearby wilderness. Traveling there, they discover that the tower's master left many surprises behind for the unwary. Module for 5 PC's of level 2. {{Dusup|159/Menace of the Icy Spire}}
