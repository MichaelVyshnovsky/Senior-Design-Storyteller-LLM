'''Dungeon magazine 87''' was the 87th issue of [[Dungeon magazine|''Dungeon'' magazine]]. It was released in July/August 2001.  It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==The Raiders of Galath's Roost==
: ''By [[Skip Williams]]. Artwork by [[rk post]]. Cartography by [[Craig Zipse]].'' '''p. 12-46'''{{dusup|87/The Raiders of Galath's Roost}}
The secret to the raiders' sinister plot lies amid the ruins of a forlorn keep. 
===Summary===
This is an adventure set in [[Mistledale]] using 3rd edition rules. The adventure focuses on the [[Zhentarim]]'s attempt to cause havoc in the dale under the cover of the recent [[drow]] raids.
