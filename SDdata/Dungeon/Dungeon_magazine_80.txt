'''''Dungeon'' issue 80''' was released in May/June of 2000. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==The Frothing Miscreant==
: ''By [[Robert A. Van Buskirk]], illustrated by [[James Crabtree]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 30-39'''
[[File:The-Frothing-Miscreant.png|thumb|left|100px]]
An ill-tempered gnome takes revenge to new heights and proves to the world that he's no small threat. Module for 4-6 PC's of levels 2-4.
