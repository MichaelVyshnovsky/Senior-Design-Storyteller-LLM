==Honor Lost, Honor Regained==
{{Main|Dungeon magazine 48/Honor Lost, Honor Regained}}
: ''By [[Paul Hamilton Beattie Jr.]], illustrated by [[Tony DiTerlizzi]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 66-70'''
"Come into my parlor" said the spider to the paladin. An adventure for a party of 3–5 characters of levels 4–6 (18–20 total levels). The adventure setting is in the frontiers of [[Cormyr]]. The main NPC is a [[paladin]] of [[Helm]], so no player characters should be overtly evil.{{Dusup|48/Honor Lost, Honor Regained}}
