==The Whispering Cairn==
{{Canon|adap}}
: ''By [[Erik Mona]], illustrated by [[Joachim Barrum]], [[Steve Prescott]], and [[Eva Widermann]], Cartography by [[Rob Lazzaretti]].'' '''p. 14–47'''
A handful of would-be [[adventurer]]s gathers to explore an ancient tomb, eager to trade their discoveries for a ticket to a better life. What they find within presages the advent of the [[Age of Worms]], an era of darkness, decay, and writhing doom. A D&D adventure for 1st-level characters.{{Cite web enhancement/Age of Worms Overload|26-29}}{{Cite web enhancement/Dungeon 124 Map and Handout Supplement|10-11}}
