'''''Dungeon'' issue 206''' was released in September of 2012 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==The Xanathar: Crime Lord of Waterdeep==
: ''By [[Derek Myers]], illustrated by [[Jason Juta]].'' '''p. 38-43'''
Meet a [[Xanathar|monstrous criminal mastermind]] who has his eyes—all eleven of them—on the [[Waterdeep|City of Splendors]] and its many riches. {{Dusup|206/The Xanathar: Crime Lord of Waterdeep}}

==Eyes on the Ball==
: ''By [[Derek Myers]], illustrated by [[Kai Carpenter]] and [[im Pavelec]], Cartography by [[Jason A. Engle]] and [[Mike Schley]].'' '''p. 45-69'''
A beholder crime lord needs your help to thwart a common enemy, and the job pays well. It’s an offer you dare not refuse. A D&D Forgotten Realms adventure for characters of levels 6–8. {{Dusup|206/Eyes on the Ball}}
