==The Artisan's Tomb==
: ''By [[Matthew Maaske]], illustrated by [[Richard Bennett]], Cartography by [[Diesel]].'' '''p. 9-11'''
In ''[[Oriental Adventures]]'', honor can reach beyond the grave. Adventure for 4-8 PC's of levels 3-5. {{Dusup|10/The Artisan's Tomb}}
