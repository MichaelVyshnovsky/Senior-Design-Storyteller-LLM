'''''Dungeon'' issue 54''' was released in July/August of 1995. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==The Fiends of Tethyr==
: ''By [[David Howery]], illustrated by [[David O. Miller]].'' '''p. 20-27, 32'''
Fiends are dangerous enough, but something even more dangerous plagues [[Tethyr]]. Module for 5-7 PC's of levels 6-8. {{Dusup|54/The Fiends of Tethyr}}
