'''''Dungeon'' issue 178''' was released in May of 2010 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Backdrop: Chessenta==
: ''By [[Brian James]], illustrated by [[Christine MacTernan]], Cartography by [[Mike Schley]].'' '''p. 68-77'''
[[Chessenta]] is a land of feuding city-states vying for the favor of ancient gods through blood sport and conquest ... yet its people have inquisitive minds and an infectious zest for life. {{Dusup|178/Backdrop: Chessenta}}
