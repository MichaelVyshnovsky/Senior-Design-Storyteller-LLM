'''''Dungeon'' issue 196''' was released in November of 2011 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Court of Stars: Baba Yaga, Mother of All Witches==
: ''By [[Alana Abbott]], illustrated by [[Goran Josic]].'' '''p. 6-10'''
[[Baba Yaga]], the hag who eats heroes and places their skulls on [[Pike (weapon)|pikes]], has a long history of being one of the most evil and dangerous old women in folklore. {{Dusup|196/Court of Stars: Baba Yaga}}

==Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles==
: ''By [[Shawn Merwin]], illustrated by [[Dave Rapoza]], Cartography by [[Mike Schley]].'' '''p. 11-28'''
The [[Moonshae Isles]] are in the western portion of the [[Sea of Swords]], near where that body of water leads to the uncharted expanses of the [[Trackless Sea]]. {{Dusup|196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==[[Eye on the Realms]]: The Ghost Knight of Galardoun==
: ''By [[Ed Greenwood]], illustrated by [[Wayne England]].'' '''p. 36-38'''
Throughout the [[Heartlands]] of [[Faerûn]], word has spread of an eerie apparition that many folk have encountered in dark and lonely places. {{Dusup|196/Eye on the Realms: The Ghost Knight of Galardoun}}

==Baba Yaga's Dancing Hut==
: ''By [[Craig Campbell]], illustrated by [[Noah Bradley]] and [[Bernadette Carstensen]].'' '''p. 52-107'''
An exploration of [[Baba Yaga's Hut]] and all its hazards and inhabitants. {{Dusup|196/Baba Yaga's Dancing Hut}}

==Monster Manual Updates: [[Choker]]==
: ''By [[Logan Bonner]], illustrated by [[Izzy]].'' '''p. 108-109'''
The greedy chokers hide in caverns and among trees, attacking and strangling victims with their flexible arms. {{Dusup|196/Monster Manual Updates: Choker}}
