'''''Dungeon'' issue 171''' was released in October of 2009 in online format only. It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Treed!==
: ''By [[Tim Eagon]], illustrated by [[Stephen Crowe]], Cartography by [[Kyle S. Hunter]].'' '''p. 15-22'''
An [[Owlbear]] has been tormenting a tribe of [[Elves]] and trapped a [[Gnome]] from the [[Feywild]]. The PCs have the opportunity to find passage to the Feywild and earn the elves trust if they act quickly. Module for 5 PC's of level 7.

==The Eye of Justice==
: ''By [[Eytan Bernstein]] and [[Erik Scott de Bie]], illustrated by [[Sarah Stoner]].'' '''p. 89-97'''
The [[Eye of Justice]] is a heretical. hypervigilant branch of those faithful to [[Torm]], god of justice.

==Campaign Workshop: Hero Battle: Drizzt==
: ''By [[Chris Sims]] and [[Chris Youngs]], illustrated by [[Evan Shipard ]], Cartography by [[Jason Engle]].'' '''p. 98-103'''
Face off against the legendary dark elf hero in this ready-to-play encounter, with several hooks for possible expansion into a full-scale adventure.
