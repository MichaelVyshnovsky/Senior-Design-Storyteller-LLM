Secrets of Sokol Keep, designed by Pieter Sleijpen, is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5th edition ruleset. It is part of the Tyranny of Dragons series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Phlan, and is an adventure for 3 to 7 characters of 1- to 4- levels.
