'''''The Vampire of Skullport''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Waterdeep|Waterdeep]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Skullport]], and is an adventure designed for 11{{th}}- to 16{{th}}-level characters. It is part one of the ''Undying Threat'' Trilogy.

{{Quote|''Something is wrong in the endless gloom of Skullport, and the Baron of Blood has tasked you with setting things right.''|}}
