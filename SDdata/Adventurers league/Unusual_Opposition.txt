'''''Unusual Opposition''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Tomb of Annihilation|Tomb of Annihilation]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Chult]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 11{{th}}- to 16{{th}}-level characters. 

{{Quote|''The trail leading from the Fane of the Whispered Fang has grown cold, but you can still taste the machinations of the yuan-ti in Chult’s humid, stagnant air. Because of this, you must venture deeper still into the jungle and petition the aid of an unlikely ally—the fabled Ramshackle King. His assistance is crucial to the effort to save Chult!''|}}
