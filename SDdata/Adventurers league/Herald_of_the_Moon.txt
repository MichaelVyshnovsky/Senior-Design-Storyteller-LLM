'''''Herald of the Moon''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Rage of Demons|Rage of Demons]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Cormanthyr]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 5{{th}}- to 10{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''Elanil Elassidil, an elf bard of no small legacy, has put out a call for trustworthy agents. It is time, it seems, for a quietly hidden piece of elven history to become known to the world. Meira Faerenduil, lost knight of Myth Drannor, has been discovered, and must be brought back to civilization.''|}}
