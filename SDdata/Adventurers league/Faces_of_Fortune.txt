Faces of Fortune The Story of Fai Chen is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Avernus Rising series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Avernus, and is an adventure designed for 5- to 10-level characters. It is part one of the Behind Infernal Lines series.
