'''''The Courting of Fire''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Tyranny of Dragons|Tyranny of Dragons]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Phlan]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1st-4th level characters.

{{Quote|''An exiled cultist and his [[kobold]] minions are spotted searching for long-forgotten ruins in the [[Dragonspire Mountains]]. Rumors say he looks for a precious gift to give a fearsome dragon that dwells there. What he hopes to attain with his gift is unknown, but can't be good for the citizens of [[Phlan]].''|}}
