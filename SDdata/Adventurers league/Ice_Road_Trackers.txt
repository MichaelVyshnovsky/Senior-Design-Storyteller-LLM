'''''Ice Road Trackers''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Plague of Ancients|Plague of Ancients]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Icewind Dale]], and is an adventure designed for 1{{st}}- to 2{{nd}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''A trek toward Icewind Dale turns deadly when an avalanche blocks the heroes’ passage through the [[Spine of the World]]. Rescued by a menagerie of strange creatures, the heroes are asked to return the favor in exchange for answers about what’s happening in the cold and frozen North.''|}}
