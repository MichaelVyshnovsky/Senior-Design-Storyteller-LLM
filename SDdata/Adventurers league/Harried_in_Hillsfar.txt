'''''Harried in Hillsfar''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Rage of Demons|Rage of Demons]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Hillsfar]], the village of [[Elventree]], and the [[Cormanthor]] forest, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 2{{nd}}-level characters.
{{Quote|''In the village of Elventree, near the oppressive city of Hillsfar, a recent string of strange occurrences has the locals on edge. The factions have gathered here on the borders of the forest of Cormanthor to determine what's happening. Is this the machinations of Hillsfar, or something more? Five one-hour mini-adventures for 1st–2nd level characters.''|}}

==Summary==
The adventure starts off as adventurers stumble across a madman on the [[Moonsea Ride]], just south of [[Hillsfar]]. His ravings prophesy strange occurrences around the city and the [[Cormanthor]] forest. Upon investigating the sites mentioned, the adventurers encounter [[demon]]ic goats, a diseased [[drow]], a disputed [[Spring of Blessed Life|spring]], an old outpost of [[House Dlardrageth]], and a neglected church of [[Waukeen]].
