In Dire Need is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Storm Kings Thunder series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Stagwick, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 5- to 10-level characters.
