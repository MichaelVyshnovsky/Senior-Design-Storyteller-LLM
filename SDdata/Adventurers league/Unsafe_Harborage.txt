'''''Unsafe Harborage''''', designed by [[Greg Marks]] and [[Martine Lassen]], is an adventure in the [[Adventurers League/Dreams of the Red Wizards|Dreams of the Red Wizards]] storyline published for the [[Adventurers League]]. It is the second part of the Shadow Games series.

{{Quote|''Thayan agents plague Waterdeep's Dock Ward and you're tasked with discovering who's pulling their strings, but you aren't the only ones interested in the Red Wizards.''{{Cite adventurers league/Unsafe Harborage|1}}|}}
