'''''Breaking Umberlee's Resolve''''', designed by [[Ashley Warren]] and [[Chris Lindsay]], is an adventure in the [[Adventurers League/Dreams of the Red Wizards|Dreams of the Red Wizards]] storyline published for the [[Adventurers League]]. It is the first part of the Sea of Fallen Stars series.

{{Quote|''The search for a missing young woman kicks off a treacherous journey on the Sea of Fallen Stars.''{{Cite adventurers league/Breaking Umberlee's Resolve|1}}|}}
