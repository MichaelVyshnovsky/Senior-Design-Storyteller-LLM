'''''The Ark of the Mountains''''' is an Epic Adventures module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Storm King's Thunder|Storm King's Thunder]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Beregost]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 16{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''The flying galleon Thunderbound descends on Beregost, unleashing the wrath of the cloud giants! To save the town from annihilation, heroes from far and wide must awaken the lost machine entombed under Durlag’s Tower. Dare you board the Ark of the Mountains and do battle in the burning skies?''|}}
