'''''A Wrinkle in the Weave''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Waterdeep|Waterdeep]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Waterdeep]], and is an adventure designed for 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters. It is part one of the ''Folded Time'' Trilogy.

{{Quote|''The [[Blackstaff]] has discovered an oddity in the [[Weave]] that surrounds Waterdeep. The enigma may have been there all along or it might be new, and the Blackstaff intends to figure it out using an ancient artifact from the reign of Emperor [[Shoon III]].''|}}
