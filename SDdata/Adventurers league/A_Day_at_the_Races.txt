'''''A Day at the Races''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Tomb of Annihilation|Tomb of Annihilation]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Port Nyanzaru]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters. It is the first part of the ''The Jungle Has Fangs'' trilogy.
