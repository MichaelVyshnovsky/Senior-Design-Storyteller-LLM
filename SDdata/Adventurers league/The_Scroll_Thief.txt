'''''The Scroll Thief''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Tyranny of Dragons|Tyranny of Dragons]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Phlan]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1st-4th level characters.

{{Quote|''Scholars Square is a relatively quiet corner of Phlan, but a series of odd thefts have the headmasters of verious schools in the area concerned. The headmasters' pleas for help have gone unanswered by the Black Fist, and the Lord Sage of Phlan decided to reach out to you and your kind to bring those responsible to justice.''|}}
