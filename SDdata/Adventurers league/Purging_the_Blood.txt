'''''Purging the Blood''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Waterdeep|Waterdeep]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Waterdeep]], and is an adventure designed for 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters. It is part three of the ''Folded Time'' Trilogy.

{{Quote|''Still falling through time, Waterdeep has grown into a small city with a problem, a vampire problem, but an old friend offers a solution.''|}}
