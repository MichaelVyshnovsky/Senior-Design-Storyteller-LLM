'''''Vile Bounty''''', designed by [[Greg Marks]], is an adventure in the [[Adventurers League/Dreams of the Red Wizards|Dreams of the Red Wizards]] storyline published for the [[Adventurers League]]. It is the first part of the Shadow Games series.

{{Quote|''Ships have gone missing in the Sea of Swords and Queen Serissa wants to know why.''{{Cite adventurers league/Vile Bounty|1}}|}}
