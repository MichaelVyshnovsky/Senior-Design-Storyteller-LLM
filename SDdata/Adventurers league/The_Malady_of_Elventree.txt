'''''The Malady of Elventree''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Rage of Demons|Rage of Demons]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Elventree]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 5{{th}}- to 10{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''An escaped duergar slave stumbles into the village of Elventree. With her she brings a malady that grips the small settlement in a bout of madness that seems unshakable. Can you find the source of the madness and save yourself and the village’s inhabitants?''|}}
