'''''Embers of Elmwood''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Elemental Evil|Elemental Evil]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Mulmaster]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''A derelict raft drifts into [[Mulmaster]]’s harbor carrying a young woman. Delirious with hunger, the only words she seems able to speak so far have been “ash and fire.” The only other clue to her identity is an ornate tattoo. Is the woman insane, or is something nefarious at play?''|}}
