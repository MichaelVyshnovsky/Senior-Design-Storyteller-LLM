'''''A Change of Address''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Waterdeep|Waterdeep]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Undermountain]], and is an adventure designed for 17{{th}}- to 20{{th}}-level characters. It is part one of the ''Home Sweet Home'' Trilogy.

{{Quote|''The hand behind all of the Baron of Blood’s troubles is revealed, and he vows to make himself the worst kind of neighbor.''|}}
