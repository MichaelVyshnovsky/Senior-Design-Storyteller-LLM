Suits of the Mists is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Curse of Strahd series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Barovia and Phlan, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1- to 2-level characters. It is part one of Misty Fortunes and Absent Hearts.
