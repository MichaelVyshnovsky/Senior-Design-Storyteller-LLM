'''''Beneath the City of the Dead''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Waterdeep|Waterdeep]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Waterdeep]], and is an adventure designed for 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters. It is part two of the ''Umbral Aristocracy'' Trilogy.

{{Quote|''The treasure hunt has led to the City of the Dead. Beneath that well-manicured park is an ancient
crypt where the treasure awaits you!''|}}
