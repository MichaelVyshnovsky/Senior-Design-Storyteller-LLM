'''''Expedition to the Supreme Forge''''', designed by [[Greg Marks]] and [[Paul Gabat]], is an adventure in the [[Adventurers League/Dreams of the Red Wizards|Dreams of the Red Wizards]] storyline published for the [[Adventurers League]]. It is the fourth part of The Cold Dark series.

{{Quote|''Allies reveal the location of the Supreme Forge: the origin of Xorvintroth's unique blend of artifice and magic. The answers to controlling the city's dangerous artifacts lie within for those brave enough to recover them.''{{Cite adventurers league/Expedition to the Supreme Forge|1}}|}}
