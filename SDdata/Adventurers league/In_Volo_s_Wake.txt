'''''In Volo's Wake''''' is an [[Adventurers League]] module set in the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'' using the ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is an introductory adventure for ''[[Volo's Guide to Monsters]]'', and was designed by [[Monica Valentinelli]], [[Shawn Merwin]], and [[Rich Lescouflair]]. It focuses on events in the vicinity of [[Phandalin]], and is designed for 1<sup>st</sup>&ndash;to 4{{th}} level characters.

{{Quote|''The town of Phandalin, nestled in the foothills to the east of the [[Sword Mountains]], continues to grow since being rebuilt after the devastation wrought by rampaging [[orc]] hordes. It recently survived violent [[Redbrands|gang activity]], [[Nezznar|drow machinations]], and even the attention of a [[Venomfang|green dragon]]. But can it withstand a new threat, bizarre but no less perilous: the fame brought by a visit from none other than loremaster [[Volothamp Geddarm]]?''{{Cite adventurers league/In Volo's Wake|1}}|}}

==Synopsis==
''In Volo's Wake'' consists of six "mini-adventures", each of which is intended to take 1-2 hours and highlight one or more creatures from the ''Volo's Guide to Monsters'' sourcebook:
;''The Green Skin of Treachery'': The party seeks to rescue a noble named [[Eric Merryweather]] from [[blight]]s and [[goblin]]s in the [[Crooked Forest]].
;''The Mottled Fur of Rage'': [[Sildar Hallwinter]] asks the party to rescue his [[Alana Hallwinter|adopted daughter]] from [[gnoll]]s in the Crooked Forest.
;''The Filthy Claws of Sin'': The party travels to [[Neverwinter Wood]] to find a missing [[elf]] named [[Delsy]], and they encounter a trio of [[hag]]s.
;''The Cold Scales of Death'': The party travels toward [[Old Owl Well]] to find an eccentric man known as Master [[Aumaro]], who seeks their help to defeat a group of [[yuan-ti]].
;''The Tentacled Maw of Evil'': The party assists a [[mind flayer]] named [[Cerali]] to discover what has caused its [[elder brain]] to act strangely.
;''The Many Eyes of Doom'': A mysterious [[beholder]] known as [[Gavmogon]] has been behind the strife encountered by the party in the preceding adventures, and so they travel to its sanctum to end the threat.
