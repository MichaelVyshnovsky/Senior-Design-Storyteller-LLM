'''''The Skull Square Murders''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Waterdeep|Waterdeep]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Skullport]], and is an adventure designed for 5{{th}}- to 10{{th}}-level characters. It is part one of the ''Skullport Shakedown'' Trilogy.

{{Quote|''When Volothamp Geddarm hires you to recover a missing key, you find yourself wrapped up in a series of murders in Lower Skullport. Can you find the killer before they find you?''|}}
