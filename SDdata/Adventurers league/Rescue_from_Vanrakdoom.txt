Rescue from Vanrakdoom is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Waterdeep series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Undermountain, and is an adventure designed for 11- to 16-level characters. It is part two of the Undying Threat Trilogy.
