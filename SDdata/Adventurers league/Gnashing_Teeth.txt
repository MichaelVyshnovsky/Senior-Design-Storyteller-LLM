'''''Gnashing Teeth''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Plague of Ancients|Plague of Ancients]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Icewind Dale]], and is an adventure designed for 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''The goliaths of Wyrmdoom Crag are a proud, but thankfully, generous people. Having provided you with shelter from the deadly wilds of Icewind Dale, they’ve asked you to look into the strange things that have been happening in and around their home.''|}}
