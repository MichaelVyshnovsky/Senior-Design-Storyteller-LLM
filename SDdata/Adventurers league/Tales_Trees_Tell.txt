'''''Tales Trees Tell''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Tyranny of Dragons|Tyranny of Dragons]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Phlan]] and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''Despite the shaky alliance that exists with the elves of the [[Quivering Forest]], they do not suffer trespass in their realm lightly, especially from common folk from nearby Phlan. A woodworker's recent blunder into the forest might set off a diplomatic incident. Can you help find him and mollify the aggravated elves?''|}}
