'''''Uncertain Scrutiny''''', designed by [[Greg Marks]] and [[Ben Heisler]], is an adventure in the [[Adventurers League/Dreams of the Red Wizards|Dreams of the Red Wizards]] storyline published for the [[Adventurers League]]. It is the second part of the Storm King’s Descent series. 

{{Quote|''In search of King Hekaton, you must enter the dread land of Thay—but getting in will prove less difficult than getting out.''{{Cite adventurers league/Uncertain Scrutiny|1}}|}}
