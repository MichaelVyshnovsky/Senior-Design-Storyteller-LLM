'''''Shackles of Blood''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Rage of Demons|Rage of Demons]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Hillsfar]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''The Red Plumes have increased patrols in the region surrounding Hillsfar, and a string of disappearances has followed in their wake. Naturally, this has roused the suspicion of the factions. Join your factions and find out the truth behind the missing farmers. ''|}}
