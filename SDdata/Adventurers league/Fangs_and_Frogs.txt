'''''Fangs and Frogs''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Waterdeep|Waterdeep]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Undermountain]], and is an adventure designed for 5{{th}}- to 10{{th}}-level characters. It is part three of the ''Vampire Hunt'' Trilogy.

{{Quote|''Your chase to wipe out the vampires of Undermountain leads to the Slitherswamp. The master vampire is close at hand!''|}}
