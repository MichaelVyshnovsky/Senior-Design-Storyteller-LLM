'''''Cloaks and Shadows''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Elemental Evil|Elemental Evil]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Mulmaster]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''The Cloaks of Mulmaster have taken an interest in your progress, and may be looking to sponsor adventurers for future work. You’ve been offered an interview with one of their ranking members at the Theater of the Stars, but you never know what to expect in the City of Danger.''|}}
