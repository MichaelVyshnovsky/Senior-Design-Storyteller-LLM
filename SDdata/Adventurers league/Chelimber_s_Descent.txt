'''''Chelimber's Descent''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Storm King's Thunder|Storm King's Thunder]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on the [[Marsh of Chelimber]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''Deep in the Marsh of Chelimber lie the ruins of a great wizard's tower. SEER has received intelligence, which she imparts via her emissary Hsing, that deep beneath the marsh in the remains of an old tower is knowledge of older magics. This could be just what Parnast needs to resist the onslaught of Bad Fruul and his armies!''|}}
