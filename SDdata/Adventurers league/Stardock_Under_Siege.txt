Stardock Under Siege is an Epic Adventures module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Waterdeep series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Undermountain and Stardock, and is an adventure designed for 1- to 16-level characters.
