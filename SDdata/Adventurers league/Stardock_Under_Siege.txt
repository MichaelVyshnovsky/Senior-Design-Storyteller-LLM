'''''Stardock Under Siege''''' is an Epic Adventures module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Waterdeep|Waterdeep]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Undermountain]] and [[Stardock]], and is an adventure designed for 1{{st}}- to 16{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''An illithid invasion fleet threatens Stardock, and only you and your allies can stop it!''|}}
