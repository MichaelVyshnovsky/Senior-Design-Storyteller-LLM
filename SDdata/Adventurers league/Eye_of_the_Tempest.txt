'''''Eye of the Tempest''''' is a 5{{th}} edition adventure module set in the [[Forgotten Realms]]. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Elemental Evil|Elemental Evil]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It was authored by [[Pieter Sleijpen]] and is an adventure designed for three to seven 5{{th}}- to 10{{th}}-level characters.
{{Quote|''A storm of unparalleled fury has been ravaging the peaks of the [[Earthspur Mountains]] for a [[tenday]], and the [[Monastery of the Yellow Rose]] sits in its eye for now. Some [[monk]]s have fled the monastery to the safety of [[Mulmaster]] and beseech you to convince their more obstinate brothers to retreat to the city before the eye of the storm shifts, and the monastery is in terrible danger. Can you brave the elements and convince the monks to escape?''}}
