Eye of the Tempest is a 5 edition adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms. It is part of the Elemental Evil series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It was authored by Pieter Sleijpen and is an adventure designed for three to seven 5- to 10-level characters.
