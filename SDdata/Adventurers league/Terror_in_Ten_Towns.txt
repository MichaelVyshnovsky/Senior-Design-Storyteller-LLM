''''' Terror in Ten Towns''''' is an Epic Adventures module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Plague of Ancients|Plague of Ancients]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Icewind Dale]], and is an adventure designed for 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''As a beastly army approaches Ten-Towns from the south, heroes take the lead in fortifications and defense. But can the heroes' optimistic hard work overcome the worst enemy - the towns' distrust of one another?''|}}
