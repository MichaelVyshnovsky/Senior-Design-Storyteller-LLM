'''''Whispers in the Dark''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Tomb of Annihilation|Tomb of Annihilation]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Chult]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters. It is the third and final part of the ''The Jungle Has Fangs'' trilogy.

{{Quote|''The temple lay just ahead, and within the answers to a great many questions about the recent goings on in Port Nyanzaru. The journey here has been long and arduous and hopefully soon at an end. Leave your trepidations at the door; there’s exploring to be done!''|}}
