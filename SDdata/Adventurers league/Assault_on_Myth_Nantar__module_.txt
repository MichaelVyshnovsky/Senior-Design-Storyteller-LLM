'''''Assault on Myth Nantar''''' is an epic adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}}-edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Dreams of the Red Wizards]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It is an epic adventure involving multiple parties of adventures, centered around an attempt by the [[Red Wizards]] of [[Thay]] to conquer the fabled city of [[Myth Nantar]]. It is the third part of the Sea of Fallen Stars series.
{{Quote|''Thay has set its sights on taking the underwater city of Myth Nantar. Can you stop a full-on invasion by its undead armada, or will the elves be looking for a new home?''|}}
