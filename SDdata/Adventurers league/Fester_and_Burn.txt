Fester and Burn is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Tomb of Annihilation series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Port Nyanzaru, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 5- to 10-level characters. It is the first part of the The Rot from Within trilogy.
