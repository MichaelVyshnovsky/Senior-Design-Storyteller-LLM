'''''The Harrowing of Hell''''', designed by [[Greg Marks]] and [[Toni Winslow-Brill]], is an adventure in the [[Adventurers League/Dreams of the Red Wizards|Dreams of the Red Wizards]] storyline published for the [[Adventurers League]]. It is the sixth part of the Storm King's Descent series.

{{Quote|''King Hekaton's soul is trapped in the Nine Hells—and the characters must brave trials on Avernus, Acheron, and in the mind of the storm giant king to win it back from the Lord of Lies.''{{Cite adventurers league/The Harrowing of Hell|1}}|}}
