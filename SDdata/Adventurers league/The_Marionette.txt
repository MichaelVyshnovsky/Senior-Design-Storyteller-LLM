'''''The Marionette''''' is an adventure module set in the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'' and ''[[Ravenloft]]'' settings using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Curse of Strahd|Curse of Strahd]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters and is part four of ''Misty Fortunes and Absent Hearts''.

{{Quote|''A Vistani fortuneteller has called you out by name during the Burgomaster’s private reading. She raved of an army of the dead, a delicate powderbox, and a beautiful yet dangerous woman. Now the Burgomaster wants to know why you’re more important than he is… Part Four of Misty Fortunes and Absent Hearts''|}}
