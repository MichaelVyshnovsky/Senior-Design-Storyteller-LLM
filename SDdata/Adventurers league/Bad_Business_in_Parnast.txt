Bad Business in Parnast is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Storm Kings Thunder series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Parnast, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1- to 4-level characters.
