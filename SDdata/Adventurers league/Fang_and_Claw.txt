'''''Fang and Claw''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Avernus Rising|Avernus Rising]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Avernus]], and is an adventure designed for 17{{th}}- to 20{{th}}-level characters. It is part one of the ''Red Hunt'' series.

{{Quote|''Zariel’s Lieutenant General Everbleed has fled with Commander De La Reue’s soul, only to be captured by Yeenoghu’s brood preparing to rampage! Dara implores you to recover the commander, though his soul has been taken to a most unusual place. Part One of the Red Hunt series of adventures.''|}}
