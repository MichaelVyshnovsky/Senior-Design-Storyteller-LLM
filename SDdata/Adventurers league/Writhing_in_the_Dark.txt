'''''Writhing in the Dark''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Rage of Demons|Rage of Demons]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Sporedome]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 5{{th}}- to 10{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''An unexpected envoy arrives in Sporedome and offers to give aid against the fire giants of Maerimydra, if you are willing to help his people with a small problem.''|}}
