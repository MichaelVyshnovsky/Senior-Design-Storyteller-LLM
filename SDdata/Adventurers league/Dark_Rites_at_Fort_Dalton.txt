'''''Dark Rites at Fort Dalton''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Elemental Evil|Elemental Evil]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Mulmaster]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''Fort Dalton along the River Lis was long ago destroyed. Now rumors tell of foul cults practicing their dark rites within the fort’s ruins. Are the rumors of elemental cults true or are the rumors a cover for some other sinister plot?''|}}
