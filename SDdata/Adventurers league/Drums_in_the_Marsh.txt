'''''Drums in the Marsh''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Tyranny of Dragons|Tyranny of Dragons]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Phlan]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''For the past several nights, drums have been heard on the wind, coming from the Twilight Marsh. Each morning, more turn up missing from the nearby farms, leaving only muddy, clawed foot prints heading towards the marsh. Can you help find the lost and bring justice to the abductors?''|}}
