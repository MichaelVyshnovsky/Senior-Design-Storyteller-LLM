A Thousand Tiny Deaths is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Tales from the Yawning Portal series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Blasingdell, and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1- to 4-level characters.
