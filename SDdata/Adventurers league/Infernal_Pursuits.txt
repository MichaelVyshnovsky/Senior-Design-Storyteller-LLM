Infernal Pursuits is an Epic Adventures module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Avernus Rising series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on infernal war machines, and is an adventure designed for 1- to 10-level characters.
