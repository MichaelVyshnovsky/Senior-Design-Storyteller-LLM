'''''Parnast Under Siege''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Storm King's Thunder|Storm King's Thunder]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Parnast]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''Despite the adventurers' best efforts, more creatures joined under Bad Fruul's banner. No longer content to simply attack caravans or isolated homesteads, the hill giant warlord descends upon Parnast at the head of a great force of humanoids. SEER warns the adventurers (directly now) in time to rally the town and prepare defenses, but a heroic effort is required to break the siege.''|}}
