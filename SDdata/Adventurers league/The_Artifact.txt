'''''The Artifact''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Curse of Strahd|Curse of Strahd]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Barovia]] and [[Phlan]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 1{{st}}- to 2{{nd}}-level characters. It is part ten of ''Misty Fortunes and Absent Hearts''.

{{Quote|''An expedition to the Amber Temple reveals another major minion of the enemy and uncovers a secret weapon that may help defeat them.''|}}
