Over the Edge is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 5 edition ruleset. It is part of the Tomb of Annihilation series of adventures published for the D&D Adventurers League. It focuses on Port Nyanzaru. It contains five adventures, each with a play time of about one hour, and is designed for three to seven 5- to 10-level characters.
