'''''Cauldron of Sapphire''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Tomb of Annihilation|Tomb of Annihilation]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Chult]], and is an adventure designed for three to seven 17{{th}}- to 20{{th}}-level characters. It is the third part in the ''Broken Chains'' series.

{{Quote|''Just off the northern coast of Chult churns the waters of the Cauldron, a single volcano with a terrible history. Legends from the jungle lands say that it vomits forth lava, stone, and monsters if the peninsula is threatened - and yet the recent events did not set if off. So why is it threatening to explode now?''|}}
