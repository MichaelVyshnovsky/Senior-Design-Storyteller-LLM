'''''Chaos in the City of Splendors''''' is an Epic Adventures module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurers League/Waterdeep|Waterdeep]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurers League|D&D Adventurers League]]. It focuses on [[Waterdeep]], and is an adventure designed for 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}}-level characters.

{{Quote|''The hunt for the dragon’s cache has attracted ne’er-do-wells and fortune seekers to Waterdeep from far and wide and the gangs of Waterdeep are not about to let such a bounty of talent go to waste. Chaos and shenanigans await the brave and the bold in the City of Splendors!''|}}
