'''''Underworld Speculation''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 5{{th}} edition ruleset. It is an introductory adventure for ''[[Xanathar's Guide to Everything]]''. It focuses on [[Sylgar's fish bowl]], and is an adventure designed for 1{{st}}- to 4{{th}} level characters.

{{Quote|''Operating as secret agents for the [[Lords of Waterdeep]], a promising lead takes you deep into the world of the [[Xanathar]], but what will it take for you to return?''}}
