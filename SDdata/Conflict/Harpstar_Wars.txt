The Harpstar Wars were a series of conflicts between the Harpers and the malaugrym in the late 12 and early 13 centuries DR.
The seeds of the conflict were planted with the rediscovery of the malaugrym in the . 
The war began in earnest in the , after Elminster created a magic item called the Harp of Stars, tricked the malaugrym into stealing it, and then sent the Harpers to get it back. Among the many properties of the Harp of Stars, it could record the memories of malaugrym who used it, thus if it could reveal a great deal about the shapeshifters plans and society. However, the Harp of Stars was lost or destroyed at the conclusion of the war.
