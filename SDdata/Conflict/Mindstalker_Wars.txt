The '''Mindstalker Wars''', also known as the '''War of Cloven Thoughts''', were a series of wars fought between the [[illithid]]s of [[Oryndoll]] and the [[dwarves]] of [[Shanatar]]. 

They began when the illithids attacked the eastern subkingdoms of Shanatar in {{Yearlink|-8100}}. The war lasted until {{Yearlink|-8080}}.
