The '''War of the Purple Dragon''' was a brief conflict that occurred in the [[Hordelands]].

==Events==
===The Battle===
The combined forces of [[Solon]] and [[Gaumahavi]], a unique [[dragon]], attacked the city of [[Kushk]] located along the [[Jumpa River]] near [[Ra-Khati]].

===Aftermath===
Kushk was left in ruins. Many of the buildings were destroyed by the dragon's fury.
