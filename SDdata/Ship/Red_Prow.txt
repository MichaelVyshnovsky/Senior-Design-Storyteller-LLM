The '''''Red Prow''''' was a slave ship out of [[Calimport]] in [[Calimshan]].

It sank under the weight of [[gold]] and [[gem]]s in its hold, then rose again as a [[ghost ship]]. By [[1372 DR]], it was home to [[wraith]]s and "weirder things" that rode people as if they were [[horse]]s.<ref>
