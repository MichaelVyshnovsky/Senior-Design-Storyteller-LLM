The Undying Gaze was an independant Cormyrean ship in the 14 century DR.
The Undying Gaze followed the standard naming customs of independant Cormyrean sailing ships, being named for a weapon belonging to one of Cormyrs rulers. In its case, the ship was named for Undying Gaze, the eye-studded war shield of King Draxius the Neverdying.
Like other independant Cormyrean ships, this vessel was used in the nations ongoing efforts to combat pirates.
The Undying Gaze operated in the Sea of Fallen Stars around 1370 DR.
