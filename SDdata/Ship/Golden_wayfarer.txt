The '''''Golden Wayfarer''''' was a mercantile sailing ship, originating from either [[East Faerûn|east]] or [[northeast Faerûn]], in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref group="note" name="origin">The exact place of origin for the ''Golden Wayfarer'' is not specified but, as written in the [[Sea of Fallen Stars (sourcebook)|''Sea of Fallen Stars'']], it could possibly originate from either [[Aglarond]], [[Altumbel]], [[Impiltur]], or [[Thesk]].

==Activities==
This ship primarily dealt in the trading of [[spices]].

==History==
The ''Golden Wayfarer'' operated in the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]] around [[1370 DR]].
