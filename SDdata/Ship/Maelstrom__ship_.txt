The '''''Maelstrom''''' was the personal [[caravel]] of [[Vurgrom the Mighty]], a famed [[pirate]] of [[Immurk's Hold]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The ship had a crew of 40 well-trained pirates, and was armed with two medium [[ballista]]e. One of the ballistae could be replaced with a [[fire projector]] when raiding.
