The '''''Harebrained Harpy''''' was a ship, of a type known as a raker, based in [[Waterdeep]] and operating in the [[Sea of Swords]] to the north in the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Crew==
Among its crew was the ship's navigator [[Respen Moongleam]], a priest of [[Selûne]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1367}}, the ''Harebrained Harpy'' was attacked by pirates flying the colors of [[Luskan]], led by the enigmatic [[Black Admiral]]. The ''Harpy'' was sunk with all hands on board, but Respen was rescued from the freezing waters to face a far worse fate—Captain [[Khnarek|Kulleras]].
