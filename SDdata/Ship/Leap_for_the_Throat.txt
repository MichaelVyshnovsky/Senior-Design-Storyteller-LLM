The '''''Leap for the Throat''''' was a pirate vessel active in [[Zakharan seas|Suq Bay]] along [[Dihl al-Suq]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
[[Almari bint-Kafira]] was the captain of the ''Leap for the Throat''. The waters of Suq Bay were her hunting grounds and fat merchant vessels her favored prey.

==Crew==
In addition to the [[Corsair (kit)|corsairs]] who manned the vessel, several notable mages lent magical support during raids. [[Michal Bluestar]], [[Fayiz Mikali]], and [[Safana al-Sitar]] proved to be a deadly asset at sea.
