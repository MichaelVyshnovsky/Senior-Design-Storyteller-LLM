The '''''Ravager''''' was the [[caravel]] of the pirate [[Gonchklas]].

By [[1372 DR]], it was a wallowing wreck with tattered sails, and one of the most feared [[ghost ship]]s on the [[Sword Coast]]. The crew of [[zombie]]s and [[skeleton]]s attacked other ships on sight.
