The '''''Djinni's Flower''''', or the '''''Floating Rose''''', was the sailing vessel of the [[Corsair (kit)|corsair]] and pirate [[Ghunayya]].

==Activities==
Active in the waters of the [[Crowded Sea]], the ''Djinni's Flower'' targeted Zakharan merchant vessels so it could return their goods to [[Masud Jazayir]].
