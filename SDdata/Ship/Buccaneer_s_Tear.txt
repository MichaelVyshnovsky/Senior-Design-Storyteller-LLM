The Buccaneers Tear was a skyship owned and operated by Company Yargo during the late 15 century DR. It was captained by the debonair Shil Yargo.
The ship resembled a large galleon that took flight in the sky rather than across the waves of the Realms.
In the , the Shil and her crew navigated the Buccaneers Tear from the city of Neverwinter up to Pirates Skyhold in order to investigate rumors about lost skyships found on the soaring earthmote.
