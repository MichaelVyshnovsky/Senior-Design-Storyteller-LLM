Selara was a large boat from the town of Lonelywood in Icewind Dale, in the late 13 century DR.
Selara was a sizable hand-built fishing boat that could fit up to seven humanoids on board and safely transport them across the frigid Maer Dualdon lake.
Selara was built by Thom Farold of Lonelywood in the . The boat was named after Selara, Thoms late wife and the boat would be used by Thom and his friend, Young Ned, to fish in Maer Dualdon.
