'''Mhair's kayak''' was a personal watercraft operated by [[Mhair]], a riverine boating enthusiast.

==Description==
The [[kayak]] was a sleek craft made from polished wood, propelled along by a double-sided [[paddle]].

==History==
Mhair kept his kayak for personal use while using [[Mhair's catamaran|his catamaran]] to transport prisoners. He kept both in a secret boathouse beneath the [[Sharran]] [[Temple of Mystra (Wheloon)|Temple of Mystra]] in [[Wheloon]], connected to the [[Wyvernflow]] river via an underground passage.
