The Dragonfang was a pirate ship based out of Jahaka Anchorage during the late 15 century DR.
The Dragonfang was captained by the Chultan wereboar Elok Jaharwon. Mad Kalita served as Eloks first mate. The crew included eighteen Chultan pirates and a "witch" of the sea that was known as the Eye of the Deep.
Elok Jaharwon became captain of the Dragonfang after staring a mutiny and tossing the previous captain over the ships side.
