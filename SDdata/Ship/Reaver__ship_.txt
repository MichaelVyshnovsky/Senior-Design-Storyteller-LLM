The '''''Reaver''''' was a feared pirate vessel active in [[Zakharan seas|Suq Bay]] along [[Dihl al-Suq]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

The ''Reaver'' flew a standard of flying crossed [[cutlass]]es.

==Description==
This large [[galleon]] was filled with 100 [[Corsair (kit)|corsairs]].

Armaments consisted of a pair of [[wikipedia:en:ballista|ballista]] and a [[liquid star]] projector that was capable of fully engulfing a ship in flames.

==Activities==
Captained by a bloodthirsty savage, the ''Reaver'' struck terror into the hearts of those sailing Suq Bay.

==Crew==
[[Anwar Tulam]] captained the ''Reaver''. Several [[wizard]]s went to sea with him as well.
