The '''''Salty Sails''''' was a ship that sunk along the [[Sword Coast]] in [[1440 DR]]. A monument to the ship, mounted with plaques commemorating the deceased crew members, was erected in [[Luskan]]'s graveyard, and was still standing as of the 1490s DR.
