A Tormentor was a four-wheeled infernal war machine that could rapidly transport small teams and carry a moderate amount of cargo. 
A Tormentor had four bladed iron wheels. It was designed for raiding and scouting.
The blades of a Tormentors wheels could damage other vehicles or standing creatures when driving it close to them. It could carry up to  in cargo.

* Bitter Breath, a warlord in Avernus, drove a Tormentor that had an Infernal Screamer.
