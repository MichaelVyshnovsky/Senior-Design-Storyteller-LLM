Bottom Feeder was a riverboat run by a crew of dwarves.
Bottom Feeder was as 20-foot-long (6.1 meters) square-bottomed riverboat built by dwarves. The crew propelled the ship using long poles to push it along. A locker on board stored a number of crossbows, which were used by the ships crew to defend the ship.
Bottom Feeder plied the waters of the River Chionthar from Baldurs Gate to Scornubel, occasionally even as far as Iriaebor.
