The Halavanrii family was one of the wealthiest families in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The Halavanrii estate lay along the affluent Emerald Dragon Way in the heart of the Golden City.
The Halavanriis were the fourteenth-wealthiest family in Suldolphor, giving them considerable influence within the city.
