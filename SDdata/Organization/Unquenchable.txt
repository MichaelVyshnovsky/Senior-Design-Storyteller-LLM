The '''Unquenchable''', or '''abd Multahib''', was an [[efreet]] organization within the [[City of Brass]] on the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]].
 
==Organization==
The Unquenchable were composed of 3,000 efreet who were fanatically loyal to their Sultan. They wore white robes and black turbans to distinguish themselves.

==Activities==
Members of this organization served as the palace guard, protecting the Sultan from any threat.

==Base of Operations==
The City of Brass served as the base of the Unquenchable.
