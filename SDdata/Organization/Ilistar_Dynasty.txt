The '''Ilistar Dynasty''' ruled over the city-state of [[Westgate]] from the {{YearlinkName|616}}, until the {{YearlinkName|927}}. The Ilistar Dynasty began when the [[androsphinx]] King [[Lyonarth]] and the [[lamia noble]] [[Nessmara]] killed each other, and [[Farnath Ilistar I|Farnath Ilistar]] was chosen to be the next king of Westgate.
