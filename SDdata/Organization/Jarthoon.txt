'''Jarthoon''' was a noble house of [[Cormyr]] of the 12{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=drag281">{{Cite dragon/281/The New Adventures of Volo: Lost Treasures of Cormyr, Part 4|76-77}}

==Base==
They were based at Jarthoon, or Jarthroon, which lay west of [[Suzail]] on the northern shore of the [[Lake of Dragons]]. It was long lost by the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref>{{Cite dragon/279/The New Adventures of Volo: Lost Treasures of Cormyr, Part 2|82-83}}
