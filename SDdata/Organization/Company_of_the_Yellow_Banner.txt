The '''Company of the Yellow Banner''' were an [[:Category:Adventuring companies|adventuring party]] operating in the late 15th century [[DR]].

==History==
Little was known of the Company or its achievements before they landed in [[Chult]] except that they had found a ''[[ghost lantern]]'' containing the spirit of an [[moon elf]] [[warlock]] named [[The Starfallen]], whom they quested to raise from the dead. They were killed to a man in the [[Tomb of the Nine Gods]]<ref>{{cite book/Tomb of Annihilation|103}} while looking for the ''[[Eye of Zaltec]]'', which they hoped would resurrect The Starfallen.

==Members==
* Lord [[Brixton]]: The leader of the group. Brixton was a knight of [[Cormyr]]. He choked to death on poisonous gas.<ref>
* [[Devlin Bashir]]: A [[Calishite]] [[wizard]] who had been cursed to look like a [[wikipedia:goat|goat]] at some point in his past. After getting separated from his companions, he was killed by [[undead]] [[dwarves]].<ref> He owned a magical [[staff]].<ref>{{cite book/Tomb of Annihilation|141}}
* [[Bravus Boulderborn]]: A [[gold dwarf]] [[cleric]] of [[Moradin]]. Bravus was eaten alive by locusts.<ref>
* [[Sephirius]]: Better known as "Seph", Sephirius was a [[dragonborn]] [[paladin]] of [[Bahamut]].<ref>
* [[Seward]]: A [[Chondathan]] [[ranger]]. His remains were used to create a [[flesh golem]]. It was Seward who carried the ''ghost lantern''.
