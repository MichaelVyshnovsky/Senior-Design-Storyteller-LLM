The Scimitar tribe was a tribe of goblins dwelling in the Vast Swamp of Cormyr.
The Scimitar tribe had territory in the center of the Vast Swamp.
Around 1367 DR, Scimitar territory was bordered by the territories of the Kyara hobgoblins, Blood Moon orcs, and Sharp-tooth lizardfolk.
The Scimitar tribe was known to live in the Vast Swamp around 1367 DR.
