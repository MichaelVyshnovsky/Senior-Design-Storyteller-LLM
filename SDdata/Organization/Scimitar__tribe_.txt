The '''Scimitar''' tribe was a tribe of [[goblin]]s dwelling in the [[Vast Swamp]] of [[Cormyr]].

==Base of Operations==
The Scimitar tribe had territory in the center of the Vast Swamp.

==Relationships==
Around 1367 DR, Scimitar territory was bordered by the territories of the [[Kyara]] [[hobgoblin]]s, [[Blood Moon]] [[orc]]s, and [[Sharptooth|Sharp-tooth]] [[lizardfolk]].

==History==
The Scimitar tribe was known to live in the Vast Swamp around [[1367 DR]].
