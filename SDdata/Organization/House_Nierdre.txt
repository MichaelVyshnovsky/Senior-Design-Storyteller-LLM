House Nierdre was a sun elf house originally founded in Myth Drannor.
The houses estate in Leuthilspar was constructed from a rare and unusual shiny black stone. The house symbol was a silhouette of a black dragon.
During the Weeping War, only two of the houses members survived and escaped to the island of Evermeet where the house has gradually replenished its numbers. By 1367 DR, the house was led by Lord Maiele Nierdre.
