The '''Chant Masters''' were a religious organization in [[Zakhara]] whose members followed the teachings of [[Jisan]], the [[Zakhara|Zakharan]] goddess of bounty.{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|30}}

The Chant Masters did not have a favored weapon or symbol.

==Activities==
These [[Mystic (Zakhara)|mystics]] gained their holy power through song and verse.

They maintained excellent relationships with the [[rawun]] of western Zakhara.

==Members==
As of [[1367 DR]], the Chant Masters had 400 members.

==Base of Operations==
The Chant Masters were active within the hills of the [[Pearl Cities]].
