The '''Way of Transcendence''' was an eccentric order of [[mystic]]s who were devoted to [[Loviatar]], the Maiden of Pain.

==History==
The Way of Transcendence was both an organization of individuals as well as a form of philosophy. When asked about their particular beliefs, members of the Way of Transcendence simply smiled knowingly.
