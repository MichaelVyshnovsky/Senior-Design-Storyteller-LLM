'''The Preachers''' were a faction of the [[holy slayer]] organization known as the [[Everlasting]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|16}}

==Activities==
The Preachers served the Everlasting by being a direct representative to the [[moralist]] mosques of [[Hajama]] and their imams.
