The '''Darakh Dynasty''' ruled over the nation of [[Narfell]] from {{yearlink|-399}} to {{yearlink|-150}}. The dynasty began when [[Jesthren Darakh]] slew [[Orlathaun Orgolath]] and took the ''[[Crown of Narfell]]''. 

==Monarchs==
; Nentyarch Jesthren:
:Reigned −399 DR – {{yearlink|-322}}. First Nentyarch of the Darakh Dynasty and [[half-fiend]] son of the [[demon lord]] [[Orcus]]. 
; Nentyarch [[Heldakar Darakh|Heldakar]]:
:Reigned −322 DR – {{yearlink|-299}}. Half-fiend son of the demon lord Orcus. His mother was sacrificed to Orcus after his birth, beginning a tradition for all of Orcus' future progeny for the Darakh Dynasty.
; Nentyarch [[Yannos Darakh|Yannos "the Slayer"]]:
:Reigned −299 DR – {{yearlink|-270}}. Half-fiend son of the demon lord Orcus.
; Nentyarch [[Garthelaun Darakh|Garthelaun "the Goreclaw"]]:
:Reigned −270 DR – {{yearlink|-235}}. Half-fiend son of the demon lord Orcus.
; Nentyarch [[Ilithkar Darakh|Ilithkar]]:
:Reigned −235 DR – {{yearlink|-181}}. Half-fiend son of the demon lord Orcus. Overthrown by his cousin [[Rheligaun Darakh]].
; Nentyarch Rheligaun "the Horned":
:Reigned −181 DR – -150. Half-fiend son of the demon lord [[Fraz-Urb'luu]]. Disappeared with the destruction of Narfell during the [[Great Conflagration (war)|Great Conflagration]].
