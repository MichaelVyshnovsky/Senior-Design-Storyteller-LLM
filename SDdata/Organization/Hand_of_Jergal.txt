The '''Hand of Jergal''' was an elite order of [[priest]]s devoted to [[Jergal]], the Lord of the End of Everything.<ref name="PaP-p33" >

==Organization==
The priests of the Hand of Jergal commanded groups tasked with avenging slights against Jergal or his [[Church of Jergal|church]]. The most common of these offenses was [[Raise dead|raising]] or [[Resurrection (spell)|resurrecting]] someone without paying tribute to Jergal. Another common offense was looting a tomb under the church's protection.
