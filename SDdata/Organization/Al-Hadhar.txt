The term '''''Al-Hadhar''''' ({{Pronounce|ahl|HAH|dar|refs=}}) referred to the sedentary inhabitants of the towns and cities of [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.

==Activities==
''Al-Hadhar'' had easy lives compared to their desert nomad kin. These people never dealt with the constant search for water, food, and shelter that dominated the lives of ''[[Al-Badia]]''.

''Al-Hadhar'' came from all walks of life including the nobility, merchant families, laborers, and craftsmen. Most respected honor and followed the teachings of the [[Zakharan pantheon|Enlightened Gods]].
