The City Guard of Hudid served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Humility".
The composition of the city guard as of  was 2,000 regular city guards and 200 clerics. An additional 1,000 soldiers and 500 cavalry from the Sword of the True Gods were also stationed in Hudid. Hudids navy was relatively new, having only three ships constructed as of 1367 DR.
Hudids growing navy was concerned with the increase in pirate activity on the Crowded Sea.
