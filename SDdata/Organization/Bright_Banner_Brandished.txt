The Bright Banner Brandished was an adventuring company in Neverwinter.
In 1261 DR, the wizard Ashemmi joined, having left her home in the Neverwinter Woods. In 1266 DR, the company were trapped in subterranean crypts north of Mirabar by the lich Orvanath. The entire company died, except Ashemmi, who used a teleport scroll to escape.
