The '''Azure Wolves''' were a mighty mercenary company active east of the [[Sword Coast]] in the mid&ndash;15{{th}} century [[DR]]. They were so renowned that any mercenary active in the region knew their name even a decade after they had ceased activity.

==History==
The Azure Wolves&mdash;and their leader, General [[Varnoth Wender]]&mdash;were widely feared. Their fame peaked around the {{YearlinkName|1471}},{{Stormwreck-time}} after which the injured and exhausted General Varnoth left the company to live a life of atonement at [[Dragon's Rest]] on [[Stormwreck Isle]].

==Notable Members==
* General [[Varnoth Wender]], the company's feared and fearsome leader.
