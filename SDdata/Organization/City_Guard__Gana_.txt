The City Guard of the Gana served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Riches".
The composition of the city guard as of  was 5,000 infantry, 2,000 mercenaries, and a navy of 30 ships. Gana also supported a rookery in the nearby Al-Yabki Mountains that housed five highly trained Zakharan rocs and riders. Gana was also accused of hiring privateers to harass and hinder their rival Jumlat.
The navy spent much of its time protecting Ganas pearl diving interests in the region.
