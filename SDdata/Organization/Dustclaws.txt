The '''Dustclaws''' were a [[Luskan]] gang lead by a [[half-orc]] named [[Duulgrin]].{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|880}} The gang's symbol was a dripping claw inside a circle.{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|841}}

==Activities==
In [[1480 DR]], the gang had sixty members and operated out of a tavern.{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|907}} The gang's leader was killed by [[Sithe]] during [[Kalen Dren]]'s attempt to clean up the city.{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|4972}} After Duulgrin's death, the gang chose Sithe as their leader and she lead the gang for a brief time.{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|4983}}
