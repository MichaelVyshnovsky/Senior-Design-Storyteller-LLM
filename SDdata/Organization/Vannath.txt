'''Vannath''' was one of the newest noble families of the [[patriar]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
The family originally hailed from [[Neverwinter]], but fled that city following the [[eruption of Mount Hotenow]] in the {{YearlinkName|1451}}.
