The eagle tribe was part of the Azuposi nation of the Pasocada Basin of northern Maztica in the 14 century DR.
In addition to inhabiting the Pasocada Basin, the eagle tribe maintained a center near the heart of Michaca, the City of Gold.
The eagle tribe was one of six separate tribes that composed the Azuposi people. They represented the northern direction on the compass and their sacred animal was the eagle.
