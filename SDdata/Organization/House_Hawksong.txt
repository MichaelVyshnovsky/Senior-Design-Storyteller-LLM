'''House Hawksong''' was a [[moon elf]] house known for its members' service to [[Evermeet]] as riders of [[eagle]]s and [[pegasus|pegasi]]. 

==Base of operations==
The Hawksong mansion was located in the city of [[Leuthilspar]] on Evermeet. It consisted of three towers connected by bridges and no one outside the house was allowed to enter, except members of the [[House Ahmaquissar|Ahmaquissar]] and [[House Nightstar|Nightstar]] houses.

==History==
Around [[1367 DR]], the house was led by [[Tiatha Hawksong]] who had not been seen since [[1357 DR]], but the family's official line was she was still alive during this period.

==Members==
* [[Tiatha Hawksong]], leader of the house in 1367 DR.
