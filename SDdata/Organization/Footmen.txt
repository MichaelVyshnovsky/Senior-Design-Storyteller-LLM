The Footmen served as the city watch of Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.
The footmen had 10,000 members broken into four watches of 2,500 men. Each watch was further divided into 125 patrols of 20 men. A mamluk from the Faithful was in charge of each patrol and reported to the closest qadi with anyone who was apprehended.
The footmen were active within the city of Huzuz.
Chancellor Makin al-Mutrattab was in charge of the footmen.
