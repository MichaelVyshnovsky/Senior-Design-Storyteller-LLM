The '''Footmen''' served as the city watch of [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Organization==
The footmen had 10,000 members broken into four watches of 2,500 men. Each watch was further divided into 125 patrols of 20 men. A [[mamluk]] from the [[Faithful Mamluk Society|Faithful]] was in charge of each patrol and reported to the closest ''qadi'' with anyone who was apprehended.

==Base of Operations==
The footmen were active within the city of Huzuz.

==Members==
Chancellor [[Makin al-Mutrattab]] was in charge of the footmen.
