The '''Nightfire Lances''' were a mercenary organization of the [[Vilhon Reach]] in the late 9{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
They were cofounded by [[Kalgrathur Daycloaks]] sometime before {{yearlink|882}}; he was their leader in that year. After his conquest of [[Maurmurra]], they served to protect him from his own army.
