The Magebanes were a group of adventurers who operated circa 1369 DR. They were warriors who were notable for disdaining and distrusting all sorts of magical spells and items.
In the , the Magebanes attended a party hosted by Lord Partic Thistle at Thistleflame Manor in Suzail. The occasion was King Azoun Obarskyr IVs birthday, and Lord Thistle hoped to improve the standing of adventuring companies in Cormyr by having them present themselves as a gesture of fealty to the King.
After the festivities, it appeared that Lord Thistle had been murdered by extraordinary means, but the Magebanes aversion to using spells or magical items made them an unlikely culprits.
