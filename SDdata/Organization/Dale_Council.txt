The '''Dale Council''' was a group of six elected councilors, headquartered in [[Highcastle]], that governed [[High Dale]] within the [[Dalelands]]. They were chaired by a 7th member, known as the [[High Constable]], who was elected by the members of the council.The well-organized council was responsible for law and order within, and defense of, the dale; trade regulation; and relations with other nations.

==Members==
Each councilor served a six-year term, with elections for a single seat being held every year. Their members included:

===[[1358 DR]]===
*[[Blakkal Mord]]: Posing as a leather-worker, Blakkal was in fact a secret agent of the [[Cult of the Dragon]].

===[[1367 DR]]===
*[[Irreph Mulmar]]: The adventurer Irreph served as High Constable in the late 14{{th}} century, with a brief reprieve during the [[Zhent]] occupation around the [[Time of Troubles]] of [[1358 DR]] when he led the defense of the dale.
*[[Tyran Greene]]: A veteran of the [[Pegasus Archery Company]].
*[[Varana Brighthelm]]: Despite the office she held, she still found time to foray into adventures.
*[[Darun Lockmaster]]: Darun was Highcastle's master armorsmith and locksmith.
*[[Serena Whitelock]]: One of the more affluent sheep farmers within High Dale.
*[[Renoen|Renoen the Fair]]: A charming and charismatic woman, she was only the [[wizard]] of note in Highcastle.
*[[Francan Fireblade]]: This retired [[Cormyr]]ean adventurer retired in Highcastle after the Time of Troubles.
