The '''Council of the Cat Lords''' was the governing body of [[Nathlekh]] on the [[Dragon Coast]].

==Activities==
The mysterious Cat Lords of the council loosely governed the city which held a substantial population of various werecats including [[werelion]]s and [[weretiger]]s.
