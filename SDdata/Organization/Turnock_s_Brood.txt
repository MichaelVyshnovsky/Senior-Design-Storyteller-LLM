'''Turnock's Brood''' was the name given to the over sixty students of the [[Magister]] [[Turnock Draether]] after his death in {{yearlink|762}}. Over forty of them attempted to avenge him against his slayer, the Magister [[Cauldyn Darthus Salanger]], before they succeeded.
