The '''Foxmantles''' were one of the merchant-noble families of [[Sembia]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. Their businesses included farming, pressing [[wine]], growing dyestuffs, salting meat, and tanning hides and furs. Their family crest was three vigilant eyes in purple set on a slant.
