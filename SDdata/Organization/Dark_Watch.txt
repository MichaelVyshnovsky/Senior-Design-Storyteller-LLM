The '''Dark Watch''' was a [[mercenary]] company based in the [[Orsraun Mountains]] and active in the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]] region.

==Organization==
The company was a group of archers mounted on [[hippogriff]]s.

==Activities==
Usually the Dark Watch worked for pirate gangs who wanted to destroy another pirate group.

==Possessions==
These mercenaries fought with [[longsword]]s and [[longbow]]s.

==History==
Around [[1357 DR]], they fought on the losing side during the war against the [[arcanaloth]] [[Yrkhetep]]. During the [[Battle of Five Crowns]], the original leader of the Dark Watch, [[Thurindar Pelamar]], was killed by a member of the [[Company of the Singing Dawn|Sunshafts]].

==Members==
Their leader [[Lothar Pelamar]] was the brother of Thurindar.

==Prices==
The Dark Watch charged their employers a daily salary of 100 [[gold piece]]s.
