The '''Doom Raiders''' were an adventuring party that specialized in cleaning out the lairs of [[lich]]es (known as "[[dooms]]") during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. They eventually joined the reformed [[Zhentarim]] in the late 1480s.

==History==
In [[1489 DR]], the Doom Raiders were sent to establish a Zhent foothold in Waterdeep.

In [[1492 DR]],{{WDH-time}} [[Davil Starsong]] and other Zhents were captured by the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]], following the [[Gralhund Villa Bloodbath]].
