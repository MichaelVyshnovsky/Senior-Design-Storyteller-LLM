The '''Grey Fire''' was a group of [[holy slayer]]s who operated within [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|26-27}}

The symbol of the Grey Fire was a javelin that had a grey shaft and red feathers.

==Activities==
Holy slayers of the Grey Fire worshiped [[Najm]], the Zakharan god of adventure and curiosity.

Through an arrangement with the [[Everlasting]], the Grey Fire's territories included [[Huzuz]], the [[Pearl Cities]], and [[South Zakhara]].{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|14}}

The Grey Fire was responsible for the assassination of the Sultan of [[Sikak]].
