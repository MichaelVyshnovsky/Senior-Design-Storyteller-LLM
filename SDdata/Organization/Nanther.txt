Nanther was a noble family of Melvaunt in the Moonsea region in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. It was the most influential mercantile house of Melvaunt circa the year 1373 DR.
* Woarsten Nanther, Lord of the family.
* Oreal Nanther, heir of the family.
* Dundeld Nanther, patriarch of the Nanther family.
* Killian Kreel (previously Killian Nanther), fallen prodigy of the family.
* Lyran Nanther would-be ruler of Shadowdale.
