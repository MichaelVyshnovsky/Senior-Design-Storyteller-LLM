The Phelaniityrs were a noble sun elf family of the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. They were the only sun elf noble family.
* Halitan Phelaniityr, patriarch circa 1372 DR
* Aduniron Phelaniityr, nephew to Halitan
* Aliril Phelaniityr, daughter to Halitan
* Schirlyndra Phelaniityr, daughter to Halitan
