The Coramshite pantheon was one of the human proto-pantheons that eventually merged with others to form the Faerûnian pantheon. Its members were worshiped in south-western Faerûn, around what was later Calimshan.
The primary deities of the pantheon were called the Nine
*Anachtyr - worshiped by the Coramshites as the deity of justice, retribution, law, judgement, analysis, philosophy, order, and wisdom.
*Bhaelros - the god of storms, natural destruction, wild beasts, hunting, and impulsive spontaneity.
*Chassaura () - the goddess of agriculture and natural bounty, herblore, and medicine.
*Haruk () - the god of fire, war, the sun, and gold.
*Ilmater - the god of suffering, self-sacrifice, pain, endurance and perseverance, punishment, healing.
*Jaristra - goddess of trade and travel, means of transport, taxation, wealth, logistics/storage/preservation.
*Mraethra - goddess of hearth, nurture, the home, family, love, friendship, mothering and training.
*Nalanrune - goddess of night, darkness, mystery, the moon, thievery and trickery.
*Qistarr () - goddess of divine and arcane, but not primal, magic.
