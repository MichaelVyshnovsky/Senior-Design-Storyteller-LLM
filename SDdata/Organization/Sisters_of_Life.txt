The '''Sisters of Life''' were a religious order devoted to [[Isis]] in [[Mulhorand]] around [[1369 DR]].

This order served with followers of [[Anhur]] and [[Osiris]] as members of the [[Guardians of Skuld]], who protected Mulhorand's capital, [[Skuld]].<ref>
