Marhana Shipping was a trading company in the very late 14 century DR. It was solely owned by the tycoon Behroun Marhana, based in New Sarshel and trading throughout the Sea of Fallen Stars, after he inherited it from the Marhana shipping family at some point after the Spellplague.
