The Ebon Claws was an organization that attempted to wrestle control of Westgates criminal underbelly in the late 14 century DR.
According to a mad beggar named Salty Jack, the Ebon Claws laired in the Sewers of Westgate.
The Ebon Claws seemingly appeared out of nowhere in the  and started a guild war, giving a pretty good account of themselves. The infamous Night Masks quickly became the Ebon Claws foes and the streets of Westgate began erupting into violence at night. The gangs alleged leader was Altama, a female wererat, however, the gangs true leader was a vampire named Tasheni.
