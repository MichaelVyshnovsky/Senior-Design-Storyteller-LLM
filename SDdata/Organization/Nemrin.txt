Nemrin was one of the noble houses of the Lost Kingdom of Anauria.
House Nemrin was one of three human families that composed Anaurias nobility, along houses Maluradek and Thardresk, the later of which encompassed the Anaurian noble family.
During the Era of Upheaval, centuries after Anaurias collapse, some lasting traces House Nemrin could be found amongst the ruins that were scattered across the Anauroch desert.
