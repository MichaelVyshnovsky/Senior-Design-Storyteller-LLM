'''Bladesemmer''' ({{Pronounce|BLAYD|sem|mer|refs=}}) was a noble family in the city of [[Waterdeep]], with a marked history of over two hundred years as of the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite web enhancement/Noble Houses of Waterdeep|2}} They were well known for their [[sword]]-forging and exotic-design [[armor]] crafting. 

==Base of Operations==
The family owned a villa in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]], on the northeast corner of [[Sul Street]] and [[Rough Road]].

==Members==
*[[Taeros Bladesemmer]]: The family's patriarch
*[[Onya Bladesemmer]]: Lady of House Bladesemmer
*[[Dhannan Bladesemmer]]: Taeros and Onya's eldest daughter, and heir to the Bladesemmer fortune
*[[Gutrek Bladesemmer]]: Dhannan's younger brother, sent to Eriabor to continue his studies and become a paladin, one of the 35 Knights of the Eternal Order.
