The '''Soargyl''' family was a noble house of [[Sembia]]. It was seated in the city of [[Selgaunt]] and was an old enemy of House [[Uskevren]]. Their family badge was silver [[swan]]s on a backdrop of green, and their motto was "Always even in the end."

They were rumored to use [[necromancy]].

==Members==
* [[Rildinel Soargyl|Lord Rildinel Soargyl]], head of the family until his death during the destruction of the first [[Stormweather Towers]]
* [[Loargon Soargyl|Lord Loargon Soargyl]], Rildinel's first son
* [[Blester Soargyl]], Rildinel's son
* [[Saclath Soargyl|Lord Saclath Soargyl]]
* [[Alale Soargyl]]
* [[Boarim Soargyl|Lord Boarim Soargyl]], head of the family until [[1371 DR]], when a [[lesser dread]] invaded [[Sarntrumpet Towers]] and slew him and his wife
* [[Mora Soargyl]], Boarim's wife
* [[Rorsin Soargyl|Lord Rorsin Soargyl]], son of Boarim and Mora

==Properties==
The family owned a mansion, [[Sarntrumpet Towers]], in the heart of Selgaunt.
