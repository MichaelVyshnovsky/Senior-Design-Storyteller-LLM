The '''Shadow Tongue''' was a crime ring operating in [[Laothkund]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Structure==
The leader of the Shadow Tongue was the necromancer [[Sathra]]. A smaller section operating as the higher-ups within the organization took the name of the '''Shadow Cadre'''.{{cite book/Stardeep|24-26|3}}

==Base of Operations==
The Shadow Tongue had a lair in [[Laothkund]].{{cite book/Stardeep|18|3}} There was a vault in the building, which served as [[Sathra]]'s base of operations.{{cite book/Stardeep|45|5}}

==Activities==
In {{yearlink|1375}}, the Shadow Tongue were involved in protection rackets, traffic in infernal drugs, illicit slave trade in children, and a scheme to implicate the members of the ruling council in a conspiracy with [[Thay]].

==History==
The leader of the Shadow Tongue, the necromancer Sathra, had called for the theft of the sword ''[[Angul]]'' by midwinter of the {{yearlinkName|1375}}. After the thief [[Gage]] stole it back from them, Sathra decided to pursue the thief and the blade's rightful owner into the [[Yuirwood]],{{cite book/Stardeep|115|10}} where she was slain by [[Kiril Duskmourn]].{{cite book/Stardeep|119|10}}
