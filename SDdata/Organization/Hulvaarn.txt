The Hulvaarn family was a human farming family that lived in Nightstone.
This patriarchal family were led by Nestor Hulvaarn, until he was slain by a bombarding group of cloud giants. After the raid, there were five remaining members Godrick, Prennis, who were the parents, Jehenna, their daughter, and Ellis and Ghalt, their twin sons.
The Hulvaarns were avid turnip and potato farmers.
