The '''Hulvaarn''' family was a [[human]] farming family that lived in [[Nightstone]].

==Organization==
This patriarchal family were led by [[Nestor Hulvaarn]], until he was slain by a bombarding group of [[cloud giant]]s. After the raid, there were five remaining members: [[Godrick Hulvaarn|Godrick]], [[Prennis Hulvaarn|Prennis]], who were the parents, [[Jehenna Hulvaarn|Jehenna]], their daughter, and [[Ellis Hulvaarn|Ellis]] and [[Ghalt Hulvaarn|Ghalt]], their twin sons.

==Activities==
The Hulvaarns were avid [[turnip]] and [[potato]] farmers.
