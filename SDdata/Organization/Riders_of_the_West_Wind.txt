The '''Riders of the West Wind''' was a military order devoted to [[Shaundakul]], the Rider of the Winds.

==Organization==
The Riders of the West Wind were composed of [[windwalker (class)|windwalkers]] and a few [[ranger]]s. They hired themselves out as [[mercenaries]] to guard caravans passing through uncharted territories. They even planned to venture to fabled [[Anchorome]].
