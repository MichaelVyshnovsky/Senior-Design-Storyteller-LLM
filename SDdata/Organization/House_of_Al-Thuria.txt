The '''House of Al-Thuria''' was one of the richest merchant families in [[Huzuz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Organization==
Each of their three large factories specialized in making leather goods including [[leather armor]], [[saddle]]s and tack, and [[sandal]]s and other common items.

[[Jamila al-Thuri]] was the matriarch of the family.

==History==
This prominent [[Dwarf|dwarven]] family specialized in all kinds of leather goods.

==Base of Operations==
The House of Al-Thuria was based in the [[Clothiers' District]].
