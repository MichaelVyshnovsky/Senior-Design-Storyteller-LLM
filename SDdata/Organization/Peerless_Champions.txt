The '''Peerless Champions''' was a group of "bravos" (blademasters) led by [[Myrelas]] the Gallant, that operated out of [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]] as of [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|13-14}}

==Activities==
The Peerless Champions were a group of vigilantes that enforced the law in and protected the city of Sambral.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|14}}{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|50-52}}

The Peerless Champions were self-proclaimed rivals of the [[Flashing Blade at Dusk Troupe]].
