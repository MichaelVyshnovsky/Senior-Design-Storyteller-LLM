The Peerless Champions was a group of "bravos" (blademasters) led by Myrelas the Gallant, that operated out of Sambral, one of the Windrise Ports of Laerakond as of 1479 DR.
The Peerless Champions were a group of vigilantes that enforced the law in and protected the city of Sambral.
The Peerless Champions were self-proclaimed rivals of the Flashing Blade at Dusk Troupe.
