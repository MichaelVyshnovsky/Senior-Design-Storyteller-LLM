The '''Bai Kabor''' was one of the tribes of the nomadic [[Bedine]] people that lived in the [[Anauroch]] desert during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Organization==
Like all the tribes of the Bedine, the Bai Kabor were led by a single Sheikh.

==History==
The Bai Kabor were among the tribes that banded together to face the shared threat of the [[Zhentarim]] in the {{YearlinkName|1360}}. During the conflict, they fought alongside the [[Bait Mahwa]] in the Bedine's decisive victory against the Zhents at [[Orofin]] that year.
