The '''Nighteyes''' was a prominent [[thieves' guild]] located in [[Calimport]] some time before the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Organization==
The guild was run by [[Nelazra]], the guildmaster, and her lieutenant [[Shadowhands]].

==History==
The Nighteyes were eventually slain to a man by the [[Brothers Six]], a group of [[mage]]s who wished to control all trade in Calimport and wanted to eliminate their competition.
