The '''Snow People''' were a tribe dwelling in the land of [[Thar]] in [[north Faerûn]]. They had a number of villages there.

==Members==
[[Yostur Ulhmond]] was a strong young [[fighter]] of the Snow People, and an [[adventurer]] in the 1350s [[DR]].
