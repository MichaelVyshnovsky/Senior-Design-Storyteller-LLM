The '''Kardryn''', which was also called the '''Swords of Justice''', was the city watch of [[Yeshpek]] in the [[Lake of Steam]] region of [[south Faerûn]].<ref name=EotSS-p167">

The word "kardryn" translated as ''ratcather'' in an ancient local dialect.<ref name=EotSS-p167"/>

==Activities==
All members of the Kardryn carried [[iron]] stunning bars which were used with brutal efficiency to restore order, typically when fights broke out in taverns. Their uniforms consisted of [[leather armor]] dyed purple. All members of the Kardryn received martial training from Yeshpek's standing army–the [[Yevven Riders]].<ref name=EotSS-p167"/>

==Organization==
The Kardryn utimately served the [[Khaldomaun]] of Yeshpek. They numbered 1,200 strong during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. They were led by a pair of equal ranking members–[[Haslet Quarren]] and [[Dalanthla Dreist]].<ref name=EotSS-p167"/>
