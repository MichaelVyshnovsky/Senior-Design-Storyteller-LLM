Clan Kepeshkmolik was one of the dragonborn clans of the nation of Tymanther.
Members of Clan Kepeshkmolik used mother-of-pearl disks that resembled the waxing then waning moon across their brow as their distinctive clan piercings.
Clan Kepeshkmolik was one of the oldest Thymari clans, dating back to the foundation of Tymanchebar, and one the most important and influential clans after the foundation of Tymanther.
* Kepeshkmolik Thymara, the founder of Tymanther after the Spellplague.
* Kepeshkmolik Ashoka, the last surviving daughter of Thymara in 1487 DR.
* Kepeshkmolik Tarhun, the Vanquisher of Tymanther in 1479 DR, and until his death in 1486 DR.
* Kepeshkmolik Narghon, the patriarch in 1487 DR.
* Kepeshkmolik Uadjit, a scion of the clan in 1487 DR.
* Kepeshkmolik Dumuzi, the first Chosen of Enlil after his return during the Second Sundering.
