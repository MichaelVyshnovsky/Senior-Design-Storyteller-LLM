The '''Obould dynasty''' was a line of seventeen [[orc]] kings ruling the Kingdom of [[Many-Arrows]], starting in the {{YearlinkName|1371}}.{{Fact}} It was preceded by the [[Broken Arrow]] tribe, whose king was [[Obould|Obould I]]. It was succeeded by the usurper warlord [[Hartusk]] in the {{YearlinkName|1484}}.{{Fact}}

The Obould dynasty ended when [[Obould XVII]] was assassinated in 1484 DR by [[drow]], and a usurper, Hartusk, took the throne.{{Fact}}

After Hartusk was killed in the [[War of the Silver Marches]], [[Lorgru]], son of Obould XVII, returned from his exile and reclaimed the throne of Many Arrows.{{Fact}}
