The '''Sailors of the Mountainous Waves''' were a group of sea-faring [[dwarves]] active around {{Yearlink|-4600}} on the island of [[Ruathym]], which was then known as [[Arauwurbarak]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes}}

==History==
Originally from [[Haunghdannar]], the dwarves constructed the fortress of [[Sonnmorndin]] on Ruathym to use as a base of operations for their sailing excursions.
