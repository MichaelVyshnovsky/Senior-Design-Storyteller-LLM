The '''Black Watch''' was a dark group of mercenaries who operated in the [[Moonsea]] area.

==Equipment==
Members of the Black Watch dressed in black clothing and wore [[chain mail]] with their [[demon|demon's]] eye sigil.

==History==
In [[1340 DR]], the organization was hired by [[Gensor]], a [[wizard]] working for [[Porphyrys Cadorna]], to take control of the [[Cadorna Textile House]] and the library located in the ruins of [[Phlan]].
