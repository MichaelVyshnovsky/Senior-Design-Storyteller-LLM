'''Hook, Line, and Sinker''' was an adventuring band in the [[Savage Frontier]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]],{{Cite web/GTM Icons of the Realms: SKT}} formed sometime after [[1485 DR]] in response to the rise of [[giant]] activity in the region.

==Activities==
This adventuring band was solely dedicated to the activity of [[giant-killer|giantslaying]].

==Members==
Hook, Line, and Sinker had only five members. Three of the primary members each bore one of the organization's words as their nickname.
* [[Cazi Alphelandra]], a [[wild elf]] with a penchant for large weaponry.
* [[Crag Ungart]], a strategic [[shield dwarf]] that acted as a secondary leader.
* [[Helena Nostrum|Helena "Hook" Nostrum]], the leader of the group.
* [[Lai Antolio|Lai "Line" Antolio]], a master at building contraptions.
* [[Solara Thann|Solara "Sinker" Thann]], a righteous [[paladin]].
