The Granary Workers Guild of Purskul and Outlying Villages was a guild based in the city of Purskul, the Granary City, in Amn that represented its and the surrounding areas granary laborers, millers, and bakers in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The Granary Workers Guild operated out of the Grainhouse, an ancient granary on Chaunteas Font in eastern Purskul. The monumental granary was said to be the oldest structure in town and, after renovation, was used as the guildhall, its offices, and meeting halls.
