The '''Highmoon Trading Coster''' was a dominant trading organization that controlled much of the overland trade in the [[Sword Coast]]. It was led by [[Duldelph Maremmon]]. The Highmoon Coster had exclusive rights to [[kaorph]] (a blue wine) and certain [[spices]] such as [[arispeg]], [[marka (spice)|marka]] and [[delph]].
