The '''Coin Spinners''' were a [[Luskan]] street gang that was based in the old [[Clearlight]] temple of [[Tymora]].{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|841}} The Coin Spinners were active at least as early as [[1475 DR]].{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|675}} The gang took its name from the coin that symbolized the [[deity|goddess]] and its symbol was a coin that incorporated both Tymora's and [[Beshaba]]'s symbols.

The gang used charitable activities such as as feeding the poor{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|675}} as a front for criminal acts, which included protection rackets.{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|756}}
