The '''Knights of the Shadow Sword''' was a military order devoted to [[Shaundakul]], the Rider of the Winds.

==Base of Operations==
The order operated out of [[Shaundakul's Throne]] in [[Myth Drannor]].

==Organization==
The Knights of the Shadow Sword were founded by a [[half-elf]] named [[Jax Nightsong]]. The order was composed of [[crusader]]s, [[ranger]]s, and [[windwalker (class)|windwalkers]]. They dedicated themselves to ridding the forests around Myth Drannor, as well as the city itself, of evil creatures.
