The '''Eorn/Lorndessar Dynasty''' ruled over the city-state of [[Westgate]] from the {{YearlinkName|1018}}, until the {{YearlinkName|1248}}. The dynasty began when [[Sarvyn Eorn]] was named King of Westgate through the great praise of the city after King [[Tarnhart Campion II]] died without an heir. 

==Monarchs==
; King Sarvyn "the Old" 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|1018}} – {{Yearlink|1111}}. [[Mercenary]] lord proclaimed King by the public. 
; King [[Roadaeron Lorndessar|Roadaeron]] 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|1111}} – {{Yearlink|1117}} and {{Yearlink|1162}}. Grandnephew of Sarvyn. He was overthrown by his Mage Royal [[Alzurth]] and forced to flee the city. He reclaimed the throne after 45 years in exile.
; King [[Blaervaer Lorndessar|Blaervaer "the Reluctant"]] 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|1162}} – {{Yearlink|1191}}. Third son of Roadaeron.
; King [[Roadaeron Lorndessar II|Roadaeron II]] 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|1191}} – {{Yearlink|1192}}. Firstborn son of Blaervaer.
; King [[Maeraedryn Lorndessar|Maeraedryn]] 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|1192}} – {{Yearlink|1219}}. Second son of Blaervaer.
; King [[Ilphael Lorndessar|Ilphael]] 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|1219}} – {{Yearlink|1233}}. Grandson of Maeraedryn.
; Queen [[Shlanarnla Durovree|Shlanarnla]] 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|1233}} – {{Yearlink|1236}}. Fiance of Ilphael. Only Queen in Westgate's history.
; King [[Verovan Lorndessar|Verovan]] 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|1236}} – {{Yearlink|1248}}. Great-grandson of Blaervaer. Last king of Westgate.
