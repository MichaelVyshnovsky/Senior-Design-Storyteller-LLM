The '''Darkstar''' tribe was a tribe of [[orc]]s dwelling in the [[Vast Swamp]] of [[Cormyr]].

==Base of Operations==
The Darkstar tribe had territory in the center of the Vast Swamp.

==Relationships==
Around 1367 DR, Darkstar territory was bordered in the north by the [[Broken Dagger (tribe)|Broken Dagger]] [[goblin]]s, by the [[Norgath]] [[hobgoblin]]s in the east, and the [[Dragonslayer (tribe)|Dragonslayer]] [[lizardfolk]] in the south.

==History==
The Darkstar tribe was known to live in the Vast Swamp around [[1367 DR]].
