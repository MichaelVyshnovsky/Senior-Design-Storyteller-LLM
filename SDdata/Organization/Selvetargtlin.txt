The '''Selvetargtlin''', meaning the '''"Warriors of Selvetarm"''', was the primary religious organization dedicated to the worship and service of the [[god]] [[Selvetarm]], [[Lolth]]'s champion.{{Cite polyhedron/112/Forgotten Deities: Selvetarm}}

==Organization==
Their ranks were largely made up of males,

==Possessions==
The Selvetargtlin were known for wearing blood-red robes, often over a coat of [[chain mail]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1375}}, the Selvetargtlin launched an attack against the [[Promenade of the Dark Maiden|Promenade]] of [[Eilistraee]]. They were led by a [[Drow Judicator|judicator]], [[Dhairn]]. Although the followers of Selvetarm failed to take the temple, they did considerable damage to the followers of Eilistraee.
