}}The '''Moon-spinners''' were a [[holy slayer]] organization in [[Zakhara]] who followed the teachings of [[Selan]], the Zakharan goddess of beauty.{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|27}}

The symbol of the Moon-spinners was a long white [[scarf (clothing)|scarf]], which they used to whip or strangle their victims.

==Activities==
Holy slayers from the Moon-spinners were obsessed with making Selan the most-worshiped deity on the [[Afyal|Isle of the Elephant]].

They were in direct opposition with followers of the [[The Lost One|Lost One]].

==Base of Operations==
The Isle of the Elephant was the domain of the Moon-spinners.
