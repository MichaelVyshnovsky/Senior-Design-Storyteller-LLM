'''Vaylan''' was a noble family of [[Cormyr]].

==Crest==
The family crest (as presented on a signet ring) was a [[sword]], point down, with a [[snake]] wrapped around the blade.

==Base==
The family had a seat in [[Arabel]].

==Members==
* [[Olaf Vaylen]], killed defending Arabel in the [[Goblin War]] in [[1371 DR]].
* Lady [[Kestrel Vaylan]], the widow of Olaf.
