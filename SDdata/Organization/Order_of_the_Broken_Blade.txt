The '''Order of the Broken Blade''' was an order of honored warriors and clergy who were injured in [[Tempus]]' service and could no longer fight in the front lines.

{{Quote|Yes. We are...were...warriors. Wounded in battle, our injuries now keep us far from the front lines. Still, we are fit to guard holy sites to our lord.|[[Everard]] of [[Easthaven]].
}}

==Activities==
These faithful Tempuran were assigned to guard temples of Tempus.

==History==
The order was founded some time during or before the late 13{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Notable Members==
* [[Everard]], head of the [[Temple of Tempus (Easthaven)|temple of Tempus]] in [[Easthaven]] and protector of ''[[Jerrod's stone]]''.
