Clan Churirajachi was one of the dragonborn clans of the nation of Tymanther.
Clan Churirajachi had a prominent role in the defense of Tymanther during the First Tymanther-Unther War in 1487 DR.
*Churirajachi Versengethor, the Vanquisher of Tymanther who preceded Tarhun.
*Churirajachi Kerashna, a member of the Lance Defenders in 1487 DR.
