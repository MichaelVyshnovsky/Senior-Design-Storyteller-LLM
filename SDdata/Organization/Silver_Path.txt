The '''Silver Path''' was a knightly order devoted to the goddess [[Selûne]], Our Lady of Silver.

Less fanatical than the [[Swords of the Lady]], the Silver Path comprised [[ranger]]s.

They existed during the time of the ancient empire of [[Netheril]], which fell in {{Yearlink|-339}}.
