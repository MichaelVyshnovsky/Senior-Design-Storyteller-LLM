The Azuposi pantheon consisted of a number of great spirit revered as gods by the Azuposi people of the Pasocada Basin of northern Maztica.
The following is a list of the great spirits of the Azuposi pantheon
* Sussistinako, the Spider Woman, one of the two first great spirits.
* The Sun, one of the two first great spirits.
* Shotokunungwa, ruler of the stars.
* Iyatiku, the Corn Mother, a maker of plants, animals, and humans.
* Alosaka, the Germinator, a maker of plants, animals, and humans.
* Masauwu, the Skelton Man, protector.
* War Twins, ravager and later friend to the Azuposi.
