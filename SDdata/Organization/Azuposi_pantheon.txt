The '''Azuposi pantheon''' consisted of a number of great [[spirit]] revered as [[god]]s by the [[Azuposi]] people of the [[Pasocada Basin]] of northern [[Maztica]].

==Members==
The following is a list of the great spirits of the Azuposi pantheon:
* [[Sus'sistinako]], the Spider Woman, one of the two first great spirits.
* The [[Sun (Maztica)|Sun]], one of the two first great spirits.
* [[Sho'tokunungwa]], ruler of the stars.
* [[Iyatiku]], the Corn Mother, a maker of plants, animals, and [[human]]s.
* [[Alosaka]], the Germinator, a maker of plants, animals, and humans.
* [[Masauwu]], the Skelton Man, protector.
* [[War Twins]], ravager and later friend to the Azuposi.
