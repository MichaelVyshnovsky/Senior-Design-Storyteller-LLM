'''Auglamyr''' was the name of an elven house of [[Cormanthyr]]. Their symbol was a multi-headed [[gold dragon]].

==History==
The House already existed from long before {{yearlink|-97}}, when Symrustar Auglamyr was born.{{Cite web/Mintiper's Chapbook/Part 5}} Several of its members were involved in the events before the laying of the ''[[Mythal]]'' of [[Myth Drannor]], including the sisters Holone and Sylmae, who were in service to the [[Coronal]] at the time.

Per sages, House Auglamyr survived the [[Weeping War]].{{cite web/Candlekeep Forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2010)/04-01 Auglamyr}}
