The '''Blackriver Bandits''' were a division of the [[Neverwinter]] [[thieves' guild]] with power and influence in the city's [[Neverwinter/Docks District|Docks District]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|The docks belong to us Blackriver Bandits, I tell you!|A Blackriver thug}}

==Organization==
The gang was led by four bosses, and was a part of the Neverwinter Thieves Guild.

==Base of Operations==
The Blackriver Bandits had four hideouts scattered around the Docks District.

==Possessions==
The Bandits wore custom dark-grey [[armor]]. They also used distinct and custom-made [[dagger]]s, [[mace]]s, [[bastard sword]]s, and [[axe]]s.

==Relationships==
The Blackriver Bandits were enemies of the [[Bloodsailors]].

==History==
''Circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, the Bandits were in a gang war with the Bloodsailors. Some believed that they were destroyed by the adventurer known as [[Eater-of-Garm]] during this conflict.
