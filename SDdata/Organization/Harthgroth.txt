The Harthgroth were the largest of the Nar tribes of Narfell from the 14 to 15 centuries DR.
When the tribe was led by the veteran warrior Thalaman Harthgroth, they could rally 4,000 riders to any cause.
They viewed outlanders as potential sources of material wealth via trade, rather than targets for robbery to violence.
