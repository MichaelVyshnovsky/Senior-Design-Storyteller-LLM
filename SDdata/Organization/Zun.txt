The Zun ( century, the patriarch was Lungar Zun but by 1372 DR, the family of thirty-one nobles was headed by his son Lungar Zun II, with his consort, Triine Zun, and their son Olsztel.
The Zun family villa was located at the southeast corner of Tarsars Street and Saerdoun Street in the North Ward. It rested near the northern edge of the Cliffwatch and had a commanding view of the countryside below and to the east of town from the four-story mansion. To the north, just across the street, was Endcliff Tower.
