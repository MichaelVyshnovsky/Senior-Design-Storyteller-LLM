The '''Wind of Fate''' was a [[holy slayer]] organization in [[Zakhara]] who followed the teachings of [[Haku]], the Zakharan god of freedom and personal independence.{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|27}}

The symbol of the Wind of Fate was a [[Blowpipe|blowgun]].

==Activities==
Experts in desert lore and survival, members of the Wind of Fate infiltrated every major desert tribe within the two great deserts of the Land of Fate.

==Organization==
The Wind of Fate was the only holy slayer organization where all of the members were male.

==Base of Operations==
Holy slayers from the Wind of Fate were active within the [[High Desert]] and the [[Haunted Lands]]. Their citadel was hidden within the [[Genies' Anvil]].
