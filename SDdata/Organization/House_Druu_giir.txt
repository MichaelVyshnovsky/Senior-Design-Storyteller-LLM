House Druugiir was a drow house of Menzoberranzan.
House Druugiir occupied a rectangular-shaped compound southeast of the tip of the Westrift. The small compound was fenced by stalagmites.
House Druugiir was one of the most powerful mercantile houses, second only to House Baenre.
The house was dominated by elderly male wizards.
