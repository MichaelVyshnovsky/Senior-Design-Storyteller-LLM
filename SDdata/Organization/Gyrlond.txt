'''Gyrlond''' was a noble house of Cormyr.{{Cite dungeon/194/Eye on the Realms: Thormil's Secret}}

==History==
In the year [[1479 DR]], the Gyrlonds concerned themselves with methods of farming capable of producing riches relatively quickly. In that same year they held a country estate called [[Gyrlondposts]], situated to the east of [[Stonewatch]].
