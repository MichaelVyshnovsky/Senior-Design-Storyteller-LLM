The '''Order of the Unicorn''' was a group of former [[adventurer]]s who turned to [[Lurue]] and spread her influence across [[Faerûn]].<ref name=PaP-p39">

==History==
The Order was composed of many smaller groups which separated from the [[Knights of the Unicorn]] following the [[Time of Troubles]] in order to do good deeds over a larger area. They referred to this as "chasing the [[unicorn]]." Each separate group within the Order of the Unicorn was commanded by one of the original members of the Knights of the Unicorn who were visited by Lurue and her [[Silver Herd]] outside of the [[elven]] castle of [[El'lahana Raikeil]].<ref name=PaP-p39"/>
