The Silver Turban Legion, or the Feathers, was a military organization operating out of the Citadel of Ice and Steel on the Elemental Plane of Air.
250 djinni riding griffons comprised the Silver Turban Legion. Only females were permitted to join.
The Silver Turban Legion served as shock troops for the djinn.
The Citadel of Ice and Steel within the Elemental Plane of Air served as base of all djinni military organizations.
