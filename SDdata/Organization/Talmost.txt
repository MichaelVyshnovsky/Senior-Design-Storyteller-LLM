'''Talmost''' ({{Pronounce|TALL|most|refs=}}) was a [[Waterdeep|Waterdhavian]] house that was known as one of the city's foremost clothiers and furriers. They managed widespread trapping and fur-trading operations in [[the North]] and operated prosperous [[silk]] trading and crafting businesses in [[Calimshan]]. They had close ties to the [[League of Skinners and Tanners]] and the [[Solemn Order of Recognized Furriers and Woolmen]].

Thanks to recent events, they had a reciprocal emnity with [[Melshimber|House Melshimber]].

==History==
House Talmost first came to prominence during the reign of the [[Kingdom of Man]].<ref>{{Cite web enhancement/Environs of Waterdeep}}
