The '''Faceless Guards''' were the guardians of the [[Gate of Heaven]] within the [[Yikarian Empire]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{Cite dragon/241/Campaign Classics: The Roof of the World}}

==Activities==
The elite [[yak-folk]] warriors of the Faceless Guard were tasked with protecting the Gate of Heaven, the provincial heart of the Empire that housed the capital city of [[Nathong]] as well as the [[Emperor of the Lotus Throne]] and his [[Lotus Court|court]].

Members of the Faceless Guard donned blank masks to honor their savage [[Faceless God|god]]. They possessed skills both martial and arcane, making them formidable foes on any battlefield. The Faceless Guard was composed entirely of yak-folk.
