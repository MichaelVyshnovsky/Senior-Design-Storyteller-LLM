The '''Hallwinters''' were a [[human]] family of [[knight]]s who were renowned up and down the [[Sword Coast]]. Their coat of arms depicted a [[crown]] with six blunt points.

==History==
Members of the Hallwinters served as knights in cities like [[Neverwinter]] and [[Waterdeep]]. They had been doing so honorably since before [[Ruining|the destruction of Neverwinter]], and were still famous as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Members==
Notable members included:
* [[Chanelle Hallwinter]], a knight of Neverwinter interred beneath the city's streets.
* [[Sildar Hallwinter]], a former member of Waterdeep's [[Griffon Cavalry]].
* [[Alana Hallwinter]], Sildar's adopted daughter.{{Cite adventurers league/In Volo's Wake|15}}
