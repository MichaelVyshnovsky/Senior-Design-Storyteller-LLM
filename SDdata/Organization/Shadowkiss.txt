The '''Shadowkiss''' was a criminal organization that operated across the [[Dragon Sea (Abeir)|Dragon Sea]]. Their base of operations was the city of [[Lylorn]], in the [[Dusk Ports]] of [[Laerakond]].

==Activities==
The Shadowkiss engaged in illegal activities such as drug and poisons selling across Laerakond.
