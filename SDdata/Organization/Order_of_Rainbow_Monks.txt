The '''Order of Rainbow Monks''' were a religious organization found in the [[Hordelands]].{{cite book/Storm Riders|27}}

==Activities==
The rainbow monks worshiped [[Byubo]]. They also helped travelers passing through the region along the treacherous route.

==Base of Operations==
The Order of Rainbow Monks were based deep within the [[Katakoro Shan]] mountain range where the [[Gogrus River|Gogrus]] and [[Akundi River|Akundi]] rivers met at [[Rainbow Falls]].
