'''Yrkhetep's Devoted''' were a group of [[neutral evil]] [[cleric]]s fanatically devoted to serving the [[arcanaloth]] known as [[Yrkhetep]].

==History==
In [[1358 DR]], Yrkhetep's Devoted were an infantry unit within the evil forces of Yrkhetep that fought to conquer [[Chondath]]. They were particularly known to have fought in the [[Battle of the Five Crowns]] in [[Chondalwood]].
