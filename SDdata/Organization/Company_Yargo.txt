'''Company Yargo''' was a mercenary company and one of the [[Five Companies]] of [[Yaulazna]]. As of the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, it was headed by captain [[Shil Yargo]].

==History==
The [[halfling]] family that led Company Yargo descended from [[Yargo]], who acted as the then-earthbound Yaulazna's pirate king before the [[Spellplague]].
