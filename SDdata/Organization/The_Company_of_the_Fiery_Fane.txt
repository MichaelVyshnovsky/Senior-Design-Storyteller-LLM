'''The Company of the Fiery Fane''' was an [[adventuring company]] in the early 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite polyhedron/58/The Everwinking Eye: Adventures in Maskyr's Eye|25}}{{Cite web enhancement/Environs of Waterdeep|9}}

The group was founded by Szuszalan, the Warrior Maid, who was formerly of the famous all-female adventuring company known as the [[Circle of Steel]]. She wrote of their exploits in her biography, ''[[Walking on a Swordblade]]'', published in the {{YearlinkName|1352}}.
