The '''Red Shields''' was a strong merchant organization, previously a strong mercenary company, that protected caravans leaving [[Scornubel]] as well as serving as the city's army. The Shields made regular trading operations to [[Amn]], [[Neverwinter]] and [[Waterdeep]].
