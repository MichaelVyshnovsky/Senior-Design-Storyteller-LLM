The '''Shield of Myth Drannor''' was a company from [[Myth Drannor]] appointed to the watchpost at [[Helmgrove]] in order to serve as an early threat to the invading demonic horde in the [[Weeping War|Fall of Myth Drannor]]. 

==History==
The entire company was destroyed by the horde.
