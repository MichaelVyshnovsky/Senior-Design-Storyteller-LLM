Turcassan was a former noble house of Cormyr. It was situated beyond the Royal Gardens in Suzail.
The house betrayed the Crown when Melineth Turcassan, in the kings absence, sold the city of Suzail to pirates, although Jotor Turcassan remained loyal to the crown.
* Melineth Turcassan, father-in-law to King Duar Obarskyr
* Daverna Turcassan, daughter of Melineth and Queen to King Duar
* Jotor Turcassan
* Relve Turcassan, most capable human battle leader in Cormyr at the founding of the realm
