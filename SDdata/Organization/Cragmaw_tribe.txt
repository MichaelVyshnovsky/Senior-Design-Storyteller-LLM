The Cragmaw were a tribe of goblins, bugbears, and hobgoblins operating in the Neverwinter Wood and on the Triboar Trail in the late 15 century DR.
The members of the tribe sharpened their teeth, which led to the name of the tribe. King Grol ruled the tribe as a whole from Cragmaw Castle, while Klarg led a smaller group from the Cragmaw Hideout.
