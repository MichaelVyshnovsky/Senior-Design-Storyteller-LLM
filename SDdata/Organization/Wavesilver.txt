The '''Wavesilver''' ({{Pronounce|WAYV|sil|vər|refs=}}) family were members of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|nobility]] in [[Waterdeep]] ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1372}}. They were primarily in the merchant shipping business.

==Organization==
The Wavesilvers were a wealthy family that made their fortune shipping goods in and out of Waterdeep. From at least the {{YearlinkName|1357}}, to at least the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, the family was led by Lord [[Bleskos Wavesilver|Bleskos]] with his consort [[Apryl Wavesilver|Apryl]]. The designated heir was their second son, [[Aristed Wavesilver]].

==Base of Operations==
[[File:WavesilverVilla-1372.png|thumb|270px|''The Wavesilver family villa (labeled $20) ''circa'' 1372 DR.]]
The [[Wavesilver Villa]] was located in the [[Sea Ward of Waterdeep]] on the southwest corner of [[Ivory Street]] and [[Flint Street]]. Across Ivory Street to the north was the [[Blue Alley]], a few doors to the east was the abode of [[Myrna Cassalanter]], and the [[Tower of Luck]] was a short walk to the west. Just to the south, down Flint Street, was the [[House of Wonder]].
[[File:WavesilverVilla-WDH.png|thumb|270px|''The Wavesilver family villa (outlined in red), ''circa'' 1492 DR.]]

==History==
The Wavesilvers were of [[Chondathan]] descent and the family was ennobled in the {{YearlinkName|1251}}.
