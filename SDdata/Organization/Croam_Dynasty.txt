The '''Croam Dynasty''' ruled over the city-state of [[Westgate]] from the {{YearlinkName|75}}, until {{YearlinkName|429}}. The Croam reign began when [[Hygaarth Croam]] was elevated to the throne after King [[Kergaard Twinblade]] died of plague without leaving any heirs. The last Croam to hold the title of King abdicated the throne in {{YearlinkName|137}}, choosing to install the first [[Croamarkh]] in his place. 

==Monarchs==
; King Hygaarth "the Pious" 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|75}} – {{Yearlink|95}}. Priest of [[Ilmater]] elevated to the throne after helping Westgate during a ravaging plague. 
; King [[Sorentah Croam|Sorentah]] 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|95}} – {{Yearlink|135}}. First son of Hygaarth.
; King [[Dalious Croam|Dalious the "Errant King"]] 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|135}} – {{Yearlink|137}}. Third son of Sorentah who abandoned the throne after ruling for two years.
; The [[Croamarkh]]s 
: Reigned {{Yearlink|137}} – {{Yearlink|257}}. After the disappearance of King Dalious, Westgate continued to be ruled in the name of the Croam family by their representatives, the Croamarkhs.
