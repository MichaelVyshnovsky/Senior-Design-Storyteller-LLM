The Naskurl family was a noble merchant family of the city of Tantras in the Vast. The head of the family sat on the High Council that governed Tantras.
In 1358 DR, shortly after the Time of Troubles, the Naskurls were allied with the Baraedin, Mithertul, and Onsil families.
