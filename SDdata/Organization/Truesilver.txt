House Truesilver was a noble house of Cormyr, whose estate was situated beyond the Royal Gardens in Suzail.
The family was known for its militarism and it pushed to expand the Imperial Navys influence in the late 15 century DR. The Truesilvers and Dauntinghorns shared similar views on Cormyrs military might.
