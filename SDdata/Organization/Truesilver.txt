'''House Truesilver''' was a noble house of [[Cormyr]], whose estate was situated beyond the [[Royal Gardens]] in [[Suzail]].

==Activities==
The family was known for its militarism and it pushed to expand the Imperial Navy's influence in the late 15{{th}} century DR. The Truesilvers and [[Dauntinghorn]]s shared similar views on Cormyr's military might.{{Cite dungeon/198/Backdrop: Suzail}}

==Members==
===Pre–13{{th}} Century===
* [[Aosinin Truesilver]], cousin to King [[Galaghard]].
* [[Damia Truesilver]], a Truesilver by marriage not blood who opposed Regent [[Salember Obarskyr]].<ref>
* [[Glorin Truesilver]], cousin to [[Elvarin Crownsilver]].
* [[Tanalusta Truesilver]], daughter of Damia Truesilver, married King [[Rhigaerd II]], mother of [[Sulesta]] and King [[Azoun IV]].<ref>

===13{{th}} Century===
* [[Beldarm Truesilver]], warrior and possible secret identity of the [[Hullack Hawk]] around the beginning of the 13{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dragon/281/The New Adventures of Volo: Lost Treasures of Cormyr, Part 4|77}}

===14{{th}} Century===
* [[Ayesunder Truesilver]] ({{Pronounce|EYE|SUN|DUR|refs={{Cite web/Ed Greenwood's Patreon/Elven Lore}}}}), Warden of the Port of [[Marsember]], head of the family in the 1360s DR, head of the Imperial Navy.
* [[Beldred Truesilver]], a knight who rode with [[Alusair Obarskyr]].
* [[Braegor Truesilver]], involved in the [[abraxus affair]].<ref>

* [[Taldeth Truesilver]], rumored to have pursued [[Tanalasta Obarskyr]] for courting.

===15{{th}} Century===
* [[Laendra Truesilver]], eldest daughter of [[Oskar Truesilver]]. She appeared stiff and shy while attending [[Foril Obarskyr I]]'s funeral. When the [[Shadovar]] besieged [[Suzail]], she joined the fight.  
* [[Oskar Truesilver]], head of the Truesilver family in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
