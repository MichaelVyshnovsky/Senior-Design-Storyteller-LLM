Husteem () was a noble house of Waterdeep.
The family owned a villa in the Sea Ward, on the east side of the Street of the Singing Dolphin between Delzorin Street and Sighing Maidens Walk, across from the Tower of Luck.
* Boreas Husteem - Orbos fourth son and heir.
* Daervin Husteem
* Luna Husteem - Orbos consort.
* Orbos Husteem - head of the house.
* Orlpar Husteem - brother of Orbos.
