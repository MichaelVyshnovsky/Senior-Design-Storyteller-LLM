'''Husteem''' ({{Pronounce|hus|TEEM|refs=}}) was a noble house of [[Waterdeep]].

==Base of Operations==
The family owned a [[Husteem Villa|villa]] in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]], on the east side of the [[Street of the Singing Dolphin]] between [[Delzorin Street]] and [[Sighing Maiden's Walk]], across from the [[Tower of Luck]].
