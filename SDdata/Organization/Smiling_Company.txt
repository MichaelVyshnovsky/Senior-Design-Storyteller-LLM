The Smiling Company was a band of adventurers that operated out of Yartar during the late 15 century DR. They were fairly successful, having journeyed and quested together for nearly a decade.
The company was part of a larger group of warriors, though other members had since ceased their adventuring ways.
Every year the Smiling Company made notable contributions to the Tymoran temple in Yartar, the Happy Hall of Fortuitous Happenstance.
