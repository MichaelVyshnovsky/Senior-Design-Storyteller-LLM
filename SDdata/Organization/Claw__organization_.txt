The '''Claw''' were a mercenary company based in [[Evermoors]].

==Tactics==
Usually, the Claw had no true organization, with basic tactics but their regenerative abilities proved to be very effective.

==Contracts==
They charged 1000 [[gold piece]]s per month for their services.

==Leadership==
The Claw was a company of [[troll]]s directed by four [[human]] [[wizard]]s directed by four [[mind flayer]]s under the leadership of the [[alhoon]] [[Killithor]], who ruled as a dictator.
