'''Clan Treg''' was one of the [[dragonborn]] [[Thymari#Clans|clans]] of the nation of [[Tymanther]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Hand of Darkness|37-38}}

==History==
Clan Treg was one of the most successful [[Thymari]] clans dedicated to artistry and [[gem|jewel]]ry. They had operatives in [[Ruinspoke]] to mine the veins of precious metals nearby the village.
