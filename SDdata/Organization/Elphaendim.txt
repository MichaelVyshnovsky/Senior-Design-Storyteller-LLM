Elphaendim was one of the merchant organizations and a noble families from the city of Arrabar in the Vilhon Reach.
House Elphaendim employed a sizable mercenary and guard forces as well as house mages, like other merchant houses of Arrabar.
by the late 14 century DR, the Elphaendim family was led by Thalammose Elphaendim, a respected patriarch, often called The Old Elf behind his back for his quiet demeanor and rumored impressive archaist skills. Thalammoses favorite scion was his great-niece Cauvra Elphaendim. The young girl was still a few years away from her sixteenth birthday celebration in 1373 DR, and the patriarch felt protective of his great-niece. The Old Elf took to mentoring Cauvra as he saw burgeoning wizardly skills in the young woman. Another famous family member was Brastynbold Elphaendim. A boisterous bear of a man with many stories of his brawls and feats of strength circulating the noble circles Arrabar.
