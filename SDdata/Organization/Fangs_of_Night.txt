The '''Fangs of Night''' were a [[ninja]] clan in the city of [[Ojy-do]] in [[Koryo]], active around [[1357 DR]].

The five highest-ranked warriors of the clan each carried one of the ''[[Throwing Stars of Ojy-do]]'', magical stone [[shuriken]] that never missed their targets.
