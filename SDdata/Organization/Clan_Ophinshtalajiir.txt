'''Clan Ophinshtalajiir''' was one of the [[dragonborn]] [[Thymari#Clans|clans]] of the nation of [[Tymanther]].

==Description==
Members of Clan Ophinshtalajiir used two light [[jade]] rings on the right side of neck as their distinctive clan piercings.

==History==
Clan Ophinshtalajiir was one of the oldest [[Thymari]] clans, dating back to the foundation of [[Tymanchebar]]. 

==Known members==
* [[Ophinshtalajiir Kaijia]], the matriarch of the clan in [[1487 DR]].
* [[Ophinshtalajiir Perra]], the ambassador in [[Chessenta]] in 1487 DR.
* [[Ophinshtalajiir Rahdia]] a soldier of the [[Lance Defenders]] in 1487 DR.
* [[Ophinshtalajiir Rimi]], another soldier of the Lance Defenders in 1487 DR.
* [[Ophinshtalajiir Sepideh]], a field commander of the Lance Defenders in [[1486 DR]].
* [[Ophinshtalajiir Shaushka]], the Vanquisher of Tymanther in the earlier years of the 15th century [[DR]].
