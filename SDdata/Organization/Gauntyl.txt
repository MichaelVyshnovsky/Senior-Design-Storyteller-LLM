The '''Gauntyl''' ({{Pronounce|GONE|til|refs=}}) family was a notable part of the [[nobility of Waterdeep]], first gaining nobility status in {{YearlinkName|1097}}. 

==Organization==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, the patriarch of the Gauntyl family was Lord [[Elemos Gauntyl]] with [[Thicia Gauntyl|Thicia]] as acting consort. [[Vhass Gauntyl|Vhass]] was [[Elemos Gauntyl|Elemos's]] eldest son and designated heir. During this period, there were twenty-nine living members of the Gauntyl noble family.

==Activities==
The Gauntyl family were most known for their mercenary nature, earning notoriety in fighting and exploring. They also gained their fortune in mining and trading.

==Base of Operations==
[[File:CoSW-TempleOfBeauty.png|thumb|right|270px|''Map showing location of the Gauntyl villa (labeled <code>$9</code>) ''circa'' 1372 DR.'']]
