The '''Hulmaster''' family was the ruling family of the town of [[Hulburg]], on the northeast shores of the [[Moonsea]]. The line began with [[Ivar Hulmaster|Ivar Nanthon]], who changed his surname to Hulmaster after the [[lich]] [[Aesperus]] (who was also his uncle) "granted" him the right to rule in the [[1050 DR|Year of the Keening Gale, 1050 DR]].

==Members==
* [[Ivar Hulmaster]]: The fifth [[harmach]] of Hulburg and the first of the Hulmaster line.
* [[Rivan Hulmaster]]: Ivar's son, famous for ending Aesperus's rule of the lands in and around Hulburg.
* [[Argyl Hulmaster]]
* [[Angar Hulmaster]]: Argyl's son. Had [[Griffonwatch Keep]] built as a defense against the monster hordes of [[Thar]] to the west.
* [[Lendon Hulmaster]]: Had the stink and slag of the furnaces moved downwind of the town in the early 15th century [[DR]], creating an opening for the [[Tailings]] to grow.
* [[Grigor Hulmaster]]: Harmach until his death in [[1480 DR]].
* [[Bernov Hulmaster]]: Grigor's brother.
* [[Terena Hulmaster]]: Grigor's sister.
* [[Isolmar Hulmaster]]: Grigor's son.
* [[Kara Hulmaster]]: Grigor's niece and Captain of the [[Shieldsworn]].
* [[Geran Hulmaster]]: Bernov's son and briefly harmach.
* [[Sergen Hulmaster]]: Not a Hulmaster by birth, but by adoption. Attempted to have the family killed in [[1479 DR]].
* [[Natali Hulmaster]]: Isolmar's daughter and Grigor's heir. Became harmach after his death.
* [[Kirr Hulmaster]]: Isolmar's son.
