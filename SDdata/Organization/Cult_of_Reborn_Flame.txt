The '''Cult of Reborn Flame''' was a [[primordial]] cult dedicated to [[Achazar|Achazar, the Raging Flame]], that operated out of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Quench the Fire of the Raging God|16}} It was the most far-reaching of the primordial cults of the region, and while its headquarters were located in [[Imdolphyn]],{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|33}} and [[Tarmalune]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Stirring the Embers|4}}

==History==
In [[1479 DR]], the Cult of Reborn Flame began to develop a [[ritual]] to try to awaken Achazar from his slumber.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Quench the Fire of the Raging God|40-48}} and even the will of Achazar himself.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Pyrophobia|37}}
