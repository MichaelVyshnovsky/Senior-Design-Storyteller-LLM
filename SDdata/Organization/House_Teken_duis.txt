House Tekenduis was formerly a noble drow house in the district of West Wall in Menzoberranzan.
House Tekenduis resided in a two-stalagmite compound in the northwest part of the city in West Wall.
House Tekenduis was the seventeenth house in 1297 DR. After the elimination of House DeVir, it became the sixteenth house.
In 1319 DR,
The ruined manor remained abandoned in West Wall.
