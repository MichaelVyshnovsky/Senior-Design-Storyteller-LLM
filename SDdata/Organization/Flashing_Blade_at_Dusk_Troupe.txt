The '''Flashing Blade at Dusk Troupe''' was a group of "bravos" (blademasters) led by [[Davien Mecatoris]], that operated out of [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]] as of [[1479 DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|7}}

==Activities==
The Flashing Blade at Dusk Troupe were a group of vigilantes that enforced the law in and protected the city of Sambral.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|14}}{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|50-52}}

The Flashing Blade at Dusk Troupe were self-proclaimed rivals of the [[Peerless Champions]] troupe.
