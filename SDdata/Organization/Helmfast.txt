The '''Helmfast''' ({{Pronounce|HELM|fast|refs=}}) family was part of the [[nobility of Waterdeep]], first gaining nobility status in {{YearlinkName|1292}}.

==Organization==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1357}}, the patriarch of the Helmfast family was Lord [[Kelvar Helmfast]], 'The Old Captain', and his acting consort was [[Ranya Helmfast|Ranya]]. [[Edwind Helmfast]] was Kelvar's eldest son and designated heir. During this period, there were thirty-two living members of the Helmfast noble family.

The Helmfasts also had trade and a holding in [[New Waterdeep]] in [[Maztica]],

==Activities==
House Helmfast were shipwrights by trade. The family had access to properties and exclusive trade agreements with New Waterdeep in Maztica and some family members worked there as harbormasters. 

==History==
The family first received their nobility ranking in {{YearlinkName|1292}}.

In the year of [[1357 DR]], Lady [[Durndatha Greatgaunt]] of [[Cormyr]] married Lord [[Harmantle Helmfast]], becoming Lady Helmfast.
However, in [[1358 DR]], she was murdered by a [[Zhentarim]] agent sent to kill her by the wizard [[Ultcantle]]. Lady Helmfast managed to thwart the scheme to poison multiple [[wine]] cellars across the city, but ultimately she perished in the attack. As of [[1359 DR]], the [[demigod]] [[Siamorphe]] had embodied Lady Helmfast.<ref>

Sometime before [[1370 DR]], House Helmfast was part of a sponsored expedition to Maztica, establishing a connection to Waterdeep and creating New Waterdeep. 

==Base of Operations==
The Helmfast noble villa was located in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] and consisted three floors.<ref><ref> The property could be found near [[Golden Serpent Street]] and the infamous [[Catchthief Alley]].<ref>
