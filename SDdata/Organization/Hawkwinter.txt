The Hawkwinters ( Century===
In 1372 DR House Hawkwinter contained 41 nobles. The patriarch was Eremoes Hawkwinter. Genos Hawkwinter was to be the next heir after Eremoes.
Lady Harriana Hawkwinter was a knight of the Order of the Gauntlet in the service of Helm.
