'''Artemel''' ({{Pronounce|ARR|tem|el|refs=}}) was a [[Nobility of Waterdeep|noble]] house of [[Waterdeep]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Organization==
The house was officially ennobled in [[1233 DR]].

From at least the Year of the Prince, [[1357 DR]], to at least the Year of Wild Magic, [[1372 DR]], the family was led by Lord [[Bresnoss Artemel|Bresnoss]] with his consort [[Lydda Artemel|Lydda]]. The designated heir was [[Ord Artemel]]. Around this time there were 31 living members of the house.

==Activities==
Their family trade was that of money-lending and were well-known for their interest in hunting [[boar]].

==Base of Operations==
[[File:Sea Ward-Artemel-Ammakyl-Silmerhelve Villa.png|thumb|right|270px|''The Artemel family villa (labeled <code>$26</code>) ''circa'' 1372 DR.'']]
The family's estate, [[Artemel Villa]], was in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]], on the northeast corner of [[Vondil Street]] and [[Sul Street]].
