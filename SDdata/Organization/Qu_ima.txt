The '''Qu'ima''' were a moderately sized tribe of [[Nar (ethnicity)|Nars]] found in  [[Narfell]] who always weighed their options before acting. There were 1,900 Qu'ima as of [[1357 DR]].

==Activities==
This tribe was known to exile members who behaved against the decisions of the tribe, as in the case of [[Horse (individual)|Horse]].

==Base of Operations==
The Qu'ima were found in the western reaches of Narfell between the [[Hark's Finger]] and the [[Giantspire Mountains]].
