The '''Gospoda''' served as the city council of [[Almorel]] in the [[Hordelands]].

==Activities==
This organization met frequently within the [[Gospoda-Veche]] to debate the current affairs of the city. [[Mechnik]] [[Nikitsky]] was the head of the Gospoda in [[1367 DR]].
