The '''Brothers and Sisters of the Pure Flame''' was an order of [[monk]]s who worshiped [[Kossuth]], the Tyrant Among Fire.

==Activities==
Members of the Brothers and Sisters of the Pure Flame maintained neutrality with their dealings and outlook on life. The order maintained their own abbey and participated in strict martial training.
