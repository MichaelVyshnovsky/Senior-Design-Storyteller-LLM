The '''Vilhon Mercenary Corps''' were a renowned group that traveled around the ''Forgotten Realms'' completing various contracts for noble lords and city-states.{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|52}}

==History==
In [[1479 DR]], it was rumored [[Caleb Randol|Lord Caleb Randol]] of [[Kovel Mansion]] commissioned the group to undermine [[Black Fist]] operations north of [[Phlan]].
