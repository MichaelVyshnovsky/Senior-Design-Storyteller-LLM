The Pahlemn Dynasty was the first of six dynasties of the Second Age of Calimshan.
Like the rulers before them, the leaders of the Pahlemn Dynasty were bakkals, or high priests of their people.
The rulers of this dynasty were relatively peaceful. They did not war with the dwarves of Shanatar, but they maintained control of the Mir region.
The bakkals palace was located in the city of Calimport.
The Pahlemn Dynasty succeeded the previous Marekh Dynasty in , after the former dynastys rulers all died from the Sunset Plague. The Pahlemns ruled for an unprecedented 421 years, ending in  with the rise of the warlike Tavihr Dynasty.
One of the sabbans in the city of Calimport was named after this dynasty, Pahlemn Sabban in Caravan Ward.
