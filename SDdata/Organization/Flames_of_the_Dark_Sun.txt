The '''Flames of the Dark Sun''' was an order of [[Cyricist]] [[assassin]]s that operated out of the [[Cloud Peaks|Cloud Peak mountains]] in [[Amn]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
The assassins focused on destroying other cells of Cyric-worshipers that they viewed as unfaithful to their view of the One True Faith.

==Base of Operations==
The order maintained a close association with the Cyricist priests at the [[Mountain of Skulls]] in the Cloud Peaks.

==Relationships==
They were dedicated to the eradication of the rival [[Twin Towers of the Eternal Eclipse]] temple, found within the [[Small Teeth]] mountains.

==History==
The Flames were founded by Watchful Skull [[Tynnos Argrim]] some time during or before the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.
