House Durothil was an elven house. It claimed to be one of the founding houses of the gold elves. It had a reputation for producing excellent warriors and wizards, many of whom joined Evermeets army. The houses estate was a wooden construction in Leuthilspar that had been magically grown from a grove of trees.
The house crest consisted of a yellow and white portrayal of the head of a gold dragon.

* Aeramma Durothil, a sorcerer and member of the Circle of Reilloch Domayr, killed in 1374 DR.
* Soliania Durothil, a priestess who runs the Temple of Sehanine in Leuthilspar in 1368 DR.
* Nymmestra Durothil, a wizard who was exiled from Evermeet due to her deliquent conduct. She found a new life in Silverymoon and became a member of the Harpers.
