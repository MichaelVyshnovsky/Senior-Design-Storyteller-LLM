The '''Churches Royal''' were the few [[:Category:Religious organizations|religious organizations]] officially supported by the monarch of [[Cormyr]] or by members of their immediate family.{{Cite dungeon/198/Cormyr Royale: The Royal Court of the Forest Kingdom|49-50}}

==Members==
Recorded Churches Royal included the [[Church of Tempus]] and [[Church of Tyr]], which were supported by King [[Azoun Obarskyr IV]].

The nomination of the [[Church of Chauntea]] as a Church Royal by Crown Princess [[Tanalasta Obarskyr]] moments before her death in the {{YearlinkName|1371}}, created such an uproar that no churches were granted that privilege ever since.
