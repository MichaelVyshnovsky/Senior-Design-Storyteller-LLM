'''Jesper''' was the name of a prominent family in the [[hamlet]] of [[Sevenecho (hamlet)|Sevenecho]] in [[the Vast]] in the 1360s [[DR]].

==History==
As one of the more prominent families, the Jespers contributed a member to the council of elders that governed Sevenecho, around [[1369 DR]].
