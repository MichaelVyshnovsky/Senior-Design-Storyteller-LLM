The Jhansczil () family was a significant member of the nobility of Waterdeep, first gaining nobility status in the . Like House Amcathra, House Roaringhorn, and House Ilzimmer, House Jhansczils influence also reached out to the town of Amphail.
As of , the patriarch of the Jhansczil family was Lord Harkas Jhansczil with his consort Ariel and their eldest son as the designated heir, Arrikes. During this period, there were twenty-nine living members of the Jhansczil noble family.
The Jhansczil family thrived thanks to trading, mercenary fighting, and horse breeding.
The family villa and main base was located in the Sea Ward. 
* Harkas Jhansczil Patriarch and leader of the noble family in the mid-to-late 14 century.
* Ariel Jhansczil The wife and consort of Harkas.
* Arrikes Jhansczil The eldest son and heir of Harkas and Ariel.
