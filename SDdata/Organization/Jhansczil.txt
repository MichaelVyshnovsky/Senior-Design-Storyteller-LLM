The '''Jhansczil''' ({{Pronounce|JAN|ss|zil|refs=}}) family was a significant member of the [[nobility of Waterdeep]], first gaining nobility status in the {{YearlinkName|1248}}. Like House [[Amcathra]], House [[Roaringhorn]], and House [[Ilzimmer]], House Jhansczil's influence also reached out to the town of [[Amphail (village)|Amphail]].

==Organization==
As of {{Yearlink|1372}}, the patriarch of the Jhansczil family was Lord [[Harkas Jhansczil]] with his consort [[Ariel Jhansczil|Ariel]] and their eldest son as the designated heir, [[Arrikes Jhansczil|Arrikes]]. During this period, there were twenty-nine living members of the Jhansczil noble family.

==Activities==
The Jhansczil family thrived thanks to trading, [[mercenary]] fighting, and [[horse]] breeding.

==Base of Operations==
The [[Jhansczil Villa|family villa]] and main base was located in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]].
