The '''Paladins of the Hand''', also known as the '''Twelve Champions of Torm''', was an order of [[paladin]]s and other holy servants of [[Torm]] that were active in the [[Shining Plains]] during the 12{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1158}}, the Paladins of the Hand fought in the defense of [[Lothtia]] in the Shining Plains against the [[orc]] army of [[Thoktor Ironfangs]].

==Notable Members==
* [[Harondath]], the paladin that famously bore the ''[[Veloghon of Vigilance]]'' during the battle of the plains.
