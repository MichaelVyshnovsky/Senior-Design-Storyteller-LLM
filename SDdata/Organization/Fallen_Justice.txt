The '''Fallen Justice''' was a group of [[spellthief|spellthieves]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
The organization fought old foes of [[Ramman]], such as followers of [[Gilgeam]] and [[Hoar]].

==Structure==
The leader of the organization was [[Erilon Amadar]].

==History==
All the members of the Fallen Justice were originally scouts who became drenched in the blood of Ramman during his killing in the [[Time of Troubles]] in [[1358 DR]], gaining [[spellthief]] abilities. The organization remained operative until at least {{yearlink|1373}}.
