The Thundercall were an orc tribe who lived in the Far Horns Forest during the time of Netheril.
The Thundercall tribe lived in the northern Far Horns, and were famed for sounding their warhorns in order to instill fear in their enemies before battle. Once the Netherese city of Unity was founded in , however, they werent able to dominate the forest as they once had and were kept on the backfoot.
