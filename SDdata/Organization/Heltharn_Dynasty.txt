The '''Heltharn Dynasty''' was the fourth{{Cite dragon/346/Impiltur: The Forgotten Kingdom|63}} of the ruling dynasties of [[Impiltur]].

==Overview==
House Heltharn was the family of [[paladin]] war-captain [[Imphras I|Imphras Heltharn the Great]], who united what were four independent cities against a [[hobgoblin]] horde in [[1095 DR]]. After his victory the second realm of Impiltur was formed.

The dynasty included many paladins as kings and its queens were [[sorcerer|sorceresses]] or [[wizard]]esses. It included six kings, one queen, two regents, and five others who never ruled but were marked for succession.

Its last king was [[Imbrar II]], who died without an heir in the [[1385 DR|Year of Blue Fire, 1385 DR]].
