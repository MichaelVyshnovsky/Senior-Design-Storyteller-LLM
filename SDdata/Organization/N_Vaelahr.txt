The NVaelahr (elven for Shadow Soldiers) was the covert branch of the AkhVelahr military in the elven empire of Cormanthyr that existed up until the fall of Myth Drannor at the close of the Weeping War. 
* Aolis Ildacer, First Shadow
* Amara Ildacer, Second Shadow
* Ualair, secret Master
* Parthar, a gnome agent transformed into a yugoloth to act as spy
