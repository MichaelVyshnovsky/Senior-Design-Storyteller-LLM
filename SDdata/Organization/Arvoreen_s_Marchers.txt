'''Arvoreen's Marchers''' was a [[knight]]ly order of [[Arvoreen]] dedicated to the protection of [[Tethyr]] and the elimination of the lands' monsters. They were highly respected amongst both the [[Hin]] and [[Tethyrian]]s alike.

==Members==
The Marchers consisted of primarily [[halfling]] [[paladin]]s, though there were some [[gnome]]s within their ranks.
