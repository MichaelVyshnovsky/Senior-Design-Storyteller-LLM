The '''Trueblades''' were an order of [[crusader]]s devoted to the god [[Torm]] the True, based in the city of [[Tantras]] in [[the Vast]].

==Base==
They were based in a sword-temple in the city of Tantras.<ref group="note">As Torm-worshipers, the Trueblades and likely to be attached to the [[Temple of Torm's Coming]] in Tantras.

==Activities==
The Trueblades fought for justice and order in the name of Torm. In particular, they performed damage control during warfare and disasters.

==History==
In [[1358 DR]], during the [[Time of Troubles]], the Trueblades worked to perform damage control after the cataclysmic battle between the gods Torm and Bane in Tantras that destroyed large sections of the city.

The Trueblades were still active in [[1374 DR]], commanded by [[Phindrake Korlaunt]].{{Cite web/Class Chronicles: Crusaders, Swordsages, Warblades}}
