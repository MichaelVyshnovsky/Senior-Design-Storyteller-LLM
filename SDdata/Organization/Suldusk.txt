The '''Suldusk''' was a [[Wild elf|wild elven]] tribe in the [[Forest of Tethir]]. They were the native tribe of that great forest. Around [[1374 DR]], they operated a cell of the [[Eldreth Veluuthra]], primarily aimed at protecting their tribal lands from [[human]]s.
