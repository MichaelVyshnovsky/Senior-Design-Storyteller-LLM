'''Clan Belarrak''' was one of the prominent [[dragonborn]] clans of [[Relmaur]], in [[Laerakond]].

==History==
As of [[1479 DR]], High Crown [[Harangor Urlingscar]], ruler of Relmaur, allowed members of clan Belarrak to participate in the government of the nation, by appointing members to the [[Crown Council]].
