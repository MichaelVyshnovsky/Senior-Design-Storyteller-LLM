The Knights Kuldar, also known as Barakmordanna, was an holy order of knights that venerated the Triad. They operated throughout the realm of Tethyr in the 13 and 14 centuries DR.
The order was lead by a trio of holy warriors, one each from the Tyrran, Tormtar, and Ilmatari faiths.
They helped the Tethyrian people by offering aid and succor to those too weak or ill to provide of themselves. In some instances, they avenged those that fell victim to undeserved grievance and outright injustice.
The Knights Kuldar were headquartered in the fortified monastery of Barakmordin, situated on the Ithal Road between the cities of Saradush and Darromar.
They held loose bonds with the Janessar, a valiant order of warriors in Calimshan that fought against slavers and slaving operations.
