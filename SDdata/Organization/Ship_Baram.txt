'''Ship Baram''' was one of the five factions of [[Luskan]] ruled by a [[High Captains (Luskan)|High Captain]]. It was named for and originally led by High Captain [[Baram]].

==Relationships==
Ship Baram was allied with [[Ship Taerl]] in [[1376 DR]].

==Members==
* [[Barri Baram]], leader by [[1489 DR]]
* [[Baram]], former leader and oldest of the original High Captains
