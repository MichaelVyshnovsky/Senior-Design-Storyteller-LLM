The Razorhides were a gang of street thugs in Veltalar during the late 14 century DR.
The leader of the Razorhides was Dherk.
The Razorhides traded in travelers dust. They were known to steal, and were rumored to kill freelance thieves, cutting out their limbs and spreading them around the docks.
The Razorhides made their base of operations at an abandoned brewery in Veltalars wharf.
