Cyrics Legion was an army amassed by Szass Tam, the Zulkir of Necromancy in Thay, in the mid-to-late 14 century DR. It was named for the god Cyric. 
The Legion was the successor to Tams undead Myrkuls Legion, which suffered a major defeat in an attempted invasion of Rashemen in the . Following the Time of Troubles, Cyrics Legion was made into Tams largest military force, and he continued to swell its ranks with undead in preparation for civil conflict with his Thayan rivals.
Cyrics Legion was comprised at least partly of dread warriors. Tam placed an emphasis on increasing his numbers of these undead soldier during of the 1360s and 1370s DR.
