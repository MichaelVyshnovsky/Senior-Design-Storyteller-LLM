The '''Yevven Riders''' were the standing army of [[Yeshpek]] in the [[Lake of Steam]] region of [[south Faer√ªn]].<ref name=EotSS-p167">

==Base of Operations==
The Yevven Riders operated out of the [[Crownarch]], a stronghold located in the southwest section of the city.<ref name=EotSS-p167"/>

==Organization==
The Riders were led by High Rider [[Lassratha Nalorth]], a giant of a woman with a fiery temper. Each Rider was a skilled horseman proficient with the [[scimitar]], [[hand crossbow]], and [[javelin]].<ref name=EotSS-p167"/>

==Activities==
The High Rider kept her troops is top fighting shape. Patrols of forty soldiers ranged around Yeshpek constantly. They carried a special powder that when placed on designated beacon fires, could be used to create signals and relay information back to base.<ref name=EotSS-p167"/>

The Riders also trained all of the soldiers in Yeshpek's city watch called the [[Kardryn]].<ref name=EotSS-p167"/>
