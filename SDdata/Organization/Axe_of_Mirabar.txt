The '''Axe of Mirabar''' was the two-thousand strong militia of [[Mirabar]], consisting of [[human]]s and five hundred [[dwarves]].

==Organization==
It was commanded by [[Torgar Hammerstriker]].

==Activities==
The Axe was responsible for keeping the mines of Mirabar clear of monsters.

==Possessions==
Its members wore the crest of Mirabar (a deep-red double-bladed axe with a pointed haft and a flaring, flat base on a black field) on their shield or armor. They fought with [[crossbow]]s, [[lance]]s, and [[hammer]]s, riding [[Horse#Mountain pony|mountain ponies]], and [[rothé]] in the winter.

==Members==
New members had to take an oath to protect the [[Marchion]] of Mirabar.
