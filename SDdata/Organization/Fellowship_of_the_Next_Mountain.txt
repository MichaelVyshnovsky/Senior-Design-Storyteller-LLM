The '''Fellowship of the Next Mountain''' was a military order devoted to [[Shaundakul]], the Rider of the Winds.

==History==
The order formed following the [[Time of Troubles]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Organization==
The Fellowship of the Next Mountain was composed of [[ranger]]s and [[windwalker (class)|windwalkers]]. These individuals usually operated alone, scouting trails through unknown parts of the [[Sword Coast North]] and [[Moonsea North]].
