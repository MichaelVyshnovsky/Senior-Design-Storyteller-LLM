The '''Blood Moon''' tribe was a tribe of [[orc]]s dwelling in the [[Vast Swamp]] of [[Cormyr]].

==Base of Operations==
The Blood Moon tribe had territory on the west side of the Vast Swamp.

==Relationships==
Around 1367 DR, Blood Moon territory was bordered in the west by the [[Sharptooth|Sharp-tooth]] [[lizardfolk]]s and by the [[Kyara]] [[hobgoblin]]s and [[Scimitar (tribe)|Scimitar]] [[goblin]]s in the north.

==History==
The Blood Moon tribe was known to live in the Vast Swamp around [[1367 DR]].
