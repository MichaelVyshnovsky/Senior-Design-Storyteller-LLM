The '''kavalrachni''' was the [[duergar]] cavalry that protected the underground city of [[Gracklstugh]].

==Activities==
The kavalrachni rode atop great female [[steeder]]s, giant-sized [[tarantula]]s native to the [[Underdark]].

==Possessions==
They carried with them [[heavy crossbow]]s, which they wielded with masterful skill.

==Members==
The majority of the kavalrachni hailed from [[Clan Xundom]].
