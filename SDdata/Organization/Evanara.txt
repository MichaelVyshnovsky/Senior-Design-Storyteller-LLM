House Evanara was an insular sun elf house. As of 1368 DR, it was led by the fighter-wizard Nylaathria Evanara. It was notable for producing war wizards. The houses estate was a large grassy compound filled with moon horses and several small villas.
The house colors were blue and white and its symbol featured a prancing white horse.
* Nylaathria Evanara, fighter-wizard and leader of the house in 1368 DR.
* Rathal Evanara, high priest of the Temple of Corellon Larethian in 1368 DR.
* Tanyl Evanara, chief war wizard of the Tower of the Sun.
