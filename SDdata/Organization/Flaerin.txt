The '''Flaerin''', known as the '''Beast-Lords''' by their human neighbors, were a group of [[doppleganger]]s that inhabited the [[Castle of Beasts]] in the late 9{{th}} century [[DR]]. The [[Magister]] [[Sharglar Dulrathran]] seized their castle at some point before {{yearlink|887}}.
