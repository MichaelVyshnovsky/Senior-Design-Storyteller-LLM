House Cragsmere () was a noble family of Waterdeep, of Illuskan and Netherese descent,
The family owned a villa in the North Ward, on the southeast corner of Copper Street and Vondil Street.
*Japhyl "the Hawk" Cragsmere Patriarch of the family during the mid-to-late 14 century DR
*Arven Cragsmere Lady-consort to Lord Japhyl
*Shan "Lady Hawk" Cragsmere The eldest daughter to Lord Japhyl and Lady Shan, heir to the familys estate
*"Raeraeth Cragsmere" The founder of House Cragsmere, who faked his death and continued living in Waterdeep under the disguise of "Hurlblar Corthyn"
