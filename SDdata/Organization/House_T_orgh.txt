'''House T'orgh''' was a [[drow]] house of [[Menzoberranzan]] in the late 14{{th}} and late 15{{th}} centuries [[DR]].

==History==
Originally a commoner family when its daughter T'risstree rose to a degree of power as a priestess and an Arach-Tinilith instructor by 1372 DR, it had become the 16{{th}} ranked house by [[1480 DR]].

==Notable Members==
* [[T'risstree T'orgh]]: Instructor at [[Arach-Tinilith]] in [[1372 DR]]
