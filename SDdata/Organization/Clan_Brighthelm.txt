'''Clan Brighthelm''' was one of the [[dwarven]] families that dwelled in the [[Northwest Faerûn|far north]] of [[Faerûn]]. They made their home in the settlement of [[Ironmaster]], located on the [[Sword Coast North|Sword Coast]] in the [[Frozenfar]].

Like their fellow dwarves of Ironmaster, they forbade all non-dwarves from traveling within their borders.

==Notable Members==
* [[Augrek Brighthelm]], a resident of [[Bryn Shander]] who served as deputy
* [[Gwert Brighthelm]], an older member of the family that spoke to outsiders
