The '''Freedom Warriors''' were a loose confederation of [[Cormyrean]] nobles and wealthy merchants who did not trust the [[Alusair Obarskyr|Regent]].

==Activities==
Some feared [[Cormyr]] would become a despot's plaything while others however had more sinister interests.
