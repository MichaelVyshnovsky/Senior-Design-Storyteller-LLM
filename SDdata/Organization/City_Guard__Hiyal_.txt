The '''City Guard''' of [[Hiyal]] served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Intrigue".

==Organization==
The composition of the city guard as of {{Yearlink|1367}}{{AQ-time}} was 8,000 infantry, 1,500 cavalry, 2,000 palace guards, 2,000 city guards, two [[mercenary barbarian]] units totaling 1,800 men, five units of [[mamluk]]s totaling 5,000 men, and three units of mamluk cavalry totaling 900 men.

==Activities==
Some of the command was split among the royal family, with Prince [[Anjar bin Alurah|Anjar]] commanding the regular troops and Princess [[Hannah bint Alurah|Hannah]] commanding the city guard. The sultana's other children had mamluk units dedicated to them as well. Sultana [[Alurah bint Asrah|Alura]] commanded the cavalry, palace guard, and navy.
