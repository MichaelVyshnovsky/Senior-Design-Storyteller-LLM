The '''Soleilon Dynasty''' was the first established dynasty to rule over the city-state of [[Westgate]]. It was established in {{Yearlink|-137}} when the righteous [[paladin]] [[Gen Soleilon]] slew the [[vampire]] King [[Orlak]] and claimed the throne. The Soleilon Dynasty would reign until {{Yearlink|-27}}.
