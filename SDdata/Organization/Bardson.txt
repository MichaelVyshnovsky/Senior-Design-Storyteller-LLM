Clan Bardson was one of the warrior clans of Tethyrs First Age.
The Bardsons held land in the east of Tethyr.
* Zahyra Bardson, who married into Clan Ithal and became Tethyrs first court vizera.
The clan was in existence from at least the time of the Eye Tyrant Wars until the Age of Shoon.
In the , Clan Bardson allied with Clans Ithal and Rivarrow to fight against ogre tribes. They successfully removed the ogres from the lands between the River Ith and the Sulduskoon River as far east as the tip of the Forest of Tethir. The conquered territory was split among the three clans, and they built up the defenses of their new territories over the next twenty years or more.
