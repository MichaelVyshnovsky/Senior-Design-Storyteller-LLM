The Azure Legion, or the Thunderbolts, was a military organization operating out of the Citadel of Ice and Steel on the Elemental Plane of Air.
10,001 jann comprised the Azure Legion.
This unit attacked with arrows and spears. Each officer wielded a wand of lightning, among other powerful magical items.
The Citadel of Ice and Steel within the Elemental Plane of Air served as base of all djinni military organizations.
