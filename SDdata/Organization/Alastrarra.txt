Alastrarra was the name of an elven house of Cormanthyr. Their symbol was a falcon.
Alastrarra was reckoned as a first house of Cormanthyr and possessed a telkiira that was black with memories. They were rivals of House Starym.
* Lady Speaker Namyriitha Alastrarra, matriarch and mother of Iymbryl
* Ornthalas Alastrarra, third son and heir of Namyriitha and brother to Iymbryl
* Naeryndam Alastrarra, half-brother and elder sibling of Namyriitha
* Iymbryl Alastrarra, Mage of Many Gems. Killed in 241 DR by hobgoblins while on patrol.
