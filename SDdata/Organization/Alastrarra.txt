'''Alastrarra''' was the name of an elven house of [[Cormanthyr]]. Their symbol was a [[falcon]].

==History==
Alastrarra was reckoned as a first house of Cormanthyr and possessed a ''[[tel'kiira]]'' that was black with memories. They were rivals of House [[Starym]].
