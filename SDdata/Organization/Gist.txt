The Gist family was one of the noble patriar families of Baldurs Gate. They owed their wealth to their ownership of the majority of the citys production of dyes.
*Felonius Gist, the 14 century art dealer, who once purchased six stone statues that were in fact petrified adventurers.
*Haverford Gist, a charitable nobleman in the late 15 century DR.
*Leander Gist, a patriar from the late 15 century DR.
