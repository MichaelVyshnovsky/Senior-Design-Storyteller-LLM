The '''No-Haree''' were the personal guard of the High Lord of Oceans of [[Tabot]].

==Description==
They were zealous warriors who would die to protect the High One. Their dress was entirely white, with white [[robe]]s, furs, and enamelled [[shield]]s.

==History==
''Circa'' {{YearlinkConvert|2607|SY}}, they served High Lord [[Ning D'Ahn]], protecting him from such threats as [[ninja]] assassins.
