The Halanthor was the local merchants guild of Scornubel in the Western Heartlands in the late 15 century DR.
Circa the late 1490s DR, representatives of The Halanthor included the half-elf named Lurandural Veltree who did business with Andred Kuroone and Orstrar Galannavruk, dried sundries merchants of Zazesspur. Goods traded included thael leaf, herbs, spices, and dried apricots.
