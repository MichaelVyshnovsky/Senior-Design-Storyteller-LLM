The '''Knights of the Wind's Four Quarters''' was a group of holy [[crusader]]s devoted to [[Akadi]], the Lady of Air.

==Activities==
The members of this group performed tasks and special quests for elders within the [[Church of Akadi]]. They all participated in an ongoing vendetta against the followers of [[Grumbar]] and his [[Church of Grumbar|church]].
