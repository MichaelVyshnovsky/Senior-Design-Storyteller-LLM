The hobgoblin pantheonThough never explicitly called the "hobgoblin pantheon", Volos Guide to Monsters and prior material makes it very clear that hobgoblins as a race once had an individual pantheon. For the purposes of this wiki, the pantheon is referred to as such. was an extinct pantheon whose deities were once worshiped by hobgoblins.
The hobgoblin pantheon once had several individual gods, until all but two of them were cut down by Maglubiyet. After this, the pantheon dissolved and its remaining members were forced to join Maglubiyets united goblinoid pantheon.
Some known members of the pantheon were
* Nomog-Geaya, surviving member and tyrannical deity
* Bargrivyek, surviving member and deity of duty
