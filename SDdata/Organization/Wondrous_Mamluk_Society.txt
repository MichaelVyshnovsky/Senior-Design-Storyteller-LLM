}}The '''Wondrous Mamluk Society''', also known as the '''Wondrous''', was a military organization in [[Zakhara]].{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|22}}

==Activities==
This wealthy group of [[mamluk]]s served the [[Alad bin Alaq bin Alonka|Padishah]] of [[Afyal]]. Since the padishah did not want to risk these troops unnecessarily, they were typically used as palace guards or bodyguards during parades.

==Organization==
The Wondrous were led by [[Akima Abd al-Garib]].

==Base of Operations==
The Wondrous were based on the island of [[Afyal]].
