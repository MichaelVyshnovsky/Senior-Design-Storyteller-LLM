The Hamarchs Hands were a group of adventurers striving to rid Urmlaspyr of all evil in 1358 DR. They were named after Urmlaspyrs elected mayor, the Hamarch.
These adventurers often tangled with wicked organizations including smugglers from Westgate, the Zhentarim, and the Red Wizards from Thay. The would often intercede as a hit team when the Helms failed.
Destrin Chamaerlyn was the head of two dozen other individuals of the Hamarchs Hands in 1358 DR.
