The '''Society of the Purple Lotus''' was an organization in [[Shou Lung]] during the 14th century [[DR]].
 
==Activities==
The Society of the Purple Lotus was dedicated to destroying the Mandarinate that ruled Shou Lung and reestablish the rule of noble families.
