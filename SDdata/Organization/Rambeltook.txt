The '''Rambeltooks''' were a family in the town of [[Ghars]] in [[Cormyr]] in the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Mayor [[Tobald]] had dinner with the Rambeltook family on the night of [[Kythorn 21]] of the {{YearlinkName|1367}}. Returning, he sighted the alleged [[ghost]] of the bandit chieftain [[Fastred]].
