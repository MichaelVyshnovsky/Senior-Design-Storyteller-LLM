The Rambeltooks were a family in the town of Ghars in Cormyr in the mid–14 century DR.
Mayor Tobald had dinner with the Rambeltook family on the night of Kythorn 21 of the . Returning, he sighted the alleged ghost of the bandit chieftain Fastred.
