'''The Openers''' was a secretive band of individuals in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
The Openers dedicated themselves to discovering, mapping, and unlocking the secrets of the ancient [[Netherese]] ''[[gate]]s'' that were believed to be both underneath and nearby the village of [[Hardbuckler]].
