Nalander was a noble family of Cormyr in the late 15 century DR.
In the , Lord Arkanon Nalander schemed to place someone on the Dragon Throne other than a member of the Obarskyr family. He was backed by persons in Sembia. His fellow schemers included Lord Elbert Oldbridle.
