The Thunderstaff family was a Waterdhavian noble family.
This family made its money through caravan mastering, mercenary fighting, and magecraft.
* Baerom Thunderstaff II, patriarch as of 1372 DR
* Marle Thunderstaff, consort of Baerom
* Arsten Thunderstaff II, grand-nephew of Baerom and heir to the family
