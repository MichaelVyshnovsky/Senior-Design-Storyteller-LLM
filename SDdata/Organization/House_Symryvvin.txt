House Symryvvin was for centuries the 18th drow house of Menzoberranzan.
House Symryvvins compound in West Wall was five-sided, surrounded by both stalagtites and stalagmites. It was located just northeast of the tip of the Westrift.
* Hesken-Paj Symryvvin Matron mother
* Halavin Symryvvin A student at Arach-Tinilith, fond of wearing jewelry. She attempted to kill Quenthel Baenre in 1372 DR.
