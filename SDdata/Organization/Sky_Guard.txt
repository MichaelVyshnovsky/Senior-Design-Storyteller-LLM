The '''Sky Guard''' was one of the military organizations that served as the police force and core of the military forces of the kingdom of [[Akanûl]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
The Sky Guard was in charge of the aerial defense of the city of [[Airspur]] and the rest of the kingdom.

==Membership==
The Sky Guard was made up of [[windsoul genasi]], who were mounted on trained [[wardrake]]s.

The Sky Guard was under the leadership of the [[Four Stewards|Steward of Fire]]. ''Circa'' [[1479 DR]], the Steward of Fire was [[Magnol]].
