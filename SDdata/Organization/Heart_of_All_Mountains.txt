The '''Heart of All Mountains''' comprised the bulk of the armed forces of the [[dao]] within the [[Great Dismal Delve]].
 
==Organization==
Nine thousand dao heavy infantry made up the Heart of All Mountains.

==Activities==
The infantry was drilled and indoctrinated in loyalty to the [[Kabril Ali al-Sara al-Zalazil|Great Khan]] on a regular basis. The khan used them to deal with [[noble dao]] who troubled him. They also hunted down merchants who owed taxes.

==Base of Operations==
The [[Sevenfold Mazework]] within the Great Dismal Delve served as base of all dao military organizations.
