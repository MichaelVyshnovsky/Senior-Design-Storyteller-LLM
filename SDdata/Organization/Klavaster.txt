The '''Klavaster''' family was one of the wealthiest families in [[Suldolphor]] in far eastern [[Calimshan]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Base of Operations==
The Klavaster estate lay along the affluent [[Emerald Dragon Way]] in the heart of the Golden City.

==Activities==
The Klavasters were the ninth-wealthiest family in Suldolphor, giving them considerable influence within the city.
