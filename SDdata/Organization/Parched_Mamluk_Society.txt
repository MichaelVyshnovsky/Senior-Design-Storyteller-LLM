The '''Parched Mamluk Society''', also known as the '''Parched''', was a military organization in [[Zakhara]].{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|21}}

==Activities==
The Parched was one of the smallest [[mamluk]] organizations in Zakhara. They were dedicated to a single task: to ensure the security of [[Talab]]'s water supply. They were in charge of maintaining the system of aqueducts that brought the water from the distant [[Al-Sayaj Mountains]]

==Organization==
The Parched were led by [[Akbara Abd al-Yahruq]], the Most Respected Warrior.

==Base of Operations==
Talab was the center of operations for the Parched.
