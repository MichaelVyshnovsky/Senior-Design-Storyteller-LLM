The Hunabar () family was a notable part of the nobility of Waterdeep, first gaining nobility status in .
As of the , prominent members of the house included patriarch Haskar, consort Amonra, and Haskars eldest son and heir Haskar Hunabar II.
The Hunabar family were most notable for importing and trading in textiles and fashions.
The Hunabar Villa was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep on the southeast corner of Hassantyrs Street and Ilzantil Street, and consisted of two-story buildings.
As of 1368 DR, Horth Hunabar had in his possession a dagger of homing, a magical dagger specifically crafted as a throwing weapon that would teleport back to its scabbard.
