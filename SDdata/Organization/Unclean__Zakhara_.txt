The Unclean was a faction within the Everlasting in 1367 DR.
The Unclean were based in the fortress of Sarahin located deep within the Haunted Lands.
The shairs of the Unclean provided the Shadow Caliph with a unique source of power and information given their relationship with genies.
The Unclean were led by a shair named Akim al-Kalaas. The Unclean was the smallest faction of the Everlasting with only 15 members as of 1367 DR.
