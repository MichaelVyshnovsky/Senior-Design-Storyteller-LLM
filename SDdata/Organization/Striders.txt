The Striders, also called the Newts, were part of the standing army of the efreet in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire.
The Striders were a well-disciplined cavalry consisting of firenewts. Their ranks were 10,000 strong.
The Striders were often used to attack fierce resistance from azer scattered across the Elemental Plane of Fire.
The City of Brass served as the base of the Striders, though they spent much of their time in the field at conquest.
