The City Guard of the Free City of Qadib served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Wands".
The composition of the city guard as of  was 2,000 infantry, 500 cavalry, and 700 mamluks of the Studious Mamluk Society. The cavalry was commanded by Prince Anir. Additionally, 100 jann composed the palace guard. Qadibs navy consisted of five ships.
During an invasion, each of Qadibs universities was expected to provide magical support in the form of 100 sorcerers, elemental mages, or shairs.
The navy patrolled the coastal waters of the Great Sea and Al-Haul River.
