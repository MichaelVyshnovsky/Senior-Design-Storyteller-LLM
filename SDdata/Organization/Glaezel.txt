The Glaezel family was a prominent business-owning family of Baldurs Gate in the late 14 century DR.
By the late 14 century DR, the Glaezel family was a prominent and a long-standing family that owned many businesses and buildings across Baldurs Gate. Manthuran Glaezel became the familys head some years before the late 14 century DR. The familys properties included Glaezels Dock in the Seatower district of the Lower City.
