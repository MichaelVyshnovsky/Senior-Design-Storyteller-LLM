The '''Vyndahla''' clan was the third-largest family in the criminal underworld of [[Calimshan]]. In truth, it was simply a puppet group of the [[Rundeen]].<ref name=EotSS-p72> The [[Darkstalker Wars]] robbed them of much of their power, but in [[1370 DR]] they still controlled criminal activities in [[Almraiven]] and [[Volothamp]].<ref name=EotSS-p72 />

==See also==
* The four major crime families of Calimshan in 1370 DR were:
** [[El Pesarkhal]]
** [[El Kahmir]]
** El Vyndahla
** [[El Ahimar]]
