Ebonhawk was a noble house of Cormyr of the late 14 century DR.
The Ebonhawks were based in the Warmshores region, which had been newly established as of 1371 DR. This was an area of hills west of Suzail on the coast of the Dragonmere, at the western border of Cormyr.
They raised crops vital to the kingdom in the early 1370s DR.
