Vammas was one of the  numerous noble families that composed the patriar of Baldurs Gate.
Vammas made its fortune from controlling the majority of trade with Chult.
The familys city estate, known as Vammas House, stood in the Temples District of the Gates Upper City, between two crossings Belltoll Street and Wendserpent Street in the north and Sornbanner Street and Wendserpent Street to the south.

A late 15 century DR painting by Oskar Fevras depicted two contemporary eldest sisters, scions of the Vammas family, conspiring to shake up their noble bloodline. Both sisters appeared be humans with light skin tone and dark hair.
