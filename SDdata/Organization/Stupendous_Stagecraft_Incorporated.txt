The '''Stupendous Stagecraft Incorporated''', or '''S.S.I.''' for short, was a theater troupe of some renown based in the city of [[Phlan]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Great. High drama and mud wrestling all rolled into one.''|[[Volothamp Geddarm]] about the ''[[Pools]]''.}}

==Organization==
S.S.I.'s star was the resident thespian of Phlan named [[Ward T. James]] in the 14{{th}} century DR.
