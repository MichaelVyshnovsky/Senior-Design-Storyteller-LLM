The '''Council''' was the governing body of the city of [[Tsurlagol]] in [[the Vast]].

It consisted of the city's highest [[noble]]s and leading [[merchant]] lords. From among their number, by means of a secret ballot they chose their leader, called the [[Ven]], and the [[Ven's Voice]]. The Ven was a secret ruler of the city, and Voice served as a spokesperson for both the Ven and the Council.
