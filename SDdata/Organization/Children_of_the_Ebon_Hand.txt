The '''Children of the Ebon Hand''', who were called the '''Kenswa-Tokashow''' in the tongue of the [[Eshowe]], were a militant order who served [[Eshowdow]], the Shadow Giant within the [[Chultan pantheon]].

==Base of Operations==
The Children of the Ebon Hand operated out of the [[Valley of Lost Honor]] within the [[Chult]]an jungles.

==Organization==
The group was composed of [[crusader]]s, warriors, and rogues. They served as Eshowdow's military arm in Chult. They organized themselves into strike groups of three called Triads.

==Activities==
The Children assaulted [[Tabaxi (tribe)|Tabaxi]] settlements and planned for the ultimate destruction of [[Mezro]].
