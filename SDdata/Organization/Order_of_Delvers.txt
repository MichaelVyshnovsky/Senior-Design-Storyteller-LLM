The Order of Delvers was a dwarven guild of delvers, those dwarves dedicated to exploration and discovery. It had chapters in many dwarven settlements.
The leader of each chapter was known as a Delvemaster. 
Each member of the Delvers had a magical backpack that only they could open.  
Delvers armor was a type of enchanted heavy armor used by the members of the guild during their dangerous subterranean missions.
