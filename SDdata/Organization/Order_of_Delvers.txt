The '''Order of Delvers''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] [[guild]] of delvers, those dwarves dedicated to exploration and discovery. It had chapters in many dwarven settlements.{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|358}}

==Organization==
The leader of each chapter was known as a Delvemaster.{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|?}} 

==Possessions==
Each member of the Delvers had a [[magic|magical]] backpack that only they could open.{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|962}}  

''[[Delver's armor]]'' was a type of [[enchanted]] [[heavy armor]] used by the members of the guild during their dangerous subterranean missions.
