}}The '''Storm Which Destroys''' was a [[holy slayer]] organization in [[Zakhara]] comprised mostly of fanatical [[moralist]]s.{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|27}}

The symbol of the Storm Which Destroys was an obsidian blade.

==Activities==
Some considered the Storm to be a covert force of the [[Cities of the Pantheon|Pantheon]], but rumors abounded that the Storm was too extreme for the likes of the Pantheon.

==Organization==
[[Rimaq al-Nimar]] of [[I'tiraf]] was rumored to give orders to the Storm.

==Base of Operations==
The Storm used [[Al-Sayaj Mountains]] within the [[Cities of the Pantheon|Pantheon Lands]] as their base.{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|25}}

==Relationships==
The Prince of [[Kadarasto]], [[Nari ibn Aman]], allied himself with the Storm, allowing them to form a small base within the catacombs of his city.{{cite book/Ruined Kingdoms/Campaign Guide|29}}
