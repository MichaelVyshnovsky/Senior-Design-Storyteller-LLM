The Easy Blades Adventuring Band was an adventuring company that operated in Cormyr and Sembia in the mid-14th century DR.
The Easy Blades were chartered in Suzail. They were often suspect of conducting extensively smuggling operations along the coast of Sembia.
One member was Whelgar Taerncole, though hed retired before 1358 DR.
