'''Saravho's''' was a coster that was headquartered in [[Khôltar]], [[south Faerûn]], ''circa'' the [[1372 DR|Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR]].{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks archive|72}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 56: Khôltar, Part 7, Leaving the Farrgaunlar}}

==Activities==
This business signed up individual merchants, travelers, and smaller wagon trains to travel together in caravans and provided security in the way of their own guards or hired mercenaries and adventurers.

==Base of Operations==
Saravho's maintained offices on the [[North Way]], one of the main thoroughfares in Khôltar.
