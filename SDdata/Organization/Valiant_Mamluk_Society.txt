The Valiant Mamluk Society, also known as the Valiant, was a military organization in Zakhara.
This mamluk organization specialized in engaging enemies with quick lightning strikes.
Several platoons of the Valiant were stationed within the Chamber of Judges to guard the qadi and scribes working their and to maintain order.
The Valiant maintained a large contingent in the North Center of Huzuz to aid in security and monitor the caravans entering the city through that ward.
The Valiant were led by General Kalin Abd al-Bas.
Qudra served as the main base for this group, though they maintained units within Huzuz and Utaqa as well.
