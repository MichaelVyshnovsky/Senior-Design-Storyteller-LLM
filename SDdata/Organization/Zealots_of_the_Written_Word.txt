The '''Zealots of the Written Word''', who were often simply called the '''Zealots''' or the '''Carmendines''' after their founder, was an adventuring group devoted to [[Deneir]], the Lord of All Glyphs.

==Organiztion==
The group was founded by [[Carmendine]].

==Activities==
The Zealots would travel with Deneirrath [[cleric]]s on missions and holy quests in order to raise funds for the [[Church of Deneir|church]].
