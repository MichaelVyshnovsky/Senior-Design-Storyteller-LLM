The '''Angels of Death''' were part of the standing army of the [[efreet]] in the [[City of Brass]] on the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]].
 
==Organization==
Fully 35,000 [[efreet]] armed with [[short bow|composite short bows]] made up the Angels of Death.

==Activities==
The Angels of Death were the archers of the efreet army. The archers were both mounted and unmounted. Mounted archers rode [[nightmare]]s, [[ghost mount]]s, or skeletal [[horse]]s.

==Base of Operations==
The City of Brass served as the base of the Angels of Death.
