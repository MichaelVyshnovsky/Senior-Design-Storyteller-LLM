Ormantrel was one of the Palace families who served the Crown of Cormyr in the 15 century DR.
By the late 15 century DR, Ormantrels were career officiers for multiple generations. Like other officers and chamberlains, the Ormantrel family resided at Cormyrs Royal Court south of Castle Obarskyr.
