The City Guard of Halwa served as a police force within the city as well as a standing army for the "City of Solitude".
The composition of the city guard as of  was 3,000 guardsmen, 500 palace guards, and 1,000 mercenary cavalry. Zarad also had a personal guard of 200 men and could call upon another 200 jann if needed.
The mercenaries for the cavalry were hired from the surrounding desert tribes on five year contracts.
