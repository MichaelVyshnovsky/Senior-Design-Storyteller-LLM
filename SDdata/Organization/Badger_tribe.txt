The badger tribe was part of the Azuposi nation of the Pasocada Basin of northern Maztica in the 14 century DR.
In addition to inhabiting the Pasocada Basin, the badger tribe maintained a center near the heart of Michaca, the City of Gold.
The badger tribe was one of six separate tribes that composed the Azuposi people. They represented the northeast direction on the compass and their sacred animal was the badger.
