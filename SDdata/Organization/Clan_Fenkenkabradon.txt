Clan Fenkenkabradon was one of the dragonborn clans of the nation of Tymanther.
Members of Clan Fenkenkabradon used branching steel piercings like miniature antlers jutting from their temples as their distinctive clan piercings.
* Fenkenkabradon Ishkhanak, the clan elder in 1487 DR.
* Fenkenkabradon Dokaan, the commander of the Lance Defenders in the late years of the 15th century DR.
