The '''Branded''', or '''abd Wusum''', were located in the [[City of Brass]] on the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]].
 
==Activities==
The [[efreet]] [[mamluk]]s of the Branded guarded the [[Marrake al-Sidan al-Hariq ben Lazan|Sultan's]] personal harim. There were 150 members of the Branded. All were known to be discreet and observant individuals who were immune to bribery attempts.

==Base of Operations==
The City of Brass served as the base of the Branded.
