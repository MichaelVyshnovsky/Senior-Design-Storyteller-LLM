The Narabrusk family was one of the wealthiest families in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The Narabrusk estate lay along the affluent Emerald Dragon Way in the heart of the Golden City.
The Narabrusks were the fifth-wealthiest family in Suldolphor, giving them considerable influence within the city.
