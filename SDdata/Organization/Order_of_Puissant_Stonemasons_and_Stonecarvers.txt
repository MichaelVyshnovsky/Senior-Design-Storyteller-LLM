The '''Order of Puissant Stonemasons and Stonecarvers''' was an honorary order whose members were devoted to [[Gond]], the Wonderbringer.<ref name="FaA-p63" >

==Organization==
The trade of all members of this organization was focused on creating, shaping, and generally working with stone to creat artful designs and additions to existing structures.
