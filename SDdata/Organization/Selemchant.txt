'''House Selemchant''' was a powerful family of [[Amn]] in the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Lands==
Along with the other noble merchant families, they were prominent and powerful in the capital, [[Athkatla]]. They owned land by the [[Tethir Road]], between [[Trademeet]] and [[Murann]], north of the [[Alibakkar]] Estates.

==Members==
* [[Thayze Selemchant]], Lord of House Selemchant ([[1332 DR|1332]]–[[1362 DR]]), Meisarch and founder of the [[Council of Six]].
