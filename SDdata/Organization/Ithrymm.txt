Ithrymm was a powerful merchant family of Archenbridge.
It was said that the Ithrymm as well as the Baulaukiir, and the Tantal families outshone Sembian merchants in trade.
The Ithrymm had a grand family estate in Archenbridge.
In the late 14 century DR, the Ithrymm were considered one of the wealthiest and most powerful families on Archenbridge.
