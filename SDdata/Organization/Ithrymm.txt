'''Ithrymm''' was a powerful merchant family of [[Archenbridge]].

==Activities==
It was said that the Ithrymm as well as the [[Baulaukiir]], and the [[Tantal]] families outshone [[Sembia|Sembian]] merchants in trade.

==Base of Operations==
The Ithrymm had a grand family estate in Archenbridge.

==History==
In the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], the Ithrymm were considered one of the wealthiest and most powerful families on Archenbridge.
