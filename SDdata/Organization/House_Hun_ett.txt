House Hunett was an extinct drow noble house of Menzoberranzan. It was formerly the fifth house.
* SiNafay Hunett Matron Mother, adopted as ShiNayne DoUrden of House DoUrden after Hunetts fall.
* Masoj Hunett Apprentice to Gelroos, killed by Drizzt DoUrden
* Gelroos Hunett "The Faceless One", slain by Masoj.
* Alton DeVir Took the identity of Gelroos and adopted into House Hunett after House DeVir fell.
