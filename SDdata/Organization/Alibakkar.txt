'''House Alibakkar''' was one of the most powerful and influential [[Amn]]ian mercantile families during the 14{{th}}, and 15{{th}} centuries [[DR]]. They had significant holdings in both [[Trademeet]], and the capital city of [[Athkatla]].

==Holdings==
The family owned nearly every parcel of land along the [[Tethir Road]] between the cities of Trademeet and [[Murann]].

==History==
House Alibakkar was founded in the {{YearlinkName|1302}}.

As of the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, the Alibakkars had been involved in a great feud with [[Lurraxol|House Lurraxol]] for a number of generations. According some knowledgeable citizens of Trademeet, the two houses traced their lineage back to the same family.

By the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, the Alibakkar family openly held the title of [[Namarch]] in the ruling [[Council of Five (Amn)|Council of Five]].
