The '''Morn''' family were nobles that ruled over the area of [[Daggerdale]] for centuries until mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. Since the arrival of [[Zhentarim]] agents, only two known survivors of the family were alive as of [[1367 DR]].

==Members==
* [[Colderan Morn]], twice-deceased great-grandfather of Randal Morn.
* [[Belasaria Morn]], wife of Colderan, killed by [[drow]] poisons.
* [[Randal Morn]], Lord of Daggerdale.
* [[Silver Cormaeril]], sister of Randal Morn.
