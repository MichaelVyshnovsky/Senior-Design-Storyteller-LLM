The Snobeedles were a family of halflings who owned and ran the Snobeedle Orchard and Meadery in Undercliff just outside of Waterdeep. By , they were Waterdeeps richest halfling family.
* Blossom Snobeedle, an old druid.
* Dasher Snobeedle, a member who ran away to join a gang of halfling wererats called the Shard Shunners.
