The '''Court of Rhythm''' was a religious organization in [[Zakhara]] whose members followed the teachings of [[Hajama]], the Zakharan god of courage.{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|30}}

The Court of Rhythm favored the short sword.

==Activities==
[[Mystic (Zakhara)|Mystics]] of the Court of Rhythm gained their spiritual power through pounding [[drum]]s in rhythm, usually with many others of their organization. They were popular among dancing groups, but singing and chanting mystics did not care for them.

==Base of Operations==
Members of the Court of Rhythm were found within the [[Cities of the Ancients|Ruined Kingdoms]], in [[Afyal]], and scattered throughout the [[Crowded Sea]].
