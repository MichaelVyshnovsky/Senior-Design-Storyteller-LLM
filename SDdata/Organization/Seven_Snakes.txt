The Seven Snakes were a mercenary and bandit group based out of Daggerford who predominantly did "dirty works" for the Zhentarim. Their primary contact was Nelkin Danniker.
In the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, Kella Darkhope hired them in order to occupy the village of Nightstone.
