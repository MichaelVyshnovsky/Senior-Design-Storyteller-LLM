The red triumvirate was a cabal of red dragons during the Time of Dragons.
 They possessed lands to the south-east of the territory of King Orchtrien.
The red cabal was a foe of King Orchtrien.
The red triumvirate was almost certainly made up of red dragons.While the red triumvirate is never explicitly stated to be made up of red dragons, throughout the story it is stated that wyrms control almost all territory thus, it is assumed for the purposes of this article that the triumvirate, and other forces facing King Orchtrien, are dragons.
In , the red triumvirate lost a significant battle to the forces of King Orchtrien.
