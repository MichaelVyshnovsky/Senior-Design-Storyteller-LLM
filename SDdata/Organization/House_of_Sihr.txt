The '''House of Sihr''', also known as the '''jann of the High Desert''', was one of the largest and most powerful groups of [[jann]] in [[Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name=LoF:AGtZ-p39">

==Organization==
All jann of the House of Sihr were [[Zakharan pantheon|enlightened]]. The House of Sihr was formed from many smaller tribes, each led by a sheikh who answered to Amir [[Bouladin al-Mutajalli]].

==Base of Operations==
The scorching wasteland known as the [[Genies' Anvil]] was the home of the House of Sihr.
