The Exalted Mamluk Society, also known as the Exalted, was a military organization in Zakhara.
The Exalted were bitter rivals with the Devoted, leading to frequent conflicts in the region of the Cities of the Pantheon.
The Exalted were led by Jamala Abd al-Raffa, the Most Respected Warrior. The Exalted was a moralist group dedicated to making Zakhara more civilized and law-abiding.
Itiraf and Hilm were the primary bases of operation for the Exalted.
