The Sons of Gondegal were a gang of brigands operating in Cormyr in the late 1370s DR.
They named themselves for Gondegal, the Lost King, who led an uprising in Cormyr and briefly seized the city of Arabel in the . They even carried his standard a gray wolfs head with red eyes. Its unknown what connection they had to their namesake.
In the late 1370s DR, the Sons of Gondegal started threatening caravans and farms between Arabel and the Shadow Gap. Theyd escaped capture by 1379 DR, but Warden Dauneth Marliir promised their heads to Steel Regent Alusair Obarskyr before long.
But they had ambitious plans to infiltrate the Kings Forest and from there conquer Arabel.
A known member was Fadlimi.
