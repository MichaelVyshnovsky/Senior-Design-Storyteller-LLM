'''Clan Flameshade''' was a [[duergar]] clan of the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Clan Flameshade members were hired by [[Thrinn|Sergeant Thrinn]] to help the [[True Soul]] of [[the Absolute]] [[Nere]] enslave more people for the further excavations of [[Grymforge]].
