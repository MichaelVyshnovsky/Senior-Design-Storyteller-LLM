'''Clan Roaryl''' was a noble house of [[Eskorn]], and the ruling dynasty of the kingdom as of [[1479 DR]].

==History==
Clan Roaryl was founded by the legendary hero [[Aunglass Roaryl]] after he defeated the dragon [[Eskornamundyr]]. The clan later became the fifth ruling dynasty of Eskorn.
