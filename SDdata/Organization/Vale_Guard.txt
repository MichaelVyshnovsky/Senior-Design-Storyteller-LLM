The '''Vale Guard''' were [[Evereska]]'s soldiers that patrolled entryways to the city.

==Description==
Contingents of Vale Guards were led by single [[kanqat]]s, and were typically made of around 100 archers.

==History==
Most of the Vale Guard perished during the Evereska-[[Phaerimm]] war.
