The '''Calim Caliphates''', more commonly referred to as the '''Calim Empire''', was the great kingdom founded by the noble [[djinn]]i [[Calim]] and his people, the [[Djen]].

==Geography==
At its peak, the empire stretched from the southern [[Sword Coast]] to the [[River Agis]] and along the [[Marching Mountains]] to the [[Alimir Mountains]] and south to the [[Shining Sea]].

==Government==
For all but the last few decades of its existence, the empire was under the sole control of Calim, who ruled as [[Alzhedo dictionary#C|caleph]].

==History==
Most of the history of the Caliphates were tied with that of Calim.
