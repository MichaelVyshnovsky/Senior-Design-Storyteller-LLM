'''P'Karnis''' was a [[Merrow (ogre)|merrow]] kingdom in [[Serôs]], under the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]].

==History==
P'Karnis was one of the many merrow kingdoms that united alongside the [[koalinth]]s of Serôs in the [[Third Serôs War]]. Two years after the war began, in {{Yearlink|-1537}}, the kingdom of P'Karnis fell.
