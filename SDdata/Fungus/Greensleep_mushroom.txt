'''Greensleep mushrooms''' were a [[fungus]] found in the [[Moonsea]].

==Description==
The fungus was green color and could be ground into powder.

==Usage==
This mushroom could cause drowsiness if consumed. Rumors spoke of agents of the [[Cult of the Dragon]] using greensleep for vile purposes.{{Cite adventurers league/Shadows over the Moonsea|18}}

==History==
Around the {{YearlinkName|1489}}, the Cult of the Dragon spy [[Verik]] sought to poison the drinking water of a small village outside [[Phlan]] with ground greensleep mushrooms. He was working under the command of [[Ixas]] who was searching for a [[fiend|fiendish]] item of power.
