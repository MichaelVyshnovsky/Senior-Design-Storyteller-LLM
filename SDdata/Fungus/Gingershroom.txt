A '''gingershroom''' was an unusual type of [[fungus]]. Its smell was reminiscent of [[ginger]].

==Availability==
Gingershrooms grew in the subterranean Botanical Garden beneath the [[Cloak Tower]] of [[Neverwinter]] in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}.
