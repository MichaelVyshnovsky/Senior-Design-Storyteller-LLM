'''Marrult''' were a kind of edible [[fungus]] found in the [[Underdark]].

===Description===
Marrult grew throughout the Underdark. It was resembled thick circular scabs and was found growing in clusters on the walls of tunnels and caves. It was one of the main ingredients in [[drow green wine]].
