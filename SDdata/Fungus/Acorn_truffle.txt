Acorn truffles were edible fungi found in the Underdark and damp forests of the Western Heartlands.
Acorn truffles were sought after by rangers and druids. These mushrooms were a delicacy for many forest beasts.
Found in the Underdark and forest beds, these mushrooms were valued around 20 gold pieces per specimen.
These fungi were used to alchemically create the essence of acorn truffle. The substance had a vibrant color and enticing smell. This essence could be mixed with certain types of salts to create potions of animal speaking.
