'''Browncap''' was a species of edible wild [[mushroom]].{{Cite dragon/277/The New Adventures of Volo: Dragonwing Stew|93}}

==Description==
Browncaps were considered to be a staple of [[dwarf|dwarven]] cuisine in the areas where these mushrooms were available. Dwarves served browncaps golden-panned, or fried.{{Cite web/theedverse/2022-11-03 - Dwarven Cuisine}} According to [[Elminster]], their taste was reminiscent of portobello mushrooms found on [[Earth]].
