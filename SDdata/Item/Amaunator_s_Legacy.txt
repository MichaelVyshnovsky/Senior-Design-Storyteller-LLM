Amaunators Legacy was a unique enchanted bastard sword created by Pouldinus, one of the Archwizards of Netheril.
This sword was an extremely old shiny brass weapon.
Amaunators Legacy was a weapon with a powerful +3 enchantment and its magics were deadly to the undead. When the sword was used against the undead, it burned and sizzled them with magical flames. There was also a small chance of the sword unleashing the Beltyns burning blood spell at any creature it stricken. Additionally, the sword allowed the wielder to cast the sunray spell once per day at the range of .
Amaunators Legacy was created more than 3,000 years before Dale Reckoning by Pouldinus to be gifted to his friend, a stern warrior who venerated Amaunator, the Keeper of the Eternal Sun. Kalamnin used the sword to help follow the deitys edicts exactly. Amaunators Legacy remained in Kalamnins hand throughout his travels and left only when the warrior was slain in battle against several dozens of nycadaemons. Amaunators Legacy was claimed by the fiends summoner, the Sorcerer named Ylmandi. The summoners family owned the bastard sword until the fall of Netheril during Karsuss Folly. 
The sword reappeared once again in the  in the hands of a halfling priest of Waukeen of the Golden Lodge in the town of Lonelywood in Icewind Dale.
