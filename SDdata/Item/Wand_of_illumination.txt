A '''''wand of illumination''''' was a [[magic item]] capable of producing light.

==Powers==
These [[wand]]s mimicked the effects of several [[wizard]] [[spell]]s including ''[[dancing lights]]'' (1 charge), ''[[light]]'' (1 charge), ''[[continual light]]'' (2 charges), and ''[[sunburst]]'' (3 charges).

A ''wand of illumination'' could be recharged.

==Notable Owners==
[[Akim al-Kalaas]], a [[sha'ir]] of the [[Unclean (Zakhara)|Unclean]] in [[Zakhara]], carried a ''wand of illumination''.{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Cardsheets}}
