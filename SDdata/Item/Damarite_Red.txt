'''Damarite Red''', or '''Bloodcheese''', was a [[cheese]] crafted in [[Damara]] within the [[Bloodstone Lands]]. It was also sold through [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]].

==Description==
This bloodcheese was hearty and pungent. It had a sharp flavor courtesy of [[goat]]'s [[milk]]. A black rind helped this cheese travel well.

==Prices==
Damarite Red could be purchased in the following sizes:
* Wheel, {{SI|1|lb|k}}, 5 [[gp]]
* Oblong Whey, {{SI|50|lb|k}} 20 gp
