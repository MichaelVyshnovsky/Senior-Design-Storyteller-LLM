Damarite Red, or Bloodcheese, was a cheese crafted in Damara within the Bloodstone Lands. It was also sold through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
This bloodcheese was hearty and pungent. It had a sharp flavor courtesy of goats milk. A black rind helped this cheese travel well.
Damarite Red could be purchased in the following sizes
* Wheel, , 5 gp
* Oblong Whey,  20 gp
