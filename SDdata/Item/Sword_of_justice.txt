A '''''sword of justice''''' was a type of [[enchanted]] [[greatsword]] associated with the faith of [[Tyr]], the God of Justice.

{{Quote|Deliverence. Justice. Vengenace.|Inscription along the base of the blade.}}

==Powers==
A ''sword of justice'' possessed a ''+1 enchantment'' and granted its wielder the ability to invoke ''[[Shield of faith|Tyr's protection]]'', a shimmering aura of protective energy.

==History==
These types of blades could be found in the Realms ''circa'' the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Notable Owners==
* [[Anders]], an agent of the [[archdevil]] [[Zariel]] who posed as a [[paladin]] of [[Tyr]].
