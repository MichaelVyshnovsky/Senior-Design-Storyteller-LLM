A sword of justice was a type of enchanted greatsword associated with the faith of Tyr, the God of Justice.
A sword of justice possessed a +1 enchantment and granted its wielder the ability to invoke Tyrs protection, a shimmering aura of protective energy.
These types of blades could be found in the Realms circa the late 15 century DR.
* Anders, an agent of the archdevil Zariel who posed as a paladin of Tyr.
