Druuls Kiss was an enchanted curvy-braided dagger from the ruined city of Myth Drannor.
Druuls Kiss possessed a potent +5 enchantment and allowed the wielder to cast the poison spell. This magic came at a price to the wielder, as Druuls Kiss dramatically reduced the owners charisma.
In the , members of the Cult of the Dragon occupied the ruined city of Myth Drannor. One of the Cults Overseers stationed in the depths of the Elven Catacombs wielded Druuls Kiss and the sceptre of Leogans. The Overseer answered directly to Kya Mordrayn, the Wearer of Purple who helmed the operations in Myth Drannor.
