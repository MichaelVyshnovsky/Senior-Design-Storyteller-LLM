Wyrmwizz was a type of ale brewed in Skullport.
It was an extremely low quality, muddy beverage.
Wyrmwizz was produced at Gyudds Distillery in Skullport. As of 1493 DR, this ale was available from the Black Tankard and the Worms Gullet taverns.
One of the regular buyers of Wyrmwizz from Gyudds Distillery was Azrok from Undermountain. He had a storage room where he kept it stored.
