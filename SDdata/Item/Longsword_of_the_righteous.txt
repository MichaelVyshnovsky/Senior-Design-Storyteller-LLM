Longsword of the righteous was a longsword enchanted to be especially effective against undead creatures.
Longsword of the righteous was an imposing and severe-looking weapon, blessed with divine holy magics. The sword turned undead creatures struck by it to dust. Additionally, this blade enhanced a paladins divine spellcasting abilities.
Dragonbait a saurial paladin and a one-time companion of Artus Cimber during their foray into the jungles of Chult in the late 15 century DR.
