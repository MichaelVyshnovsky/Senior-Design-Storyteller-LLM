'''Helm's Hold Journeybread''' was a type of long-lasting hard biscuit from [[Helm's Hold]], a [[Helm]]ite city and cathedral located in the north of the [[Sword Coast North|Sword Coast]].

==Powers==
The simplest of breads and travelers rations, these small biscuits were cheap and sold in big batches. They were known to have minor curative qualities.

==History==
In the mid-to-late-15{{th}} century [[DR]], Journeybread was available for purchased at the citadel of Helm's Hold, as well as being served at the [[Beached Leviathan]] tavern of [[Neverwinter]]'s [[Blacklake District]].
