A ring of spell power was an enchanted ring worn by wizards. It could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.
The wearer could activate a ring of spell power to increase the destructive power of their spells and other magical attacks.
* Shevarith Kendia, a rashemi wizard who worked as an adventurer on behalf of Laeral Silverhand, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.
