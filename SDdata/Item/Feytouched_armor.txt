Feytouched armor was a type of enchanted armor that had materials from the Feywild worked into it. They offered a level of protection comparable to that of hide or leather armor.
Those who wore feytouched armor had enhanced reflexes and could make themselves invisible for brief periods.
* Voldini, a baelnorn lich, owned a suit of feytouched armor.
