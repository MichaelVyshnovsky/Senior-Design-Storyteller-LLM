'''Marble''' was a [[hardstones|hardstone]] widely used in the Realms as a building material.{{Cite dragon/72/Gems Galore|20}} Marble was sometimes called '''[[wikipedia:en:calcite|calcite]]''' because that was its primary ingredient.

==Description==
Marble was highly prized in construction because of its strength, weight, and beauty. It came in many colors, including black, gray, pink, and white, and polished to a reflective smooth finish. This material was extremely heavy but was easy to quarry to exact specifications. A typical price was 3 gp per pound (0.45 kg).

==Usages==
[[File:Marble Stone.png|thumb|200px|''A chunk of marble.'']]
Fine marble was used for carving statues and other decorations.

The [[Great Library]] of [[Mezro]] in [[Chult]] had a facade of rose marble.

Idols of the [[snake]] god of [[poison]] [[Llash]] were cubic black marble carvings of three-headed snakes.

===Powers===
No magical use for marble has been found.
