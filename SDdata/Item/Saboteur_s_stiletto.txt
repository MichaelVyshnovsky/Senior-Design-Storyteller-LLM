Saboteurs stilettos were enchanted daggers created to be used in assassination and subterfuge.
This bronze stiletto was covered in a never depleting coat of bubbling acid.
The blade was unaffected by its acid, remaining shining and sharp. The weapons possessed a +1 enchantment and burned its targets with acid upon contact.
In the , a saboteurs stiletto was wielded by a yuan-ti pureblood agent Luaire Farwander of House SeSehen. The spy used the dagger against a group of adventurers hired by SaSani, the leader of one of Samargols merchant companies. The stiletto was taken off Luaire Farwanders dead body.
