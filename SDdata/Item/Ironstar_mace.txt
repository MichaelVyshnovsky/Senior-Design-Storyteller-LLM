The '''Ironstar mace''' was the signature weapon of [[Clan Ironstar]] constructed from their secret alloy, [[darksteel]]. 

==Description==
The [[mace]]s were the size of a footman's mace yet weighed less than 1 lb (0.5 kg), and were very difficult to shatter.
