The '''cursed Imaskari boots'''<ref group="note">Note that these boots are never referred to by name in the book; they are merely described. were an [[Imaskari]] relic unearthed from the ruins of [[Solon]] by the slaves of [[Ambuchar Devayam]].{{cite book/Blood Charge|54}}

==Description==
These boots were sized correctly to whoever found them.

==Powers==
If worn for longer than ten minutes, the wearer would develop a terrible case of athlete's foot.
