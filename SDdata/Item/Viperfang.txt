'''''Viperfang''''' was a unique [[magic]] weapon located in [[Waterdeep]] as of the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.

==Description==
The weapon had a black curved blade that was serrated. The [[dagger]] weighed {{SI|4|lb|k|long=yes}}.

==Powers==
''Viperfang'' functioned as a ''[[dagger of venom]]'' but also carried the ''[[Corrosive (weapon quality)|corrosive]]'' [[weapon quality]]. Its enchantment also made it easier for the wielder to strike and harm foes during combat.

==History==
''Viperfang'' was created by [[Hadrhune]] specifically for [[Alauneth Orrane|Alauneth the "Black Viper" Orrane]] who wielded it as of the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.
