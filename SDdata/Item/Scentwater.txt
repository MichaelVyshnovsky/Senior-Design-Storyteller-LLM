'''Scentwater''' was water mixed with [[perfume]] for bathing purposes.

Scentwater baths in large [[copper]] bathtubs were one of the services offered to guests of [[The Worried Wyvern]] [[inn]] in the [[hamlet]] of [[Sevenecho (hamlet)|Sevenecho]] in [[the Vast]] around [[1370 DR]].
