'''''Wilted Dreams''''' was a type of [[potion]] made by the [[hag]] named [[Auntie Ethel]] from within her acrid workshop beneath the [[Gnarled Teahouse]] of the [[Putrid Bog]].

==Description==
It smelled distinctly like a ''[[potion of sleep|sleep potion]]'' that had soured.

==Powers==
Anyone that drank from the ''Wilted Dreams'' potion suffered the next time they slept as their psyche was assaulted by some magical means.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, only one bottle of this potion remained within Ethel's [[Ancient Abode]] beneath the swamp.
