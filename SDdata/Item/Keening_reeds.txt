Keening reeds were a special type of arrow created by the Batiri goblins of Samarach.
These arrows were crafted using a species of long and slender reed native to the jungles of the Chultan Peninsula. Topped with thick steel arrowheads, keening reeds were not designed to cause damage.
When keening reeds were loosed, they produced a loud high-pitched whistle that had a chance of deafening its targets and always gave them a headache.
By the , keening reeds were in use by the Shattered Spear and the Stone Knives tribes of Chultan Batiri.
