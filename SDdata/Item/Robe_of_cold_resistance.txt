A '''''robe of cold resistance''''' was a type of [[item enchantment|enchanted]] [[robe]] common among [[wizard]]s throughout the [[Sword Coast]] region. It was more common in the northern reaches of the region, in places such as [[Icewind Dale]] and [[Neverwinter]].

==Description==
These robes were largely white, with some yellow flourishings around the chest and shoulders.

==Powers==
Whoever wore one of these robes was imbued with a minor resistance to harm from severe cold.

==History==
Young [[mage]]s in the Sword Coast region would often receive one of these robes as a gift from their instructor for completing their first five years of study.
