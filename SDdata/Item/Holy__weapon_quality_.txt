'''Holy''' weapons were those imbued or otherwise [[item enchantment|enchanted]] with [[good]] power that was especially harmful when wielded against [[evil|wretched]] foes.

==Powers==
Holy weapons inflicted more grievous wounds whenever they struck an [[evil]]-aligned creature or being. Similarly, any evil person that attempted to pick a holy weapon up were severely weakened until they let it go.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Erathaol]] wielded a holy [[quarterstaff]]'' that was [[flaming]] at one end and covered in [[Frost (property)|frost]] at the other.
* [[Mielikki]]'s twin ''[[Hornblades]]'' were ''+5 defending holy [[keen]] [[mighty cleaving]] [[scimitar]]s''.<ref name=FPp48-9">
* The ''[[Rod of Four Moons]]'', the personal weapon of the moon goddess [[Selûne]], was a ''+5 defending [[disruption]] holy [[heavy mace]]''.<ref name=FPp55">
* [[Fatal Touch]], the favored weapon of [[Kelemvor]], was a ''+5 [[ghost touch]] holy [[bane (property)|undead bane]] [[bastard sword]]''.
* [[Ever Watchful]], the favored weapon of [[Helm]], was a [[keen]] holy [[lawful]] [[Bastard sword|bastard]] [[sword]].
* [[Dawnspeaker]], the favored weapon of [[Lathander]], was a ''+5 [[disruption]] [[ghost touch]] holy [[heavy mace]]''.{{cite book/Faiths and Pantheons|37,38-39}}
* [[Apollo]], an interloper [[deity]] who originated from another [[earth|plane of existence]], used a holy [[composite longbow]].

In addition, holy weapons were often favored by [[celestial]] combatants such as [[Word archon]]s, and by followers of good-aligned deities such as [[Triadic knight]]s.

==Notable ''Holy'' Weapons==
* The [[Lance of Faerûn]], enchanted [[lance]]s created by the [[church of Torm]] to enhanced the abilities of their mounted combatants.
* [[One Thousand Broken Dreams]], a unique magical elven warblade enchanted against [[drow]]s.
* The [[Baneblades of Demron]], a set of holy blades created in [[Myth Drannor]].
* [[Holy avenger]] blades would gain holy qualities in the hand of a paladin.
* [[Hadryllis]], the legendary, [[Intelligent item|intelligent]] founding-[[sword]] of [[Rashemen]].
* The [[Axe of heavenly fire]], a [[Item enchantment|magical]] [[greataxe]] that was considered sacred to the [[church of Tyr]].
* [[First Light]], a holy [[longsword]] sacred to the [[Church of Lathander]].
