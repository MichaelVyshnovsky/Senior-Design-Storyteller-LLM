Shields of shielding were enchanted shields that were imbued with protective magics.
A shield of shielding allowed the wielder to cast the shield spell once per day.
It was believed that the shields of shielding were named in the style of Extremely Unimaginative Jimmy, the man credited with creating the words of "waterfall" and "fireplace." During the Absolute crisis in the , a shield of shielding was among offerings made for the deity Helm at the Stormshore Tabernacle in Baldurs Gate. The magic item was sealed away in the temples basement vault. Anyone daring enough to steal a shield of shielding would end up being cursed by the gods and attacked by Helms servant devas.
