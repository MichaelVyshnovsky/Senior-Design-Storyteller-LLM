Mandolins were small pear-shaped string instruments.
When played as accompanying instruments, mandolins provided a delicate fluttering sound. They were also performed as solo instruments.
Mandolins produced in Calimshan were available for purchase through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
 Canaith mandolin  One of the seven Instruments of the Bards. Magical mandolins that had healing powers and provided protection from lightning, among other magical powers.
* Adagio Jones, a member of the bardic group Adagio Jones and The Goodwinds, had a mandolin that he played for ballads and songs that involved the groups string ensemble.
