Maelstroms Mesh was a set of enchanted light armor crafted by a sorcerer named Maelstrom.
Maelstroms Mesh possessed a simple +1 defensive enchantment. The armor was embedded with countless paralyzing spider poison glands that discharged the effects of a hold person spell when the wearer of the armor suffered damage.
Maelstroms Mesh was among the items ensorcelled with nasty surprises by Maelstrom sometime before the late 14 century DR. The light armor was created for the sorcerers employer at the time. Since then, Maelstroms Mesh changed many hands. As of the , Maelstroms Mesh was worn by Mordecai, the foul-tempered cheating proprietor of the Track in Westgate.
