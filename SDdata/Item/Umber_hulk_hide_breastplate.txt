Umber hulk hide breastplate was a type of medium armor crafted using umber hulk chitin and imbued with the creatures natural magic.
These breastplates were created through stacking together thick umber hulk plates and hide. They offered sturdy physical protection, similar to a +2 enchantment and gave the wielder complete immunity to any mind-affecting abilities and spells.
As of the , umber hulk hide breastplates were sold by a drow merchant who did business from the Undergate, Westgates unsavory underbelly.
