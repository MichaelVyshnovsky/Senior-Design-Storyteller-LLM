'''Powderkegs''' were a type of small [[keg]] used to store and transport either [[gunpowder]]<ref name ="drag321-p38">{{Cite dragon/321/The Way of the Gun|38}} or [[smokepowder]].

==Description==
Powderkegs were constructed of wood

==Trivia==
Gunpowder and smokepowder were typically sold within either powder horns or powderkegs.
