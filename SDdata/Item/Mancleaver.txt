A '''mancleaver''' was a distinctive and lightweight [[battleaxe]] wielded by some warriors of the [[Many-Arrows]] [[orcs]] during their occupation of the [[Neverwinter/River District|Tower District]] of [[Neverwinter]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{NWDistricts}}

==Description==
These axes were adorned with [[Orc language|orcish]] runes representing despair. The blood of the orcs' enemies was known to be left smeared on the weapon.

==Notable Owners==
The warband leader [[Kartok Paingiver]] was rumored to wield a ''mancleaver''.
