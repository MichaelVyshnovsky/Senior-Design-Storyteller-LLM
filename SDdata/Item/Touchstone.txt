Touchstones were a variety of smooth, hand-sized enchanted stones.
Whenever a touchstone was touched by a bare hand it would would let out a short tune.
* The Rolling Gnome in Felshroun was known to sell these.
Jestra Illowhand devised a spell for creating gemstones and rock crystals of a similar nature, as she liked to preserve music to hear on loop in the privacy of her town.
