* In the [[Eastern Heartlands]], some farmers in [[Cormyr]] and the [[Dalelands]] were known to make plum wine.{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-07-15 - Beverages of the Heartlands}}

* On the island nation of [[Kozakura]].
