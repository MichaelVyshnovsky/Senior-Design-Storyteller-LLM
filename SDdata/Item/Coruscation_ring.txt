Coruscation rings were enchanted bands that allowed the spellcasting wearer to illuminate targets with radiating orbs.
These simple rings counters bore engravings that projected provocative lights.
The arcane radiance enchantment weaved into these items added additional effects to the wearers damage-dealing spells. All targets that were damaged by the wearers spells were immediately illuminated by radiating orbs above their heads that showered them in bright light, making them visible, vulnerable, and exposed. The radiance lasted for twelve seconds.
