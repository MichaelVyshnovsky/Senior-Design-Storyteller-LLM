Baldurian seafood stew was a signature dish from the harbors of Baldurs Gate.
A hearty and versatile stew, these dishes exuded fragrance of garlic, fried tomato paste, and usually consisted of a wide variety of days catch seafood. Common versions were filled with clams, prawns, fish, and peas.
Created by the busy bustling inhabitants of Baldurs Gates harbors, this recipe became a staple on the city by the late-14 century DR, and even served in the Western Heartlands.
