The Moons Head Brace was an alcoholic mixed drink from the city of Ravens Bluff, served at the Silvermoon Playhouse in the late 14 century DR.
The Moons Head Brace was white in color and tasted of wild mushrooms. It was strong enough to send the imbibers head spinning.
