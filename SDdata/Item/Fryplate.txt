'''Fryplate''' was a simple and hearty dish favored by common folk and traveling merchants alike. Fryplate was often served across communities of [[Northwest Faerûn]] and [[Savage Frontier]]. One restaurant of note that served fryplates was the [[Maid of the Moors]] of [[Mornbryn's Shield]].

==Description==
A typical fryplate was cheap and filled bellies. The dish was assembled out of fried [[bacon]] and fried [[tomato|tomatoes]]. Then the chunky fried goodies were covered with a generous helping of sliced [[almond|almonds]] and fried wild [[mushroom|mushrooms]]. The platter was then smeared with rich [[butter|buttered]] scrambled [[egg|eggs]], cooked with [[peppercorn]]s, and a drizzle of [[brandy]].
