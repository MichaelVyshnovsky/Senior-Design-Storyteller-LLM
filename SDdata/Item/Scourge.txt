Scourges were an exotic variety of whips.
Scourges were short, multi-tailed whips laced with barbs. or even bone.
The barbs on scourges were often dipped in poisons that could be delivered through injury, such as greenblood oil, medium spider venom, and large scorpion venom. Beyond lashing opponents with them, scourges could be used to disarm someone.
 Cat-o-nine-tails  A scourge with nine thongs in which the barbs were replaced with tight knots.
 Ebon lash  an enchanted scourge that was developed by the Red Wizards of Thay to inflict intense agony upon their foes.
 Scourge of fangs  an enchanted scourge used exclusively by evil clerics. They had adamantine handles and five construct snakes in place of the normal cords.
 Scourge of pain  a scourge used by priestesses of Loviatar.
 Scourge of Tiamat  a variant of the scourge of fangs that used vipers in place of standard snakes.
