The '''''Spear of Darkness''''' was a unique melee weapon wielded by the [[avatar]] of [[Set]], the Lord of Evil within the [[Mulhorandi pantheon]].

==Description==
The weapon was a large jet-black [[spear]].

==Powers==
This weapon carried a powerful ''+4'' [[enchantment]], making it easier for Set to strike and harm opponents during combat.
