Sorrow was a unique, enchanted glaive that gradually twisted the soul of the wielder over time.
Every time the wielder of Sorrow wielded another creature with its place, their psyche became a little more fractured and damaged. It was said that every drop of blood tarnished their soul, a process that would continue until nothing else remained.
The wielder could use Sorrow to magically ensnare their foe in a tangle of vines.
It was said to have once belonged to the "shade" of an individual, or perhaps a member of the shade species. It was taken from that individual around the time Ketheric Thorm was defeated, and the shadow curse spread outward from Moonrise Towers.
As of the , Sorrow remained stored in a secret vault within the depths of the Emerald Grove.
