A '''''ring of reverse alignment''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[ring]] that completely flipped the wearer's nature.

==Powers==
These rings caused the wearer's true nature to take on the traits of its polar opposite. 

==Notable Owners==
[[Mefraset]], a [[Fangs of Set|Fang of Set]], placed a a ''ring of reverse alignment'' that prompted her to help a group of [[adventurer]]s against her previous allies.
