An '''''amulet of illusions''''' was a [[magic]] [[amulet]] that could be found in [[Waterdeep]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The ''amulet'' was green and had decorations that looked like eyes.

==Powers==
When the wearer of an ''amulet of illusions'' was in danger, it would automatically turn them ''[[Invisibility|invisible]]''.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Farideh]], a [[tiefling]] [[warlock]] who worked as an [[adventurer]] on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]], the [[Open Lord]] of Waterdeep, during the late 15{{th}} century DR.
