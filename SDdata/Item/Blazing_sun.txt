The '''''blazing sun''''' was a magical crystal orb held by the [[Cult of the Dragon]] in the midâ€“1480s [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}

==Description==
The orb was carved from crystal, and emblazoned with a symbol of [[Amaunator]].

==Powers==
The ''blazing sun'' protected the holder from elemental threats such as heat, cold, [[acid]]s, and shocks, and greatly improved the power of light, [[positive energy]], [[alignment]]-based spells while also improving the holder's concentration and thoughtfulness.

==History==
The orb was taken from the Cult of the Dragon by [[Harper]] agents, who assaulted [[Cult of the Dragon safehouse|their safehouse]] in the hopes of capturing the [[White Dragon Mask]].
