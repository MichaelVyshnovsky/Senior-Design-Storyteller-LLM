Champagne du le Stomp was a rare vintage of sparkling wine produced and distributed by the Wizard of Wines winery in Barovia.
The drink was a quality and rare vintage, known for its rich taste.
In the late 15 century DR, Arik Lorensk at the Blood on the Vine Tavern in the village of Barovia sought bottles of Champagne du le Stomp, the rarest and most precious vintage of the time. Several bottles of the wine were recovered and gifted to Arik by adventurers from Neverwinter.
