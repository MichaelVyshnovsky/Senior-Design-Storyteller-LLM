'''Aquamarine''' was a [[Precious stones|precious stone]] found mainly in northern [[Faer√ªn]], and was used as adornment by many [[Uthgardt|barbarian]] tribes of the region.

==Description==
This gemstone was a transparent form of [[wikipedia:en:beryl|beryl]], blue-green in color. Aquamarine was hard and durable, making it suitable for jewelry worn by active beings. A typical specimen had a base value of 500 [[gp]].

==Powers==
An aquamarine was a material component in casting an extremely rare and secret spell, ''[[Orgonil's ritual]]'', that made an exact copy of another gemstone, regardless of flaws, scratches, or carvings.{{Cite web/Princes of the Apocalypse Online Supplement|3}} and the primary ingredient in enchanting an ''[[aquamarine of spell extending]]''.

==Trivia==
* Aquamarine was a low-value "common" stone among the drow, and was not used often.
* Aquamarines were favored by the gods [[Deep Sashelas]] and [[Eldath]].{{Cite web enhancement/Deity Do's and Don'ts|11}}
* Priests of [[Istishia]] sometimes carved their symbolic wave into an aquamarine worn on a ring, medallion, or brooch.
