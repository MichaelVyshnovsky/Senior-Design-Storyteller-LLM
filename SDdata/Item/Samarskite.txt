'''Samarskite''' was a [[semi-precious stones|semi-precious]] gem used for mourning jewelry or to adorn black ceremonial dress.

==Description==
Samarskite was a rare-earth mineral,<ref group="note">In reality, samarskite contains uranium and other rare-earth elements and is radioactive. hard and dense, with a velvet-black metallic luster, usually cut [[wikipedia:cabochon|cabochon]]. A typical specimen had a base value of 50 gp.

==Powers==
One or more samarskite gems, carried or worn on the body, was known to protect against undead by reducing the severity of their attacks by a small amount.

==Trivia==
The lesser deity [[Vhaeraun]] was known to occasionally send samarskites to show his favour or disfavour to his followers.
