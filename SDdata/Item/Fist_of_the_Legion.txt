Fists of the Legion were enchanted maces created to be used against orcs who once menaced the Sword Coast.
These bludgeons bore a 1 enchantment that guided the welders hand toward the target and dealt additional magical damage upon a hit. Additionally, a Fist of the Legion had a coin-toss of a chance to stun and daze a creature it struck.
These weapons dated back to the , when they were forged to be wielded by the Sword Coast Legion, a militaristic organization consisting of clerics and paladins against growing orcish attacks on the settlements of the Sword Coast.
In the , a Fists of the Legion was wielded by a member of the Shadow Cultists who worshiped the King of Shadows within the Crossroad Keep.
