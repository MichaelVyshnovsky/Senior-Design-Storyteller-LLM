The Heroes of the Realms was a line of clay model figures depicting various renowned adventurers. They were made in the 14 century DR,Given the examples of adventurers given within Fires Eye, its possible that the adventurers covered by the line was limited to those who were renowned within the Vast. each figure being custom made, by the Toysmiths toy business in Ravens Bluff.
Sometime in the late 14 century DR, a group of adventurer friends of Dawn of the Desert Moon and Opal Starlight donated Heroes of the Realms figures to their shelter for orphaned youth, the Desert Moon Sanctuary And Starlight Cavalcade. These figures included personages of the druid Morra Starson
