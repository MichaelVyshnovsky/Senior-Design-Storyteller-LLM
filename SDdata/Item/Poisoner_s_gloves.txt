Poisoners gloves were enchanted gloves that strengthened the poison damage dealt by the wearer.
These simple leather gloves appeared clean and well-cared for. However, they had a strong, cold, acrid smell.
Poisoners gloves possessed a type of envenom enchantment that turned any poison damage dealt by the wearer, be it of magic spell, or simple weapon coating nature, into an affliction, possibly rendering the target weakened and afflicted by the poison.
In the , a pair of poisoners gloves was kept in Malus Thorms office in the House of Healing, within the deadly Shadow-cursed Lands.
