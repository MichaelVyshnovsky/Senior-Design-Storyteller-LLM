Stolyarofs table lute was a unique deep-voiced oversized lute that once belonged to Ahndri Stolyarof sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.
In the  this unique but mundane instrument was sold at the Chromatic Scale musical instrument shop located on the second floor of The Glitter Gala gem and jewelry vendor of the Lower City of Baldurs Gate. Stolyarofs table lute was among several unique instruments in Thomas C. Quirkiliouss inventory.
