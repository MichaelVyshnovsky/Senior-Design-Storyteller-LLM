'''Stolyarof's table lute''' was a unique deep-voiced oversized [[lute]] that once belonged to [[Ahndri Stolyarof]] sometime in or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}} this unique but mundane instrument was sold at the [[Chromatic Scale]] musical instrument shop located on the second floor of [[The Glitter Gala]] [[gem]] and jewelry vendor of the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]]. Stolyarof's table lute was among several unique instruments in [[Thomas C. Quirkilious]]'s inventory.
