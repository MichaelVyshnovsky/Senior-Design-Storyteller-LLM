'''''Bane wands''''' were [[enchanted]] [[wand]]s that held magic unleashed upon command.

==Description==
''Bane wands'' had {{SI|1'3|'"|long=yes}}  shafts of braided design. They were usually made using bone, [[ivory]], or [[wood]]. The total number of charges varied from wand to wand.

==Powers==
Each of these items held several charges of the ''[[bane (spell)|bane]]'' [[spell]]. The magics held within the wand could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of a wand's last charge, it disintegrated.

*[[Nym (Icewind Dale)|Nym]], a [[drow]] proprietor of rare and magical goods who traveled [[Icewind Dale]] and [[Dorn's Deep]] in the late 13{{th}} and early 14{{th}} centuries [[DR]].
