'''Breek''' was a soup served at the [[Flying Stag]] inn, in [[Voonlar]] ''circa'' the [[1368 DR|Year of the Banner, 1368 DR]].{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks archive|23}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 17: A Place to Stay}}

==Description==
Breek was prepared by boiling [[beef]] from all parts of a [[cow]] (including hooves) into a broth and then straining out the solid bits. This broth then became the stock for a soup of [[wild onion]]s, [[radish]]es, and hot [[spices]].
