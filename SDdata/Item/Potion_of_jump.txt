Potions of jump was a potion that granted its imbiber effects similar to that of the jump spell.
One of the recipe for a potion of jump required the alchemist to prepare a sublimate of wispweed first and then, carefully mix it with a certain type of ashes.
* Lupperdiddle Swires, a tiny gnomish adventurer, who reportedly, bounced  into the air in the late 15 century DR.
* Cult of the Dragon necromancers of the Cult of the Dragon, under the command of Kya Mordrayn, were known to carry potions of jump in 1369 DR. These spellcasters could be found in the Tower of Bones on Polyandrium as well as Castle Cormanthor.
* Scouts of the drow House Dhuurniv were outfitted with potions of jump in the late-14 century DR.
