Star pots, also called fire pots, were ceramic incendiary devices found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.
These ceramic jars were filled with liquid star and fitted with a thick wax wick. The jars were constructed to be strong enough to avoid accidents or light impacts and were typically used with siege engines, though they could be thrown by hand to great effect assuming the wielder was strong enough to ensure they shattered upon impact.
When shattered a star pot would douse any nearby objects with flaming liquid star oil. The liquid star burned for several minutes following the impact.
