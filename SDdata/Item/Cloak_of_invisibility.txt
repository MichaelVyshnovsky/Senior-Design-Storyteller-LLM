A cloak of invisibility was a powerful magical cloak that allowed its wearer to become invisible for a cumulative total of 2 hours.  It then regains 1 hour of use per 12 continual hours of not being used.
The wearer activated the cloaks power by pulling up its hood. The cloak of invisibility then turned the wearer and everything being worn or carried invisible for as long as the hood was pulled up.

*Daveaks cloak the enchanted cloak from Chult, found in the late 15 century DR. This cloak allowed the wearer to turn invisible at will and sages argued about its origins and even how its enchantments were weaved.
The mercenary Jarlaxle Baenre owned a cloak of invisibility as part of his extensive collection of magic items.
