A '''''wand of metal and mineral detection''''' was a [[magic item]] that revealed the location of metals.

==Powers==
The effective range of these wands was {{SI|30|ft|long=yes}}. A ''wand of metal and mineral detection'' would pulse in the wielder's hand and point toward any detected source of metal or mineral. The wielder could also choose to concentrate on finding only a specific type of metal or mineral such as [[gold]] or [[diamond]].

==Notable Owners==
[[Korbus Brightjewel]], a [[gnome]] jeweler who lived in [[Daggerford]], owned a ''wand of metal and mineral detection''.
