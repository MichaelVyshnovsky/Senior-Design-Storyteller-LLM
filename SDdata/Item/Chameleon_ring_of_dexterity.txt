Chameleon rings of dexterity were minor enchanted rings beneficial to rogues and thieves.
These bands of precious metal were enchanted to slightly boost the wearers dexterity and improve their ability to hide in the shadows.
In the  a chameleon ring of dexterity was among the possessions of Elytharra Dinnesmore, an enchantress and wife of Speaker Ulbrec Dinnesmore of Targos in Icewind Dale.
