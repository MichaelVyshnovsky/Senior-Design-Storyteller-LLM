'''''Chameleon rings of dexterity''''' were minor [[enchanted]] [[ring]]s beneficial to [[rogue]]s and [[thieves]].

==Powers==
These bands of precious metal were enchanted to slightly boost the wearer's dexterity and improve their ability to hide in the shadows.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1312}} a ''chameleon ring of dexterity'' was among the possessions of [[Elytharra Dinnesmore]], an [[enchantress]] and wife of Speaker [[Ulbrec Dinnesmore]] of [[Targos]] in [[Icewind Dale]].
