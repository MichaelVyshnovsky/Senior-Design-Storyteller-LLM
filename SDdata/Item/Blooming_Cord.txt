The Blooming Cord was an artifact enchanted belt that boosted the wearers force of personality and mental prowess.
The Blooming Cord was a delicate belt made of a blooming vine that exuded an emboldening aura and a gentle scent.
The belt increased the wearers damage dealt via lucky and devastating attacks and well as granted them better awareness of their surrounding. It also increased the wearers charisma and wisdom. The Blooming Cord was a part of a set that, when used together, boosted the magics of all items in the set a staff of flowers, the Woven Vine, and the Blooming Cord.
The Blooming Cord was among many magical treasures hidden deep within the mega-dungeon of Undermountain and its Wyllowwood level.
