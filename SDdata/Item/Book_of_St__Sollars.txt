The '''''Book of St. Sollars''''' was a religious tome dedicated to [[St. Sollars|St. Sollars the Twice-Martyred]], a saint of [[Ilmater]] the Broken God. It consisted of numbered verses.

A copy rested on the altar of the [[Abbey of St. Sollars]] around [[1359 DR]].
