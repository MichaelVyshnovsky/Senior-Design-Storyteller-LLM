The '''''empowered dragonscale''''' was a [[red dragon]]scale found in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], taken from the wyrm [[Daeronnax]].{{DDO-time}}
{{Quote
|A glowing red dragonscale from a large red dragon.
}}
==Powers==
The dragonscale greatly improved the bearer's health, fortitude, and resistance to fire.

==History==
The dragonscale was taken from the [[red dragon]] [[Daeronnax]], the master of [[Clan Daeronnax]] in the [[Stonelands]], after he kidnapped the adventurer [[Tristran of Daggerford]].
