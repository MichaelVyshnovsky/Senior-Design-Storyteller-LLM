Oil of taggit was a contact or ingestion poison used to render an person or creature unconscious.
The oil could be administered to food or drink, or used to coat a piercing or slashing weapon.
While it caused a being to lose consciousness, it did not inflict any physical harm upon the subject.
* Shandie Freefoot, the halfling rogue of Baldurs Gate kept with her a vial of oil of taggit.
