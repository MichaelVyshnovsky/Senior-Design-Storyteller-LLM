'''''Oil of taggit''''' was a contact or ingestion [[poison]] used to render an person or creature unconscious.

==Description==
The oil could be administered to food or drink, or used to coat a piercing or slashing weapon.{{Cite comic/Evil at Baldur's Gate 4|10}}

==Powers==
While it caused a being to lose consciousness, it did not inflict any physical harm upon the subject.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Shandie Freefoot]], the [[halfling]] [[rogue]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] kept with her a vial of ''oil of taggit''.
