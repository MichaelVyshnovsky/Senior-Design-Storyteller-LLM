'''Gunpowder wands''' were some of the first [[pistol|handgun]]s known outside of the island nation of [[Lantan]].

==Description==
These firearms were plated in [[silver]] and carried up to two shots. Unlike other Lantanese firearms, which used the substance [[smokepowder]],<ref> as their name implied gunpowder wands operated on [[gunpowder]] that had been [[divine magic|divinely]] [[item enchantment|enchanted]] into a non-inert state by high-ranking [[priest]]s of [[Gond]] with their [[deity]]'s blessing.<ref>{{Cite web/theedverse/2021-05-04 - Gunpowder Wand and Working Gunpowder}}

==Notable Owners==
* [[Wendeira Illathos]], a devout worshiper of Gond, owned a consecrated pair of gunpowder wands that she kept in her boots. She would use them whenever she went out hunting or needed to defend her shop, [[Wendeira's Wondery]].
