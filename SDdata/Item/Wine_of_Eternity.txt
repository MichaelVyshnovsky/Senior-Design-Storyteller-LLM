Wine of Eternity was a very rare enchanted chalice that possessed incapacitating magics.
The chalices magics, or the effects created by the liquid within, could render a single powerful foe incapacitated. The sleeping ensorcellment lasted indefinitely until the affected creature could break free from the magics.
