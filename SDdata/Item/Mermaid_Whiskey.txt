'''Mermaid Whiskey''' was a [[whiskey]] exported from [[Tethyr]].

==Description==
This whiskey was described as having a very elegant and refined flavor, with its expressiveness being compared to a [[bard]]'s song. It was commonly sold in a wonderfully crafted blue bottle bearing a depiction of a [[mermaid]].

==Availability==
It was sought after by the wealthy in [[Baldur's Gate]] and could be frequently found within their mansions. It was also stocked by taverns in the city, notably the [[Elfsong Tavern]].
