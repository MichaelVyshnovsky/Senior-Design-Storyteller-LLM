A '''samisen''' ({{Pronounce|sah|mee|sen|refs=}}) was a stringed instrument common in the land of [[Wa]].

==Description==
A samisen was similar to a [[wikipedia:Guitar|guitar]], or [[lute]]. It was plucked.

==Reputation==
Among all the instruments found in Wa, none brought more respect to a musician than the samisen.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Naomi (consort)|Naomi]], the favored consort of [[Benju Matsutomo]] of [[Aru]].
* [[Sono]], a household servant of Benju.
