Soraevoras spear was a unique polearm, sacred to the goddess Angharradh, The Triune Goddess from the elven island of Evermeet.
Soraevoras spear was enhanced with Angharradhs divine magic, granting it a +2 enchantment to damage that guided the spear toward its target. The spear dealt piercing and additional divine damage upon each strike. Soraevoras spears unique nature only made it usable by goodly elf favored souls.
