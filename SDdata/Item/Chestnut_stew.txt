'''Chestnut stew''' was a rather simple vegetable dish that had been around in the [[Icewind Dale]] region for a long time prior to the late-15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
This dish consisted of roasted [[chestnut]]s, [[lentil]]s, and [[bread]] that was a day old.
