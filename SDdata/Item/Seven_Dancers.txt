The '''''Seven Dancers''''' were a set of unique [[gold]]en statuettes dedicated to [[Sune]], the Lady Firehair.

==Description==
These precious items depicted goddess Sune and were studded in [[rubies]], [[opal]]s, and [[sapphire]]s.

==History==
As of the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], the ''Seven Dancers'' were among the major treasures recovered from the numerous cliff caves along the [[Horn Shore]] on the [[Tethyr Peninsula]]. The statuettes were found between the {{YearlinkName|1360}} and the {{YearlinkName|1370}}.
