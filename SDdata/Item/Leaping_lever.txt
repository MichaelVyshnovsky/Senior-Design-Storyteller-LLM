'''Leaping levers''' were tools in the Realms popular among [[bard]]s and performers who used them to soar through the air with dramatic jumps.

==Description==
A leaping lever resembled an offset seesaw.

==History==
These items were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]''.

==Notable Users==
* Leaping levers were favored by bards.
