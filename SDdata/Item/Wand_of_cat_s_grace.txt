'''''Cat's grace wands''''' were [[enchanted]] [[wand]]s that held multiple charges of beneficial spells unleashed upon command.

==Description==
''Cat's grace wands'' were {{SI|1'3|'"|long=yes}} items of angular design. They were usually made using bone, [[ivory]], or [[wood]]. The total number of charges varied from wand to wand.

==Powers==
Each of these mages' accessories held several charges of the ''[[cat's grace]]'' spell. The magics held within the wand could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of a wand's last charge, it disintegrated.
