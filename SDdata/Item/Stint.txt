Stint was a strong gnomish pale ale from the city of Ravens Bluff in the Vast.
Stint was a variety of aggressively smelling ale made with mushrooms and spices. It was sometimes served with two highsunfeasts, Brick or Rosto.
Stint was a staple of a gnomish out-of-the way café in Ravens Bluff called Le PoTeete Café in the late 14 century DR. It served only gnomish pale ales, with Greentart and Rockgrit being the only alternatives.
