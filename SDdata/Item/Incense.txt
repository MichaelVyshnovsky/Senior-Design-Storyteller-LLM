'''Incense''' was common in many religious rituals and [[spell]]s across [[Toril]]. The form of incense commonly used at altars and in temple fires was known as '''temple incense'''.

{{quote|''You will grow to like Jaheira's incense in time. Like - chewing herbs and soap, yes?''|[[Minsc]] of [[Rashemen]]}}

==Description==
Incense was most commonly found in the form of sticks that could be lighted directly, or blocks that could be burned in [[brazier]]s or [[censer]]s. A stick would burn for about half an hour. and would produce a hazy cloud with a radius of {{SIrange|10|20|feet|long=yes}}.

Incense was known for its pleasant smell, which served as a "[[perfume]] of the [[god]]s". It was sometimes said to help carry the prayers of the faithful to their deities.

===Varieties===
A wide variety of incense with [[magical]] properties existed, such as:
* ''[[Incense of consecration]]''
* ''[[Incense of the Gorgon]], ''a foul smelling form of incense that created a magical cloud with the effect of petrification.
* ''[[Incense of meditation]]''
* ''[[Incense of obsession]]''
* ''[[Incense of somnolence]]'', a sleep-inducing incense from the land of [[Halruaa]].
* ''[[Mesquite]]'', a form of healing incense that the [[Azuposi]] derived from a plant of the same name.

==Uses==
===Religion===
* Incense was commonly used throughout [[Toril]] at altars and in temple braziers. Temple incense was known to be enhanced with added [[thael leaf]] that have it roasted nuts, [[almond]]s, and [[herb]]al scent.{{Cite web/theedverse/2024-1-6 - Andred Kuroone, Lurandural Veltree, Jhalanavress Tanthavur, and Orstrar Galannavruk}}
* Incense was an essential material in a [[priest's pack]] and was also usually carried by [[acolyte]]s of temples.
* [[Cloud giant]]s were known to scatter incense into the wind each morning as a religious practice.
* It had religious significance in [[Sambral]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]], making it very popular there.{{Cite organized play/LFR/The Burning Scent of Perfumed Swords|50-52}}

===Other===
* Bundles of incense stick were one of many items that had to be annually gifted to a [[Djangkong]] by their descendants.
* [[Wizard slayer]]s were known to ritually inhale incense on a regular basis, starting at a young age.
* Certain kinds of incense were used as [[insect repellent]] in places like [[Chult]].

==Availability==
* A number of incenses with magic-enhancing properties were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]''.
* Incense was one of the major imports of the [[Mulhorand]] empire.
* Incense was one of the major exports of the [[Yikarian Empire]].{{Cite dragon/241/Campaign Classics: The Roof of the World}}
* Certain temple incenses came from the [[Anauroch]] desert. The [[D'tarig]] and the [[Bedine]] collected [[resin]] from [[cassia]], [[myrrh]], [[frankincense]] and [[dragons' blood]] trees, then sold it to outlanders, many of which were merchants sponsored by the [[Zhentarim]].
* Incense commonly found in the [[Western Heartlands]] and the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] had a sweet scent of [[sandalwood]].
