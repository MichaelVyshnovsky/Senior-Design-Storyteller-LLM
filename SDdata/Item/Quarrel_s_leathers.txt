Quarrels leathers was a set of unique enchanted leather armor created by an obsessive half-drow warlock named Quarrel in the late 14 century DR.
The armor was stitched together from fragments of armor that one was worn by warlock Ammon Jerro, Quarrels beloved idol. This mismatched armor possessed a +2 enchantment and allowed the wearer to cast the bestow curse spell. The armor could also be only worn by warlocks, half-elves, and those who venerated evil.
Quarrels leathers were the warlocks favorite armor. He wore the leathers into battle during the Quarrels search for the source of Ammon Jerros arcane powers.
