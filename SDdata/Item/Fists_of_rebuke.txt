'''''Fists of rebuke''''' were [[enchanted]] [[gauntlet]]s worn by [[fighter]]s. They could be found in [[Waterdeep]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
A wearer of the ''fists of rebuke'' benefitted from enhanced combat reflexes, which allowed them to swiftly counterattack most of their enemies' blows.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Tommus Tallstep]], a [[halfling]] fighter, possessed a fine pair of ''fists of rebuke'' that were nice enough to wear to a dinner with members of the [[nobility of Waterdeep]].
