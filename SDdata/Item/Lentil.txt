'''Lentils''' were a type of edible legume found in the [[Realms]].

==Availability==
Lentils could be found in the [[Savage Frontier]] and [[Vilhon Reach]].<ref>{{Cite polyhedron/97/The Living City: The Hand of Mercy and Marigold's Menagerie|8}} They were also sold in [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']], under its "Aurora's Larder" section.

==Notable Uses==
*[[Lentil stew]], served alongside smoked [[cheese]] could be found in the City of Splendors, [[Waterdeep]], including the kitchens of the [[Blackstaff Tower]] as [[Khelben Arunsun]] was quite found of the dish.
*[[Beldabar's Rest]], an inn beneath the city of [[Yartar]], served lentils in soup alongside [[onion]]s and [[rice]].
*In [[Port Nyanzaru]] of [[Chult]], lentils featured in both [[asa wat]] (sea [[tuna]] stewed with lentils, [[red onion]]s, and spicy [[berbere]] spice) and [[atkilt wat]] (a milder dish with [[chard (vegetable)|chard]] instead of fish).
*People of [[Icewind Dale]] prepared [[chestnut stew]]s using lentils.
