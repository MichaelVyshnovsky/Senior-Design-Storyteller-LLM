Guardian orbs, were wizardly orb implements that boosted the wielders abilities, popularized in the 15 century DR.
Those mages who touched a guardian orb had their mental prowess boosted by the items magics and they felt their resolve and defenses steeling.
In the 15 century DR, various guardian orbs could be found among bandit spellcasters of the Sword Mountains and the Crags, areas surrounding the city, markets of Neverwinter, wielded by servants of the vampire lord Ozit who laired in the ruins of Illefarn.
