Beastmasters Chain was an enchanted amulet that allowed the wearer to befriend beasts.
Beastmasters amulet was decorated with a green gem set into the hand-shaped body. The gem was shaped like a cats slivered pupil.
Beastmasters Chain allowed the wearer to cast the animal friendship spell.
In 1492 DR, Beastmasters Chain was kept in worg pens of the Cult of the Absolute inside the Shattered Sanctum. The goblins of the cult used the amulet to train worgs they used in raids on settlements of the Western Heartlands.
