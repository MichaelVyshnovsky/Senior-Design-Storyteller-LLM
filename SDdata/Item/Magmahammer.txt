'''''Magmahammer''''', or '''''Magmammer''''', was a [[mattock]] that was the favoured [[weapon]] of [[Dumathoin]], the [[dwarf|dwarven]] [[deity]] of mining, exploration, and the riches of the earth.<ref name=frcs-3e-p238">

==Description==
''Magmahammer'' was composed of solid magma.

==Powers==
This weapon had a mighty ''+5'' enchantment. It had the combined powers of a ''[[maul of the titans]]'' and a ''[[mattock of the titans]]''.
