Bouillabaisse was a popular, hearty form of seafood stew.
This aromatic dish typically consisted of some form of fish, clams, and mussels simmered in a light fish and tomato stock, and served with rouille. Some taverns even included a serving of aldani meat.
People often chose to soak herb breads in this dish.
This dish could be found served in many taverns, homesteads, and seafaring vessels. It could particularly be found throughout the Sword Coast and in port cities such as Baldurs Gate, Neverwinter, and Waterdeep.
Baldurian seafood stew A regional variety of the dish unique to Baldurs Gate.
Sword Coast Bouillabaisse This regional variety of the dish often used cod or haddock for its fish, Other ingredients typical of this version of the dish included bay leaves, a pinch of fennel, a large leek, a yellow onion, parsley, saffron, and white wine.
This dish was especially popular among peasantry, sailors, and swashbucklers.
