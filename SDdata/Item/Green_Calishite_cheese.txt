Green Calishite was a spicy cheese crafted in Calimshan. It was also sold through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
The Calishites added curry to give this cheese some extra kick while preserving it at the same time. The spice also added a pale beige, Its taste was called rumnund in Calimshan and it had a long-lasting shelf life and was the least-crumbly among a wide variety of curry cheeses made in the nation.
Green Calishite cheese could be purchased in the following sizes
* Loaf, 1/2 lb. (0.23 kg.) 4 sp
This cheese was considered to be far too spicy for the tastes of most northerners. It was said that a northerner could be incapacitated if they took an unsuspecting bite of it.
Traditionally, Green Calishite was eaten by itself or used to thicken sauces. The cheese paired well with wines, cordials, and spirits.
