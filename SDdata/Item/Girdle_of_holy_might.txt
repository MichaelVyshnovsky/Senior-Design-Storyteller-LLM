Girdles of holy might were enchanted belts that were used by clerics to enhance their spell-casting abilities.
These belts more enchantments that improved clerics spell-casting by granting them addition spells of mediocre to high power.
The Sword Coast Legion was a military cleric organization that was outfitted by the girdles of holy might to aid in their fight against ravaging orc gangs in the , the Year of the Black Horde.
