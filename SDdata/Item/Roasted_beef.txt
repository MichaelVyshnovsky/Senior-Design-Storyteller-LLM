'''Roasted beef''' was a dish made from a sizable chunk of meat from a [[cow|bovine beast]] common across [[Faer√ªn]].

==Description==
Roasted beef was considered properly prepared when it was thoroughly cooked yet remained drippingly moist.{{cite web| url = https://web.archive.org/web/20211224220308/http://candlekeep.com/downloads/so_saith_ed-Part-20.zip| title = So Saith Ed Oct - Dec 2008| accessdate = 24-12-2021| date = 24-12-2021}}

==Availability==
*In [[Arrabar]], [[Vilhon Reach]], roasted beef was a popular dish served smothered in gravy. Beef roast was one of the best dishes on the menu available at ''aszraun'' called [[Dark She Looks Upon Me]].
*In [[Arabel]], [[Cormyr]], a plateful of sliced roast beef could be purchased at the [[Hungry Man]] restaurant.
*[[Maid of the Moors]] in the town of [[Mornbryn's Shield]] in the [[Savage Frontier]] served a roast beef platter for 6 [[cp]] and a whole roast 3 [[gp]].
*In the city of [[Neverwinter]], roasted beef prime rib was cooked in [[cognac]], [[mustard]], white [[wine]], and whole [[peppercorn]]s. The roast was then sliced and drowned in the sauce it was cooked in.
*In the city of the [[Ravens Bluff]], the tavern called [[The Seaside Salvage Company]] served a wide variety of simple roasts, including beef, valued at 4 [[silver]] [[coin]]s per serving.
*In [[Sembia]], [[Selgaunt]]an roasted beef was often served with wine, [[carrot]]s, [[onion]]s, pickled [[asparagus]] in a spicy [[Turmish cream sauce]], accompanied by [[oxtail soup]] and fried [[wheat cake]] smothered in jellied [[pears]].
*In [[Waterdeep]], the City of Splendors, the Master of the Kitchens of [[Cragsmere|House Cragsmere]], [[Klonstahansz Almurrhand]], was renowned for his masterfully prepared moist and juicy roast beef and roast [[boar]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1348}}, the Lady Lord of [[Eveningstar]], [[Tessaril Winter]], welcomed the [[Knights of Myth Drannor]] with a feast of roast beef and [[Sembian]] semi-sweet [[wine]].

In the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], guests of the monastery of [[The Great Mother's House at Berbusk]] were often treated with a feast that included [[chicken]], roast beef, [[pork]], [[sausage]]s, and various fruits and vegetables, all raised by the monks themselves.
