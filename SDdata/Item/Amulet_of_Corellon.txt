'''''Amulets of Corellon''''' were enchanted [[amulet]]s used by [[cleric]]s of [[Corellon Larethian]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
''Amulets of Corellon'' were small [[silver]] crescents on a blue string.

==Powers==
These crescent amulets were imbued with divine magics that allowed its wearer to cast ''[[protection from evil]]'' on themselves and three allies. These items could be used exclusively by clerics of Corellon.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Halbenet Soladonel]]: a [[moon elf]] [[cleric]] of [[Corellon Larethian]] who worked with other warriors of [[Waterdeep]], performing missions on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
