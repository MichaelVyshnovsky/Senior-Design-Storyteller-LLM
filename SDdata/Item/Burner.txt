'''Burners''' were scientific items in the Realms often used by [[wizard]]s during laboratory experiments and research with [[brazier]]s.

==Description==
A burner was a small slab of wax where [[linen]] rags were coiled into wicks and light aflame to provide constant heat within a brazier.

==Availability==
* ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' sold burners at a price of 5 [[silver piece]]s.

==Notable Users==
* Burners were favored by wizards.
