The Mages Friend was a magic ring that once belonged to L. De Hurst of the Arcane Tower in the Underdark.
After years of being stored away and disused, the ring became covered in dust and cobwebs.
Befitting its original owner, a cleric of Mystra, the Mages Friend granted its wearer insights into arcane knowledge and religious lore.
