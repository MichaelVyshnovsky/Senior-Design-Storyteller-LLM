Waukeens curing clasp was an enchanted cloak clasp created by the Church of Waukeen of Ravens Bluff during the goddesss absence from the Realms.
Waukeens curing clasp was a finely-crafted coin-shaped clasp of pure gold. The front of the item was decorated with Waukeens profile.
The wearer of Waukeens curing clasp could tap into the items magic to heal themselves or other creatures. The clasp had a limited amount of healing it could produce per day.
