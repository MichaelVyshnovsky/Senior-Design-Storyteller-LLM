Impact was a quality that could be applied to bludgeoning weapons to double the chance of its wielder scoring a critical strike.
To craft an impact weapon, a spellcaster needed weapon of impact available.
* Craftmaster, the personal weapon of the craftsman god Gond, was a +4 impact returning seeking throwing warhammer.
* The Great Mallet of Silvanus, the personal weapon of the Oak Father, was a +5 energy aura impact mighty cleaving maul.
