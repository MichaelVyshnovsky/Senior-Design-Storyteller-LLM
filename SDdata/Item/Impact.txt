'''''Impact''''' was a [[weapon quality|quality]] that could be applied to bludgeoning weapons to double the chance of its wielder scoring a critical strike.

==Creation==
To craft an ''impact'' weapon, a spellcaster needed ''[[weapon of impact]]'' available.

==Notable ''Impact'' Weapons==
* ''[[Craftmaster]]'', the personal weapon of the craftsman god [[Gond]], was a ''+4 impact [[returning]] [[seeking]] [[throwing]] [[warhammer]]''.
* The ''[[Great Mallet of Silvanus]]'', the personal weapon of the [[Silvanus|Oak Father]], was a ''+5 [[energy aura]] impact [[mighty cleaving]] [[maul]]''.
