'''Strength''' was a [[armor quality|quality]] that could be applied to [[armor]], giving its wearer increased strength. Varying degrees of strength enhancement were available for this armor quality.

==Creation==
The spell ''[[bull's strength]]'' was used in the [[item enchantment|enchantment]] of armor with this quality.
