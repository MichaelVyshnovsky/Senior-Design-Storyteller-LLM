Ogrums Siegebow was a unique and powerful enchanted heavy crossbow once wielded by one-orc army Slogg Ogrum.
Despite Ogrums Siegebow being slightly harder to aim with, missiles that left the weapon packed a mighty wallop, similar to that of a powerful +6 enchantment. Additionally, the crossbow generated an infinite supply of bolts of fire.
Ogrums Siegebow was a mystery, and its origins were unknown. The first time the weapon appeared was in the , when Slogg Ogrum singlehandedly besieged the town of Priapurl in the Eastern Heartlands. When the orc was defeated with the help of the Mindgulph Mercenary Company, Ogrums vast arsenal of weapons was uncovered, however, no one really uncovered the reasons for the orcs siege of the town.
In the , Ogrums Siegebow was sold in Westgate, among countless unique and rare weapons and armor on the citys Market Triangle.
