'''Brassla''' was a traditional [[Luskan]] dish of boiled rice which was then added to finely chopped and fried [[eel]], [[fish]], [[frog]], [[mouse]], [[onion]], and [[oyster]].

==History==
The dish was available at the [[Seven Sails Inn]] in Luskan around [[1370 DR]].
