The '''''fiendish moon''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[sickle]] that appeared to blaze with fire. It could be found in [[Waterdeep]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
A strike from the ''fiendish moon'' had a similar effect as the ''[[vampiric touch]]'' spell, inflicting harm while restoring some of the wielder's vitality.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Farideh]], a [[tiefling]] [[warlock]], possessed a ''fiendish moon'' while adventuring in Waterdeep.
