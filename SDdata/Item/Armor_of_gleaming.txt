An '''''armor of gleaming''''' was a [[magic item|magically]] enchanted [[armor]] that was always spotlessly clean.

==Description==
Any suit of medium armor, such as [[hide armor|hide]], [[chain shirt]]s, [[scale armor]], [[breastplate]]s, and [[half plate]], or even heavy armor such as [[ring mail|ring]], [[chainmail|chain]], [[splint mail|splint]], and [[plate mail]], could be enchanted to become an ''armor of gleaming''.

==Powers==
The enchantment present in an ''armor of gleaming'' ensured that it never became dirty.
