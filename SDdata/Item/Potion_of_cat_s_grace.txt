Potions of cats grace were common magical concoctions that increased the imbibers dexterity.
Potions of cats grace greatly increased the drinkers dexterity, similarly to the effects of the cats grace spell.
Circa 1369 DR, the ruins of Myth Drannor still held magical potions left behind by the remnants of the city. Potions of cats grace in delicate crystal decanter were among them., and as far as the Nelanther Isles.
Circa 1373 DR, potions of cats grace were part of a standard equipment for the wizards and agents of Thay.
