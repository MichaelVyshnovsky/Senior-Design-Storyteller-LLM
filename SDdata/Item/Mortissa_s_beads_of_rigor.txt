Mortissas beads of rigor wan an enchanted necklace made out of finger bones, from the ruins of Myth Drannor.
The magic necklace allowed the wearer to cast the halt undead spell. Mortissas beads of rigor held seven charges of the spell.
In 1369 DR, Mortissas beads of rigor could be uncovered in the Lower Keep levels of the Elven Catacombs, hidden within a locked old chest among possessions of orog marauders who laired there.
