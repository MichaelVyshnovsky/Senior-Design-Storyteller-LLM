'''''Quills of memory''''' were [[enchanted]] [[quill]]s that could be found in [[Waterdeep]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
A ''quill of memory'' could be used to record an encounter with a enemy. This record helped the owner to learn of their foes' weak points after they had faced them at least once.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Vajra Safahr]], the [[Blackstaff]] of Waterdeep during the late 15{{th}} century DR.
