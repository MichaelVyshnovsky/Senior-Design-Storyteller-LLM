Hauberks of divinity were enchanted suits of paladin chainmail armor, encountered in Chult in the late 15 century DR.
These heavy chainmails were imbued with a +4 enchantment to the wearers armor. Additionally, these hauberks improved the wearers vitality. However, the most notable magical feature was its ability to awaken faith even in the hearts of the most stoic unbelievers.
Dragonbait a saurial paladin and a one-time companion of Artus Cimber during their foray into the jungles of Chult in the late 15 century DR.
