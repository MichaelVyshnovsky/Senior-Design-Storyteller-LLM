A '''''scarab of venom''''' was an [[enchanted]] piece of jewelry that was favored by [[warrior]]s of [[Set]].

==Description==
This item was a [[gemstone]].

==Powers==
Warriors of Set would place the ''scarab of venom'' on the hilts of their [[sword]]s. The [[magic]] of the ''scarab'' would then coat the blade with [[poison]] that caused excrutiating pain and often killed within minutes. The ''scarab of venom'' could be used twice per day and the venom lasted for several minutes before evaporating.
