Dragons Breath Beer was a strong beer exported from Sembia and sold through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
This beer had a strong and harsh flavor. It was best served with dark rye bread and Death Cheese.
Dragons Breath Beer could be purchased in the following containers
* Hand keg (12 cp)
* Cask (6 sp)
* Barrel (2 gp)
* Butt (6 gp)
* Tun (10 gp)
* Dragons Breath was available from the Den of the Seven Vales inn and the Mithrest in the Guards District of Athkatla, as well as the Belching Dragon tavern in Nashkel.
