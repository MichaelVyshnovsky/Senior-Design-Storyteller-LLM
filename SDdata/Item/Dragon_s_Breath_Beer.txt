'''Dragon's Breath Beer''' was a strong [[beer]] exported from [[Sembia]] and sold through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]''.

==Description==
This beer had a strong and harsh flavor. It was best served with dark [[rye bread]] and [[Death Cheese]].

==Prices==
Dragon's Breath Beer could be purchased in the following containers:
* Hand keg (12 [[cp]])
* Cask (6 [[sp]])
* Barrel (2 [[gp]])
* [[Butt]] (6 gp)
* [[Tun]] (10 gp)

==Availability==
* Dragon's Breath was available from the [[Den of the Seven Vales]] inn and the [[Mithrest]] in the [[Athkatla/Guards District|Guards District]] of [[Athkatla]], as well as the [[Belching Dragon]] tavern in [[Nashkel]].
