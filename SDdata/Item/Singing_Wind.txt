The '''''Singing Wind''''' was a powerful enchanted [[bard]] [[lute]] from [[Chult]].

==Powers==
The ''Singing Wind'' was imbued with mighty magics that allowed the player to shake the earth with their thunderous music, make the wind howl and sing along, and make the flames dance.
