Hideos kama were enchanted kamas that originated from the Kyo Prefecture in Kara-Tur.
Despite appearing as a simple peasant tool of trade, Hideos kama were deadly weapons with a +2 enchantment and magics that enhanced the wielders parrying while in combat.
Hideos kama were created by Hideo to arm peasants for the defense of their home â€“ Kyo Prefecture. The simple folk were harassed by a band of marauding pirates, and with the help of these magic implements, they survived the battle. Soon after, Hideo himself fell out of favor with the locals and perished in poverty as he used up all of his money in the development and production of these kama.
By the late 14 century DR, several of Hideos kama found way west and ended up in the hands of merchants and adventurers of the Sword Coast and the city of Neverwinter. In the , Okuzos Imports store in Westgates Harbor Loop had Hideos kama in stock, brought straight from Kara-Tur.
