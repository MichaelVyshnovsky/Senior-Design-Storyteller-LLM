Ring of stamina were enchanted rings that fortified the wearer against ailments of the body.
Rings of stamina were silver bands that radiated magical glow.
These rings magically enhanced the wearers stamina and fortitude.
In 1369 DR, an elvish ring of stamina could be found in the ruined Elven Catacombs underneath Castle Cormanthor inside one of the long-dried out wells.
