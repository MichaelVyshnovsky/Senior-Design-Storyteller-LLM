'''''Ring of stamina''''' were enchanted rings that fortified the wearer against ailments of the body.

==Description==
''Rings of stamina'' were [[silver]] bands that radiated magical glow.

==Powers==
These rings magically enhanced the wearer's stamina and fortitude.

==History==
In [[1369 DR]], an [[elf|elvish]] ''ring of stamina'' could be found in the ruined [[Elven Catacombs]] underneath [[Castle Cormanthor]] inside one of the long-dried out wells.
