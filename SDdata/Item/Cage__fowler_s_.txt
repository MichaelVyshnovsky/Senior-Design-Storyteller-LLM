Cages, also called fowlers cages, were tools sometimes used by rangers or woodsmen throughout Faerûn.
Cages were typically constructed from brass or bamboo imported from Hungtse province in Shou Lung. They collapsed to a flat panel for easy storage when not in use. A bamboo cage weighed , while brass cages weighed around .
These items were used to train birds and small animals.
Cages were available for purchase through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue for 8–12 gold pieces depending on the material used for their construction.
