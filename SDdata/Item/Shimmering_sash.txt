'''''Shimmering sashes''''' were [[magical item]]s that could be found in [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate. They were only used by [[holy slayer]]s.

==Description==
These [[sash]]es were brightly colored and were layered with embroidery depicting protective texts and wards.

==Powers==
Three times per day the wearer of this sash could alter his or her image just like a ''[[blur]]'' [[spell]]. When using this power the wearer appeared to shimmer like heated desert air.
