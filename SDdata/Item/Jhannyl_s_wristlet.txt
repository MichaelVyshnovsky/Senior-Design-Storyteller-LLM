'''''Jhannyl's wristlet''''' was a magical item used by the [[Harpers]].

==Powers==
This delicate bracelet, crafted from shimmering silver metal and adorned with intricate filigree and dangling charms, possessed abilities that exclusively safeguarded and impacted the wearer. It provided additional protection from attacks, particularly against ''[[Poison (spell)|poison]]'', [[polymorph]], and [[petrification]] attacks. The protection provided accumulated alongside the protections provided by other magical items and spells. However, if more than one of these bracelets was worn, their magic negated each other, offering no protective benefits to the wearer whatsoever.

In addition, the bracelet automatically bestowed the ability to [[water breathing|breathe underwater]] whenever the wearer was submerged. It also invoked a ''[[feather fall]]'' effect whenever the wearer fell or was subjected to reverse gravity magic. Once per day, it effortlessly [[Remove disease|cured any diseases]] the wearer encountered. Finally, the bracelet had the power to cast the ''[[heal]]'' spell on the wearer once per day, either upon the wearer's volition or when after significant wounds, whichever came first.
