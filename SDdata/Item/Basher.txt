Basher was the favorite enchanted lute that belonged to an adventuring bard Calliope in the late 15 century DR.
Basher traveled the world with its owner, playing musing and smiting foes from Chult to the Spine of the World. It was believed that this lute played and sounded better with age.
Apart from rare magic that improved the sound of the lute with age, Basher possessed the terrible power that allowed the used to cast the power word, kill spell.
*Calliope a bard adventurer who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
