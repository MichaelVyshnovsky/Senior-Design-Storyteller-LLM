The Woven Vine was an artifact enchanted necklace that boosted the wearers physique.
The Woven Vine was a delicate necklace of woven vines covered in gentle flowers.
When worn, the woven necklace boosted the wearers ability to land powerful lucky attacks and increased their chances of avoiding incoming damage. In addition, the necklace dramatically increased the wearers constitution. The Woven Vine was a part of a set that, when used together, boosted the magics of all items in the set a staff of flowers, the Woven Vine, and the Blooming Cord.
The Woven Vine was among many magical treasures hidden deep within the mega-dungeon of Undermountain and its Wyllowwood level.
