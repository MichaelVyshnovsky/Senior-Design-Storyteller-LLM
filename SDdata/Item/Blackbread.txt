'''Blackbread''' was a type of [[bread]] made from finely ground flour and [[Amn]]ian [[molasses]].

==Description==
Molasses gave blackbread its dark color. It was similar to a type on bread on [[Earth|another world]], known as [[Wikipedia:pumpernickel|pumpernickel]].

==Availability==
Blackbread was available in Amn. It was also available from the [[Friendly Flounder]] tavern in [[Waterdeep]].

==Prices==
On average it would cost three [[silver piece]]s for a loaf of blackbread or ten for four loaves.
