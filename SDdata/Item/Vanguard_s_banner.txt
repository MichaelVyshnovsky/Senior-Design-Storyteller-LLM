The vanguards banner was an artifact from the frigid region of Icewind Dalem used to inspire combatants in battle.
The vanguards banner was a crude gonfalon decorated with an image of an upright sword. Every friendly combatant was filled with inspiration and bravery, affected by the banners quiet and deep power.
