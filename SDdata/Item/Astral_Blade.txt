Astral Blade was a magical longsword with an intricate hilt that originated from the Shining Knight Arms and Armor store of Neverwinter.
Astral Blade was a magical weapon of +1 enchantment that dealt sonic damage as well as regular slashing damage.
This blade was created by Marrok, the Master Blacksmith of Shining Knight Arms and Armor of Neverwinter in 1372 DR, to aid the heroes investigating the Wailing Death epidemic.
* Alaffinel a disgraced half-celestial on the run circa 1372 DR.
