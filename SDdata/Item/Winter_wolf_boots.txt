Winter wolf boots were warm enchanted boots that carried magical properties of winter wolves pelt they were made of.
This footwear of soft white fur granted the wearers resistance to mundane and magical cold. Additionally, the boots slightly improved the wearers charisma.
Circa , Oswald Fiddlebender crafted various magic items of clothing using pelts of winter wolves that followed the Cultists of Auril plaguing Icewind Dale. Some of other items included winter wolf cloaks, winter wolf gloves, winter wolf scarves, and Winter wolf hats.
