Blood Iron was a unique enchanted short sword created by Hekedder, a wicked barbarian shaman, many years before the late 13 century DR.
The weapon was a crudely forged iron sword. The blades metal was covered in gouge marks and dents from the shamans unskilled hammer strikes. Blood Irons handle was a simple wrapping of bright red leather.
Despite its crude appearance, Blood Iron possessed a powerful +4 enchantments that guided the wielders hand towards the target and ensured the weapons damage to be much deadlier than that of a regular weapon. Additionally, Blood Irons necromantic magics healed the wielder with each successful strike. However, due to the weapons dark enchantments, it could only we wielded by creatures of neutral or outwards evil morals.
Blood Iron was forged by Hekedder, was a shaman of low morals. He forged and enchanted the crude blade and gifted the sword to his cousin. In the years that followed, the cousin and all subsequent wielders of Blood Iron found themselves hated because of its evil necromantic taint, which was much reviled by the barbarian tribes of the Frozen North. Hekedders creation became infamous among his people long before the , with them thinking that even uttering the swords name to be a curse.
