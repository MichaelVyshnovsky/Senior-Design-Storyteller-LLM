Bellows were items in the Realms often used by wizards and craftsmen to increase the airflow to a furnace and produce more heat.
Bellows were typically made from wood and calfskin with a brass nozzle.
* Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold bellows for around 5–10 gold pieces depending upon their size.
* Ember Me More, a specialty shop in Ravens Bluff, sold bellows.
Besides their use in forges, sizeable bellows were used in pipe organs to push air through pipes, thereby producing sound. And there existed a bellows-powered variant of shawms developed by Faerûnian gnomes.
* Bellows were favored by wizards and craftsmen.
