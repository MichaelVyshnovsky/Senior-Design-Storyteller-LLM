'''''Daggerford's medal of honor''''' was an enchanted [[amulet]] that was presented as the decoration of the highest honor for individuals who performed significant acts of valor in service of [[Daggerford]].

==Description==
''Daggerford's medal of honor'' was a shining and well-polished item.

==Powers==
''Daggerford's medal of honor'' possessed an enchantment that improved the wearer's defense against creatures not native to the [[Prime Material plane]], as well as improving their charisma, persuasion, and general defenses against magical and mundane effects. Additionally, the ''medal of honor'' allowed the wearer to cast the ''[[aura of glory]]'' spell once per day.

==History==
In [[1372 DR]], ''Daggerford's medal of honor'' was presented to the hero of Daggerford and their companions, [[Purfbin Doogrick]] and [[Raegen Brunegar]] by Duke [[Pwyll Daggerford]] for the group's heroic deeds of rescuing the Duke and his sister from the [[Zhentarim]], removing the organization's puppet city leader, and stopping the vengeful [[pit fiend]] [[Gorgomorz]] from razing Daggerford to the ground.

The fame of the [[baatezu]]'s defeat spread throughout the [[Abyss]] and denizens of the lower planes knew the medal and were fearful of its owner.
