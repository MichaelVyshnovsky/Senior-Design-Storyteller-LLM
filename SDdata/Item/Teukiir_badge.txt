'''''Teukiir badges''''' were [[magic item]]s that could transport the wearer to the [[Silver Safehold]]â€”a strange [[demiplane]] created by [[Khelben Arunsun]] and the [[Sword Heralds]].

==Description==
A ''teukiir badge'' was made from [[mithral]] and bore a mark depicting a rolled-up [[scroll]] sealed by a moon. The mark was set in white enamel and encircled by a dozen blue and white stars.

==Powers==
The [[magic]] of a ''teukiir badge'' could be used once per day and would only transport the wearer.

==Notable Owners==
All members of the [[Tel Teukiira]] bore these badges.
