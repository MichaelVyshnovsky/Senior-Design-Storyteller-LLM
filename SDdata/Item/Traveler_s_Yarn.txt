Travelers Yarn was a type of magical spirits available in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.
The enchanted beverage was on the menu at the Yawning Portal tavern of Waterdeep, with its bottles stashed away in Durnans cabinet. One serving of Travelers Yarn was priced at ten gold coins.
