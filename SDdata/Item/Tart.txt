'''Tarts''' were palm-sized pastry shells, a type of walkabout street food that could be found for sale in cities all across [[Faerûn]]. Often being sold from street carts, market stalls, or shuttered serving windows.

==Description==
The typical shape of a tart was curved at the edges so it could hold sweet or savory fillings such as jam, [[Lemon tree|lemon]] curd, or minced meat.{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-03-01 - Street Food in Cormyr and the Wider Realms}}

==Types of Tarts==
;Berry tart: A thick pastry dough tart filled with [[jam]] and topped with a variety of fresh berries, found in the [[Western Heartlands]] and [[Baldur's Gate]].
;Hand tarts: A tart the was the size of a [[humanoid]]'s palm. They were quite common in the inns, taverns, and private homes of [[Neverwinter]]. Common fillings included [[almond]]s and [[gooseberry|gooseberries]].<ref>{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-11-25 - Neverwinter Cuisine}}

==Notable Users of Tarts==
[[File:ME-Tart.png|thumb|200px|''A pair of tarts.'']]
===General===
* It was quite common for inns of average quality in the Realms to serve tarts filled with either [[cherry|cherries]] or some type of berries as the desert in a four-course meal.{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-10-06 - Four-course meals of the Realms}}
* It was quite common for high-end establishments in the Realms to serve fruit tarts as the first of two desserts in a full-course meal, what was known as the "First Sweet." Such tarts were typically filled with either cherries, [[gooseberry|gooseberries]], or other local varieties. They also were often stewed in brandy or cordial.
* [[Smoked fish tart]]s were made using [[silverfin]]s in the fishing village of [[Kalathtyr]] in [[Amn]].

===Businesses===
[[File:Berry Tart bg3.png|thumb|''A many-berry [[jam]]-filled tart from [[Baldur's Gate]].'']]
* [[Aurora's Emporium]] and [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']] sold tarts baked in [[Waterdeep]] across all of Faerûn. These were shipped in wooden crates to prevent them from breaking during transport.
* [[The Fast Sails]], a tavern in [[Tulmene]], served a wide variety of tarts to its patrons at a price of 2 [[copper piece]]s for a set of three. The types of tart fillings on offer were bacon, clam, [[eel]], [[fish]], and [[seagull|seabird]].
* [[Feldepost's Inn]] offered its patrons tarts with a mushroom filling.
* [[Harnsar's Board]] offered its patrons tarts with an apple filling.{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|30}}
* [[High Flagon]], a gambling hall in [[Waterdeep]], offered its patrons both sweet and savoury tarts, each for 2 and 3 [[silver piece]]s respectively. These sweet tarts tended to have a filling of one or more [[blackberry|blackberries]], [[blueberry|blueberries]], [[cranberry|cranberries]], gooseberries, or [[raspberry|raspberries]]. While savoury tarts tended to be filled with small game, such as [[rabbit]] or [[quail]].{{Cite dragon/302/Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The High Flagon|77}}
* [[Oak-Father's Boon]], an inn in [[Mosstone]], served its patrons [[upland tart]]s, [[forester's tart]]s, [[streamside tart]]s, and [[bounty tart]]s.<ref name="VGtBGII-p104>

===Races & Sentient Creatures===
* Tarts made with various types of berries were a common dessert item among [[elves]], typically sweetened with a mash of berry juices.
* When it came to portable individual meals, [[gnome]]s preferred very sweet dessert tarts.
