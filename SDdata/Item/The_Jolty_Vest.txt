The Jolty Vest was an enchanted medium armor created by the inventor called Yrre the Sparkstruck sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.
The Jolty Vest was a sturdy set of chainmail armor made out of an especially tough material that slightly reduced slashing damage received. The armor also could discharge accumulated charges of magical lighting onto the attacker. However, there was a chance the lightning discharge would hurt the wearer as well as the attacker.
The magic armor was a creation of Yrre the Sparkstruck, an inventor obsessed with lightning and electively. In the , The Jolty Vest fell into the hands of the Black Network and was among the possessions of Brem, the Zhentish smuggler and merchant who operated out of a cave hideout underneath Waukeens Rest.
