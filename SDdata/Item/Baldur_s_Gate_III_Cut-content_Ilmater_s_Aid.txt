Ilmaters Aid was an enchanted amulet associated with the Ilmatari faith.
The amulet appeared to shine brighter whenever the wearer shed some of their blood.
Ilmaters Aid bolstered the spellcasting ability of the wearer whenever they suffered physical or psychic harm. When they were badly injured or weakened, any non-cantrip spells they cast that damaged others were bolstered with additional force energy.
As of the , this amulet could be found within the Shattered Sanctum.
