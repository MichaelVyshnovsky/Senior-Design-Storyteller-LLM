'''Doming hammers''' were a type of metalworking tool often used in [[armor]]-crafting, resembling a double-headed [[hammer]].

==Description==
[[File:Master Doming Hammer.png|thumb|''An ornate doming hammer.'']]
Doming hammers had smooth, round heads that were used to [[wikipedia:Sinking_(metalworking)|sink]] metal sheets and plates, used in conjunction with a [[raising hammer]].
