A main-gauche was a large dagger that had a basket hilt.
The main-gauche was used as a defensive tool in the wielders off hand which was usually their left. The basket hilt of the weapon made it much easier to parry and even disarm opponents.
* Tinris, an ancient enchanted main-gauche made of everbright steel that was wielded by the Smiling Viper.
*Mordmorgan, a swashbuckler-adventurer from Cormyr in the mid-14 century DR.
*Loresingers, specialty priests of Milil, favored using the main-gauche in combat.
