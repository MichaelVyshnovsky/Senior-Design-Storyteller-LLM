Volos rejuvenating ring was an enchanted ring created by Elminster Aumar for his friend Volothamp Geddarm some years before the late 14 century DR.
This small unassuming ring granted the wearer increased vitality and, once per day, the wearer could cast cure critical wounds spell.
In the , the writer found himself swept away into a plot that crossed the merchant princess SaSani, with whom he was enamored, and an interloper deity from another world called Zehir. When the gods avatar was slain by a group of adventurers who worked for SaSani, Volo gifted them this magic ring as a gesture of a personal gratitude.
