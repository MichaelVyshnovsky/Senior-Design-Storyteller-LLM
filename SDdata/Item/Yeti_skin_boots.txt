Yeti skin boots were types of enchanted footwear that carried magical properties of a yeti pelt they were made of.
These boots granted the wearers resistance to mundane and magical cold.
Circa , Oswald Fiddlebender crafted various magic items and clothing using pelts of yetis that plagued Icewind Dale. Such yeti fur items were a great protection against harsh weather of the Frozenfar.
