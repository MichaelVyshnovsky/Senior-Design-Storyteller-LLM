Usk Fine Old was a good clarry wine created by Thamalon Uskevren, of the noble Uskevren family of Selgaunt, Sembia, in the 14 century DR.
Usk Fine Old was consumed heated and mulled. It was a light, sharp, and spicy wine, made with pears.
