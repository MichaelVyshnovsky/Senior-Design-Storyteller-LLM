'''''Camaradestave''''' was a unique [[magic]]al [[club]] that was wielded as a [[quarterstaff]] by the [[avatar]] of [[Cyrrollalee]], the [[halfling]] [[deity]] of hospitality of hospitality and friendship.<ref name=frcs-3e-p240">

==Powers==
''Camaradestave'' was a ''+1'' weapon. It was able to [[charm monster|charm]] any creature that it struck, providing that it was unable to resist.
