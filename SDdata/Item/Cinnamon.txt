'''Cinnamon''', sometimes referred to as '''cinnamon sticks''' when in its pre-ground form, was a type of [[spices|spice]] found in the Realms.

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where cinnamon were known to be found included:
* Cinnamon was sold in [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']].
* In [[Loudwater]], the [[Loudwater Apothecary|local apothecary]] sold cinnamon.<ref>
* In [[Ravens Bluff]], the [[:Category:Ravenian houses|noble house]] of [[Gultoss]] prominently sold cinnamon.<ref>
* In [[interior Faerûn]], cinnamon was common in the republic of [[Turmish]] due to imports and trade.<ref>{{Cite archive/Greenwood's Grotto/2023-02-10/Turmishan spices}}
* In [[south Faerun]], the town of [[Innarlith]].<ref>
* A species of [[tea]] grew on the [[Chultan Peninsula]] that was locally brewed with cinnamon, [[sugar]], and [[milk]].
* Cinnamon was exported as far north as the [[Ten-Towns]] of [[Icewind Dale]] where it was eaten with [[oatmeal]].
===Beyond Faerûn===
* In [[east Zakhara]], one could find cinammon being sold in the open bazaar of [[Dihliz]].<ref>

==History==
In [[1369 DR]], a shipment of exotic foodstuffs that included cinnamon was sent from [[Luskan]] to [[Icewind Dale]].

==Notable Cinnamon Products==
* Cinnamon possessed some medicinal properties, namely, it was used as a disinfectant agent, and a treatment for nausea.<ref>
* [[Blood-drops]] was a dish that cinnamon was regularly used as a spice for.
* Some were known to use cinnamon in [[cookie]]s.<ref>
* [[Dragon's bite (beverage)|Dragon's bite]], a mixed alcoholic beverage, featured powdered cinnamon.{{Cite web/theedverse/2020-06-18 - Dragon's bite}}
* [[Gravia's hot apple cider]], a product of the Maxillian Manor orchards in [[Daggerford]], contained two cinnamon sticks per batch.
* '''Kender pak''', a type of [[kender]] sweet[[bread]] that was baked out of six grains, [[caramel]], and cinnamon.
* Some variations of the [[Mummy|mummification]] process included several preserving and aerobatic plants and substances, such as cinnamon, [[ginger]], [[rose]], [[myrrh]], [[frankincense]], and [[pomegranate]].
* [[Spiced wine]] was sometimes flavored with cinnamon.<ref>

==Notable Users of Cinnamon==
===Businesses===
* [[Harbreeze Bakery]] sold cinnamon cookies.
* The [[Jellied Eel]] served [[rice]] infused with cinnamon.<ref>
* [[Oak-Father's Boon]] served [[bowl of hares]], a dish that involved stewing diced [[hare]] for half a day in a sauce that featured cinnamon.
* [[Salamantha's Sundown]] served grilled [[grouse]] that was marinated in cinammon, [[rosemary]], and [[sage (spice)|sage]].<ref>{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|31}}
* The [[Stone Stallion]] inn served [[buttermilk]] sprinkled with cinnamon.<ref>
===Regions & Settlements===
* In the [[Vilhon Reach]], the beverage [[Vilhon Cider]] was often served with cinnamon.<ref>

==Trivia==
* A variety of things in the Realms had a scent reminiscent of cinnamon. These included the body odor of [[burbur]]s,<ref> [[peranox]],<ref> and the wood of a [[vunwood|vunwood tree]].<ref>
* ''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'' sold cinnamon-scented [[bath oil]], [[beeswax]], and [[Exotic scented lamp oil|lamp oil]].<ref>
* The [[deity]] [[Nobanion]] had a peculiar sentimental fondness for cinnamon.
* The deity [[Shu (deity)|Shu's]] [[divine realm]] had the scent of cinnamon wafting through the air.
* The dandyish priest of [[Selûne]] [[Mifano]] scented his breath with cinnamon.
