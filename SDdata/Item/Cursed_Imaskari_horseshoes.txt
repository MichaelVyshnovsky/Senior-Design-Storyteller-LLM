Cursed Imaskari HorseshoesNote that these horseshoes are never referred to using this name in the book the name of the horseshoes is not given, they are merely described. were an Imaskari relic unearthed from the ruins of Solon by the slaves of Ambuchar Devayam.
These horseshoes were made from black obsidian.
When fitted on a horse, they made the horse completely black, and rendered them invisible in the night. However, any damage to the horse caused it to shatter like glass, most certainly killing it in the process.
