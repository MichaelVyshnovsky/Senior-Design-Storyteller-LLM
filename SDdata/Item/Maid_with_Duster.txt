'''''Maid with Duster''''' was a portrait painting created in or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The portrait depicted a young maid dressed in a frilly blue blouse and white kerchief. She was painted holding a feather duster as if the woman was presenting it proudly to the observer.The art is very clearly depicting a woman pulling and forming a yarn thread, not holing a feather duster.

==History==
[[File:Maid With Duster.png|thumb|''The Maid With Duster painting in a frame.'']]
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, copies of the ''Maid with Duster'' painting were found in certain homes and establishments of the city of [[Baldur's Gate]], the [[Western Heartlands]], and within the ruined apothecary shop in the blighted village of [[Moonhaven]].
