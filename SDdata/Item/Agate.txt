'''Agate''', the [[gem|gemstone]], came in many forms and colors, most of which were considered [[ornamental stones]] or [[hardstones]] in the ''Realms''. 

==Description==
Agates were a form of [[chalcedony]], which made them part of a large family of gems and minerals.
