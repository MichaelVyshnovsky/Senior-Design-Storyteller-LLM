Agate, the gemstone, came in many forms and colors, most of which were considered ornamental stones or hardstones in the Realms. 
Agates were a form of chalcedony, which made them part of a large family of gems and minerals.
Common varieties in the Realms included
* Banded agate
* Eye agate
* Fire agate
* Frost agate
* Moss agate
* Tiger eye agate
* Iris agate
* Turritella
