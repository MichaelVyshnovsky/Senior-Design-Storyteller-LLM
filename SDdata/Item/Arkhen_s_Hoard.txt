'''Arkhen's Hoard''' was a variety of blended [[wine]], produced in [[Sembia]]. It was exported as far as the [[Western Heartlands]], the lands along the [[River Chionthar]], and the city of [[Baldur's Gate]], as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Arkhen's Hoard was a middling quality blended wine. It had notable sweet [[jam]] and [[tabacco]] flavor notes.
