Wandering hunters boots were a type of enchanted footwear made by the inhabitants of the nomadic Wandering Village in Icewind Dale.
These boots were masterfully and painstakingly sewn together, and lined with soft wolfs fur. The lining was enchanted to make the hunters footfalls silent, improving their ability to sneak, stalk, and hide.
By the , wandering hunters boots were crafted for all young hunters of the Wandering Village. Friends and visitors to the village could purchase these boots from Tahvo the Huntmaster, along with other supplies.
