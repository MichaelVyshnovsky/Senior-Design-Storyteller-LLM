Sailors harps were old decaying harps that were enchanted to become a debilitating magical weapon.
Sailors harps were so old they crumbled after a single-use. However, that was by design. These decaying musical instruments were enchanted to unleash a deafening burst of cacophonous raucous when in the hands of a bard. After that, sailors harps fell apart and turned into dust.
These harps were popular among sailors and pirates who had musical or bardic inclinations. By the , sailors harps could be found in the hands and sold by sailors visiting Neverwinter and pirates of the Nelanther Islands.
