'''Olive oil''' was a type of vegetable [[oil]], derived from the fruit known as [[olive]]s.{{Cite web/Realmslore/Uthmere (Part Three): Daily and Nightly Life in Uthmere}}

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where olive oil was known to be produced, sold, and/or exported included:
* [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]] sold olive oil at a standard price of five [[gold piece]]s per gallon.
* In [[east Faer没n]]'s [[Old Empires]] region, olive oil was a major export of [[Chessenta]].<ref> Neighboring Chessenta, another major producer of olive oils were the [[Blade Kingdoms]].
* In east Faer没n's [[Unapproachable East]] region, the nation of [[Thay]] produced and exported olive oil all across Faer没n.<ref>
* In [[southwest Faer没n]], olive oil was a major export of the nation [[Tashalar]].
===Beyond the Prime Material===
* In the [[divine realm]] of [[Olympus]], the town of Thrassos was best known for its olive oil.<ref>

==History==
Olive oil had been around on [[Toril]] since at least the days of the ancient empire of [[Netheril]], where it could be found sold in its cities' markets.

==Olive Oil Products==
* [[Broaz]], a type of dip, had olive oil as an ingredient.<ref>{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-01-05 - Dips in the Realms}}
* ''[[Oil of beauty]]''.<ref>
* [[Rouille]], a type of sauce, had olive oil as an ingredient.
* Olive oil was a primary ingredient of the [[olive oil crust pie]]s served at the [[Oak-Father's Boon]].
