'''Amberthroat''' was an expensive alcoholic beverage originating in the city of [[Tashluta]] in [[Tashalar]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Availability==
It was sold in the [[Dragon's Head Tavern]] in [[Waterdeep]] for 9 [[gp]] for a glass.
