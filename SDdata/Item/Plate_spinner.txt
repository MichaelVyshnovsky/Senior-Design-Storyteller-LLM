'''Plate spinners''' were tools in the Realms popular among [[bard]]s and performers who used them to dazzle crowds with balancing tricks.

==Description==
A plate spinner consisted of a peg and special wooden plates that would not break.

==History==
These items were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' for 3 [[copper piece]]s per peg, 2 [[silver piece]] per plate, and 1 silver piece for a stand that could hold up to four spinning plates.

==Notable Users==
* Plate spinners were favored by bards.
