'''Safflower oil''' was a type of vegetable oil in the Realms,{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|24}} derived from the [[safflower|plant of the same name]].<ref>{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|25}}

==Availability==
* [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]] sold safflower oil at the standard price of two [[silver piece]]s per {{SI|1|gal|long=yes}} in its "Aurora's Larder" section.

==Notable Users==
* [[Madame Freona's Tea Kettle]], a restaurant in [[Phlan]], utilized safflower oil in some of its dishes.
