Wood woad shield was an enchanted wooden shield that was imbued with nature magics that sustained wood woads.
This intricately grown and decorated wooden shield provided adequate defense to the wielder. Additionally, it allowed the wilder to use the ensnaring strike spell. The spell could be used after each time the wielder had a chance to rest for at least four hours.
Wood woad shield was believed to had been formed when a dryad cursed her ex-lover, changing his body from flesh and bone to bark and heartwood sometimes before the late 14 century DR.
