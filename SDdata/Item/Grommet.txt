'''Grommets''' were a type of small metal item, able to be used as a mechanical component or for making clothes.

==Availability==
* Grommets were made by "dressers", metalworkers who created various small, delicate, and ornamental works.{{Cite dragon/419/Eye on the Realms: Clan Harhund and Dead Dwarf Mine|35}}
* [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']] sold grommets in their "Tailors' Corner" section for 5 [[copper piece]]s a piece.
* Members of the [[Church of Gond]] often had access to these.
