Ornabra was a specialty stew dish served at the ramshackle Owlroost Head Inn of the Amnian town of Purskul as of the late 14 century DR.
Ornabra was a type of stew prepared with potatoes, beef, and goat meat. When sold to be taken out of the dining hall, it was drained and wrapped in a shield-sized flatbread. The dish slowly oozed its thick juices and could be eaten on the go.
As of the late 14 century DR, ornabra was a cheaper offering of the Owlroost Head Inn. It was popular among traveling caravaners.
