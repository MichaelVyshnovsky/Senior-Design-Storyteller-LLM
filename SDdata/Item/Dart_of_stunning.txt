'''''Dart of stunning''''' was a type of [[dart]] used by the [[drow]].

==Powers==
When the dart struck a living target, the creature and everyone within {{SI|10|ft|long=yes}} became temporarily stunned, rendering them reeling and unable to think coherently or act.
