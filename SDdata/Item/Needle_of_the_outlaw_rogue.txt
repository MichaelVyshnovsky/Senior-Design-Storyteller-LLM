The '''''needle of the outlaw rogue''''' was an intricate [[enchanted]] [[dagger]] from the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
The weapons possessed a minor enchantment that made it hum with a longing for vengeance.

==History==
The ''needle of the outlaw rogue's'' history was a mystery. It could be found in the [[Western Heartlands]], along the banks of the [[River Chionthar]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
