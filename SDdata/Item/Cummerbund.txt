Cummerbunds were a form of broad, fitted belt found in the Realms.
* Borgils Bold Buckles in the village of Blisterfoot Inn sold elaborate, curlicued metal cummerbunds with raised ornamental compartments for concealing coin purses or knives.
* Chalasses Fine Clothing in Immersea sold luxury cummerbunds.
In the 14 century DR, cummerbunds were a quite common accessory in parts of Cormyr and in the city of Waterdeep.
* The avatar of Oghma typically wore a cummerbund with a medallion-shaped buckle that had an exquisitely beautiful scene painted on it.
* The statuette Silver Supplicant was adorned in what looked to be a cummerbund of spider silk.
* Simbul.
* Storm Silverhand wore a black cummerbund.
