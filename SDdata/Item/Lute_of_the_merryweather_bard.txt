The '''''lute of the merryweather bard''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[musical instrument]] from the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
This decorated [[lute]] hummed with magic, barely containing the voices of [[sprite]]s and [[spirit]]s.

==History==
The ''lute of the merryweather bard'' could be found in the [[Western Heartlands]], along the banks of the [[River Chionthar]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
