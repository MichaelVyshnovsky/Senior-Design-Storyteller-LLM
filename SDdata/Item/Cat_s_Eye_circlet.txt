A Cats Eye circlet, or a Cats Eye headband, was a magic circlet that allowed the wearer to see in complete darkness.
These beautiful circlets were often adorned with cats eye agates, gems that, if enchanted, could bestow darkvision.
* Catti-brie, who was gifted this before she went to chase Drizzt DoUrden, who headed back into the Underdark to his home city of Menzoberranzan. Her circlet was enchanted by Alustriel of Silverymoon.
