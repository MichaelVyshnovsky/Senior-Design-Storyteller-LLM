Gloves of dexterity were a type of enchanted leather gloves. They were thin and flexible.
A creatures inherent dexterity would be improved when wearing both gloves in a pair. The magical effect would not activate if only one of two gloves was worn.
* A number of drow were known to possess a pair of these gloves, including Hamadh, Maas, Paelinn, LukTharin, and Riggilla the Dark.
* Imket, a curst fighter enslaved to the wizard Sonjar, was known to possess a pair of these gloves.
* The Mulhorandi wizard Nezram was known to possess a pair of these gloves.
* A pair of these gloves was owned by the magelord Rimmen of the Scarlet Eye.
* Eadred, a paladin from Myth Drannor, stuck in a cursed stasis owned a pair of ancient gloves of dexterity.
* In the Domains of Dread, both Hazlik and Strahd von Zarovich were known to possess a pair of these gloves.
* A pair of these gloves were owned by the wemic Shaqar.
