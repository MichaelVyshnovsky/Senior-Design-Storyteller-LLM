The '''''axe of heavenly fire''''' was an impressive, [[Item enchantment|magical]] [[greataxe]] that was considered sacred to the [[church of Tyr]].

==Powers==
This [[weapon]] was a ''+3 [[Holy (weapon quality)|holy]] greataxe'' that emitted a glowing radiance as if it were permanently under the effects of ''[[light]]''.

==History==
Previously held in the possession of the [[Justiciar of Tyr|Lord High Justiciar]] [[Deren Eriach]], of the [[Abbey of the Just Hammer]] in [[Tasseldale]], the ''axe of heavenly fire'' was bestowed upon [[Madeiron Sunderstone]], the personal bodyguard of [[Open Lord]] [[Piergeiron the Paladinson|Piergeiron]].
