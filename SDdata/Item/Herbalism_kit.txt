'''Herbalism kits''' were sets of tools commonly used by herbalists and apothecaries to prepare and identify medicines and [[potion]]s and to identify and use medicinal [[herb]]s.

==Description==
A typical herbalism kit contained clippers, a [[mortar and pestle]], [[gloves]], [[vial]]s, and pouches to manipulate and store herbs and to prepare them for use.

==Powers==
A herbalism kit was required to brew ''[[Potion of healing|potions of healing]]'' and [[antitoxin]]s. Its tools also aided the user in identifying edible plants and [[poison]]s, and helped in the study of magic that dealt with plants.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Taela Summerhawk]] and her husband Larlow, [[halfling]] apothecaries from [[Nightstone]] in the late 15th century [[DR]].
