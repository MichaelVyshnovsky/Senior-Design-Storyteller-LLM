'''Forest leaf''' was a soup served at the [[Flying Stag]] inn, in [[Voonlar]] ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1368}}.{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks archive|23}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 17: A Place to Stay}}

==Description==
Forest leaf was a thin broth, almost [[tea]]-like, made from a secret combination of boiled roots and leaves, served with berries floating in it.
