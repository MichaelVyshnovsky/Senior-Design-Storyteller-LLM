The Mighty Cloth was a type of enchanted monks garb from Port Nyanzaru in Chult.
The Mighty Cloth was enchanted to constantly bestow the bulls strength onto its wearer, and it possessed the unwavering bull magic that made the wearer impossible to push or knock off their feet. This massive strength boost also made it much harder for the wearer to be restrained. They also could perform a bull rush, fiercely and powerfully tacking their opponents down.
Sometime before the late 15 century DR, The Mighty Cloth was worn by Kimigha Poji, one of the monks of the port city of Nyanzaru, who fought against the Headless Serpent, Secret Venom gang.
