A '''heavy mace''' was a bludgeoning weapon and a larger form of the [[light mace]].

==Description==
Made all of metal, it had a longer and thicker haft and a larger head than the light mace, and was more durable. A typical example weighed {{SI|8|lb|k|long=yes}}.

==Weaponology==
This weapon was intended to be used one-handed and was simple enough for most combatants to use without much training.

==Price==
The average heavy mace cost around 12 [[gold piece]]s.
