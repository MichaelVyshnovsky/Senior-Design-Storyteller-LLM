An earring of protection was an enchanted piece of jewelry that guarded against physical attacks.
These items helped protect the wearer from physical attacks as well as giving them a better chance of resisting the effects of harmful spells, poisons, etc. Their beneficial effects would combine with similar items that magically protected the wearer.
* Emirikol, a plane-traveling wizard, wore an earring of protection.
* Ongamar Tathloon wore a golden +3 earring of protection.
