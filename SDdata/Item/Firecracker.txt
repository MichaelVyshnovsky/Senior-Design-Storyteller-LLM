'''Firecrackers''' were a small variety of [[fireworks]] developed and produced in the nation of [[Shou Lung]].

==Description==
Like other fireworks, the substance that allowed firecrackers to "pop" were fireseeds, the magically limited kernels of [[smokepowder]].{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-07-20 - Firecrackers & Fireworks in the Realms}}

==Availability==
* Some of the [[Church of Gond]]'s temples produced and sold firecrackers.
* Shou Lung.

==Varities==
* ''[[Noisome spirit chasers]]'', [[enchanted]] firecrackers that warded off [[spirit]]s.<ref>

==Notable Users==
* The [[Blade Kingdoms]] imported firecrackers from Shou for use in the [[Festival of Blades]].
