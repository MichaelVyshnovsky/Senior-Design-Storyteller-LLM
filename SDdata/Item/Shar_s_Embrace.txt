'''''Shar's Embraces''''' were [[enchanted]] [[cloak]]s created by [[Shar]]ran [[priest]]s.

==Powers==
These dark cloaks blessed by divine magic of Shar allowed their wearers to bathe the an area in unnatural ''[[darkness]]'' three times per day. Additionally, it gave the boon of ''[[darkvision]]'' to their owners.

==History==
''Shar's Embrace'' was one of the somewhat common magic items sold by the [[Church of Shar]] to allowed the goddess' fitful to embrace the powers of the Mistress of the Night. One of the places where one could purchase these items in [[1374 DR]] was the temple of Shar in [[Westgate]]'s [[Undergate]], [[The Dark Lady's Promise]], created by the High Priestess [[Velisha]].
