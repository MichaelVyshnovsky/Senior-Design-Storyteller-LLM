A '''''ring of windwarding''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[ring]] that shielded the wearer from all winds.

==Powers==
This ring created a shield surrounding the wearer for {{SI|10|ft|long=yes}}, protecting them from all forms of wind both natural and magical. This also included protection from [[air elemental]]s and similar creatures.
