Soraevoras chain was a unique suit of chainmail that was sacred to the goddess Angharradh, The Triune Goddess, from the elven island of Evermeet.
This beautiful  chainmail was expertly crafted by elven artisans of Evermeet and tailored specifically to Soraevora Aeravands body. The chainmail was decorated with a tabard that hid underneath a chain that held the holy symbol of Angharradh in front of the wearers heart. The armor possessed a +2 protective enchantment, gave them limited spell resistance, and improved the wearers reflexes. The magical divine nature of Soraevoras chain ensured that it could only be worn by a favored soul who was an elf and who was both good and chaotic in spirit.
This chainmails existence predated the 14 century DR, likely by many centuries. The armor was worn a favored soul of Angharradh the sun elf named Soraevora Aeravand of Evermeet. In the , Soraevoras deity sent her visions of doom and the elf embarked on a journey to the Sword Coast North to stop Zehir, a yuan-ti interloper deity. Soraevoras chain accompanied her on the perilous quest.
