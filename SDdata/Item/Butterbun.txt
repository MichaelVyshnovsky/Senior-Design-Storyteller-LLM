'''Butterbuns''' were common baked goods from the [[Western Heartlands]].

==Description==
Butterbuns were small baked [[bread]] rolls, sometimes sprinkled with [[sesame]] seeds. Butterbuns were baked in batches of one dozen at a time.

==Availability==
Butterbuns were served at the [[Waukeen's Rest]] inn, a prominent layover spot between [[Elturel]] and [[Baldur's Gate]] in the 1490s [[DR]].
