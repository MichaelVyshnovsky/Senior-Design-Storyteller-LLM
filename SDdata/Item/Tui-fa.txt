The tui-fa, also known as the tonfa (), was a weapon unique to the continent of Kara-Tur.
A tui-fa was a hardwood rod with a small off-center handle protruding from its side. Spanning a length of approximately , it was originally a millstone handle but has since evolved into a martial arts implement. 
With the tui-fa, a skilled practitioner could proficiently engage in blocking, weapon interception, weapon destruction, and effective offensive maneuvers. It was often used in regions where conventional weapons were prohibited or restricted. 
* Both shugenja and wu jen were known to utilize the tui-fa.
