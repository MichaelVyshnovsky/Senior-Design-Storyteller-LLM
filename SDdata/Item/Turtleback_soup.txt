'''Turtleback soup''' was one of the more notable dishes from the city of [[Velen]] in the nation of [[Tethyr]].

==Description==
A soup of green color and distinctly briny taste, served with sliced [[cucumber]].

==History==
As of the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], turtleback soup could be found on [[Blackthorn's Brew]]'s menu, a [[festhall]] on the [[Captain's Cliff]] in Velen. The dish was mentioned among other notable foods included in [[Volothamp Geddarm]]'s published guide to [[Amn]] and Tethyr.
