'''Rootweal''' was a red [[wine]] that originated from the forests of the [[Yuirwood]] in [[Aglarond]].

==Description==
The beverage had the color of red [[silk]] and was a smooth, full-bodied wine. Its flavor was described as "a forest meadow alive in the glad light of the sun."
