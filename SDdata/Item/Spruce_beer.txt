Spruce beer was an alcoholic beverage created by the humans of the High Moor, using the spruce trees of the Misty Forest.
Spruce beer was a foul alcoholic drink made with twigs and braces of the spruce tree, sugar extracted from sugar beets, and molasses. The High Moor barbarians consumed the resulting drink during ceremonies and celebrations.
