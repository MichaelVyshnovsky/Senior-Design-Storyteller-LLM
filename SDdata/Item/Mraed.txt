Mraed was a type of a nut-based dessert prepared in the lands of Calimshan, Tharsult, and the Tashalar.
Mraed was one of many types of desserts found throughout the Realms, similar to the Earths [https//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Halva halva]. It was a very sweet and nutritious treat of shaped and pressed raisins, almonds, pecans, dates, coconut milk, and durr (what was known as semolina on Earth). High fat content ensured the desserts long self life..
