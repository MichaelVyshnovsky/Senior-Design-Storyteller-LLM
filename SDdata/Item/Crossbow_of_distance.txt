A '''''crossbow of distance''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[crossbow]] capable of firing [[bolt]]s to greater distances than their mundane counterparts.

==Description==
These weapons came in various forms and appeared to be normal crossbows.

==Powers==
Any bolt fired from a ''crossbow of distance'' had its effective range doubled.

==Notable Owners==
Lord [[Orgauth]], the ruler of [[Zhentil Keep]] following the city's [[Fall of Zhentil Keep|fall]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], carried a ''crossbow of distance''.
