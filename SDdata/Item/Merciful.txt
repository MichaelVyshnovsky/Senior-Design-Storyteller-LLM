'''''Merciful''''' was a magical [[weapon quality|quality]] that could be applied to weapons. It allowed the wielder to cause only nonlethal damage to a target, if chosen. If this quality was applied to ranged weapons, then the effects were applied to the ammunition.

==Creation==
The spell ''[[cure light wounds]]'' was used in the creation of  ''merciful'' weapons.
