'''''Powder of the black veil''''' was a type of [[magic]]al, sooty black powder.

==Powers==
Through application by means of a packet or blowtube, the user of ''powder of the black veil'' could create a {{SI|10|ft|long=yes}} cloud that caused those within it to suffer temporarily from a magical blindness.

==Availability==
* The [[Diviner's Guild]] of [[Ravens Bluff]] was known to sell ''powder of the black veil'' for around 4,700 [[gp]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite polyhedron/88/The Living City: The Ravens Bluff Diviner's Guild|16}}
