Powder of the black veil was a type of magical, sooty black powder.
Through application by means of a packet or blowtube, the user of powder of the black veil could create a  cloud that caused those within it to suffer temporarily from a magical blindness.
* The Diviners Guild of Ravens Bluff was known to sell powder of the black veil for around 4,700 gp in the 14 century DR.
* Among the warriors of the drow city of Sshamath, this was a commonly carried powder while out on patrol.
