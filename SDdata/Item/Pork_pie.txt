'''Pork pie''' was a dish of [[pork]] meat baked in a flaky pastry pie known in [[Faerûn]] as of the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Availability==
* The [[Inn of the Dripping Dagger]], an establishment in [[Waterdeep]], offered patrons pork pies that were either mild or hotly spiced at a cost of one [[copper piece]]. For those traveling, they also offered them cold or wrapped in ice and leaves.<ref>
* [[Hriiat Fine Pastries]] in Waterdeep was known for savory "bite-pies", including one filled with pork.<ref name="VGtW-p94>
* The [[Sleeping Cat]] restaurant in [[Wheloon]] served pork pastries in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. 
* The [[Rising Moon]] inn in [[Highmoon]] was known for its filling pork pies in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century DR, courtesy of chef and [[Harper]] agent, [[Rhiia Duskmantle]].
* [[Rose's Tea Room]] in [[Raven's Bluff]] served hot, spicy pork pies to the crowds from the city's [[Coliseum (Raven's Bluff)|Coliseum]].

==History==
As of [[1358 DR]], the sage [[Warthendel]] owned a [[magic]] [[skillet]] that could produce a pork pie (or an [[omelet]] or fried [[fish]]) once every 30 minutes.

Mayor [[Tobald]] tucked into a huge pork pie at the [[Bold Bard]] in [[Ghars]], [[Cormyr]], in [[Eleint]] of [[1367 DR]]. It looked so good, [[Jasper (servant)|Jasper]] ordered one himself.
