Hellpick was a sturdy dwarvish magic warhammer that once belonged to a hero named Durlow Ironbar, some years before 1281 DR.
The weapon possessed a potent +3 enchantment.
Like many dwarven weapons, the Hellpick was blessed by the dwarven deity Moradin upon its creation. Its first known wielder was warrior Durlow Ironbar, who smashed countless skulls with this weapon across numerous battlefields.
