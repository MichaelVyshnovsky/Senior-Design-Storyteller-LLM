Birthright was an enchanted and fancifully embroidered bycocket hat designed to magically boost the wearers charisma.
Once adorning a wearers head, Birthright magically affected their appearance and charisma, making them more appealing, pleasant, and charming. This magic was especially useful for warlocks, bards, and sorcerers as their command of the Weave did not come from their natural wisdom or sharpness of the mind.
In the , Birthright was in stock at Sorcerous Sundries, a wizards shop in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate, where it was sold by the proprietors magical simulacrum or by his tiefling clerk named Rolan.
