'''Scalpels''' were sharp medicinal tools designed to cut the flesh open, used by doctors and morticians of the [[Realms]] and, occasionally, wielded as weapons.

{{quote|''The objective of the scalpel, sisters, is to soothe, for the scalpel, indeed, is an extension of Shar.''|[[Malus Thorm]] of [[Reithwin]]}}

==Description==
Usually, scalpers were thin and extremely sharp [[knives]] made of metal.

*[[Ellandra Tolbert]], a noblewoman and a famous [[necromancer]]-[[anatomist]] from the city of [[Waterdeep]].
