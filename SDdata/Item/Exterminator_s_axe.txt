Exterminators axe was the enchanted battleaxe that belonged to the duergar slaver Gekh Coal circa 1492 DR.
Exterminators axe was a heavy two-handed axe, crafted with masterful skill. The blade and the haft were covered in intricate engravings.
This axe was a scourge of pests. When wielded against plants, insects, or small-sized creatures, it dealt additional fire damage.
In the , exterminators axe was in possession of Gekh Coal, the leader of a small duergar raiding squad who massacred deep gnome fluorite miners and attacked a peaceful myconid colony in the vicinity of the Moonrise Towers in the Underdark.
