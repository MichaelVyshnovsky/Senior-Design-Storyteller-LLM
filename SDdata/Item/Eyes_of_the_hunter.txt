The '''''eyes of the hunter''''' were an [[enchanted]] pair of [[Optic aid|lenses]] used by those who fought with [[ranged weapons]]. They could be found in [[Waterdeep]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
A wearer's vision was clearer through the ''eyes of the hunter'', giving them an edge when targeting enemies' vital points.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Saarvin]], a [[dragonborn]] [[ranger]] and [[adventurer]] possessed ''eyes of the hunter'' while working with [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
