Cornbread, also spelled as two words – corn bread, was a variety of golden-color bread baked using corn or Maztican maize.
Cornbread buns were baked in the town of Shadowdale in the Dalelands and served by a platterful. Storm Silverhand liked to eat her corn bread cheese-laced.
Vaasan cornbread was served as a dessert, covered in colorful artistically layered frosting, sometimes white and pink color.
In the Vast, corn bread could be found in the town of Suiters Hill – the regions only producer of Maztican maize.
