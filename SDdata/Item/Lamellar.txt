Lamellar was a type of armor used in Zakhara.
Lamellar was similiar to scale armor. It was made by overlapping metal plates called lamellas connected via metal links.  Lamellar was lighter in weight than standard scale mail and cost less to produce. This armor was one of the more favored heavy armor variants in Zakhara.
* Do-maru A corselet of lamellar covering the stomach, chest, shoulders, and back.
