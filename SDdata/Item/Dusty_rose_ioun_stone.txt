A dusty rose ioun stone was a type of ioun stone that offered an individual significant physical protection.
This type of ioun stone radiated a rosy color that evoked feelings of calm and serenity.
The dusty rose stone possessed an enchantment with protection properties that it bestowed upon the person that used it.
One of these stones was hidden away within the dungeon complex beneath the asylum of Spellhold.
