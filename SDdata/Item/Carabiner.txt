'''Carabiners''' were a kind of clip used to connect items together.

==Usage==
Though expensive, a well-made carabiner was extremely useful for [[adventurer]]s and mountaineers. It allowed two items to be securely hooked together, such as a [[rope]] and a [[spike]] or [[piton]].
