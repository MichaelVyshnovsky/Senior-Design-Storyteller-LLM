The '''Overmantle of Halgondas''' was a device that affected [[Mantle (Cormathan)|Cormathan mantles]].

==Powers==
Per [[Ilimitar]], the Overmantle allowed the user to merge their [[Mantle (Cormathan)|mantle]] with that of their target, preventing it from warding off spells and making it impossible to separate the targets. It activated upon breakage.

==History==
The Overmantle remained in the [[Vault of Ages]] until {{yearlink|241}}, when [[Ilimitar]] used it in a duel with the [[Srinshee]].
