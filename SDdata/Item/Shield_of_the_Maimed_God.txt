The Shield of the Maimed God was a divinely enchanted tower shield that belonged to Charissa Maernos in the late 14 century DR.
The Shield of the Maimed God was a sturdy and well-balanced heavy shield. Its edges were engraved with Tyrs holy runes. These mysterious symbols radiated divine protection.
This tower shield possessed a +1 enchantment to its already significant defensive properties.
In 1374 DR, Charissa Maernos wielded the Shield of the Maimed God during her pursuit of vengeance agains the Night Masks of Westgate who were responsible for her familys downfall.
