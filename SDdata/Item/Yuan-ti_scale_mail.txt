Yuan-ti scale mail was a type of enchanted medium armor made by the yuan-ti, the serpentfolk of Chult.
These sets of robust magic armor were forged together using yuan-ti shed skin, giving it green reptilian hue.
Made out of exotic material, yuan-ti scale mail was a protective type of medium armor that acted like a set of leathers it allowed the wearer full mobility, making it more useful for dexterous individuals, rather than physically strong warriors. The armors magic also boosted the wearers initiative and creation in battle.
