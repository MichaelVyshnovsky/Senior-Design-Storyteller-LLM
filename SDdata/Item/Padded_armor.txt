'''Padded armor''' was a type of light armor.

==Description==
Padded armor was made up of several quilted layers of cloth and [[wikipedia:Batting_(material)|batting]]. Padded armor was poorly ventilated and soon became hot. It was known to become fouled with grime and sweat and even infested by fleas and [[lice]].

==Variations==
;Aketon: a padded undercoat{{Cite polyhedron/109/Races of Cerilia|13}} or light suit of padding that was commonly worn beneath [[field plate]].<ref>

==Usage==
Being one of the cheapest forms of armor, padded armor was commonly distributed to conscripts in times of war. Padded armor was a common sight in northern [[Zakhara]]. The military of [[Koryo]] wore a heavy padded armor in order to stay warm in the cold winters.
