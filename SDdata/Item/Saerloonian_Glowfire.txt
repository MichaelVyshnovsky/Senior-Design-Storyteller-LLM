Saerloonian Glowfire  was a wine exported from Saerloon. It was also sold through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue,
This renowned wine had a slight luminescence, adding a mystical quality to the wine. Neither sweet nor dry, this aromatic wine conjured images of refreshing summer breezes.
Saerloonian Glowfire could be purchased in the following containers
* Hand keg (5 sp)
* Cask (26 sp)
* Barrel (7 gp)
* Butt (22 gp)
* Tun (54 gp)
