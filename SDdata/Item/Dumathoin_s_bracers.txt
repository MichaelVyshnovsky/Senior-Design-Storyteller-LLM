'''''Dumathoin's bracers''''' were a pair of magical [[bracers]] from the [[dwarf|dwarven]] city of [[Thunderholme]].
{{Quote
|Hold the gates! No green-skin will step one foot into Thunderholme!
|[[Dumathoin Thunderstone]], Captain of the Guard
}}
==Powers==
The bracers offered great protection to the wearer from physical and magical threats, including freezing, burning, acidic, sonic, and shocking assaults. They also improved the wearer's agility and speed quite a bit.

==History==
The bracers were once used by [[Dumathoin Thunderstone]], Captain of the Guard during an attack upon Thunderholme. The bracers were later found in the lair treasury of the [[shadow dragon]] [[Aurgloroasa]] in the 1480s [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}
