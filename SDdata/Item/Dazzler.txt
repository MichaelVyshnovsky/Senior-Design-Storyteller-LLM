Dazzlers were colorfully decorated and magically mesmerizing bucklers from the city of Westgate.
The shield front was decorated with a bright, colorful spiral pattern around a face in its center. The face images eyes seemed to have been following weaker-minded creatures that stared into them. Bright patterns and simple enchantment made the wearer of the shield capable of mesmerizing their opponents at will as if by the daze spell.
In the , Dazzler shields were sold in Westgate, among countless unique and rare weapons and armor on the citys Market Triangle.
