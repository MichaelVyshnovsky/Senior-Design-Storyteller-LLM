'''''Lifedrinker morningstars''''' were [[enchanted]] bludgeons that sapped their victims' vitality, found in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
These [[morningstar]]s possessed ''+2'' enchantments and pulsated with purple necrotic magics. When wielded in combat, ''Lifedrinker morningstars'' dealt necrotic damage to their targets and sapped their life force, granting the wielder ''[[regeneration]]''.

==History==
In the late 15{{th}} century DR, ''lifedrinker morningstars'' were in use among heroes, bandits, and [[orc]]s of the [[Sword Mountains]], the city of [[Neverwinter]], and the [[Old Owl Well]]. One such weapon was wielded by pirate named Lieutenant [[Gadain]], who worked for the [[Luskan]]ite buccaneer [[Kajin]].
