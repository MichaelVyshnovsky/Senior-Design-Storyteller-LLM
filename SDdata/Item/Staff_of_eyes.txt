A '''staff of eyes''' was a magical staff with powers of vision.

==Description==
It was a spiraling staff with a large piece of [[amber]] at the top, and inlaid with [[silver]].

==Powers==
It contained the ''[[arcane eye]]'', ''[[Remove blindness or deafness|remove blindness]]'', and ''[[see invisibility]]'' spells.

==Cost==
It was worth around 34,700 [[gold piece]]s.
