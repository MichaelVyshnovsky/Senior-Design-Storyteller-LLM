'''''Türkhaari''''' was a traditional [[Thymari]] [[pickle]] dish, made from [[gourd]]s, [[pepper]], ''[[charchuka]]'', [[lemon]], and [[herbs]] and [[spices]], and ''talsh'', a resin from the ''[[nychaki]]'' herb.

==Locations==
The ''türkhaari'' was a traditional delicacy of [[Tymanther]].
