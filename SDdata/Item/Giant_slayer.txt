'''''Giant slayers''''' were a variety of [[magic]]al [[sword]]s and [[axe]]s that were [[Item enchantment|enchanted]] specifically  to slay [[giant]]s.

== Powers ==
In addition to its other given magical enhancements, a ''giant slayer '' was particularly destructive to true giants and [[giant-kin]] such as [[troll]]s, [[ogre]]s, [[ogre magi]], [[titan]]s, and [[ettin]]s.<ref>{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1992|53|''Sword +2, [[giant slayer]]''}} As well as the [[giant#Zakharan_Giants|giants]] of [[Zakhara]]. It also had a chance to knock giants down on a hit.

==Notable Giant Slayers==
* ''[[Balduran's Giantslayer]]'', a [[greatsword]] version of this weapon that was owned by [[Balduran]].
* ''[[Blade Dlardrageth]]'', a [[broad sword]] version of this weapon that was owned by the [[cambion]] [[Xhalph Dlardrageth]].
