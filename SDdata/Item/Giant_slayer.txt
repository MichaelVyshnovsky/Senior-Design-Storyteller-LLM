Giant slayers were a variety of magical swords and axes that were enchanted specifically  to slay giants.
In addition to its other given magical enhancements, a giant slayer  was particularly destructive to true giants and giant-kin such as trolls, ogres, ogre magi, titans, and ettins. As well as the giants of Zakhara. It also had a chance to knock giants down on a hit.
* Baldurans Giantslayer, a greatsword version of this weapon that was owned by Balduran.
* Blade Dlardrageth, a broad sword version of this weapon that was owned by the cambion Xhalph Dlardrageth.
