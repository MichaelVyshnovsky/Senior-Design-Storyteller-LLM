Unlucky thiefs gloves was a pair of enchanted gloves of dubious magics.
The gloves possessed a helpful enchantment that significantly boosted the wearers manual dexterity and sleight of hand. However, if the gloves wearer used this magic to steal something, a small piece of coal appeared in the thiefs pocket, as if the magic was judging them.
In the , a pair of unlucky thiefs gloves was displayed among splendorous enchanted rewards for those willing to partake in Akabis Wheel of Fortune game in the Circus of the Last Days during its stay in the Rivington neighborhood of Baldurs Gate.
