An '''''amulet of Silvanus''''' was a [[magic]]all amulet associated with the [[Silvanite]] faith.

==Description==
The amulet had a depiction of a waterfall. It offered the wearer a sense of peace and serenity.

==Powers==
The wearer of the amulet could cast the spell ''[[lesser restoration]]''.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, one of these amulets could be found beneath a large rock, outside of the [[Emerald Grove]].
