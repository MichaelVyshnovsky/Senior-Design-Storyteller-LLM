Sarsauce fowl was a baked poultry dish found in Faerûn.
Sarsauce fowl was a daily-fare dish of sautéed mushrooms, various poultry meats, and cream. The dish was baked similarly to a casserole. Elminster considered sarsauce fowl one of his favorite foods found in the Realms, second only to fire-seared barbfin or smoked salmon.
