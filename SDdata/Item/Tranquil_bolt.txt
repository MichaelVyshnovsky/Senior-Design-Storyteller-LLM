Tranquil bolts were a type of enchanted crossbow ammunition especially effective against spellcasters.
Tranquil bolts were enhanced with a simple +1 enchantment that increased the damage and guided the bolts towards their targets. Each bolt unleashed the silence spell after piercing its target.
Some years before the , the infamous Band of the Key and its leader Heglyf, commissioned the creation of brightly-decorated enchanted tranquil bolts from Deneirian priests. These bolts are created in large batches to aid the adventurers in their hunt for occultist.
