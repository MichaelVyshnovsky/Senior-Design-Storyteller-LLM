'''''Arrows of dispelling''''' were [[enchanted]] [[arrow]]s that were especially useful when used against spellcasters.

==Powers==
Even though their magic did not deal additional damage, ''arrows of dispelling'' possessed an invaluable enchantment that ''[[Dispel magic|dispelled]]'' any magics that were in effect on the target.

==History==
By the mid and late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], ''arrows of dispelling'' were used across the [[Sword Coast]]. [[Halbazzer Drin]] of [[Sorcerous Sundries]] carried ''arrows of dispelling'' in stock in [[Baldur's Gate]].
