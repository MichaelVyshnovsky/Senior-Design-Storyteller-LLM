Natures Snare was an enchanted staff found in the druidic enclave of the Western Heartlands in the late 15 century DR.
Natures Snare was a wooden staff of wriggling thread-like vines wrapped around its shaft.
The wielder could unleash its fly trap enchantment upon a successful strike, ensnaring the target in place. However plant creatures and beasts were unaffected by this weapons magic.
