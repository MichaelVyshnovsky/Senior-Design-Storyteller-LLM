'''Baguette''' were a type of [[bread]] roll from [[Greenfields]] in the [[Western Heartlands]].

==Description==
The baguette was a type of thin and elongated bread with soft center and crispy hard outside. This bread was considered to be hearthy and often used to soak up [[soup]]s or [[sour cream]].

==Availability==
Baguettes could be found around the [[Greenfields]] of the [[Western Heartlands]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
