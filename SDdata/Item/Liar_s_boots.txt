Liars boots were a variety of enchanted boots that could aid in evading pursuers,
When activated, liars boots would leave footprints in a direction of the wearers choice (which must be mentally concentrated upon) and left no footprints of the actual direction the wearer walked with them. This effect could be activated twice per day.
The maximum length by which a false trail could be made or extended out to was . Upon going beyond that limit or removing the liars boots, the wearers real footprints would begin to appear while their false ones dissipated.
Most guilds in the Dalelands possessed one or more pairs of liars boots.
