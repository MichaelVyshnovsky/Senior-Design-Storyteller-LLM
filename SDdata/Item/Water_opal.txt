'''Water opal''' was a rare and valuable [[gem stones|gem stone]]. They were favored by the deities [[Deep Sashelas]] and [[Savras]].{{Cite web enhancement/Deity Do's and Don'ts|10-15}}

==Description==
Water opal was a clear to translucent gemstone with small flashes of color. A typical specimen had a base value of 1,000 gp.

==Powers==
An ''[[elixir of health]]'' could be made without any spellcasting by grinding a water opal to powder and mixing it with holy water.

==Reputation==
Water opals were considered prized gems among the [[drow]]. They often used them on their sculptures and scrying crystals.
