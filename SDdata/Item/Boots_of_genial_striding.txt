Boots of genial striding were a pair of enchanted boots that allowed the wearer to move unhindered by terrain.
These boots magic was evident when one examined the soles. They warped and shifted akin to clay being molded by a potter. This magic was the enchantment similar to the constant effects of the freedom of movement spell that allowed the wearer to easily stride across even the most difficult of terrains.
In the , a pair of boots of genial striding belonged to the orog Blurg of the Society of Brilliance, during his stay within a friendly myconid colony of the Ebonlake Grotto in the Underdark.
