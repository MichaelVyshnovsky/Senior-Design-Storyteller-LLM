Potion of bulls strength was a common magical potion that gave the imbiber great strength.
These potions were usually of a blood-red color and were brewed using the horns and hearts of young bulls.
Potion of bulls strength were dispensed in simple glass potion bottles or decorative glass decanters.
Consuming a bottle of a potion of bulls strength was similar in effect to the spell of the same name - bulls strength, cast at a highly proficient level.
