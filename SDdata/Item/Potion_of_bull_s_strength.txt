'''''Potion of bull's strength''''' was a common magical [[potion]] that gave the imbiber great strength.

==Description==
These potions were usually of a blood-red color and were brewed using the horns and hearts of young [[ox|bull]]s.

''Potion of bull's strength'' were dispensed in simple [[glass]] potion [[bottle]]s or decorative glass [[decanter]]s.

==Powers==
Consuming a bottle of a ''potion of bull's strength'' was similar in effect to the spell of the same name - ''[[bull's strength]]'', cast at a highly proficient level.
