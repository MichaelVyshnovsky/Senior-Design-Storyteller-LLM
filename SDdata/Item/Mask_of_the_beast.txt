A mask of the beast was a magical mask that allowed the wearer to better interact with beasts.
Masks of the beast were shaped in the likeness of a beasts visage
The mask granted its wearer the benefits of the animal friendship spell up to three times a day. It then recharged its magic at every dawn.
* Azaka Stormfang, a weretiger who worked as a guide in Port Nyanzaru in the late 15th century DR.
