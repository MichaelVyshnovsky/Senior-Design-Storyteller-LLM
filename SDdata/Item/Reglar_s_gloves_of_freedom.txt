'''''Reglar's gloves of freedom''''' was a [[magic item]] that could break magical [[charm]]s.

==Description==
These sturdy leather gloves had silver [[pearl]]s sewn into the stitching.

==Powers==
The wearer of ''Reglar's gloves of freedom'' could break the enchantment placed upon a charmed individual by shaking their hand. The magic only worked if the target was charmed against their will. Wearing these gloves did not protect the wearer from such enchantments.

==History==
Specialty priests of [[Waukeen]] called [[goldeye]]s had a chance of receiving a pair of ''Reglar's gloves of freedom'' as one of the magic items they were given upon their admission to the clergy.
