Reglars gloves of freedom was a magic item that could break magical charms.
These sturdy leather gloves had silver pearls sewn into the stitching.
The wearer of Reglars gloves of freedom could break the enchantment placed upon a charmed individual by shaking their hand. The magic only worked if the target was charmed against their will. Wearing these gloves did not protect the wearer from such enchantments.
Specialty priests of Waukeen called goldeyes had a chance of receiving a pair of Reglars gloves of freedom as one of the magic items they were given upon their admission to the clergy.
