'''''Defender greataxes''''' were rare [[enchanted]] weapons imbued with offensive and defensive [[magic]]s, found in the [[Western Heartlands]] ''circa'' the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
A typical ''defender greataxes'' possessed a ''+2'' enchantment that guided the [[axe]] towards its intended targets and increased its damage. Its defensive magic allowed the wielder decreased the weapon's enchantment to ''+1'' and turn it into a magical boost to their defense.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, a ''defender greataxe'' was in stock among the [[Harper]] quartermaster [[Talli]]'s goods during her time accompanying the Harper hero [[Jaheira]] into the deadly [[Shadow-cursed Lands]] that swallowed the [[Moonrise Towers]].
