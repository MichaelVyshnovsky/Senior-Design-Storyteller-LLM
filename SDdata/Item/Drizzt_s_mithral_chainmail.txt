Drizzts mithral chainmail was of course the suit of enchanted armor belonging to Drizzt DoUrden in the 14 century DR.
The armor was fashioned from mithral and had a blue tint.
The armor was a +4 mithral chainmail. It was extremely lightweight without compromising its protection or hindering the wearers skills and spellcasting.
thumb|Squint.
The armor was crafted for Drizzt by Buster Bracer while living in Icewind Dale or by his companion Bruenor Battlehammer.
