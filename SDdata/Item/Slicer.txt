'''''Slicer''''' was a [[magic]]al [[short sword]].

==Description==
Slicer was a curved short sword, with a large, golden knuckleguard. The sword could be found among the lower reaches of the [[drow]], above [[Xanathar]]'s outer sanctum. It had a ''+3'' enchantment.
