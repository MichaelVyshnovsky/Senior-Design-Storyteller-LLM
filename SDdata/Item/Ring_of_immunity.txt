A '''''ring of immunity''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[ring]] that protected the wearer from all forms of disease and infection.

==Description==
A ''ring of immunity'' appeared to be a simple ring made from [[silver]].

==Powers==
The powers of these rings protected the wearer from all mundane and magical diseases such as [[lycanthropy]]. Additionally, wearing the ring ensured that the wearer would heal from any wounds at the maximum possible natural rate regardless of the amount of sleep or rest they received.

==History==
The initial creation of these items was related to the incarnations of [[Thoth]].
