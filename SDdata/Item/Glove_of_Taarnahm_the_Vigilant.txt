The '''''glove of Taarnahm the Vigilant''''' was a [[magic]]al [[chainmail]] gauntlet that granted its wearer the ability to throw melee [[weapon]]s with incredible skill. Any weapon that was cast with the glove would be done as if it had the ''[[throwing]]'' and ''[[returning]]'' qualities.

==Creation==
The [[spell]]s ''[[magic stone]]'' and ''[[telekinesis]]'' were used in the [[Item enchantment|enchantment]] of this glove.
