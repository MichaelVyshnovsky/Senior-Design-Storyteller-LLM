'''Whistles''' were small [[:Category:Wind instruments|wind instruments]] used to signal danger, give orders, or attract wild animals.

{{quote|''In all human souls ring<br/>the songs of Truth,''<br/>
''But their music is muffled<br/>by clamorous bells<br/>and raucous whistles.''|
[[Gaumahavi]], Great Dragon of the Desert Winds{{cite book/Storm Riders|47}}}}

{{TOC limit|3}}

==Description==
[[File:Call hounds.png|thumb|right|300px|''Whistles could order animals around, or even summon them.'']]

===Types of whistles===
====Signal whistles====
[[Signal whistle]]s made a high pitched noise and were used to communicate a message, usually an alarm or an order. Many soldiers and guardsmen used signal whistles as a way to call for backup in case of trouble.{{Cite adventurers league/A Wrinkle in the Weave|5}}

====Animal calls====
Some whistles were created with the dedicated purpose of attracting or repelling animals. Such instruments affected one specific family of animals such as [[bat]]s, [[cat]]s, [[horse]]s, or even [[mountain lion]]s.

===Construction===
Whistles could be made out of many common materials, for example [[wood]] or [[bone]].{{Cite adventurers league/Poisoned Words|8}} They were used by [[human]]s and other races, such as [[hobgoblin]]s and [[bugbear]]s.

==Notable usages==
* The traffic wardens of Waterdeep used whistles and small colored [[flag]]s to order drovers around.
* In many [[goblin]] tribes, male adults were required to keep a whistle with them at all time. Those were used to communicate across long distances, especially underground.
* In [[Ra-Khati]], whistles were used alongside [[gong]]s, [[bell]]s and household items to create noise every morning, in a ceremony welcoming the new day.
===Spell components===
Whistles were necessary to cast various magical [[spell]]s.
* The ''[[Mordenkainen's faithful hound]]'' and ''[[call hounds]]'' spells required the caster to use a [[silver]] whistle.
* ''[[Bird charm I]]'' required the caster to carve a whistle out of [[coral]] to be used as the spell's [[material component]].
* Similarly, ''[[bird charm II]]'' required a whistle made of fine [[jade]].
* The [[maztican]] spell ''[[huntsman's call]]'' required the caster to use a whistle crafted from the claw of a [[jaguar]].

==Notable vendors==
* Whistles designed to attract or repel animals could be purchased from [[Aurora's Emporium]] for 1 [[gp]].

==Notable whistles==
* The ''[[whistle of shrieking]]'' was a magical ivory whistle used by [[Tasloi]] priests to increase the morale of their allies.
* The ''[[whistle of warning]]'' allowed nearby allies to react with unusual swiftness, make a close leap or draw a sword.{{Cite LFR/DRAG2-3/Of Wild and Darkened Waters|30}}

==Rumors & Legends==
===Divine gifts===
It was said that on occasions, the [[Lords of the Golden Hills]] who took a liking to a mortal while traveling the [[multiverse]] would offer them a magical golden whistle that could mimic the singing of any bird.
===Magical whistles===
According to rumors told in the [[Yawning Portal]], magical whistles existed that could grant their users supernatural flying abilities, or the power to animate dead creatures.
