Soul Biter was a mysterious enchanted short sword imbued with a sliver of nightshades power-draining negative energy.
Soul Biter was a short and stout pitch-black blade that radiated Negative Energy planes magic.
Soul Biter possessed the +2 enchantment and was able to sap strength and life force out of its target upon a successful hit.
Soul Biter was wielded by one of the skeleton lords, part of the undead swarm unleashed in the Dwarven Dungeons of Myth Drannor during the Weeping War. The nightmare gate remained open until dispelled in 1369 DR by the Veiled Ones. The blades origin, as well as where the gate led remained undiscovered by the heroes from Phlan.
