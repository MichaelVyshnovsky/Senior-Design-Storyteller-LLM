'''Throwing axes''' were a light, martial melee weapon of the [[axe]] family that could be used as a ranged weapon.

==Description==
Throwing axes were similar to a [[handaxe]], but lighter and balanced for throwing. Their wooden shafts were about {{SI|1.5|ft|long=yes}} long. . Their blades were typically single-edged with a plain back.

==Weaponology==
Classified as a light weapon, throwing axes could be used while grappling. Some training was required to be able to hit a target with a throwing axe, thus its classification as a martial weapon. The average maximum range of a throwing axe was typically {{SI|50|ft|long=yes}}.

==Availability==
Business and merchants in the Realms where one could purchase throwing axes from included:
* A number of merchants in [[Athkatla]]'s market [[Waukeen's Promenade]] commonly sold throwing axes, including the fruit merchant [[Enge]] and [[Ribald Barterman]] from his store [[Adventurer Mart]]. In the [[Athkatla/Bridge District|Bridge]] district, one could find them for sale from the innkeeper of [[Delosar's Inn]] and at the street-side stall of [[Bel Dalemark]].
* A number of general stores in the southern districts of [[Baldur's Gate]] sold throwing axes.
* [[Candlekeep Inn]].
* The inn within the hamlet of [[Friendly Arm]] sold throwing axes.
* [[Maheer el Saddith]], a traveling merchant.
* [[Maltz's Weapon Shop]].
* [[Ulgoth's Beard inn]]'s owner sold throwing axes on the side, alongside a variety of general adventuring equipment.<ref>
* Weapon merchants in the city of [[Ust Natha]]'s bazaar sold throwing axes.
* The [[Zephir]]'s owner [[Zakee Rafeha]] sold throwing axes in his inn on the side, alongside a variety of general adventuring equipment.
