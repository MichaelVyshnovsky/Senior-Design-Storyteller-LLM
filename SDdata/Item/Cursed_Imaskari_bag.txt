The '''cursed Imaskari bag'''<ref group="note">Note that this bag is never referred to using this name in the book: it is merely described. was an Imaskari relic unearthed from the ruins of Solon by the slaves of [[Ambuchar Devayam]].{{cite book/Blood Charge|54}}

==Description==
The bag was half-rotten.

==Powers==
The bag contained 1 copper piece, 1 silver piece, 1 gold piece, and 1 platinum piece. Whenever one of those coins was spent, it returned to the bag within 24 hours.
