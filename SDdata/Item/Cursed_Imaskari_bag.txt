The cursed Imaskari bagNote that this bag is never referred to using this name in the book it is merely described. was an Imaskari relic unearthed from the ruins of Solon by the slaves of Ambuchar Devayam.
The bag was half-rotten.
The bag contained 1 copper piece, 1 silver piece, 1 gold piece, and 1 platinum piece. Whenever one of those coins was spent, it returned to the bag within 24 hours.
