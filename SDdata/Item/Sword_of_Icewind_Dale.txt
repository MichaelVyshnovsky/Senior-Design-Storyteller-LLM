The '''''sword of Icewind Dale''''' was a [[magic]]al [[sword]].

==Powers==
This enchanted sword had a ''+1'' enchantment. It dealt double damage to [[fire]]-based monsters.

==History==
In [[1358 DR]], a group of [[pirate]]s wished to present the enchanted weapon to [[Kurth|High Captain Kurth]] in [[Kurth Tower|his tower]]. However, they were intercepted by the [[Heroes of Ascore]] whilst waiting for Kurth. After a difficult battle, the heroes claimed the sword as their own.
