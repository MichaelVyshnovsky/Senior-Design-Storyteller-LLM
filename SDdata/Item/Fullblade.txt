Fullblades, also known as an ogres greatsword, were a type of large exotic sword.
Fullblades were enormous, two-handed swords that were longer than greatswords by  
Medium-sized creatures were only capable of wielding a fullblade two-handed and even then needed special training. Large-sized creatures were capable of wielding it with either one or both hands.   Due to their size, fullblades dealt extremely heavy damage and had a higher propensity for lethal blows.
Fullblades were often favored by fighters and paladins.
