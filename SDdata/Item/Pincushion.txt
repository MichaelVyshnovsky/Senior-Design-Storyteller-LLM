Pincushions were a type of sewing equipment commonly used by seamstresses to hold pins and sewing needles.
* Foxilon Cardluck used pincushions when tailoring a new outfit for his friend Priam Agrivar.
* The night hag coven of Auntie Pinch, Auntie Pillage, and Auntie Plunder kept a pincushion in their demiplane abode.
Some in the Realms would refer to bodies pierced with a profuse number of arrows as resembling a pincushion.
