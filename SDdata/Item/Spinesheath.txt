The spinesheath was a unique enchanted stiletto.
The blade of this stiletto was specially designed to pierce through armor.
The spinesheath possessed a +1 enchantment.
This stiletto first belonged to an assassin in Calimport by the name of Beledor, until one day he was poisoned by a bribed servant. The weapons name was derived from his tendency to plunge its blade it into the backs of his targets.
Over time spinesheath changed hands, until ending up in the possession of a lizard king lairing in the Dragons Eye in 1281 DR.
