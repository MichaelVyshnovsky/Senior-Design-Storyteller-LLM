Spiders lyres were enchanted lyres used by the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 century DR.
Appearing unassuming at first glance, these plucked stringed instruments had strings as sharp as an assassins blade. Those who played the lyre had to do it with utmost caution or slice their fingertips off.
Spiders lyres possessed magic that could be heard across the Shadow-cursed Lands by the mad drider named Karniss who served The Absolute.
In the , spiders lyres were carried by ranking members of the Cult of the Absolute, such as Minthara Baenre. When plucked, the instrument summoned Karniss, a guide who aided True Souls across the deadly lands of shadows in the Western Heartlands towards the Moonrise Towers.
