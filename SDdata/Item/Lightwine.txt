Lightwine was a fermented alcoholic drink from Twilight Tor in the Starmetal Hills.
thumb|A light pod.
Lightwine was made out of light pods that fell from the skies during the Simril season in the town of Twilight Tor and across the Starmetal Hills, where they were devoured by fish that dwelt in the regions lakes. This wine was fermented by magical means by the Master of Lights of Twilight Tor. Aged fortified and strengthened lightwine was consumed during the Liars Night festival in Neverwinter.
When consumed, lightwine boosted the imbibers speed and granted them sped up regeneration.
thumb|A bottle of aged lightwine.
In the late 15 century DR, lightwine was a common drink created during the annual Simril celebration in Twilight Tor.
