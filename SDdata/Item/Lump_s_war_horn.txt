Lumps war horn was a unique war horn created and carried by the brilliant ogre mercenary, Lump the magnificent.
The mouthpiece of the horn was encrusted with bits of food.
When sounded, Lumps war horn would alert Lump and his allies Chock and Fank. The mercenaries would respond to the call, aiding whomever sounded it during their next battle. In return, the trio of ogres expected to feast upon any creatures they slew during the fight.
The ogres would typically try to avoid harming whomever sounded the horn, but that was not a complete certainty.
