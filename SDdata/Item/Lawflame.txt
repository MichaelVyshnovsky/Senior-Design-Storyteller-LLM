Lawflame was the sword of state for the dukes that ruled over Daggerford.
It was a +1 flame tongue bastard sword that possessed sentient intelligence and a unique personality.
Lawflame granted its wielder several different powers. Once per day they could become aware of evil beings, sense magical energies, and gain sight of a location a significant distance away.
The sword was originally given to the first duke of Daggerford Tyndal, by Elorfindar Floshin.
