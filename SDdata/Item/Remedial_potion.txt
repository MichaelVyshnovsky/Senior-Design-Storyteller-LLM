'''''Remedial potion''''' was a type of curative [[potion]] found in the [[Western Heartlands]] and the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
''Remedial potions'' were viscous liquid that glittered with [[magic]].

==Powers==
This potion provided no healing but cured ''[[blindness]]'', ''[[silence]]'', ''[[paralyzation]]'' , and ''[[poison]]''.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Esther]], an adventurer who worked for the [[Society of Brilliance]] ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
