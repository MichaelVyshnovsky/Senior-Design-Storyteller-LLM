'''Pigeon pie''' was a [[Faerûn]]ian savory game pie made of [[pigeon]] meat.{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 56: Khôltar, Part 7, Leaving the Farrgaunlar}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 35: A First Glance at Delzimmer}} It was common in some cities, such as [[Delzimmer]] and [[Khôltar]], for the destitute citizenry to sling stones at local pigeons in order to fell them and use their meat in pigeon pies.

==Availability==
* Delzimmer.
* Khôltar.
* In [[northwest Faerûn]], in the hamlet of [[Zelbross]], the [[the Last Place (inn)|Last Place]] inn kept its own domesticated pigeons for use in making pigeon pies.

==History==
The idea of pigeon pie existed in Faerûn as far back as the year {{Yearlink|-6048}},{{Cite web/Presenting...Seven Millennia of Realms Fiction}} where it could be found eaten by some in the [[Calim Caliphates]].
