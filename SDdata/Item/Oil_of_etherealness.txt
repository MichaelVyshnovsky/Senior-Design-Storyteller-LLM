'''''Oil of etherealness''''' was a [[magic]]al substance that, when applied to a creature, gave them the effect of the [[spell]] ''[[etherealness]]''. The oil affected the creature, the equipment it was wearing and belongings it was carrying. It took about 10 minutes to apply and lasted for on hour.{{Cite web/Hoard of the Dragon Queen/Tyranny of Dragons Online Supplement|2}}

==Description==
The oil appeared a cloudy gray, and the condensation would evaporate quickly from its vial.

==Notable Owners==
===Religions===
* [[Goldeye]]s, the [[specialty priest]]s of [[Waukeen]], had a chance of receiving some ''oil of etherealness'' as one of the magic items they were given upon their admission to the clergy.
