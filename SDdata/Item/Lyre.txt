A lyre was an ancient and portable stringed musical instrument.
Lyres provided good accompaniment and background music for poems and epic recitals through the ages.
* The lyre was particularly associated with Amn. Such lyres were sold in Halambar Lutes & Harps in Waterdeep, a well-known musical instrument shop.
* Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold lyres under the "Bards Emporium" section of their catalogue for around 40 gold pieces.
thumb|A simple lyre from the Sword Coast.
* Cli lyre, oe of the seven Instruments of the Bards. Magical lyres that held protective spells, as well as other magical powers.
* Dragonbone lyre, a lyre carved of dragon bone. A certain haunting tune played on this could subdue the undead guardian of the Shadow Swamp.
* Lyre of building
* Lyre of progression
* Spiders lyre, a variety of enchanted lyre used by the Cult of the Absolute.
* Apollo, a member of the Olympian pantheon, had the instrument as his holy symbol and owned a unique magical lyre that was fashioned for him by his fellow Olympian Hermes.
* Bonnie Harper, a member of the bardic group Adagio Jones and The Goodwinds, had a lyre that she played for soft songs.
