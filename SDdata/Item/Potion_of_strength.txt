Potions of strength were magical brews that made the consumer stronger for a prolonged period of time.
Imbibing one of these potions gave the drinker a surge of power, slightly increases their strength. The muscles seemed to ripple and bulge, filled with pulling magic. The effects of one dose of a potion of strength lasted for a bit over three hours.
Circa the , a vial of the potion could be found in the ruined Mantors Library in the city of Phlan, within a nest of a hungry basilisk.
In the , agents of the Cult of the Dragon, who took over the ruins of Myth Drannor under the leadership of Kya Mordrayn, carried potions of strength as part of the standard cult equipment.
