Hamarhraft was an enchanted maul of dwarven origin that had been imbued with the power of thunder.
The great hammer had an inscription, written in dwarvish, that alluded to the importance of making a strong first impression.
Whenever the wielded of Hamarhraft leapt or jumped onto the group, their landing was followed by a thunderous wave that extended out .
As of the , Hamarhraft could be found within a locked chest in one of the upper floors of the Waukeens Rest inn. It was not entirely clear who remained in possession of the hammer.
