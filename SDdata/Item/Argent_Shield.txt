The Argent Shield was one of the enchanted shield created in the Seldarines Hand citadel on the Spine of the World.
This silver shield bore markings of the Seldarines Hand and was designed to protect its wielder physically and magically.
The Argent Shield possessed a potent defensive +3 enchantment and granted the wielder limited resistance to magic. Its magics were attuned exclusively to elves, rendering the shield unusable by anyone else.
After completion of construction of Seldarines Hand circa the , the inhabitants of the citadel lived in a close alliance with the dwarves of Dorns Deep. The troops of the elven citadel were outfitted with the best armor and weapons created by the crafters of both races. By the , the Seldarines Hand had been ruined for centuries and came to be known as the Severed Hand. After the citadels fall, the Argent Shield remained within the ruins, discard by invading orcs and goblins as trash due to its magical attainment. Maiden Ilmadia Bariel, who served Ilmatari priest Revered Brother Poquelin, reclaimed the enchanted shield from the Hands ruins and bore it until her death in 1281 DR at the hands of the Heroes of Icewind Dale.
