'''Prestidigitation''' involved a variety of objects in the Realms popular among [[bard]]s and performers who used them to appear to perform [[magic]] tricks.

==Description==
Items used for prestidigitation performances included marked playing cards, hollow-topped hats, pocket scarves, foldable canes, flowering rods, and retractable [[knives]].

==History==
These items were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' for 3–4 [[gold piece]]s.

==Notable Users==
* Prestidigitation items were favored by bards.
