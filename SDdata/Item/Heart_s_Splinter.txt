The Hearts Splinter was an enchanted rapier found in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.
The Hearts Splinter had an intricately decorated hilt and guard that had an gem stone at the designs center.
thumb|The Hearts Splinter in Calliopes hands.
The Hearts Splinters magic allowed the owner to attack their opponents at a distance as well as in melee range. Additionally, upon a successful hit, the target could be affected by magical paralyzing fear, incapacitating them for a short period of time.
*Calliope a bard adventurer owned this rapier during her time working with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
