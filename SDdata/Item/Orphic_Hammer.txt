The '''''Orphic Hammer''''' was a [[:Category:Legendary items|legendary]] [[warhammer]] that held the power to destroy supernatural, and otherwise indestructible restraints, and release any creature that had been rendered immobile or otherwise hindered.

{{Quote|The Orphic Hammer is one of Master Raphael's most prized possessions. You can't have it. You can't even touch it. And I would suggest you don't think about it.|Raphael's archivist.}}

==Description==
The hammer was cast from [[infernal iron]], and said to have been crafted in the fires of the [[Nine Hells]].

==Powers==
The ''Orphic Hammer'' possessed a ''+3 [[Item enchantment|enchantment]]'' and granted the wielder inherent resistance to [[magic]]al spells.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, the hammer remained in the possession of [[Raphael]], son of [[Mephistopheles]], and was stashed away within the archives of the [[House of Hope (Avernus)|House of Hope]] in [[Avernus]].

==Reputation==
The ''Orphic Hammer'' was the only means of freeing the [[githyanki]] heir [[Orpheus]] from the ''[[Astral Prism]]''.
