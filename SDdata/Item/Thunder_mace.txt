Thunder mace was an enchanted bludgeon that often was accompanied by a shield from Waterdeep, in the late 15 century DR.
Thunder mace, along with its sister shield, appeared to be crafted out of gold or golden-colored metal.
Aptly named, thunder mace was imbued with thunder magic, allowing the wielder to deal additional electric damage to a target. Upon a successful strike, thunder mace could gain a temporary damage boots.
*Halbenet Soladonel a moon elf cleric of Corellon Larethian who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
