'''''Thunder mace''''' was an enchanted bludgeon that often was accompanied by a [[shield]] from [[Waterdeep]], in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
''Thunder mace'', along with its sister shield, appeared to be crafted out of [[gold]] or golden-colored metal.

==Powers==
Aptly named, ''thunder mace'' was imbued with ''thunder'' magic, allowing the wielder to deal additional electric damage to a target. Upon a successful strike, ''thunder mace'' could gain a temporary damage boots.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Halbenet Soladonel]]: a [[moon elf]] [[cleric]] of [[Corellon Larethian]] who worked with other warriors of [[Waterdeep]], performing missions on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
