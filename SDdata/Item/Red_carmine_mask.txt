'''''Red carmine masks''''' were [[leather]] [[helmet]]s that bore a minor [[Item enchantment|enchantment]], created sometime in or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
These leather helmets were dyed in red color, reminiscent of spilled [[gladiator]]ial blood.

==Powers==
When worn, a ''red carmine mask'' slightly boosted the wearer's reaction to danger.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, one such helmet could be found in the [[Szarr Palace]] in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].
