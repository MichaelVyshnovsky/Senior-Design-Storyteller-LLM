Insanitys Kiss was a unique potion formulated by the hag named Auntie Ethel, originating from her laboratory beneath the Gnarled Teahouse of the Putrid Bog.
The potion was kept in an earthenware jug, plugged by a stopper adorned with a single feather. When holding the jug, it appeared as if there was some enraged creature trapped within.
Whoever drank from Insanitys Kiss was thrown into a murderous frenzy and became immediately hostile towards anyone around them.
As of the , there was only one known bottle of Insanitys Kiss in existence.
