'''''Dragongleam''''' was an [[item enchantment|enchanted]] [[spear]] that remained in possession of the [[Cult of the Dragon]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The spear had the phrase "Tiamat's eyes shine" inscribed upon its crossguard in the [[Iokharic]] script.

==Powers==
''Dragongleam'' was empowered with ten charges of the spell ''[[daylight]]''.

==History==
As of the 1480s [[DR]],{{ToD-time}} it could be found within the cult's [[The Hunting lodge (Greypeak Mountains)|hunting lodge]] in the [[Greypeak Mountains]].

==Notable Owners==
* [[Talis]] the White, the Dragon Cult's white [[wyrmspeaker]].
