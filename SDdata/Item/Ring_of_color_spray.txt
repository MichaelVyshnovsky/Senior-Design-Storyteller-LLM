A '''''ring of color spray''''' was a [[magic]]al ring that allowed the wearer to shoot out a vibrant array of colored light towards others.

==Description==
The central gem of the ring shone with every color of the visual spectrum when hit with light.

==Powers==
The wearer of the ring could cast the spell ''[[color spray]]''.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, one of these rings could be found in a [[harpy]] nest outside of the [[Emerald Grove]].
