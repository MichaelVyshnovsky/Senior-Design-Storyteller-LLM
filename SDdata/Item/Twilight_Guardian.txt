The Twilight Guardian was a unique enchanted greatsword from the ruined city of Myth Drannor.
It was decorated with a regal insignia.
The sword possessed a +2 enchantment that enhanced its damage and guided the wielders hand toward the target. Additionally, the Twilight Guardian granted the wielder significantly increased protection from magics and spells cast by orcs and orogs.
In the , the Twilight Guardian was in the scaly hands of the lizardmen followers of the sorcerer named Malgi Hi, who claimed a part of the ancient Dwarven Dungeons underneath Myth Drannor.
