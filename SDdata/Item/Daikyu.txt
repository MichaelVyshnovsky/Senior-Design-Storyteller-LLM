A '''daikyu''' ({{Pronounce|digh|kyoo|refs=}}) was a type of [[longbow]]. It was the largest variety of bow in use in [[Kara-Tur]], but was rarely seen outside of that continent.

==Description==
A daikyu was a large bow with a bottom grip. This allowed the bow to be easily fired from horseback or while kneeling., that anyone under {{SI|4|ft}} was unable to use it. 

==Notable Users of Daikyu==
===Classes===
* [[Samurai]]s commonly used daikyu.
===Races & Sentient Creatures===
* Some [[hobgoblin]]s were known to fight with daikyu.
* The daikyu was a common weapon among [[Ogre magi]].
