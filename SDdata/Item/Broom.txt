A '''broom''' was a common cleaning tool found in [[Faerûn]].

==Description==
Typical brooms had a 4–6 foot (1.2–1.8 meter) long pole with a flexible [[straw]] or similar head attached by thread or a ring.

==Availability==
* ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' offered brooms for 1 [[sp]] each, alongside other household implements.
* [[Tatha's Broomworks]] was a famed producer of brooms.{{Cite dragon/301/Elminster's Guide to the Realms: Tatha's Broomworks|74}}
