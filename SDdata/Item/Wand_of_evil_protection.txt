Wands of evil protection were enchanted wands that held multiple charges of protective spells unleashed upon command.
Wands of evil protection were  shafts with a round flat head. The total number of charges varied from wand to wand.
Each of these items held several charges of the protection from evil spell. The magics held within the wand could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of a wands last charge, was destroyed and consumed by magic.
*Nym, a drow proprietor of rare and magical goods who traveled Icewind Dale and Dorns Deep in the late 13 and early 14 centuries DR.
