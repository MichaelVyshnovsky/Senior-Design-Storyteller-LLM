'''''Potion of fox's cunning''''' were common magical concoctions that increased the imbiber's intelligence.

==Powers==
''Potion of [[fox]]'s cunning'' greatly increased the drinker's intelligence, similarly to the effects of the ''[[fox's cunning]]'' spell.

==History==
By [[1372 DR]] these potions could be found in wide use in many places across the [[Sword Coast]], such as [[Neverwinter]], [[Moonsea]], and as far as the [[Nelanther Isles]].

==Notable Owners==
*[[Red Wizards]] of [[Thay]] often carried a ''potion of fox's cunning'' along with other magical items ''circa'' [[1373 DR]]..
