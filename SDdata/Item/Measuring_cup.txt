'''Measuring cups''' were a type of cookware container in the Realms, used to measure the volume of liquid or dry ingredients.<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/In the Hall of the Mountain Mage|9}}

==History==
Sometime in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], the cargo ship ''[[The Gentle Lady]]'' was delivering a shipment of farmer and merchant goods to a small coastal community in the [[Dragon Reach]].{{Cite organized play/LC/How Long Can You Tread Water?|9-10}} Among the goods being carried by the ship was a crate full of cookware that included eleven [[tin]] measuring cups.{{Cite organized play/LC/How Long Can You Tread Water?|12}}
