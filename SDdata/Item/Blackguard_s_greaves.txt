Blackguards greaves were a pair of enchanted medium armor ornate-plated boots that were once worn by a blackguard and later by a death knight they became.
The boots possessed a simple enchantment that allowed the wearer move faster and further in combat, similarly to the permanent effects of the longstrider spell, and increased their skills and proficiency in acrobatics.
In the , blackguards greaves were worn by the guardian death knight called That Which Lurks, who, along with That Which Guards and That Which Watches, guarded the entrance to the chamber of the Bhaalist Murder Tribunal in the Undercellar of Baldurs Gate.
