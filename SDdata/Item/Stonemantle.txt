'''''Stonemantle''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[quarterstaff]] wielded by the [[avatar]]s of [[Geb]].

==Powers==
''Stonemantle'' carried a ''+3'' enchantment. It could also duplicate the effects of any [[spell]] or [[magical item]] relating to [[elemental earth]], such as ''[[transmute mud to rock]]''.
