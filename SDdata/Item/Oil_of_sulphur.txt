'''''Oil of sulphur''''' was a magic [[potion]] that originated in [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.

This item was often used by [[genie]]s who sought revenge against their masters.

==Description==
''Oil of sulphur'' was contained within a flask like many other magical potions. In fact, it actually appeared to resemble other beneficial potions or oils when examined, masking its true nature as that of a deadly liquid.

==Powers==
This oil would burst into intense flame 10-30 minutes after it was applied, causing vicious burns if applied to living flesh.

==Uses==
[[Efreet]] enjoyed using ''oil of sulphur'' during massages.
