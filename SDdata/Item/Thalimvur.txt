'''Thalimvur''' was a peculiar [[halfling]] and [[gnome]] delicacy found throughout [[Faerûn]].

==Description==
The dish was cooked using an unlikely protein – [[maggot]]s. These insects were simply fried in [[maple syrup]] or [[maple sap]] and served.
