Wands of the sun were magical items that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.
These wands discharged a golden ray that was up to . The effects of the ray were similar to the sunscorch spell. The ray never missed its target and blasted it with burning damage. Targets wearing metallic armor or holding metallic weapons suffered more damage.
These wands were favored by powerful members of the Brotherhood of the True Flame.
