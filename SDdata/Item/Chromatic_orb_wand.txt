Chromatic orb wands were enchanted wands that held multiple charges of destructive spells unleashed upon command.
Chromatic orb wands were on average  long scepter-shaped items topped with a glowing orb. They were usually made using ivory, or wood.
Each of these mages accessories held several charges of the chromatic orb spell. The magics held within it could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of a wands last charge, it disintegrated.
