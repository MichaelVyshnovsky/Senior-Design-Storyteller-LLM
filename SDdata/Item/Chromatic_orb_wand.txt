'''''Chromatic orb wands''''' were [[enchanted]] [[wand]]s that held multiple charges of destructive spells unleashed upon command.

==Description==
''Chromatic orb wands'' were on average {{SI|1'3|'"|long=yes}} long scepter-shaped items topped with a glowing orb. They were usually made using [[ivory]], or [[wood]].

==Powers==
Each of these mages' accessories held several charges of the ''[[chromatic orb]]'' spell. The magics held within it could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of a wand's last charge, it disintegrated.

==History==
''Chromatic orb wands'' were rare and ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1312}}, sometimes appeared in the hands of spellcasters of [[Icewind Dale]] in [[Northwest Faerûn]].
