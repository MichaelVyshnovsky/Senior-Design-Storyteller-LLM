A titanstring bow was a type of enchanted longbow that relied on the raw physical strength of the owner for its damage.
Titanstring bows were proof that the thin and slender archer stereotype was a false one. This powerful bow could only be fully pulled by an individual of great strength and wrists of iron.
Apart from a regular +1 enchantment to the bows aim and damage output, a titanstring bows power was dependent on the wielders muscles.
In the , titanstring bows could be purchased from one of the Zhentarims smugglers and merchants named Brem, who laired underneath the Waukeens Rest inn, and from a bugbear merchant Lann Tarv who served the Absolute in the Moonrise Towers.
