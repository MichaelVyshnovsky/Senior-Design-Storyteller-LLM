'''Exorcessum''' was a [[greatsword]] created by [[deity|gods]] outside of [[Toril]]{{Cite digital book/Spinner of Lies|1697}} wielded by the [[Deva (aasimar)|deva]] [[Demascus]].  

==Description==
It had [[magic]] runes on each side which allowed Demascus to call magical abilities. The runes on one side were red and contained martial abilities while the runes on the other side were white and cast beneficial magic.{{Cite digital book/Spinner of Lies|248}}  

The sword had multiple configurations and could be split to form two long swords. The final configuration was said to blaze as hot as a star with the capability of incinerating a god.{{Cite digital book/Spinner of Lies|1692}}
