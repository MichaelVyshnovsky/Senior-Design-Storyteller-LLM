A Calishite lamp was a type of lamp from Calimshan.
Calishite lamps were finely crafted, engraved metal lamps with pointed wick holders. They typically were used with exotic scented lamp oils and could last up to 12 hours with 1 pint (473 ml) of oil. Lamps of different materials were available for purchase and special order through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
They were also a common choice for genie prisons.
These lamps were popular in many childrens stories involving genies .
