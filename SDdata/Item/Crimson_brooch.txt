The crimson brooch was an enchanted piece of jewelry from the ruins of Myth Drannor.
This amulet was a small piece of blood-red jewelry.
The crimson brooch possessed an enchantment that increased the wearers resistance to magic spells.
In 1369 DR, the crimson brooch could be found in one of the ancient coffins in the tombs of the Deep Halls of Myth Drannors Dwarven Dungeons.
