'''Old Smoke''' was a favorite [[ale]] among connoisseurs around the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]] brewed at [[Gunderman Brewery]].

==Description==
Old Smoke was a mellow, golden-hued ale with a smoky aftertaste.

==Prices==
A keg of Old Smoke typically sold for 80 [[gp]] in [[Mulmaster]] or [[Telflamm]], but could be had for just 70 gp in [[Sembia]] and [[Cormyr]] where it was easier to find.
