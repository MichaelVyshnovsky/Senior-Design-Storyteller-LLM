A mageshield was an enchanted shield wielded by the inhabitants of ancient Myth Drannor.
A mageshield was ovoid in shape and was decorated with engravings of white stars along the border.
The shield was enchanted to protect its wielder from necromantic spells. Any death spells that could affect the wielder were deflected back at the caster.
In the , a member of the Veiled Ones adventuring group, Corran DArcey, received a mageshield from the baelnorn Miroden Silverblade to be used against the Cult of the Dragon and its leader Kya Mordrayn.
