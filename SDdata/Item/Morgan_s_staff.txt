Morgans staff was a unique magic weapon located in Waterdeep as of the .
The quarterstaff was made from polished cherry and walnut that were intertwined for the entire length of the staff. The staff weighed .
Morgans staff possessed both the thundering and shock weapon qualities. Additionally, the quarterstaffs charges could be used to cast baleful polymorph, chain lightning, and thunderlance.
Morgans staff was created by Laeral Silverhand and carried by her when masquerading as Irusyl Eraneth. It remained unclear how she settled on the weapons name.
