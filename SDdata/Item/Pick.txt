A pick, also known as a pickax or pickaxe was a hand tool for digging and breaking up hard or rocky ground. They were often used alongside shovels and spades. 
It was considered essential for excavating ore.
* Serrated picks, a type of pick that was similar to the standard variety but had longer, more saw-like heads.
* War picks, otherwise known as military picks, were picks adapted and designed for the purposes of warfare.
* Pickaxes were one of many tools considered useful for traveling through the Elemental Plane of Earth.
