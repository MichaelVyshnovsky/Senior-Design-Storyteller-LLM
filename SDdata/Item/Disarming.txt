'''''Disarming''''' was a [[weapon quality]] that let a weapon be used to disarm another.

==Powers==
It made it easier for the wielder to disarm an opponent's weapon. It also eliminated any advantage the wielder's opponent received from weapon size or from two-handed weaponry.
