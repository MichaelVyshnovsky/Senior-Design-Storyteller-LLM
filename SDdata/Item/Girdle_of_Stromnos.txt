The girdle of Stromnos was an enchanted belt created by Presdon of the Vast.
The belt was a plain in appearance but distinctly wide girdle.
The girdle of Stromnos possessed a simple enchantment that significantly enhanced its wearers strength, making them as strong as a hill giant.
The girdle of Stromnos was created by Presdon of the Vast several centuries before 1281 DR. The enchanter once encountered a hungry hill giant named Stromnos while gathering spell components. Presdon survived the encounter and named the belt after Stromnos. The girdle then was passed along to the enchanters bodyguard Dendes the Wry. The belt was lost some time late outside Ordulin as Dendes was filled in the line of duty.
For the several centuries , the girdle of Stromnos was though lost, until it reappeared in the . The belt was worn by Flozem, one of the two bodyguards employed by Marketh of the Dorns Deep in the Spine of the World.
