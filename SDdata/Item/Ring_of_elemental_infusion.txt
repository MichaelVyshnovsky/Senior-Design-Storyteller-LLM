'''''Rings of elemental infusion''''' were [[enchanted]] [[ring]]s that absorbed elemental magics.

==Description==
These rings were [[golden]] bands with a single mysterious [[gemstone]] set at the center. The stone tended to change its color when the wearer was not looking.

==Powers==
A ''ring of elemental infusion'' made its enchantment evident once the wearer successfully hit a target with a [[cantrip]] that death [[acid]], cold, fire, lightning, or thunder damage. It became infused with the spell's elemental magics that could be channeled by the wearer into any successful weapon attack, giving it an elemental edge.
