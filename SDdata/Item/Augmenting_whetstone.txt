An augmenting whetstone was a type of enchanted whetstone that would temporarily bestow enchantments.
This variety of enchanted whetstone generally granted weapons that touched it a temporary +2 enchantment, though more expensive variations offered higher enchantments. With the most expensive augmenting whetstone giving a +6 enchantment.
In 1479 DR,The date for all Year 1 RPGA sanctioned adventures is considered to be 1479 DR for the purposes of this wiki. a group of adventurers were potentially rewarded one of these by the Solemn Order of Recognized Furriers and Woolmens for clearing undead from the guilds tomb within the City of the Dead.
