A dart is a small sharp implement intended to be used as a ranged weapon, either to be thrown or blown from a blowpipe.
 Acid dart  A hollow dart that breaks on impact, causing acid damage.
 Barbed dart  A heavy fletched dart often used by lizardfolk.
 Stun dart  A hollow dart that releases poison gas on impact.
* Asps Nest a variety of enchanted poisoned dart that was created by a member of the Shadow Thieves.
* Dart of Fire a variety of enchanted darts that unleashed elemental fire.
* Dart of Ice a variety of enchanted darts that unleashed elemental ice.
* Static dart a variety of enchanted darts that unleashed a small burst of electrical energy.
