'''''Rings of cantrips''''' were a type of [[item enchantment|enchanted]] [[ring]] developed by [[Kyristan]].

==Description==
These rings were typically made of [[brass]] and engraved with symbols both inside and out.

==Powers==
While wearing a ''ring of cantrips'', a [[bard]] or [[wizard]] was capable of casting any type of [[cantrip]] [[magic]]. They were capable of creating anywhere from five to eight magical effects per day, with each lasting as long as the wearer could concentrate.
