An amulet of misty step was a magical item from Menzoberranzan that allowed the wearer to readily teleport short distances. They were regarded as signifying status of high regard and often given as gifts to solicit favor.
thumb|Another depiction of an amulet of misty step.
These necklaces were crafted using a number of vividly blue gems unique to a single gem mine in the drow city of Menzoberranzan.
The wearer of one such necklace could readily transport themself over short distances in a moments time.
* Minthara Baenre, Nightwarden of the cult of the Absolute.
