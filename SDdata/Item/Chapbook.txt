A '''chapbook''' was a short [[book]] which could contain about any content, from political opinions to crafting guides. In [[Waterdeep]], chapbooks  often contained memoirs or romantic stories.{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2010)/The Hunting Horn}}

==Notable Chapbooks==
* [[The Hunting Horn]], an erotic chapbook distributed by [[Amnian]] caravaneers.
