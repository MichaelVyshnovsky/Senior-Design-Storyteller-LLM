A chapbook was a short book which could contain about any content, from political opinions to crafting guides. In Waterdeep, chapbooks  often contained memoirs or romantic stories.
* The Hunting Horn, an erotic chapbook distributed by Amnian caravaneers.
In addition to most broadsheet vendors, the following places sold chapbooks in Waterdeep
* Melvars Chapbooks & Folios
* Serpentil Books & Folios
