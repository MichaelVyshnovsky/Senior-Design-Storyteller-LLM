An abjurers necklace was a protective enchanted amulet worn by wizards. It could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.
The wearer of an abjurers necklace could use it to cast the shield spell when they came under attack.
* Shevarith Kendia, a rashemi wizard who worked as an adventurer on behalf of Laeral Silverhand, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.
