An '''''abjurer's necklace''''' was a protective [[enchanted]] [[amulet]] worn by [[wizard]]s. It could be found in [[Waterdeep]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
The wearer of an ''abjurer's necklace'' could use it to cast the ''[[shield]]'' spell when they came under attack.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Shevarith Kendia]], a [[rashemi]] wizard who worked as an [[adventurer]] on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]], the [[Open Lord]] of Waterdeep, during the late 15{{th}} century DR.
