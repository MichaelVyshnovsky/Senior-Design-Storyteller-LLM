A '''''mantle of spell resistance''''' was a variety of [[magic]]al [[cloak]] that offered the wearer protection from harmful or debilitating [[magic]]al spells.

==Description==
''Mantles of spell resistance'' varied in appearance depending on numerous factors. At least one cloak was crafted from red linen with cloth-of-gold lining,{{Cite adventurers league/Pool of Radiance Resurgent|22}} while another was crafted from fabric that was as dark as the night's sky.{{Cite adventurers league/Shadows in the Stacks|28}}

Creation of a ''mantle of spell resistance'' required a casting of ''[[spell resistance]]''.

==Powers==
The cloak bestowed upon the wearer significant protections from magical spells. Quite fittingly, a casting of ''[[spell resistance]]'' was used to create a ''mantle of spell resistance''.

==Notable Mantles of Spell Resistance==
The ''[[Mantle of Deziwai]]'' was a unique ''mantle of spell resistance'' worn by a legendary [[githyanki]] warrior who fought in the great rebellion against the [[Illithid Empire]].{{Cite adventurers league/Stardock Under Siege|31}}
