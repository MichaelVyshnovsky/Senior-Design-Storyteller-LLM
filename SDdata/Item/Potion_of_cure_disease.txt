A '''''potion of cure disease''''' was a [[potion]] capable of duplicating the curative effects of the ''[[cure disease]]'' [[spell]].

==Powers==
When consumed, these potions cured the imbiber of most magical and natural forms of disease.

==Notable Owners==
A caravan driver who traversed the [[Path of Ultimate Bliss]] in [[Ra-Khati]] carried several ''potions of cure disease''.
