'''Laeral's tears''', also known as '''beryllonite''' (particularly on [[Earth]]), was a gemstone named after the famous [[sorcerer|sorceress]] [[Laeral Silverhand]].{{Cite dragon/72/Gems Galore|19}} They were signs of favor from the god [[Finder Wyvernspur]] and signs of ''disfavor'' from the god [[Hoar]].{{Cite web enhancement/Deity Do's and Don'ts|10-15}}

==Description==
This gemstone was soft and brittle but deposits often produced large crystals which could be facet cut into impressive colorless gems that held a glossy, lustrous shine. A typical specimen had a base value of 100 to 200 gp.

==Powers==
It was a little known fact that this [[Fancy stones|fancy stone]] was one of the "nine secrets" that could be turned into an ''[[Ioun stone]]''. Of the nine gems, Laeral's tears was the rarest.

The upper echelons of the [[witches of Rashemen]] had a secret use for this gem. Through a complicated process of treatment and spellcasting, Laeral's tears could instantaneously heal wounds sustained in battle. The subject had to be wearing the gem for the protection to work, and once it healed its quota of damage, the stone shattered and became useless.
