'''Abyss' Alive and Kicking''' was a type of alcoholic brew found in the city of [[Zelatar]], on the [[Abyss]]al plane of [[Azzagrat]].

==Description==
This intoxicating concoction was a [[wheat]] brew served with active living [[yeast]].

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1370}}, Abyss' Alive and Kicking could be purchased from [[demonic]] breweries and taverns in Zelatar. Unlike many of the drinks favored by the [[fiend]]s who inhabited the realm, Abyss' Alive and Kicking was suitable for [[human]] or consumption.
