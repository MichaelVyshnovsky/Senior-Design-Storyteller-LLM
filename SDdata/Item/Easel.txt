'''Easels''' were items in the Realms often used by [[wizard]]s or scribes who used them as writing tables.

==Description==
An easel was an adjustable wooden structure that was extremely stable.

==History==
These items were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' for 40 [[gold piece]]s.

==Notable Users==
* Easels were favored by wizards and scribes.
