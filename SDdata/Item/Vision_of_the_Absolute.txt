The Vision of the Absolute was an enchanted pole arm that belonged to one of the True Souls of the Cult of the Absolute in the late 15 century DR.
The Vision of the Absolute was a spear with a head covered in dangerous and intimidating barbs.
The spearheads construction and its enchantment caused the weapon to strike at the targets eyes with no error. The more eyes the creature had, the more effective the weapon was. It also had a chance of causing blindness upon a successful hit.
In the , the Vision of the Absolute was used for hunting an owlbear near the Shattered Sanctum in the Western Heartlands. In the hunt, the spear was snapped in two, with its head lodged in the owlbears flesh and haft left with the dying rent True Soul of the Absolute. Even though the Vision was snapped in two, it was easily reparable if two pieces were reunited.
