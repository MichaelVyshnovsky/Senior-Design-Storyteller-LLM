Cheese handwheels were small handfoods of cheese, often served in taverns,
Cheese handwheels were prepared using various, usually strong, cheeses and stuffings. Onion-and-mushroom handwheels were consumed in Cormyr, while in Waterdeep they were served with dubious meat roasts, and twists of saltbreads.
*In Baldurs Gates Blushing Mermaid, cheese handwheels were a popular food among visiting sailors.
*In the Dalelands, a handwheel of cheese and a loaf of hard bread was a common meal. The Drunken Lion offered cheese handwheels accompanied by ale, wine, zzar, and a platter of hard biscuits.
*In the Vasts city of Tantras, the Net of Stars served handwheels, cheap drinks, and roundloaves of stuffed salty bread.
*In Waterdeeps Dock Ward, Alamuths was a popular eatery among sailors who often ordered longwater wine, a handwheel of strong Waterdhavian cheese and chicken-and-onion handpies. The Blue Jack tavern sold platters filled with cold meats, pickles, handwheels, and hot biscuits topped with melted butter and garlic sauce.
