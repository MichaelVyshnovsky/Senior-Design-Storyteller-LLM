'''Long Jing''', also known as '''West Lake Dragon Well''', was a green [[tea]] from [[Kara-Tur]]. 

==Geography==
The tea was grown only in the mythical [[Zhejiang]] province in only a couple of tea gardens. Long Jing was imported and shipped from the far east at great expense.
