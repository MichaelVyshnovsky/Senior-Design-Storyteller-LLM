'''Whip sashes''' were special weapons used by [[Holy slayer]] [[assassin]]s in [[Zakhara]].

== Description ==
Outwardly, these weapons appeared as regular [[silk]] [[sash]]es. Underneath the silk rested a [[leather]] sash that had metal [[wire]] with a steel tip.

== Weaponology ==
These weapon were used in a similar manner to that of a [[Whip|horsewhip]] or a [[wikipedia:en:Garrote|garrote]]. 

==Notable Users==
* Whip sashes were a favored weapon among [[Moon-spinners]].
