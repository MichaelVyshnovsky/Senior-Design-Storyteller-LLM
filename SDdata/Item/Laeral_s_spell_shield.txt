'''Laeral's spell shield''' was a [[Item enchantment|magical]] [[steel]] [[shield]] named after the creator of the first of its type, [[Laeral Silverhand]].

==Powers==
It was a ''+1 large steel shield'' that gave its bearer protection against [[force]]-based spells, and reflected non-magical projectiles such as [[arrow]]s. 

==Creation==
The [[spell]]s used to craft this shield were ''[[Shield (spell)|shield]]'', ''[[Spell resistance]]'' and ''[[wall of force]]''.
