Robes of light were enchanted robes that protected its wearers from darkness.
These soft yellow robes were weaved out of the finest silk.
These aptly named magic pieces of clothing were imbued with a permitting aura of light that illuminated the area of  around them.
Robes of light gained popularity on the Sword Coast, in the cities like Neverwinter and Daggerford in the late 14 century DR. One of these robes was sold by Boonmoppet the imp from the Western Farmlands.
