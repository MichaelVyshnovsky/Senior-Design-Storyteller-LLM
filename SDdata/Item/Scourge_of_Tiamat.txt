A scourge of Tiamat was, much like a scourge of fangs, a scourge with snakes in place of normal cords.
The snakes that made up the scourges cords were all vipers, a size smaller than the wielder a copy was designed for.
A scourge of Tiamat was functionally fairly similarly to a scourge of fangs. One notable difference was that its vipers could spit out corrosive venom from up to , affecting a target with both poison and acid.
These weapons dated back to the height of the sarrukh empire of Okoth. Most copies of these weapons that existed by 1373 DR were ones recovered from that empires scattered ruins.
These weapons were typically favored in Unthalass by the Dark Scaled Ones, high-priests of Tiamat.
