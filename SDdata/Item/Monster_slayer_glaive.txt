A monster slayer glaive was an enchanted weapon used by professional monster hunters to keep the ugly parts of the job at the other end of the polearm.
This glaive possessed a simple +1 enchantment that guided the hands of the wielder towards the target and boosted the weapons damage. Its monster slayer enchantment made the weapons effective against monstrosities, always landing more severe wounds than to any other type of creature.
