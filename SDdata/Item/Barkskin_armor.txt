Barkskin Armor was a suit of enchanted hide armor imbued with protective forest aegis magic.
The hide of this armor was decorated with bark motifs reminiscent of the oak tree.
The forest aegis that gave the armor its protective quality was similar to that of a permanent barkskin spell. It also improved the wearers constitution, allowing them to resist any magical or mundane effect that was aimed at the wearers physique.
