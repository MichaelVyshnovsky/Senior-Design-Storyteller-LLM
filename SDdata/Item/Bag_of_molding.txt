Bags of molding were magic containers with seemingly useless enchantments woven into them.
These bags enchantments caused all food placed inside to grow mold and spoil immediately.
In the , a bag of molding was displayed among splendorous enchanted rewards for those willing to partake in Akabis Wheel of Fortune game in the Circus of the Last Days during its stay in the Rivington neighborhood of Baldurs Gate.
