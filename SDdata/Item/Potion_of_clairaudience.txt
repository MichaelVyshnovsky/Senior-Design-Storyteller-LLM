A '''''potion of clairaudience''''' was a [[potion]] capable of greatly increasing the imbiber's hearing.{{Cite card/AD&D Trading Cards/1991|439}}

==Powers==
When consumed this potion increased the imbiber's hearing as per the ''[[clairaudience]]'' spell. The potion also allowed the imbiber to hear within unknown areas up to {{SI|90|ft}} distant. These effects lasted for twenty minutes.

==Availability==
* The [[Diviner's Guild]] of [[Ravens Bluff]] was known to sell ''potions of clairaudience'' for around 375 [[gp]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite polyhedron/88/The Living City: The Ravens Bluff Diviner's Guild|16}}

==Notable Owners==
[[Ardasir al-Darah]], of the [[Jann of the Haunted Lands]], carried a ''potion of clairaudience''.
