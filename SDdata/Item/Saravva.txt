'''Saravva''' was an [[Amn]]ian [[wine]] made from [[grape]]s of the vineyards near the [[halfling]] city of [[Esmeltaran]]. It was tinted green and possessed a particularly tart taste.

==Availability==
This was one of the few wines available from the [[Harfin Draether]] tavern in [[Athkatla]].
