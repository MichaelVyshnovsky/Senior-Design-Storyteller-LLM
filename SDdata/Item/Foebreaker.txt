Foebreaker was a unique enchanted maul from the city of Baldurs Gate.
Foebreaker possessed a strong +2 enchantment that guided the wielders hands towards the target and made the mauls attacks more powerful and deadly. Its enchantments also made Foebreaker into a truly lethal weapon what dealt normal damage even to creatures and objects that otherwise would be immune or resistant to bludgeons.
In the , Foebreaker was locked safely away deep within magically sealed, trapped, and protected vaults of Ramaziths Tower. The towers owner, Lorroakan, kept the weapon locked inside the "Karsus" vault.
