The Lady Scepter was one of the possessions of the Guardian of Moonshorn Tower, a temple to Mystra.
The scepter could flash, and be worn on the head somehow.
The last person to hurl the scepter had been disintegrated on the spot.
The mage Mardasper Oblyndrin wielded the Lady Scepter during his guardianship of Moonshorn Tower. Hed seen the last wielder be disintegrated for hurling the scepter. It could also flash, sometimes on command, and sometimes as a sign from the goddess Mystra.
