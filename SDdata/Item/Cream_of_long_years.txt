A cream of long years was a magical item that countered aging and preserved undead.
The cream was thick and lumpy. Jars of it normally contained six applications.
When applied to the skin of a living person, cream of long years reduced their age by one year overnight. Multiple applications in the same 24-hour period did not stack. When applied to the skin of a corporeal undead, it stopped them from rotting for three months.
In the , the lich Ezzat had recently developed the recipe for such a cream. He used it to preserve himself.
The cream was derived from the potion of longevity.
