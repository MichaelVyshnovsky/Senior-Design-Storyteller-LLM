Inshulas armor was a unique set of light armor worn by Inshula sar Mashewe, a mercenary hunter from the nation of Tashalar active in the late 14 century DR.
This light armor was custom-tailored to fit Inshulas lanky, muscular form. It was enchanted to improve the wearers defense and was especially effective against beast attacks.
In the , the armor accompanied its owner on her travels from Tashluta to the jungles of Samarach and whilst working with Sword Coast adventurers for the merchant SaSani of Samargol.
