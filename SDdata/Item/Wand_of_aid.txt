Wands of aid were enchanted wands that held multiple charges of healing magics unleashed upon command.
An average wands of aid was  long. They were usually made using bone, ivory, or wood. The total number of charges varied from wand to wand.
Each of these mages accessories held several charges of the aid spell. The magics held within the wand could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of its charge, a wands of aid disintegrated.
*Nym, a drow proprietor of rare and magical goods who traveled Icewind Dale and Dorns Deep in the late 13 and early 14 centuries DR.
