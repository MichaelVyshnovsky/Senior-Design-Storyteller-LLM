'''Baldurian mash''' was a common type of a vegetable dish served in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]], the [[Western Heartlands]], and the [[West Faerûn]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The dish was a {{SI|1|lb|k|long=yes}} assembly of plated mashed vegetables, roasted [[potato]]es, with [[tomato]]es and [[spring onion]]s on top. All of that piping hot food was crowned with a perfectly fried [[egg]].

==History==
As of the late 15{{th}} century DR, platters of Baldurian mash were served in eateries across the [[Chionthar Valley]], west of [[Elturel]] and in the city of Baldur's Gate, as well as the [[Waukeen's Rest]] inn along the [[Risen Road]].
