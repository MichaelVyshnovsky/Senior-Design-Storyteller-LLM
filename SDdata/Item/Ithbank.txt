'''Ithbank''' was a [[wine]], produced in [[Tethyr]]. It was exported as far as the [[Western Heartlands]], the lands along the [[River Chionthar]], and the city of [[Baldur's Gate]], as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Ithbank was known for its layered expressive flavor akin to a [[bard]]'s melody. It was sold in tall [[cork]]ed bottles of green glass.
