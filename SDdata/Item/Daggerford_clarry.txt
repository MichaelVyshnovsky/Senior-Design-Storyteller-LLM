'''Daggerford clarry''' was a blended [[wine]] made out of various [[grape]]s that were grown by the farmers outside of [[Daggerford]], [[Liam's Hold]], and [[Gillian's Hill]].

==History==
In [[1372 DR]], Daggerford clarry was one of the most popular local wines produced in Daggerford. The clarry was a more expensive option and a staple at the [[Black Stone Inn]].
