Despair of Athkatla was a unique fancifully-adorned enchanted wizards staff created and wielded by Lorroakan of Athkatla in the late 15 century DR.
The staff possessed a +2 enchantment that made its physical attacks stronger and guided the wielders arm towards the targets. Additionally, Despair of Athkatla harnessed its creators short temper and flooded the wielders mind with bright shades of burgundy and carmine, focusing their magic into spell resistance and boosting their spell-slinging aim.
Despair of Athkatla was created sometime before the , when it was wielded by the mage Lorroakan, the master of Ramaziths tower and the Sorcerous Sundries shop of Baldurs Gate.
