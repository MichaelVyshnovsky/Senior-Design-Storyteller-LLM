'''''Steel Rain''''' was an enchanted [[longbow]] wielded by Sheikh [[Ardasir al-Darah]] of the [[Jann of the Haunted Lands]].

==Powers==
The longbow helped Ardasir's [[arrow|sheaf arrows]] fly true and caused them to inflict more physical harm. ''Steel Rain'' was truly a lethal weapon When also combined with Ardasir's great strength.
