'''Roast boar fritters''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] game meat dish.

==Description==
The fritters were cooked using [[salt]], [[flour]], diced [[apple]]s and [[date]]s, served together with thinly sliced roasted [[boar]]. This dish was considered a staple of dwarven cuisine and could be found throughout [[Faerûn]]'s dwarven communities, where the ingredients were available.
