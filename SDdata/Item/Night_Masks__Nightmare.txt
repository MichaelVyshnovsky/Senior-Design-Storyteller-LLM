Night Masks Nightmare was a popular and strong alcohol imported from Rashemen to Westgate.
Night Masks Nightmare was a dark and dangerous swirl that was said to give nightmares to anyone who drank it.
By the late 14 century DR, Night Masks Nightmare was a popular drink popular across Westgates inns and bars, including Black Eye Tavern and Bent Mermaid Inn.
