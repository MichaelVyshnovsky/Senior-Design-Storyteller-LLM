Potions of feather fall were magic potions that made the imbiber light as a feather.
Even the vial in which the potion was kept was light and gently glided down back into the hand that tossed it into the air.
One of the recipes of this potion called for a sublimate of autumncrocus plant, combined with a certain alchemical essence.
