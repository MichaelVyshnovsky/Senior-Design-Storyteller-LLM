The '''''fiendish brand''''' was a [[magic]] [[rod]] usable by [[warlock]]s. It could be found in [[Waterdeep]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
A warlock could focus their ''[[eldritch blast]]s'' through the ''fiendish brand'' in order to concentrate them into a powerful [[spear]] of eldritch energy.

==Notable Owners==
[[File:Farideh with fiendish brand WoW.jpg|thumb|''Farideh wielding the ''fiendish brand''.'']]
* [[Farideh]], a [[tiefling]] warlock who worked as an [[adventurer]] on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]], the [[Open Lord]] of Waterdeep, during the late 15{{th}} century DR.
