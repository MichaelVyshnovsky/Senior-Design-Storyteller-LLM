'''Griddle cake''' was a dish known in [[Faerûn]] in the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]], commonly enjoyed as a morningfeast.

==History==
[[Jasper (servant)|Jasper]] made griddle cakes, [[sausage]], and other things as part of a large breakfast for himself, [[Benelaius]], [[Lindavar]], and [[Kendra]] on the morning of [[Eleint 20]], [[1367 DR]], to celebrate solving the murders in [[Ghars]], [[Cormyr]].
