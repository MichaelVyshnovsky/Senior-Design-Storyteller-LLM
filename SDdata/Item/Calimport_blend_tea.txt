'''Calimport Blend Tea''' was an herbal [[tea]] blend popular in [[Calimshan]] with its popularity on the rise along the [[Sword Coast]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Calimport blend was made using the wild [[herb]]s that grew in the country of Calimshan. In the late 15{{th}} century DR, Calimport tea was bottled and imported. This tea could be found served hot as a popular mid-day special at the [[Cutlass (inn)|Cutlass]] inn in Luskan.
