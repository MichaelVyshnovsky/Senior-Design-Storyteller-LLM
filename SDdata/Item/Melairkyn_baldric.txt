'''Melairkyn baldrics''' were a variety of [[item enchantment|enchanted]] [[baldric]]s developed by the [[dwarves]] of [[Clan Melairkyn]].

==Description==
These baldrics were made of [[mithril]] in a [[chain mail]] style and had Clan Melairkyn's symbol as a seal.

==Powers==
These baldrics granted their wearers a ''+2 protective enchantment'', as well as the ability to understand both [[Dwarvish]] and its [[Dethek|written form]].
