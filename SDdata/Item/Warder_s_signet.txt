The '''''Warder's Signet''''' was a unique ''[[ring of protection]]'' that originated from the god [[Ao]] himself.

==Description==
It appeared as an unadorned band made of gold.

==Powers==
The ring held a powerful ''+3 [[Item enchantment|enchantment]]''.

==History==
Legends about the ring said it was created by Ao and gifted to [[Helm]] for his dutiful service during the [[Time of Troubles]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}. Helm then bequeathed the ring to his devoted followers in [[Faerûn]].

==Rumors & Legends==
It was possible to manifest the ''Warder's signet'' by use of a ''[[deck of many things]]'' by drawing the ''Key'' card.
