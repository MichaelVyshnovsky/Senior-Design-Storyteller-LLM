'''Watermelon sorbet''' was a kind of dessert.

==Description==
The dessert consisted of [[watermelon]] mixed with sweetened water.

==Reputation==
Watermelon sorbet was especially popular in [[Neverwinter]] around [[Midsummer]], when it was served with [[squash soup]] and [[caprese]].
