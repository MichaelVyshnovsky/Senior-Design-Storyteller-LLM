'''Pearls of the Moon''' was a variety of [[wine]] made and sold by [[Arlho's Fine Flasks]].

==Description==
This wine was made from specifically green [[grape]]s, which gave it a rather nauseous color. But it was considered to be quite delicious and silky smooth.
