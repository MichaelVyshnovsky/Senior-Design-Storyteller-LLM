'''Tar makeup''' was a substance in the Realms used to darken the skin.

==Description==
Tar makeup was available in small containers.

==History==
A small jar of tar makeup that lasted for 25 applications was available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' for 3 [[gold piece]]s.

==Notable Users==
* Tar makeup was sometimes used by [[thieves]] or [[rogues]] to help them hide in the shadows.
