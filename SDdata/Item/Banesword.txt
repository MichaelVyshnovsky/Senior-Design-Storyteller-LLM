A '''''banesword''''' was a [[Item enchantment|magical]] ''+1 [[lawful (item quality)|lawful]] [[longsword]]'' that were commonly forged for the followers of the deity [[Bane]], commonly found among their possessions.

==Description==
The blades and hilts of the [[sword]]s were decorated with the symbols of the ''Black Lord''.
