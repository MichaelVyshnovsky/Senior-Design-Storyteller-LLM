Lovers Avarice was a potion created by the green hag Auntie Ethel from her underground lair beneath the Putrid Bog.
While the potion smelled sweet like honey with a hint of roses, there was a lingering scent of rot that accompanied it as well.
The imbiber of Lovers Avarice found themselves feeling weak-willed and less insightful for a matter of a few minutes.
As of the , only one bottle of Lovers Avarice could be found in Auntie Ethels Ancient Abode.
