The Needles Rest was an enchanted pair of claw-like weapons, encountered in Chult in the late 15 century DR.
It was believed that Needles Rest were crafted using actual claws of Ormalagos, the green dragon who once plagues Chult. The resulting weapons possessed a powerful +5 enchantment and drastically enhanced the wielding druids powers.
Qawasha a druid of the Emerald Enclave from Chult in the late 15 century DR.
