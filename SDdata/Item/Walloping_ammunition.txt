'''''Walloping ammunitions''''' were ammunitions that knocked people down.

==Description==
''Walloping ammunitions'' were not a specific kind of ammunitions. Any ammunition could be enchanted to become one.

==Powers==
''Walloping ammunitions'' were ammunitions that had so much power behind them that they could push people down who were not strong enough to withstand it.
