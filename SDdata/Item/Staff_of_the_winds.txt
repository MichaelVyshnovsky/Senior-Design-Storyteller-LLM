The staff of the winds was a magical item that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.
These magical items were available in a wide variety.
These staves granted to following powers to a wizard at the expense of one charge alter normal winds, flying jambiya, solid fog, wind blade, and  wind wall.
The following powers consumed two charges for regular wizards and only one charge for wind mages conjure air elemental, cloudkill, wind carpet, and summon wind dragons.
Wind mages gained exclusive access to the windtomb power at the expense of four charges.
Any mage holding the staff gained the advantage of the feather fall spell so long as they maintained contact with the staff.
