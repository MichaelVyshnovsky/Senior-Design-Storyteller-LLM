The '''''Legacy of the Masters''''' was a unique set of ''[[gauntlets of weapon expertise]]'' that originated from the kingdom of [[Threskel]].

==Powers==
The gauntlets allowed the wearer to attack with greater precision and force.

==History==
Throughout their history, the ''Legacy of the Masters'' were owned by many different warriors, many of whom died in particularly gruesome ways.

By the {{YearlinkName|1368}} they had come into the possession of [[Meilum]], the self-declared greatest swordsman of the [[Sword Coast]].

As of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, the gauntlets could be found in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]].
