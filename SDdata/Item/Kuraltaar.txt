Kuraltaar was a magical diamond shield relying on the power of a bound glabrezu to provide great power to its bearer.
Kuraltaar was forged from an alloy of silver with arjale and tantulhor, two rare ores found in the Nine Hells, in its case the fiery layer of Phlegethos. This gave it a silvery appearance with a greenish hue, and caused it to be extremely resistant to physical attacks while retaining a level of flexibility.
This magical shield had the ability to impart its bearer with great magical and elemental resistances akin to those of the fiend bound within, including immunity to poison and electricity.
Whenever the special protections provided by Kuraltaar were used, the image of a powerful and muscular glabrezu would cover the user.
