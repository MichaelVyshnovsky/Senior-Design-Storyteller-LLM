Brazen Bands were enchanted defensive bracers that increased the wielders mundane defense, as opposed to giving them magical protection.
These bands possessed magics that attracted the enemys attacks and canceled their inertia, offering mundane protection as effective as a full set of heavy chainmail. But unlike actual chainmails, the wearer was unrestricted or encountered by the armor.
In the , a set of Brazen Bands belonged to Bashshar al Rachid, a famous collector, merchant, and the wealthiest djinni on Toril, who found himself trapped in a genie prison.
