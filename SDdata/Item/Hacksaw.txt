Hacksaws were a type of saw used to cut through metal.
Hacksaws were made by dwarven craftsmen. The blades were made from hardened steel, with a superior version including diamond-tipped blades. One could even find hacksaws made of adamantine.
* In Athkatla, one could find hacksaws for sale in the market Waukeens Promenade.For Duty & Deity states that every nonmagical item featured in the Arms and Equipment Guide can be purchased at Waukeens Promenade.
* Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold common hacksaws for 5 gold pieces and hacksaws of superior quality for 20 gp.
