'''''Amlath'hana''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[longbow]] wielded by [[Corellon]]'s [[avatar]].

==Powers==
This powerfully enchanted bow would never miss a target up to {{SI|1|mi}} distant. [[Arrow]]s shot from it caused considerably more physical harm compared to other bows.
