'''Cayenne pepper''' was a variety of [[red pepper]] found in some areas of [[Faer√ªn]], [[Zakhara]], and [[Kara-Tur]].

==Description==
Cayenne believed to possess some aphrodisiacal properties{{Cite dragon/130/Better Living Through Alchemy|39}} and had medicinal use as a stimulant. [[Wizard]]s used cayenne pepper as a material component to cast the ''[[Spice (spell)|spice]]'' [[spell]].{{Cite polyhedron/118/Zee Chef|11}}

==Availability==
Dried oriental spices, such as cayenne exported out of [[Shou Lung]], could be found on the [[Sword Coast North]] and in the panties of [[Krypthome]].{{Cite dungeon/52/Welcome to the Krypthome|32}}

In [[the Vast]], cayenne was available in the city of [[Ravens Bluff]]. [[Embrol Sludge's Eatery & Shell Shoppe]] served a specialty [[stew]] generously spiced with red peppers, cayenne peppers, [[laurel (plant)|laurel]], [[garlic]], and [[saffron]].{{Cite polyhedron/46/The Living City: Embrol Sludge's Eatery and Shell Shoppe|21}}

Inhabitants of the [[Weeping Desert]] of the [[Haunted Lands]] in [[Zakhara]] used cayenne pepper to season a spicy [[rice]] dish simmered in [[coconut]] milk.

Cayenne was also an ingredient in preparing [[Kender Stumblenoodles]], a dish from the world of [[Krynn]], brought to [[Toril]] by some unknown means and prepared by the cok named [[Kaga]] from the city of [[Daggerford]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

Cayenne pepper was used by the inhabitants of the [[Malatran Plateau]] of [[Kara-Tur]] and was considered a valuable trade commodity there.
