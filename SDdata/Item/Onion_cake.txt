'''Onion cakes''' were a traditional type of handfood prepared in the [[Heartlands]], the [[Vilhon Reach]], and the [[Sword Coast North]] in celebration of [[Greengrass]].

==Description==
Onion cakes were thick [[cornmeal]] fritters prepared with copious finely diced and fried [[onion]]s. This was a popular food and a staple for the annual Greengrass celebrations.
