'''Pig's Head''' was an [[Amn|Amnian]] delicacy prepared out of a whole pig or hog's head.

==Description==
The traditional Amnian cooking method included the head being baked in quality [[oil]]s and dusted with various [[spices]].

==History==
In [[1492 DR]], the [[goblin]] followers of [[the Absolute]] outside of the [[Emerald Grove]] enjoyed this particular sophisticated dish in their camp.
