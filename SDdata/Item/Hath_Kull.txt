Hath Kull was a cursed longsword that could be found in the catacombs of Temple Darkmoon.
The longsword functioned as a -2 weapon, making it more difficult to hit foes with.
In 1358 DR, the weapon and other enchanted items laid in a room deep beneath the evil temple known as Darkmoon. When removed from its location, a trap was sprung the door slammed closed, and margoyles assailed the victim.
