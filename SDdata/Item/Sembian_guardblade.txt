A '''''Sembian guardblade''''' was a magical [[rapier]] produced in [[Sembia]].

==Powers==
It was a ''+1 [[rapier]]'' with the ''[[defending]]'' special ability.

==Users==
They were often carried by nobles in Sembia, especially those who wielded twin swords, and they were worth around 8000 [[gold piece]]s.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Thazienne Uskevren]], a Sembian noblewoman and a [[rogue]] carried her family's ''guardblade'' in the mid to late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].
