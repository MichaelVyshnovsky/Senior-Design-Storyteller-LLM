'''''Skullbreaker''''', also known as '''''Skullcrusher''''', was a [[mace]] belonging to the [[dwarf|dwarven]] [[cleric]] [[Ambergris (person)|Ambergris]].

==Description==
Skullbreaker was nearly 4 ft (1.2 m) in length, with a core of polished hardwood and a black leather handle.

==History==
Ambergris carried the weapon with her during her time with the [[Bounty Hunters of Cavus Dun]]. After her capture by [[drow]] in [[Port Llast]] in [[1484 DR]], the mace fell into the hands of the [[drider]] [[Yerrininae]]. After the battle and once Drizzt and Entreri had found Ambergris and freed her Drizzt pointed to where Skullcrusher and Ambergris happily ran over and reclaimed her weapon.
