An ear horn or ear trumpet was a horn-like device used to aid hearing by those who were deaf.
As the name suggested, it was a horn held up to the ear.
A dwarf-made ear horn made of brass was valued at 1 gp.
The ear horn of hearing was a magical ear horn that eliminated deafness while used.
Spell keys, which were needed to enable spellcasting on the plane of Bytopia, for the divination school of magic could take the form of ear trumpets or spyglasses, as these represented discerning more information.
* Ghorso, an aged duergar smith working in the Terminus Level of Undermountain used an ear horn.
* Azrok, a hobgoblin warlord of Undermountain, had a dwarven ear horn among the treasures in his war chest.
