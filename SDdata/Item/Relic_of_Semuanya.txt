A relic of Semuanya was a small enchanted trinket used by the Semuanya-worshiping lizardfolk of the Mere of Dead Men in the late 14 century DR.
A relic of Semuanya was a small beast claw carved out of limestone. It radiated a minor magical aura.
The trinket granted its owner a minor immunity to acid damage and improved their chances of resisting catching a disease, or shaking its effects off faster.
In the , lizardfolk who served the duo of black dragons, Voaraghamanthar and Waervaerendor, carried a relic of Semuanya. It could be recovered from the Dragon Caves, the black dragon siblings lair, once a shrine dedicated to Tyr Grimjaws at the heart of the Mere of Dead Men.
