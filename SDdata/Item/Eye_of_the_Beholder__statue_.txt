The '''Eye of the Beholder''' was a large statue located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]] before that city's destruction.

==Location==
The statue sat within Zhentil Keep's [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]] behind the [[Black Altar]].

==Description==
The statue depicted a [[beholder]] sitting atop a spire of black stone. The Eye of the Beholder was carved from a single large piece of stone.

==History==
The statue stood as a symbol honoring Zhentil Keep's alliance with beholders that helped keep the city safe.
