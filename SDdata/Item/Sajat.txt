}}'''''Sajats''''' ({{Pronounce|sah|JAHT}}) were tiny tambourines used by dancers in [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.{{Cite dragon/190/Sounds of Wonder & Delight}}

==Description==
A ''sajat'' was small enough to be held by the fingers.

==History==
Dancers and other performers used ''sajats'' to accentuate their body movement. The ''sajat'' could also be used to set the tempo for a performance.
