The '''''hat of wizardry''''' was a magical hat worn by some [[wizards]].

==Description==
The antiquated, cone-shaped ''hat of wizardry'' was decorated with golden crescent moons and stars.

==Powers==
Wizards were able to use the ''hat of wizardry'' as a [[Spell focus]]. Additionally, the hat allowed wizards to attempt to cast one [[cantrip]] per day that they weren't familiar with and didn't prepare beforehand.
