Pepper cheese was a spicy cheese from Tethyr. It was available for purchase from Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
Pepper cheese consisted of a mixture of goat and cow cheese, mixed with southern spices. The rind was covered with peppercorns, which gave the cheese its unique appearance.
The spicy and tangy flavor of this cheese provided a good pairing with southern dishes.
