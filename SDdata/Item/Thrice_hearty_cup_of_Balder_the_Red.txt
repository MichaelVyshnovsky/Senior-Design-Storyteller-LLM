The thrice hearty cup of Balder the Red was a very rare enchanted tankard that produced beneficial magical effects.
The mugs magics or the effects created by the liquid inside, granted a powerful boon to its user creature. It increased their defense and boosted their effectiveness in battle, almost always guaranteeing a win.
