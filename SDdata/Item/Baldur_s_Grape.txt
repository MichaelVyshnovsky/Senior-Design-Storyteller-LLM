Baldurs Grape was a variety of quality red wine, produced in the city of Baldurs Gate, as of the late 15 century DR.
Baldurs Grape was a quality beverage with a powerful velvety taste with a pleasant tart finish. This wine was good enough to grace the Grand Dukes dinner table. Baldurs Grape was packaged in ornate oblong green glass bottles.
