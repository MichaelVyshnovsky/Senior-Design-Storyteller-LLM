'''''Slasher''''' was a [[magic]]al [[longsword]].

==Description==
Slasher was a serrated longsword with a golden crossguard and brown grip. The sword, which didn't have a pommel, could be found in the lower reaches of [[Xanathar]]'s outer sanctum. It had a ''+4'' enchantment.
