'''Horns''' were a type of wind musical instrument.

==Description==
Several variants of horns existed, such as [[chordal horn]]s, [[fanfare horn]]s, [[hunting horn]]s, and [[Glaur (instrument)|glaur]]s. Horns were typically made of brass, [[bronze]], horn or shell.

The common horn, also known as a valveless trumpet, was traditional to kings' courts.

==Availability==
Trumpets and other horns were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]''.

==Notable Users of Horns==
===Religions===
* It was common for [[cleric]]s of [[Eilistraee]] to be proficient with horns, as well as [[flute]]s and [[harp]]s.
