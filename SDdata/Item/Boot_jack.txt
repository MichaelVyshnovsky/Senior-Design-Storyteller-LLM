'''Boot jacks''' were a type of small everyday metal tool in the Realms used to aid in the removal of [[boots]].

==Availability==
* In the city of [[Waterdeep]], the [[Most Careful Order of Skilled Smiths & Metalforgers]] produced most of the city's boot jacks.

==Notable Owners==
* Some inns in the Realms were known to have boot jacks on access for their patrons, such as [[Rumnthorygh's Welcoming House]] in [[Mosstone]] and the [[Watchful Eye (Essembra)|Watchful Eye]] in [[Essembra]].
