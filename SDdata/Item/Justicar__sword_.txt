Justicar, also referred to as Justiciar, was the powerful enchanted longsword wielded by the god of justice, Tyr.Tyrs sword is not actually named Justicar/Justiciar in Faiths & Avatars, but it is presumed to be the same sword and is included here.
It was gifted to him either by Ao or Tyrs predecessor. Mystra aided in its creation.
After Tyrs death, which occurred while fighting off a demonic invasion of Celestia during the Era of Upheaval, the sword was stuck in a white stone in the heart of Martyrdom, Ilmaters divine realm in Celestia.
Justicar was a +3 sword of sharpness that could enable the wielder to sense lies, know a beings alignment, and see invisible things. or a +5 vorpal longsword.
