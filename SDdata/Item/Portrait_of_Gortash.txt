Portrait of Gortash was a unique painting of the one-time Grand Duke Lord Enver Gortash, created in the late 15 century DR by an unknown painter.
The portrait was an image of Lord Enver Gortash in his younger years. He appeared to stand tall in his fine robes, holding a cane, looking devilishly dashing. The paintings frame was lovingly polished.
