The '''helm of reflection''' was a [[magic item]] that could be found within the [[Hordelands]].

==Description==
This item was available in a variety of helmet types.

==Powers==
Especially useful to warriors, this helm reflected ''any'' magic used against the wearer of the helm back upon the caster.
