'''Trochus''' [[shell]]s, also known as '''great top shells''', were valuable seashells that were used for adornment.{{Cite dragon/72/Gems Galore|22}}

==Usages==
Giant rare specimens were prized for their own sake, while smaller common ones were cut and polished for their [[mother of pearl]]. Those that had a certain "fire luster" provided [[nacre]] instead.
