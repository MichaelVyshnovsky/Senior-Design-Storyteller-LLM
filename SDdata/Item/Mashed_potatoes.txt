'''Mashed potatoes''' was a simple dish of boiled potatoes found in the [[West Faerûn|west]] and [[Northwest Faerûn]].

==Description==
A typical mash was a playful of creamy paste flavored with [[black pepper]] and [[nutmeg]] and sprinkled with chopped chives or [[parsley]],

==Availability==
Mashed potatoes were known to be prepared and served in eateries of [[Baldur's Gate]] and roadside inns in the [[Western Heartlands]], such as [[Waukeen's Rest]].{{Cite adventurers league/The Waydown|9}} The dish's simplicity made it a staple in the messhalls of [[Ravens Deep]], [[Ravens Bluff]]'s [[Underdark]] settlement outpost in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].
