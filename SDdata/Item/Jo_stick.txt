A jo stick was a small stick used in combat, and was often used by monks.
These sticks were approximately  long. They were slightly quicker to use than the longer bo sticks. They were very commonly used in Kara-Tur.
Places in the Realms where one could purchase jo sticks included
* The Weaponers of Cormyr.
* Kane 
* May Tang Lien
* Loy Ho-dun
* Kanes jo stick
