A '''jo stick''' was a small stick used in combat, and was often used by [[monk]]s.

==Description==
These sticks were approximately {{SI|3|ft}} long. They were slightly quicker to use than the longer [[bo stick]]s. They were very commonly used in [[Kara-Tur]].

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where one could purchase jo sticks included:
* The [[Weaponers of Cormyr]].
