Quarrels mace was a unique enchanted bludgeon wielded by an obsessive half-drow warlock named Quarrel in the late 14 century DR.
This mace possessed a minor +1 enchantment that guided the wielders hand toward the target and increased the weapons damage. Additionally, it singed the targets with magical flames. It was believed that Quarrel imbued the mace with some of his sour nature and fiery temper.
Quarrels mace was the warlock weapon of chose. He carried the bludgeon into battle during the warlocks search for the source of Ammon Jerros arcane powers.
