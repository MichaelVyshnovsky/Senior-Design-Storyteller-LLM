'''''Moonfall's steps''''' were a pair of [[enchanted]] [[boots]] worn by [[cleric]]s from [[Waterdeep]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
As the wearer of ''moonfall's steps'' charged into battle, the boots caused spiritual power to lash out randomly at their enemies.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Halbenet Soladonel]], a [[moon elf]] cleric of [[Corellon Larethian]] who performed missions on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
