Moonfalls steps were a pair of enchanted boots worn by clerics from Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.
As the wearer of moonfalls steps charged into battle, the boots caused spiritual power to lash out randomly at their enemies.
*Halbenet Soladonel, a moon elf cleric of Corellon Larethian who performed missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
