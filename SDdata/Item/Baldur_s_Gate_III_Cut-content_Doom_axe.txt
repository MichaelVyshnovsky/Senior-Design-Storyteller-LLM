A '''''doom axe''''' was an [[Item enchantment|enchanted]] [[greataxe]] that instilled terror in any creature it struck.

{{Quote|Bringer of that which it is.}}

==Powers==
Whenever a ''doom axe'' struck a creature, they became filled with incredible sense of dread. During that time, they became unable to to heal or ''[[regenerate]]''. [[Undead]] creatures were especially weakened, and became less capable in battle.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, one of these axes remained in possession of the [[goblin]] trader [[Grat]].
