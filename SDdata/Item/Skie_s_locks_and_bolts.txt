Skies locks and bolts was a magic item that wizard locked everything nearby.
This item looked like a small ornate lock and silver key.
When the key was inserted into the lock and turned all doors, windows, and related objects within  would slam shut and become wizard locked. These items would remained locked until successfully dispelled, the key was twisted again, or Skies locks and bolts was moved further than 50 feet away.
Specialty priests of Waukeen called goldeyes had a chance of receiving a Skies locks and bolts as one of the magic items they were given upon their admission to the clergy.
