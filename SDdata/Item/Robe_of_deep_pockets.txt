The '''''robe of deep pockets''''' was an [[item enchantment|enchanted]] [[robe]] owned by [[Amelior Amanitas]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
This robe had ten small pockets on it.

==Powers==
Each of the pockets on this robe could hold and disguise up to {{SI|10|lbs.|k|long=yes}}.
