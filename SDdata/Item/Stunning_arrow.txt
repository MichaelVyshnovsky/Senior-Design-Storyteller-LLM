'''''Stunning arrows''''' were a type of [[enchanted]] [[bolt]] ammunition for [[crossbow]]s that would stun any creature it struck.

==Powers==
''Stunning arrows'' were enchanted as a part of their creation. Each arrow possessed a ''+1'' enchantment to damage dealt and accuracy. Additionally, these arrows had a coin-toss chance of stunning the target, leaving them briefly vulnerable in the heat of battle.

==History==
''Circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1312}}, ''stunning arrows'' were used by [[orc]]s and [[goblin]]s who joined the [[Legion of the Chimera]] to bring destruction to the [[Ten Towns]] and [[Kuldahar]] of [[Icewind Dale]].
