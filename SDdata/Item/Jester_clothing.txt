'''Jester clothing''' was a costume in the Realms popular among [[bard]]s and, more specifically, jesters.

==Description==
A typical jester's outfit included white gloves, a three-cornered hat with [[bell]]s, a star-shaped collar with bells, a poofy satin shirt, bright stockings, large shoes, and baggy pantaloons.

==History==
These items were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' for 25 [[gold piece]]s for the complete outfit.

==Notable Users==
* Jesters outfits were favored by bards and jesters.
