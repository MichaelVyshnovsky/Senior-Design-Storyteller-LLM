The Helm of Torms Sight was a powerful magical helm that granted the wearer the ability to commune with Torm, the God of Duty.
For some time, it was kept safe within the grand cemetery in the holy city of Elturel.
At some point, Torm himself presented the helm to one of his most loyal paladins, Lannish Fogel of Elturel.
In the , the city of Elturel was brought down to the plane of Avernus in the Nine Hells. During the crisis, Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard of Baldurs Gate became trapped in the city with his retinue of guards. He sought out the Helm of Torms Sight to gain an upper hand in the situation, but they were thwarted by warring devils and demons.
