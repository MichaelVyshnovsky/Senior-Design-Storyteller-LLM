Butterflies in the Stomach was a potion made by Auntie Ethel, the green hag that dwelled in the Gnarled Teahouse on the Western Heartlands as of the late 15 century DR.
It was formulated with ingredients that included ergot and nutmeg.
This potion was meant to recreate the physical sensation many people felt when falling in love. Unfortunately, or perhaps purposefully, it caused internal hemorrhaging within the digestive tract.
As of the , Butterflies in the Stomach could only be found within Auntie Ethels Ancient Abode.
