'''Hamarfae''' was an ancient [[Elven language|Elven]] script, distinct from [[Espruar]], used to write the (defunct by [[1372 DR]]) [[Seldruin]] language of [[Elven High Magic]].

==History==
It was practically forgotten by the late 15th century [[DR]].
