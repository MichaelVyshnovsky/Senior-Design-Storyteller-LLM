'''Merenthe''' was a medicine used to bring sleep while under conditions of extreme pain.

==Powers==
Merenthe allowed its user to sleep, even if they were in pain.

==History==
Merenthe was a part of [[Orthil Voldovan]]'s provisions during his run from [[Scornubel]] to [[Waterdeep]] in the late {{yearlinkName|1357}}. He kept it in a red flask.
