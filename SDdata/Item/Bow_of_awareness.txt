Bow of awareness was an enchanted halfling short bow designed to be wielded by the small folk as a longbow.
A bow of awareness possessed a simple +1 enchantment that improved the weapons aim and damage output. Additionally, it ensured that the wielder was seldomly caught unawares, magically boosting their initiative in battle.
