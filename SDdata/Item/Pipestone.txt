'''Pipestone''', was also known as '''[[wikipedia:en:catlinite|catlinite]]'''.

==Description==
Pipestone was a soft stone, easily carved. It occurred in colors from brown to red. A typical stone was priced at 1 gp per pound (4 sp 5cp per kilogram).

==Powers==
[[Wizard]] spells that required some type of wood as a material component could use pipestone instead. This did not work for [[divine magic|divine spells]].
