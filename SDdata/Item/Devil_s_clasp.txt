A devils clasp was a protective magic item. They could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.
The clasp could reflect a portion of the power of incoming attacks back at the wearers attackers in a fashion similar to a hellish rebuke spell.
* Farideh, a tiefling warlock, was said to possess a devils clasp while adventuring in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.
