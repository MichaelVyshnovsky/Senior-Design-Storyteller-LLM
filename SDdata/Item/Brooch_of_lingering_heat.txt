The brooch of lingering heat was one of the artifacts reserved for the archmage of Menzoberranzan. It was in the possession of Gromph Baenre.
It granted the wearer the ability to fill Narbondel with twenty-four hours of heat, allowing the citys inhabitants to tell time even in the Underdark.
