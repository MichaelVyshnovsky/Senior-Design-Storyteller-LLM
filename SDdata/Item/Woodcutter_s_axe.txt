Woodcutters axes were enchanted tools of lumberjackss trade in the Moonsea region.
These magic woodcutters axes were created to increase the work efficiency of individual lumberjacks as hands-on logging was the most efficient method. The enchantments placed on woodcutters axes guided the weapon towards the "sweet spot" on the targets body. This effectively was a +2 enchantment when wielded in battle. The same magic made these aces especially deadly against various shapechanger foes.
By the , woodcutters axes were used by lumberjacks in the Moonsea region and the city of Voonlar. One such weapon could be found stuck in a tree stump on the outskirts of Voonlar circa the Order of the Crippled Fang crisis and a devastating fiend assault on the city.
