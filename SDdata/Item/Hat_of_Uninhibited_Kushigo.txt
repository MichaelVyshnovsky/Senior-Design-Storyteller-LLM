The Hat of Uninhibited Kushigo was a unique monk hood that once belonged to the famous monk Kushigo sometime before the late 15 century DR.
The wearer of the hood received a boost to their defense against magic attacks after landing a successful unarmed strike on their target. This magical defense lasted for six seconds.
Once, long before the late 15 century DR, the Hat of Uninhibited Kushigo, along with the Garb of the Land and Sky, was worn by Kushigo, a warrior who gave up his heavy shell of armor for freedom of movement after suffering a great loss. In the , the hood could be found in Harper quartermaster Tallis inventory during the mission into the Shadow-cursed Lands under the command of the High Harper Jaheira.
