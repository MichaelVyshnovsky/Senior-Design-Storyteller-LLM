'''Bonecrusher''' was the enchanted weapon of the spirit hunter of the [[White Worm]] tribe of [[Vaasa]].

==Powers==
It was a ''+3 [[maul]]'' of gigantic size.

==History==
In [[1359 DR]], Bonecrusher was wielded by [[Hedweck]], the current spirt hunter.
