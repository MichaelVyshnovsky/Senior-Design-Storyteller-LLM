An everburn blade was a variety  of enchanted flaming greatsword.
When the everburn blade was drawn from its scabbard, flames erupted down the length of its blade.
The wielder of an everburn blade inflicted flaming damage whenever they struck a foe.
* Commander Zhalk, a cambion office in service of Archduchess Zariel.
