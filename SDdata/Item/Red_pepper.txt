Red pepper, also known as chime pepper or whip pepper, was a vegetable found throughout Faer√ªn.
Red peppers were quite common and a versatile mild tasting vegetable. When dried and crushed, it was used as a flavorful spice, often used as a base for many stews across the Realms.
In the Western Heartlands, red peppers could be found across the trade ways, in taverns, and villages of the region.
Amruthars markets in Thay were stocked with the widest variety of peppers of all shapes and sizes, including the mild-flavored red peppers.
In Selgaunt, Sembia, red pepper oil was used for frying.
Chessentas simple on the road meals often included skewers of red peppers, chicken, and mushrooms, roasted on the campfire.
Beyond Toril, neogi cooked druhga, a type of thick stew of meat and red peppers.
