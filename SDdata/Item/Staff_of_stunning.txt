A '''''staff of stunning''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[quarterstaff]] capable of knocking opponents unconscious.

==Description==
A ''staff of stunning'' was usually made from [[yew]].

==Powers==
It addition to making it easier for the wielder to strike and harm their opponents during combat, a ''staff of striking'' also increased the chance that they would be knocked out. Targets remained unconscious for 3–12 minutes.

==History==
These staves were often seen being used by sailors on the [[Alamber Sea]].
