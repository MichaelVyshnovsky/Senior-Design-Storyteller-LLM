Corrosive flails were rare enchanted weapons that exuded corrosive magic.
Corrosive flails possessed a simple +1 enchantment that guided the wielders hand towards the target and boosted its damage.  These flail constantly produced corrosive magic that managed to damage the target even when the weapon missed.
*Derryth Bonecloak, a potion maker from Baldurs Gate who gathered fungi in the Undermountains Ebonlake Grotto for her shop in the late 15 century DR.
