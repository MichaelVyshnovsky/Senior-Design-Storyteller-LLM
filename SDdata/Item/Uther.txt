Uther was a dye found in the city of Qadib in Zakhara.
The city of Qadib was well known for its dyemakers. Uther was a crimson yellow-orange dye used in coloring many fabrics, including felt. The merchants of Qadib exported Uther throughout Zakhara.
Around 1367 DR, many of the citizens throughout the City of Wands were identified by the uther colored clothing, such as their gold-colored robes or yellow fezzes.
