'''Nib''' was the [[Waterdhavian]] term for a [[copper|copper piece]] (cp).

==Description==
The coin was about the size of a thumbnail.

==History==
In the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], a fee of 1 nib per day was charged for the rental of a stall in the [[Market (Waterdeep)|Market]].
