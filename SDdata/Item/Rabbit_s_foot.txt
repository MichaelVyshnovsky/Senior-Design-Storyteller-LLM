A '''''rabbit's foot''''' was an [[magic item]] that provided a small degree of good luck.

==Powers==
This item slightly boosted good luck if worn in a visible location. However, any herbivores who saw the ''rabbit's foot'' would take a disliking to the wearer.

==Notable Users==
[[Alora]], a [[halfling]] from the [[Western Heartlands]], wore a ''rabbit's foot''.
