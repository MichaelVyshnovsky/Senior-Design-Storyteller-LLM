'''''Bitter Divorce''''' was a [[magic]]al [[wand]] created by [[Auntie Ethel]].

==Description==
It was formed from twisted bits of tree root along wits bits of hair and flesh. The name "Connor" was inscribed along the shaft.

==Powers==
The wand could be used to bring back the recently deceased as a form of mindless, [[zombie|zombified undead]].

==History==
This wand was crafted by Ethel in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, with the aim of raising a man named [[Connor Vinderblad]]. Ethel planned to offer up his undead corpse to his wife [[Mayrina]], fulfilling their deal to "bring back" Connor in exchange for their unborn baby.
