The '''''quarterstaff of Alcedor''''' was a unique [[magic]] weapon created by [[Alcedor Kolat]] of [[Waterdeep]] for his own use.

==Description==
The [[quarterstaff]] measured {{SI|7|ft|long=yes}} long and weighed {{SI|4|lb|k|long=yes}}. It was made from a gnarled [[Duskwood (tree)|duskwood]] branch. Both ends of the staff were shod in [[brass]].

==Powers==
The ''quarterstaff of Alcedor'' was imbued with the ''[[Spellstrike (weapon property)|spellstrike]]'' and ''[[shattermantle]]'' [[weapon qualities]].
