'''''Onyx spears''''' were [[enchanted]] and deadly [[spear]]s wielded by the [[Batiri]] of the [[Stone Knives tribe]] in [[Samarach]].

==Description==
There crude [[polearm]]s had spearheads carved of razor-sharp [[onyx]], more deadly than those made of [[steel]].

==Powers==
''Onyx spears'' had an enchanted ''[[keen]]'' edge that could deliver devastating damage with a luckily aimed strike.

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, Batiri [[goblin]]s of the Stone Knives tribe wielded such spears. However, it was unknown if the weapons were of their own making or taken from one of their many enemies.
