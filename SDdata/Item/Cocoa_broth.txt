'''Cocoa broth''', also known as '''hot cocoa''', was a beverage closely associated with [[halfling]]s.

==Description==
Cocoa broth was made using [[kaeth]] and a number of spices from [[Tukan]]. It was described as salty, sweet, and spicy at the same time. 

==Reputation==
It was rumored that cocoa broth was first created when a halfling [[mage]] ''[[wish]]ed'' for "the perfect fireside beverage".
