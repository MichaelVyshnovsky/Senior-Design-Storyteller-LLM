'''Fowl liver''' was a collective name given to livers of birds, such as [[geese]], [[duck]]s, [[chicken]]s, both domestic and wild, used for culinary purposes across the [[Realms]].

==Usages==
*Lords and nobility of [[Cormyr]] started their complex and long-winded meals with savory [[tart]]s of [[spice]]d [[chicken]] livers cooked in [[wine]].{{cite web| url = https://web.archive.org/web/20211028234155/http://candlekeep.com/downloads/So_Saith_Ed_Part25_%282010-2016%29.zip| title = So Saith Ed 2010-2016| accessdate = 25-11-2021| date = 25-11-2021}}
*The wayside caravan stop along the [[Trade Way]] called [[The Way Inn]] served [[braised bustard on buns]], a dish of old hard [[bread]] toasted and smudged with [[gravy]] made of [[onion]]s, chicken livers, and offal.
*Hot pies of many meats, including chicken livers were made for order at the [[Moonstone Mask]] of [[Neverwinter]]. [[Sausage]]s of [[duck]] livers could be sound sold from slab at Neverwintran market stalls.{{Cite dragon/277/The New Adventures of Volo: Dragonwing Stew|93}}
*In the city of [[Calaunt]] of [[the Vast]], a food called [[softmeats]] was sold by those [[thieves]] who wanted to make "good honest coin." This dish was a [[spice]]d pâté of pulverized fowl liver.
*[[Secomber]]'s the [[Singing Sprite]] inn served the dish called [[The Hungry Knight]], consisting of rolled [[flatbread]]s of chicken livers cooked in [[garlic]] and [[butter]] with [[pepper]]s, [[potato]]es and [[dill]]weed.
*In [[Hill's Edge]], [[The Worried Wyrvern]] inn served chicken livers in [[mushroom]] sauce as a side dish for the eveningfeast.
*A grandiose dish – [[stuffed turkey pie]] was a dish found in the [[Watchful Eye]] of [[Essembra]]. It called for finely-chopped [[turkey]] liver as a part of the pie's stuffing.
*The [[Emerald Chicken]] dish from the [[Owlroost Head Inn]] in [[Purskul]] was a spicy treat of chopped onions, greens, [[rice]], and chicken livers. The [[Zultail]] club of [[Crimmor]] in [[Amn]] served [[lamb]] in [[sugar]] [[mustard]] crust on a bed of chicken lovers.{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|31}}

==Notable Consumers==
*Liver or brain pâtés were some of the favorite foods of [[beholder]]s.
