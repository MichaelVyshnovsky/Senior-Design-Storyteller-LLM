Highwater ale was a cheap curiously-flavored ale that was imported from Highcastles the Shield and Keep tavern.
Highwater ale was a clear liquid with a very distinct minty flavor that was considered to be an acquired taste
In the , Highwater ale was rarely found outside Highcastle, even though by the , Highwater ales popularity reached the Sword Coast and the ale could be purchased at the inns of Daggerford, such as Happy Cow and Black Stone Inn, as well as Liams Holds own Holdfast Inn.
If asked, Highcastle locals eagerly shared their belief that Highwater ales peculiar flavor came from the bones of monsters that lived deep inside the towns well that was used to brew this libation.
