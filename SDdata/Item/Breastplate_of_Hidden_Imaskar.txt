A '''''breastplate of Hidden Imaskar''''' was a set of [[enchanted]] armor of [[Imaskari]] design.

==Powers==
This [[mithral]] [[breastplate]] carried a protective enchantment that transcended the known limits of such magic. It also granted immunity to [[poison]] and reduced, though never nullified, damage from [[acid]].

==History==
A ''breastplate of Hidden Imaskar'' was uncovered in the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, in an ancient Imaskari ruins underneath the barrow of the great spirit [[Okku]] in [[Rashemen]], north of the city of [[Mulsantir]].
