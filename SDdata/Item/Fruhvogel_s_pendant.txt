'''''Fruhvogel's pendant''''' was an enchanted [[amulet]] that originated from ancient [[Myth Drannor]].

==Description==
''Fruhvogel's pendant'' consisted of a delicate chain and a luminous bright green gemstone.

==Powers==
This pendant gave its wielder enchanted speed of reaction and initiative in combat.

==History==
In [[1369 DR]], ''Fruhvogel's pendant'' could be found within one of the ancient ruined libraries on the Halls of Light level of [[Myth Drannor]]'s [[Dwarven Dungeons]].
