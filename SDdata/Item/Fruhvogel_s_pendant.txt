Fruhvogels pendant was an enchanted amulet that originated from ancient Myth Drannor.
Fruhvogels pendant consisted of a delicate chain and a luminous bright green gemstone.
This pendant gave its wielder enchanted speed of reaction and initiative in combat.
In 1369 DR, Fruhvogels pendant could be found within one of the ancient ruined libraries on the Halls of Light level of Myth Drannors Dwarven Dungeons.
