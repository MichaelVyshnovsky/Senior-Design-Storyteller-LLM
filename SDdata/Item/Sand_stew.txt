'''Sand stew''' was a type of dish unique to the [[Bedine]] tribes of [[Anauroch]].

==Description==
Sand stew was a slow-cooked broth whose ingredients typically included the leaves of [[palm]] trees, the roots of desert sand [[grass]], desert [[lizard]]s, and [[bat]]s.
