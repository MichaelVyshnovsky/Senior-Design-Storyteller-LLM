Khormalashalal was an enchanted spear that was wielded by the marshal of Evermeets armies, Keryth Blackhelm.
Khormalashalal was a blackened spear with a trefoil-blade. The blades were inscribed with various silver protective runes. The wooden shaft was decorated with depictions of dragons and warriors.
The spear could be wielded when mounted, when it acted like a +3 heavy horse lance, or on foot where it was a +4 weapon. The wielder could throw the spear with accuracy up to  and it would always return to their hands after striking a target. Khormalashalal was especially deadly when wielded against orcs, goblins, drow, and undead.
