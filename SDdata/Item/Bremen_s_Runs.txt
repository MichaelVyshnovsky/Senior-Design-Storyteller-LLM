Bremens Runs was a colorfully named alcoholic drink brewed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale.
The libation was sold as early as the , in the city of Bremen. The drink was also served at The Salty Dog tavern in Targos where it was the cheapest intoxicant available. Sold for a single gold piece at the bar by its portly proprietor Gohar who was happy to share the towns latest rumors with paying customers.
