The '''mini-blade''' was a tiny blade that was easily concealed between the knuckles.

==Uses==
Mini-blades practically gave the cutpurse their name, allowing a brash [[Rogue|thief]] to slice the strings of an unwary victim's coin purse with ease.

==Prices==
Mini-blades could be purchased for 1 [[Silver]].
