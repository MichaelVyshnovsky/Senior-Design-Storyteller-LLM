'''''Rings of knowing''''' were [[enchanted]] [[ring]]s that boosted the wearer knowledge.

==Description==
''Rings of knowing'' were simple bands of precious metal with several impeded [[gemstone]]s.

==Powers==
When worn, a ''ring of knowing'' magically slightly expanded the wearer's mind and imbued it with knowledge of the arcane lore.

==History==
A ''ring of knowing'' belonged to a trio of merchants in the [[Underdark]] under the [[Spine of the World]], [[Myrvek]], [[Wode the Guileful]], and [[Heggr Splitsteel]], in [[Mirtul]] of the {{YearlinkName|1312}}.
