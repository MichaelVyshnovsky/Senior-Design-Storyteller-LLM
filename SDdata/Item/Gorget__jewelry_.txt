'''Gorgets''' were a collar-like form of [[jewelry]], full and broad in the front, that were worn by some upperclass in the Realms. Both their design and name were derived from the [[armor]] of the [[gorget|same name]].

==Availability==
* ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)]]'' sold luxury one-size-fits-all gorgets in the "Jewelry" section of its catalogue for 50 [[gold piece]]s. These gorgets were [[filigree]]d of the finest [[gold]] and inset with [[Kozakura]]n [[pearl]]s. They also had a [[silk]] fastening band, [[linen]] in coloration and of a [[jacquard]] weave, that were sewn by women in [[Sembia]].
