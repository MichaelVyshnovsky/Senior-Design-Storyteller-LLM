'''''Wands of shatter''''' were aptly-named magic [[wand]]s that allowed the wielder to cast ''[[shatter]]'' spell.

==Powers==
''Wands of shatter'' held up to ten charges of the spell and could discharge it at will.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Tarbash Dellkisel]], a [[gnome]] treasure hunter who scavenged the ruins of [[Myth Drannor]] with his counsin [[Robalend Lukka]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.
