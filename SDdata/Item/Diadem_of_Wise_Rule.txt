The '''''Diadem of Wise Rule''''' was an enchanted crown worn by [[Siamorphe]], the Divine Right.

==Description==
A small golden sun hovered near the ''Diadem'' at all times.

==Powers==
The ''Diadem of Wise Rule'' functioned like a ''[[helm of brilliance]]'', but all of the magical abilities issued forth from the hovering sun. The powers of the ''Diadem'' could not be exhausted.
