A quirt was a short form of whip used to motivate stubborn mounts into moving forward.
* Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold quirts that fit comfortably in the hand under the "Wilderness Gear" section of the catalogue.
* The year 110 DR was referred to as the "Year of the Quirt".
