A '''''ring of avian control''''' was a magical item that could be found in [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.{{cite book/Land of Fate/Fortunes and Fates|50}}

==Description==
This magic item was available in a wide variety of ring types.

==Powers==
The wearer of this ring could completely control any bird with lower intelligence. The wearer could control multiple birds at once depending on how large or strong they were. Complete concentration was needed to maintain control of the birds and give them any order.

Bird-like creatures or bird hybrids could not be controlled by these rings.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Legov Nairb]], the operator of the [[Raven Express]], owned a ''ring of avian control''.{{Cite polyhedron/98/The Raven Express: They Deliver Messages - And More|31}}
