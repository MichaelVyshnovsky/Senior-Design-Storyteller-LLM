A '''''moon-touched sword''''' was a magical sword.

==Powers==
When unsheathed in darkness, the sword emitted moonlight, bright enough to light up an area {{SI|15|ft|long=yes}} in radius and dimly for an additional {{SI|15|ft|long=yes}} in all directions.
