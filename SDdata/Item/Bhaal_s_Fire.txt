Bhaals Fire was an enchanted longsword associated with Bhaal, the once-dead god of murder.
Little was known about the lore and history of Bhaals Fire, save for its name and the powers it held.
Bhaals Fire possessed a powerful +3 enchantment. Every time it struck true, the sword seared its victims with flames and on occasion rendered them unable to take action for a few moments.
As of the , Bhaals Fire could be found in the possession of Maiden Ilmadia, a rogue elf that schemed to annihilate an entire city of drow.
