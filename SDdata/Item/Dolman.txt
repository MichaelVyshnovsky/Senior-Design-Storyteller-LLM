A '''dolman''' was a type of clothing worn in [[Zakhara]].

==Description==
These garments were floor-length robes that had sleeves. They were very similar to [[aba]]s but were much more common in urban areas.

==Prices==
Dolman could be purchased for 5–8 [[silver piece]]s.
