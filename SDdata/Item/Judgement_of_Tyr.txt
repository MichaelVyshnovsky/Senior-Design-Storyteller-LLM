The '''''Judgement of Tyr''''' was the unique enchanted [[amulet]] owned by [[Nayeli Goldflower]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The ''Judgement of Tyr'' had engraving of [[Tyr]]'s ''[[holy symbol]]'' on its face and the emblem of [[Daggerford]] on the inside, that Nayeli Goldflower liked being close to her heart, defending her oaths to the Even-Handed God.

==Powers==
The ''Judgement of Tyr'' possessed the ability to envelops its wearer and her allies with the effects similar to that of the ''[[aura of purity]]'' spell, removing all negative effects, both mundane and of magical nature.

==Notable Owners==
*Nayeli Goldflower: a [[paladin]] from the city of Daggerford and an adventurer who worked with other warriors of [[Waterdeep]], performing missions on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
