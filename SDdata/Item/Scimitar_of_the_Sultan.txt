A Scimitar of the Sultan was a magical sword whose powers were boosted when in combat with undead.
While the enchantment on the sword was rather bare, it was boosted threefold when wielded against the undead.
In , three Scimitars of the Sultan were offered as a down payment by Sheik Kassim Arslan to the adventurers he charged with finding and retrieving Princess Shadalah, then missing.
