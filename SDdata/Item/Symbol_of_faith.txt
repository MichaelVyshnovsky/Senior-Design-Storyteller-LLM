Symbols of faith were powerful holy symbol relics used by clerics of the Church of Helm in the 15 century DR.
Symbols of faith served as an enchanted boon that increased the wearers proficiently with healing magic at the same time as boosting their defense. Additionally, these holy symbols increased the wearers attack power, and slightly increased their will and fortitude.
In the late-15 century DR, a symbols of faith could be recovered from a bandits camp run by wizard Desram, hidden in the Sword Mountains, later raided by adventurers in service of Neverwinter. Another such holy symbol was stashed in the ruins of Illefarn among other treasures of the vampire lord named Ozit.
