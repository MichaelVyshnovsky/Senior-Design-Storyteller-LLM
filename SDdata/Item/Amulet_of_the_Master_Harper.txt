The '''''Amulet of the Master Harper''''' was a powerful medallion associated with the [[Harpers]] that was present in [[Faerûn]] as of the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
The amulet granted its wearer a number of potent boons. First, it offered them significant protection from any physical attacks made upon them. Second, it improved their fine motor skills when working with certain objects that required finesse, such as traps that had to be detected and unarmed, or non-[[magic]]al locks and barriers. Lastly, the ''amulet of the Master Harper'' ensured its wearer always had a voice to sing or carry out the [[verbal component]]s of their spells, by granting them complete immunity from spells and effects that ''[[silence|tried to silence them]]''.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, the amulet was in the possession of [[Imix|Fire Lord Imix]] when he took up temporary residence in [[Yaga-Shura's keep]] in the [[Marching Mountains]].

==Reputation==
The ''amulet of the Master Harper'' was was highly sought after by [[bard]]s and [[rogue]]s alike. It was valued at over 3,500 [[gold pieces]].
