Belchorzhs Teeth was an enchanted pair of claw-like weapons, encountered in Chult in the late 15 century DR.
Some believed that Belchorzhs Teeth were, in fact, teeth of a Chultan beholder Belchorzh. These claws possessed a potent +4 enchantment and enhanced a druid wielders powers. Additionally, the wielder was granted the ability to see invisibility.
Qawasha a druid of the Emerald Enclave from Chult in the late 15 century DR.
