'''XYZ balm''' was an [[alchemical]] balm sold by [[hedge wizard]]s.

==Powers==
It was a general purpose ointment used to cure burns, scrapes, and [[poison]]s such as [[poison ivy]]. It wasn't magical and couldn't heal those outright, instead speeding up the body's recovery.

==History==
Despite being nonmagical, it still required water that had passed down a unicorn's horn for its synthesis.{{Cite dragon/163/Hedge Wizards|24}} [[Hedge wizard]]s often traded in it.

==Reputation==
It was popular with thieves.
