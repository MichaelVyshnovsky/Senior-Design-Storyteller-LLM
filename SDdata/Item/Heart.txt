The HeartThe items was called the Heart of... with the players main characters name added to the items description. This made the weapon uniquely named for every play through of Icewind Dale II. was a mysterious enchanted longsword that bore the name of one of the heroes of Targos in Icewind Dale in the early 14 century DR.
The Heart possessed a simple +1 enchantment that also granted the wielder protection from cold.
Nothing of the swords past was known. However, sometime before Mirtul of the , it came to be possessed by the Gallaway Trade Depot. The Heart ended up being available for purchase from Deirdre Gallaway in her trading companys store in Targos during the crisis of the Legion of the Chimera, that brought mercenaries from Luskanto aid defenses of the Ten Towns. The leader of the group that would soon become famed heroes of Icewind Dale discovered the sword while perusing Gallaway Trade Depot. The weapon was several dwarf generations old and of distinct dwarven design and make. The blade bore the adventurers name clearly etched onto it. Despite never encountering the weapon before, they felt a strange familiarity with it. It was not in the best shape, covered in signs of age and battle scars on the metal.
