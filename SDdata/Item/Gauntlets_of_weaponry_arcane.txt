'''''Gauntlets of weaponry arcane'''''  were [[magic]]al leather gloves that allowed their wearer limited [[arcane]] ability.

==Description==
These gauntlets were crafted from [[dragon]]hide and decorated with [[rune]]s of [[silver]] thread.

==Powers==
Once per day, the wearer could cast the [[spell]] [[Magic weapon (spell)|''magic weapon'']], which would last while at least one of the gauntlets was grasping the affected [[weapon]].
