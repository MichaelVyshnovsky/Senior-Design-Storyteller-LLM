A '''tree bed''' was a simple [[gnome|gnomish]] contraption consisting of a canvas sheet and wooden frame.

==Availability==
* ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' sold tree beds for 8 [[gold piece]]s and offered a pitched roof for an additional 2 gold pieces.

==Uses==
Tree beds provided a safe and comfortable place to sleep up off the ground by lashing them to most kinds of [[tree]]s.
