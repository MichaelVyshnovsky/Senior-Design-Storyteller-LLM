Tintorams Select was a blackberry brandy famous throughout the Realms.
This distilled wine was known for its potency and rich flavor. It had a strong aroma of sweetness, and was dark purple&mdashalmost black&mdashin color.
Tintorams Select was bottled in purple glass flasks with long, narrow necks. Its cork was sealed with lead.
Tintorams Select was distilled from blackberries by halflings in the Purple Hills.
A bottle of Tintorams Select was used to poison Zaranda Star, when she was staying at the Ith-Side Inn in Ithmong in Tarsakh of the .
A flask of Tintorams Select was considered a valuable gift.
