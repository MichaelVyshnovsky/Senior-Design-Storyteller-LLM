'''''Marksmanship hat''''' was a type of [[enchanted]] headwear found in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
This [[leather]] hat was decorated with an image of open [[silver]]y wings.

==Powers==
''Marksmanship hat's'' minor enchantment aided the wearer with their aim and guided the unleashed missiles towards their targets.

==History==
One such hat belonged to [[Roah Moonglow]], a merchant and an agent of [[Zhentarim]] who worked with the members of the [[Cult of the Absolute]] in the {{YearlinkName|1492}} and visited the cult's lair in the [[Moonrise Towers]] to trade.
