Marksmanship hat was a type of enchanted headwear found in the Western Heartlands in the late 15 century DR.
This leather hat was decorated with an image of open silvery wings.
Marksmanship hats minor enchantment aided the wearer with their aim and guided the unleashed missiles towards their targets.
