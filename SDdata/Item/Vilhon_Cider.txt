Vilhon Cider  was a cider crafted in Nimpeth. It was also sold through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
The strong fruity flavors of this heady cider made it quite exquisite. Vilhon Cider was best served hot with cinnamon or cloves.
Vildon Cider could be purchased in the following containers
* Hand keg (5 sp)
* Cask (25 sp)
* Barrel (7 gp)
* Butt (20 gp)
* Tun (40 gp)
