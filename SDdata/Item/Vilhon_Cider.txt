'''Vilhon Cider'''  was a cider crafted in [[Nimpeth]]. It was also sold through [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']].

==Description==
The strong fruity flavors of this heady cider made it quite exquisite. Vilhon Cider was best served hot with [[cinnamon]] or [[clove]]s.

==Prices==
Vildon Cider could be purchased in the following containers:
* Hand keg (5 [[sp]])
* Cask (25 sp)
* Barrel (7 [[gp]])
* [[Butt]] (20 gp)
* [[Tun]] (40 gp)
