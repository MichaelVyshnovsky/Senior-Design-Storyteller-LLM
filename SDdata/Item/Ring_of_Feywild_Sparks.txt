A ring of Feywild Sparks was a rare enchanted piece of jewelry that boosted a wild mages magical abilities.
Despite the rings mundane appearance, it connected the wearer with the sporadic bursts of magic of the Feywild. Its Feywild Sparks enchantment allowed a wild magic sorcerer to turn every one of their spells into wild magic as long as their tides of chaos ability was active.
In the , a ring of Feywild Sparks belonged to the malicious green hag of Baldurs Gate named Auntie Ethel. The ring was stashed in her underground lair under the Blushing Maiden Inn in the Lower City.
