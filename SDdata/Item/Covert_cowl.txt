Covert cowls were enchanted masked hood relics worn by the followers of Deep Duerra.
Covert cowls enchantment allowed the wearer to land especially devastating attacks much easier while obscured by shadow or darkness. It also made the wearer more dextrous and boosted their reaction time.
It was believed that covert cowls were once worn by Deep Duerras own rebellious children, using their magic to keep themselves safe from the dangers of the duergar society, until Duerra confronted her children with great rage.
