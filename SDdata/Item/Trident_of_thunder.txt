Tridents of thunder were enchanted tridents wielded by the marel raid leaders in the Moonsea.
Apart from a +3 enchantment, tridents of thunder dealt deafening sonic thunderclap damage upon a successful strike.
Circa 1373 DR, marel King Uhlmor regularly sent squads of raiders to plunder passing ships. One such raid targeted the Grim Voyager, Kettia Freeborns ship that transported the companions of the Hero of Daggerford. In the ensued battle, the marel raiders were thwarted, and their leaders trident of thunder was the heroes reward.
