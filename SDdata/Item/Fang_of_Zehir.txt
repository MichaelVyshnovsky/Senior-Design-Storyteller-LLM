The '''''Fang of Zehir''''' was an enchanted [[sword]] wielded by the [[yuan-ti]] known as the [[Herald of Zehir]]. It contained some of his power.

==Powers==
It was a [[adamantine]] ''+4 [[keen]] [[falchion]]'' empowered by the god [[Zehir]] to inflict more damage and a [[poison]] known as the "blood of Zehir".

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, the sword could be found in the possession of the Herald of Zehir, in the [[Temple of the World Serpent]].
