Vegetable broth was one of the simplest soups available in the realms and often used as a base for other dishes.
Vegetable broth was a richly flavored soup of slowly simmered vegetables over the course of several hours. In the Western Heartlands, it was served with a piece of mushy tofu floating in the bowl.
In the late-14 century DR, vegetable broth was a common food across the Western Heartlands and the city of Baldurs Gate.
In Stargath House inn of Purskul, spicy vegetable broth was served with mussels as one of the establishments staples.
