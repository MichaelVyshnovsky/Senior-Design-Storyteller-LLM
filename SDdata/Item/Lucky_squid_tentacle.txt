'''''Lucky squid tentacles''''' were [[enchanted]] trinkets used by sailors and pirates of the [[Nelanther Isles]] in the [[Sea of Swords]].

==Powers==
When tossed into the fray, ''lucky squid tentacles'' created an area of magical ''[[entangle]]ment''. Each trinket was a single-use enchanted item.

==History==
''Lucky squid tentacles'' were in common use in the late-14{{th}} century [[DR]] in the Nelanther Isle. Notably, Captain [[Allendry]] of the ''[[Midnight Rose]]'' offered these wondrous items for sale, despite [[squid]]s not having a reputation of being particularly lucky.
