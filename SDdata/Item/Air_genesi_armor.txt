'''''Air genesi armor''''' was a type of intricate [[enchanted]] [[plate mail]] created by, and for, [[air genasi]].The word [[genasi]] is continuously misspelled in the game. It is unknown if it is an error or intentional.

==Description==
These sets of armor were decorated with things of swirling designs that covered the entirety of their surface.

==Powers==
''Air genesi armor'' granted the wearer protection against wind and air magic and increased their reflexes. When worn, the plate mail glowed with gentle blue radiance.

==History==
''Air genesi armor'' were crafted for the use of air genasi, who, like the other genasi, were known to make great warriors.
