'''''Yeti skin gloves''''' were types of [[enchanted]] gloves that carried magical properties of a [[yeti]]' pelt they were made of.

==Powers==
These gloves granted the wearers resistance to mundane and magical cold.

==History==
''Circa'' {{YearlinkName|1312}}, [[Oswald Fiddlebender]] crafted various magic items and clothing using pelts of yetis that plagued [[Icewind Dale]]. Such yeti fur items were a great protection against harsh weather of the [[Frozenfar]].
