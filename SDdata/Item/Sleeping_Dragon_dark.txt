'''Sleeping Dragon dark''' was a drink brewed by [[Thrun Samallahan]] at the [[Sleeping Dragon]] inn.

==Description==
The drink was made from the local oats and [[wikipedia:barley|barley]] and had a nutty flavor with a pleasant bite.
