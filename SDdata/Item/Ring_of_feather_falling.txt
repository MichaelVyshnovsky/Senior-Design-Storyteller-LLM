A '''''ring of feather falling''''' was a common magical ring that protected the wearer from injury or death from falling.

==Description==
''Rings of feather falling'' were crafted with an intricate feather pattern around the whole ring.) that the stone had gravitational intervention powers.

''Rings of feather falling'' gave off a faint [[transmutation]] aura. They typically sold for 2,200 [[gp|gold pieces]].

==Abilities==
The ring automatically activated the same effect as the ''[[feather fall]]'' spell, whenever the wearer dropped suddenly more than five feet (150 centimeters),

==Creation==
Such a ring could be made by anyone trained in the art of crafting magic rings who also knew how to cast the spell ''feather fall''.

==Notable Wearers==
===Individuals===
* [[Arrk]], the [[troll]] mercenary from the [[Lake of Steam region]]
===Organizations===
Flying [[hippogriff]] cavalry of the [[Blade Kingdoms]] were equipped with a single-use ''rings of feather falling'', to get the riders to safety when they were knocked off the flying beasts or their mount was killed in battle.
