'''''Faltering Will''''' was a unique [[potion]] concocted and made by the [[green hag]] [[Ethel]] that dwelled within a [[Sunlit Wetlands|swamp]] along the [[River Chionthar]] in the [[Western Heartlands]]. A single jar of the substance remained within her [[Ancient Abode]] deep beneath her [[Gnarled Teahouse]].

==Description==
The potion had a saccharine smell that often evoked feelings of returning to home.

==Powers==
''Faltering Will'' did exactly as its name implied; the potion degraded the personal willpower of its imbiber.

==History==
There was only one known sample of this substance in existence as of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
