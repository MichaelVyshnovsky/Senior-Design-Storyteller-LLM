Faltering Will was a unique potion concocted and made by the green hag Ethel that dwelled within a swamp along the River Chionthar in the Western Heartlands. A single jar of the substance remained within her Ancient Abode deep beneath her Gnarled Teahouse.
The potion had a saccharine smell that often evoked feelings of returning to home.
Faltering Will did exactly as its name implied the potion degraded the personal willpower of its imbiber.
