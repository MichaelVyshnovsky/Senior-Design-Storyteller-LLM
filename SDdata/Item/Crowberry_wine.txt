'''Crowberry wine''' was a type of [[wine]] unique to the region of [[Sossal]].

==History==
In [[1373 DR]], [[Stival Chergoba]] fell asleep while on guard duty and dreamed that he drank large amounts of crowberry wine at a harvest festival in Sossal.
