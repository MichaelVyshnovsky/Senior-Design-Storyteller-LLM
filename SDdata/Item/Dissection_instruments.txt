'''Dissection instruments''' were scientific items in the Realms often used by [[wizard]]s to harvest [[material component]]s from dead organisms or to study their anatomy.

==Description==
A set of dissection instruments included [[knives]], pins, blotters, [[sponge]]s, lenses, ''etc.''

==History==
These items were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' for 10 [[gold piece]]s.

==Notable Users==
* Dissection instruments were favored by wizards.
