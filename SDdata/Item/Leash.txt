'''Leashes''' were tools used to restrain and guide animals, often through public areas without causing alarm.

==Description==
A leash was a length of [[chain]] fitted to some type of [[Collar (animal)|collar]].

==Availability==
* [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']] sold chain leash and collar assemblies fit for use on common [[cat]]s (2 [[sp]]) and [[dog]]s (4-5 sp), [[wolves]] (6 sp), and [[dire wolves]] (8 sp).<ref>

==Notable Users==
* [[Ranger]]s or woodsmen throughout [[Faerûn]], being people that often interacted with animals, sometimes employed the use of leashes.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, five [[bear]]s roused from their annual hibernation by overzealous hunters were led in short leashed [[muzzle]]s through the streets of [[Zhentil Keep]] as the front of a procession of captive beasts, monsters, and sentient beings as part of a [[Cyricist]] religious festival.<ref>
