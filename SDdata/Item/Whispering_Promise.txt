The Whispering Promise was a unique ring that blessed its wearer with a magical boon.
The ring emanated a comforting voice that continuously chanted a prayer while it was worn.
Whenever the wearer of the Whispering Promise healed another being, their inherent defenses and combat prowess were enhanced for short time.
As of the , it was in the possession of the goblin merchant called Grat, after it was taken from imprisoned Volothamp Geddarm.
