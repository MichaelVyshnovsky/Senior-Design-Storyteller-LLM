A '''jitte''' ({{Pronounce|jit|teh|refs=}}) was a weapon unique to the continent of [[Kara-Tur]].

==Description==
The jitte was a defensive weapon and lacked a sharp edge. It was a tapered [[iron]] bar between {{SIrange|1|1.5|ft|c|long=yes}} in length that incorporated a short hook near its handle. The jitte's main purpose revolved around blocking incoming attacks and seizing weapons. Additionally, it could be utilized for delivering impactful strikes. 

==Notable Users==
===Classes===
* [[Monk]]s were known to utilize the jitte.
* [[Shugenja]] were known to utilize the jitte.
