A jitte () was a weapon unique to the continent of Kara-Tur.
The jitte was a defensive weapon and lacked a sharp edge. It was a tapered iron bar between  in length that incorporated a short hook near its handle. The jittes main purpose revolved around blocking incoming attacks and seizing weapons. Additionally, it could be utilized for delivering impactful strikes. 
* Monks were known to utilize the jitte.
* Shugenja were known to utilize the jitte.
