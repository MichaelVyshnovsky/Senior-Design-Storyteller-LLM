'''Sparkers''' were simple, non-magical items in the Realms often used by [[wizard]]s during scientific experiments to induce a fiery spark into a [[beaker]] of vapor.

==Description==
A sparker was a small hand-held device consisting of [[flint]] and [[steel]] set on a [[wire]] loop [[hinge]].

==History==
These items were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' for 5 [[silver piece]]s.

==Notable Users==
* Sparkers were favored by wizards.
