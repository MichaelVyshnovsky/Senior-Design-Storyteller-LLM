'''''Strange conduit rings''''' were pieces of [[enchanted]] jewelry created using the arcane theories of the [[Oerth]]ian [[wizard]] [[Tenser]] sometime before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
Despite his studies being merely speculative, Tenser's writing on arcane displacement stated that the focused minds of those concentrating on active [[spell]]s could unlock their hidden potential. This magic [[ring]] allowed the individual concentrating on maintain a spell to boost their offensive output, dealing additional psychic damage to their targets with each successful weapon strike.
