Strange conduit rings were pieces of enchanted jewelry created using the arcane theories of the Oerthian wizard Tenser sometime before the late 15 century DR.
Despite his studies being merely speculative, Tensers writing on arcane displacement stated that the focused minds of those concentrating on active spells could unlock their hidden potential. This magic ring allowed the individual concentrating on maintain a spell to boost their offensive output, dealing additional psychic damage to their targets with each successful weapon strike.
