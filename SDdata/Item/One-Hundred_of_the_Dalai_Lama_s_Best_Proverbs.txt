'''''One-Hundred of the Dalai Lama's Best Proverbs''''' was a book listing the proverbs of a [[Dalai Lama]] of the [[Padhran religion]] in [[Ra-Khati]].

A copy was located at the Blue Grotto of the [[Rainbow Monastery]] in Ra-Khati around [[1359 DR]].
