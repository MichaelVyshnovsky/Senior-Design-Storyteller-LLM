Mordecais morningstar was an enchanted bludgeon wielded by Mordecai of Westgate, active in the late 14 century DR.
Despite its generic appearance and absence of any markings indicating the morning stars creator, the weapon possessed a +1 enchantment that guided the wielders hand toward targets and dealt additional magical damage upon each strike.
In the , Mordecais morningstar was the favorite weapon of Mordecai, the foul-tempered cheating proprietor of the Track.
