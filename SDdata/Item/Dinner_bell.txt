A dinner bell was a type of very small bell, traditionally used to announce that the meal of dinner was being served.
* Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold both brass and silver dinner bells under its "Household Accoutrements" section, at prices of 1 electrum piece and 1 gold piece respectively. Advertised as being cast from the finest samples of either metal, these bells notably had bone handles that were carved in the shape of a swans head.
* Dinner bell of Nethbarne, an enchanted silver dinner bell that belonged to the thief Nethbarne.
* Strahd von Zarovich kept a permanent unseen servant in Castle Ravenloft that was armed with a crystal dinner bell.
