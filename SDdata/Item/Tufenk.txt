Tufenks were special weapons originating in Zakhara.
These weapons looked like elongated blowpipes.
Tufenks were used to project Greek fire over short distances. The Zakharan term for Greek fire was "oil of liquid star" or simply "liquid star." This weapon required the use of both hands to operate safely.
Tufenks cost 21 gp.
