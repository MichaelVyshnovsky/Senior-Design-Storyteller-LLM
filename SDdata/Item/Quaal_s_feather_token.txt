'''''Quaal's feather tokens''''' ({{Pronounce|kwal|refs={{Cite dragon/93/Ay pronunseeAYshun gyd|28}}}} {{Pronounce|kwayl|refs=

==Description==
The forms of ''Quaal's feather tokens'' were as diverse as their magical effects. The only common feature was that most of them were ornamented with feathers,

[[Elf|Elven]] mages sometimes used the feathers of [[greatswan]]s as the main component to create ''Quaal's feather tokens''.
