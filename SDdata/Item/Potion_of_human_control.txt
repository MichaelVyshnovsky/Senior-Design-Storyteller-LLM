A '''''potion of human control''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[potion]] that controlled [[human]]s.<ref name="DMG2R-p193>

==Powers==
The imbiber of this potion was able to easily control humans, [[humanoid]]s, and [[demihuman]]s as if they were ''[[Charm person|charmed]]''. The effects of the potion lasted for 5â€“30 minutes.
