Shadow-cloaked ring was an enchanted ring imbued with shadow magics.
Despite its gilded appearance,shadow-cloaked ring never gleamed as if constantly in shadow.
The rings shadowthief enchantment increased all damage the wearer did to targets obscured by shade or darkness.
In the , shadow-cloaked ring was found on a powerful shadow mastiff who led a pack within the Shadow-cursed Lands of the Western Heartlands. The same pack that hounded True Soul Korliss leading to the cultists death of hunger.
