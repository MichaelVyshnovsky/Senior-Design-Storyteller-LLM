'''Tarnsar''' was a [[dwarf|dwarven]] [[potato]]-based dish found throughout [[Faerûn]].

==Description==
Tarnsar was a fry of sliced potatoes, [[onion]]s, and thin slices of [[boar]]. The dish was considered to be a staple of dwarven cuisine, prepared where the ingredients were readily available.
