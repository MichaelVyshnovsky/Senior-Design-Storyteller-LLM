Tahllap noodles was a noodle dish from the City of Splendors, Waterdeep, popular in the late 15 century DR.
The dish consisted of boiled noodles, served with fresh vegetables and goats cheese.
In the late 15 century DR, Tahllap noodles were served at the Bright Bell, a busy eatery in Waterdeeps Castle Ward, on the Warriors Way, south of Bazaar Street. The dish was accompanied by a tankard of weak mulled wine.
