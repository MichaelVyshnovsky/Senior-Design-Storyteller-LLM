A '''''Staff of Frost''''' was [[magic]]al [[staff]] that could cast a number of spells related to ice and cold.

== Powers ==
The staff granted upon its wielder protection from [[Cold domain|cold]]-related [[spell]]s and spell-like abilities. It held ten charges&mdash;a number of which would be restored every day at dawn&mdash;and could cast the spells ''[[cone of cold]]'', ''[[fog cloud]]'', ''[[ice storm]]'' and ''[[wall of ice]]''.
