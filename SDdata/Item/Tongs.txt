Tongs were items in the Realms often used by wizards or craftsmen to grasp burning hot objects.
Tongs were elongated pieces of metal connected at one end.
These items were available for purchase through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue for 1 to 3 gold pieces depending on the size.
* Tongs of the armorer, an enchanted pair of tongs that were used by the best craftsmen of ancient Myth Drannor.
* Tongs were favored by wizards.
