The Outlander was the enchanted bow found in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.
The Outlander appeared to be a crude wooden bow, carved with magical sigils that glowed with eerie green radiance.
thumb|Saarvin and his enchanted bow.
When the Outlander was used to shoot, the bows magic could summon additional arrows and send them towards the wielders enemies. These ensorcelled arrows took aim at random targets, never sending more that one arrow per target.
*Saarvin a dragonborn ranger adventurer who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
