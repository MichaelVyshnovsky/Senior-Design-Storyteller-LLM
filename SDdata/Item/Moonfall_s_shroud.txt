Moonfalls shroud was an enchanted hood worn by clerics from Waterdeep, in the late 15 century DR.
These shrouds were of gentle blue color with silver trim. The front of moonfalls shroud was decorated with an image of silver crescent.
Moonfalls shroud purpose was enhancing the wearers healing magic. When a cleric who wore this shroud used healing spells on another person, it also extended the effects of healing onto the wearer.
*Halbenet Soladonel a moon elf cleric of Corellon Larethian who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
