Celestial was the script used to write the Celestial language.
The Xanathar of the late 15 century DR received a receipt for fish-food for his goldfish Sylgar from Skullport that was written in the Celestial script. It read "Bill of sale Five pounds of premium goldfish food delivered to Skullport for Mr Xanathar".
Xenk Yendars daggersword bore an inscription on the blade in Celestial reading "Neither Virtue Nor Blade Shall Break."
