A '''''rod of blind walking''''' was a [[magic item|magical]] [[rod]] that allowed a blind user to perceive the location of nearby objects.

==Powers==
The ''rod'' gave its user a psychic awareness of the location of nearby objects, allowing for maneuverability around obstacles that was often superior to what could be achieved by creatures with functional eyes. This power did not work if the ''rod of blind walking'' was wielded by someone with functional eyesight.

==Notable Owners==
The [[drow]] [[wizard]] [[Dostrealt]] owned a ''rod of blind walking''.
