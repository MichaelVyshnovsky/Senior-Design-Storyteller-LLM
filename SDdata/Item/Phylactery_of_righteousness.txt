Phylacteries of righteousness were magical items that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate. They were only carried or used by holy slayers.
A phylactery of righteousness was actually a collection of sacred writings that were worn on the users arm.
This item provided heightened combat skills against the "Unenlightened" or anyone who had offended the users god.
