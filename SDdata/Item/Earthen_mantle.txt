The '''''earthen mantle''''' was a magical [[cloak]] found in the [[Underdark]] in the mid-1480s [[DR]].{{DDO-time}}
{{Quote
|It smells faintly of wet dirt and freshly cut grass.}}
==Description==
The cloak was quartered with white and red, with a diamond in the center.

==Powers==
The ''earthen mantle'' greatly improved blasts of [[evocation]] and force-based magic, and oddly improved the wearer's balance.

==History==
An ''earthen mantle'' could be found in the treasures of Mistress [[Laerion'vyr]]'s [[Drow outpost (Stonelands)|drow outpost]] in the [[Stonelands]].
