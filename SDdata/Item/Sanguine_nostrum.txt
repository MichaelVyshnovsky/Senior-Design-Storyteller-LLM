Sanguine nostrum was a magical seasoning for an enemys heart.
Sanguine nostrum came in vials with each vial holding one application. The actual content came on powder form.
As mentioned above, sanguine nostrum was a seasoning. It was mixed with blood of a dead person who was dead for a minute at maximum and then to the heart of the person. On eating this heart, the eater received the benefits of a moment of prescience spell. The strength of this spell grew stronger as stronger the person whose heart the user ate was.
