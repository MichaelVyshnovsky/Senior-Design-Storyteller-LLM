Simbulmyn bracers were enchanted arm-guards worn by the Simbulmyn of Aglarond.
The Simbulmyn served as the witch-queen of Aglaronds, the Simbuls personal guard and spies, and as such, they needed powerful defensive items that steeled their minds and bodies. Simbulmyn bracers possessed a +2 defensive enchantment, granted them indomitable will, and magical toughness, and improved their reaction in battle. Additionally, the wearer received resistance to negative energy. Simbulmyn bracers were designed to be used by humans, elves, and half-elves exclusively.
In the , a pair of the Simbulmyn bracers fell into he hands of Mother Feodorra, a charm and magic item peddler from the Rashemi city of Mulsantir.
