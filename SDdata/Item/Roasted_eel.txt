Roasted eel was a simple yet popular dish from the city of Arrabar in the Vilhon Reach.
Eel, just like lamb and beef, was a popular food in Arrabar. Eel was eaten buy poor and rich of the city. A simple roasted dish of eel could be prepared in large open-air fire pits. This snake-like fish was roasted and served along with peppers and turnips.
On Tarsakh 13 of the , roasted eel was served at Emriana Matrells grand sixteenth birthday party, traditional grand event and coming of age celebration. Along with eel, the event was catered with platters of saltfish, cheeses, heavily spiced and sauced meats and vegetables, as well as a plethora of candy and sweetmeats.
