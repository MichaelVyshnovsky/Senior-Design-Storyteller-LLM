'''Halurskan wine''' was a fermented alcoholic beverage created by [[Halurska the Fat]], a baked of the city of [[Berdusk]] long before the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The [[wine]] was a black and nutty-tasting alcoholic beverage made from [[mushroom]]s. It was commonly served by the [[Church of Milil]] during revels at [[Evensong Tower]], a many-spired temple dedicated to [[Milil]].
