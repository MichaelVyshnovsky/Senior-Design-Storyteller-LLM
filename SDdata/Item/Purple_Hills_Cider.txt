'''Purple Hills Cider''' was an alcoholic cider made from a combination of [[apple]]s, [[cherry|cherries]], [[gooseberry|gooseberries]], [[plum]]s, [[quince]]s, and other fruits from the [[Purple Hills]] region.

==Description==
It had the kick of a strong wine with a fruity flavor

==Availability==
Purple Hills Cider was produced by the [[Kraljaom Distillery]] in [[Myratma]]. It was sold there and by [[Aurora's Emporium]].
