Purple Hills Cider was an alcoholic cider made from a combination of apples, cherries, gooseberries, plums, quinces, and other fruits from the Purple Hills region.
It had the kick of a strong wine with a fruity flavor
Purple Hills Cider was produced by the Kraljaom Distillery in Myratma. It was sold there and by Auroras Emporium.
