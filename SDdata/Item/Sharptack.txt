'''''Sharptack''''' was the [[sword]] that was wielded by the [[avatar]] of [[Dugmaren Brightmantle]], the [[dwarf|dwarven]] [[deity]] of invention, scholarship, and discovery.<ref name=frcs-3e-p238">

==Powers==
This [[magic]]al sword had a ''+4'' enchantment. It was able to cast the ''[[feeblemind]]'' [[spell]] two times per day.
