'''Heat mats''' were items in the Realms often used by [[wizard]]s to absorb heat during experiments.

==Description==
Common heat mats were quilted in [[Sembia]] while a stronger version was made from a white crystalline substance found in the [[Orsraun Mountains]].

==History==
These items were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' for 5 [[copper piece]]s for the quilted variety and 5 [[gold piece]]s for the crystalline variety.

==Notable Users==
* Heat mats were favored by wizards.
