Glorious Revolutions were enchanted heavy crossbows that were used by the leaders of the Glorious Revolution in the early 13 century DR.
These crossbows possessed a +2 enchantment that made the weapon deadlier and guided its bolts toward the target. It was especially effective at hitting evil creatures. Its magics also increased the wielders spell resistance.
These ranged weapons were used by the leaders of the Glorious Revolution, a popular uprising against the tyrannical Duke Bogob in the .
