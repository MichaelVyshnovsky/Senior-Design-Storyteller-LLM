'''Eel pie''' was a savory pie made with [[eel]] meat as a filling.{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-03-01 - Street Food in Cormyr and the Wider Realms}}{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-11-25 - Neverwinter Cuisine}}

==Availability==
* [[The Broken Lance]], a cozy tavern in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]], had eel pies as its house specialty.
* In [[Cormyr]], this dish was quite common and popular in the city of [[Marsember]].
* In [[Neverwinter]], some impoverished folk were known to bake eel pie.
* In the coastal areas of [[Turmish]], eel pies were a staple of local cuisine, often served along with [[fishhead soup]], [[chive]]s, and [[asparagus]].
