Avners Abyssal Ale was a type of alcoholic drink found in the city of Zelatar, on the Abyssal plane of Azzagrat.
This intoxicating drink was a heavy dark type of porter beer.
As of the , Avners Abyssal Ale could be purchased from demonic breweries and taverns in Zelatar. Unlike many of the drinks favored by the fiends who inhabited the realm, Avners was suitable for consumption by inhabitants of the Prime Material plane. Further from the Abyss, Avners Abyssal Ale was served at the Wandering Treant inn on the plane of Bytopia.
