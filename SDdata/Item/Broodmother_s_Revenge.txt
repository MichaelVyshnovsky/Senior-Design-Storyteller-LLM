An Broodmothers Revenge was a magical amulet worn by the archdruid Kagha of the Emerald Grove.
Each of the amulets gems resembled the eyes of a viper.
The effects bestowed upon the wearer of the amulet was referred to as venomous revenge. Whenever the wearer was healed, the weapon they suddenly dripped with a form of poison.
