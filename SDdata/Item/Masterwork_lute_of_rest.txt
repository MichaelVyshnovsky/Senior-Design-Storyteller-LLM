A '''''masterwork lute of rest''''' was a rare enchanted [[bard]] [[lute]] from [[Chult]].

==Description==
This masterfully crafted wooden [[Instruments of the Bards|instrument]] had a prominent blue hue to its body.

==Powers==
[[File:Lute of rest.png|thumb|''Another depiction of a lute of rest'']]
The enchantments placed on a ''lute of rest'' accentuated the music created by the item by soothing the souls of all who heard it.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Birdsong]]: A [[tabaxi]] [[bard]] from [[Chult]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
