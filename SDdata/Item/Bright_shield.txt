Bright shields were enchanted heavy shield created to be used by dungeon-delving adventurers some time before the late 14 century DR.
Bright shields possessed a simple yet effective +1 enchantment that increased the wielders protection and illuminated  around the shield with a yellow light.
Bright shields were birthed from the eternal dilemma among adventurers carrying a torch for light or wielding a shield for protection. Over the decades, many magic items were created to satisfy both needs, however, bright shields were among the more practical items. In the , bright shields could be purchased from armor vendors of the Market Triangle in the city of Westgate.
