'''Savander''' was an entree on the menu served at the [[Flying Stag]] inn, in [[Voonlar]] ''circa'' the [[1368 DR|Year of the Banner, 1368 DR]].{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks archive|23}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 17: A Place to Stay}}

==Description==
Savander was a hot and spicy meat pie made from steamed [[horse]] meat and [[quail]], baked.
