'''Midnight tears''' were an ingested [[poison]] that only took effect at the stroke of midnight.

==Acquisition==
The bodies of two [[hellwasp]]s could be mixed with [[tar]] to create a vial of midnight tears.{{Cite adventurers league/Losing Fai|10}}

==Effects==
If not neutralized before midnight, midnight tears caused great damage to the imbiber.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Umbraxakar]], the [[shadow dragon]] of [[Undermountain]], was known to have midnight tears within his hoard.
