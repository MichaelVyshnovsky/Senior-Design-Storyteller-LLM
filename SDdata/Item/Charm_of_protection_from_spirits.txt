'''''Charms of protection from spirits''''' were bijou sheets of paper that held protective magics.

==Description==
''Charms of protection from spirits'' were [[ink]]ed with treats to spirits on red or yellow pieces of paper.

==Powers==
These paper charms created a protective barrier that was almost impregnable by spirits. Only spirits of indomitable will could enter buildings protected by such charms. However, ''charms of protection from spirits'' did not stop spirits from using magics on the areas protected by them. In order to be activated, a ''charm of protection from spirits'' was attached to the lintel of the protected area's door.

===Creation===
The more powerful a ''charms of protection from spirits'' was, the more proficient the crafter of wondrous items had to be. They were created using the  ''[[protection from spirits]]'' and ''[[heighten spell]]'' magics.
