'''''Turbans of multiplication''''' were [[magical item|magical]] [[turban]]s that could be found in [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate. They were only carried or used by [[holy slayer]]s.

==Description==
These turbans were available in a wide variety of colors and cloths.

==Powers==
Once per day, the wearer of this turban could create ''[[mirror image]]''s of himself or herself as if cast by a moderately powerful [[mage]].
