Turbans of multiplication were magical turbans that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate. They were only carried or used by holy slayers.
These turbans were available in a wide variety of colors and cloths.
Once per day, the wearer of this turban could create mirror images of himself or herself as if cast by a moderately powerful mage.
