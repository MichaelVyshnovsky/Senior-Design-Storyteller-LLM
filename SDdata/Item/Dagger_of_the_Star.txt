The '''''Dagger of the Star''''' was a unique [[dagger]] that was rumored to originate from the heavens.

==Powers==
In addition to its ''+4 [[Item enchantment|enchantment]]'' the ''Dagger of the Star'' had a rather unique magical property: every time the weapon struck, there was a chance its wielder would ''[[invisibility|disappear from sight]]'' for an entire minute.

The [[imp]] [[Cespenar]] was aware of the weapon and knew it could be further enchanted, a process that would involve five [[star sapphire]]s being embedded into its hilt.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1369}}, the ''Dagger of the Star'' was interred within a crypt within the fourth level of [[Watcher's Keep]] in [[Tethyr]].

==Reputation==
Due to its unique magical properties, the dagger was favored by a number of [[assassin]]s.
