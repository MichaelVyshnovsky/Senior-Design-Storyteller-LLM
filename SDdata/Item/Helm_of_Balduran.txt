The helm of Balduran was a magical helmet that once belonged to the famous explorer Balduran, before he founded the settlement of Baldurs Gate. It was highly sought after by historians, collectors, and adventurers alike.
Despite its centuries-long history, the helm of Balduran still gleamed as bright the day it was crafted.
The helm offered significant protections that were magical in nature, but the origin of which was largely unknown. It granted the wearer both the vitality and favor of the famous adventurer.
At some point prior to the , Baldurans helm and cloak came into the possession of the elven warrior Vail and his fellow adventurers. Unfortunately, when the group attempted to raid the tower of the wizard Ramazith Flamesinger,Ramazith Flamesinger was a fighter according to Forgotten Realms Adventures and Volos Guide to the Sword Coast. they were petrified. However, prior to their raid, Vail hid the helm behind a trapped painting that hung within the second story of the Helm and Cloak inn.
As of the , the helm of Balduran resided in Wyrmway, beneath Wyrms Rock, safely hidden behind the lifeless body of Ansur, Baldurans once close friend.
