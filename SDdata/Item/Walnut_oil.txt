'''Walnut oil''' was a type of vegetable oil in the Realms,

==Availability==
* [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]] sold walnut oil at a standard price of two [[gold piece]]s per {{SI|1|gal|long=yes}} in its "Aurora's Larder" section, but also at a price of one gold piece per {{SI|1|gal|long=yes}} in its "Priest's Alcove" section under [[material component]]s for [[divine magic]] [[spell]]s.

==Usages==
[[Druid]]s used this oil as a material component for the ''[[reflecting pool]]'' spell.
