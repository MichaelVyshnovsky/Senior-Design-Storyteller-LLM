'''''Arcane dice''''' were [[enchanted]] [[dice]] that could be used by [[wizard]]s in combat. They could be found in [[Waterdeep]] as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
A roll of these [[magic]] dice had the chance empower a wizard's [[spell]]s and other [[magical]] attacks to score lucky hits on their target.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Shevarith Kendia]], a [[rashemi]] wizard who worked as an [[adventurer]] on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]], the [[Open Lord]] of Waterdeep, during the late 15{{th}} century DR.
