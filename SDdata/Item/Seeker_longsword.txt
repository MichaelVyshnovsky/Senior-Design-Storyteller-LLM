'''''Seeker longswords''''' were [[enchanted]] blades popularized in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
These magic [[longsword]]s possessed ''+2'' enchantments that guided the blades towards the enemies vital organs, maximizing their deadliness.

==History==
In the 15{{th}} century DR, ''seeker longswords'' were in wide use, found in the city of [[Neverwinter]], sold at the [[Mercenary Arms Depot]], wielded by inhabitants of the [[Old Owl Well]] and [[the Crags]].
