Seeker longswords were enchanted blades popularized in the 15 century DR.
These magic longswords possessed +2 enchantments that guided the blades towards the enemies vital organs, maximizing their deadliness.
In the 15 century DR, seeker longswords were in wide use, found in the city of Neverwinter, sold at the Mercenary Arms Depot, wielded by inhabitants of the Old Owl Well and the Crags.
