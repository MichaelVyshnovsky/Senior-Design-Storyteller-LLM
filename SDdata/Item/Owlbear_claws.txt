Owlbear claws were enchanted claw-like weapons, encountered in Chult in the late 15 century DR.
Unlike their more mundane counterparts, owlbear claws were designed after claws of the dreaded owlbear. They possessed a +2 enchantment and slightly improved the wielders druidic powers.
Qawasha a druid of the Emerald Enclave from Chult in the late 15 century DR.
