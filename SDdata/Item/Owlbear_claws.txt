'''''Owlbear claws''''' were [[enchanted]] claw-like weapons, encountered in [[Chult]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
Unlike their more mundane counterparts, ''owlbear claws'' were designed after claws of the dreaded [[owlbear]]. They possessed a ''+2'' enchantment and slightly improved the wielder's [[druid]]ic powers.

==Notable Owners==
[[Qawasha]]: a druid of the [[Emerald Enclave]] from [[Chult]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
