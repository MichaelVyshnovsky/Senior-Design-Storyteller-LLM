The '''ocarina''' was a tiny glass flute-like instrument with an oval or round-shaped enclosed body, several finger holes and a mouth piece projecting from the body.

==Geography==
Among other products, ocarinas were produced in [[Galandor's Glassworks]], a small shop in [[Kirinwood]], a village in southern [[Cormyr]].

==Prices==
Ocarinas were sold for 40 to 60 [[gp]].
