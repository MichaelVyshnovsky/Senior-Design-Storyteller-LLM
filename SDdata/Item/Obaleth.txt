'''Obaleth''' was a [[cheese]] with a strong flavor and sharp taste made locally to the [[hamlet]] of [[Sevenecho (hamlet)|Sevenecho]] in [[the Vast]] in the 1360s and 70s [[DR]].{{Cite dungeon/75/Into the Nest of Vipers|66}}

==Locations==
[[The Worried Wyvern]] [[inn]] in Sevenecho had Obaleth cheese for sale on its excellent menu.

==Prices==
It was priced at 1 [[gold piece]] per wheel, rather than the typical 4 [[sp|silver pieces]].
