Springstep boots were enchanted boots that boosted the wearers movement in battle.
These elegant and nimble leather books had a bounce and spring back with each step of the wearer.
Springstep boots possessed a minor enchantment that slightly boosted the wearers movement speed every time they dashed in combat. The boosting effects ended if the wearer was restrained, entangled, or hindered by similar means.
In the , a pair of springstep boots was stored in the treasure room within the ruined Shattered Sanctum in the Chionthar Valley. Presumably, the boots were among treasures collected in the Cult of the Absolutes raids on nearby communities.
