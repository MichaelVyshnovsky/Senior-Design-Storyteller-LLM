Shadowfell gloves were a variety of magical gloves woven from Shadowfell thread.
These were supple black gloves.
Once per day, the wielder of shadowfell gloves could cause a harmful arcane spell they cast to deal that harm through necrotic energy.The text is unclear on what it means by "Shadowfell thread." This could be thread infused with Shadowfell energy or it could be thread originating from the Shadowfell.
Shadowfell gloves were highly prized by warlocks and wizards alike.
