'''''Spider dart''''' was a type of [[dart]] used by the [[drow]].
==Powers==
When a living target was struck with this dart, it released a poison. In addition, upon impact, the dart itself transformed into a [[large spider]]. The spider acted independently and attacked indiscriminately.
