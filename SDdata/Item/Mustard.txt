'''Mustard''' was a type of tangy-tasting condiment.

==Description==
This condiment's ingredients consisted of the ground seeds of the [[mustard (plant)|mustard plant]], [[vinegar]], and [[salt]].

==Availability==
* [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']] sold mustard for five [[silver piece]]s per {{SI|1|oz}}.
* In [[Cormyr]], mustard was a major export of the port-city [[Marsember]] and it was the nation's primary port for shipping the condiment far across the Realms.<ref>

==Notable Users==
===Businesses===
* Many of the higher quality inns found in cities and towns were known to use mustard for [[fire-eggs]].{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-10-06 - Four-course meals of the Realms}}
* The [[Harfin Draether]] tavern's most popular dish, its famed roasted fowl, was served with mustard and a variety of sauces.
* The [[High Helm]] often used mustard as a marinade for their food.<ref>
* The [[Hungry Man]] regularly offered its patrons mustard.<ref>
* The [[House of Good Spirits]] offered small, circular loaves of [[bread|saltbread]] with [[cheese]] and mustard.
* The [[Pampered Traveler]] always had mustard on hand as a condiment option for which to garnish their meat.<ref>
* The [[Silver Taproom]].<ref>
* The [[Sword Tables Dining Lounge]] often offered mustard as a condiment option for its mixed-meat skewers.
* [[The Zultail]], a club in [[Crimmor]], served [[sheep|lamb]] glazed with mustard-sugar.<ref>{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|31}}

===Individuals===
* [[Emmeline Hallowleaf]] recommended using Cormyte mustard for her [[Feast Day Cheese Bake]] recipe in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
* [[Micah|"Mustard" Micah]], a reliable connection for [[Acquisitions Incorporated]] who was an avid consumer of mustard.<ref>

===Races & Sentient Creatures===
* Mustard was commonly used by [[human]]s in the Realms as a simple and cheap form of sauce to marinade meat in.<ref>

===Regions & Settlements===
* In the town of [[Luthbaern]], a popular practice was to cut [[Luthland cheese]] up into cubes and serve them covered in mustard.
* On the [[Malatran Plateau]] in [[Kara-Tur]], mustard seeds were used as a pickling spice, together with [[black peppercorn]]s, [[red chiles]], [[clove]]s, [[allspice]], [[coriander]], [[ginger]], and [[mace (spice)|mace]].{{Cite organized play/LJ/A Gathering of Heroes Interactive}}
