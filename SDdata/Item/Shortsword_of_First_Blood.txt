Shortsword of First Blood was an enchanted shortsword found in the Underdark circa 1492 DR.
Shortsword of First Blood was a golden masterfully-crafted weapon with a superbly keen blade.
This sword held the enchantment known as breaking the unbroken. It made the swords attacks, aimed at creatures previously unscathed, more powerful.
In the , shortsword of First Blood was in possession a dead duergar, part of the raiding squad who massacred deep gnome fluorite miners and attacked a peaceful myconid colony in the vicinity of the Moonrise Towers in the part of Underdark underneath the Western Heartlands.
