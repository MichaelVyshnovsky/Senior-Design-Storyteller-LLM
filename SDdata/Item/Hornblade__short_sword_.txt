The '''''Hornblade''''' was the favored weapon of the [[Yondalla's Children|halfling]] [[goddess]] [[Yondalla]], the Protector and Provider.

==Powers==
After the [[Time of Troubles]] of [[1358 DR]], it was considered a ''[[vorpal]] [[short sword]] +4''. When it struck, it glowed silver.

After the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, it was a ''+5 [[lawful (weapon quality)|lawful]] [[defending]] [[speed]] short sword''.

==Reputation==
Based on the ''Hornblade'', the short sword was the favored weapon of Yondallan [[cleric]]s.
