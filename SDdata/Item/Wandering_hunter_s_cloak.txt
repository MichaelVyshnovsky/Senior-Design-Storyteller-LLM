Wandering hunters cloaks were enchanted cloaks made by the inhabitants of the nomadic Wandering Village in Icewind Dale.
These cloaks were masterfully and painstakingly sewn together, and their outer surface was made out of soft wolfs fur, enchanted to mask the hunters scent and to allow them to hide in the darkness and shadow.
By the , wandering hunters cloaks were crafted for all young hunters of the Wandering Village. Friends and visitors to the village could purchase these cloaks from Tahvo the Huntmaster, along with other supplies.
