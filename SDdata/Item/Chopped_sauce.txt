'''Chopped sauce''', known on [[Earth]] as '''chutney''', was a variety of spicy sauce in the Realms.{{Cite dragon/277/The New Adventures of Volo: Dragonwing Stew|92}}

It was typically made of a mix of fruits, vegetables, and [[spice]]s, such as sour [[apple]] and spiced [[lime]] or [[tomato]], onion, and hot spices.

==Notable Chopped Sauces==
* [[The Last Jack]], an inn in the city of [[Ravens Bluff]], was known for its fiery [[tomato]] and [[onion]] chutney.
