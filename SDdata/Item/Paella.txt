'''Paella''' was a common seafood dish found in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]], the [[Western Heartlands]], and [[west Faerûn]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Paella was typically made with [[clam]]s and [[shrimp]], and was delicately [[spice]]d. The dish had a distinct aroma of the sea.
