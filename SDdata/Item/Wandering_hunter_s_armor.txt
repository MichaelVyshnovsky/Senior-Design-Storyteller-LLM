Wandering hunters armors were a type of hide armor made by the inhabitants of the nomadic Wandering Village in Icewind Dale.
These sets of light armor were masterfully and painstakingly sewn together, and lined with great white wolves fur that inhabited the Fell Wood in the Frozenfar. These armors were enchanted to protect the wearer from winters cold.
By the , wandering hunters armors were crafted for all young hunters of the Wandering Village. Friends and visitors to the village could purchase these boots from Tahvo the Huntmaster, along with other supplies.
