'''''Wandering hunter's armors''''' were a type of [[hide armor]] made by the inhabitants of the nomadic [[Wandering Village]] in [[Icewind Dale]].

==Powers==
These sets of [[light armor]] were masterfully and painstakingly sewn together, and lined with great white [[wolves]]' fur that inhabited the [[Fell Wood]] in the [[Frozenfar]]. These armors were enchanted to protect the wearer from winter's cold.

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1281}}, ''wandering hunter's armors'' were crafted for all young hunters of the Wandering Village. Friends and visitors to the village could purchase these boots from [[Tahvo]] the Huntmaster, along with other supplies.
