A robe of summer was an set of magical robes that offered some level of protection to the wearer.
The robe was woven from especially thin fabric that was pleasantly warm on the skin.
Anyone who wore a robe of summer gained inherent physical resistance to ice, frost, and other forms of extreme cold, whether mundane or magical in nature.
