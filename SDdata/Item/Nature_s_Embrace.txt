The Natures Embrace was an enchanted druidic robe, encountered in Chult in the late 15 century DR.
It was believed that the Natures Embrace was blessed by nature itself, granting the wearer cats grace and armor of a turtle, similar to that of a +4 enchantment.
Qawasha a druid of the Emerald Enclave from Chult in the late 15 century DR.
