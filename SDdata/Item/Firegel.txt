'''Firegel''' was an [[alchemical]] coating that protected objects from fire.

==Description==
Firegel was thick and grainy.

==Powers==
The [[alchemical]] coating could reduce the chances that an object was damaged by flames. It was worthless on creatures.

==History==
The substance was very challenging to synthesize,{{Cite dragon/163/Hedge Wizards|24}} requiring a combination of very advanced alchemical and herbal skills; [[hedge wizard]]s often traded in it. It sold for about 400 [[gold pieces]].
