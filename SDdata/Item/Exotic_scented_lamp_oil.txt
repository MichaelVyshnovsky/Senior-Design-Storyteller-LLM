'''Exotic scented lamp oil''' was a type of [[oil|lamp oil]] from [[Calimshan]].

==Description==
Any oil-burning [[lamp]] could be fueled with scented oils. Typically, [[Calishite lamp]]s were a popular choice, due to their long-burning and aesthetic properties.

Several different scented oils were available for purchase through ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]''. Imported directly from Calimshan, the options included:

* [[Cinnamon]]
* Frankincense
* Lily of the valley
* Myrrh
* [[Patchouli]]
* [[Pine]]
* [[Rose]]
* Sandalwood
* Violet
