Exotic scented lamp oil was a type of lamp oil from Calimshan.
Any oil-burning lamp could be fueled with scented oils. Typically, Calishite lamps were a popular choice, due to their long-burning and aesthetic properties.
Several different scented oils were available for purchase through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue. Imported directly from Calimshan, the options included
* Cinnamon
* Frankincense
* Lily of the valley
* Myrrh
* Patchouli
* Pine
* Rose
* Sandalwood
* Violet
