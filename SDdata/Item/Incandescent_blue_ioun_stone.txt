An incandescent blue ioun stone was a type of spherical ioun stone.
These blue ioun stones increased the wisdom of the wearer.
In 1311 DR, a specific ioun stone of this variety lost to its owner, the illithid called Oinchalkolp. The mind flayer ordered a group of adventurers to fetch the githzerai-crafted stone.
