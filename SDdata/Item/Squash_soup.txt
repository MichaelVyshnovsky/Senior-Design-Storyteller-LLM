'''Squash soup''' was a variety of soup traditionally prepared in the [[Sword Coast]] during [[Midsummer]], especially by [[Church of Sune|Sunites]].

==Description==
This soup largely consisted of a [[chicken]] broth with [[onion]]s and [[squash]].
