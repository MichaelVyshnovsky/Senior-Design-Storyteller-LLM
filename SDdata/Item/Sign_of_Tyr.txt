A sign of Tyr was an enchanted defensive bracer of the Tyrran faith in the late 15 century DR.
A sign of Tyr was a single leather armlet with a golden disc attached to it. The disk was decorated with Tyrs holy symbol, proudly announcing the wearers devotion to the God of Justice.
The enchantments imbued into a sign of Tyr covered the wearer and their allies with an aura of protection that made it harder for foes to land a successful strike on the affected creatures.
*Nayeli Goldflower a paladin from the city of Daggerford and an adventurer who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
