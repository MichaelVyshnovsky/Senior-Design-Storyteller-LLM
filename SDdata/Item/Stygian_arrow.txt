A '''''Stygian arrow''''', or an '''''arrow of darkness''''', was an enchanted type of [[arrow]].
{{Quote
|Black as the [[Styx]], beautiful as a starless night.
|Item description from ''[[Baldur's Gate III]]''
}}
==Description==
Stygian arrows could be shot from standard [[bow]]s. When they hit a surface, they created a zone of ''[[darkness]]''.

==History==
Such arrows were known to be found around the [[Western Heartlands]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

* [[Talli]], a [[Harper]] quartermaster who followed [[Jaheira]] into the [[Shadow-cursed Lands]] in the 15{{th}} century DR.
