'''''Lightning arrows''''' were a type of [[enchanted]] ammunition often used by various [[bow]]men of the Realms.

==Powers==
These unassuming [[arrow]]s exploded into bursts of lightning sparks on impact, dealing electricity damage to their targets.

==History==
In the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]], many enchanted ''lightning arrows'' were a common sight along the [[Sword Coast]], the [[Moonsea]], and used by numerous individuals and factions.
