The '''''harpoon of sudden stillness''''' was a unique [[enchanted]] [[harpoon]] crafted by [[Laeral Silverhand]] for [[Joppa]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was one of many pieces of so-called "[[Silverhand gear]]" crafted for [[adventurer]]s who helped to protect [[Waterdeep]].

==Description==
[[File:Harpoon sudden stillness WoW wallpaper.jpg|thumb|''Joppa preparing to throw the ''harpoon of sudden stillness''.'']]
Silverhand crafted this broad-bladed [[spear]] to react to and recognize Joppa's commanding presence.

==Powers==
The ''harpoon of sudden stillness'' could be hurled at a foe, which it would attempt to impale, paralyze, and then bring to Joppa's side. This left the foe stunned and unable to move.

==Notable Owners==
* Joppa, a [[human]] [[monk]] to whom Silverhand also gifted the ''[[seal of tranquility]]''.
