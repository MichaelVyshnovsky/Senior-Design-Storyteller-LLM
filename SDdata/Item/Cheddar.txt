==Description==
This white-yellow cheese was often formed in the shape of wheels{{Cite dragon/344/A Dark and Stormy Knight}} It could be heated up, and emitted a potent smell. Cheddar was sometimes paired and cooked with [[tomato]]es.

==Reputation==
The great [[sage]], [[Elminster]], often enjoyed eating cheddar, especially when conversing with reputable [[mage]]s, such as [[Mordenkainen]].{{Cite dragon/196/Three Wizards Too Many|83}}
