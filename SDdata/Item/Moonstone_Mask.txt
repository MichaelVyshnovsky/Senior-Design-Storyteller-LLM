A '''''moonstone mask''''' was a [[enchanted]] mask that enhanced the wearer's senses.

==Description==
Weighing {{SI|1|lb|long=yes}}, the mask was fashioned from [[silver]] and studded with numerous small [[moonstone]]s.

==Powers==
This mask provided the wearer with [[darkvision]] and great skill in listening and spotting hidden things.

==Creation==
Creating one required the [[spell]]s ''[[Darkvision (spell)|darkvision]]'' and ''[[clairaudience/clairvoyance]]''.

==Availability==
It was worth around 12,000 [[gold piece]]s.
