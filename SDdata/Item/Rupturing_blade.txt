A rupturing blade was an enchanted rapier that could grow deadlier when the wielder spilled blood.
Rupturing blade possessed a minor +1 enchantment that guided the wielders hand towards the target and boosted its damage. Additionally, when the wielder pricked themselves on the blade and spilled blood, the rapiers point split into heated barbs that rent and burned the targets.
In the , a rupturing blade could be found in the hands of Brem, an agent of the Zhentarim who laired in the Zhent hideout underneath the Waukeens Rest inn in the Western Heartlands.
