Marjoram was a common type of herb seasoning found in the Realms.
* Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold majoram at a price of 5 copper pieces per  in its "Auroras Larder" section.
* Marjoram could be found for sale in the markets of Waterdeep.
* Powdered marjoram was commonly used in the elven dish Vaervren to season vegetables.
* The Painted Boat, a restaurant in the Dragon Reach off the southern edge of the Harbor District of Ravens Bluff, commonly kept majoram stocked in the kitchens drying rack.
