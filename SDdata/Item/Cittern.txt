Citterns were pear-shaped musical instruments, similar to yartings.
Citterns had four sets of paired strings that produced a rich array of tones and were said to "add spice to peasant music" by the Patriarch of Song in Waterdeep.
* Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold citterns among its catalogue of instruments.
* Black Dugals Music Shoppe in the city of Ravens Bluff was known to sell citterns.
* The Cittern of War A variety of enchanted citterns that were approved of by the Church of Tempus.
* Mac-Fuirmidh cittern One of the seven Instruments of the Bards. These were magical citterns that had protecting and healing magic, as well as other magical powers.
* Tangled Strings A magic cittern with music that can clear a confused mind.
