'''''Coroniir''''', or "crowns" in [[Common]], was an [[elf|elven]] board game that resembled a [[crown wars|Crown War]] battle between two [[coronal]]s. The human game of [[lanceboard]] ([[chess (game)|chess]]) was derived from this game.{{Cite digital book/Shadowbane|1734}}

==History==
In [[1368 DR]],The first date in the in-game journal is [[Uktar 12]], [[1368 DR]]. [[Gorion's Ward]] borrowed a coroniir set from [[Mizhena]] in order to challenge [[Tristian]], an avid fan of the game. Having a mild gambling addiction, Tristan was able to be convinced by Gorion's Ward to bet his [[holy symbol]] on the game.

The [[lich]] [[Ezzat]] owned a ''Coroniir'' set, which he sometimes played with his ''[[staff of frost]]''.
