Eyes of Doom were enchanted helmets used by Bane-worshiping Zhentarim agents in the late 14 century DR.
The name of the Eyes of Doom was in reference to two lenses that were part of the items design and were mounted over the wearers eyes. Those to observed a person wearing Eyes of Doom saw two swirling infinite voids through the helmets opening. A prominent holy symbol of Bane was inscribed upon these items.
The wearer was able to cast the doom spell once per day by simply meeting their targets gaze with the helmets void eyes.
Before Banes demise during the Time of Troubles, Eyes of Doom were worn by Bane-worshiping Zhents. After the Zhentarim adopted first Cyric and the Banes son Iyachtu Xvim, the helmets quickly lost favor and were sold, entering the general population. With Banes return in the , the Zhentarim embarked on a mission to reclaim Eyes of Doom.
As of the late 14 center DR, Eyes of Doom were spread as far as the Nelanther Isles off the Sword Coast.
