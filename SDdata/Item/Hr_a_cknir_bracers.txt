The Hracknir bracers were a set of magical bracers of githyanki origin. They channeled energy from the Astral Plane and offered numerous spell-like powers to their wearer.
The bracers were crafted from "planar silver". They allowed the wearer to tap into "latent astral energies" that permeated the Weave.
