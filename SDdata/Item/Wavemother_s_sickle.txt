Wavemothers sickle was an enchanted Umberlant sickle found in the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Wavemothers sickle had a gentle shape that resembled a pitiless crescent moon overlooking a storming sea. The weapon itself resembled an item shaped out of coral or possibly of driftwood. It possessed a +2 enchantment to the damage it dealt and guided the wielders hand towards the target. Each slash with the sickle could leave bleeding lacerations and harm the targets with magical frost. Additionally, Wavemothers sickle possessed a watery guidance that made all attacks with the cycled highly advantageous against wet targets.
As of the , Wavemothers sickle was wielded by Flood Tide Allandra Grey of the Water Queens House in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate.
