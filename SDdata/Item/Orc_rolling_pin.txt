'''''Orc rolling pin''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[rolling pin]] that could also be used as a menacing bludgeon.

==Powers==
This rolling pin, also functioning as a [[club]], possessed a ''+1'' enchantment and upon a successful slice could stun its target for 24 seconds.

==History==
One of these [[orc]]ish cooking tools was wielded by a raging orc matron of the [[Bonegnasher (tribe)|Bonegnasher]] tribe, outside of the [[Old Owl Well]] in [[1374 DR]].
