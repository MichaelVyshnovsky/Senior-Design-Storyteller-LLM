Greataxes of flame were enchanted weapons what contained magics of the Elemental Plane of Fire.
Greataxes of flame possessed a simple +1 enchantment that improved the wielders aim and the axes damage. The weapon discharged a burst of magical flames upon a successful strike, dealing additional fire damage.
