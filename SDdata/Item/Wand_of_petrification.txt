The '''''wand of petrification''''' was a unique [[wand]] owned by the [[half-drow]] [[mage]] [[Narcosa|Madame Narcosa]].{{Cite dungeon/20/The Ship of Night|13}}{{Cite dungeon/22/Letters|2}}<ref group="note" name="details">The ''wand of petrification'' went undescribed in ''The Ship of Night'', only later being detailed in the Letters Column of ''Dungeon'' #22.

==Description==
This wand was made of thin, rusty [[iron]]. Set at its tip was a fossilized [[shark]]'s tooth measuring {{SI|2|ft|long=yes}} in length.

==Powers==
When activated, this wand emitted a dark gray beam out to {{SI|40|ft|long=yes}} that caused those struck by it to become [[petrified]]. The wand was only usable by mages.
