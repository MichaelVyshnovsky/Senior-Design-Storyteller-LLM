'''Potions of specialization''' were a type of [[potion]] that could help with retraining.

==Description==
These potions appeared as bottles of dense, purple liquid, with a pink egg-shaped cloud floating in the middle.

==Powers==
When drunk, this potion enabled the imbiber to respecialize, replacing certain skills or abilities in favor of others.
