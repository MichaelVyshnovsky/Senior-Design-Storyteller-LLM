'''Shocking burst''' was a powerful magical [[weapon quality|quality]] that could be added to a weapon. When a weapon with this ability hit a foe in a vital area, it released a blast of electricity that harmed the creature. A shocking burst weapon also had the [[shock]] ability.

==Creation==
A spellcaster needed a ''[[call lightning]]'' or ''[[lightning bolt]]'' spell to create a weapon with this ability.

==Uses==
Some veteran [[Thayan gladiator]]s had their horns, claws, and other natural weapons enhanced as ''shocking burst'' weapons. 

[[Helmed horror]]s could enhance their blades with magical qualities, including ''shocking burst'' among others.
