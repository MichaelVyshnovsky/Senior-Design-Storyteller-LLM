Wands of Melfs acid arrow were enchanted wands that contained destructive magic that could be unleashed upon command.
Wands of this type had long, strong shafts measuring  in length. They were usually made using bone, ivory, or wood and the wands top part held a sophisticated swirling design. The total number of charges varied from wand to wand.
Each of these items held several charges of Melfs acid arrow spell. The magics held within the wand could be discharged at will using a command word. Upon expenditure of a wands last charge, it disintegrated.
A wand of Melfs acid arrow could be created from a horn of an adult black dragon.
*Antasillix, a fiendish green dragon of Unther in the 1370s DR.
*Horrsin Zespar, one of the first Extaminaar who was tasked with subterfuge in Waterdeep in the late 14 century DR.
*Vesryn Aelorothi, a feyri sorcerer of House Aelorothi of Siluvanede in the early 1370s DR.
