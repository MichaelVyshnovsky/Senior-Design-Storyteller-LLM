'''''The Giving Star''''' was an enchanted [[morning star]] that could only be used by non-good-aligned creatures.

==Description==
This sinister-looking weapon was made out of black and silver metal.

==Powers==
''The Giving Star'' had a ''+3'' enchantment as well as magic that could cast ''[[cure light wounds]]'' on the wielder upon a successful strike. Additionally, the weapon could increase the wielder's strength for five minutes.

==History==
Created by a [[Cormyr]]ian [[necromancer]] named [[Dameth|Dameth the Wan]], the ''Giving Star'' was sold to a [[cleric]] of [[Myrkul]] to aid the evil god's devotee against good-aligned adventurers. The next owner of the morning star was a [[paladin]] of [[Lathander]] who confiscated the weapon from the Myrkulite. The ''Giving Star'' was stored at a mountain temple until it was raided by [[giant]]s in [[1190 DR]], after which the history of the weapon. By [[1281 DR]], the morning star had found its way into the stock of [[Conlan]], the proprietor of [[Kuldahar]]'s blacksmith shop.
