The Reviving Hands was a pair of enchanted gauntlets that boosted the wearers healing magics.
These gilded gauntlets lining had a message stitched into it "Yes all right, all right, stop moaning and get up."
When the wearer of The Reviving Hands cast any type of healing spell, the target also received the effects of blade ward. When the wearer revived a target creature, they also were granted a death ward. The gauntlets also slightly boosted the wearers physical strength and allowed them to cast the revivify spell once a day.
In the , a pair of the Reviving Hands was available for sale at the Stormshore Tabernacle of Baldurs Gate.
