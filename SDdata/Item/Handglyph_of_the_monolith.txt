A handglyph of the monolith was a powerful handglyph used by the Geomancers of Kadar to summon a fearsome earth monolith.
These items summoned a powerful earth monolith which served the wielder for up to nine hours. The summoned earth monolith would be strongest if the substrate of the surrounding area was composed of clay, mud, or earth. A weaker earth monolith would be summoned if the substrate was sand or rough stone. A substrate of worked stone would prevent the monolith from appearing.
