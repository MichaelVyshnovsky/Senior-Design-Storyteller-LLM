Travelers pearls were magic items created by marid and given to mortals.
This item appeared as a large pearl with rosy coloration. It gave of a slight hint of alteration magic.
If given as a gift, the previous owner of the travelers pearl could speak a command word and teleport themselves and a companion to the pearls current location, often resulting in the old owner being transported directly into the new owners treasury. They pearl was destroyed once the previous owner uttered the command word.
