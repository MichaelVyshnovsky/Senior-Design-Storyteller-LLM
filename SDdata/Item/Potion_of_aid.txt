'''''Potions of aid''''' were fairly common [[potion]]s that were often used to heal mental and physical pain.

==Powers==
The effect of the ''potion of aid'' was similar to that of the ''[[aid]]'' spell. The potion granted a health boost and heightened morale to its imbiber.

==History==
''Potion of aid'' came into popularity during the [[Time of Troubles]], commonly created by priests to ease the pain, both physical and mental, and hinder the sense of fear from cowards, making them into heroes. By the second half of the 14{{th}} century, the ''potions of aid'' were a common commodity often sold by arcane, general, and temples' vendors.
