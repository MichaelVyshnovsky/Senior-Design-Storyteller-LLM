'''''Radagan's Red Rum''''' was a variety of expensive magical spirits found in the city of [[Waterdeep]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
''Radagan's Red Rum'' was on the menu at the [[Yawning Portal]] tavern of Waterdeep. One serving of the drink was priced at ten [[gold]] [[coin]]s.
