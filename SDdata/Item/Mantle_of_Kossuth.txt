Mantles of Kossuth were a type of traditional enchanted garb worn by Kossuthan clergy and other powerful worshipers of the Lord of Flames.
These cloaks were brilliant red in color and adorned in designs reminiscent of dancing flames.
Mantles of Kossuth gave the wearer resistance to mundane and magical flames.
