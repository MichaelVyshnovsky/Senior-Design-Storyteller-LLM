'''Guldathen nectar''', sometimes called '''Guldathan nectar''', was an expensive [[elf|elven]] [[wine]] from the [[Forest of Tethir]] popular in [[Waterdeep]] and [[Daggerford]].

==History==
In [[1363 DR]], Guldathen nectar was served at the [[Elfstone Tavern]] of Waterdeep. In [[1372 DR]], this elven wine could be purchased in the [[Happy Cow]] of Daggerford.
