A '''kupuk sled''' was a type of sled used on the [[Great Glacier]].

==Description==
A kupuk sled was constructed similar to a [[dog sled]]. However, it was wider, at about {{SIrange|3|4|ft|long=yes}} wide and shorter, at about {{SIrange|5|7|ft|long=yes}} in length. It was pulled by a single [[kupuk]] and could transport loads of up to {{SI|1000|lb|k|long=yes}}.
