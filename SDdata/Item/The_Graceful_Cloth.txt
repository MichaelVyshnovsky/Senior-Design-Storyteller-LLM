The Graceful Cloth was a type of enchanted monks garb from Port Nyanzaru in Chult.
The Graceful Cloth was enchanted to constantly bestow cats grace onto its wearer, and it possessed the nimble as a cat magic that made the wearers reaction quicker and more nimble and boosted their jumping distance.
Sometime before the late 15 century DR, The Graceful Cloth was worn by Roahton Mobar, one of the monks of the port city of Nyanzaru, who fought against the Headless Serpent, Secret Venom gang. The cloth later found its way into the hands of Lady Esther, an adventurer in service of the Society of Brilliance of Baldurs Gate.
