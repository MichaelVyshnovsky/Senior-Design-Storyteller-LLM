'''''Giantbreaker''''' was a legendary [[crossbow]] that possessed an incredibly impactful shot.

==Powers==
Whenever a bolt fired from ''Giantbreaker'' struck a creature, they would reel back for a matter of moments.

==History==
''Giantbreaker's'' name originated from a story of when an [[elven]] [[ranger]] wielded it to great effect. Using only this weapon, the elf managed to defeat nearly two dozen [[hill giant]]s on their own.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Brem]], a [[Zhentarim]] weapon's merchant who operated on the [[Sword Coast]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
