'''Burnsalve''' was an [[alchemical]] ointment used to treat burns.

==Powers==
Applied to a burn, it minimized the impairments that the wound caused. Stronger versions also allowed burns to heal faster.

==History==
The substance could be made with herbal skills, but the stronger versions were difficult to synthesize,{{Cite dragon/163/Hedge Wizards|24}} requiring a combination of solid alchemical and herbal skills, or exemplary skills in both areas for the strongest version. [[Hedge wizard]]s often traded in it.{{cite book/The Magic Encyclopedia Volume Two|109}}
