Umberlees blessed band was an enchanted Umberlant ring forged sometime before 1372 DR.
This ring was in a sea-side forge and called in the salty waters of Umberlees domain. The metal surface of the band was rippled with a pattern reminiscent waves in a sea.
When the wearer of Umberlees blessed band touched the ring to their forehead and uttered the name of the Bitch Queen, a water elemental was summoned under the wearers command. The effects of the ring were similar the summon water elemental spell and could be activated once per day.
In 1372 DR, Umberlees blessed band was discovered in the stomach of a dead fish, washed ashore the Crabs Claw Coast region of the Sword Coast. How the band ended up inside a fish remained a mystery.
