The Lay of Larethian was a painting portraying the deity Corellon Larethian created sometime before the late 15 century DR.
The painting depicted the elvish god Corellon not as a beautiful blonde male, but as an elven lass with dark hair, lounging naked on a maroon muslin.
In the , The Lay of Larethian painting could be found hanging in the Szarr Palace in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate.
