'''''Chieftain Halberd''''' was a powerful and [[magic]]al [[halberd]].

==Description==
Chieftain Halberd was an enchanted polearm kept at a [[kenku]] settlement in [[Undermountain]]. The halberd could be acquired by relocating all the kenku eggs to their nest. It had a ''+5'' enchantment.
