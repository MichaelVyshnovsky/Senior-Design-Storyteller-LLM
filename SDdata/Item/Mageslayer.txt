Mageslayer was an enchanted mace.
The mace was visibly brimming with power.
In late , Mageslayers powers had been explained to Oprion Blackstone by Fzoul Chembryl. The first considered using it to assault a clone of Manshoon when the latter revealed himself to exist after his presumed death, but later decided against it.
