The Bag of Nails was the enchanted quiver that belonged to its namesake, a tabaxi hunter Bag of Nails in late 15 century DR.
The Bag of Nails enchantment increased its owners speed drastically, being invaluable to rangers and other users of bows.
This magic items original name was unrevealed, but it belonged to an aging hunter of the city of Omu, Bag of Nails. The only thing the tabaxi wanted in his old age was the hunters death however, Bag went mad in his advanced years. Eventually, the enchanted quiver found its way into the hands of the immortal bara of Mezro from Cormyr, Artus Cimber. He named this piece of enchanted gear after its previous owner.
