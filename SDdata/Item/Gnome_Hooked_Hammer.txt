'''Gnome hooked hammers''' were a type of two-handed martial [[hammer]], specially designed by [[gnome]]s for use by members of their race.

==Description==
Gnome hooked hammer had relatively short shafts, with a hammer on one end and a sharp-pointed hook (curved only slightly more than a [[pickaxe]]) on the other. On average they weighed around {{SI|6|lb|k}}.

==Weaponology==
Due to their size, these hammers were well suitable for fights in cramped spaces.<ref> The hooked end of these hammers could be utilized to trip opponents.

==Notable Users==
* [[Nadul DaRoni]], a renowned gnomish inventor, was skilled in fighting with gnome hooked hammers.<ref>{{Cite web/Realms Personalities/Nadul DaRoni}}
