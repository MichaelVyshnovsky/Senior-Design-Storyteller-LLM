'''''Staves of corrosion''''' were [[enchanted]] [[staves]] that exuded [[acid]].

==Description==
''Staves of corrosion'' were simple-looking wooden [[quarterstaves]] from {{SI|6'|'"|long=yes}} to {{SI|9'|'"|long=yes}} long. They were sometimes carved out of stout [[oak]] and impeded with [[jade]] stones.

==Powers==
In battle, ''staves of corrosion'' exuded [[acid]] when hitting a target. Some stronger versions of the item existed, bearing potent damage and aiming enchantments.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1312}}, ''staves of corrosion'' could be found in the [[Icewind Dale]] region of [[northwest Faerûn]].
