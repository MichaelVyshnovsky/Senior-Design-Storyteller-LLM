'''''Footman's Folly''''' was a unique [[enchanted]] [[shield]] wielded by distinguished warriors of [[Neverwinter]].

==Description==
''Footman's Folly'' was a small, smoothly polished shield made of metal.

==Powers==
''Footman's Folly'' possessed a powerful ''+2'' enchantment to the wielder's defense, additionally, it increased the wielder's defense further against [[arrow]]s, [[bolt]]s, and other missiles.

==History==
''Footman's Folly'' was considered to be a badge of honor among the most distinguished soldiers in service of Neverwinter, the Jewel of the North. Over the years, the shield gained notoriety, rumored to the unlucky. Many of those who used ''Footman's Folly'' fell in battle, believing that its minor magical defenses would be enough to help them in the face of overwhelming odds. According to the legends, this was the origin of the item's name.

In the {{YearlinkName|1312}}, ''Footman's Folly'' found its way into the blue hands of [[Xuki]], a [[wizard]] member of the [[Legion of the Chimera]] that menaced [[Icewind Dale]]. With her death, the shield changed owners once more.
