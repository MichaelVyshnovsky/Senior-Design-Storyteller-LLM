'''''Rings of improved evasion''''' were [[enchanted]] [[ring]]s created by the [[Red Wizards]] of [[Thay]] to reduce mortality among apprentice [[battle mage]]s.

==Powers==
When worn, these magic rings granted the wearer extreme nimbleness, making it significantly easier for them to avoid harm.
