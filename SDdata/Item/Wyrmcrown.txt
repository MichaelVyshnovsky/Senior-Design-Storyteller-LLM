The '''Wyrmcrown''' was a powerful [[dragon]] [[artifact]].

==Abilities==
The Wyrmcrown allowed its bearer to magically poison any dragon who was blood-related to it, or a dragon whose blood the bearer had tasted. The Wyrmcrown could affect any dragon within the continent of [[Laerakond]].

==History==
As of [[1479 DR]], the Wyrmcrown was in the possession of the Empress Dragon [[Gauwervyndhal]].
