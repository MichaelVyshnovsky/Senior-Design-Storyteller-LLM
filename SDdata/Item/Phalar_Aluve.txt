Phalar Aluve was an enchanted longsword associated with the faith of Eilistraee.
Phalar Aluve was inscribed with a pattern that glowed gold when the sword was drawn. Part of the inscription featured a single line written in the drow language.
It possessed a +1 enchantment and magically enhanced the owners artistic performances.
As of the , the sword remained embedded in a stone outcropping in the Underdark, near Ebonlake Grotto.
