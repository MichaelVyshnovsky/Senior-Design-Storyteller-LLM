A '''''ceremonial kris''''' was an enchanted [[Rashemi]] [[dagger]], found in [[Waterdeep]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The hilt of a ''ceremonial kris'' was made out of black metal and encrusted with a big purple [[gem]]stone in its center. The blade was a typical snaking shape of a kris, and it was made out of a bright green material.

==Powers==
[[File:Shevarith Kriss.jpg|thumb|''[[Shevarith Kendia]], harnessing the ceremonial kris's magic.'']]
This green blade allowed the wielder to cast ''[[poison spray]]'' upon a successful strike.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Shevarith Kendia]]: a [[Rashemi]] [[wizard]] who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
