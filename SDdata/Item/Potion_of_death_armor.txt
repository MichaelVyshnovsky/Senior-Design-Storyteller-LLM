'''Potions of death armor''' were magical [[Potion|potions]] that granted the imbiber the effects similar to those of the ''[[death armor]]'' [[spell]].

==Powers==
When the potion was consumed, it created an aura of magic around the imbiber. The aura caused damage in retaliation to any creature that landed a strike on the imbiber.
