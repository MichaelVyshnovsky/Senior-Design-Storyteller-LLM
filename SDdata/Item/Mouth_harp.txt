The '''mouth harp''', also referred to as the '''Jew's harp''' on [[Earth]], was a type of pocket-sized instrument found in the Realms.{{Cite web/theedverse/2021-07-06 - Small Pocket Instruments}}

==Description==
This instrument was characterized by a two-pronged, bottle-shaped frame with a metal tongue that was affixed at one end of its base. This tongue would be held between the teeth of a musician and allowed them to alter the sound produced by their mouth cavity.<ref>

==Notable Owners==
* [[Renee Handly]] played a variety of different instruments as a member of the [[bard]]ic musical band [[Cobalt Flame]].<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/Following Cobalt Flame|4}} These included the mouth harp, [[flute]], [[pan pipe]]s, and [[trumpet]].<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/Following Cobalt Flame|13}}

==Availability==
Mouth harps were used on the [[Malatran Plateau]] of [[Kara-Tur]] where they were considered a valuable trade commodity.{{Cite organized play/LJ/A Gathering of Heroes Interactive}}
