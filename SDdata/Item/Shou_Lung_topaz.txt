'''Shou Lung Topaz''' was found in [[Shou Lung]] and the other mysterious nations to the east of [[Faerûn]]. It was imported to western [[Faerûn]] by travelers from those lands.

==Description==
This bold yellow stone was a form of [[wikipedia:en:corundum|corundum]] making it harder than regular [[topaz]] and able to make fine gems.

==Powers==
This [[gem stones|gem stone]] recalled the fiery yellow of some cats' eyes and was used in the construction of magic items associated with felines. Rumor gave it many magical properties but what they might have been has not yet been documented.
