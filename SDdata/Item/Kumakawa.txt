Kumakawa was a type of enchanted light hide armor crafted out of a displacer beasts hide.
Kumakawa armor possessed a +3 enchantment to the wearers defense. Additionally, the items magics imbued the wearer with reflexes of a predator, increasing their evasion and dexterity.
These sets of potent enchanted armors were named after their inventor, a famed artificer Kumakawa, renowned for working with the most exotic skins and hides.
*Ammon Jerro the warlock enemy of the King of Shadows and a reluctant ally of Kalach-Cha in 1374 DR.
*Tusk an orc leader of elf-massacring Gutripper tribe from Thar and the brother of Grunt "Elf" Steelstorm, ally of the Hero of Daggerford in 1373 DR.
