Broken Promises was a potion created by Auntie Ethel from within her laboratory beneath the gnarled teahouse of the Putrid Bog.
The potion bottle contained a fat, juicy parasite sitting at the bottom of its liquid contents.
This potion gave an individual a great boost of strength for a few minutes time, after which they began to feel normal again. Unfortunately their strength was sapped from them after their next nights rest, an affliction that lasted indefinitely.
