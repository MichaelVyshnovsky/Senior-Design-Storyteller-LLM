Thamalons Own was a pear wine created by Thamalon Uskevren, of the noble Uskevren family of Selgaunt, Sembia, in the 14 century DR.
Thamalons Own was the color of straw.
By the mid-14 century DR, Thamalons love for winemaking paid off. He created Storm Ruby, Usk Fine Old, and Thamalons Own wines.
