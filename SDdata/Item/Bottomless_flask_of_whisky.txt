The bottomless flask of whiskyThe flask is never given an actual name the name of this article is chosen only out of expedience. was an enchanted bronze flask that produced whisky. The elven brigand Kiril Duskmourn had one.
The flask was forged of bronze, with a greenish blue patina of verdigris covering a deranged face. Kiril Duskmourn believed it may have been forged by elves outside of her lineage she knew the face to be that of a god of the vine, though she didnt remember their name
The flask produced whiskey whenever prompted. To Kiril Duskmourns recollection, it had never failed to provide alcohol.
As of , Kiril had carried the flask for years.
