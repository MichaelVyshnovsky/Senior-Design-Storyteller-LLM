The '''''whip of frost, fire, and fear''''' was a type rechargeable, [[item enchantment|enchanted]] [[wand]] that was favored by evil [[cleric]]s.

==Description==
This wand was the size of a [[rod]] and constructed of [[steel]]. Nine flexible [[electrum]] tentacles, each {{SI|4|ft|long=yes}} in length, were attached to its shaft.

==Powers==
This wand's effect active whenever it was unsheathed or wielded. When struck by its tendrils a victim would either suffer severe cold, severe burns, or an effect equivalent to that of the ''[[wand of fear]]''.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Sespetralee]], a cleric of [[Loviatar]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].
