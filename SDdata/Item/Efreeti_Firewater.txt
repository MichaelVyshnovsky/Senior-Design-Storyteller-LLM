Efreeti Firewater was a variety of magical spirits available in the city of Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.
The enchanted beverage was on Yawning Portal taverns menu in Waterdeep with its bottle stashed away in Durnans cabinet. One serving of Efreeti Firewater was priced at ten gold coins.
