The '''''Lance of Nhamashal''''' was an enchanted [[lance]] that originated on [[Evermeet]].

==Description==
The lance was black in coloration with [[silver]] runes running in a spiral pattern down its length.

==Powers==
The ''Lance of Nhamashal'' functioned as a +5 weapon, but this was increased to +8 when wielded against [[orc]]s, [[giant]]s, or [[tanar'ri]].
