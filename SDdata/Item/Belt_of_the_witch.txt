The belt of the witch was an enchanted belt worn by warlocks. It could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.
The wearer of a belt of the witch could activate it to cast hypnotic pattern.
* Farideh, a tiefling warlock who worked as an adventurer on behalf of Laeral Silverhand, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.
