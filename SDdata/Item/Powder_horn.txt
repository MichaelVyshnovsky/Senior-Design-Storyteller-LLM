Powder horns were a type of small portable vessel used to store and transport either gunpowder or smokepowder.
A member of the Bregan Daerthe walks with a powder horn strapped to their waist.
Powder horns typically took the form of a shoulder-strung horn or leather flask, typically waterproof.
* Rock gnomes could often be found carrying powder horns for use with pistols.
* The people of Lantan often had powder horns for use with their firearms.
* Powder horns could sometimes be found in use by those with firearms in the Nelanther Isles.
Gunpowder and smokepowder were typically sold within either powder horns or powderkegs.
