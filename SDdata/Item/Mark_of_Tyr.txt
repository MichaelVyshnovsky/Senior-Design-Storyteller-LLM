'''''Marks of Tyr''''' were [[amulet]]s of the [[Tyr]]ran faith in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
''Marks of Tyr'' were round [[gold]]en disks decorated with Tyr's ''[[holy symbol]]''. The gold discs hung off a thong that consisted of multiple segments, tied together.

==Powers==
A ''mark of Tyr'' possessed an enchantment that allowed its owner to compel their opponent to disarm themselves for a short period of time, deterring them from attacking or taking any offensive actions.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Nayeli Goldflower]]: a [[paladin]] from the city of [[Daggerford]] and an adventurer who worked with other warriors of [[Waterdeep]], performing missions on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
