Malatran Plateau, Kara-Tur
Yogurt was a fermented milk product, often made alongside cheeses and curds in several regions of Toril.
In Calimshan, Yogurt was used to served minced pigeon hearts in lambs bones.
Goats milk yogurt was a common type of produce in various lands of Zakhara, including Jumlat and the Pearl Cities.
In the secluded Malatran Plateau, some chefs of the Saiyama tribe served succulent roasted goat rubbed with sage, roasted tubers, and goats milk yogurt.
