'''Grilled rothé ribs''' was a simple and popular dish, slowly cooked meat of [[rothé]] beasts.

==Description==
Grilled rothé ribs were usually smothered in [[honey]], various [[herb]]s, and slowly grilled on open fire. This dish was a popular treat commonly prepared during festivals dedicated to [[Midsummer]] celebrations on [[Faerûn]].

==History==
In the later 15{{th}} century [[DR]], grilled rothé ribs were often cooked by the residents of the [[Western Heartlands]], [[human]]s and [[goblin]]s alike.
