'''''The Bait''''' was a [[magic]]al [[axe]] found in the second level of the catacombs of [[Darkmoon|Temple Darkmoon]].

==Description==
The axe had a ''+2'' enchantment and was located behind a jammed door to the north of the catacombs. It was found beside the magical [[short sword]] known as ''[[Sting]]''.
