'''Bhaerulb''' was a peculiar [[halfling]] and [[gnome]] delicacy found throughout [[Faerûn]].

==Description==
The main ingredients for this [[curried]] [[stew]] were [[mealworm]]s and [[onion]]s. Some recipes used [[leek]]s instead of onions. However, it was a poor substitute.
