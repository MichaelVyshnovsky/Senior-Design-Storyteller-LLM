A '''''ring of vigor''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[ring]] that had restorative powers.

==Description==
The ''ring'' was a simple golden band with a single rectangular aquamarine [[gem]]stone.

==Powers==
Although it did not always function, a ''ring of vigor'' bestowed the wearer with a "cleansing touch" that allowed them to [[magic]]ally heal and cleanse themselves of negative ailments.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Nayeli Goldflower]], a [[paladin]] from [[Daggerford]], had a ''ring of vigor'' in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
