'''Chauntea's plate''' was a type of salad named after the [[Chauntea|goddess of the earth and growing things]]. It went well with a spicy [[vinegar]] dressing.

==Availability==
* It was served as an opening course at a royal feast held by Lord [[Partic Thistle]] at [[Thistleflame Keep]].
