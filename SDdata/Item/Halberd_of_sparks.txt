Halberds of sparks were somewhat common enchanted halberds from the Spine of the World.
These halberds had unassuming appearance of ordinary weapons. The  polearm was topped with a clever-like axe head with a barbed tip, useful to exploit gaps in opponents armor.
Halberds of sparks were enchanted with a +1 enchantment and the ability to deal additional lightning damage to its targets.
In 1281 DR, one of the halberds of sparks was located within one of the crypts in the Vale of Shadows on the Spine of the World.
