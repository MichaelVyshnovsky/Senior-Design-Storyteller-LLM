A '''''poisoner's robe''''' were a variety of [[magic]]al [[robes]] that were associated with [[poison]]s.

==Description==
The garment smelled particularly acrid and actually stinged to the touch.

==Powers==
Whenever the wearer of the ''poisoner's robes'' cast [[spell]]s utilizing poison, those spells became significantly more potent. Unfortunately, the appeared to also poison the wearer themself a bit.

==History==
At some point, during or before the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, someone wearing these robes was consumed by the [[phase spider matriarch]] of the [[Whispering Caverns]].
