'''Unio''' [[shell]]s were prized freshwater [[pearl]] [[mussel]]s that were used for adornment.{{Cite dragon/72/Gems Galore|22}}

==Occurrence==
Such mussels were dredged from freshwater rivers using drag-nets.

==Usages==
Unio shells were commonly used as small decorative embellishments such as buttons and gambling tokens.
