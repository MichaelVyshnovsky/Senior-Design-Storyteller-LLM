'''''Elixirs of defense''''' were [[potion]]s found in [[Waterdeep]], in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
''Elixirs of defense'' were usually bottled in small crystal [[decanter]]s with octahedron-shaped bottle stoppers.

==Powers==
The effects of imbibing an ''elixirs of defense'' were self-explanatory. The potions fortified the user's defenses, improving the chances of them staying unaffected by their opponents' attacks.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Raika]]: a [[half-orc]] [[barbarian]] who worked with other warriors of [[Waterdeep]], performing missions on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
