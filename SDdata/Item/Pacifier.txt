Pacifier was a unique enchanted hand crossbow used in the 15 century DR.
This magic crossbow was undoubtedly crafted by a master. It possessed unparalleled power and accuracy of a +2 enchantment.
Pacifier was created sometime before or in the late 15 century DR. It appeared in the hands of many individuals around the city of Neverwinter, the merchants of the Mercenary Arms Depot, Thayan operatives in the Neverwinter Wood, eye of flame cultists in The Crags, bandits of the Goblin Forest, and Fletcher Thanes mercenaries in the ruins of Illefarn.
