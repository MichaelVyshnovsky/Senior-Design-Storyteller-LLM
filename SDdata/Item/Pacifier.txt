'''''Pacifier''''' was a unique [[enchanted]] [[hand crossbow]] used in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
This magic crossbow was undoubtedly crafted by a master. It possessed unparalleled power and accuracy of a ''+2'' [[item enchantment|enchantment]].

==History==
''Pacifier'' was created sometime before or in the late 15{{th}} century DR. It appeared in the hands of many individuals around the city of [[Neverwinter]], the merchants of the [[Mercenary Arms Depot]], [[Thay]]an operatives in the [[Neverwinter Wood]], [[eye of flame]] cultists in [[The Crags]], bandits of the [[Goblin Forest]], and [[Fletcher Thane]]'s mercenaries in the ruins of [[Illefarn]].
