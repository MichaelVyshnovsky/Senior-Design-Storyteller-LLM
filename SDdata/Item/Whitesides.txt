'''Whitesides''' was a variety of [[cheese]] found in the ancient [[Athalantar]]. The cheese was pressed into large wheels.

==History==
Whitesides cheese was among the cheeses sold by [[Bethgarl Nreams]], a famed cheese merchant from Athalantar.
