The Shadows Blade was a unique enchanted shortsword also known as a short sword of backstabbing.
The Shadows Blade possessed a powerful +3 enchantment that made the sword more deadly and helped to guide the wielders arm towards the target.
The Shadows Blade was a weapon specifically designed to be used by assassins and was in high demand among those who used blades to settle differences. Many of the swords owners did not keep it for very long. In the , The Shadows Blade, the original or a replica, was wielded by Purvis, an assassin who was hired by the speaker of Lonelywood.
