'''''Potions of lore''''' were magic [[potion|concoctions]], the arcane knowledge in a bottle, always in high demand among scholars and adventurers delving deep into magical mysteries-filled dungeons.<ref name="nwn"  />

==Description==
''Potions of lore'' were often red

==Powers==
Drinking a ''potion of lore'' was similar in effect to the ''[[identify]]'' spell.

==History==
''Potions of lore'' became popular and wide-spread in the areas surrounding [[Neverwinter]], [[Nelanther Islands]], [[Cormyr]], and many other places ''circa'' [[1372 DR]].
