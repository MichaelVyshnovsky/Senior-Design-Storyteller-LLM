'''''Protection from evil rings''''' were [[enchanted]] [[ring]]s, imbued with protective magics.

==Description==
''Protection from evil rings'' were simple bands of [[silver]] with three large imbedded [[gemstone]]s.

==Powers==
Each of these rings gave its wearer the ability to cast the ''[[protection from evil]]'' spell twice per day.

==History==
Several of these rings could be found in the [[Ten Towns]] and wider [[Icewind Dale]] ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1312}}.
