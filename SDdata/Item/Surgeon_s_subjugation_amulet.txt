A surgeons subjugation amulet was an enchanted amulet designed to be used by the most depraved surgeons.
The wearer of a surgeons subjugation amulet could cause paralysis in their target upon especially damaging and painful strikes. This ability was usable once per day.
A surgeons subjugation amulet was worn by a depraved surgeon fanatic of Shar. The surgeon, Malus Thorm, resided in the House of Healing of the shadow-cursed town of Reithwin in the late 15 century DR.
