A '''''surgeon's subjugation amulet''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[amulet]] designed to be used by the most depraved surgeons.

==Powers==
The wearer of a ''surgeon's subjugation amulet'' could cause ''[[paralysis]]'' in their target upon especially damaging and painful strikes. This ability was usable once per day.

==History==
A ''surgeon's subjugation amulet'' was worn by a depraved surgeon fanatic of [[Shar]]. The surgeon, [[Malus Thorm]], resided in the [[House of Healing]] of the [[Shadow-cursed Lands|shadow-cursed]] town of [[Reithwin]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
