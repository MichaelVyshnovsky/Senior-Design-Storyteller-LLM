A '''''doomblade''''' was a magical [[sword]] which held a captured [[demon]].

==Reputation==
The [[archdevil]] [[Nergal (archdevil)|Nergal]] claimed there were a dozen [[demon]]s who were trapped in the shape of [[sword]] in [[Toril]] as of {{yearlink|1372}}, and that he knew a spell to accomplish that. The spell's duration was of a century.
