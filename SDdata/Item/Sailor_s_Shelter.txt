The Sailors Shelter was a simple in appearance, yet enchanted amulet that helped seafaring folk remain fearless in the face of peril.
The amulet was nothing but a worn knotted piece of rope with small beads made of coral.
The Sailors Shelter had almost palpable protective magics weaved into the ropes fibers. The wearer of his amulet was granted improved magical protection from fear and panic.
In the , the Second Mate of Captain Lastri Kassireh on board of the Vigilant wore the Sailors Shelter. When the ship was damaged in a strange storm due to sabotage and was wrecked on the shores Samarach in Chult, the Second Mate was captured by a cannibalistic Batiri of the Shattered Spear tribe. He was eager to offer the enchanted amulet to any mercenary brave enough to rescue the unfortunate man.
