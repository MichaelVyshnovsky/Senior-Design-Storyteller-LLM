The ''''Amulet of Dreams''''' was a [[magical item]] that could be found in [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|26}}

==Powers==
While wearing an ''Amulet of Dreams'', the owner could summon up to three [[Sandman|sandmen]]. The sandmen would obey the owner's commands if they were fed a jewel worth at least 100 [[gp]] or if they temporarily drained some of the owner's health. The sandmen would attack the owner if they were not allowed to feed.
