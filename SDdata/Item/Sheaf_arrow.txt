Sheaf arrows, also called war arrows,  were arrows that were designed to cause the most physical harm.
Far shorter than flight arrows, sheaf arrows measured between  and  in length. Sheaf arrows were heavy arrows terminating with sharpened steel tips. These arrows could only be effectively fired with a longbow.
Sheaf arrows were primarily used in warfare.
* Ardasir al-Darah, a janni of the Haunted Lands, carried sheaf arrows.
* Elorfindar Floshin of Daggerford carried enchanted sheaf arrows.
