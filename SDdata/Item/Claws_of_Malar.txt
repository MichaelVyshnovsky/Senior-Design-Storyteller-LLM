'''''Claws of Malar''''' were unique [[claw bracer]]s that belonged to followers of [[Malar]] and were used in ritualistic hunts, ceremonies, and as personal weapons.

==Description==
Typically created by the carrier, they were fashioned to mimic clawed beast paws, such as [[lion]], or other clawed beast such as a [[tiger]], or [[bear]].

==Powers==
These weapons possessed a ''+1 [[Item enchantment|enchantment]]'' [[wounding]]'' claw bracers.{{Cite dungeon/126/Vampires of Waterdeep: Blood of Malar}}

==Variations==
The ''[[True Talons of the God]]'' were a specialized variety of these claws, only crafted from Malarite followers in the [[Divine Den]] in [[Bezentil]].
