Allspice, also called pimento or everry, was an expensive spice produced by a berry-bearing bush.
In the Cormyrean city of Gladehap, Wyrmkindler Sausage Works used one thruppinch of allspice berries per  of stag meat in preparation for their ever-popular stag sausages.
Elves of the ancient city of Myth Drannor in the forest of Cormanthor were quite fond of allspice-seasoned pumpkin soup. A single whiff of the soup evoked memories of a legend about Aerdrie Faenya, which told of her transforming the heads of slain orcs into pumpkins. The story symbolized elves using orcs heads more effectively than they ever did themselves.
Bakers of the wizardly nation of Halruaa used sugar, cardamom, mace, and allspice mixture to dust freshly fried sweet buns and hangmans nooses.
Dwarves of the Northkingdom used allspice to prepare sweet, savory, and spicy Tide-Me-Overs.
Allspice, or pimento, was sold across the Realms via Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue, priced at 40 gold pieces per .
On the Malatran Plateau in Kara-Tur, allspice was used as a pickling spice, together with mustard seeds, black peppercorns, red chiles, cloves, allspice, coriander, ginger, and mace.
Allspice berries could be used to create enchanted oil that could improve ones strength. A vial of such oil could cost up to 750 gp.
In the , Gravias hot apple cider, prepared by Gravia Maxillian of Daggerfords farmlands, called for two allspice berries per pot. Secretly a werewolf, Gravia drugged her cider with Zakharan knockout drops to capture unwitting travelers to be hunted by her bloodthirsty family.
