'''Prootwaddle tea''' was a soothing and relaxing [[tea]] from the [[Blade Kingdoms]]. The concoction was consumed hot. For maximum enjoyment, this tea was to be consumed in a warm bath, surrounded by comfy towels and fuzzy slippers.

==Notable Users==
*Princess [[Milianna Mannicci da Sumbria]], of the ruling [[Mannicci]] family from the city-state of [[Sumbria]] ''circa'' [[1217 DR]].
