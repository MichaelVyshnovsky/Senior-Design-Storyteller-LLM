The Eye of the Storm was an enchanted rapier found in Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.
The Eye of the Storm was an ordinary-looking long and thin finesse blade. The magic rapier had a large hand guard on its hilt.
thumb|Calliope and her Eye of the Storm blade.
The wielder of the Eye of the Storm could cast the thunderwave spell upon a successful strike with a coin-toss of a chance pushing the target away. The spell could be cased via the blade at a ranged target.
*Calliope a bard adventurer owned the Eye of the Storm during her time working with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
