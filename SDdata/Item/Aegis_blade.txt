An '''''aegis blade''''' was an [[enchanted]] [[sword]] meant to be wielded by a [[swordmage]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Tyranny's Bitter Frost|32}}

==Description==
Any heavy or light [[blade]] could be enchanted to become an ''aegis blade''.

==Powers==
''Aegis blades'' were magically precise and sharp weapons, especially when a target was struck in a vital area. When wielded by a swordmage, it allowed them to utter a word of power to mark all foes within {{SI|15|feet|long=yes}} with their ''[[aegis of shielding]]'' or ''[[aegis of assault]]''.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Zaol]], a fallen [[angel of vengeance]] who dual-wielded an ''aegis blade'' [[longsword]] and a ''[[piercing songblade]]''.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Tyranny's Bitter Frost|23}}
