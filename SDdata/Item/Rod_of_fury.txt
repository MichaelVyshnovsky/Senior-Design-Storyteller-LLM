A '''rod of fury''' was a magical [[rod]] that protected a spellcaster and could be used to enhance their spells.

==History==
They were originally a creation of the [[wizard]]s of [[Mulhorand]].

==Description==
The end of the rod was usually carved into the shape of a [[Mulhorandi pantheon|Mulhorandi deity]].

==Powers==
The rods gave varying levels of protection to the bearer. Once per day, a spellcaster bearing the rod could freely increase the power of a [[spell]].

==Creation==
Creation of a ''rod of fury'' required a ''[[protection from evil]]''/''[[protection from good|good]]''/''[[protection from chaos|chaos]]''/''[[protection from law|law]]'' spell and the ability to increase the potency and effectiveness of a spell.

==Cost==
Depending on the quality of the rod, it could be worth between 26,800 [[gold piece]]s and 95,600 gold pieces.
