Rice paper was a type of material and writing surface.
Rice paper was available for sale in the Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue for 1 gp per sheet.
* The spell withdraw required a scroll of rice paper as a material component.
* In Kozakura, screens of rice paper were used as interior walls and doors.
* Rice paper could be used to make scrolls.
* Ninjas would use rice paper to conceal shallow pits. They hid within the pit and the rice paper was concealed with a thin layer of dirt.
