'''Rice paper''' was a type of material and writing surface.

==Trade==
Rice paper was available for sale in the ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' for 1 [[gp]] per sheet.

==Usage==
* The spell ''[[withdraw]]'' required a scroll of rice paper as a [[material component]].
* In [[Kozakura]], screens of rice paper were used as interior walls and doors.
* Rice paper could be used to make scrolls.
* [[Ninja]]s would use rice paper to conceal shallow pits. They hid within the pit and the rice paper was concealed with a thin layer of dirt.
