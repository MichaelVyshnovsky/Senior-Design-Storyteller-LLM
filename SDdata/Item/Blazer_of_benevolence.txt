The '''''blazer of benevolence''''' was an set of magically [[item enchantment|enchanted]] clothes that were especially beneficial to [[magic]]-wielding [[bard]]s of the Realms.

==Description==
The blazer comprised an embroidered velvet doublet set over a fine shirt with a frilled collar and puffed out upper sleeves. It was bound with a wide belt with a buckle that featured an elaborate depiction of a songbird.

==Powers==
The outfit imbued the wearer with a bolstered sense of self-confidence and assertiveness. Whenever a bard donned the blazer, and sang or played a [[bardic inspiration|song to inspire]] their comrades, their own vitality was strengthened, if temporarily.

==History==
This set of magic clothing belonged to [[Volothamp Geddarm]] in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
