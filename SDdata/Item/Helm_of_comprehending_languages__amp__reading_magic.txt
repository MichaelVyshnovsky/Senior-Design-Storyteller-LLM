A '''''helm of comprehending languages & reading magic''''' was a [[magic item]] that enabled the wearer to understand most tongues.

==Description==
These items looked like normal [[helmet]]s.

==Powers==
When worn, this item granted the wearer the ability to understand most languages and writings, including magical writings.

==Notable Owners==
[[Alcedor Kolat]], a powerful [[mage]] from [[Waterdeep]], owned a ''helm of comprehending languages & reading magic''.
