'''''Acid-etched hand crossbows''''' were [[enchanted]] [[crossbow]]s popularized in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
These magic [[hand crossbow]]s coated all [[bolt]]s shot out of it with corrosive [[acid]]. Additionally, these weapons possessed ''+2'' or ''+3'' enchantments to the the damage and magics that guided the missiles towards their targets.

==History==
In the 15{{th}} century DR, ''acid-etched hand crossbows'' were in use in the city of [[Neverwinter]], sold at the [[Mercenary Arms Depot]], and found among savage inhabitants of the [[Old Owl Well]] during its occupation by [[orc]]ish bandit bands.
