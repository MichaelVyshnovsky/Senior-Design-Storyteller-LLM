Luthers Protector was a staff enchanted for the use of dwarven monks of Myth Drannor, named after the decorated hero, Magister Luther.
Luthers Protector was a simple staff in its appearance carved out of beechwood.
Luthers Protector possessed the +3 enchantment and improved the wielding monks initiative in battle.
In 1369 DR, Luthers Protector could bee found in the Deep Halls of Dwarven Dungeons in the scaly hands of one of the marauding lizardfolk led by a mysterious sorcerer Malgi Hi, looted from one of the ancient tombs of the dungeon.
