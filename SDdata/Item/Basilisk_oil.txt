'''''Basilisk oil''''' was a magical substance that had power to counter ''[[petrification]]''.

==Description==
This substance was a thick grey [[oil]], extracted from a slain [[basilisk]]'s gullet.

==Powers==
''Basilisk oil'', when applied to a ''petrified'' creature, had similar effects to the ''[[stone to flesh]]'' spell.
