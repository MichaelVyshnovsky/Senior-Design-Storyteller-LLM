'''Arundoon sauce''' was a condiment which paired well with [[Humanoid|people]].

==Use==
Arundoon sauce was to be applied prior to cooking people, preferably alive.

==History==
The [[lich]] [[Thalon]] had a preserved decanter of arundoon sauce in his lair as of the {{YearlinkName|1357}}. He poured it on [[Laeral Rythkyn]], a [[Harper]] [[wizard]], before cooking her.
