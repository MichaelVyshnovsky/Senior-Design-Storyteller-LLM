Belts of bodily health were enchanted girdles imbued with health-fortifying magics.
Belts of bodily health appeared to be simple leather belts with a large hexagonal buckle.
Belts of bodily health possessed enchantments that improved the wearers vigor, making them tougher, and healthier.
*Nayeli a paladin adventurer who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
