'''''Belts of bodily health''''' were enchanted girdles imbued with health-fortifying magics.

==Description==
''Belts of bodily health'' appeared to be simple leather [[belt]]s with a large hexagonal buckle.

==Powers==
''Belts of bodily health'' possessed enchantments that improved the wearer's vigor, making them tougher, and healthier.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Nayeli]]: a [[paladin]] adventurer who worked with other warriors of [[Waterdeep]], performing missions on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
