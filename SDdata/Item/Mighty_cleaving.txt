'''''Mighty cleaving''''' was a magical [[weapon quality|quality]] that could be added to a weapon. It allowed a trained warrior to make another attack after knocking out one foe.

==Creation==
To craft a ''mighty cleaving'' weapon, a spellcaster had to have the spell ''[[divine power]]'' available.

==Notable examples==
* The ''[[Great Mallet of Silvanus]]'', the personal weapon of the [[Silvanus|Oak Father]], was a ''+5 [[energy aura]] [[impact]] mighty cleaving [[maul]]''.
