Syl-Pashan Sup was a type red Calishite wine, available in the Western Heartlands, along the River Chionthar, and the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
This red wine had a bold and loud meaty taste. It was said to hit the tongue of the drinker like a raging bull.
