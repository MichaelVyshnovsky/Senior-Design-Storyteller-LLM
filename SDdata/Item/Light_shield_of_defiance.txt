'''''Light shields of defiance''''' were [[enchanted]] [[shield]]s that offered limited protection to the wearer, popularized in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Powers==
''Light shields of defiance'' magically improved the wielder's defenses as well as protections against mundane and magical effects. Additionally, these shields granted their masters ''[[Regenerate|regeneration]]'' when they suffered significant harm. These shields could only be used by [[cleric]]s and [[fighter]]s.

==History==
In the 15{{th}} century DR, various ''shields of defiance'' could be found among bandits and heroes of the [[Sword Mountains]], areas surrounding and markets of the city of [[Neverwinter]].
