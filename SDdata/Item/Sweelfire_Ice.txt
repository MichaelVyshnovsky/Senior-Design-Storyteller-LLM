Sweelfire Ice was a chilled dessert dish from the city of Velen in the nation of Tethyr.
A simple delight of a cupful of ice, topped with cream and then topped with sweet tamarind sauce.
As of the late 14 century DR, Sweelfire Ice could be found on Blackthorns Brews menu, a festhall on the Captains Cliff in Velen. The dish was mentioned among other notable foods included in Volothamp Geddarms published guide to Amn and Tethyr.
