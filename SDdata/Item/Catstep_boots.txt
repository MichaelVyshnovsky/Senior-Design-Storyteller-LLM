'''''Catstep boots''''' were a variety of [[enchanted]] [[boots]] that imparted upon their wearer innate qualities associated with [[cat]]s.

==Powers==
When worn, these boots improved the acrobatic and athletic qualities of their wearer. They also dampened the harm that would normally be inflicted from falling great heights.

==History==
In the late 15{{Th}} century [[DR]], a group of [[adventurer]]s in [[Waterdeep]] discovered a pair of ''catstep boots'' among the belongings of a small group of [[thieves]] in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]].<ref>{{Cite organized play/LFR/Heirloom|19}}
