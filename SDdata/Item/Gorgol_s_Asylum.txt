Gorgols Asylum was one of the unique Fading Rings that possessed defensive magic only usable by sorcerers.
Gorgols Asylum was a cold metallic latticework ring.
Gorgols ring possessed a +3 enchantment similar to a powerful ring of protection. Additionally, the ring gave the wearer the ability to cast globe of invulnerability.
In 1369 DR, Gorgols Asylum was in the possession of a mysterious monument, the Great Stone Head, located in the Nightingale Court in the ruins of Myth Drannor. In exchange for one of the eidolons, small totem statues depicting various gods and demons, the monument dispensed powerful magic rings, one being the Gorgols Asylum.
