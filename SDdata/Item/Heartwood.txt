'''''Heartwood''''' was a worked piece of magical wood found in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. Due to its unique properties, it was able to be used similarly to a [[docent]] of [[Eberron]].{{DDO-time}}
{{Quote
|A small heart carved of the very center of an ancient tree.
}}
==Description==
When installed in a [[warforged]], ''heartwood'' spread green mossy growth across their body.

==Powers==
The living wood greatly enhanced the protective and regenerative properties of a warforged's form while it was used.

==History==
''Heartwood'' was found in the treasure pile of the [[red dragon]] [[Daeronnax]], the master of [[Clan Daeronnax]] in the [[Stonelands]] after he kidnapped the adventurer [[Tristran of Daggerford]].
