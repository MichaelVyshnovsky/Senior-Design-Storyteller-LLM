Iriaeboran North Brew was a local brew from Iriaebor and was also sold through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
This beer was appreciated by the commoners more than those with more refined palettes. It had a dark amber color combined with a bitter aftertaste. Iriaeborans called this the "bite of the north winds".
Iriaeboran North Brew could be purchased in the following containers
* Hand keg (9 cp)
* Cask (5 sp)
* Barrel (12 sp)
* Butt (4 gp)
* Tun (8 gp)
The blacksmiths assistant Dovo had a mug of North Brew at the Bold Bard in Ghars, Cormyr, in Eleint of 1367 DR.
