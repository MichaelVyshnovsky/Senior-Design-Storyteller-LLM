The Eye of Ranman was a magical amulet intended for a monks use only. This item was a part of the set of magic items of the Ranmans Meditations.
The Eye of Ranman was a heavy deep green crystal on a chain.
Along with other artifacts of Ranmans, the Eye of Ranman was usable only by monks and granted the wearer wisdom, along with increased movement speed that exceeded that of an average humanoid.
The Eye of Ranman belonged to the dwarves of Harldain Ironbars House of Gems. Circa 1369 DR, the amulet could be found in the Room of Words of the House of Gems in the ruins of Myth Drannor.
