The '''''nafir''''' ({{Pronounce|nah|FEER}}) was a [[Zakhara]]n wind instrument similar to a trumpet.{{Cite dragon/190/Sounds of Wonder & Delight}}

==Description==
''Nafirs'' resembled long, thin trumpets and were made from metal.

==History==
Though less common in south Zakhara, the ''nafir'' was used widely in both [[Qudra]] and the [[Free Cities of Zakhara|Free Cities]] as a signalling device and to send military orders into the field.
