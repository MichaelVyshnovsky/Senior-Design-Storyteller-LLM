==History==
In the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], wimples were a popular variety of headwear among women in [[Waterdeep]], often worn alongside a [[veil (clothing)|veil]].

==Notable Wearers==
* [[Fang (priest)|Fangs]], a variety of [[specialty priest]] in the [[Church of Talona]], typically wore a black wimple that covered their face and ears.<ref>
* [[Priest]]esses in the [[Church of Sune]] sometimes wore an attractive wimple, red in coloration, with a v-shaped crown piece as part of their priestly garments.
