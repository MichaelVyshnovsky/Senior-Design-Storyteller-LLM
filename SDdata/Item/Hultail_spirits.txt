'''Hultail spirits''' was a uniquely strong alcoholic drink from [[Cormyr]].

==Description==
Hultail spirits were famous for their potency. Once consumed, it almost instantly rendered the drinker inebriated for a short period of time. A side effect of imbibing this liquor was a feeling of intense strength and invincibility.
