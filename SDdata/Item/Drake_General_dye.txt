'''Drake General dye''' was a rare and expensive [[dye]] found in the [[Western Heartlands]] and the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, Drake General dye was valued at fifty [[gold]] pieces per bottle. It was created exclusively by Imperial Dyemaster [[Sigurd (Imperial Dyemaster)|Sigurd]] and his apprentice, [[Aurora (apprentice)|Aurora]]. This flame-red and gold dye was advertised for those adventurers who valiantly rush into the world of treasures and adventure.
