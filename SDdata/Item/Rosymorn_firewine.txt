Rosymorn firewine was a variety firewine distilled by the Rosymorn Monastery of Lathander before the late 15 century DR.
Rosymorn firewine had a pleasant color but had a harsh strong smell of alcohol and a weak soft flavor.
Rosymorn firewine was produced by the Rosymorn Monastery before its fall to a force of githyanki sometime in or shortly before the . Bulbous green glass bottles of Rosymorn firewine could be found in the city of Baldurs Gate and in the locales of the Chionthar Valley in the late 15 century DR.
