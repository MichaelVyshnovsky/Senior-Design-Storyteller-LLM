The '''''Reflections on Umberlee''''' was a painting by [[Vin Valle]] of [[Baldur's Gate]], created in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, [[Lady Jannath]] hosted the art show in her [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] estate, titled "Strange Tides: Self-Portraits from the Sword Coast." ''Reflections on Umberlee'' was one of the pieces on display with Vin Valle invited to the event. Lady Jannath asked her butler, [[Tarhun Mnemonis]], to remind the artist of their picture being displayed upon arrival.
