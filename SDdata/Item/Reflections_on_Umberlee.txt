The Reflections on Umberlee was a painting by Vin Valle of Baldurs Gate, created in the late 15 century DR.
In the , Lady Jannath hosted the art show in her Lower City estate, titled "Strange Tides Self-Portraits from the Sword Coast." Reflections on Umberlee was one of the pieces on display with Vin Valle invited to the event. Lady Jannath asked her butler, Tarhun Mnemonis, to remind the artist of their picture being displayed upon arrival.
