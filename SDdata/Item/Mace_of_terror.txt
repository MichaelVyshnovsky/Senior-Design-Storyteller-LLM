A '''''mace of terror''''' was a [[magic]]al [[weapon]] that could induce terror in its victims.

==History==
The spirit animal [[Wongo]] imbued its spirit into a mace of terror, which it left in the hands of a [[mummy]] in the [[Tomb of the Nine Gods]].
