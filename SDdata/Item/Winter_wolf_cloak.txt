'''''Winter wolf cloaks''''' were [[enchanted]] [[cloak]]s that carried magical properties of [[winter wolves]]' pelt they were made of.

==Powers==
These cloaks granted the wearers resistance to mundane and magical cold. Additionally, the cloaks slightly improved the wearers' charisma.

==History==
''Circa'' {{YearlinkName|1281}}, [[Oswald Fiddlebender]] crafted various magic items of clothing using pelts of winter wolves that followed the [[Cultists of Auril]] that plagued [[Icewind Dale]]. Some of other items included ''[[winter wolf scarves]]'', ''[[winter wolf gloves]]'', ''[[winter wolf hat]]s'', and ''[[winter wolf boots]]''.
