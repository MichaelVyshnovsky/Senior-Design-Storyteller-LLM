Moons circlet and an enchanted clerics circlet from Waterdeep, in the late 15 century DR.
Moons circlet was a band of shaped silvery metal, with its front arrowing down in a V-shape.
Moons circlet allowed the wearer to cast the cure wounds spell, extending their healing capabilities.
