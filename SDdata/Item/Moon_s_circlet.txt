'''''Moon's circlet''''' and an enchanted [[cleric]]'s [[circlet]] from [[Waterdeep]], in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
''Moon's circlet'' was a band of shaped [[silver]]y metal, with its front arrowing down in a V-shape.

==Powers==
''Moon's circlet'' allowed the wearer to cast the ''[[cure wounds]]'' spell, extending their healing capabilities.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Halbenet Soladonel]]: a [[moon elf]] [[cleric]] of [[Corellon Larethian]] who worked with other warriors of [[Waterdeep]], performing missions on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
