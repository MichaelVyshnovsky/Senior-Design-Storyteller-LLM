The whispering masks were a collection of magical wooden masks used by the hag Auntie Ethel to control the guardians of her inner sanctum.
The wearer of the mask was bombarded with incomprehensible whispers and a putrid stench that overwhelmed their senses and thoughts, forcing them under Ethels control. While this effect could be resisted for a time, it took a strong sense of willpower.
As of the , Ethel had enthralled four individuals into her flock by means of the whispering masks, naming them the Masks of Regret, Servitude, Terror, and Vengeance.
