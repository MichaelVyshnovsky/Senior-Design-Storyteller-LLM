Maelstroms Punch was the specialty alcoholic drink from The Track in the city of Westgate.
Maelstroms Punch was a quite mundane and average libation brewed in-house by The Tracks employees. What made it standout among many other swirls was the sorcerer who placed minor enchantments on each batch that highbred the drinkers senses.
By the , Maelstroms Punch was created in and popularized by the Track, the underground racing rink, and gambling establishment on the Market Triangle of Westgate.
