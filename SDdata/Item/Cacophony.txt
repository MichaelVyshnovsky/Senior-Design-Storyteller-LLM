Cacophony was an enchanted quarterstaff that allowed the wielder to dispense thunderous smite onto their enemies, created sometime before the late 15 century DR.
Cacophony possessed a simple +1 enchantment that strengthened the weapons blows and aided the wielders aim. The stars know was engraved with the word "thunder" in Gnomish, Sylvan, Elvish, Celestial, Primordial and many other languages that all merged into the powerful thunderword. Each smash with the weapon dealt additional thunder damage to the targets and once per day, the wielder could channel the thunderword into a powerful strike of thunderous smite.
In the , Cacophony belonged to Lady Esther, an adventurer in service of the Society of Brilliance of Baldurs Gate.
