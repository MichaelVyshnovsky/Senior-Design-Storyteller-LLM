A '''''caduceus staff''''' was a type of magical [[staff]], so named for being in the shape of the [[caduceus]] symbol, that had minor curative effects.{{Cite organized play/LC/Potions, Lotions, & Notions: Closed!/Treasure|2}}

==Description==
These staves were carved from [[oak]]wood.

==Powers==
These staves only functioned in the hands of either a [[cleric]] or [[druid]]. These staves could cast an effect similar to the [[spell]] ''[[cure light wounds]]'' twice per day, as well as ''[[cure serious wounds]]'' once per day.
