'''Coconuts''' were a type of hard-shelled fruit that grew on coconut [[palm]]s.

==Habitats==
Coconuts typically grew in tropical regions such as [[Maztica]], [[Mad Monkey Island]], as well as the island chains [[Jazayir al-Sartan]], [[Nada al-Hazan]], and the [[Steaming Isles]]. Occasionally they grew in desert regions, such as the [[Raurin|Desert of Desolation]].

==Prices==
''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' was known to sell dried and fresh coconuts for 50 and 10 [[gold piece]]s respectively.

==Coconut Products==
* The interior of a coconut was used for a variety of food, including coconut milk, coconut soup, and copra cake (dried coconut meat). 
* The thick layers of fibers (known as "[[Midani dictionary#Q|qinbar]]" in [[Midani language|Midani]]) that coconut shells had could be used for a variety of purposes.
* '''Grum''', an alcohol made from fermented coconut juice.{{Cite dragon/157/The Ecology of the Wemic|50}}

==Notable Users of Coconuts==
===Organizations===
* On [[Jazayir al-Alfar]] isle, the [[Haifami]] tribe were known to construct clothes from coconut fibers, while the [[Tarangu]] tribe used coconuts as weapons.
===Races & Sentient Creatures===
* The [[ashira]], tree [[spirit]]s endemic to Zakhara, lived in coconut trees (as well as trees bearing other fruits) and survived off of the coconuts they produced.
* [[Banderlog]]s and some [[monkey]]s were known to use coconuts as weapons.

===Regions & Settlements===
* In some parts of [[Kara-Tur]], coconut fibers were used to construct [[shield]]s.
* On the [[Malatran Plateau]], inhabitants often used coconut shells as containers or drinking vessels.{{Cite polyhedron/102/Heroes of Malatra|14}}
* In the land of Maztica, the [[poison]] known as [[kurari]] was often transported inside coconut shells.
* In the land of [[Zakhara]], coconut fibers were often used to construct [[rope]]s for use on ships.
