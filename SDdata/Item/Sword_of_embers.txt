A sword of embers was an enchanted short sword that could be found in Waterdeep as of the late 15 century DR.
The blade glowed red with heat, and was searing hot to the touch.
Attacks with this blade could reliably burn enemies, inflicting terrible injuries that left them weakened.
* Tommus Tallstep, a halfling fighter who worked as an adventurer on behalf of Laeral Silverhand, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, during the late 15 century DR.
