'''Fruit porridge''' was a common simple variety of [[porridge]] found in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
This dish of [[oatmeal]] or [[barley]] was a chunky mixture overwhelmingly flavored with [[banana]]s and served topped with berries.

==Availability==
Fruit porridge could be found being served in homes and eateries of the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] and inns alongside the [[Risen Road]] and the [[Chionthar Valley]].
