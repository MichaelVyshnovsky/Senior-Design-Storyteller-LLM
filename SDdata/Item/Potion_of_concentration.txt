Potions of concentration were magical brews that ensued clarity of the mind and razor sharp focus of the imbiber that aided them in combat.
Potions of concentration, when consumed before battle, granted the imbiber assiduousness enough to land a true strike.
Circa 1372 DR, the Green Hag of Lizard Marsh, who feuded with a lizardman shaman Uzzak-Szalzik, possessed a rich collection of magic potions, tinctures, and brews. Among them were potions of concentration of her own creation. These potions were available to any of the green hags allies against her sworn lizardfolk enemy.
