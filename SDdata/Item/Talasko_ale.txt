'''Talasko ale''' was the house-brand brew at [[Mother Talasko's]] in [[Khôltar]], [[south Faerûn]], ''circa'' the [[1372 DR|Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR]].{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks archive|70}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 55: Khôltar, Part 6, Looking Around the Farrgaunlar}}

==Description==
Compared to other ales, this drink was thin and sour. It cost 3 [[copper piece]]s and was served in a hard-crust bread bowl or the staff would pour it into whatever container the customer brought to the counter. The serving size was about a pint.
