Justlance was an adamantine returning spear, and the cleric Marrecs favored weapon in .
Justlance was an adamantine spear, with the tip fashioned in the form of a unicorns horn.
Justlance always returned to the wielders hand after being thrown, unable to be parted from its wielder for long. Blessed by a deity, rot did not adhere to the spear.
In , Marrec had been the wielder of Justlance for some time. Those who survived combat with him learned to fear its enchantment.
