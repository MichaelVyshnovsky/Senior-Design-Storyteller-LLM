'''''Potions of shocking grasp''''' were concoctions that imbued the drinker with lightning magics.

==Description==
''Potions of shocking grasp'' were bottled in a variety of containers, typically made of [[ceramic]]s, crystal, or [[glass]]. Each [[potion]] had enough liquid for a single dose.

==Powers==
When consumed, a ''potion of shocking grasp'' energized the diners hands, charging them with magical electricity. The imbiber could land a powerful shocking burn onto a target of choice via single touch. Effects of the potion were similar to the ''[[shocking grasp]]'' [[spell]].

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1312}}, these potions could be found in the [[Ten Towns]] of [[Icewind Dale]], notably, brewed and sold by an eccentric tinkerer [[gnome]] [[Oswald Fiddlebender]].
