'''Mynteer''', also known as '''[[wikipedia:en:phenakite|phenakite]]''', was a hard, clear, and rare [[gem|gemstone]] found in the Realms.

==Description==
These [[semi-precious stones]] occurred in crystals, usually with inclusions so that it yielded only small [[wikipedia:facet|faceted]] gems. A typical specimen had a base value of 50 gp.

==Powers==
This stone was powdered and mixed to make magical [[ink]] to inscribe scrolls for casting spells related to the movement of objects via levitation, telekinesis, or magical force, such as ''[[unseen servant]]'', ''[[Tenser's floating disk|Tenser's floating disc]]'', ''etc.''
