Decision was a unique enchanted flaming falchion once wielded by General Anali Cyl of Neverwinter during the Luskan-Neverwinter war of 1372 DR.
The falchion was an enchanted blade coated in magic flames. In addition to its +2 enchantment, Decision dealt fire damage to its targets.
Decision was the family heirloom blade belonging to a wealthy family of Neverwinter. When the Jewel of the North entered the war with its bitter rival neighbor Luskan in the , led by traitor Aribeth de Tylmarande, Analis family attempted to ship her to Waterdeep. She was to be wed to a wealthy merchant family scion in the City of Splendors, away from Neverwinter. Anali much preferred to stand and die in defense of her home. She grabbed Decision off the wall and stormed off. As the war raged on, Anali Cyl became a famous General, wielding the family blade in battle.
By the , General Anali Cyls falchion fell into the hands of Aldanon, Sage of Neverwinter. The magic sword was stored in the basement of his manse in the Blacklake District.
