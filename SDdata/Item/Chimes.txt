'''Chimes''' were [[musical instruments]] in the Realms popular among [[bard]]s.

==Description==
These small metallic percussion instruments made a lovely sound when struck.

==Availability==
''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' sold chimes for 5–75 [[gold piece]]s depending on the size and quantity.

==Notable Chimes==
* ''[[Chime of Exile]]''
* ''[[Chime of hunger]]'', [[curse]]d variants of ''chimes of opening'' that induced severe hunger pangs.
* ''[[Chime of interruption]]'', a variety of magical chimes designed to interfere with [[spell]]casting.
* ''[[Chime of opening]]'', a variety of magical chimes that could open almost anything through sonic vibrations.

==Notable Users==
* Chimes were among the favored instruments of by bards in [[Faerûn]] and a number of the magical varieties that existed had their effects enhanced when played by a bard.<ref>
