Chimes were musical instruments in the Realms popular among bards.
These small metallic percussion instruments made a lovely sound when struck.
Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold chimes for 5–75 gold pieces depending on the size and quantity.
* Chime of Exile
* Chime of hunger, cursed variants of chimes of opening that induced severe hunger pangs.
* Chime of interruption, a variety of magical chimes designed to interfere with spellcasting.
* Chime of opening, a variety of magical chimes that could open almost anything through sonic vibrations.
* Chimes were among the favored instruments of by bards in Faerûn and a number of the magical varieties that existed had their effects enhanced when played by a bard.
