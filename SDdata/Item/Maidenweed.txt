'''Maidenweed''' was an [[alchemical]] draught used to prevent pregnancies.

==Powers==
When drank, it prevented pregnancy in the drinker for about a month.

==History==
The synthesis of this concoction required a certain aptitude with herbal lore.{{Cite dragon/163/Hedge Wizards|24}} [[Hedge wizard]]s often traded in it.
