Golden girdles of Urnst, or simply golden girdles, were enchanted belts imbued with defensive magics.
Golden girdles were weaved out of red silk and strewn with flakes of gold.
The wearer of a golden girdle gained increased protection against slashing damage.
The first golden girdles were created ages before the late 14 century DR by a powerful wizard who sought to protect themselves from a group of evil swordsmen.
In the , a band of the Knights of Solamnia from the world of Krynn became trapped in a massive Planar Sphere that crash-landed in the city of Athkatla in Amn. One of the Knights was in possession of a golden girdle.
