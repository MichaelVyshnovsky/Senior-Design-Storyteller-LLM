A '''''potion of undead control''''' was an [[magic item]] that granted the imbiber control over a specific forms of [[undead]].

==Powers==
When consumed, these [[potion]]s gave the imbiber the ability to charm specific kinds of undead. The undead remained charmed for 5â€“20 minutes. Different versions of this potion existed with each targeting a specific undead including [[ghast]]s, [[ghost]]s, [[ghoul]]s, [[shadow]]s, [[skeleton]]s, [[spectre]]s, [[wight]]s, [[wraith]]s, [[vampire]]s, and [[zombie]]s.

==Notable Owners==
[[Pizentios]], a vile [[necromancer]] from [[Zakhara]], carried a ''potion of undead control''.
