'''Toffee apples''' were simple dessert treats made with fresh [[apple]]s, dipped in sweet sauces.

{{quote|''Warning - do NOT attempt to talk and eat at the same time!''|Note on [[Nell Kirschtorte]]'s toffee apples.}}

==Description==
A typical toffee apple was a plump sweet and juicy apple skewered on a wooden stick and dipped in toffee and then [[honey]].

==Availability==
These small toffee treats were a staple of [[Nell's Pie Shop]] of Liam's Hold ''circa'' [[1372 DR]].
