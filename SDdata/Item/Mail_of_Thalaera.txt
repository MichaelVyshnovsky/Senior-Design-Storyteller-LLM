The '''''Mail of Thalaera''''' was an enchanted suit of [[elven]] [[armor]] originating on the island of [[Evermeet]].

==Description==
This armor was held together by [[silver]] and matte black links.

==Powers==
The ''Mail of Thalaera'' carried a +5 enchantment. Additionally, the armor imparted a significant resistance to [[magic]] to its wearer and made them more likely to resists the effects of any form of fire, [[acid]], or [[dragon breath]].
