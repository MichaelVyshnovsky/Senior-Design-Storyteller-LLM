'''''Helmets of grit''''' were leather [[helmet]]s, decorated with feathers and [[enchanted]] to boost the wearer's agility.

==Powers==
The helmet's magic was palpable to the touch; anyone who touched it felt a surge of stamina. It granted the wearer a slight boost to their dexterity and nimbleness. Additionally, when the wearer was significantly hurt, the helmet granted them a boost to speed and reaction in battle.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, one of the ''helmets of grit'' was stored within the [[Szarr Palace]] in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].
