The Ongild was a large magical emerald, named after the Halruaan wizard who was responsible for its creation.
The artifacts was last known whereabouts was on Skadaurak Island in Mintarn, in the stomach of the red dragon Hoondarrh.
Whoever held the Ongild regenerated a small amount every hour. It also granted them the ability to cast delayed blast fireball, prismatic spray, reverse gravity and spell turning once per day.
