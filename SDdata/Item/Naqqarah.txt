The '''''naqqarah''''' ({{Pronounce|nah|KAR|rah}}) was a small [[Zakhara]]n [[drum]] often worn at the side.{{Cite dragon/190/Sounds of Wonder & Delight}}

==Description==
The ''naqqarah'' was fixed with a leather thong worn over the shoulder. The body of the drum was made of fire-hardened clay with hide stretched over the head. It was played with a pair of thin sticks.

==History==
''Naqqarahs'' were popular among messengers and criers throughout the Land of Fate.
