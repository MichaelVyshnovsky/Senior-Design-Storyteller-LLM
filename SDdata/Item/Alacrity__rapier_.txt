Alacrity was an enchanted rapier of unknown origins found on the Sword Coast North in the late 14 century DR.
Alacrity possessed a +1 enchantment that made the blade deadlier and guided the wielders hand toward the target. Its magics also boosted the wielders intelligence and made it easier to defend oneself in combat, making it perfect for both wizards and swashbucklers.
The exact origins of the rapier were unknown. In the , one such blade was found in the town of Port Llast, sold by Haljal to the friends of the Church of Waukeen.
