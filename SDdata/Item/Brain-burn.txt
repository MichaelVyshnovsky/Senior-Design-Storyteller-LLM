'''Brain-burn''' was a deadly [[poison]] that ruined any chances at magically drawing information from a dead body.

==Powers==
Those struck with brain-burn died immediately and, furthermore, their corpses could not be [[Speak with dead|interrogated magically]].

==History==
The moneylender [[Caethur]] employed brain-burn on the weapons he used to assassinate, including those he employed to kill a number of his debtors in early spring of the {{yearlinkName|1373}}. Amongst those killed by brain-burn were [[Kamburan]], [[Hammuras]], [[Aldurl Nael]], two others, and his own two bodyguards.
