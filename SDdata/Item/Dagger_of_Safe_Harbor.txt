The Dagger of Safe Harbor was a unique and famous enchanted dagger created by Gullwyn, a seafaring merchant, centuries before the 13 century DR.
The Dagger of Safe Harbor possessed a potent +3 enchantment that increased its damage and guided the wielders hand toward the target. The dagger allowed its owner to cast Otilukes resilient sphere once per day, shielding them from any and all harm.
In his long merchant and sailor career, Gullwyn witnessed countless men being swept off the ships during deadly storms, all because of a split second of fear. To protect himself while showing respect to the mighty power of the seas, Gullwyn created the Dagger of Safe Harbor that could shield its owner from all effects of the world for a short period of time. In the years that followed the weapons creation, the Dagger of Safe Harbor saved Gullwyns life on at least three occasions.
