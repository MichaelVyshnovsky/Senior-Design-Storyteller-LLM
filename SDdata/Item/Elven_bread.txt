Elven bread, or elf-bread, was a highly nutritional bread produced in Evermeet. It was available for purchase through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
Elven bread was a rolled pastry loaf. It was fine-grained, light, and sweet
Eating too much elven bread was thought to make people fat.
It was also rumored that an Elven bread-based diet played a role in Elminsters longevity.
