Wands of stoneskin were magic wands favored by wizards who frequented battlefields and needed additional protection from enemies swords and arrows.
Wands of stoneskin held up to fifty charges of the stoneskin spell. When cast, it turned the targets skin into stone, making them well protected and resistant to physical damage.
In 1373 DR, wands of stoneskin could be found in the hands of servants of Orcus during the demon lords attack on the city of Zhentil Keep in the Moonsea region.
*Ningal was an air genasi who was organizing rebellion against Mulhorand in the late 14 century DR.
