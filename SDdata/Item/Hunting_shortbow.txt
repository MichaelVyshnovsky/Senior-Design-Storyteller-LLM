Hunting shortbows were a variety of simple enchanted bows that were used against monstrosities.
Hunting shortbows were strong and thick ash-wood weapons.
These bows were imbued with a rangers magic that felt like a ghosts wisp in the shooters hands. The wielder of this shortbow could place a hunters mark on a creature once per day. Additionally, the weapon made it easier to shoot a monstrosity, and it possessed a simple +1 magic of guidance and damage boost.
* Dammon, a tiefling smith from the town of Elturel in the late 15 century DR.
