The Snow Maidens Reaver was a powerful enchanted bastard sword created by a Chosen of Auril Ihanora the Merciful 1099 DR.
The enchanted bastard sword was created out of magic ice with only its grip and pommel being made out of metal, silver and steel.
The Snow Maidens Reaver possessed a +2 enchantment and possessed a fearsome ability - a chance to turn its targets to solid ice upon a successful strike.
