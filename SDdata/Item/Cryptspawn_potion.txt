'''''Cryptspawn potions''''' were a sort of immune-system enhancing [[potion]] that existed in the period between the [[Spellplague]] and the [[Second Sundering]].

==Description==
The liquid within these potions was viscous and had a smell evocative of dead bodies.

==Powers==
Ingestion of a ''cryptspawn potion'' granted imbibers an enhanced resistance against all manner of [[disease]]s and [[poison]]s, as well as [[necrotic energy]]

==History==
In the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], ''cryptspawn potions'' could be found in use by many members of the [[Zhentarim]].<ref>
