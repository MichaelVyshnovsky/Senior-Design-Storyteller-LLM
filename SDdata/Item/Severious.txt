'''''Severious''''' was a [[magic]]al and powerful [[longsword]].

==Description==
Severious was owned by the adventuring [[fighter]] and member of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]], [[Beohram]]. This unique weapon was found among his remains in the lower reaches of the [[drow]] above [[Xanathar]]'s sanctum. The powerful longsword had a ''+5'' enchantment.
