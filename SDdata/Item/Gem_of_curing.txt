'''''Gems of curing''''' were enchanted faceted [[gem]]stones imbued with healing magic.

==Powers==
''Gems of curing'' served as a tool of healing. Each gem possessed multiple charges of healing [[spell]]s.

==History==
''Gems of curing'' could be found in [[Waterdeep]] ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1370}}. They were available for sale in the many wondrous markets of the City of Splendors and often used by adventuring [[clerics]] exploring the dangerous depths of [[Undermountain]].
