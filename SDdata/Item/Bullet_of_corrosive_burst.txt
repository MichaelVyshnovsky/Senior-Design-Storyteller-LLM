'''''Bullets of corrosive burst''''' were a type of [[enchanted]] [[sling]] [[bullet]] that burned their targets with [[acid]].

==Powers==
''Bullets of corrosive burst'' were imbued with magical currents that made them feel lighter, more balanced, and each bullet was enchanted to burst with acid upon reaching its target. Additionally, there was a ten percent chance a bullet would create additional acid, making it even more dangerous.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1312}}, ''bullets of corrosive burst'' were used by [[Aurilite]] [[Stormsister]]s and postulants who joined the [[Legion of the Chimera]] to bring destruction on the [[Ten Towns]] and [[Kuldahar]] of [[Icewind Dale]].
