The Cape of the Red Prince was a mundane cloak that could be found in the Western Heartlands during the late 15 century DR.
The cape was made from a rich fabric that was dyed red and featured gold-colored embroidery of two dragons, flanking either side of a broad scimitar.
The cape presumably belonged to or was associated with the "Red Prince", an enigmatic reptilian humanoid that was depicted wearing plate mail armor and wielding a scimitar.
As of the , a portrait of the Red Prince could be found within the Waukeens Rest inn.
