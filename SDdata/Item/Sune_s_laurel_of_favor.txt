Sunes Laurel of Favor was a divinely enchanted wreath from the Seldarines Hand citadel on the Spine of the World.
This never-wilting wreath was made out of woven together laurel leaves and roses.
The wearer of this garland was blessed with beauty and charisma, as well as magical protection from harm, similar to that of a +1 enchantment.
Sunes Laurel of Favor was a gift of peace and comradely from the human Church of Sune to the clerics of the Church of Hanali Celanil in the citadel of the Seldarines Hand. The gift helped cementing kinship between the churches and the elven priestesses of Hanali wore Sunes Laurel of Favor every time they were visited by humans.
