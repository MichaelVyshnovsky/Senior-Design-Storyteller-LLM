'''''Lenses of keen sight''''' were a set of magic lenses from [[Waterdeep]], in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Seemingly ordinary spectacles, ''lenses of keen sight'' were two lenses in silvery metallic frames.

==Powers==
''Lenses of keen sight's'' enchantments allowed the wearer to spot weaknesses in their targets. When the wearer landed a strike on one of these imperfections and weak spots, their damage output was increased.

==Notable Owners==
*[[Halbenet Soladonel]]: a [[moon elf]] [[cleric]] of [[Corellon Larethian]] who worked with other warriors of [[Waterdeep]], performing missions on behalf of [[Laeral Silverhand]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
