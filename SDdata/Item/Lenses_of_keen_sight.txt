Lenses of keen sight were a set of magic lenses from Waterdeep, in the late 15 century DR.
Seemingly ordinary spectacles, lenses of keen sight were two lenses in silvery metallic frames.
Lenses of keen sights enchantments allowed the wearer to spot weaknesses in their targets. When the wearer landed a strike on one of these imperfections and weak spots, their damage output was increased.
*Halbenet Soladonel a moon elf cleric of Corellon Larethian who worked with other warriors of Waterdeep, performing missions on behalf of Laeral Silverhand in the late 15 century DR.
