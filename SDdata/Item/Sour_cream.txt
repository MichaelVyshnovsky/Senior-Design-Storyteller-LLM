Sour cream was a dairy product eaten in the Savage Frontier, the Vast, and Tethyr.
In the Western Heartlands and the city of Baldurs Gate, sour cream was often eaten with baguettes.
In the Savage Frontier, sour cream could be found in the town of Yartar. Beldars Rest inn had fried potatoes on offer, flavored with a sauce of parsley, chives, and sour cream.
Further south, sour cream was consumed in Tethyr. Morgans Inn in the city of Velen served highsunfeast of marinated herring topped with spur cream.
In the city of Ravens Bluff on the Dragon Reach, massive two-pound potatoes were filled with butter, cheese, and sour cream. This simple dish was worthy of the nobilitys table, including Lauren Devillarss feasts.
During his visits to Earth to meet Mordenkainen and Rautheene in Ed Greenwoods estate, Elminster was known to have some of the Earths sour cream with potato chips.
