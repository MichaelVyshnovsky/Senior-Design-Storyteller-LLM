Shield amulets were pieces of enchanted jewelry imbued with defensive magics.
Some of these amulets were small pieces of jewelry carved of stone. Each items command word was often engraved on the amulet.
These amulets held several charges of the shield spell, activated with a simple uttering of their command words. When activated, magic shield briefly enveloped the wearer, repelling attacks and completely diffusing magic missiles thrown at it. Each amulet could contain up to 24 charges of the spell.
In the , shield amulets could be purchased at the traveling carnival perched east of the Amnian town of Nashkel.
