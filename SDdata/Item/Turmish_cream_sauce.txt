'''Turmish cream sauce''' was a condiment originating in [[Turmish]] in the [[Vilhon Reach]].

==Description==
It was both creamy and spicy.

==History==
[[Eckert]] cooked and served Turmish cream sauce on pickled [[asparagus]] as a side dish to roast beef, [[carrot]]s, and [[onion]]s for [[Talbot Uskevren]], and [[Lommy]] in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.
