The Judgment of Abd Hikmat was an item that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.
This item was a simple scroll with nonmagical script.
This scroll was the judgment of Abd Hikmat, a long-dead qadi whose name literally meant "slave of wisdom," concerning ownership rights to the pearl banks of Jumlat and Gana. Abd Hikmat ruled that Jumlat broke the Law and awarded more of the banks to Gana.
