'''Whalebone Spiced''' was a type of spirit from the [[Whalebones]] isles, brought to [[Waterdeep]] by the city's many sailors. From there, it spread to the [[Western Heartlands]], along the [[River Chionthar]], and the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
This spiced alcohol was of amber color and sold in wide-bottomed and thin-necked bottles of green [[glass]].
