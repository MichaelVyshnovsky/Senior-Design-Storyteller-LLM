Blackguards gauntlets were a pair of enchanted medium armor ornate-plated gauntlets that were once worn by a blackguard and later by a death knight they became.
Blackguards gauntlets allowed the wearer to cast an empowered version of the inflict wounds spell once per day. Additionally, the gauntlets increased the wearers physical strength and magically guided their hands and weapons towards the targets, similarly to that of +1 offensive enchantments.
In the , blackguards gauntlets were worn by the guardian death knight called That Which Watches, who, along with That Which Guards and That Which Lurks, guarded the entrance to the chamber of the Bhaalist Murder Tribunal in the Undercellar of Baldurs Gate.
