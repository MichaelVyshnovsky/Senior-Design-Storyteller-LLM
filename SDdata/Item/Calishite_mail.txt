Calishite mail was a general term for magic chainmail from Calimshan with the additional quality of keeping the wearer cool in that countrys hot desert climates.
This armor weighed about  and produced an abjuration aura. It could be crafted by a mage trained in the enchantment of magical weapons and armor who could also cast the endure elements spell. 
Calishite mail provided no protection from fire it simply regulated ones body temperature, keeping it at normal levels.
Calishite mail was sought after particularly in the region of the Calim Desert and elsewhere along the Shining Sea. A suit typically sold for 3,840 gold pieces.
