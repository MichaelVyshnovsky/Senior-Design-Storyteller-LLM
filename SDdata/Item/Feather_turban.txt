'''''Feather turbans''''' were wondrously decorative magical apparel that originated in [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.

==Description==
These [[turban]]s were woven with colorful [[peacock]] feathers.

==Powers==
A ''feather turban'' allowed the wearer to create a ''[[gust of wind]]'' three times per day. Additionally it also allowed them to assume ''[[wind shadow]]'' form once per day and summon a ''[[wind carpet]]'' once per month.
