The Earring of Prince Mamoon was an item that could be found in Zakhara, the Land of Fate.
These pieces of jewelry were hoops of jet coral covered with intricate calligraphy.
These earrings were fashioned by Prince Mamoon of the marids. He created them for his students of marqab, a school for shairs in Gana. Anyone who presented one of these earrings gained acceptance into the school.
