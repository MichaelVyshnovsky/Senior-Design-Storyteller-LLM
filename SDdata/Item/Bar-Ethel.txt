Bar-Ethel was a magical sword whose powers were boosted when in combat with undead.
The sword shone and hummed softly. Its name meant "True Death" in some language.
Of a rather common enchantment, the power of Bar-Ethel was boosted four-fold when wielded against the undead.
In , Bar-Ethel was found in the eastern High Priests Cell within the Halls of the Upper Priesthood in the Pyramid of Amun-Re in Raurin. It was guarded by three wraiths.
