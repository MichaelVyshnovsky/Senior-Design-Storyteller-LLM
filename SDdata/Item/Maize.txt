'''Maize''', also known as '''mayz''' or '''corn''', was a cereal grain found in the Realms.

{{quote|Oh, corn fields. Grandpa [[Retsotnert Doogrick|Retsotnert]] once invented a corn-picking machine. Let's just say the memory of whizzing corn ears has never left my nightmares.|[[Purfbin Doogrick]]}}

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where maize was known to be cultivated included:
* In [[interior Faerûn]], maize was cultivated in [[Cormyr]] and [[Sembia]].
* In [[north Faerûn]], maize was cultivated in [[the Dalelands]] and [[Shadowdale]]. In the [[Battledale]] region, it was cultivated in the village of [[Lowdner]].
* In [[northwest Faerûn]], maize was cultivated in the walled abbey of [[Goldenfields]]. In the 15{{th}} century [[DR]] of this region, several tiers of tilled earth were cut into the sloping sides of [[Mount Hamarhaast]] in the [[Ice Spires]]. There maize and many other crops were cultivated by [[yak folk]].
* In [[northeast Faerûn]], a strain known as [[moon corn]] was cultivated in [[Vaasa]].
* In [[west Faerûn]], corn was cultivated by the town of [[Crimmor]].
* In the [[Vilhon Reach]], corn was grown by the farmers of the city-state of [[Lomatra]] of the [[Blade Kingdoms]].
* The land of [[Maztica]].

==Usages==
{{quote|We have grown to adulthood never knowing of mayz until the last few weeks. It is a wondrous plant, but unique to ... "Maztica?"|[[Halloran]] of the [[Golden Legion]], the {{YearlinkName|1361}}}}<br/>
* The [[Azuposi]] people of Maztica regarded cornmeal as sacred and used it in ceremonies.
* In the land of [[Kara-Tur]], some would mash up and ferment corn into a beverage known as [[sake]].
* Corn starch was an ingredient in [[The Bitnez Pavlova Extraordinaire!]].
* Maize flour was the main ingredient for making [[tortilla]]s.
* Powdered corn was a [[material component]] in the [[spell]] ''[[rope trick]]'' as well as ''[[phantom plow]]''.
* Dried corn husks were used as mattress stuffing at [[Nat Wyler's Bell]] inn of [[Phlan]].
* [[Onion cake]]s from the Heartlands, the Vilhon Reach, and the Sword Coast North, were traditional food during the [[Greengrass]]. They were prepared using cornmeal and fried onions.{{Cite web/theedverse/2022-09-27 - Traditional Holiday Confectionary}}

==Rumors & Legends==
* The [[human]] inhabitants of Maztica believed that the [[deity]] [[Qotal]] gifted them maize after having grown jealous from all the worship that the goddess [[Maztica (goddess)|Maztica]] received from them.
