Common hunting traps were shaped like small cages and could capture a variety of animals live. Lethal hunting traps were baited and consisted of opposing blades that sprung closed to decapitate the target for a swift death.
Hunting traps were available for purchase through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue, where the price was based upon the traps size. Traps for rabbits sold for 4 gold pieces, traps for boar for 7 gp, and traps for bears for 12 gp. The lethal variety of a hunting trap, which was effective against any animal, sold for 10 gp.
