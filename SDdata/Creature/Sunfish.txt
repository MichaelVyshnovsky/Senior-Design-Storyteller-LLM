Sunfish was the name given to several species of fresh and saltwater fish.
Species of sunfish had a distinct flat and round shape, making them a popular decorative motif in the ancient Netheril. Sunfish traveled and fed in schools, sometimes found in the shallows, hunting minnows who came up from under the banks towards sunlight.
Freshwater ponds of the forest of Cormanthor was home to a series of sunfish that was considered a game fish by locals. A species of tadpole-sized sunfish inhabited Arabellan ponds and marshes.
Sunfish stew was a fanciful dish prepared for the patriars of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
