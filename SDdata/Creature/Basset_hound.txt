'''Basset hounds''' were a breed of domestic [[dog]] found in the Realms.{{Cite organized play/LC/Dog Days of Summer|18}}{{Cite organized play/LC/The Call of the Wilde/Round Two|16}}

==Description==
In terms of dogs, a basset hound was considered to be of medium size, though they had rather short legs.

==History==
Circa 1370's [[DR]], basset hounds were among the many breeds of puppies being sold in [[Ravens Bluff]] by the pet store [[You Can Buy Love]]. Unbeknownst to the public, these puppies were all once [[rat]]s that had been transformed by the owners with a ''[[wand of polymorph]]''.
