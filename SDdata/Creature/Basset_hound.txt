Basset hounds were a breed of domestic dog found in the Realms.
In terms of dogs, a basset hound was considered to be of medium size, though they had rather short legs.
Circa 1370s DR, basset hounds were among the many breeds of puppies being sold in Ravens Bluff by the pet store You Can Buy Love. Unbeknownst to the public, these puppies were all once rats that had been transformed by the owners with a wand of polymorph.
