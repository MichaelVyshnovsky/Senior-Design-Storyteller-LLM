'''Geonids''' were creatures originating from the [[Elemental Plane of Earth]].

==Behavior==
Geonids were known to enjoy collecting all sorts of curios.

==Society==
===Homelands===
Geonids dwelt primarily in natural caves and enjoyed collecting curios.

A significant number of Geonids dwelt on the [[Snout of Omgar]].
