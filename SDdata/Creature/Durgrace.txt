Durgrace was a rare species of fish that inhabited the Lake Glorfindar in the Starmetal Hills and remained active through the winter months under the lakes ice.
This fish was considered to be a lucky catch due to its growth. Durgrace could grow to a massive size, a lake monster and a fishers dream.
They were edible and were a part of a winters heros feasts served during the annual Simril celebration in Twilight Tor. The feast also consisted of cooked brown bream, blackjaw, and candlefish.
