The Carmathan Red, also called the Carmathan Horse-Ox, was a breed of horse found in Carmathan in the Bloodstone Lands.
The breed of horse was renowned for its strength and durability. They were favored for use as plowhorses.
Carmathan Reds were bred and sold exclusively in the duchy of Carmathan in Damara, usually during Ravensburgs horse market each spring. Their popularity and use had spread throughout the Bloodstone Lands as of the mid&ndash14 century DR.
