A skin kite is an animated slice of skin that is constantly in danger of rotting away.
No two skin kites look identical, as their bodies are made of the skin type and color of their recent prey. It is not uncommon for skin kites to have random clumps of hair, furthering their gruesome appearance. Normally, skin kite weigh  and have a wingspan of .
