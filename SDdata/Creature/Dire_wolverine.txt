'''Dire wolverines''' were a larger and more dangerous variety of the common [[wolverine]].

==Combat==
These beasts were capable of becoming enraged while fighting others.

==Ecology==
Dire wolverines were known to attack settlements, killing livestock and raiding food stores in the process.
