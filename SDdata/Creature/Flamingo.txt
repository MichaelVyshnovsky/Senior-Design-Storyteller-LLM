'''Flamingos''' were aquatic birds known to exist in [[Maztica]].

==Combat==
When attacking, flamingos used their beaks.

==Ecology==
Flamingos, along with other waterfowl such as [[duck]]s, [[goose|geese]], [[stork]]s, and [[ibis]]es, could be found swimming in the large pool on the grounds of the [[Palace of Kukul]] in [[Tulom-Itzi]].
