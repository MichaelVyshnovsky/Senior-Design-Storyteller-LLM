Flame sprites were a variety of sprites, enhanced by elemental fire, exclusively found around Mount Hotenow following the Spellplague.
These sprites had fickle temperaments and an everlasting love of fire.
A group of flame sprites.
Flame sprites typically fought with fire arrows and a type of arrow that would temporarily daze their enemies.
These fey were the result of a well-meaning wizard seeking to provide the sprites that lived around the increasingly dangerous Mount Hotenow an edge in protecting their homes. The wizard used elemental fire from Mount Hotenow itself to enhance them.
Flame sprites sometimes partnered up with adventurers in pursuit of greater fire.
