A dinichthys was a type of gigantic marine fish that could swallow man-sized prey.
Demonic dinichthys A fiendish variant of dinichthys. These were exclusively found within a branch of the River Styx in the Gaping Maw, the layer of the Abyss belonging to Demogorgon. They were impervious to harm from water-based spells and possessed the ability to occasionally gate in a nalfeshnee.
