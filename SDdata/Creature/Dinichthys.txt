A '''dinichthys''' was a type of gigantic marine [[fish]] that could swallow man-sized prey.

==Species==
;[[fiendish creature|Demonic]] dinichthys: A fiendish variant of dinichthys. These were exclusively found within a branch of the [[River Styx]] in the [[Gaping Maw]], the layer of the [[Abyss]] belonging to [[Demogorgon]]. They were impervious to harm from water-based [[spell]]s and possessed the ability to occasionally ''[[gate]]'' in a [[nalfeshnee]].<ref>
