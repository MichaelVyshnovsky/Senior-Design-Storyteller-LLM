'''Thoqqua''' ({{Pronounce|THAK|wa|refs={{Cite dragon/93/Ay pronunseeAYshun gyd|30}}}}), also called '''rock worms''', were worm-like [[elemental]]s native to the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]].{{Cite web enhancement/Deity Do's and Don'ts|13,15}}

==Description==
A thoqqua's long body was hot enough to melt rock.

==Behavior==
Thoqqua were foul-tempered. They spent most of their time burrowing underground in search of minerals to eat.

==Combat==
Thoqqua used their bodies to slam their enemies. This was particularly effective because the touch of a thoqqua resulted in fire damage.

==Society==
Thoqqua were natives of the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]]

They were usually found alone or in pairs.
