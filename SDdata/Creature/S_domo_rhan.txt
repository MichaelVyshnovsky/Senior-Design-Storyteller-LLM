The sdomo rhan was a giant beetle that was said to dwell on the Naktong Moors of Koryo.No game statistics are given for the sdomo rhan. It is unknown if it is a unique creature or a race of creatures. It is similar to the dokufu, a spider that transforms into an old woman, in Oriental Adventures (3rd edition).
The sdomo rhan had the power to transform into a beautiful woman, in which form it would lure men to their dooms.
