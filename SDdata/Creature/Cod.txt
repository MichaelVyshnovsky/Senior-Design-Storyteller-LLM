Cod were a mundane species of fish found in the Realms.
The flesh of these fish was white in coloration and had a flaky quality.
Polar cod
A species of cod endemic to the arctic waters of the Ahtahqugotak Sea.
Rock cod
A species of cod found in the Sea of Fallen Stars.
Cod were quite common all across the Sea of Moving Ice.
The cod was a popular catch among both fisherman and markets in the North. Cooked cod was a popular food item, Cod tongue was considered a delicacy in the Nelanther Islands. However, those individuals who actually tried it thought otherwise. Sea chips were made from pressed and fried cod, and served as snacks at the Sharks Finn in Ravens Bluff.
These fish were the namesake of Cod Lane, a road in the city of Waterdeep.
