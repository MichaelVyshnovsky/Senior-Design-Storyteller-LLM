'''Warthogs''' were a species of [[pig]]-like omnivores related to [[boar]]s.

==Description==
Warthogs had large tusks that they used to defend themselves. They measured {{SI|2.5|ft|long=yes}} tall at the shoulder.

==Personality==
Warthogs were aggressive creatures that often fought to the death, though only were aggressive when threatened or cornered or if their territory was threatened.

==Ecology==
===Habitats===
Warthogs were near exclusively found in warm climates.

On the continent of [[Faerûn]], warthogs could be found in the [[Midwood]] and [[Starwood]] reaches of the [[Cormanthor]] forest,<ref> in the [[Calim Desert]], in the [[Plain of Standing Stones]],<ref> and in the [[Wyllowwood]] of [[Undermountain]].

Beyond Faerûn they could be found on the continent of [[Zakhara]], inhabiting badlands, forests, and hills. There they were commonly hunted by [[jungle giant]]s.<ref>

Beyond the [[Prime Material plane|Prime Material]], in the [[plane]] of [[Qui]], a field in sector five of the plane was populated with a herd of warthogs during the Season of the Unicorn.<ref>
