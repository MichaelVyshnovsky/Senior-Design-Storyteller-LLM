A '''berrygobbler''' was a small, scurrying, [[wikipedia:en:Chipmunk|chipmunk]]-like rodent common in the [[Heartlands]] and other areas of [[Faerûn]].

In warmer climates of Faerûn, such as [[Calimshan]] or [[Tashalar]], there were black-furred and speedy berrygobblers called "'''slinks'''".
