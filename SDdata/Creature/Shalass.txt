The shalass, sometimes referred to as the silver shalass, was a type of fish endemic to the North.
The shalass bore a resemblance to trout, were covered in silver scales, and on average grew to be  in length.
These fish were considered to be a delicacy throughout the North.
According to Volothamp Geddarm, they were best caught in a particular type of fish basket known as a cone net. However, expert fisherfolk were known to simply stab them with spears from a bank or raft.
The shalass were primarily known to inhabit the Dessarin River. and Laughingflow.
* The Red Rune Inn was claimed by many to make the finest shalass stew in all of Faerun.
