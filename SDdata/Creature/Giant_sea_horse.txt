'''Giant sea horses''' were a large version of their smaller kin.

==Description==
They looked like large sea horses of flamboyant color, with a long body and a curled tail.

==Behavior==
Giant sea horses were naturally shy creatures and try to avoid contact with surface creatures.

==Combat==
Giant sea horses defended themselves by headbutting an opponent.

==Ecology==
===Diet===
These creatures were a herbivorous species.
===Habitats===
Giant sea could potentially be found in nearly all but the coldest of oceans.

They could often be found in the seas and ocean around the [[Moonshae Isles]].
===Relationships===
[[Triton]]s and giant sea horses had good relationships. They also were [[sea elves]]' favored mounts and could be trained in combat. [[Locathah]] were also known to occasionally capture and train them.

Giant sea horses were sometimes found as courtiers or servants in the [[Citadel of Ten Thousand Pearls|palace]] of [[Kalbari al-Durrat al-Amwaj ibn Jari]].

A giant sea horse could carry up to 450 pounds and drag up to 2,250 pounds.
