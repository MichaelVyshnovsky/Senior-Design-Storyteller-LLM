'''Tanystropheus''' ({{Pronounce|tan|is|TRO|fee|us|refs={{Cite dragon/93/Ay pronunseeAYshun gyd|30}}}}) was a species of [[dinosaur]].

==Description==
Tanystropheus had a long, serpentine body with four thin legs. Most of their body was composed of its neck. It had a small head and long jaws filled with sharp teeth.

==Combat==
Tanystropheus were ambush predators. They hid just under the water, waiting for prey. Then launched themselves from the water and grabbed onto their target, dragging it back under. It was strong enough to drag prey that weighed up to {{SI|500|lb|k}}.

==Abilities==
Tanystropheus were excellent swimmers due to their webbed rear feet.

==Ecology==
Tanystropheus were carnivorous and solitary creatures.
===Habitats===
Tanystropheus were most often encountered in rivers and swamps. On the continent of [[Kara-Tur]], they could be found on the [[Malatran Plateau]] in the [[Riving of Laughing Idols]].<ref>{{Cite organized play/LJ/The Soulless Shu|4}}
