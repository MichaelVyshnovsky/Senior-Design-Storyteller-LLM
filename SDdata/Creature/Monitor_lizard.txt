'''Monitor lizards''' were a species of large carnivorous [[reptile]]s.

==Combat==
These lizards could be aggressive, using their jaws to attack enemies or tear at prey. 

==Ecology==
Many monitor lizards were quite capable swimmers and silent stalkers.
===Habitats===
These creatures could be found in [[northwest Faerûn]], in the swamp known as the [[Mere of Dead Men]],{{Cite dragon/258/Voaraghamanthar, "the Black Death"}} as well as the [[Eastern Shaar]] of [[southeast Faerûn]].
===Relationships===
Monitor lizards were sometimes tamed by [[troglodyte]]s for use as guard animals of their warrens.

==History==
During the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]], the  [[Ginger Palace]] on the [[Dragon Coast]] boasted an outdoor reptile park that was home to several monitor lizards.
