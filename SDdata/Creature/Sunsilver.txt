'''Sunsilver''' were a variety of fish in [[Aglarond]].

==Description==
They were tiny and bright fish.

==Behavior==
They liked to snatch gnats from the air.

==History==
In the {{yearlink|1246}}, sunsilver fish were kept in the gardens of the Royal Palace of [[Aglarond]].
