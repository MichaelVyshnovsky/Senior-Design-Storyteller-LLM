A weasel was a small furry mammal.
Like ferrets, these animals were small, slender-bodied predators.
Weasels could be extremely aggressive but chose to attack smaller prey.
Giant weasel The over-sized cousins of the standard weasel.
In the land of Kara-Tur wild weasels could be found in its temperate forests, plains, and swamps. As well as in its more arctic regions.
These animals could be trained and domesticated.
Weasels were a favored animal of the deities Garagos, Garl Glittergold, and Thoth.
* Brakar, the familiar of Chaid al Farid al Fuqani.
* Tamin, the familiar of a sorceress Qara, who traveled with Kalach-Cha circa 1374 DR.
* Some Ysgardian dwarves could assume the form of a weasel.
