A '''weasel''' was a small furry mammal.

==Description==
Like [[ferret]]s, these animals were small, slender-bodied predators.{{Cite dragon/137/Treasures of the Wilds|17}}

==Behavior==
Weasels could be extremely aggressive but chose to attack smaller prey.

==Sub-Species==
;[[Giant weasel]]: The over-sized cousins of the standard weasel.

==Ecology==
===Habitats===
In the land of [[Kara-Tur]] wild weasels could be found in its temperate forests, plains, and swamps. As well as in its more arctic regions.
===Relationships===
These animals could be trained and domesticated.{{Cite dragon/84/A cast of strange familiars|17}}

Weasels were a [[favored animal]] of the [[deities]] [[Garagos]], [[Garl Glittergold]], and [[Thoth]].{{Cite web enhancement/Deity Do's and Don'ts|10-15}}

==Notable weasels==
* [[Brakar (weasel)|Brakar]], the familiar of [[Chaid al Farid al Fuqani]].
* [[Tamin]], the familiar of a sorceress [[Qara]], who traveled with [[Kalach-Cha]] circa [[1374 DR]].

==Trivia==
* Some [[Ysgardian dwarves]] could assume the form of a weasel.
