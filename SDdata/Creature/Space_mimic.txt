'''Space mimics''' were [[wildspace]]-based counterparts to [[mimic]]s. Unlike the standard mimic, they could cast [[arcane magic]].

==Notable Space Mimics==
A space mimic was among the staff of [[Feldyn]]'s [[Old Monster Shop]] in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Southern Ward of Waterdeep|Southern Ward]].
