'''Eastern vampires''' were a different breed of [[vampire]] that had the ability to turn [[invisibility|invisible]] at will. However, unlike their western cousins, they could not take on a gaseous form or charm their victims.

==Habitat==
Eastern vampires were known to lair in the vicinity of nomadic settlements in the steppes of [[High Meadows]], [[Hordelands]].

==Notable Eastern Vampires==
* [[Xi-Chu]] was a vampire who ruled over the [[Taring]] village of [[Gigit]] in [[Kutuk Valley]] in [[Shou Lung]] in [[1358 DR]].
