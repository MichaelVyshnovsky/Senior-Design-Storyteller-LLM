'''Portal drakes''' were a type of [[drake]] that dwelled in the [[Underdark]] and spent their time looking for magical items.

==Description==
Portal drakes were the size of a "large housecat." They had gray wings.

==Combat==
Portal drakes used their magical abilities to swap spaces with their targets or to ''[[teleport]]'' their targets into the air over a pit or cliff.

==Notable Portal Drakes==
* [[Preelix]], a portal drake living in [[Undermountain]].
