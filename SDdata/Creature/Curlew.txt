'''Curlews''' were a species of bird found in the Realms.

==Description==
These birds had the long, curved style of beak typical of birds that were excellent fishers.

==Ecology==
===Diet===
Curlews were an omnivorous species. Primarily eating various berries, grains, insects, and seeds.
