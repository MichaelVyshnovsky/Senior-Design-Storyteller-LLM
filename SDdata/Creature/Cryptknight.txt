'''Cryptknights''' were [[undead]] unique to the [[Tomb of Martek]]. They had an aura of ''[[fear]]'' about them.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|121}}

==Description==
Most cryptknights were white-robed, strange figures.In Module TSR 09054, I5 Lost Tomb of Martek, which was adapted into Desert of Desolation, there is an entry for Cryptknights: they are stated to resemble the people they were in life, they are only semi-intelligent, and they are medium-sized.

==Abilities==
All cryptknights continuously radiated ''[[fear]]''. Their capabilities for physical damage varied somewhat. Some of them were immune to nonmagical weapons.In Module TSR 09054, I5 Lost Tomb of Martek, which was adapted into Desert of Desolation, there is an entry for Cryptknights: they lack an immunity to nonmagical weapons, but some of the cryptknights in the module have such an immunity. It is also stated that 70% of cryptknights have an aura of fear, though 100% of the cryptknights in the module have it.

==History==
In life, one cryptknight was killed at the same time [[Mobius Tower]] became time-trapped, while trying to assassinate the Sheik.In Module TSR 09054, I5 Lost Tomb of Martek, which was adapted into Desert of Desolation, there is an entry for Cryptknights: they are stated to be created from people who died as they became time-trapped in the Tower of Mobius. It is also stated that some of them found their way out of the tower.
