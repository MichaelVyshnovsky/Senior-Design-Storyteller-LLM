'''Dogfish''' were a mundane species of [[fish]] in the Realms.

==Ecology==
In [[Cormanthor]], these fish could be found in the [[River Ashaba]]. Beyond there, they could be found in parts of the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]], such as around the [[Pirate Isles]].<ref>{{Cite dungeon/66/Operation Manta Ray|49}}
