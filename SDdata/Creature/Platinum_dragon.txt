'''Platinum dragons''' were the rarest of [[metallic dragon]]s; in fact, most believed that there was only one platinum dragon. They represented the archetype of good-aligned metallic dragons.

== Description ==
Platinum dragons had platinum scales and were huge, even by dragon standards. Their bodies and eyes radiated powerful auras of light, sometimes making onlookers unable to discern the color of their scales. Two of the only three known platinum dragons on [[Realmspace]] were wingless wyrms.

A rare few [[Dragonborn of Bahamut|dragonborn]] [[sorcerer]]s who drew their magic through their [[Sorcerer#Dragon_sorcerer|draconic heritage]] were able to manifest traits of platinum dragons as well.

==History==
[[Bahamut]] was the only platinum dragon known to most non-dragon scholars, and they believed he was a unique type of dragon. Even so, those scholars were wrong about this&mdash;Bahamut was actually unique among his kind for the fact that he had wings{{Cite polyhedron/73/Bahamut and Tiamat|19}}

== Known Platinum Dragons ==
All known platinum dragons were members of the [[Draconic pantheon]].
* [[Bahamut]]
* [[Lendys]]
* [[Tamara]]

==Rumors & Legends==
King [[Strohm IV]] of [[Tethyr]] was said to have been protected by a platinum dragon, but who this dragon was is lost to history.
