Warblers were a variety of songbird that could be found on Toril.
Pine warbler A species that was native to the Land of the Lions. These warblers were noted for being blue-throated and having lovely voices.
Tereep A species that was native to the Silver Marches. These small warblers were noted and named for their particularly loud call.
The existence of warblers on Toril could be traced as far back as the days of Netheril.
