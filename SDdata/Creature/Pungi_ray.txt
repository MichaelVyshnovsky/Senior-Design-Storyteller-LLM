The spines on the backs of pungi rays were lined with poison.
Pungi rays lived largely solitary lives, though occasionally they could be found in pairs or groups of three.
Like all species of ray, pungi rays were carnivorous creatures. They primarily fed upon mollusks and crustaceans.
Like most species of ray, pungi rays inhabited tropical shallow waters.
In the Realms, they were only known to be found in the land of Zakhara.
