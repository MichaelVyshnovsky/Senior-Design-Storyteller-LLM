A bonegouge assassin was a type of demon found within the Abyss. Bonegouge assassins were rumored to originally be creations of Orcus, and were wholly devoted to whatever task they were bid to complete. Bonegouge assassins were unique in their loyalty and the skill and determination with which they completed their tasks, which set them apart from others of their kind.
The bonegouge assassins were able to make use of their natural stealth and their ability to produce clouds of smoke in combat.
Bonegouge assassins relied on their claws, bone darts, and the element of surprise when fighting their enemies. Bonegouge assassins were able to teleport in combat to ensure that they retained superiority on the field.
