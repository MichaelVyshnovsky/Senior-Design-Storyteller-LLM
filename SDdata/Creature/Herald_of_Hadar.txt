Heralds of Hadar were a type of star spawn created by the dying star Hadar.
Hadars spawn were typically described as sinister, even fiendish, humanoids.
Heralds of Hadar could drain away healing magic and life energy to strengthen themselves.
Heralds attacked with their claws and some strange, but nasty, magic.
Heralds of Hadar fed on life energy, channeling it back to their creator as Hadar attempted to prevent its demise.
