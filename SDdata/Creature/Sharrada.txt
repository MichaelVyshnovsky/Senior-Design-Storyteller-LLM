Sharrada () were a type of cattle common around the the town of Eveningstar in Cormyr.
Sharrada were known for their long horns and shaggy fur.
The animals were known to be capable of surviving winters far harsher than a human might survive.
Sharrada were beef cattle raised for their meat. They were kept in the walled fields of the High Pasture near the border of the Stonelands.
