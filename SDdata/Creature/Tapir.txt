'''Tapirs''' were a largely peaceful species of mammal, related to [[boar]]s.<ref group="note" name="note1" />

==Description==
These creatures had wide mouths, dull teeth, and soft hooves.

==Abilities==
Tapirs were adept at swimming and could remain underwater for several minutes.

==Combat==
These creatures were ill-equipped for combat. If cornered, they would resort to biting.

==Ecology==
===Habitats===
Tapirs could be found in tropical forests, living along rivers.{{Cite polyhedron/102/Tribes of the Nubari|11}} and in the jungles and tropical forests of [[Maztica]],<ref> such as the [[Narabatun Jungle]].<ref>

Beyond [[Toril]], tapirs could be found in the gardens of the legendary [[Spelljammer (spelljammer)|Spelljammer]].
===Relationships===
These creatures were commonly hunted on the Malatran Plateau by the [[Simbara]] tribe of [[Nubari]].
