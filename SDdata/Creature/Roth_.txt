Rothé () were herd animals and one of the primary livestock of humans in Faerûns northern areas and of the drow of the Underdark below.
These magical beasts resembled musk-oxen.
The Axe of Mirabar, the militia of the city of Mirabar, rode rothé in the winter.
Many in the Realms were known to make strings for instruments from the dried intestines of rothé and their horns were hollowed out and turned into drinking implements.
Rothé back steaks were a staple of dwarven cuisine in the areas where the beasts were present.
Rothe where sheared for their wool.
 Surface rothé  Herd species living in northern Faerûn.
 Deep rothé  This sub-species communicated with magical flashing lights in the Underdark.
 Ghost rothé  These rothé were larger than average, and were white with magical abilities that grant them transportation and silence.
