A wren was a type of passerine bird.
Needle wrens kept the locust population in check in the rimwood area of the forest of Cormanthor south of Shadowdale. They were received gratefully by local farmers. In the , a huge swarm of fyreflies caused a fire that destroyed many of the pines in which the wrens lived, causing fears among the farmers that a locust plague would result.
* Cormanthor, rimwood (needle)
A wren was the smallest creature a caster could change into with the help of the polymorph self spell.
