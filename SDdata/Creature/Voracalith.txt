A voracalith was a type of demon found within the Abyss. Voracaliths were famed for their gluttony, and were known to roam the Abyss devouring everything in their way. Voracaliths were simple minded, and were often lured in to fights by various demon lords using fresh meat.
Voracaliths were able to make use of tentacles in combat, and had a natural acid within their stomachs.
Voracaliths fought with the intention of pulling away enemies and devouring them. Voracaliths did not make use of any tactics.
