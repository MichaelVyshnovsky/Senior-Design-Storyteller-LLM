A '''nothosaurus''' ({{Pronounce|noth|o|SAR|us|refs={{Cite dragon/93/Ay pronunseeAYshun gyd|28}}}}) was a species of ichthyosaur.
==Description==
Nothosaurs were large marine creatures. They had large flippers that were strong enough to allow them to "walk" on land. They had a long neck that was capable of moving very rapidly.
==Behaviour==
Nothosaurs were known for being very aggressive.
==Combat==
Nothosaurs attacked prey with great speed. 
==Habitat==
Nothosaurs were usually encountered in tropical oceans and seas. 
==Biology==
Nothosaurs were carnivorous and solitary creatures. They were air breathers and were often seen laying on rocks under the sun, soaking up some sun after a hunt.
