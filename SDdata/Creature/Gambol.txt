Gambols were large baboon-like primates found in jungles.
A typical gambol stood  tall and weighed upwards of . Silky blue hair covered their purple skin. Their faces were bare and elongated into a muzzle.
Gambols were fiercely territorial creatures that would attack anything entering their claimed area on sight. They constantly prowled their territory to ensure its security.
Gambols were ferocious when engaged in combat. They used their impressive reach to claw and bite their targets. Ranged attacks on gambols were difficult since they never stopped moving. Their innate agility made it easy for them to dodge incoming attacks as well.
These creatures were capably of blasting out a terrifying howl that would cause most creatures within  to panic for several minutes. Creatures could only fall victim to this sonic attack once per day.
Gambols could cover distances quickly by dashing, climbing, or leaping.
Gambols were carnivorous creatures that considered anything smaller than themselves to be a prey item.
Gambols were present on the Chultan Peninsula, prowling the stretch of the Black Jungles that pressed through the Dolsel Gap in to western Thindol.
