An '''arrowhawk''' was a creature from the [[Elemental Plane of Air]].

==Description==
Arrowhawks had four wings and four eyes (one pair on either side of its beak). They were covered in blue scales with the occasional tuft of yellow feathers. Adults (11â€“40 years old) were about {{SI|10|ft|long=yes}} in length, with a wingspan of {{SI|15|ft|long=yes}}. Adults weighed about {{SI|100|lbs|k}}.

==Abilities==
Arrowhawks had the ability to fire a ray of electricity at their enemies.

==Ecology==
Arrowhawks were able to fly as soon as they hatched and the majority spent their entire lives in the air without ever setting down. Their ability to [[levitate]] aided them in flight.
