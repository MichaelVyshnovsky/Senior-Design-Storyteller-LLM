The '''diatryma''' ({{Pronounce|dee|ah|TRY|mah|refs=}}) was an ancient large, flightless bird native to the [[Underdark]].

==Description==
The gigantic bird was about {{SIrange|8|12|ft|long=yes}} tall.

==Ecology==
The [[drow]] used their hugely plumed feathers for decoration. known as a ''[[feather of diatryma summoning]]''. By blowing into the feather, Jarlaxle was able to summon a diatryma bird.<ref>
