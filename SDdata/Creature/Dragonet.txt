The '''dragonets''' were a group of tiny lesser [[dragon]]s.

==Description==
Dragonets as a whole were very small, with most being less than {{SI|4|ft}} in length; only the mole dragonet was larger. They tended to be draconic in form.

==Abilities==
Dragonets were intelligent, possessed of differing magical abilities, and capable of becoming mages' familiars.

==Types of Dragonets==
There were many different types of dragonets.

*The [[Crow's-nest dragon]], also called the spiretop dragon or spiretop drake.<ref><ref>
*[[Draken]]
*[[Faerie dragon]]
*[[Firedrake (dragonet)|Firedrake]]
*[[Geyser dragon]]
*[[Mole dragon]]
*[[Pavilion dragon]]
*[[Pseudodragon]]

==Ecology==
Dragonets inhabited a variety of terrains in a range of ecological niches.
