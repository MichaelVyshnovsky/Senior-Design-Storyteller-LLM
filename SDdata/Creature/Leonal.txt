Leonals were the mightiest kind of guardinal. They lived in Elysium and the House of Nature.
Leonals resembled muscular, humanoid lions of regal appearance.
Leonals had a variety of spell-like abilities and were incredibly fast and agile. 
Talisid the Celestial Lion was the leader of both the leonals and all the guardinals. However, some leonals would serve Mystra on occasion.
Around 1373 DR, a leonal was roaming the Shaar.
