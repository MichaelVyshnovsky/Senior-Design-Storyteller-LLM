'''Shadow snakes''' were large, venomous snakes that lived in the jungles of southern [[Faer√ªn]].{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|668}} They may have had some connection to the [[Shadowfell]]. [[Yuan-ti]] were said to keep shadow snakes as pets.

==Description==
Shadow snakes were twice as long as a [[human]] and had greyish-black skin that was shadowy in substance.{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|668}}

==Combat==
Shadow snakes were ambush predators that surprised prey by stepping through shadows.{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|674}} The bite of this venomous snake could turn a victim's limb into a shadowy substance.{{Cite digital book/Venom in Her Veins/Kindle|668}}

==History==
Shadow snakes were thought to have been the creation of the god [[Zehir]]. For this reason, they were greatly revered by the yuan-ti.
