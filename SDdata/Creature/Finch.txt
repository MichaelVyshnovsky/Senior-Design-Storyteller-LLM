A finch () was a species of songbird that could be found in the Realms.
Golden finches a species of finches with colorful plumage. They could be found in Cormyrs Farsea Marshes and Tun Marshes, as well as the Black Plain of Sembia.
Snow finches
In general, finches were known to inhabit the Starwood area of Cormanthor. In Kara-tur, multi-colored finches could be found inhabiting the Valley of Wings.
* The staff of birdcalls allowed its wielder to mimic the call of a finch.
