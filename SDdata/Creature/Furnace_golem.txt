'''Furnace golems''' were a variant of [[iron golem]] used in [[Wildspace]]. By consuming [[magical item]]s, they were able to [[spelljamming|spelljam]].

==Notable Furnace Golems==
A furnace golem acted as a bouncer for the [[Honored Mages' Guild of Bral]], barring non-mages from entering.
