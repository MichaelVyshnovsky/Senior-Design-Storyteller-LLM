A '''badger mouse''' was a [[mouse]]-like rodent roughly the size of a [[badger]]. They were native to the [[Duskwood]] and were most abundant during the spring, when [[ash wheat]] was in good supply. Badger mice were preyed upon by [[puff adder]]s.<ref>
