'''Pompano''' were a mundane species of [[fish]] that inhabited the [[Dragon Reach]].

==Usage==
* [[The Painted Boat]], a restaurant in the Dragon Reach off the southern edge of the [[Ravens Bluff/Harbor District|Harbor District]] of [[Ravens Bluff]], was supplied with pompano by ''[[The Flying Fish]]'', a single mainsail sloop owned by the establishment. Pompano were offered among the restaurants menu of fish at a cost of 2 [[silver piece]]s.{{Cite polyhedron/79/The Living City: The Painted Boat Restaurant|15,17}}
