'''Mahi-mahi''' was a species of saltwater fish inhabiting the [[Great Sea]] and the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]].

==Availability==
In the city of [[Ravens Bluff]], on the [[Dragon Reach]] in the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]], mahi-mahi was served at the [[Shark Fin]] restaurant for 3 [[silver]] [[coin]]s per serving.

In the nation of [[Dambrath]], mahi-mahi was fished from the calm waters of the [[Bay of Dancing Dolphins]] of the [[Great Sea]].
