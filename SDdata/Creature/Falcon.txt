'''Falcons''' were small birds of prey, often used in hunting.

==Description==
Falcons looked similar to [[hawk]]s but were smaller in size.

==Ecology==
===Diet===
Falcons were obligate carnivores. Larger falcons were known to prey on small [[humanoid]]s.
==Habitat==
Falcons made their nest in trees and in underground burrows. 
===Relationshps===
Falcons were a favored animal of the [[Mulhorandi pantheon|Mulhorandi]] god [[Anhur]] and the [[halfling]] god [[Arvoreen]]. They were sacred to the goddesses [[Milil]] and [[Red Knight]] as well.{{Cite web enhancement/Deity Do's and Don'ts|10,13}} 

[[Moon elf|Moon elves]] had a special fondness for falcons and often kept them as pets. [[Centaur]]s within the forest of [[Cormanthor]] kept small flocks of falcons for use as guards and scouts.<ref> In some [[human]] lands, such as [[Cormyr]], formal education among the aristocracy included falconry. Some [[ranger]]s kept falcons as animal companions.{{Cite dungeon/75/Into the Nest of Vipers|66}}
===Usages===
At the market, a well-trained falcon could be sold for up to 1,000 [[gp]].
