'''Doomspinner spiders''' were a type of monstrously sized [[spider]] that emerged in the Realms following the [[Spellplague]].

==Abilities==
These creatures had a [[poison]]ous bite and they spun elaborate webs to catch their prey with.

==History==
Around the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], doomspinner spiders could be found being used by the [[List of drow houses|drow house]] known as [[House Xorlarrin]].
