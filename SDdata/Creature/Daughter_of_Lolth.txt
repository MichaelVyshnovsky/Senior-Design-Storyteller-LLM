A Daughter of Lolth, also referred to as Lolths daughter, was a type of massive spider that could be found throughout the Sword Coast North region of Faerûn. They appeared in underground locations deep beneath the earth, and were used by drow during their raids on the surface world.
These enormous, bloated spiders were nearly as tall as some men.
These spiders could spin a large group of webbing in a broad area in order to trap its prey in place. In addition to slashing with its forelegs,
they could also launch a blast of caustic substance from its mouth.

One of these spiders was known to dwell within the depths of Cragmire Crypts.
