Memedi were lesser spirits native to Kara-Tur.
Memedi were incorporeal spirits with several different forms.
Djim Djim were the spirits of priests and looked like ancient humans wearing religious robes.
Djangkong Djangkong resembled human skeletons with transparent bones.
Gendruwo Gendruwo were mischievous spirits and were able to shape-shift.
Panaspati Panaspati resembled humans but instead their heads were between their legs.
Setan gundul Setan gundul appeared as small human children with topknots.
Uwil Uwil were the spirits of deceased sohei.
Wedon Wedon appeared as humans covered completely in white sheets made from silk.
