Death dogs were vicious and hateful two-headed dogs who had a taste for humanoid flesh.
Death dogs had a deep burning hate for everything. They were aggressive and attacked travelers and explorers on sight.
A death dogs saliva had a potent poison that caused a rotting disease on a creatures flesh. Left unattended, it brought death in a matter of days.
* The wizard Draven Millovyr kept his possessions guarded by a glyph of warding spell that summoned death dogs against trespassers.
