'''Giant goats''' were a rare, larger than usual species of [[goat]].
==Description==
On average a giant goat grew to be {{SI|5|ft}} tall at the shoulder. They grew long, sharp horns.

==Combat==
When confronting opponents, giant goats mimicked their smaller kin by charging and ramming with their horns.
==Ecology==
Giant goats were rather reclusive.
===Diet===
Giant goats were a herbivorous species.
===Habitats===
These creatures were typically found in hilly regions.

Giant goats were known to inhabit the [[Stonelands]],{{Cite adventurers league/In Dire Need|13}} and [[Deadstone Cleft]].
===Relationships===
Some creatures were known to domesticate and herd giant goats, such as [[stone giant]]s.

Occasionally giant goats were used as [[mount|mounts]], especially by [[druid]]s that frequented mountainous regions.{{Cite dragon/209/Branching Out All Over|14}}
