Giant goats were a rare, larger than usual species of goat.
On average a giant goat grew to be  tall at the shoulder. They grew long, sharp horns.
When confronting opponents, giant goats mimicked their smaller kin by charging and ramming with their horns.
Giant goats were rather reclusive.
Giant goats were a herbivorous species.
These creatures were typically found in hilly regions.
Giant goats were known to inhabit the Stonelands, and Deadstone Cleft.
Some creatures were known to domesticate and herd giant goats, such as stone giants.
Occasionally giant goats were used as mounts, especially by druids that frequented mountainous regions.
A giant goat could be summoned by the conjure animals spell.
