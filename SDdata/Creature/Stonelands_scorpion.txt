'''Stonelands scorpions''' were a species of [[giant scorpion]]s that inhabited crags in the [[Stonelands]] of [[Cormyr]].

==Description==
These giant arachnids had orange and yellow carapace two relatively small pincers, and a large [[venom]]ous tail stinger.

==Combat==
Stonelands scorpions hunted in small swarms and liked to ambush their prey from under the ground after burying them selves in the dirt. They attacked straightforward manner, using their claws and attempting to pierce their victims with the venomous stingers.

==History==
In the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], a number of Stonelands scorpions was encountered by a group of heroes from the world of [[Eberron]] who were sent to rescue missing adventurers from a recently discovered ancient tomb.
