A '''moorhound''' was a huge, shaggy breed of [[dog]] with long legs and powerful muscles. 

==Description==
On average, this breed's back was up to the height of a [[human]] man's waist and their bodies were {{SI|5|ft|long=yes}} long.

==Ecology==
===Habitats===
In the years following [[1385 DR]], packs of wild moorhounds became prevalent across [[Corwell]], the pastoral heartland of [[Gwynneth]] Isle.{{Cite dragon/376/Realmslore: Sarifal|64}}
