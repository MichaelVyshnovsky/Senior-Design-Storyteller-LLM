A moorhound was a huge, shaggy breed of dog with long legs and powerful muscles. 
On average, this breeds back was up to the height of a human mans waist and their bodies were  long.
In the years following 1385 DR, packs of wild moorhounds became prevalent across Corwell, the pastoral heartland of Gwynneth Isle.
