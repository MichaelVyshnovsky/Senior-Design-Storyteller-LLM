'''Satabayi''' were a type of insect that lived in [[Chult]].{{Cite adventurers league/A City on the Edge|25}}

==Description==
Satabayi were flying insects that gathered in swarms. Satabayi were carriers of a [[disease]] that they passed onto humanoids. The disease was noted for causing dizziness and affecting the eyesight of those afflicted.

==History==
During the events of the [[death curse]] around [[1490 DR]],{{ToA-time}} they could be found on the outskirts of [[Port Nyanzaru]] in Chult.
