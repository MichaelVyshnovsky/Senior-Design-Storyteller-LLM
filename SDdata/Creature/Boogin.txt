Boogins were crossbreeds of orcs and quaggoths.
Boogins looked like large orcs with shaggy fur.
In the , a band of five boogins led by the half-orc Grunch were conducting a search in the caverns beneath Old Skull Tor.
By the , the boogin population was increasing in the far northern Upperdark, as well as the Underdark beneath Cormanthyr.
