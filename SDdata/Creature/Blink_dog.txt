A '''blink dog''' was a mysterious, intelligent [[Dog|canine]] creature. Most blink dogs had yellow-brown fur and large ears. They were well known for their ability to ''[[blink]]''â€”a supernatural [[teleportation]] ability.

==Abilities==
Blink dogs had an average intelligence. They had powerful senses, including the ability to see in low light and see in the dark, as well as possessing acute scent detection and tracking.

A blink dog's most well-known ability was its ability to ''blink'', as well as use ''[[dimension door]]''.

==Society==
===Languages===
Blink dogs spoke their own language. 
===Relationships===
They had a long hatred of [[displacer beast]]s, and would attack them on sight.

Blink dogs were a [[favored monster]] of the deities [[Baravar Cloakshadow]], [[Marthammor Duin]], and [[Urogalan]].{{Cite web enhancement/Deity Do's and Don'ts|10, 13, 15}}
