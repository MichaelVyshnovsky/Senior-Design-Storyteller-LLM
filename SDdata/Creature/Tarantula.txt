'''Tarantulas''', also known as '''reikh'irud''' in [[Anauroch]], were a mundane type of [[spider]].

==Description==
These spiders were covered in dark brown hair.

==Behavior==
Tarantulas were generally not aggressive unless disturbed or accidentally agitated.

==Ecology==
===Habitats===
Tarantulas could be found in the lands of Anauroch In the [[Raurin]] desert, both [[giant spider|giant]] and [[huge spider|huge]] varieties of tarantula could be found.

===Usages===
The [[hishnashaper]]s of Maztica used the dried-up bodies of tarantulas to create a type of [[construct]] known as a [[spiderwalker]].

Skilled [[alchemist]]s often utilized tarantulas in the formation of [[poison]]s that caused paralyzation in a victim.
