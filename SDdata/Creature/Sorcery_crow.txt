Sorcery crows were red-feathered songbirds with the ability to see into the Weave. These creatures were often kept as alarms that detected magic effects.
A sorcery crow resembled a scarlet red crow with glowing eyes.
When trained to do so, sorcery crows squawked whenever they detected magic within a  radius. Some sorcery crows were trained to only do so within a range of .
In the late 14 century, sometime after the destruction of the Soulmonger, the Red Wizards of Thay-operated Crawling Palace of Ras Nsi used sorcery crows as part of its defenses.
Mother Sibonseni, Waukeenar high priestess of the Hall of Gold in Port Nyanzaru, kept sorcery crows in the entry hall of her villa.
While stationed in the ruins of Mezro, Flame Brokenbarrel of the Flaming Fist kept a sorcery crow as security.
