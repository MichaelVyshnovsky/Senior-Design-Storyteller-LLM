'''Prawns''' were small aquatic crustaceans commonly fished for food across [[Faer√ªn]].

==Usages==
* In [[Cormyr]], [[prawn-and-cress sandwich]]es were a popular hors d'oeuvre, consumed by the nobility as of the 14{{th}} century [[DR]] and the [[Dragontriumph Hall]] was known for its greasy [[batter-fried prawn]]s in the 15{{th}} century DR.
* In a Cormyrean city of [[Crimmor]] the [[Old Wheel]] feasthall served [[longbean-and-radish salad]] dressed in [[groundspear]]s juice, [[mint]], and prawn mix.{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|30}}
* In the city of [[Westgate]], [[pan-fried prawn]]s were a local delicacy served at the [[Blais House]] inn.
* Grilled prawns, [[bass]], [[onion]]s, and [[carrot]]s on skewers were a commoners feast in the nation of [[Sembia]] and its city [[Selgaunt]].
* Prawns, [[oyster]]s, and [[fish]] were consumed by the [[sahuagin]] of the [[Shining Sea]] as well as farmed by the [[sea elves]] of the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]].
* The [[Oak-Father's Boon]] in [[Tethyr]]ean town of [[Mosstone]] served a staple dish of hand-sized silky [[salmon]] and prawn [[tart]]s.
