'''Elk''' were the largest members of the [[deer]] family. They were favored by [[Uthgar]] and the sleeping god [[Ulutiu]].{{Cite web enhancement/Deity Do's and Don'ts}}

==Description==
Elk had a good sense of smell and were adept at hiding.

==Behavior==
Elk would flee from most predators or hunters, but on occasion they would turn and defend themselves against creatures smaller than themselves.

==Combat==
When forced to fight, they would use their hooves.

==Ecology==
Elk could be found alone or with herds. in the [[Ama Basin]] where they were raised by the [[Issacortae]], the [[dragonborn]] kingdom of [[Relmaur]], and in the [[Howling Mountains]].

==Uses==
* [[Othovir]], a craftsman of [[Triboar]], was famous for creating masterful tack and harnesses from elk hide.
* In the [[Ama Basin]], the [[Pazruki]] were known to cure elk hides into thick, inflexible [[leather armor]].

==Trivia==
* Countess [[Zaranda Star]]'s bedspread in [[Morninggold Keep]] was embroidered with elks and [[penguin]]s.
