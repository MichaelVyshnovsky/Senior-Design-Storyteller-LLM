+The adventure is recommended for characters with more than 250,000 xp. In 2 edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons, level advancement occurs at different xp values for different classes. The "slowest" class category in the revised 2 edition Players Handbook is the Warrior (encompassing the Fighter, Paladin, and Ranger classes), which reaches level 9 at exactly 250,000 xp.
No Time to Lose is a one round adventure module set in the Living City organized play program and part five of the High-Level Planar Campaign series of modules. It was published in 2000 and is intended for teams of high level adventurers.
