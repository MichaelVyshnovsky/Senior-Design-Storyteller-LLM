'''''The Gift of Song''''' is a one-round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program penned by [[Catie Martolin]] and [[Rhonda Hutcheson]]. It was published in 2001 for the 3{{rd}} edition, and intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{Quote|''The long-awaited dedication of the Temple of Milil in Ravens Bluff is cause for a grand celebration. Only a fool would turn down an invitation to the most anticipated party of the year and only a worse fool would try to crash that party. Let the music begin. An event for characters levels 1-14.''}}
