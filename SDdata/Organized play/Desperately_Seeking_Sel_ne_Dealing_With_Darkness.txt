'''''Desperately Seeking Selûne: Dealing With Darkness''''' is the High Tier installment of the tournament adventure module ''[[Desperately Seeking Selûne]]'', set in the [[Living City]] organized play program, and is the final chapter of the ''Red Threats'' series of modules. It was published in 1999, and is intended for a party of adventurers of 7{{th}} level and up.

{{Quote|The Midsummer’s Festival has come to Ravens Bluff again. You’ve been invited to enjoy yourself in the Temple District, while others of you have been asked to provide security during the event. But just what dark menace may rear its ugly head in this atmosphere of good cheer? Are you willing to find out? This adventure is designed for high-level characters.<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/Desperately Seeking Selûne|1}}}}
