'''''Lost Temple of the Fey Gods''''', designed by [[Bill W. Baldwin]], is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 4th-edition ruleset. It focuses on the [[Yuirwood]], [[fey]], and the [[Cult of Voldini]].

{{Quote|''Faeries, [[sprite]]s, [[nymph]]s, and [[dryad]]s&mdash;the fey creatures of [[Faerûn]]'s wild places are often dismissed as nuisances and pranksters. Those who truly understand the fey know that as with all things natural, there is a darker side to these creatures of fancy. Could a rash of stolen food and sleep dust traps point to a more sinister threat lurking on the wild side of reality? A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in [[Aglarond]] for characters levels 1&ndash;4.''|}}

==Summary==
The adventure starts off in the village of [[Halendos (village)|Halendos]], where adventurers investigate accounts of problems with [[fey]] creatures in the area. Upon venturing into the nearby woods, the adventurers encounter a group of [[pixie]]s, who playfully test the group to either entertaining them or their pet [[ogre]].

When midnight falls, the [[Temple of the Yuir Gods]] emerges and the adventurers must battle past its guardians to gain access to the inner sanctum. There they find a dying [[dryad]], [[Nimiwi]], and eventually confront the source of the fey troubles, the [[Cult of Voldini]].
