'''''A Vial Secret''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1997 and is intended for adventurers of different levels in 2{{nd}}-edition {{dnd}}. Set in [[Procampur]], it is the second in the 11-part [[Cracked Jewel Saga]].
