'''''A Goodly Apple''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and part two of the ''Crimson Sanction'' series. It was published in 1995, and is intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{Quote|''Missions, missions. So many missions. Who will you be helping now?''}}
