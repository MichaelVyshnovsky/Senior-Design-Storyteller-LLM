'''''A Land Remembered''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1999, and is intended for adventurers of level 4 and up.

{{Quote|Maskin Farthing has a problem. His daughter, Briella, has followed a fool's errand to colonize a dangerous island, a land that time forgot. He wants her back. Badly. What's a merchant to do? Hire a team of foolish adventurers, of course!.}}
