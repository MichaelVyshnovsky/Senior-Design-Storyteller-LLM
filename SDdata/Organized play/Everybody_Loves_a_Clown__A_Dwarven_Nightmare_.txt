'''''Everybody Loves a Clown (A Dwarven Nightmare)''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1996, and is intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{Quote|A dwarf in an inn asks you for your assistance. His home has been usurped. To help him you will travel beyond Ravens Bluff to where few have gone before.}}
