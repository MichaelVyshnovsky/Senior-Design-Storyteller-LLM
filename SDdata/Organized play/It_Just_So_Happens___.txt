'''''It Just So Happens...''''' is a one-round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in and is intended for a group of adventurers of any levels.

{{Quote|"Dare much!" entreated the renowned high priest as he greeted you. Yes indeed, a worthy ambition for seasoned adventurers such as you. However, this day’s events may dissuade you of such a bold (some would say "foolish") undertaking}}
