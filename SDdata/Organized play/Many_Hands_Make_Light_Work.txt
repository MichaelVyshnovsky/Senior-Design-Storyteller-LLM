'''''Many Hands Make Light Work''''' is an adventure in the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] organized play campaign for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 4{{th}} edition.

{{Quote|''Westgate is a dangerous city at any time, but today it's about to live up to its name. Today, a band of pious Sisters have arrived in your precinct and they need a little help with renovations and &hellip;removals. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the Dragon Coast for character levels 1&ndash;4.''}}
