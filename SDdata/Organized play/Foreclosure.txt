'''''Foreclosure''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It is the second installment of the three-part ''Investigations'' series of modules, which were all published at Origins '95, though it also refers to itself as being part one of a two-part series called ''Ice Mage''. It's intended for adventurers of all levels.
