Beneath Haunted Halls is an adventure in the Living Forgotten Realms organized play campaign for Dungeons & Dragons 4 edition. As a Weekend in the Realms module, it was only released in a very limited quantity and exclusively for public events. Beneath Haunted Halls ties in with the 2 edition adventure Haunted Halls of Eveningstar.
