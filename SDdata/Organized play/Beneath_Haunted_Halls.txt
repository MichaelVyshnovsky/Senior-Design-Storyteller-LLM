'''''Beneath Haunted Halls''''' is an adventure in the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] organized play campaign for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 4{{th}} edition. As a [[Living Forgotten Realms/Weekend in the Realms|Weekend in the Realms]] module, it was only released in a very limited quantity and exclusively for public events. ''Beneath Haunted Halls'' ties in with the 2{{nd}} edition adventure ''[[Haunted Halls of Eveningstar]]''.
