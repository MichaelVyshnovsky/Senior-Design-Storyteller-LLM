'''''The Swearing Stone''''' is a one-round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and part three of the ''Chaos Quest'' series of modules. It was published in 1998 and is intended for a group of adventurers of any levels.

{{Quote|Infidelity from the faithful, lies from the truthful, and deception from the honorable. Can anyone in Ravens Bluff be believed or counted upon? Can you?}}
