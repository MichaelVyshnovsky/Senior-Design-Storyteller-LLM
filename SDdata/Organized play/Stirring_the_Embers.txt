'''''Stirring the Embers''''' is an adventure in the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] organized play campaign for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 4{{th}} edition.

{{Quote|''All is not well in the Great Port of Tarmalune and no one speaks of it. People are disappearing toward some fell purpose and you are next. A double-length Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in Tarmalune for characters levels 1&ndash;4. It is recommended that you play the Embers of Dawn mini-campaign adventures in sequential order with one character for the best enjoyment and play possibilities within the mini-campaign, but it is not required.''}}
