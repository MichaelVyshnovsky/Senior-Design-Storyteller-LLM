'''''The Icy Queen's Crossing''''' is an adventure in the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] organized play campaign for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 4{{th}} edition. As a [[Living Forgotten Realms/Weekend in the Realms|Weekend in the Realms]] module, it was only released in a very limited quantity and exclusively for public events. ''The Icy Queen's Crossing'' ties in with the novel ''[[The Fall of Highwatch]]''.
{{Quote|''In a region so dangerous that even the most savage of Nar tribes fear trespassing there, a group of heroes has a chance to change the face of a war-torn land.''<br />
''This Dᴜɴɢᴇᴏɴs & Dʀᴀɢᴏɴs® adventure is designed for five characters of 2nd level but contains notes on adjusting the adventure for groups of four or six characters of levels 1&ndash;4. Specifically created for Weekend in the Realms, this adventure can be played in the ''Living Forgotten Realms''® campaign, and includes a full-color poster map, encounter write-ups, monster stat blocks, enemy tactics, and even stats for new monsters. The PCs interact with the plots and characters of the Fᴏʀɢᴏᴛᴛᴇɴ Rᴇᴀʟᴍs novel ''The Fall of Highwatch''.}}
