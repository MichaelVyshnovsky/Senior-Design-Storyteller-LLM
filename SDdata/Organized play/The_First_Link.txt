'''''The First Link'''''  is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and the first half of the ''Chain That Binds'' series of modules. It was published in 2000, for both 2{{nd}} and 3{{rd}} edition, and is intended for a group of 4 to 7 adventurers of all levels.

{{Quote|Not everything that happens in the City of Ravens is plainly visible. Heinous crimes can always be found if one looks hard enough. But the motivations for these acts may be disturbing to some, and the solutions to the problems are never as easy as we would like.}}
