'''''Swan Song''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 2001, and is intended for adventurers of level 1 through 10.

{{Quote|Cancelled concerts, quiet taverns, and postponed poetry readings&mdash;where have all the bards gone?}}
