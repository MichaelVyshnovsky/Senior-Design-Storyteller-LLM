Crown of Salt is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 2-edition ruleset. It is part of the Adventurers Guild series of adventures published for the Adventurers Guild Retail Play Program. It promotes both the Sea of Fallen Stars and Of Ships and the Sea sourcebooks. It starts and concludes in Selgaunt but is focused on the Sea of Fallen Stars, and is an adventure designed for four to six 5- to 6-level characters.
