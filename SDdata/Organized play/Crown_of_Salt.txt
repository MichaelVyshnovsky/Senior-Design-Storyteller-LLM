'''''Crown of Salt''''' is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 2{{nd}}-edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Adventurer's Guild|Adventurer's Guild]]'' series of adventures published for the [[Adventurer's Guild|Adventurer's Guild Retail Play Program]]. It promotes both the [[Sea of Fallen Stars (sourcebook)|''Sea of Fallen Stars'']] and ''[[Of Ships and the Sea]]'' sourcebooks. It starts and concludes in [[Selgaunt]] but is focused on the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]], and is an adventure designed for four to six 5{{th}}- to 6{{th}}-level characters. 
{{Quote|1370 DR has been a bad year for Andeth Ilchammar, mayor of Sembia's port city of Selgaunt.}}
