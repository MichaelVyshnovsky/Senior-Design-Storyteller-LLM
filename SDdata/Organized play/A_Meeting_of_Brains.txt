'''''A Meeting of Brains''''' is a one-round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It is the ninth installment of the Living City's ''High Level Underdark Campaign'' and the second part of its ''Illithid Trilogy''. It was published in and is intended for a group of adventurers of any levels.
