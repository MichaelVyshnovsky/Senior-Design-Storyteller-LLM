'''''Perfect Ten''''' is a one-round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1999 and is intended for a group of adventurers of any levels.

{{Quote|There are few things that are truly perfect, but once you witness something that is, you must quickly grab it or else it may just disappear forever.|Anonymous}}
