The Rogue of the World is a one round adventure module set in the Living City organized play program and the fourth installment of the Tarot series of modules. It was published in 1998, and is intended for adventurers of all levels.Notably previous installments of the Tarot series of modules, The Stars and Moon Above and Light in the Lovers Eye, both mislabeled The Rogue of the World as being the fifth and final installment of the series.
