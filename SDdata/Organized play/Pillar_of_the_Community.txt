'''''Pillar of the Community''''' is an adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program penned by [[John Terra]], part IV of ''[[The Menagerie of a Raven Lunatic]]''. It was published in 1993 for the 2{{nd}} edition, and was later republished in 1998 as part of the [[Adventurer's Guild]] program.

{{Quote|''Several days ago, Cladus Trel, a local import/export merchant, hired the six of you to serve as escorts for an autumn caravan to Thay. The price he quoted sounded great, and he even gave you a small amount of "pocket change" to handle your room and board needs at the Cracked Tankard for a few days while he got his caravan ready. But disaster struck this afternoon...''}}
