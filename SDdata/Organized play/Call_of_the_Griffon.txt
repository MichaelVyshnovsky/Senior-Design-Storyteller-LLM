'''''Call of the Griffon''''' is an adventure in the [[Living City]] organized play campaign, originally designed for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 2{{nd}} edition but later updated for 3{{rd}} edition. It is the second adventure in the ''The Menagerie of a Raven Lunatic'' series.

{{Quote|Rumors fly about a scheme to smuggle eggs of rare creatures for profit. Can the heroes stop it?}}

==Publication History==
After its initial run as a tournament adventure, ''Call of the Griffon'' was reprinted twice, first as part of the [[Adventurer's Guild]] program in 1998 (along with the rest of the The Menagerie of a Raven Lunatic series). A version of the adventure updated for 3{{rd}} edition was released in 2002 by [[Organized Play]].
