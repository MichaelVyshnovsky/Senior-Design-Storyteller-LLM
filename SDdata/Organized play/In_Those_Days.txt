'''''In Those Days''''', also referred to as '''''Chaos Quest: Peace''''', is a one-round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and part one of the ''Chaos Quest'' series of modules. It was published in 1998 and is intended for a group of adventurers of any levels.

{{Quote|You've been asked to mediate a dispute between two warring clans in the mountains south of town. Now you find that something mysterious is happening and you have to put a stop to it}}
