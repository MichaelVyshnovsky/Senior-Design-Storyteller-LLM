'''''An Army of One Thousand''''' is an adventure module set in ''Malatra – Living Jungle'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 3{{rd}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Living Jungle]]'' series of adventures published for the RPGA Network. It is an adventure designed for 1{{st}}- to 5{{th}}-level characters with conversion available for levels 6{{st}}- to 10{{th}}.

{{Quote|''The jungle drums summon the heroes to the [[Village of the Wise Ones]], where the jungle is dying, and a familiar foe is again active. The heroes must rescue other heroes of [[Malatra]], and stop the machinations of the [[Spider Katanga Mother]], lest Malatra fall to an army of one thousand. A Living Jungle adventure with separate tasks for heroes of low level (1-5) and heroes of high level (6-10).''}}

==Summary==
The events of the adventure took place in the Year of Tigers Curse ([[1374 DR]]). The [[spider katanga]] became a threat once again, amassing and filling the rivers flowing from [[Fire Mountain]] with [[poison]]. Two groups of heroes were sent to stop the evil spider creatures - one group to cause a distraction and rescue the recently captured scouting party, and another to venture into the spider katanga lair to kill the Mother of a Thousand Young before she can birth an army.{{Cite living jungle/An Army of One Thousand|1}}
