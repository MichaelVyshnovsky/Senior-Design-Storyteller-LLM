'''''The Guns of Azzagrat: The Quarry of Ebulon''''' is the two-round High Tier version of the adventure ''The Guns of Azzagrat'', set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1999 and is intended for adventurers of eighth level or higher, specifically a party whose total levels add up to at least 48{{th}}.
