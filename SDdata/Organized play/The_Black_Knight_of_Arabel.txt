'''''The Black Knight of Arabel''''' is an adventure in the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] organized play campaign for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 4{{th}} edition.

{{Quote|''Your party is sent to investigate allegations of Netherese activity near the city of Arabel: a dark rider commanding legions of shadow. Can you turn back the tide before it's too late? A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in Cormyr for characters levels 1&ndash;4.''}}
