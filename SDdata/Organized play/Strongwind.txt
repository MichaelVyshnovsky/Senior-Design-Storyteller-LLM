'''''Strongwind''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It debuted at Dire Consequences '94 and was later reprinted in [[Polyhedron 130|''Polyhedron'' #130]], though with heavy edits to remove place names specific to the [[Forgotten Realms]] setting.

{{Quote|''Rumors of a ghost ship abound the docks of Raven's Bluff. Calvin Longbottle has asked you for your assistance. Prepare to board and set sail for adventure.''<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/Strongwind|1}}}}
