'''''Ages Best Forgotten''''' is an adventure in the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] organized play campaign for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 4{{th}} edition.

{{Quote|''Someone with a long memory has found that others have uncovered something he hoped everyone else had forgotten. Can you journey to a twisted land and aid in the re-emergence of something that some feel is best left missing? A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in Thay for characters levels 7&ndash;10.''}}
