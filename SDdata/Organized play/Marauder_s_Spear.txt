'''''Marauder's Spear''''' is an adventure in the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] organized play campaign for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 4{{th}} edition.

{{Quote|''In the pre-dawn gloom, gnolls savage a dusty halfling trading encampment on the edge of the Eastern Shaar. Hapless ''hin'' and ''hurbryn'' merchants cry out for protection from these marauding dogs. Why are the gnolls attacking here in such numbers? Who is the real leader of the pack? A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the East Rift for characters levels 4&ndash;7.''}}
