'''''False Prophecy''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in July 1993 and is intended for adventurers of any level. The module received a reprint as the Adventure I of the Fall Season 1998 [[Adventurer's Guild]].

==Synopsis==
A group of adventurers of the Living City found themselves amidst a conflict between the [[Church of Waukeen]], who constructed a [[Shrine to Waukeen (Ravens Bluff)|Shrine to Waukeen]] in the city's [[Ravens Bluff/Harbor District|Harbor District]], and the devote worshipers of [[Selûne]], who believed the harbor to be their church's territory. The Selûnites concocted a scheme to defraud the shrine's money and undermine its stability with the help of [[Lar & Sons Imports]], who supplied the construction with crumbly bricks. Adventurers followed a trail of clues and a false Selûnite prophesy when the shrine collapsed. Subsequently, they uncovered the corruption and helped Lady [[Lauren DeVillars]] to apprehend the perpetrators.
