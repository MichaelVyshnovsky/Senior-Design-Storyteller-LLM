'''''Dark Lord, Morning Glory''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and the first installment of the two-part part ''Cycle of Cyric'' series of modules. It was published in 1998 and is intended for a party of adventurers of any levels.
