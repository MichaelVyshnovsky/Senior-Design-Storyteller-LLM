'''''Three Coins in a Well''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1999 and is intended for adventurers of high level.

{{Quote|In the plane of Ysgard there is a place where all questions can be answered. It is the Well of Knowledge. The heroes of Ravens Bluff are called on by the Ministry of Art to travel to the Well and discover more information about the Great Machine.}}
