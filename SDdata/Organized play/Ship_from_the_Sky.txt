'''''Ship from the Sky''''' is a tournament adventure module set in ''Malatra – Living Jungle'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 2{{nd}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Living Jungle]]'' series of adventures published for the RPGA Network. It is an adventure designed for high-level characters.

{{Quote|''Another year has gone by, and once again you have attended the annual council of tribes at the village of [[Big Chief Bagoomba]]. This year you won many honors, as you should, since you are some of the most famous heroes of the whole jungle. This year also had something different.''|}}

==Summary==
In the Year of New Peoples, the heroes of the Living Jungle faced a new arrival to the [[Malatran Plateau]] – star-faring [[oscray]]. Two ''[[spelljamming]]'' ships descended from the skies, carrying blood enemies: [[Backbiter (oscray)|Backbiter]] and [[Bentfang]]. Backbiter – a malicious leader of ill-willing oscray abducted leaders of several Malatran tribes and the heroes of the Living Jungle, were to free the hostages, and after the deadly battle was over – welcomed stranded refugee oscray led by Bentfang to the land of Malatra.
