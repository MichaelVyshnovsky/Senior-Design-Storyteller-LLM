'''''From One Dwarf to Another''''', designed by [[Joshua Freeman]], is an adventure module in the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] organized play using the ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' 4{{th}} edition ruleset.

{{Quote|''When an annoying dwarf goes missing, nobody in Ruinspoke really cares. Well, nobody except a fellow dwarf, that is. But it takes more than just one dwarf to mount a rescue mission... A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in Tymanther for characters levels 1&ndash;4.''}}
