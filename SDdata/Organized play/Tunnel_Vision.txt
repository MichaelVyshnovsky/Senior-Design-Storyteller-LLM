'''''Tunnel Vision''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program, Part V of the ''[[Destiny of the Dwarves]]'' series. It was published in 2001, and intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{Quote|''The recovery of two pieces of a lost dwarven relic has revealed the location of the third and final piece. Driven by prophecy the dwarves of the Vast struggle against time and fate to restore the Hammer of Thanes, but time is short and a darkness is trying to corrupt the quest. A balanced adventure for tiers 1 through 6.''}}

==Summary==
In [[1374 DR]], heroes of the Living City were summoned back to the [[Crimson Lagoon]], where the last of the series of expeditions took place. The Dwarven Clan Elders revealed the previous adventures' goal – to reforge the legendary ''[[Hammer of Thanes]]''. The Adventurers descended into the [[Underdark]] and were faced with ''The Test of Bravery'', ''The Test of Belief'', ''The Test of Avarice'', ''The Test of Truth'', and ''The Test of Blood'', ancient trials set up by the dwarven gods and dwarven ancestors to determine who would become the new Thane. In the end, the adventurers faced a familiar face who orchestrated the expedition – [[Citadel Urn]], who attempted to claim the title and the artifact but was slain in the process.
