'''''A Gruesome Faerie Tale''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1999 and is intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{Quote|The Independent Individuals Association would like to send you on a quest for love into an enchanted forest. One of their members is in need of companionship, but is kind of shy. The forest holds the answer to his romantic dreams, but also a danger too gruesome to believe.}}
