'''''A Day at the Races''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and part two of the ''Devil of the Deep'' series of modules. It was published in 2001, and is intended for a party adventurers of levels 1 through 19.

{{Quote|As the city prepares for the running of the Grimmy Cup, the warm-up to the city's annual regatta, rumors of treachery begin to circulate the docks. Can you get to the bottom of this?}}
