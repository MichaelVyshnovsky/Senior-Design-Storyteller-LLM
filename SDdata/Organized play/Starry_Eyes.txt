'''''Starry Eyes''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in December 1999 and is intended for adventurers of any level.

{{quote|''Traveling Tormish bring word to Ravens Bluff that the village of Maerstar is in need of adventurers. Someone, or something, has been slaying the village’s prized riding horses. They have been unable to spot the foe, let alone defeat it. A long journey like this one is bound to bring great adventure, and unknown danger.''}}
