The original adventure states on its opening page that the total page count is 23. The PDF that was used to make this article stretched the content out to 55 pages and thus that numbering is used for the purposes of citations and indexing.
A Handful of Dust is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the Living City organized play program. It debuted at Gen Con 93 and was reprinted the next year in Polyhedron #98.
