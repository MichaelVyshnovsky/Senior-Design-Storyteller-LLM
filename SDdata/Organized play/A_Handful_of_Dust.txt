'''''A Handful of Dust''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It debuted at Gen Con '93 and was reprinted the next year in [[Polyhedron 98|''Polyhedron'' #98]].

{{Quote|''An errand for an alchemist is no easy task in this fast-paced Living City adventure for fleet-footed PCs.''}}
