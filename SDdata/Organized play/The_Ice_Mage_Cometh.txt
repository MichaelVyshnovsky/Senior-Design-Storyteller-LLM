'''''The Ice Mage Cometh''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It is the final installment of the three-part ''Investigations'' series of modules, which were all published at Origins '95, and the second half of the two-part ''Ice Mage'' series. It's intended for adventurers of all levels.
