'''''A Small Problem''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1998 as a sequel to ''[[A Small Threat]]'', and is intended for adventurers of level 4 and up.

{{Quote|The mission is over, and you are on your way home. Only the weather stands between you and the end of your adventure. But a short overnight stop at Burrowbluff soon blossoms into an adventure sure to test the depths of your courage.}}

==Synopsis==
''A Small Problem'' takes place two years after ''A Small Threat'' and around 1-2 years after the conclusion of the [[Three Women War]].<ref name="CLC-p2>{{Cite organized play/LC/A Small Problem|2}}
