A Small Problem is a one round adventure module set in the Living City organized play program. It was published in 1998 as a sequel to A Small Threat, and is intended for adventurers of level 4 and up.
A Small Problem takes place two years after A Small Threat and around 1-2 years after the conclusion of the Three Women War.
