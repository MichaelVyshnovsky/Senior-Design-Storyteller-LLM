'''''Where Have You Gond?''''' is a one-round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1998 and is intended for a group of adventurers of any levels.

{{Quote|Will you get out of bed very early in the morning when someone asks you to help track down a missing priest?}}
