'''''Out of Hatred''''', designed by [[Claire Hoffman|Claire]] and [[Keith Hoffman]], is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 4th-edition ruleset.

{{Quote|''His thirst for vengeance not yet satiated, the Necromancer strikes at [[Waterdeep]] once again. Will heroes step forward to end this menace? This adventure concludes the major quest, Quest for the Necromancer, and follows the story begun in [[The Woolmen's Restless Tomb|WATE1-3]] and continued in [[Mystery of Deepwater Harbor|WATE1-4]]. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in Waterdeep for characters levels 11-14.''|}}
