'''''Ineffable Tome''''' is an adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program penned by [[Greg Dreher]]. It was published in 2001 for the 3{{rd}} edition, and intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{Quote|''Skilled adventurers are needed immediately at the Temple of Mystra. An ally of the temple has stolen a powerful evil artifact, and it must be protected as it is taken to a place where no evil force can reach it. Expect trouble, as the evil foes from which the artifact was taken will stop at nothing to get it back. An adventure for Tiers 3-6.''}}
