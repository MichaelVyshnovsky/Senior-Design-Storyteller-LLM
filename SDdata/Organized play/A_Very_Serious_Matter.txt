'''''A Very Serious Matter''''' is a two-round tournament adventure module, set in the [[Living City]] organized play program, published in 1997. It consisted of two smaller, related adventures, one of standard difficulty that was intended for a party of adventurers of any level and one of higher difficulty for adventurers that were at least of 7{{th}} level.

{{Quote|The city's elite need your help once again. You can’t help but wonder if this will be another trip to an “exotic” destination such as the sewers or Crow's End. Oh for a chance to go somewhere truly unique!}}
