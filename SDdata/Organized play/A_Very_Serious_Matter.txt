A Very Serious Matter is a two-round tournament adventure module, set in the Living City organized play program, published in 1997. It consisted of two smaller, related adventures, one of standard difficulty that was intended for a party of adventurers of any level and one of higher difficulty for adventurers that were at least of 7 level.
