'''''Salvation''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1999.

{{Quote|You are given the opportunity to enter the Abyss to free a wrongly imprisoned soul you do not know. To do so, you must enter the Abyss with nothing, not even the clothes on your back. If you are killed your own soul might be forced to serve a tanar’ri lord for eternity. Sometimes it’s not for the excitement, adventure or reward. Sometimes, it’s for real.}}
