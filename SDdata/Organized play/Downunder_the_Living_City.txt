'''''Downunder the Living City''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It debuted at Gen Con '91, was reprinted a year later in [[Polyhedron 77|''Polyhedron'' #77]] with minor changes, and reprinted again in 1998 as part of the [[Adventurer's Guild]] organized play program.
