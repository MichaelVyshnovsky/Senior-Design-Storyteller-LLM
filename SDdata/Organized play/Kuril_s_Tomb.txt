'''''Kuril’s Tomb''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1998, and intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{Quote|''Members of the Church of Mystra have noticed a disturbance in the Weave, the balance of magical forces in the land. They request that you investigate the situation.''}}
