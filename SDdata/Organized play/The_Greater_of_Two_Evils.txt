'''''The Greater of Two Evils''''' is an adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program penned by [[Clint Heiman]] and the final installment of the ''[[The Menagerie of a Raven Lunatic]]'' series. It was published in 1994 for the 2{{nd}} edition, and was later republished in 1998 as part of the [[Adventurer's Guild]] program.
