The Greater of Two Evils is an adventure module set in the Living City organized play program penned by Clint Heiman and the final installment of the The Menagerie of a Raven Lunatic series. It was published in 1994 for the 2 edition, and was later republished in 1998 as part of the Adventurers Guild program.
Index based on the original LC tournament edition.
