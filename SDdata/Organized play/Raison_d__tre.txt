'''''Raison d'Être''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 2001, and is intended for 1{{st}} level adventurers. Half of its page count is taken up by sample character sheets.

{{Quote|So, you want to become an adventurer? Well what have you gotten yourself into now? Finding a lost child wasn’t exactly what you had in mind, was it?}}
