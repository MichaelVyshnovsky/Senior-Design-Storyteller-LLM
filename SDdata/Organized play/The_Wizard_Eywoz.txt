'''''The Wizard Eywoz''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in January 1999, and is intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{quote|''"Wizard seeks brave companions for aging quest. Clever wit required. Bashers need not apply." Beware of pathetic puns & ridiculous riddles!''}}
