'''''The Prospect''''', designed by [[Pierre van Rooden]], is an adventure in the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] organized play campaign for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 4{{th}} edition.

{{Quote|''The Fall of Stars is the most famous adventurers' club in Faerûn. Who can say no when offered membership in the Stellar Fellowship, for a mere errand? But of course, things are never that simple. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the Dalelands for characters levels 1&ndash;4.''}}
