'''''Dragon's Knowledge''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program, Part II of the ''[[Cycle of Dragons]]'' series. It was published in 2000, and intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{Quote|''Draconic Lore speaks of a creature called the Dragon Sage. This creature holds the knowledge of all dragons and speaks in the riddles of the wyrm to give prophecy to all who would hear him. Information has been uncovered that leads you to far away lands to speak with him. But other forces are intent that you do not reach your destination so that an even darker prophecy may be fulfilled. Part 2 in the Cycle of Dragons series. A 2 Time Unit adventure for Tiers 1&ndash;6.''}}

==Summary==
Following the draconic massacre above [[Hidden Hollow]] in the winter of the [[1373 DR]], heroes of the Living City continued investigating the looming danger of the [[Tiamat|Dark Lady]] and searing for the fabled [[Dragon Sage]]. The mages and sages of the [[Ministry of Art]] found a reference to the mystic figure in one of the prophecies of [[Alaundo]] the Mad – the Sage was in the [[Moonshae Isles]]. With wizardly aid, they were transported to the [[Isle of Gwynneth]], where they faced the sinister child-abducting [[Crimson Order]], led by a massive [[brown dragon]] prince named [[Torilsbane]]. The heroes of the Living City offered help in finding missing children of [[Corwell Town]]. Subsequently, their search led the heroes to [[Sunken Caves]] and the children, ensorcelled with magical ''[[charm]].'' When the adventurers promised to return the kids back to their parents, one of them – a child that looked to be a [[sun elf]], revealed himself to be the [[Nether]], the Dragon Sage. Nether and adventurers faced Torilsbane in battle and ended his life, saving the children of [[Caer Corwell]]. The Dragon Sage gave them the next destination in uncovering the truth behind the Dark Lady – the [[City of Splendors]].
