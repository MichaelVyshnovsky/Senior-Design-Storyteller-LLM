'''''A Night at Sharkey's Bar and Grill''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and part one of the ''Devil of the Deep'' series of modules. It was published in 2001, and is intended for adventurers of levels 1 through 14.

{{Quote|Members of the Navy are missing and the city needs your help to find them. Do you have what it takes to face one of the city's oldest enemies?}}
