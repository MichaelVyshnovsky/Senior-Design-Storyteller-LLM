'''''The Stars Don't Lie''''' is a one-round adventure module, set in the [[Living City]] organized play program, penned by [[Lee Sharp|Lee]] and [[Michelle Sharp]]. It was published in 2000 for the 3{{rd}} edition and is intended for adventurers of levels one through six.

{{Quote|''"The death of many follow the fall of one." Is this vague message from the stars something worth worrying about or is it just someone's attempt to ruin our celebration? Let's find out... An adventure for characters levels 1-6.''}}
