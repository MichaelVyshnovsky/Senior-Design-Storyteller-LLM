'''''Masks of the Dohi-ri''''' is a tournament module set in ''Malatra – Living Jungle'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 2{{rd}} edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Living Jungle]]'' series of adventures published for the RPGA Network. The adventure is intended for heroes of all levels.{{Cite organized play/LJ/Masks of the Dohi-ri|1-2}}

{{Quote|''You are most honored to be invited to the Dohi-ri tribe's annual Festival of the Mask, a time when the Nubari villagers and their guests create masks to represent aspects of their lives.''|}}

==Summary==
Heroes of the Living Jungle participated in the annual [[Dohi-ri]] celebration – the [[Festival of the Mask]], Zurich which they embarked on a journey to assemble and decorate masks from the boon of the jungle. The heroes traveled to marked destinations to collect jungle dyes and other ornamentations. They confronted a tribe of slaver [[tasloi]], a band of [[leopard katanga]] pretending to be fellow [[Festival Walk]]ers, visited an ancient shrine to an ancestor [[grippli]] race, parleyed with an evil [[nereid]] and a peaceful [[water naga]]. Eventually, one of the heroes was named the new Masked One, and their mask was [[enchanted]].
