'''''Full Circle''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and the final installment of the ''Elementally Speaking'' series of modules. It was published in 2001 and is intended for a party of adventurers of level 5 and up. A special issue of the ''[[Trumpeter]]'' is included with this module.

{{Quote|The city has decided to make a pre-emptive strike against the Circle of Four, to end the threat of this group forever. Adventurers are being called by the score into the mayor's office, you included. With the destruction caused in previous Circle member appearances in mind, you are sure that Charles O'Kane is worried about the safety of the populace. The conclusion of the ''Elementally Speaking'' series.}}
