'''''Twilight of the Dirty Turtles''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and the final installment of ''Gang Wars'', an informal three-part series of modules. It was published in 2000 and is intended for a party of adventurers of any level.

{{Quote|The Shadow Leaderâ€™s Crow's End gang wars seem to have claimed another victim when the enigmatic leader of the Dirty Turtles street gang goes missing. Seeking any allies it can find, the City Watch asks a group of adventurers to look into the matter. An adventure for Tiers 1-5.}}
