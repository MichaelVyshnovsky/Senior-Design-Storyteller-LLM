'''''Taps!''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module designed by [[Eugene Luster]] and set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1998, and intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{quote|''Ever wonder what made you a hero?''}}

==Summary==
In [[1371 DR]], a group of heroes from the Living City enjoyed a meal at the newly opened [[Dancing Bear Inn]] when a group of wandering [[zombie]]s attacked commoners in the streets. Volunteering to investigate the strange occurrence, the adventurers tracked the [[undead]] to [[Forrest Funeral Home]]. Its proprietor, [[Jonathan Forrest]], succumbed to madness and, with the unwilling help of a jeweler [[mage]], [[Felicia Cartier]], and eager aid from the [[Talassan]] [[Stormlord]] named [[Electra Storm]], he began accumulating an army of several hundred zombies and [[skeleton]]s, stored around the funeral home. Subsequently, the Living City heroes ended the undead infestation by reasoning with the madman, slaying him, or arresting him on behalf of the [[City Watch (Ravens Bluff)|City Watch]] of the Bluff.
