'''''Along the Wheel of Fates''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and the final installment of the ''Tarot'' series of modules. It was published in 1998, and is intended for adventurers of all levels.<ref group=note>Notably previous installments of the ''Tarot'' series of modules, ''[[The Stars and Moon Above]]'' and ''[[Light in the Lover's Eye]]'', both mislabeled ''Along the Wheel of Fates'' as being the fourth installment of the series. The correct sequencing was finally provided in the fourth installment, ''[[The Rogue of the World]]''.

{{Quote|The ending to a tale, whose beginning is finally revealed to all. The rewards are great but the challenges will be deadly, and I am stating that for all who dare traverse the Wheel of Fates.}}
