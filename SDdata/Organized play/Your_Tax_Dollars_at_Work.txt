'''"Your Tax Dollars at Work"''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It debuted at Gen Con '92 and was reprinted two years later in [[Polyhedron 97|''Polyhedron'' #97]].{{TOC limit|2}}
