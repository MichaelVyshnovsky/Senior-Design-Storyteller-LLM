'''''Another Serious Matter''''' is a two-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program, published in 1999, and part two of ''The Saga of the Diabolical Grasp'' series of modules. It consisted of two smaller, related adventures, one of standard difficulty that was intended for a party of adventurers of any level and one of greater difficulty for players of at least 8{{th}} level.

{{Quote|It's time to rise, go forth, and conquer once again. The Wizards Guild needs your services for another far-flung mission.}}
