'''''A Stab in the Dark''''' is an adventure in the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] organized play campaign for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 4{{th}} edition.

{{Quote|''Somewhere beneath the dirty streets of Westgate's docks' district, one shining light of justice is being held hostage by a force intent on extinguishing any resistance. A rescue mission must be mounted, and it all starts at&hellip; a masquerade ball? A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the Dragon Coast for character levels 7&ndash;10.''}}
