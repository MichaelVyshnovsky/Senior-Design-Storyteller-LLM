'''''A Boy and His Mammoth''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1998, and intended for adventurers of all levels. The adventure included an issue of ''[[Ravens Bluff Trumpeter]]''.

{{Quote|''Ravens Bluff has been unbelievably dull over the past weeks and the war is beginning to seem like the good old days. You're standing on a corner, leafing through a special edition of the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter in hopes of finding something to break the monotony.''}}

==Summary==
The spring of [[1371 DR]] in the city of Ravens Bluff was quiet. Too quiet. Adventurers of the Living City had to search for employment and adventure via that month's issue of ''[[Ravens Bluff Trumpeter]]''. They found themselves assessing a mad [[necromancer]] from the [[Ministry of Art]] – [[Matthew Hopkins]] and his [[ghost]]ly wife, they ventured in the [[Abyss]] under a misleading contract with Mistress [[Charnela]], visited the recently attacked and vandalized [[Shrine to Eilistraee (Ravens Bluff)|Shrine to Eilistraee]] by [[human]] bigots, and embarked on a quest to recover a missing [[minimal]] [[mammoth]] that belonged to [[Victor Wilkinson]] and his young son [[Thomas Wilkinson|Thomas]]. The search for the missing pachyderm led adventurers to deal with an eccentric and carnivorous geriatric [[faerie dragon]] and got them involved in [[fey]] warfare – a conflict between [[Oberon (pixie)|Oberon]] and [[bog sprite]]s. Subsequently, after a wild celebration, the mammoth named [[Billie]] was returned to its owners. However, something was odd about the beast...
