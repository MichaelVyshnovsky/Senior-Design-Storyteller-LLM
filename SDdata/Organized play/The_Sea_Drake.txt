'''''The Sea Drake''''', designed by [[Jean-Philipe Chapleau]], is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 4th-edition ruleset.

{{Quote|''Valiant seafarers travel between the Moonshae Isles, bringing supplies and information to the many people. You have been hired as guard aboard the ''Sea Drake''. The weather is rainy and the fog is thick. It's a perfect time to sail the seas of the Moonshaes. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in The Moonshae Isles for characters levels 4&ndash;7.''}}
