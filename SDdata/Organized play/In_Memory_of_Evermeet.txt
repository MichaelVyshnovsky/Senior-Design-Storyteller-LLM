'''''In Memory of Evermeet''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1999, and is intended for adventurers of all levels.

{{Quote|Children have been disappearing throughout Ravens Bluff. The daughter of an influential merchant has gone missing and he wants you to find her.}}
