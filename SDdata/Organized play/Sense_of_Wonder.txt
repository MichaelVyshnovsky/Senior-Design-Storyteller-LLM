'''''Sense of Wonder''''', designed by [[M. Sean Molley]], is an adventure module set in the Forgotten Realms using the Dungeons & Dragons 4th-edition ruleset.

{{Quote|''You are summoned to help a [[cleric]] of [[Gond|Gond Wonderbringer]], who seeks the location of a lost temple to the Lord of All Smiths. Think of all the marvelous inventions, lost since the [[Spellplague]] came crashing down on the nation of [[Lantan]], that might lie waiting to be rediscovered and brought back to the lands of [[Faerûn]]! A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the [[Nelanther Isles]] for characters levels 1&ndash;4.''|}}
