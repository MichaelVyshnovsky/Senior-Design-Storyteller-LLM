The adventure takes place on the 10 anniversary of Alice Clarksons death, which is stated to have occured on Eleint 13, 1348 DR. And date given for Cedric Clarksons last journal entry is Eleint 11, 1358 DR. This would place the adventure between the 12 and 13 of Eleint in the year 1358 DR.
