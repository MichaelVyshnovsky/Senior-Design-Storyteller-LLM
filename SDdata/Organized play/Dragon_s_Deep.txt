Dragons Deep is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the Living City organized play program, Part III of the Cycle of Dragons series. It was published in 2000, and intended for adventurers of all levels.
Whisper, a shadow dragon prince servant of Tiamat, was sent to the City of Splendors in the  to build a network of spies and thieves under the name of "The Shadowed Man." He aimed to discover the identities of the Masked Lords of Waterdeep. In the , the ambassador of Ravens Bluff in Waterdeep was killed. A group of heroes from the Living City was tasked with the delivery of his familys rod of resurrection to return Chalnath Tressoon to the land of the living. The rod, however, was quickly snatched by Whisper and his Shadowhands. The heroes embarked on a quest to find the elusive "Shadowed Man," aided by a secret Masked Lord, the bard named Danilo Thann, and Lady Mage of Waterdeep in disguise. Eventually, the search brought them to Undermountain, where the shadow dragon made his lair. After a confrontation, Whisper was slain, leaving behind the third of the fabled dragons stones, taken from the bodies of Tiamats princes. The next stop was the city of Westgate to find the ambassadors brother.
