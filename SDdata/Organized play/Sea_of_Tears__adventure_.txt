'''''Sea of Tears''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1997 and is intended for a party of adventurers of any levels

{{Quote|A quest to help the temple of elemental water leads to a mythical sea where adventure and treasure awaits. For those who miss life on the high seas, this adventure is guaranteed to "wet" your appetite.}}
