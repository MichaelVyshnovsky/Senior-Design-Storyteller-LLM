A Shot in the Dark, Part I is a one-round adventure module set in the Living City organized play program and the first installment of the High Level Underdark Campaign. It was published in 1998 and is intended for a group of at least 5 exceptional adventurers.
Index based on the General Release of the module. Any pages from the Premiere Release will be denoted with the letter P.
