'''''Silver Lining''''', designed by [[Brad Gardner]], is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 4th-edition ruleset.

{{Quote|''The commander of the [[Watchwall]] is looking for [[adventurer]]s for a special mission into the [[Tannath Mountains]]. The  Watchwall is always undermanned and the commander thinks he may have found some new recruits. But war, like politics, can make for strange bedfellows. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in [[Aglarond]] for characters levels 1 – 4. This adventure is the beginning of both the “Invisible Road” and “Circle of Stones” Major Quests.''|}}
