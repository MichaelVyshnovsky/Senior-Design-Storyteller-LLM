'''''The Vesperin Initiative''''', designed by [[Brad Gardner]], is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 4th-edition ruleset.

{{Quote|''A trade consortium is being formed. All the players have been selected. Now if they could just agree to meet and finalize the trade pact. Why can’t they all just get along? A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the [[Dalelands]] for characters levels 11&ndash;14. Final part of the ‘Conspiracy of Ravens’ quest, originally started in DALE1-1 ''[[The Prospect]]'' and DALE1-3 ''[[Master and Servant]]''.''|}}
