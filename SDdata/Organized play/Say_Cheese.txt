'''''blank''''' is a one-round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in and is intended for a group of adventurers of any levels.

{{Quote|Merchants. Mystery. Theft. Betrayal. Danger. Cheese. Ah, what? An event featuring that most famous product of the state of Wisconsin. This is a light-hearted event, with seriously “fun” role-playing. That is about the only serious thing one should expect ... For tiers 1-3 and characters counting for 6 or fewer levels.}}
