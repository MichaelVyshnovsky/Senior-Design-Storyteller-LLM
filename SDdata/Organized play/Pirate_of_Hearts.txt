'''''Pirate of Hearts''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1997 and is intended for adventurers of up to 8{{th}} level in 2{{nd}}-edition {{dnd}}. Set in Procampur, it introduces [[Procampur City Expansion Packet|an expansion]] of the Living City program and is the first in the 11-part [[Cracked Jewel Saga]].
