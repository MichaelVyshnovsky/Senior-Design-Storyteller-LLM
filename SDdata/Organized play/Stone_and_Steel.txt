'''''Stone and Steel''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and part three of the ''High-Level Planar Campaign'' series of modules. It was published in 1999 and is intended for a party of adventurers of level 36 and up.

{{Quote|The case of the Great Machine has been cracked. Ravens Bluff Elite has been asked to start cracking its mysteries.}}
