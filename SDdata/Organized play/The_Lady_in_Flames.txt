'''''The Lady in Flames''''', designed by [[Brad Gardner]], is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 4th-edition ruleset.

{{Quote|''[[Fey]] creatures wreathed in blue flames have been leaving the [[Spiderhaunt]] forest to harrass the citizens of [[Shadowdale]]. [[Addee Ulphor|Lady Ulphor]] requests that you delve into the forest and retrieve or destroy the source of their newfound powers. A Living Forgotten Realms fairy tale set in the [[Dalelands]] for characters levels 4-7. This adventure is the second part of the'' Byar's Seven ''major quest, which started in'' DALE1-2 [[Blades for Daggerdale]].|}}
