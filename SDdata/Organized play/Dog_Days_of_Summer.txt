'''''Dog Days of Summer''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program. It was published in 1999, and is intended for adventurers of level 4 and up.

{{Quote|Something is amiss in Ravens Bluff today. An excess of robberies, a dearth of dogs, and all the cats are happy. Can you allow this to happen?}}
