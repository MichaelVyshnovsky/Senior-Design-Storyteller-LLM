'''''Into the Depths''''' is a one round adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and part six of the ''High-Level Planar Campaign'' series of modules. It was published in 2000 and is intended for teams of high level adventurers.

{{Quote|''A quest for information on the Great Machine gets dangerous. Visits to Ribcage and the planes of Hell are in your future, and you will meet with strange creatures who all want something from you. The information you seek will cost you heavily, but may benefit Ravens Bluff in ways you cannot imagine. An adventure in the planes for Living City characters levels 10}}
