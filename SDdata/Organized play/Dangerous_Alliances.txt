'''''Dangerous Alliances''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program, part one of the ''Living City at War'' series of modules, and is intended for a party of adventurers of any levels. It was published in 1993 and later published once more as part of the [[Adventurer's Guild]] in 1999.
