'''''A Wish for Temptation''''' is a one-round tournament adventure module set in the [[Living City]] organized play program and part six of the ''Living City at War'' series of modules. It was published in August 1996 and was republished a month later in [[Polyhedron 123|''Polyhedron'' #123]]. The original publication was designed for six characters of any level, while the ''Polyhedron'' reprint states it should be played by a party of adventurers levels 6&ndash;7 and includes six pre-generated characters.
