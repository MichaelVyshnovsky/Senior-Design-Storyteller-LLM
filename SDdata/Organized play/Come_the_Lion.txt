'''''Come the Lion''''' is an adventure module set in ''Malatra – Living Jungle'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 3{{rd}}-edition ruleset. It is part of the ''[[Living Jungle]]'' series of adventures published for the RPGA Network. It is an adventure designed for 6{{th}}- to 12{{th}}-level characters.
{{Quote|''As [[Tiger (spirit)|Tiger]] has come, so shall the [[Vizerathu]], the spirit of the [[Lion]]. Yet Lion only appears to those who come to it pure of heart and purpose, with the strength to protect it and the courage to die for it and all of Malatra if need be. The Heroes Journey is upon you - across the jungles, plains and dreamlands to a place not quite in Malatra, but a part of it. There it waits in the [[Valley of the Lion]] guarded by a most unexpected protector. Stray from your path and you will be lost. Risk everything and win, and Malatra just might have a chance. An adventure for high (6th - 12th) level heroes only.''|}}
