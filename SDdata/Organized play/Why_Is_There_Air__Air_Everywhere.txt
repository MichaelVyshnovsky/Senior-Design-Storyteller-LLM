'''''Air Everywhere''''', round two of '''''Why Is There Air?''''', focuses on the players traveling into the [[Plane of Air]] to confront the nefarious [[Red Wizard]] [[Pisner Everett]]. The story is continued from round one, ''[[Why Is There Air?/Storm Streak|Storm Streak]]''.
