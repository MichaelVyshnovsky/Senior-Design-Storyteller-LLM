'''''What in the World Is This?''''' is an adventure in the [[Living City]] organized play campaign, originally designed for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 2{{nd}} edition but later updated for 3{{rd}} edition. It is the first adventure in the ''The Menagerie of a Raven Lunatic'' series.

{{Quote|Reality depends on constants, such as time, distance, and causality. When those things are removed, what is real, and what is not?}}

==Publication History==
After its initial run as a tournament adventure, ''What in the World Is This?'' was reprinted twice, first as part of the [[Adventurer's Guild]] program in 1998 (along with the rest of the ''The Menagerie of a Raven Lunatic'' series). A version of the adventure updated for 3{{rd}} edition was released in 2002 by [[Organized Play]] that expanded upon various elements of the story and added stronger ties to the Living City setting.
