'''''Heirloom''''', designed by [[Claire Hoffman|Claire]] and [[Keith Hoffman]], is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 4th-edition ruleset.

{{Quote|''An old tutor asks the adventurers to recover a stolen family heirloom for a down-on-their-luck Waterdhavian noble family. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in Waterdeep for characters levels 1&ndash;4.''}}
