'''Sembia: Gateway to the Realms''' is a seven part series focusing on the residents of [[Stormweather Towers]] in [[Selgaunt]]. It was originally titled the '''Sembia series'''. The first novel, ''[[The Halls of Stormweather]]'', is an anthology, and multiple authors wrote the rest of installments. 

The series was first released between 2000 and 2003, with cover artwork by [[Terese Nielsen]]. Beginning in 2007, the series is being reprinted, with new cover art by [[Raymond Swanland]].

An eight entry into the series, ''[[Pride of the Lion]]'' by [[Ed Greenwood]] was cancelled in late 2002 due to the authors other work commitments.<ref>http://forum.candlekeep.com/topic.asp?TOPIC_ID=243
