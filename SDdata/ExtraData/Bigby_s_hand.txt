Bigbys hand might refer to any of a family of spells created by the fabled wizard Bigby. It is the name of the 5th edition spell that has many varieties, each of which appeared as a separate spell in previous editions
* Bigbys interposing hand appeared in 1st, 2nd, 3rd/3.5, and 5th editions.
* Bigbys forceful hand appeared in 1st, 2nd, 3rd/3.5, and 5th editions.
* Bigbys grasping hand appeared in 1st, 2nd, 3rd/3.5, and 5th editions.
* Bigbys clenched fist appeared in 1st, 2nd, 3rd/3.5, and 5th editions.
* Bigbys crushing hand appeared in 1st, 2nd, 3rd/3.5 editions, and 5th editions.
* Bigbys icy grasp appeared in 4th edition.
* Bigbys grasping hands appeared in 4th edition.
