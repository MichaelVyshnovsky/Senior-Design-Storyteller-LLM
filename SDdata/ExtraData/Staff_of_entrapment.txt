A '''staff of entrapment''' was a type of short [[brass]] staff originally made in [[Netheril]] and containing the ''[[dimensional anchor]]'' and ''[[Otiluke's resilient sphere]]'' spells. It was worth around 37,000 gold pieces if fully charged, but most were missing their charges when found.
