'''Skin dancers''' were creatures that wore the flesh of [[human]] beings in an attempt to appear human.

==Abilities==
If a skindancer stole another person's skin, their victim became a skin dancer. A skindancer would be returned to normal if a ''[[restoration]]'' spell was cast on them.
