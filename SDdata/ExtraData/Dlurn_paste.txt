'''Dlurn paste''' was a type of fish paste for which the city of [[Derlusk]] was known for in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], derived from the salted [[dlurn]] [[fish]] that was endemic to its waters.{{Cite polyhedron/117/Elminster's Everwinking Eye: Dapplegate and Derlusk|21}}{{Cite web/The Border Kingdoms/Derlusk}}

==Description==
Dlurn paste had a green coloration of a nauseating hue, but a pleasant smell. It had a long shelf life and was highly [[salt]]y, requiring many who partook in it to drink.

==Usages==
This fish paste was commonly used as a condiment, with many spreading it thickly across [[biscuit]]s.
