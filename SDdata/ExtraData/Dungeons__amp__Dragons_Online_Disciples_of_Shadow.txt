'''''Disciples of Shadow''''' is a partially free adventure pack for ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Online]]'' where the player characters must investigate [[Sharran]] cultists in [[Cormyr]]. The adventure pack was added to the game on May 22, 2013 as part of Update 18.

The final part of Disciples of Shadow requires the purchase of ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Online: Shadowfell Conspiracy]]''.
