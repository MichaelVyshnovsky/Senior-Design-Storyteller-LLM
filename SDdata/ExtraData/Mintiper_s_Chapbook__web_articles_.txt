'''''Mintiper's Chapbook''''' was a series of articles on the [[Wizards of the Coast]] website, published between 2001 and 2002, written by [[Eric L. Boyd]], writing as a chronicler of the "famous" in-universe bard, [[Mintiper Moonsilver]]. Each article presented an excerpt or summary of the song, poem, or story, followed by analysis by the [[Keeper of the Vault|Keeper]] of the [[Vault of the Sages]] in [[Silverymoon]].
