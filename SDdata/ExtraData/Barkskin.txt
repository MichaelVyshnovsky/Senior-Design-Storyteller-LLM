'''''Barkskin''''' was a druid evocation and a [[transmutation]] or [[alteration]] [[spell]] that toughened the skin to a bark-like consistency. 

==Effects==
''Barkskin'' toughened the skin of the creature touched, giving it natural armor. In general, the quality of the armor improved the higher the caster's power. The ''barkskin'' evocation drew upon the caster's constitution for the strength of the armor&mdash;a tougher caster could bestow tougher armor. The spell would not stack with any other effect that increased natural armor.
