[[File:Magic Circle Icon.png|thumb|100px|Unused icon in Baldur's Gate III]]A '''''magic circle''''' was an effect created by a series of spells designed to either protect against or entrap extraplanar beings of particular alignments. Such spells included:
* ''[[Magic circle against chaos]]'',
* ''[[Magic circle against evil]]'',
* ''[[Magic circle against good]]'', and
* ''[[Magic circle against law]]''.
