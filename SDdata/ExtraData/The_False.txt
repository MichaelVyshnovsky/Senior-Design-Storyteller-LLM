'''The False''' were the souls of those individuals who intentionally betrayed their [[deity|deities]] after making a commitment to them during their lifetimes. If judged and found guilty by [[Kelemvor]], they were punished for all eternity. The punishment varied depending on the severity of the crime. The punishment could be anything from escorting visitors to the [[City of Judgment]], to unspeakable tortures.
