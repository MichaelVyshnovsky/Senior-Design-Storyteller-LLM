'''Kingpin''' was a title used within the [[Baldurian]] criminal organization known as [[the Guild]]. It was held by the crime bosses that oversaw a gang occupying a specified region, typically a single [[:Category:Districts of Baldur's Gate|district]] within the [[Lower City|Lower]] or [[Outer City]].

They all operated under their [[guildmaster]], competing with one other for increased wealth and influence.
