|content=The entire [[cosmology]] of Toril was transformed during the [[Spellplague]], the [[World Tree]] was uprooted and the divine dominions began to float in the [[Astral Sea]]. The [[Cosmology#Fiendish_Planes|Fiendish planes]] scattered as well, and the [[Elemental planes]] collapsed into the [[Elemental Chaos]]. Some dominions suffered or were destroyed, some prospered, and others merged as the deities that called them home united against the devastating upheaval.
