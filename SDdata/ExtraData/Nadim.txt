"'''Nadim'''" was the [[Alzhedo]] word for [[Alzhedo dictionary#N|"club"]]; it was also a common male given name. Individuals named Nadim included:
* [[Nadim el Jhotos]], [[Caleph Arcane]] and father of Emperor [[Shoon VII]] and
* [[Nadim el Cajaan|Nadim yn Rahimat el Cajaan]], a [[Calishite]] prince who conquered the city of [[Tzindylspar]].
