"Nadim" was the Alzhedo word for "club" it was also a common male given name. Individuals named Nadim included
* Nadim el Jhotos, Caleph Arcane and father of Emperor Shoon VII and
* Nadim yn Rahimat el Cajaan, a Calishite prince who conquered the city of Tzindylspar.
