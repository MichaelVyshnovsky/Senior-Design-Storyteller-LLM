"'''Nishan'''" was a common male given name in the [[Lands of Intrigue]]. Individuals named "Nishan" include:
* King [[Nishan Ithal]] of the [[Ithal Dynasty]], the third king of [[Tethyr]],
* King [[Nishan Ithal II]], the last king of the Ithal Dynasty,
* King [[Nishan Tornamn]] of the [[Tornamn Dynasty]], who reigned as puppet of Tethyr, and 
* Prince [[Nishan Rhindaun|Nishan]] of the [[Rhindaun Dynasty]], whose mother, sister, and daughter were all queens of Tethyr.
* [[Nishan (pirate)|Nishan of the Nine Wonders]], a pirate in the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]]
