The '''First Age of Shou Lung''' was a period of ancient history in [[Kara-Tur]] in the 2{{nd}} and 3{{rd}} millennia before [[Dale Reckoning]]. It was a time of empires and lesser states that were considered successors to [[Imaskar]] and precursors to [[Shou Lung]].

The First Age was determined by scholars to commence with the founding of the empire of [[Anok-Imaskar]] in {{Yearlink|-2487}}.
