A '''hunt''' was a religious community of worshipers of [[Malar]], the Beast Lord.

Each hunt was required to celebrate a "[[High Hunt]]" four times per year; one for each of the seasons.{{Cite dungeon/126/Vampires of Waterdeep: Blood of Malar}}
