The term '''Unseelie fey''' was used to generically refer to any [[fey]] who was [[evil]] or otherwise generally untrustworthy in their dealings with mortals. It could also refer to:
*Any fey member of the [[Unseelie Court]], or
*[[Unseelie]], a species of fey found in the Yuirwood.
