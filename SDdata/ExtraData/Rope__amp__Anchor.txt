The Rope & Anchor was a popular tattoo design in the city of Neverwinter in the late 14 century DR.
Rope & Anchor was an image of a blueish anchor with orange tangle of a rope within. It was believed to be a symbol of willpower.
By the , the tattoo design of Rope & Anchor was a well-known classic among pirates and sailors of the Sea of Swords. One tattooist parlor of note that offered Rope & Anchor tattoos was Rusty Als Tattoo & Pawn located in the Docks District.
