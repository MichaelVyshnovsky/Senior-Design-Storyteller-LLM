The '''''Rope & Anchor''''' was a popular [[tattoo]] design in the city of [[Neverwinter]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
''Rope & Anchor'' was an image of a blueish [[anchor]] with orange tangle of a [[rope]] within. It was believed to be a symbol of willpower.

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, the tattoo design of ''Rope & Anchor'' was a well-known classic among pirates and sailors of the [[Sea of Swords]]. One tattooist parlor of note that offered ''Rope & Anchor'' tattoos was [[Rusty Al's Tattoo & Pawn]] located in the [[Neverwinter/Docks District|Docks District]].
