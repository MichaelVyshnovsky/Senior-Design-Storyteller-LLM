'''Lampblack''' was a type of black pigment found in the Realms, made from the soot of [[oil lamp]]s.

==History==
Lampblack existed on [[Toril]] as far back as the [[Netherese Empire]].<ref>

==Usages==
* Some used lampblack for the purpose of disguising themselves.{{Cite polyhedron/91/The Living City: The Swineherd's House|19}}
* Older versions of the [[spell]] ''[[friends]]'' required the caster to coat their face in lampblack as a [[material component]].

==Notable Users==
* [[Sigird Sigirdson]], a [[thief]] in [[the Vast]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], was known to sometimes use lampblack in order to disguise himself.
