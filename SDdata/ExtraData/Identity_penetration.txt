'''''Identity penetration''''' was a [[psionic]] power of the [[telepathy]] [[discipline]] that uncovered another creature's real identity.

==Effects==
If successful, the ''identity penetration'' power correctly discerned the target's true identity, regardless of disguise, [[illusion]]s, [[polymorph]]ing, and so on. It targeted one individual with unlimited range, but required an initial ''[[contact]]''.

==Prerequisite Powers==
It required an initial ''[[contact]]''.

==Users==
''Identity penetration'' could be learned by [[psionicist]]s.
