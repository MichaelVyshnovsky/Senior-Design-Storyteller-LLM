Identity penetration was a psionic power of the telepathy discipline that uncovered another creatures real identity.
If successful, the identity penetration power correctly discerned the targets true identity, regardless of disguise, illusions, polymorphing, and so on. It targeted one individual with unlimited range, but required an initial contact.
It required an initial contact.
Identity penetration could be learned by psionicists.
* Vhondryl, a psionicist in Skullport and head of the Hall of Mental Splendor
* Ryvvik Dlardrageth, daemonfey psionicist and third-in-command of House Dlardrageth
