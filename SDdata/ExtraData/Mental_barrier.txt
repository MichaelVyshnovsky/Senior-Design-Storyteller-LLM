'''''Mental barrier''''' was a [[psionic]] devotion of the [[telepathy]] [[discipline]] that allowed the [[psionicist]] to protect their mind with a wall of thought.

==Effects==
''Mental barriers'' protected against a ''[[psionic blast]]'' but could be penetrated with a ''[[psychic crush]]''.

===Individuals===
