'''Mhairshaulk''' was the second [[sarrukh]] realm. It was contained on super-continent of  [[Merrouroboros]] before the [[Tearfall|Sundering]] of [[Abeir-Toril]] and afterward on the [[Chultan Peninsula]].

==History==
Around {{Yearlink|-34800}}, Mhairshaulk was founded by the sarrukh.

Around {{Yearlink|-33500}}, the [[yuan-ti]] gained control of the realm.

==Races==
It was believed that the Mhairshaulkans created many races, some of which later became extinct. Those that had survived to modern times included:
* [[nagas]]
* [[pterafolk]]
* [[troglodytes]]
* [[yuan-ti]]
