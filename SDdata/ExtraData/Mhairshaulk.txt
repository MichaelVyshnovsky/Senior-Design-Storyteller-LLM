Mhairshaulk was the second sarrukh realm. It was contained on super-continent of  Merrouroboros before the Sundering of Abeir-Toril and afterward on the Chultan Peninsula.
Around , Mhairshaulk was founded by the sarrukh.
Around , the yuan-ti gained control of the realm.
It was believed that the Mhairshaulkans created many races, some of which later became extinct. Those that had survived to modern times included
* nagas
* pterafolk
* troglodytes
* yuan-ti
