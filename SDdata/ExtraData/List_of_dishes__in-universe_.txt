This is a list of dishes known to exist in the Realms, excluding [[bread]]s, [[drink]]s, [[sauces]], and desserts. It includes information on the cuisine of the dish, establishments and cities where they could be found, and each of the dish's main ingredients. Cuisines are broken down into the nation of origin, or general area of the content that has shared culinary influences, and well as racial cuisines for non-[[human]] people of the Realms.
