'''''Shadow Under Thunderholme''''' is an adventure pack for ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Online]]'' where the player characters delve into the long-lost [[dwarf|dwarven]] ruins of [[Thunderholme]] and face the [[Cult of the Dragon]] and the [[shadow dragon]] [[Aurgloroasa]]. The adventure pack was added to the game on March 10, 2014 as part of Update 21.
