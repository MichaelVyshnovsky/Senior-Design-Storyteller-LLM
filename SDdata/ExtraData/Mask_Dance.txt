The '''Mask Dance''' was a [[Cormyr|Cormyran]] dance performed by a paid performer, usually in a private club.  The dancer wore nothing but a mask while gyrating about on a stage.  The Mask Dance first became popular in [[1476 DR]] and remained popular as of [[1479 DR]].
