'''''A Study in Sable''''' is a stand-alone quest in ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Online]]'' where the player characters must use detective work to uncover the perpetrator of a brutal attack on Sir [[Ebermund Harker]] in [[Sable Manor]]. The quest was added on November 12, 2013 as part of Update 20.
