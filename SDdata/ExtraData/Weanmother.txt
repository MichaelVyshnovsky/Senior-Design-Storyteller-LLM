A '''weanmother''' or '''word-wean mother'''

==Activities==
The role was delegated to a noble daughter of a noble drow house. Their job was to tend to the needs of a drow infant for ten years, teaching her or him the [[Drow language]] (including [[Drow Sign Language]]), how to manifest her or his spell-like abilities, and above all to teach the child their place in drow society, all while putting them to work.
