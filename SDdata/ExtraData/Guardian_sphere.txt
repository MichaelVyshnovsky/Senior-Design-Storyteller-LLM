The Guardian sphere was a group of spells granted to clerics, crusaders, and monks that produced zones of protection, often reactive to threats or by way of a semi-sentient creature.
Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.
Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast
* blessed watchfulness
* light/darkness
* sacred guardian
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast
* iron vigil
* silence, 15 radius
* wyvern watch
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast
* continual light/darkness
* glyph of warding
Intermediate casters had the ability to cast
* abjure
* dimensional anchor
Those with a high level of skill had access to
* dispel evil/good
* unceasing vigilance of the holy sentinel
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast
* blade barrier
* forbiddance
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast
* symbol
