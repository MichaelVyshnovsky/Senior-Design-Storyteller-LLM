The '''Arkhan''' was the traditional title for the ruling monarch of [[Raumathar]].

==History==
The first individual to hold the title of Arkhan was [[Umathar Nhalass I|Umathar I]] after he united the various tribes of Raumathar and began the rule of the [[Nhalass Dynasty]].
