"'''Rahimat'''" was a common male [[Alzhedo]] given name. Individuals named Rahimat included:
* [[Rahimat el Taisar|Rahimat yn Fahd el Taisar yi Almraiven]], a tailor and member of the [[Knights of the Shield]],
* [[Rahimat yi Manshaka]], an alias for Grand [[Yrshelem]] of Caravans [[Rafi al Shuree]], and
* Rahimat el Cajaan, the father of [[Alzhedo dictionary#Y|Ynamalik]] [[Nadim el Cajaan]], conqueror of the [[Netherese]] city of [[Tzindylspar]].
