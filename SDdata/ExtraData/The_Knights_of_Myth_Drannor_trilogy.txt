{{DEFAULTSORT:Knights of Myth Drannor, The}}
The '''Knights of Myth Drannor trilogy''' is a trilogy of books written by [[Ed Greenwood]]. It follows the journey of the [[Knights of Myth Drannor]], a party named by the members of Ed Greenwood's home campaign. Ed Greenwood had previously written about the adventuring group in other novels, as well as in a short story in the video game manual of ''[[Eye of the Beholder III: Assault on Myth Drannor]]'', known as ''[[Moonrise over Myth Drannor]]''.
