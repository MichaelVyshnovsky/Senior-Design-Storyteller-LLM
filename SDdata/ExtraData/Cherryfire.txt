'''Cherryfire''' was a type of drink found in [[Cormyr]].

==History==
In [[1369 DR]], [[Ondrin Dracohorn]] kept a belt flask of cherryfire mixed with [[mint wine]] and drank it when speaking with [[Vangerdahast]], who thought it was a strange combination.
