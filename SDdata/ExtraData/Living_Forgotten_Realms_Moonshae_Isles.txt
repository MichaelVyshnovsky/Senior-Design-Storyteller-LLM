The '''Moonshae Isles''' region of the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] campaign was assigned to players in the Western United States. It was set in the [[Moonshae Isles]].


==Adventures==
===MOON1-1 Nature's Wrath===
{{Main|Nature's Wrath}}
{{Quote|''Faith in Chauntea runs strong amongst the Ffolk, and the village of Warlsbry is no exception. The village has fallen from favor and now the beasts of field and sea bring their wrath against the village. You must return the village to her good graces or it will not survive the coming winter. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in The Moonshae Isles for characters levels 1&ndash;4.''}}
* '''Designer(s):''' [[Michael Mockus]]
* '''Released:''' September 2008
* '''Levels:''' 1{{st}} &ndash; 4{{th}}
