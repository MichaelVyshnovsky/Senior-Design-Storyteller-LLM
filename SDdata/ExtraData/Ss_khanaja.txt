'''Ss'khanaja''' was a city inhabited mainly by high-class [[naga]] and their staff, with some [[yuan-ti]] and [[lizardfolk]] making their homes in the [[Underdark]] passage that the city was built around. It was located in the [[Serpent Hills]] of [[Najara]], in the west.
