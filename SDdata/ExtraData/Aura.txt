An '''aura''', also known as a '''dweomer''', was a magical emanation from a person or object that was supernaturally detectable. Many [[divination spells]] functioned by recognizing auras. [[Undead]], liars, secret doors, and [[law]]ful, [[chaotic]], [[good]], and [[evil]] creatures

[[Cleric]]s always had particularly powerful auras that matched the [[alignment]] of their deity. Many [[paladin]]s were surrounded by auras of courage.
