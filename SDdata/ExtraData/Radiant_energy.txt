'''Radiant energy'''<ref group="note">This term is meant to include "radiant damage", that could be inflicted by some weapons and spells. was a type of [[energy]] associated with certain good and benevolent creatures, along with those associated with the [[sun]], the [[Positive Energy plane]], as well as the [[Upper planes]].

==Description==
Radiant energy was said to appear as a combination of "heat and light", When wielded offensively, either by means of a weapon or by magic, it both burned a target and burdened their soul with overwhelming power.

==Properties==
This form of energy was extremely harmful to [[fiend]]s and the [[undead]], especially [[shadow]]s.<ref name=MM34e-p172"> It would often negate the regenerative properties of various types of undead and powerful fiends alike.

==History==
The [[red dragon|red]] [[great wyrm]] [[Imvaernarhro]] was known to have studied a variety of potent and unique spells, many of which harnessed the powers of radiant energy.

==Notable Manifestations==
* Many [[aasimar]] were imbued with radiant energy and could manifest it in fantastic ways.
* [[Balhiir]]s emanated radiant energy when they consumed excessive magical energy.
* Devices from the [[illithid]] [[nautiloid]] the ''[[Id Ascendant]]'' fired beams of radiant energy.
* The "heart" of the [[chardalyn dragon]] was said to be made of pure radiant energy.
* [[Chaos shard|Prismatic shards]] were a type of [[elemental]] that exuded an aura of radiant energy and they could attack by firing beams of radiant energy from their crystalline bodies.
* Cultists of [[Tyranthraxus]] could shroud themselves in multicolored flames of radiant energy.
* A certain pool within the [[Vault of Song]] was charged with radiant energy.
* [[Solar dragon]]s exuded a [[dragon breath|breath weapon]] that comprised a mote of radiant energy that burst upon impact.

==Notable Wielders==
* [[Celestial patron]] [[warlock]]s of a certain power could transform their bodies into vessels for radiant energy.
* [[Doomguide]]s of [[Kelemvor]] could channel radiant energy into their weapons in a manner reminiscent of their god's own weapon, ''[[Fatal Touch]]''.
* Clerics of deities of the [[life domain]] and [[paladin]]s could imbue their weapons with radiant energy.
* [[Psionic]]s utilizing their [[Psychokinesis|psychokinetic]] powers could channel power from the Positive Energy plane and emit a beam of [[ectoplasm]] charged with radiant energy.
* [[Sun Soul monk]]s channeled radiant energy in a number of their martial arts techniques.
