A '''blingdenstone warpick''' is a +1 magic [[heavy pick]] from the [[drow]]-infested city of [[Blingdenstone]]. Most of them are owned by the homeless [[deep gnome]]s in the [[Silver Marches]]. They are worth around 8,000 gold pieces.
