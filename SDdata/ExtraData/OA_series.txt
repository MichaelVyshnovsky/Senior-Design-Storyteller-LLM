The '''OA series''' was a series of seven 1st and 2nd edition [[Kara-Tur]]-based adventures published between 1986 and 1990. OA1 to OA4 were not branded with the Forgotten Realms logo, but the wiki accepts them to be part of Forgotten Realms canon.
