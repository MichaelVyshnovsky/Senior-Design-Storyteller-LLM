The '''Sandvoyagers Guild''' was a [[Durpari]] [[guild]] of desert guides operating in the [[Raurin]]. They guided caravans from [[Bralizzar]] to the lands north of the the desert, and back.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|5}}

==Overview==
The Guild reopened the northwards trade from Durpar after the [[River Athis]] dried.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|12}} They raised the Sandvoyagers' Guideposts, a number of poles from which hung silvery bangles:{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|26}} those bangles marked the Sandvoyagers Track, the path they took across the desert.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation/Map}} The Sandvoyagers' Guild had built a compound on the [[Oasis of the White Palm]].{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|71}}

===Members of the Sandvoyagers Guild===
*[[Tolnus Granicus]]

==Slaver Ring==
As of {{yearlink|1356}}, a slaver ring had captured the guildmaster, [[Tolnus Granicus]].{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|79}} These slavers were also posing as members of the Guild in Bralizzar{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|14}} and in the Oasis of the White Palm,{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|73}} which allowed them to go on with their unsavory activities, such as looting the bodies of dead [[Airlancers]].{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|32}} These slavers had [[drow]] allies, and made use of the [[Temple of Set]] directly beneath the Guildhall.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|78-79}}
