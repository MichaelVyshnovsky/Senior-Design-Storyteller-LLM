'''''Steal This Hook!''''' was a series of web articles written first by [[Doug Beyer]] and then by [[Robert Wiese]] on the [https://dnd.wizards.com/ Dungeons & Dragons] website published monthly (initially twice-monthly) from September 2004 to May 2009. It presents a variety of plot hooks and adventure ideas, first for the ''Eberron'' setting but later also for the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'', ''Greyhawk'', ''Ravenloft'', and ''d20 Modern'' settings. With the transition to 4{{th}} edition, [[Nentir Vale]] was included.

For completeness, all articles are listed below, with only those articles with content intended for the ''Forgotten Realms'' having citation links.
{{quote|This column brings you, the DM, a bevy of ideas to steal for your campaign. Hooks include '''Eberron''', '''Forgotten Realms''', and even '''d20 Modern''' locales.|Original series summary}}
