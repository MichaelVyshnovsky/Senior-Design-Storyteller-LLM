The '''Elemental Earth sphere''' was a group of spells that manipulated the [[element of earth]]. [[Druid]]s were granted unlimited access and [[clerics]] were granted minor access to these spells.

==Spells in the Sphere of Elemental Earth==
Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:
* ''[[strength of stone]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[soften earth and stone]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[meld into stone]]''
* ''[[stone shape]]''
Intermediate clerics and druids had the ability to cast:
* ''[[adamantite mace]]''
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[spike stones]]''
* ''[[transmute rock to mud|transmute rock to mud/transmute mud to rock]]''
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:
* ''[[stone tell]]''
* ''[[transmute water to dust|transmute water to dust/improved create water]]''<ref group="note">This spell was removed from the elemental earth sphere in the ''[[Player's Option: Spells & Magic]]'' sourcebook. See page 187.
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[animate rock]]''
* ''[[antimineral shell]]''
* ''[[conjure earth elemental|conjure/dismiss earth elemental]]''
* ''[[earthquake]]''
* ''[[transmute metal to wood]]''
