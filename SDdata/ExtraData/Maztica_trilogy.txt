The '''Maztica trilogy''' is a series of books set in the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'' on the continent of [[Maztica]] at the time of its "discovery" by [[Cordell]] of [[Amn]]. The trilogy was written by [[Douglas Niles]].

The main character is [[Erixitl]], a priestess of [[Qotal]] and former slave.
