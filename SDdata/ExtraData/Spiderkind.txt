Spiderkind was a grouping of all spiders and creatures that resembled spiders (arachnoid creatures). Spiderkind were revered by Lolth-worshiping drow. Arachnomancers could communicate with all spiderkind. 
Nearly all spiders and arachnoid creatures were considered spiderkind. Scorpions, however, were not traditionally considered spiderkind. The following is a list of creatures classified as spiderkind
