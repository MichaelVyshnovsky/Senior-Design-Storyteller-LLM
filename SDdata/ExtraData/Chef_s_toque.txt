A chefs toque was a variety of hat in the Realms that was commonly worn by chefs, often as part of an ensemble outfit called a "chefs whites."
These hats were white in coloration.
* The kobold chefs of Xanathar wore such hats.
* Norver
* Gorstaf, a duergar piemaker from Gracklstugh in the late 15 century DR.
* Jandar Chergoba, head chef of the Cassalanter familys villa.
* Zastreva, a cook in the Water Queens House in Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR, wore a chefs toque.
* Across the Sword Coast these hats were typically worn by only the finest of chefs.
* Chefs toques were worn by cooks of the Palace of Manniccis in Sumbria, a city-state of the Blade Kingdoms.
A chefs toque was widely considered in the Sword Coast to be a great status symbol for chefs.
