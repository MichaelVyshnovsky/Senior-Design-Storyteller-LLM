'''ProFantasy Software Ltd.''' is a company that develops mapping software for tabletop role-playing and war games.https://www.profantasy.com/products/default.asp

==History==
ProFantasy Software Ltd was founded in 1993 and became an established map-making software company.https://www.profantasy.com/fratlas/features.htm [[Robert Lazzaretti]] was involved in designing the symbols.https://www.profantasy.com/fratlas/cc2symbols.htm Since its release, their atlas received three updates.http://www.wizards.com/dnd/article.asp?x=dnd/ex20020218a The Interactive Atlas product completely sold out, and remains out of print over two decades later.https://www.profantasy.com/fratlas/ordering.htm
