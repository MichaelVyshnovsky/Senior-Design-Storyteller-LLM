The '''War sphere''' was a group of spells granted to [[crusader]]s. These spells focused on giving advantage in large-scale conflicts, rather than individual or small party confrontations.

==Spells in the Sphere of War==
Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner crusaders of this sphere could cast:
* ''[[courage]]''
* ''[[morale]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[emotion perception]]''
* ''[[rally]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[adaptation]]''
* ''[[caltrops]]''
* ''[[fortify]]''
Intermediate divine casters had the ability to use:
* ''[[entrench]]''
* ''[[leadership|leadership/doubt]]''
* ''[[tanglefoot|tanglefoot/selective passage]]''
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[disguise]]''
* ''[[illusory artillery]]''
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:
* ''[[gravity variation]]''
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[illusory fortifications]]''
* ''[[shadow engines]]''<ref group="note">This spell is listed as "shadow engine" on page 188 of the ''[[Player's Option: Spells & Magic]]'' sourcebook, but the spell description on page 108 of * ''[[Tome of Magic 2nd edition]]'' uses the plural form.
