An Urphong was a title similar to a mayor or local lord in Rashemen.
Urphongs ruled over the towns and village scattered throughout Rashemen. The Urphong was supported by a city watch, or other such small fighting force, which was often referred to as the "Urphongs Guard" or "Urphongs Host."
