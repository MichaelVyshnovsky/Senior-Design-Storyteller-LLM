An '''Urphong''' was a title similar to a mayor or local lord in [[Rashemen]].

==History==
Urphong's ruled over the towns and village scattered throughout Rashemen. The Urphong was supported by a city watch, or other such small fighting force, which was often referred to as the "Urphong's Guard" or "Urphong's Host."
