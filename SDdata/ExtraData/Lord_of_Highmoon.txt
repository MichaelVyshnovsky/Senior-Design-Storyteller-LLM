The '''Lord of Highmoon''' was the title of the military, governmental and legal authority of [[Deepingdale]] and its capital city of [[Highmoon]]. This lordship was not hereditary, but rather appointed to a deserving candidate by the dale's town council, a collection of upstanding citizens of the city and surrounding countryside.
