Xanathar may refer to one or more of several beholders
* Xanathar (title) the title held by the second and subsequent leaders of the Xanathars Thieves Guild, derived from the personal name of its founder.
* Xanathar (original) the original beholder named Xanathar whose identity was later claimed by another.
* Xanathar (Kirueskai) the beholder named Kirueskai, also known as Kirukeskai, or The Eye, who claimed the identity of Xanathar in the 14 century DR.
* Xanathar (Xandulzrithral) an elder beholder who peacefully retired from the position in order to avoid becoming a target.
* Xanathar (Izulktur) who held the title of Xanathar for a short time in the 15 century DR.
* Xanathar (Zushaxx) the Xanathar of the late 15 century DR.
It might also refer to
* Xanathars Thieves Guild the thieves guild led by Xanathar.
* Eye of Xanathar the Eleven Eyes of the Xanathar, or the eyes of Xanathar, a covert organization that ensured success of the Xanathars Guild.
* Xanathar longswords enchanted blades used by the members of the Xanathars Thieves Guild circa 1372 DR.
* Xanathars Wrath an adventure part of the D&D Adventurers League.
* Xanathars Guide to Everything a 5 edition sourcebook.
