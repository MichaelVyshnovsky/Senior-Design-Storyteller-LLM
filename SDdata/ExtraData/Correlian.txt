'''Correlian''' was a [[constellation]] in the [[elf|elven]] tradition. It consisted of thirteen stars. <ref group="note">By its name, this constellation may be associated with the elven deity [[Corellon]].

==Observation==
It could be observed in autumn.

==Significance==
In [[moon elf]] tradition, the appearance of Correlian marked the official end of summer.

The [[liquor]] known as [[Elverquisst]] was distinctive for displaying a replica of Correlian as part of its drinking ritual.
