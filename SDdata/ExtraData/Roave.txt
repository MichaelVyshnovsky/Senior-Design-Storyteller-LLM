'''Roave''' were a mundane species of spider that were found in abundant numbers in the [[upperdark|upper]] [[Northdark]], around the city of [[Menzoberranzan]].{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-02-02 - Common Menzo Vintages}}

==Description==
These spiders were rather small, with a [[human]] palm being capable of holding five of them at once, and they were black in coloration.

==Usages==
The inhabitants of Menzoberranzan were known to use the internal juices of this species to brew [[roavrae]].
