The '''families of Amn''' were a series of royal dynasties, high houses, aristocratic families and mercantile houses of the [[Amn|merchant nation]].

==List of families==
;Royal dynasties:
* [[Parhek]], the final royal dynasty that ended with the death of King [[Dhanar Parhek|Dhanar]] in [[1276 DR]].
* [[Torlath]], the founding royal family of Amn that started with the legendary King [[Esmel Torlath I|Esmel]].
* [[Wavegold]], the middle royal dynasty of Amn.

;High Houses:
:These families each "owned" a seat on the [[Council of Five (Amn)|Council of Five]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
* [[Selemchant]], one of the, if not the most powerful family, from the ''[[Athkatla|City of Coin]]''. They sponsored the Athkatla's arcane enforcers, the [[Cowled Wizards]].
* [[Alibakkar]], this rich family of merchants has had a seat in Amn's ruling government since the [[Council of Six (Amn)|Council of Six]].
* [[Dannihyr]], an enigmatic house from [[Eshpurta]], who had secret ties to the [[Shadow thief of Amn|Shadow Thieves]].
* [[Ophal]], a historied, noble family from the city of [[Crimmor]].
* [[Nashivaar]], this [[Esmeltaran]] noble family had long-standing connections within the [[church of Cyric]].
