The Legacy of the Drow is the third series of novels about Drizzt DoUrden written by R. A. Salvatore. It was written after the Dark Elf trilogy, but chronologically it follows the Icewind Dale trilogy. It precedes the Paths of Darkness series.
The series is available in full as the Legacy of the Drow Gift Set (individual volumes) and as the Legacy of the Drow Collectors Edition (omnibus).
