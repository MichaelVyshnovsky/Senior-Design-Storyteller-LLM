The '''Healing sphere''' was a group of spells granted to [[cleric]]s, [[crusader]]s, and [[druid]]s without limitations, and to [[monk]]s and [[shaman]]s with minor access only. Spells in this sphere could cure or cause damage to health, but not bring beings back to life.

==Spells in the Sphere of Healing==
Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:
* ''[[cure light wounds|cure/cause light wounds]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[cure moderate wounds|cure/cause moderate wounds]]''
* ''[[slow poison]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[cure blindness or deafness|cure/cause blindness or deafness]]''
* ''[[cure disease|cure/cause disease]]''
* ''[[hold poison]]''
* ''[[repair injury]]''
Intermediate clerics and druids had the ability to cast:
* ''[[cure serious wounds|cure/cause serious wounds]]''
* ''[[fortify]]''
* ''[[neutralize poison|neutralize poison/poison]]''
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[cure critical wounds|cure/cause critical wounds]]''
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:
* ''[[heal|heal/harm]]''
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[regenerate|regenerate/wither]]''
