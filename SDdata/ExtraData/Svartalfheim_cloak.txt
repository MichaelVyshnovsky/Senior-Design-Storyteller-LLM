'''''Svartalfheim cloaks''''' were magical [[cloak]]s from the realm of [[Svartalfheim]].{{Cite organized play/LC/Three Coins in a Well|36}}

==Description==
These cloaks were black and blue in coloration, made of a light material, and had a fur collar.

==Powers==
These cloaks granted their wearers increased powers of stealth, on par with that of a ''[[cloak of elvenkind]]'', and granted the power to cast ''[[wraithform]]'' once per day.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1372}},{{LCRB}} a group of [[adventurer]]s from the city of [[Ravens Bluff]] on the world of [[Toril]] traveled to Ysgard in search of the legendary [[Well of Mimir]].<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/Three Coins in a Well|2}} While on their journey they came into the possession of one of these cloaks.{{Cite organized play/LC/Three Coins in a Well|21}} Other sources told of the Ravenians having broken into [[Okalnir|Brimir's hall]] and stolen the cloak from his bedroom<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/Three Coins in a Well|24}} or having won a ''Svartalfheim cloak'' from Queen [[Titania]] for having competed against [[fey]] of the [[Seelie Court (divine realm)|Seelie Court]] in a series of challenges.<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/Three Coins in a Well|27}}
