'''''Avatar Series ''''' is a trilogy of comics illustrating the ''[[Avatar series]]'' novels. It was published by [[wikipedia:en:DC Comics|DC Comics]] in 1991. It was written by [[Barbara Kesel]], edited by [[Elliot S. Maggin]], and drawn by [[Dameon Willich]]. It occurs in [[1358 DR]]{{Cite web/Presenting...Seven Millennia of Realms Fiction}}, during the [[Time of Troubles]].
