[[Emerlin III]] was the last king of the lost [[dwarven]] kingdom of [[Thunderholme]]. He was betrayed and murdered by his high priest [[Dagan]] who was in turn manipulated by the [[shadow dragon]] [[Aurgloroasa]].<ref name=CotD-p39>
