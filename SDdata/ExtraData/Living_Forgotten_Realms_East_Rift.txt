The '''East Rift''' region of the [[Living Forgotten Realms]] campaign was assigned to players in the Pacific United States. It was set in the area comprising the [[East Rift]] and its environs.

==Adventures==
===EAST1-1 These Hallowed Halls===
{{Main|These Hallowed Halls}}
{{Quote|''The Crafty Kobold Salvage Company has fearlessly plumbed forgotten treasures from the depths of the earth for many years. Now the owner's son and his expedition have gone missing and he needs adventurers of uncommon mettle to brave the dangers of the Underdark, find the lost explorers, and perhaps even unlock the ancient secrets of Lodestone Deep. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the East Rift for characters levels 1&ndash;4.''}}
* '''Designer(s):''' [[Bill Benham]]
* '''Released:''' August 2008
* '''Levels:''' 1{{st}} &ndash; 4{{th}}

===EAST1-2 Marauder's Spear===
{{Main|Marauder's Spear}}
{{Quote|''In the pre-dawn gloom, gnolls savage a dusty halfling trading encampment on the edge of the Eastern Shaar. Hapless ''hin'' and ''hurbryn'' merchants cry out for protection from these marauding dogs. Why are the gnolls attacking here in such numbers? Who is the real leader of the pack? A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the East Rift for characters levels 4&ndash;7.''}}
* '''Designer(s):''' [[Art Severance]], [[Mike Rizzo]]
* '''Released:''' December 2008
* '''Levels:''' 4{{th}} &ndash; 7{{th}}

===EAST1-3 Unbidden===
{{Main|Unbidden}}
{{Quote|''An attack by foul creatures has forced the closure of a dwarven mining operation. Adventurers are needed to investigate the threat and make the mine safe for reopening. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the East Rift for characters levels 7&ndash;10.''}}
* '''Designer(s):''' [[Thomas J. Scott]]
* '''Released:''' January 2009
* '''Levels:''' 7{{th}} &ndash; 10{{th}}

===EAST1-4 Darkness in Delzimmer===
{{Main|Darkness in Delzimmer}}
{{Quote|''A simple caravan run to the free city of Delzimmer takes an unexpected turn. It's said that there is no honor among thieves. Unfortunately, their struggles can still cause collateral damage, and the future of the Crafty Kobold Salvage Company hangs in the balance. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the East Rift for characters levels 1&ndash;4.''}}
* '''Designer(s):''' [[Roy Thalman]]
* '''Released:''' April 2009
* '''Levels:''' 1{{st}} &ndash; 4{{th}}

===EAST1-5 Mole Hunt===
{{Main|Mole Hunt}}
{{Quote|''You have been tasked to travel through the Chondalwood in an attempt to retrieve a doppelganger spy working for the dwarves of Eartheart. * '''Designer(s):''' [[Jeanette D. Martin]], [[Sara D. Green]]
* '''Released:''' August 2009

===EAST1-6 The Fardrop Incident===
{{Main|The Fardrop Incident}}
{{Quote|''The Crafty Kobold Salvage Company has lost contact with an expedition to the abandoned Underchasm outpost of Fardrop. {{Main|Ends and Means}}

A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in the East Rift for characters levels 4&ndash;7.''}}
