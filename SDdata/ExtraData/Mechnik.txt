'''Mechnik''' was the title given to the leader of the [[Gospoda]] in the city of [[Almorel]] in the [[Hordelands]].

==Activities==
The Mechnik served as the head of the ruling council of Almorel. They moderated meetings of the Gospoda while serving the general welfare of the city.
