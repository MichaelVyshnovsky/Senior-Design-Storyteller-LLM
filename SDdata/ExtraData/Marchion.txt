"'''Marchion'''" was the official title for the ruler of the city of [[Mirabar]].<ref group="note">According to the ''[[Forgotten Realms Style Guide]]'', "marchion" is a term unique to the Forgotten Realms. In the real world, the corresponding term is [[wikipedia:Marquis|"marquis"]] or [[wikipedia:Marquess|"marquess"]] and referred to the ruler of a [[wikipedia:March (territory)|march]]. The feminine version of the word is "marchioness". The title was a hereditary one.

The title was also used in the kingdom of [[Cormyr]] for lords of border territories and was a rank of great honor. Marchions were below dukes in rank and above counts or earls. The feminine form of the word was "marchioness".{{Cite dragon/407/Crowns and Mantles: The Ranks and Titles of Cormyr|21}}

"Marchion" was a word in the [[Common]] tongue. In the [[Chondathan language]], the word was ''mahrsar'' ({{Pronounce|MAR|sarr}}). In Chondathan, the word for "marchioness" was ''mahrsara''.{{Cite dragon/407/Crowns and Mantles: The Ranks and Titles of Cormyr|22}}
