A '''whorlspell''' was a phenomenon of [[Avernus]] in the [[Nine Hells]].

==Effects==
Per the [[archdevil]] [[Nergal (archdevil)|Nergal]], whorlspells were natural phenomena in Avernus that snatched objects from other [[planes]] and deposited them in that one. and sometimes even creatures capable of slaying [[fiend]]s.
