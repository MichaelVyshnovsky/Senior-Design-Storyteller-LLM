'''Roulette''', also known as the '''wheel of chance''', was a type of game of chance that people would gamble over.

==Description==
This game involved a sort of wheel, These depressions alternated in coloration from black to red{{Cite polyhedron/41/The Living City: Volodar's Stardust Inn|26}} [[Dice]] would also be rolled across the table on a checkered lane of red and black squares.

==History==
During the days of [[Netheril]], in the small city of [[Imbrue]] this was one of many games of chance that one could play at the [[House of Tyche]].

Centuries later, in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], one could find wheels of chance in a variety of establishments in the [[Sword Coast]], [[north Faerûn]], and [[west Faerûn]].
