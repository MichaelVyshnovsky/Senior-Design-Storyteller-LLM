The '''Gnome domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[gnome]]-inspired [[spell]]s and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
Their [[illusion]] spells gained some potency.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[silent image]]'', ''[[gembomb]]'', and ''[[minor image]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[minor creation]]'', ''[[hallucinatory terrain]]'', and ''[[fantastic machine]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[screen]]'', ''[[Otto's irresistible dance]]'', and ''[[summon nature's ally]]'' ([[earth elemental]]s and [[animal]]s only).
