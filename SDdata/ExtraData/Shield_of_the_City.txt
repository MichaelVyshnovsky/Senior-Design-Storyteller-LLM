The '''Shield of the City''' was a medal awarded to members of the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|city watch]] for outstanding acts of military service.  Occasionally a noble who participated in a battle would receive the award.{{Cite dungeon/189/Eye on the Realms: A Deadly Civic Honor}}  

==Description==
The award was a large, gold, shield-shaped badge worn on the chest.
