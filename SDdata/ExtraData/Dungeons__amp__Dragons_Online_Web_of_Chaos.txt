'''''Web of Chaos''''' is a  free adventure pack for ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Online]]'' where the player characters face strange [[drow]] cultists in [[Stormreach]], only to discover [[Lolth]] and her plots for both [[Eberron]] and [[Faer√ªn]]. The adventure pack was added to the game on February 27, 2012 as part of Update 16, and served as an introduction to ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Online: Menace of the Underdark]]'' and further [[Forgotten Realms]] content.
