The '''ometsuke''' were officers in the government of [[Wa]] responsible for the punishment of [[daimyo]]. Like all officers of the [[shogun]]ate, to be an ometsuke was considered an honored role. Any appointment to such a position had to be confirmed by the shogun himself.
