A '''Nawab''' was the title of the leading, or most wealthy, merchant in a city or town within the [[Shining Lands]].

==Activities==
Nawabs served as a mayor of sorts. They were responsible for appointing judges, maintaining a security force, collecting taxes, and performing other various functions of local government.
