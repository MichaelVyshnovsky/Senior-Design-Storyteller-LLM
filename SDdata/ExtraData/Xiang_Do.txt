'''Xiang Do''' was a martial art taught at [[Xiang Temple]] in [[Telflamm]] in the mid-1370s [[DR]]. The [[monk]] [[Raidon Kane]] was a practitioner.

==Leaping Tiger Stance==
The Leaping Tiger Stance was a stance taught as part of the ways of the monks in [[Xiang Temple]] in [[Telflamm]]. [[Raidon Kane]] used a similar stance while fighting [[Chun (Telflamm)|Chun]] in {{yearlink|1375}}.{{cite book/Stardeep|33|4}}<ref group=note>The Leaping Tiger is probably intended to call back to the Leaping Dragon Stance, which came up in a different D&D product: [[Wikipedia:Tome of Battle: The Book of Nine Swords|''The Book of Nine Swords'']].
