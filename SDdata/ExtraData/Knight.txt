A '''knight''' was an armored, mounted warrior serving a lord in feudal societies. In the [[Forgotten Realms|Realms]], the term could also refer to, among other things:
* [[Eldritch knight]], a warrior trained equally in the arts of [[Fighter|combat]] and [[Wizard|magic]],
* [[Triadic knight]], a warrior worshiping [[the Triad]] as a unit,
* [[Purple Dragon knight]], a member of the [[Purple Dragons]] of [[Cormyr]],
* [[Knight of the Blue Moon]], an elite knight of [[Waterdeep]],
* [[Knight of the Flying Hunt]], an elite defender of [[Nimbral]],
* [[Knights in Silver|Knight in Silver]], a member of the army of [[Silverymoon]]
* [[Knights of Holy Judgment|Knight of Holy Judgment]], a [[paladin]] in service to [[Tyr]],
* [[Knights of the Merciful Sword|Knight of the Merciful Sword]], another type of paladin in service to [[Tyr]],
* [[Knights of Ilmater|Knight of Ilmater]], a [[paladin]] in service to [[Ilmater]],
* [[Knights of Myth Drannor|Knight of Myth Drannor]], a member of a famous adventuring company,
* [[Knights of Samular|Knight of Samular]], another type of paladin in service to [[Tyr]],
* [[Knights of the Black Gauntlet|Knight of the Black Gauntlet]], a crusader in service to [[Bane]],
* [[Knights of the Bleeding Shield|Knight of the Bleeding Shield]], a member of another order in service to [[Ilmater]],
* [[Knights of the Eternal Dragon|Knight of the Eternal Dragon]], someone dedicated to the destruction of [[Xavarathimius]],
* Knight of the [[Golden Star]], a warrior in service to [[Corellon Larethian]],
* [[Knight of the Weave]], a warrior worshiping the [[Weave]] itself,
* [[Knights of the Shield|Knight of the Shield]], a member of a secret society based in the [[Lands of Intrigue]],
* [[Vengeance knight]], an elite fighter in service to the Knights of the Shield,
* [[Silverhair Knights|Silverhair Knight]], a [[cleric|priest]] in service to [[Eilistraee]],
* [[Death knight]], a powerful [[undead]] warrior,
* [[Darksong Knight]], another kind of warrior in service to Eilistraee,
* [[Thayan knight]], a general of the army of [[Thay]]
* [[Griffon Knights|Griffon Knight]], a defender of the town of [[Alamontyr]],
* [[Knights of the North|Knight of the North]], a secret society of adventurers from the [[Moonsea]] region,
* [[Warlock Knights|Warlock Knight]], one of the lords of [[Vaasa]], or
* [[Moon Knights|Moon Knight]], a warrior in service to [[Sehanine Moonbow]].

It could also refer to a specific type of character in various editions of the [[Dungeons & Dragons]] rules:
* [[:Category:Elven knights (2e)|Elven knights]], a kit in 2{{nd}} edition
* [[Knight (class)]], a class in 3{{rd}} edition,
* [[Knight (build)]], a fighter build in 4{{th}} edition, or
* [[Knight (NPC)]], a generic nonplayer character in 5{{th}} edition.
