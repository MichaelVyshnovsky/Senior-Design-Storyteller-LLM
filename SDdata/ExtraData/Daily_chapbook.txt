'''Daily chapbooks''', also known as a '''dailies''', were short printed document which usually contained tabloid-style news or political rants. They were common in [[Amn]].{{Cite dragon/416/Eye on the Realms: The Awakener of Golems|31}}

==Structure==
Dailies usually all followed the same structure:
* The headline news, called ''high clack''
* Some opinion columns, called ''screeds''
* The ''altara'', publishing sermons and decrets from local [[:category:temples|temples]]
* Some serialized fiction works, usually an epic saga, a farce, and a romantic story featuring feuding families
* A page of word puzzles, called ''thoughtfuls''
* A humorous page of spicy riddles and jests
