The Shield Belt of Storms was a magical golden chain mail belt, made from beljurels, gold, platinum, and steel. It augmented physical armour with magical defences, and added lighting to a weapon wielded by the wearer.
The belt was covered in ornate golden scales with shapes like oak leaves, shields and swords, and was adnorned with sixteen gems. In profile, the belt appeared to be the head of an ice eagle, with a large sea-green gem as its eye.
The gems are older than the belt itself, having been enchanted before the belts creation.
