Dorn was a common dwarven and half-orc male first name.
It could also refer to
*Dorn Black Axe, a dwarven warrior from the Cavern of Cloven Heads
*Dorns Deep, a dwarven stronghold in Icewind Dale
*Dorn Graybrook, a human half-golem
*Dorn Heldeion, a member of Oebles Council of Nine Merchants
*Dorn Il-Khan, a half-orc blackguard
*Dorn Redbear, a human barbarian
*Dorn Strongcroft, a dwarven tunnel worker
*Hamminas Dorn, the Lord Speaker of Tarmalune
