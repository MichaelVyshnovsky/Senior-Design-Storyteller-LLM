==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, the short poem was recited by the [[devil]] [[Raphael]] to victims of a [[nautiloid]] crash who had been infected with [[illithid]] [[tadpole]]s. When asked, Raphael said that he was neither the cat nor the mouse in the lullaby; he was the fox.
