The '''Sun sphere''' was a group of spells granted to [[druid]]s. These spells focus light and heat for comfort or for destruction.

==Spells in the Sphere of Sun==
Many spells were available to druids who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:
* ''[[light|light/darkness]]''
* ''[[sunscorch]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[continual light|continual light/darkness]]''
* ''[[starshine]]''
Intermediate divine casters had the ability to use:
* ''[[blessed warmth]]''
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[moonbeam]]''
* ''[[rainbow]]''
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:
* ''[[the great circle|the great circle/the black circle]]''<ref group="note">This spell was removed from the summoning sphere in the ''[[Player's Option: Spells & Magic]]'' sourcebook. See page 188.
* ''[[Sol's searing orb]]''
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[sunray]]''
