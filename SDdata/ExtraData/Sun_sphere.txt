The Sun sphere was a group of spells granted to druids. These spells focus light and heat for comfort or for destruction.
Many spells were available to druids who had chosen this sphere.
Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast
* light/darkness
* sunscorch
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast
* continual light/darkness
* starshine
Intermediate divine casters had the ability to use
* blessed warmth
Those with a high level of skill had access to
* moonbeam
* rainbow
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast
* the great circle/the black circleThis spell was removed from the summoning sphere in the Players Option Spells & Magic sourcebook. See page 188.
* Sols searing orb
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast
* sunray
* Moonweb
* Heliodor
