The '''Mir&ndash;Jhaamdath War''' broke out in {{Yearlink|-5032}} when [[Jhaamdath]] got into contact with the realm of [[Mir]]. Mir lost some battles and had to make an alliance with [[Coramshan]] in {{Yearlink|-5007}}. The war ended in {{Yearlink|-5005}} and both sides had to give up their intentions to get hold on the land around the Lake of Steam. Mir and Coramshan united to the present realm of [[Calimshan]].
