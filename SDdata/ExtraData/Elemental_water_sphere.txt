The '''Elemental Water sphere''' was a group of spells that manipulated the element of [[water]]. [[Druid]]s were granted unlimited access and [[clerics]] were granted minor access to these spells.

==Spells of the Elemental Water Sphere==
Junior casters could cast:
* ''[[Create or destroy water|create/destroy Water]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[watery fist]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[water breathing|water/air breathing]]''
* ''[[water walk]]''
Intermediate clerics and druids had the ability to cast:
* ''[[lower water|lower/raise water]]''
* ''[[reflecting pool]]''<ref group="note">This spell was removed from the ''[[divination sphere]]'' and added to the elemental (water) sphere in the ''[[Player's Option: Spells & Magic]]''. See pages 186-187.
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[produce ice]]''
* ''[[transmute rock to mud|transmute rock to mud/transmute mud to rock]]''<ref group="note">This spell was removed from the elemental water sphere in the ''[[Player's Option: Spells & Magic]]'' sourcebook. See page 187.
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:
* ''[[part water]]''
* ''[[transmute water to dust|transmute water to dust/improved create water]]''
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[conjure water elemental]]''
* ''[[tsunami]]''
