Lost Empires is a group of four novels each written by a different author. Publications were by Wizards of the Coast from 1998&ndash2000.  
The books are
# The Lost Library of Cormanthyr by Mel Odom (Mar 1998)
# Faces of Deception by Troy Denning (Nov 1998)
# Star of Cursrah by Clayton Emery (Feb 1999)
# The Nether Scroll by Lynn Abbey (Sep 2000)
