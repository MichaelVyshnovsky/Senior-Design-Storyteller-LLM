The '''Airlancers''' were a [[Durpari]] force of [[pegasus]] cavalry. They protected Durpari settlements in [[Raurin]].{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|5}}

==Overview==
The [[Sandvoyagers Guild]] were under the protection of the Airlancers.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|13}} Amongst other tasks of the Airlancers were looking for lost caravans{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|22}} and keeping a lookout for enemies, including the servants of [[Khalitharius]] (as of {{yearlink|1356}}), and escorting any suspicious individuals to the local authorities.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|62}} and were part of law enforcement in [[Bralizzar]].

An army of airlancers looking for whoever had destroyed the [[Oasis of Akhir]] was destroyed by Khalitharius and his forces. The equipment of the defeated was looted by slavers posing as the Sandvoyagers' Guild.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|32}} One lone survivor may have held on to life just long enough to deliver a mithril disk and an account of the fact to the adventurers who released [[Khalitharius]].

==Abilities==
All airlancers rode pegasi, with which they could communicate in complex ways.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|5}} Many, perhaps most, airlancers were drawn from the ranks of men with incredible strength.{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|31}}{{cite book/Desert of Desolation|15}}
