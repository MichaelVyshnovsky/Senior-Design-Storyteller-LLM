The '''Imaskari alphabet''' was the script used to write the [[Roushoum]] language of ancient [[Imaskar]]. This script had completely fallen out of contemporary use; it was only found inscribed on a few ancient artifacts and the walls of Imaskari ruins.{{Stub}}
