A list of words and phrases from the [[Uibilaqthraxx]] beholder language, an ancestor of the [[Quevquel]] language.

==D==
; ''daeth''
: wise, far-sighted, thoughtful, or strategic.{{Cite dragon/418/Eye on the Realms: The High Priest of Beholders|56}}

==H==
; ''haera''
: a gathering or alliance.

; ''haeromm''
: the members of a gathering or alliance.

==L==
; ''lurl'uk'lok'lahassarruin''
: "little-kin" or similar. Referred to small beholderkin such as [[gazer]]s. Led to the Quevquel word ''sarruin''.
