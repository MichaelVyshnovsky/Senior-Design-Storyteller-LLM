The '''Necromantic sphere''' was a group of spells granted to [[cleric]]s and, to a lesser degree, [[monk]]s and [[crusader]]s. These powerful spells could restore life force, limbs, or even forgotten experiences. Wielded by an evil priest, they were extremely deadly.

==Spells in the Necromantic Sphere==
Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:
* ''[[dispel fatigue]]''
* ''[[invisibility to undead]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[aid]]''
* ''[[restore strength]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[animate dead]]''
* ''[[cure blindness or deafness|cure/cause blindness or deafness]]''
* ''[[cure disease|cure/cause disease]]''
* ''[[feign death]]''
* ''[[negative plane protection]]''
* ''[[remove paralysis]]''
* ''[[speak with dead]]''
Intermediate divine casters had the ability to use:
* ''[[suspended animation]]''
* ''[[unfailing endurance]]''
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[raise dead|raise dead/slay living]]''
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[breath of life|breath of life/death]]''
* ''[[regenerate|regenerate/wither]]''
* ''[[reincarnate]]''
* ''[[restoration|restoration/energy drain]]''
* ''[[resurrection (spell)|resurrection/destruction]]''
