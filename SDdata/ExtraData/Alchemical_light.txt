An '''alchemical light'''<ref group="note" name="NN">The device is never given a formal name. However, the narration calls it an "alchemical light", and thus, that is the name chosen in this article. was an alchemical device. It contained two separate liquids in a glass vessel; when stirred vigorously, these liquids mixed together, creating a faint light. Though the light wasn't strong, it used neither magic nor fire, creating no smoke. The thief [[Gage]] from [[Laothkund]] was known to use them, as of {{yearlink|1375}}.{{cite book/Stardeep|20-21|3}}{{cite book/Stardeep|268-269|26}}
