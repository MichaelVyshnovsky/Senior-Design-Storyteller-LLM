'''Swordcaptain''' was a title that was used in multiple militaries throughout the Realms.

==Uses==
* The [[Purple Dragons]] used this title to denote their lieutenants.
* While referred to in-person as "civilars", the sergeants of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]] held the title of swordcaptain.
* During the 14{{th}} century, the [[Zhentilar]] used this title for the lieutenants within their army.
