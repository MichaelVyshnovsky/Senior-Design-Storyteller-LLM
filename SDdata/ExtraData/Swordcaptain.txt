Swordcaptain was a title that was used in multiple militaries throughout the Realms.
* The Purple Dragons used this title to denote their lieutenants.
* While referred to in-person as "civilars", the sergeants of Waterdeeps City Watch held the title of swordcaptain.
* During the 14 century, the Zhentilar used this title for the lieutenants within their army.
