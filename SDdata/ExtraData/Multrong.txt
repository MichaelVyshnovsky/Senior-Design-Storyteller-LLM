The '''Multrong''', who was also referred to as the '''Eastlord''', was the title given to the ruler of [[Mulptan]] in [[Rashemen]].

==History==
The title of Multrong was granted by Rahsemen's [[Iron Lord]]. The Multrong's decisions heavily influenced Multpan's trade and commerce.
