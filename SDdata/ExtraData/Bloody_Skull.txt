'''Bloody Skull''' is the name shared by two [[orc]] tribes:

* [[Bloody Skull (Earthfast Mountains)]], a tribe known for warring with the [[dwarves]] of [[Earthfast]] in the 12{{th}} through 14{{th}} centuries [[DR]].
* [[Bloody Skull (Thar)]], also called the "Blood Skulls," known for destroying [[Glister]] and attacking [[Hulburg]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
