'''Uraeus''' was a [[snake|serpentine]] symbol unique to [[Mulhorand]]. Within the culture of Mulhorand, it symbolized wisdom that was otherwordly.

==Notable Users==
* Within the [[Church of Osiris]], many high [[priest]]s wore [[wig]]s that were emblazoned with the uraeus.
