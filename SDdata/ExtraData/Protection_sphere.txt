The '''Protection sphere''' was a group of spells granted to [[cleric]]s, [[shaman]]s and, to a lesser degree, [[crusader]]s Beneficial auras and magical shields were the main focus of this sphere.

==Spells in the Sphere of Protection==
Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:
* ''[[endure cold or heat|endure cold/endure heat]]''
* ''[[protection from evil|protection from evil/good]]''
* ''[[ring of hands|ring of hands/ring of woe]]''
* ''[[sanctuary]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[barkskin]]''
* ''[[resist acid and corrosion]]''
* ''[[resist fire or cold|resist fire/resist cold]]''
* ''[[withdraw]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[dispel magic]]''
* ''[[line of protection|line of protection/destruction]]''
* ''[[magical vestment]]''
* ''[[negative plane protection]]''
* ''[[protection from fire]]''
* ''[[remove curse|remove/bestow curse]]''
* ''[[remove paralysis]]''
Intermediate divine casters had the ability to use:
* ''[[protection from evil, 10' radius|protection from evil/good, 10' radius]]''
* ''[[protection from lightning]]''
* ''[[repel insects]]''
* ''[[spell immunity]]''
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[antiplant shell]]''
* ''[[impregnable mind]]''
* ''[[dispel evil|dispel evil/good]]''
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:
* ''[[antianimal shell]]''
* ''[[forbiddance]]''
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[antimineral shell]]''
* ''[[impervious sanctity of mind]]''
