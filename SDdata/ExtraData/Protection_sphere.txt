The Protection sphere was a group of spells granted to clerics, shamans and, to a lesser degree, crusaders Beneficial auras and magical shields were the main focus of this sphere.
Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.
Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast
* endure cold/endure heat
* protection from evil/good
* ring of hands/ring of woe
* sanctuary
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast
* barkskin
* resist acid and corrosion
* resist fire/resist cold
* withdraw
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast
* dispel magic
* line of protection/destruction
* magical vestment
* negative plane protection
* protection from fire
* remove/bestow curse
* remove paralysis
Intermediate divine casters had the ability to use
* protection from evil/good, 10 radius
* protection from lightning
* repel insects
* spell immunity
Those with a high level of skill had access to
* antiplant shell
* impregnable mind
* dispel evil/good
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast
* antianimal shell
* forbiddance
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast
* antimineral shell
* impervious sanctity of mind
