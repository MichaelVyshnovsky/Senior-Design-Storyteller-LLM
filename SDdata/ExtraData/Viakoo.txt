'''Viakoo''' was the capital city of an [[aearee]] empire. It was located south-west of [[Phwiukree]], although where this corresponds to in modern times is unknown.

==History==
* {{Yearlink|-31000}}: While the [[primordial]]s and [[Deity|gods]] were engaged in war, the aearee founded Viakoo.
* {{Yearlink|-30700}}: The [[lammasu]] massacre a thousand of the Viakoo aearee. In reaction they begin experiments on land-bound wyrms, creating the first  of the [[wyvern]]. With this new weapon the empire expands rapidly.<ref>
