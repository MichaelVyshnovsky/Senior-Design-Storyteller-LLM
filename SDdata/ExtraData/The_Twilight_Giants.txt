'''The Twilight Giants''' series is a group of three novels written by [[Troy Denning]] and Published by [[TSR, Inc.]] from 1994&ndash;1995 and then reprinted in 2005 by [[Wizards of the Coast]]. The novels take place in a little known part of [[the North]] called [[Hartsvale]].

The books are:
# ''[[The Ogre's Pact]]'' (September 1994)
# ''[[The Giant Among Us]]'' (February 1995)
# ''[[The Titan of Twilight]]'' (September 1995)
