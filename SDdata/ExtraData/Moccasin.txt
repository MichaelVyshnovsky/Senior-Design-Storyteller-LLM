'''Moccasins''' were a type of leather footwear worn on both the continents of [[Faerûn]] and [[Maztica]].

==Notable Wearers==
[[File:ME-Moccasins.png|thumb|''Various moccasins.'']]
===Individuals===
* [[Baylee Arnvold]], was an archaeologist and [[ranger]], wore knee-high [[snakeskin]] moccasins made from the hide of [[jaculi]].<ref>
* [[Brenna Graycloak]] had a pair of [[antelope]]-hide moccasins, made and gifted to her by the [[Harper]] [[Galvin]] around [[1362 DR]] after she had lost her own shoes.<ref>
* [[Chuhotl]], an [[Eagle Knight]], wore moccasins.<ref>
* [[Erixitl]], a [[Maztican]] [[priestess]] of [[Qotal]], wore moccasins.<ref>
* [[Haarn Brightoak]], a [[druid]] and member of the [[Emerald Enclave]], wore moccasins.<ref>
* [[Keane]], a [[wizard]] from the [[Moonshae Isles]].<ref>
* [[Kixitiloc]], a [[Payit]] [[warrior]], wore moccasins.<ref>
===Regions & Settlements===
* Some of the [[wild elves]] that inhabited the [[Tangled Trees]] wore moccasins.<ref>

==Trivia==
[[Druth]]s, a species of [[fish|flatfish]] found in the [[Akanamere]], were described by some as resembling the detached sole of a moccasin.<ref>
