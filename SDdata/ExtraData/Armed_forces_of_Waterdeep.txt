The '''armed forces of Waterdeep''' were several organizations that kept the peace throughout [[Waterdeep|the city]]. They were divided into four branches, along with a specialty group of elite adventurers.

; [[Griffon Cavalry]]
:
:Elite airbourne division of the [[City Guard (Waterdeep)|City Guard]], led by [[Moedt Belabranta|Lord Moedt]].
; [[City Guard (Waterdeep)|City Guard]]
:
:Waterdeep's army, patrolling the city and the surrounding countryside.
; [[City Navy (Waterdeep)|City Navy]]
:
:The force assigned to protect the seas around the city and the busy merchant shipping lanes.
; [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]]
:
:The police force of the city, with powers of arrest over its citizens.
; [[Gray Hands]]
:
:An elite force of adventurers answering directly to the [[Lords of Waterdeep]], for those who need a more powerful option.
