'''Architecture''' was the field of the design and planning of buildings and other large structures.

==History==
In any decent-sized city, a sage specialized in architecture could be found with a 5% chance, or 15% in [[Waterdeep]] and [[Sembia]], around [[1358 DR]]. The term "architect" itself was largely unknown in Waterdeep in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref>
