The '''Creation sphere''' was a group of spells granted to [[cleric]]s that could be used to create things for the benefit of those under their care.

==Spells in the Sphere of Creation==
Junior casters could use:
* ''[[light|light/darkness]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[create holy symbol]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[continual light|continual light/darkness]]''
* ''[[create food and water]]''
Intermediate clerics had the ability to cast:
* ''[[addition]]''<ref group="note" name="removed">This spell was removed from the creation sphere in the ''[[Player's Option: Spells & Magic]]'' sourcebook. See page 186.
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[blessed abundance]]''
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:

* ''[[heroes' feast]]''
* ''[[the great circle|the great circle/the black circle]]''
* ''[[wall of thorns]]''<ref group="note" name="removed"/>

* ''[[changestaff]]''<ref group="note" name="removed"/>
* ''[[chariot of Sustarre]]''<ref group="note" name="removed"/>
