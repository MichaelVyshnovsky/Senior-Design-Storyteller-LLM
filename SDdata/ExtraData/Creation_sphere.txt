The Creation sphere was a group of spells granted to clerics that could be used to create things for the benefit of those under their care.
Junior casters could use
* light/darkness
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast
* create holy symbol
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast
* continual light/darkness
* create food and water
Intermediate clerics had the ability to cast
* additionThis spell was removed from the creation sphere in the Players Option Spells & Magic sourcebook. See page 186.
Those with a high level of skill had access to
* blessed abundance
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast
* animate object
* blade barrier
* heroes feast
* the great circle/the black circle
* wall of thorns
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast
* changestaff
* chariot of Sustarre
