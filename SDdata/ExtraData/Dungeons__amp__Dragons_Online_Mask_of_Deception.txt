'''''Mask of Deception''''' is a stand-alone quest in ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Online]]'' where the player characters are sent by the [[Harpers]] to infiltrate a [[Cult of the Dragon]] safehouse in the [[Stonelands]] that holds the white [[dragon mask]] in their possession. The quest was added on December 11, 2014 as part of Update 24.
