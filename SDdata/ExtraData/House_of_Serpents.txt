'''House of Serpents''' is a trilogy by [[Lisa Smedman]] that tells the story of [[Arvin]], a [[Psionicist|psionically]] gifted ropemaker from [[Hlondeth]], who gets caught up in the intrigues of the ruling [[yuan-ti]].

# ''[[Viper's Kiss]]'' (March 2005)
# ''[[Vanity's Brood]]'' (March 2006)
