The '''King of Stars''' was a ballad. Sometime before {{yearlink|1375}}, the construct [[Cynosure (construct)|Cynosure]] pranked [[Delphe]], a [[Keeper of the Cerulean Sign]] stationed in [[Stardeep]], claiming that a [[chicken]] had invaded the [[Stardeep#Causeway|Causeway]], and that the chicken sang that particular ballad.{{cite book/Stardeep|4-5|1}}
