{{HeadingA|Welcome}}
Well met, traveler! This wiki covers the rich and popular ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'' campaign setting for ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' from [[TSR, Inc.]] and [[Wizards of the Coast]], including Realmslore from the ''[[Oriental Adventures]]'', ''[[Al-Qadim]]'', [[Maztica Campaign Set|''Maztica'']], ''[[The Horde]]'', [[Planescape Campaign Setting|''Planescape'']], and [[Spelljammer: AD&D Adventures in Space|''Spelljammer'']] settings, covering [[:category:sourcebooks|sourcebooks]], [[:category:novels|novels]], [[:Category:Video games|video games]], [[:Category:comics|comics]], and more across all editions. See the aims and scope of this wiki [[Forgotten Realms Wiki:About|'''here''']].

Follow us on '''[https://bsky.app/profile/frwiki.bsky.social Bluesky]''', '''[https://dice.camp/@FRWiki Mastodon]''', and '''[https://twitter.com/FRWiki Twitter]''' to have lore come to you.

{{HeadingA|Helping Out}}
Well met, sage! Do you see an error? Maybe you know of some missing lore? Perhaps you want to write an article about a favorite subject? Or maybe you want to really get involved. Don't be shy, click "edit" and get in there! If you're new to wikis, then check out the [[Wikia:Help:Tutorial 1|Wikia tutorial]]. Look at some of the following links to learn [[Forgotten Realms Wiki:About|about]] the Forgotten Realms Wiki, how we [[:Category:Policies|do things here]], and see some of our [[Special:Recentchanges|recent work]]. Don't worry about making a mistake, experienced editors will catch it and fix it. Just remember we accept only [[Forgotten Realms Wiki:Canon|canon and licensed lore]], not homebrew or player characters. Don't just [[Forgotten Realms Wiki:Plagiarism|copy out the sourcebook]], write in your own words. Finally, always give a source for your information, and explain what you've done in the "summary" box. Happy scribing!
