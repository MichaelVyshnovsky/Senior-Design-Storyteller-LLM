An undying patron was one possible sponsor for a warlock. If a bargain was made between a warlock and a mortal being who had conquered death in some fashion, the warlocks patron gave them access to certain powers, including a suite of spells that expanded their choices of available spells.
 1 level  false life, ray of sickness
 2 level  blindness or deafness, silence
 3 level  feign death, speak with dead
 4 level  aura of life, death ward
 5 level  contagion, legend lore
