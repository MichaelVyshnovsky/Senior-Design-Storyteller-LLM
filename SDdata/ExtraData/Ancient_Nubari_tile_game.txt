A tile-based game that was played by the [[Nubari (ancient)|Ancient Nubari]] of the [[Malatran Plateau]], usually for the purpose of teaching their children.{{Cite organized play/LJ/Monkey City|8}}

==Description==
This game came in a box, measuring {{SI|1|ft}} wide and {{SI|0.5|ft}} long.{{Cite organized play/LJ/Monkey City|9}} They were made of a hard form of [[ceramic]]{{Cite living jungle/Living Jungle Campaign: Player Information Guide|3}} These boxes had a lid that was [[hing]]ed from the inside, which was opened by simultaneously pressing down upon the top left corners and bottom right corners of their long side.

Within each box were twenty-four colored tiles. Each of these tiles were marked on both sides with symbols that came in pairs. The goal of the game was to turn the tiles over so that each pair showed.
