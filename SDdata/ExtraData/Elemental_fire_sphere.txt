The '''Elemental Fire sphere''' was a group of spells that manipulated the element of [[fire]]. Originally, [[druid]]s were granted unlimited access and [[clerics]] were granted minor access to these spells.  Optionally, [[cleric]]s were denied these spells.

==Spells in the Sphere of Elemental Fire==
Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:
* ''[[firelight]]''
* ''[[log of everburning]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[fire trap]]''
* ''[[flame blade]]''
* ''[[heat metal|heat/chill metal]]''
* ''[[produce flame]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[flame walk]]''
* ''[[protection from fire]]''
* ''[[pyrotechnics]]''
Intermediate clerics and druids had the ability to cast:
* ''[[produce fire|produce/quench fire]]''
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[animate flame]]''
* ''[[wall of fire]]''
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:
* ''[[conjure fire elemental|conjure/dismiss fire elemental]]''
* ''[[fire seeds]]''
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[chariot of Sustarre]]''
* ''[[fire storm|fire storm/fire quench]]''
