'''The Hooded One''', or '''THO''' (as she is commonly referred to online), is a member of [[Ed Greenwood]]'s gaming group and his official spokesperson on the [http://forum.candlekeep.com Candlekeep.com forums]. She has worked as an editor for several publishers, but does not give out her real name since she also works in national security.
{{Quote|To the sages and scribes of Candlekeep, and to The Hooded One for gracing the loreseekers of cyberspace with her tireless efforts and effortless charm.|A dedication to THO in the opening of ''[[The City of Splendors: A Waterdeep Novel]]''.<ref>}}
