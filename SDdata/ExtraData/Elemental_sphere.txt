The Elemental sphere was a large group of spells encompassing divine magic that manipulated the four elements&mdashair, earth, fire, and water. Originally, druids were granted major access and clerics were granted minor access to these spells. Optionally, clerics got only minor access to the Elemental Earth and Elemental Water spheres.
Junior spellcasters could use
* create/destroy water
* firelight
* log of everburning
* strength of stone
* wind column
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast
* dust devil
* fire trap
* flame blade
* heat/chill metal
* produce flame
* soften earth and stone
* watery fist
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast
* flame walk
* meld into stone
* protection from fire
* pyrotechnics
* stone shape
* water/air breathing
* water walk
* wind servant
* zone of sweet air
