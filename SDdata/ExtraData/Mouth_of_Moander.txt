The '''Mouth of Moander''' was the supreme servant of [[Moander]], the Rotting God, during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
Following Moander's demise in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}, the Mouth of Moander also served as the head of the dead [[god]]'s cult that was secretly influenced by [[Lolth]]. The Mouth of Moander was typically a beautiful [[human]] female, though exceptions could occur.

==Mouths of Moander==
* [[Mogion]] of the [[Survivors of Moander]] cult until.
* [[Dalchatha Maereegh]] of the [[Hidden Glades]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century DR.
