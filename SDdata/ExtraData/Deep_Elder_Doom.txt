The ''' Deep Elder Doom''' was the highest-ranking member within the [[church of Myrkul|church]] of [[Myrkul]] up until the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]]. They served as the head of the [[Conclave of Doom]] within the [[Skullspire]] in [[South Faerûn|southern Faerûn]].

==Deep Elders==
*[[Haaeluth Muribaert]]: This quiet but cold-hearted [[priest]] held the post beyond the destruction of Myrkul's avatar in [[1358 DR]]. He maintained his status as head of the Skullspire temple as his congregation converted to the worship of [[Cyric]] after the [[Time of Troubles]] and again to [[Kelemvor]] a [[1368 DR|decade later]].
