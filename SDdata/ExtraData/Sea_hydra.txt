'''Sea hydras''' were a rare aquatic offshoot of [[hydra]]s.

==Description==
These creatures largely resembled the common hydra of the surface, but had flippers in place of legs.

==Ecology==
These creatures were only found in the lower depths of the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]], an area that was approximately {{SIrange|1201|2400|ft|long=yes}} beneath the sea.
