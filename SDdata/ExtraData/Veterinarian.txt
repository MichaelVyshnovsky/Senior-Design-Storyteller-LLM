'''Veterinarians''' were individuals who made a profession out of treating animals for [[disease]]s, disorders, and injuries.

==Availability==
Veterinary care was often provided to [[horse]]s, [[mule]]s, and other [[mount]]s at stables, such as [[Sendever's Stables]] in [[Wheloon]] and [[Wangle's Livery]] in [[Thunderstone]].

==Organizations==
* The [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Stablemasters' and Farriers' Guild]] provided veterinary aid to its members.<ref>

==Notable Veterinarians==
* [[Adam Smith|Adam]] and [[Eve Smith]], metalworkers in [[Ravens Bluff]], had experience with caring for and diagnosing the diseases of beasts of burden. Such as [[donkey]]s, horses, mules, and [[oxen]].<ref>{{Cite polyhedron/103/The Living City: The Horse Businesses|10}}
* [[Illumor Sendever]], the operator of Sendever's Stables.
* [[Mistukqu]]
* [[Wangle Towerstruck]], a [[ranger]] who managed Wangle's Livery.
* [[Youatt]], a veterinarian who lived in the [[Daggerford/Caravan Quarter|Caravan Quarter]] of [[Daggerford]].
