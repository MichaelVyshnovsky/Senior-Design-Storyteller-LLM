The '''H series''' was a series of four ''Forgotten Realms'' 1{{st}}-edition adventures published by [[TSR, Inc.]] between 1985 and 1988. The series focused on the [[Bloodstone Pass]] and area. The series used traditional role-playing combined with the Battlesystem rules.
