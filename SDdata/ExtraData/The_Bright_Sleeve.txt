The Bright Sleeve was an award bestowed by the city of Waterdeep on individuals for acts of bravery above the call of duty.
The award was a sleeve made out of cloth of gold embroidered with the name of the recipient.  The sleeve had pins which resembled laurel leaves.  The end of the sleeve resembled flames.
