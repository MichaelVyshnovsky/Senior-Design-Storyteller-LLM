"'''Imphras'''" was the given name of several members of the [[Heltharn Dynasty]] of [[Impiltur]]. Individuals named "Imphras" include:
* [[Imphras I|Imphras the Great]], the founder of the dynasty
* [[Imphras II]], the founder of the [[Knights of Imphras II]]
* [[Imphras III]]
* [[Imphras IV]], husband of Queen [[Sambryl]]
* [[Imphras V]]
