A '''list of dragons'''. Both [[true dragon|true]] and [[lesser dragon]]s are included.

==Key==
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
;Name
The dragon's primary name, and most relevant aliases.

;Kind
The dragon's specific kind. Dragons that have been altered, such as [[dracolich]]es or [[vampiric dragon]]s, are marked as such.

;Age
The dragon's [[Dragon aging|age category]]. This field only applies to true dragons.

;Sex
The dragon's sex, represented as F for female and M for male.

;Area(s)
The dragon's most recent known habitat, in the format "Specific place, broader region" (unless only the broader region is known). In some cases, multiple areas are provided.

;Status
{|class="prettytable"
|style="background:#daf0db; font-size:90%;" width="100px" align="center"|'''Living'''
|style="background:#faf4d1; font-size:90%;" width="100px" align="center"|'''Undead'''
|style="background:#DEE4E7; font-size:90%;" width="100px" align="center"|'''Dead/destroyed'''
|style="background:#ffffff; font-size:90%;" width="100px" align="center"|'''Unknown'''
|}

;As of
The dragon's most recent appearance or mention, represented as a year or time period. For dragons that are dead or destroyed, the year of death is provided if known. If the year of death is unknown, the year in which they were reported as dead is used, with the year of last sighting provided in parentheses if known.
</div>
