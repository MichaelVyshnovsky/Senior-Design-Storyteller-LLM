A list of dragons. Both true and lesser dragons are included.
Name
The dragons primary name, and most relevant aliases.
Kind
The dragons specific kind. Dragons that have been altered, such as dracoliches or vampiric dragons, are marked as such.
Age
The dragons age category. This field only applies to true dragons.
Sex
The dragons sex, represented as F for female and M for male.
Area(s)
The dragons most recent known habitat, in the format "Specific place, broader region" (unless only the broader region is known). In some cases, multiple areas are provided.
Status
As of
The dragons most recent appearance or mention, represented as a year or time period. For dragons that are dead or destroyed, the year of death is provided if known. If the year of death is unknown, the year in which they were reported as dead is used, with the year of last sighting provided in parentheses if known.
