The '''Lion's Mouth''' was a secret ritual held in the [[Lion's Shrine]] of the fortress of [[Sarahin]].{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|27}}

==History==
[[Holy slayer]]s of the [[Everlasting]] undertook this ritual to prove their courage to their superiors. The ritual occurred in a pit housing a fully-grown [[lion|lioness]], though the exact nature and events of the ritual were a secret.
