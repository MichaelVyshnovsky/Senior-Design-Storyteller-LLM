The '''Golden Plains''' was the name for the area of land between the [[Nagaflow]] and [[Arran]] rivers.  The land was named for its tall, golden grasses.  After the [[spellplague]] struck in [[1385 DR]], the Golden Plains became an area of magical chaos known as the [[Plaguewrought Land]].
