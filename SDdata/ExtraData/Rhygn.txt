The '''Rhygn''' were a warrior culture, ruled by a king, that lived within [[Mertwig Valley|a valley]] in [[the Vast]] until a foreign power came and conquered their land.

==History==
The father of the would-be king [[Mertwig]] waged a bloody war with the Rhygn that lasted for two days and a single night, ending in the death of all the Rhygn people. Following this conflict, the Rhygn people were buried within [[Rhygn burial mounds|two great mounds]].
