The Erevis Cale trilogy is a series of books set in the Forgotten Realms. The trilogy was written by Paul S. Kemp and released from 2003 to 2005 with cover art by Terese Nielsen.
An Omnibus edition was published in June 2010, collecting the three novels, as well as the short stories "And All the Sinners, Saints" and "Soulbound."
# Twilight Falling (July 2003)
# Dawn of Night (June 2004)
# Midnights Mask (November 2005)
The trilogy is preceded by stories about Erevis Cale in the Sembia series, specifically The Halls of Stormweather and Shadows Witness.
