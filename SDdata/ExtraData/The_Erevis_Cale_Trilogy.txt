'''The Erevis Cale trilogy''' is a series of books set in the [[Forgotten Realms]]. The trilogy was written by [[Paul S. Kemp]] and released from [[List_of_novels_in_chronological_order#2003|2003]] to [[List_of_novels_in_chronological_order#2005|2005]] with cover art by [[Terese Nielsen]].

An Omnibus edition was published in June 2010, collecting the three novels, as well as the short stories "And All the Sinners, Saints" and "''[[Realms of the Dragons#Soulbound|Soulbound]]''."

=== Erevis Cale ===
# ''[[Twilight Falling]]'' (July 2003)
# ''[[Dawn of Night]]'' (June 2004)
# ''[[Midnight's Mask]]'' (November 2005)

The trilogy is preceded by stories about [[Erevis Cale]] in the [[Sembia: Gateway to the Realms|Sembia series]], specifically ''[[The Halls of Stormweather]]'' and ''[[Shadow's Witness]]''.
