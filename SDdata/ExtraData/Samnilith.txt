"'''Samnilith'''" was an official title in the Kingdom of [[Tethyr]]. The Samnilith was a racial ambassador responsible for acting as spokesperson for the [[Gnome|gnomish]] population of that nation. The Samnilith also sat on the Royal Privy Council with four other such religion or racial emissaries.

In the {{YearlinkName|1370}}, the Samnilith was lady [[Simonne Whitebrow]].
