The '''Bailiff of the Wide''' was the official of [[Baldur's Gate]] who organized, scheduled and recorded the information about every merchant and vendor who set up shop within the [[Upper City|Upper City's]] [[The Wide|open marketplace]]. They worked under the authority of the Baldurian [[Purse Master]], the officer charged with overseeing city funds.

==Notable Individuals==
* [[Jedren Hiller]]: This rather unscrupulous bailiff served during the 1470s [[DR]].
