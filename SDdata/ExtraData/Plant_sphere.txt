The '''Plant sphere''' was a group of spells granted to [[druid]]s and [[shaman]]s. [[Ranger]]s also had minor access to this sphere. These spells could be used to aid agriculture or bring aspects of plant life to bear on problems of attack, defense, and survival.

==Spells in the Sphere of Plants==
Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:
* ''[[detect snares and pits]]''
* ''[[entangle]]''
* ''[[locate animals or plants]]''
* ''[[log of everburning]]''<ref group="note">This spell was removed from the plant sphere in the ''[[Player's Option: Spells & Magic]]'' sourcebook. See page 187.
* ''[[pass without trace]]''
* ''[[shillelagh]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[barkskin]]''
* ''[[goodberry|goodberry/badberry]]''
* ''[[trip]]''
* ''[[warp wood|warp/straighten wood]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[plant growth]]''
* ''[[slow rot]]''
* ''[[snare]]''
* ''[[spike growth]]''
* ''[[tree]]''
Intermediate divine casters had the ability to use:
* ''[[hallucinatory forest|hallucinatory forest/revealed wood]]''
* ''[[hold plant]]''
* ''[[plant door]]''
* ''[[speak with plants]]''
* ''[[sticks to snakes|sticks to snakes/snakes to sticks]]''
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[antiplant shell]]''
* ''[[commune with nature]]''
* ''[[pass plant]]''
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:
* ''[[liveoak]]''
* ''[[transport via plants]]''
* ''[[turn wood]]''
* ''[[wall of thorns]]''
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[changestaff]]''
