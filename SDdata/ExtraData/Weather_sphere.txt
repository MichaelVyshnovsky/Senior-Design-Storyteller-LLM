The '''Weather sphere''' was a group of spells granted to [[druid]]s. These spells could temporarily alter the weather or local conditions for good or ill.

==Spells in the Weather Sphere==

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:
*''[[faerie fire]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[obscurement]]''<ref group="note">This spell is incorrectly listed as 1st leve in the ''[[Player's Option: Spells & Magic]]'', page 188.
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[call lightning]]''
* ''[[weather prediction]]''
Intermediate divine casters had the ability to use:
* ''[[control temperature, 10' radius]]''
* ''[[protection from lightning]]''
* ''[[weather stasis]]''
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[control winds]]''
* ''[[rainbow]]''
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:
* ''[[weather summoning]]''
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[control weather]]''
