'''Srindym''' was a poison.{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|129-133|8}}

==Use==
It could be added to [[moonwine]] and almost certainly to other drinks, and was deadly to drink.

==History==
The [[elf]] [[Laurlaethee Shaurlanglar]] attempted to murder [[Elminster Aumar]] with srindym on {{yearlink|261}}, in [[Myth Drannor]]. It didn't work.
