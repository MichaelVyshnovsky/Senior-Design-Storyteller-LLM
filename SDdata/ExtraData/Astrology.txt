'''Astrology''' was the field of observing the movements of [[:Category:Stars|stars]] and [[constellation]]s to predict events.

In any decent-sized city, a sage specialized in astrology could be found with a 30% chance, around [[1358 DR]].

==Notable Astrologers==
* [[Galileus]]: The 14{{th}} century astrologer from the [[Sword Coast]].
