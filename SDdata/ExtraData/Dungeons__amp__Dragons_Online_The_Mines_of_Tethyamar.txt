'''''The Mines of Tethyamar''''' is an adventure pack for ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Online]]'' where the player characters are lured to the ruins of [[Tethyamar]] by [[dwarf|dwarven]] promises of riches ripe for the taking, but end up delving too deep for their own good. The adventure pack was added to the game on July 12, 2017 as part of Update 36.
