'''Orc-King''' was a title used by an [[orc]] ruler, equivalent to a king.

==History==
In [[580 DR]], after fighting a civil war, [[Grimmerfang]] won the throne of [[Vastar]]. He became Orc-King Grimmerfang, succeeding the earlier [[Overking]] [[Ologh]].
