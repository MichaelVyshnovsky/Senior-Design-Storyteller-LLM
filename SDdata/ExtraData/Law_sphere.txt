The '''Law sphere''' was a group of spells granted to lawful [[crusader]]s. This sphere counterbalanced the sphere of chaos, promoting obedience, order, and the power of cooperation.

==Spells in the Sphere of Law==
Many spells were available to those who had chosen this sphere.

Beginner spellcasters of this sphere could cast:
* ''[[command]]''
* ''[[protection from chaos]]''
Novice divine spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[calm chaos]]''
* ''[[enthrall]]''
* ''[[hold person]]''
Somewhat skilled spellcasters were able to cast:
* ''[[dictate]]''
* ''[[rigid thinking]]''
* ''[[strength of one]]''
Intermediate divine casters had the ability to use:
* ''[[compulsive order]]''
* ''[[defensive harmony]]''
Those with a high level of skill had access to:
* ''[[champion's strength]]''
* ''[[impeding permission]]''
Powerful spellcasters of such a sphere were able to cast:
* ''[[legal thoughts]]''
Divine spellcasters who had mastered this sphere could cast:
* ''[[control weather]]''<ref group="note">This spell was removed from the law sphere in the ''[[Player's Option: Spells & Magic]]'' sourcebook. See page 187.
