'''Feldspar''' was a type of mineral found on [[Toril]]. It came in many varieties, some of which were [[gem]] quality and could be facet cut or tumbled smooth into cabochons and polished. Known forms of feldspar included:
__NOTOC__
* [[Lynx eye]]
* [[Microcline]]
* [[Moonstone]]
* [[Nelvine]]
* [[Sanidine]]
* [[Sunstone (gem)|Sunstone]]

==Uses==
* Feldspar was a material component for casting the ''[[moonbeam]]'' spell.
