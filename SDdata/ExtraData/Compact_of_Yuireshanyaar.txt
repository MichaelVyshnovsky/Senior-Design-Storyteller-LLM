The '''Compact of Yuireshanyaar''' was an agreement between the elves of [[Sildëyuir]] and those of the [[Yuirwood]]. Amongst other things, it forbid Yuirwood elves from establishing themselves too close to the megaliths leading to Sildëyuir.{{cite book/Stardeep|68|6}} [[Kiril Duskmourn]] slew several young elves in the early-to-mid 1360s [[DR]], under the influence of ''[[Angul]]'', for breaching the compact.{{cite book/Stardeep|71-72|6}}
