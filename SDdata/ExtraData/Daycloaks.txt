The '''Daycloaks''' were a clan that had been exiled from [[Halruaa]] by the 9{{th}} century [[DR]].{{cite book/Secrets of the Magister|55}}

==History==
Once an important clan within [[Halruaa]], the Daycloaks had since lost their fame and holdings.

==Rumors & Legends==
As far as [[Kalgrathur Daycloaks]] could determine by the year {{yearlink|889}}, his family had been exiled because they'd opened [[portal|gate]]s to other planes, to let in hordes of [[outsider]]s into a rival family's home.
