Haramaras bow was a non-magical, masterfully crafted bow, originally belonging to Haramara Alenuath but by 1368 DR in the possession of Thalanil Alenuath. It was an item of great significance to House Alenuath. It was said that Haramara slew the red dragon Skarra with a single arrow from the bow.
