The '''FRE series''' (Forgotten Realms Epic) was a series of three 2nd edition adventures by [[Ed Greenwood]], published by [[TSR, Inc.]] in 1989. They roughly follow the course of the novels in [[Richard Awlinson]]'s [[Avatar trilogy]].
