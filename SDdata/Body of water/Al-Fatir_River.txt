'''Al-Fatir River''', also called the '''Tepid River''', was a slow-flowing river found in the [[Pearl Cities]] region of [[Zakhara|west Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Geography==
Originating in [[Al-Yabki Mountains]] north of [[Gogal Pass]], the Tepid River flowed eastward from the mountain range to the city of [[Jumlat]] on the coast of the [[Golden Gulf]]. The Tepid River measured {{SI|70|mi|k|long=yes}} in length.
