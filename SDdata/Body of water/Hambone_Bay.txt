Hambone Bay was a small body of water located on the western end of Luirenstrand in Luiren.
Hambone Bay abutted the central coastline of Luiren. It was bordered on the south by the Krenalir Peninsula and the Mortick Swamp, on the west by the coastal cities of Chethel and Beluir, and to the north by a portion of Luirens central northern coastline.
