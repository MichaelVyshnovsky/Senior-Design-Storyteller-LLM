Risuak Lake was a small, fresh-water lake in Alpuk, on the Great Glacier, known for the quality of its fishing.
Risuak Lake was located northwest of the Lugalpgotak Sea and was connected to that great body of water by a stream.
The village of Vekkak was on its shores, and Gotokok was found along the stream to the south.
