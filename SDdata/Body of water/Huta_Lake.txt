'''Huta Lake''' was one of the smaller lakes forming the [[Teardrops]] in [[Narfell]].

==Geography==
Huta Lake was the southernmost lake of the Teardrops. It was also the only lake of the Teardrops not connected to the other lakes by small rivers.
