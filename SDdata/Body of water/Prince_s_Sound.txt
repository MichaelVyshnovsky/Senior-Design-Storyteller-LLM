'''Prince's Sound''' was a located on the coast of the kingdom of [[Gnarhelm]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Prince's Sound was found along the shore of southwestern Gnarhelm near the border with [[Callidyrr]] along the [[Sea of Moonshae]]. The only settlement of note found on Prince's Sound was the village of [[Olafstaad]] on its northern shore.
