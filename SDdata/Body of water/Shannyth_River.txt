The '''Shannyth River''' was the only named river found on the isle of [[Moray]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Shannyth River originated near the center of the [[Orcskull Mountains]] in southern Moray. If flowed north for {{SI|10|mi|k|long=yes}} before reaching the [[Breasal Marsh]]. The river formed the western border of the marsh for {{SI|25|mi|k|long=yes}} before joining [[Lac Dynnegall]] on its southern shore. The river exited the lake on its north shore, joined an unnamed river from the [[Trollclaw Range]], and continued to flow northeast before reaching the coast of the [[Sea of Moonshae]] near [[Caer Moray]].
