The '''Pasocada River''' was a prominent river found within the [[Pasocada Basin]] of northern [[Maztica]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Geography==
The river wound through much of the Pasocada Basin, originating in the east in an unnamed mountain and flowing west through the [[Long Canyon]] before turning south at the [[Raven Falls]] and [[Great Raven Pueblo]] to eventually empty into the [[Eastern Ocean]]. The main arm of the Pasocada River stretched for approximately {{SI|300|mi|k|long=yes}}. The [[Opallin River]] branched from the Pasocada River near [[Opallinoc]] and flowed south into the lands of the [[Dog People]], [[wolf tribe]], and [[Rockjaw clan]] of [[desert dwarf|desert dwarves]].

==Notable Locations==
Settlements found along the banks of the Pasocada River included the Great Raven Pueblo, [[Lomaraj]], [[Yapoza]], Opallinoc, [[Nozoma]], and [[Esh Alakar]].
