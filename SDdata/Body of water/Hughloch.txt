The '''Hughloch''' was a small freshwater lake located in southwest [[Gnarhelm]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Hughloch was located south of [[Hardangheight]] close to the coast of the [[Sea of Moonshae]] and on the border of [[Callidyrr]]. The lake was part of the [[Ironflow]] as it swept down from the mountains into the sea. The Hughloch was one of the smaller lakes found in Gnarhelm, measuring only {{SI|10|mi|k|long=yes}} in length.
