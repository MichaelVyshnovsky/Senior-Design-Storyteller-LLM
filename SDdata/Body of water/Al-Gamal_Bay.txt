'''Al-Gamal Bay''' was a small naturally occurring bay found in [[north Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Geography==
Al-Gamal Bay was located approximately {{SI|60|mi|k}} southwest of mighty [[Qudra]] along the coast of the [[Great Sea]].

==History==
The bay was the site for an unnamed battle in [[Zakhara]]'s history.
