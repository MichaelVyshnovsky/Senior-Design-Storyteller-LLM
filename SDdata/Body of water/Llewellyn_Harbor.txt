'''Llewellyn Harbor''' was a small natural bay located in the kingdom of [[Callidyrr]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Llewellyn Harbor was located along the southeastern coast of Alaron on the [[Strait of Alaron]].

==Notable Locations==
The town of [[Llewellyn]] was the only settlement found along Llewellyn Harbor.
