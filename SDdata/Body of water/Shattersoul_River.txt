The '''Shattersoul River''' was a river located in the freezing reaches of [[north Faerûn]].{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|56}}

==Geography==
The river originated in the frigid [[Titan Peaks]] that sat atop the [[Great Glacier]]. It flowed south off the great ice wall until it joined with the [[River Pelauvir]],{{Cite dungeon/177/Realmslore: Vaasa|81}}
