The Rashturiver was a river in Amn that fed Lake Weng.
This river began in the Cloud Peaks northeast of Lake Weng and due west of Citadel Rashturl. It had a waterfall near its source and had a twisted course until it reached the Amnurflow.
The river was named for the famous Amnian general Rashturl.
