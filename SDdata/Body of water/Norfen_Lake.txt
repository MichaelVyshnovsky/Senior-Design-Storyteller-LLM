'''Norfen Lake''' was the only freshwater body of water located on the island of [[Norland]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Norfen Lake was located in northern Norland on the northern edge of the [[Frostfenn]]. It emptied into the [[Sea of Moonshae]] via a small unnamed river. The lake covered approximately {{SI|100|sq mi|k}}.
