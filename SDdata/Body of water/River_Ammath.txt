The '''River Ammath''' was located in [[Dambrath]].

==Description==
The River Ammath flowed along the eastern edge of the [[Gnollwatch Mountains]] and north across the [[Hills of the Dead Kings]] before emptying into [[Melanith Bay]] near [[Hazuth]], making it the largest river within the kingdom of Dambrath.
