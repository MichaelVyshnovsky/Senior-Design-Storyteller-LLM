The Teardrops was the name given to a series of small lakes found within the eastern half of Narfell as well as the western borderlands of Rashemen.
The lakes of the Teardrops were strewn in a roughly linear pattern found due east of Harks Finger.
The individual lakes of the Teardrops are listed below
* Lake Mereth
* Huta Lake
* Muirenon Lake
* Lake Muskar
The Teardrops supported a bounty of fish and game, attracting hunters from the various tribes of Nar inhabiting the region.
