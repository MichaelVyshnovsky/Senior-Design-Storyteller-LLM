The '''Teardrops''' was the name given to a series of small lakes found within the eastern half of [[Narfell]] as well as the western borderlands of [[Rashemen]].

==Geography==
The lakes of the Teardrops were strewn in a roughly linear pattern found due east of [[Hark's Finger]].

The individual lakes of the Teardrops are listed below:
* [[Lake Mereth]]
* [[Huta Lake]]
* [[Muirenon Lake]]
* [[Lake Muskar]]

==Description==
The Teardrops supported a bounty of fish and game, attracting hunters from the various tribes of [[Nar (ethnicity)|Nar]] inhabiting the region.
