'''Dagger Falls''' was a waterfall on the [[River Tesh]] in [[Daggerdale]], lying just west of [[Dagger Ford]] and the nearby [[Dagger Falls|town of the same name]].
   
==Ecology==
The sheer drop of the falls was too steep for [[salmon]] returning from the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]] to ascend.
