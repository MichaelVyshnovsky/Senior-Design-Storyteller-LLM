'''Stormy Bay''' was a broad inlet that formed along the norther coast of the [[Moonsea (lake)|Moonsea lake]], in the [[Moonsea|region that shared its name]] in [[North Faerûn|northern Faerûn]].{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|56}}

==Description==
Stormy Bay earned its name from the horrendous weather that wracked its coast.{{Cite adventurers league/Shadows over the Moonsea|5}} The storms disrupted activities along the northern coast of the sea, muddying the trail of the [[Iron Route]].{{Cite adventurers league/Shadows over the Moonsea|9}}

==Geography==
It was adjacent the coast of the Moonsea between the cities of [[Zhentil Keep]] and [[Phlan]].

==Notable Locations==
Within the bay were select few notable site, including the a secluded isle that house a [[The Village|small fishing village]],{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|80}}
