Lake Sunder was a freshwater lake located in northern Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.
Lake Sunder was located in northeast Gnarhelm at the foot of the Lisbunt Mountains near the coast of the Sea of Swords. It was one of the larger lakes found in the kingdom, covering approximately .
