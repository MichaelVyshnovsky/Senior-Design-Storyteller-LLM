'''Lake Sunder''' was a freshwater lake located in northern [[Gnarhelm]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Lake Sunder was located in northeast Gnarhelm at the foot of the [[Lisbunt Mountains]] near the coast of the [[Sea of Swords]]. It was one of the larger lakes found in the kingdom, covering approximately {{SI|150|sq mi|k}}. The [[Farfair River]] entered the lake on its southwest shore and exited to the sea on the northeast shore.
