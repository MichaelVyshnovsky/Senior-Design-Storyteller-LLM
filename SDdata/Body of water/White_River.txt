The '''White River''' was small frigid river found in the [[Earthspur Mountains]].

==Geography==
The White River was fed by runoff from the [[Glacier of the White Worm]]. It flowed northwest from the glacier through the mountains before draining into the [[Moonsea (lake)|Moonsea]].
