The Stojanow river, also known to have been called the Barren River To the west of the river was the Grass Sea, on most of the east bank was the Quivering Forest.
It was used as the primary means to transport ore and minerals from the Dragonspines to the cities of Phlan and Melvaunt.
The city of Phlan covered both banks of the river as it entered the Moonsea. Along the banks of its shores were several fortified hamlets, including Kabels Hill.
A massive silver pyramid was built on a large island in the middle of the river, known as the Sorcerers Isle, sometime around . This was used as a wizards school until it was later abandoned.
The waters of Stojanow river near the city of Phlan were unnaturally murky due to pollutions.
