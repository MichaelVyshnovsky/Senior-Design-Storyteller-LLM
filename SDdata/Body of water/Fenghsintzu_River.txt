'''Fenghsintzu''' was a large river in [[T'u Lung]]. It was called by many names including the '''Great Snake''', '''Water Father''', the '''Liquid Spirit''', and '''Heart-road'''.

==Geography==
The source of this river was located west of [[Durkon]]. A {{SI|1000|miles|long=yes}} south of Durkon, the [[Rendah River]] flowed into it.
===Flora & Fauna===
Animals that were commonly found within the waters of this river included [[crocodile]]s and [[buffalo|water buffalo]]. In the far southern end of this river, one could find large [[giant sea snake|giant water serpents]] that were liable to sink barges.

==History==
The main temple of the [[Black Leopard Cult]] stood at the mouth of the Fenghsintzu until {{YearlinkConvert|-757|KY}}, when it was destroyed<ref> by the army of General [[Wo Can]] of [[Shou Lung]].

In the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], naval officers of T'u Lung frequently sent expeditions up this river to its source.
