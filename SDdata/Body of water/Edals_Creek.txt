'''Edals Creek''' was the southeastern tributary of the [[Neverwinter River]], located in the heart of the [[Neverwinter Wood]].

==Description==
Edals Creek was a small two-prong waterway that began in Neverwinter Wood and trickled northwest to merge into the Neverwinter River.
