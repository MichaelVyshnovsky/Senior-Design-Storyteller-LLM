The River Thazarim was a river located in the country of Thay. 
The River Thazarim originated in the southwest region of the Sunrise Mountains in Thay where it flowed west then southwest to empty into the Alamber Sea. The river formed a border between Thazalhar, the Priador, and Pyarados.
