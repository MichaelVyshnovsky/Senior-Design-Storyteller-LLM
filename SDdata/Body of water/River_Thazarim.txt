The '''River Thazarim''' was a river located in the country of [[Thay]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dlc}} 

==Geography==
The River Thazarim originated in the southwest region of the [[Sunrise Mountains]] in Thay where it flowed west then southwest to empty into the [[Alamber Sea]]. The river formed a border between [[Thazalhar]], the [[Priador]], and [[Pyarados (tharch)|Pyarados]].

==History==
The forces of [[Mulhorand]] were defeated by Thay at the River Thazarim in the {{YearlinkName|976}}.
