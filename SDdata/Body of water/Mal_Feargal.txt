'''Mal Feargal''' was a lake located on the island of [[Snowdown]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Mal Feargal was the northernmost and smallest of Snowdown's three freshwater lakes. It was found on the north side of the island east of [[Andover Heights]]. Mal Feargal was not fed by any rivers. The town of [[Llandrain]] was located along its southern shore.
