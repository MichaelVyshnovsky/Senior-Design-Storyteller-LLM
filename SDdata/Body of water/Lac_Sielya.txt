'''Lac Sielya''' was a small freshwater lake located in northern [[Gnarhelm]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Lac Sielya was located on Gnarhelm's eastern arm halfway between [[Leechhome]] in the northeast and [[Salmon Bay]] in the southwest. Lac Sielya was one of Gnarhelm's smaller lakes measuring only {{SI|7|mi|k|long=yes}} in length.
