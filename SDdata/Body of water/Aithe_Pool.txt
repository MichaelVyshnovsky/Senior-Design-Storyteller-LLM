The '''Aithe Pool''' was a pond found in the town of [[Aithelar]] in the kingdom of [[Callidyrr]] on [[Alaron]] in the [[Moonshae Isles]].

==Activities==
The Aithe Pool was used by Aithelar's population for bathing, drinking, and watering mounts.
