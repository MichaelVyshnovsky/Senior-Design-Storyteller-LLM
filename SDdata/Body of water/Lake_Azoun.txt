'''Lake Azoun''' was a small crescent-shaped pond found within the city of [[Suzail]].

==Location==
The lake was located toward the north central portion of the city.

==Description==
Lake Azoun separated [[Castle Obarskyr]] and the [[Royal Gardens]] on its north side from the main court on the south side. A small bridge connected the two.
