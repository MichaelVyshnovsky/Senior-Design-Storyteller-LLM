The '''River Catherine''' was a stream found in the [[Serpent Hills]] in the realm of [[Najara]].

==Geography==
The stream wound through the hills and flowed south until it gave way to the [[Serpent's Tail Stream]].

===Flora & Fauna===
[[Crocodile]]s, aquatic [[snake]]s, [[trout|horned trout]], and [[salmon|scaled salmon]] could all be found within the waters of Lake Catherine.

==Notable Locations==
* [[Great Petrified Serpent]], the central archway of the magnificent serpentine landmark crossed over the River Catherine at its widest point.
