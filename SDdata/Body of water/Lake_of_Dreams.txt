The '''Lake of Dreams''' was the only larger freshwater lake found on the [[elven]] island homeland of [[Evermeet]].

==Geography==
The lake lay in central Evermeet due west of the [[Silver Hills]]. The [[River Shaelyn]] exited the Lake of Dreams on its eastern shore.

==Notable Locations==
The [[Summer Palace]] floated above the center of the Lake of Dreams.
