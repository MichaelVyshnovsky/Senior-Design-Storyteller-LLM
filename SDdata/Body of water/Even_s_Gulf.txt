Evens Gulf was a gulf in the Easting Reach that separated Impiltur and the Great Dale.
The Gulf of Lath was the northernmost edge of the Easting Reach, which, in turn, was part of the much larger Sea of Fallen Stars. The Buzzard Beak Harbor was located immediately to the gulfs south. Evens Gulf was fed by two major rivers the Easting River what continued to separate Impiltur and the Great Dale, and the Forest Blood River on the Great Dales side.
* Ilmwatch, a large fortress-town that stood on the infamous Buzzard Beak Harbor and Gulf of Lath.
* Solin, a coastal town in the Great Dale.
* Uthmere, a walled port town of the Great Dale.
