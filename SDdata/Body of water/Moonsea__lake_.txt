The '''Moonsea''', originally known as the '''Dragon Sea''', was a deep natural lake just north of [[Cormanthor]]. The Moonsea gave its name to the whole [[Moonsea|Moonsea region]], as the name Moonsea applied to both the lake and the lands around the lake.

{{quote|''Traveling from the [[Dalelands]] to the Moonsea is a bit like walking from a temperate, lush forest into a raging snowstorm: It's unexpected, uncomfortable, and if you aren't prepared for it, it could kill you.''|[[Elminster]] of [[Shadowdale]]}}

==Rumors & Legends==
The residents of [[Elmwood]] a town on the shores of the Moonsea, told legends of a vicious [[dragon turtle]], the size of Elmwood, who dwelt in the depths of the lake.
