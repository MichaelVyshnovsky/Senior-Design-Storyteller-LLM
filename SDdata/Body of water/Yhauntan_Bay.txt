'''Yhauntan Bay''', was a natural bay found on the [[Dragon Reach]] on the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]].

==Geography==
Yhauntan Bay was located on the western side of the Dragon Reach where it branched north from the Sea of Fallen Stars. Underwater its seabed overlooked an area known to the people of [[Ser√¥s]] as the [[Haunted Plains]].
===Flora & Fauna===
The bay had a high density of [[fish]], being a frequently fished area.<ref> It was also host to a large forest of [[kelp]], comparatively larger than that found beneath the [[Ravens Bluff Harbor]].<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/And the Deep Blue Sea|10}}

==Notable Locations==
* The city of [[Yhaunn]] was founded on a rock quarry along the bay.
* [[Keryvyr]], a kingdom of [[sea elves]] within the depths of the bay that stood along the cliffs overlooking the Haunted Plains. It existed from {{YearlinkConvert|797|TS}}
* The [[Silent Monastery]], an [[Order of the Dark Moon]] [[monastery]] that was built inside of an [[earthmote]] that floated {{SI|300|ft|long=yes}} above the bay sometime following the [[Spellplague]].<ref>{{Cite organized play/LFR/Taken|42}}

==Inhabitants==
The [[Lurker in the Shallows]] was a mysterious creatures that dwelled beneath the waters of Yhauntan Bay.
