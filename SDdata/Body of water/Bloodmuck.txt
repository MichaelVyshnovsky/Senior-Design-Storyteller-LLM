==Geography==
The Bloodmuck served as the source of a short winding [[Tormel River]] that flowed from the lake towards the [[Moonsea]] to the south, where it met the sea at the city of [[Melvaunt]]. The lake was located to the northwest from the hills of the [[Fangjaws Hold]], a collection of stone and turf lodges of the [[Skullsmashers]] [[ogre]]s.
