'''Gaping Face Cascade''' was a waterfall in [[Amn]].

==Description==
This waterfall burst forth from a spring in the side of a rock face in the [[Troll Mountains (Amn)|Troll Mountains]] and was the start of the [[Fireflow|Gaping Stream]].
