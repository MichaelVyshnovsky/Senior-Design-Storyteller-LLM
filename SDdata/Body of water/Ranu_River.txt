The Ranu River was a river in the Kuong Kingdom in Malatra.Like many locations in the Kuong Kingdom, the Ranu River has not been labelled on any map of Malatra. The rivers that are depicted are likely to be other named rivers.
At the mouth of the Ranu River lay the trade town of Srelanat. From here, the Srelanat Road went overland to the Kunong River.
