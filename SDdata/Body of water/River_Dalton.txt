The '''River Dalton''' was found just south of the [[Moonsea (lake)|Moonsea]].

==Geography==
The river branched to the east from the [[River Lis]] a short distance from the Moonsea. It flowed east then south along a dense forest before emptying into the swampland of the [[Flooded Forest]].
