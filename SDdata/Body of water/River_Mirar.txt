The River Mirar, known as the Tanlur to the ancient dwarves, was a river in the North.
The river traveled down from the Spine of the World and through the Valley of Khedrun, until finally ending in Luskan
Further upstream, Mirabar was situated on the rivers north bank.
Mirabar was known to hold a  long white water khyek race on this river during their annual Midsummer festival.
