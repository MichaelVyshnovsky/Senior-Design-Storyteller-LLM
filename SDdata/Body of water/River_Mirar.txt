The '''River Mirar''', known as the '''Tanlur''' to the ancient [[dwarves]], was a river in [[the North]].

==Geography==
The river traveled down from the [[Spine of the World]] and through the [[Valley of Khedrun]], until finally ending in [[Luskan]]

Further upstream, [[Mirabar]] was situated on the river's north bank.

==History==
Mirabar was known to hold a {{SI|20|mile|long=yes}} long white water [[khyek]] race on this river during their annual [[Midsummer]] festival.

* [[Mirabar]]
* [[Luskan]]
