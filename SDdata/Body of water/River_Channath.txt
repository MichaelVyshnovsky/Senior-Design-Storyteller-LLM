The '''River Channath''' was a waterway found in the southern [[Shaar]] near the border of [[Lapaliiya]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Geography==
The river originated in the [[Channathwood]] and [[Rathgaunt Hills]]. It flowed west through the [[Shaareach]], forming part of the northern border of the [[Swagdar]]. The River Channath joined the [[River Talar]] and continued west to the [[Shining Sea]].
