The '''Lake of Snows''' was a rather placid lake situated in the eastern reaches of the [[Giant's Run Mountains]].

==Geography==
The Lake of Snows was overlooked by [[Mount Dodild]] and [[Breakback Pass]]. It was south of the city of [[Teziir]].<ref>
