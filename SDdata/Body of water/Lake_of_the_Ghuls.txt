The '''Lake of the Ghuls''' was a small body of water found in [[Zakhara]], the Land of Fate.

==Geography==
This lake was located in the [[High Desert]], specifically in the northern section of the notorious valley known as the [[Pit of the Ghuls]].

==Description==
The Lake of the Ghuls was filled with salty water that was not potable.
