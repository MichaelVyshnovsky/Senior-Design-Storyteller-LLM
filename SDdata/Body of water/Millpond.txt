The '''Millpond''' was a natural lake in the town of [[Shadowdale (town)|Shadowdale]].

==Geography==
The lake was formed at the intersection of the [[River Ashaba]] and the [[Duckrace]]. [[Mirrorman's Mill]] was located along the banks of the Millpond.
