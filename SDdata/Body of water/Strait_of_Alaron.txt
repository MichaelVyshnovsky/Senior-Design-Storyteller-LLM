The '''Strait of Alaron''' was a large channel connecting the [[Sea of Moonshae]] with the [[Sea of Swords]] in the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The strait passed between the islands of [[Snowdown]] and [[Gwynneth]] in the southern Moonshae Isles. It continued north past the entire length of Gwynneth's eastern shores before passing by the southern end of [[Alaron]] to meet the Sea of Moonshae east of [[Oman's Isle]]. The Stait of Alaron was quite large, measuring over {{SI|200|mi|k|long=yes}} in length with an average width of {{SI|25|mi|k|long=yes}}.
