The '''Berun's River''' was the largest and the easternmost tributary of the [[Neverwinter River]], located in the [[Neverwinter Wood]].

==Description==
The river's mouth was located in the foothills just north of [[Berun's Hill]], east of the Neverwinter Wood. The river flowed west until it merged with the Neverwinter River.
