Saeraethras Stream was a minor waterway in the Delimbiyr Vale that merged into the Delimbiyr River on the western border of Daggerfords farmlands.
Saeraethras Stream began in the northern parts of the Misty Forest and trickled west, parallel Delimbiyr, down the Sword Hills, through the deep crags of the Lonely Barrens, crossing the Trade Way just south of the hamlet of Gillians Hill. From there, Saeraethras Stream curved northeast, passing through the crag at the bottom of the Wild Hills and merged into the River Delimbiyr in a small delta, west of Daggerfords farmlands and east of the Lizard Marsh.
The stream gained its name in the . It was named after the adventuring bard Saeraethra, who inhabited its shores, was killed defending the land against a band of hobgoblin raiders.
