The '''Sambar Run''' was a small straight that separated the islands of [[Lantan]] proper and [[Suj]] on the [[Trackless Sea]].

==Geography==
The Sambar Run was narrow, stretching for approximately {{SI|100|mi|k|long=yes}} from west to east between the islands. Several cities and towns were located along either shore of the straight.
