The '''Lisloch''' was a freshwater lake located in eastern [[Gnarhelm]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Lisloch abutted the [[Lisbunt Mountains]] on its southern and western shores. The lake was fed by an unnamed river flowing down from the mountain and emptied into the [[Sea of Swords]] on the far eastern coast of Gnarhelm near the island of [[Amarune (island)|Amarune]]. The Lisloch was one of the larger lakes found in Gnarhelm, covering approximately {{SI|150|sq mi|k}}.
