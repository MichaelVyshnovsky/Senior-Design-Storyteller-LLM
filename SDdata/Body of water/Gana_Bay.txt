'''Gana Bay''' was nestled in the southeast corner of [[Zakhara|west Zakhara]] in the [[Pearl Cities]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Gana Bay measured approximately {{SI|30|mi|k|long=yes}} wide by {{SI|60|mi|k|long=yes}} in length, forming a roughly rectangular shape.

==Geography==
The bay offered natural protection to the important trade city of [[Gana]] from the storms of the northern reaches of the [[Crowded Sea]]. It was filled with run-off from [[Al-Sabiya River]].

* Gana
* Al-Sabiya River
