The '''Gulf of Lath''' was located on the western side of [[Lantan]] proper on the [[Trackless Sea]].

==Geography==
This narrow gulf cut into Lantan for roughly {{SI|75|mi|k|long=yes}}, running northeast into the island. The city of [[Lethtar]] was the only settlement found along its shores.
