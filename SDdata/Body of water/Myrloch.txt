The '''Myrloch''' was a large freshwater lake located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Myrloch was located at the heart of [[Myrloch Vale]] in central Gwynneth. The lake was easily the largest freshwater body of water found within the Moonshae Isles, covering approximately {{SI|1000|sq mi|k|long=yes}}. The Myrloch filled the basin between the [[Cambro Mountains]] in the east with [[the Highlands]] to the south and west.
