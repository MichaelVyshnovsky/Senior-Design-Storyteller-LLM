Nukqup Lake was a lake in the Nakvaligach region of the Great Glacier.
Nukqup Lake was a fresh water lake with an average depth of between . Due to a high content of dissolved minerals, the lakes water was black and bitter to the taste. Despite the bitternesss, the water was potable.
Nukqup Lake was located in eastern Nakvaligach near the Keryjek Ridge.
No fish lived within Nukqup Lake.
