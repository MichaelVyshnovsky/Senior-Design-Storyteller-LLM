'''Nukqup Lake''' was a lake in the [[Nakvaligach]] region of the [[Great Glacier]].

==Description==
Nukqup Lake was a fresh water lake with an average depth of between {{SIrange|5|20|ft|long=yes}}. Due to a high content of dissolved minerals, the lake's water was black and bitter to the taste. Despite the bitternesss, the water was potable.

==Geography==
Nukqup Lake was located in eastern Nakvaligach near the [[Keryjek Ridge]].

==Flora & Fauna==
No fish lived within Nukqup Lake.
