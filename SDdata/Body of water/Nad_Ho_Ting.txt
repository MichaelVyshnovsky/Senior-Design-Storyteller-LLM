'''Nad Ho Ting''' was a lake in [[Tabot]],{{Cite interactive atlas|?}} sometimes called '''Nad Ho Ting Tso'''; "tso" meant "lake" in the [[Tabotan language]].

==Geography==
Nad Ho Ting lay amongst the mountains of the [[Kun-Yen Shan]] in northwestern Tabot and was fed by three tributaries. The towns of [[Nad Ho]] and [[Fij]] were situated to the south and [[M'tsin Gompa]] stood in the northeast.

==Description==
Nad Ho Ting remained frozen for all but a few months in a year.

==Activities==
[[Gold]] mines in the mountains north of Nad Ho Ting were worked by the lamas of M'tsin Gompa around {{YearlinkConvert|2607|SY}}.
