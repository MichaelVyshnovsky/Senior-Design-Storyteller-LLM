The '''Churning Bay''' was violent gulf on the [[Vilhon Reach]] where the city of [[Hlondeth]] was founded.

==Geography==
The bay was located along the northern shore of the Vilhon Reach near its western end.

==Description==
Dangerous even to skilled sailors, the frothing waters of this narrow gulf were home to powerful tides that left many broken ships along the floor of the gulf.
