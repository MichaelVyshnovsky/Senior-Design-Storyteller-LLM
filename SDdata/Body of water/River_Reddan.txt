The '''River Reddan''' was a river in the [[Dragon Coast]] region of the [[Eastern Heartlands]].{{Cite dragon/365/Backdrop: Cormyr|45}}

==Geography==
The river ran east by northeast from the [[Lake of the Snake]] towards the [[Redwater (lake)|Redwater]].<ref group="note">While the [[Lake of the Snake]] is not labeled on the map from ''[[Dragon magazine 365]]'', its relation to the [[Giant's Run Mountains]] on the map from the ''[[The Vilhon Reach (sourcebook)|Vilhon Reach sourcebook]]'', clearly indicates its location in relation tot he River Reddan.
