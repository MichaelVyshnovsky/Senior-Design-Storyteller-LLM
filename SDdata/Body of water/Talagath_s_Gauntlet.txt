'''Talagath's Gauntlet''', which was also called the '''Straits of Suldophur''', the '''Strait of Storms''', or the '''Wide Water''',<ref name=EotSS-p141"> was a small strait that connected the [[Lake of Steam]] and the [[Shining Sea]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}<ref name=EotSS.M">

==Geography==
The strait was located in the south central section of the Lake of Steam, passing by the [[Neth Stand]] as it stretched south. Talagath's Gauntlet was approximately {{SI|60|mi|k|long=yes}} long and {{SI|15|mi|k|long=yes}} to {{SI|30|mi|k|long=yes}} wide.<ref name=EotSS.M"/>
