The '''River Rith''' was a river that flowed south from the northwest of the [[Border Kingdoms]] near the village of [[Arnglar]] to join the [[River Scelptar]] not far from the town of [[Manywaters]]. The river was bridged from Manywaters on the eastern bank, allowing a road west to [[Irl]].{{Cite web|url=http://www.wizards.com/default.asp?x=dnd/archfr/frbk|title=The Border Kingdoms Archive|author=[[Ed Greenwood]]|publisher=[[Wizards of the Coast]]|accessdate=2008-04-25}}
