'''Harloch''' was the largest lake located on the island of [[Snowdown]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Harloch sat near the center of the island between [[Andover Heights]] in the west and [[Maloren Sods]] in the east.

==Notable Locations==
The village of [[Harloch (village)|Harloch]] was located on the lake's eastern shore on [[Snowdown Ride]].
