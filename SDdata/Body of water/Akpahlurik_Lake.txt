'''Akpahlurik Lake''' was a lake in the [[Alpuk]] region of the [[Great Glacier]].

==Description==
Akpahlurik Lake was a salt water lake with an average depth of between {{SIrange|5|20|ft|long=yes}}. Along the southern shore, oil desposits oozed from the lake bed. The oil was skimmed and collected from the water.

==Geography==
Akpahlurik Lake was located in eastern Alpuk, between the [[Lugalpgotak Sea]] and the [[Keryjek Ridge]].
