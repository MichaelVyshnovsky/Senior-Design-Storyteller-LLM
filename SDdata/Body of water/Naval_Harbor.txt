The '''Naval Harbor''' was part of the waterfront of [[Waterdeep]], City of Splendors.

==Location==
The harbor was located to the west of Waterdeep's [[Great Harbor]] and south of the southern tip of the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
[[Stormhaven Island]] partially enclosed the Naval Harbor from the southwest. The rest of the harbor was barricaded with walls. A pair of chain gates on either side of the harbor allowed access to the Great Harbor in the east and the [[Sea of Swords]] in the west.

==Activities==
The fleet of Waterdeep's navy was based in the Naval Harbor. Commericial and personal vessels were not permitted in this area.

==Defenses==
A pair of stout watchtowers—[[Harborwatch Tower]] and [[Smuggler's Bane Tower]]—watched over the Naval Harbor.
