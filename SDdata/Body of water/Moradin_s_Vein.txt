The '''Moradin's Vein''', also called '''River of Gold''', was a magical river of molten [[gold]] that flowed wherever the [[Dwarf|dwarven]] [[god]] [[Moradin]] willed it. The river was actually Moradin's lifeblood{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|3384}} and could be used for [[magic|magical]] travel.<ref>{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|?}}

In the {{YearlinkName|1480}}, [[duergar]] from [[Drik Hargunen]] used [[rune magic]] to contaminate the river with the [[Stoneplague]] disease.{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|4155}} Several unscrupulous dwarves mined the contaminated river,{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|821}} which spread the Stoneplague among the dwarves of [[East Rift]] who handled the contaminated bullion.<ref>{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|?}} The plague was ended when the human hero [[Torrin Ironstar]] journeyed to Drik Hargunen and destroyed the rune.{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|4358}}
