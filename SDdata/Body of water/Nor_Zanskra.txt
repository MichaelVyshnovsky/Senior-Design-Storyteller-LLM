}}'''Nor Zanskra''' was a large lake found in the [[Hordelands]] at the northern end of the [[Katakoro Plateau]].

==Geopgraphy==
Nor Zanskra located at the eastern base of a portion the [[Katakoro Shan]] range that formed the southern border of [[Khazari]].

==Description==
Nor Zanskra was a large lake stretching for nearly {{SI|100|mi|k}} along the base of the mountains.

The lake was formed from glacial run-off from the massive [[Katakoro Glacier]].
