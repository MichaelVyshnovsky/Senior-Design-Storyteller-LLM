'''Kingsbay''' was a natural bay located between the islands of [[Snowdown]] and [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Kingsbay was located off of the coast of southeast Gwynneth along the [[Strait of Alaron]]. The bay's eastern side bordered the island of Snowdown.
