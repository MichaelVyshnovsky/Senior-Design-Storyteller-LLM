'''Utaak Stream''' was a stream in the southeastern portion of [[Alpuk]] that connected the [[Sea of Frozen Tears]] and [[Taak Lake]] with the [[Lugalpgotak Sea]].{{Cite dungeon/177/Realmslore: Vaasa|80}}

The [[Iulutiun]] villages of [[Puttak]] and [[Yinntut]] were located alongside it.
