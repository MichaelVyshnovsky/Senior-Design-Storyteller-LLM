'''Northwind Strait''' was a channel that connected the [[Trackless Sea]] with the [[Sea of Moonshae]] in the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: Moonshae Isles|11}}

==Geography==
The strait was located on the northern side of the Sea of Moonshae in [[Norheim]] between the large island of [[Giantspine]] and the smaller islands of the archipelego to the east. Northwind Strait was approximately {{SI|50|mi|k|long=yes}} long and {{SI|18|mi|k|long=yes}} wide.
