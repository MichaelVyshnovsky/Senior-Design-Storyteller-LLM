The '''Quag River''' was a small waterway found in the far eastern corner of [[Calimshan]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}<ref name=EotSS.M">

==Geography==
The river flowed through the [[Spider Swamp]]. It originated on the northern end of the swamp near the edge of the [[Forest of Mir]] and flowed south and southwest for approximately {{SI|75|mi|k|long=yes}} until it reached the coast and the [[Shining Sea]].<ref name=EotSS.M"/>
