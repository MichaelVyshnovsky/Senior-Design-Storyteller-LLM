The '''River Sarren'''<ref group="note">The map page 53 of ''[[The Shining South (1993)|The Shining South]]'' as well the one on page 11 of ''[[The Forgotten Realms Atlas]]'' uses the spelling "Ssarren". was located in [[Durpar]] in the [[Shining Lands]] as of the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Geography==
The River Ssarren originated in the [[Giant's Belt Mountains]] within [[Veldorn]], flowing briefly south into Durpar before emptying into the [[Golden Water]].

==Notable Locations==
Two Durpari cities were found at the mouth of the river, [[Turelve]] on the west shore and [[Orpher]] on the east.
