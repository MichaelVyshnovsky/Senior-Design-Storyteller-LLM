The '''Strait of the Leviathan''' was a channel of the [[Trackless Sea]] that separated the islands of [[Moray]] and [[Gwynneth]] in the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: Moonshae Isles|11}}

==Geography==
The strait joined the [[Sea of Moonshae]] with the Trackless Sea, cutting into the heart of the Moonshae Isles from the southwest. The Strait of the Leviathan was approximately {{SI|75|mi|k|long=yes}} long.
