The'''Trank River''' was a river found in the [[Moonsea North]] region of [[North Faer√ªn]].{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|56}}

==Description==
It was a calm and easily-navigable river with seldom-encountered rushing rapids.{{Cite adventurers league/Drums in the Marsh|11}} 

==Geography==
It was located north of the [[Moonsea (lake)|Moonsea]] proper, in the northernmost reaches of the [[Eastern Heartlands]]. 

===Geographical Features===
The Trank River originated in the [[Vanishing Hills]] and flowed southward for approximately {{SI|40|mi|k}}.{{Cite adventurers league/Drums in the Marsh|14}} 

As the waters continued streaming southward they flowed around the archipelago known as the [[Ringcurrent Islands]], forming a circular eddy around them.{{Cite adventurers league/Drums in the Marsh|16}} 

The [[Phlan Path]] crossed over the Trank River at the Trank River Bridge.{{Cite adventurers league/Drums in the Marsh|7}} 

==History==
In the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], the [[lizardfolk]] of the [[Twilight Marsh]] carried out a series of raids against the farms nestled along the shores of the river, abducting farmers to appease their [[black dragon]] master, [[Throstulgrael]].{{Cite adventurers league/Drums in the Marsh|9}} 

==Notable Locations==
There were three notable farmsteads along the eastern bank of the Trank River, the [[Renon Farm]], [[Alyin Farm]], and the [[Tralio Farm]].

The [[Crossing Inn]] was also situated at the east bank of the river at the point where it crossed the Phlan Path.{{Cite adventurers league/Drums in the Marsh|7}}
