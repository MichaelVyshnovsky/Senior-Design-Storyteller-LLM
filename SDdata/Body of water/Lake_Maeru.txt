'''Lake Maeru''' was a small body of water found in eastern [[Halruaa]].

==Geography==
Lake Maeru was nestled at the foot of the [[Muaraghal Mountains]]. The [[River Maeru]] flowed south then west from the lake before meeting [[Lake Halruaa]] near the center of the country.

==Notable Locations==
The small town of [[Mithtar]] was found along the shore of Lake Maeru.
