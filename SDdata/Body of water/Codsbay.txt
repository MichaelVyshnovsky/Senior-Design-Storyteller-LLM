The '''Codsbay''' was a small part of the [[Sea of Moonshae]] located on the northern coast of the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the kingdom of [[Oman]] in the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The bay was located along the [[Strait of Oman]] at the mouth of the [[Codsrun Creek]] were it emptied into the sea at the [[Codsbay Mire]].
