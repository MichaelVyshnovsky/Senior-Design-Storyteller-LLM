The Strait of Oman was a narrow channel of the Sea of Moonshae that separated the islands of Omans Isle and Gwynneth in the Moonshae Isles.
The strait measured approximately  from end to end. Several settlements were located along its length. The Codsrun Creek emptied into the strait at the Codsbay on Gwynneths north shore.
