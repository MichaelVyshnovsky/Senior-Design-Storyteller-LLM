The '''Ironflow''' was a small river located in western [[Gnarhelm]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Ironflow originated in the southern [[Hardangheight]], flowing south from the mountain range's southwestern slopes. The river widened to form the [[Hughloch]] near the coast of the [[Sea of Moonshae]]. The Ironflow was approximately {{SI|25|mi|k|long=yes}} long.
