The '''River of Gems''' was a clearwater river that flowed the length of the [[Plain of Standing Stones]] in [[Anauroch]].<ref name=FR13-p61-62>

==Geography==
The River of Gems ran from the southern edge of the [[High Ice]], an area known as the Ice Wall, south through the Plain of Standing Stones to a large sinkhole known as [[The Throat (Anauroch)|the Throat]]. The river itself was located at the bottom of a deep, [[gemstone]]-studded gorge.

===Flora & Fauna===
[[Giant bat|Giant bats]] were known to lair within caves pocketed throughout the gorge.
