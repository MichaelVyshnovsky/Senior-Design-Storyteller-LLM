The '''Vudlur''' was one of the watercourses of the [[Esmelflow]] in [[Amn]].

==Geography==
This creek began at a spring in the western [[Snakewood]]. It joined with the [[Golden Creek]] before flowing into the [[Splendarrllur]]. The [[Star Bridge]] spanned its waters.{{Axe-Star Bridge confusion}}

==History==
The creek was named by the [[Dwarf|dwarves]]; ''vudlur'' means "woods stream" in [[Dwarvish]].
