The '''Shalane River''' was the name of the short river that flowed from [[Impresk Lake]] in the barony of [[Impresk]] in [[Erlkazar]] into [[Shalane Lake]] in the barony of [[Shalanar]]. The river was suitable for travel by experienced boaters.
