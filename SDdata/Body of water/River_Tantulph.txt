The '''River Tantulph''' flowed from the east out of the [[Windrise Ports]] in [[Laerakond]] and into the [[Dragon Sea (Abeir)|Dragon Sea]].

==Geography==
The River Tantulph ran through the city of [[Imdolphyn]], separating the docks from the upper city. Its delta was known for its plenty of fishing spots.{{Cite organized play/LFR/Quench the Fire of the Raging God|52}}
