The Farfair River was the largest river in the kingdom of Gnarhelm on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.
The Farfair River was located in eastern Gnarhelm near the border of Callidyrr. The river originated where the Lisbunt and Fairheight Mountains met. It flowed west then north around the base of the Lisbunt Mountains before reaching Lake Sunders southern shore. The river continued on to the Sea of Swords, exiting from the lakes eastern shore near the coast. The Farfair River flowed for approximately  along its entire length.
