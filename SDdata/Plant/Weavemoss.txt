Weavemoss was a species of lichen, sometimes referred to as the Wizards Beard.
Weavemoss were small but distinctly long, in appearance, light purple lichen. This species of moss was known to grow in the areas ripe with the weave magic.
Weavemoss was known to grow in the underground locations in the Western Heartlands.
Weavemoss cold be used to create a vitriol for alchemical purposes. The said vitriol of weavemoss was used to brew elixirs of arcane cultivation.
In the late 15 century DR, a handful of Wizards Beard was valued at fifteen gold coins.
