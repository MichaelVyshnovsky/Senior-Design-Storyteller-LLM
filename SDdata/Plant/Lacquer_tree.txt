'''Lacquer trees''' were type of [[:Category:Trees|tree]], notable for their use in making [[lacquer]].

==Description==
The tree's resin was [[poison]]ous and had to be handled carefully.

==Usages==
[[File:Dark Lacquer.png|thumb|''Black lacquer.'']]
* Though toxic, the tree's sap could be made into a valuable lacquer for sealing and protecting wood and other materials.
* [[Aurora's Emporium]] offered an ornamental lacquer wood storage box, stained deep red or black with [[gold]] detailing, imported from [[Wa]].
