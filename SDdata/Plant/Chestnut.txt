'''Chestnuts''' were a type of deciduous tree that could be found in the Realms.<ref  name="EE.C-p10 /> Their name was also used to refer to the edible nuts that they produced.

==Habitats==
* Chestnut trees could be found in the [[midwood]] area of [[Cormanthor]], crowding the hills north of [[Mistledale]].<ref  name="EE.C-p10>
* In [[interior Faer√ªn]], chestnut trees could occasionally be found throughout the lowlands of the [[Cormyr]] countryside.<ref>{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2007)/Cormyr Countryside}}
* Chestnut trees could be found in the [[Moonshae Isles]].
* In [[Sembia]], the plains of that region were dotted by many groves of chestnuts.
* In [[the Vast]], chestnut trees stood in the forested land surrounding the [[Blackwood Manor]].
* People of [[Icewind Dale]] prepared [[chestnut stew]]s with [[lentil]]s and [[bread]].
* Sweet chestnut trees grew in [[Tethyr]].
* In the [[Kara-Tur]] region, chestnut orchards were commonplace on the island nation of [[Wa]].

==Usages==
* Finely crushed and powdered chestnuts were used in [[Flaming gullet (beverage)|flaming gullets]].{{Cite web/theedverse/2020-06-18 - Delayed blast}}
* Throughout the [[Savage Frontier]], people would use the nuts from these trees in baking [[nut cheese]].
* [[Durinbold cheese]] from the [[Western Heartlands]] was doused in [[honey]] and kept wrapped in chestnut leaves.
