'''Yahllavur-fern''' was a species of plant found abundantly growing across the [[Eastern Heartlands|Eastern]] and [[Western Heartlands]], favored for use in personal hygiene.{{cite web| url = https://web.archive.org/web/20240405041010/http://www.candlekeep.com/library/articles/sse/sse_010203-06.htm| title = So Saith Ed Jan – Mar 2006| accessdate = 5-4-2024| date = 23-1-2006}}

==Description==
Yahllavur-fern leaves were among several others used to clean off one's posterior following [[nightsoil]] egestion. Comparatively to other plants used for the same purpose, yahllavur-fern had smaller leaves but was significantly more common.
