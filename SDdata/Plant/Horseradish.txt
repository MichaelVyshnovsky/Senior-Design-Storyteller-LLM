'''Horseradish''' was a type of root vegetable found in the [[Realms]].{{cite book/Volo's Guide to the Sword Coast|152}}

==Availability==
* [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']] sold horseradish at a price of 1 [[copper piece]] per {{SI|1|ounce|long=yes}} in its "Aurora's Larder" section.
