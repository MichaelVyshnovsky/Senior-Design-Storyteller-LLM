'''Lyrwood''' was a tree whose wood was used in the creation of [[magical item]]s. It was extinct in the wild as of {{yearlink|1374}}.

==Description==
Arrows made of lyrwood were so black they resembled lines of darkness.{{cite book/Lady of Poison|128|13}} 

==Availability==
The [[Nentyarch]] in [[Yeshelmaar]] claimed the lyrwood in the arrow shafts he gifted to [[Anom]], [[Cirid]] and [[Fallon]] was harvested from the [[Shadow wood]] of the Mystic's Dreams, and could no longer be easily made as of early {{yearlink|1373}}.{{cite book/Darkvision|4|1}}
