'''Surt''' was a vegetable found in [[South Faerûn|southern]] and [[Southeast Faerûn|southeastern]] Faerûn.{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks archive|49}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 41: Delzemaeran Delicacies, Part 3}}

==Description==
The root of the surt plant was a large, bulbous, tuber with a strong flavor. It was a main ingredient in [[mlael]], a somewhat spicy brown gravy favored by the denizens of [[Delzimmer]] ''circa'' the [[1372 DR|Year of Wild Magic, 1372 DR]].
