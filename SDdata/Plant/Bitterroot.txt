'''Bitterroot''' was an edible [[herb]] with meaty thick leaves and light pink or white flowers that could be found growing in the [[Delimbiyr Vale]] and [[Cormyr]].

==Usage==
Bitterroot was used to brew beverages 
*[[Bitterroot beer]] was an alcoholic drink brewed in [[Candlekeep]] and was a somewhat popular drink in the taprooms of [[Daggerford]]. 
*[[Bitterroot tea]] was a non-alcoholic drink brewed out of dried bitterroot and steeped in hot water, often served in [[Arabel]] and [[Zhentil Keep]], and  favored by both [[Storm Silverhand]] and the [[Simbul]].
