'''Frostroses''' were a species of hardy northern [[rose]]s that inspired an adventuring company – [[The Winter Rose]].

==Description==
Petals of a frostrose remained crimson even during the coldest winters of the [[North Faerûn]]. They could easily be seen when growing amid the white of snow.
