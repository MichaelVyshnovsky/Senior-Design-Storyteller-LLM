Maple was a type of broad-leafed deciduous tree.
The red maple could be found in the midwood area of the forest of Cormanthor.
* Maples were one of the three primary trees that grew in the Kings Forest of Cormyr. Beyond there, they could occasionally be found throughout the lowlands of the Cormyr countryside.
* Maples were quite common in Cormanthor. They were particularly known to surround Lake Sember and to be found in the Flooded Forest.
