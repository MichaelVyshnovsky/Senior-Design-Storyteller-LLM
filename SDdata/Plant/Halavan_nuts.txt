Halavan nuts were a staple food nut across Faerûn.
The bush itself was low and thorny the nut looked like a large cashew, but tasted more like a pistachio.
They grew wherever conditions were hilly, rocky and windswept. This included also eastern Calimshan, south of Amn, and the edges of the Shaar. The nuts were a staple food in the Tashalar and they were eaten across the rural areas of eastern Faerûn.
They were eaten roasted or raw.
