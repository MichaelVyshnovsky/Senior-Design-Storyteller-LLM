'''Cherry trees''' were a variety of trees in the Realms that grew a fruit of the same name.

==Description==
These trees grew flowers. There were several varieties of cherries, including red and black cherries.

==Availability==
* In the [[Border Kingdoms]], cherry farms stood in the forest of [[Hawkgarth]].{{Cite polyhedron/124/Elminster's Everwinking Eye: The Border Kingdoms: Hawkgarth|6}}
* The groves near [[Essemore]] in the [[midwood]] area of the forest of [[Cormanthor]].
* In [[Kara-Tur]], cherry trees grew in [[Wa]], such as in the [[tea garden]]s of [[Rukimbaru]] and in the Garden of the [[Bloom Lady]] at the foot of [[Mount Matazan]].
===Beyond Toril===
* On the desert world of [[Athas]], where their fruit was sometimes enchanted into ''[[potion fruit]]''.<ref>{{Cite organized play/LJ/Dark Suns|18}}

==Usages==
===Fruit===
* It was quite common for inns of average quality in the Realms to serve cherry-filled [[tart]]s as the desert in a four-course meal.<ref>{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-10-06 - Four-course meals of the Realms}}
* [[Ration|Dry provision]]s sometimes included dried cherries.<ref>
* The flowers of these trees could be used as a [[material component]] by [[shugenja]] for the ''[[detect curse]]'' [[spell]].
* [[Nell's Pie Shop]] in [[Liam's Hold]] served [[cherry pie]]s, stuffed with red and black cherries.
* Imported fruit-infused ''[[balaumo]]'' was sold in caffees of [[Arrabar]] in the [[Vilhon Reach]]. Namely, [[Efusio's Cafe]] offered a wide variety of tobacco blends, including cherry-flavored.
* Alcohol-soaked cherries were used in making [[cherrybread]], popular in  [[Vilhon Reach]], [[Border Kingdoms]], and the region of the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]], with it's popularity reaching the [[Sword Coast]] in the late 15{{th}} century DR.
===Wood===
* [[Hand drum]]s made of solid cherry wood could be purchased from [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']] for 20 [[gp]] a piece.
