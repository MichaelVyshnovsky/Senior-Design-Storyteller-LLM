'''Barley''' was a type of [[grass]] cultivated for a cereal grain.

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where barley was known to be cultivated included:
* In [[east Faerûn]], barley was among the primary crops grown in the [[Greenfields (Old Empires)|Greenfields]] plains of [[Unther]], in [[Tethyr]]'s barony of [[Tanistan]], the Tethyrian county of [[Hazamarch]], and the Tethyrian county of [[Varyth]].
* In [[interior Faerûn]], barley was cultivated in the lands surrounding the [[Sea of Fallen Stars|Inner Sea]],{{Cite dragon/228/The Athalantan Campaign|34}} as well as around the town of [[Ghars]] in [[Cormyr]] in the 1360s DR, a time when it was quite the talk among its farmers.
* In [[north Faerûn]], barley was cultivated in the Dalelands.
* In [[northwest Faerûn]], barley was cultivated in the walled abbey of [[Goldenfields]].
* In [[west Faerûn]], barley was cultivated in the city-state of [[Cursrah]], in the small town of [[Nashkel]] in [[Amn]], Amn's capital [[Crimmor]], and in the [[Western Heartlands]].
* In the [[Unapproachable East]], barley was one of the major cereal grain crops of [[Thay]].
* In the [[Moonshae Isles]], barley was grown on the island of [[Snowdown]].<ref>
===Beyond Faerûn===
* In the [[Hordelands]], barley was one of main food crops grown in the countries of [[Khazari]] and [[Ra-Khati]].
===Beyond Toril===
* In [[Arborea]], the [[plane]] considered by many to be the "breadbasket" of the [[Outer Planes]], one could find many fields of barley.<ref>
* In the [[Outlands]], fields of barley could be found in [[Tir na Og]].<ref>

==History==
The year [[233 DR]] was marked by a bountiful barley harvest in the lands surrounding the Inner Sea, making good [[beer]] easier to find in the region.

==Usage==
*[[Fillian's Brew]] was created using a special mix of [[hops]], barley, and sixty-four other secret ingredients.
* Barley was using in the brewing of a number of alcoholic beverages. Such beverages included barley beer,{{Cite dragon/299/Elminster's Guide to the Realms: Moon Mountain Brewery|80}} [[slaker]], and [[Sleeping Dragon dark]], and [[Railur's Red]].{{cite web| url = https://web.archive.org/web/20211028234155/http://candlekeep.com/downloads/So_Saith_Ed_Part25_%282010-2016%29.zip| title = So Saith Ed 2010-2016| accessdate = 25-11-2021| date = 25-11-2021}}
* Whenever [[rice]] was scarce or too valuable, people in [[Kara-Tur]] were likely to substitute it in recipes with barley.<ref>

==Rumors & Legends==
According to [[Shou]] legends, barley was once a plentiful crop in the province of [[Chukei]].

==Trivia==
The [[deity]] [[Bimasara]] of Ra-Khati's [[Padhran religion]] was typically depicted with one hand holding a sheath of barley.
