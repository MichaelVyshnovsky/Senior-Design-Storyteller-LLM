'''Mikka''' was a [[lichen]] found in the [[Alpuk]] region of the [[Great Glacier]].

==Description==
Mikka was a black lichen which grew under river stones found along streams in the Alpuk.

==Usuage==
Mikka had a flavor similar to [[mint]]. It was crunchy and was used in soups and stews in [[Iulutiun]] cuisine.
