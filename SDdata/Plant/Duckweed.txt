'''Duckweed''' were fine-leafed flowering aquatic plant found in hot sweltering [[Chult]]an climate.

==Usages==
Duckweed was a part of [[gas spore]]s' diet. The bloated creatures used their rhizome growths to suck in molds, lichens, pond scum, and duckweed.

==Availability==
Duckweed could be found growing in the [[Lake Grunglung]] in Chult, along with other aquatic plants such as [[lily pad]]s, and surrounded by lazy [[catfish]].
