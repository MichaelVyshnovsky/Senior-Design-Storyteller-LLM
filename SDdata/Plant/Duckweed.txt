Duckweed were fine-leafed flowering aquatic plant found in hot sweltering Chultan climate.
Duckweed was a part of gas spores diet. The bloated creatures used their rhizome growths to suck in molds, lichens, pond scum, and duckweed.
Duckweed could be found growing in the Lake Grunglung in Chult, along with other aquatic plants such as lily pads, and surrounded by lazy catfish.
