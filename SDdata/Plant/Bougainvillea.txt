'''Bougainvillea''' was a species of fragrant{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|54}}

==Habitats==
Bougainvillea and over vines grew in abundance in the [[Valhingen Graveyard]] of [[Phlan]] in the [[Moonsea]], along with swaying [[blueleaf]]s and tangling [[witchgrass]].

Noble gardens of [[Arrabar]] in the [[Vilhon Reach]] were filled with overpowering scents of flowering plants, including bougainvilleas, [[passion vine]]s, [[orchid]]s, and [[wandering heart]]s. Notably, the flowers could be found in the gardens surrounding mansions of [[House Matrell]] and [[House Pharaboldi]].
