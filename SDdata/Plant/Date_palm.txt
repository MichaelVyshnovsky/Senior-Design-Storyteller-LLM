'''Date palms''' were a variety of [[palm tree]] in the Realms that grew a fruit of the same name.

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where dates were known to be cultivated or grow wild included:
* In [[east Faer没n]], they grew in the nation of [[Calimshan]] and in the [[Methwood]].
* In [[north Faer没n]], they grew around oases in [[Anauroch]] and were tended to by the local [[Bedine]] people. Some particular oases they were found around included [[Bhaerlith]], [[Haunga]], and the [[Lion's Eye Oasis]].
* In [[south Faer没n]]'s [[Land of the Lions]] region, people cultivated dates.
* In [[west Faerun]]'s [[Lands of Intrigue]] region, dates were cultivated in the land of [[Imnescar]].
===Beyond Faer没n===
* In the [[Hordelands]], dates were an export of the town [[Somraggah]].
* Dates were prevalent in the continent of [[Zakhara]], being considered a common daily food item.<ref>
* Some particular locations in Zakhara where dates grew included the orchards of the fortress [[Sarahin]]{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|28}} and the [[Oasis of the Golden Falcon]].
* South of Zakhara, in the [[Crowded Sea]], one could find dates being grown on the island chain [[Nada al-Hazan]].
* Wala-wala were a variety of dates found abundantly across the [[Malatran Plateau]] in [[Kara-Tur]]. [[Spice]]d dates were a valuable trade good.{{Cite living jungle/Plague|24-25}}
===Beyond the Prime Material===
* In the plane of [[Elysium]], date palms could be found in the [[divine realm]] of [[Quietude]].

==Notable Date Products==
* In the lands of Calimshan and [[Tashalar]], dates were used in a type of handfood known as date cakes.<ref>{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2007)/Handfoods of the Realms}}

==Notable Users==
===Organizations===
* The [[Shad'iar]], the nomadic tribes of the Land of the Lions, often applied dates to their simple foods. They also used dates in the brewing of a beverage they called [[hadii]].

===Regions & Settlements===
* Dates were commonly served as part of breakfast in the land of Zakhara<ref> and used as treats for [[camel]]s. In this land dates were typically carried in leather bags, as the material helped to keep them fresh, with bag fulls ranging in price from 2 to 5 [[silver piece]]s.<ref>
* Wood from date palms was [[enchanted]] and used for construction of some of the famous ''[[Halruaan skyship]]s''. The material was gathered in the [[Bay of Taertal]]. One such date palm wood vessel was the ''[[Avariel (ship)|Avariel]]'', [[Basel Indoulur]]'s ''skyship''.
* [[Honey]]ed dates were a popular food among the inhabitants of the [[shield dwarf]] kingdom [[Roldilar]].
