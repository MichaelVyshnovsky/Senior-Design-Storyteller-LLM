Lantanese healing herbs were strange herbs from Lantan that possessed strong curative properties.
Lantanese healing herbs were low-growing curious plants. Each of its leaves had a big fluid-filled bladder on top, weighting around . The liquid was a healing juice that could be easily extracted by rolling the leaf. Each bladder had curative wipers on par with the cure moderate wounds spell.
Not surprisingly, Lantanese healing herbs originated from the island of Lantan. There, the strange plant was cultivated for its medicinal properties.
