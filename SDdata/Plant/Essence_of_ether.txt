'''Essence of ether''', was a somnolent [[drug]] and an illicit substance, inhaled to induce drowsiness and sleep.

==History==
In the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], members of [[the Guild]] worked with smugglers who brought [[drug]]s, [[weapon]]s, [[firewine]], and other substances into [[Baldur's Gate]]. Bowls of {{SI|0.8|lb|k|long=yes}} essence of ether were among such illicit goods.
