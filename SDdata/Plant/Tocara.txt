'''Tocara''' was a type of tuber found growing in the [[Bashan Do Province]] of [[T'u Lung]].

Tocara was one of the ingredients in the [[paper]] used to make the [[Edu'sascar]], a set of magical cards, at [[S'tung Monastery]].
