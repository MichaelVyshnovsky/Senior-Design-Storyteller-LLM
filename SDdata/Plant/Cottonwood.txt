Cottonwood was a type of tree that could be found in parts of Faerûn.
* In north Faerûn, cottonwood grew in the forest of Cormanthor.
* In the Unapproachable East the cottonwood grew in the interior uplands of Aglarond, in Aglaronds Yuirwood, and at the edge of Yuirwood in the meadow Halendos.
Cottonwood was a common given name among pixies.
