'''Cottonwood''' was a type of tree that could be found in parts of [[Faerûn]].

==Habitats==
* In [[north Faerûn]], cottonwood grew in the forest of [[Cormanthor]].
* In the [[Unapproachable East]] the cottonwood grew in the interior uplands of [[Aglarond]], in Aglarond's [[Yuirwood]], and at the edge of Yuirwood in the meadow [[Halendos]].
