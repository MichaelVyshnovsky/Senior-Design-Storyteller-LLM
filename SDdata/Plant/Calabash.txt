'''Calabash''' was a fruit-bearing vine that grew in [[Maztica]] and was one of the main food sources for the [[Azuposi]] [[human]]s who dwelled there.

==Availability==
Calabash vines were known to grow in the [[Pasocada Basin]] of Maztica. These fruits were cultivated by the Azuposi, along with [[pigweed]], [[goosefoot]], [[squash]], [[prickly pear]], [[juniper]], [[mayz]], [[beeweed]], [[pinyon pine]], various beans, [[melon]]s, as well as other types fruit.
