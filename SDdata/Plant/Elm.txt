'''Elms''' were a type of broad-leafed deciduous tree.

==Usages==
Elmwood was strong yet flexible, making it a good material used for crafting bowshafts, [[arrow]]s, and [[bolt]]s.

==Habitats==
* In [[north Faerûn]], elms could be found in the the east [[Starwood]] area of the forest of [[Cormanthor]] in [[north Faerûn]].<ref>
* In [[northwest Faerûn]], elms could be found in the [[Ardeep Forest]]. 
* In [[west Faerûn]]'s [[Western Heartlands]], elms could be found in the [[Reaching Woods]]. and [[Cloak Wood]].
