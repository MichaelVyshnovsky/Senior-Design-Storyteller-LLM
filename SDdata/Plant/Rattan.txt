'''Rattan''' was the name given to a collection of palm tree species and a type of material created out of them, found exclusively in the nations to the south-east from [[Faerûn]] and the nations on Faerûn's southern borders.

==Availability==
*In the southern [[wizard]] nation of [[Halruaa]], rattan was used to weave practice [[sword]]s. Their weight and size let trainees grow accustomed to [[steel]] blade without suffering wounds beyond bruising. Private troops of the [[Halruaan magehound]] [[Kiva]] in the town of [[Zalasuu]] used rattan swords in their training in the {{YearlinkName|1370}}.{{Cite dragon/210/Primitive Weaponry|79}} compared to other wood and allowed some level of defense, a good material to weave shields used in Halruaa.
*[[Tasloi]] tribes living in the vicinity of the [[Lake Ise-ko]] in [[Kozakura]] used rattan to make sturdy furniture.
*[[Leather]] and rattan were used as bumpers on the rafts on the [[Whitewater]] in the [[Ravens Bluff]].
