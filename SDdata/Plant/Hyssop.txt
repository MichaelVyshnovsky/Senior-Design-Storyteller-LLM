'''Hyssop''' was a type of plant found in the Realms, that was commonly used as a seasoning

==Availability==
* ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' sold bergamot under its "Aurora's Larder" section for 5 [[copper piece]]s per {{SI|1|ounce}}.

==Usages==
* In addition to its use as a seasoning, hyssop could be used to help treat a myriad of medical conditions, including cuts and wounds, jaundice, respiratory ailments, and as a blood purifier. In the ancient city-state of [[Cursrah]], it was sometimes mixed with [[fennel]] to form a poultice.
* Hyssop was one of many herbs that could be utilized in the [[mummy|mummification]], having been used in ancient Cursrah when mummifying [[Amenstar]].
* In [[Halruaa]], hyssop was sometimes steeped alongside [[jasmine]] to make a type of [[hygiene|herbal bath]] known as a "sunrise tryst".
* Certain [[conjuration]] spells could also be aided by a tailored infusion of hyssop.<ref group="note" name="level">More specifically, this effects 5{{th}} level conjuration spells.
