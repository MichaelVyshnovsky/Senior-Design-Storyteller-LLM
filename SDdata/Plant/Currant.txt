Currants were a type of berry in the Realms.
Places in the Realms where currants were known to grow included
* Calimshan and
* Chessenta
* The Dalelands
* In the 14 century DR, Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold dried currants for a single silver piece per .
* Currants were often featured in the mixture of wild berries known as brackleberry jam.
* Dried currants were commonly used in fruitcakes.
* Currants were used alongside oranges to flavor the Calimshan ale known as Golden Sands Orange.
* Currants were one of the many types of edible berries in the Realms that could be used as material components for the spell goodberry.
