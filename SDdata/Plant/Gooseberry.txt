Gooseberries were a common type of wild berry in the Realms.
Places in the Realms where gooseberries were known to grow included
* Chessenta
* Cormanthor, including around the ruins of Myth Drannor.
* Cormyr
* Gooseberries grew throughout much of the The Dalelands.
* Tethyr, particularly in the Purple Hills region.

* It was quite common for high-end establishments in the Realms to serve fruit tarts with a gooseberry filling as the first of two deserts in a full-course meal, what was known as the "First Sweet."

* Gooseberries were often used by inhabitants of Waterdeep in cooking a local delicacy known as melverfew.
* Gooseberries were one of the fruits used to make Purple Hills Cider.
* Gooseberries were sometimes brewed into a tea in the Dalelands, the Western Heartlands, and Waterdeep.
* Gooseberries were one of the many types of edible berries in the Realms that could be used as material components for the spell goodberry.
