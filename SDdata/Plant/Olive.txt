'''Olives''' were a variety of fruit, grown from trees of the same name, found in the Realms.

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where olives were known to be cultivated or grow wild included:
* In [[east Faerûn]]'s [[Old Empires]] region, olives were cultivated in the nation [[Chessenta]].
* In east Faerûn's [[Unapproachable East]] region, olive trees could be found in the farms of [[Thay]], though they were not considered a major export.<ref>
* In [[interior Faerûn]], people in the [[Land of the Lions]] cultivated olives, such as the citizens of [[Ankhapur]].
* In interior Faerûn's [[Vilhon Reach]] region, olives were among the major produce of the lush and disputed regions of the [[Hills of Apion]], fields of the city-state of [[Lomatra]], and the [[Valley of Umbricci]] in the [[Blade Kingdoms]].
* In [[northeast Faerûn]], they grew in the [[Dragonjaw Mountains]] of [[Thesk]], around the village of [[Tegea]].<ref>
* In [[south Faerûn]], one could find olives in the [[Shaar]].
* In [[southwest Faerûn]], olives were prevalent in and major exports of the nations of [[Lapaliiya]] and [[Tashalar]]. 
* In Lapaliiya, olive groves could be found on the western slopes of the [[Dun Hills (Lapaliiya)|Dun Hills]], and in the small town of [[Zashuma]] (who specialized in growing them).
* In [[west Faerûn]]'s [[Lands of Intrigue]] region, olives were cultivated in [[Calimshan]],{{Cite dragon/277/The New Adventures of Volo: Dragonwing Stew|92}} the nation of [[Amn]]'s [[Small Teeth]],<ref><ref> around [[Lake Esmel]],<ref> and in the nation of [[Tethyr]]<ref>&mdash;such as near [[Zazesspur]].

===Beyond Faerûn===
* Olives were prevalent in the continent of [[Zakhara]], being considered a common daily food item.<ref>

===Beyond the Prime Material===
* In [[Arborea]], the [[plane]] considered by many to be the "breadbasket" of the [[Outer Planes]], one could find many olive orchards.<ref> Including in a [[divine realm]] on that plane known as [[Olympus]].<ref><ref>
* [[Pluton]], a layer of the [[Gray Waste]] that was connected to Arborea by [[Mount Olympus]], also had olive trees. Though they were all black, in a perpetual state of gradual death that was unlikely to bear fruit.<ref>

==History==
In [[1358 DR]], a group of [[goblin]]s and [[orc]]s living within the [[Dungeon Level]] of [[Undermountain]] had fourteen [[jar]]s of black unpitted olives stored with other food for animal feed.

==Notable Olive Products==
* [[Olive oil]]
* A common handfood in the Realms was wheels of sharp yellow [[cheese]] with diced olives inside.<ref>

==Notable Users==
===Businesses===
* [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']] sold olives imported from both Chessenta and the Shaar, selling them per {{SI|1|lb|k}} for three [[gold piece]]s and ten gold pieces respectively.
* The [[Battlescarred Bard]], an inn in [[Trade Way]], served black olives as part of its dish [[chopforest]].<ref>
* The [[Maid of the Moors]], a restaurant in the hamlet of [[Mornbryn's Shield]], were served as part of their garden salads.<ref>
* [[Morgan's Inn]] served olives alongside [[celery]] as part of its cold lunch offering.<ref>
===Organizations===
* The [[Order of St. Uzurr]] supported itself, in part, by cultivating and selling olives.
* The [[Shad'iar]], the nomadic tribes of the [[Land of the Lions]], often applied olives to their simple foods.
===Regions & Settlements===

* Olives were a common facet of [[Turmish]] cuisine.<ref>{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2004)/Turmish Cuisine}}
* Olives were commonly served as part of breakfast in the land of Zakhara.<ref>
