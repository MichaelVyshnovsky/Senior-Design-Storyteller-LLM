'''Sorghum''' was a [[grain]] cultivated as food during summer months. It could also be used as a sweetener.

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where sorghum was known to be eaten included:
* In [[Chult]], where the [[Tabaxi (tribe)|Tabaxi tribe]] grew Sorghum in the Agricultural Quarter of [[Mezro]].
* In [[Kara-Tur]], where sorghum was one of several grains&mdash;allong with [[barley]] and [[millet]]&mdash;seen as a poor person's alternative to [[rice]]. It was often eaten either as a thick gruel or pounded into cakes. Sorghum was cultivated in [[Ma' Yuan]], such as by the [[monk]]s of the [[Strong Claw monastery]] near [[Yen Ching]].

==History==
During the invasion of [[Shou Lung]] by the [[Tuigan]] in the {{YearlinkName|1359}}, a decisive battle in the Ma' Yuan Province came to be known as the [[Battle of the Sorghum Field]].

In the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], sorghum-derived sweeteners could be purchased for 3 [[silver piece]]s per {{SI|1|pint|l|long=yes}}.
