Skunk cabbage was a common plant, useful as a part of certain magic spells.
Skunk cabbage was among items available through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogues Wizards Laboratory section. Each cabbage head was sold for mere 3 copper pieces.
Skunk cabbages main use was as a material component for the stinking cloud spell.
*Azimer, a demented lich master of Myth Drannors School of Wizardry.
*Zaranda Star, a former adventuress, countess, and then Queen of Tethyr in the late 14 century DR.
