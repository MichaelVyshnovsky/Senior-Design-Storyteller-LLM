Carrots were a type of hardy, root vegetable found in the Realms and often used in stews and salads.
These vegetables grew to a much larger size on Toril compared to those on Earth.
Places in the Realms where carrots were known to be produced included
* Cormyr, particularly around the village of Eveningstar and the town of Ghars.
*The Oak-Fathers Boon inn in Mosstone of Tethyr served a spicy carrot and ginger soup as one of its specialities.
* The North, where carrots were cultivated in the walled abbey of Goldenfields
* North Faerûn, where farmers in the Battledale and Mistledale regions cultivated carrots In the Moonsea region, farmers in Elmwood cultivated carrots.
* South Faerûn, where they were cultivated in the Arnaden region.
* The Western Heartlands, such as in the gnome village of Anga Vled.
* The Vast, at the village and vale of Maskyrs Eye carrots and other root vegetables grew well in the vale.
* Carrots were grown throughout much of Kara-Tur.
