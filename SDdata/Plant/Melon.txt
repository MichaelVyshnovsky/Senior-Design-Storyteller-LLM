Melon was a plant bearing fleshy sweet fruit that varied in color from yellow to green, commonly grown in Velarsburg and the Dalelands.
Melons were often consumed raw for their sweetness and refreshing flavor.
Like other types of fruit, melons were used to make sweet pies, such as those served in Harfin Draether tavern of Athkatla.
