'''Blackberries''' were a common type of wild berry in the Realms, closely related to the [[dewberry]].

==Habitats==
Places in the Realms where blackberries were known to grow included:
* [[Cormyr]]
* [[Dalelands]],
* [[The Vast]]
* [[Kozakura]], such as on the island of [[Miyashima]].{{Cite dungeon/33/Mad Gyoji|66}}
* [[Unapproachable East]], throughout the nation of [[Aglarond]].

==History==
The existence of blackberries on [[Toril]] dated all the way back to the days of [[Netheril]].

==Usages==
* Homemade wines in Cormyr and the Dalelands were often made from them.{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-07-15 - Beverages of the Heartlands}} Blackberry wine was also found in the land of [[Thay]].
* The [[High Flagon]] in [[Waterdeep]] offered its patrons sweet [[tart]]s with blackberry filling.{{Cite dragon/302/Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The High Flagon|77}}
* In [[Neverwinter]], blackberry-and-[[apple]] was a commonly prepared flavor of sweet pie.<ref>{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-11-25 - Neverwinter Cuisine}}
* The [[Moonstone Mask]] festhall in Neverwinter offered its patrons blackberry pies.
* Blackberries were sometimes brewed into a [[tea]] in [[the Dalelands]], the [[Western Heartlands]], and Waterdeep.<ref>{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-12-07 - Tea in the Forgotten Realms}}
* During the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], two swine herders in the Vast known as [[Harkina]] and [[Swithin]] would sell blackberry preserves and [[pie]]s for 2 and 3 [[silver piece]]s respectively.{{Cite polyhedron/91/The Living City: The Swineherd's House|15}}
* Blackberries were one of the many types of edible berries in the Realms that could be used as [[material component]]s for the [[spell]] ''[[goodberry]]''.
* Medicinally, some used blackberries in the treatment of dysentery or to act as an astringent.
