Dathlil was a common ground flower that was used by herbalists to combat poison.
The petals of the dathlil were tiny and white.
Dathlil petals could be consumed as a tea or whole to combat poison. The efficacy varied depending on the poison and individual, ranging from no effect to complete neutralization of the poison.
