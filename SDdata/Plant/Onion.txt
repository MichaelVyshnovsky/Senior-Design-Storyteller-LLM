'''Onion''' was a root vegetable eaten raw or dried.

==Description==
On [[Toril]], onions grew to a much larger size than they did on [[Earth]].{{Cite dragon/277/The New Adventures of Volo: Dragonwing Stew|94}}

==Availability==
In most economies, {{SI|1|oz|long=yes}} of dried onions sold for 5 [[silver piece]]s. Places in the Realms where onions were known to be cultivated included the following:
* In the [[Heartlands]], onions were a staple of [[Sembian]] kitchens and pantries.
* In [[north Faerûn]], onions were cultivated by farmers in the [[Battledale]] and [[Mistledale]] regions of the [[Dalelands]].
* In [[northwest Faerûn]], onions were cultivated in the walled abbey of [[Goldenfields]].
* In [[south Faerûn]], onions were cultivated in the [[Land of the Lions]].
* In [[west Faerun]]'s [[Lands of Intrigue]], onions were a common food in [[Calimshan]] and [[Tethyr]]. Along the [[Sword Coast]], they were cultivated in the hamlet of [[Bowshot]].

Wild onions could be found growing in the [[Hordelands]]{{cite book/The Horde/Volume I|10-11}} and on the [[Island of Viledel]] in the [[Korinn Archipelago]].

==Usages of Onion==
===Culinary Usages===
* Onion was sometimes used to flavor [[crackers]].
* Whole onions were among the multitude of things [[Stuffed giant squid|stuffed into giant squids]] in the town of [[Tulmene]].
* The recipe for [[Amnian Dragoon Soup]] called for one chopped onion.
* [[Bhaerulb]], a peculiar [[gnome]] and [[halfling]] delicacy, typically used onions.{{cite web| url = https://web.archive.org/web/20211028234155/http://candlekeep.com/downloads/So_Saith_Ed_Part25_%282010-2016%29.zip| title = So Saith Ed 2010-2016| accessdate = 25-11-2021| date = 25-11-2021}}
* [[Brassla]], a traditional [[Luskan]] dish, featured onions that were finely chopped and fried.
* [[Dolmat]], a traditional [[West Zakhara]]n dish of boiled stuffed onions with [[mutton]], [[rice]], and [[almond]]s.
* [[Elf|Elven]] dishes were traditionally not made with onions, as the Fair Folk considered their flavor to be overpowering. They usually reserved onions as "wayfaring staples," something to mask the foul taste of rancid food in times of strife and struggle.
* [[Marruth]] was commonly cooked with spiced onions.
* Onions were a common ingredient, alongside [[rosemary]] and [[thyme]], used to season a full leg of [[sheep|mutton]], be it roasted, smoked, or simply stewed.
* They were of course a key ingredient in [[onion cake]].{{Cite web/theedverse/2022-09-27 - Traditional Holiday Confectionary}}
* [[Onion soup]].
* [[Squash soup]] commonly featured onions.
* [[Tarnsar]], a staple [[dwarf|dwarven]] dish, typically featured onions.{{Cite web/theedverse/2022-11-03 - Dwarven Cuisine}}
* [[Thaek bun]]s were typically stuffed with onions.
* [[Vaervren]], an elven [[wild rice]] dish, had onions sautéed in [[wild boar]] fat until soft and then cooked with other vegetables for the duration of a long elven song.{{Cite web/theedverse/2023-02-22 - Vaervren}}

===Miscellaneous Usages===
* Onions were found to be useful in treating the [[disease]] known as [[scurvy]].

==Notable Users==
===Businesses===
* The [[Battlescarred Bard]] in Amn served chopforest, a dish consisting of onions alongside a green [[salad]] with [[olive]]s.
* [[Beldabar's Rest]] in [[Yartar]] served onions in soup alongside [[lentil]]s and [[rice]].
* The [[Grinning Lion]] of [[Waterdeep]] served fried onions.
* The [[Owlroost Head Inn]] in [[Purskul]] served cooked and chopped onions in its specialty dish, [[Emerald Chicken]].
* [[Rumnthorygh's Welcoming House]] in [[Mosstone]] typically stuffed their [[stuffed chicken]] with onions.
* [[The Sailors' Own]] in Waterdeep served onions alongside various stewed meats.
* The [[Stag Triumphant]] in Mosstone typically topped their [[omelet]]s with onions.
* [[Stargath House]] served lots of cooked onions in its specialty dish, the [[Plate of Silver]].

===Regions & Settlements===
* In [[Cormyr]], [[partridge]], hocks, onions, and [[mushroom]] [[stew]]s were a common dish. Onion- and mushroom-stuffed [[cheese handwheel]]s were available in [[Suzail]] and the kitchens of [[Castle Obarskyr]].
* In the [[Blade Kingdoms]], the smell of [[ale]] and fried onions was a signature aroma of a home with a staffed kitchen and of the annual [[Blade Festival]].
* In the [[Savage Frontier]], onions were fried with liver and served in some eateries.
* Onions, grilled [[prawn]]s, [[bass]], and [[carrot]]s on skewers were a commoners' feast in Sembia and its city of [[Selgaunt]]. Selgauntan [[roasted beef]] was often served with onions.

==Trivia==
* [[Mindflayer]]s that were healthy from brain-rich diets excreted a thin film of slimy, glistening mucus that gave off a faint odor that was sometimes described as similar to onions, although this smell was imperceptible from afar.
