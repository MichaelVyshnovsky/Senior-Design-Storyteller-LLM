Kelemvors Kiss was a type of poisonous shrub that grew in the most wild forests of Shadowdale, in the Dalelands.
The thorn of Kelemvors Kiss secreted a potent and lethal poison.
The poison of Kelemvors Kiss could kill a person in short order, anywhere from a matter of hours up to two full days.
There did a exist a treatment for Kelemvors Kiss poisoning. A victim could drink a solution of boiled mugwort prepared in theriac, which had been blessed by the god Silvanus.
In or around the , the healer Nettie learned about Kelemvors Kiss and inscribed it into her copy of Flora of the Dalelands.
