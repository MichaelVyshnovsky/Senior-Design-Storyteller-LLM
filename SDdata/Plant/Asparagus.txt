'''Asparagus''', otherwise known as '''greenspears''' or '''greenneedles''', were a type of vegetable found in the Realms.{{Cite dragon/200/Elminster's Notebook: Lord Kuldak Maurancz|144,166}}

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where asparagus was known to be produced were as follows:
* West of the [[Trade Way]], the village of [[Cragmyr]] cultivated asparagus that they sold to merchants traveling to either [[Amn]] or [[Baldur's Gate]].
* In [[west Faerûn]], asparagus was cultivated in the village of [[Anga Vled]]{{Cite dragon/269/The New Adventures of Volo: Hin Nobody Knows|86}} and the city-state of [[Cursrah]].
*Greenspears were among the staples grown and consumed in virtually every household in [[Turmish]], where it was steamed or pooled and served in [[butter]]. Older and harder asparagus was tossed in stews and soups.

==Notable Asparagus Products==
* Some used juiced asparagus or the roots of the asparagus plant in the making of sedatives or the treatment of heart problems.<ref>
* [[Oxtail soup]] was served in [[Sembia]] alongside pickled asparagus in a spicy [[Turmish cream sauce]].
* Asparagus was swerved on the side with [[steak]] medallions – a dish for the nobility of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Notable Users of Asparagus==
===Businesses===
* [[Felzoun's Folly]] tavern in [[Waterdeep]] served bite sized portions of [[sausage roll]]s or [[chicken]]-and-[[cheese]] pastry with a side of fresh asparagus with melted butter.
* [[Morgan's Inn]] would often serve asparagus, mushrooms, and fried yellow peppers together in a broth of cream over a lightly smoked silverfin.
* [[The Old Troll's Foot]] in [[Kirinwood]] offered a spicy asparagus-and-artichoke bean broth. And [[The Old Oyster]] in [[Monksblade]] had a chopped roast [[almond]]s and asparagus broth on its menu.
* [[The Old Wheel]] served a dressing of groundspears juice on cold mixed greens.<ref>{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|31}}
* [[Worried Wyvern (Hill's Edge)|The Worried Wyvern]] inn served [[omelet]]s with asparagus and spiced river [[fish]].<ref>

===Races & Sentient Creatures===
* Asparagus was a staple in the diet of many elves, who were known to eat them both raw and steamed with [[herb]]s.
