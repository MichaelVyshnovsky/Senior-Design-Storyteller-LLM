'''Cedar''' was type of coniferous [[:Category:Trees|tree]], part of a family of trees known as evergreens.

==Varieties==
===Blue===
The blue cedar was found in the groves near [[Essemore]] in the [[midwood]] area of the forest of [[Cormanthor]].<ref>

==Habitats==
Cedars could be found in various places in [[Faerûn]], as well as on [[Abeir-Toril]]'s other continents.
* In [[east Faerûn]], cedars grew in the [[Methwood]] and along the edge of the [[Sword Mountains]].
* In [[north Faerûn]], cedars flourished in and around [[Cormyr]]. And lined the perimeter of the northern [[Starwood]], the home of [[Myth Drannor]].
* In [[west Faerûn]], cedars grew in the [[Forest of Tethir]] and the hilly grasslands that surrounded [[Cursrah]].
* In the [[Unapproachable East]], the rising uplands known as the [[Gulthmere Forest]] were dotted with cedars.
===Beyond Faerûn===
* On the islands that comprised the nation of [[Kozakura]], forests at higher elevations tended to consist of primarily cedars.
* Cedars were known to grow in the coniferous forests of the [[Moonshae Isles]].
* Beyond [[Toril]], cedar trees grew in the plane of [[Arborea]] and in [[Finder Wyvernspur]]'s realm - [[Fermata]].

==Usage==
* Cedarwood was used by the [[Azuposi]] of [[Maztica]] to form prayer sticks, which were burned for protection, healing, and purification.
* The [[Coopers' Guild]] of [[Waterdeep]] was known to use cedarwood to construct {{SI|20|ft|long=yes}} long [[cask]]s.
* The nation of [[Mulhorand]] was known to import cedar lumber from the Sword Mountains for construction and craft.

==Trivia==
In the cedar abundant forest of Guthmere, this tree was the namesake of the village [[Cedarsproke]].
