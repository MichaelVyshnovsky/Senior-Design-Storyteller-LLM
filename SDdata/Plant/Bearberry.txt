'''Bearberries''' were a type of edible berry in the [[Sword Coast North]] region that were named after the fact that they were most commonly eaten by [[bear]]s. They were believed to possess some curative properties.

==Varieties==
;Kinnikinnick: A variety of bearberry that grew in [[Hartsvale]].

==Usages==
* In colder climes, inns served bearberries alongside [[muskox]] soup.{{Cite dragon/418/Inns in an Instant|41}}
* Bearberries were valued by some as a tanning agent, typically being used for the tanning of bear and [[aberration]] hides into leather.
* Due to their curative properties, some used bearberries in conjunction with [[honey]] and distilled water to brew [[potion of healing|''potions of greater healing'']].
