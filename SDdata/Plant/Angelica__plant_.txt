'''Angelica''' was a plant harvested in [[Faer√ªn]] that was used for food, medicine, and certain magical purposes.

==Availability==
[[Aurora's Emporium]] sold angelica seasoning for 5 [[cp]] per {{SI|1|oz.|long=yes}}, and candied angelica for 1 [[gp]] per {{SI|1|oz.|long=yes}}.

==Usage==
* Angelica was believed to be useful for treating illnesses in the lungs, spleen, liver, eyes, and ears. A relatively simple medicinal cure for respiratory diseases was made from angelica, [[acacia]], [[knotweed]], and [[coriander]].{{Cite dragon/130/Better Living Through Alchemy|39}}
* The floral crown of an angelica plant could be used for the special [[ink]] for recording the ''[[message]]'' spell.{{Cite dragon/62/Pages from the Mages|19}}
* Certain [[divination]] spells could be aided by a preparation of angelica.
* Dried angelica flowers could serve as a mild [[insect repellent]].
