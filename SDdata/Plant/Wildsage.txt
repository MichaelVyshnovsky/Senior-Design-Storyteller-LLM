'''Wildsage''' was a type of [[herb]] grown in [[Maskyr's Eye]] in [[the Vast]].{{Cite polyhedron/57/The Everwinking Eye: At Home in Maskyr's Eye|20}}

==Availability==
Wildsage was cultivated by the farmers of Maskyr's Eye, alongside their other crops.

==Culinary==
Giving food a flavor not unlike buttered [[leek]], wildsage was added to nearly every dish in the vale,{{Cite polyhedron/57/The Everwinking Eye: At Home in Maskyr's Eye|21}}
