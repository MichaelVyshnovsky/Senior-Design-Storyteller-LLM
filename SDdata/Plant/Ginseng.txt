'''Ginseng''' was a type of [[herb]] that could be found in the Realms.

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where ginseng was known to grow or be cultivated were:
* In [[Faerûn]], [[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue'']] sold ginseng in its section of [[wizard]] supplies.

===Beyond Faerûn===
* The nation of [[Wa]].
* In [[Marsember/Xiousing|Xiousing]], a small [[Shou]] district in the city of [[Marsember]], ginseng was grown by [[Shen's Herb Garden]].{{Cite dungeon/195/Backdrop: Xiousing|6}}

==Usages==
* The process for creating a [[homunculus]] that was devised by the [[alchemist]] [[Askrim]] required a large leaf from a ginseng or alternatively the whole plant.<ref>{{Cite dragon/97/Pages from the Mages IV|35}}<ref>
* Ginseng was a [[material component]] for the ''[[shocking grasp]]'' [[spell]].{{Cite dragon/62/Pages from the Mages|20}}
* Some used ginseng in the making of [[incense]].
* Some were known to cultivate ginseng for medicinal purposes.
* Ginseng could be used to make [[tea]].
* The root was believed to possess aphrodisiacal qualities.{{Cite dragon/269/Extra Healing}}

==History==
In the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], ginseng was one of many goods transported along the [[Spice Road]].<ref>
