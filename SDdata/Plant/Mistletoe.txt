Mistletoe was a type of small plant in the Realms.
* In interior Faerûn, mistletoe was common in Chondalwood.
* In north Faerûn, mistletoe was extremely prevalent in Druids Grove of Shadowdale.
* In northeast Faerûn, mistletoe could be found growing in the land of Aglarond.
* In the Moonshae Isles region, mistletoe grew on the isle of Gwynneth.
* In the region of the Sea of Moving Ice, Frozenfar, a tough parasitic species of mistletoe, known as frozenfar mistletoe, could be found. It was known for thriving in harsh cold climate.
* Mistletoe could be found growing in the plane of Jotunheim.
* Mistletoe was believed to protect one against spirits.
* Frozenfar mistletoe in was often used in various purification rituals and was known to protect against rot.
* Mistletoe was required as a material component for casting a variety of spells in the Realms, including Briartangle, mold touch, and smoke ghost. 
* Many of the oldest versions of druidic spells used mistletoe as a material component.
* Runesticks were typically bound in mistletoe and holly.
* Mistletoe was one of the plants that asperii and feystags were known to graze on.
* The Bacchae typically wore vegetative crowns that were sometimes made of mistletoe.
* Mistletoe was considered holy to most druids. They typically felt that the proper way to harvest this plant was with a sickle made of either silver or gold and catching it in a bowl before it touched the ground. Not harvesting in this manner was believed to reduce the potency of mistletoe.
* Whenever worshipers of Silvanus performed a vigil they would anoint themselves with a powder made of crushed mistletoe leaves and acorns, mixed with water from either rain or a spring.
* A cluster of mistletoe was one of the symbols of the Earthmother.
