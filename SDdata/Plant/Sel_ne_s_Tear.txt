'''Selûne's Tear''' was a flower that grew in the [[Silverwisp Swamp]] on the [[Sword Coast]]. It could function as a [[spell]] component when burned, allowing the summoning of a [[ghost]].

==Description==
Selûne's Tears had green leaves and dark blue flowers.
