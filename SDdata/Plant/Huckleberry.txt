Huckleberry was a variety of bush in the Realms that bore a fruit of the same name. It was distantly related to blueberries, in particular the bilberry.
Huckleberries could be found growing in Cormanthor
* Some used huckleberries in the treatment of certain medical conditions, such as dropsy and typhoid.
* During the winter, pheasants in Cormanthor would gather beneath clumps of these bushes for warmth.
