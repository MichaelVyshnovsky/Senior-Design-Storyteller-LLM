The orchid was a family of flowering plant.
*Night orchid, a blue and purple plant that possessed alchemical value and dark beauty.
*Yellow ladys slipper, a rare species of orchid that once bloomed in the Vast.
Furthinghome, as of 1368 DR, was notable for its cultivation and export of orchids, amongst other flowers.
Orchids, along with other fragrant flowers, were grown in abundance in gardens of Arrabar, in the Vilhon Reach.
The deity of rot Moander created the Seed of Moander that cold take on an appearance of a beautiful blooming orchid.
