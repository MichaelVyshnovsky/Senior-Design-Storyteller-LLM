'''Spellshields''' were a type of plant native to the [[Savage Frontier]] region. Their berries by themselves were considered to be a [[herb]].

==Usage==
Digesting the berries of this plant raw imparted a slightly improved chance at resisting the effects of some [[spell]]s.
