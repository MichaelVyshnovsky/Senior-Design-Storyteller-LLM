Tekkil was a succulent plant found in swamps that possessed fat red leaves containing milky sap.
Tekkil leaves were chewed to release the milky juice inside of them. This juice acted as a painkiller but also caused lethargy and stupor. A dose of tekkil was valued at 5 gold pieces.
