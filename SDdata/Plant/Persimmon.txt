'''Persimmons''' were a type of fruit, grown from [[tree]]s of the same name, that was found in the Realms.

==Description==
Persimmon trees were noted for growing sweet-smelling blossoms.

==Availability==
* In [[northeast Faerûn]], persimmons could be found in the city of [[Telflamm]] having been imported via the [[Golden Way]].
* In [[northwest Faerun]], they were regularly eaten in the [[aevendrow]] city of [[Callidae]].
* In the [[Moonsea]] region, the [[Bounty of the Goddess (Voonlar)|Bounty of the Goddess]] temple in [[Voonlar]] grew and sold persimmons.{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks archive|9}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 7: The House of the Helm}}
* In the [[Western Heartlands]], the [[Silvershield estate]] in [[Baldur's Gate]] had a persimmon orchard.
* Beyond [[Faerûn]], persimmons could be found growing in [[Shou Lung]].
* Beyond [[Toril]], persimmons could be found on the desert world of [[Athas]], where they were sometimes [[enchanted]] into ''[[potion fruit]]''.<ref>{{Cite organized play/LJ/Dark Suns|18,24}}

==Usages of Persimmons==
* [[Marsemban tart]]s were typically topped with a sauce made up of persimmons, [[almond]] [[milk]], [[leek]]s, and [[wine]].<ref>
* The [[Swordsmith's House]] in [[Highcastle]] served [[chicken]] and mountain [[duck]] with a persimmon-and-[[orange]] sauce.<ref>
