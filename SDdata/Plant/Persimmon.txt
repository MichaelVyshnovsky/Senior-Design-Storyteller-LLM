Persimmons were a type of fruit, grown from trees of the same name, that was found in the Realms.
Persimmon trees were noted for growing sweet-smelling blossoms.
* In northeast Faerûn, persimmons could be found in the city of Telflamm having been imported via the Golden Way.
* In northwest Faerun, they were regularly eaten in the aevendrow city of Callidae.
* In the Moonsea region, the Bounty of the Goddess temple in Voonlar grew and sold persimmons.
* In the Western Heartlands, the Silvershield estate in Baldurs Gate had a persimmon orchard.
* Beyond Faerûn, persimmons could be found growing in Shou Lung.
* Beyond Toril, persimmons could be found on the desert world of Athas, where they were sometimes enchanted into potion fruit.
* Marsemban tarts were typically topped with a sauce made up of persimmons, almond milk, leeks, and wine.
* The Swordsmiths House in Highcastle served chicken and mountain duck with a persimmon-and-orange sauce.
