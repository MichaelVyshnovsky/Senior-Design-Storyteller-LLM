'''Grapefruit''' was a species of edible citrus fruit found in [[Calimshan]] and [[the Vast]].

==Availability==
Grapefruits could be found being sold in the markets of the [[Ravens Bluff/Temple District|Temple District]] of [[Ravens Bluff]].

==Usage==
[[Custard]] decorated with grapefruit wedges and dusted with [[cinnamon]] was a desert dish from the ancient Calimshan cuisine.

Ravenaar folk made appetizers of baked grapefruit, notably, in the kittens of the [[DeVillars]] estate.{{Cite organized play/LC/Day of the Raven|5}}
