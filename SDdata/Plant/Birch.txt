'''Birch''' was a type of deciduous [[:Category:Trees|tree]].

==Habitat==
Birch trees grew throughout [[Faerun]], though were considered relatively rare. 
* In [[interior Faerûn]], birches grew throughout the lowlands of the [[Cormyr]] countryside.<ref>{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2007)/Cormyr Countryside}}
* In [[north Faerûn]], birches grew throughout [[the Dalelands]].
* Birch was known to grow in the [[Earthwood]].
* In the [[Silver Marches]], birch trees were known to grow in the [[Cold Wood]].
* Birch was known to grow in the forests of the lowlands of the [[Moonshae Isles]].
* In the [[Unapproachable East]], birches were common across the nation of [[Aglarond]].
* Outside of the [[Prime Material plane]], these trees were known to grow in the forests of the [[Beastlands (plane)|Beastlands]], and in [[Tymora]]'s garden in [[Brightwater]] of [[Arvandor (layer)|Arvandor]].

==Varieties==
'''Water birch''' grew in the [[Vast Swamp]] of [[Cormyr]].

Silver birch trees decorated with silver filigree grew in a grove in [[Verenestra]]'s bower in the [[Seelie Court (divine realm)|Seelie Court]].<ref>

==Uses==
''[[Wand of flame strike|Wands of flame strike]]'' were carved out of scorched birch wood.
