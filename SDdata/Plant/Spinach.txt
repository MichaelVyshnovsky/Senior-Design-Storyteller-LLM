Spinach was a variety of a green leafy vegetable found in some areas of Faerûn and primarily used as food.
Spinach was served at the Elfsong Tavern of Baldurs Gate.
Spinach was used as a type of pie filling in Cormyr
Runsun was a variety of common trail food—a pie made with meat cooked in spinach-and-mint or spinach-and-hot-pepper simmer sauce.
In the late 14 century DR, Dradeels Vegetable Surprise, made with spinach, was a recipe developed by Dradeel, a mad elvish mage and a survivor of Baldurans final voyage to the isles of Anchorome.
In the , Emmeline Hallowleaf, mother to Shadowheart, one of the Absolute Adversaries, knew a recipe for Feast Day Cheese Bake made with various vegetables, including spinach, which she may have had shared with her daughter once the war with The Absolute was over.
