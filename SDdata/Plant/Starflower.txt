A '''starflower''' was an unusual flowering plant. Its petals grew in a stellar pattern and it had large green leaves.

A starflower was found still growing in a bowl in the Alchemy Chamber of the [[Cloak Tower]] of [[Neverwinter]] in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}.
