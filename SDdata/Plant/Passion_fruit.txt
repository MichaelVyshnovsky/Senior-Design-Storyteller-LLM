'''Passion fruit''' was a type of delicate fruit grown in [[Zakhara]].

==Availability==
The eatery called the [[Golden Oasis]] in the city of [[Tajar]] served passion fruits as a part of its feasts. The gardens of the Fortress of [[Sarahin]] in the [[Haunted Lands]] grew [[lemon]]s, [[tangerine]]s, [[orange]]s, [[pomegranate]]s, and passion fruits.
