Fenugreek was a type of plant found in the Realms that was commonly used in cooking.
* Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold fenugreek under its "Auroras Larder" section for 3 silver pieces per .
* These plants could be used medicinally as a stimulant.
* Certain spells in the evocation and necromancy schools of magic could be aided by a tailored infusion of fenugreek.More specifically, this effects 5 level evocation spells and 7 level necromancy spells.
