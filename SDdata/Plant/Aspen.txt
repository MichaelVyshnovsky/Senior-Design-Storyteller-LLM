'''Aspens''' were a type of deciduous [[:Category:Trees|tree]] in the Realms.

==Habitats==
* In [[Maztica]], aspens grew in the mountainous forests of the [[Pasocada Basin]].
* In the [[Moonshae Isles]], aspens grew in the forests of its lowlands, and the [[Myrloch Vale]].{{Cite dragon/376/Realmslore: Sarifal|65}}
* In [[interior Faerûn]], aspens grew throughout the lowlands of the [[Cormyr]] countryside.<ref>{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2007)/Cormyr Countryside}}
* In [[northwest Faerûn]], aspens grew in the hills of [[Hartsvale]].
* In the [[Hordelands]], aspens were one of the three primary varieties of tree found in the southern forest of [[Shalhoond]].
* In the [[Unapproachable East]], aspens could be found within the [[Ashenwood]].
* Beyond the [[Prime Material plane]], aspens could be found in the valleys of the [[demiplane]] known as [[Barovia]].

==Usages==
* Aspens were one of a handful of tree species whose bark and twigs were prized by [[giant beaver]]s.
* Some [[Wand of enemy detection|''wands of enemy detection'']] were carved from the wood of an aspen.

==Rumors & Legends==
Much like the Pasocada Basin that they inhabited, the [[Azuposi]] believed that aspens could be found in the forests of their afterlife.

==Notable Aspens==
* [[Red Tree]], a legendary large aspen in [[Immil Vale]] that had the [[spirit]]s of two [[Wychlaran|Witches of Rashemen]] tied to it.
