'''Kale''', also known as '''broadleaf''',{{Cite dragon/200/Elminster's Notebook: Lord Kuldak Maurancz}} was a green vegetable grown in [[Faerûn]].

==Locations==
Kale was grown in many places across Faerûn, particularly in the village of [[Cragmyr]] of the [[Sword Coast]].

This vegetable was known to be eaten in [[Sembia]] in soups, and was often paired with the versatile [[turnip]].

==History==
In the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], [[Wigluf]] used his [[magic]]al abilities to spawn a great sky-reaching crop of vegetables, including both turnips and kale.
