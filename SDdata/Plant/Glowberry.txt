A '''glowberry''' was an unusual type of plant. It grew produced berries that bore a faint glow.

==Availability==
Glowberries grew in the subterranean [[Botanical Garden]] beneath the [[Cloak Tower]] of [[Neverwinter]] in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}.
