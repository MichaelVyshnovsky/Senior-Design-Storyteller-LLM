'''Cucumbers''' was a type of cylindrical fruit that grew on a creeping vine plant of the same name in the land of [[Kara-Tur]].

==Usages==
Besides being used as food, some used cucumbers in the treatment of inflammation.<ref>

==Notable Users==
===Businesses===
* [[Feldepost's Inn]], an old establishment in the town of [[Beregost]], served cucumber buns.
===Races & Sentient Creatures===
* [[Kappa]]s, a race of creatures native to Kara-Tur, were quite fond of cucumber.
==Regions & Settlements==
* [[Zakharan]]s enjoying fried [[crimson battan]] in a mixture that included cucumber juice.
