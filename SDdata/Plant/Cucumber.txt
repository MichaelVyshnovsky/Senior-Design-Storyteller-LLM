Cucumbers was a type of cylindrical fruit that grew on a creeping vine plant of the same name in the land of Kara-Tur.
Besides being used as food, some used cucumbers in the treatment of inflammation.
* Feldeposts Inn, an old establishment in the town of Beregost, served cucumber buns.
* Kappas, a race of creatures native to Kara-Tur, were quite fond of cucumber.
* Zakharans enjoying fried crimson battan in a mixture that included cucumber juice.
