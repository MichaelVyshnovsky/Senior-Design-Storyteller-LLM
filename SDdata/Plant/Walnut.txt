'''Walnuts''' were a type of tree found in the Realms. Its name was also used to refer to the lumber milled from it and the seed of its fruit.

==Varities==
;Archendale walnut: a species of walnut tree that was endemic to the valley of [[Archendale]].
;Butternut: a species of walnut tree that could be found in [[Cormanthor]] along the [[Duathamper|River Duathamper]].<ref>

==Habitats==
* In the town of [[Essembra]], several huge and old walnut trees stood in the garden of [[Ilmeth Manor]].
* In the [[Unapproachable East]], walnut trees grew along the banks of the [[River Umber]].
* In the [[Western Heartlands]], walnut trees grew in the [[Forgotten Forest]].
===Beyond Faer√ªn===
* In the [[Hordelands]], walnut trees grew around the shores of the [[Lake of Mists]], within the [[Great Wild Wood]], and at the lowest levels of the [[Katakoro Plateau]].
* In [[Zakhara]]'s [[Crowded Sea]], walnut trees were prevalent on the higher slopes of the [[Steaming Isles]].

==Usages==
===Seeds===
* Some [[spell]]s required the shell of one or more walnuts as a [[material component]], including ''[[crushing walls]]'' and ''[[blood link]]''.
* [[Walnut oil]] could be extracted from walnuts.
* Walnuts were used as an ingredient in a variety of dishes. Including [[tonguelash]], [[Secret of the Trees]], [[fruitcake]], and [[nut cheese]].
* In [[Crimmor]], the [[Harnsar's Board]] offered its patrons [[pear]]s filled with candied walnuts.{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|30}}

===Wood===
* Some used walnut wood to construct various types of furniture. The noble family [[Talendar]] had a lucrative business of shipping furniture made from Archendale walnut to the kingdoms of [[Amn]], [[Calimshan]], and [[Tethyr]].
* In [[Skullport]], the interior of the [[Lodge of the Beastlord]] had walls lined with walnut paneling.
* Walnut was one of the many woods that members of the [[College of Rune Magics]] would use to construct ''[[runestick]]s''.

==Trivia==
* The [[spellbook]] ''[[Against the Undead]]'' had a cover made of walnut wood.
* The cautionary [[hin]] tale of [[Dennon Nosmile]] taught the halfling youth that it was unwise to try to open a walnut with naught but their teeth.
