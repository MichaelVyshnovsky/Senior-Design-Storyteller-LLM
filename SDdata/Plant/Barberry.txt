Barberry was a species of shrub in the Realms that grew a fruit of the same name.
Places in the Realms where barberries were known to grow included
* In northwest Faerûn, barberry could be found in parts of Waterdeep, such as in the gardens near Rhinzen Halnians tower.
In the mid–14 century DR, Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue sold pints of jam made from barberry for a price of five silver pieces.
