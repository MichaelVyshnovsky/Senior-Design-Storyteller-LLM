'''Barberry''' was a species of shrub in the Realms that grew a fruit of the same name.

==Habitats==
Places in the Realms where barberries were known to grow included:
* In [[northwest Faerûn]], barberry could be found in parts of [[Waterdeep]], such as in the gardens near [[Rhinzen Halnian]]'s tower.

==History==
In the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]], ''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue (in-universe)|Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' sold pints of jam made from barberry for a price of five [[silver piece]]s.
