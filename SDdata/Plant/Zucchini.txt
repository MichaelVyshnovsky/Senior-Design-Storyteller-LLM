'''Zucchinis''',<ref> otherwise known as '''marrows''', were a type of green gourd vegetable that grew in the Realms.<ref name=VGC-p81>

==Usage==
Zucchinis were believed to be especially delicious when slowly baked in open fire, smothered in [[Amn]]ian [[herb]]s.

==Availability==
Places in the Realms where marrows were known to be produced included:
* The region of [[Cormyr]], particularly in the farming [[village]] of [[Bogbrook]],<ref name=VGC-p81 />
* The [[Hordelands]], in the [[halfling]] realm of [[Delmyr]].{{Cite dragon/269/The New Adventures of Volo: Hin Nobody Knows|85}}
* The [[Western Heartlands]], particularly in the village of [[Anga Vled]].{{Cite dragon/269/The New Adventures of Volo: Hin Nobody Knows|86}}
* The town of [[Gaj]] produced marrows among other vegetables that made up the staples of the town's cuisine.{{Cite web/theedverse/2022-12-15 - Gaj}}
* Zucchini were grown and eaten by the [[Nubari (ancient)|Ancients]] of the [[Malatran Plateau]]. The vegetables from that region were long and thin spears. In the late 14{{th}} century DR, the tribes rediscovered the vegetable and found an ancient recipe for [[Zucchini Saiyama style]].{{Cite living jungle/Recipe for Disaster|6}}
