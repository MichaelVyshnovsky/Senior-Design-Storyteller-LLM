'''Cherrynut''' was a species of tall [[tree]]s found in the southern [[wizard]] nation of [[Halruaa]].

==Description== 
Cherrynut trees were found growing along the southern wall of the [[Khaerbaal]]'s detention hold. The trees were often shrouded in abundant [[hanging moss]]. The trees' branches were sturdy enough to be climbed by dexterous individuals.
