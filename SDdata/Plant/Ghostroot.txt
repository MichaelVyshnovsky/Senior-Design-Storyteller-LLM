'''Ghostroot''' was a type of plant native to the [[Savage Frontier]] region, named for the pasty white appearance of its roots. The roots by themselves were considered to be a [[herb]].

==Usage==
After digesting one of the roots of this plant raw, an individual was capable of ''[[repel undead|repelling undead]]'' as though they were a [[cleric]].
