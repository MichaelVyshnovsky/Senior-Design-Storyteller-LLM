'''Mandrakes''', also known as '''witchroots''', were a type of plant found in the Realms.{{Cite dungeon/45/All Things Nice|32-33}}

==Powers==
Mandrakes could be used as an anesthetic.{{Cite dragon+/12/The Barber of Silverymoon|4}}

==Habitats==
Beyond the [[Prime Material plane|Prime Material]], mandrakes could be found on the [[plane]] of [[Ysgard]].

==Usages==
* A complicated extract of mandrake roots was used by [[fensir]] to brew a special restorative potion that could reverse [[petrification]].
* The roots of a mandrake were one of the ingredients for [[potion of undead control|''potions of skeleton control'']].
* The roots of a mandrake were required to construct a ''[[rod of resurrection]]'', which would be wrapped over its bound [[amethyst]].{{Cite dragon/101/Charging isn't cheap|30}}
* The roots of a mandrake were needed as a [[material component]] to cast the [[spell]]s ''[[cacodemon]]'', ''[[create homunculus]]'', and ''[[ghost gauntlet]]''.
