'''Basil''' was a species of fragrant [[herb]] that received culinary usage across [[West Faerûn|west]], [[Northwest Faerûn|northwest]], and [[central Faerûn]].

==Availability==
Jarred dried basil could be found among other [[spice]]s and herbs used for cooking in the City of Splendors, [[Waterdeep]]. Dried herbs and spiced found in the ruins of the ancient city of [[Myth Drannor]] included basil.{{Cite dragon/95/Into the Forgotten Realms|52}}

''[[Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue]]'' offered basil in several directories, each packaged and priced differently. The ''Priest's Alcove'' offered 4 oz bags of basil for 7 [[gp]], a single ounce from ''Aurora's Larder'' was sold for a single [[sp]], and a pint of basil [[jelly]] costed 5 sp.

==Usages==
Some inhabitants of [[Cormyr]] prepared wild roasted [[rabbit]]s, flavored with [[shallot]]s and basil. In [[Monksblade]], the [[Firedagger Inn]] served locally-picked [[mushroom]]s, fried in [[butter]] with a sprinkle of basil, [[firefruit]] or [[lemon]] juice.

[[Neverwinter]]'s own [[Moonstone Mask]] offered a [[mussel]]-and-basil soup. During summer months, [[caprese]] salads were served in Neverwinter, made with basil, [[tomato]]es, and [[cheese]], sprinkled with [[salt]] and drizzled with [[olive oil]].

The [[Jester's Pride]] inn in the village of [[Julkoun]] added basil, chopped [[garlic]], lemon, or other fruits or berries to  reheated stews. Another [[Faerûn]]ean food that used basil was fireseared [[trout]] under a shield of basil-shot [[potato]], flanked with a ladle of sour [[apple]] and spiced [[lime]] chopped-sauce.{{Cite dragon/277/The New Adventures of Volo: Dragonwing Stew|92}}

The [[Battlescarred Bard]] inn along the [[Trade Way]] in [[Amn]] served a salad called [[Chopforest]] that was garnished by basil.

In the land of [[Calimshan]], the cooking technique called ''asarth'' involved sautéing things in butter along with various seasonings, herbs, and butter, including basil, [[onion]]s, [[leek]]s, garlic, [[chive]]s, [[ginger]], leaves of limes, and [[hotleaves]].

===Medicine===
Some believed that basil was a curative herb, beneficial for the afflictions of nervous system.

===Magic===
The ''[[find familiar]]'' [[spell]] required [[material component]] – 100 [[gold]] [[coin|pieces]] worth of [[incense]], basil, [[savory]], and [[catnip]].

==Trivia==
Basil's distinct sweet herbal aroma was imitated by the [[bloodthorn]] plant, mixed with the scent of [[mint]]. [[Saurial]]s exuded aroma of basil when they felt desire or longing.
