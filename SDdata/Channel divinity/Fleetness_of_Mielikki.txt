'''''Fleetness of Mielikki''''' was a [[divine]] [[spell]] granted to select worshipers of [[Mielikki]] via their [[channel divinity]] power. It blessed them with the burst of speed and surefootedness over any terrain of her sacred white [[hart]], for several seconds.
