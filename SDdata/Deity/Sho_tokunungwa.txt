'''Sho'tokunungwa''' was a great [[spirit]] of the folklore and myth of the [[Azuposi]] people of the [[Pasocada Basin]] of northern [[Maztica]].

==History==
Sho'tokunungwa was one of the earliest spirits of the [[Azuposi pantheon]], existing shortly after [[Sus'sistinako]] and the [[Sun (Maztica)|Sun]]. The spirit ruled over the stars in the night sky.
