The Tyranny domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with tyrannical spell and powers.
Their compulsion spells (e.g., dominate person) gained potency.
Novice spellcasters were able to use the command, enthrall, and discern lies spells. Clerics of some experience could cast fear, greater command, and geas/quest. Those that were masters of this domain had access to bigbys grasping hand, mass charm, and dominate monster.
