The '''Tyranny domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with tyrannical [[spell]] and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
Their compulsion spells (''e.g.'', ''[[dominate person]]'') gained potency.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[command]]'', ''[[enthrall]]'', and ''[[discern lies]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[fear]]'', ''[[greater command]]'', and ''[[geas/quest]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[bigby's grasping hand]]'', ''[[mass charm]]'', and ''[[dominate monster]]''.
