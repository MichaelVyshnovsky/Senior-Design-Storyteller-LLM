The '''Endurance domain''' was a deity domain that granted [[divine]] spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with endurance [[spell]]s and powers.{{cite book/Book of Exalted Deeds|86}}

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
Once per day, clerics of this domain could supernaturally improve the overall health of their body for a single minute.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[endure elements]]'', ''[[bear's endurance]]'', and ''[[refreshment]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[sustain]]'', ''[[stoneskin]]'', and ''[[mass bear's endurance]]''. Those that were masters in this domain had access to ''[[globe of invulnerability]]'', ''[[spell turning]]'', and ''[[iron body]]''.
