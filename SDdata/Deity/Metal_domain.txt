The '''Metal domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with metal-related [[spell]] powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
They gained proficiency with a chosen kind of [[hammer]] weapon.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the [[Magic weapon (spell)|''magic weapon'']], ''[[heat metal]]'', and ''[[keen edge]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[rusting grasp]]'', ''[[wall of iron]]'', and ''[[blade barrier]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[transmute metal to wood]]'', ''[[iron body]]'', and ''[[repel metal or stone]]''.
