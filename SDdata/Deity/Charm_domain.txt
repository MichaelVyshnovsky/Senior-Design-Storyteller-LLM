The '''Charm domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with charm-related [[spell]]s and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
Once a day, they could become more charismatic for a minute.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[charm person]]'', ''[[calm emotions]]'', and ''[[suggestion]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[emotion]]'', ''[[charm monster]]'', and ''[[geas/quest]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[insanity]]'', ''[[demand]]'', and ''[[dominate monster]]''.
