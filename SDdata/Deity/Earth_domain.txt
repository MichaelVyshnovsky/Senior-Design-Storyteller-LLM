The '''Earth domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[Earth (element)|earth]]-based [[spell]]s and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
The spellcaster gained the ability to turn away and destroy [[:Category:Creatures with the air subtype|air creatures]] the way a good cleric [[Turn undead|turned undead]], and to rebuke, command, and strengthen [[:Category:Creatures with the earth subtype|earth creatures]] the way an evil cleric rebuked undead.

==Spells==
As they advanced, a priest with this domain could enchant stone projectiles with ''[[magic stone]]''; use ''[[soften earth and stone]]'' to make clay, sand, or mud; sculpt with ''[[stone shape]]''; create defenses with ''[[spike stones]]'' and ''[[wall of stone]]''; protect an ally with ''[[stoneskin]]'' and themselves with ''[[iron body]]''; and trigger a small ''[[earthquake]]''. Finally, they could summon an ''[[elemental swarm]]'' of [[earth elemental]]s.
