The Pride domain was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like clerics with spells and powers themed around excessive vanity, hubris, and conceit.
The spellcaster gained the ability to turn certain failure when resisting a damaging effect  into potential success.
Novice spellcasters were able to use the hypnotism, eagles splendor, and heroism spells. Clerics of some experience could cast divine power, mass reduce person, and forbiddance. Those that were masters of this domain had access to greater heroism, greater spell immunity, and mass charm monster.
