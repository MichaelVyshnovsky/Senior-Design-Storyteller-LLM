Olurobo was a deity known in the continent of Katashaka.
He had children called the nyama-nummo, including Akongo and the Sleeper.The nyama-nummo are named after elements of the religion of the Dogon people of Mali, with the concept of nyama (roughly, human nature) and the Nummo spirits or deities.
Olurobo and the nyama-nummo were worshiped by the ancient Tabaxi tribe of Katashaka they followed no other.
