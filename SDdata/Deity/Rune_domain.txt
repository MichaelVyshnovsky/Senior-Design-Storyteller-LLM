The '''Rune domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with rune-related [[spell]]s and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
They gained the ability to write magical scrolls.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[erase]]'', ''[[secret page]]'', and ''[[glyph of warding]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[explosive runes]]'', ''[[lesser planar binding]]'', and ''[[greater glyph of warding]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[drawmij's instant summons]]'', ''[[symbol]]'', and ''[[teleportation circle]]''.
