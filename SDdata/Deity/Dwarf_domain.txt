The '''Dwarf domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[spell]]s and powers useful to [[dwarves]].

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
For a spellcaster with this domain, their body's fortitude increased.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the [[Magic weapon (spell)|''magic weapon'']], ''[[endurance]]'', and ''[[glyph of warding]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[greater magic weapon]]'', ''[[fabricate]]'', and ''[[stone tell]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[dictum]]'', ''[[protection from spells]]'', and ''earth [[elemental swarm]]''.
