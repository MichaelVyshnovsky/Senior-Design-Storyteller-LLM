The '''Fey domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[spell]]s and powers associated with [[fey]].

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
The [[cleric]] gained resistance to the [[spell]]-like powers of [[fey]] creatures.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[faerie fire]]'', ''[[charm person]]'', and ''[[inspired aim]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[blinding beauty]]'', ''[[tree stride]]'', and ''[[heroes' feast]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[liveoak]]'', ''[[unearthly beauty]]'', and ''[[summon nature's ally IX]]''.
