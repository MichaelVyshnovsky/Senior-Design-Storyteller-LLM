The '''Pain domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[spell]]s and powers themed around pain.{{cite book/Book of Vile Darkness|81}}

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
A [[cleric]] with the Pain domain could convert an injury dealt from one blow into healing for him or herself, once a day.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[angry ache]]'', ''[[sadism]]'', and ''[[wrack]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[liquid pain]]'', ''[[thousand needles]]'', and ''[[pox]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[wave of pain]]'', ''[[symbol#pain|symbol of pain]]'', and ''[[eternity of torture]]''.
