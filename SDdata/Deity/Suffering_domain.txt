The '''Suffering domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[spell]]s and powers themed around suffering.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
They gain the ability to perform a ''pain touch'', which temporarily reduced the subject's strength and agility.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[bane (spell)|bane]]'', ''[[endurance]]'', and ''[[bestow curse]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[enervation]]'', ''[[feeblemind]]'', and ''[[harm]]''. Those that were masters in this domain had access to ''[[eyebite]]'' (sicken), ''[[symbol]]''  (pain), and ''[[horrid wilting]]''.
