The '''Community domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[spell]]s and powers themed around community.{{cite book/Book of Exalted Deeds|86}}

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
The priest gained the ability to use ''[[calm emotions]]'' innately once a day, and they also gained a knack for diplomacy.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[bless]]'', ''[[status]]'', and ''[[prayer (spell)|prayer]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[Rary's telepathic bond]]'', ''[[greater status]]'', and ''[[heroes' feast]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[Refuge (spell)|refuge]]'', ''[[sympathy]]'', and ''[[mass heal]]''.
