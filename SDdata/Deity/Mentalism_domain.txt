The '''Mentalism domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[spell]]s and powers based around mentalism.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
A priest with this domain could use [[abjuration]] magic to temporarily ward their mind or the mind of another against external effects, giving them the will to resist. It lasted one hour and could be done once a day.

==Spells==
As they advanced, a priest with this domain could cause a foe to act randomly with ''[[lesser confusion]]'', to read minds with ''[[detect thoughts]]'', to see or hear from afar with ''[[clairaudience/clairvoyance]]'', to ''[[modify memory]]'', to distract others with a ''[[mind fog]]'', to communicate with allies via a ''[[Rary's telepathic bond]]'', to repel creatures with ''[[antipathy]]'', to shield a mind with ''[[mind blank]]'', and finally to voyage to the [[Astral Plane]] using ''[[astral projection]]''.
