The '''Dream domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s access to dream- and mind-related [[spell]]s and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
Because they were experienced in analyzing dreams and nightmares, priests with this domain were immune to fear.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[sleep]]'', ''[[augury]]'', and ''[[deep slumber]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[phantasmal killer]]'', [[Nightmare (spell)|''nightmare'']], and ''[[dream sight]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[greater scrying]]'', ''[[power word, stun]]'', and ''[[weird]]''.
