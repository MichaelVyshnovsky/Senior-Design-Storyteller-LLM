The '''Light domain''' was a set of [[spell]]s that were granted by deities whose portfolio included the aspect of light. [[Cleric]]s who chose this domain were granted free access to these spells (they could cast them at any time without having to prepare them in advance).

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Spells==
Novice Light domain clerics could cast ''[[burning hands]]'', ''[[faerie fire]]'', ''[[flaming sphere]]'', and ''[[scorching ray]]''. Experienced casters could cast ''[[daylight]]'', ''[[fireball]]'', ''[[guardian of faith]]'', and ''[[wall of fire]]''. The most powerful of these clerics were able to cast the mighty ''[[flame strike]]'', and gain the ability to ''[[scrying (spell)|scry]]''.

==Powers==
In addition, Light domain clerics gained access to the ''[[channel divinity]]'' power known as ''[[radiance of the dawn]]''.
