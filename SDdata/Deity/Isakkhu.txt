'''Isakkhu''' was a male [[deity]] worshiped by [[barbarian]]s dwelling in the [[Earthfast Mountains]]

==Description==
The deity took the form of a mountain.

==Personality==
Isakkhu was dependable and reliable. 

==Worshipers==       
At the end of each day, [[cleric]]s of Isakkhu asked him to return the sun the next morning and he never failed to deliver.
