The '''drow domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters drow spell powers.

==Powers==
Those who worshiped deities of the [[Dark Seldarine]] were granted a specific suite of [[spell]]s, as well as improved reflexes. Novice casters of this domain were able to cast ''[[cloak of dark power]]'', ''[[clairaudience/clairvoyance]]'', and ''[[suggestion]]''. Skilled casters were able to cast ''[[discern lies]]'', ''[[spiderform]]'', and ''[[greater dispelling]]''. Those who had mastered the drow domain could cast ''[[word of chaos]]'', ''[[greater planar ally]]'', and even ''[[gate]]''.
