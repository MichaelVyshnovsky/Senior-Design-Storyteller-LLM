The '''Charity domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with charity-related [[spell]]s and powers.{{Cite dragon/355/Seven Saintly Domains|26}}

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
They could temporarily bequeath a portion of their health to an ally.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[goodberry]]'', ''[[aid]]'', and ''[[create food and water]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[imbue with spell ability]]'', ''[[mass cure light wounds]]'', and ''[[heroes' feast]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[Refuge (spell)|refuge]]'', ''[[Mordenkainen's magnificent mansion]]'', and ''[[mass heal]]''.
