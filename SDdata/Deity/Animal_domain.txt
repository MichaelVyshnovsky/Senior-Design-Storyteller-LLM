The '''Animal domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with animal-related [[spell]]s and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
They gained the ability to [[speak with animals]] once a day and they could study knowledge of nature.

==Spells==
As they advanced, a priest with this domain could control animals with ''[[calm animals]]'', ''[[hold animal]]'', and ''[[dominate animal]]''. They could summon animals to their aid with ''[[summon nature's ally IV]]'' and later ''[[summon nature's ally VIII|VIII]]''. They could ''[[commune with nature]]'' but also create an ''[[antilife shell]]'' to keep out living creatures. Finally, they could polymorph others into animals with ''[[animal shapes]]'' and themselves into any creature with ''[[shapechange]]''.
