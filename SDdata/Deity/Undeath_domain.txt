The '''Undeath domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[undeath]]-related [[spell]]s and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
They could [[turn undead|turn]] or rebuke [[undead]] additional times per day.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[detect undead]]'', ''[[desecrate]]'', and ''[[animate dead]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[death ward (spell)|death ward]]'', ''[[circle of doom]]'', and ''[[create undead]]''. Those that were masters in this domain had access to ''[[control undead]]'', ''[[create greater undead]]'', and ''[[energy drain]]''.
