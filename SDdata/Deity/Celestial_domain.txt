The '''Celestial domain''' was a [[cleric]]al domain that granted celestial [[spell]]s and powers.{{cite book/Book of Exalted Deeds|86}}

==Patrons==
* [[Domiel]]
* [[Pistis Sophia]]

==Powers==
The [[cleric]] gained the ability to [[smite]] evildoers once per day, as a [[paladin]] could.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[vision of heaven]]'', ''[[consecrate]]'', and ''[[blessed sight]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[lesser planar ally]]'', ''[[heavenly lightning]]'', and ''[[call faithful servants]]''. Those that were masters in this domain had access to ''[[heavenly lightning storm]]'', ''[[holy aura]]'', and ''[[gate]]''.
