The '''Healing domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with healing [[spell]]s and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
Their healing spells increased in potency.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[cure light wounds]]'', ''[[cure moderate wounds]]'', and ''[[cure serious wounds]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[cure critical wounds]]'', ''[[healing circle]]'', and ''[[heal]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[regenerate]]'', ''[[mass heal]]'', and ''[[true resurrection]]''.
