The '''Nobility domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[spell]]s and powers themed around nobility.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
They gained the ability to inspire their allies to greater courage and talent once a day.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[divine favor]]'', ''[[enthrall]]'', and ''[[magic vestment]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[discern lies]]'', ''[[greater command]]'', and ''[[geas/quest]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[repulsion]]'', ''[[demand]]'', and ''[[storm of vengeance]]''.
