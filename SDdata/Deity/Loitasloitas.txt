Loitasloitas was a deity worshiped by the tasloi that inhabited the island of Tasloiomitasloi.
The priests of Loitasloitas had access to spells from the spheres of All, Animal, Combat, Healing, and Protection.
* Gisloi, a priest in the 14 century DR that acted as a moral leader to his fellow tasloi and preached that disobeying the priests of Loitasloitas was heresy.
