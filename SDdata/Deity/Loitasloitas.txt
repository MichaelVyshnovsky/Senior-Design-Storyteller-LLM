'''Loitasloitas''' was a [[deity]] worshiped by the [[tasloi]] that inhabited the island of [[Tasloiomitasloi]].

==Worshipers==
The [[priest]]s of Loitasloitas had access to [[spell]]s from the [[sphere]]s of [[All sphere|All]], [[Animal sphere|Animal]], [[Combat sphere|Combat]], [[Healing sphere|Healing]], and [[Protection sphere|Protection]].
===Notable Worshipers===
* [[Gisloi]], a priest in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]] that acted as a moral leader to his fellow tasloi and preached that disobeying the priests of Loitasloitas was heresy.
