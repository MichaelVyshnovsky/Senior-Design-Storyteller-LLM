The '''Strength domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters strength-enhancing [[spell]] powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
They gained the ability to call upon their deity for supernatural strength once a day.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[enlarge person]]'', ''[[bull's strength]]'', and ''[[magic vestment]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[spell immunity]]'', ''[[righteous might]]'', and ''[[stoneskin]]''. Those that were masters this domain had access to ''[[Bigby's grasping hand]]'', ''[[Bigby's clenched fist]]'', and ''[[Bigby's crushing hand]]''.
