The '''Fire domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with [[fire]]-related [[spell]]s and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
The spellcaster gained the ability to turn away and destroy [[:Category:Creatures with the water subtype|water creatures]] the way a good cleric [[Turn undead|turned undead]], and to rebuke, command, and strengthen [[:Category:Creatures with the fire subtype|fire creatures]] the way an evil cleric rebuked undead.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[burning hands]]'', ''[[produce flame]]'', and ''[[resist energy]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[wall of fire]]'', ''[[fire shield]]'', and ''[[fire seeds]]''. Those that were masters of this domain had access to ''[[fire storm]]'', ''[[incendiary cloud]]'', and ''[[elemental swarm]]''.
