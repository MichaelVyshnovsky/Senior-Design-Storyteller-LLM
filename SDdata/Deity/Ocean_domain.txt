The '''Ocean domain''' was a deity domain that granted divine spellcasters like [[cleric]]s with oceanic-themed [[spell]]s and powers.

==Deities==
{{ListDeitiesByDomain|{{PAGENAME}}}}

==Powers==
They gained the ability to [[Water breathing|breathe underwater]], lasting for a minute to up to twenty minutes in total throughout the day.

==Spells==
Novice spellcasters were able to use the ''[[endure elements]]'', ''[[sound burst]]'', and ''[[water breathing]]'' spells. Clerics of some experience could cast ''[[freedom of movement]]'', ''[[wall of ice]]'', and ''[[Otiluke's freezing sphere]]''. Those that were masters in this domain had access to ''[[waterspout]]'', ''[[maelstrom]]'', and water ''[[elemental swarm]]''.
