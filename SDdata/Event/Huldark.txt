Huldark was an annual Waukeenar festival celebrated by the church, clergy, and Waukeens worshipers across the Realms on Eleasis 17.
The autumn Waukeenar festival was celebrated everywhere where Waukeen was worshiped. The events goal was to celebrate the fall harvest and bounty of the land brought by hard work. During the festival, worshipers of Waukeen planted new fruit trees and vegetables, followed by a feast.
