'''Fate's Day''' was a holiday celebrated in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[Zakhara]].

==Description==
Fate's Day was observed on the last day of [[Masta]]. The natives of Huzuz used this day to recognize the miracles and wonders provided by [[Fate]]. Any strange occurrences on this day were seen as omens worthy of note.
