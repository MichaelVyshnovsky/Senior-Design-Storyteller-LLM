The '''Festival of Remembering''' was a solemn [[dwarf|dwarven]] holy day dedicated to remembrance of the dead.{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|1060}}

During the ceremony, loved ones toasted the dead with a glass of [[ale]] flavored with [[honey]] and bitters to symbolize sweetness and sorrow.{{Cite digital book/The Gilded Rune/Kindle|1214}}
