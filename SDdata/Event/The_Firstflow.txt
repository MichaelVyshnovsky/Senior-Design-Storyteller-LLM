The '''Firstflow''' was a festival observed by worshipers of [[Eldath]], the Goddess of Singing Waters.

==Activities==
This festival was held at different times of the year depending the climate and locale. It celebrated the winter ice breaking up and allowing rivers and streams to flow again for the remainder of the year.
