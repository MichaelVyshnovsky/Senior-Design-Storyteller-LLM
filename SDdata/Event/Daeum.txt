The '''Daeum''', which was also called the '''Thanks to the Guide''', was a religious event observed by worshipers of [[Kelemvor]], the Lord of the Dead.

==Activities==
The ritual began each night after the sun set. Kelemvor and his [[Church of Kelemvor|church]] were celebrated by faithful followers. At the close of the ceremony, goods of the recently deceased were distributed among those in attendance.
