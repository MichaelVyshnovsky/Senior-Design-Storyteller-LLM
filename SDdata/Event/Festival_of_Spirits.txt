The '''Festival of Spirits''' was a religious festival in the [[Plain of Horses]]. It was the most commonly observed among the few formal holidays and festivals held in that land.

==Activities==
This day saw Plainsmen families gather at a local [[Wikipedia:ovoo|''obo'']], a structure built of stone and wood reaching {{SI|10|ft|long=yes}} in height. They made offerings of foods such as [[butter]] and [[cheese]] to the local [[spirit]]s.
