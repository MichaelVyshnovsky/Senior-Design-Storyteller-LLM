The Ritual of the Dark Flames, or Ritual of Dark Flames,
While the exact specifications of the ritual were undisclosed, it required the use of a black onyx gem worth at least 100 gp.
The Ritual of the Dark Flames allowed a loyal devotee of Cyric to conjure and wield black flames born from negative energy itself. The use of this supernatural power was etremely taxing on the mind and will of its wielder.
It was regularly performed upon members of the Company of the Ebon Spur, to bolster their prowess on the battlefield.
