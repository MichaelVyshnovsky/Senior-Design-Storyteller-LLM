The Feast of the Ancestors was a holiday celebrated in Huzuz, the City of Delights, in Zakhara.
The Feast of the Ancestors occurred annually on the 25 of Magarib. Inhabitants of Huzuz honored their ancestors and relative by offering gifts to living members of their family, and by remembrance and prayers for the deceased.
