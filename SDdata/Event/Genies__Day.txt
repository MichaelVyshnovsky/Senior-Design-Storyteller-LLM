'''Genies' Day''' was a celebration that occurred in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[Zakhara]].

==Description==
This annual event, which occurred on the 12{{th}} of [[Nau]], was a day devoted to honoring and thanking the various [[genie]] races and individual genies for their protection and services.

==History==
Genies' Day begun the year following {{Yearlink|1347}}{{AQ-time}}, when all genie races rose together to protect golden Huzuz from an invasion attempt.
