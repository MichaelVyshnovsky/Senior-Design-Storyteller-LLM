'''''Telhut''''' was a religious ceremony observed by the [[Uthgardt]] barbarians of the [[Savage Frontier]].

==Description==
A ''telhut'' was held during the Uthgardt [[Runemeet]] in the month of [[Eleint]]. At this time the [[shaman]]s of a tribe initiated all boys who completed a [[Runehunt]] into manhood.
