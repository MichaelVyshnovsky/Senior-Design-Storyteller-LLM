The '''Summer Assembly''' was a holiday in the [[Plain of Horses]]. It was one of the more commonly observed among the few formal holidays and festivals held in that land.

==Activities==
It was a time for Plainsmen families and tribes to meet up, discuss issues, and share news and gossip. It lasted several days.
