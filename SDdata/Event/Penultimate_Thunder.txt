The '''Penultimate Thunder''' was a celebration observed by worshipers of [[Hoar]], the Doombringer, every 11{{th}} of [[Eleint]].

==Activities==
The celebration marked the long-awaited triumph of Hoar over [[Ramman]] and included feasts of wild game, fruits, bread, and [[mead]].
