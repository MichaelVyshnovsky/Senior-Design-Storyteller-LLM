The '''Lament for the Fallen''' was a religious event observed by worshipers of [[Kelemvor]], the Lord of the Dead.

==Activities==
This lengthy ceremony was performed as a form of last rites for an area where a large number of people had died such as a battlefield or a destroyed village or fortress.
