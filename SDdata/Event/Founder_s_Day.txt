'''Founder's Day''' was a holiday celebrated in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[Zakhara]].

==Description==
Observed annually on the 10{{th}} of [[Taraq]], Founder's Day celebrated the birthday of the very first [[Grand Caliph]] who founded golden Huzuz.
