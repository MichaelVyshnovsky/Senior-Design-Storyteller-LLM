The '''Silver Hills''' were a prominent geographical feature located on the [[elven]] island homeland of [[Evermeet]].

==Geography==
The Silver Hills were located on the western banks of the [[River Ardulith]]. They stretched from Evermeet's northwest coast through the center of the island along the river before terminating just upriver from where the [[River Shaelyn]] joined the Ardulith. The hills stretched for approximately {{SI|150|mi|k|long=yes}}.

==Description==
The hills stood {{SI|3000|ft|long=yes}} over the river valley. Large conifers covered the slopes of the Silver Hills.

==Inhabitants==
The Silver Hills were home to group of [[pegasi]] and [[giant eagle]]s

==History==
[[Sunstone (magic item)|Sunstones]] could be found within the Silver Hills.
