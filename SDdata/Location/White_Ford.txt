'''White Ford''' was a [[hamlet]] and the second most important settlement in [[Archendale]].

[[File:White Ford.png|thumb|right|260px|''Citizens walking the streets of White Ford.'']]

==Geography==
White Ford was located along the [[River Arkhen]], at the mouth of the [[Arkhen Vale]].

===Trade Routes===
; The [[West Road]]
:Going southeast this road led to [[Archenbridge]], {{SI|45|miles|k}} away

==Trade==
The proximity of White Ford to nearby metropolises such as [[Highmoon]] and [[Archenbridge]] contributed to mercantile traffic.

In the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], White Ford was a place where the trade company [[Mirksha, Mirksha and Mirksha|Mirksha, Mirksha, and Mirksha]], trading with [[Cormyr]] and [[Sembia]], established a post and warehouse.
