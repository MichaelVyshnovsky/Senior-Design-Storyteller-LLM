The '''Swordbelt''' was the name given to the land of central [[Amn]]. The northernmost part was the foothills of the [[Small Teeth]] mountains, and the southernmost part was the [[Wealdath]]. The westernmost part was the [[Sword Coast]], and the easternmost point was the town of [[Brost]].
