Baluths Hole was an urban path in Scornubel during the mid-14 century DR.
Baluths Hole was one of the paths leading to Belgon Bradaskors home in Scornubel.
In , Sharantyr forced Besmer Altuth to take him to Belgon Bradaskors home, after the latter failed to mug her. He offered her a choice of paths, between Rat Stair and Baluths Hole. She chose the latter.
