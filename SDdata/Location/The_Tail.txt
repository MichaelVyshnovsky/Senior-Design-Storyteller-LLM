The '''Tail''' was a name given to a small peninsula found in [[Durpar]] in the [[Shining Lands]].

==Geography==
The tail jutted out from Durpar's southern shore along the [[Golden Water]]. The peninsula was blocked inland where it met the coast by the mountain known as [[Adama's Tooth]].
