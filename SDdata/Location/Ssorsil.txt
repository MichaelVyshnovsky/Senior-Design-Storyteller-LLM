'''Ssorsil''' was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Ssorsil was located on the plains of western Halruaa northeast of the [[Halar Hills]]. The town sat south of the [[High Aluar]], north of the [[River Halar]], and west of the [[River Aluar]].
