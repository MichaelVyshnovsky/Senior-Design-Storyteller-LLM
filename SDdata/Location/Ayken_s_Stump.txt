'''Ayken's Stump''' was a small village in the [[High Duchy of Langalos]] during the 8{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Trade==
The people of the village were loggers.

==History==
The entire village was annihilated by [[Saeraede Lyonora|the Slayer]] in the {{yearlinkName|767}}. Within the next tenday, reports of their disappearance were related to the High Duke by his seneschal, [[Bresmer]].
