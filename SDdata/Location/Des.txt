'''Des''' was a small coastal town located in the nation of [[Var]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dSWIc}}

==Geography==
Des was located in eastern Var along the southern shore of the [[Golden Water]]. The town sat due north from [[Myrmyr]].
