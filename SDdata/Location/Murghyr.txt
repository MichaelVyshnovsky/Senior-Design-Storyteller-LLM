Murghyr was the capital city of the country of Murghôm during the mid–14 century DR. 
Murghyr was located in western Murghôm on the east bank of the River Murghol where it joined the River Rauthenflow just south of the Ganathwood near the border of Mulhorand.
The Mulhorandi precept of Murghyr in the , was an esteemed priest of Horus-Re named Imthalos.
