'''Vurpar''' was a small town located in the nation of [[Durpar]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dSWIc}}

==Geography==
Vurpar was located along the northwestern shore of the [[Golden Water]] in central Durpar. It sat directly southeast of its larger neighbor, [[Orpher]].
