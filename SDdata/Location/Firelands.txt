The '''Firelands''' was a region of the [[Underdark]] located beneath the jungles of [[Chult]].

==Geography==
The Firelands were located beneath the jungles of [[Chult]]. Lava tubes to the [[Peaks of Flame]] provided connections to the surface.

==Description==
The Firelands were full of lava tubes and other volcanic features. In the center of the Firelands was the [[Burning Rift]], which contained innumerable ''[[portal]]s'' to the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]].

==Inhabitants==
The Firelands were home to [[azer]], [[flamesnake]]s, [[magmin]]s, and [[salamander]]s that had migrated from the Plane of Fire through ''portals''.
