'''Sumuk Island''' was an island within the [[Golden Gulf]] of [[interior Zakhara]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2bSWIc}}

==Geography==
Sumuk Island sat approximately {{SI|40|mi|k|long=yes}} offshore due west of the mainland near the city of [[I'tiraf]] and the [[Cities of the Pantheon]] region. Sumuk Island was {{SI|40|mi|k|long=yes}} long and {{SI|30|mi|k|long=yes}} wide. Two smaller islands lay off of its north shore. Coral reefs surrounded these unamed islands. An ancient ruined mosque was located on the southeast coast of Sumuk Island in [[1367 DR]]{{AQ-time}}.
