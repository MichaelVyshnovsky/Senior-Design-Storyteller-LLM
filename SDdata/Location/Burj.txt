'''Burj''' was a small town found within the [[Cities of the Pantheon]] of [[interior Zakhara]].

==Geography==
Burj rested at the feet of [[Al-Sayaj Mountains]]'s western slope where [[Al-Hadi River]] originated. It was several hundred miles east of [[I'tiraf]].
