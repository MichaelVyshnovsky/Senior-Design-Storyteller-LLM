The '''Drowned Forest'''<ref group="note">The [[Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas]] lists this location as the "Browned Forest", but this is believed to be a typo. was a marshland located in the kingdom of [[Callidyrr]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The marsh was located along the southwestern edge of the [[Dernall Forest]] in central Callidyrr. A small portion of the Drowned Forest bordered the coast of the [[Sea of Moonshae]].
