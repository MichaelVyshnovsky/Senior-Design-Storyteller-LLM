The Drowned ForestThe Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas lists this location as the "Browned Forest", but this is believed to be a typo. was a marshland located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.
The marsh was located along the southwestern edge of the Dernall Forest in central Callidyrr. A small portion of the Drowned Forest bordered the coast of the Sea of Moonshae.
