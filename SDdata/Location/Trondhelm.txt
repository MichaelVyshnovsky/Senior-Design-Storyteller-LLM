'''Trondhelm''' was a small village located on [[Oman's Isle]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The village was located within the heart of the [[Old Stone Wood]] on the western coast of the island.

==Inhabitants==
[[Thelgaar Ironhand]], a Jarl of [[Oman]] during the mid 14{{th}} century [[DR]], was born in Trondhelm. Like the rest of the settlements on Oman's Isle, Trondhelm was overrun and ruled by [[giant]]s by the later part of the 15{{th}} century DR.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|11}}
