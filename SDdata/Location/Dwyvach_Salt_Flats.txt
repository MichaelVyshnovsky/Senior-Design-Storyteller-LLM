The '''Dwyvach Salt Flats''' was a plain located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The [[salt]] flats were found in the far southwestern corner of Gwynneth. They were bordered on the west by the [[Trackless Sea]], and on the east by the [[Mere Adammair]] and an open area of plains. The Dwyvach Salt Flats covered much of Gwynneth's western coast south of [[Kimball Moor]].
