'''Turab''' was a small town found in the northeast region of the [[Pearl Cities]] of [[west Zakhara]].

==Geography==
Though [[Tajar]] and [[Sikak]] were nearby, Turab was the only small settlement found on the long stretch of coast along the [[Golden Gulf]] in this region of the Pearl Cities. Tajar was approximately {{SI|60|mi|k|long=yes}} to the north. Turab sat along the rocky coast of the Golden Gulf near [[Maribar Island]].
