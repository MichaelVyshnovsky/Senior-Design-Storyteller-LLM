Iewara was a town located on the island of Kanshu in Kozakura.
Iewara, the largest settlement on Kanshu, served as the bustling center of trade with Kozakura and Wa. The town thrived on the export of timber and boasted a substantial fishing fleet. Iewara carried a raw and rustic ambiance, housing a population of rugged woodsmen and fishermen.
The region encompassing Iewara belonged to the clan of bamboo spirit folk, under the ownership of Lord Betsui Kando.
