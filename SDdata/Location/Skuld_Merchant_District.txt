The Merchant District was a walled section of the capital city of Skuld in Mulhorand during the midâ€“14 century DR.
The Merchant District was located in the southwest part of Skuld. The districts southern border ran along the north bank of the River of Shadows, and the western border abutted the Alamber Sea. 
Walls surrounded the Merchant District. A gate was located on the north wall that connected to the northwest section of the city, and another gate was located on the east wall that connected to the Warehouse District. The Bridge Gate to the south spanned the River of Shadows and connected to the naval yard.
