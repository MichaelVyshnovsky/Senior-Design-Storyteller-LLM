DjannathThe Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas labels this as Otennath. was a small town located in the nation of Luiren, land of the halflings, in the Shining South during the 14 century DR.
Djannath was located along the coast of Hambone Bay in central Luiren. The town sat along a road at the midway point between Beluir to the southwest and Shoun to the northeast.
