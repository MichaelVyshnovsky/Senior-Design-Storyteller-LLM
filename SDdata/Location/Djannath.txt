'''Djannath'''<ref group="note">The ''[[Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas]]'' labels this as Otennath. was a small town located in the nation of [[Luiren]], land of the [[halfling]]s, in the [[Shining South]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dSWIc}}

==Geography==
Djannath was located along the coast of [[Hambone Bay]] in central Luiren. The town sat along a road at the midway point between [[Beluir]] to the southwest and [[Shoun]] to the northeast.
