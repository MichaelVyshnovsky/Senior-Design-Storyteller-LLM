The '''Horn of Sahu''' was a coral reef found on the north end of the island of [[Sahu]] in [[east Zakhara]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Geography==
Roughly triangular in shape, the Horn of Sahu reached outward into the sea for approximately {{SI|30|mi|k|long=yes}}.

==Inhabitants==
The reef was infested with [[shark]]s, offering a barrier of natural protection to the island from seaborne attacks.
