Tyrluk () was a small village at the base of the Storm Horn Mountains in Cormyr.
In the , Tyrluk, along with Espar, Eveningstar, and Waymoot, was plagued by rogue dragons.
In Tarsakh of the , Tyrluk was sacked at the beginning of the Goblin War. By the end of the war all that was left was the smithy and the Old Mans Face inn, and they were both left in poor condition.
