Eldaths Patch was a vacant lot in Neverwinter as of .
It was the last vacant lot in Neverwinters Silverlade Street. There were blueleaf trees growing on it, and it was bordered on its north end by the walls of the Melaraunt family gardens. There was a marker on the lot showing it was consecrated to Eldath.
Eldaths Patch had a refuge added upon it during the reign of Magister Maldiglas Turntower at its north end, where the blueleaf trees grew thickest. By the , it was so popular with young lovers (and some nobility) that dogs had been banned from it, after some incidents.
