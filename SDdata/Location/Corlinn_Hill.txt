Corlinn Hill was a county of Neverwinter that was located in the hills northeast of that city.
The ruling human family of Corlinn Hill were wealthy and powerful during the glory days of Neverwinter.
Corlinn Hill was utterly destroyed in the eruption of Mount Hotenow in the , which also destroyed Neverwinter and its royal family. The surviving heir to Corlinn Hill was subsequently raised in a townhouse in Waterdeep, but grew up to inherit nothing but a title. They became a paladin sworn to Bahamut before returning to Neverwinter in the late 15 century DR.
