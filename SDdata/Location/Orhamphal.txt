'''Orhamphal'''<ref group="note">This is mislabeled as Hamphal in the ''[[Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas]]''. was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Orhamphal was located in northern Halruaa on the eastern bank of the [[River Aluar]] where it was joined by the [[High Aluar]]. The town sat just south of the looming [[Mount Alue]] and the [[Nathaghal Mountains]].
