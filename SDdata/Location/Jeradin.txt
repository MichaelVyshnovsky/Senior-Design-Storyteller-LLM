'''Jeradin''' was a town located in the [[Eastern Shaar]] during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dlc}} 

==Geography==
Jeradin sat along the southern foothills of the [[Unthangol Mountains]] just south of the source of the [[River Angol]]. The town sat along a trail in between the [[Council Hills]] to the east and the town of [[Hardcastle (city)|Hardcastle]] to the west.
