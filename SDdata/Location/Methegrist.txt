Methegrist was a fortress in the Moonwood that served as the headquarters of the Moonwatchers, a small group of paladins devoted to Helm who had appointed themselves defenders of the Moonlands. The fortress was a ruin as of the 15 century DR.
The imp Fortenesti infiltrated the Moonwatchers and caused them to turn on each other. The Moonwatchers fractured and Methegrist fell into ruin.
* Jahar, the first paladin to succumb to manipulation, still haunted Methegrist as of the 15 century DR.
