The '''Staring Ranger''' was a rock formation used as a landmark south of [[Nashkel]].

==Description==
It was a man-high standing stone.

==Geography==
It lay south of [[Nashkel]]'s settled area. Between it and the [[Eggs of the Dragon]], the [[Magister]] [[Maldiglas Turntower]] had placed a [[refuge]].
