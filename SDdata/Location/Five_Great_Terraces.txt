The '''Five Great Terraces''' was a large enclosure found in [[Manass]] in [[Khazari]].

==Description==
A graveled square surrounded the Five Great Terraces, which were stacked upon one another. Barracks for troops were positioned along three of the sides.

==Uses==
The governor of Manass used this area to review his troops as well as entertain elite guests and dignitaries. It was a crime to visit the Five Great Terraces unless specifically invited by the governor.
