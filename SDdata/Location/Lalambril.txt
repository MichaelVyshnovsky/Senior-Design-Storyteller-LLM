Lalambril was a short-lived fiefdom in the Border Kingdoms.
The place was in the eastern end of the Border Kingdoms, on the coast of the Lake of Steam. It was small.
In the , Lalambril had a "Lord Bard".
Lalambril had been short-lived, but had existed through the year 1400 DR.
