The '''Fens of the Fallen''' was a small forest located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Fens of the Fallen were located in [[Myrloch Vale]] along the southeast shore of the [[Myrloch]]. The forest was bordered to the south by [[the Highlands]].

==Activities==
The Fens of the Fallen were notoriously dangerous, for it was within this forest where [[Kazgaroth]] rose.
