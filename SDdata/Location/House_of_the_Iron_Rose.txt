The '''House of the Iron Rose''' was a sumptuous boarding house in [[Eshpurta]] in early [[1370 DR|1370 DR, the Year of the Tankard]]. Typically, only ranking officers and wealthy adventurers stayed there. The House of the Iron Rose offered the finest quality accommodations to those willing to pay for excellence.
