'''Ayakar''', nicknamed the '''Carnelian Star Among Opals''', was coastal trading town in [[Turmish]] that was famous for its widely-popular bazaar. It received ships from far and wide across the diverse nation.{{Cite adventurers league/Breaking Umberlee's Resolve|6-7}}

{{Quote|May your bread be sour, and your salt be fresh.|A common Ayakaran welcoming to visitors.}}

==Description==
The town seemed precariously built as its famous bazaar jutted of the edge of a cliff face, but was in fact quite structurally sound. It comprised nine large domed structures built over spacious and beauteous atriums. The residential area of town was situated inland from the coast line.

The scent of seawater permeated the air around Ayakar all year round, and a thing layer of salt.

===Culture===
The Ayakaran people primarily venerated the goddess [[Sel没ne]], as she held influence over the tides. They tended to speak in rather long prose, preferring poetic language and quirky idioms.

Many locals carried on their person, long ornamental [[blade]]s.

A favored meal often given as a gesture of welcome was a loaf of [[sourdough]] bread served with a small bowl of [[salt]], for which the town was famous.

==Geography==
The town was situated in northern Turmish on a cliff overlooking the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]].

==Trade==
Ayakar's bazaar attracted shoppers from far and wide. Within its nine domes were several public services buildings, along with numerous businesses, including: its renowned bakery, a bustling tea house, an angler's shop for all types of fishers, a sailmaker's establishment, the [[spice]] market, and a shop that specialized in traveler's gear.

The town housed several [[fermentation]] vats, which collected naturally-formed [[yeast]] from the sea, to be used in producing famous Ayakaran sourdough bread.

Fishing was also a robust industry in Ayakar, as the nearby bay was teeming way many unique species of fish. 

==Notable Locations==
In addition to its notable businesses and trading space, Ayakar housed a public meeting space, a hospital run by [[cleric]]s of Sel没ne, and public bathing houses.{{Cite adventurers league/Blood in the Water|30}}
