Ithal Pass was a fortified town in the Iltkazar Highlands in Tethyr, in the southern region of County Surkazar, in the late 14 century DR.
Once a small crossroads town supported by local dairy farmers, the city swelled in size with the influx of soldiers from the duke of Suretmarchs army. The garrison worked to fortify the town to repel the forces of the Knights of the Black Gauntlet, who had invaded nearby Kzelter.
* Vales Rest This neglected taproom was the oldest and only inn in town, offering a warm atmosphere to its clientele.
* Soldiers Slake A converted barn served the military soldiers and officers while the towns wall was being built.
