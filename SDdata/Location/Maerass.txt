'''Maerass'''<ref group="note">The ''[[Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas]]'' has this mislabeled as Marrass. was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Maerass was located in central Halruaa along the southern bank of the [[River Maeru]] just upriver from where it joined [[Lake Halruaa]]. The town sat northwest of [[Zalazuu]] and the [[Bay of Azuth]].
