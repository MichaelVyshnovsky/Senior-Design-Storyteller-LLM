Wetikak was a small village in the Nakvaligach region within the Great Glacier.
Wetikak was located in western Nakvaligach, near the Tuutsaas Chain, and to the north of the Igotak Sea.
The inhabitants of Wetikak were belligerent and violent to outsiders. The village was led by Tukurshuk around 1359 DR, a crazed egomanic who saw himself as the son of Ulutiu.
