'''Follebu''' was a small village located on the island of [[Norland]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Follebu sat along the northern fringes of the [[South Jotunhammer Mountains]] within the region of Norland called [[Grondsgrunn]] which was controlled by [[giant]]s. The village was found at the end of a long unnamed bay on the island's western coast on the [[Trackless Sea]].
