The Rookery was a district within the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire. 
Gambling houses, fighting pits, and pleasure gardens filled this ward and were never closed.
In addition to the gamblers, thieves, and miscreants who occupied this area, plenty of firenewts populated the Rookery.
* The Kalian
This section of the City of Brass was renowned throughout the Plane of Fire for being the most dangerous and lawless part of the city. The Rookery was so dangerous it caused even the Sultans guards to avoid treading there.
