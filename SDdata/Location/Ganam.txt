'''Ganam''' was a small town located within the [[Haunted Lands]].

==Inhabitants==
Roughly 100 people made up the town of Ganam around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}} Most worshiped [[Hajama]] and would not divulge much information to those who weren't believers.
