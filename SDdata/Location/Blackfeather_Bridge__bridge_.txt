'''Blackfeather Bridge''' was an old bridge that spanned the [[River Ashaba]] near the town of [[Blackfeather Bridge (village)|Blackfeather Bridge]] in [[Featherdale]].

==Description==
This black-painted bridge was found just north of the town.

==History==
Blackfeather Bridge was constructed to replace a bridge built under [[Rauthauvyr]].
