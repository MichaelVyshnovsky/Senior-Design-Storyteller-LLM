'''Chethel''' was a coastal city in the [[halfling]] realm of [[Luiren]].

==Geography==
Chethel was located on the coast of [[Luirenstrand]] (the [[Great Sea]]). Directly to the southeast of the city was the [[Mortick Swamp]].

==History==
The city was founded in [[47 DR]].<ref name=tGHotR-p61/> In [[148 DR]], the city was half-destroyed by [[Merrow (ogre)|merrow]] from Mortick Swamp.<ref name=tGHotR-p63> In [[1105 DR]], the city received widespread damage from a storm over the Great Sea.

==Trade==
Chethel was one of Luiren's main trading cities. Its inhabitants were particularly skilled at shipbuilding.

==Inhabitants==
In addition to halflings, one-tenth of the city's population were [[elf|elves]] and [[half-elf|half-elves]].
