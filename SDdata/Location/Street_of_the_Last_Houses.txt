The Street of the Last Houses, also referred to as the Boneyard by the lower classes, was a necropolis located in the City of Brass in 1367 DR.
The Street of the Last Houses was found in the Plume ward of the city.
The efreet honored dead individuals of note including mages, nobles, and wealthy merchants by interring them into the small necropolis. The poor and lower classes of the City of Brass viewed any type of burial as a repugnant form of heresy.
