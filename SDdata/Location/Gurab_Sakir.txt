'''Gurab Sakir''' was a small town nestled within the [[Furrowed Mountains]] separating the [[Haunted Lands]] from [[North Zakhara]].

==Inhabitants==
Isolated hill people occupied the town of Gurab Sakir. They were hostile toward anyone associated with [[Qudra]] or the [[mamluk]]s.
