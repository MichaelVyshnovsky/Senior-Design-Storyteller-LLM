The Three Dolphins Door was a gate of Castle Obarskyr.
The gate opened into the Promenade.
The doors swung inwards.
In the evening after the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the , Storm Silverhand, the intemperate Marchioness Immerdusk, Arclath Delcastle, a heir of House Arclath, and Amarune Whitewave, a mask dancer, were taken out of the castle by a coach through the Three Dolphins Door by the War Wizard Welwyn Tracegar, then under mind-control by the fugitive wizard Elminster Aumar.
