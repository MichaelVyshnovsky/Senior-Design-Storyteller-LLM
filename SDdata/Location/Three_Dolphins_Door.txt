The '''Three Dolphins' Door''' was a gate of [[Castle Obarskyr]].

==Location==
The gate opened into the [[Promenade (Suzail)|Promenade]].

==Construction==
The doors swung inwards.

==History==
In the evening after the [[Council of the Dragon]] in late [[Mirtul]] of the {{yearlinkName|1479}}, [[Storm Silverhand]], the intemperate Marchioness [[Immerdusk]], [[Arclath Delcastle]], a heir of House [[Arclath]], and [[Amarune Whitewave]], a mask dancer, were taken out of the castle by a coach through the Three Dolphins Door by the [[War Wizard]] [[Welwyn Tracegar]], then under mind-control by the fugitive [[wizard]] [[Elminster Aumar]].
