The Katakoro Plateau was the only large plateau in the entire Hordelands.
The roughly triangular Katakoro Plateau was enclosed by the Kun-Yen Shan range to the south, the A-Ling Shan range to the west, and the Yellow Serpent Border Cliffs to the east.
By , the Katakoro Plateau stood at the eastern border of the Imaskar Empire. During the Middle Kingdoms period ( to ), Katakoro had become a province of the eastern Upper Kingdom of the empire, home to the cities of Ormui, Hoazyu, and Auyzolu.
The Imaskar Empire fell in , but the eastern provinces of Katakoro and Khati emerged relatively unscathed. Thus, in , Kujawa claimed power in Khati and declared himself Emperor, founding a new realm, Anok-Imaskar, covering Katakoro. His empire would collapse with his death in , leaving Katakoros fate unrecorded.
In 1360 DR, Raja Ambuchar Devayam of Solon led his undead armies in a conquest of Ra-Khati and a number of settlements on the Katakoro Plateau.
