'''Safe Cove''' was a small coastal fishing village found on the [[Island of Firethorns]] in the [[Corsair Domains]] of [[northwest Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Geography==
Safe Cove was found along a natural bay on the western side of the island. The village was approximately {{SI|45|mi|k|long=yes}} southwest of [[Hawa]]. It sat in the forested region of the island between the northern and southern land rises.
