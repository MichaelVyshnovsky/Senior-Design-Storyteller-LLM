'''Erebos''' was a large city on the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]].  The city could call up 8,000 troops and had a large fleet of ships with 5,000 marine soldiers.  An ancient temple of [[Tiamat]] stood near the sea.

* Bronze Windlass Inn
* Central Market

* Red Dog Tavern
