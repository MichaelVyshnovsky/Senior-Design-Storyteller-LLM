==Geography==
The First Rise was located in the side of a cliff, and houses were built inside and around it.

==Inhabitants==
The First Rise was home mostly to [[dwarves]] , although many ship captains and those not
wealthy enough to live on the [[Second Rise]] also called the First Rise home.
