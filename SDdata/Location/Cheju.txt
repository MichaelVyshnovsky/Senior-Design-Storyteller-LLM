Cheju was a settlement on the island of Saishu in the kingdom of Koryo in Kara-Tur.Like many sites in Koryo, Cheju shares a name with a real-world counterpart Jeju, formerly transliterated in English as "Cheju", an island and province of Korea. Cheju shares this origin with the island of Saishu itself and the island of Quelpart.
Cheju stood at the end of a narrow bay that cut deep into the island.
By 1357 DR, Dan Zor, the former king and later governor of Saishu, was granted the honorary title of "Master of Cheju" by King Wanang Sun.
