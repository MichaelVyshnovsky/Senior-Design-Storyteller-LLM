'''Arch Wood''' was a forest on the northern border of [[Archendale]].

==Geography==
Throughout history, the majority of the forest was never been logged. The Arch Wood was a dark, dense forest full of [[oak]], [[ash]], [[elm]], [[shadowtop]] and [[duskwood (tree)|duskwood]] trees.

==Inhabitants==
*[[Owlbear]]s
*[[People of the Black Blood]]
*[[Quamara Keleidsa]]{{Cite dungeon/121/Secrets of the Arch Wood|67}}

==Notable locations==
* The Arch Wood had ruins in it including the remains of an old [[elven]] school of wizardry.
* It contained the ruined estate of [[House Keleidsa]], which was guarded by a [[baelnorn]] before being taken over by [[Quamara Keleidsa]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dungeon/121/Secrets of the Arch Wood|67-79}}
