'''Darmesh''' was a small town located in the nation of [[Dambrath]] in the [[Shining South]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dSWIc}}

==Geography==
Darmesh was located in southern Dambrath on the [[Hethar Peninsula]]. The town lay on the southern coast with the [[Great Sea]] near the midpoint of the peninsula southeast of [[Hethar]].
