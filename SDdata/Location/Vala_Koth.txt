ValaKoth was a githyanki city in the Astral Plane.
ValaKoth was  away from the Elserryn Cluster, on the petrified body of a dead god.
ValaKoth had an astral fleet of three planar raiders, five astral brigs, and twenty astral skiffs. Despite these forces, they often found themselves attacked by small armies, usually in retaliation for something.
As of , the forces of ValaKoth had yet to successfully extract concessions out of Crosswinds Keep, or even destroy the location.
