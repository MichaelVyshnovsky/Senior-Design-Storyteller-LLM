'''Vala'Koth''' was a [[githyanki]] city in the [[Astral Plane]].

==Geography==
Vala'Koth was {{SI|100|miles|k|long=yes}} away from the [[Elserryn Cluster]],{{cite web/Astral Encounters/Crosswinds Keep}} on the petrified body of a dead god.

==Forces==
Vala'Koth had an astral fleet of three [[planar raider]]s, five [[astral brig]]s, and twenty [[astral skiff]]s. Despite these forces, they often found themselves attacked by small armies, usually in retaliation for something.

==History==
As of {{yearlink|1372}}, the forces of Vala'Koth had yet to successfully extract concessions out of [[Crosswinds Keep]], or even destroy the location.
