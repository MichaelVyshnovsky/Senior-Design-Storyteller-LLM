'''Stormpemhauder''' was a village of [[forest gnome]]s in the [[Spiderhaunt Wood]], which lay between [[Daggerdale]] and the [[Desertsmouth Mountains]] west of the [[Dalelands]]. 

It was one of the many settlements of gnomes linked by the [[magic]]al transportation network known as the [[Trail of Mists]].{{Cite web/Mintiper's Chapbook/Part 6}}
