'''Fukiow''' was a large mining city in [[Chukei Province]] of [[Shou Lung]] around [[1357 DR]].

It stood on the harsh, windswept plains of the Chukei Plateau, not far enough from the abandoned and haunted city of [[Kuo Meilan]].
