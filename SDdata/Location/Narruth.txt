'''Narruth''' was a small island located within the [[Land of the Lions]] region of [[South Faerûn]].<ref name=EotSS-p140-141"><ref name=EotSS.M">{{Cite interactive atlas|B1cSElc}}

==Location==
Narruth lay in the northwest corner of the [[Lake of Steam]] west of [[Mintar]] and north of the island of [[Olodel]].<ref name=EotSS-p140-141"/><ref name=EotSS.M"/>

==Inhabitants==
The island served as the base and home for a group of warrior-merchants called the [[Baldovans]]. The Baldovans operated their own trading coster from the island. The group did not welcome uninvited visitors.<ref name=EotSS-p140-141"/>
