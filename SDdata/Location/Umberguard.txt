'''Umberguard''' was a county in the [[Northern Polarate]] of [[Anadia]]. It was one of four counties in the polarate that allowed [[spelljammer]]s to land, along with [[Anadjin (county)|Anadjin]], [[Hairfoot]], and [[Powderpuff (county)|Powderpuff]].
