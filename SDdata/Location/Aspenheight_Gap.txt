The '''Aspenheight Gap''' was a mountain pass located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The pass separated the [[Aspenheight]] in the north from [[the Highlands]] in the south. The Aspenheight Gap connected the western region of [[Myrloch Vale]] with [[the Shrouded Lands]]. The pass was approximately {{SI|20|mi|k|long=yes}} long.
