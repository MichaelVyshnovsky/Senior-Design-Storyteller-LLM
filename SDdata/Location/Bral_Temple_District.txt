The Temple District was one of the thirteen districts of Bral, the sole city on the surface of the Rock of Bral. It was the main religious district of Bral, housing many of the citys temples.

* The Graveyard
* House of the Path and the Way
* Pantheistic College of Celestian
* Pantheist Temple of Tyr
* Planar Church of Olympus
* Planar Church of the Seven Heavens
* Shrines dedicated to polygot faiths.
