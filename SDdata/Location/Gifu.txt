'''Gifu''' was the [[shogun]] capital of [[Kozakura]] from [[1046 DR]]. Gifu was in [[Tairayama Province]] near the [[Moro Province]] border. It was selected because was at center of lands controlled by [[Hojo clan]]. after [[Hojo war]] however the city fall under total control of [[Takenaka Okawa]]
