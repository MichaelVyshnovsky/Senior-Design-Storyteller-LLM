'''Azim Island''' was part of [[Al-Tarik Islands]] in the [[Golden Gulf]] and [[Crowded Sea]] within [[west Zakhara]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2bSWIc}}

==Geography==
The island was located northeast of the much larger [[Tarikat Island]]. Azim Island was longer than it was wide, measuring roughly {{SI|40|mi|k|long=yes}} long and {{SI|15|mi|k|long=yes}} wide. The entire island was surrounded by coral reefs. There were no settlements on Azim Island as of [[1367 DR]]{{AQ-time}}.
