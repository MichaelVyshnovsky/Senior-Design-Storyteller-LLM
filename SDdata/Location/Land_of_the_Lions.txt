The '''Land of the Lions''', also known as the '''Lake of Steam''' or the '''Arnaden''' after the [[Lake of Steam|lake of the same name]], was a region in [[south Faerûn]].

==History==
By [[1369 DR]], the [[Twisted Rune]] placed a magical gate in the area to allow for instantaneous movement to other locations.
