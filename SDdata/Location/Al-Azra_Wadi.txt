Al-AzraWadi, or the Blue Valley, was a village located on the Island of Gardens within the Genies Turban off the coast of northwest Zakhara in 1367 DR.
The village sat between two natural coves, providing beautiful scenery for the natives. It faced the Island of Firethorns, which could be seen across Chaos Bay.
The jungle surrounding the village was covered with beautiful blue orchids.
Al-AzraWadi was ruled by Alima bint Khayri.
Most of the villages inhabitants were former corsairs.
