'''Amry''' was a coastal settlement found along the eastern stretch of the [[Dragon Coast]], in the greater [[Eastern Heartlands]] of [[Interior Faerûn|central Faerûn]]. It was located just north of the [[Gulthmere Forest]], some ways west of [[Telpir]].

==Government==
The settlement of Amry was governed by a local council.

==Trade==
Along with the nearby settlement of Telpir, Amry enjoyed a good trading relationship with the [[Fellowship of the Purple Staff]].

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, Amry was regularly imperiled by the bands of pirates that operated in the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]].

==Inhabitants==
* Gulden Thorib, High Gnome of the Council of Amry.
