The Valley of the Eye was a dangerous valley that laid in the heart of the Deepwing Mountains, Vilhon Reach.
Waters of the Vilhon Reach ran into the hidden Valley of the Eye. It was rumored to be a home to a colony of beholders, however, eyewitness account was scant.
