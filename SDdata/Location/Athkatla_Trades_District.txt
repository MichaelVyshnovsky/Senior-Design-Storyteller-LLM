The '''Trades District''' of [[Athkatla]] was home to a great number of the city's merchants.

==Notable Locations==
* The [[Antlers of Andurl]]: a [[festhall]] and hunter's dining club connected with the [[Tessaerlee]] cult.{{Cite dragon/425/Eye on the Realms: That Which Slithers|55}}
* The [[Atrium]], a financial hub through which most of Athkatla's paperwork flowed.{{Cite dungeon/88/Thirds of Purloined Vellum|52}}
* Dunstul's Pump: this literal watering hole was a popular meeting place for Athkatlans.
* The [[Black Frog]]: a popular local tavern.
* The [[North Side]], a neighborhood populated by Athkatlans with plenty of wealth but little influence within the city.{{Cite dungeon/88/Thirds of Purloined Vellum|55}}
* The [[Waft]], a street lined with shops selling aromatic goods, from [[perfume]] to [[spice]].{{Cite dungeon/88/Thirds of Purloined Vellum|49}}
