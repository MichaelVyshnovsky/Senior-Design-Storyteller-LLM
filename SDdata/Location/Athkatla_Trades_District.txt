The Trades District of Athkatla was home to a great number of the citys merchants.
* The Antlers of Andurl a festhall and hunters dining club connected with the Tessaerlee cult.
* The Atrium, a financial hub through which most of Athkatlas paperwork flowed.
* Dunstuls Pump this literal watering hole was a popular meeting place for Athkatlans.
* The Black Frog a popular local tavern.
* The North Side, a neighborhood populated by Athkatlans with plenty of wealth but little influence within the city.
* The Waft, a street lined with shops selling aromatic goods, from perfume to spice.
