The '''Twilit Land''' was the name given to the coastal area of a stretch of the [[Sword Coast North]], lying between what would be in the 14th century [[DR]] [[Neverwinter]] and [[Waterdeep]].

This area of land began to be settled around the [[64 DR|Year of the Boisterous Orc, 64 DR]], by the [[Northmen]].<ref name=tGHotR-p61>
