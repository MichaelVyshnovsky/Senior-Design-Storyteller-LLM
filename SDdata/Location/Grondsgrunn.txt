'''Grondsgrunn''' was a region of the island of [[Norland]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]] that was controlled by [[giant]]s.{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Grondsgrunn was found in the southern region of Norland where the [[South Jotunhammer Mountains]] were located. As of the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, the giant-controlled territory ended at the unnamed pass that separated the South Jotunhammer Mountains from the [[North Jotunhammer Mountains]].
