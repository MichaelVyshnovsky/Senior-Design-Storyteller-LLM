'''Jasim Island''' was an island within the [[Golden Gulf]] and [[Crowded Sea]] of [[interior Zakhara]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2bSWIc}}

==Geography==
Jasim Island was located west of the southwest corner of the [[Cities of the Pantheon]] region of Zakhara. It was the northernmost island in [[Al-Faraq Islands]]. The island sat roughly {{SI|15|mi|k|long=yes}} west of the mainland near [[Fanus]]. The interior of the island was elevated above the perimeter, and the entire western coast was covered with coral reefs. Jasim Island was approximately {{SI|45|mi|k|long=yes}} long and {{SI|15|mi|k|long=yes}} wide. The island was uninhabited as of [[1367 DR]]{{AQ-time}}.
