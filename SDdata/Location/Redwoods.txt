The Redwoods was a forest of redwood trees in the foothills of the Storm Horns in Cormyr.
When Mystra was slain by Helm in the , her death caused widespread destruction in the area surrounding Castle Kilgrave and razed the Redwoods, creating the Helmlands. Over the following century the land slowly recovered and by the late 15 century DR the Redwoods had largely regenerated.
