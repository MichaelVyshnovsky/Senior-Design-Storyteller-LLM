Orcskull Rise was a ridge located along the Trade Way between Scornubel and Triel.
Orcskull Rise was a rocky ridge, where a mining trail crossed the Trade Way. It was surrounded by hills, along one of which the aforementioned mining trail stretched.
Orthil Voldovans caravan stopped by Orcskull Rise, using the ridge for protection, along its passage between Scornubel and Triel in late .
Belgon Bradaskor informed Sharantyr, when she sought information on how to catch up with the caravan, that Orcskull Rise was a likely stop for Orthil.
