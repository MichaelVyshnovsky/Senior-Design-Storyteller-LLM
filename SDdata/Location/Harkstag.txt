Harkstag was a village located northeast of Serpents Cowl during the 13 century DR.
The village of Harkstag was known to be the home of the Vigilant Riders, who protected the region against armies of orcs and goblinoids circa 1265 DR. During the mid-to-late 14 century DR, the ruins of the village served as a hideout for yuan-ti of Najara.
