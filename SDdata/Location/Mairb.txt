'''Mairb'''<ref group="note">The map included in [[The Shining South (1993)]] has this mislabeled as Maerb. was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Mairb was located in the northwest corner of Halruaa. The town lay in the shadow of [[Mount Talath]] and the [[Nathaghal Mountains]] southwest of the [[High Aluar]] river.
