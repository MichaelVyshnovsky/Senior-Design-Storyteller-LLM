The Meat Halwa was a lake in Raurin. After the pharaoh Amun-Re died, his curse dried it out the fight between Martek and Khalitharius filled its dry banks with molten glass, rendering it part of the Skysea.
The Meat Halwa was formed by the waters from five surrounding small hills. Much of the city of Medinat Muskawoon was built on them, and around the lake. The lake emptied into the river Athis, to flow northwestward and eventually reach the sea.
The city of Medinat Muskawoon was built around the Meat Halwa. After the Curse of Amun-Re took effect, the level of the underground waters, themselves fed by the River Athis, fell enough that the outflow from the surrounding hills was not enough to keep the water in place. Thus, the lake dried, even though it was not fed by the river Athis.
