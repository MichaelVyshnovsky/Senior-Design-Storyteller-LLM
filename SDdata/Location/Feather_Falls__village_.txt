Feather Falls was the formerly largest settlement in Featherdale, though by the late 14 century DR it was second to Blackfeather Bridge. It was located at the base of the waterfall after which it was named. The village served as the primary dock for boats that navigated down the River Ashaba.  
 Cholandrothipes Tower The tower of the now-deceased mage of Featherdale was full of secret passages, traps and magical guardians.
 Darwinns Trading Post A reasonable shop that made available several types of magic potions. 
 House of Morning A temple of Lathander run by Morninglord Jallian Horgontivar.
Featherdale was also home to the ruins of a temple dedicated to Leira.
