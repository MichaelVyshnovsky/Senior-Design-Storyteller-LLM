'''Umukek'''<ref group="note">[[Eric L. Boyd]] named this island after one of the cartographers of the ''[[Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas]]'', Linda Kekumu. was the northernmost named island in [[Faerûn]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1aSWlc}}

==Geography==
Umukek was located to the northwest of the [[Ice Peak]], and to the west of the [[Sea of Moving Ice]]. Thus, the island was located at a similar latitude to the [[Reghed Glacier]].

==History==
Between ''circa'' [[-1000 DR|−1000]] and [[-500 DR|−500 DR]], the [[Illuskan]]s of [[Ruathym]] colonized Umukek, and other islands, such as [[Tuern]].{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|24-25}}

==Inhabitants==
Though [[Northmen]] once colonized the isle, there were no known inhabitants of Umukek by the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].
