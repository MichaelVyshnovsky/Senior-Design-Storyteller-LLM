Amarune was a small island found directly off the east coast of the kingdom of Gnarhelm within the Moonshae Isles.
Amarune sat just  off shore from the mainland of Alaron where the unnamed river of the Lisloch emptied into the Sea of Swords. Amarune was small, covering just . There were no settlements of note found on the island.

A natural harbor, sheltered by a narrow opening through the mountains, was found on the islands western coast. Much of the islands exterior was framed with an unnamed mountain range, while the interior was covered with meadows and forests.
