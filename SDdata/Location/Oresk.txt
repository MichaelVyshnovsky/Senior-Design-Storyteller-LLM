Oresk was a small island in the Pirate Isles on the Sea of Fallen Stars. 
Oresk was found directly southeast of Bulta in the southeastern region of the isles.
There were several natural harbors found along the islands coastline.
Oresk was one of three pirate strongholds in the Pirate Isles. A young woman named Azla ruled the pirates here as of 1359 DR.
