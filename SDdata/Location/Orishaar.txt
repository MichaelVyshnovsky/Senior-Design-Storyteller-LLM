Orishaar was an ancient kingdom of moon elves created during the First Flowering. 
The kingdom was a trade partner of Aryvandaar, and this link was a good excuse for the Ilythiiri dark elves to attack the moon elves and take their land. Around , the kingdom was destroyed by the Ilythiir kingdom of dark elves during the second war of the Crown Wars. As time past, the ruins were engulfed by the Duskwood and the Shaar.
