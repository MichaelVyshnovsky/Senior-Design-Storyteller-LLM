'''Lhaoda''' was one of the flying enclaves of [[Netheril]].

Along with [[Tith Tilendrothael]], it was brought down from the sky and destroyed by the magic of the [[phaerimm]] in the {{YearlinkName|-427}}. The other enclaves established wards to guard against suffering the same fate.
