The '''Shrine of the Black Sword''' was a small crystal [[wikipedia:pyramid|pyramid]] located south of [[ThaolaÂ’'s Wineshop]] in [[Leuthilspar]] on the island of [[Evermeet]].

==Geography==
As a symbol of remembrance of all slain [[elves]], the pyramid was set in its own grounds and black marble columns lead up to it.

==Inhabitants==
The grounds were maintained by a single elf who lived in a nearby cottage. 

==History==
On [[Fallrite]] each year, [[Amlaruil Moonflower|Queen Amlaruil Moonflower]] used to visit the shrine with a group of nobles to remember the dead.
