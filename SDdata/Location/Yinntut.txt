Yinntut was a small village in the Alpuk region within the Great Glacier.
Yinntut was located in eastern Alpuk, between the Lugalpgotak Sea and the Keryjek Ridge.
The inhabitants of Yinntut were ukeu from Lilinuk and other locations in Alpuk. Despite having received permission to migrate, other villages were hesitant to pursue commerce and other relations with the villagers due to old prejudices.
