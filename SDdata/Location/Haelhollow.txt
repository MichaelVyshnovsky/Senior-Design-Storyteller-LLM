Haelhollow was a location along the Trade Way in the Western Heartlands.
The Trade Way crossed Haelhollow, leaving one of its sides across the road from the other. The Blackrocks around Haelhollow were a rocky area, and Haelhollow in particular boasted a number of stone formations akin to ramparts around it.
Haelhollow was an expected camp spot for large caravans according to Thoadrin circa . Late that year, the mage Aumlar Chaunthoun unleashed a spell over Haelhollow in an effort to damage Orthil Voldovans caravan in order to better capture Shandril Shessair it took Korthauvar Hammantles intervention to prevent the spell from actually killing her.
