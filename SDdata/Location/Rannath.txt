Rannath was a vale in Thay.
Rannath was a vale that ran from the northeast to the southwest across the Surague Escarpment northwest of Pyarados.
Matter from other planes had entered the zone in , which made it a likely location for many abyssal creatures such as bodaks or bebiliths.
The Red Wizards of Thay slew Magister Baerzlus Anagathiir in that location after a long and extreme spellbattle in , straining the Weave to its limit and in fact fraying it somewhat. Though Azuth repaired most of the damage, visits from beings from the Abyss became a normal feature of the zone well into .
