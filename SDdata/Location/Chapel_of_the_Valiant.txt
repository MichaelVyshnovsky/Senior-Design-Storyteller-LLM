The Chapel of the Valiant was a room in Castle Obarskyr.
In the aftermath of the massacre during the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the , the bodies of several lords, including Jassur Dragonwood, Lord Ambrival, Obraerl Foulweather, Montror Barelder, Lyrannus Tantorn, Lord Hardivyper, Lord Ravenhill, and Lord Briarbroke, were taken to the Chapel of the Valiant and identified. Per Glathra Barcantles orders, there were three War Wizards she wanted to have them studied by before any priests or kin were allowed access.
