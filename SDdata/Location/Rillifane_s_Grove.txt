'''Rillifane's Grove''' was a grove of old [[oak]] trees. It was used by [[moon elf|moon]] and [[sun elf]] priests to contact the [[Seldarine]]. It was also used for ceremonies, funerals, and marriages by [[wood elf|wood]] and [[wild elf|wild elves]] who traveled to [[Leuthilspar]].
