The Rubbans Tears was an archipelago and coral reef system found on the southern end of the island of Sahu in east Zakhara in 1367 DR.
The Rubbans Tears covered roughly  of the Crowded Sea of the southern coast of Sahu. The archipelago comprised ten islands of various sizes.
The coral reefs and waters separating the islands were infested with dangerous sharks.
