'''Naarlith''' was a small group of islands located in the [[Great Sea]] south of the [[Shining Lands]].

==Description==
Four islands made up Naarlith. They were located roughly 120 miles (190 km) due south from [[Estagund]]'s coastal city of [[Kolapur]].
