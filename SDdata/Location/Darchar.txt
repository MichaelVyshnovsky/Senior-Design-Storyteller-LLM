'''Darchar''' was an ancient [[giant]]-ruled kingdom in [[Faerûn]] during the [[Time of Giants]] of the [[Dawn Age]] ({{Yearlink|-30000}} to {{Yearlink|-24000}}).

==Geography==
Darchar was roughly located in the eastern portion of the region that later became known as [[Amn]], around the [[Cloud Peaks]] mountains north of [[Keltormir]].<ref group="note">Darchar is not discussed in the article from [[Dragon magazine 362|''Dragon'' #362]] that the map in the infobox originates from, but this geographic information can be inferred from the image.

==History==
Darchar was one of the giant kingdoms that warred with the ancient [[dragon]] kingdoms during the Dawn Age.

Darchar managed to survive beyond the [[Thousand Year War]], existing well into {{Yearlink|-11000}}.<ref group="note">While Darchar is not discussed in the ''Dragon'' #362 article, this historic information can be inferred from the map.
