Heldapan was the capital of Durpar located toward the center of the country.
Heldapan was situated on the eastern coast of the River Heldar near the Golden Water.
Large docks were suited to handle the countless arrival of ships carrying goods.
The Council of Merchants was based in Heldapan. Each of the Chakas of 31 also had some kind of establishment here.
Heldapan was known throughout the Shining South for its splendid markets where nearly anything imaginable could be purchased.
