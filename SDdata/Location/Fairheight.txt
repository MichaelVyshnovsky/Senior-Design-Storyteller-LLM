Fairheight was a county located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.
Fairheight was the smallest of Callidyrrs counties. It was located along the far northern border with Gnarhelm. The vast majority of the county was filled by the Fairheight Range and the mountains foothills.
Several mining towns, including Blackstone, White Rock, and Highrock, were found in Fairheight.
