The '''Undercity''', also known as the '''Eldercity''', was a ruined, underground city located in vast cavern under [[Baldur's Gate]]. 

==Description==
The remains of an older city that once stood there, the Undercity consisted mostly of broken-down shells of buildings haunted by [[undead]].

==Geography==
An entrance to the Undercity was found through the [[thieves' maze]] located underneath the connected safe houses used by Baldur's Gate's [[thieves' guild (Baldur's gate)|thieves' guild]].
