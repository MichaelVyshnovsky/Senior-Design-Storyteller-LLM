'''Wassen''' was a small farming town nestled along the [[Goliad River]] in the [[Barony of Morov]] in [[Damara]].

==Activities==
Travelers who preferred quieter surroundings often stopped in Wassen as opposed to nearby [[Morovar]] found just downstream.

==Inhabitants==
The inhabitants of Wassen were largely neutral on all political tidings, keeping themselves removed from most of the volatile situations plaguing the region.

A scant 250 people called Wassen home in [[1357 DR]].
