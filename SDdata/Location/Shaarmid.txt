Shaarmid was small town located on the plains of the Shaar.
Shaarmid sat near the center of the vast Shaar. It lay north of Lake Lhespen and south of the Firesteap Mountains.
Shaarmid was founded by Lapal survivors of the gnoll massacre of Lhesper in the .
Every four years, in the month of Eleasis, attendees of the Dance of the Shimmering Blades passed through Shaarmid on their way to the festival site.
