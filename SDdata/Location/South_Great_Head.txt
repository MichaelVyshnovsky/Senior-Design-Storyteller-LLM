The '''South Great Head''' was a peninsula located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The South Great Head formed the southern tip of Alaron. It jutted into the [[Sea of Swords]] and [[Strait of Alaron]] for approximately {{SI|40|mi|k|long=yes}}. Much of the peninsula was covered by the plains of [[Bregonshire]] with the [[Rushdown Wood]] covering a small section in the north.
