'''Dabab Island''' was part of [[Al-Qirmiza Islands]] in the [[Crowded Sea]] within [[west Zakhara]].

==Geography==
Dabab Island was by far the largest of the sixteen islands comprising the archipelago. It measured {{SI|60|mi|k|long=yes}} wide by {{SI|60|mi|k|long=yes}} long. Dabab Island was located in the northwest corner of al-Qirmiza Islands, roughly {{SI|180|mi|k|long=yes}} due south of [[Gana Bay]].

==Description==
The perimeter of Dabab Island was a mix of rocky and sandy beaches. A lightly forested landrise was found in the central area of the island.
