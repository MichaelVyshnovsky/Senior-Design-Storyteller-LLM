Dabab Island was part of Al-Qirmiza Islands in the Crowded Sea within west Zakhara.
Dabab Island was by far the largest of the sixteen islands comprising the archipelago. It measured  wide by  long. Dabab Island was located in the northwest corner of al-Qirmiza Islands, roughly  due south of Gana Bay.
The perimeter of Dabab Island was a mix of rocky and sandy beaches. A lightly forested landrise was found in the central area of the island.
