The '''Town of Shadowdale''' was an unwalled farming community situated within [[Shadowdale]], in the [[Dalelands]] and was the largest population center of the dale. 

==Geography==
It straddled the [[North Ride]] where it intersected [[Shadowdale Trail]] and crossed the [[River Ashaba]].
