The '''Battering Wave''' was a temple of [[Umberlee]] in the city of [[Tsurlagol]] in [[the Vast]].

By [[1367 DR]], the temple was led by [[Thogonia Grim]], who was still in charge by [[1370 DR]]. She was served by six priests of fanatical beliefs.
