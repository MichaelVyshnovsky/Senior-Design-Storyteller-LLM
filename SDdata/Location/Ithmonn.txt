'''County Ithmonn''' was an agricultural region in central [[Tethyr]].<ref name=LoI1:T-p85/>

==Geography==
Ithmonn was located in the [[Crown Lands]] north of the [[River Ith]] and bordering the [[Purple Marches]] in the west.<ref name=LoI1:T-map>

The county was the agricultural center of Tethyr and was blanketed with crops of all varieties and farms containing all manner of livestock.<ref name=LoI1:T-p85/>

==Government==
The Count of Ithmonn in [[1370 DR]] was [[Malcor Grannox]], who was also the Law Chancellor of Tethyr. He ruled from [[Loranse]], a manor just west of [[Darromar]] along the river.<ref name=LoI1:T-p85/>
