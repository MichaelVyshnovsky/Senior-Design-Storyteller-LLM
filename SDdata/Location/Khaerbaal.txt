Khaerbaal was a city located in Halruaa along the Great Sea.
Located on the southern coast of Halruaa, Khaerbaal was sheltered from pirates and rough ocean currents by the Bay of Taertal.
Khaerbaal was ruled by the wizard Grozalum.
This city served as one of two bases for Halruaas navy, housing 50 ships and 20 skyships.
Most of the population of Khaerbaal were preoccupied with shipping and trading. The naval base was located on the nearby isle of Rulasuu.
