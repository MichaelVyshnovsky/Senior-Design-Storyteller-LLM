'''Borth''' was a small coastal town located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Borth was found along the coast of the [[Strait of Alaron]] in the northeastern region of the kingdom of [[Corwell]] in southern Gwynneth. Borth sat just north of [[Kingsbay]].
