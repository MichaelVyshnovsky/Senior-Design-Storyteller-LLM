The Violet Spire was the tower of Yaravindar Ipurnos in the Glittering Spires in the Hordelands. He used it until at least .
The minaret-type tower was slender and made of purple granite.
The tower had no doors or stairs, with all entry being a matter of keyed portals. A small host of surviving Imaskari constructs and summoned elementals staffed and defended it.
The magician Yaravindar Ipurnos raised this tower after the destruction of Inupras. He continued to dwell in the tower until at least .
