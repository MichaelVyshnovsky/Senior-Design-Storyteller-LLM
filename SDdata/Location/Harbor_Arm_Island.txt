Harbor Arm Island was an "island" that sat in the mouth of the River Mirar in the city of Luskan.
The island was a tall, rocky spire that served to shelter Whitesails Harbor against waves and storms from the south. It was barren and uninhabited.
Harbor Arm Island was the northernmost of the five islands that sat in the mouth of the River Mirar (the others being Blood Island, Closeguard Island, Cutlass Island, and Fang Island).
In the , the island was seized by Deudermont and his allies as part of their battle to drive Arklem Greeth and the Arcane Brotherhood out of the city.
