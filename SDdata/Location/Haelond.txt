'''Haelond''' was an abandoned mining outpost located in the mountains of [[Gontal]].{{Cite dragon/366/Gontal|52}}

== History ==
Haelond was a mining outpost run by the [[Elkannath (family)|Elkannath]] family of the [[Twelvefold]].

In [[1469 DR]], Haelond was abandoned when it was overrun by mysterious monsters that swarmed out from the [[Abeiran Underdark|Underdark]].
