The '''Shaareach''' was a small plain found in the western [[Shaar]] near the border of [[Lapaliiya]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Geography==
The open plains of the Shaareach were surrounded by the [[Misty Vale (forest)|Misty Vale]] to the west, the [[Shaareach Forest]] to the south, the [[Wormbones]] to the north, and the [[Rathgaunt Hills]] to the east. The [[River Talar]] flowed through the southern region of the plains and the [[River Shaar]] flowed in from the north.
