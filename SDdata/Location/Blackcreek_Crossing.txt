'''Blackcreek Crossing''' was a small family landholding in [[Shadowdale]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Geography==
The place was a few tens of miles away from [[Freedale]], and not that much further from the town of [[Shadowdale]].

==Government==
Blackcreek Crossing was largely independent.

==History==
As of the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century, Blackcreek Crossing was one of many small landholdings named after the family inhabiting it.

==Inhabitants==
The holding was named after its local family, presumably [[Blackcreek]].
