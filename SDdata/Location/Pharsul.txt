'''Pharsul''' was a coastal city located in the nation of [[Durpar]] in the [[Shining Lands]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dSWIc}}

==Geography==
The city was located on the northern coast of the [[Golden Water]] in eastern Durpar southeast of the capital at [[Heldapan]].

==Activities==
Pharsul's position on the Golden Water made it a prime destination for trade coming to and from nearby [[Ulgarth]].
