The Long Beach was a stretch of sandy coastland along northwestern Threskel, where the land met the waters of the Sea of Fallen Stars.
A number of small fisher communities sprang up across the beach throughout the years. Its waters rich with great populations of fish.
As of the late 14 century DR, few human settlements remained along the Long Beach. Fishing boats faced the risk of being swallowed up the Great Bone Wyrms dragon servitors as they gulped down great groups of aquatic animals in the sea.
