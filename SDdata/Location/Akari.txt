'''Akari''' was the largest of the [[Prioto Islands]], a small group of islands in the [[Yellow Sea]] south of [[Kozakura]].

==History==
They were resettled by [[Shou Lung]] in [[1357 DR]]. Akari was the site of the main [[Shou]] colony.
