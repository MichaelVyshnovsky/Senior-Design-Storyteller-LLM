Torriage Wood was a small forest located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.
Torriage Wood was located on the eastern coast of central Alaron where the Swanmay River met the Sea of Swords. The forest was located on the rivers north bank. The western edge of the forest bordered the Central Plains. It covered roughly  of land.
The forest sheltered a single Moonwell.
