The '''Larrauden''' was a river in [[Faerûn]].

==Description==
The Larrauden had frost along its banks on some days, presumably during winter.

==Geography==
[[Buckralam's Starn]] could be found in the same vale where the Larrauden crossed the [[Gar's Road]].
