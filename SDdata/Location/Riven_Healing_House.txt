The '''Riven Healing House''' was a temple of [[Ilmater]] found in the small town of [[Manywaters]] in the [[Border Kingdoms]].

==Description==
Riven Healing House was a temple with high pillars. Behind the temple was a series of interconnected buildings and the [[Riven Rock Pool]], all surrounded by a wall. A small orchard and a garden was also found behind the temple. A mansion that was usually unoccupied was rented out by the priests to visitors.
