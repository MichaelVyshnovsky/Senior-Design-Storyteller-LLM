'''Huddagh''' was a settlement in [[Sembia]] that lay along the [[Way of the Manticore]].{{Cite interactive atlas|?}}

==Description==
Huddagh was well known for its unique purple mineral water wells that seemingly never failed. The water was used by several local churches in creation of ''[[holy water]]''. Among other trades, Huddagh was home of many renowned jewelers and potters.

==Geography==
Huddagh lay east of [[Daerlun]] and [[Skultan]] and west of [[Mulhessen]] along the Way of the Manticore. It was at the intersection of [[Dornsar's Ride]] toward [[Kulta]].

==Notable Locations==
*[[Old Hazard Hill Farm]], a monastic retreat of the [[Tymoran]] faith, located to the north of Huddagh.

==Notable Inhabitants==
*[[Eldrisel Tylosar]], a [[human]] [[cleric]] of [[Shaundakul]] ''circa'' [[1370 DR]].
