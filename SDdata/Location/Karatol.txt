'''Karatol''' was a small island located in the [[Golden Water]] of the [[Shining Lands]] in [[1367 DR]].

==Description==
This tiny island was made entirely of beautiful purple coral. Much of the fabulous coral jewelry made in [[Durpar]] was harvested here.
