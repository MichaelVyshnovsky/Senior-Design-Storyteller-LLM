The '''Grove of Corellon''' (also '''Corellon's Grove''') was a place containing the greatest of [[Evermeet|Evermeet's]] temples to the deity [[Corellon Larethian]], as well as shrines to many of the other [[Seldarine]]. 

==Geography==
The grove was located south of the village of [[Elion]], in the middle northern part of the island.
