'''Elchul'''<ref group="note">This is mislabeled as Eichul on page 10 of [[The Shining South (1993)]]. was a small farming village located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Elchul was located on the plains in eastern Halruaa north of the [[River Maeru]]. The town lay near the halfway point between [[Lake Halruaa]] in the west and [[Lake Maeru]] in the east.

==Notable Locations==
The [[Astral Inn]] was located in Elchul.<ref name="tSS-p10>
