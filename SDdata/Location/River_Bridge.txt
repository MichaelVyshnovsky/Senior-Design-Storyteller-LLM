The '''River Bridge''' spanned the [[River Thunn]] within the city of [[Westgate]].

==Location==
The River Bridge was found in the southeast section of the city. It connected the main portion of Westgate on the River Thunn's western bank with the smaller section of the city found along the eastern bank via the [[Silverpiece Way]] and [[Rivergate Way]], respectively.
