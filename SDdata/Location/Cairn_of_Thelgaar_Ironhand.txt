The '''cairn of Thelgaar Ironhand''' was the burial site of Jarl [[Thelgaar Ironhand]] of [[Oman]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|11}} 

==Description==
The burial site was located in the [[Old Stone Wood]] on the western side of [[Oman's Isle]] near the village of [[Trondhelm]] where Thelgaar was born.

==Rumors & Legends==
Local legend stated that Jarl Thelgaar was buried with his wealth, which included his [[greatsword]] ''[[Ironhand's Reckoning]]'', an enchanted [[longship]] that could never sink, and powerful magical [[armor]]. These riches were believed to be guarded by a variety of traps and primal spirits.
