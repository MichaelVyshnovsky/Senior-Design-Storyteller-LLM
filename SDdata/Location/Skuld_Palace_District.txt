==Location==
The Palace District was situated in the northeast corner of Skuld. It was bordered on the west by the [[Alamber Sea]]. 

==Description==
Aside from the nearby [[Skuld/City of the Gods|City of the Gods]], the Palace District was lavish and orderly when compared to the rest of the cappital. This district was only accessible via a single gate found on the south wall.
