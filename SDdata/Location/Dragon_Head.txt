'''Dragon Head''' was a rocky peninsula located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Dragon Head jutted from the southern shore of [[Gwynneth]] into the [[Sea of Swords]] for approximately {{SI|40|mi|k|long=yes}}. The vast majority of the peninsula was composed of rocky mountainous terrain. The region of the peninsula closest to the mainland was covered by part of the [[Llyrath Forest]].
