'''Hayyir''' was a small town found within [[interior Zakhara]] and the [[Cities of the Heart]].

==Geography==
Hayyir was settled in the fertile river valley of an unnamed river. It was found along the eastern banks of the North Quarter of [[Suq Bay]] due east of [[Roq Island]] approximately {{SI|100|mi|long=yes}} north of [[Wasat]].
