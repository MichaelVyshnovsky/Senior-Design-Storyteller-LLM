'''Summerfields''' was a farm belonging to the [[Klaeverdar]] clan as of the late mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The farm was east of [[Amnwater]].

==History==
[[Olbrinhannar Klaeverdar]] headed one of the [[Klaeverdar]] branches from that farm. One of the potential successors to [[Gueladdeth Klaeverdar]] in {{yearlink|1370}}, [[Meldrethe Klaeverdar]], lived there.
