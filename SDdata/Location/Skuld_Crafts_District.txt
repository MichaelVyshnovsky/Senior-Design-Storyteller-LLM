The '''Crafts District''' was a walled section of the capital city of [[Skuld]] in [[Mulhorand]] during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|490 Skuld}}

==Location==
The Crafts District was located in the east section of Skuld. 

==Description==
Walls surrounded the Crafts District. A pair of gates allowed access to this area of Skuld–one on the western wall allowing access to the [[Skuld/Central Ward|Central Ward]], and another on the southern wall near the [[East Gate (Skuld)|East Gate]].
