'''Bray''' was a small village located on the isle of [[Moray]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The village was located on the northeastern coast of Moray along the end of the [[Strait of the Leviathan]] where it met the [[Sea of Moonshae]]. The lands surrounding Bray were large open plains with the exception of an unnamed forest located just south of the village.
