'''Foresters' Hall''' was a long, low meeting hall used by the [[Foresters]]: an elite part of the [[Aglarond]]an army. The hall was decorated in the style of the [[Yuir]] elves, with runes and artifacts. It is used for long celebrations, feasting and drinking.
