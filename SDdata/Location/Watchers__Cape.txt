Watchers Cape was a protrusion of land into the Sea of Fallen Stars located in the country of Threskel in the Old Empires. 
Watchers Cape sat on the eastern side of the mouth of the Bay of Chessenta in northwest Threskel.
The Drakelight, a massive lighthouse garrisoned by soliders form Soorenar, Luthcheq, and Mordulkin, sat along the the tip of Watchers Cape.
