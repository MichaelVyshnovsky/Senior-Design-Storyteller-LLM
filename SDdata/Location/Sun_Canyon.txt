'''Sun Canyon''' was a canyon in the [[Pasocada Basin]] in [[Maztica]]. It was carved by the [[Dunobo Springs|Danozi Wash]], and was an important location for the [[Azuposi]], since it was the heart of the Azuposi nation. In addition, the city of [[Michaca]] was located in it.
