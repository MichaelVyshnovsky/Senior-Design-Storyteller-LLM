The '''Glade''' was an area of trees in [[Leuthilspar]] on the island of [[Evermeet]].

==Geography==
The location contained a small stream and a lake, and was where [[sylvan]] races like [[centaur]]s and [[faun]]s often chose to stay while visiting. It was the most peaceful place in the city.
