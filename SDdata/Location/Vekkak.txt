'''Vekkak''' was an [[Iulutiun]] village on the shore of [[Risuak Lake|Lake Risuak]]. Its claim to fame was that its [[Ulutiun dictionary#I|''iquemelum'']] (council of elders) was particularly adept at predicting the weather.

==Geography==
Vekkak was found in [[Alpuk]], northwest of the [[Lugalpgotak Sea]] and north of the village of [[Gotokok]].
