Harimmon was one of the river ports along the coast of the Sword River in the Sword Lands, Laerakond.
Harimmon was built around an old, semi-ruined castle.
As of 1479 DR, Harimmon was a decandent town crowded with shady dealers. The town seemed to attract a lot of sellswords, rumors, and stolen goods.
