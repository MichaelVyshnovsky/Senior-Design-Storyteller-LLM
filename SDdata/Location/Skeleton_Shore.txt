The '''Skeleton Shore''' was a coast line along the [[Dragonmere]] in the [[Dragon Coast]].{{Template:Cite book/The Veiled Dragon/Paperback (1996)|1)}}{{Cite dragon/365/Backdrop: Cormyr|45}}

==Geography==
The shore ran from [[Teziir]] in the west all the way to [[Westgate]].

===Geographical Features===
The most notable feature of the Skeleton Shore were the Cliffs of Karthaut, This seemingly innocuous tower was the domain of [[Aeroth]], a Wearer of Purple for the [[Cult of the Dragon]], and the lair of the [[green dragon|green]] [[wyrmling]] [[Eldrisithain]].
