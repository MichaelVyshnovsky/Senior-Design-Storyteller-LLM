'''Rangfjell '''(meaning "'''Honor Mountain'''" in the [[giant language]]) was an ancient [[giant]]-ruled kingdom in [[Faerûn]] during the [[Time of Giants]] of the [[Dawn Age]] ({{Yearlink|-30000}} to {{Yearlink|-24000}}).

==History==
Rangfjell was one of the giant kingdoms that warred with the ancient [[dragon]] kingdoms during the Dawn Age.
