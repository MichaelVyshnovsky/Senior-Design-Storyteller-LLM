'''Guge''' was an ancient kingdom situated on the [[Katakoro Plateau]].

==Geography==
At the height of its power, the kingdom of Guge encompassed the entirety of the Katakoro Plateau, stretching as far north as the [[Hollow Crown Mountains]].
