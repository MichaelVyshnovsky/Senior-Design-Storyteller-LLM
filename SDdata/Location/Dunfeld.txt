'''Dunfeld''' was a small town located in the nation of [[Dambrath]] in the [[Shining South]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dSWIc}}

==Geography==
Dunfeld was located in eastern Dambrath along the [[Trader's Way]]. The town sat just west of the [[Southern Lluirwood]] and north of the [[River Ammath]].
