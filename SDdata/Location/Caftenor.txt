Caftenor was an island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.
Caftenor was the southernmost island within the Korinn Archipelago, sitting just  off the coast of northern Gnarhelm near the Lake of the Leech. Several small unnamed islands were found just off the islands coast. The village of Caftenor was located near center of the island on the eastern coast.
A large population of halflings made their home on Caftenor.
