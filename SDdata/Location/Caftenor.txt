'''Caftenor''' was an island within the [[Korinn Archipelago]] in the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: Moonshae Isles|11}}

==Geography==
Caftenor was the southernmost island within the Korinn Archipelago, sitting just {{SI|20|mi|k|long=yes}} off the coast of northern [[Gnarhelm]] near the [[Lake of the Leech]]. Several small unnamed islands were found just off the island's coast. The village of [[Caftenor (town)|Caftenor]] was located near center of the island on the eastern coast.

==Inhabitants==
A large population of [[halfling]]s made their home on Caftenor.
