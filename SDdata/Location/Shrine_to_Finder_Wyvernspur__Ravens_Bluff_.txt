This shrine to the [[Faerûnian pantheon|Faerûnian deity]] [[Finder Wyvernspur]] was a statue in [[Ravens Bluff]] constructed sometime in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
This shrine was located in the small outdoor garden of the [[Tymora]]n temple complex [[Hall of Luck (Ravens Bluff)|Hall of Luck]], in the [[Ravens Bluff/Holyhouses|Holyhouses]] neighborhood of the [[Ravens Bluff/Temple District|Temple District]] of Ravens Bluff.

==History==
An individual known as [[Joel|Joel the Rebel Bard]] was said to have constructed this statuary during a visit to the city of Ravens Bluff.

==Inhabitants==
A local Ravenaar [[bard]] tended to this shrine, serving as its Shrine Keeper.
