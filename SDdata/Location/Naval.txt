'''Naval''' was a small town located within the [[Cities of the Ancients]] region of [[east Zakhara]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Geography==
The town was situated along the eastern banks of [[Al-Iltifat River]] approximately {{SI|200|mi|k|long=yes}} downstream from where the river originated in the fringes of the [[Haunted Lands]].
