'''Xiang Temple''' was a [[monastery]] for [[monk]]s in the city of [[Telflamm]] in the nation of [[Thesk]]. It taught its initiates to fight with both weapons and their own bodies.{{cite book/Forgotten Realms Campaign Guide|183}}

==Appearance==
The Xiang Temple was brightly painted on the outside, while the inside was covered with incense smoke.

==Services==
The temple was a sanctuary for the common person. The martial art known as [[Xiang Do]] was taught here.

==History==
By {{yearlink|1479}}, Xiang's monks stayed out of the politics of Telflamm, but no faction wanted to provoke them.
