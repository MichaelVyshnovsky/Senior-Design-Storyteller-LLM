'''Doubloon''' was a bandit hideout that became flying city.

==Description==
Doubloon was filled with rogues that were able to counterfeit [[gold]] coins.

==History==
The city disappeared entirely in 3417 NY ({{Yearlink|-442}}) and has not been seen since.
