The ancient city of '''Teshyll''' was a center for trade in the old [[Calim Empire]]. It was best known for the famous [[Harem Palace]] of [[Calim]].<ref name=EotSS-p79/>

==Geography==
The ruins of the city were found in the [[Calim Desert]] near the [[Trade Way]] about mid-way from [[Calimport]] to [[Memnon]] in the region known as the [[Teshyllal Wastes]].<ref name=EotSS-p79/>

==Trade==
In its day, before the [[First Era of Skyfire]], the city of Teshyll served as a central caravan stop for traders and a massive marketplace for the farmers from around the fertile plains of the [[Teshyllal Wastes|Teshyllal Fields]]. The city imported goods from the other territories within the [[genie]] empire and exported farm produce.<ref name=EotSS-p79/>

==History==
The city of Teshyll was destroyed by the armies of [[Memnon (efreeti)|Memnon]] sometime during the Era of Skyfire. By [[1370 DR]], only the corners of a few building remained intact.<ref name=EotSS-p79/>

==Notable Locations==
The architectural marvel, the [[Harem Palace]], was [[Calim]]'s primary [[Alzhedo dictionary#J|''jhasinnadah '']], containing thousands of slaves.<ref name=EotSS-p79/>
