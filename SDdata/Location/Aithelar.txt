'''Aithelar''' was a small coastal town located in the kingdom of [[Callidyrr]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Aithelar was found in northern Callidyrr in the county of [[Stagford]] on Alaron's eastern coast with the [[Sea of Moonshae]]. The village sat just west of the ominous [[Dernall Forest]] and north of the [[Drowned Forest]].
