'''Hulm''' was a small village located in [[west Zakhara]] in {{Yearlink|1367}}{{AQ-time}}.{{Cite interactive atlas|B2bSWIc}}

==Geography==
Hulm was located in the valley known as the [[Pit of the Ghuls]] within the foothills of a branch of the [[Mountains of the Lizard's Tongue]]. The village sat directly south of the [[Lake of the Ghuls]], and east of the neighboring village of [[Quabah]].
