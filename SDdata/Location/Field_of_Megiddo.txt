The '''Field of Megiddo''',{{Cite organized play/LC/Another Serious Matter|35}} was a plain on the [[Avernus]] layer of [[Baator]]. It was one of the many battlefields of the [[Blood War]].{{Cite organized play/LC/Another Serious Matter|25}}

==Geography==
This battlefield was located somewhere close to [[Ankhwugaht|Ankhwugaht Prime]], a small [[divine realm]] in Avernus belonging to the [[Mulhorandi pantheon|Mulhorandi god]] [[Set]].<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/Another Serious Matter|46}}

==History==
Sometime in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], a group of [[adventurer]]s from [[Ravens Bluff]] found themselves in the plane of Baator, at a spot overlooking the Field of Megiddo,{{Cite organized play/LC/Another Serious Matter|9,23-24}} At that time, a group of [[blue dragon]]s were in flight from Megiddo, having taken a severe beating from [[tanar'ri]], and they quickly sprung an attack upon the Ravenians.<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/Another Serious Matter|13,28}} When the adventurers eventually managed to return to the [[Prime Material plane]], they startled their benefactor [[Simon Regulus]], who had just managed to find the Field of Megiddo in an ornate [[mirror]] he was [[scrying]] with.

==Rumors & Legends==
According to some legends, the Field of Megiddo was destined to one day be the site of the final battle of the Blood War.
