The '''Mulgate''' was the easternmost of the two gates leading into the [[Southbank]] of [[Silverymoon]], (the other being [[Blacklar Gate]].) Within the city, the [[Moonway]] began at the Mulgate on its journey toward the [[Moonbridge]].
