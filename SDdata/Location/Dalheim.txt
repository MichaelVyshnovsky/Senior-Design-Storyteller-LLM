'''Dalheim''' was a [[Northlander]] settlement on the island of [[Llorgh]] in [[Norheim]] in the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: Moonshae Isles|11}}

==Geography==
The village was located near the center of Llorgh on [[Northwind Strait]] on the island's eastern shore. Dalheim was the only settlement on the island.
