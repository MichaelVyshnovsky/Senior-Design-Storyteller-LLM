'''Mazti''' was a secluded [[Payit]]lan town on the shores of the [[Gulf of Cordell]].

==Description==
Mazti was a minor town with population somewhere between 5,000 and 15,000 [[Maztican]]s. The town stood to the west of [[Ulatos]], encircled by the thick [[Bomak Jungle]]. Mazti's closest western neighbor was the [[Pezelac]] town of [[Palenqi]].
