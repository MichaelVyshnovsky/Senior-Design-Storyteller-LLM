'''Norland''' was an island in the [[Moonshae Isles]] that was inhabited primarily by [[Northlander]]s.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: Moonshae Isles|10-11}}

==Government==
At one time [[House Kendrick]] exerted nominal control over the island but as of [[1479 DR]] the isle was ruled by the warrior maiden known as the [[Hergatha|Storm Maiden]].

==Locations==
The largest settlement on the isle, [[Rogarsheim]], was nominally ruled by Jarl [[Rault|Rault the Wise]].

==Inhabitants==
The population was a mix of Northlanders, [[Ffolk]], and [[dwarves]].

==Geography==
The [[South Jotunhammer Mountains]] was a mountain range home to many [[giant]]s, mostly [[firbolg]]s.
