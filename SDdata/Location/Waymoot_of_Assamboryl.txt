The waymoot of Assamboryl was a landmark in Cormanthor.
It was approximately two days north of Druindars Rock and one day south of Six Thorns.
In , Galan Goadulphyn left for the waymoot of Assamboryl after being injured from stray spells from the mage duel between Delmuth Echorn and Elminster in Druindars rock. He expected the trip to take two days.
