'''Jazirat al-Gawwar''' was an island in [[Nada al-Hazan]] in the [[Crowded Sea]].<ref name=GV:NH-p7>

==Rumors & Legends==
A variety of rumors surrounded the island. Some believed it was overrun with [[evil]] creatures, others that it was a secret fortress of the [[Brotherhood of the True Flame]] or a stronghold of the [[holy slayer]]s sect known as [[Wordless Ones]]. Some believed that it was granted to [[genie]]s by the First Caliph in return for the secrets of making ships. And finally, some claimed that it was the last outpost of the fallen empires of [[Nog]] and [[Kadar]].<ref name=GV:NH-p7 />
