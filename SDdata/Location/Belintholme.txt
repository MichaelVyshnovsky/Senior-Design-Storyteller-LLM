'''Belintholme''' was a small island located off of the southeastern coast of [[Evermeet]] on the [[Trackless Sea]].{{Cite web/Portal-Master Portals: The Elven Unification Portal}}

==Geography==
The island sat roughly {{SI|100|mi|k|long=yes}} southeast off of the southern tip of Evermeet.

==Description==
Belintholme was a forested island that was roughly circular in shape. It was the largest of the islands found off the nearby coast of Evermeet. Several smaller unnamed islands sat off of Belintholme's northeast coast.
