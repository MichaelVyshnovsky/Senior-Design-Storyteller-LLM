'''Lahib Island''' was found on the [[Great Sea]] off the coast of [[north Zakhara]].

==Geography==
The island measured roughly {{SI|30|mi|k|long=yes}} in length and {{SI|20|mi|k|long=yes}} in width. It sat off the coast within eyesight across from the small town of [[Mina]]. The center of the island was covered in dense forest with surrounding brushland.
