Chawdik was a poor island that was part of the Korinn Archipelago in the Sea of Swords
Chawdik was a small and humble island located to the west of its much larger neighbor, the isle of Ventris. The islands large gnomish population resided on the islands southern coast.
*Gofin, or Gofus, a gnomish fisher from the isle of Chawdik in the 14 century DR.
