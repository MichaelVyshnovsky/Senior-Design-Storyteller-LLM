The '''North Center''' was a ward of [[Huzuz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name="CoD.M>

==Activities==
Found in the [[Northwest District]], this ward controlled all trade passing through the [[River Gate]] and the [[Gate of the Ghost]]. Most of the caravans entering Huzuz passed through the North Center.

==Inhabitants==
A [[Tasked genie|tasked]] [[administrator genie]] named [[Sandara al-Sahil]] ran the North Center. She was aided by a large contingent of [[mamluk]]s of the [[Valiant Mamluk Society|Valiant]].
