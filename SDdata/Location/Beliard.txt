Beliard was a small trading village that was home to, as well as a meeting place for, the many cattle ranchers that drove their herds across the nearby hills. 
It was located at the crossroads where the Stone Trail intersected the Dessarin Road, just a bit a few miles east of Stone Bridge.
Sometime around the , four merchants went missing in Beliard. The cause of this disappearance was a doppelganger that had assumed the form of one of Blasko Halamars young apprentices, Hagarl.
Some time after the Ordning was abolished, around the , groups of hill giants began to steal livestock from Beliards animal pens.
