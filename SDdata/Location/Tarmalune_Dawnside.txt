'''Dawnside''' was one of the wards of [[Tarmalune]].{{Cite dragon/376/Tarmalune and the Windrise Ports|46}} It was located to the northwest of the city.{{Cite dragon/372/Backdrop: Tarmalune|56}}

==Geography==
This ward was crowded with ostentatiously decorated mansions and ornate coaches, and any building not a mansion was likely to be a club of some sort. Buildings in Dawnside were constantly demolished and rebuilt into more fashionable shapes.

==Inhabitants==
Dawnside was home to newly rich families and individuals, and was the most popular ward of Tarmalune among [[adventurer]]s.
