'''Protain''' was a small town located in the nation of [[Durpar]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dSWIc}}

==Geography==
Protain was located in eastern Durpar along the southern fringes of the [[Scarlet Jungle]]. The town sat due east of the nearby [[River Lesh]].
