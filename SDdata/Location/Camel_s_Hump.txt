The '''Camel's Hump''' was a large island found in the [[Corsair Domains]] of [[northwest Zakhara]] around [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}} The island was part of an archipelago called the [[Desert's Virtues]].

This island was named for its gradual elevation which rose to a peak that resembled a [[camel]]'s hump.

==Geography==
The Camel's Hump was located approximately {{SI|60|mi|k|long=yes}} due west of the [[Island of Firethorns]]. The island itself measured {{SI|100|mi|k|long=yes}} in length and {{SI|45|mi|k|long=yes}} in width. An elevated northeast to southwest swath of arid land split the forested coasts of the island. The town of [[Ravenwing]] sat on the island's western coast.
