Lhair was a small town located in the nation of Halruaa during the 14 century DR.
Lhair was located along the River Lhair in the shadow of the Lhairghal Mountains where it emptied into the Great Sea on the western half of the Bay of Taertal in southwest Halruaa. It was the only settlement found along the river and the westernmost settlement in Halruaa.
The House of the High One Ascendant, a temple of Azuth, was located in the mountains near Lhair.
