Imnescar was a small town in southwest Amn.
Imnescar was considered an agricultural center for the nations south and was famous for its fruits. These included oranges, tangerines, grapes, dates, lemons, limes, avocados, and artichokes.
Imnescar was also known to be a popular waystation for those traveling along the Trade Way.
In the , Prince Imnel Torlath and his armies fought ogres on the Small Teeth, restoring trade between Crimmor and Murann. The site of the battle was known as Imnels Scar, and would later became the town of Imnescar.
