'''Imnescar''' was a small town in southwest [[Amn]].

==Trade==
Imnescar was considered an agricultural center for the nation's south and was famous for its fruits. These included [[orange]]s, [[tangerine]]s, [[grape]]s, [[date]]s, [[lemon]]s, [[lime]]s, avocados, and [[artichoke]]s.

Imnescar was also known to be a popular waystation for those traveling along the [[Trade Way]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|479}}, Prince [[Imnel Torlath]] and his armies fought ogres on the [[Small Teeth]], restoring trade between [[Crimmor]] and [[Murann]]. The site of the battle was known as ''Imnel's Scar'', and would later became the town of Imnescar.
