'''White Rock''' was a small mining town located in the kingdom of [[Callidyrr]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
White Rock was found at the northern end of the eastern branch of the [[High King's Road]] on the northern side of [[Whitefish Bay]]. The town was located in central [[Fairheight]] on the southern slopes of the [[Fairheight Range]].
