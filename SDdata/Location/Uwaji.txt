Uwaji was the shogunate capital of Wa, where the shogun lived and met. It was one of the largest and most important cities of Kara-Tur. It was on the island of Tsukishima, bordered on the west by the Ikuyu Mountains. The busy Tawata Road linked Uwaji with Rukimbaru.
 Bridge of Whispers  This magical bridge echoed back words that were spoken to it.
 Dyogo  This suburb of Uwaji fell into the sea during a great earthquake.
 Uwaji Castle  The fortress of the shogun was beautiful and massive in size.
 Palace of Imperial Prosperity  The mansion of the shogun was located within Uwaji Castles grounds.
