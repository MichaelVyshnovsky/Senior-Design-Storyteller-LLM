The Loyal Maids Hall was a room in Castle Obarskyr.
The hall was well lit.
In , there were always two Purple Dragons stationed in the Loyal Maids Hall. In the aftermath of the massacre during the Council of the Dragon in late Mirtul of the , the War Wizard Surgol Velard called upon those two soldiers to help him move the mask dancer Amarune Whitewave, whom he had incapacitated with a wand of sleep, believing her too suspicious. Those soldiers returned to their post just in time to miss Storm Silverhand and Mirt.
