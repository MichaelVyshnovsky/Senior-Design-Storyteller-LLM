'''Harab''', also known as the '''Isle of War''', was a large island of the [[Crowded Sea]] region south of the [[Cities of the Pantheon|Pantheist League]]. It did not possess any unified government.<ref>

==Trade==
Harab exported [[iron]] and [[silver]], feathers, teak, [[spices]] and musk.
