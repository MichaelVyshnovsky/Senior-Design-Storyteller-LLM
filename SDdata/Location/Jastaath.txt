'''Jastaath''' was a petty kingdom located in ancient [[Narfell]] that predated the [[Narfell#History|Empire of Narfell]]. Ancient texts claimed that it was the first [[Nar]] kingdom to practice [[Nar demonbinder|demonbinding]].

==Location==
The kingdom was centered on [[Jastaath Castle]], located on [[Mount Nar]].

==History==
Jastaath was ruled by priest-kings who were said to be the first of the Nars to parley with [[demon]]s. They were said to have been powerful enough to control the weather.

The kingdom disappeared and their capital was abandoned for unknown reasons,
