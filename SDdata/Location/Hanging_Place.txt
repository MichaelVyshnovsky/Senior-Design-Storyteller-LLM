The Hanging Place was an open-aired section of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the .
The Hanging Place was located near the center of Almorel directly in front of the Raumkremyl along the Golden Way.
As one might guess from the locations name, the Hanging Place was a square used for public executions. However, during times of war and conflict the Hanging Place was also used as a mustering area for Almorels militia.
