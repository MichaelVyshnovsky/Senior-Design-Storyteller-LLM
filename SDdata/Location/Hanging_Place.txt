The '''Hanging Place''' was an open-aired section of [[Almorel]] on the [[Lake of Mists]] in the [[Hordelands]] in the {{YearlinkName|1357}}.{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The Hanging Place was located near the center of Almorel directly in front of the [[Raumkremyl]] along the [[Golden Way]].

==Activities==
As one might guess from the location's name, the Hanging Place was a square used for public executions. However, during times of war and conflict the Hanging Place was also used as a mustering area for Almorel's militia.
