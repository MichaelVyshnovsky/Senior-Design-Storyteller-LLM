The crypt of Augathra the Mad was located in the ruins of the Netherese floating citadel of Synod.  
Augathra the Mad found the ruins around  and, discovering a unique interaction between the Weave and the Sharn Wall, stayed to research a way to end her dark torment as the chronicler of the Black Chronology.
