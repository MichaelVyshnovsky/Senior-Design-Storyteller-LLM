Immurks Hold was the largest city found within the Pirate Isles on the Sea of Fallen Stars as of 1367 DR. The city was named for its founder, the pirate Immurk.
The city was situated on the western coast of Dragonisle.
thumb|A map of the Pirate Isles showing Immurks Hold on Dragonisle.
Power was the only respected law in Immurks Hold. Ruling pirate captains enforced their edicts with bands of thugs who were just as eager to loot as they were to actually enforce any law. A set of pirate traditions, known as Immurks Code, served as the accepted code of conduct.
Practically any form of trade associated with the sea was conducted within Immurks Hold.
Each and every inhabitant of the city, regardless of age or sex, was expected to come to the citys defense during an attack.
Immurks Hold was founded in 1182 DR.
In 1367 DR, the population of Immurks Hold was around 5,000. The vast majority of these residents were pirates or other criminals.
