'''Nishtul''' was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Nishtul was located in central Halruaa. The town sat on the eastern shore of the [[River Aluar]] where it emptied into [[Lake Halruaa]] on the lake's northeast side.
