Ambral Isle was an island in the Sea of Fallen Stars.
At some point before the founding of Waterdeep, Princess Elvandaruils bracelet washed ashore after a flight spell failed on her.
Jokes at the expense of the intelligence of the inhabitants were common among the inhabitants of the Dragonmere, the Dales, and the Vast.
