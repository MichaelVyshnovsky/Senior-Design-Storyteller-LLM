Six Thorns was a landmark in Cormanthor.
It was approximately three days north of Druindars Rock and one day north of the waymoot of Assamboryl.
In , Galan Goadulphyn left for Six Thorns after being injured from stray spells from the mage duel between Delmuth Echorn and Elminster in Druindars rock. He expected the trip to take three days.
