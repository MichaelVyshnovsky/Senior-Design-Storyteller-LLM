'''Six Thorns''' was a landmark in [[Cormanthor]].

==Geography==
It was approximately three days' north of [[Druindar's Rock]] and one day north of the [[waymoot of Assamboryl]].

==History==
In {{yearlink|241}}, [[Galan Goadulphyn]] left for Six Thorns after being injured from stray spells from the mage duel between [[Delmuth Echorn]] and [[Elminster]] in Druindar's rock. He expected the trip to take three days.
