Rushdown Wood was a small forest located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.
Rushdown Wood was located in southeast Alaron east of the High Kings Road along the coast of the Sea of Swords. It was the only forested section of Alaron found south of the Dernall Forest.
A single Moonwell was sheltered within Rushdown Wood near the coast.
