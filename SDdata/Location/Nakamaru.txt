Nakamaru was a city in Wa known to maintain the largest quarter for the residences of foreign merchants and dignitaries.  Nakamaru was also the original home province of the Marasuuri family. 
The presence of so many Shou inhabitants in the city was because of special permission granted them by the Empress Bishkammon.
