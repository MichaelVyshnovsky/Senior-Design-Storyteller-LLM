The Eagle Hills were a geographical feature located on the elven homeland of Evermeet.
The Eagle Hills were located on the eastern banks of the River Ardulith. They stretched from Evermeets northwest coast through the center of the island along the river before terminating just upriver from where the River Shaelyn joined the Ardulith. The hills stretched for approximately .
The hills stood  over the river valley. Large conifers covered the slopes of the Eagle Hills.
The Eagle Hills were home to group of pegasi and giant eagles
Sunstones could be found within the Eagle Hills.
