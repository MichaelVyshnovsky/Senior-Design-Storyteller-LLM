'''Dultann''' was a small village on the island of [[Gwynneth]].

==Description==
Dultann consisted of a pond owned collectively by the residents, a woodcarver's shop, and a tavern that were clustered among a few [[sheep]] farms.

==Geography==
This village was located on the eastern edge of the [[Myrloch Vale]].

==History==
In [[1359 DR]],{{Cite dragon/362/Grand History of the Realms: The Moonshaes|29-30}}<ref group="note" name="Year">[[Dragon magazine 362|''Dragon'' #362]] provides an exact date for the events of ''[[Halls of the High King]]'', which did not give an exact date within itself. the inhabitants of this village were wracked with fear over tales of the [[Risen Cult of Bane]] and their leader the [[Gauntather|"Dark Druid"]].
