Dultann was a small village on the island of Gwynneth.
Dultann consisted of a pond owned collectively by the residents, a woodcarvers shop, and a tavern that were clustered among a few sheep farms.
This village was located on the eastern edge of the Myrloch Vale.
In 1359 DR,Dragon #362 provides an exact date for the events of Halls of the High King, which did not give an exact date within itself. the inhabitants of this village were wracked with fear over tales of the Risen Cult of Bane and their leader the "Dark Druid".
