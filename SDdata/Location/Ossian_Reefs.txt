The '''Ossian Reefs''' were a coral reef formation found off the coast of the isle of [[Moray]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Ossian Reefs stretched along the entire western coast of Moray, from the [[Last Head]] cape in the south all the way to the rocky [[Trollclaw Range]] in the north near the [[Trackless Strait]]. The reefs were approximately {{SI|100|mi|k|long=yes}} long.
