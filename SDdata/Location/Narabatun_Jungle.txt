The Narabatun Jungle was a jungle in Maztica.
The Narabatun Jungle was the name of the jungles in the northern part of Far Payit. The valley and the city of Ixtzul was located in it.
There were villages of Payits, the humans native to Far Payit, located throughout the jungle. In addition, wild halflings, jaguar lords, and tabaxi also lived there.
