'''High Horn Pass''' was a pass leading east to west through the [[Storm Horns]] mountain range into [[Cormyr]], passing close to the mountain [[High Horn (mountain)|High Horn]] and to the stronghold of the same name, [[High Horn (hold)|High Horn]]. It was frequented by [[Lizardfolk|lizardmen]] who used it to try to pass into Cormyr.
