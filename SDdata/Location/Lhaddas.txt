'''Lhaddas''' was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Lhaddas was located in eastern Halruaa. The town lay along the south bank of the [[River Maeru]] north of the [[Kilmaruu Swamp]].
