'''Widaan'''was one of the nine towns of the [[sahuagin]] kingdom of [[Kurrimal]] and its predecessor [[Aleaxtis]].

==Geography==
This town was located within the [[Alamber Sea]].

==Government==
Like all nine of the towns of Kurrimal, this town was governed by a prince of the same name, [[Widaan (character)|Widaan]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1370}}, when the kingdom of Kurrimal was founded as the successor of Aleaxtis,<ref> this town's name was changed to that of the prince put in charge of governing it.
