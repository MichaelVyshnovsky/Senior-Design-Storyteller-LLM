The '''Pearl Banks''', named for their abundance of treasured [[pearl]]s, were a large coral reef system found off the coast of the south and southeast region of the [[Pearl Cities]] of [[west Zakhara]].

==Geography==
The reef stretched for nearly {{SI|400|mi|k|long=yes}} around the coast of the Pearl Cities, beginning in the west near [[Ajayib]] and continuing all the way to [[Jumlat]] in the northeast.
