'''Edouura'''<ref group="note">The map included in [[The Shining South (1993)]] accessory has this mislabeled as "Edounra." was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Edouura was located in north-central Haluraa. It sat along the east bank of the [[River Aluar]] in the foothills of an arm of the [[Nathaghal Mountains]].
