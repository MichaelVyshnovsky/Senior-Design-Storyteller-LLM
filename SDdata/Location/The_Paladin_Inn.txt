The Paladin Inn was an inn in Velprintalar, Aglarond, in 1368 DR. Its patrons were the wealthier nobles and merchants visiting the city, including visiting dignitaries. A room with a breakfast of fruit and ale was five gold pieces, while a supper of fish or fowl with Aglarondan wine was five silver pieces.
