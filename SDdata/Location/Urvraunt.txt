Urvraunt was an enclave of Netheril that was destroyed during Karsuss Folly, notable for its distance from Netheril.
When the enclave crashed, it came to a rest near the Yellow Sea at the eastern end of the Mountains of Amara, north of the Talmaruk Islands.
After crashing during Karsuss Folly in , the enclave was ruined but remained somewhat intact. Due it its incredibly remote location, it stayed completely unknown to the wider world through the 15 century DR.
