Maeruhal was a small city located in eastern Halruaa.
Maeruhal was a small settlement located within close proximity to the famed electrum mines of Halruaa.
Five skyships were permanently docked in Maeruhal. All of them belonged to Halruaan Elders.
In addition to the skyships, Maeruhal boasted two companies of elite dwarven warriors.
The renowned craftsman, Boronuil Ironfist made his home in Maeruhal
