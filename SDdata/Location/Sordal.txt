'''Sordal''' was a small village located on the island of [[Norland]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Sordal sat along the western edge of [[South Jotunhammer Mountains]] within the region of Norland called [[Grondsgrunn]] which was controlled by [[giant]]s. The village was found on Norland's eastern coast with the [[Sea of Moonshae]]. It was the southernmost village on Norland.
