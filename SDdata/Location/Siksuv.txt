'''Siksuv''' was a small village in the [[Nakvaligach]] region within the [[Great Glacier]].

==Geography==
Siksuv was located in eastern Nakvaligach.

==Inhabitants==
In {{Yearlink|1359}} the inhabitants of Siksuv lacked leadership. This was a result of the villages [[Ulutiun dictionary#U|''urit'']] and [[Ulutiun dictionary#Y|''yaaurit'']] becoming fatally ill after consuming spoiled meat.
