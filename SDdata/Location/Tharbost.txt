Tharbost was a mountain in the Storm Horns.
It was one of the highest peaks in the range, and it overlooked the Tunlands.
By the late 14 century DR, Tharbosts peak had been shattered by a battle between dragons, leaving behind a plateau with a jagged ridge along its western edge, which provided some protection against the strong winds prevalent in the mountain range.
In early spring of , Elminster Aumar and the Simbul held brief parlay with a manifested Mystra after the meeting of the Rightful Conspiracy.
