Ingdals Arm was a human settlement in Aglarond. It was the westernmost settlement in Aglarond, and was not far from the edge of the Yuirwood. It bordered Altumbel.
In 1368 DR, Ingdals Arm was a quiet fishing town with a population of around one thousand, made up of roughly half humans and half half-elves.  The adventurer known as The Masked One made a home in Ingdals Arm.
It was settled in 756 DR.
The settlement was the site of the Battle of Ingdals Arm in 1065 DR Fennibal "Thunder" Bale was a hero of the Battle of Ingdals Arm, who famously charged into the enemy infantry, single-handedly pushing them all back.
