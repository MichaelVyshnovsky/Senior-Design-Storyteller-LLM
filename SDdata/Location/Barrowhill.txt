'''Barrowhill''' was a [[human]] town in the [[Border Kingdoms]], south of [[Applegarth]].

==Description==
Barrowhill was one of many names the town carried over the years. It was built on an old hill that hid an ancient tomb underneath.

==Notable Inhabitants==
*[[Bendoglaer Syndrath]], Bard of Barrowhill, author of a ballad ''[[Death to All Mages]]'', written in [[975 DR]].
