'''Zhal''' was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Zhal was located in the southern region of the [[Muaraghal Mountains]] in southeast Halruaa, making it the easternmost settlement found in the entire country. It lay just south of a tributary of the [[River Maeru]].
