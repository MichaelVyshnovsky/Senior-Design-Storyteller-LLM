The '''Gate of the People''' was the main entrance for the [[Public Gardens of Huzuz|Public Gardens]] in [[Huzuz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Commoners used this gate the enter the beautiful gardens. Though [[mamluk]]s were stationed here, they did not bar entrance to the gardens to any unless specifically instructed to do so.
