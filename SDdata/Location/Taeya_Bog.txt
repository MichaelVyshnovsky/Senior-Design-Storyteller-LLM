The '''Taeya Bog''' was a marshland located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The bog was located in northeast Gwynneth along the coast of the [[Sea of Moonshae]] and the [[Strait of Alaron]]. The Taeya Bog sat at the edge of the [[Winterglen]]. The [[Tunnels of Dwarvenhome]] ran beneath the bog to connect to the island of [[Alaron]]. Roughly {{SI|120|sq mi|k|long=yes}} were covered by the Taeya Bog.
