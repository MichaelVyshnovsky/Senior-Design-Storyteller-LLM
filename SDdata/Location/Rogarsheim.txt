Rogarsheim was the Northlander capital on the island of Norland in the Moonshae Isles.
The city was located on the Bay of Norland along the coast of the Sea of Moonshae near the center of the island.
Jarl Rault "the Wise" ruled Norland from Stormbanner Hold in Rogarsheim in the . The population of Rogarsheim was a mix of Northlanders and Ffolk. There were plenty of dwarves here as well who were eager to mine the north and South Jotunhammer Mountains. It was also not uncommon to see fey creatures from Gwynneth who came to Rogarsheim to trade.
Most of Rogarsheims industry was centered around fishing, hunting, and shipbuilding.
