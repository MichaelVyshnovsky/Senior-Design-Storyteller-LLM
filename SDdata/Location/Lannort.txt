Lannort was a hamlet in the North founded in the 15 century DR.
Lannort lay a short distance downstream of Loudwater on the Delimbiyr River, in the upper Delimbiyr Vale.
Lannort was home to the noxious Falthryn and Daughters Tannery, and Galdeguts Boneyard, operated by Morluth Galdegut. Both businesses were linked by merchants map to Samrils Fine Blades in Secomber, to whom they supplied leather and bone.
