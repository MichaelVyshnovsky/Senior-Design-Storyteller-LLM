The '''Deepglen''' was a small valley located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Deepglen was found nestled along the western end of the [[Black Mountains]] in the northern [[Myrloch Vale]]. The [[Deepglen Stream]] flowing down from the mountains passed through the center of the valley. The Deepglen was approximately {{SI|10|mi|k|long=yes}} long.

==Notable Locations==
A trio of [[Moonwell]]s were found in the forests surrounding the Deepglen.
