Tyraturos was large, centrally-located tharch of Thay, that controlled and regulated much of the nations overland trade.
During the rule of Tharchion Dimon, in the mid-14 century DR, the tax stations along Tyratauros trade routes collected reasonable tolls from Thayan caravans. The roads were well-patrolled and kept safe from possible dangers.
Settlements
*Tyratauros Known as one of the filthiest and most unpleasant cities in all of Thay, the tharchs third-largest city was nonetheless vital to its trade economy.
