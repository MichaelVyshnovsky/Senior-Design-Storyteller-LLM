'''Thurgurs''' were [[efreet]] military zones within the [[Elemental Plane of Fire]].

==Geography==
These zones were located all across the Elemental Plane of Fire, protecting the [[City of Brass]] and routing slaves back to the efreet capital.

==Inhabitants==
An efreet thurgur could contain as many as four large fortresses housing 40 efreet.
