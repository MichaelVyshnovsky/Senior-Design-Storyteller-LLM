'''Sumcha's Tower''' was a tower in [[Velprintalar]], [[Aglarond]]. It was built around [[1358 DR]] by [[Sumcha]], who claimed to be a merchant prince who moved to the city from [[Sembia]]. He lived in the tower with his family and servants.
