The '''Weeping Princess Pool''' was a rainwater pool located near the city of [[Crimmor]] in [[Amn]].{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|28}}

==Description==
The pool was located four hills southwest of the city. Its fancy name was a marketing ploy used by local merchants, who sold its water to snobbish folks.

==Trade==
{{SI|1|gallon|L|long=true}} of ''clearwater'' from the pool was sold for about 6 [[gp]] in ornate, stoppered flagons.
