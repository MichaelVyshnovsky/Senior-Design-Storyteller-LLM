Moki, also written as moKi, was a settlement in the kingdom of Koryo in Kara-Tur.Text sources in Kara-Tur The Eastern Realms render the name as "moKi", while maps in the book and in The Forgotten Realms Atlas render it as "Moki". The latter name is used in this article for ease of writing and wiki coding.
Moki lay on the eastern coast, facing the Straits of Koryo.
Moki was once afflicted by the ivory plague until the shukenja Pitalla used the leaping fish to cure it. 
Around 1357 DR, Moki Bay was dominated by the pirate Crow Dz Pong, who terrorized coastal settlements like Moki.
