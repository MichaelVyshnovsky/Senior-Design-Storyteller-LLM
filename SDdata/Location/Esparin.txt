'''Esparin''' was a kingdom in the [[Stonelands]] that was annexed into [[Cormyr]] in the 12th century [[DR]] after the wedding of Queen [[Enchara]] and Cormyr's crown prince, [[Palaghard I|Palaghard]].

==Geography==
The kingdom occupied most of the Stonelands, northwest of the early kingdom of Cormyr.

==History==
Palaghard was known as the conqueror of Esparin. However, Queen Enchara married Prince Palaghard in the [[1162 DR|Year of the Prancing Centaur, 1162 DR]], and with their union the kingdom was annexed by Cormyr

==Notable locations==
;Ruins:
* [[Lost Palace of Esparin]]

==Inhabitants==
The ruins were inhabited entirely by mad [[lich]]es bound there by [[Vangerdahast]]'s predecessors.
