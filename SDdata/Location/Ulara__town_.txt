'''Ulara''' was a small town located in the nation of [[Estagund]] in the [[Shining Lands]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B1dSWIc}}

==Geography==
Ulara was a town located in western Estagund. The town sat in the foothills of the eastern side of the [[Thrulaliel Mountains]] along the [[Great Trader's Way]].
