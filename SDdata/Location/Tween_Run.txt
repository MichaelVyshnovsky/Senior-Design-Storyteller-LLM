The '''Tween Run''' was a group of alleys and buildings that were located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The Tween Run was what locals of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] collectively called the small alleys and buildings that ran between the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] and the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]]. The area of the Tween Run south of [[Telshambra's Street]] lay within the Southern Ward, while the area north lay in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]].
