'''Cambro''' was a small village located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The village was located in central Gwynneth. It lay in the foothills of the northern branch of the [[Cambro Mountains]] north of the [[Cambrent Gap]].
