Nad Ho was a town in Tabot.
It stood at the southern foot of the Kun-Yen Shan in northwestern Tabot, amidst the forests there, and south of the high mountain lake named Nad Ho Ting. A road ran east to Fij.
In , korobokuru were sighted in the mountains west of Nad Ho. The dzong-pon (sheriff) of the district proposed an expedition to their lands to locate their mines and open trade with them.
