'''Budennov''' was a small town located along the shores of the [[Lake of Mists]] in the [[Hordelands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Geography==
Budennov was found along the southern shore of the Lake of Mists northwest of [[Strelsi]]. The [[Clearflow River]] and [[Dead Dwarf Bridge]] were located due west of Budennov. It was located approximately {{SI|35|mi|k|long=yes}} north of the [[Golden Way]].
