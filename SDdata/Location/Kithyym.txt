'''Kithyym'''<ref group="note">The map included in [[The Shining South (1993)]] labels this town as Kithtym. was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Kithyym was located on the coast of southern Halruaa. The town lay along the shore of the eastern half of the [[Bay of Taertal]] on the [[Great Sea]] just south of [[Halagard]].
