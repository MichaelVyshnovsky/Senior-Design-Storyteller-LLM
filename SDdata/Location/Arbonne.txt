==Geography==
It lay on the shore of the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]], somewhere south of [[Procampur]].

==Trade==
Apart from a few outlying farms, Arbonne made its living off the sea. It had its own shipbuilder, known as [[Justin|Justin of Arbonne]].
