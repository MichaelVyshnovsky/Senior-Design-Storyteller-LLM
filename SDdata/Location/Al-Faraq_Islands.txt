Al-Faraq Islands were a large archipelago found near the center of the Crowded Sea within interior Zakhara.
The northern reach of the archipelago was found approximately  off the coast of the Cities of the Pantheon and the town of Fanus. The islands stretched southeast from there into the Crowded Sea for another . A smaller arm of the archipelago also ran parallel to the southwest coast of the Cities of the Pantheon.
Seventeen individual islands were part of the archipelago. The Zakharans named some of these uninhabited islands as listed below
* Sarir Island
* Mushir Island
* Latif Island
* Jasim Island
