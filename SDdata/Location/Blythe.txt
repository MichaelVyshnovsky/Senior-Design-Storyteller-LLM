'''Blythe''' was a small town located in the kingdom of [[Callidyrr]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Blythe was found on Alaron's eastern coast with the [[Sea of Swords]]. The town was near the center of Callidyrr just a few miles south of the [[Tearglade]] in the county of [[Stirgewick]].
