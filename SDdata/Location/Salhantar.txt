'''Salhantar'''<ref group="note">The map included in ''[[The Shining South (1993)]]'' has this mislabeled as Salhantaz. was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Salhantar was located in central Halruaa just south of an arm of the [[Nathaghal Mountains]]. The town sat on the eastern shore of the [[River Aluar]] north of [[Aluarim]].
