The Wild Wood was a small forest located in the city of Daerlun in Sembia in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The forest was located near the center of Daerlun.
The entire forest was enclosed by a stone wall. A small pond was found near the forests center.
The Wild Wood was part of the temple grounds for the House of Firehair, a temple dedicated to Sune the goddess of beauty.
