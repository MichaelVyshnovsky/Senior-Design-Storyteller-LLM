The Old Stone Wood was a forest located on Omans Isle, in the kingdom of Oman within the Moonshae Isles.
The Old Stone Wood covered much of the western region of Omans Isle, touching the southern shore of the island along the Strait of Oman, passing west of the Grampalt Highlands along the coast, and continuing north past the western shore of the Iron Bay.
