The '''Old Stone Wood''' was a forest located on [[Oman's Isle]], in the kingdom of [[Oman]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The Old Stone Wood covered much of the western region of Oman's Isle, touching the southern shore of the island along the [[Strait of Oman]], passing west of the [[Grampalt Highlands]] along the coast, and continuing north past the western shore of the [[Iron Bay]]. The forest was approximately {{SI|100|mi|k|long=yes}} in length and about {{SI|20|mi|k|long=yes}} in width on average.

==Notable Locations==
The forest contained the island's only [[Oman Moonwell|moonwell]], located near the [[Cairn of Thelgaar Ironhand]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|11}}
