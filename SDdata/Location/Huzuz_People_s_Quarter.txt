The Peoples Quarter was one of the four quarters of Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.
The Peoples Quarter encompassed the Peasants District, Pilgrims District, and South District. The Peoples Quarter was the religious epicenter of Huzuz, as well as the entire Land of Fate. Pilgrims from all over the continent traveled here to worship in the historical Golden Mosque.
