The '''Trollclaw Ford''' was a hamlet located on the [[Winding Water]] was crossed by the [[Coast Way]], connecting [[Baldur's Gate]] to [[Waterdeep]]. It would appear to be named after the [[Trollclaws]], a group of hills to the immediate east of the ford.

It was ruined by the mid-1360s [[DR]].
