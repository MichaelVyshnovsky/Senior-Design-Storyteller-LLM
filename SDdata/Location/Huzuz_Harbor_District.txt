The Harbor District was an unofficial designation for a region of Huzuz, the City of Delights, in 1367 DR.
The Harbor District included the Trade Harbor and Caliphs Harbor, and the surrounding buildings located outside the walls of Huzuz along the south bank of Al-Sarif River outside the cities northern gates.
This district was populated by sailors, laborers, and travelers from all across the Land of Fate and beyond.
