The '''Harbor District''' was an unofficial designation for a region of [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}<ref name="CoD.M>

==Description==
The Harbor District included the [[Trade Harbor]] and [[Caliph's Harbor]], and the surrounding buildings located outside the walls of Huzuz along the south bank of [[Al-Sarif River]] outside the cities' northern gates.

==Inhabitants==
This district was populated by sailors, laborers, and travelers from all across the Land of Fate and beyond.
