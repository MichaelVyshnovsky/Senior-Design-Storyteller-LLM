'''Lillemaren''' was a small town located on [[Oman's Isle]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Lillemaren was located on the [[Sea of Moonshae]] on the east coast of Oman's Isle. It was several miles north of the foothills of the [[Grampalt Highlands]].

==History==
The village was reduced to ruins after [[giant]]s passed through the region during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|11}}
