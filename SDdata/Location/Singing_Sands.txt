'''Singing Sands''' was a reach across the [[River Umber]] from the [[Tannath Gap]] in [[Aglarond]].{{cite book/Spellbound/Campaign Guide|127}}

==Description==
The stretch of the river was bordered by wind-blown dunes, with only dry [[grass]] and small trees.

==Government==
[[Aglarond]] controlled the area, and [[Thesk]] was not interested in more than that as of {{yearlink|1368}}

==Trade==
Thesk found the area to be of minimal interest and did not contest it from Aglarond.

==Defenses==
Forces from [[Emmech]] regularly patrolled the area.

==History==
The zone had been the site of the [[Battle of Singing Sands]] in {{yearlink|1194}},{{cite book/Spellbound/Campaign Guide|48}}

==Rumors & Legends==
The name of the zone had been given in the aftermath of the Battle of the Singing Sands, after the funeral chants of Aglarondan women.
