'''Githim''' was a small town located in the nation of [[Halruaa]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2aNEIc}}

==Geography==
Githim was located along the coast of the [[Great Sea]] in southern Halruaa. The town lay south of [[Lake Halruaa]] and west of the [[Bay of Azuth]].
