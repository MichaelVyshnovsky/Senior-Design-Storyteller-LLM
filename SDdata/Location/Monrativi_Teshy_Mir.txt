'''Monrativi Teshy Mir''' was [[Alzhedo]] for the "[[First Kingdom of Mir]]".<ref name=EotS-p59> It was the term used by the [[Calimshan|Calishites]] to refer to a piece of land in [[Monteshi]] that was contested between Calimshan and the kingdom of [[Tethyr]]. [[Shoonach]], the capital of the [[Shoon Imperium]] was located there.<ref name=EotSS-p92/>
