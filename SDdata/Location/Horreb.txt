'''Horreb''' was an ancient fallen civilization south of [[Barze]]. It was known for its potent magical [[artificer]]s.{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|48}}

==History==
Horreb took part in the defeat of the [[primordial]] [[Maram of the Great Spear]] in {{Yearlink|-357}}.{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|61}}

About a year later, Horreb was conquered and leveled by [[Tyranthraxus]] and his Twisted Ones, who also killed its rulers.{{Cite dungeon/170/Monument of the Ancients|80}}
