Oldturret Farm was a farm belonging to the Klaeverdar clan as of the late mid-14 century DR.
The farm was northwest of Elturel.
Baerbuckle Klaeverdar headed one of the Klaeverdar branches from that farm Gueladdeth Klaeverdar, the clan matriarch, dwelt there in .
The farms namesake was an old keep, supposedly destroyed when a matriarch of a previous generation, Jaedreene Klaeverdar, fought through a whole night with a small army of invaders, the "evil ones out of Dragonspear".
