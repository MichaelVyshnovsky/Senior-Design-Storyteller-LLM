'''Reluraun's Tomb''' was a tomb in the [[Ardeep Forest]] as of [[1370 DR]]. It was the final resting place of [[Reluraun]], an [[elf|elven]] warrior who was a hero to the [[elves]]. Reluraun's body was said to be clad in magic [[chain mail]] and lying with a magical ''[[dragon slayer]]'' sword.{{Cite dragon/128/Welcome to Waterdeep|8}}

==Inhabitants==
As of [[1372 DR]], Reluraun was a mad and aggressive flying pair of skeletal hands and eyeballs, attacking anyone who ventured too near. His tomb was guarded by three [[baelnorn]]s.
