'''Moray''' was the western most settlement of the [[Ffolk]] and the most wild of the southern [[Moonshae Isles]]. By the [[1479 DR|Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR]], the [[Black Blood Tribe]] of [[lycanthropes]] controlled most of the island.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: Moonshae Isles|13}}

==Notable Locations==
===[[Breasal Marsh]]===
A marsh home to [[Lizardfolk]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: Moonshae Isles|19}}

===[[Caer Moray]]===
This small port village of 1,500 was one of the few remaining outposts of humanity on Moray.

===[[Dennin's Delve]]===
A ruined [[dwarf|dwarven]] stronghold that contained an inactive portal to [[Alaron]] isle.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: Moonshae Isles|20}}

===[[Dynnegall]]===
This small village on the edge of Lac Dynnegall was known primarily as a place to harvest peat.

===[[Kork]]===
A village on the eastern shore of Moray, the population was supported by [[Amn]].  The village was too far from the island's moonwells to attract much attention from the Black Blood Tribe.

===[[Farview]]===
A village protected by Northlanders, It lies on the edge of the [[Shannyth Forest]]. This forest provides lots of wood for the Storm Maiden to build her ships.

==Geography==
Moray lay to the south of [[Norland]], west of [[Gwynneth]], and north of [[Flamsterd]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: Moonshae Isles|16}}
