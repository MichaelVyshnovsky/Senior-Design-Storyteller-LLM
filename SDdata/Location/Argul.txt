'''Argul''' was a ruined castle located in northern [[Eskorn]], [[Laerakond]].

==History==
Argul was built by [[dragonborn]] slaves to serve as a lair for the [[dragon]] lord [[Argularragh]]. Over time, people named the castle after the dragon.
