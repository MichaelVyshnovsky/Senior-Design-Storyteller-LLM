The '''Shou Quarter''' was a district of [[Westgate]] that was home to many immigrant [[Shou]]. 

==Geography==
Much of the architecture was of [[Kara-Tur]]an design. The quarter was connected to the rest of Westgate by a bridge over the [[River Thunn]].
