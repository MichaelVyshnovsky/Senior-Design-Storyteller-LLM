'''Calimmon''' was the county bordering [[Calimshan]] in the southwestern portion of [[Ankaram Duchy]] in [[Tethyr]].

Calimmon County was ruled by Lady [[Kyrin Hawkwinter]] immediately after the [[Tethyrian Interregnum#Reclamation Wars|Reclamation Wars]]. She ruled from the county seat at the keep of [[Daraqadim]].

The garrisons [[Fort Agis]], [[Fort Tufenk]], [[Iltakar Garrison]], and [[Fort Calim]] were located in Calimmon.
