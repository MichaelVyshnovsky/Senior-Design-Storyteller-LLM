'''Galardoun''' was an independent village located between [[Triel]] and [[Scornubel]].  The village was abandoned in the [[1380 DR|1380s DR]] after a wasting disease killed most of the inhabitants.  The village was best known as the origin of the [[Ghost Knight of Galardoun]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Eye on the Realms: The Ghost Knight of Galardoun}}
