'''Nozoma''' was a small [[Azuposi]] pueblo found within the [[Pasocada Basin]] of northern [[Maztica]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Geography==
Nozoma was located in the eastern lands of the Azuposi in [[badger tribe]] territory. The village sat along the eastern bank of the [[Pasocada River]] within the [[Long Canyon]]
