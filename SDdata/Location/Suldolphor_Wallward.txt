The '''Wallward''' was a loosely defined poor neighborhood located in [[Suldolphor]] in far eastern [[Calimshan]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Wallward bordering the Golden City's interior outer wall. The buildings found here along the wall were much lower quality when compared to those located inward.{{Cite interactive atlas|Suldolphor}}

==Activities==
Wallward was usually crammed with visiting travelers, [[adventurer]]s, and anyone else wishing to save some money within the expensive city.
