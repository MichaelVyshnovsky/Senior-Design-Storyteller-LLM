The '''Shoulder of Lehreng''', or simply the '''Shoulder''', was a rocky plateau found within the [[Land of the Lions]] region of [[south Faerûn]].<ref name=EotSS-p167">{{Cite interactive atlas}}<ref name=EotSS.M">

==Geography==
The low plateau covered the expanse between the [[Alimir Mountains]] in the west, the [[Lake of Steam]] in the north, [[Talagath's Gauntlet]] in the east, and the [[Shining Sea]] in the south.<ref name=EotSS-p167"/><ref name=EotSS.M"/>

==History==
The Shoulder of Lehreng was named for a mythical giant, Lehreng, who was infamous in children stories told in the region.<ref name=EotSS-p163">

==Notable Locations==
The town of [[Tulmon]] and [[Suldolphor]] were located along the edge of the Shoulder.<ref name=EotSS-p167"/><ref name=EotSS.M"/>
