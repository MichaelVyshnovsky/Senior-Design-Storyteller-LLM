The '''Triboar Trail''' was a stretch of foothills and rolling, sparsely wooded plains extending between the [[Sword Mountains]] to the south and the [[Neverwinter Wood]]s to the north. The area got its name from the [[Triboar Trail|trail of the same name]]. The Triboar Trail was also sometimes referred to as the '''Triboar Cutoff ''' or named after the town of Conyberry as the '''Cony Gap'''.
