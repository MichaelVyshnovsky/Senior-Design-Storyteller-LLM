Greymantle was a Chondathan town built near the Nagawater to supply Chondathan armies with horses from the Golden Plains.
Greymantle was established during the period of time known as the Calmwaters, a century of relative peace and prosperity in Chondath that lacked any significant historic events. Greymantles founding year was sometime between the , the assassination of Florentine Dempster of Turmish, and the , the year dwarves of Alaoreaum revealed themselves to the humans of the Vilhon Reach.
