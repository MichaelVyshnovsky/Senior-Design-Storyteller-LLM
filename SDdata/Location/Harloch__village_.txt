'''Harloch''' was a small village located on the island of [[Snowdown]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The village was located near the center of the island on the eastern shore of [[Harloch]] for which it was named. Snowdown's one road, [[Snowdown Ride]], passed through Harloch. 

==Inhabitants==
[[Heinrich Mucklepratt]] was a low-ranking employee of the mayor in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|13}}
