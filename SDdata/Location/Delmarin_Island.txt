'''Delmarin Island''' was a small, remote, and desolate island located in the [[Sea of Swords]] west of [[Orlumbor]], somewhat north of [[Mintarn]], and east of the [[Korinn Archipelago]]. It was surrounded by perilous reefs. It was inhabited by [[fog giant]]s.
