The '''Basilisk Gate''' was a gate and local landmark in [[Baldur's Gate]], situated in the outer city wall, that separated the [[Lower City]] district of [[Eastway (Baldur's Gate)|Eastway]] from the [[Outer City]] district of [[Stonyeyes]].

The gate acted as the eastern passageway in and out of the city along the [[Coast Way]], which passed through the Outer City and eventually connected to the nation of [[Elturgard]] to the east,{{Cite adventurers league/Stopped at the Gate|3}} and the [[Lands of Intrigue]] to the south.

==Description==
Basilisk Gate was distinctive in that it was lined with a number of statues,

Within the gate were offices of the [[Flaming Fist]], along with a few single-occupant prison cells.{{Cite adventurers league/Stopped at the Gate|17}}
