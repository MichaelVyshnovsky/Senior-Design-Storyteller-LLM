'''Durrar Island''' was a small island in the [[Crowded Sea]] within [[west Zakhara]].{{Cite interactive atlas|B2bSWIc}}

==Geography==
The island was located due south of the [[Pearl Banks]] and the city of [[Safaq]] on the mainland of the [[Pearl Cities]] region. Durrar Island was wider than it was long, measuring roughly {{SI|40|mi|k|long=yes}} wide and {{SI|20|mi|k|long=yes}} long. The entire island was surrounded by coral reefs and two small unamed islands sat off its northeast corner. There were no settlements on Durrar Island as of [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}
