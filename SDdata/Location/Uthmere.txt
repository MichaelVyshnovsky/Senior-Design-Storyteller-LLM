Formerly a port town, the walled village of '''Uthmere''' was left high and dry when the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]] receded following the [[Spellplague]].  As of [[1479 DR]] the village functioned primarily as a trading post.

==Notable locations==
* [[The Hall of the Just]] - Former temple of [[Tyr]] which was converted to serve as the city hall.
