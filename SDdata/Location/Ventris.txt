Ventris was an island within the Korinn Archipelago in the Moonshae Isles.
Ventris was found near the center of the Korinn Archipelago. It was the largest single island among the group, covering approximately . An unnamed mountain passed through the center of the island, and forests were found along both the eastern and western coast.
Populations of dwarves, halflings, and elves lived on the island.
