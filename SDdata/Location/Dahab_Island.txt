'''Dahab Island''' was found on the [[Great Sea]] off the coast of [[north Zakhara]].

==Geography==
Dahab Island was located approximately {{SI|30|mi|k|long=yes}} off the coast of mainland [[Zakhara]] and {{SI|45|mi|k|long=yes}} southwest from the coastal town of [[Mina]]. The northwestern half of the island was lightly forested while the southeastern half was mostly covered in sandy dunes. Dahab Island was approximately {{SI|30|mi|k|long=yes}} long and {{SI|20|mi|k|long=yes}} wide.
