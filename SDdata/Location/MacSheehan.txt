'''MacSheehan''' was a small town located on the island of [[Gwynneth]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
MacSheehan was located in the kingdom of [[Corwell]] just north of the [[Corwell Road]] in southern Gwynneth. The settlement sat in the foothills of the hidden kingdom of [[Synnoria]] along the southern end of the [[Cambro Mountains]].
