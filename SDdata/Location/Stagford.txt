Stagford was a county located in the kingdom of Callidyrr on the island of Alaron within the Moonshae Isles.
The county of Stagford was found in western Callidyrr. Most of the county was filled by the dark expanse of the Dernall Forest. The Swanmay River formed Stagfords eastern border with Talffolk.
The settlements in Stagford were split between the Dernall Forest and the western coast of the Sea of Moonshae. These included
* Hickorydale
* Doncastle
* Codfin
* Aithelar
* Holyhead
* Horsa
