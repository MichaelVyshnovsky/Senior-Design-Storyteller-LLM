'''Stagford''' was a county located in the kingdom of [[Callidyrr]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The county of Stagford was found in western Callidyrr. Most of the county was filled by the dark expanse of the [[Dernall Forest]]. The [[Swanmay River]] formed Stagford's eastern border with [[Talffolk]].

==Notable Locations==
The settlements in Stagford were split between the Dernall Forest and the western coast of the [[Sea of Moonshae]]. These included:
* [[Hickorydale]]
* [[Doncastle]]
* [[Codfin]]
* [[Aithelar]]
* [[Holyhead]]
* [[Horsa]]
