==Geography==
Uldfest lay along the northwestern slopes of the [[Armridge Mountains]], making it the westernmost settlement in Sossal. It was connected to [[Shannath]] to the southeast by an unnamed road, and the [[Reach (trail)|Reach]] trail connected with Uldfest from the southwest.
