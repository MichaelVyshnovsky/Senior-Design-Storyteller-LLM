'''Fillet Lane''' was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and [[gravel]] in [[Waterdeep]], laying primarily in the city's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] and partially in its [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]].

==Geography==
This lane ran west to east, from the Dock Ward to the Southern Ward, being intersected in the east by the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]]. Further to the west, [[Zastrow Street]] met it at a bend.

At its western end, Fillet Lane ended at [[Snail Street]]. which was intersected by [[Slop Street]].

==Notable Locations==
* A stout, post-mounted barrel stood on the corner with the Way of the Dragon. Serving as a kiosk, it had [[broadsheet]]s, employment inquiries, general notices, and handbills posted on it.<ref>
* The [[Old Xoblob Shop]], a curio shop on the corner with Zastrow Street
* The [[Skewered Dragon]], a dilapidated tavern that ran between this lane and [[Net Street (south)|Net Street]].
