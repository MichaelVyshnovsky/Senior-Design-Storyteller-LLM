'''Cod Lane''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Cod Lane originated on [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] on the waterfront within Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It branched northeast toward [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]] was terminated at a dead end before connecting with the larger avenue.
