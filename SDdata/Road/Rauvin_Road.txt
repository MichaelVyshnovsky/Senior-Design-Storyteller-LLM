The '''Rauvin Road''' was a road that passed through the [[Rauvin Vale]] from [[Rivermoot]] in the west, to [[Jalanthar]] in the east. Due to its rather secure location, it was only lightly-patrolled by the forces of the [[Silver Marches]].
