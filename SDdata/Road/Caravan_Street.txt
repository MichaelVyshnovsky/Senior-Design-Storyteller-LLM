'''Caravan Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Caravan Street was located in the northeast section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]]. The road originated on the southeast side of [[Caravan Court]] where it branched south. Caravan Street continued south through the Southern Ward before ending on the [[Specterwalk]]. The road was intersected by [[Olaim's Cut]], [[Robin's Way]], [[Tilman's Lane]], and [[Tulmaster's Street]].

==Notable Locations==
[[The Redbridle Stables]] was located on the northern section of Caravan Street on the east side.
