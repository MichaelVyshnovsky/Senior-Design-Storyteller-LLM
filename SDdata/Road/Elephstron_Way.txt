'''Elephstron Way''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Located in the [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]] in central Zhentil Keep, Elephestron Way cut through the middle of the quarter, dividing into northern and southern halves. The road ran west to east, originating at the western gate to the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] on [[Wyvern Road]] and terminating at the eastern wall of the Inner Quarter. Elephstron Way connected with many other roads within the Inner Quarter including the [[Street of Bones]], [[Imperceptor Street]], [[Raven Street]], [[Flostren's Memory]], [[Wyrms' Way]], and the [[Street of the Gods]].

==Notable Locations==
Several [[Zhentilar]] guardposts were located along Elephstron Way. Other prominent landmarks included the [[Black Altar]], and the estates of Lord [[Marsh Belwintle]] and Lord [[Naerh]].
