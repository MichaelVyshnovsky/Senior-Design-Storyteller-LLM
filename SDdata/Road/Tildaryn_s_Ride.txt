'''Tildaryn's Ride''' was a small roadway found in eastern [[Sembia]] that connected [[Ordulin]] in the west with [[Yhaunn]] in the east on the coast of the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]].

==Description==
Tildaryn's Ride ran in nearly a straight line from west to east, extending where the [[East Way]] terminated in Ordulin.

==History==
The roadway's namesake was [[Antur Tildaryn]], the man who built it. Antur was a businessman and an investor who rose from rags to riches in Yhaunn long before the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{cite web| url = https://web.archive.org/web/20211028234155/http://candlekeep.com/downloads/So_Saith_Ed_Part25_%282010-2016%29.zip| title = So Saith Ed 2010-2016| accessdate = 25-11-2021| date = 25-11-2021}}
