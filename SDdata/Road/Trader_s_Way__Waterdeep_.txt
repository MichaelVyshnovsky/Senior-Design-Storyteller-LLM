'''Trader's Way''' was a prominent road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Trader's Way originated on [[Julthoon Street]] at the border of the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] and [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. It branched southeast then east, passed by the north side of the [[Market (Waterdeep)|Market]], then merged with the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] on the far east side of the Market at a large intersection that also included [[Bazaar Street]] and [[Suldown Street]]. Other roads connectin with Trader's Way included [[Gothal Street]], [[Melshar's Street]], [[Tchozal's Race]], [[the Sutherlane]], [[Mendever Street]], [[Sul Street]], [[Shield Street]], and [[Copper Street]].
