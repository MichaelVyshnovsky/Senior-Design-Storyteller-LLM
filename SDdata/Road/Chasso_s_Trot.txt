Chassos Trot was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Chassos Trot was located in the northeastern section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It ran east to west, originating on the High Road at the border with the North Ward and passing west to eventually end on Diamond Street just south of Ivory Street. Along the way Vondil Street was intersected by Mendever Street, Sul Street, Shield Street, and Copper Street.
