'''Chasso's Trot''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Chasso's Trot was located in the northeastern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. It ran east to west, originating on the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] at the border with the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] and passing west to eventually end on [[Diamond Street]] just south of [[Ivory Street]]. Along the way Vondil Street was intersected by [[Mendever Street]], [[Sul Street]], [[Shield Street]], and [[Copper Street]].
