'''Mendever Street''' was a large road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Mendever Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] with a small portion extending into the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It ran north to south, nearly through the entire length of the ward. Mendever Street originated on [[Heroes' Walk]] where it branched south, eventually terminating on [[Trader's Way (Waterdeep)|Trader's Way]] just north of the [[Market (Waterdeep)|Market]]. It was intersected by many other roads including [[Wrightstone Street]], [[Chasso's Trot]], [[Vondil Street]], [[Delzorin Street]], [[the Rough Road]], [[Zarimtar Street]], [[Whim Street]], [[Julthoon Street]], and [[Heard Lane]].
