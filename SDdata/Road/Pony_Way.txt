'''Pony Way''' was a minor avenue of rock and gravel in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Geography==
Pony Way ran from north to south, shooting off south from the corduroy-log paved road [[Immar Street]], and terminating at a cobblestone paved avenue by the name of [[Saerdoun Street]].

Along the way, Pony Way was intersected from the east by [[Stallion Street]] and from the west by [[Horn Street]].

==Notable Locations==
* The [[Gralhund]] family villa.
