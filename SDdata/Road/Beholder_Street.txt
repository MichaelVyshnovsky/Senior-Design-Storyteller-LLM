'''Beholder Street''' was a large road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Beholder Street was located in east Zhentil Keep in the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]]. The road originated where the [[Street of Dragons]] met the wall of the [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]] in the north, then passed south through the city before terminating in the southeast corner. Along the way Beholder Street was crossed by the [[Street of Swords (Zhentil Keep)|Street of Swords]], [[Dusk Street]], [[Traders' Way (Zhentil Keep)|Traders' Way]], [[Realm Street]], [[Ale Street]], and [[Moonsea Avenue]].

==Notable Locations==
Prominent locations along Beholder Street included the [[Four Dwarf Miners]], [[the Gorey Eye-Beast]], [[the Serpent's Eye]], and a barracks for the [[Zhentilar]].
