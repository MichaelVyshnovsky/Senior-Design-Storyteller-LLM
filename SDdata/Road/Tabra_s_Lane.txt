'''Tabra's Lane''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Tabra's Lane was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. It originated on the west side of [[Flint Street]] across from the [[House of Wonder]]. Tabra's Lane branched west at several points along Flint Street and split further within a small neighborhood.
