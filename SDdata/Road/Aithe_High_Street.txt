'''Aithe High Street''' was a the main road that passed through [[Aithelar]] in [[Callidyrr]] on the [[Moonshae Isles]] in {{YearlinkName|1345}}.{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
Aithe High Street passed through the center of town, running north to south. It intersected many of Aithelar's other streets including [[Bottle Lane]], [[Whitefish Alley]], [[Eel Street (Aithelar)|Eel Street]], [[Rope End Lane]], [[Finn's Alley]], [[Aerynn's Street]], [[Blackdorn's Way]] and [[Blaedar Street]].
