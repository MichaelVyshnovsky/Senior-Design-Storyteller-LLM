'''Melshar's Street''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Melshar's Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It originated in the north on [[Julthoon Street]] where it branched south, passed through the western edge of [[Trader's Way (Waterdeep)|Trader's Way]], then turned southwest to end on [[Calamastyr Lane]].
