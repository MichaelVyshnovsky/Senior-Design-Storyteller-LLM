Niles Way
Guards Court was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved cul-de-sac in the southern section of the Castle Ward of Waterdeep.
A portion of a 1492 DR map, showing an unlabeled Guards Court running southeast off of High Hillock Lane.
This cul-de-sac originally stretched south off of Niles Way, ending in a dead-end block of buildings that were bounded by Crossbow Lane, Niles Way, and Snail Street.Guards Court is not labeled on the map included with Waterdeep Dragon Heist
