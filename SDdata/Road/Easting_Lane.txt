Easting Lane was a street in the built-up part of Hultail in Cormyr.
Easting Lane was a street that stretched from southwest to northeast along the southeastern edge of built-up Hultail. In the 15 century DR, the street was where Harrafe Orlimburs red-roofed barn with the spire stood across the street from the home and workshop of an aging seamstress named Avarelle Myntdown.
