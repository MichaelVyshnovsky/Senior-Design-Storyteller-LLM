The Farmers Road was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The Farmers Road in northcentral Daggerford left the northern arm of Wall Street at the Farmers Gate and cut south, then east between the Happy Cow and River Shining Tavern, and south once more to Dukes Way. Its segment between Market Way and the Farmers Gate was sometimes referred to as Fields Lane.
Notable buildings located along the Farmers Road included
* The Grand Playhouse
* The Harvest House
* Farmers Society
* The Happy Cow
