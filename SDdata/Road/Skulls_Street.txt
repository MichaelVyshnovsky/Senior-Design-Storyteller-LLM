'''Skulls Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Skulls Street was located in the north section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. Skulls Street ran east to west, originating on the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] at the border with the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] and ending on [[Heroes' Walk]]. Skulls Street was intersected by [[Copper Street]], [[Shield Street]], [[the Fanebar]], and [[Sul Street]].
