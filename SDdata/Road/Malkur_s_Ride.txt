'''Malkur's Ride''' was a street of hard-packed dirt and [[gravel]]<ref name=CoS.M"> in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].Across the editions this street went unlabeled on maps of Waterdeep. It was only revealed by [[Ed Greenwood]] on November 14{{th}}, 2021.

==Location==
This street stretched from north to south, going south from [[Zendulth Street]] and terminating at the [[Beaconmarch]]. At its midpoint, the alleyway [[Selvur Alley]] stretched between it and the Beaconmarch.

==History==
Malkur's Ride was named for [[Malkur]], a notorious [[adventurer]] and [[mage]] of the early 1300s [[DR]], who frequently galloped down it on a ''[[phantom steed]]'' while experimenting with a speed-enhancing [[spell]].
