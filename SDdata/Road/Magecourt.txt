The '''Magecourt''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The Magecourt was located in the southwest section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The Magecourt branched southwest from [[Shando Street]], branching further several more times forming unnamed alleys within a small neighborhood.
