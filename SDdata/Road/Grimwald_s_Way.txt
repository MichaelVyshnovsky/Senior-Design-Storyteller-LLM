Grimwalds Way was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Grimwalds Way was located in the northwestern section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. The road originated on Westwall Street in front of the Seawatch Tower where it branched due east, cutting southeast at the junction with the Street of Whispers. Seawatch Street intersected the eastern half of Grimwalds Way.
