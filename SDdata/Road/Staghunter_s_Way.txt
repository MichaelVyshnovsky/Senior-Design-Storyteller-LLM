Staghunters Way was a small corner road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Staghunters Way was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It cut the corner between the Street of Glances and Gorl Street, passing through Sighing Maidens Walk along the way. Wrightstone Street connected with the east side of Staghunters Way.
