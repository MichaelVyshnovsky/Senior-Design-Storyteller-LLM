'''Tarnath Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Tarnath Street was located in the west section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road branched due east from the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] just northeast of the [[Market (Waterdeep)|Market]]. It ended on [[Suldown Street]] after several blocks. Tarnath Street also connected with [[Ilzantil Street]], [[Shando Street]], and [[Vhezoar Street]].
