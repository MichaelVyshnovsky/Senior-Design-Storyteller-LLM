'''Heard Lane''' was a cobblestone-paved street in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
Heard Lane stretched from southwest to northeast between two main thoroughfares, terminating in the north on [[Sul Street]] and terminating in the south upon [[Mendever Street]].

==Notable Locations==
* The [[God Catcher]], one of the [[Walking Statues of Waterdeep]], was situated at the intersection of Heard Lane and Sul Street since [[1385 DR]].
