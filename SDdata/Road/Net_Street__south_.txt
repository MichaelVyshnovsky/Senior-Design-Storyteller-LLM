'''Net Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Net Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It branched west off of the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]] then turned southwest to terminate on [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] on the waterfront with an intersection with [[Keel Alley]]. [[Snail Street]] and [[Pressbow Lane]] connected with Net Street near its midpoint.

==Notable Locations==
The [[Hanged Man (Waterdeep)|Hanged Man]] tavern was located at the intersection of Net Street and Snail Street and [[Full Sails]] tavern was located on the waterfront. The [[Blushing Mermaid (Waterdeep)|Blushing Mermaid]] sat on the intersection with Keel Alley.
