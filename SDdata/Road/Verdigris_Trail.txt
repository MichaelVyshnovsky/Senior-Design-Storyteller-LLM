The Verdigris Trail was a nearly-straight dirt trail found in the Moonsea North.
The  long trail linked the community of Verdigris with Castle Vathar to the east.
It was set into the narrow strip of land between the cold steppes of the Ride and the northern slopes of the Dragonspine Mountains.
The Verdigris Trail crossed the Razacks Tears stream just north of Verdigris.
