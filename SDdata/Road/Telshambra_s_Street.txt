Telshambras Street was a road paved in hard-packed dirt and gravel, that stood on the boundary line of the city of Waterdeeps Southern Ward and Trades Ward.
This road ran from east to west, spanning between the High Road and the Way of the Dragon. Along the way it acted as the southern terminus of Blackmul Street and the terminus for both ends of the curving Ilisars Alley.
