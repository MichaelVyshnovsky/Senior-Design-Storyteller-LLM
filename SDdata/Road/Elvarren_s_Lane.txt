'''Elvarren's Lane''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Elvarren's Lane was located in the northwest section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It originated on [[Calamastyr Lane]] in the north, branching due south and turning southeast to end on [[Tchozal's Race]].
