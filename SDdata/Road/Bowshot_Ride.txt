Bowshot Ride was a street in Silverymoon.
Bowshot Ride was a north&ndashsouth street in Northbank that ran from the Docks to the Old Wall, passing through the former gatehouse of Helmers Wall, for a total distance of about 350 feet. It intersected the Ghostwalk along the way.
