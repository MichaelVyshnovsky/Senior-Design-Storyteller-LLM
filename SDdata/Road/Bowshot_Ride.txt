'''Bowshot Ride''' was a street in [[Silverymoon]].

==Description==
Bowshot Ride was a north&ndash;south street in [[Northbank]] that ran from the Docks to the [[Old Wall]], passing through the former gatehouse of [[Helmer's Wall]], for a total distance of about 350 feet. It intersected the [[Ghostwalk (street)|Ghostwalk]] along the way.

==Notable Locations==
* [[Helmer's Wall]]
* The [[Wayward House]]
