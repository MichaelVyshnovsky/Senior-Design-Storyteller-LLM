The '''High King's Road''' was the main thoroughfare in the kingdom of [[Callidyrr]] on the island of [[Alaron]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
The High King's Road stretched from one end of Callidyrr to the other. The road was branched in the north within the [[Fairheight Range]], beginning in the mining settlements of [[Blackstone]] and [[White Rock]] and traveling nearly {{SI|175|mi|k|long=yes}} to the south to terminate at [[Kythyss]] on the southern coast. The road crossed the [[Swanmay River]] near its midpoint within the [[Dernall Forest]] at the town of [[Horsa]].
