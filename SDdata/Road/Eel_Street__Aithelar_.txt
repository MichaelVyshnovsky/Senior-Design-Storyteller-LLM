'''Eel Street''' was a small street found in the coastal town of [[Aithelar]] in [[Callidyrr]] on the [[Moonshae Isles]] in {{YearlinkName|1345}}.{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
Rope End Lane passed between the eastern outer edge of Aithelar and ended at [[Aithe High Street]] near the center of town.
