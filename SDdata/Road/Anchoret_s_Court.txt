Anchorets Court was a small courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Anchorets Courty was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward. It sat at the southern end of Anchorets Alley just north of the Specterwalk.
A public well sat in the center of the courtyard.
The home of Kapplian Mulmastyr stood on the eastern side of Anchorets Court near Caravan Street.
