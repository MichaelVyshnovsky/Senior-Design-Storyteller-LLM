'''Anchoret's Court''' was a small courtyard that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Anchoret's Courty was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]]. It sat at the southern end of [[Anchoret's Alley]] just north of the [[Specterwalk]].

==Description==
A public well sat in the center of the courtyard.

==Notable Locations==
The home of [[Kapplian Mulmastyr]] stood on the eastern side of Anchoret's Court near [[Caravan Street]].
