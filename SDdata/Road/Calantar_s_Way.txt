Calantars Way () was a long road running north to south in the nation of Cormyr. The southernmost point of Calantars Way was the city of Suzail.
Calantars Way crossed the Starwater River at Calantars Bridge, just south of the town of Hilp. 
Calantars Way had been well-cared-for over the years by those who used it.
Calantars Way was surveyed and built by the Calantar noble family in the late 900s DR as a cart road.
