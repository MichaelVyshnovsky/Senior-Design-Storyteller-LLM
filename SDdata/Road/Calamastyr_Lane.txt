Calamastyr Lane was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Calamastyr Lane was located in the northern section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward. The road originated on Julthoon Street where it branched southeast toward the Market where it terminated on Bazaar Street. Several other roads connected with Calamastyr Lane along its length including Swords Street, Gothal Street, Elvarrens Lane, Tchozals Race, and Melshars Street.
