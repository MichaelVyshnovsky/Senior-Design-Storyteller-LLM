'''Helve's Trail''' was a road in the southern [[the Vast|Vast]]. It ran from [[Maerstar]] in the south, where it met the [[North Road]], then into the [[Earthfast Mountains]], where it ended at [[High Haspur]] and connected with the [[High Trail]].
