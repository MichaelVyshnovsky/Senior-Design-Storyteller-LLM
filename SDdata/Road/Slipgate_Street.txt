'''Slipgate Street''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Slipgate Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. The road originated on the [[Northyard]] where it branched west into a small neighborhood.
