'''Phull Lane''' was a street of hard-packed dirt and [[gravel]]<ref name=CoS.M"> in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Geography==
In the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], this street ran from northeast to southwest, coming off of [[Trollkill Street]] and terminating at [[Immar Street]]. In later years, the area that Phull Lane terminated at would be designated as part of [[Whaelgond Way]].

==Notable Locations==
* [[Phull Villa]], the family estate of the noble house [[Phull]], stood on the west corner of where Phull Lane met Trollkill Street.
* [[Snome Villa]], the family estate of the noble house [[Snome]], stood on the east corner of where Phull Lane met Trollkill Street.
