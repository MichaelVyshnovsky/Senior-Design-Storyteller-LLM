The Rough Road was a street that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
The Rough Road was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It originated on Diamond Street just east of the Street of the Singing Dolphin. The Rough Road branched southeast then turned due east at the junction with Feather Street, eventually ending on Shield Street at the border with the North Ward. Other roads crossing the Rough Road included Mendever Street, Cloaksweep Alley, and Sul Street.
