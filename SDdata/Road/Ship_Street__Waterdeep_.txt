'''Ship Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Ship Street connected [[Fish Street (Waterdeep)|Fish Street]] with [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It branched south from Fish Street to meander south toward the waterfront and end on Dock Street. Other roads connecting with Ship Street included [[Aline's Way]], [[Keel Alley]], [[Odd Street]], [[Presper Street]], [[Shipwright's Square]], [[Shrimp Alley]], [[Siren Street]], and [[Sternpost Street]].
