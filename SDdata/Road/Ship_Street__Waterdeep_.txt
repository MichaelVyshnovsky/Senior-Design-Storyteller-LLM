Ship Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Ship Street connected Fish Street with Dock Street in Waterdeeps Dock Ward. It branched south from Fish Street to meander south toward the waterfront and end on Dock Street. Other roads connecting with Ship Street included Alines Way, Keel Alley, Odd Street, Presper Street, Shipwrights Square, Shrimp Alley, Siren Street, and Sternpost Street.
