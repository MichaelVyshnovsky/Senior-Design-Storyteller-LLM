'''Black Well Court''' was a small courtyard that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Black Well Court was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. The court sat between [[Book Street]] and [[Snail Street]], with the main entance branching west off of Book Street. A well sat in the center of the court.

==History==
The well found in the center of Black Well Court was polluted with black water, thus giving the court its name. It was also infested with a variety of monsters. It was sealed by order of the [[Lords of Waterdeep]]. The court served as a meeting place for various street gangs and cultists and was heavily patrolled by the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]].
