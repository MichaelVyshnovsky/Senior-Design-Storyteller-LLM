Spices Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Spices Street was located in the south section of Waterdeeps Dock Ward. It originated on the western side of the Way of the Dragon, branching west the turning southwest near its midpoint to end on Dock Street on the waterfront. Other roads connecting with Spices Street included Wharf Street and Fishgut Alley.
