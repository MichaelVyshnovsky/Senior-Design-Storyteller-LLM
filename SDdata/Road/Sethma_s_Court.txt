Sethmas Court was a cobbled courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Sethmas Court was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward. The courtyard sat directly southwest of Tulmasters Street.
The court was named for Sethma, an old lady who lived here during the early 14 century DR. For unknown reasons Sethmas Court was a gathering place for all kinds of birds flying over the city.
