==Location==
Mace Way was located in southern Zhentil Keep within the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]]. The street was found in the far western section of the quarter running roughly north to south in between [[Teshwave Street]] and [[Twilight Street]] near the [[River Trail Gate]].
