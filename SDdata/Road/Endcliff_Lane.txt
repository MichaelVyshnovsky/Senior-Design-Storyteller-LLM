'''Endcliff Lane''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Endcliff Lane was located in the east section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. Endcliff Lane branched due east from [[Nindabar Street]] and ended on [[Saerdoun Street]] a short distance away from the outer wall in front of [[Endcliff Tower]].
