Sighing Maidens Walk was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Sighing Maidens Walk was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It originated on the the Street of Glances where it branched southwest to end on the Street of the Singing Dolphin. Sighing Maidens Walk was intersected by Ivory Street, Sniff Alley and Staghunters Way.
