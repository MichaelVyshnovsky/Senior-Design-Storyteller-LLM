The Boomgarden was a cul-de-sac, paved in hard-packed earth and gravel, in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.
This cul-de-sac trailed northward off of Fillet Lane. Along the way it was intersected by Slop Street.
* Amalaes, a beauty shop that stood at the head of the cul-de-sac.
* Booms Garden, a clothing store.
* Mhaalauvurs, a jug store at the northwestern corner of the streets intersection with Fillet Lane.
* Rooming houses dominated much of this street.
* Sorting houses dominated the southeastern block of this street, wherein goods brought from warehouses were assembled into specific in-city customer orders and crated for delivery.
