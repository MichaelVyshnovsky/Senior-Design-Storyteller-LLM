Duirs Alley was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Duirs Alley was located in the eastern section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward. It connected the Street of Bells in the west with the Street of the Sword in the east via passing through a neighborhood of buildings. Lhorils Alley branched off of the southern side of Duirs Alley.
Patrons of the Elfstone Tavern often exited the buildings rear onto Duirs Alley where they displayed their magical prowess.
