Pressbow Lane  was a street in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.
This road ran from north to south, stretching between Shrimp Alley and Net Street. From the northeast it was met by Snail Street.
This road was named for the pressbow, a type of factory machine.
* Coopers Rest, the guildhall of the Coopers Guild.
* The Hanged Man, a tavern on the corner of Pressbow Lane and Net Street.
* Jesters Clubhouse, the guildhall of the Jesters Guild.
* The Sleeping Wench, a tavern on the corner of Pressbow Lane and Snail Street.
* The workshop of the leatherworker Albryn Telnhand, disputed inventor of the pressbow.
