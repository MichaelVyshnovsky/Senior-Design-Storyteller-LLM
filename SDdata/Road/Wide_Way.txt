The Wide Way was a street of corduroy-logs in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.
This street ran southeast off of Slipstone Street, running roughly parallel to Nethpranters Street. Along the way it intersected Quill Alley and eventually terminated at the Court of the White Bull.
Warehouse A warehouse stood on one of the corners of where the Wide Way met Quill Alley. By 1368 DR, this warehouse was abandoned and its cellar was being used as a hidden temple to Loviatar.
The Zoarstar Guildhall of the Scriveners, Scribes, & Clerks Guild, which stood on the northwest corner of where Quill Alley intersected with the Wide Way.
