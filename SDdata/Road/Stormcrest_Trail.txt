The '''Stormcrest Trail''', also called the '''Tsurlagol Road''', was a road in the southern [[the Vast|Vast]], running from [[Tsurlagol]] in the south to [[Sevenecho (hamlet)|Sevenecho]] in the north, where it met the [[North Road]].
