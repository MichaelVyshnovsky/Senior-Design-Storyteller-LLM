'''Nanze Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Nanze Street was located in the western and central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. It branched off of the [[Fieldway]] in the west and reconnected with the Fieldway in the east a few blocks east of the [[Northyard]]. [[Soot Alley]] and [[Firewine Alley]] both connected with Nanze Street.
