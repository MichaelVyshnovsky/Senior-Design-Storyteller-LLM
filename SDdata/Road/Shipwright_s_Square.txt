'''Shipwright's Square''' was a small courtyard that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Shipwright's Square was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It was bordered to the southwest and southeast by [[Aline's Way]], to the northeast by [[Keel Alley]], and to the northwest by [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]]. The largest entrance into the square branched southwets off of Keel Alley.
