'''Nag Street''' was minor avenue of hard-packed earth and [[gravel]] in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
This road ran from northeast to southwest, extending off of the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]] and terminating at [[Tower Trail]].
