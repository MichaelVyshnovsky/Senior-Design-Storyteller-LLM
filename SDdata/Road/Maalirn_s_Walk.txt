'''Maalirn's Walk''' was a north&ndash;south road bordering the eastern side of the Dukar Quarter of [[Myth Nantar]].

==Geography==
The road arched in places with links to the [[Dukars' Academy]], and was popular with younger [[merfolk]] who enjoyed racing along it, despite the hazards.
