'''Reavers' Run''' was the main street in [[Luskan]]'s [[Luskan/South Bank|South Bank]].

==Description==
Reavers' Run was a wide boulevard&mdash;and the slums to the southwest.

==Geography==
The road ran from the [[South Gate (Luskan)|South Gate]] northwest to the city market before terminating at the [[Short Span]], a bridge leading to [[Blood Island]] It was also the quickest route to get from the South Gate to [[Dark Arch|the bridge]] leading to [[Closeguard Island]].

==Notable Locations==
* [[Captains' Close]], a walled park on the western side of the street that contained the palatial homes for the [[High Captains of Luskan|High Captains]] of [[Ship Taerl]] and [[Ship Suljack]].
