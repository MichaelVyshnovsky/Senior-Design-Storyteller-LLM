'''Loklee Street''' was a street in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] that began in [[Baldur's Gate/Heapside|Heapside]] and curved to [[Baldur's Gate/Eastway|Eastway]].<ref group="note">The game does not specify how long the street was, this article presumes Loklee Street was a long continuous street that ended in a Y crossing in Eastway.

==History==
[[File:Loklee Street.png|thumb|''A map of Loklee Street, showing the location of Tav's home.''|270x270px]]
Loklee Street got its name from from one of the earliest names of Baldur's Gate sometime before [[0 DR]].{{Cite web/theedverse/2022-03-26 - Baldur's Gate History}} ''Circa'' the summer of the {{YearlinkName|1477}}, the adventuring detective named [[Tav]] lived on Loklee Street.
