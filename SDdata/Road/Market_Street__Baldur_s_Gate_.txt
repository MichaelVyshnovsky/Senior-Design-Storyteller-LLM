Market Street was a short street located in the Eastway district of Baldurs Gate, on the border with the Brampton neighborhood.
Market Street cut through a city block, east to west. The city block was sandwitched between Nuthkhals Way to the west and Stormshore Street to the east.
In the , a suspicious half-orc butcher named Magnus Shank was said to had been selling monster meat to the customers. This accusation came at the time of a slew of murders in the Gate, and the butchery was thought to be involved and hide a more sinister secret than monster meat. The half-orcs supplier was a tiefling Selwyn Leventide.
