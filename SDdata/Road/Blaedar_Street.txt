'''Blaedar Street''' was a street found in the coastal town of [[Aithelar]] in [[Callidyrr]] on the [[Moonshae Isles]] in {{YearlinkName|1345}}.{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
Blaedar Street was located in southern Aithelar. It ran northeast between [[Blackdorn's Way]] and [[Bottle Lane]], intersecting [[Aithe High Street]] near its center.
