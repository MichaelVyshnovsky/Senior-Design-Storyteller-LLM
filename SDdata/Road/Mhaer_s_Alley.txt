Mhaers Alley was a hard-packed dirt and gravel paved,
This street ran north off of Spendthrift Alley and terminated on Wall Way.
Mhaers Alley was host to a great variety of shops across both of its sides, all along its length. Including one of the greatest concentrations in Waterdeep of shops that sold cord, rope, thread, wire, and trimmings for clothing. Some of the known stores that stood along Mhaers Alley included the following
* Caer Bannog
* Chukis Moneylenders
* Diabels Potions & Curatives
* Huers Sundries
* Maries Dry Goods
* The Old Druids Herbs & Seeds Shoppe
* Salavants Scriveners
