Vondil Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Vondil Street was located in the easterm section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It ran east to west, originating on the High Road at the border with the North Ward and passing west to eventually end on Diamond Street. Along the way Vondil Street was intersected by Mendever Street, Sul Street, Shield Street, and Copper Street.
