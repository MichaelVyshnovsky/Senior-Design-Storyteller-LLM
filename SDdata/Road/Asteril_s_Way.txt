'''Asteril's Way''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Asteril's Way was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It originated on [[Sternpost Street]] near the intersection with [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]], branching south then southwest to end on [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] on the waterfront. Several unnamed alleys branched off of the southeast side of Asteril's Way
