'''Tower March''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Tower March was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. It ran in between the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] at the border with the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] and the [[Trollmarch]], passing roughly northeast through the ward. Tower March merged with [[Horn Street]] near its midpoint and was also intersected by [[Windborne Way]], [[Thunderstaff Way]], [[Whaelgond Way]], [[Immar Street]], and [[Watch Alley]].
