'''Palfrey Lane''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Palfrey Lane was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It ran east to west, connecting [[Warrior's Way]] with the [[Street of Silver]].
