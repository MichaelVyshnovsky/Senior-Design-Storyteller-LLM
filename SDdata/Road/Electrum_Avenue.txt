'''Electrum Avenue''' was a small road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Electrum Avenue was located in the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] in the northeast section of the city. The road branched from [[Fortress Street]] in the north and passed south then east in a curve before ending on [[Ghost Street]]. The [[Street of Delights]] connected with Electrum Avenue along its curve.

==Notable Locations==
A [[Zhentilar]] armory was located on Electrum Avenue.
