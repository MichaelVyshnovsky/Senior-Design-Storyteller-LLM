'''Hammer Street''' was a major road in the asteroid city of [[Bral]].

==Description==
Hammer Street began near the docks at the intersection of [[Low Street]] and [[Dock Street (Bral)|Dock Street]] and extended uphill through the [[Bral/Low City|Low City]] and into the [[Middle City]], where it curved around the starboard edge of the [[Bral/Temple District|Temple District]]. On its journey, it covered about ~{{SI|2900|ft.|long=yes}} and crossed [[Privateer's Way]], [[Eel Street]], [[Galleon Street]], [[Squid Street]], [[Lamp Street]], [[Man O' War Street]], [[Mainmast Street]], and [[Temple Street]]. It formed the port border of [[Giff-town]] and passed through a good portion of the [[Dwarven District]].
