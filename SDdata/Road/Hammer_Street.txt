Hammer Street was a major road in the asteroid city of Bral.
Hammer Street began near the docks at the intersection of Low Street and Dock Street and extended uphill through the Low City and into the Middle City, where it curved around the starboard edge of the Temple District. On its journey, it covered about ~ and crossed Privateers Way, Eel Street, Galleon Street, Squid Street, Lamp Street, Man O War Street, Mainmast Street, and Temple Street. It formed the port border of Giff-town and passed through a good portion of the Dwarven District.
