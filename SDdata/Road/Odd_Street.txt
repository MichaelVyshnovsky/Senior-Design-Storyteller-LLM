'''Odd  Street''' was secondary avenue of cobblestone in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
This road ran from northeast to southwest, extending off of the intersection of [[Fish Street (Waterdeep)|Fish Street]] and [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]] until terminating at [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]].
