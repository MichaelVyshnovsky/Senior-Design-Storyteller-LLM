Ussilbran Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Ussilbran Street was located in the southern section of Waterdeeps North Ward. The road ran north to south, originating in the north at the intersection of Saerdoun Street and Lion Street, and passing south to end on Tarsars Street. Ussilbran Street was intersected by Sulmor Street, Endcliff Lane, and Hassantyrs Street
