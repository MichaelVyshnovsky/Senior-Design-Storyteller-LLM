Flint Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Flint Street was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It ran north to south, branching from Ivory Street in the north and ending on the western half of Delzorin Street in the south. Pharras Alley and the Street of Spells connected with Flint Street east side near its midpoint, while Tabras Lane connected on its west side.
