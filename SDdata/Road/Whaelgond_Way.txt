Whaelgond Way was a large road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Whaelgond Way was located in the north and central sections of Waterdeeps North Ward. It stretched through much of the North Ward, orginating in the north on Immar Street and passing southeast then due south to eventually end on Suldown Street. Along its path Whaelgond Way was intersected by Sashtar Street, Tower March, Horn Street, Saerdoun Street, Delzorin Street, Sulmor Street, Hassantyrs Street, the Street of the Manticore, and Tarsars Street.
