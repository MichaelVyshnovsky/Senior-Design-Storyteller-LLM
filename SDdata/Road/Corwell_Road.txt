The '''Corwell Road''' was the inland trade route that stretched from [[Kingsbay (town)|Kingsbay]] in the east to [[Pontswain]] in the west on the isle of [[Gwynneth]] in the [[Moonshaes]], passing [[Caer Corwell]] at its midpoint.
