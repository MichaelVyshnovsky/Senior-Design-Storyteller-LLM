'''Brindul Alley''' was a notorious, hard-packed dirt and [[gravel]]-paved, alleyway in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Geography==
This alleyway was crescent shaped and opened south off of [[Sorn Street]].

==Notable Inhabitants==
Brindul Alleyway was notorious for an apparition that wandered it at at night, known as the [[Hand That Sings]].
