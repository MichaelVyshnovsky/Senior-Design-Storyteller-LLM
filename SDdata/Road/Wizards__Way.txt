'''Wizards' Way''' was a small road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Wizards' Way was found within northwest Zhentil Keep in the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]]. It ran from the wall separating the [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]] from the Common Quarter in the south to Zhentil Keep's main exterior wall and [[Border Street]] in the north. Wizards' Way was crossed by [[Warrior Street]], [[Fortress Street]], and the [[Street of Delights]] along the way.

==Notable Locations==
The lavish [[Silver Trumpet Inn]] was located at the southern end of Wizards' Way.
