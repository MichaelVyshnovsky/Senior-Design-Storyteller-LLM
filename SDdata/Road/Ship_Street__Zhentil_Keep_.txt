Ship Street was located in Zhentil Keeps Harbor District in the south section of the city. The road ran west to east for a single block in between Canter Street and the gate to the Common Quarter and the docks.
A pair of warehouses used by the Zhentarim flanked both sides of Ship Street near the docks.
