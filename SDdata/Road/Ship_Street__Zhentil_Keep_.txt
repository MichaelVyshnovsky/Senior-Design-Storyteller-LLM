==Location==
Ship Street was located in Zhentil Keep's [[Zhentil Keep/Harbor District|Harbor District]] in the south section of the city. The road ran west to east for a single block in between [[Canter Street]] and the gate to the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] and the docks.

==Notable Locations==
A pair of warehouses used by the [[Zhentarim]] flanked both sides of Ship Street near the docks.
