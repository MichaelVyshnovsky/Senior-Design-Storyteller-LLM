'''The Reach Road''' was a trail in the [[Dalelands]] that ran through [[Harrowdale]].

==Geography==
The trail connected [[New Velar]] in Harrowdale to the north with [[Scardale Town]] in [[Scardale]] to the south. It ran along the eastern edge of the [[Cold Field]] and the coast of the [[Dragon Reach]].
