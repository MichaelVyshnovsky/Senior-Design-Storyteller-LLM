'''Sidle Street''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Sidle Street was located in the west section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road ran east to west in between [[Copper Street]] and the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]].
