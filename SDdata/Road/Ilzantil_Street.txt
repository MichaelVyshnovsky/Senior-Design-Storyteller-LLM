Ilzantil Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Ilzantil Street was located in the west section of Waterdeeps North Ward. Ilzantil Street originated in the north, branching south from Delzorin Street to eventually end on Tarnath Street. The road ran due south parallel to the High Road to the west. Ilzantil Street also connected with Sulmor Street and Hassantyrs Street.
