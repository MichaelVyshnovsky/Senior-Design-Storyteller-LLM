'''Ilzantil Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Ilzantil Street was located in the west section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. Ilzantil Street originated in the north, branching south from [[Delzorin Street]] to eventually end on [[Tarnath Street]]. The road ran due south parallel to the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] to the west. Ilzantil Street also connected with [[Sulmor Street]] and [[Hassantyr's Street]].
