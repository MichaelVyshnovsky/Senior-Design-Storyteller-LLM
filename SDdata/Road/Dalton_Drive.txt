'''Dalton Drive''' was a small street in the [[Torchtown]] neighborhood of [[Ravens Bluff]].{{Cite interactive atlas|4750 Raven's Bluff}}

==Location==
Considered rather hidden by some, Dalton Drive spanned only a single block in length, connecting [[Sylgarth Street]] and [[Broken Bit Lane]].

==Notable Locations==
* The [[Cup of Coins]], guildhall of the city's [[Merchants Guild (Ravens Bluff)|Merchants Guild]], stood on the corner of where Dalton Drive met Broken Bit Lane.
