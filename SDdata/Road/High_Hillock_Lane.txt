'''High Hillock Lane''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
High Hillock Lane was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It ran north to south in between [[Niles Way]] in the north and [[Soldier's Street]] in the south. High Hillock Lane also connected with [[Crossbow Lane]] and [[Guards Court]].
