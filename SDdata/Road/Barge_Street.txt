==Location==
Barge Street was located in the Zhentil Keep's [[Zhentil Keep/Harbor District|Harbor District]] in the south section of the city. The road was located in the northwest corner of the Harbor District. It originated at the east gate to the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] and passed southwest.

==Notable Locations==
[[Oparil's Tower]], and dusty [[inn]], was located on Barge Street.
