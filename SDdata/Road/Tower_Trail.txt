The '''Tower Trail''' was minor avenue of hard-packed earth and [[gravel]] in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
This road extended off of [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] and ran runly paralel to it until terminating further south at the same street. It was met at the northeast by [[Hog Street]] and [[Nag Street]].
