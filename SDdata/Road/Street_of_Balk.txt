The '''Street of Balk''' was a road found within [[Tulmon]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p165">

==Notable Locations==
Many small chapels were located along the Street of Balk, including those to [[Chauntea]], [[Lathander]], and [[Oghma]].<ref name=EotSS-p165"/>
