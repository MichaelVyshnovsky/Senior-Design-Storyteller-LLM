'''Dancer's Mask Lane''' was a street in [[Silverymoon]].

==Description==
Dancer's Mask Lane was a curved street in the west side of [[Northbank]] that ran from the [[Moorgate]] to the wall in almost a half circle. Along its journey, it intersected [[Rallowglass Ride]] and the [[Ghostwalk (street)|Ghostwalk]].
