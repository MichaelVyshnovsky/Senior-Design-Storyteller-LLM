The Harbor Loop was a small rounded court found in the city of Westgate.
The Harbor Loop was found in the eastern section of the city. It began and ended along Silverpiece Way, looping toward Westgates harbor. The small street known as the Wizards Cut connected to the middle of the Harbor Loop. Several prominent families, including the Cormaerils and Ssemms, owned docks touching the street.
