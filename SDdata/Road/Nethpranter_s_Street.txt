Nethpranters Street was a street of corduroy-logs in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.
This street ran southeast off of Sleepers Walk, running roughly parallel to the Wide Way. Along the way it intersected Slipstone Street and Quill Alley, until eventually terminating on the Court of the White Bull.
