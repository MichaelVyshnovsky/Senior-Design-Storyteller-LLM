'''Lutemaker Street''' was a street in [[Lyrabar]].{{cite book/The Rage||1}}

==Description==
Showing little of Lyrabar's weal, the district around Lutemaker street bore crumbling brick tenements and rookeries.

==Notable Locations==
The pawnbroker [[Hezza]] had a shop on that street.

==History==
On [[Hammer 16]] of {{yearlink|1373}}, [[Gorstag Helder]] stole an [[emerald]] pendant from [[Hezza]]'s pawn shop. The pawnbroker caught him in the act, and after a fight, the thief escaped.
