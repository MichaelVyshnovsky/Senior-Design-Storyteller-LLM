Lutemaker Street was a street in Lyrabar.
Showing little of Lyrabars weal, the district around Lutemaker street bore crumbling brick tenements and rookeries.
The pawnbroker Hezza had a shop on that street.
On Hammer 16 of , Gorstag Helder stole an emerald pendant from Hezzas pawn shop. The pawnbroker caught him in the act, and after a fight, the thief escaped.
