==Location==
Star Street was located in east Zhentil Keep in the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]]. The road ran north to south, in between [[Dusk Street]] in the north and [[Realm Street]] in the south. Star Street was intesected by [[Traders' Way (Zhentil Keep)|Traders' Way]] near its midpoint.

==Notable Locations==
[[Joseph's Locks]] was the only prominent location along Star Street.
