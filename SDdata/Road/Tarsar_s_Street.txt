'''Tarsar's Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Tarsar's Street was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road ran roughly east to west in between [[Whaelgond Way]] in the west and [[Saerdoun Street]] in the east. Tarsar's Street was also intersected by [[Ussilbran Street]], [[Nindabar Street]], and [[Belzound Street]].
