Tarsars Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Tarsars Street was located in the southern section of Waterdeeps North Ward. The road ran roughly east to west in between Whaelgond Way in the west and Saerdoun Street in the east. Tarsars Street was also intersected by Ussilbran Street, Nindabar Street, and Belzound Street.
