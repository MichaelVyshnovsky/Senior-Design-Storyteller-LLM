'''Twelvedog Court''' was a small courtyard that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Twelvedog Court was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. The courtyard was located off of the eastern end of [[Burnt Inn Street]] south of the [[Fieldway]].
