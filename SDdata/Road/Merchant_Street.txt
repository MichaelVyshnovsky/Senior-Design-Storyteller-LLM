'''Merchant Street''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Merchant Street was located in Zhentil Keep's [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] in the southwest corner of the city. The road ran west to east roughly parallel to [[Border Street]] and the outer southern wall of the city. Merchant Street branched from the southern tip of [[Nool Street]] and passed east through the city before ending at the wall of the [[Zhentil Keep/Harbor District|Harbor District]] near the [[North Force Gate]]. Merchant Street was intersected by [[Beggars' Way]], [[Dixon Street]], and [[Brigand Way]].
