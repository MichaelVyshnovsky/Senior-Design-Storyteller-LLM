'''Wind Walk''' was a [[:Category:Roads|pathway]] nestled within the [[Sunset Mountains]] in the [[Western Heartlands]].{{Cite web |url=http://www.interplay.com/bgdarkalliance/bgda.html |title=[[wikipedia:en:Interplay Entertainment|Interplay Entertainment]] - ''[[Baldur's Gate: Dark Alliance]]'' - World - Areas - Winding Walk|accessdate=2024-02-16 |publisher=[[wikipedia:en:Interplay Entertainment|Interplay Entertainment]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20030704024130/http://www.interplay.com/bgdarkalliance/bgda.html |archivedate=2003-07-04 |quote= }}

==Description==
Travelers were urged to proceed cautiously to avoid a deadly fall from the cliff's edge.

==Geography==
The road led to the dwarven mines of the [[Bruenghor Clan]].

==History==
[[Vahn]], [[Adrianna]], and [[Kromlech Bruenghor]] traveled to the Sunset Mountains through a ''[[portal]]'' located in [[Xantam]]'s [[Xantam's Thieves Guild|Thieves Guild]]. The adventurers fought their way to the dwarven mines.
{{Sectstub}}

==Inhabitants==
The Wind Walk was infested by [[gnoll]]s, which posed a hazardous to travelers. The unlucky travelers could sometimes encounter [[ogre]]s, as well as savage [[yeti]]s and prowling [[wolves]].
