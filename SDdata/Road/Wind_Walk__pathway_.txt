Wind Walk was a pathway nestled within the Sunset Mountains in the Western Heartlands.
Travelers were urged to proceed cautiously to avoid a deadly fall from the cliffs edge.
The road led to the dwarven mines of the Bruenghor Clan.
Vahn, Adrianna, and Kromlech Bruenghor traveled to the Sunset Mountains through a portal located in Xantams Thieves Guild. The adventurers fought their way to the dwarven mines.
The Wind Walk was infested by gnolls, which posed a hazardous to travelers. The unlucky travelers could sometimes encounter ogres, as well as savage yetis and prowling wolves.
