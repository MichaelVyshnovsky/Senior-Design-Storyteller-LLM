Stallion Street was a minor avenue of dirt and gravel in the North Ward of Waterdeep.
Stallion Street ran from east to west, shooting off south-west from Trollmarch, then passing through Immar Street and Pony Way. It terminated in a dead-end, in the center of a block of buildings between Pony Way and Whaelgond Way.
* The residence of Maerik Thaelcloak.
* The editorial office of the Waterdeep Wazoo.
