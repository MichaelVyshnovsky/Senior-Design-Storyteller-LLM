'''Mraedara's Street''' was a road that crossed the entirety of [[Ravens Bluff/The Shutters|The Shutters]] neighborhood in the [[Ravens Bluff/Crow's End|Crow's End]] District of [[Ravens Bluff]] from north to south, and ended in the [[Pumpside]] neighborhood.

==Geography==
Mraedara's Street was only three city blocks in length. It began at [[Karen Court]] in The Shutters in the north, and moved straight south, crossing [[Hammerstone Street]], [[Drover's Run]], and ended on [[Conant Court]].
