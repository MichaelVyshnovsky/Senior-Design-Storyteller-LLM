The '''Dark Road''' was a street located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The Dark Road was located in central Zhentil Keep within the [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]]. The street touched two sections of the wall of the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]], curving around the northeast corner of the Inner Quarter. Along its path the Dark Road was intersected by the [[Lord's Way]] and [[Gold Street]].

==Notable Locations==
Several prominent landmarks of Zhentil Keep were located along the Dark Road including the [[Tower High]], [[the Last Stop]], and the residence of Magistrate [[Osconivon]].
