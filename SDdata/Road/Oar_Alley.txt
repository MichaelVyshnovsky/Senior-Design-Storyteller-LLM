'''Oar Alley''' was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and [[gravel]] in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
This alleyway ran from southwest to northeast, extending off of [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]] and terminating into a dead end.

==Notable Locations==
* The [[Blue Mermaid]] tavern stood at the southwest corner of this alley.
* [[Shippers' Hall]].
