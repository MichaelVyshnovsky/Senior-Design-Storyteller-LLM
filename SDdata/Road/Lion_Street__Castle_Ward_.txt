'''Lion Street''', which was also called '''Sea Lion Street''', was a small road located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Lion Street was found in southern Waterdeep within the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It passed north and northwest, branching from [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] in the south and ending at [[Tarnished Silver Alley]] to the north near the southern base of [[Mount Waterdeep]].
