'''Siren Lane''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Siren Lane was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. The road ran north to south, connecting [[Tharleon Street]] in the north to [[Keltarn Street]] in the south.
