'''Sternpost Street''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Sternpost Street ran from the southwest on the waterfront with [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] to the northeast to end on [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]] within Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. [[Asteril's Way]] merged with Sternpost Street just a few buildings south of its intersection with Ship Street.
