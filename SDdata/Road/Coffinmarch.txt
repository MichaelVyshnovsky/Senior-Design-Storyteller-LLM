The '''Coffinmarch''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The Coffinmarch was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]]. The road branched east off of the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] and ended at the entrance to the [[Waterdeep/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]] on [[Zendulth Street]]. Both [[Spindle Street]] and the [[Winter Path]] intersected the Coffinmarch.

==Notable Locations==
[[Dunblast Roofing Company]] sat on the south side of the eastern end of the Coffinmarch direcly in front of the City of the Dead.
