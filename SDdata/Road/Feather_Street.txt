'''Feather Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Feather Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. It originated on [[Delzorin Street]] where it branched southwest and ended at a major intersection that included [[Haltovar Street]], [[Zarimtar Street]], [[the Sutherlane]], and [[Darselune Street]]. Feather Street was also intersected by the [[Rough Road]].
