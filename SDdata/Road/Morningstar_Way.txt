'''Morningstar Way''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Morningstar Way was located in the northwest section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. It ran north to south, branching from the [[Street of Glances]] and terminating on the [[Street of Lances]] near the [[Trolltower]]. Morningstar Way was intersected by [[Aureenar Street]] near its midpoint.
