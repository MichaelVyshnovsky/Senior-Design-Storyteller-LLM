Kaoth Alley was a small road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Kaoth Alley was located in the southern section of Daggerford near the River Gate. The alley was nestled between Wall Street and Water Street.
Notable buildings located along Kaoth Alley included
* Watermens Circle
* Mariners Alliance
