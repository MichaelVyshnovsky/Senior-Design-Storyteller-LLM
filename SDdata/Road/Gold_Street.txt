Located in the Inner Quarter in central Zhentil Keep, Gold Street branched west from Raven Street near the center of the quarter and passed northeast to terminate at the wall of the Common Quarter. Gold Street crossed Flostrens Memory and the Dark Road.
The residences of Lord Ssorg and Magistrate Osconivon were located on Gold Street. The High Hall and Detention Center sat on the eastern end of Gold Street. The Zhentilar maintaned a barracks and armory on Gold Street.
