'''Cedar Street''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Cedar Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It intersected the southern end of [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] near the waterfront.
