'''Wagonwheel Ride''' was a small street found in the city of [[Westgate]].

==Location==
Wagonwheel Ride was found in the west section of the city. It ran was connected with, and ran perpendicular to, [[Westgate Market Street]] and [[Silverpiece Way]]. [[Sword Lane]] bisected Wagonwheel Ride near its middle.
