The '''Street of the Gods''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Located in the [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]] in central Zhentil Keep, the Street of the Gods cut north to south, touching the wall of the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] and ending south [[Elephstron Way]]. Along the way, the Street of the Gods was crossed by [[Gold Street]] and the [[Lord's Way]].

==Notable Locations==
The residences of Lord [[Phandymm]], Lord [[Ssorg]] and Lord [[Halaster (Zhentil Keep)|Halaster]] were located on the Street of the Gods. Zhentil Keep's [[Arena (Zhentil Keep)|Arena]], a [[Zhentarim]] warehouse, and a [[Zhentilar]] guardpost were other notable locations.
