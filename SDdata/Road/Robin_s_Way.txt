'''Robin's Way''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Robin's Way was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]]. The road branched southeast from the [[Rising Ride]] and ended on [[Caravan Street]] a few blocks away. [[Juth Alley]] and [[Tezambril's Street]] also connected with Robin's Way.
