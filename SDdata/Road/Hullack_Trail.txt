The '''Hullack Trail''' was a trail running through southeastern [[Cormyr]].

==Locations==
Following the shores of the [[Thunder River]], [[Wyvernwater]], and the [[Wyvernflow]], it linked [[Thunderstone]], [[Hultail]], [[Juniril]], and [[Wheloon]], as well as the [[Unknown Shrine]], via short connecting trails.

==History==
The petitioners and adventurers duped at the [[Temple of Mystra (Wheloon)|Temple of Mystra]] in Wheloon in the [[1374|Year of Lightning Storms, 1374 DR]] were told to cross the Hullack Trail and go overland to the Unknown Shrine, but they could choose to travel along the trail and go around.
