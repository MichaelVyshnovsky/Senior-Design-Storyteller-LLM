==Location==
Ocean Way was located in the [[Zhentil Keep/Harbor District|Harbor District]] of south Zhentil Keep. The road was located on the eastern side of the Harbor District. It originated on [[Border Street]] and passed northeast to terminate along the waterfront of the docks. [[Sullen Street]] connected with Ocean Way.
