Cobble Road was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.
Cobble Road was located in the Common Quarter of west Zhentil Keep. The road originated at a major intersection directly in front of the Trail Gate and passed southeast through the quarter. It bordered the wall of the Inner Quarter for most of its length. Cobble Road was intersected by Wyvern Road, Plague Street, Fortress Street, Nool Street, Dixon Street, and Pain Street.
The popular Zhentilar tavern called the Axe and the Minotaur was located on Cobble Road.
