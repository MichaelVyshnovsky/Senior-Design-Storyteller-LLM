Battle Way was located in Zhentil Keeps Common Quarter in the east section of the city. The road originated on Realm Street, branching south near the eastern outer wall before connecting with Vice Street and turning southwest to terminate several blocks later.
The Serpents Eye inn and a Zhentilar guardpost were located on Vice Street.
