==Location==
Battle Way was located in Zhentil Keep's [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] in the east section of the city. The road originated on [[Realm Street]], branching south near the eastern outer wall before connecting with [[Vice Street]] and turning southwest to terminate several blocks later.

==Notable Locations==
The [[Serpent's Eye]] [[inn]] and a [[Zhentilar]] guardpost were located on Vice Street.
