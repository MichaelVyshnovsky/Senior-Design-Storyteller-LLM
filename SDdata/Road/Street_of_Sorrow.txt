The '''Street of Sorrow''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The Street of Sorrow was located in east Zhentil Keep in the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]]. Originating on [[Dusk Street]] in the north, the Street of Sorrow passed south through the city before terminating at [[Moonsea Avenue]] in southeast Zhentil Keep. The road was intersected by [[Traders' Way (Zhentil Keep)|Traders' Way]], [[Realm Street]], [[Stall Street]], and [[Ale Street]].

==Notable Locations==
Several barracks for the [[Zhentilar]] were located along the Street of Sorrow.
