The Street of Sorrow was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the midâ€“14 century DR.
The Street of Sorrow was located in east Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. Originating on Dusk Street in the north, the Street of Sorrow passed south through the city before terminating at Moonsea Avenue in southeast Zhentil Keep. The road was intersected by Traders Way, Realm Street, Stall Street, and Ale Street.
Several barracks for the Zhentilar were located along the Street of Sorrow.
