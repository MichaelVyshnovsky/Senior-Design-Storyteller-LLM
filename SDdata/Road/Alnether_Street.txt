'''Alnether Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Alnether Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. The road orignated on [[Bazaar Street]], branching south where it ran parallel to the [[Street of Silver]] to the west and [[Warrior's Way]] to the east. Alnether Street merged with the western end of [[Cymbril's Walk]] near the Street of Silver's intersection with [[Keltarn Street]]. [[The Prowl]] branched off the southeast end of Alnether Street.
