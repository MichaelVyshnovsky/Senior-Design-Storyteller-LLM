Alnether Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Alnether Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward. The road orignated on Bazaar Street, branching south where it ran parallel to the Street of Silver to the west and Warriors Way to the east. Alnether Street merged with the western end of Cymbrils Walk near the Street of Silvers intersection with Keltarn Street. The Prowl branched off the southeast end of Alnether Street.
