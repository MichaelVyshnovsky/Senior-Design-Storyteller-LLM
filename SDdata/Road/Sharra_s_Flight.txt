Sharras Flight was a small back alley and courtyard that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Sharras Flight was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. The alley branched east off of the Street of Whispers at several locations, passing through a small neighborhood and connecting with the southern side of Moonstar Alley. An entrance to the Waterdeeps sewers was also located here.
The alley was named for Sharra of the Invisble Dragon.
