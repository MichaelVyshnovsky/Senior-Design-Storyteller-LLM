The '''Street of Spells''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The Street of Spells was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. It ran between [[Pharra's Alley]] and [[Flint Street]], bording much of the [[House of Wonder]]'s southeast side.
