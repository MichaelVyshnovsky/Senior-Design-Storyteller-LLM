Fishgut Alley was a small backalley that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Fishgut Alley was located in the south section of Waterdeeps Dock Ward. It was wedged between Spices Street to the north and west, and Wharf Street to the east and south. Fishgut Alley originated on Spices Street, branching south before brancing again several times to eventually terminate at two locations on Dock Street.
