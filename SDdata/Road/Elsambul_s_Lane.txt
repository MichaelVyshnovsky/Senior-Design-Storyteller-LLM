Elsambuls Lane was a back alley located in Waterdeep, City of Splendors, during the 14 century DR.
Elsambuls Lane was located in the north section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward. The alley ran north to south in between the Street of the Sword to the east and Warriors Way to the west. It was intersected by Lamp Street in its northern half and by Cymbrils Walk in its southern half.
