Goblin Way was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.
Goblin Way was found within northwest Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. It connected Fortress Street in the north with Warrior Street in the south.
It was one of Zhentil Keeps smaller streets at roughly  in length.
A Zhentilar barracks was found at the intersection of Goblin Way with Warrior Street.
