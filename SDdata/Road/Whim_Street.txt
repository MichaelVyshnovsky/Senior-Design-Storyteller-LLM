'''Whim Street''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Whim Street was located in the southeast section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. The road passed roughly east to west, branching from [[Sul Street]] in the east to eventually merge with [[the Sutherlane]] after turning southwest. [[Mendever Street]] also connected with Whim Street.
