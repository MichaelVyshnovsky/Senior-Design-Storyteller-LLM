Hammestone Street was one of the two streets that formed the southern border of The Shutters neighborhood in the Crows End District of Ravens Bluff.
Hammestone Street began at the Ravens Bluff Sanitation Facility, located in the Pumpside neighborhood, and moved west in a straight line, crossing Helhavryns Walk, Landleen Lane, Burning Shield Lane, Stonekeep Concourse, and ended on Mraedaras Street where a forested area took over the eastern side of the district.
*Brothers Galgolar Pawnshop, located at the very beginning on the street on Mraedaras Street.
