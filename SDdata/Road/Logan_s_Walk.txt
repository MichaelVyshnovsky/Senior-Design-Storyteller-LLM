'''Logan's Walk''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Logan's Walk was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. It branched northeast off of [[Gawenknife Street]], passing through a small neighborhood before ending on [[Stench Alley]].
