The Street of Stelae was a prominent avenue located in the City of Brass on the Elemental Plane of Fire in 1367 DR.
This street was one of the main avenues of the city, running west from the Charcoal Palace in the Pyraculum to the harbors.
Hundreds of large pillars lined the street, decorated with scenes of the efreet dominance over the other races of the Plane of Fire.
