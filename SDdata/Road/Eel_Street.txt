Eel Street was a major road in the asteroid city of Bral.
Eel Street began with an intersection with Lamp Street on the starboard side of the asteroid and ran to its port side at Anchor Street, covering most of the width of the Low City. Along the way, it crossed Hammer Street, Grand Street, and Dragon Street, for a total distance of roughly . It formed the trailing-edge borders of Giff-town and Shou-town.
