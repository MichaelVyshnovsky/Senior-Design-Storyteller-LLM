'''Coachlamp Lane''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Coachlamp Lane was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]]. The road originated on the southern end of the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] where it branched east, crossed [[Carter's Way]], then turned southeast to end within a neighborhood at a deadend.

==Notable Locations==
[[Hlakken Stables]] and [[Tehmak's Coaches]] were both located along Coachlamp Lane.
