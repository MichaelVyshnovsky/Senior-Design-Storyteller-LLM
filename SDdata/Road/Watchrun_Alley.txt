'''Watchrun Alley'''<ref group="note">Map 7 of [[City System]] names this alley as "Watchturn Alley", but multiple other sources are consistent with the name Watchrun Alley so this is believed to be an error. was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Watchrun Alley was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. The alley ran between [[Gut Alley]] in the northeast and [[Nelnuk's Walk]] in the southwest. It originated on Gut Alley, branching southwest to end on Nelnuk's Walk. Along its path Watchrun Alley was intersected by [[Redcloak Lane]] and [[Drawn Sword Alley]].

==History==
The ally's name was derived from the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]] to hurry across this section of the Dock Ward. [[Thieves]] and pranksters were known to string trip wires across the alley to cause havoc.
