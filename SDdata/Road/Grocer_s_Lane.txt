Grocers Lane was a small street located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Grocers Lane was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter. The street ran west to east through the Adventurers Quarter, dividing it into a northern and southern half. Weeping Maidens Run connected with the north side of Grocers Lane near its midpoint, and Snake Alley connected from the south on its western end.
