Gorl Street was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Gorl Street was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It originated on the the Street of Glances where it branched southwest to end on the Street of the Singing Dolphin just one building north of Ivory Street. Gorl Street also connected with Sniff Alley and Staghunters Way.
