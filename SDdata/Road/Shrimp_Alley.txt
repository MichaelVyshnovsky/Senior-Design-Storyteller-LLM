Shrimp Alley, which was also called Shrimp Street, was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Shrimp Alley was located in Waterdeeps Dock Ward. The alley off of the western side of Snail Street at the intersection with Presper Street. Shrimp Alley passed south for a bit before turning southwest near its intersection with Pressbow Lane to terminate on Ship Street. Shrimp Alley was intersected on its north side near the midway point by the Street of Six Casks.
