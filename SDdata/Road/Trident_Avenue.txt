Trident Avenue was located in south Zhentil Keep within the Harbor District. The road originated in the east near the waterfront and passed west for several blocks before ending at the wall of the Common Quarter.
A Zhentarim warehouse and barracks for the Zhentilar were located on Trident Avenue.
