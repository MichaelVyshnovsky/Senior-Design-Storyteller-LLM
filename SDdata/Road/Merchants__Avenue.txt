'''Merchants' Avenue''' was located in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Merchants' Avenue branched off of the [[Road to Enlightenment]] near the juncture of the [[South District]], [[Peasants' District]], and [[Pilgrims' District]]. The road traveled northwest toward the [[Grand Bazaar]] in the [[Merchants' District]], serving as a boundary between the [[Central District]] and Peasants' District for much of its length.
