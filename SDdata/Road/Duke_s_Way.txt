Dukes Way was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Dukes Way began at the Caravan Gate entering Daggerford from the west. It continued east through the center of the city before terminating at the Ducal Castle which covered most of eastern Daggerford.
The Hill Road intersected Dukes Way at the base of the landrise the separated the ducal estate from the rest of Daggerford, while the High Road branched south from the Hill Road near the drill field. Notable buildings located along Dukes Way included
* The Ducal Castle
* Daggerford Inn
* The Grand Playhouse
* Kryptgarden Scrolls
* Morninglow Tower
* Oryvs Cloth Emporium
