'''Shield Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Shield Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] and formed part of the western border of the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road branched south from [[Skulls Street]] and continued due south to eventually end on [[Trader's Way (Waterdeep)|Trader's Way]] at the border with the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. Other streets intersecting Shield Street included [[Immar Street]], [[Chasso's Trot]], [[Vondil Street]], [[Delzorin Street]], [[Rough Road]], [[Sulmor Street]], [[Hassantyr's Street]], [[Wrightstone Street]], [[Julthoon Street]], and [[Manycats Alley]].
