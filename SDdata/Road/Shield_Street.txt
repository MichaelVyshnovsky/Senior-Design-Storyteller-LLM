Shield Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Shield Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward and formed part of the western border of the North Ward. The road branched south from Skulls Street and continued due south to eventually end on Traders Way at the border with the Castle Ward. Other streets intersecting Shield Street included Immar Street, Chassos Trot, Vondil Street, Delzorin Street, Rough Road, Sulmor Street, Hassantyrs Street, Wrightstone Street, Julthoon Street, and Manycats Alley.
