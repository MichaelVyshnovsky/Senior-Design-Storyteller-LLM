'''Brightbuckler Street''', or '''Red Sails Way''', was a street that stretched north across the western length of [[Tlasbras]], a neighborhood in the [[Southside District]] of [[Ravens Bluff]].

==Geography==
This street stretched between the western ends of [[Hippocampus Street]] to the south and [[Zeldazar Street]] to the north.

Along the way between those two ends, the eastern side of Brightbuckler Street was a terminus to  [[Whisper Lane]] and was intersected by both [[Castles Way]] and [[Deepaxe Way]].

==Notable Locations==
* [[Pazlar's Starcharts and Landmaps]] stood on the corner of Brightbuckler and Castles Way.
