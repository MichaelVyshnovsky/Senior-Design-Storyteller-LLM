'''Pass Ride''' was a small road found in far eastern [[Tethyr]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}<ref name=EotSS.M">

==Geography==
Pass Ride branched from [[Ithal Road]], connecting the keep of [[Barakmordin]] in the north with the town of [[Kzelter]] to the southeast. Pass Ride travelled between the [[Omlarandin Mountains]] and [[Forest of Mir]], cutting through the western section of the [[Plains of Pehrrifaal]], for approximately {{SI|120|mi|k|long=yes}} before terminating at Kzelter.<ref name=EotSS.M"/>

==Notable Locations==
The town of [[Ithal Pass]] was located along the road's halfway point.<ref name=EotSS.M"/>
