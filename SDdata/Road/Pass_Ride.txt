Pass Ride was a small road found in far eastern Tethyr.
Pass Ride branched from Ithal Road, connecting the keep of Barakmordin in the north with the town of Kzelter to the southeast. Pass Ride travelled between the Omlarandin Mountains and Forest of Mir, cutting through the western section of the Plains of Pehrrifaal, for approximately  before terminating at Kzelter.
The town of Ithal Pass was located along the roads halfway point.
