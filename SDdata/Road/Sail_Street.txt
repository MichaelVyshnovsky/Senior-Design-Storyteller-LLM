'''Sail Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Sail Street originated in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] where [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] met [[Fishgut Court]]. The road passed west into the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] near the waterfront to end at the junction with [[Coin Alley]]. Sail Street was intersected by [[Lion Street (Castle Ward)|Lion Street]], [[Tarnished Silver Alley]], [[Lackpurse Lane]], [[Sakiir's Street]], and [[Belnimbra's Street]].
