The '''Pevadaas Trail''' was a small trail found within the [[Alimir Mountains]] in [[Calimshan]] near the [[Lake of Steam]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}<ref name=EotSS.M">

==Geography==
The trail branched from the [[Narrows Trail]], cutting through the northern half of the mountain range and passing from the northeast near the shore of the Lake of Steam to the southwest toward the [[Spider Swamp]]. The Pevadaas Trail was roughly {{SI|60|mi|k|long=yes}} long.<ref name=EotSS.M"/>

==Notable Locations==
The [[Monastery of St. Fanal]] was located along the Pevadaas Trail on the northwestern edge of the Alimir Mountains.<ref name=EotSS.M"/>
