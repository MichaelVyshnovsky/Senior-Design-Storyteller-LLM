'''Weeping Maiden's Run''' was a small street located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}

==Location==
Weeping Maiden's Run was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]]. The street cut through the Adventurers' Quarter, entering from the northwest where it branched southeast from [[Slop Street]], before ending on [[Grocer's Lane]] at [[Kolat Towers]].
