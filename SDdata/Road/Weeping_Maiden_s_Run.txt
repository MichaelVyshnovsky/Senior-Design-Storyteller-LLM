Weeping Maidens Run was a small street located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Weeping Maidens Run was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter. The street cut through the Adventurers Quarter, entering from the northwest where it branched southeast from Slop Street, before ending on Grocers Lane at Kolat Towers.
