'''Haltovar Street'''<ref group="note">Haltover Street is used in some sources with less frequency. was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Haltovar Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. It branched south from [[Darselune Street]] and [[Feather Street]] at a major intersection with [[the Sutherlane]] and [[Zarimtar Street]]. Haltovar Street terminated on [[Julthoon Street]] at the border with the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. [[Gulzindar Street]] intersected Haltovar Street on its west side near its midpoint.
