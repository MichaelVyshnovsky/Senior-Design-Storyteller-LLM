The Street of Nine Steps was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
The Street of Nine Steps was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward. The road branched east from Caravan Street just south of Tilmans Lane, turned south, east, then north through a small neighborhood, and eventually ended on Wall Street near the Trollwall.
The Old Monster Shop was located on the eastern end of the Street of Nine Steps.
