'''Copper Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Copper Street was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] and formed part of the western border of the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road branched south from [[Skulls Street]] and continued due south to eventually end on [[Trader's Way (Waterdeep)|Trader's Way]] at the border with the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]].
