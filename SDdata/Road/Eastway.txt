The Eastway was a road from Bryn Shander to Easthaven within the Ten Towns of Icewind Dale.
The gravel road began in Bryn Shander traveling east toward the community of Easthaven and the banks of Lac Dinneshere.
*Lac Dinneshere
By 1351 DR, the road aided Easthaven in building a fishing fleet rivaling Caer-Dineval.
