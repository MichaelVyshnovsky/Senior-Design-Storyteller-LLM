Wastrel Alley was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Wastrel Alley was found in Waterdeeps Dock Ward. The road originated on Belnimbras Street in the north where it branched south and passed southwest to terminate at Fish Street just southeast of Fishgut Court. Other roads that intersected Wastrel Alley included Adder Lane and Nelnuks Walk.
A tavern called Darths Dolphyntyde was located on the east side of Wastrel Alley.
