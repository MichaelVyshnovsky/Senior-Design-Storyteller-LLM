'''Wastrel Alley''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Wastrel Alley was found in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. The road originated on [[Belnimbra's Street]] in the north where it branched south and passed southwest to terminate at [[Fish Street (Waterdeep)|Fish Street]] just southeast of [[Fishgut Court]]. Other roads that intersected Wastrel Alley included [[Adder Lane]] and [[Nelnuk's Walk]].

==Notable Locations==
A tavern called [[Darth's Dolphyntyde]] was located on the east side of Wastrel Alley.
