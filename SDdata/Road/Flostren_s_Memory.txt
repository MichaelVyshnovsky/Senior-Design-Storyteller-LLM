'''Flostren's Memory''' was a street located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Flostren's Memory was located in central Zhentil Keep within the [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]]. The street ran in between [[the Lord's Way]] in the north and the wall of the [[Zhentil Keep/Harbor District|Harbor District]] in the south. Flostren's Memory was crossed by [[Gold Street]] and [[Elephstron Way]].

==Notable Locations==
The residences of Lord [[Taradril Aumraven]], Lord [[Naerh]], and Lord [[Marsh Belwintle]] were located along Flostren's Memory. A [[Zhentilar]] guardpost sat at the intersection with Elephestron Way.
