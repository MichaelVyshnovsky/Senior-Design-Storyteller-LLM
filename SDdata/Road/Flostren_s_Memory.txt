Flostrens Memory was a street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the midâ€“14 century DR.
Flostrens Memory was located in central Zhentil Keep within the Inner Quarter. The street ran in between the Lords Way in the north and the wall of the Harbor District in the south. Flostrens Memory was crossed by Gold Street and Elephstron Way.
The residences of Lord Taradril Aumraven, Lord Naerh, and Lord Marsh Belwintle were located along Flostrens Memory. A Zhentilar guardpost sat at the intersection with Elephestron Way.
