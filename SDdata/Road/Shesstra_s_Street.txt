'''Shesstra's Street''' was a road paved in hard-packed dirt and [[gravel]] in the city of [[Waterdeep]],

==Geography==
This road ran from east to west, cutting through [[Snail Street]] as it spanned between [[Book Street]] and [[Gut Alley]].

Just north of [[Blackstar Lane]], an unnamed, dead-end alleyway opened off of Shesstra's Street.<ref><ref><ref>
