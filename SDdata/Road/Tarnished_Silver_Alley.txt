'''Tarnished Silver Alley''' was a small road located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5265}}

==Location==
Tarnished Silver Alley originated on [[Sail Street]] near the border with the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. The road branched north and then passed west near the base of [[Mount Waterdeep]]. It then merged with [[Coin Alley]] just west of the [[Old Temple Lot]]. [[Lion Street (Castle Ward)|Lion Street]] merged with Tarnished Silver Alley near its midpoint. The road was found in southern Waterdeep within the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]].

==Notable Locations==
A tavern called [[the Sailors' Own]] was found where Tarnished Silver Alley met Sail Street.
