'''Spiderweb Alley''' was a small backalley that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Spiderweb Alley was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. The alley branched off of [[Snail Street]], cutting west briefly before terminating at a dead end. Spiderweb Alley was intersected on its north side by [[Three Daggers Alley]] and on its south side by [[Ghemmer's Alley]].
