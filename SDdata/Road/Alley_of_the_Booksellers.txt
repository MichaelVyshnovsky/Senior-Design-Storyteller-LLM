The Alley of the Booksellers was a small but important street in Rogosto in 1367 DR.
Many researchers and wizards frequented the Alley of the Booksellers, patronizing the many shops offering books and scrolls for sale. The selection of reading material available here was stocked with selections from all regions of Zakhara.
Several prominent sages lived on the street, selling books and offering their expertise in order to fund their own research.
