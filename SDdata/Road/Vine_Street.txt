Vine Street was a road in the city of Loudwater in the Delimbiyr Vale in the 14 century DR.
It lay in the western part of Loudwater.This is assumed from the similar names and the nature of the traffic.
It was a major thoroughfare, taking over from Pools Way where it turned toward the docks and receiving much traffic of pedestrians, horses, and wagons. It was dense with the shops that attracted them.
