Street of the Tusks was a street of hard-packed earth and gravelIn the 1-edition sourcebook Waterdeep and the North, this locations name was misprinted as Street of the Turks.
This road began at Burnt Wagon Way and ran south to the High Road, crossing Spendthrift Alley and Vellarrs Lane along the way.
* Auroras Realms Shop, the Trade Wards outlet of the retail chain Auroras Emporium, was located in the northwest corner of where this street met the High Road.
An entrance to the sewers of Waterdeep was located at the mouth of a dead-end alley opening that was just east off this street.
