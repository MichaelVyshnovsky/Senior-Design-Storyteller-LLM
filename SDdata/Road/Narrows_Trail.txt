The Narrows Trail was a small trail found along the slopes of the Alimir Mountains near the Lake of Steam.
The trail originated in the north, branching from Darkhopes Road northwest of Mintar. The Narrows Trail passed south, wedged between the eastern face of the Alimir Mountains and the western shore of the Lake of Steam. The trail covered approximately  before ending at Tulmon.
