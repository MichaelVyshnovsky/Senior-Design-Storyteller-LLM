'''Prestige Lane''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Prestige Lane was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. It originated on the eastern end of the [[Fieldway]], branching southwest through a neighborhood and crossing [[Saltpork Street]] to end at [[Twelvedog Court]].
