'''Toll Street''' was a small road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Toll Street was located in Zhentil Keep's [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] in the west section of the city. The road ran east to west in between [[Pain Street]] in the east and [[Dixon Street]] in the west. It measured {{SI|300|ft|long=yes}} in length.

==Notable Locations==
Several notable buildings were located along Toll Street where it intersected others streets. [[The Whipwalker's Cloak]] sat at its junction with Pain Street, and the business of the [[Celebrated Embalmer Morge]] at the junction with Dixon Street.
