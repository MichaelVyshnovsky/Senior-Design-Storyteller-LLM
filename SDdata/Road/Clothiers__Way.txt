'''Clothiers' Way''' was located in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
Clothiers' Way branched off of the [[South Road]] at the [[Gulf Gate]], entering Huzuz from the south and passing northeast between the [[Peasants' District]], [[Northwest District]] and [[Clothiers' District]] before terminating at the [[Grand Bazaar]] in the [[Merchants' District]].
