'''Bridge Street''' was a road located in the city of [[Skuld]] in [[Mulhorand]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|490 Skuld}}

==Location==
Bridge Street was found in the western section of Skuld in the [[Skuld/Merchant District|Merchant District]] near the coast of the [[Alamber Sea]]. It ran north to south through the city, originating in the south at the [[Bridge Gate (Skuld)|Bridge Gate]] near the [[Skuld/Naval Yard|naval yards]] and the [[River of Shadows]], and passing north to end at another gate located on an inner city wall.

==Notable Locations==
A moneychanger and market were located along Bridge Street.
