The '''Dragoneye Way''' was a small road connecting [[Suzail]] to [[Marsember]] in [[Cormyr]].{{Cite web|url=http://wizards.com/DnD/Article.aspx?x=dnd/drback/20080718|title=Cormyr|author=[[Brian R. ==Description==
The Dragoneye Way ran west to east from Suzail along the northwest coast of the [[Dragonmere]] for approximately {{SI|50|mi|long=yes}} before reaching Marsember.
