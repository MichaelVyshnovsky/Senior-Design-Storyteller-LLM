Rhabie Promenade was a main street that passed through several districts within the city of Ravens Bluff.
The Promenade was a diagonally oriented street extending from a five-point intersection in the Swordspoint neighborhood of the citys Temple District. It extended southwest through the Altarside neighborhood, and continued in the Crows End district, through Anvil, before it wrapped around to the western wall of the Harbor District in Bitterstone.
