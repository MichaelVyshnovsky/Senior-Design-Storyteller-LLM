'''Rhabie Promenade''' was a main street that passed through several districts within the city of [[Ravens Bluff]].

==Description==
The Promenade was a diagonally oriented street extending from a five-point intersection in the [[Swordspoint]] neighborhood of the city's [[Temple District (Ravens Bluff)|Temple District]]. It extended southwest through the [[Altarside]] neighborhood, and continued in the [[Crow's End]] district, through [[Anvil]], before it wrapped around to the western wall of the [[Harbor District (Ravens Bluff)|Harbor District]] in [[Bitterstone]].
