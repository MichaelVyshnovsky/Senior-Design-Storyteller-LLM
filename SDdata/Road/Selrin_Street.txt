'''Selrin Street''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Selrin Street was located in the northeast section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. Selrin Street connected [[Horn Street]] with the [[Trollmarch]]. It was intersected near its midpoint by [[Immar Street]].
