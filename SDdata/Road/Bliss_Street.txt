'''Bliss Street''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Bliss Street was located in the western section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. It branched east from [[Westwall Street]] and ended on the western curve of [[Ivory Street]] after passing several buildings.
