'''Canter Street''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Canter Street was located in the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] in the southwest corner of Zhentil Keep. The road originated at the entrance to the [[Zhentil Keep/Harbor District|Harbor District]] and passed west through the city to end at the intersection of [[Beggars' Way]] and [[Fish Street (Zhentil Keep)|Fish Street]]. Canter street was crossed by [[Brigand Way]] and [[Dixon Street]].

==Notable Locations==
A [[Zhentilar]] guardpost was located along Canter Street at the entrance to the Harbor District.
