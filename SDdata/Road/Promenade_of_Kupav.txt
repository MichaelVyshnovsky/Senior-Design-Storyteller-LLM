The '''Promenade of Kupav''' was a semi-circular path in [[Myth Nantar]] that covered the gap between the [[Three Gates Reef]] and the city's quarters. The promenade was paved with mosaics, and was extensively damaged during the [[Twelfth Serôs War]].{{cite book/Sea of Fallen Stars|173}}
