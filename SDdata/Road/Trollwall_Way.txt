'''Trollwall Way''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Trollwall Way was located in the western and central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. It ran along the [[Trollwall]] on the western side of the Field Ward in between the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] and the [[Trollyard]].
