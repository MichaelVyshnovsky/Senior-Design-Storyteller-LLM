'''Anchoret's Alley''' was a small side alley that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Anchoret's Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]]. It originated on [[the Forcebar]] where it branched south through a small neighborhood, eventually ending on [[Anchoret's Court]] just north of the [[Specterwalk]].
