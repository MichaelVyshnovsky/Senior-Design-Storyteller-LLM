==Location==
Zhent Avenue was located in southern Zhentil Keep within the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]]. The street ran in between [[Bridge Street]] in the east and the major intersection of [[Teshwave Street]], [[Center Street]], and [[Twilight Street]] in the west. Zhent Avenue ran the length of the Foreign Quarter, roughly bisecting it into a northern and southern half.
