Zhent Avenue was located in southern Zhentil Keep within the Foreign Quarter. The street ran in between Bridge Street in the east and the major intersection of Teshwave Street, Center Street, and Twilight Street in the west. Zhent Avenue ran the length of the Foreign Quarter, roughly bisecting it into a northern and southern half.
