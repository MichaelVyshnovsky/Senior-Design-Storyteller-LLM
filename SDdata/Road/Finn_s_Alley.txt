'''Finn's Alley''' was a small street found in the coastal town of [[Aithelar]] in [[Callidyrr]] on the [[Moonshae Isles]] in {{YearlinkName|1345}}.{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
Finn's Alley was found in central Aithelar between [[Callidyrr Ride]] and [[Bottle Lane]]. It intersected [[Aithe High Street]] near the center of town.
