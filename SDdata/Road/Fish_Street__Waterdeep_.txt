'''Fish Street''' was a street that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Fish Street was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It ran through the center of the Dock Ward, connecting [[Snail Street]] in the east with [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] in the west. Other streets connecting with Fish Street included [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]], [[Bitter Alley]], [[Odd Street]], [[Two Flask Alley]], [[Wastrel Alley]], and [[Gut Alley]].
