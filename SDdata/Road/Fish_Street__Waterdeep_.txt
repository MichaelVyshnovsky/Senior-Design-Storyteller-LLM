Fish Street was a street that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Fish Street was located in Waterdeeps Dock Ward. It ran through the center of the Dock Ward, connecting Snail Street in the east with Dock Street in the west. Other streets connecting with Fish Street included Ship Street, Bitter Alley, Odd Street, Two Flask Alley, Wastrel Alley, and Gut Alley.
