The Street of the Manticore was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
The Street of the Manticore was located in the central section of Waterdeeps North Ward. The road ran roughly east to west in between Vhezoar Street in the west and Whaelgond Way in the east. Several smaller roads intersected the Street of the Manticore including Little Street and Sprangalars Swordcut.
