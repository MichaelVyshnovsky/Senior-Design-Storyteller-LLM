The '''Street of the Manticore''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The Street of the Manticore was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road ran roughly east to west in between [[Vhezoar Street]] in the west and [[Whaelgond Way]] in the east. Several smaller roads intersected the Street of the Manticore including [[Little Street]] and [[Sprangalar's Swordcut]].
