Winter Walk was a small street located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.
Winter Walk was located in western Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. It originated on Nool Street in the west near the citys exterior wall, then passed east until it connected with Dixon Street. Silver Street intersected Winter Walk near its origin.
