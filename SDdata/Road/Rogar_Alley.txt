'''Rogar Alley''' was a small road found in the city of [[Daggerford]] in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
Rogar Alley was located in the western section of Daggerford. It connected to the [[High Road (Daggerford)|High Road]] at two different points and [[Wall Street (Daggerford)|Wall Street]] near the [[Caravan Gate]]. [[Guild Alley]] branched off the southern arm of Rogar Alley.

==Notable Locations==
Notable buildings located along Rogar Alley included:

* [[Oleg's Weaponforge]]
* [[Taverner's Association]]
* [[Scribes' Friendship]]
* The [[Merchants' Guild (Daggerford)|Merchants' Guild]]
* [[Derf's Skin Art]]
