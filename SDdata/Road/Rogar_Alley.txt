Rogar Alley was a small road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Rogar Alley was located in the western section of Daggerford. It connected to the High Road at two different points and Wall Street near the Caravan Gate. Guild Alley branched off the southern arm of Rogar Alley.
Notable buildings located along Rogar Alley included
* Olegs Weaponforge
* Taverners Association
* Scribes Friendship
* The Merchants Guild
* Derfs Skin Art
