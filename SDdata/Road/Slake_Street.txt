'''Slake Street''' was a street in the Living District on the eastern side of [[Arrabar]] in the [[Vilhon Reach]].

==Geography==
Slake Street was a street that was located not far from one of the large city square markets of the Living District. The district was located to the east in Arrabar, the area inhabited by most commoners of the city.
