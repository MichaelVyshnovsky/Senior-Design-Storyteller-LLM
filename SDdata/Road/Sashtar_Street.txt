'''Sashtar Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Sashtar Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. It originated on the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] at the border with the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] where it branched east, turning northeast at the intersection with [[Thunderstaff Way]] to end on [[Immar Street]]. Sashtar Street was also intersected by [[Windborne Way]].
