Sashtar Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Sashtar Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeeps North Ward. It originated on the High Road at the border with the Sea Ward where it branched east, turning northeast at the intersection with Thunderstaff Way to end on Immar Street. Sashtar Street was also intersected by Windborne Way.
