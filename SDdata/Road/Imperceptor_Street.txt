'''Imperceptor Street''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Located in the [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]] in central Zhentil Keep, Imperceptor Street cut south from [[Elephstron Way]] near the [[Black Altar]] to end at the wall of the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]].

==Notable Locations==
A [[Zhentilar]] guardpost and barracks were located on Imperceptor Street, as was [[Edwin's Mercantile Traders]].
