Imperceptor Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.
Located in the Inner Quarter in central Zhentil Keep, Imperceptor Street cut south from Elephstron Way near the Black Altar to end at the wall of the Common Quarter.
A Zhentilar guardpost and barracks were located on Imperceptor Street, as was Edwins Mercantile Traders.
