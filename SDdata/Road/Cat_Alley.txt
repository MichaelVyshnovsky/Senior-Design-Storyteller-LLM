'''Cat Alley''' was an alley that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Cat Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. The alley originated on the [[Street of Bells]] where it branched west to pass through a small neighborhood east of the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]]. A smaller alley called [[the Cat's Tail]] branched off the south end of Cat Alley.
