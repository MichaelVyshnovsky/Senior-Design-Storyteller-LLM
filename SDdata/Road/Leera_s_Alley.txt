'''Leera's Alley''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Leera's Alley branched slightly southwest off of [[Gut Alley]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]], connecting with [[Drawn Sword Alley]] a short way to the west just south of [[Watchrun Alley]].
