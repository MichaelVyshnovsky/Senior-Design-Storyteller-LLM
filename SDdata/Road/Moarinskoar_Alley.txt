Moarinskoar Alley was a small alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Moarinskoar Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It branched west off of Sul Street through a neighborhood before terminating at a dead end. It was enclosed by Mendever Street, Vondil Street, and Delzorin Street.
The alley was named for a notable member of the Irlingstar noble family.
