Ivory Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Ivory Street was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It originated on Diamond Street where it branched west, crossing the Street of the Singing Dolphin where it turned northwest to end on the western edge of the Street of Glances. Ivory Street was intersected by Sighing Maidens Walk, Flint Street, the Skulkway, Bliss Street, Ignorance Alley, the Street of Whispers, and Torch Lane.
