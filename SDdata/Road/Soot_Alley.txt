'''Soot Alley''' was a small alley that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Soot Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. The alley meandered between [[Nanze Street]] in the north and the [[Fieldway]] in the south, connecting to both roads at several points.
