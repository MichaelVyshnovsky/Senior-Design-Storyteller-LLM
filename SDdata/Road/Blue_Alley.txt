The '''Blue Alley''' was an alley in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite web/Ed Greenwood's Patreon/How Magic Works}}

A mad wizard lured [[adventurer]]s into the alley to test their mettle, including [[undead]] [[skeleton]]s still wearing ball gowns and fancy dress attire and dancing with each other.
