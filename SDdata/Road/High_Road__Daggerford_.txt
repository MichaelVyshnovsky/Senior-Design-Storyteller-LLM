The High Road, which was also called Tanners Way
The High Road was found in the center of Daggerford in between Dukes Way in the north and the Trade Way in the south. The smaller Rogar Alley also connected to the High Road.
Notable buildings located along the High Road included
* The Grand Playhouse
* Korbuss Jewelry and Fine Ornaments
* Nartans Fine Foods
* Scribes Friendship
* Lady Luck Tavern
* Kryptgarden Scrolls
* Merchants Guild
* Dervals Bright Blade
