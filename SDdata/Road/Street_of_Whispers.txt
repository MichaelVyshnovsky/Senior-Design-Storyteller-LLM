The Street of Whispers was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
The Street of Whispers was located in the western section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It ran roughly north to south, originating on the Street of Glances where it branched south and eventually ended with Seawatch Street on Dobs Loss. The Street of Whispers was also intersected by Diamond Street, Grimwalds Way, Shark Street, Gondwatch Lane, Ivory Street, Moonstar Alley, and Sharras Flight.
