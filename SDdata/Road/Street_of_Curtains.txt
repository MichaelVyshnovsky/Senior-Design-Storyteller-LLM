The '''Street of Curtains''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The Street of Curtains was located in the northeast section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It originated in the west on [[Snail Street]] and branched east through [[Book Street]] to end on the northern end of [[Zastrow Street]] just off of the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]].
