Aundles Walk was a street in the rich Temple District of Athkatla.
Aundles Walk began at the intersection of Oblade Street and the Street of the Seven Altars with the Godsmoot to its west. From there, Aundles Walk ran southwest.
*The Temple Bell, once home of the Flamaerdown family, later turned into a shop that dealt in religious magic, items, and paraphernalia as of the late 14 century DR.
*The Pride of Athkatla, an enclosed marketplace comprising rows upon rows of curtained boutiques and mercantile stalls that stood on the intersection of Aundles Walk, Oblade Street and the Street of the Seven Altars.
*The Harfin Draether, a quality tavern that stood across the street from the Pride of Athkatla.
