The '''Northyard''' was a courtyard that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The Northyard was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. It sat along the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] where it passed into Waterdeep through the [[North Gate]]. [[Slipgate Street]] branched off the southwestern section of the Northyard and [[Leanwall Alley]] connected with the eastern side.
