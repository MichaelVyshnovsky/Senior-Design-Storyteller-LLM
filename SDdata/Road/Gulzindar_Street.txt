The Lions
Gulzindar Street was a cobblestone paved avenueIn the map included in the 1-edition accessory City System, this locations name was misprinted as "Gulzinder Street".
The right end of Gulzindar Street terminated on Haltovar Street. Up until the mid-14 century DR, its left end terminated on the Lions road. By 1368 DR, that section of road was re-designated as part of Jelzars Stride.
At its midsection, Gulzindar Street was fully intersected by Darselune Street. The road also acted as the northern terminus of Caldwell Street and the southern terminus of Seawatch Street.
* The Field of Triumphs famed Lion Gate entrance stood at the left end of Gulzindar Street.
