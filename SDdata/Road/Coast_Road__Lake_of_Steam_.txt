The '''Coast Road''' was a paved trail along the north banks of the the [[Lake of Steam]] in [[south Faerûn]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}<ref name=EotSS.M"> It was rarely ever featured on [[map]]s made by northerners.

==Description==
The Coast Road was narrower than the more prominent [[Golden Road]].

==Geography==
The trail began where the [[Lake Way]] ended in the west at the city-state of [[Ankhapur]]. Coast Road cut east along the shore of the Lake of Steam, terminating at the city-state of [[Saelmur]].<ref name=EotSS.M"/>
