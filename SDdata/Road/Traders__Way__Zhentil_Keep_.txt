Traders Way was located in east Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. The road originated on Star Street, branching southwest to end at the wall of the Inner Quarter after several blocks. Traders Way was intersected by Black Street, Beholder Street, and the Street of Sorrow.
A Zhentilar barracks was located along Traders Way at its junction with the Street of Sorrow.
