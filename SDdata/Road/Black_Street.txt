'''Black Street''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Black Street was located within the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] in eastern Zhentil Keep. The road ran north to south in between the [[Street of Swords (Zhentil Keep)|Street of Swords]] to the north and [[Ale Street]] to the south. Along the way Black Street was intersected by [[Dusk Street]], [[Traders' Way (Zhentil Keep)|Traders' Way]], and [[Realm Street]].

==Notable Locations==
A barracks for the [[Zhentilar]] was located on Black Street.
