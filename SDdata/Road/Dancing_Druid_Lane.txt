The '''Dancing Druid Lane''' was a short curved street in [[Suzail]] to the northeast of the [[Bolliver's Wharf]] and the location of [[the Osculatory]].

==Description==
The Dancing Druid Lane began on the crossing of the [[Court Close]] and [[Silverscales Street]],{{cite web| url = http://candlekeep.com/library/articles/sse/sse_1112-04.htm| title = So Saith Ed Dec 2004 | accessdate = 13-04-2021| date = 13-04-2021}} just two buildings away from the waters of [[Dragonmere]]. The street ran east whilst forming a gentle curve southwards and ended just past the Osculatory.
