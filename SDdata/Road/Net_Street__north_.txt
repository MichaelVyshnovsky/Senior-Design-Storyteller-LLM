'''Net Street'''<ref group="note"> The [[Waterdeep: Dragon Heist]] map has this street mislabled as Crook Street. was a small road located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The road was found in southern Waterdeep within the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. Net Street ran north to south in between [[Lackpurse Lane]] near the juction of [[Dretch Lane]] in the north and [[Belnimbra's Street]] to the south.
