'''Stabbed Sailor Alley''', which was also simply called '''the Stabbed Sailor''', was a small alley that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Stabbed Sailor Alley was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The alley branched east from [[Vhezoar Street]], joining [[Shattercrock Alley]] after passing [[Margaster Villa]], and finally ending on [[Whaelgond Way]]. [[Little Street]] and [[Sprangalar's Swordcut]] connected with the north side of Stabbed Sailor Alley.

==Description==
A cluster of large [[shadowtop]] trees towering to nearly {{SI|90|ft|long=yes}} ensured the alley was always shaded.

==History==
This alley was the scene of a violent clash between Waterdeep's citizens and members of the [[Cult of the Dancing Bear]].
