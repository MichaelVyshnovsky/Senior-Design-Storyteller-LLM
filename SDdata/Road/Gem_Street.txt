'''Gem Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Gem Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. The road originated on [[Waterdeep Way]] where it branched south then southeast, eventually terminated at the base of [[Mount Waterdeep]].
