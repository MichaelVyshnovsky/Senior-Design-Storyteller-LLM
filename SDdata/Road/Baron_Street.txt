Baron Street was located in the Zhentil Keeps Harbor District in the south section of the city. The road was located on the eastern side of the Harbor District. It originated at the wall to the Common Quarter then passed west before ending at the docks.
A Zhentarim warehouse and Terns Shipwright Service were located along Baron Street.
