'''Brian's Street''' was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and [[gravel]] in the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Etymology==
This street was named for [[Brian|Brian the Swordmaster]], one of the [[Masked Lord]]s of Waterdeep.<ref>{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2011)/Magical Sword Makers}}

==Geography==
This street ran from west to east. At its western end, Brian's Street ended at the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]], while at its eastern end the street terminated at the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]].

The street was met at the south by [[Mouse Alley]], which connected it to [[Blackcloak Alley]].
