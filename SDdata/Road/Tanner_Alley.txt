'''Tanner Alley''' was a small alley that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Tanner Alley was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. It ran between [[Gawenknife Street]] in the west with [[Stench Alley]] and [[Logan's Walk]] in the east. Tanner Alley branched several times.
