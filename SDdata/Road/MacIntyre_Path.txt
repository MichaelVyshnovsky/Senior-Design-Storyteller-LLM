'''MacIntyre Path''' was a main street that ran northâ€“south throughout the section of [[Ravens Bluff]] north of the [[Fire River]].

==Description==
MacIntyre Path began at the [[North Road (Ravens Bluff)|North Road]] in the [[Sixstar]] neighborhood of the [[Uptown District (Ravens Bluff)|Uptown Distict]]. It extended south through [[Tentowers]], into the [[Swordspoint]] and [[Altarside]] neighborhoods in the [[Temple District (Ravens Bluff)|Temple District]], and ended at [[Unicorn Lane]], within [[Anvil]] in the city's [[Harbor District (Ravens Bluff)|Harbor District]].

There was a break in the road between [[Dragonscale Street]] and [[Griffon Trail]].
