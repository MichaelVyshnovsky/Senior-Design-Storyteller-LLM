'''Siren Street'''<ref group="note">The FR Interactive Atlas map of Waterdeep names this as "Starn Street" but the zoomed in section of the same map lists it as Siren Street. was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Siren Street was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It branched south off of [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]] one building south of the intersection with [[Odd Street]], passing through a block of buildings south and west to end on [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] on the waterfront.
