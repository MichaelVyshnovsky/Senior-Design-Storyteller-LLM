'''Vhezoar Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Vhezoar Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road ran north to south in between [[Delzorin Street]] to the north and [[Tarnath Street]] to the south. Other roads intersecting Vhezoar Street included [[Hassantyr's Street]], [[Sulmor Street]], the [[Street of the Manticore]], and [[Stabbed Sailor Alley]].
