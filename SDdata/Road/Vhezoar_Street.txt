Vhezoar Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Vhezoar Street was located in the central section of Waterdeeps North Ward. The road ran north to south in between Delzorin Street to the north and Tarnath Street to the south. Other roads intersecting Vhezoar Street included Hassantyrs Street, Sulmor Street, the Street of the Manticore, and Stabbed Sailor Alley.
