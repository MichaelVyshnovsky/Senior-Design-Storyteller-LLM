'''Landleen Lane''' was a road that crossed the entirety of [[Ravens Bluff/The Shutters|the Shutters]] neighborhood in the [[Ravens Bluff/Crow's End|Crow's End]] District of [[Ravens Bluff]] from north to south, and ended in the [[Pumpside]] neighborhood.

==Geography==
Landleen Lane was only five city blocks in length. It began at [[Karen Court]] in the Shutters to the north, moved straight south, crossing [[Hammerstone Street]], [[Drover's Run]], came to a moot with [[Samarglast Lane]] and the [[Conant Court]], passed [[Kalikoolan Lane]] and turned west, becoming [[Stonekeep End]] on the edge of [[Pumpside]].
