Snowdown Ride was a well-maintained road found on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles.
Snowdown Ride cut through the heart of Snowdown for nearly . It began in the north at the town of Llandrain and continued south the southwest through the island to end at Pengram. Along the way were Caer Westphal and Harloch, as well as all three of Snowdowns lakes..
