'''Snowdown Ride''' was a well-maintained road found on the island of [[Snowdown]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Description==
Snowdown Ride cut through the heart of Snowdown for nearly {{SI|70|mi|k|long=yes}}. It began in the north at the town of [[Llandrain]] and continued south the southwest through the island to end at [[Pengram]]. Along the way were [[Caer Westphal]] and [[Harloch (town)|Harloch]], as well as all three of Snowdown's lakes..
