Fishwife Alley was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and gravel in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.
This curving road ran from north to south, running north off of Slop Street until intersecting with Curtain Alley and terminating at Brians Street.
West of this alley, the street Mouse Alley ran roughly parallel.
* A stout, post-mounted barrel stood on this streets intersection with Weeping Maidens Run. Serving as a kiosk, it had broadsheets, employment inquiries, general notices, and handbills posted on it.
* The Temple of Good Cheer, a temple of Milil and Lliira within a row house, stood at the corner of Fishwife Alley and Slop Street.
