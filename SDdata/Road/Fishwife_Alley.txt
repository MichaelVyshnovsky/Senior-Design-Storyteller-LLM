'''Fishwife Alley''' was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and [[gravel]] in the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Geography==
This curving road ran from north to south, running north off of [[Slop Street]] until intersecting with [[Curtain Alley]] and terminating at [[Brian's Street]].

West of this alley, the street [[Mouse Alley]] ran roughly parallel.

==Notable Locations==
* A stout, post-mounted barrel stood on this street's intersection with [[Weeping Maiden's Run]]. Serving as a kiosk, it had [[broadsheet]]s, employment inquiries, general notices, and handbills posted on it.<ref>
* The [[Temple of Good Cheer]], a temple of [[Milil]] and [[Lliira]] within a row house, stood at the corner of Fishwife Alley and Slop Street.<ref>
