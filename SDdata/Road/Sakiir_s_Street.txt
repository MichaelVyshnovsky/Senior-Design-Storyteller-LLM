'''Sakiir's Street''' was a road located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Sakiir's Street was found in southern Waterdeep within the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] and [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It originated on [[Soothsayer's Way]] just south of the feet of [[Mount Waterdeep]]. The road passed south to end at [[Fishgut Court]] and [[Sail Street]]. Along its path Sakiir's Street was intersected by [[Lackpurse Lane]], [[Rainrun Street]], [[Belnimbra's Street]], and [[the Slide]].
