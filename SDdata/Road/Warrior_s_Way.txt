Warriors Way, which was also seen as Warriors Way, was a major road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Warriors Way was located in Castle Ward, spanning much of the ward. The road ran north to south, parallel with the Street of Silver to the west and the Street of the Sword to the east. Warriors Way originated on Bazaar Street in the north, where it branched south to end on Waterdeep Way. Warriors Way was intersected by Selduth Street, Cymbrils Walk, Lamp Street, Buckle Alley, and Palfrey Lane.
