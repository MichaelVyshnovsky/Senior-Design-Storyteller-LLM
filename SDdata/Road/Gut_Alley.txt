Gut Alley was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Gut Alley branched south off of Belnimbras Street in Waterdeeps Dock Ward. It continued south past the intersection with Leeras Alley before turning southwest just after the intersection with Blackstar Lane. Gut Alley turned south again briefly at the intersection with Adder Lane before turning southeast to end on Fish Street. Other roads intersecting Gut Alley included Watchrun Alley, Drawn Sword Alley, Shesstras Street, and Picklock Alley.
