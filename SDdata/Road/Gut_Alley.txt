'''Gut Alley''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Gut Alley branched south off of [[Belnimbra's Street]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It continued south past the intersection with [[Leera's Alley]] before turning southwest just after the intersection with [[Blackstar Lane]]. Gut Alley turned south again briefly at the intersection with [[Adder Lane]] before turning southeast to end on [[Fish Street (Waterdeep)|Fish Street]]. Other roads intersecting Gut Alley included [[Watchrun Alley]], [[Drawn Sword Alley]], [[Shesstra's Street]], and [[Picklock Alley]].
