'''Howling Cat Court''' was a small courtyard in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
This courtyard extended north off of [[Mulgomir's Way]].<ref> It was situated in the block of buildings bounded by the [[Street of Silks]] and the [[Street of Silver]].

==History==
On one occasion, a dozen furious and heavily armed [[minotaur]]s were released from stasis on this street by an [[evil]] [[mage]].

==Reputation==
Howling Cat Court was a hotbed of felonious activity. In addition to [[thieves]] often lurking around it, the court was infamous for being a clandestine meeting place among street gangs or [[prostitute]]s and their clients. Because of this reputation, Howling Cat Court was frequently visited by [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|Watch]] patrols of triple the normal strength.
