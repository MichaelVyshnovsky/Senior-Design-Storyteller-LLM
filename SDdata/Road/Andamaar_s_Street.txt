Andamaars Street was a lesser avenue, paved in corduroy-logs, situated between the North Ward and Trades Ward of Waterdeep.
Andamaars Street marked the southern border of North Ward and the northern border of Trades Ward. It traveled from northwest to southeast, extending off of Zendulth Street and terminating at the High Road.
On the Trades Ward side, Andamaars Street was met from the south by Lamp Street and Theln Lane, On the North Ward side of the street, Andamaars acted as the western terminus of Undals Lane.
When Waterdeeps walls expanded in 1101 DR to accommodate more settlers, the new perimeter approximated what would come to be the overall shape of Andamaars Street.
