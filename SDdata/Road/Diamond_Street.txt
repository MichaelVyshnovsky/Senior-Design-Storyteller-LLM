Diamond Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Diamond Street was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. It originated on Ivory Street, branching south then southwest to cross the Street of the Singing Dolphin and eventually end at Westwall Street. Diamond Street was intersected by Delzorin Street, Vondil Street, Chassos Trot, the Rough Road, Satchel Alley, Seawatch Street, the Street of Whispers, and Pharras Alley.
