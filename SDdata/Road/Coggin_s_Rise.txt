Coggins Rise was a trail that ran through the hills from Stagwick to Castle Hartwick. The hills in the region were covered with white-barked aspens. It took between a half hour and an hour to travel the full distance by horse.
It was off Coggins Rise where Princess Brianna and her firbolg bodyguard Morten were ambushed by Ice Spire ogres led by Goboka in 1366 DR.
