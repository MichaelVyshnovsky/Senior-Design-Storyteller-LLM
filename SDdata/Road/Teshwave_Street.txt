==Location==
Located in southern Zhentil Keep within the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]], Teshwave Street was a small road in the western Foreign Quarter that passed between [[Mace Way]] and the main intersection with [[Zhent Avenue]] in front of the [[Force Bridge]]. Teshwave Street also connected with [[Center Street]] and [[Twilight Street]] at the intersection.
