Sammarins Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Sammarins Street was located in the south section of Waterdeeps North Ward. The road followed originated near where Andamaars Street entered the City of the Dead, passing north the curving to the east and ending on Mhalsymbers Way. Golden Serpent Street also intersected Sammarins Street.
Hriiat Fine Pastries sat near the midpoint of Sammarins Street.
