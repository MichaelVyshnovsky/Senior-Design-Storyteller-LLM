'''Sammarin's Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Sammarin's Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road followed originated near where [[Andamaar's Street]] entered the [[Waterdeep/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]], passing north the curving to the east and ending on [[Mhalsymber's Way]]. [[Golden Serpent Street]] also intersected Sammarin's Street.

==Notable Locations==
[[Hriiat Fine Pastries]] sat near the midpoint of Sammarin's Street.
