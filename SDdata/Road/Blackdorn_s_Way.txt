Blackdorns Way was a street found in the coastal town of Aithelar in Callidyrr on the Moonshae Isles in .
Blackdorns Way was found in southwest Aithelar in between Finns Alley and Aithe High Street. Blaedar Street branched to the northeast from the middle of Blackdorns Way. Callidyrr Ride merged with Blackdorns Way where the Silver Boar was located.
