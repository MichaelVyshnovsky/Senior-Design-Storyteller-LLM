'''Stonebow Street''' was a street that stretched from the [[Promenade (Suzail)|Promenade]] to the curved [[Blade Lane]] in the eastern part of [[Suzail]] in [[Cormyr]].

==Description==
The street crossed [[Torch Street]] to the south and [[Swordstars Lane]] to the north.
