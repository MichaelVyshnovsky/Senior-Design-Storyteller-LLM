'''Shark Street''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Shark Street was located in the western section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]]. The road passed east to west in between the [[Street of the Singing Dolphin]] and [[Seawatch Street]]. It was intersected by the [[Street of Whispers]] near its midpoint.
