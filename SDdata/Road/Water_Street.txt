Water Street was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Water Street ran through the southern central section of Daggerford near the River Gate. It originated on the Trade Way, branching south before making a right-angle turn to continue east until ending at the Hill Road. Kaoth Alley was connected to Water Street.
Notable buildings located along Water Street included
* The Black Stone Inn
* Daggerfords constable
* The Blacksmiths Guildhouse
* The Flying Fish
