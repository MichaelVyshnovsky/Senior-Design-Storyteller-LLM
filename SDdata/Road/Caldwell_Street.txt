'''Caldwell Street''' was a dirt and [[gravel]] paved minor avenue in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Locations==
Caldwell stretched from north to south, with the northern end terminating on [[Gulzindar Street]] and the southern end terminating on [[Julthoon Street]].In the map included in the 1{{st}}-edition accessory ''[[City System]]'', this location's name was misprinted as "Gulzind'''e'''r Street".
