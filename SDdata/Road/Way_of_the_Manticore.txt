The Way of the Manticore was a long road that connected the nations of Cormyr and Sembia.
The westernmost point of the Way of the Manticore was located at the town of Hilp, where it met Calantars Way. The easternmost part of the Way of the Manticore was the city of Selgaunt in Sembia.
From west to east, the Way of the Manticore ran through 
* Hilp
* Wheloon
* Dreamers Rock
* Monksblade
* Battlerise
* Daerlun
* Huddagh
* Mulhessen
* Selgaunt
