Satchel Alley was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Satchel Alley was located in the western section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward. The alley cut diagonally in between Westwall Street and Diamond Street.
The alley was named for the bags of valuables that jewelers used to carry through here before lurking thieves made it too risky.
