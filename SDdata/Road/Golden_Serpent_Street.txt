'''Golden Serpent Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Golden Serpent Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road branched west off of [[Saerdoun Street]] south of the [[Cliffwatch]], winding through the southern North Ward and curving south to end on [[Sammarin's Street]] near the [[Waterdeep/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]]. Along its path Golden Serpent Street was intersected by [[Nindabar Street]], [[Catchthief Alley]], [[Mhalsymber's Way]], [[Gelthoon Street]], [[Shando Street]], [[Undal's Lane]], and [[Black Cat Way]].
