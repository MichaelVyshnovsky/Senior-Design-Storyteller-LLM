Golden Serpent Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Golden Serpent Street was located in the south section of Waterdeeps North Ward. The road branched west off of Saerdoun Street south of the Cliffwatch, winding through the southern North Ward and curving south to end on Sammarins Street near the City of the Dead. Along its path Golden Serpent Street was intersected by Nindabar Street, Catchthief Alley, Mhalsymbers Way, Gelthoon Street, Shando Street, Undals Lane, and Black Cat Way.
