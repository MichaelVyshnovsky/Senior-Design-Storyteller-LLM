The Street of Bones was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-14 century DR.
The Street of Bones was located in central Zhentil Keep within the Inner Quarter. The street branched south from Elephstron Way near the western gate connecting to the Common Quarter. It terminated several blocks later near a different section of the wall to the Common Quarter. Along the way the Street of Bones was crossed by Rogues Way.
The Thieves Guildhall and Four Dwarf Miners were located along the Street of Bones, as was an armory for the Zhentilar.
