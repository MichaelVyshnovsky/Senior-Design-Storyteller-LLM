The '''Street of Bones''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The Street of Bones was located in central Zhentil Keep within the [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]]. The street branched south from [[Elephstron Way]] near the western gate connecting to the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]]. It terminated several blocks later near a different section of the wall to the Common Quarter. Along the way the Street of Bones was crossed by [[Rogues' Way]].

==Notable Locations==
The [[Thieves' Guildhall]] and [[Four Dwarf Miners]] were located along the Street of Bones, as was an armory for the [[Zhentilar]].
