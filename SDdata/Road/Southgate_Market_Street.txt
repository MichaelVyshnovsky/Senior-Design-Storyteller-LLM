'''Southgate Market Street''' was a small branched street found in the city of [[Westgate]].

==Location==
Southgate Market Street was found in the south section of the city. The street passed through the [[South Gate (Westgate)|South Gate]] to connect with [[Westgate Market Street]] after branching several times.

==Inhabitants==
[[Mintassan]], [[Tersonm]] and [[Gondeth]], all powerful [[wizards]]s, lived on Southgate Market Street.
