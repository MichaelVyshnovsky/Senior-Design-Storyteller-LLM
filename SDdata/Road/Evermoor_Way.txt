The '''Evermoor Way''' is the road from [[Triboar]] to [[Bridge Gate]] in [[Everlund]], and extending to [[Silverymoon]]. Everlund has taken on the responsibility of keeping the road clear of danger from Everlund to Calling Horns, however has been hard pressed to do so in recent times.

==Travel Times or Distances==
* ''Silverymoon'' to ''Everlund'', '''50 miles'''
* ''Silverymoon'' to ''Yartar'', '''320 miles''', via the Evermoor Way. 
* ''Everlund'' to ''Yartar'', '''270 miles'''
