'''Lathin's Cut''' was a short, hard-packed dirt and [[gravel]] paved, alleyway in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Geography==
This alleyway ran in a straight line west from the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]], cutting through [[Jembril Street]], and ended on the west side of [[Burdag Lane]].

==History==
Lathin's Cut was named after the man who had his own home leveled, upon his death, to create the empty space the alleyway occupied as he wished to make it easier for the devout to reach the [[Plinth]].

==Notable Locations==
;The Plinth: An all-faiths temple and the only temple in all of the Trades Ward.

==Reputation==
Lathin's Cut was considered by locals to be a landmark. Both they and visitors to Waterdeep often rendezvoused at the alley.
