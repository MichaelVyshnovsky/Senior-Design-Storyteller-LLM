'''Shadow Alley''' was a small road located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5271}}

==Location==
Shadow Alley was located in the southwest section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. The alley ran north to south parallel to the [[Street of Silks]] to the west and the [[Street of Silver]] to the east. Shadow Alley branched south from [[Mulgomir's Way]] and ended at [[Cage Street]] behind the [[Dragon's Head]] tavern. [[Lemontree Alley]] branched east off of Shadow Alley near its southern end.
