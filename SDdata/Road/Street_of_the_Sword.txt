The '''Street of the Sword''' was a major road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}
==Location==
The Street of the Sword was located in [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]], spanning much of the ward. The road ran north to south, parallel with [[Warrior's Way]] to the west and the [[Street of Bells]] to the east. The Street of the Sword originated on [[Bazaar Street]] in the north, where it branched south to end on [[Waterdeep Way]]. Along the way, the Street of the Sword was touched or crossed by many streets and alleys, including [[Selduth Street]], [[Cymbril's Walk]], [[Lamp Street]], [[Buckle Alley]], and [[Cat Alley]].
