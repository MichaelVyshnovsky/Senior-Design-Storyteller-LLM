The Street of the Sword was a major road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
The Street of the Sword was located in Castle Ward, spanning much of the ward. The road ran north to south, parallel with Warriors Way to the west and the Street of Bells to the east. The Street of the Sword originated on Bazaar Street in the north, where it branched south to end on Waterdeep Way. Along the way, the Street of the Sword was touched or crossed by many streets and alleys, including Selduth Street, Cymbrils Walk, Lamp Street, Buckle Alley, and Cat Alley.
