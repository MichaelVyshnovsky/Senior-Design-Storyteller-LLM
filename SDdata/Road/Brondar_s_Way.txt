Brondars Way was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Brondars Way was located in the central section of Waterdeeps North Ward. The alley ran north to south, branching from Delzorin Street and passing south to end on Hassantyrs Street. Brondars Street was intersected by Sulmor Street near its midpoint and Thalaumburs Trot connected with the southern end.
