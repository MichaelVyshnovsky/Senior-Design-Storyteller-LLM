'''Black Eel Street''' was street that crossed the [[Baldur's Gate/Seatower|Seatower]] district in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].

==Description==
Black Eel Street began at Baldur's Gate's western city wall and moved in a relatively straight line across the Seatower district, passing the beginning of [[Wendserpent Street]], one block west of the water, and ending at [[Glaezel's Dock]] overlooking the [[Gray Harbor]].
