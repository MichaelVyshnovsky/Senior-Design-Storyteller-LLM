'''Windborne Way''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Windborne Way was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. It ran north to south in between [[Thunderstaff Way]] in the north and [[Delzorin Street]] in the south. Windborne Way was intersected by [[Sashtar Street]], [[Tower March]], and [[Saerdoun Street]].
