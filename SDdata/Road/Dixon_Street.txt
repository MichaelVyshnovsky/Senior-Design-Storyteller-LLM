Dixon Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the midâ€“14 century DR.
Dixon Street was located in southwest Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. The road originated in the north, branching southwest from Cobble Road and passing south until ending at Border Street along the southern wall. Along its path Dixon Street was intersected by Crypt Street, Canter Street, Pride Street, Toll Street, Merchant Street, and Brigand Way.
The celebrated embalmer Morges business was located along Dixon Street.
