'''Dixon Street''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Dixon Street was located in southwest Zhentil Keep in the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]]. The road originated in the north, branching southwest from [[Cobble Road]] and passing south until ending at [[Border Street]] along the southern wall. Along its path Dixon Street was intersected by [[Crypt Street]], [[Canter Street]], [[Pride Street]], [[Toll Street]], [[Merchant Street]], and [[Brigand Way]].

==Notable Locations==
The 'celebrated' embalmer [[Morge]]'s business was located along Dixon Street.
