Dock Street was a large road that ran the entire expanse of the Dock Ward in the city of Waterdeep.
Dock Street ran parallel to the waterfront in the Dock Ward, beginning in the north by branching southeast off of Sail Street next to The Sailors Own tavern at Fishgut Court and continuing southeast to end in the southeast corner of the Dock Ward. Along the way Dock Street was intersected by many roads including Fish Street, Odd Street, Sternpost Street, Asterils Way, Cod Lane, Ship Street, Net Street, Spices Street, Wharf Street, Cedar Street, and the Tower Trail.
