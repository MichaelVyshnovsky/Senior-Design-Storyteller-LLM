'''Dock Street''' was a large road that ran the entire expanse of the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Dock Street ran parallel to the waterfront in the Dock Ward, beginning in the north by branching southeast off of [[Sail Street]] next to [[The Sailors' Own]] tavern at [[Fishgut Court]] and continuing southeast to end in the southeast corner of the Dock Ward. Along the way Dock Street was intersected by many roads including [[Fish Street (Waterdeep)|Fish Street]], [[Odd Street]], [[Sternpost Street]], [[Asteril's Way]], [[Cod Lane]], [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]], [[Net Street (south)|Net Street]], [[Spices Street]], [[Wharf Street]], [[Cedar Street]], and the [[Tower Trail]].
