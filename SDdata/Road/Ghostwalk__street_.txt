The '''Ghostwalk''' was a street in [[Silverymoon]].

==Description==
The Ghostwalk was located in [[Northbank]]. Running west to east for under 500 feet, it began at [[Dancer's Mask Lane]] in the west&mdash;across from the [[Golden Oak]] inn&mdash;crossed [[Bowshot Ride]] and the [[Moonway]], and ended at the [[Old Wall]].
