Snake Alley was a small street located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Snake Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter. The alley meandered through the southern section of the Adventurers Quarter, branching several times and connnecting with Grocers Lane, Slop Street, and Kerrigans Court.
