'''Snake Alley''' was a small street located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}

==Location==
Snake Alley was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]]. The alley meandered through the southern section of the Adventurers' Quarter, branching several times and connnecting with [[Grocer's Lane]], [[Slop Street]], and [[Kerrigan's Court]].
