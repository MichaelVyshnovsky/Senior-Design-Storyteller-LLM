'''Spendthrift Alley''' was an alley in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] that had developed into a major street by the mid 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Geography==
This road opened east off of the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]], passing through [[Vellarr's Lane]] and the [[Street of the Tusks]], then terminated at [[Rivon Street]]. Along the way, the road [[Mhaer's Alley]] jut off north from it. The length of Spendthrift was lined with streetlamps.

==Notable Locations==
* Dating back to its time as an alley, at cheap prices. The bazaar was frequently crowded, putting people at a small risk of falling victim to a [[thief]].
* [[Costumers' Hall]], the guildhall of the [[Order of Master Taylors, Glovers, and Mercers]].
