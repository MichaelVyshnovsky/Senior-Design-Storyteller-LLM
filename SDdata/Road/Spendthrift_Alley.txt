Spendthrift Alley was an alley in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep that had developed into a major street by the mid 14 century DR.
This road opened east off of the High Road, passing through Vellarrs Lane and the Street of the Tusks, then terminated at Rivon Street. Along the way, the road Mhaers Alley jut off north from it. The length of Spendthrift was lined with streetlamps.
