'''Howser's Trail''' was a small mountain trail found in the [[Soravia|Duchy of Soravia]] within the eastern region of the [[Bloodstone Lands]] near the border with [[Narfell]].

==Description==
The trail connected the small town of [[Cutrock]] with [[Howser's Mine]], passing west to east through the peaks of the [[Giantspire Mountains]] for roughly {{SI|75|mi|k|long=yes}}.

* Cutrock
* Howser's Mine
