'''Lhoril's Alley''' was a small alley that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Lhoril's Alley was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It branched off of the southern end of [[Duir's Alley]] in between the [[Street of Bells]] and the [[Street of the Sword]], connecting with [[Waterdeep Way]].

==History==
The alley was named for a [[Lhoril|wizard]] who battled a [[tanar'ri]] here and perished. Her [[ghost]] was believed to haunt the alley as running footsteps could be heard at all times of the day.
