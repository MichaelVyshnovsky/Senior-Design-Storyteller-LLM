Wall Street was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR..
Wall Street ran along the inside of Daggerfords city walls, fully encircling the entire city.
Many of Daggerfords roads connected with Wall Street including Dukes Way, the Hill Road, Horse Way, Fields Lane, Tymora Alley, Rogar Alley, Kaoth Alley, and the Farmers Road. Since Wall Street encircled the perimeter of all of Daggerford, many buildings were located along its length. The most notable of these are listed below
* Dragonback Inn
* Daggerfords barracks
* The Table of the Sword
* Farmers Society
* The Caravan Gate
