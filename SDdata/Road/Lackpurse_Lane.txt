'''Lackpurse Lane''' was a prominent border road located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Lackpurse Lane served as part of the border between the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] and [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It originated on [[Belnimbra's Street]] in the east, branching northwest before passing due west past [[Dretch Lane]] before turning south to end on [[Sail Street]] near the waterfront. The road was intersected by [[The Reach (Waterdeep)|the Reach]], [[Net Street (north)|Net Street]], [[Eel Street (Waterdeep)|Eel Street]], [[Sakiir's Street]], [[Ward's Way]], and [[Cook Street]].

==Notable Locations==
[[The Grey Griffon]] tavern was located on Lackpurse Lane on the west side of the intersection with Eel Street.
