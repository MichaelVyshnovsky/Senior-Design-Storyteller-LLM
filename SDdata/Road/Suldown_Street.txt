Suldown StreetThe entry for this street is written as "Suldoun Street" in some sources. was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Suldown Street was located in the south section of Waterdeeps North Ward. The road originated on the High Road at the broder with the Castle Ward where it branched northeast into the North Ward. Suldown Street turned due east where it met Whaelgond Way, eventually ending on Saerdoun Street near the Cliffwatch and outer city wall. Suldown Street was also intersected by Shando Street, Tarnath Street, Gelthoon Street, Belzound Street, Brahiirs Street, and Nindabar Street.
