'''Thunderstaff Way''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Thunderstaff Way was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road branched southeast from [[Immar Street]], turned due south at the intersection with [[Tower March]], and ended at [[Trollskull Alley]]. Thunderstaff Way was intersected by [[Sashtar Street]], [[Windborne Way]], [[Saerdoun Street]], and [[Horn Street]].
