'''Moonsea Avenue''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Moonsea Avenue was located in the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] in the southeast section of Zhentil Keep. The road branched west from [[Battle Way]] and terminated at the barrier to the [[Zhentil Keep/Harbor District|Harbor District]]. Moonsea Avenue was intersected by the [[Street of Sorrow]], [[Beholder Street]] and [[Hasbeen Road]] near its midpoint.

==Notable Locations==
The [[Gorey Eye-Beast]] tavern was located on Moonsea Avenue.
