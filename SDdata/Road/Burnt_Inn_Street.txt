'''Burnt Inn Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Burnt Inn Street was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. The road began and end on the [[Fieldway]], branching off the western end and terminating on the eastern end just north of [[Twelvedog Court]]. [[Flipcoin Alley]] branched off the southern end of Burnt Inn Street.
