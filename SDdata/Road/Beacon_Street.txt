'''Beacon Street''' was a hard-packed dirt and [[gravel]] paved alleyway in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Geography==
This alley ran in a straight line from north to south, situated along blocks of buildings between [[Blackmul Street]] and the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]]. It stood directly south of [[Shoor Street]], being connected to its western end by a short unnamed alley.
