Boltspring Alley was a back alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 century DR.
Boltspring Alley was located in the west section of Waterdeeps Field Ward. The alley branched south off of the western section of the Fieldway and connected with the western curve of Burnt Inn Street. It branched several more times, running through the neighborhood bordering the north wall of the Sea Ward.
