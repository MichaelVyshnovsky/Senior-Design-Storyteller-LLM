'''Boltspring Alley''' was a back alley that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Boltspring Alley was located in the west section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]]. The alley branched south off of the western section of the [[Fieldway]] and connected with the western curve of [[Burnt Inn Street]]. It branched several more times, running through the neighborhood bordering the north wall of the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]].
