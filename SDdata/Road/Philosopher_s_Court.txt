'''Philosopher's Court''', which was also called '''Philosopher's Square''' or the '''Foolsquare''', was a small courtyard that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Philosopher's Court was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. The buildings forming the court were enclosed to the south by the [[Street of Curtains]], to the east by [[Book Street]], to the west by [[Snail Street]], and an unnamed alley bordered the north.

==History==
The location derived its name from the fact that it was a common meeting place for intellectuals to gather to debate and argue. Drunks often slept their intoxication off here as well.
