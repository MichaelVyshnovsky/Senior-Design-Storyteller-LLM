Kerrigans Court was a courtyard located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Kerrigans Court was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter. The court connected with Slop Street along the southern edge of the Adventurers Quarter.
