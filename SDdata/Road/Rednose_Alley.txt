Rednose Alley was a small back alley that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Rednose Alley was located in the north section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward. The alley branched west from the north side of Tezambrils Street. It encircled a small villa within the neighborhood.
Many of the warehouses lining the alley served as homes for destitute youths who fought each other regularly.
The back side of the Saddlers & Harness-Makers Hall faced Rednose Alley.
