'''Manysteps Alley''' was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and [[gravel]] in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Geography==
This road ran from north to south, standing between and running parallel to both [[Zastrow Street|Zastrow]] and the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]].

==Inhabitants==
This alleyway was home to a variety of soothsayers, shady moneylenders, message runners, and [[thieves]]. It was generally avoided by those who displayed their wealth.
