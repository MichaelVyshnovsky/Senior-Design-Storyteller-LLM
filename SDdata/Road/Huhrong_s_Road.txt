The '''Huhrong's Road''' was a prominent roadway found in northern [[Rashemen]].<ref name=FRA-p7">{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Geography==
The Huhrong's Road originated in Rashemen's capital city of [[Immilmar]] near [[Lake Ashane]]. The road passed east just north of the [[Ashenwood]] before connecting with [[Mulptan]] and continued northeast along the outskirts of the [[Endless Wastes]] until reaching [[Nathoud]].<ref name=FRA-p7"/>
