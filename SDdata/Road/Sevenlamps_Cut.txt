'''Sevenlamps Cut''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Sevenlamps Cut was located in the western section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It ran east to west between [[Swords Street]] and the [[Street of Silks]] southeast of [[Turnback Court]].

==History==
The road was named for the [[enchanted]] everburning lamps placed by [[Ahghairon]]. It was known to be a good place to hire spellcasters or [[priest]]s for healing.
