'''Dusk Street''' was a road located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Dusk Street was located in Zhentil Keep's [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] in the eastern section of the city. Originating along [[Border Street]] at the outer wall of the city, Dusk Street passed southwest for several blocks until terminating at the barrier to the [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]]. Dusk Street was intersected by [[Beholder Street]], [[Black Street]], and [[Star Street (Zhentil Keep)|Star Street]].

==Notable Locations==
A [[Zhentilar]] guardpost was located along Dusk Street where it connected with Border Street at the outer city wall.
