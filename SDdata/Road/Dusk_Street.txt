Dusk Street was a road located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the midâ€“14 century DR.
Dusk Street was located in Zhentil Keeps Common Quarter in the eastern section of the city. Originating along Border Street at the outer wall of the city, Dusk Street passed southwest for several blocks until terminating at the barrier to the Inner Quarter. Dusk Street was intersected by Beholder Street, Black Street, and Star Street.
A Zhentilar guardpost was located along Dusk Street where it connected with Border Street at the outer city wall.
