'''Soldier's Street''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Soldier's Street was located in the south section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] just west of [[Castle Waterdeep]]. The road originated on [[Soothsayer's Way]] where it branched northwest toward Castle Waterdeep to end on [[Watchmen's Way]] at the [[Bell Tower (Waterdeep)|Bell Tower]].
