The '''Tethyamar Trail''' was a trade road that ran the length of [[Daggerdale]]. Locally, it was often referred to as '''the Daggerdale road'''.{{Cite dragon/294/Elminster's Guide to the Realms: The Leaning Tree|70}}

==Geography==
The Tethyamar Trail started a few miles east of the southern point of [[Spiderhaunt Wood]] at the [[North Ride]]. It traveled north through the rolling hills of [[Daggerdale]] all the way to the town of [[Dagger Falls]], where it joined the [[Tesh Trail]].    

===Geographical features===
;[[Dagger River]]: A river that eventually fed into the larger [[River Ashaba]] in [[Shadowdale]].
;[[Dagger Falls (waterfall)|Dagger Falls]]: The waterfall on the [[River Tesh]].

==Notable locations==
===Inns and taverns===
;[[Teshford Arms]]: An inn just outside Dagger Falls.

===Landmarks=== 
;[[Serpentsbridge]]: A stone bridge that spanned the Dagger River.

===Settlements===
;[[Anathar's Dell]]: Just southwest of a red-topped crag halfway between the northern tip of the Spiderhaunt Woods and Serpentsbridge.    
;[[Dagger Falls]]: Just before the trail crossed the River Tesh.    

==History==
Before [[human]]s settled in Dagger Falls, the [[dwarves]] of [[Tethyamar]] used the Tethyamar Trail to ship metals between their southern and northern holdings.{{Cite dungeon/192/Backdrop: Daggerdale|5}}
