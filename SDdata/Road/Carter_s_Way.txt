Carters Way was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Carters Way was located in the southern section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward. The road originated on the Specterwalk where it branched south cross Coach Street and eventually end at a deadend in a neighborhood south of Coachlamp Lane.

* Builders Hall
* The Full Cup
* Zestras Hot Soups, an eatery that offered salted sprouts, various soups, and spiced sausages in gravy.
