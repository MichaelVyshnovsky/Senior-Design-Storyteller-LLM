'''Carter's Way''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Carter's Way was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]]. The road originated on the [[Specterwalk]] where it branched south cross [[Coach Street]] and eventually end at a deadend in a neighborhood south of [[Coachlamp Lane]].
