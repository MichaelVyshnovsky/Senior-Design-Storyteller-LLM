Woodside Way was a small avenue found in the city of Westgate.
Woodside way was located in the south section of the city. It branched from Silverpiece Way and met with Westgate Market Street near the Lords Water pool. Several prominent castles were found along Woodside Way.
* House of Silks
* Castle Thorsar
* Castle Athagdal
* Castle Urdo
* Castle Guldar
