'''Immar Street''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Immar Street was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. Immar Street originated on the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] at the border with the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]], branching east and southeast to end on [[Selrin Street]] near the eastern outwer wall of the city. Immar Street was intersected by [[Whaelgond Way]], [[Thunderstaff Way]], [[Windborne Way]], [[Tower March]], [[Pony Way]], [[Black Dog Alley]], [[Phull Lane]], [[Sashtar Street]], [[Watch Alley]], [[Stallion Street]], and [[Brassfeather Lane]].
