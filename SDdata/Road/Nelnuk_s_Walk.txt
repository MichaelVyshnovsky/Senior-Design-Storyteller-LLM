Nelnuks Walk was a small road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Nelnuks Walk was located in Waterdeeps Dock Ward. The road originated on the eastern side of Wastrel Alley, branching southeast then turning east to connect with the southern end of Drawn Sword Alley. The southern end of Watchrun Alley terminated at Nelnuks Walk.
A blind alley branching off of the southern side of Nelnuks Walk contained an entrance to Waterdeeps sewers.
