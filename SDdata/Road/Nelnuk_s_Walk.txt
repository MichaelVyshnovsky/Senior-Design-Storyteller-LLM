'''Nelnuk's Walk''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Nelnuk's Walk was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. The road originated on the eastern side of [[Wastrel Alley]], branching southeast then turning east to connect with the southern end of [[Drawn Sword Alley]]. The southern end of [[Watchrun Alley]] terminated at Nelnuk's Walk.

==Description==
A blind alley branching off of the southern side of Nelnuk's Walk contained an entrance to Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Sewers|sewers]].
