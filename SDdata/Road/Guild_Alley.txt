Guild Alley was a small road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Guild Alley was located in southwest Daggerford. It originated from the southern part of Rogar Alley and cut south until ending at Daggerfords drill field.
Notable buildings located along Guild Alley included
* Olegs Weaponforge
* Headquarters for the Taverners Association
