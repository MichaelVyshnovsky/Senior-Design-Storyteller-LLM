'''Dock Street''' was a major road in the asteroid city of [[Bral]].

==Description==
Dock Street was the major road that overlooked the [[Docks (Bral)|Docks]] of the [[Rock of Bral]] on its leading edge. The street ran from the starboard edge of the city about {{SI|1400|ft.|long=yes}} to the port edge, overlooking the [[Yards (Bral)|Yards]]. At its starboard end, it merge into [[Lamp Street (Bral)|Lamp Street]], while it merged into [[Anchor Street]] at its other end.

The road was intersected by [[Low Street]], [[Hammer Street]], [[Grand Street]], and [[Dragon Street]] and formed the leading-edge border for [[Giff-town]] and [[Shou-town (Bral)|Shou-town]].
