The '''Passar''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The Passar was located in the southeast section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. The road ran east to west, connecting [[Nindabar Street]] in the west with [[Saerdoun Street]] in the east.
