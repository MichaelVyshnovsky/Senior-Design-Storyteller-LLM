'''Jester's Court''' was a courtyard that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Jester's Court was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. It was enclosed by [[Selduth Street]] to the north, the [[Street of Silks]] to the west, the [[Street of Silver]] to the east, and [[Howling Cat Court]] to the south.
