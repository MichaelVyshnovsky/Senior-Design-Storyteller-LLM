The Hill Road was a road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The Hill Road originated from Dukes Way near Morninglow Tower near the center of Daggerford. The road passed southeast through the city along the base of the landrise that separated the Ducal Castle from the rest of Daggerford. The Hill road terminated at the southern part of Wall Street at the River Gate. The Trade Way connected to the Hill Road near its midpoint.
Notable buildings located along the Hill Road included
* Farrels Fine Jewelry and Apparel
* Chateau Elite
* Taverners Confederation
* Daggerfords jail
* Mikitan Shipyards
