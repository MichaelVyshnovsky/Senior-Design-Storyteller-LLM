'''Curtain Alley''' was a minor avenue of hard-packed earth and [[gravel]] in the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Geography==
This curving road ran from north to south. Running north off of [[Slop Street]] until terminating at [[Fishwife Alley]].
