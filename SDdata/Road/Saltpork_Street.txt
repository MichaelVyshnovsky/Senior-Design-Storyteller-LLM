Saltpork Street was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep during the 15 century DR.
Saltpork Street was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Field Ward. Saltpork Street began and ended on the Fieldway, branching first in the west just north of Twelvedog Court and reconnecting in the east near the High Road. Prestige Lane intersected Saltpork Lanes western section.
