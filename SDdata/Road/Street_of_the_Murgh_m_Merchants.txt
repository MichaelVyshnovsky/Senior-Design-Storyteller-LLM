The Street of the Murghôm Merchants was a street found in the city of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The Street of the Murghôm Merchants was located in the Furdah Rustauk District in southern Dhaztanar.
The street provided access to the Furdah Rustauk District, and eventually the rest of Dhaztanar, for anyone entering by the citys southern Little Gate.
