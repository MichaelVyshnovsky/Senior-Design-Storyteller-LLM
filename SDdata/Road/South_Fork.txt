The '''South Fork''' was a major road in [[Amn]] that formed the vital route between [[Purskul]] and [[Amnwater]].

==Geography==
The road connected the two trade cities and ran mostly parallel to the northern [[Lake Way]]. It passed quite closely to the [[Seventeen Sentries]] [[standing stone]]s.
