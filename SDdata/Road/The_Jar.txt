The '''Jar''' was a small courtyard that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The Jar was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]]. The courtyard was found on the south side of [[Tilman's Lane]]

==Notable Locations==
The [[Old Monster Shop]] was located on the Jar.
