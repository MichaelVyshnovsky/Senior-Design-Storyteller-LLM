'''Tchozal's Race''' was a road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Tchozal's Race was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. The road originated in the north on the border with the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward]], branching south and west from [[Julthoon Street]] before curving west of the [[Market (Waterdeep)|Market]] to end on [[Swords Street]]. Other roads crossing Tchozal's Race included [[Trader's Way (Waterdeep)|Trader's Way]] and [[Calamastyr Lane]].
