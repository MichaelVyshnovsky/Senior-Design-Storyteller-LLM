Handaramaturs Court was a cul-de-sac of hard-packed dirt and gravel in the North Ward of Waterdeep.Across the editions this cul-de-sac went unlabeled on maps of Waterdeep. It was only revealed by Ed Greenwood on November 14, 2021.
This court stretched north off of Selvur Alley, not far from where it met Malkurs Ride.
Handaramaturs Court was named for the sage Handaramatur, who lived upon the street in the early 1300s DR up to his untimely death in 1331 DR.
