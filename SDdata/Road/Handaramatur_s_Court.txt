'''Handaramatur's Court''' was a cul-de-sac of hard-packed dirt and [[gravel]]<ref name=CoS.M"> in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].Across the editions this cul-de-sac went unlabeled on maps of Waterdeep. It was only revealed by [[Ed Greenwood]] on November 14{{th}}, 2021.

==Location==
This court stretched north off of [[Selvur Alley]], not far from where it met [[Malkur's Ride]].

==History==
Handaramatur's Court was named for the sage [[Handaramatur]], who lived upon the street in the early 1300's [[DR]] up to his untimely death in [[1331 DR]].
