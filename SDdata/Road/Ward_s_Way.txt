Wards Way was a small road located in the city of Waterdeep.
The road was found in southern Waterdeep within the Dock Ward and Castle Ward. Wards Way  ran north to south, originating on Soothsayers Way just south of Castle Waterdeep and branching south to end at Belnimbras Street. Along its length Wards Way was intersected by Lackpurse Lane, Rainrun Street, and Aruns Alley.
