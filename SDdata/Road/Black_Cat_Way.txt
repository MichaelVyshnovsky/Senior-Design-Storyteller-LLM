Black Cat WayThe Forgotten Realms Interactive Atlas has this street mislabeled as "Black Cart Way." was a road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Black Cat Way was located in the south section of Waterdeeps North Ward. The road branched south off of the southwest side of Golden Serpent Street just south of the intersection with Undals Lane and Downybeard Tobacconist. Black Cat way passed southeast before turning sharply east at its southern end to merge with Sammarins Street. Unnamed alleys branched off both the east and west sides of Black Cat Way.
