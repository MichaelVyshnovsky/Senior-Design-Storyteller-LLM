'''Burning Shield Lane''' was a road that crossed the entirety of [[Ravens Bluff/The Shutters|The Shutters]] neighborhood in the [[Ravens Bluff/Crow's End|Crow's End]] District of [[Ravens Bluff]] from north to south, and ended in the [[Pumpside]] neighborhood.

==Geography==
Mraedara's Street was only four city blocks in length. It began at [[Karen Court]] in The Shutters in the north, and moved straight south, crossing [[Hammerstone Street]], a moot where it met [[Samarglast Lane]] and [[Drover's Run]], crossed [[Conant Court]], and ended at [[Kalikoolan Lane]].
