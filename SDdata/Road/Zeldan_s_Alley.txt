Zeldans Alley was a small backalley road that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
Zeldans Alley was located on the eastern side of Waterdeeps Castle Ward. It ran in between Warriors Way and the Street of the Sword, passing north to south through a small neighborhood. A public well was located near the center of Zeldans Alley.
