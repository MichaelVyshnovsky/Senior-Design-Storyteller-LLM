Tymora Alley was a small road found in the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Tymora Alley branched south off of Wall Street at the midway point between the northern Farmers Gate and Delfen Ondabarls tower. Tymora Alley cut south then east before terminating at the landrise that separated the ducal estate from the rest of Daggerford.
Notable buildings located along Tymora Alley included
* Wiston Apothecary
