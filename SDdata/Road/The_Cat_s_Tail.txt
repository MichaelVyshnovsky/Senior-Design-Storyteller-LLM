The '''Cat's Tail''' was an alley that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The Cat's Tail was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. The alley branched off of the southern end of [[Cat Alley]] in between the [[Street of Bells]], the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]], and [[Waterdeep Way]].
