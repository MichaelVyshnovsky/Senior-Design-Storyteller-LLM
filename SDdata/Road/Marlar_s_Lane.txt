'''Marlar's Lane''' was a small road that was located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
Marlar's Lane was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]]. The road branched west off of the smaller [[Aka Way]], running parallel to [[Tharleon Street]] to the south before terminating at a dead end.
