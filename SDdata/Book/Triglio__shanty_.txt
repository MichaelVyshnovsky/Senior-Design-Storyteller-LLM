'''''Triglio''''' was a sea shanty known to the fishermen of [[Targos]] in [[Icewind Dale]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. They sung it regularly while working on [[Maer Dualdon]]. The general store [[Triglio]] in Targos was named for the song.
{{quote|
''Trig-lee-oh, lads, an' 'oist upon the line.''<br/>
''Trig-lee-oh, lads, an' brinq yon fishers in.''|Lines from ''Triglio''}}
