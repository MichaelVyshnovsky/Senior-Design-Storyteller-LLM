'''"The Joy of Extradimensional Spaces"''' is a short adventure module for 1{{st}}&ndash;level players, collected in the anthology ''[[Candlekeep Mysteries]]'', where players explore a permanent ''[[Mordenkainen's magnificent mansion]]''.
{{TOC limit|2}}
==Synopsis==
In [[1492 DR]], a group of [[adventurer]]s seeks a remedy for a [[curse]] levied on a village. On the advice of a local [[wizard]], they are referred to the sage [[Matreous]] who resides at [[Candlekeep]], but when they arrive they find that Matreous has vanished after tinkering with the [[magic]]al tome titled ''[[The Joy of Extradimensional Spaces]]''.
