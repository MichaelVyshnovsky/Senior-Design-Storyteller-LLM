'''''Encyclopedia Magica Volume IV''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory. 

{{quote|''The Encyclopedia Magica is a four-volume series that describes every magical item for the AD&D game. These items have been collected from rule books, campaign settings, supplements, adventures, and magazines dating back to 1975. This essential reference tool puts the power of more than 20 years of AD&D history at your fingertips!''}}

==Description==
''Encyclopedia Magica Volume IV'' is the fourth and final volume in a series of four 2{{nd}} edition books containing a vast collection of magical items. This fourth volume covered magical items starting with letters S-Z, from ''Theories on Converging Transitions (spellbook)'' to ''Zweiback of Zymurgy''. It also contains an index covering the entire set, and a table for rolling random magic items from the complete series, and includes some of the most numerous categories with entries on swords, staves, and wands.
