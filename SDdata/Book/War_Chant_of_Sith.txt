War Chant of Sith was a powerful war song empowered by a bards magic.
The chants powerful magics became apparent when a talented bard began to sing it in battle. The bards allies who heard the performance received an improved defense any damage dealt to them was reduced as if by magic, and they began to regenerate, slowly healing any wounds on their bodies.
By the , War Chant of Sith was performed only by the most experienced bards of the North. It was heard being performed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale and the village of Kuldahar.
