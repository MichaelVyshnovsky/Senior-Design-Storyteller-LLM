Since the adventure is said to take place mid-summer (p. 58), it is fairly clear the adventure is set in . The adventure contains a dating error, claiming Steelhearts parents fought in the Bloodstone Wars in , prior to her being hatched. At the same time, Steelheart is stated to be 27 years old. This would date the adventure to , which in turn would render a lot of the references to current events anachronistic, such as the repeated references to the Zhentarims allegiance to Cyric&mdashthe Cyricist faction was purged in  (Cloak & Dagger, p. 16). This wiki chalks this discrepancy up to a mistake by the author.
