'''''And That's Why You'll Hear Johnny Cryin'''''<ref group="note">The shanty's actual name was not given in ''[[Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn]]'', this article is using the best applicable line as the title. was a crude sea shanty popular among sailors and buccaneers of [[Athkatla]]n docks in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Contents==
{{quote|''Oh, me mother went a sailin',<br/>And she became a captain too.<br/>She pulled her mate o'er her knees<br/>And spanked 'im black an' blue!<br/><br/>The mate then ran ashore on leave<br/>And cried an' cried all day.<br/>Said me mother was an [[orc]],<br/>An' that she'd have t' pay!<br/><br/>The mate then roused up all th' crew,<br/>An' they cried out for 'er head.<br/>But me mother, she's a smart one;<br/>She jus' winked 'er eye an' said,<br/><br/>Johnny, ye're a dumb one;<br/>does ye think that I was blind?<br/>If ye hadn't given reason,<br/>I'd ne'er spanked yer sore behind.<br/><br/>When the winds picked up an' tossed the ship<br/>From yonder to an' fore,<br/>ye went an' screamed jus' like a girl<br/>An' forgot yer sailor lore.<br/><br/>Because when ye are a sailor,<br/>Ye put other thoughts behind.<br/>Ye gives the ship yer courage,<br/>An ye pays yer fear no mind.<br/><br/>Well, the crew all did agree with her.<br/>They threw Johnny o'erboar',<br/>And that's why you'll hear Johnny cryin'<br/>When the winds they start to roar!<br/><br/>An' that's why you'll hear Johnny cryyyyyin'<br/>When the wiiiinds they start tooooo roooaaar!''|A patron of the [[Sea's Bounty Tavern]]}}

==History==
''And That's Why You'll Hear Johnny Cryin'' could be heard drunkenly performed at the Sea's Bounty Tavern in Athkatla's [[Athkatla/Wave District|Wave District]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.
