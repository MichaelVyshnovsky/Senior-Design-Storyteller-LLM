'''''Vs.''''' was a column by various authors published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]''. The series debuted in September 2000 ([[Dragon magazine 275|Dragon #275]]) and ended in August 2001 ([[Dragon magazine 286|Dragon #286]]). 

The column explained combat tactics and skills of various races and classes, and the best way to fight them.
