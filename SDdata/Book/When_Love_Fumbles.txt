When Love Fumbles was a book from the city of Baldurs Gate, printed sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.
By the , When Love Fumbles could be found in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate. A copy belonged to Lady Firelia Jannath and could be found among the many books in her Lower City estate.
