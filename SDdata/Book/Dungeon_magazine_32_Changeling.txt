'''"Changeling"''' is SideTrek adventure set in the [[Troll Hills]] for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 2{{nd}} edition published in [[Dungeon magazine 32|''Dungeon'' #32]]. It was designed for a party of 4&ndash;8 adventurers, ranging in levels from 8&ndash;10.
