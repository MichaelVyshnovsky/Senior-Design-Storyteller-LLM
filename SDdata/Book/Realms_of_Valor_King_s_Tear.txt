"Kings Tear" is a short story by Mark Anthony included in the 1993 anthology Realms of Valor.
The necromancer Kelshara steals the Tear of Everard from Everard Abbey, and it is up to would-be-loremaster Tyveris to retrieve it, but first he must reaffirm his faith in Oghma.
