Cormyr is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.
Cormyr provides a comprehensive overview of the kingdom of Cormyr, including its geography, borders, and historical background. It contains details about the Cormyrean nobility, the monarchy, and the key figures who govern the kingdom. This includes insights into the ruling Obarskyr dynasty. It describes Cormyrean culture, customs, and societal norms. This includes discussions of religion, entertainment, and the role of bards in preserving the kingdoms history. It also provides information on Cormyrs legal system, its codes of conduct, and the role of the War Wizards in maintaining order and security.
The accessory profiles of notable cities and locations within Cormyr, such as Suzail (the capital), Marsember, and Arabel. These descriptions provide a sense of the kingdoms urban and rural areas. It also provides information on various organizations, guilds, and factions operating within Cormyr. This includes groups like the Purple Dragons (Cormyrs military), the Harpers, and the Fire Knives.
Cormyr contains ideas and adventure hooks for Dungeon Masters to create campaigns and adventures set in Cormyr. These can involve political intrigue, quests for noble houses, and investigations into threats to the kingdom. The accessory is illustrated with maps and artwork that help bring the kingdom of Cormyr to life and assist players and DMs in visualizing the setting.
