'''''Wyrms of the North''''' was a column by [[Ed Greenwood]] published in [[Dragon (magazine)|''Dragon'' magazine]]. The series debuted in June 1996 (''[[Dragon magazine 230|Dragon #230]]'') and ended in May 1999 (''[[Dragon magazine 259|Dragon #259]]''), and later reprinted on the [[Wizards of the Coast]] website.

Despite the title of the series, the articles covered famous dragons from across western [[Faerûn]], along the [[Sword Coast]] from [[Icewind Dale]] to [[Calimshan]]. They offered a detailed look into the lore, actions, abilities, magic and other topics on each dragon.
