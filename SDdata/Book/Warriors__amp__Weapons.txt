'''''Warriors & Weapons''''' is a 5{{th}}-edition [[Dungeons & Dragons]] guide featuring characters, classes, equipment, and character-building ideas intended for readers between 8 and 12 years old.{{cite web |url=https://dnd.wizards.com/products/fiction/novels/warriors-weapons |title=Product Overview |accessdate=2019-08-19 |publisher=[[Wizards of the Coast]]}}

{{quote
|<big>'''''Explore the magical worlds of D&D<sup>®</sup>'''''</big>

''You hold in your hands an immersive, one-of-a-kind guide to the brave and bold heroes, along with their weapons, armor, clothing, and equipment, from the world's most beloved tabletop role-playing game―DUNGEONS & DRAGONS<sup>®</sup>. Featuring amazing new illustrations and expert insights, ''Warriors & Weapons'' provides would-be adventurers with all the information you need to start building your own character ad putting together your adventuring party. If you're eager to create your own D&D adventures, this guidebook provides the perfect starting point to worlds of fantasy and an epic tale all your own.''
