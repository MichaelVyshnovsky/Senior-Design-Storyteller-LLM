[[Dragon+]] was a mobile application developed by marketing agency [http://dialectinc.com Dialect Inc.] in collaboration with [[Wizards of the Coast]] for Apple and Android products.{{cite web |url=http://dnd.wizards.com/articles/news/dragonplus |title=Dragon+ |accessdate=2015-12-18 |last=Kidd |first=Trevor |authorlink= |coauthors= |date=2015-04-30 |format= |work=Dungeons & Dragons |publisher=Wizards of the Coast |pages= |language=  |quote= }}

==History==
After the [[Dungeon magazine|''Dungeon'']] and [[Dragon (magazine)|''Dragon'' magazines]] were placed on hiatus in December 2013, Wizards of the Coast concentrated on the release of the 5th edition of the [[Dungeons & Dragons]] game. Rather than bringing the magazine out of hiatus, Wizards began to develop a platform that would both collate all of its social media content and also deliver a free bi-monthly collection of content in a similar vein to that which was found in the old magazines.
==Content==
Dragon+ contains interviews, spotlights on Dungeons & Dragons multimedia; articles on classic D&D products; official rules clarifications; fiction; lore; humor and more.
