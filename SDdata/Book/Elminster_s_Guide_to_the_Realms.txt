'''''Elminster's Guide to the Realms''''' was a column by [[Ed Greenwood]] published in [[Dragon (magazine)|''Dragon'' magazine]]. The column debuted in June 2001 in the  25th Anniversary Issue ([[Dragon magazine 284|''Dragon'' #284]]) and ended in August 2004 ([[Dragon magazine 322|''Dragon'' #322]]).
