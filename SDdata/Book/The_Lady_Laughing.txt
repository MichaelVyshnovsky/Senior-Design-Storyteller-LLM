The Lady Laughing was a ballad penned sometime before the early 14 century DR.
The Lady Laughing was one of many ballads in Mintiper Moonsilvers repertoire. The Company of Crazed Venturers was present at the famed bard Mintipers performance in Neverwinter sometime in the early-to-mid 14 century DR, during which he performed The Lady Laughing.
