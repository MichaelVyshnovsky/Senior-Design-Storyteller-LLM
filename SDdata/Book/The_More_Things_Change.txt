'''"The More Things Change"''' is a short story by [[Elaine Cunningham]], first published in the 1994 anthology ''[[Realms of Infamy]]'', and reprinted in the 2007 anthology ''[[The Best of the Realms III]]''.

==Summary==
Elaith Craulnober shares an encounter with his betrothed [[Amnestria]] in the realm of [[Evermeet]], his ideal life set before him. Later, after having been rejected by his ''[[moonblade]]'', [[Elaith Craulnober]] leaves Evermeet and Amnestria behind, joining a band of mercenaries in [[Waterdeep]]. Decades later, ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1390}}, Elaith, now a crimelord of Waterdeep, arranges for his daughter [[Azariah Craulnober|Azariah]] to be trained by [[Arilyn Moonblade]], the famed [[half-elf]] assassin.
