Curse of Strahd is an adventure module for the 5-edition of Dungeons & Dragons, published by Wizards of the Coast in 2016. Set in the Ravenloft campaign setting, the module revolves around the vampire Strahd von Zarovich and the Demiplane of Dread that serves as his prison. It is intended for 4 players, taking them from level 1 to 10.
