'''''The Moondapple Stag''''' was a ballad penned sometime before the early 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Contents==
''The Stag'' was one of many songs, ballads, and musical compositions in [[Mintiper Moonsilver]]'s repertoire. The [[Company of Crazed Venturers]] was present at the famed [[bard]] Mintiper's performance in [[Neverwinter]] sometime in the early-to-mid 14{{th}} century DR, during which he performed several songs, including ''The Moondapple Stag''.
