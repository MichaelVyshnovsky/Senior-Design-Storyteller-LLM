'''''The Cormyte's Boast''''' was a song written by Master Bard [[Chanthalas]] of [[Cormyr]].

==Contents==
{{quote|And in this land I'll proudly stand<br />Until my dying day, sir;<br />For whate'er king o'er all command,<br />I'll still be a Cormyte brave, sir.|An excerpt from ''The Cormyte's Boast''}}<br/>

In the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], ''The Cormyte's Boast'' was used as an example of a typical Cormyte in ''[[Volo's Guide to Cormyr (in-universe)|Volo's Guide to Cormyr]]'' by Master Traveler [[Volothamp Geddarm]]. The ballad illustrated them as being "honest, self-confident, and loyal."
