Cobblers and Pies Baked Beans Can Be Fun was a cookbook penned by the eccentric street vendor Hephtos, more commonly known as Friar Cookpot, in the late 14 century DR.
Cobblers and Pies, along with  1001 Ways to Cook Beans, were collections of colorful and overly-complicated recipes, most of which contained beans and grains. The books author was commonly seen cooking and serving his recipes across the street from Ravens Bluffs market or by the Harbor Gate.
