'''''Bazaar of the Bizarre''''' was a long-running column published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]''. The series debuted in July 1979 ([[Dragon magazine 27|Dragon #27]]) and ended in November 2013 ([[Dragon magazine 429|Dragon #429]]). They would discuss various magical items, classes, spells and weapons in the D&D multiverse.
