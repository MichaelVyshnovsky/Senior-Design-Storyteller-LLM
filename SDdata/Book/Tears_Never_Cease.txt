'''''Tears Never Cease''''' was a ballad written by [[Halindar Droun]], [[Bard]] of [[Beregost]] in the {{YearlinkName|1330}}.

{{quote|''In the name of a kingdom<br/>Many fell things are done.<br/>In the name of a love<br/>Fairer things are won.''|Halindar Droun, Bard of Beregost}}

==Contents==
''Tears Never Cease'' was a dramatic sad ballad that sang about loss, love, atrocities committed by kingdoms' rulers, and things accomplished by committed lovers.
