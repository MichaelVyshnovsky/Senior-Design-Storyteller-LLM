'''"The Walls of Midnight"''' is a short story by [[Mark Anthony]] included in the 1994 anthology ''[[Realms of Infamy]]''.

==Summary==
[[Ravendas]], a former member of the [[Flaming Fist]] from [[Baldur's Gate]], is sent on a mission by the [[Zhentarim]] to retrieve an ancient magical object, the [[Finger of Ckai-el-Ckaan]]. Arriving at [[Gurthang]], the old [[sorcerer]]'s tower in the [[Sunset Mountains]], she finds its walls insurmountable. With the help of [[Marnok]], a [[mage]] she meets there, she overcomes the fortress's defenses and escapes, sacrificing Marnok, with the prize in her hands.
