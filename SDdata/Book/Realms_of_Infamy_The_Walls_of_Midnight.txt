"The Walls of Midnight" is a short story by Mark Anthony included in the 1994 anthology Realms of Infamy.
Ravendas, a former member of the Flaming Fist from Baldurs Gate, is sent on a mission by the Zhentarim to retrieve an ancient magical object, the Finger of Ckai-el-Ckaan. Arriving at Gurthang, the old sorcerers tower in the Sunset Mountains, she finds its walls insurmountable. With the help of Marnok, a mage she meets there, she overcomes the fortresss defenses and escapes, sacrificing Marnok, with the prize in her hands.
