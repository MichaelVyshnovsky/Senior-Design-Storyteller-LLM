'''The Dragon's Bestiary''' was a long-running column in [[Dragon magazine]] which introduced new monsters within specific themes. The series debuted in June 1979 ([[Dragon magazine 26|Dragon #26]]) and ended in September 2013 ([[Dragon magazine 427|Dragon #427]]).
