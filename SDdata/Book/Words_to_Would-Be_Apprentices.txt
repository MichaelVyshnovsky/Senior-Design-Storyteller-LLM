Words to Would-be Apprentices was a book penned by Khelben Arunsun, the Blackstaff of Waterdeep in the .
Khelben Arunsuns book was a collection musings and lessoned aimed at his potential future pupils. The book was also a warning to  future wizards about the dangers of the Art.
By the late 14 century DR, a copy of Words to Would-be Apprentices was collected in the annals of the citadel library of Candlekeep on the Sword Coast.
