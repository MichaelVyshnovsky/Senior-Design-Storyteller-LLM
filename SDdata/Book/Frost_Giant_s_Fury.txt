'''''Frost Giant's Fury''''' is a series of ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'' comic books published by [[wikipedia:en:IDW Publishing|IDW Publishing]]. It is a direct continuation of the previous series ''[[Shadows of the Vampire]]'' and ''[[Legends of Baldur's Gate]]'', and is itself continued in ''[[Evil at Baldur's Gate]]'' and ''[[Infernal Tides]]''.
