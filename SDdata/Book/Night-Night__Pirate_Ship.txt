'''''Night-Night, Pirate Ship''''' was a collection of thematic sea-sailing nursery rhymes, compiled and printed sometime in or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Contents==
One nursery rhyme of note included in the [[book]] was called ''[[Umberlee]] Remembers.''

{{quote|''''Toss your coin on whispered prayer,''<br/>''Down, down, down,''<br/>''Spare us Sea Queen, blessed and fair,''<br/>''Down, down, down,''<br/>''To your depths we're dragged with care,''<br/>''Down, down, down.''|Expert from ''Umberlee Remembers''}}

==History==
''Night-Night, Pirate Ship'' could be found in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] and sold by [[Thomas C. Quirkilious]] of [[Chromatic Scales]]. Another copy belonged to [[Lora]], a sailor who inhabited the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of the Gate.
