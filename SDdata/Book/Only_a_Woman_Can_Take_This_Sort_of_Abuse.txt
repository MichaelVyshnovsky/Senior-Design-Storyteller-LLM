'''"Only a Woman Can Take this Kind of Abuse"''' is a short story by [[Ed Greenwood]] revolving around the [[Seven Sisters]], based on a number of suggestions provided by attendees of [[Wikipedia:Gen Con|Gen Con]] 2003's ''Spin a Yarn with Ed Greenwood'' seminar, and published on the [[Wizards of the Coast]] website that same year.
