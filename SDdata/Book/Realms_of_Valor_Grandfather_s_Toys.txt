"Grandfathers Toys" is a short story by Jean Rabe included in the 1993 anthology Realms of Valor. The story features characters from Rabes novel Red Magic.
The druid Galvin is asked to search a forgetful wizards tower for a little girl, only to find the task surprisingly grueling&mdashand dangerous.
