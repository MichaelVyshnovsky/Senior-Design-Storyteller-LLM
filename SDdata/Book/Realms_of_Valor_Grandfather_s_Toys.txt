'''"Grandfather's Toys"''' is a short story by [[Jean Rabe]] included in the 1993 anthology ''[[Realms of Valor]]''. The story features characters from Rabe's novel ''[[Red Magic]]''.

==Summary==
The [[druid]] [[Galvin]] is asked to search a forgetful wizard's tower for a little girl, only to find the task surprisingly grueling&mdash;and dangerous.
