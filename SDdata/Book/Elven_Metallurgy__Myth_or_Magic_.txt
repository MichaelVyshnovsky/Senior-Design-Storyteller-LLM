Elven Metallurgy, Myth or Magic? was a book penned by Tharil Iraldrorn sometime before the late 14 century DR.
A copy of Elven Metallurgy belonged to Manshoon XII, a clone of Manshoon, in the . The book was stored in the mages library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep.
