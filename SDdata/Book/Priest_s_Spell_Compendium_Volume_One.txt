'''''Priest's Spell Compendium Volume One''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory.
{{quote|'''''The Ultimate Collection!'''''

''The Priest's Spell Compendium is a three-volume series describing every priest spell for the [[AD&D]] game. Spells have been collected from rule books, campaign settings, supplements, adventures, and magazines dating back to 1975. Early entries have been updated to be immediately usable, making this the player's and DM's definitive source for priest spells.''}}

==Description==
''Priest's Spell Compendium Volume One'' specifically tailored for clerics, druids, and other priestly classes. It contains alphabetically sorted [[spell]]s from ''[[a day in the life]]'' to ''[[frost whip]]''.

The compendium is an extensive collection of divine spells, gathered from various ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' sourcebooks, campaign settings, and adventure modules. Its primary aim is to provide a comprehensive and easily accessible reference for players and Dungeon Masters alike.

Each spell entry includes detailed information such as the spell's name, level, range, duration, area of effect, components required, and a thorough description of what the spell does. ''Priest's Spell Compendium Volume One'' not only revisits classic spells familiar to long-time players but also introduces modifications and clarifications to existing spells where necessary. It also addresses any ambiguities in the spell descriptions from earlier publications, providing clarifications to ensure a consistent interpretation of the rules.
