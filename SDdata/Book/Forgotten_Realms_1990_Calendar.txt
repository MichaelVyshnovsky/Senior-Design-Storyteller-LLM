The '''Forgotten Realms 1990 Calendar''' was a calendar published by [[TSR, Inc.]] in 1989. It displays fourteen artworks from {{Dnd}} and ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'' products. Each month displays an artwork with the source and an original quote.
{{quote|
The land of the Forgotten Realms is a land of excitement, adventure, and mystery. From the saga of the Avatar to the flying ships of Halruaa, from the jungles of Chult to the Moonshae Isles, from the streets of cultured Waterdeep to the lands of the barbarian Horde, the borders of this ultimate fantasy world contain all the rich variety of fantastic adventure.

[[Jeff Butler]], [[Clyde Caldwell]], [[Jeff Easley]], [[Larry Elmore]], and [[Jim Holloway]], artists who define the fantastic, bring you 14 unforgettable visions of the Forgotten Realms game world, the home world of epic novels, new games, and excited adventures for the Advanced Dungeons & Dragons 2nd Edition game.
|Back-cover text}}
