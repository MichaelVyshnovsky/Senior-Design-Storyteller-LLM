The '''''Saturday Morning Adventures''''' comics adaptation of ''Dungeons & Dragons'' is a four-issue series published by [[wikipedia:en:IDW Publishing|IDW Publishing]], part of their ''Saturday Morning Adventures'' line which also includes ''Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles'' and ''G.I. Joe''. The miniseries is written by [[David M. Booher]]  and [[Sam Maggs]], with art by [[George Kambadais]]. It follows the characters of the 1983–1985 [[w:c:dungeonsdragons:Dungeons & Dragons (TV series)|''Dungeons & Dragons'']] cartoon series.
