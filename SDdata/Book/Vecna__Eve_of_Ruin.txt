'''''Vecna: Eve of Ruin''''' is a high-level adventure for 5{{th}}-edition ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' published in May 2024. A preorder-exclusive tie-in adventure, ''[[Vecna: Nest of the Eldritch Eye]]'', was made available in April, 2024.
{{Quote|
'''Who Will Save Existence?'''

The evil lich-god Vecna has unearthed secrets he can use to unravel and remake the multiverse. Stopping Vecna will require working with three of the multiverse's most famous archmages, traveling to far-flung locales, and rebuilding the legendary ''Rod of Seven Parts''.

''Vecna: Eve of Ruin''™ is a high-stakes DUNGEONS & DRAGONS® adventure in which the fate of the multiverse hangs in the balance. The heroes begin in the Forgotten Realms® and travel to Planescape®, Spelljammer®, Eberron®, Ravenloft®, Dragonlance®, and Greyhawk® as they race to save existence from obliteration.}}
