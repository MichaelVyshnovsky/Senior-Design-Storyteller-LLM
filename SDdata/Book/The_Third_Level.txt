'''"The Third Level"''' is a short story by [[R.A. Salvatore]], first published in the 1994 anthology ''[[Realms of Infamy]]'', and reprinted in the 2011 anthology ''[[The Collected Stories: The Legend of Drizzt Anthology]]''.

==Summary==
After demonstrating his profound skill, fourteen-year-old [[Artemis Entreri]] finds himself invited to the [[House Basadoni|Basadoni]] cabal of thieves. By winning a game of chance and deception, he becomes a lieutenant of that prestigious organization within only a few months of joining.
