'''''Tome of Magic: Pact, Shadow and Truename Magic''''' is an accessory for the 3.5{{th}}-edition of the {{dnd}} ruleset. It presents new three magical systems: [[pact magic]], [[shadow magic]], and [[truename magic]].

{{quote|
'''Unlock the magical power of vestiges, shadows, and syllables.'''

The ''Tome of Magic'' supplement presents three new kinds of magic that you can integrate easily into any Dungeons & Dragons® campaign. These magic "subsystems" function alongside the existing D&D® magic systems and offer new game mechanics, character options, and adventure possibilities. Within this tome, you'll find three new standard classes—one for each kind of magic—as well as new spells, feats, [[:Category:Prestige classes|prestige classes]], monsters, and magic items tied thematically to each.|Back-cover text}}

==Description==
''Tome of Magic'' is devoted to new forms and styles of magic as a path to power and presents three magical systems: [[pact magic]], [[shadow magic]], and [[truename magic]]. These new paths are embodied in three new alternate base classes. The book is arranged into three sections, one for each of the new character classes and their unique take on magic. Each chapter includes several feats, prestige classes, magic items and monsters associated with that style of magic.
