'''''Dragon Annual 3''''' was published by [[TSR, Inc.]] in December of 1998.  It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==By Dragons Ruled and Divided==
: ''By [[Ed Greenwood]], Illustrated by [[Stephen Daniele]], Cartography by [[Linda Kekumu]].'' '''p. 6–17'''
The Draconic Domains of the [[Sword Coast North]]. This article details the territories of all the dragons featured in the ''[[Wyrms of the North]]'' series. {{drasup|1998/By Dragons Ruled and Divided}}

==[[Ecology]] of the Steel Dragon==
: ''By [[Jay Knioum]], Illustrated by [[Stephen Schwartz]].'' '''p. 74–80'''
A scholarly treatise on the [[Steel dragon|Steel Dragon]]. {{drasup|1998/The Ecology of the Steel Dragon}}

==[[Arcane Lore]]: The Prayers of Stromp and Havvel==
: ''By [[Loren Coleman]], Illustrated by [[Bob Klasnich]].'' '''p. 82–86'''
To see how impressive dwarven magic can be, one need only look to the earth. {{Drasup|1998/Arcane Lore: The Prayers of Stromp and Havvel}}

==[[The Dragon's Bestiary]]: Urban Pests==
: ''By [[Johnathan M. Richards]], Illustrated by [[David Day]].'' '''p. 106–111'''
What happens between adventures is often the talk of the town. {{Drasup|1998/Dragon's Bestiary: Urban Pests}}

* [[Acid pigeon]]
* [[Piranha ant]]
* [[Polyroach]]
* [[Rat burglar]]
* [[Stone termite]]

==[[Rogues Gallery]]: Faces of Deception==
: ''By [[Troy Denning]], Illustrated by [[Mike Vilardi]].'' '''p. 112–118'''
Don't be fooled by these faces of deception. {{Drasup|1998/Rogues Gallery: Faces of Deception}}
