Dragon Annual 3 was published by TSR, Inc. in December of 1998.  It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms
 By Ed Greenwood, Illustrated by Stephen Daniele, Cartography by Linda Kekumu. p. 6–17
The Draconic Domains of the Sword Coast North. This article details the territories of all the dragons featured in the Wyrms of the North series. 
 By Jay Knioum, Illustrated by Stephen Schwartz. p. 74–80
A scholarly treatise on the Steel Dragon. 
 By Loren Coleman, Illustrated by Bob Klasnich. p. 82–86
To see how impressive dwarven magic can be, one need only look to the earth. 
 By Johnathan M. Richards, Illustrated by David Day. p. 106–111
What happens between adventures is often the talk of the town. 
* Acid pigeon
* Piranha ant
* Polyroach
* Rat burglar
* Stone termite
 By Troy Denning, Illustrated by Mike Vilardi. p. 112–118
Dont be fooled by these faces of deception.
