'''''Monster Cards''''' were a series of cards published by [[TSR, Inc.]] in 1981. 

==Description==
Their purpose was to provide a Dungeon Master with a visual aid and basic statistics about a specific {{dnd}} creature. Each set contained twenty {{SI|3|in|c}} by {{SI|5|in|c}} cards. One side contained an illustration of a certain creature, while the reverse side contained statistics and basic lore.
