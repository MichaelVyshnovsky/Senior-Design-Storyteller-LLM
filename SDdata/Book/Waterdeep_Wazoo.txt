'''''The Waterdeep Wazoo''''' was a broadsheet periodical newspaper published in [[Waterdeep]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The newspaper was published by [[Gaxly Rudderbust]],

Gaxly authored some of the newspaper's articles himself, but the paper also accepted editorials from freelance contributors.

==History==
In [[1492 DR]],{{Waterdeep-time}} [[Jarlaxle]] anonymously submitted to the paper an expos√© on the rising [[devil]] worship cults among Waterdhavian [[Nobility of Waterdeep|noble families]]. That same year, an article written by Gaxly was also published, speculating on the origins of the [[Black Viper]] and linking her to the [[Cassalanter]] family.

==Reputation==
The ''Wazoo's'' news were followed by noble Waterdhavian families and various organizations, such as the [[Order of the Gauntlet]].
