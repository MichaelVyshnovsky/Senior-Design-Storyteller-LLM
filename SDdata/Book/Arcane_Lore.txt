'''''Arcane Lore''''' was a column in [[Dragon (magazine)|''Dragon'' magazine]] by various authors. It was published from July 1987 ([[Dragon magazine 123|Dragon #123]]) to July 2003 ([[Dragon magazine 309|Dragon #309]]). 

While similar to ''[[Bazaar of the Bizarre]]'', which had a focus on magical artifacts, this column focused on spells and other magical lore.
