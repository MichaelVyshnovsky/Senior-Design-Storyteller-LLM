Dawnbringer is a standalone novel by Samantha Henderson.
* The novel has the distinction of being set farthest in the "future" of the Forgotten Realms, with much of the action taking place in the Year of Unseen Enemies, 1600 DR. This is 121 years after the then-current year of the setting and 4th-edition rules, the Year of the Ageless One, 1479 DR.
