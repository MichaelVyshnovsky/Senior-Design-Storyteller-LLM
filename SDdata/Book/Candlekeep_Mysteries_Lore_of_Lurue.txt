'''"Lore of Lurue"''' is a short adventure module for 8{{th}}&ndash;level players, collected in the anthology ''[[Candlekeep Mysteries]]'', where players are pulled into a [[magic]]al book telling the story of how the city of [[Silverymoon]] got its name.
{{TOC limit|2}}

==Synopsis==
In [[1492 DR]], a group of [[adventurer]]s in [[Candlekeep]] open the tome ''[[Lore of Lurue]]'' and find themselves transported into a [[demiplane]] conjured by the book itself. The adventurers must navigate to the end of the story to return home, encountering [[fey]] creatures and cultists of [[Malar]] along the way, and learning how the [[deity]] [[Lurue]] is connected to the founding of Silverymoon.
