'''''Dragons Ye Should Know''''' was a [[book]] published by the sage [[Myrindas]] of [[Port Kir]] in the year [[1354 DR]].
{{Quote|''...the icy claws that wait at the cold end of the world.''|Myrindas describing [[Arauthator]].}}

==Description==
This book detailed a number of notable [[dragon]]s in the Realms, such as the [[white dragon]] Arauthator.

==History==
This book was later quoted by [[Volothamp Geddarm]] in his own chapbook detailing the most notable dragon rulers of the [[Sword Coast North]] region, [[Wyrms of the North (in-universe)|''Wyrms of the North'']].
