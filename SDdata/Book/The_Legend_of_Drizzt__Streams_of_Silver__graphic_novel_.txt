==Reprints==
The ''The Legend of Drizzt: Streams of Silver Graphic Novel'' series was reprinted in 2007 as a paperback and hardcover omnibus by [[wikipedia:en:Devil's Due Publishing|Devil's Due Publishing]]. It was released as a paperback omnibus in October 2016 by [[wikipedia:en:IDW Publishing|IDW Publishing]].
