'''''Tales of Far Travels''''' was a [[book]] written by [[Seroun]] of [[Calimport]] and published in the {{YearlinkName|1286}}.
{{quote|''Neglect not small things, for all ruling and war and magecraft are naught but small things, one built upon another. Begin then with the small, and look close, and you will see it all.''|Excerpt from ''Tales of Far Travels''}}

==History==
The book collected the author's accounts of ventures to the nations outside [[Calimshan]] and shared lessons learned from the experiences. The book was published in the {{YearlinkName|1286}}.{{Cite web/The Candlekeep Collection}}
