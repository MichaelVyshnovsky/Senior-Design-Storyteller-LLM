'''"Masqueraider"''' is a short ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' adventure originally published in [[Dungeon magazine 14|''Dungeon'' #14]], then reprinted in the anthology ''[[Dungeons of Despair]]''.
{{TOC limit|2}}
{{Quote|"Masqueraider" is an AD&D® adventure for 4&ndash;6 characters of 2{{nd}} to 5{{th}} level. The adventuring party should be composed of varied classes, but no particular class is essential for successfully completing the adventure.<br />
This adventure takes place in the country of Cormyr, in the FORGOTTEN REALMS™ setting.}}
