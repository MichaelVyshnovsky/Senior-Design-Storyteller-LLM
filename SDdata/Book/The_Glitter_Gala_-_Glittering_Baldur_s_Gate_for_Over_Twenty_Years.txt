The Glitter Gala - Glittering Baldurs Gate for Over Twenty Years was a self-promotional material published by The Glitter Gala shop of Baldurs Gate, penned in the late 15 century DR.
The pamphlet featured images of patriars of the Gate wearing jewelry designed by Madame Omotola. The main-stage drawing was 
detailed design of a lavish enchanted wedding ring created for Lady Fireliia Jannath of the Jannath patriar family.
The Glitter Gala - Glittering Baldurs Gate for Over Twenty Years was published in the , soon after Omotola designed the ring for Lady Jannaths wedding to the renowned painter Oskar Fevras. The ring created by The Glitter Gala was called Til Death Do Us Part.
