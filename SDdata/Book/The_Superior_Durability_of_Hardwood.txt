'''''The Superior Durability of Hardwood''''' was a [[book]] that promoted the craftsmanship of [[Odd Valros]], a [[coffin]]-maker from [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Choose Valros, because the end is always near!''|Excerpt from ''The Superior Durability of Hardwood''}}

==Contents==
Odd Valros's book advertised the deluxe coffins made of [[hardwood]]. It claimed that this superior [[wood]] helped preserve corpses and protected them from vermin such as [[rat]]s and [[maggot]]s for thrice the time other materials did.

==History==
A copy of ''The Superior Durability of Hardwood'' could be found inside [[Candulhallow's Funeral Arrangements]], or Candulhallow's Tombstones, in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of Baldur's Gate in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
