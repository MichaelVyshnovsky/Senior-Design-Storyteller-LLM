The Superior Durability of Hardwood was a book that promoted the craftsmanship of Odd Valros, a coffin-maker from Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
Odd Valross book advertised the deluxe coffins made of hardwood. It claimed that this superior wood helped preserve corpses and protected them from vermin such as rats and maggots for thrice the time other materials did.
