Thesis on Conditional Ruptures was a book by the wizard Archveult Tattercloak.The Dragon article contains a disclaimer that not all the books it lists are necessarily written by a given spell inventor, and could instead be written by an apprentice or relative.
The book detailed several spells, including teleport, phase door, shadow door, maze, and Archveults skybolt.
