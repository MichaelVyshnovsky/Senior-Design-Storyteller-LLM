'''''Secrets of the Magister''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory for the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]''.  

{{quote|'''''[[Mage]]s kill each other to become the Magister.'''''

The Magister of Mystra is one of the most mysterious&mdash;and misunderstood&mdash;beings in the [[Forgotten Realms]] setting. Some say the Magister is simply the most powerful mortal mage in all of [[Toril]]. Perhaps, but there is something more, something unique, about being so touched by the Mother of All Magic.

The mages of [[Faerûn]] roll up their sleeves and craft the spells and items that they hope will empower them to win the office of the Magister. A few will succeed. More will wish they'd never taken up magic in the first place.

Here at last, one can learn all the secrets, abilities, and sacred duties of the Magister. Plus, discover the fate of [[Nouméa Drathchuld|Lady Nouméa]] and the identity of her successor as Magister. Also, read guidelines on how a PC mage might become the Magister.

This book holds the history of the office, new powers, [[spell]]s, [[magic item]]s, a roster of select Magisters from the past, and guidelines on how to incorporate the Magister into your campaign.

'''''One Magister became a god; others have hoped to.'''''}}

==Description==
''Secrets of the Magister'' is the 13{{th}} and final book in the [[FOR series]]. It was one of the last ''Forgotten Realms'' books published for ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons''. 

''Secrets of the Magister'' focuses on the [[Magister]] of [[Mystra]], including the powers they wield, the challenges they face, and the secrets they guard.

It provides a history of the Magister’s office, tracing its origins, notable individuals who have held the title, and the evolution of its responsibilities over the centuries. It also contains detailed descriptions of the unique powers and abilities granted to the Magister, including spells, magical items, and other arcane resources. ALso included is a selection of new magical items and spells associated with the Magister’s office.

''Secrets of the Magister'' contains profiles of significant Magisters throughout history, offering insights into their lives, achievements, and the impact they have had on the Realms.

For Dungeon Masters, the accessory provides numerous adventure hooks and plot ideas centered around the Magister and their secrets.
