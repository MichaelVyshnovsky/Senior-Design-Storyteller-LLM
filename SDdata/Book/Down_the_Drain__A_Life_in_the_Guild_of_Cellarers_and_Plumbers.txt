'''''Down the Drain: A Life in the Guild of Cellarers and Plumbers''''' was a [[book]] written by [[Orlar Sarluk]]. It was published in the year [[1356 DR]].

==Contents==
This book detailed Sarluk's experiences working in the [[Cellarers' & Plumbers' Guild]] of [[Waterdeep]], as well as his experiences working in [[Waterdeep/Sewers|the city's sewers]].
