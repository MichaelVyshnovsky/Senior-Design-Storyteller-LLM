'''''Habits of Patriars''''' was a self-help [[book]] from the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Content==
The book cautioned its readers of the dangers of overindulgence and self-gratification so common among the [[patriar]]s of Baldur's Gate.

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, ''Habits of Patriars'' could be found in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of Baldur's Gate and was available in the [[House of Grief]]'s garden.
