Habits of Patriars was a self-help book from the city of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
The book cautioned its readers of the dangers of overindulgence and self-gratification so common among the patriars of Baldurs Gate.
By the , Habits of Patriars could be found in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate and was available in the House of Griefs garden.
