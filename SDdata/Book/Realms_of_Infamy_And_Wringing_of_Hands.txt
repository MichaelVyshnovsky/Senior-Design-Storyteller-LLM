'''"And Wringing of Hands"''' is a short story by [[Jane Cooper Hong]] included in the 1994 anthology ''[[Realms of Infamy]]''.

==Summary==
[[Tine]], a small-time [[alchemist]], moonlights as a [[poison]]-maker and accomplice to the [[assassin]] [[Renek]]. Tine's conscience and desires are not always in concordance with the malignant life of hired killers.
