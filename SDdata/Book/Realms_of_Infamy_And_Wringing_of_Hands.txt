"And Wringing of Hands" is a short story by Jane Cooper Hong included in the 1994 anthology Realms of Infamy.
Tine, a small-time alchemist, moonlights as a poison-maker and accomplice to the assassin Renek. Tines conscience and desires are not always in concordance with the malignant life of hired killers.
