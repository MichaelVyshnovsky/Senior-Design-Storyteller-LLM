'''''The Dance of Kings''''' was a ballad penned by [[Sarauntra Tynorora|Sarauntra "The She-Bard" Tynorora]] in the mid-15{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{quote|And so our just and beneficent ruler smiles<br/>On proudest lords in stately conclave met<br/>Seeing no little deceit, no shortage of wiles<br/>Though they work no open regicide. Yet.|Excerpt from ''The Dance of Kings''}}

==History==
''The Dance of Kings'' ballad was first performed in the {{YearlinkName|1446}}.
