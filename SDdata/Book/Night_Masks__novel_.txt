'''''Night Masks''''' is the third book of the [[The Cleric Quintet]] by [[R.A. Salvatore]].

{{quote|''[[Carradoon]], city of shadows, city of assassins. Through its dark streets flit the killers of the [[Night Masks]], led by the thin, sinister killer known as [[Ghost (assassin)|Ghost]]. In the city's evil alleyways [[Cadderly Bonaduce|Cadderly]], struggling against the horrible [[Chaos Curse]], must fight an additional battle against sudden, silent death.''}}
