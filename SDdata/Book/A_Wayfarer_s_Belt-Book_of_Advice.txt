'''''A Wayfarer's Belt-Book of Advice''''' was a [[book]] penned by [[sage]] [[Rasthiavar]] of [[Iriaebor]] in the late 13{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Tell ye of the [[balhiir]]? Ah, a curious creature, indeed. I hear it was first-the short version, ye say? Very well; ye are paying. The short version is thus: a curious creature, indeed. Thank ye, good sir; fair day to ye.''|Excerpt from ''A Wayfarer's Belt-Book of Advice''}}

==History==
''A Wayfarer's Belt-Book of Advice'' was published in the {{YearlinkName|1282}}. One notable part of the book talked about a rare magic-eating creatures known as balhiir.{{Cite web/The Candlekeep Collection}}
