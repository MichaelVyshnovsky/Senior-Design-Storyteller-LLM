'''''Prospaer on Nameless Horrors''''' was a book on [[magic]].

==History==
In the {{yearlinkName|764}}, the sorceress [[Dasumia]] commanded her apprentice [[Elminster Aumar]] to fetch ''Prospaer on Nameless Horrors'' from her side table in a well-warded room, the Blue Chamber, in her own [[Castle of the Lady]], along with the other books the ''[[Orbrum]]'' and the ''[[Tome of Three Locks]]''.
