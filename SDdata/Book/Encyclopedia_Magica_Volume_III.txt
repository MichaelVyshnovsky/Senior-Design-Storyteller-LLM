Encyclopedia Magica Volume III is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory.
Encyclopedia Magica Volume III is the third in a series of four 2 edition books containing a vast collection of magical items. This third volume covered magical items starting with letters P-S, from Pick of Earth Parting to Thesis on Conditional Ruptures (spellbook).
