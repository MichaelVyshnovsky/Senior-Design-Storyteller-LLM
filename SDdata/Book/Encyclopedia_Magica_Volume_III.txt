'''''Encyclopedia Magica Volume III''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory.

{{quote|''The Encyclopedia Magica is a four-volume series that describes every magical item for the AD&D game. These items have been collected from rule books, campaign settings, supplements, adventures, and magazines dating back to 1975. This essential reference tool puts the power of more than 20 years of AD&D history at your fingertips!''}}

==Description==
''Encyclopedia Magica Volume III'' is the third in a series of four 2{{nd}} edition books containing a vast collection of magical items. This third volume covered magical items starting with letters P-S, from ''Pick of Earth Parting'' to ''Thesis on Conditional Ruptures (spellbook)''.
