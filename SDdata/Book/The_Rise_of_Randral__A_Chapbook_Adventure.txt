The Rise of Randral A Chapbook Adventure was a chapbook penned by Sandreth Yendrel of Neverwinter in 1326 DR.
Sandreth Yendrels chapbooks events took place in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep and featured such characters as Garandal the Chancellor and Largreth the Good Knight. The Rise of Randral had at least seven or more chapters.
