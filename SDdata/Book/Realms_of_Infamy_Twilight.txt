'''"Twilight"''' is a short story by [[Troy Denning]] included in the 1994 anthology ''[[Realms of Infamy]]''.

==Summary==
A recounting of the [[giant]] legend of the fall of [[Ostoria]], beginning with [[Ulutiu]]'s affair with [[Othea]] and its subsequent revelation to her husband [[Annam All-Father|Annam]]. Annam murders Ulutiu over the incident, thereby damaging his relationship with Othea. The legend then tells of how Othea is betrayed by her son, [[Lanaxis]].
