A is for Anchors was a book one of a long-running series of mystery novels found on the Sea of Swords and the Nelanther Islands in the late 14 century DR.
By the , the series reached the S is for Scurvy A Pirate Potboiler volume, and anyone interested in reading the series all the way from A is for Anchors was thoroughly intimidated by the prospect. Many copies of these books could be found in the pirate-ridden Spindrift Town of the Nelanther Islands.
