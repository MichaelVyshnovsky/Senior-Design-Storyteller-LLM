Swords of the Iron Legion is a 1-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons adventure that was set in the Forgotten Realms.
Swords of the Iron Legion was edited by Skip Williams, with a cover by Larry Elmore, and was published by TSR in 1988 as a 64-page book. It is a collection of 11 mini-scenarios, some of which are for use with the BATTLESYSTEM rule-set.
* How to Use this Book
* The Storm of Greshlyrr
* Those Who Forget the Past
* The Hand of Tyr
* Rumors of War
* Battle of the Five Crowns
* The Khans Mighty Army
* The Gratitude of Princes
* The Final Battle
* Firefights
