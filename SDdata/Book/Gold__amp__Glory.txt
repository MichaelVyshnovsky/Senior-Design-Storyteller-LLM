Gold & Glory is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms. 
Gold & Glory details various mercenary organizations of the Realms and is the 15 book in the FR series. The book includes details on thirty mercenary groups, ranging from small bands to substantial armies. Additionally, it offers a considerable amount of background history for the Realms.
