'''"Vampires of Waterdeep: The Fireplace Level"''' is a adventure for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 3.5{{th}} edition published in [[Dungeon magazine 128|''Dungeon'' #128]] and the third installment of the ''Vampires of Waterdeep'' series of modules.

{{Quote|As the party faces an ancient foe who has never been defeated, their hunt threatens to unleash the buried ghosts of a forgotten feud between two of Waterdeep's powerful merchant houses. Part three of ''Vampires of Waterdeep''. Module for 4 PC's of level 14, scaleable for levels 12–16.}}
