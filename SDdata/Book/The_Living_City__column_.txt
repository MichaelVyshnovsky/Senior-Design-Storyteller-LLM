'''''The Living City''''' was a column by various authors published in ''[[Polyhedron Newszine]]''. This column centered on the RPGA [[The Living City|Living City]] Adventures, set in [[Ravens Bluff]]. It explored various establishments, organizations and individuals. It also offered several adventures for players.
