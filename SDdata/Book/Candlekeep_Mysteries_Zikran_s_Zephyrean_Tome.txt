"Zikrans Zephyrean Tome" is a short adventure module for 10&ndashlevel players, collected in the anthology Candlekeep Mysteries, where players are tasked with freeing a noble djinni trapped in a wizards spellbook.
In 1492 DR, a group of adventurers in Candlekeep investigate the magically sealed tome Zikrans Zephyrean Tome and discover the noble djinni Gazre-Azam is trapped inside. Gazre-Azam offers the adventurers a single casting of wish in exchange for tracking down and defeating the person responsible for his imprisonment the evil archmage Zikran.
