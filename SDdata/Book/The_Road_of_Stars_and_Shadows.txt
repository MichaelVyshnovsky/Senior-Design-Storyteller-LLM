The Road of Stars and Shadows was an ancient ballad about magical gates found throughout Faerûn.
The ballad titled The Road of Stars and Shadows was known to had been in existence since ancient times, long before the 14 century DR. Its subjects were famous and legendary gates, tavel-links known to many inhabitants of Faerûn by reputation, connecting the world of Toril to other planes and dimensions as well linking various locations on Toril. Some led to adventures, and others spelled doom, or cursed all who passed through into twisted shapes of beasts and monsters.
