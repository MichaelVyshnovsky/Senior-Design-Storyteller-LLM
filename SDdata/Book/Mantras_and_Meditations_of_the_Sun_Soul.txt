Mantras and Meditations of the Sun Soul was a religious work of the Order of the Sun Soul, published sometime before the late 15 century DR.
In the , a copy of Mantras and Meditations of the Sun Soul belonged to Old Jol and was found in his humble home in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate.
