"Shemshimes Bedtime Rhyme" is a short adventure module for 4&ndashlevel players, collected in the anthology Candlekeep Mysteries, where players are trapped in the Firefly Cellar of Candlekeep as they deal with a curse.
In 1492 DR, a group of adventurers spend the night in the Firefly Cellar below the House of Rest in Candlekeep. When a curse that forces its victims to hum a rhyme begins afflicting them, the adventurers and scholars present are locked inside the cellar for safety. The quarantined adventurers must discover how the curse is connected with the sinister tome titled Shemshimes Bedtime Rhyme.
