'''''The Collectanea Rubrum''''' was an [[arcanabula]] that outlined all manner of dark arcana and malign formulae.

==Description==
The script of ''The Collectanea'' was a combination of plain writing and esoteric [[rune]]s.

==Content==
The book outlined the process of [[transmutation|transmuting]] metals, specifications of craftable weapons, [[alchemical]] recipes, and information regarding the reanimation of dead flesh.

==Notable Owners==
* [[Ilyn Toth]], the [[Red Wizard]] who masqueraded as a healer in the village of [[Moonhaven]].
