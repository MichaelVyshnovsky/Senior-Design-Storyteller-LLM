'''''Down Another Tankard''''' was a ballad penned by [[Qardrella Havreth]] in [[1400 DR]].

{{quote|''I down another tankard, let it warm me well,<br/>Drink weaving its usual memory-fooding [[spell]]<br/>And taking me to fancies bright beyond compare<br/>Where I am lord of all, ruling castles in the air.''|An excerpt from ''Down Another Tankard''}}

==Contents==
''Down Another Tankard'' was a song about joys of clouding one's head with alcohol. It was published and popularized in the {{YearlinkName|1400}} by [[minstrel]] Qardrella Havreth.
