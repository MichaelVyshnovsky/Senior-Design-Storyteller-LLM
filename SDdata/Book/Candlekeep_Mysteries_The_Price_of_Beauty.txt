"The Price of Beauty" is a short adventure module for 5&ndashlevel players, collected in the anthology Candlekeep Mysteries, where players investigate a mysterious bathhouse in the High Forest.
In 1492 DR, a group of adventurers examine The Price of Beauty, a magical tome in Candlekeep, and discover that the book can create a portal leading the the Temple of the Restful Lily, a combined temple of Sune and bathhouse. The adventurers must investigate the Temple to discover the fate of a missing acolyte of Candlekeep.
