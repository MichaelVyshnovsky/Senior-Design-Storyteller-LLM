'''"The Price of Beauty"''' is a short adventure module for 5{{th}}&ndash;level players, collected in the anthology ''[[Candlekeep Mysteries]]'', where players investigate a mysterious bathhouse in the [[High Forest]].
{{TOC limit|2}}
==Synopsis==
In [[1492 DR]], a group of [[adventurer]]s examine ''[[The Price of Beauty]]'', a [[magic]]al tome in [[Candlekeep]], and discover that the book can create a portal leading the the [[Temple of the Restful Lily]], a combined temple of [[Sune]] and bathhouse. The adventurers must investigate the Temple to discover the fate of a missing acolyte of Candlekeep.
