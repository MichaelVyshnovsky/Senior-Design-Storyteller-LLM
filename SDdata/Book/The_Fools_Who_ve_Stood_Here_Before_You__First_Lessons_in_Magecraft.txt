'''''The Fools Who've Stood Here Before You: First Lessons in Magecraft''''' was a book written by [[Malchor Harpell]] in the {{YearlinkName|1365}}.

==Description==
The book included a section on [[spell]] inventors. Instead of focusing on well-known [[wizard]]s, the author offered short accounts of several lesser-known spellcrafters, including [[Archveult Tattercloak]], [[Caligarde]], [[Grimwald]], [[Nchaser]], and [[Nulathoe]].
