The Imaskarran Tome of Resonance was a heavy and tattered tome with a single rune on the cover, translating simply to "Resonance". The Imaskarran Tome of Resonance detailed the process of creating a wand of lightning bolts from the silk gland of an ettercap and a fenberry, using the spell lightning bolt.
