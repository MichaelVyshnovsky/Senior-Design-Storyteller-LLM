The Wondrous Potions of the Brothers of Ilmater was an Ilmatari tome that collected recipes of the Broken Gods clergy.
The book collected recipes and arcane formulae for various potions, alchemical substances, and items often used by the Church of Ilmater. The formulas included a version of a holy water recipe that used an amethyst and powdered silver as its ingredients. A recipe for tanglefoot bag called for quicksilver and belladonna, while a formula for thunderstone required diamond dust and an amethyst.
As of the , several copies of The Wondrous Potions of the Brothers of Ilmater existed in the Sword Coast North region. One of these books could be found in Fort Locke, and another was stashed in an infamous warlocks, Ammon Jerros, hidden Haven.
