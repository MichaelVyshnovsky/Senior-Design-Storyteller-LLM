'''''Uni and the Hunt for the Lost Horn''''' is an adventure module for the 5{{th}}-edition revised {{Dnd}} ruleset, released on September 25, 2024, on D&D Beyond and for [[Adventurers League]]. It is based on the [[w:c:dungeonsdragons:Dungeons & Dragons (TV series)|''Dungeons & Dragons'']] cartoon series of 1983–1985. As such, it is set in [[The Realm]], not the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'', but is included here for reference.
