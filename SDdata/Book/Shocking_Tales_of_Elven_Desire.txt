'''''Shocking Tales of Elven Desire''''' was a book of romance stories involving [[elves]].

[[Adzerak]], a [[lich]] and bibliophile devotee of [[Deneir]], kept a copy on his nightstand in his lair in [[Cormyr]] around [[1367 DR]].
