'''''Player's Handbook Races: Dragonborn''''' is a sourcebook for the 4{{th}}-edition ruleset of ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' for the core setting and the world of [[Nerath]]. It details the [[dragonborn]] race.

==Blurb==
{{quote|'''''Proud and honorable warriors'''

''If you want to play the ultimate dragonborn hero, this book is for you.

''This expansion of the [[Player's Handbook 4th edition|Player's Handbook]] core rulebook explores the mysteries of the dragonborn. It presents D&D players with exciting new options for their dragonborn characters, including unique racial feats, powers, [[:Category:Paragon paths|paragon paths]], and epic destinies. This book also includes ways to flesh out your dragonborn character's background and personality.''}}
