'''''Powers and Pantheons''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory for the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]''.
{{Quote|''This essential supplement follows on the heels of ''[[Faiths & Avatars]]'', which detailed the rules by which deities function in the Realms, and describes more of the religions and powers of the Realms. Each divine power is covered in depth, and each entry has information about a deity's appearance, personality, worshipers, portfolio, aliases, domain name, superior, allies, foes, symbol, worshipers' alignments, avatar, manifestations, church, and specialty priests. The information on these faiths includes their core dogma, day-to-day activities of priests, holy days and important ceremonies, major centers of worship, affiliated orders, and the priestly vestments and adventuring garb of members of the clergy. Finally, each entry contains spells specific to each of the faiths.''

''Powers & Pantheons includes:''
* ''The [[demipower]]s of the Faerûnian pantheon: [[Finder Wyvernspur]], [[Savras]] the All-Seeing, [[Sharess]], [[Valkur]] the Mighty, [[Uthgar]], [[Ulutiu]], and others.''
* ''The powers of the [[Mulhorandi pantheon|Mulhorandi]], [[Chultan pantheon|Chultan]], and the deceased [[Untheric pantheon]]s.''
* ''Expanded and clarified specialty priest classes for all religions and priesthoods.''
* ''Religion-specific spells for all faiths with priesthoods.''
* ''Color illustrations of priests from every faith in ceremonial dress.''
* ''Five showpiece temples of the Realms detailed with illustrations, color maps, and extensive descriptions of their locales, inhabitants, histories, and interiors.''
* ''The three known Elder Elemental Evils of the Realms described in [[Monstrous Compendium Forgotten Realms Appendix (MC11)|<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Monsterous Compendium</span>]]<sup>&reg;</sup> appendix form.''}}

==Description==
''Powers and Pantheons'' provides detailed information on the pantheon of deities that inhabit the Realms. The book offers in-depth descriptions of various gods, goddesses, and other divine entities, along with their respective mythologies, rituals, and the clerical domains they influence. Each deity's entry includes their alignment, symbols, clergy, and the specific powers they bestow upon their followers, giving players and Dungeon Masters a comprehensive understanding of how these deities function within the game world.

''Powers and Pantheons'' also explores the relationships between different gods, detailing alliances, enmities, and the histories that shape the interactions between these divine beings. Additionally, the book includes new spells, magic items, and other gameplay elements that are specifically tied to the deities described within.
