Powers and Pantheons is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.
Powers and Pantheons provides detailed information on the pantheon of deities that inhabit the Realms. The book offers in-depth descriptions of various gods, goddesses, and other divine entities, along with their respective mythologies, rituals, and the clerical domains they influence. Each deitys entry includes their alignment, symbols, clergy, and the specific powers they bestow upon their followers, giving players and Dungeon Masters a comprehensive understanding of how these deities function within the game world.
Powers and Pantheons also explores the relationships between different gods, detailing alliances, enmities, and the histories that shape the interactions between these divine beings. Additionally, the book includes new spells, magic items, and other gameplay elements that are specifically tied to the deities described within.
