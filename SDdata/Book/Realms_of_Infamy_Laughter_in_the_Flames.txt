"Laughter in the Flames" is a short story by James Lowder included in the 1994 anthology Realms of Infamy.
The adventurers Hamnet Hawklin and Gareth Truesilver are respected members of the Cormyrian Society of Stalwart Adventurers. When Uther, butler of the Societys Club Hall, informs them of a possible passageway to the realm of Cyric, Lord of Strife, the two embark on a journey through the dark alleys of Suzail.
