Dark and Hidden Ways is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory.
Dark and Hidden Ways was a booklet that was bundled with the Dungeoneers Survival Guide as the "Limited Edition" of that sourcebook, mainly as a way to get rid of unsold stock. The first scenario, Drow Magic-User and Dracolich, is set in Hooknose Crag.

*Mindflayers
*Goblins
*Pyrohydra & Xorn
*Aboleth and Cloakers
