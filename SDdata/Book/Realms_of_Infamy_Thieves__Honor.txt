'''"Thieves' Honor"''' is a short story by [[Mary H. Herbert]] included in the 1994 anthology ''[[Realms of Infamy]]''.

==Summary==
[[Teza]], a common [[horse]]-thief from [[Immilmar]], is caught up in a plot involving Prince [[Laric Balindre]] of [[Telflamm]].
