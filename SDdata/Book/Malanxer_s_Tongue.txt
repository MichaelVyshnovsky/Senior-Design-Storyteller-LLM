'''''Malanxer's Tongue''''', also known as '''''the Black Tongue''''' was a periodical [[chapbook]] published in the city of [[Iriaebor]] of the [[Western Heartlands]].

==Description==
''Malanxer's Tongue'' made a name for itself for publishing rants aimed at government officials, merchants, and public figures of Iriaebor. When an individual graced the pages of ''Malanxer's Tongue'', they were referred to as "in the Black," after the chapbook's unofficial name – ''the Black Tongue''.
