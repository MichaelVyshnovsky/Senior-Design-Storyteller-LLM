The '''''Whi-yeu Nohg''''' was a play that told of the life and deeds of [[H'Dang Li]], the first High Lord of Oceans of [[Tabot]].

By {{YearlinkConvert|2607|SY}}, [[Do'dzin Gompa]] had an annual performance of the ''Whi-yeu Nohg'' to commemorate the High One being raised and educated there.
