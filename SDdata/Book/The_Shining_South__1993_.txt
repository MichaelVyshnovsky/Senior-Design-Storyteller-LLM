The Shining South is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.
The Shining South is the 16 and final book in the FR series. It explores Halruaa, Dambrath, Luiren, Durpar, Var, Estagund, and Ulgarth. The book provides detailed descriptions of the geography of the Shining South, and the climate, terrain, and natural resources are all discussed
Each of the major regions in the Shining South has its own distinct cultures and societies. The accessory provides detailed descriptions of the people of these lands, their belief systems, social structures, and notable customs.
The Shining South also introduces new monsters native to the region, many of which are tied to the magic and unique environments of the Shining South. In addition, new magical items and spells, many associated with Halruaa’s powerful wizards, are presented for use in campaigns. 
The supplement offers numerous adventure hooks and ideas for Dungeon Masters to incorporate into their campaigns.
