Book of Lairs is a sourcebook for the Forgotten Realms setting using the 2-edition Dungeons & Dragons ruleset. Continuing the "Book of Lairs" format from sourcebooks for core D&D and other settings, it presents 35 small adventure scenarios each focusing on a monster from the Forgotten Realms setting.
