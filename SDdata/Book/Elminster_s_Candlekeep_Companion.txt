'''''Elminster's Candlekeep Companion''''' is a sourcebook by [[Justice Arman]], [[Anthony Joyce]], [[Trevor Armstrong]], [[M.T. Black]], [[Jeremy Forbing]], [[Laura Hirsbrunner]], and [[Ed Greenwood]], published independently through [[Dungeon Masters Guild]] and set in the {{SmallCaps|[[Forgotten Realms]]}} using the {{Dnd}} 5{{th}}-edition ruleset. It focuses on [[Candlekeep]] on the [[Sword Coast]].
{{Quote|
'''Elminster's Candlekeep Companion'''

[[Elminster Aumar]], [[Chosen of Mystra|Chosen]] of [[Mystra]], invites you to explore the grandest repository of knowledge in the [[Forgotten Realms]]: [[Candlekeep]], the Library Fortress! The [[Sage of Shadowdale|Sage]] of [[Shadowdale]] has assembled this companion to help seekers of wisdom navigate the Castle of Tomes. Be warned! Elminster requests that you bring a suitable entrance gift, lest you be turned away at the front gates.|Publisher's description}}
