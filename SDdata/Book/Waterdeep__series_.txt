Waterdeep (or Ed Greenwood Presents Waterdeep) is a series of six stand-alone novels, each written by a different author, that takes place in the city of Waterdeep in 1479 DR, the year in which the fourth edition of the Forgotten Realms takes place. Forgotten Realms creator Ed Greenwood oversaw the project.
