The Complete Book of Necromancers is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory.
The Complete Book of Necromancers is the 7 book in the Dungeon Masters Guide Rules series. The book introduces the necromancer as a new character class for players. Guidelines are provided for creating necromancer PCs. This includes information on attributes, abilities, and role-playing aspects specific to necromancers.
The Complete Book of Necromancers delves into the spells and magical abilities associated with necromancers. It includes a range of necromantic spells for raising zombies, skeletons, and other undead creatures, as well as spells for communicating with the dead and harnessing the powers of darkness. Different necromantic traditions and specializations within the class are described. These variations include necromantic schools, each with its unique focus and abilities. It also includes information on magical items and artifacts associated with necromancers, such as powerful staffs, dark tomes, and enchanted items related to the manipulation of death.
It provides campaign ideas and adventure hooks that involve necromancer characters and the challenges they might face. These adventures often revolve around themes of death, spirits, and the consequences of necromantic practices. It offers role-playing advice and suggestions for players who wish to portray necromancer characters convincingly. It covers topics like morality, ethics, and interactions with other characters. It also includes profiles of notable necromancer NPCs, both heroes and villains, that can be used in campaigns as allies, antagonists, or sources of knowledge.
