Best of The Dragon #2 was published by TSR, Inc. in November of 1981.  It contained the following articles related to the Forgotten Realms
 By Mike Childers and Jeff Key. p. 11
A sub-class of fighters who focus on speed and honor. (From Issue #3)
 By C. Hettlestad. p. 12
Healers are designed to allow rapid repair of other characters in large campaign games. (From Issue #3)
 By Jon Pickens. p. 14
A sub-class of fighter who uses a wereshape to inflict great damage. (From Issue #3)
 By David Mumper. p. 15
Rare and expensive specialists who read and copy magical writing. (From Issue #3)
 By Sheldon Price. p. 16
A semi-mythical character who was feared as an unknown quantity. (From Issue #16)
 By Gary Gygax. p. 27
The reasons behind the difference between indoor and outdoor scale, and how to handle spell ranges and areas. (From Issue #15)
 By Gary Gygax. p. 49
Advanced Dungeons & Dragons Magic System How and Why It Works. (From Issue #33)
 By Ed Greenwood. p. 52
Gates grant advantages to both players and DMs. (From Issue #37)
 By I. Marc Carlson. p. 54
Adding more life to your Dungeons & Dragons games by using inns and taverns as more than just a place to eat, drink and sleep. (From Issue #29)
 By R. P. Smith p. 59
A description of different vampires from around the world. (From Issue #25)
 By Jon Mattson. p. 60
More about the beast in all of us for Dungeons & Dragons. (From Issue #24)
 By Lenard Lakofka. p. 62
Dissertations on a lich. (From Issue #26)
