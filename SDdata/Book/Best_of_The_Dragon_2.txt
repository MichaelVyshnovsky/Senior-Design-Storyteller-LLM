'''''Best of The Dragon #2''''' was published by [[TSR, Inc.]] in November of 1981.  It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Samurai==
: ''By [[Mike Childers]] and [[Jeff Key]].'' '''p. 11'''
A sub-class of fighters who focus on speed and honor. (From [[Dragon magazine 3|Issue #3]])

==Healers==
: ''By [[C. Hettlestad]].'' '''p. 12'''
Healers are designed to allow rapid repair of other characters in large campaign games. (From [[Dragon magazine 3|Issue #3]])

==Berserker==
: ''By [[Jon Pickens]].'' '''p. 14'''
A sub-class of fighter who uses a wereshape to inflict great damage. (From [[Dragon magazine 3|Issue #3]])

==Scribes==
: ''By [[David Mumper]].'' '''p. 15'''
Rare and expensive specialists who read and copy magical writing. (From [[Dragon magazine 3|Issue #3]])

==The Ninja==
: ''By [[Sheldon Price]].'' '''p. 16'''
A semi-mythical character who was feared as an unknown quantity. (From [[Dragon magazine 16|Issue #16]])

==Ground and Spell Area Scales==
: ''By [[Gary Gygax]].'' '''p. 27'''
The reasons behind the difference between indoor and outdoor scale, and how to handle spell ranges and areas. (From [[Dragon magazine 15|Issue #15]])

==Good Isn't Stupid: Paladins & Rangers==
: ''By [[Gary Gygax]].'' '''p. 49'''
Advanced Dungeons & Dragons' Magic System: How and Why It Works. (From [[Dragon magazine 33|Issue #33]])

==Theory and Use of Gates==
: ''By [[Ed Greenwood]].'' '''p. 52'''
Gates grant advantages to both players and DMs. (From [[Dragon magazine 37|Issue #37]])

==Inns and Taverns==
: ''By [[I. Marc Carlson]].'' '''p. 54'''
Adding more life to your Dungeons & Dragons games by using inns and taverns as more than just a place to eat, drink and sleep. (From [[Dragon magazine 29|Issue #29]])

==Varieties of Vampires==
: ''By [[R. P. Smith]]'' '''p. 59'''
A description of different vampires from around the world. (From [[Dragon magazine 25|Issue #25]])

==A Look at Lycanthropy==
: ''By [[Jon Mattson]].'' '''p. 60'''
More about the beast in all of us for Dungeons & Dragons. (From [[Dragon magazine 24|Issue #24]])

==Blueprint for a Lich==
: ''By [[Lenard Lakofka]].'' '''p. 62'''
Dissertations on a lich. (From [[Dragon magazine 26|Issue #26]])
