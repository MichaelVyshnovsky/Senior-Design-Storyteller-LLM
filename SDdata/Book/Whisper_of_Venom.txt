'''''Whisper of Venom''''' by [[Richard Lee Byers]] is the second novel in the [[Brotherhood of the Griffon (series)|Brotherhood of the Griffon]] series.

{{quote|''The Mad King Returns!''

''[[Aoth Fezim]] and the [[Brotherhood of the Griffon]] succeeded in rescuing [[Tchazzar]], the lost king of [[Chessenta]] and a formidable red dragon. Upon his return Tchazzar rewards them greatly, sends them back to the frontlines—and names himself a god. Increasingly erratic in the war against the powerful [[dracolich]], Tchazzar begins to move in ways that make Aoth Fezim suspicious that the Brotherhood may be just a pawn in a cutthroat [[dragon|draconic]] game that puts whole kingdoms at risk—a game played for a stakes of gold and blood.''}}
