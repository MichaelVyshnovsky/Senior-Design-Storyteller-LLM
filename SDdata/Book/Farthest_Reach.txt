'''''Farthest Reach''''' was a novel by [[Richard Baker]] and the second novel in [[The Last Mythal]] trilogy.

{{quote|''The fires of hatred, vengeance, lies, and ambition burn in [[Cormanthor]].''

''[[Sarya Dlardrageth|SARYA DLARDRAGETH]] has gone to ground in the ruins of [[Myth Drannor]], seething with hatred for the [[elves]] she thought would be easier to defeat.''

''[[Malkizid|MALKIZID]] has been summoned from the [[Outer Planes]] to serve [[House Dlardrageth]], but this servant won’t rest until he’s become the master.''

''MYTH DRANNOR is a symbol of the elves' greatest failure, but can it one day rise again as their greatest victory?''}}
