'''''Black Blood''''', designed by [[Jean-Philipe Chapleau]], is an adventure module set in the ''Forgotten Realms'' using the ''Dungeons & Dragons'' 4th-edition ruleset.

{{Quote|''The [[Black Blood tribe]] holds sway over much of the island of [[Moray]]. An ambitious young man wants to break their power and make the island safe again. However, he cannot do it alone. Part 2 of “The Fisherman” Major Quest. A Living Forgotten Realms adventure set in The [[Moonshae Isles]] for characters levels 7-10.''|}}
