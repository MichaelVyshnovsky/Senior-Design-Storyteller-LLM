'''''Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue''''' was a shopping catalogue produced by [[Aurora]] in order to facilitate her mail-order shopping business, as an extension of [[Aurora's Emporium]].

==Contents==
The main body of the catalogue was taken up with illustrated listings of various goods, ranging from common everyday tools to odd and esoteric devices.

;A Word from Our Founder
The catalogue opened with a message to the customer that explained how Aurora's business worked and how to place orders using the catalogue, including deposits, rush fees, and customizations.

;Rangers' Field
[[File:FishingTackle.png|thumb|150px|''A [[fishing tackle kit]].'']]
A selection of hunting and fishing gear, as well as specialized [[ranger]] tools like [[tree bed]]s.

;Thieves' Corner
Questionably-legal tools for [[thieves]] to use in their mischief, such as [[tar makeup]] and [[caltrop]]s.

;Bards' Emporium
Props and instruments for [[bard]]s, including [[stilt]]s, [[bamsmack]]s, and [[zither]]s.

;Priests' Alcove
[[File:RazorCap.png|thumb|200px|''A priest's [[razor and cap]].'']]
[[Cleric]]al equipment like [[incense]], [[aspergillum]]s, and a wide array of [[material component]]s for rituals.

;Wizards' Laboratory
Laboratory equipment for experimentation such as [[retort]]s and [[glass tubing]], as well as various reagents for alchemical and magical processes.

;Laborers' List
[[File:Hammer-2e.png|thumb|100px|''A set of [[hammer]]s.'']]
A wide selection of practical and useful work tools like [[awl]]s, [[hoe]]s, [[shears]], and raw metals and molds for smithing.

;Inn and Tavern
Common domestic accoutrements used in taverns and inns, from [[bowl]]s and [[blanket]]s to [[mug]]s and [[mop]]s.

;Scribes' Desk
Various materials for scribes such as [[paper]], [[papyrus]], and [[pen]]s, and a rainbow of [[ink]]s and [[paint]]s. It was very popular with the sages of [[Candlekeep]].

;Storage Items
A generous selection of [[basket]]s, [[barrel]]s, [[crate]]s, and [[chest]]s.

;General Hardware
[[File:Block&Tackle.png|thumb|100px|''A [[block and tackle]].'']]
Miscellaneous useful tools, including [[paint brush]]es, [[shackles]], and [[chain]].

;Tailors' Corner
[[Sewing needle]]s, [[scissors]], and patterns, and an array of fabrics, skins, and [[dye]]s.

;Clothes and Shoes
[[File:Tabard-2e.jpg|thumb|100px|''A [[tabard]].'']]
Wardrobes full of [[slipper]]s, [[cape]]s, [[chemise]]s, and [[purse]]s.

;Boom's Garden
Various pieces of improbable and impractical gear made by [[Kar]], including armored [[corset]]s and low-cut [[plate mail]] [[bustier]]s.

;Jewelry
Popular pieces of jewelery like [[torc]]s, [[brooch]]es, and [[fillet]]s.

;Household Accoutrements
Domestic items including [[hand mirror]]s and [[water clock]]s.

;Personal Supplies
Tools for cleaning and grooming, like [[hair brush]]es, [[soap]], and [[perfume]].

;Illuminations
[[File:Warming lamp-2e.png|thumb|150px|''A [[warming lamp]].'']]
Every flavor of [[lamp]] and [[lantern]] imaginable.

;Diversions
Toys and games, as well as [[nesting doll]]s, [[rocking mount]]s, and [[kaleidoscope]]s.

;Aurora's Larder
A selection of rare and common [[herb]]s, [[spice]]s, and preserved foods.

;Breads
Various [[cracker]]s, [[bread]]s, [[noodles]].

;Cheese Shop
[[File:Nut cheese-2e.jpg|thumb|150px|''A wheel of [[nut cheese]].'']]
[[Cheese]]s like [[pepper cheese]], [[Death Cheese]], and [[yak butter]].

;Wines and Ales
Cellars-full worth of [[ale]]s, [[beer]]s, [[cider]]s, and [[wine]]s, from [[Bitter Black]] to [[Westgate Ruby]].

;Exotics
[[File:Coffee-2e.jpg|thumb|150px|''A heap of [[coffee]].'']]
Outlandish imported foods including [[tomato]]s, [[soya sauce]], [[tabacco]], and [[garum]].

;Wilderness Gear
Useful survival gear like [[tent]]s, [[hammock]]s, [[ration]]s, and [[canteen]]s.

;Priest in a Poke
[[File:Amputation saw.png|thumb|150px|''A set of surgical [[saw]]s and [[shears]].'']]
A set of practical emergency medical tools, including [[bandage]]s, [[splint]]s, and [[tourniquet]]s.

;DaRoni's Workshop
Ingenious and unique creations of [[Nadul DaRoni]], like the [[furnace wagon]] and [[underwater boat]].
