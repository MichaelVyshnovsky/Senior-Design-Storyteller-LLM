Feast of the Moon is an adventure module from the RPGA Legacy of the Green Regent campaign.
It is the Feast of the Moon and Waterdeep is full of happy revelers. In a house at the end of Dretch Lane, the partygoers find themselves being killed off, but the deaths appear to be due to drowning.
