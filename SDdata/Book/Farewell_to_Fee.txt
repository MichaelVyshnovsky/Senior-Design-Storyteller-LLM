'''Farewell to Fee''' is a short unpublished story by [[Ed Greenwood]] released in July 2024 as a special reward for Ed Greenwood's [https://www.patreon.com/EdGreenwood Patreon page].

==Summary==
The story took place in the {{YearlinkName|1390}}, recoding the final moments of Queen [[Filfaeril Obarskyr]]'s life.
