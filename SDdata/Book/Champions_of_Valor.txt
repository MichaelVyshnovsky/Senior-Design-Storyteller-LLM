'''''Champions of Valor''''' is an accessory for the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'' campaign setting using the 3.5{{th}}-edition {{dnd}} ruleset.
{{Quote|Champions of Valor ''is a comprehensive guide to playing valiant heroes in the [[Forgotten Realms]] setting. The counterpart to ''[[Champions of Ruin]]'', ''Champions of Valor'' covers what it means to be valorous in the Realms. The book describes several good-aligned guilds and organizations that characters can join, as well as the benefits for doing so. In addition, the book presents an array of new spells, feats, and [[prestige classes]] appropriate for heroes of valor, and opens up new opportunities for adventure, fame, and glory for truly heroic characters.''}}

==Description==
''Champions of Valor'' provides details on the role of good in the Realms. It has information of various villains, good-oriented organizations, new spells, feats and prestige classes.
