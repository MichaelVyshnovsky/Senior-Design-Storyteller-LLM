'''''Dangerous Tunes''''' was a history [[book]] dedicated to the subject of magical songs and singing [[spell]]s, printed sometime in or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Contents==
''Dangerous Tunes'' collected lore on spells that were performed in the form songs with the goal of ensorcelling or enthralling their listeners. Two of such songs listed in the book were ''[[Chionthar Harpy's Siren Song]]'' and ''[[Glimmergad's Stupefying Melancholy]]''.

==History==
''Dangerous Tunes'' could be found in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] and sold by [[Thomas C. Quirkilious]] of [[Chromatic Scales]] located in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of the Gate.
