'''''Works and Woes''''' was a multi-volumed work of records written by the famed [[Open Lord]] [[Ahghairon]] of [[Waterdeep]], published in the early 9{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Contents==
{{quote|The only thing the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|Watch]] may count on among lawbreaking-wizards is this—corner them where they live, and they are loathe to loose spell-work that damages overmuch.|An excerpt from Volume IV of ''Works and Woes''.}}

==History==
The fourth volume of ''Works and Woes'' included Ahghairon's opinions on how to best capture law-breaking [[mage]]s. The fourth volume was published in the {{YearlinkName|1057}}.
