'''''One Foot, Two Feet, Well Met, We'll Meet''''' was a book that collected popular [[halfling]] children's folk nursery rhymes, available in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
''One Foot'' was compelled and archived by [[Euphadora Greatquill]]. The books had tan-colored covers with gilded inlays.

==Content==
Some of the songs collected in this volume include a simple rhyme about cheerful hospitality and a poem about [[Yondalla]] and [[Garl Glittergold]]'s affection for each other.

==History==
In [[1492 DR]], a copy of ''One Foot, Two Feet, Well Met, We'll Meet'' could be found in the [[Emerald Grove]] of the [[Western Heartlands]]. The book was among possession of [[tiefling]]s who fled from the town of [[Elturel]] following the events of [[the Descent]].
