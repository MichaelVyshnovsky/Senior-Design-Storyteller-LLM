Forgotten Realms Classics Omnibus Volume 2 is the second omnibus of the Forgotten Realms comics series originally published by DC Comics. It was published in 2014 by IDW Publishing.
It is a collection of the last eleven issues of the series and the Forgotten Realms Annual #1.
