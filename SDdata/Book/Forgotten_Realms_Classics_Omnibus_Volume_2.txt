'''''Forgotten Realms Classics Omnibus Volume 2''''' is the second omnibus of the [[Forgotten Realms (comic)|Forgotten Realms comics]] series originally published by [[wikipedia:en:DC Comics|DC Comics]]. It was published in 2014 by [[wikipedia:en:IDW Publishing|IDW Publishing]].

It is a collection of the last eleven issues of the series and the Forgotten Realms Annual #1.
