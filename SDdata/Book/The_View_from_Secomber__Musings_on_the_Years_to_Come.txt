'''''The View from Secomber: Musings on the Years to Come''''' was a book written by the Sage of Histories, [[Reld Barunenail]]. It was published in [[1361 DR]].{{Cite web/The Candlekeep Collection}}

{{quote|''There are some who hold that the High Lady of Silverymoon is a deluded dreamer, doomed to fail in her fair craftings because she thinks too highly of the good in folk, and too little of the evil that lurks always near at hand. I am not one of those".''}}

==History==
As of [[1372 DR]], the original copy could be found on the shelves of [[Candlekeep]].

''The View from Secomber: Musings on the Years to Come'' referred to one of the [[Seven Sisters]], the High Lady of [[Silverymoon]], [[Alustriel Silverhand]].
