The View from Secomber Musings on the Years to Come was a book written by the Sage of Histories, Reld Barunenail. It was published in 1361 DR.
As of 1372 DR, the original copy could be found on the shelves of Candlekeep.
The View from Secomber Musings on the Years to Come referred to one of the Seven Sisters, the High Lady of Silverymoon, Alustriel Silverhand.
