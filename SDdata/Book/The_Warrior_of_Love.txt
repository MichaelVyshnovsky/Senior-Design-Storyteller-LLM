The Warrior of Love was an omnibus of erotic fiction found in the Western Heartlands.
The book held a collection of tales based on the concept of a paladin of Sune who received a divine quest from their goddess. In order to prove their devotion to love, they had to lie with someone from every town on the Sword Coast.
A copy was kept in the library of the Sharess Caress brothel in 1492 DR.
