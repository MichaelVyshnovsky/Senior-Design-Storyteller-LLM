A Guide to Magic by Elminster was a book on magic by Elminster Aumar found on the world of Adenthia.
Described as a masterpiece, it compiled magical lore that Elminster had acquired on his journeys between worlds. He left it behind on Adenthia after his defeat of Sammaster there, and it became a legendary artifact in local terms.
The book was itself and magical and bolstered the bearers vitality and wisdom, and also shielded them against magical attacks.
