'''''X the Mystic's Arcane Grimoire''''' was a powerful [[spellbook]] written by "X the Mystic" that was discovered in the [[Tomb of the Nine Gods]] in [[Chult]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
It had its title burned into its binding.

==Owner==
The ''Arcane Grimoire'' was in the possession of the [[arcanaloth]] [[Ygga Raxyg]] in the sixth and final level of the [[Tomb of the Nine Gods]].

==Powers==
The ''Arcane Grimoire'' had one to three extremely powerful [[spells]], some of which could even equal [[Acererak]]'s own magical powers.
