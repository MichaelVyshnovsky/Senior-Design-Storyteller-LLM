Under Illefarn is a  1st-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons adventure that was set in the Forgotten Realms. 
Under Illefarn was written by Steve Perrin, with a cover by Jeff Easley and interior illustrations by Luise Perenne, and was published by TSR in 1987 as a 48-page booklet with an outer folder.
While in development this module had the working title of Beneath Illefarn. This name was made public by Jeff Grubb in Dragon #119s article "The Game Wizards."
The maps in this module have many numbered rooms that dont receive descriptions/fixed content anywhere in the text, leaving them to be populated by DMs.
The adventure is a scenario designed for beginning players in the Forgotten Realms setting. The player characters are residents of the town of Daggerford, and are therefore automatically members of the town militia and thus required to deal with local perils, such as fighting lizardmen raiders, rescuing a kidnapped noblewoman, and guarding a caravan. The module describes Daggerford and its personalities, as well as the nearby dwarf and orc lairs of Illefarn.
