'''''Vaerendroon's Ineffable Enchantments''''' was an [[arcane]] [[spellbook]] penned by [[Vaerendroon]], a [[wizard]] known to trade other [[magic]] to acquire specific [[spell]]s. It contained six [[spell]]s ranging from common to unique or rare.

==Description==
This was a thin [[tome]] bound with red [[rothé]] hide. Six vellum pages were sewn into the tome, each containing a single spell. Each page was edged with [[electrum]]. Both the front and rear hide covers were stretched over slate with electrum corner caps. The title was branded in [[silver]] [[Thorass]] on the front cover.

==Powers==
''Vaerendroon's Ineffable Enchantments'' contained the following spells:

==History==
Vaerendroon once sold his tome for 90,000 [[gp]] in [[Baldur's Gate]].
