Mhuelosiun Mantles was a Hlondathan spellbook purported to describe the process for creating one or more Netherese mantles.
The libram was written by the Mage Royal of Mhaelos in the years directly prior to the , when the Crown against the Scepter Wars started.
By the , the legendary tome had popped up in the markets of Ooltul.
The value of Mhuelosiun Mantles was considered to be inestimable, and it was said that only the true name of at least one Chosen of Mystra would be a fair offer.
