This book provides new archetypal builds for [[cleric]], [[paladin]], [[Divine soul|invoker]], and [[avenger]] classes, including new character powers, feats, [[:Category:Paragon paths|paragon paths]], and epic destinies. It also includes rules for [[divine magic|divine]] domains.
{{quote|'''New options for clerics, paladins, and other divine characters.'''

This must-have book in the line of player-friendly game supplements offers hundreds of new options for D&D characters, specifically focusing on heroes who draw power from divine forces.}}
