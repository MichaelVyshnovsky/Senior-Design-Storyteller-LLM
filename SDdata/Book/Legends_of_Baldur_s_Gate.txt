'''''Legends of Baldur's Gate''''' is a series of ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'' comic books published by [[wikipedia:en:IDW Publishing|IDW Publishing]] as part of the ''[[Tyranny of Dragons]]'' storyline. It is continued in the series ''[[Shadows of the Vampire]]'', ''[[Frost Giant's Fury]]'', ''[[Evil at Baldur's Gate]]'' and ''[[Infernal Tides]]''.

==Summary==
After accidentally bringing the [[Rashemi]] [[Rashemaar berserker|berserker]] [[Minsc]] back to life, the [[wild mage]] [[Delina]] and her newfound companion find themselves fighting to stop a [[Cult of the Dragon]] plot against the city of [[Baldur's Gate]].
