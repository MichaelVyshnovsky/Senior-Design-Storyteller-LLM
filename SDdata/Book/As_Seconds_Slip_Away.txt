'''As Seconds Slip Away''' is an adventure module from the RPGA [[Legacy of the Green Regent]] campaign. It is the second part of the Tides of War mini series.

==Plot==
Intelligence suggests that the [[Zhentarim]] are planning a direct attack on [[Loudwater]] in a bid to take control of the [[Delimbiyr Vale]]. Scouts are needed to travel into Zhent territory to determine the truth of this report.

{{Quote|Can you trust the words of a traitor? Would you dare put your fate in the hands of someone who has turned their colors and switched sides? Would you risk the fate of a town and everyone in it on a change of heart? High Lord Kalahar Twohands decided that he could not.|[[Teseryne Truesilver]]}}
