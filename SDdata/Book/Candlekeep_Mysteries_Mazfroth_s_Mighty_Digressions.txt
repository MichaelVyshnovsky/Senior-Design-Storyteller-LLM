'''"Mazfroth's Mighty Digressions"''' is a short adventure module for 2{{nd}}&ndash;level players, collected in the anthology ''[[Candlekeep Mysteries]]'', where players investigate why several tomes in [[Candlekeep]] are actually [[gingwatzim]]s in disguise.

{{TOC limit|2}}
==Synopsis==
In [[1492 DR]], a group of [[adventurer]]s is attacked by a volume of ''[[Mazfroth's Mighty Digressions]]'', a tome on the subject of divinity, the multiverse, and the nature of [[magic]]. Further research reveals the tome was actually a disguised gingwatzim, a creature from the [[Ethereal Plane]] that feeds on spirit energy and that can assume the form of a small object. After interviewing the scholar who donated the tome to Candlekeep, the adventurers are compelled to investigate a book shop in [[Baldur's Gate]].
