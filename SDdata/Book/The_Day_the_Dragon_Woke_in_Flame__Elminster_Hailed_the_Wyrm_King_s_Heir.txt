The Day the Dragon Woke in Flame, Elminster Hailed the Wyrm Kings Heir was a popular ballad about the Sage of Shadowdale, Elminster, penned sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.
According to Elminster, the ballad was inspired by true events but flush with artistic exaggerations. Despite that, this ballad was one of Elminsters favorite works about himself.
