'''''The Day the Dragon Woke in Flame, Elminster Hailed the Wyrm King's Heir''''' was a popular ballad about the Sage of [[Shadowdale]], [[Elminster]], penned sometime in or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''Ha! Old, but hearty, and rife with witty flourishes - not unlike myself. I confess it to be among my favored few as well.''|Elminster Aumar}}

==Contents==
According to Elminster, the ballad was inspired by true events but flush with artistic exaggerations. Despite that, this ballad was one of Elminster's favorite works about himself.

==History==
''The Day the Dragon Woke in Flame, Elminster Hailed the Wyrm King's Heir'' was known to some [[bard]]s and [[minstrel]]s in the [[Western Heartlands]] and the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
