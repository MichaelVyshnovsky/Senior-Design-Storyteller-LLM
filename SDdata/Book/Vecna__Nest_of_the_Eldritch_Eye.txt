'''''Vecna: Nest of the Eldritch Eye''''' is a low-level adventure for 5{{th}}-edition ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' release in April 2024 as a digital-only preorder-exclusive tie-in prequel for ''[[Vecna: Eve of Ruin]]''.

{{Quote|''Vecna: Nest of the Eldritch Eye is a fifth edition Dungeons & Dragons adventure for four to six characters of level 3. This adventure takes place in the city of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast. A sinister cult has crept into the city’s bowels, and the characters must infiltrate the cult’s hideout and root out its members before harm befalls the city.''}}
