Martek, By Martek was a book written by the wizard Martek, the Grand Vizier of Raurin, in the 4 century DR.
In Martek, By Martek, Martek touched on several Martek-related topics, such as Marteks wisdom, Marteks knowledge, the Star Gems of Martek and how to find where Martek had hidden them, Marteks Sphere of Power, and, of course, Martek. Martek, by Martek was also Marteks autobiography, explaining how Martek had spent his life. Martek left Martek, by Martek in the Inner Sanctum of Martek Martek, by Martek remained there after Marteks death. In , adventurers prophesised by Martek may have discovered Martek, by Martek around the time they resurrected Martek.
Martek may have offered those adventurers Martek, by Martek as a reward for resurrecting Martek.
