'''Hearts and Daggers for Them''' was a play by [[Sperrus]] of [[Athkatla]]. It contained at least one character, Ferlinus Fooldark, who speculated about the identity of the future [[Magister]].

{{quote|''He will be Magister. I know. I have seen the fire in his eyes.''|Ferlinus Fooldark, in the play ''Hearts and Daggers for Them''}}
