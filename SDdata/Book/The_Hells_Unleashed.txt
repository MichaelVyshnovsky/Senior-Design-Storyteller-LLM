The Hells Unleashed was book dedicated to the Nine Hells, penned sometime in or beofre the late 15 century DR.
One notable chapter of the book was titled "Nine Steps to His Infernal Throne" and provided information on each of the nine layers of Hells and their rulers. It also listed spell components required to cast a scrying spell onto each of the Hells dukes. To scry on the Archduchess Zariel of Avernus, one required a heliotrope, encasing a done splinter Archduke Dispater of Dis needed a bit of the infernal iron ore Archduke Mammon of Minauros – a gold coin encased in petrified peat Phlegethos Fierna and Belial – a bit of everburn ash balanced on a scale of stone Archduke Levistus of Stygia – a coal-black eye encased in ice of its own tears Malbolges Archduchess Glasya – a coronet stained with blood of three legitimate rulers Maladominis Archduke Baalzebul – a fat Gorian fly, gorged on a petioners blood Archduke Mephistopheles of Cania – a burned book of priceless lore and lastly, the book sthated that attempting to scry on Nessuss Lord Asmodeus resuled in the casters immidiate and permanent disaperance.
In the , a copy of The Hells Unleashed belonged to the mysterious unded necromancer known as the Mystic Carrion – stashed in the creatures home in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate – the Philgrave Mansion. Another copy belonged to Franc Peartree, a one-time agent, who worked for Lord Enver Gortash and was murdered in 1492 DR.
