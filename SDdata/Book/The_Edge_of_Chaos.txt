'''''The Edge of Chaos''''' by [[Jak Koke]] is the third novel in [[The Wilds]] series.

{{quote|''On the border of a dangerous, magically unstable area called the [[Plaguewrought Lands]], the leader of a cult seeking the spread of this wild magic and an alchemist who wants to control it join forces and create an elixir that allows pilgrims to survive the Plaguewrought Lands. But only one can succeed. A young man with strange powers and a priestess of the god of death will help determine who.''}}
