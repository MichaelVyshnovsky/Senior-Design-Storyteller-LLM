Underdark is an accessory for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting using the 3-edition  ruleset.
* Chapter 1 Races
* Chapter 2 Regions and Feats
* Chapter 3 Prestige Classes
* Chapter 4 Magic and Spells
* Chapter 5 Equipment and Magic Items
* Chapter 6 Monsters
* Chapter 7 Exploring the Underdark
* Chapter 8 Geography
* Chapter 9 Adventures
Underdark introduced new classes, spells, monsters and also provided extensive lore on the geography of the Underdark.
