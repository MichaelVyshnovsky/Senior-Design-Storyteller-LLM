'''''Monster Manual 5th edition''''' is a core sourcebook for the 5{{th}} edition of ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'', published by [[Wizards of the Coast]] on September 30, 2014. It covers a range of monsters for the game.

{{quote|
'''''The A to Z Fantasy Bestiary''''

The ''Monster Manual'' presents a horde of classic ''Dungeons & Dragons'' creatures, including dragons, giants, mind flayers, and beholders&mdash;a monstrous feast for Dungeon Masters ready to challenge their players and populate their adventures.''

The monsters contained herein are culled from the D&D game's illustrious history, with easy-to-use game statistics and thrilling stories to feed your imagination.|Back-cover text}}
