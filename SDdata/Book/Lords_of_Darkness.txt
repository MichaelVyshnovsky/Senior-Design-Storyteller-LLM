'''''Lords of Darkness''''' is an accessory for 3{{rd}}-edition of {{dnd}}. 

{{Quote|'''''Evil Powers Threaten [[Faerûn]]'''''

''Sinister cabals plot in shadows, while merciless armies gather for war. Explore the inner workings of infamous factions such as the [[Red Wizards]] and the [[Zhentarim]]. Discover more recently unveiled enemies, including the [[shade]]s and the [[People of the Black Blood]]. With detailed descriptions of key villains, secret headquarters, and more, this guide covers everything a Dungeon Master needs to conspire against the heroes of the [[Forgotten Realms]] game setting.''

==Description==
''Lords of Darkness'' details twenty-eight evil-aligned groups and contains maps of evil strongholds. It also introduces new classes, monsters, spells, feats, and magic items.
