Complete Adventurer is an accessory for the 3.5-edition of  ruleset. 
* Introductions
* Chapter 1 Classes
* Chapter 2 Prestige Classes
* Chapter 3 Skills and Feats
* Chapter 4 Tools and Equipment
* Chapter 5 Spells
* Chapter 6 Organizations
This accessory focuses on the skill based character classes, replacing and also expanding Song and Silence. It presents additional base classes, prestige classes, and feats.
