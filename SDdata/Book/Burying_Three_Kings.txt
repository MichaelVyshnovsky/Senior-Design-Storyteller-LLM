'''''Burying Three Kings''''' was a stage play penned by [[Aumraerus Fethurmyr]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{quote|Ah, but there's one familiar stink my nose tastes not,<br/>Something missing that is always there. Ah! There,<br/>I catch a whiff! Friends, 'tis here, and we can begin!<br/>Aye, that's the reek unmistakeable! Dark villainy again.|Old Grauthum the Gravedigger, in Act I, Scene IV}}

==History==
''Burying Three Kings'' featured a gravedigger character, Old Grauthum. The play was first performed in the {{YearlinkName|1391}}.
