Navrils History of the Parsnip was a common book dedicated to the history of parsnips, a noble root vegetable, penned by Navril sometime before the late 14 century DR.
By the , the fortress library of Candlekeep had several copies of Navrils History of the Parsnip and many visitors continued bringing more copies as their entrance gifts. However, the librarys monks always rejected "old Navril" in favor of some regional collections of songs and minstrels sayings.
