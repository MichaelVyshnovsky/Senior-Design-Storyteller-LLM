'''''The Fallen Fortress''''' is the fourth book of [[The Cleric Quintet]] by [[R.A. Salvatore]].

{{quote|''The war rages on.''

''[[Cadderly Bonaduce|Cadderly]], scholar-priest of the [[Edificant Library]], leads the combined forces of [[Carradoon]] and [[Shilmista]] forest against [[Castle Trinity]], stronghold of his enemy [[Aballister Bonaduce|Aballister]]. But another mission calls him on a journey leading into the past.''
