'''''Road to Danger''''' is an adventure anthology for 2{{nd}}-edition ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' featuring six short adventures reprinted from issues of ''[[Dungeon magazine|Dungeon]]''. Only one adventure, "[[Dungeon magazine 26/The Inheritance|The Inheritance]]", is set in the [[Forgotten Realms]]. A sequel anthology titled ''[[Dungeons of Despair]]'' was published in 1999.

{{Quote|'''''The road to adventure is fraught with danger. Travel swiftly, and guard yourselves well.'''''<br />
''Road to Danger'' is a collection of low-level adventures for the Aᴅᴠᴀɴᴄᴇᴅ Dᴜɴɢᴇᴏɴs & Dʀᴀɢᴏɴs® game. Each adventure stands alone, but any or all of them can be inserted easily into an ongoing AD&D® campaign designed to challenge low-level characters and prepare them for even greater dangers down the road!}}

* "Grakhirt's Lair" by [[John Nephew]], originally printed in [[Dungeon magazine 1|''Dungeon'' #1]].
* "Trouble at Grog's" by [[Grant Boucher|Grant]] and [[David Boucher]], originally printed in [[Dungeon magazine 4|''Dungeon'' #4]].
* "The Stolen Power" by [[Robert Kelk]], originally printed in [[Dungeon magazine 5|''Dungeon'' #5]].
* "The Matchmakers" by [[Patricia Nead Elrod]], originally printed in [[Dungeon magazine 7|''Dungeon'' #7]].

* "[[Dungeon magazine 26/The Inheritance|The Inheritance]]" by [[Paul Culotta]], originally printed in [[Dungeon magazine 26|''Dungeon'' #26]].
