The Meanderings Upon the Friezes of Hotun-Shûl was a book penned by Skorm Caldril and translated by Triel of Baldurs Gate sometime before the late 14 century DR.
A copy of The Meanderings Upon the Friezes of Hotun-Shûl belonged to Manshoon XII, a clone of Manshoon, in the . The book was stored in the mages library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep.
