'''''Boom, Bang, Blast: The Joy of Fireworks''''' was an illustrated guide to [[fireworks]] and explosive-making penned by [[Avery Sonshal]] from [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''If you find my little book in your hands, it is most likely because you, like myself, live for the thrill of combustion. It can be a fraught road, discovering an outlet for such a passion that leads not to destruction, but to delight.''|Excerpt from ''Boom, Bang, Blast: The Joy of Fireworks''}}

==Contents==
Avery Sonshal's work collected recipes for explosives intended for mass-production.

==History==
A copy of ''Boom, Bang, Blast: The Joy of Fireworks'' could be found displayed in inside [[Felogyr's Fireworks]] in the [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of Baldur's Gate in the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
