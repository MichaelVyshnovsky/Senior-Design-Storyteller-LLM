'''''Storm-Tossed Seafarings''''' was a theatrical play penned by playwright [[Jostrin Zavalandur]] during the [[Wailing Years]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''I do hope you weren't hoping for a good long dream-voyage,<br/>Lady, for some nights oblige with peaceful oblivion<br/>And others seem alive with mischief and aray<br/>This one already, I deem, shows itself one of the latter;<br/>Lady, more than a night's worth of troubles assails us.''|An excerpt from Act II, Scene I of the ''Storm-Tossed Seafarings''}}
