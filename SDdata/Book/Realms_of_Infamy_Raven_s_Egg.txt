'''"Raven's Egg"''' is a short story by [[Elaine Bergstrom]] included in the 1994 anthology ''[[Realms of Infamy]]''.

==Summary==
At the end of his life, Lord [[Sharven]] of [[Espar]] recounts his marriage to [[Atera]] of [[Saerloon]]. Without meaning to, Sharven tells of how his own greed, combined with his growing callousness and paranoia, leads to his downfall, all while blaming his ill fortune on a court [[wizard]].
