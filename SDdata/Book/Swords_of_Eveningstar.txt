Swords of Eveningstar, by Ed Greenwood, is the first book of The Knights of Myth Drannor trilogy. The Swords of Eveningstar, the titular group that evolved into the Knights of Myth Drannor, were named after a party created by players of Ed Greenwoods home campaign.
