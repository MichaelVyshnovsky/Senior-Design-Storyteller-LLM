'''''A Darkened Wish''''' is a five-issue comic series of ''Dungeons & Dragons'' comic books published by [[wikipedia:en:IDW Publishing|IDW Publishing]]. It is set in the [[Moonshae Isles]] region, and is written by [[B. Dave Walters]] and drawn by [[Tess Fowler]].
