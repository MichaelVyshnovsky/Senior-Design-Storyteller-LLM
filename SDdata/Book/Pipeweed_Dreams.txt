Pipeweed Dreams was a lively ditty and a popular halfling drinking song, written and popularized sometime before 1368 DR.
In 1368 DR, Pipeweed Dreams was performed by the god Milil, the Lord of All Songs. He imposed the beautiful song on an audience of Sensates from Sigil who were performing a scrying ritual to experience the forbidden experience of spying on Torillian deities. The songs infectious melody caused many in the audience hum and sing along.
