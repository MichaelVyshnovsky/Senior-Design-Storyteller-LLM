'''''The Bright Throne Shattered''''' was a stage play penned by [[Baerlus Merlond]] of [[Athkatla]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{quote|So once more our kingdom totters, foes on every hand, and<br/>Every lord a traitor! Who can save the Bright Throne now?<br/>(The cry goes up once more.)<br/>Worry not, my lords, for I have a little plan.|Old Lord Sneel, in Act I, Scene III}}

==History==
''The Bright Throne Shattered'' featured a character named Old Lord Sneel. The play was first performed in the {{YearlinkName|1382}}.
