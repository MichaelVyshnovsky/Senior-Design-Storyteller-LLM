Demonomicon of Iggwilv was a column published in Dragon, Dungeon, and Dragon+. The series examined various powerful demons, along with their associated cults&mdashmaterial supposedly taken from the Demonomicon of Iggwilv, a profane work by the witch Iggwilv of Oerth.
