'''''Demonomicon of Iggwilv''''' was a column published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon]]'', ''[[Dungeon magazine|Dungeon]]'', and ''[[Dragon+]]''. The series examined various powerful [[demon]]s, along with their associated cults&mdash;material supposedly taken from the ''[[Demonomicon of Iggwilv]]'', a profane work by the witch [[Iggwilv]] of [[Oerth]].
