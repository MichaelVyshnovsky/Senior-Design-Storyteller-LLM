'''''Facemaker, Tastemaker''''' was a [[book]] penned to promote [[Facemaker's Boutique]], a shop in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Content==
The book contained a collection of various fashionable and resplendent drawings of designs: from embroidered [[cape]]s and wide-brimmed hats decorated with exotic materials and daring patterns to unique-looking gowns in fashions alien to the Gate.

==History==
Copies of ''Facemaker, Tastemaker'', from under [[Figaro Pennygood]]'s [[quill]], could be found in the {{YearlinkName|1492}} in Facemaker's Boutique located in the Lower City near the [[Manor Gate]].
