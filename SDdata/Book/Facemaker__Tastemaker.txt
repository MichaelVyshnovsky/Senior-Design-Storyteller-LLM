Facemaker, Tastemaker was a book penned to promote Facemakers Boutique, a shop in the city of Baldurs Gate the late 15 century DR.
The book contained a collection of various fashionable and resplendent drawings of designs from embroidered capes and wide-brimmed hats decorated with exotic materials and daring patterns to unique-looking gowns in fashions alien to the Gate.
Copies of Facemaker, Tastemaker, from under Figaro Pennygoods quill, could be found in the  in Facemakers Boutique located in the Lower City near the Manor Gate.
