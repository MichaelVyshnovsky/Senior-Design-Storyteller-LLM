'''''Drums of Daggerford''''' was a ballad from the [[Sword Coast]] region, composed sometime in or before the mid 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
''Drums of Daggerford'', its sheet music, and lyrics, all were recored in the [[book]] simply titled ''[[Sword Coast Songbook]]'', compiled by [[Silke Rosena]]. The book was made available for purchase in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] in or before the late 15{{th}} century DR.
