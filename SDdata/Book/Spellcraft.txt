'''''Spellcraft''''' was a column by various authors published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]''. The series debuted in September 2004 ([[Dragon magazine 323|Dragon #323]]) and ended in December 2006 ([[Dragon magazine 350|Dragon #350]]). The column was the successor of ''[[Arcane Lore]]'', continuing to explore spells and their lore.
