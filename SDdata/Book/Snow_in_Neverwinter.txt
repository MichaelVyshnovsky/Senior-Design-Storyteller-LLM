'''''Snow in Neverwinter''''' was a stage play penned by [[Rulvaerlon Dethmureon]] of [[Silverymoon]], in the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Contents==
{{quote|At first I thought you were just one more adventurer<br/>Out to steal and slay and threaten folk out of their gold<br/>But now I perceive you have higher ambitions<br/>And wish to seize thrones and behead emperors<br/>To make yourself a new world-tyrant—yet aren't raving<br/>And actually have a plan. Well, then, be welcome!<br/>A sane world-conqueror! We don’t get many of those!|Immulglair the Impressive, in Act I, Scene I}}

==History==
''Snow in Neverwinter'' was a play that featured the character named Immulglair the Impressive. The play was first performed in the {{YearlinkName|1345}}.
