'''''My Dinner With Goblins''''' was a seminal comedic [[book]] penned by [[Angus Hammerfall]] under the pen name of Artimal Penfall sometime before [[1372 DR]].

==History==
''My Dinner With Goblins'' received high praises from [[Volothamp Geddarm]], who called it the funniest thing in print.
