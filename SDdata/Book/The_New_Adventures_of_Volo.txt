'''''The New Adventures of Volo''''' was a column of sixteen articles by [[Ed Greenwood]] published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]''. The series debuted in January 2000 ([[Dragon magazine 267|Dragon #267]]) and ended in April 2001 ([[Dragon magazine 282|Dragon #282]]). It was written from the perspective of [[Volothamp Geddarm]] as part of his guide book series.
