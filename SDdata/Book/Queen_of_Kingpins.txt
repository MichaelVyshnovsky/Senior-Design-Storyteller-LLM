Queen of Kingpins was a record book compiled by the Flaming Fist mercenaries of Baldurs Gate on The Guild and Nine-Fingers Keene sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.
The book held information on the Guild, including the name and description of one of its thugs, Tusgront, who guarded the entrance to Nine-Fingers base or at least one of the Guilds hideouts in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate. The book also had detailed information on the Guilds individual thugs, whom the Flaming Fist considered to be quite dim-witted. The books tone changed when it talked about Astele Keene herself. It noted her sharp intelligence and used sullen, fearful tones.
