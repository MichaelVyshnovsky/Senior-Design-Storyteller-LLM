'''''Volo's Guide to Cormyr''''' was a book written by [[Volothamp Geddarm]]. It was a travelogue about the kingdom of [[Cormyr]]. It was written over [[1367 DR|1367]] to [[1368 DR]].

Around [[1372 DR]], one copy was kept in the Tower of Tomes of the [[Leaves of Learning]], a temple to [[Oghma]] in [[Deepingdale]]. Another copy was in the holdings at [[Candlekeep]].
