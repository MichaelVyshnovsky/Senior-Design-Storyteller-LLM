Volos Guide to the Bloodstone Lands was a book written by Volothamp Geddarm. It was a travelogue about the realms of Damara and Vaasa, known collectively as the Bloodstone Lands. It was written over 1360 to 1362 DR, but went officially unreleased.
Nevertheless, around 1372 DR, a copy was in the holdings at Candlekeep, and the original was held by an unknown party.
