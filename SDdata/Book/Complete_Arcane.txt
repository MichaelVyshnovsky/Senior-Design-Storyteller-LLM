Complete Arcane is a sourcebook accessory for the 3.5-edition of the  ruleset published by Wizards of the Coast in November 2004. It expands on arcane magic, with new magic-using classes and prestige classes, including introducing the warlock new spells and magic items expanded skills and new feats for players and campaign ideas for dungeon masters.
* Introductions
* Chapter 1 Classes
* Chapter 2 Prestige Classes
* Chapter 3 Arcane Feats
* Chapter 4 Spells and Invocations
* Chapter 5 Magic Items
* Chapter 6 Arcane Monsters
* Chapter 7 Arcane Campaigns
Complete Arcane expands upon and replaces Tome and Blood. It presents additional rules and advice for the creation and use of character classes which specialize in arcane magic. It also updated three base classes to 3-edition warlock, war mage and wu jen.
