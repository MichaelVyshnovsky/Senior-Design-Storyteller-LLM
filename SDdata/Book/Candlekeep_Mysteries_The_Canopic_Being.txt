'''"The Canopic Being"''' is a short adventure module for 13{{th}}&ndash;level characters, collected in the anthology ''[[Candlekeep Mysteries]]'', where players delve into a tomb associated with a devotee of the god [[Savras]].

{{TOC limit|2}}
==Synopsis==
In [[1492 DR]], a band of [[adventurer]]s begin to investigate the disappearance of a missing [[sage]] of Candlekeep, Great Reader [[A'lai Aivenmore]]. Their journey takes them to the [[House of the All-Seeing Orb]] temple in the far southern city of [[Tashluta]].
