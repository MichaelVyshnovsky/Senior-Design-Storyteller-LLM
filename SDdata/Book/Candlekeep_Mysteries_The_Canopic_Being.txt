"The Canopic Being" is a short adventure module for 13&ndashlevel characters, collected in the anthology Candlekeep Mysteries, where players delve into a tomb associated with a devotee of the god Savras.
In 1492 DR, a band of adventurers begin to investigate the disappearance of a missing sage of Candlekeep, Great Reader Alai Aivenmore. Their journey takes them to the House of the All-Seeing Orb temple in the far southern city of Tashluta.
