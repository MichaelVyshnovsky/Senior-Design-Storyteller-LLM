A Merchants Sagacity was a book written by the merchant Blackthorn Belgadar.
The original copy was kept in Candlekeep as well as the Leaves of Learning library, around the .
Within the book, Belgadar sarcastically wrote how books were great resources for individuals to learn about the mistakes of others in order to make more profound mistakes of their own.
He mused about the doom that resided within individuals and how it waited for the precise moment to present itself.
