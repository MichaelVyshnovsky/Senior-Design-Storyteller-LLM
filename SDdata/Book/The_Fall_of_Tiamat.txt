'''''The Fall of Tiamat''''' was a 15{{th}} century [[opera]] outlined the events of [[Tiamat]]'s demise, and subsequent banishment to the [[Nine Hells]], at [[Tiamat's temple|her temple]] in the [[Well of Dragons]]. It was performed in the [[Giant language|language]] of the [[giant]]s, one of the most-hated foes of all of [[dragonkind]].

==Performance==
The opera was known to have been performed at the [[Lightsinger Theater]] in [[Waterdeep]] in [[1492 DR]].{{Waterdeep-time}}
