'''''Delblood's Atlas of Faerûn''''' was a popular reference book containing [[map]]s of [[Faerûn]]. It was composed by the cartographer [[Delblood]]. Several copies of the atlas were available to view for free within the open book galleries of the [[Vault of the Sages]] in [[Silverymoon]] around [[1372 DR]].
