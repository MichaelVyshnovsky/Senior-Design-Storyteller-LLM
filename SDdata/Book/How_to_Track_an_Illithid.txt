How to Track an Illithid was a hand-written book penned by Golbraith Stredivas, friend and confidant of Duke Abdel Adrian, published sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.
The book collected numerous methods used to track down the dreaded mind flayers. Each of the methods was well detailed and tested, with each endeavors outcome painstakingly documented by Golbraith.
In the , the original tome of How to Track an Illithid could be found in the home of Golbraith Stredivas in Baldurs Gates Lower City.
