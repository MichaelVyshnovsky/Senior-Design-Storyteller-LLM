Kara-Tur The Eastern Realms is a boxed set for AD&D 1 edition. 
Kara-Tur The Eastern Realms is part of the Oriental Adventures campaign setting and set in Kara-Tur. It describes the eastern land of Kara-Tur and its geography, population, society, government, history, languages, and opportunities for adventure. It is the third major boxed set for the Forgotten Realms.
The boxed set is presented as a missive written by the spirit dragon Mei Lung to his friend Elminster, with many chapters written by one of Mei Lungs contacts in each country of Kara-Tur.
Wizards of the Coast considers Kara-Tur The Eastern Realms among such legacy content that "may reflect ethnic, racial, and gender prejudice that were commonplace in American society at that time", and distances itself from such prejudices.
The boxed set contains two 96-page books, four large color maps, and two plastic overlays.
