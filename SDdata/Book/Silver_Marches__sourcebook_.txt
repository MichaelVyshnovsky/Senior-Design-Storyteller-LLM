Silver Marches is a sourcebook for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting using the 3-edition  ruleset.

This sourcebook covers the Silver Marches region of northwest Faerûn. It provides details the towns and settlements of the burgeoning Silver Marches alliance, as well as the many hazards that threaten it.
