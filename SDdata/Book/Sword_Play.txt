Sword Play is the first book in the Netheril trilogy by Clayton Emery. The main character is Sunbright Steelshanks, a barbarian of the Rengarth tribe. 
Candlemas and Sysquemalyn, servants of the powerful Netherese archmage Polaris, begin betting on the life of Sunbright Steelshanks, a barbarian warrior. Sysquemalyn sends frequent deadly challenges, while Candlemas sends him to investigate the One King, a conquerer who has united orcs and humans. Sunbright joins with the half-elf Greenwillow on the way. She is lost in the Nine Hells when Sysquemalyn conjures a Demiplane that opens portals to the Hells throughout Netheril. Polaris rescues the other three, flaying Sysquemalyn alive. Sunbright allies with Candlemas to find Greenwillow again.
