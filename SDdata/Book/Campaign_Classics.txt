}}'''''Campaign Classics''''' was a column published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]''. The series debuted in January 1995 ([[Dragon magazine 225|Dragon #225]]),{{Cite dragon/225/The Wyrm's Turn: Finally (Whew!)}} with eight 2nd edition installments until December 1997, two more 3rd edition articles in 2004, and one last 4th edition appearance in 2008.

It provided new material for campaign settings that had been discontinued before.

There were also three entire ''Dragon'' magazines, ''[[Dragon magazine 315|#315: Classic Campaigns]]'', ''[[Dragon magazine 339|#339: Campaign Classics]]'', and ''[[Dragon magazine 351|#351: Campaign Classics: Beyond the World Serpent Inn]]'', dedicated to the same topic.
