The Dungeon Masters Guide 3rd edition is the original Dungeon Masters Guide for the 3-edition of the Advanced Dungeons & Dragons game. It was replaced in 2003 by the Dungeon Masters Guide 3.5, when the rules were advanced from 3-edition to 3.5-edition.
