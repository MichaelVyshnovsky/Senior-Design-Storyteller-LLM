"The Keeper of Secrets" is a short story by Ed Greenwood included in the 2004 anthology Realms of the Dragons.
In Waterdeep, Mirt the Moneylender and Durnan the Wanderer go to collect debts from a mysterious "Keeper of Secrets" and encounter a cryptic woman named Taraunramorlamurla.
