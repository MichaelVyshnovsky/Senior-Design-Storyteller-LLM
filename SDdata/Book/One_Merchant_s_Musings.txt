'''''One Merchant's Musings''''' was a [[book]] by [[Gornrel Murtarren]] in [[1360 DR]].

{{quote|''Beginnings, beginnings are easy. Any fool with a sword or a shout or a moment's witlessness can start something. Tis finishing such matters alive, and getting home again whole, that takes bold heroism. And the luck of the gods.''|An excerpt from ''One Merchant's Musings''}}

==Contents==
''One Merchant's Musings'' was a collection of thoughts and deliberations of Gornrel Murtarren, a merchant, who shared his opinions on several matters, including the importance of finishing one's matters and returning home unscathed.
