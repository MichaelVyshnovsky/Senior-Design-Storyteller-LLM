'''''How Not to Get Eaten by Owlbears: A Survival Guide for the Forgotten Realms''''' is a book by [[Anne Toole]] published in September 2024, by [[Wikipedia:DK (publisher)|DK]] (an imprint of Penguin). It purports to be a guide to the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]'' and [[dungeon]]eering by [[Falgriss]], a [[dragonborn]] [[ranger]] and assistant to [[Volothamp Geddarm]].
{{quote|
So you've decided to be an Adventurer. Good for you! Just a warning, though. There are some really horrible things lurking out there. Things with fangs and glowing eyes. Things that think you look delicious. Not to mention the magical mishaps, erratic wizards, and villainous cutthroats, you'll encounter. Keep this handy guide in your pack, and your adventures might end in fame and fortune, rather than something's stomach.|Back-cover text}}

{{quote|
A travel guide for D&D's Forgotten Realms, filled with advice and tips on where to go, what to see, and ways to avoid a horrible death

'''The Forgotten Realms are dangerous. Well, actually that's an understatement. The Forgotten Realms are a death trap...'''

But they're also very exciting, packed with amazing sights, and fun to explore. And that's where this book comes in.

An in-world guide written from the point of view of a newly invented character from D&D's lore, this book will answer your burning questions via chapters covering deadly fauna, magical mishaps, urban perils, merciless monsters and much more. Whether you want to know which is the best tavern in [[Baldur's Gate]], or have a pressing need for tips on how to escape an [[ogre]]'s lair, with this handy, humorous guide at your fingertips, your adventures will end in fame and fortune, rather than something else's stomach.
|Publisher's description}}
