'''''Beyond the High Road''''' by [[Troy Denning]] is the second book in [[The Cormyr Saga]], which chronicles the end of King [[Azoun IV]]'s rule of [[Cormyr]].

{{Quote|''The Seer [[Alaundo]] prophesied that seven scourges would sweep [[Cormyr]] away in ruin. For centuries the [[Obarskyr|royal family]] has stood watch against that day and devoted their lives to the protection of the realm.''

''But in a time when their ancient guardians slumber and their most loyal servants disappear, when a terrible evil prepares to sweep down upon their home...''

''Who will protect the royal family?''|Beyond the High Road}}
