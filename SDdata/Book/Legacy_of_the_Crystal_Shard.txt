{{quote|''Legacy of the Crystal Shard'' presents [[Icewind Dale]] in the time of the Sundering, a period that will define the future of the Forgotten Realms. In addition to providing 64 pages of in-depth information on the settlements of [[Ten Towns]] and their inhabitants, this product includes a harrowing 32-page adventure in which the player characters defend Icewind Dale against a rising threat with ties to the past.

''Legacy of the Crystal Shard'' allows characters to participate in important events connected to the Sundering and glimpse the future of the Forgotten Realms.}}
