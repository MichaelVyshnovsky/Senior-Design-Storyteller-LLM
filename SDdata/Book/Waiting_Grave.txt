The '''''Waiting Grave''''' was a ballad composed by [[Dezsmra Langrafarl]], [[Minstrel]] of [[Iriaebor]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

{{quote|''High hearts and brilliant plans so bright<br/>Give many a gallant good cause to figh<br/>Yet never any lack of black-hearted knave<br/>Keeps heroes from waiting, yawning grave.''|An expert from the ''Waiting Grave''.}}

==History==
The ballad sang about heroes and treacherous villains. The ''Waiting Grave'' was recored in a collection of ballads by Dezsmra Langrafarl – ''[[A Minstrel's Whimsy‎]]'', a [[chapbook]] published in the {{YearlinkName|1382}}.
