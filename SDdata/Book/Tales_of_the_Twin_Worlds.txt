'''''Tales of the Twin Worlds''''' was a [[book]] of poetry written by [[Anders Ui Moray]] in the late 12{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref group="note" name="Year" />

==Contents==
This poetry was very crazed and ranting, speaking of a twin world called "[[Abeir|Abihr]]" and a sleeping [[god]]. It also contained information relating to the long and complex tale of the castle [[Darkturret]].
