'''''The Wizards Three''''' was a column by [[Ed Greenwood]] published in [[Dragon (magazine)|''Dragon'' magazine]]. The series debuted in September 1992 ([[Dragon magazine 185|''Dragon'' #185]]), describing a meeting between [[Elminster]] of [[Faerûn]] and [[Mordenkainen]] of [[Oerth]] where they discussed inviting [[Dalamar]] of [[Krynn]] to join them. The series ended in September 2007 ([[Dragon magazine 359|''Dragon'' #359]]).
