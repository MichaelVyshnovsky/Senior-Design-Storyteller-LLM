'''''Five (Or Six) Heroes''''' was a children's [[book]] published sometime in or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Content==
''Five (Or Six) Heroes'' was a book written in simple and easy to understand style aimed at the young. Its protagonists was a group of unlikely heroes tho came together in defense of a [[forest gnome]] village that fell under a marauding [[gnoll]] pack's attack.
