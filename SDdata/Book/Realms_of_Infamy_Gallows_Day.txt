'''"Gallows Day"''' is a short story by [[David Cook]] included in the 1994 anthology ''[[Realms of Infamy]]''.

==Summary==
After the thief [[Therin]] is set up on his most recent "job" in [[Elturel]], his companions, under the leadership of [[Pinch]], begin to orchestrate his rescue from the gallow's noose. Pinch himself begins to tie clues of the botched heist together, and plans revenge against the group's traitor.
