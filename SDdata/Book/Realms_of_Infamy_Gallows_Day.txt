"Gallows Day" is a short story by David Cook included in the 1994 anthology Realms of Infamy.
After the thief Therin is set up on his most recent "job" in Elturel, his companions, under the leadership of Pinch, begin to orchestrate his rescue from the gallows noose. Pinch himself begins to tie clues of the botched heist together, and plans revenge against the groups traitor.
