'''The Return of the Archwizards Omnibus''' by [[Troy Denning]] is the complete anthology of the [[Return of the Archwizards]] novel series, including ''[[The Summoning]]'', ''[[The Siege]]'', and ''[[The Sorcerer]]''.

{{Quote|
<center>'''THE ENEMY'''</center><br />
Facing annihilation at the hands of monsters, the last refuge of the elves makes a desperate pact with a group of powerful enigmatic sorcerers who seem eager to destroy their foes.<br />
<center>'''OF MY ENEMY'''</center><br />
But the deeper into the conflict they sink, the more apparent it becomes that the motives—and origins—of the mysterious sorcerers may be darker than the monsters they save them from.<br />
<center>'''IS NOT ALWAYS MY FRIEND'''</center>}}
