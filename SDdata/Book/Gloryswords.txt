'''''Gloryswords: An Informal and Incomplete Overview of Adventuring Bands in the Year of the Spur''''' was a [[book]] written by [[Ragefast]] the Sage of [[Baldur's Gate]] and published in [[1369 DR]].
{{quote|'' Now in the time of which I have the honor to write, the fair realm of Cormyr was suffering from a dearth of adventurers, which is to say: an uncustomary shortage of fools... ''|Ragefast}}

==Contents==
''Gloryswords'' collected the historic records of adventuring companies and their deeds during [[1348 DR]], the year of an influx in the vocation in the Forest Kingdom of Cormyr.
