"The Unforking Family Tree" is a short comedic story by Ed Greenwood set in Waterdeep, based on a number of suggestions provided by attendees of Gen Con 2013s Spin a Yarn with Ed Greenwood seminar, and published on the Wizards of the Coast website that same year.
