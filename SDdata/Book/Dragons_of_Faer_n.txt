Dragons of Faerûn is an accessory for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting using the 3.5-edition  ruleset.

Dragons of Faerûn details dragons and draconic matters and provides information on some of the settings most notorious dragons and dracoliches, and also presents information on dragon-related organizations such as the church of Tiamat and the Cult of the Dragon. The book also includes ready-to-play adventures, new traps and treasures found within the lairs of dragons, new spells for dragons, and new monsters.
