'''''The Grand History of the Realms''''', published in September 2007, is a sourcebook detailing the history of [[Faerûn]].

{{Quote|'''''Explore the Past, Glimpse the Future'''''<br />
''From the dawn of recorded history through the ancient days of Netheril and the Fallen Kingdoms, to the Time of Troubles and the upheavals of the present day, this powerful tome presents the definitive history of the Fᴏʀɢᴏᴛᴛᴇɴ Rᴇᴀʟᴍs® world. Accompanied by anecdotes, diary entries, letters, and historical texts created by famous Faerûn personalities, an extensive and comprehensive timeline leads you through the eras of the world&mdash;including a sneak peek into upcoming events.''}}
