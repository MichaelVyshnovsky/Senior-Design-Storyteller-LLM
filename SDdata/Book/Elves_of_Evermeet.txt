'''''Elves of Evermeet''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory for the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]''.
{{Quote|''For many years, [[Evermeet]] has remained a mystery, known only as the [[elf|elves]]' place of refuge. So far, details about the island itself, its geography, rulers, and society have been sketchy. It is known that Evermeet is protected by illusory magic, that ships lose their way or vanish mysteriously if they approach the isle, and that the elven navy is the finest in the [[Forgotten Realms|Realms]]. Past publications have also revealed that Evermeet is ruled by Queen [[Amlaruil Moonflower|Amlaruil]], and that her husband [[Zaor Moonflower|Zaor]] was murdered decades ago. Beyond this, little is known.'' 

The Elves of Evermeet'' attempts to investigate and explain the inner workings of the elven island, and develop the realm as a setting for ''FORGOTTEN REALMS'' adventures. Until now, Evermeet has been reserved exclusively for the elves and officially closed to [[human]]s, [[dwarf|dwarves]], and other races. But this product will help you work Evermeet into your campaign and allow other PC races to visit, or at least interact, with the mysterious island.''

''Included are:''
* ''new elven magic (for High Mages),''
* ''elven NPCs,''
* ''tips on playing Evermeet elves,''
* ''gods and goddesses of the island, and''
* ''adventure ideas for your own campaign!''

''Bring the wonder that is Evermeet into your game, with this sourcebook. It's a mystery no more!''}}

==Description==
''Elves of Evermeet'' focuses on the island of [[Evermeet]], often referred to as the "Isle of the Elves," which plays a significant role in the lore of the Realms. It is the 5{{th}} book in the [[FOR series]].

It explores  the history, culture, and society of the elves of Evermeet. This included their origins, traditions, and the significant events that have shaped their society. It provided detailed descriptions of Evermeet's geography, including its forests, cities, and magical locations.

It included insights into the political and social structure of elven society on Evermeet, including their government, family dynamics, and societal roles. This also include details on the different subraces of elves residing on the island and their interactions.

''Elves of Evermeet'' explored the unique magical practices, spells, and artifacts found on Evermeet. This also included information on elven high magic, a rare and powerful form of spellcasting.

It introduced key non-player characters (NPCs) and influential figures within Evermeet, which Dungeon Masters can use to enrich their campaigns or create story hooks. It provided specific adventure ideas or campaign hooks set on Evermeet, allowing Dungeon Masters to create stories that explore the rich lore and environment of the elven homeland. It also included new options for players who wish to create elven characters from Evermeet, including unique skills, abilities, or background options that reflect their upbringing and culture.
