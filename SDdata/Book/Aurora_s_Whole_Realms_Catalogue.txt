Auroras Whole Realms Catalog is a 2nd-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms. 
This accessory focuses on whimsical items, offering an eclectic inventory that includes bards instruments, household items, and 14 types of cheese. The designers modeled the book after an actual turn-of-the-century mail-order catalog, meaning that exaggerated salesmanship accompanies each product description.
Auroras Catalogue was extensive. The following list only includes items that were described in detail and omits items that were mentioned solely on price lists.
