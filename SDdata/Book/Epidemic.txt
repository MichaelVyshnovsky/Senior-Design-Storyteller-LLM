Epidemic is an adventure module from the RPGA Legacy of the Green Regent campaign.
All over Loudwater, orcs are exploding! The cause is a disease that is spreading quickly through the citys populace. There isnt enough clerical magic to go around and an epidemic will soon strike if a cure is not found quickly.
