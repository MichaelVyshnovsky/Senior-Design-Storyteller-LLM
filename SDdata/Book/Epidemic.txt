'''Epidemic''' is an adventure module from the RPGA [[Legacy of the Green Regent]] campaign.

==Plot==
All over [[Loudwater]], [[orc]]s are exploding! The cause is a disease that is spreading quickly through the city's populace. There isn't enough [[cleric]]al magic to go around and an epidemic will soon strike if a cure is not found quickly.
