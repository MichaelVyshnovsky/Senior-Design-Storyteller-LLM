'''''Silicon Sorcery''''' was a column published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]''. It debuted in December 1999 ([[Dragon magazine 266|Dragon #266]]) and ended in October 2005 ([[Dragon magazine 336|Dragon #336]]). The column focused on RPG and simulation games for computers and consoles.
