The '''''Treatise on Good Wifery''''' was a book written by [[Miriam Buttercake]] of [[Ashabenford]] during the late 13{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was published in [[1298 DR]].{{Cite web enhancement/The Leaves of Learning}}{{Cite web/The Candlekeep Collection}}

As of [[1372 DR]], a copy of the book could be found in the [[Leaves of Learning]] in [[Highmoon]], while the original was located in the library of [[Candlekeep]].
