Hellgate Keep is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons adventure module for the Forgotten Realms.
Hellgate Keep is the 4 book in the Dungeon Crawl series.
Designed for intermediate to high-level PCs - typically levels 9-12 and in a standard party size of 5 or 6 members, this Dungeon Crawl adventure challenges parties of lower levels to use strategy and strength to overcome the perils of Hellgate Keep. The module includes various adventure starters, offering DMs multiple options to engage the PCs, as player groups and motivations differ. These scenarios are adaptable to any play style or campaign setting, providing an engaging entry point into the malevolent depths of what was once Hellgate Keep.
This adventure can be played as a self-contained quest but also seamlessly integrates into any Forgotten Realms campaign in the Savage Frontier, or any other fantasy world chosen by the DM.
