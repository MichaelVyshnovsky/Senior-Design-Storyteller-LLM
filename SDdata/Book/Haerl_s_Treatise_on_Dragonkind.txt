'''''Hearl's Treatise on Dragonkind''''' was a [[book]] that held a wealth of esoteric knowledge about [[dragon]]s and [[dragonkind]]. It was highly prized by the second iteration of the [[Cult of the Dragon]] that emerged during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
During the 1480s [[DR]],{{ToD-time}} cult agents utilized this book to expand their collective knowledge regarding draconic lore.

At least one copy was present on the isle of [[Tuern]] when the Cult of the Dragon fought a terrific battle{{Cite web/Tyranny of Dragons: Online Comic 3}} around that same time.
