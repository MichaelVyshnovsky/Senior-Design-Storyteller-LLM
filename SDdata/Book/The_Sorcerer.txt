'''''The Sorcerer''''' by [[Troy Denning]] is the third novel in the [[Return of the Archwizards]] trilogy.

{{quote|''The exciting conclusion of the [[Return of the Archwizards]] trilogy!''

The Sorcerer ''concludes the Return of the Archwizards trilogy by New York Times bestselling author [[Troy Denning]]. Events in this trilogy will have great impact on other [[Forgotten Realms]] novels to come, and this particular title introduces elements of change to Forgotten Realms readers.''

''In the blasted ruins of [[Tilverton]], demons stalk the shadows.''

''In [[Evereska]] and [[Cormyr]], alliances crumble.''

''In the sun-baked desert of [[Anauroch]], an empire of darkness is established.''

''In the soul of a [[Galaeron Nihmedu|tortured elf]], the future is decided.''}}

==Summary==
[[Tilverton]] has been laid to waste by the [[Shadovar]] forces, but the Shadovar paid a steep price for their victory, as the [[Cormyrian]] war wizards obliterated many of their numbers. The twisted [[Malik el Sami|Seraph of Lies]], servant of [[Cyric]] continues to plot to perform his master's will. The [[Chosen of Mystra]] and the elves of [[Evereska]] and [[Evermeet]] must make their final stand, and trust in a tortured and twisted former [[Evereskan tomb guardian|Tomb Guard]] and wielder of shadow magic.
