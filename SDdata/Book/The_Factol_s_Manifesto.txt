The Factols Manifesto is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory. It is part of the Planescape campaign setting. 
The The Factols Manifesto was published by TSR, Inc. in 1995. The book is a guide to the factions of Sigil, the City of Doors, and their leaders, known as factols.
