'''''Infernal Tides''''' is a five-issue comic series of ''Dungeons & Dragons'' comic books published by [[wikipedia:en:IDW Publishing|IDW Publishing]]. It is set in the [[Elturel]] region, and is written by [[Jim Zub]], and drawn by [[Max Dunbar]]. It is a direct continuation of the previous series ''[[Evil at Baldur's Gate]]'', ''[[Frost Giant's Fury]]'', ''[[Shadows of the Vampire]]'' and ''[[Legends of Baldur's Gate]]''.
