Infernal Tides is a five-issue comic series of Dungeons & Dragons comic books published by IDW Publishing. It is set in the Elturel region, and is written by Jim Zub, and drawn by Max Dunbar. It is a direct continuation of the previous series Evil at Baldurs Gate, Frost Giants Fury, Shadows of the Vampire and Legends of Baldurs Gate.
