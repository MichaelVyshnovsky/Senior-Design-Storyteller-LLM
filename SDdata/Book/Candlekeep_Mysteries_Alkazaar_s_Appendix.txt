'''"Alkazaar's Appendix"''' is a short adventure module for 15{{th}}&ndash;level characters, collected in the anthology ''[[Candlekeep Mysteries]]'', where players undertake an expedition that was left unfinished years prior.

{{TOC limit|2}}
==Synopsis==
In [[1492 DR]], a band of [[adventurer]]s embark on the final quest never taken by the famous adventurer [[Alkazaar]], author of ''[[Alkazaar's Thrilling Tales]]''. They journey to the great desert of [[Anauroch]] and work against the [[blue dragon|blue]] [[dracolich]] [[Zikzokrisha]] to possibly recover one of the fabled ''[[Nether Scrolls]]''.
