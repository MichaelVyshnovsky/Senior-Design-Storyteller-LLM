Level Up Your Life was a self-help book that promised to teach its readers charisma, published sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.
A copy of Level Up Your Life belonged to Gyldro Angleiron, the grumpy master smith and owner of The Rivington General in the Rivington district of Baldurs Gate in the . The book was kept in the mans home, adjacent to the Rivington Generals smithy.
