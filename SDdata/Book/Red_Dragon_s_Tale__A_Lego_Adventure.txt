Red Dragons Tale A Lego Adventure is an adventure for the Dungeons & Dragons 5-edition ruleset and set in the Forgotten Realms.
Red Dragons Tale A Lego Adventure was written by Christopher Perkins with art by Patricia de Erausquin Llorens and Alison Titus Hughes. It was published on April 1, 2024, by The Lego Group as part of the Lego Insider rewards program, as either a free downloadable PDF or a booklet that could be ordered for a number of points, and ties into the Dungeons & Dragons Red Dragons Tale Lego set.
