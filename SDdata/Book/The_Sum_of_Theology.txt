The '''''Sum of Theology''''' was a [[book]] written by the [[Oghma]]n servitor, [[Vetheera|Saint Vetheera]]. As of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], a copy of the book was available within the library of [[Candlekeep]].

==Notable Owners==
The [[ogre]] scholar [[Little One]] was in possession of this tome for some time, studying its pages within the walls of the [[Hearth]].
