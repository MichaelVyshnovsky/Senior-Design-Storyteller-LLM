The Ascension of Vlaakith was a githyanki book that contained a narrative of the ascension of Vlaakith CLVII to the throne, as well as a discussion on the importance of arcane magic to githyanki society.
The book was written in Gith. Its account of Vlaakiths rise to absolute power emphasized her reliance upon arcane magic, arguing that she would not have succeeded without it.
The books discussion on arcane power stated that wizardry was fundamental to the githyankis plans of defeating their enemies and eventually ruling the multiverse.
