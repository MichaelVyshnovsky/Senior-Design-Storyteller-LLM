'''''Dwarven Runes: A Quick Guide to Interpretation''''' was a [[book]] penned by [[Carorn Forath]] sometime before the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
A copy of ''Dwarven Runes'' belonged to [[Manshoon XII]], a clone of [[Manshoon]], in the {{YearlinkName|1370}}. The book was stored in the [[mage]]'s library of mundane and [[arcane]] tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of [[Amaunator]] underneath the [[Waterdeep/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]] of [[Waterdeep]].
