Fang, Beak, and Claw is an adventure module by Sean K. Reynolds for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting using the 3-edition Dungeons & Dragons rules. It was published on the Wizards of the Coast website on September 26, 2001. Intended for four 8-level characters including a ranger, it has the PCs investigate killings by followers of Malar in an unspecified area of Faerûn.
