Land of Preening Dragons Words of a Retired Wizard of War was a book penned by Garen Thal, a Loremaster of Cormyr, in the late 14 century DR.
Garen Thals work collected lore, thoughts, and rumination of a loremaster and a retired War Wizard on various topics including the practices and habits of Cormyte nobility, especially the young nobles from the Forest Kingdoms capital city.
