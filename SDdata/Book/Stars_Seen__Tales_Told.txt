'''''Stars Seen, Tales Told''''' was a periodical [[chapbook]] published in the city of [[Elturel]] of the [[Western Heartlands]].

==Description==
Each issue of ''Stars Seen, Tales Told'' was a relatively thin chapbook. It paid little attention to local news or gossip, focusing on positivity and praise. It also printed letters from its readers, which occasionally led to bitter in-print feuds.
