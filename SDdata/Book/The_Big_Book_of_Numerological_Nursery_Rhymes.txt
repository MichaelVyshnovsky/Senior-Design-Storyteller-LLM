'''''The Big Book of Numerological Nursery Rhymes''''' was an immensely popular collection of traditional songs and poems for children.

{{quote|''Nine are we when taken all three.<br/>Last one slain, then six remain.<br/>None survive our first less five.''|Excerpt from ''The Big Book of Numerological Nursery Rhymes''}}

==Contents==
The book collected such greatly favored tunes as "Frickle Fractal Froo" and "Little Hector Vector."

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1374}}, the ''The Big Book of Numerological Nursery Rhymes'' was considered well known and first-edition prints were exceedingly rare. [[The Collector]] of [[Neverwinter]] used one of the poems in the book as a code for his safe that held numerous rare and priceless treasures.
