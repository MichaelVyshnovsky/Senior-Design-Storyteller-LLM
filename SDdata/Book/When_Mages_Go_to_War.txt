When Mages Go to War was a ballad penned by Angarn Dunharp in the .
The song depicted event preceding a mighty spell-battle and cautioned common folk to hide behind barred doors when mages were about to throw incantations.
By the late 15 century DR, When Mages Go to War was recorded in the book simply called Sword Coast Songbook, available for purchase in the city of Baldurs Gate.
