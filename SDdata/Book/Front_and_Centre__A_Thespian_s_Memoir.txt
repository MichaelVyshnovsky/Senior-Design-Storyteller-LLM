'''''Front and Centre: A Thespian's Memoir''''' was an autobiography written by an unknown play actor. It spoke of their great joy of becoming a different individual for a short while.

{{Quote|When you choose a character to play, you don't just wear a mask - you take a little of their soul for your own.|''Excerpt from Front and Centre.''}}

==Content==
The author shared their belief that when an individual whose to take on a given role, who they were at their core changed, even if the slightest amount.
