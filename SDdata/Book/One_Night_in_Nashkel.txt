One Night in Nashkel was a pulp erotica book found in the Western Heartlands.
The book held a fictional account of the Evereskan elf Xan and his imprisonment in the Nashkel mines during the Iron crisis. The tale goes that after months of isolation, Xan finally seeks pleasure with his sentient moonblade.
A copy was kept in the library of the Sharess Caress brothel in 1492 DR.
