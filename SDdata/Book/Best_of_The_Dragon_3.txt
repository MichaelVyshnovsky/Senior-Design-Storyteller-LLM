'''''Best of The Dragon #3''''' was published by [[TSR, Inc.]] in July of 1983.  It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==The Dwarven Point of View==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]].'' '''p. 4'''
The dwarves are very much a race apart. (From [[Dragon magazine 58|Issue #58]])

==The Gods of the Dwarves==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]].'' '''p. 6'''
A half-dozen dwarven deities. (From [[Dragon magazine 58|Issue #58]])

* [[Abbathor]]
* [[Berronar]]
* [[Claggedin]]
* [[Dumathoin]]
* [[Vergadain]] 

==The Elven Point of View==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]].'' '''p. 11'''
The biggest difference to humans is their concept of time. (From [[Dragon magazine 60|Issue #60]]) 

==The Gods of the Elves==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]].'' '''p. 13'''
Some deities from the elven pantheon. (From [[Dragon magazine 60|Issue #60]])

*[[Aerdrie Faenya]]
*[[Erevan Ilesere]]
*[[Hanali Celanil]]
*[[Labelas Enoreth]]
*[[Solonor Thelandira]]

==The Halfling Point of View==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]].'' '''p. 17'''
One of the key words in halfling society is security. (From [[Dragon magazine 59|Issue #59]])

==The Gods of the Halflings==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]].'' '''p. 19'''
The gods of the halflings. (From [[Dragon magazine 59|Issue #59]])

* [[Arvoreen]]
* [[Brandobaris]]
* [[Cyrrollalee]]
* [[Sheela Peryroyl]]

==The Gnomish Point of View==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]].'' '''p. 23'''
Gnomes have struck a happy medium between the attitudes of dwarves and elves. (From [[Dragon magazine 61|Issue #61]])

==The Gods of the Gnomes==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]].'' '''p. 25'''
The gods of the gnomes. (From [[Dragon magazine 61|Issue #61]])

* [[Baervan Wildwanderer]]
* [[Flandal Steelskin]]
* [[Segojan Earthcaller]]
* [[Urdlen]]

==The Half-Orc Point of View==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]].'' '''p. 28'''
Orcs are the ultimate social Darwinists. (From [[Dragon magazine 62|Issue #62]])

==The Gods of the Half-Orcs==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]]'' '''p. 31'''
The gods of the half-orcs. (From [[Dragon magazine 62|Issue #62]])

* [[Bahgtru]]
* [[Illneval]]
* [[Luthic]]
* [[Shargaas]]
* [[Yurtrus]]

==Self Defense for Dragons==
: ''By [[Gregory Rihn]].'' '''p. 40'''
Making dragons a little more dangerous. (From [[Dragon magazine 50|Issue #50]])

==The Faerie Dragon==
: ''By [[Brian Jaeger]].'' '''p. 42'''
Magical and mischievous. (From [[Dragon magazine 62|Issue #62]])

==Bazaar of the Bizarre: Evil Dragon Armors==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]]'' '''p. 44'''
Hides with built-in bonuses. (From [[Dragon magazine 62|Issue #62]])

==The Thief: A Special Look==
: ''By [[Lenard Lakofka]]'' '''p. 46'''
Examining abilities, old and new. (From [[Dragon magazine 47|Issue #47]])

==The Druid and the DM==
: ''By [[Tim Lasko]]'' '''p. 47'''
Opportunity knocks, indoors and out. (From [[Dragon magazine 48|Issue #48]])

==It's Not Easy Being Good==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]]'' '''p. 51'''
Restrictions and rewards of paladinhood. (From [[Dragon magazine 51|Issue #51]])

==Cantrips: Minor Magic==
: ''By [[Gary Gygax]]'' '''p. 59'''
A cast of spells for would-be wizards to use. (From [[Dragon magazine 59|Issue #59]] and [[Dragon magazine 60|Issue #60]])

==Cantrips for the Illusionist==
: ''By [[Gary Gygax]]'' '''p. 66'''
Zero-level spells add spice to specialist class. (From [[Dragon magazine 61|Issue #61]])

==The Alchemist==
: ''By [[Lenard Lakofka]]'' '''p. 66'''
An alchemist is an obscure sub-class of magic-user. (From [[Dragon magazine 49|Issue #49]])

==The Archer==
: ''By [[Lenard Lakofka]]'' '''p. 78'''
Are, or were, archers really as good as they are sometimes portrayed to be? (From [[Dragon magazine 45|Issue #45]])
