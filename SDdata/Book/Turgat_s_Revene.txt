Turgats Revene was a work of fiction, a swashbuckling adventure book.
This novel tells grand adventure on the high seas story of the Captain Samuel Turgat and his sidekick Doctor Algernon.
In the 14 century DR, Turgats Revene could be found in the city of Daggerford, notably in the citys open library within Sorcellers Encapsulate.
This book inspired a young librarian to legally change his name to Samuel Turgat and take on a new profession of a the Flyingfish captain.
