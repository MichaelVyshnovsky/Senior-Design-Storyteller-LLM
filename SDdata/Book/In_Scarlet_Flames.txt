In Scarlet Flames is an adventure using the 5 edition Dungeons & Dragons ruleset made to tie in to the Magic The Gathering crossover set Adventures in the Forgotten Realms. It is primarily set in the High Moor, and is designed for a party of four to six 8-level characters.
