The Ruins of Myth Drannor is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons box set for the Forgotten Realms.
The box contains
* 128-page Myth Drannor Campaign Guide
* 32-page Myth Drannor Adventures booklet
* 4 maps
* 8 monster cards
* 8 cardstock accesory sheets
Myth Drannor Campaign Guide
* Introduction
* The High History of Myth Drannor
* The Seizing
* The Encircling Wood
* Myth Drannor Today
* The Mythal
* Wild Mythal Magic Table
* The Phaerimm
* Phaerimm Spells
* The Alhoon
* Alhoon Spells
* The Baatezu
* The Baelnorn
* Adventuring Bands
* The Presence of Lathander
* Lathanderian Priest Spells
* The Nagara
* Druth Daern
* The Spell-tome of Daern
* Shaundakul, Rider of the Winds
* NPCs of Myth Drannor
* The Magic of Myth Drannor
Myth Drannor Adventures
* Running Adventures in Myth Drannor
* The Crown of Stars
* The Wyrm Below
* What We Face in Dreams
* A Harp at Sunset
* The Arm of Valor
* Myth Drannor Encounter Tables
* Encircling Wood Encounter Tables
* Myth Drannor Feature Tables
* Myth Drannor Treasure Tables
Cardstock reference sheets
* Key to the Map of Myth Drannor as It Was
* Dragondark Tower
* The Llundlath
* The Neirdrecrypt
* The Thelaeneum
* Windsong Tower
* Mythal Magic Table
* Ruins Rubble Table
* Aratha ("Killer Beetle") and Baelnorn
* Blazing Bones and Doomsphere (Ghost Beholder)
* Dread and Feystag (Calygraunt)
* Electrum Dragon (Draco Magus Electrum) and Fang Dragon (Draco Dentus Terribilis)
* Lythlyx and Magebane
* Metalmaster (Sword Slug) and Naga, Bone
* Ormyrr and Windghost
* Xantravar (Stinging Horror) and Xaver
