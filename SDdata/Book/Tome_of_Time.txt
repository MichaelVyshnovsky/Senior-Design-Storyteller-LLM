The Tome of Time was a book in the fourth level of the Mobius Tower.
The book had platinum plates for covers.
This book contained the following spells
* Haste
* Slow
* Dimension door
* Distance distortion
* Passwall
* Teleport
* Disintegrate
* Limited wish
* PermanencyThe spell listed is "Permanent". There is no such spell in AD&D 1st edition the module I5 on which the adventure is based on uses Permanency instead. It is thus assumed for the purposes of this article that the spell is actually intended to be Permanency.
* Temporal stasis
* Time Stop
