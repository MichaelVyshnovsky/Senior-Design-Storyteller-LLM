'''''The Princess Denied''''' was a stage play penned by [[Aldaerlon Palameir|Aldaerlon "Chapbooks" Palameir]] in the beginning of the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{quote|Here we go again, hard at pursuits some seem to love<br/>Though I, lords, am not one of them.<br/>All this hiding and seeking gives me acute rectal pain.|Halderand the Guardcaptain, Act I, Scene IV}}

==History==
''The Princess Denied'' featured a character named Halderand the Guardcaptain. The play was first performed in the {{YearlinkName|1403}}.
