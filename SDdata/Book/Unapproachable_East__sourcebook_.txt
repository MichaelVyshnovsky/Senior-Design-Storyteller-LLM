Unapproachable East is a sourcebook for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting using the 3-edition  ruleset.

Unapproachable East describes locations within the Unapproachable East such as Aglarond, Rashemen, and Thay. It details the races, spells, magic items, monsters and prestige classes common to the area. It also has a chapter dedicated on the organizations one would encounter in the region.
