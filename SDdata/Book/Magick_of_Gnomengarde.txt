Magick of Gnomengarde was a spellbook that was studied and shared by all gnomes of Gnomengarde as of the late 15 century DR.
The leather-bound spellbook displayed the title "Magick of Gnomengarde" in both Gnomish and Common. It was stored on a wooden pedestal atop a ledge overlooking the workshop in which Gnomengardes inventors crafted magic items.
Magick of Gnomengarde contained the following spells
burning hands &bull detect magic &bull identify &bull mage armor &bull magic missile &bull shield &bull sleep
