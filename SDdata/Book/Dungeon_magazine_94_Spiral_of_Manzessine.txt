'''Spiral of Manzessine''' is an adventure set in the [[Underdark]]'s [[Deep Wastes]] for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 3{{rd}} edition published in [[Dungeon magazine 94|''Dungeon'' #94]]. 

==Summary==
This adventure focuses on the [[illithid]]-run [[Manzessine Prison]] and the attempted escape of some of its inmates.
