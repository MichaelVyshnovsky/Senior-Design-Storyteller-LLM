The '''''Windsong of the High Ice''''' was the longest musical composition in recorded history.{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2011)/Longest Musical Composition}} It was an improvised humming melody created by [[wraith]]-like [[wind]] spirits that chased each other through wind-sculpted passages and holes in high icy peaks.

==Rumors & Legends==
According to the [[Church of Chauntea]], the beings responsible for this music had been serving their goddess for eons.
