Storm Kings Thunder is a 5th-edition Dungeons & Dragons adventure set in the Savage Frontier region of the Forgotten Realms. It takes four to six characters from levels 1&ndash5 up through level 10.
This adventure takes place after the events described in Hoard of the Dragon Queen and The Rise of Tiamat, but does not directly depend on them. It is not set in a particular year, but is assumed to be after the Year of the Iron Dwarfs Vengeance, 1485 DR, and the orc conflict known as the War of the Silver Marches. One possible year is the Year of the Scarlet Witch, 1491 DR, as the cloud giant castle that hovers over Waterdeep during the adventure is also featured in the novel Death Masks.

Storm Kings Thunder contains a brief description of about 165 locations in Northwest Faerûn, in case the adventurers pass through any of them during their travels while they seek ancient relics. Over thirty of these locations have suggested encounters for the players to gain experience, treasure, magic items, or to move the story along. In addition, there are three featured encounters that can potentially help the party toward their goals.
