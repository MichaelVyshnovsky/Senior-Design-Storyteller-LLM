A Tendays Prayers to Gond was customary prayer dedicated to the deity Gond Wonderbringer, adopted sometime before the late 15 century DR.
In the , A Tendays Prayers to Gond was used by the Gondar of the city of Baldurs Gate and the workers of the Steel Watch Foundry.
