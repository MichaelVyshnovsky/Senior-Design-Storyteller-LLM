'''''A Tenday's Prayers to Gond''''' was customary prayer dedicated to the [[deity]] [[Gond]] Wonderbringer, adopted sometime before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Content==
{{quote|Gond grant me ACCURACY that I may record the details of my work well and truly<br/>Gond grant me ALTERATION that I may correctly imbue mechanisms with the magic of dynamic change<br/>Gond grant me DEDUCTION that I may understand how my work is flawed and how to rectify it<br/>Gond grant me INNOVATION that I may perceive new and better paths to fabrication<br/>Gond grant me INSPIRATION that I may grasp the whole and not just the parts<br/>Gond grant me INTEGRITY that I may give my coworkers their due<br/>Gond grant me OBJECTIVITY that I may see past wants and desires to what is<br/>Gond grant me PRECISION that I may be meticulous and avoid error<br/>Gond grant me PUNCTUALITY that I may coordinate efficiently with others<br/>Gond grant me SOBRRIETY that I may perform my functions at full capacity|Excerpt from ''A Tenday's Prayers to Gond''}}

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, ''A Tenday's Prayers to Gond'' was used by the [[Gondar]] of the city of [[Baldur's Gate]] and the workers of the [[Steel Watch Foundry]].
