The Sirens Yearning was a powerful song that became imbued with magic if performed by a bard.
When played, the song affected the performers opponents in a similar way as a sirens charm. Those who succumbed to the songs magics remained enthralled for ten minutes or until they suffered harm.
By the , The Sirens Yearning was in the repertoire of some of the most experienced bards. It was heard being performed in the Ten-Towns of Icewind Dale and the village of Kuldahar.
