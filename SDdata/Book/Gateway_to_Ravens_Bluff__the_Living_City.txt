Gateway to Ravens Bluff, the Living City is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory for the Forgotten Realms.
Gateway to Ravens Bluff, the Living City is the 1 book in the LC series. Until late 1990, it was available only by mail to RPGA members. It was often used in RPGA tournaments and events. It provided a structured yet flexible framework for competitive and cooperative play.
