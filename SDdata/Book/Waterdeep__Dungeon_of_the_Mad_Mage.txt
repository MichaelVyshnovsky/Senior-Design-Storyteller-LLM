'''''Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage''''' is a 5{{th}}-edition ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' adventure published by [[Wizards of the Coast]] in 2018. It takes place in the dungeon of [[Undermountain]], located under [[Waterdeep]], and follows the ''[[Waterdeep: Dragon Heist]]'' adventure through levels 5‒20.

{{Quote|<big>'''''The Greatest Dungeon of Them All'''''</big>

'''Explore the mega-dungeon of Undermountain in this adventure for the world's greatest roleplaying game.'''

In the city of Waterdeep rests a tavern called the [[Yawning Portal]], named after the gaping pit in its common room. At the bottom of this crumbling shaft is a labyrinthine dungeon shunned by all but the most daring adventurers. Known as Undermountain, this dungeon is the domain of the mad wizard [[Halaster Blackcloak]]. Long has the Mad Mage dwelt in these forlorn depths, seeding his lair with monsters, traps, and mysteries—to what end is a constant source of speculation and concern.

This adventure picks up where ''Waterdeep: Dragon Heist'' leaves off, taking characters of 5{{th}} level or higher all the way to 20{{th}} level should they explore the entirety of Halaster's home. Twenty-three levels of Undermountain are detailed herein, along with the subterranean refuge of [[Skullport]]. Treasures and secrets abound, but tread with care!}}
