On Hallowed Ground is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory. It is part of the Planescape campaign setting.
On Hallowed Ground contains information about the planar domains of deities from 20 separate pantheons. New ideas and rules for priest characters on the planes are included, as well as tips on creating, visiting and surviving divine realms, comprehensive appendices listing gods by pantheon and portfolio, and planar maps.
In addition to the background information on the religions, the book also contains rules and mechanics for conducting religious ceremonies and spells, as well as new magic items and spells related to the deities.
