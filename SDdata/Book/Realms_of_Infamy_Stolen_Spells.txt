'''"Stolen Spells"''' is a short story by [[Denise Vitola]] included in the 1994 anthology ''[[Realms of Infamy]]''.

==Summary==
Having been offered the right price, the skilled thief [[Arek Adar]] travels to the [[Sunset Mountains]] to steal an item of great value from an old [[dwarven]] hermit. When Arek returns to his client in [[Cormyr]] he is betrayed, and begins to plot his revenge.
