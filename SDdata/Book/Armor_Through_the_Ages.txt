'''''Armor Through the Ages''''' was a reference [[book]] detailing [[armor]] metallurgy practices throughout [[Toril]]'s history, up until the early 14{{th}} century [[DR]].Beyond it being a metallurgical reference book, the contents of this book were not detailed in this adventure. Thus the listed summary was partially inferred from the title of the book itself.

==History==
One copy of this tome laid within [[Bard Keep]], a temple of [[Oghma]] that stood within [[the North]] near [[Silverymoon]].
