'''''Heroes' Feast Flavors of the Multiverse: An Official D&D Cookbook''''' is a cookbook with recipes inspired by different cuisines in the D&D [[multiverse]].{{cite web |url=http://web.archive.org/web/20230627144455/https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/708747/heroes-feast-flavors-of-the-multiverse-by-kyle-newman-jon-peterson-michael-witwer-sam-witwer-official-dungeons-and-dragons-licensed/ |title=''Heroes' Feast'' Product Overview |accessdate=2023-27-06}}

{{Quote|''Explore the cuisines of the Dungeons & Dragons multiverse with more than 75 delectable new recipes from the New York Times bestselling authors of Heroes’ Feast.<br/><br/>“Celebrate with delicious game-inspired recipes to feed your fantasy, fill your hearts, and swell your bellies.”—Todd Stashwick, actor, Star Trek: Picard<br/><br/>Never adventure on an empty stomach! From the D&D experts behind the bestselling Heroes’ Feast comes Heroes’ Feast Flavors of the Multiverse,a mouthwatering cookbook stuffed with eclectic fare for solo adventurers and party quests alike. This culinary tour presents original recipes inspired by regions and settings from across the Forgotten Realms and beyond. All seventy-six dishes, developed by a professional chef from one of the country’s top test kitchens, are delicious, easy to prepare, and composed of ingredients readily found in our world.<br/><br/>The immersive recipes in Heroes’ Feast Flavors of the Multiverse are perfect for sharing and entertaining. Dishes are organized by location with options for every occasion—especially game nights!—including:''<br/>
*''otherworldly appetizers such as Talyth and Goldenstars''<br/>
*''savory main courses such as Steak of the Deep and Eldeen Banquet''<br/>
*''alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages such as Elverquist and Kaeth''<br/>
*''and desserts such as Green Ice Rime and Vada’s Vanilla Bean Buns''<br/>
''Adventure has never tasted so good!''}}
