'''''Dungeon Master's Guide II''''' is a core sourcebook for the 3.5{{th}}-edition of {{dnd}} ruleset.

{{Quote|'''''Take Your Game To A New Level'''''

''This supplement for the DUNGEONS & DRAGONS<sup>&reg;</sup> game contains a toolbox of timesaving aids and options to help you prepare and run memorable adventures and rich campaigns. Inside are ideas, advice, and ready-made material that will help you become a truly spectacular DM. With gaming advice, NPCs, devious traps, new [[magic item]]s, and a fully detailed town, ''Dungeon Master's Guide&trade; II'' helps you keep your players on the edge of their seats.
}}

==Description==
''Dungeon Master's Guide II'' provides more advice and threats for running games, adventures, and campaigns; a detailed city (Saltmarsh); new features for non-player characters; and master–student relationships, business rules, and teamwork rules for new rules for player-characters. There are also guidelines for creating prestige classes and organizations, and more features for magical items.
