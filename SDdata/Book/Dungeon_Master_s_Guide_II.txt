* Chapter 3 The Campaign
* Chapter 4 Saltmarsh
* Chapter 5 Nonplayer Characters
* Chapter 6 Characters
* Chapter 7 Magic Items
Dungeon Masters Guide II provides more advice and threats for running games, adventures, and campaigns a detailed city (Saltmarsh) new features for non-player characters and master–student relationships, business rules, and teamwork rules for new rules for player-characters. There are also guidelines for creating prestige classes and organizations, and more features for magical items.
