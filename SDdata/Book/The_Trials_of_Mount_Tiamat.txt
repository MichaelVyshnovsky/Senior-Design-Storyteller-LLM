'''The Trials of Mount Tiamat''' is a companion adventure for the ''[[Idle Champions of the Forgotten Realms]]'' that takes place in the [[Sword Coast]] and clashed a group of adventurers with the [[Cult of the Dragon]]. The adventure is designed for a party of 18{{th}} to 20{{th}} levels.{{cite web| url = https://web.archive.org/web/20220129160418/http://www.codenameentertainment.com/images/website/dnd/Adventures/TrialsofMountTiamatv1.0.pdf| title =  Codename Entertainment - The Trials of Mount Tiamat| accessdate = 29-1-2022| date = 29-1-2022}}

{{quote|''The Cult of the Dragon has succeeded in its efforts to bring the [[Dragon Queen]] to the [[Material Plane]], and has consolidated its power to fortify the cult’s stronghold at the [[Well of Dragons]].<br/>Champions from across the [[Forgotten Realms]] heed the call to band together and face this cataclysmic threat. It is here that the adventure of The Trials of [[Mount Tiamat]] begins ''}}
