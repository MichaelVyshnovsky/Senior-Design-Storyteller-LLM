Faces of Evil The Fiends  is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory. It is part of the Planescape campaign setting.
Faces of Evil The Fiends contains detailed descriptions and statistics for a wide range of fiends, from lowly imp minions to powerful archdevils and demon lords. It also includes information on fiendish cults, the politics of the lower planes, and advice on how to run campaigns focused on battling the forces of evil.
The book is intended for use by Dungeon Masters and players who want to explore the darker aspects of the games world and challenge their characters with dangerous and diabolical foes.
