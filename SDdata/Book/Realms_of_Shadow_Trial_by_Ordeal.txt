"Trial by Ordeal" is a short story by Lisa Smedman that appeared in the Realms of Shadow anthology of stories from the Forgotten Realms, published in April 2002.
In , the Year of Perditions Flame, Lord High Justice Andoris Derathar of Karsus Enclave must solve the mystery of a crime—the theft of a wizards notes concerning the Nether Scrolls—committed by either an enchantress or her shadow double, but neither have any memory of the events.
