'''''Monstrous Compendium Volume Three: Forgotten Realms Appendix''''' is a [[Forgotten Realms]] game accessory published in 1989 by [[TSR, Inc]].

{{Quote|More monsters escape from the Realms and into your AD&D 2nd Edition game! Sixty-four pages chock-full of new monsters for use in your own campaign and with the Forgotten Realms Campaign Setting. From the gnomish [[svirfneblin]] to the deadly [[dracolich]]; from the [[wemic]] to the revnant; they're all here! Battle the [[Beholderkin#Beholderkin|beholder-kin]] of the North, the dinosaurs of the [[Jungles of Chult]], and the oriental dragons of [[Kara-Tur]]. All these creatures and more are organized in 64 5-hole punched pages that will make a handy appendix to your Volume One Monstrous Compendium binder. Add them in and turn them loose on your players!}}
