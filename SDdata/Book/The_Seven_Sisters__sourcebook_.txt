'''''The Seven Sisters''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory for the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]''.
{{Quote|''Beautiful, powerful, mysterious, and deadly—the silver-haired [[Seven Sisters]] are legendary throughout the [[Forgotten Realms]].''

''[[Alustriel Silverhand|One]] rules the city of bards, [[Silverymoon]]. Another is [[Elminster]]'s beloved and the infamous [[The Simbul|Witch-Queen of Aglarond]]. The third is the [[Laeral Silverhand|Lady Mage of Waterdeep]], peer and consort of [[Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun]], and the [[Dove Falconhand|fourth]] is a [[Harper]] and one of the famous [[Knights of Myth Drannor]]. The [[Storm Silverhand|fifth]] is a leader of the Harpers and a legendary [[bard]] in her own right, and the [[Syluné|sixth]] is a dead—perhaps undead—witch. And the [[Qilué Veladorn|seventh]]? She's the one who wild tales are whispered about all across [[Faerûn]] around many a fireside late at night.''

''But who are they, really? Their very names conjure up romance, allure, and power—[[Storm Silverhand]], [[the Simbul]], [[Alustriel Silverhand|High Lady Alustriel]]. Sages say they are among the [[Chosen of Mystra]], archmages who wield magic few can dare stand against. Yet these seven do not seek to rule the world, and they often stay quiet and hidden for generations.''

''That hints at another thing. These seven seem immortal, never aging as the long years pass. Just who are these Seven Sisters, and what are their secrets?''

''Read this tome, and learn what the [[Zhentarim]], the [[Red Wizards]] of [[Thay]], and the [[Cult of the Dragon]] could not, despite performing many fell deeds at the time. Learn the stories of these great ladies, and their aims and strivings—and browse among hundreds of new and spectacular spells! Dungeons Masters and players alike will find this volume an essential sourcebook of Realmslore, and a handy guide to archsorceresses peerless in power.''}}

==Description==
''The Seven Sisters'' is the 6{{th}} book in the [[FOR series]]. 

The central content of ''The Seven Sisters'' focuses on in-depth profiles of each sister, detailing their backgrounds, personalities, magical abilities, and roles in the Realms. The sisters are:

* Alustriel Silverhand
* Dove Falconhand
* Laeral Silverhand Arunsun
* Syluné Silverhand
* Storm Silverhand
* The Simbul (Alassra Shentrantra Silverhand)
* Qilué Veladorn

Each profile includes personal histories, motivations, allies, enemies, and notable deeds.

The book also covers the extensive magical abilities of the Seven Sisters, discussing their status as [[Chosen of Mystra]] and their unique abilities and spells. There are also new magic items and artifacts associated with the sisters, detailed for use by Dungeon Masters (DMs) in gameplay.

''The Seven Sisters'' offers guidance for incorporating these characters into campaigns, providing adventure hooks and potential storylines related to the sisters.
