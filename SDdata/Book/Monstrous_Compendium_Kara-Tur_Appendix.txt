'''''Monstrous Compendium Kara-Tur Appendix''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory. It is part of the ''Oriental Adventures'' campaign setting and set in [[Kara-Tur]].

{{Quote|''Not just more monsters - mysterious, sinister creatures of the Orient! Inside you'll find 64 pages of the most fascinating monsters in the [[Kara-Tur]] Campaign Setting. From the jungle [[Bajang]] to the arctic [[yuki-on-na]], from savage spirits to beauteous new beasts-this volume captures them all. Each page is 5-hole punched to fit the'' Monstrous Compendium ''binder. Includes 4 dividers with dazzling, full-color art. A must for'' [[Oriental Adventures]] ''fans and players with a taste for the exotic.''}}

==Description==
Following the modular format of the ''[[Monstrous Compendium]]'' series, the ''Monstrous Compendium Kara-Tur Appendix'' consisted of 64 5-hole punched loose-leaf pages that could be inserted into the customizable ''Monstrous Compendium'' binder. It also included a "How To Use This Book" page with an alphabetical index and 4 pages of random encounter charts, with the remainder consisting of the descriptions of the fictional monsters. Also included were 4 full-page illustrations on heavier card stock.

Each entry within the appendix provides a detailed description of the creature, including its physical appearance, habits, habitats, combat abilities, and special powers.
