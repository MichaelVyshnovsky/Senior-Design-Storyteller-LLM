'''''Forgotten Realms''''' is a series of comic books originally published by [[wikipedia:en:DC Comics|DC Comics]].

==History==
In 1989, [[wikipedia:en:DC Comics|DC Comics]] began publishing the ''Forgotten Realms'' comic series, written by [[Jeff Grubb]]. Each issue contained twenty-six pages, illustrated primarily by [[Rags Morales]] and [[Dave Simons]].<ref>Dave Simons entry at [http://lambiek.net/artists/s/simons_dave.htm Lambiek] The 19 issues produced by Morales were his first professional works. [[Tom Raney]] illustrated issues #17 and #20. [[Elliot S. Maggin]] served as an editor for DC from 1989 to 1991 and oversaw the licensed [[TSR, Inc.]] titles, including ''Forgotten Realms''. Twenty-five issues were published in total, with the last being released in 1991.

==Dragon Magazine==
Two short stories published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]'' continues the tale begun in the ''Forgotten Realms'' comics.

* ''[[Reunification (Body & Soul)]]'' in ''[[Dragon magazine 247]]''.{{Cite web/Presenting...Seven Millennia of Realms Fiction}} This short story  tells of the crew of the ''[[Realms Master]]''.
* ''[[The Honor of Two Swords]]'' in ''[[Dragon magazine 260]]''. West meets East when [[Priam Agrivar]] follows [[Ishi Barasume]] to the secrets of her past.
