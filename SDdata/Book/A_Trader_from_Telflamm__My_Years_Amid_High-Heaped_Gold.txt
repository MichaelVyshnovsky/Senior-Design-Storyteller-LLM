'''''A Trader from Telflamm: My Years Amid High-Heaped Gold''''' was a [[book]] written by [[Uldurn Maskovert]]. It was published in [[1357 DR]].{{Cite web/The Candlekeep Collection}}

{{quote|''In Thay they trust in their spells. They bluster overmuch, and fear too little. Yet I know how to make a Red Wizard go pale with but three words. All I need say is: "Summon the Simbul".''}}

==History==
As of [[1372 DR]], a copy could be found on the shelves of [[Candlekeep]].

''A Trader from [[Telflamm]]: My Years Amid High-Heaped [[Gold]]'' referred to one of the [[Seven Sisters]], Alassra Shentrantra Silverhand, otherwise known as [[the Simbul]].
