A Trader from Telflamm My Years Amid High-Heaped Gold was a book written by Uldurn Maskovert. It was published in 1357 DR.
As of 1372 DR, a copy could be found on the shelves of Candlekeep.
A Trader from Telflamm My Years Amid High-Heaped Gold referred to one of the Seven Sisters, Alassra Shentrantra Silverhand, otherwise known as the Simbul.
