A Readers Guide to R. A. Salvatores the Legend of Drizzt is a guide written by Philip Athans in cooperation with R. A. Salvatore which was published by Wizards of the Coast, Inc. in August 2008 to celebrated twenty Years of The Legend of Drizzt Saga. 
The book is a large reference book detailing the characters, items, and locations within R.A Salvatores novels. Their book is printed in color with numerous detailed maps and illustrations.
