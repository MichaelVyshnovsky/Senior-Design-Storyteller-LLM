A Warriors Views was a book penned by Galgarr Thormspur and published in 400 DR.
A Warriors Views was a collection of musings of Galgarr Thormspur, Marshal of Maligh. He expressed his opinions on poor career choices among many adventurers in the Realms.
A copy of A Warriors Views was kept in the library-citadel of Candlekeep in the late-14 century DR.
