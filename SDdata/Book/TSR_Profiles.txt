'''''TSR Profiles''''' was a column published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]''. The series debuted in November 1985 ([[Dragon magazine 103|Dragon #103]]) and ended in March 2002 ([[Dragon magazine 293|Dragon #293]]). It went on hiatus from [[Dragon magazine 123|Issue #123]] to [[Dragon magazine 243|Issue #243]]. It was titled ''ProFiles'' after [[Dragon magazine 243|Issue #243]].

The column was a series of interviews with various authors, designers, artists, editor and key members of [[TSR, Inc.]] and [[Wizards of the Coast]] staff.
