Mindbreaker is a series of Forgotten Realms comic books to be published by IDW Publishing as a prequel to Baldurs Gate III. It follows the journeys of the Heroes of Baldurs Gate, where this time, the party investigates an illithid faction that is plaguing Baldurs Gate.
