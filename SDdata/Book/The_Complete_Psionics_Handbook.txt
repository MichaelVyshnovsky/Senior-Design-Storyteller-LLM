==Description==
''The Complete Psionics Handbook'' is the 5{{th}} book in the [[PHBR series|Player's Handbook Rules series]].

The book contains over 150 psionic powers, and includes an entire chapter on psychic combat. The book also includes psionic monsters. The book has a discussion of society's reaction to psionicists, and a section describing the role of psionics in various TSR campaign settings
