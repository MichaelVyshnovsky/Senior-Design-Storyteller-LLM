'''''The Taxonomy of the Grue''''' was a book classifying the [[:Category:Grue|various kinds]] of [[grue]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

A copy was in the library of [[Bradda]]'s [[Sage's Shop]] in [[Neverwinter]] in [[1479 DR]].
