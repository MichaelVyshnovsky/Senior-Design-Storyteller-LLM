'''''Architects Arcane: Waterdeep and the Sword Coast North''''' was a [[book]] dedicated to the powerful [[mage]]s of [[Waterdeep]] and the [[Sword Coast North]], penned by [[Paerl Nhesch]] and published in the early 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

The Blackstaff's proclamation became tradition when the son of [[Khelben]] took up the mantle in the Year of Lost Ships and as his long-time friend Ashemmon did in Ches of this year.|An excerpt from ''Architects Arcane''.}}

==History==
''Architects Arcane: Waterdeep and the Sword Coast North'' was published in the {{YearlinkName|1424}}. It included the history of the [[Blackstaff]] title being passed between several powerful mages of Waterdeep: [[Kyriani Agrivar]], [[Khelben Arunsun the Younger]], and [[Ashemmon]].
