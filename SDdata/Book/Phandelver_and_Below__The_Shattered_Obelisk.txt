'''''Phandelver and Below: The Shattered Obelisk''''' is an adventure for 5{{th}} edition ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' that occurs in [[Phandalin]].

{{Quote|''The heroes soon learn that the cult of a malevolent entity has set its sights on transforming Phandalin into the capital of its evil empire. To save the town from oblivion, the heroes must uncover a nefarious plot and confront the otherworldly cultists.''{{Cite web/Retailer Overview of 2023 Dungeons & Dragons Releases}}}}
