'''"Sarah of Yellowcrest Manor"''' is a short adventure module for 7{{th}}&ndash;level players, collected in the anthology ''[[Candlekeep Mysteries]]'', where players investigate an unsolved murder in [[Waterdeep]].

{{TOC limit|2}}
==Synopsis==
In [[1492 DR]], a group of [[adventurer]]s are approached by a [[ghost]] in [[Candlekeep]] who urges them to investigate the unsolved, grisly murder of the [[Yellowcrest]] family in [[Waterdeep]] five years prior. The investigation leads the adventurers to the nearby village of [[Greenfast]], which is plagued by a secretive cult.
