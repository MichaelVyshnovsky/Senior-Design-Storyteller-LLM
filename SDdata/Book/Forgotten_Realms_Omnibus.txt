'''''Forgotten Realms Omnibus''''' is a collection of comics set in the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]''. It was published in 2016 by [[wikipedia:en:IDW Publishing|IDW Publishing]].

It is a collection of the ''[[Dungeons & Dragons: Forgotten Realms (comic)|Dungeons &amp; Dragons: Forgotten Realms]]'', ''[[Cutter (comic)|Cutter]]'', and ''[[Neverwinter Saga]]'' series.
