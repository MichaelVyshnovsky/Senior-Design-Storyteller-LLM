The '''''North Wind''''' was a [[broadsheet]] based in [[Waterdeep]] on the [[Sword Coast North]]. It was published during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Gilding a Noble|5,44}}

==Contents==
This broadsheet featured illustrations of voguish garments and attractive models.{{Cite digital book/Downshadow||4}} It also contained gossips covering the life of high society, reporting on the life of eligible bachelors, noble maidens, and wealthy merchants.
