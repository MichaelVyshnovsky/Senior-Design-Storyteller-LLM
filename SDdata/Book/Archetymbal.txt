'''''Archetymbal''''' by [[Kastor]] was a classic work of fiction published in [[Faerûn]]. It was acclaimed as a literary masterpiece.{{Cite web/The Candlekeep Collection}} It had one volume.

The retired [[War Wizard]] and sage [[Benelaius]] had a copy in his library at his home near [[Ghars]], [[Cormyr]]. His servant [[Jasper (servant)|Jasper]] read it in [[1367 DR]].

The original manuscript was kept in [[Candlekeep]] around [[1372 DR]].
