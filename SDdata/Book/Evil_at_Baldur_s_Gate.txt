'''''Evil at Baldur's Gate''''' is a series of ''Dungeons & Dragons'' comic books published by [[wikipedia:en:IDW Publishing|IDW Publishing]]. It is a direct continuation of the previous series ''[[Frost Giant's Fury]]'', ''[[Shadows of the Vampire]]'' and ''[[Legends of Baldur's Gate]]'', and is itself continued in ''[[Infernal Tides]]''.
