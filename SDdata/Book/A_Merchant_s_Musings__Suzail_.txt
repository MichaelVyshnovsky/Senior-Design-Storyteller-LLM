A Merchants Musings was a book written by Holdryn Nalaster, a merchant of Suzail in Cormyr, and published in the .
Like other tomes of musings, Holdryns book was a connection of thoughts and recollections. One of the experiences hes recorded in A Merchants Musings was his dealings and visits to the land of Turmish of the Vilhon Reach.
The original was kept in the great fortress-library of Candlekeep around 1372 DR.
