'''''Ladies Night at the Yawning Portal''''' is a short story written by [[Ed Greenwood]] set in the [[Yawning Portal]], based on a number of suggestions provided by attendees of [[Wikipedia:Gen Con|Gen Con]] 2002's ''Spin a Yarn with Ed Greenwood'' seminar, and published on the [[Wizards of the Coast]] website in 2003.
