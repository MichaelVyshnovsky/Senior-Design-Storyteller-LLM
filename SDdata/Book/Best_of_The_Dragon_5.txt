'''''Best of The Dragon #5''''' was published by [[TSR, Inc.]] in May of 1986.  It contained the following articles related to the [[Forgotten Realms]]:

==Mind of the Monster==
: ''By [[Bruce Humphrey]].'' '''p. 10'''
Give creatures the brains they were born with. (From [[Dragon magazine 71|Issue #71]])

==Firearms: Primitive powder weapons for AD&D play==
: ''By [[Ed Greenwood]].'' '''p. 19'''
Details the use of firearms in fantasy settings.  (From [[Dragon magazine 60|Issue #60]]) 

==A Second Volley: Taking another shot at firearms, AD&D style==
: ''By [[Ed Greenwood]].'' '''p. 22'''
A sequel to "''Firearms''" from [[Dragon magazine 60|Dragon #60]] discussing small arms which developed from the handgun for Advanced Dungeons & Dragons. (From [[Dragon magazine 70|Issue #70]]) 

==Instant Adventure==
: ''By [[Michael E. Kelly]].'' '''p. 26'''
A list of a couple dozen adventures types (e.g. intrigue, salvage, one-on-one duel, etc), with the basic requirements, the amount of time it takes for a GM to prepare for the adventure, and some notes. (From [[Dragon magazine 48|Issue #48]])

==Ruins==
: ''By [[Arn Ashleigh Parker]].'' '''p. 30'''
Making adventures in ruins.  (From [[Dragon magazine 54|Issue #54]])

==Up on a Soapbox: Players don't need to know all the rules==
: ''By [[Ed Greenwood]].'' '''p. 46'''
Opining that the GM should handle all the rules elements while the players concentrate on roleplaying.  (From [[Dragon magazine 49|Issue #49]])

==The Merry Month of . . .Mirtul?==
: ''By [[Ed Greenwood]].'' '''p. 53'''
A calendar of the fantasy world of Harptos, created by Ed Greenwood.  (From [[Dragon magazine 47|Issue #47]])

==Point of View: The humanoids==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]].'' '''p. 54'''
The background and society of kobolds, goblins, hobgoblins and gnolls. (From [[Dragon magazine 63|Issue #63]])

==Magic for Merchants==
: ''By [[Lenard Lakofka]].'' '''p. 60'''
Cantrips and other magics to deal with shop-lifters.  (From [[Dragon magazine 62|Issue #62]])

==The Astral Plane==
: ''By [[Roger E. Moore]]'' '''p. 67'''
An old favorite, newly revised and updated. (From [[Dragon magazine 67|Issue #67]])
