'''''Monstrous Compendium Greyhawk Adventures Appendix''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory for ''Greyhawk''.

{{Quote|''Prepare yourselves and your characters... Here come more monsters, this time from the ''[[Greyhawk]] Campaign Setting''! These 64 pages are filled with beasties and creatures, from [[aspis]] to [[zygom]] â€“ and everything in-between! [[Crystalmist]], [[dragons]] (yes, there are more-four more, to be precise), and plenty of deadly [[:Category:Plants|plants]] await entry into your campaign. As always, these monster descriptions are organized on 5-hole punched pages, ready to be incorporated into your ''Monstrous Compendium'' binder. And, you get four full-color dividers with identification tabs! Snap them in, and set them free!''}}

==Description==
The ''Monstrous Compendium Greyhawk Appendix'' consisted of 64 5-hole punched loose-leaf pages, unnumbered, and included a "How To Use This Book" page with an alphabetical index and 4 pages of random encounter charts, with the remainder consisting of the descriptions of the fictional monsters. Also included were 4 full-page illustrations on heavier card stock.
