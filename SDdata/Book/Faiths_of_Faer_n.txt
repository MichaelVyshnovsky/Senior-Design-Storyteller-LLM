'''''Faiths of Faerûn''''' was a column published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]''. The series debuted in June 2001 ([[Dragon magazine 284|''Dragon'' #284]]) and ended in August 2004 ([[Dragon magazine 322|''Dragon'' #322]]). 

The column explored the [[Prestige class|Prestige classes]] of various deities in the Realms.
