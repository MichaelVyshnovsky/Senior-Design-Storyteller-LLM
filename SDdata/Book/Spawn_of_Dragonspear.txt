'''''Spawn of Dragonspear''''' is a gamebook in the ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons Adventure Gamebooks'' series. Published in May 1988, it is the only entry in the series to be set in the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]''.

{{Quote|Now you can experience all the thrills of playing the ADVANCED DUNGEONS & DRAGONS® Game by yourself&mdash;in book form!

ADVANCED DUNGEONS & DRAGONS® Adventure Gamebooks use a simple, unique role-playing game system based on the popular ADVANCED DUNGEONS & DRAGONS® Game, incorporating two standard six-sided dice (not included), hit points, skill points, experience points, and more.

In SPAWN OF DRAGONSPEAR, you, Kelson Darktreader, must discover the source of the evil emanating from dread Dragonspear Castle and put an end to it once and for all!

ADVANCED DUNGEONS & DRAGONS® Adventure Gamebooks read like books, play like games, and offer a thrill a minute&mdash;with YOU as the hero!}}
