'''''The Power of Words: Surviving the Fey''''' was a [[book]] penned by [[Anabil Lighthammer]] and published sometime in or before the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Content==
This voluminous tome collected information on various [[fey]] creatures. Chapters 418 to 425 detailed various encounters with [[hag]]s and included information on their gestation and reproduction cycles.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1492}}, ''The Power of Words: Surviving the Fey'' was studied by [[Mayrina]] after forming the [[Anti-Hag Support Group]], in search of ways to stop hags' sinister and malicious plots. The book's lore eventually helped Mayrina discover the recipe for ''[[hag's bane]]'' â€“ a concoction that could save the life of a child consumed by a hag.
