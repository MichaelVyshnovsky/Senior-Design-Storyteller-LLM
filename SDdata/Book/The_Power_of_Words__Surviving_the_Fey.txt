The Power of Words Surviving the Fey was a book penned by Anabil Lighthammer and published sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.
This voluminous tome collected information on various fey creatures. Chapters 418 to 425 detailed various encounters with hags and included information on their gestation and reproduction cycles.
