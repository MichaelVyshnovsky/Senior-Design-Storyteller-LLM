Apocalypto was a book that contained the poetic prophecies of an unknown author. It described their vision of the supposed end of the multiverse.
Aside from having a black cover, the tome was rather nondescript.
A copy of Apocalypto, or perhaps the original manuscript, was in the possession of Thurstwell Vanthampur as of the .
It was noteworthy that the libraries of Candlekeep did not contain a copy of this book as of that year.
