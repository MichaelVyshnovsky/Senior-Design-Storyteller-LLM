The Art of the Night was a book of erotica about certain newlywed king and queen and their first thousand nights together.
The Art of the Nights cover was embossed with an image of a man and a woman entangled in each others arms in the act of lovemaking.
The book detailed the lives and proclivities of a king and his queen after their marriage. The couple was known for turning all activities into art the art of conversation, the art of taste, the art of the body. The books salacious and romantic contents detailed and focused on the titular art of the night.
in the late 15 century DR, archwizard Gale Dekarios owned a copy of the book. It was stored in his tower located in the City of Splendor, Waterdeep. He used the book as an inspiration and a part of his romantic advancements.
