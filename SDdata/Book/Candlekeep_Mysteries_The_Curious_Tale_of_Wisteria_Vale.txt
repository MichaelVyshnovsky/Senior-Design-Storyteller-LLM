'''"The Curious Tale of Wisteria Vale"''' is a short adventure module for 11{{th}}&ndash;level characters, collected in the anthology ''[[Candlekeep Mysteries]]'', where players investigate a missing [[Harpers]] agent who has been sequestered in a [[demiplane]] resembling the town in which he grew up.

{{TOC limit|2}}
==Synopsis==
In [[1492 DR]], a band of [[adventurer]]s are hired by the [[archmage]] [[Ryllia Liadon]] to bestow a cure upon the bard [[Arrant Quill]], who had been corrupted by ancient magic.
