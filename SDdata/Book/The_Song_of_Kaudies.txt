'''''The Song of Kaudies''''' was a powerful song that became imbued with [[magic]] if performed by a [[bard]].

==Content==
When performed, the song's magic had a [[coin]]toss of a chance to negate effects of sound-based [[spell]]s and abilities such as ''[[silence]]'', ''[[great shout]]'', ''[[command]]'', and others.

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1281}}, ''The Song of Kaudies'' was performed by some of the most experienced bards. It was heard being performed in the [[Ten-Towns]] of [[Icewind Dale]] and the village of [[Kuldahar]].
