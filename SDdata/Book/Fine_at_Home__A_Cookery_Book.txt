Fine at Home A Cookery Book was a cookbook for those of means from the city of Baldurs Gate, printed sometime in or before the late 15 century DR.
The cookbook was written to be used in kitchens staffed with numerous servants and cooks. One of the recipes featured in the book was A Feast for a Tenday, a dish that required two days of cooking a complex sauce with roasted pork and was served with pasta, hard salty cheeses, hard-boiled eggs, and a side salad.
By the , Fine at Home A Cookery Book could be found in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate. A copy belonged to Chef Marjoram and the kitchen staff of Lady Firelia Jannath in her Lower City estate.
