'''''Featured Creatures''''' was a column by [[Gary Gygax]] published in [[Dragon (magazine)|''Dragon'' magazine]]. The series debuted in July 1982 (''[[Dragon magazine 63|Dragon #63]]'') and ended in January 1983 (''[[Dragon magazine 69|Dragon #69]]''). The column previewed several creatures who later appeared in ''[[Monster Manual II 1st edition]]''.
