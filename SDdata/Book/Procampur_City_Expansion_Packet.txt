The '''''Procampur City Expansion Packet''''' was a document released for the [[Living City]] organized play program run by the [[RPGA]] in 1997. The '''''Player's Edition''''' expands on the existing city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]] for use as background in RPGA campaigns.{{Cite web |url=http://rpga.org/LC/Procampur.html |title=Procampur, Shining Jewel of the Vast |accessdate=2023-09-03 |author=[[Jay Fisher]], [[Daniel Cooper]] |date=1997-10-02 |publisher=[[RPGA]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/19980717045533/http://rpga.org/LC/Procampur.html |archivedate=1998-07-17 }}{{Cite web |url=http://www.concentric.net/%7Ejlorien/page3.html |title=UCCWeb |accessdate= |author=[[Jay Fisher]], [[Daniel Cooper]] |date= |publisher=[[RPGA]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/19981202065857/http://www.concentric.net/%7Ejlorien/page3.html |archivedate=1998-12-02 }}{{Cite web |url=http://www.concentric.net/%7Ejlorien/page9.html |title=UCCWeb |accessdate= |author=[[Jay Fisher]], [[Daniel Cooper]] |date=1997-10-26 |publisher=[[RPGA]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/19981201204343/http://www.concentric.net/%7Ejlorien/page9.html |archivedate=1998-12-01 }}

==History==
In 1997, the RPGA announced the Procampur Project: an expansion for Living City set in the city of Procampur, Jewel of the Vast, overseen by the Universe Construction Company (a group of Living City designers; UCC) led by [[Jay Fisher]] and [[Daniel Cooper]]. They were split into a City Development Team (responsible for the design of the city and its lore) and a Tournament Development Team (responsible for creating the tournament adventures).

The initial plan was for a 10-part (or 11-part) series of connected adventures set in the Jewel of the Vast&mdash;what would eventually be known as the [[Cracked Jewel Saga]]&mdash;but the team was also open to adventure submissions not related to the main storyline. While players could submit adventures, locations, and characters for Procampur to the UCC, the city was not player-controlled like Ravens Bluff was and all material needed to be approved by [[TSR]].

The Expansion Packet was first made available at [[Wikipedia:Gen Con|Gen Con 1997]].
