'''Paths of Darkness Collector's Edition''' is an omnibus containing the four novels in the [[Paths of Darkness]] series by [[R. A. Salvatore]]. It contains ''[[Servant of the Shard]]'' despite it being removed from the series. It was published in February 2004 and August 2005.
