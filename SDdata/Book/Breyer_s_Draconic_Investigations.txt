'''''Breyer's Draconic Investigations''''' was a lore book that detailed arcane secrets about [[dragon]]s and [[dragonkind]]. At least one copy remained in the possession of the renewed [[Cult of the Dragon]] of the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
Full comprehension and command over the magical specifications detailed in ''Draconic Investigations'' required exceptional attention-to-detail and studiousness.

==History==
''Circa'' the 1480s [[DR]],{{ToD-time}} at least one copy of the book remained in the possession of senior most agents of the Cult of the Dragon. It was brought to the [[Lair of Lostmauth|lair]] of the [[red dragon]] [[Lostmauth]] on the isle of [[Tuern]].
