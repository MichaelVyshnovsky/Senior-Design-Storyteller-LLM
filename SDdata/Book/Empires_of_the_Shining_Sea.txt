Empires of the Shining Sea is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons boxed set for the Forgotten Realms.
Empires of the Shining Sea provides an in-depth look at the geography, history, and cultures of the regions surrounding the Shining Sea. This includes the Empires of Calimshan, Tethyr, and the Lake of Steam area. It has extensive details on the major cities and towns within these empires, including their history, architecture, politics, and daily life.
It explores the complex political dynamics and historical events that have shaped the region. This included conflicts, alliances, and the rise and fall of various rulers and factions. It also provided insights into the customs, festivals, religions, and day-to-day life of the people living in the region. It also has descriptions of new monsters, NPCs, and deities that are specific to the Empires of the Shining Sea.
Empires of the Shining Sea introduces new character classes, kits, spells, or equipment that are unique to the region, offering players additional options for character creation and development. It provides a variety of adventure hooks and ideas for campaigns set in this region, giving Dungeon Masters tools to create engaging stories rooted in the local context.
