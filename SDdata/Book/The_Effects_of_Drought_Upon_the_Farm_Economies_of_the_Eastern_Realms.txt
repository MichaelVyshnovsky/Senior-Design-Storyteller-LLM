'''''The Effects of Drought Upon the Farm Economies of the Eastern Realms''''' was a heavy tome on the topic of agriculture, written and published sometime before mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
A copy of ''The Effects of Drought Upon the Farm Economies of the Eastern Realms'' was found in the massive book collection of [[Klavikus]], a [[glabrezu]] in the service of [[Zhengyi]] the Witch-King of [[Vaasa]] in the {{YearlinkName|1359}}. Klavikus's copy was cared for and carefully marked with [[silk]]en bookmark.
