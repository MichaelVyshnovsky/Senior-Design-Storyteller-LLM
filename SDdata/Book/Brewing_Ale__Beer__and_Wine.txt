A copy of Brewing Ale, Beer, and Wine belonged to Manshoon XII, a clone of Manshoon, in the . The book was stored in the mages library of mundane and arcane tomes in his lair in an abandoned temple of Amaunator underneath the City of the Dead of Waterdeep.
