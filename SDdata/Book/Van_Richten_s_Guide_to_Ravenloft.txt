Van Richtens Guide to Ravenloft is a 5-edition Dungeons & Dragons sourcebook detailing the Domains of Dread for the Ravenloft setting. It was released on May 18, 2021.
* Chapter 1 Character Creation
* Chapter 2 Creating Domains of Dread
* Chapter 3 Domains of Ravenloft
* Chapter 4 Horror Adventures
* Chapter 5 Monsters of Ravenloft
* Appendix Spirit Board
