Encyclopedia Magica Volume II is an accessory for AD&D 2 edition. 
Encyclopedia Magica Volume II is the second in a series of four 2 edition books containing a vast collection of magical items. This second volume covered magical items starting with letters D-P, from Decoy Dust to Phylactery of Riteousness.
