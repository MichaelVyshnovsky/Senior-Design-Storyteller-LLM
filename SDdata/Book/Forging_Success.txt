Forging Success was a self-help book of dubious nature that promoted the Sharran House of Grief of Baldurs Gate in the late 15 century DR.
The book claimed to be able to help those who sought to break a cycle of lie and deceit. It diminished what some would call an art form to be nothing but self-delusion. The books long-winded text offered the reader treatments pioneered by the House of Grief and, in the end, was nothing but an advertisement for the House of Griefs services.
By the , Forging Success could be found in the Lower City of Baldurs Gate and was available in the House of Grief.
