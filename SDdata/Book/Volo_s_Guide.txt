'''''Volo's Guide''''' was a series of nine articles by various authors published in ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]''. The series debuted in January 2007 ([[Dragon magazine 351|Dragon #351]]) and ended in September 2007 ([[Dragon magazine 359|Dragon #359]]). It was written from the perspective of [[Volothamp Geddarm]] as part of his guide book series.
