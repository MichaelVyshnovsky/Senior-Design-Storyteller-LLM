'''''My Adventures in the Realms''''' was a six-volume series penned by the [[dwarven]] [[adventurer]] [[Dathlyr Greybold|Dathlyr "the Hammer" Greybold]]. It contained a number of his musings and opinions about a variety of subjects.{{Cite web/The Candlekeep Collection}}{{Cite dragon/272/The New Adventures of Volo: Quotations of the Realms}}

As of the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, a copy of the book could be found in [[Candlekeep]].
