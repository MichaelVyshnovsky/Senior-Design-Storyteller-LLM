'''Divine Contention''' is an online-only digital adventure that was released as additional content for the ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Essentials Kit]]'' for the 5th edition of ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]''. It is the third and final adventure in a trilogy called ''[[Beyond the Dragon of Icespire Peak]]'', a sequel adventure to ''[[Dragon of Icespire Peak]]'', and continues the story from ''[[Sleeping Dragon's Wake]]''.

{{Quote|After your group completes the challenges in ''Dragon of Icespire Peak'' (or another starter adventure), continue with Divine Contention!

Adventure for Levels 11-13

The final adventure in the [[Dungeons & Dragons Essentials Kit|Essentials Kit]] Bundle. The ghosts of the [[Swords of Leilon]] have returned to bring warning so you might protect their beloved town and prevent two great evils, both competing for the same prize, from wiping it from the [[Sword Coast]] once more. You must enter the [[Ethereal Plane]], prevent the followers of [[Talos]] and [[Myrkul]] from gaining possession of an artifact of great evil, and put down the forces of not one, but two vile deities.}}

==Distribution==
Originally, the ''Beyond the Dragon of Icespire Peak'' trilogy (''[[Storm Lord's Wrath]]'', ''Sleeping Dragon's Wake'', and ''Divine Contention'') was available exclusively through [[D&D Beyond]], either as standalone purchases or for free with purchase of a physical copy of the ''Dungeons & Dragons Essentials Kit.'' The product was subsequently made available for various virtual tabletop platforms.
