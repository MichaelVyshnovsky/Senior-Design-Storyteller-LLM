City of Splendors Waterdeep is an accessory for the Forgotten Realms campaign setting using the 3.5-edition  ruleset.
* Introduction
* Chapter 1 The City of Splendors
* Chapter 2 People of Waterdeep
* Chapter 3 Prestige Classes
* Chapter 4 Waterdeep Locales
* Chapter 5 Adventures in Waterdeep
* Chapter 6 Monsters of Waterdeep
* Chapter 7 Heroes and Magic
It details the city of Waterdeep, including a history of the city, detailed city maps, descriptions of key locations, game statistics for important NPCs, information on local laws, and rules for running and playing in adventures in the City of Splendors.
