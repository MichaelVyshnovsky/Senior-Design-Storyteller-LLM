Hoard of the Dragon Queen is a 2014 Forgotten Realms adventure module for the 5th edition of Dungeons & Dragons, designed by Kobold Press and the Wizards RPG Team. The module revolves around the Cult of the Dragons plans to free Tiamat, the goddess of chromatic dragons, from the Nine Hells. Hoard of the Dragon Queen is part of the Tyranny of Dragons storyline, and received a direct continuation in the adventure module The Rise of Tiamat.
