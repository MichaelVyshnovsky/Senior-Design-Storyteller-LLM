'''''A Merchant Master's Life''''' was a [[book]] written by [[Asargrym]], a merchant of [[Baldur's Gate]], and published in the {{YearlinkName|1327}}.
{{quote|''When death comes unlooked-for, it finds a way into the strongest fortress. It does no good to set extra guards at the gates.''|Excerpt from ''A Merchant Master's Life''}}

==Description==
''A Merchant Master's Life'' was a collection of life lessons and musings by the master merchant. 

==History==
A copy of the book was kept in the great fortress-library of [[Candlekeep]] as of the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.{{Cite web/The Candlekeep Collection}}
