'''"Under Mount Dread"''' is an adventure module for beginner players set in the [[Dalelands]], where they must slay monsters.
{{TOC limit|2}}

==Synopsis==
The players must attack or drive off the monsters at a dungeon once held by the evil mage [[Azazabus]].
