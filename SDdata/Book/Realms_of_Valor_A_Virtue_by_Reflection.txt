'''"A Virtue by Reflection"''' is a short story by [[Scott Ciencin]] included in the 1993 anthology ''[[Realms of Valor]]''.

==Summary==
The merchant [[Penn Othmann]] is found murdered in the palace gardens of [[Arabel]]. At the same time, the city is plagued by a series of strange incidents involving [[cat]]s. [[Myrmeen Lhal]], ruler of the city, must investigate how these happenings are connected to the mysterious [[cat lord]]s.
