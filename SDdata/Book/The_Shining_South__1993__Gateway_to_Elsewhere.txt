'''Gateway to Elsewhere''' is a short adventure for [[Dungeons & Dragons]] 2{{nd}} edition published in ''[[The Shining South (1993)|The Shining South]].''

{{Quote|''Gateway to Elsewhere is an adventure for 5-8 player characters of levels 9-11, with a total of about 70-90 levels for the entire party. The adventure introduces characters to the [[Forest of Amtar]], near the country of [[Dambrath]].''}}
