"Serpestrillvyth" is a short story by Richard Baker included in the 2004 anthology Realms of the Dragons.
The Company of the Argent Hawk, experienced dragonslayers all, take on a quest to rid a village of a troublesome green dragon. But all is not as it seems&hellip
