'''''In Sylvan Shadows''''' is the second book of [[The Cleric Quintet]] by [[R.A. Salvatore]].
{{quote|''[[Cadderly Bonaduce|Cadderly]] must leave his home to combat the evil unleashed by the [[Chaos Curse]]. To counter [[Castle Trinity]]'s plans for conquest, the cleric and his friends enter the beautiful forest of [[Shilmista]]. But among the trees and glades of the forest, Cadderly must battle a monstrous evil.''}}

== Summary ==
[[Cadderly Bonaduce]] ventures into Shilmista at the bidding of the elven prince [[Elbereth]] to help the [[wild elf|wild elves]] push back the invading force sent by [[Castle Trinity]].
