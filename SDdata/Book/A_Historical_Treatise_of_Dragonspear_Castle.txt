'''''A Historical Treatise of Dragonspear Castle''''' was a [[book]] that outlined the history of [[Dragonspear Castle]]. It covered the major events of the fortress, from its construction in [[1255 DR]], to the [[Second Dragonspear War]] of [[1363 DR]].

A copy of this book was owned by a member of the [[Shining Crusade]], who added personal annotations to its pages. The crusader died in a small cave infested with [[troll]]s.
