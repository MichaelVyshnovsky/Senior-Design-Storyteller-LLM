'''''Monster Manual'' 3{{rd}} edition''' is one of the core rulebooks for the 3{{rd}}-edition of {{dnd}}. With the release of version 3.5, the book was revised and republished as ''[[Monster Manual v.3.5]].
{{Quote|From the mundane to the magical, from the meek to the mighty, the Monster Manual brings the monsters of the ''DUNGEONS &amp; DRAGONS'' game to life with statistics, descriptions, full-color illustrations, and player character information. This book is essential for every Dungeon Master who wants to keep players on their toes!}}

==Description==
''Monster Manual'' is one the core rulebooks released for 3{{rd}}-edition and was not intended to make major changes, only to update older monsters to third edition rules.{{Cite dragon/276/Profiles: Skip Williams}}

''Monster Manual'' won the Origins Award for ''Best Graphic Design of a Roleplaying Game, Adventure, or Supplement 2000''.<ref>{{Cite web/2000 Origins Awards}}
