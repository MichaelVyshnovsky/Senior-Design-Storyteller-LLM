'''''Artwork from Baldur's Gate III''''' is a non-fiction book showing concept art and images used for the ''[[Baldur's Gate III]]'' video game. It was released by [[Wikipedia:Larian Studios|Larian Studios]] on August 3, 2023 and has a foreword by [[Joachim Vleminckx]], Art Director for the game.
