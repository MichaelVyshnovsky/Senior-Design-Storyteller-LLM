Another Dart in the Minstrels Heart was a play involving a minstrel and a heroine that they were romantically involved with.
Around the , the Ravens Bluff Playhouse put on a benefit performance of Another Dart in the Minstrels Heart to support the Hand of Mercy Childrens Hospital and Orphanage. Martin, the flamboyant recruit officer of the Silent Network, starred in the leading role and put on a dashing performance that won the adoration of many women in the audience.
