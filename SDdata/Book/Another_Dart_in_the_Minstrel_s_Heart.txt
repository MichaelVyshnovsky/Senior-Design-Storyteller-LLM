'''''Another Dart in the Minstrel's Heart''''' was a play involving a [[minstrel]] and a heroine that they were romantically involved with.

==History==
Around the {{YearlinkName|1370}},<ref> the [[Ravens Bluff Playhouse]] put on a benefit performance of ''Another Dart in the Minstrel's Heart'' to support the [[Hand of Mercy Children's Hospital and Orphanage]]. [[Martin (Silent Network)|Martin]], the flamboyant recruit officer of the [[Silent Network]], starred in the leading role and put on a dashing performance that won the adoration of many women in the audience.
