'''Storm Lord's Wrath''' is an online-only digital adventure that was released as additional content for the ''[[Dungeons & Dragons Essentials Kit]]'' for the 5th edition of ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]''. It is the first adventure in a trilogy called ''[[Beyond the Dragon of Icespire Peak]]'', a sequel adventure to ''[[Dragon of Icespire Peak]]''.

{{Quote|After your group completes the challenges in ''Dragon of Icespire Peak'' (or another starter adventure), continue with Storm Lord’s Wrath!

Adventure for Levels 7-9

The second adventure in the [[Dungeons & Dragons Essentials Kit|Essentials Kit]] Bundle. Abandoned for many years, [[Leilon]] was once a fortified settlement midway along the [[High Road]] from [[Waterdeep]] to [[Neverwinter]]. Under the paid service of the Lord Protector [[Dagult Neverember]], citizens of Neverwinter have returned, intent on reestablishing Leilon as a safe waypoint once more. Unfortunately, followers of [[Talos]] have also arrived, and they’ve brought the weather with them.}}

==Distribution==
Originally, ''Storm Lord's Wrath'' and its sequels, ''[[Sleeping Dragon's Wake]]'' and ''[[Divine Contention]]'', were available exclusively through [[D&D Beyond]], either as standalone purchases or for free with purchase of a physical copy of the ''Dungeons & Dragons Essentials Kit.'' The product was subsequently made available for various virtual tabletop platforms.
