The '''''Monstrous Compendium Annual Volume Four''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory

Creatures in this book have come from the [[Forgotten Realms|FORGOTTEN REALMS]], BIRTHRIGHT, DRAGONLANCE, [[Ravenloft|RAVENLOFT]], DARK SUN, RED STEEL, and GREYHAWK settings (and a few have just sprung from the fertile minds of their creators). This collection, which is fully indexed, features new monsters from the [[Far Realm]], and includes many creatures suitable for nautical and undersea adventures.}}

==Description==
''Monstrous Compendium Annual Volume Four'' serves as a supplement to the AD&D game, providing an extensive collection of monsters that were previously published in various 1996-1997 ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' modules, as well as ''[[Dungeon magazine|Dungeon]]'' and ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon]]''.

The structure of the ''Monstrous Compendium Annual Volume Four'' adheres to the format established by earlier ''[[Monstrous Compendium]]'' supplements. It features detailed entries for each monster, including physical descriptions, behaviors, combat tactics, and statistics necessary for gameplay. These entries are designed to be easily integrated into the existing binder system of the ''Monstrous Compendium'' series, allowing for a customizable and expansive monster reference library.

In addition to monster descriptions, the ''Monstrous Compendium Annual Volume Four'' provides lore and background information for each creature, offering insights into their habitats, societies, and roles within the AD&D world.
