The '''Astral Adventurer's Guide''' is a sourcebook included in the ''[[Spelljammer: Adventures in Space]]'' boxed set.

==Description==
The ''Astral Adventurer's Guide'' includes space-based character options, deck plans and descriptions for spelljamming ships, and more.

==Contents==
* Introduction: Vast Oceans of Adventure
* Chapter 1: Character Options
* Chapter 2: Astral Adventuring
* Chapter 3: The Rock of Bral
