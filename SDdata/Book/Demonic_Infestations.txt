'''''Demonic Infestations''''' was a book on [[demon]]ic possession.

==Description==
The book was a collection of several stories on its subject matter. It was written in [[Common]], and did not have much monetary value.

==History==
As of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], a copy could be found in the guest quarters of Vlonwelv's base in the [[Muiral's Gauntlet]] section of [[Undermountain]].
