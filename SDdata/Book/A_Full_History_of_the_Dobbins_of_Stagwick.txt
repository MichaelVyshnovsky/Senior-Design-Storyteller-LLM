'''''A Full History of the Dobbins of Stagwick''''' was a book written by [[Neville Dobbin]], the 35{{th}} Earl of [[Stagwick]]. The book was stolen by the [[verbeeg]] [[runecaster]] [[Basil (verbeeg)|Basil]] of [[Lyndusfarne]] from [[Dobbin Manor]], the home of Earl [[Ruther Dobbin]], descendant of Neville Dobbin.
