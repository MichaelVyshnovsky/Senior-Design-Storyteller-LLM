'''''Tymora's Melody''''' was a [[bard]]ic song, imbued with blessings of the Lady Luck.

==Content==
When performed by a bard, gifted with the use of ''[[the Art]]'', the song magically gave the singer's allies luck. The luck improved their chances of resisting malevolent [[spell]]s, avoiding danger, and significantly improved [[rogue]]ish skills.

==History==
By the {{YearlinkName|1281}}, ''Tymora's Melody'' was performed by some skilled bards. It was heard being performed in the [[Ten-Towns]] of [[Icewind Dale]] and the village of [[Kuldahar]].
