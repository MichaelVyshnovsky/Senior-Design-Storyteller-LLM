'''''Eye on the Realms''''' was a column by [[Ed Greenwood]] published in both ''[[Dungeon magazine]]'' and ''[[Dragon (magazine)|Dragon magazine]]''. The series debuted in November 2009 ([[Dungeon magazine 172|''Dungeon'' #172]]) and ended in December 2013 ([[Dragon magazine 430|''Dragon'' #430]]).

The focus on the column was on various groups, locations, items and individuals of the Realms.
