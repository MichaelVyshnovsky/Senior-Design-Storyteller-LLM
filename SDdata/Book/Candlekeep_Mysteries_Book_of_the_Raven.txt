'''"Book of the Raven"''' is a short adventure module for 3{{rd}}&ndash;level players, collected in the anthology ''[[Candlekeep Mysteries]]'', where players explore an abandoned chalet inhabited by [[wereraven]]s.
{{TOC limit|2}}
==Synopsis==
In [[1492 DR]], a group of [[adventurer]]s discover a treasure map inside a forgotten and neglected tome in [[Candlekeep]]. Following the map, they are led to [[Chalet Brantifax]], an abandoned castle haunted by some of its former residents and that contains a gateway to the [[Shadowfell]]. This gateway is watched over by a group of kindly wereravens who also make their home at the chalet.
