'''''Modern Alchemical Fertilizer Techniques''''' was a [[book]] on the topic of [[alchemy]] and its use in agriculture, written and published sometime before the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
A copy of ''Modern Alchemical Fertilizer Techniques'' could be found in the library of the dilapidated [[Wolover's Keep]] in the [[Veilstone Peaks]]. The fortification's past master was a mage committed to agriculture, whose library was filled with works on the similar topics in the mid-14{{th}} century DR.
