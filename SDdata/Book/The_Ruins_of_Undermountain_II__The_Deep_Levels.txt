The Ruins of Undermountain II The Deep Levels is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons boxed set for the Forgotten Realms.
The Ruins of Undermountain II The Deep Levels is the sequel to The Ruins of Undermountain. It is the second supplement about the infamous über-dungeon, Undermountain, and is followed by three adventure modules and another sourcebook.
The boxed set includes several booklets that provide detailed descriptions of the deep levels of Undermountain. They cover various new areas, including never-before-seen dungeons, traps, and enemy encounters. Detailed maps are included, which expand upon the geographical scope presented in the first set. These maps help Dungeon Masters navigate and plan sessions that explore these lower and more perilous levels.
The boxed set provides numerous new adventure hooks and quests. These are designed to integrate smoothly with the lore and challenges established in the earlier parts of Undermountain. An assortment of new monsters and villains specific to the deep levels are introduced, complete with stat blocks and lore, enabling DMs to enhance combat and storytelling.
