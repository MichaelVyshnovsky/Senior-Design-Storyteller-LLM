"Gorlists Dragon" is a short story by Elaine Cunningham included in the 2004 anthology Realms of the Dragons, featuring characters from the Starlight & Shadows series.
Gorlist seeks to live up the legacy of his mother, the famous gladiator Chindra. The arenas pit master, Slithifar, schemes to make him lose a fight versus a dragon, thereby allowing her to take him into her bed and brand him as her own.
