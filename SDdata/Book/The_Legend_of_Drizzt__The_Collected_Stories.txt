'''''The Legend of Drizzt: The Collected Stories''''' is an audiobook of short stories written by [[R. A. Salvatore]], read by a number of notable actors and musicians.
{{quote|''Cue the chaos! We're celebrating 40 years of [[elf|elves]], [[drow]], [[halflings]] and witch-hunters with an anthology of [[Dungeons & Dragons]] short stories by [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_Times New York Times] best-selling author and master storyteller, [[R.A. Salvatore]]. '''The Legend of Drizzt: The Collected Stories''' showcases just why the titular hero [[Drizzt Do'Urden]] has become a beloved character in the D&D world—and with a dungeon master’s dream line-up of narrators, listeners can travel to the [[Forgotten Realms]] for FREE—but only for a limited time!''}}
{{quote|''Experience the world of Dungeons & Dragons as never before with stories from The Legend of Drizzt. Performed by an eclectic cast, including stars from [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harry_Potter Harry Potter], [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Lord_of_the_Rings The Lord of the Rings], [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/X-Files The X-Files], [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Big_Bang_Theory The Big Bang Theory], [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Trek:_The_Next_Generation Star Trek: The Next Generation] and more!}}
