The Legend of Drizzt The Collected Stories is an audiobook of short stories written by R. A. Salvatore, read by a number of notable actors and musicians.
All stories appearing in the audiobook were previously published in other anthologies.
* "Wickless in the Nether" read by [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sean_Astin Sean Astin]
* "Bones and Stones" read by [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al_Yankovic Al Yankovic]
* "Comrades at Odds" read by [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ice-T Ice-T]
* "To Legend He Goes" read by [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Duchovny David Duchovny]
* "If Ever They Happened Upon My Lair" read by [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wil_Wheaton Wil Wheaton]
* "Dowery" read by [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Melissa_Rauch Melissa Rauch]
* "Dark Mirror" read by [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dan_Harmon Dan Harmon]
* "The First Notch" read by [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Felicia_Day Felicia Day]
* "The Third Level" read by [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greg_Grunberg Greg Grunberg]
* "Guenhwyvar (short story)" read by  [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tom_Felton Tom Felton]
* "Iruladoon" read by  [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Chiklis Michael Chiklis]
* "That Curious Sword" read by  [http//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Danny_Pudi Danny Pudi]
