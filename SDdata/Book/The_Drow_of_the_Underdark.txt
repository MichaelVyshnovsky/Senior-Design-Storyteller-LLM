'''''The Drow of the Underdark''''' is a 2{{nd}}-edition ''Advanced Dungeons & Dragons'' accessory for the ''[[Forgotten Realms]]''.

{{quote|''They are the favorite foes of many a party of adventurers. In these pages, the [[drow]] come to life, their culture and powers detailed fully for the first time. This guide presents new rules galore: new spells, new magical items, insight into the mysteries of [[Lolth]], ''[[Monstrous Compendium]]'' entries for related monsters of the [[Underdark]]&mdash;and much, much more.''

''Learn all about the drow herein, from the words of the ancient elven sage [[Erlathan Greycloak]]:''

''"Proud, graceful evil. Cruelty personified; talent wasted, beauty lost in darkness."''

''to the lore of the great sage [[Elminster Aumar|Elminster]]:''

''"Did I ever tell thee how close I came to having a drow wife? No? Well, then...''}}

==Description==
''The Drow of the Underdark'' is the 2{{nd}} book in the [[FOR series]] and provides detailed information on drow society, their unique magic, iconic deities, and their interactions with other races in the Underdark.

The book begins with an introduction to the drow, providing essential background information on their origins, physical traits, and alignment tendencies. It also touches on the different drow subraces, such as the noble-born and the surface-dwelling drow.

It explores the intricate social hierarchy of the drow, including their matriarchal society, noble houses, and the power struggles that define their existence. It explores the roles of males and females, the concept of Matron Mothers, and the significance of Lolth, the Spider Queen, as their primary deity.

Menzoberranzan, one of the most famous drow cities, is thoroughly detailed in this sourcebook. It includes maps, descriptions of key locations, and information on the city's politics, intrigue, and powerful noble houses.

''The Drow of the Underdark'' provides insights into drow magic, their proficiency with [[drowcraft]] items, and their spellcasting abilities. It also introduces new spells and magical items associated with the drow.

Itp rovides information on drow weapons, armor, and equipment, including their signature weapons.

The sourcebook includes numerous adventure hooks and plot ideas for DMs to use when incorporating the drow into their campaigns, providing a wealth of inspiration for storytelling.

The appendices contain additional resources, such as drow-specific spells and magical items, as well as a glossary of [[Drow dictionary|drow terminology]].
