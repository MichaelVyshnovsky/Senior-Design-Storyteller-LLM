'''''Rogues Gallery''''' was a column in [[Dragon (magazine)|''Dragon'' magazine]] by various authors. It was published from January 1996 ([[Dragon magazine 225|Dragon #225]]) to August 2004 ([[Dragon magazine 322|Dragon #322]]). 

The column explored the characters of ''Dungeons and Dragons''. It had entries on heroes, villains and NPC's from various novels, comics, computer games and adventure modules.
