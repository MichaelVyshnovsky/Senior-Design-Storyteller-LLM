'''''The Sentinel''''' by [[Troy Denning]] is the fifth book of [[The Sundering (series)]].

{{quote |''Embittered paladin [[Kleef Kenric]] is on a quest to stop evil forces from taking advantage of the chaos rolling across the land of [[Faerûn]] and claiming dominion over the entirety of the [[Forgotten Realms|Realms]].''

''Stubbornly clinging to his family's worship of a long-forgotten god, Kleef Kenric soon discovers that his god has blessed him with divine gifts, making him one of a new group of [[Chosen]] cropping up around the Realms. This divine gift makes him an excellent ally—and a target for those who wish corral his powers.''

''After battling his way out [[Marsember]], a city besieged on all sides in the wake of [[Second Sundering|the Sundering]], he becomes swept up in the mission of a group of odd allies—a warrior noblewoman, an accomplished thief, and a mysterious short pudgy man exuding a faint odor of decay. With the forces of [[Thultanthar|Shade]] tracking their every step, they travel to the [[Underdark]] to thwart the rise of the goddess of Death, but before long Kleef learns that his allies hide dangerous secrets—secrets that could destroy not only Kleef but the very fabric of the Forgotten Realms.''|The Sentinel}}

==Summary==
On [[Uktar 2]] in the {{yearlinkName|1486}}, the port city of Marsember was evacuated as the returned empire of [[Netheril]] invaded the kingdom of [[Cormyr]]. Topsword Kleef Kenric and his members of the [[Marsember Watch]] aided in these efforts for some time, until they encountered the [[Prince of Shade]] [[Yder Tanthul]] who was in rapid pursuit of two of the gods' [[chosen]], [[Malik el Sami yn Nasser]], Seraph of Lies and devotee of [[Cyric]]; and [[Joelle Emmeline]], a [[Berdusk]]an jewel [[thief]] and [[Heartwarder]] of [[Sune]]. With the timely aid of the young noblewoman, and skilled archer [[Arietta Seasilver]], Kleef was able to fight off many [[shade]]s and protect the two chosen, learning of their quest to return the ''[[Eye of Gruumsh (relic)|Eye of Gruumsh]]'' to the temple of [[Grumbar]] in the [[Underchasm]] before the end of the [[Second Sundering]].

After Yder Tanthul was turned away the company, the Prince of Shade commandeered the ''[[Wave Wyvern]]'', the [[Seasilver]] family vessel that held Arietta's parents, Lord [[Farnig Seasilver|Farnig]] and [[Elira Seasilver]]. Yder killed Lord Farnig and sailed off with Lady Elira as bait to draw out Arietta, Kleef, and the two chosen they took under their protection. The group enlisted the aid of [[Falrinn Greatorm]] and the ''[[Lonely Roamer]]'', sailing out across the [[Dragonmere]] and the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]]. They set a trap for the ''Wyvern'' near a partially submerged [[earthmote]], managing to rescue Lady Elira, setting her on her way to [[Westgate]] before continuing their journey southeast to [[Turmish]]. Yder Tanthul began to telepathically appear in Kleef's dreams, gleaning information about their location and activities.

Once they reached land the party and Greatorm parted ways, the former going to [[Xorhun]] and joining up with a group of caravans led by [[Faroz]]. The caravans were regularly harried by bands of [[orc]]s from the [[Stonelands]] that also sought after the ''Eye of Gruumsh''. While Kleef did all in his power to remain awake, in order to prevent Yder from learning more during his slumber, he eventually succumbed to fatigue. After crossing the [[River Arrabar]], the party made their way to the [[Chondalwood]]. There they enlisted aid from several [[treant]]s and a [[satyr]] called [[Theamont]]. Using the [[Forest Way]], they were able to put some distance between themselves, the orcs and the shades.

Using the time they bought for themselves, the group built a small raft to continue their journey downriver, eventually arriving at the Underchasm. They crossed several magically-supported bridges connecting numerous earthmotes before arriving at [[Sadrach's Spire]]. After turning away the orc leaders and rebuffing an ambush led by the shades, the party hurried to the gates of the spire. There they encountered the young [[Myrkul]]ite [[Gingrid]], granddaughter of the ancient [[Sadrach]]. Both Kleef and Arietta discovered their status as chosen, to [[Helm]] and [[Siamorphe]] respectively. Kleef's dedication to ever-present vigilance allowed him to summon Helm's avatar to break down the gates of the spire, while Arietta's noble and commanding presence granted her power over the keep's defenses.

Finally within Grumbar's temple, the party managed to set forth the ''Eye of Gruumsh'' as an offering to the earth [[primordial]]. They defeated the Shadovar during a final ambush within the temple's innermost chamber, killing the Prince of Shade Yder Tanthul. Malik revealed his true status as the chosen of [[Cyric]], attempting to kill Arietta, while Joella sacrificed herself to save the life of the woman she had come to love. While Malik skulked away after the betrayal, he was caught and apprehended by Kleef and Arietta, just before they made their way back home to Cormyr.
