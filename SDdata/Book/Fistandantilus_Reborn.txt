Fistandantilus Reborn, by Douglas Niles, is the second novel in The Lost Gods trilogy. Unlike the other entries in the series, Fistandantilus Reborn takes place on the world of Krynn in the Dragonlance setting, but characters and events from the novel are carried over into the Forgotten Realms-set sequel Tymoras Luck.
