In the Cage A Guide to Sigil is a 2-edition Advanced Dungeons & Dragons accessory. It is part of the Planescape campaign setting.
The book provides an in-depth exploration of the city of Sigil. It details the various factions and organizations that operate within the city, as well as the history, culture, and geography of Sigil.
The book also includes information on the Sigilian dialect, which is spoken by many of the citys inhabitants, as well as rules for creating and playing characters native to Sigil. In addition, it introduces new spells, magic items, and monsters that are unique to the Planescape setting.
In the Cage A Guide to Sigil was published by TSR, Inc. in 1995 and was written by Monte Cook, who was also one of the lead designers of the Planescape campaign setting.
