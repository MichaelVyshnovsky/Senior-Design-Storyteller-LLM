The Faceless Lord was a book written by Iggwilv that described her personal experiences with Juiblex, the demon lord of oozes. It was a short volume.
It could be found in Iggwilvs personal library in the Palace of Hearts Desire.
While the The Faceless Lord was never published, it was later rewritten into the famous Demonomicon of Iggwilv.
