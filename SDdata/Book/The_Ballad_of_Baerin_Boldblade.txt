'''''The Ballad of Baerin Boldblade''''' was a ballad penned by [[Thormar Daern]], Minstrel of [[Amn]], in the early 15{{th}} century [[DR]].
{{quote|And when the gates were burst, and the foe rode in<br/>And did their murderous worst, to all my bleeding kin<br/>Leaving the fortunate ones dead; then, ladies and saers,<br/>Ah, then, fearing worse, I fled.|Excerpt from ''The Ballad of Baerin Boldblade''}}

==History==
''The Ballad of Baerin Boldblade'' was first performed in the {{YearlinkName|1421}}.
