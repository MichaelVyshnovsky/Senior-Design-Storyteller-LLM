'''The blush''' was a plague that afflicted the [[Border Kingdoms]] during the {{YearlinkName|1374}}.

==History==
The disease appeared on [[Marpenoth 23]]. It first struck the village of [[Logfell]] north of the [[Qurth Forest]].
