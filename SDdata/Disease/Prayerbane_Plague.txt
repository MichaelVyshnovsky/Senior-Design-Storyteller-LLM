The '''Prayerbane Plague''' was a fatal [[disease]] that struck [[Impiltur]] in the 4{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|375}}, the wife and son of King [[Darthorn Mirandor III]] of Impiltur died tragically from the Prayerbane Plague. In grief, the king drowned himself that year.
