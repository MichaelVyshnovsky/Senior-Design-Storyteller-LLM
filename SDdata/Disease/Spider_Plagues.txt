The Spider Plagues referred to a devastating disease outbreak that struck ancient Calimshan in the .
Victims of the Spider Plagues developed sores on their bodies which were filled with spider eggs. When these eggs hatched, they consumed the infected individual, killing them.
The Plagues impacted all cities in Calimshan and its territories in the surrounding region. The plague wiped out 32% of the general population, and 41% of Calimport.
