The '''Coast Plague''' was a fatal [[disease]] that killed thousands of people in [[Amn]] during the {{YearlinkName|1345}}.

==History==
The plague spread via [[caravan]]s. It first appeared in [[Athkatla]], [[Crimmor]], [[Murann]], and [[Purskul]], where it was estimated to have killed 20% of the people it infected. It ultimately spread as far as [[Beregost]] in the north and [[Zazesspur]] in the south.
