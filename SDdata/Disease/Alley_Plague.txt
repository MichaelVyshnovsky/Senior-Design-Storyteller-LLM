The Alley Plague was a disease outbreak that struck the Shoon Imperium in the .
The plague was spread through the air, especially in the vicinity of corpses of its victims.
The disease entailed pox and impaired the bloods ability to clot.
The Alley Plague ravaged Almraiven and Calimport as well as the docks of Manshaka. In most cities, casualties climbed to 17%, while in Calimport, casualties rose to 21%, owing to the accumulation of rotting corpses in the streets of Upper Calimport.
