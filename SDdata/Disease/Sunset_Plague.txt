The Sunset Plague was a fatal disease that nearly destroyed the city of Calimport in .
Almost everyone in Calimport was killed by the Sunset Plague after less than one tenday. This included more than half of the ruling Marekh family, leaving Daud yn Khadil el Marekh&mdasha ship captain&mdashto assume the throne.
Fear of the plague meant that the city lay abandoned for a century following the outbreak. With their capital lost, the power of the Marekh Dynasty was irrevocably broken, and it dissolved just twelve years later.
