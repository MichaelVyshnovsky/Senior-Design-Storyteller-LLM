The '''Purplethroat Plague''' was a fatal [[disease]] that struck [[Cormyr]] in the 4{{th}} century [[DR]]. 

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|303}}, King [[Gorann Obarskyr]] of Cormyr died of the Purplethroat Plague as part of the machinations of his sister, [[Gorauna Obarskyr]]. He was the first of several Cormyrean kings to be killed by her.

In the {{YearlinkName|334}}, King [[Belmuth Obarskyr|Belmuth "the Bastard" Obarskyr]] of Cormyr died of Purplethroat Plague.

In the {{YearlinkName|340}}, King [[Artreth Obarskyr]] of Cormyr died of Purplethroat Plague, followed by his younger brother and successor, King [[Zoumdan Obarskyr]]. Zoumdan's successor, King [[Imbrus Obarskyr II]], likewise died of the Purplethroat Plague the [[341 DR|next year]].
