Quirish was a tribal language of the Hordelands.
The grammar of Quirish had a subject&ndashobject&ndashverb order. Long I and E vowels were drawn out. Consonants, especially its Ks, were harsh. The language was nasal, and Ls were also aspirated.
Quirish did not have a written script.
Scholars classified Quirish both as an Eastern and as a Southern Imaskari language. It was strongly influenced by Muhjuri and by the Shou Chiang languages of Shou Lung.
