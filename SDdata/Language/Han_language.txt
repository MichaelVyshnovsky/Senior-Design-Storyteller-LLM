'''Han''' was a language spoken by the ancient [[Han (ethnicity)|Han]] people of [[Kara-Tur]].

==History==
Legend told that after the creation of the land of Kara-Tur, the gods themselves handed down the Han language to the Han people.

By the 14th century [[DR]], Han was studied and employed only by philosophers and sages of Kara-Tur.

==Related languages==
It was the root tongue for the [[Koryo language|Koryo]], [[Kozakuran language|Kozakuran]], and [[Wa-an language|Wa-an]] languages, and possibly others.
