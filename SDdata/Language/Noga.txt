'''Noga''' was the language of the ancient kingdom of [[Nog]] in the [[Cities of the Ancients|Ruined Kingdoms]].

==Description==
Compared to [[Midani]], Noga had a cadence that seemed strange to those not fluent in it.

==Speakers==
As late as [[1367 DR]],{{AQ-time}} Noga could still be heard amidst the bazaars of the mainland Ruined Kingdoms, though Midani was the official language by that time.{{Cite dragon annual/1999/Speaking in Tongues|29}}

==Related Languages==
Noga was in the [[Midani languages]] subgroup, being related to the Midani tongue spoken in [[Zakhara]]. This placed it also in the [[Untheric languages|Untheric]] group of [[Rauric languages|Rauric]] languages.
