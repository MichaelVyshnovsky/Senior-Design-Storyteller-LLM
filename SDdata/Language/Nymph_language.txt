The Nymph language was the racial language of nymphs, grain nymphs, and unseelie nymphs. It was noted for having a musical quality to it.
Beyond nymphs themselves, Nymph was a common secondary language among unicorns.
It was also fairly common as a secondary language among druids devoted to certain nature deities. druids of Eldath, and the druids of Silvanus. Other forms of specialty priests in the Realms for whom Nymph was a commonly known secondary language were the Eldathyn peacemen.
