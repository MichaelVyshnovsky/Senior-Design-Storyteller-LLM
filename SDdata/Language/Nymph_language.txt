The '''Nymph language''' was the racial language of [[nymph]]s, [[grain nymph]]s, and [[unseelie nymph]]s.<ref> It was noted for having a musical quality to it.{{Cite dragon/240/Ecology of The Nymph|74}}

==Speakers==
Beyond nymphs themselves, Nymph was a common secondary language among [[unicorn]]s.

It was also fairly common as a secondary language among [[druid]]s devoted to certain nature deities. [[Druid (Eldath)|druids]] of [[Eldath]], and the druids of [[Silvanus]]. Other forms of [[specialty priest]]s in the Realms for whom Nymph was a commonly known secondary language were the Eldathyn [[peacemen]].
