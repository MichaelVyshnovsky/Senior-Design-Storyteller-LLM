The term "'''Low Ulutim'''" described a grouping of closely related [[Ulou languages]]<ref name=D:SiT-p28>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|28}} spoken by small populations of people in various [[Faerûn]]ian locales.<ref name=D:SiT-p26>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|26}}

==Languages==
The Low Ulutim languages were spoken at lower latitudes than the [[High Ulutim languages]]. The following were Low Ulutim languages:<ref name=D:SiT-p28/>
* [[Rengardt]] (dead language)
* [[Narfelli language|Narfelli]] (dead language)
* [[Midani|Uloushinn]] (also considered a dialect of [[Midani language|Midani]], a member of the [[Rauric languages]] family<ref name=D:SiT-p29>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|29}})
* [[Andt]]
* [[Erakic]]
* [[Naric]]
