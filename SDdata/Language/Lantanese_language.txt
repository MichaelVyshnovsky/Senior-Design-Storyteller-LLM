'''Lantanese''', also known as '''Lantanna''',

==Speakers==
Other regions where Lantanese was commonly spoken included the [[Nelanther Isles]] and [[Tethyr]].<ref name=FRCS3e-pp31-32>

==Script==
Literate Lantanese speakers used the [[Iokharic|Draconic alphabet]].<ref name=FRCS3e-p85/> Scholars suspected that the [[Lantanna]] people learned of this language through trade with early [[Halruaa]]n or [[Nimbral]]i traders.

==Related Languages==
Some scholars classified Lantanna as an [[Imaskari languages|Imaskari language]],[[Thomas M. Costa|Tom Costa]] placed Lantanna in the [[Imaskari languages]] grouping in his "Speaking in Tongues" [[Dragon (magazine)|''Dragon'']] article based on the suggestions of [[Eric L. Boyd]] and others that the [[Lantanna]] people descended from the [[Imaskari]], but this has not been established as canon. See {{Cite web/Candlekeep forum|title=Rashemi & Jotun Languages ... Need Some Help.|author=[[Thomas M. Costa]]|topicid=20231|postid=470450|whichpage=1|date=2015-06-13|accessdate=2016-12-26}}

==Examples==
Common female names in Lantanese were Avilda, Bersace, Charissa, Melsany, Phaerilda, Ravace, and Umbrasy. Male names might be Eberc, Fodoric, Koger, Lambrac, Midoc, Norbert, Samber, or Tibidoc. Common Lantanese surnames included Angalstrand, Decirc, Lamstrand, SeKorc, SeLangstra, and SeMilderic.
