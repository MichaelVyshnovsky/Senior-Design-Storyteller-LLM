'''Mongrel''' was the pidgin language of [[mongrelfolk]], derived from the [[Common tongue]].<ref group="note" name="distinct">The ''[[Monstrous Manual]]'', ''[[The Complete Book of Humanoids]]'', and ''[[Curse of Strahd]]'' describe mongrelfolk as not having their own "distinct tongue", but merely speaking a "debased Common" or "fragmented Common". However,  ''[[Drizzt Do'Urden's Guide to the Underdark]]'' denotes them as having their own language.

==Phonology==
In addition to elements of Common, Mongrel speech was interspersed with animalistic noises and cries,{{Cite dragon/242/The Ecology of the Mongrelman|86}} as well as gestures. These included growls, whistles, grunts, and howls.
