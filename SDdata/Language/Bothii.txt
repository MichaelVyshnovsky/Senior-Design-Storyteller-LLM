'''Bothii''' was the language spoken by the [[Uthgardt]] barbarian tribes and the inhabitants of [[Hartsvale]].<ref name=D:SiT-p26>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|26}}

Bothii was treated by some as a dialect of [[Illuskan language|Illuskan]].<ref name=FRCS-p168> Bothii itself consisted of two dialects, one spoken by the Uthgardt, the other by the Hartsvalers.<ref name=D:SiT-p28>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|28}}
