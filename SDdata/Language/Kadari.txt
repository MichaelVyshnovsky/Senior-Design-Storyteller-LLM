Kadari was the language of the empire of Kadar, and the Necromancer Kings of Sahu. As of 1367 DR it was a dead language known by very few.
*Kitab al-Asfr, a spellbook of the infamous geomancer Balshareska in form of a papyrus scroll, containing divine and arcane magic
*The Art of Necromancy by necromancer and scholar Kazerabet contained a Kadari translation dictionary.
A few words in Kadari were conveyed in the form of names
KazerabetAngel of the Dark
SahuSerene Eternity (meaning Death)
SumulaelHarvester of Death
ZaribelAngelThe source does not make it entirely clear that this name is in Kadari, not Midani.
