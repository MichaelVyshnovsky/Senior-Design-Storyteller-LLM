'''Oigur''' was a language spoken in the [[Hordelands]].

==Description==
Oigur was a nasal language. Vowels pronounced close and toward the front of the mouth were drawn out. It also had harsh consonant sounds, especially its Ks, and the sound of L was aspirated. Its grammar had a subject&ndash;object&ndash;verb order.

==Speakers==
Oigur was spoken primarily by the nomadic [[Oigur]] tribe.

==Script==
Oigur did not have a written script.<ref name=D:SiT-p29>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|29}}

==History==
Scholars classified Oigur as one of both the [[Southern Imaskari languages|Southern]] and the [[Eastern Imaskari languages]].<ref name=D:SiT-p29 /> It was strongly influenced by the [[Shou Chiang languages]] of [[Shou Lung]] and the tongue of [[Muhjuri]].
