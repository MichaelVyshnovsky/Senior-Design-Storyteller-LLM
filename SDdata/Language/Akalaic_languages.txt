Akalaic was a relatively large and diverse subgroup of the Chessan languages, including one of the languages spoken by the Arkaiuns of Dambrath.
The Akalaic languages grouping included several living and several dead languages spoken in diverse regions of Faerûn
* Arkian (dead language)
* Eshowan (dead language)
* Telfir (dead language)
* Akalan
* Akûrian
* Shaaran
* Sespechian
