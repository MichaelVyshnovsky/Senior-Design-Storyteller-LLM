The '''Midani languages''' composed a sub-group of the [[Untheric languages]] group of [[Rauric languages]].<ref name=D:SiT-p29>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|29}}

==Languages==
This grouping included the dead languages of [[Noga]] and [[Kadari]], as well as [[Midani language|Midani]], the language of [[Zakhara]], and [[Maran]].<ref name=D:SiT-p29 />

==History==
The Midani languages branched off from the other Untheric languages by mixing with the [[Jannti]] languages of [[genies]].<ref name=D:SiT-p29 />
