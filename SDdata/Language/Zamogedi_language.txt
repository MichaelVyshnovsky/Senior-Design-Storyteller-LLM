Zamogedi was a tribal tongue of the Hordelands bordering Shou Lung.
Zamogedi was an nasal language, with long I and E vowels drawn out.
Zamogedi was spoken primarily by the nomadic Zamogedi tribe.
Zamogedi did not have a written script.
Scholars classified Zamogedi as an Eastern Imaskari language. It was strongly influenced by the Shou Chiang languages of Shou Lung.
