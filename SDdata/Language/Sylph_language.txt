The '''Sylph language''' was the racial language of [[sylph]]s, a type of [[fey]], that was noted for having a musical quality to it.

==Speakers==
Beyond sylphs themselves, Sylph was a common secondary language among some [[planewalker]]s<ref> and [[druid]]s devoted to certain nature deities. [[Druid (Eldath)|druids]] of [[Eldath]], [[Druid (Mielikki)|druids]] and druid/[[ranger]]s of [[Mielikki]], the druids of [[Silvanus]], and druids of [[Shiallia]]. Other forms of [[specialty priest]]s in the Realms for whom Sylph was a commonly known secondary language included the Eldathyn [[peacemen]] and the Shiallian [[woodwife|woodwives]].
