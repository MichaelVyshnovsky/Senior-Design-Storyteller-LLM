The Sylph language was the racial language of sylphs, a type of fey, that was noted for having a musical quality to it.
Beyond sylphs themselves, Sylph was a common secondary language among some planewalkers and druids devoted to certain nature deities. druids of Eldath, druids and druid/rangers of Mielikki, the druids of Silvanus, and druids of Shiallia. Other forms of specialty priests in the Realms for whom Sylph was a commonly known secondary language included the Eldathyn peacemen and the Shiallian woodwives.
