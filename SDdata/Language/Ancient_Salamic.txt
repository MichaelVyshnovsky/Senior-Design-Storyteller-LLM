'''Ancient Salamic''' a dead language used by some [[priest]]s in [[Zakhara]] before the time of [[Enlightenment]].

==Notable Speakers==
[[Haroushin]], a scholarly [[ethoist]] priest from [[Muluk]], was fluent in Ancient Salamic.
