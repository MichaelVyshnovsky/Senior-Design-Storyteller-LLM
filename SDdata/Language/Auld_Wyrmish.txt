'''Auld Wyrmish''',{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-02-23 - Relation of Arragrakh to Auld High Wyrmish}} being present during the [[Dawn Age]].{{Cite web/Candlekeep Forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2008)/10-27 - Old Draconic, Window Screens, and Masks of the Moonstone Mask}}<ref group="note" name="age">This age can be inferred by [[Ed Greenwood]] naming "the [[giant]] races, or the [[titan]]s" as the contemporary races that would have possibly utilized the Old Draconic script. It was the precursor to the more widely spoken [[Aragrakh]] and the [[Draconic language|Draconic]] spoken in the [[Present Age]].{{Cite web/theedverse/2019-02-24 - Draconic Language Tree Theory Confirmed & Draconic names for Draconic}}

==Derivations==
Over time Auld Wyrmish developed and changed into having distinctly formal and casual (or "low") versions of the language. These versions were referred to by sages of other races as "Auld High Wyrmish" or "Old High Wyrmish"<ref>{{Cite web/Candlekeep Forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2008)/10-27 - What is Old Draconic}} and "Wyrmish" respectively. The former was utilized in early [[:Category:Draconic spells|draconic spells]].
