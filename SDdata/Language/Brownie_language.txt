The '''Brownie language''' was the racial language of [[brownie]]s, a type of [[fey]].

==Speakers==
Beyond brownies themselves, Brownie was a common secondary language in [[Faer√ªn]] among [[forest gnome]]s, [[lightfoot halfling]]s, [[rock gnome]]s, and [[tallfellow halfling]]s.<ref> Beyond [[demihuman]]s, Brownie was also commonly spoken by other [[fey]] creatures, such as [[dobie]]s,{{Cite dragon/206/The Dragon's Bestiary: The good, the bad and the clumsy|73}} [[grig]]s,<ref> [[killmoulis]], and [[stwinger]]s. And [[elven cat]]s who attached themselves to brownie families typically spoke Brownie to a rudimentary degree.

Brownie was also common as a secondary language among [[druid]]s devoted to certain nature deities.
