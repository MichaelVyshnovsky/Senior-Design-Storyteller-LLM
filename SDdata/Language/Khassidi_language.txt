'''Khassidi''' was a language in the [[Imaskari languages]] group,<ref name=D:SiT-p29>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|29}} spoken in the western [[Hordelands]].

==Description==
Khassidi, like other languages in the same region, had round and broad vowel sounds with rolled R sounds.

==Speakers==
This tongue was spoken by the nomadic [[Khassidi]] tribe.

==Script==
Khassidi did not have a written form.<ref name=D:SiT-p29 />

==History==
It was considered to be one of the [[Northern Imaskari languages]]<ref name=D:SiT-p29 /> and was strongly influenced by the empire of [[Raumathar]].
