'''Dalat''' was a language of the [[Hordelands]].

==Description==
Dalat had harsh consonant sounds, especially its Ks, and the sound of L was aspirated. Its grammar had a subject&ndash;object&ndash;verb order.

==Speakers==
Dalat was spoken primarily by the nomadic [[Dalat]] tribe.

==Script==
Dalat did not have a written script.<ref name=D:SiT-p29>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|29}}

==History==
Scholars classified Dalat as one of the [[Southern Imaskari languages]].<ref name=D:SiT-p29 /> It was strongly influenced by the [[Muhjuri]] language.
