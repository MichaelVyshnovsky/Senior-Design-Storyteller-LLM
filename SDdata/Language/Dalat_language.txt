Dalat was a language of the Hordelands.
Dalat had harsh consonant sounds, especially its Ks, and the sound of L was aspirated. Its grammar had a subject&ndashobject&ndashverb order.
Dalat was spoken primarily by the nomadic Dalat tribe.
Dalat did not have a written script.
Scholars classified Dalat as one of the Southern Imaskari languages. It was strongly influenced by the Muhjuri language.
