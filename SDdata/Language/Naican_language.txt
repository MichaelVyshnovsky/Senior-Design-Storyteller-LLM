'''Naican''' was a language in the [[Imaskari languages]] group,<ref name=D:SiT-p29>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|29}} spoken in the [[Hordelands]].

==Description==
Naican had round and broad vowel sounds with rolled R sounds. Many of its consonant sounds were harsh, such as C and K. The sound of L was heavily aspirated. Its grammar had a subject&ndash;object&ndash;verb order.

==Speakers==
This tongue was spoken by the nomadic [[Naican]] tribe.

==Script==
Naican did not have a written form.<ref name=D:SiT-p29 />

==History==
It was a hybrid language derived from both the [[Northern Imaskari languages|Northern]] and [[Southern Imaskari languages|Southern]] Imaskari language groups.<ref name=D:SiT-p29 /> It was influenced by the empire of [[Raumathar]] and also the language of [[Muhjuri]].
