The '''Chessan language group''' was a part of the [[Faerûnian languages|Faerûnian language family]] and included the [[Chessentan language|language]] of [[Chessenta]].<ref name=D:SiT-p28-29>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|28-29}}

==Subgroups==
The Chessan group contained two diverse subgroups,<ref name=D:SiT-p28-29/> the [[Chessentic languages|Chessentic]] group&mdash;which included languages spoken around the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]] near Chessenta&mdash;and [[Akalaic]]&mdash;including languages spoken in the [[Shaar]], the [[Shining South]], and the [[Chultan Peninsula]].<ref name=D:SiT-p26>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|26}}
