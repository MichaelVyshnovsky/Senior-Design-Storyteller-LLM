The Dolphin language was the language spoken by dolphins, a highly intelligent aquatic species of mammal that inhabited the oceans of Toril.
Beyond dolphins themselves, the Dolphin was commonly known in the oceans of Zakhara by the pahari. It was also a common secondary language among aquarians and sea druids, two types of specialty priests in the Church of Deep Sashelas. Though of the two, it was the aquarians who specialized in the language.

Serusan, a pidgin language used by the various aquatic races of Serôs, featured elements of both the dolphin language and aquatic elvish. The latter was not only noted for being somewhat akin to dolphin language, but many aquatic elvish poems and songs were said to mimic it in sound.
* Ashakar Piscelene, a triton wizard, was capable of speaking this language.
* Oelyvia Reefglider, a sea elven woman who worked as a barmaid at Bindles Blade, had an uncanny ability to sing in Dolphin and would occasionally entertain guests with her lyrical abilities.
* Some of the descendants of Selana, a prophet among the merfolk of Serôs, were magically capable of understanding and speaking the Dolphin language.
