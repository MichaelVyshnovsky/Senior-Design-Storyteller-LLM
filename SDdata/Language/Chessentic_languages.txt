The '''Chessentic languages''' composed a branch of the [[Chessan languages]] that included the modern language [[Chessentan language|Chessentan]].<ref name=D:SiT-p28>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|28}}

==Languages==
The Chessentic languages were those Chessan languages spoken around the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]] near [[Chessenta]].<ref name=D:SiT-p26>{{Cite dragon/Speaking in Tongues|26}} The group included two dead languages and two living languages:<ref name=D:SiT-p28/>
* [[Auld Chessic]] (also known as "Alambic", dead language)
* Thresk (dead language)<ref group="note">Though never expressed explicitly, Thresk was probably the language once spoken in [[Threskel]].
* [[Chessentan language|Chessentan]] (also known as "Chessic")
* [[Alambit]]
