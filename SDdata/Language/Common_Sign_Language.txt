'''Common Sign Language''' was a system of [[sign language]] used by [[deaf]] and mute people and those who learned it to communicate with them.{{Cite adventurers league/When the Lights Went Out in Candlekeep|27}}

==Description==
Common Sign Language shared signs and vocabulary with [[thieves' cant]], enough that those who knew thieves' cant could discern the sign language.

==Users==
Two members of the [[Avowed]] in [[Candlekeep]] communicated in Common Sign Language in the late 1400s [[DR]].
