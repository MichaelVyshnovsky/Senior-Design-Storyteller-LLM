'''Outland priests''' were [[cleric]]s, priests, and [[druid]]s who came to [[Zakhara]] from distant lands to practice their beliefs.<ref group="note">Outland priest was a [[cleric]] class kit for the [[Al-Qadim]] setting.

==Requirements==
Members of both sexes and all races could be considered an outland priest, though no [[Zakharan (ethnicity)|Zakharan]] could ever be considered an outland priest.

==Role==
Though most Zakharan religions preached tolerance, they carefully monitored outland priests and their strange practices and gods.

==Possessions==
Outland priests carried whatever equipment and gear was acceptable for their chosen god or goddess.

==Benefits==
Outland priests had access to clerical magic that many, if not all, Zakharan priests were unfamiliar with.

==Hindrances==
Distrust followed an outland priest through the Land of Fate, especially when dealing with other native priests of the [[Zakharan pantheon|Enlightened gods]].
