'''Yakuza''' were roguish, territorial criminals who worked in the underworld and collected payment in exchange for protection. In [[Shou Lung]], they were called '''tong shu'''. On the [[Rock of Bral]], they were founded by Underbaron [[Ozamata Ku Murawa]].
