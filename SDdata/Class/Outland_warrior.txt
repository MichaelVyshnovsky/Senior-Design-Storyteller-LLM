Outland warriors were special warriors found in Zakhara who traveled there from some other location.Outland Warrior was a fighter class kit for the Al-Qadim setting.
These warriors had no special requirements.
Outland warriors served a wide variety of roles in the Land of Fate. Most native Zakharans viewed them with a healthy does of curiosity and/or fear.
Outland warriors typically carried armor and weaponry foreign to Zakhara.
These warriors did not have any special benefits.
Outland warriors stood out in Zakhara like a sore thumb. Their accents were thick, their armor was usually heavy, and their swords were curiously straight. They attracted attention wherever they roamed.
