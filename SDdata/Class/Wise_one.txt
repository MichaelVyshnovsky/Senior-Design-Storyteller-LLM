'''Wise ones''' were specialty priests devoted to [[Curna]], which was how [[Oghma]] was known in the lands of [[Estagund]], [[Durpar]], and [[Var the Golden]].<ref name=FaA-p134">

==Abilities==
These individuals possessed a knack for identifying a [[magic item]]'s general function. This skill grew as the wise one aged. Wise ones excelled at casting any [[divination sphere]] spells, doing so at a higher skill level than their rank portrayed.<ref name=FaA-p134"/>

Wise ones were granted several powers as they progressed including ''[[comprehend languages]], [[friends]], [[zone of truth]], [[hold person]]'', and ''[[blind|blinding flash]]''. Each of these abilities could be used once per day. Additionally, wise ones gained the permanent benefits of ''[[free action]]''.<ref name=FaA-p134"/>

==Possessions==
Wise ones wielded blunt weapons. The typically carried a [[shield]] and wore non-metallic [[armor]].<ref name=FaA-p134"/>
