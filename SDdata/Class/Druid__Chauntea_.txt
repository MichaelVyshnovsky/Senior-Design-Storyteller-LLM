'''Druids''' were [[specialty priest]]s devoted to [[Chauntea]], the Great Mother.

==Culture==
Druids were both male and female and could be any race.

==Abilities==
These speciality priests possessed all of the skills and abilites of common [[druid]]s. They were usually fluent in several sylvan languages.

==Possessions==
Chantean druids wielded a variety of weaponry including [[sickle]]s, [[club]]s, [[spear]]s, [[dart]]s, [[dagger]]s, [[scimitar]]s, [[sling]]s, and [[staves]]. They wore non-metallic [[armor]]s such as [[padded armor]], [[leather armor]], and armor made from animal products such as [[hide armor]].
