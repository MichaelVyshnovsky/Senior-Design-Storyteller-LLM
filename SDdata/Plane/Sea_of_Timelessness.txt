The '''Sea of Timelessness''' was a [[divine realm]] within the [[Elemental Plane of Water]] belonging to [[Istishia]], the [[power]] of [[elemental water]].

==Cosmography==
This realm existed within the [[Great Wheel cosmology|Great Wheel]] cosmological model.

==Rumors & Legends==
In the [[Waveriding]] ceremony performed by Istishia's clergy, [[water elemental]]s were said to bring the gifts bestowed upon them back to this realm.
