The Inn of the Crooked Staff was an inn located in the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands.
The inn was located in the northcentral section of the city near the coast of the Lake of Mists. It sat directly across the street from the Raumkreml which was the seat of government in Almorel during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The inn was owned by a Raumviran named A. Grekov.
