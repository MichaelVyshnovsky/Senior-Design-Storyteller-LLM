Arkhens InvocatoriumVolos Guide to the North and The Savage Frontier both use the spelling "Arkens Invocatorium". Since Silver Marches is a more recent publication, the spelling therein of "Arkhens" takes precedence. was a college of magic in Silverymoon that specialized in sorcery. The school was run by Arkhen the Icy, and was part of the Conclave of Silverymoon. The Invocatorium was once situated in a stone fortress on the east side of Silverymoon, but later moved into an entirely new building, filled with animated constructs.
