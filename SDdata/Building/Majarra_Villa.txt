'''Majarra Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Majarra]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the eastern section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] on the south side of [[Sulmor Street]] where it was intersected by [[Ussilbran Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The walled compound contained three buildings arranged around a central courtyard and garden. The buildings of Majarra Villa were three or four storys tall.
