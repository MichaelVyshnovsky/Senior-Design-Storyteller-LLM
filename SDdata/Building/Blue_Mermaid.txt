The '''Blue Mermaid''' was a tavern in the [[Dock Ward of Waterdeep|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was owned by Mother [[Jalyth Hlommorath]] in the mid-1360s DR.

==Location==
This tavern stood along [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]], between [[Keel Alley]] and [[Oar Alley]].<ref>

==Atmosphere==
[[Volo]] described it as a perfectly safe place with perfectly safe patrons, and accordingly he called it "boring".

==Description==
The tavern was well appointed and clean, if a bit tired.

==Services==
It was known for good [[ale]] and bad [[wine]].

==Features==
From a dry well in the cellar, a passage known as the Blue Mermaid Passage led down into [[Skullport]].
