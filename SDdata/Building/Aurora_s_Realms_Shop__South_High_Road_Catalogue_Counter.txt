Auroras Realms Shop, South High Road Catalogue Counter was an outlet of the Auroras Emporium retail chain in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep in the mid–14 century DR. It was one of six such outlets in the city.
It was located along the Way of the Dragon, next to the Red Gauntlet tavern.
This shop sold the various items found in Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
It also sold a variety magic items, with expected delivery times of 10–30 days.
In the mid-1360s DR, Mril Juthbuck worked here as a counter clerk and Logros Hlandarr as a service-mage. In addition, there were four guards who worked shifts of two and two.
