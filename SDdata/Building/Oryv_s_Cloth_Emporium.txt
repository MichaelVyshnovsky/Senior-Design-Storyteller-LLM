'''Oryv's Cloth Emporium''' was a textile shop located in the town of [[Daggerford]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
It was located in Daggerford's [[Caravan Quarter]]{{Cite interactive atlas|3990 Daggerford}}

==Services==
The shop's owner [[Oryv]] specialized in selling fabrics that had been brought into town from other regions. He often arranged for mercantile expeditions to travel across the realms, searching for deals that allowed him to maximize his profits.
