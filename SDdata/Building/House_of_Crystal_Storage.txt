'''House of Crystal Storage''' was a warehouse in [[Waterdeep]] that was owned by the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Guild of Glassblowers, Glaziers, and Spectacle-Makers]].

==Location==
This warehouse was located in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of Waterdeep, close to the [[House of Crystal]].

==Structure==
This warehouse was four-stories tall.
