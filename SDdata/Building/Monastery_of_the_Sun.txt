The Monastery of the Sun was a monastery dedicated to Amaunator located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The temple stood four storys tall.
The Monastery of the Sun was located in Waterdeeps Trades Ward in a neighborhood bordered by the High Road to the west, Lamp Street to the south, and Andamaars Street to the north and east.
