'''Nalasso's Scrollworks''' was a shop in the city of [[Suzail]] in [[Cormyr]] that specialized in [[scroll]]s and scroll-related paraphernalia.

==Location==
This shop was located along [[Torch Street]].

==Services==
Nalasso's often purchased large hollow bones from the business [[Sundarl Nightsoils]]. With these bones the proprietors would fashion fine [[scroll tube]]s, chased with [[silver]] mounts and caps.
