The '''Mocking Maiden''' was an [[inn]] and [[tavern]] in the city of [[Calaunt]] in [[the Vast]], operating around [[1358 DR]].

It was a merry and busy place, but of excellent quality for only moderate prices. The staff focused on good food and on keeping [[adventurer|adventuring]] patrons happy.
