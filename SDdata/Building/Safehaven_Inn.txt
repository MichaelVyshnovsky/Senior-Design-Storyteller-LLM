The Safehaven Inn was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The two buildings of the inn stood three stories tall.
The Safehaven Inn was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter. The buildings sat on the eaastern side of the Adventurers Quarter, with parts of the two buildings touching Slop Street, Snake Alley, and Grocers Lane
