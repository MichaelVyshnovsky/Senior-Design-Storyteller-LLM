The '''Safehaven Inn''' was an [[inn]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The two buildings of the inn stood three stories tall.{{Cite interactive atlas|3530 Safehaven Inn and Beer Golem Tavern}}

==Location==
The Safehaven Inn was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]]. The buildings sat on the eaastern side of the Adventurers' Quarter, with parts of the two buildings touching [[Slop Street]], [[Snake Alley]], and [[Grocer's Lane]]{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}
