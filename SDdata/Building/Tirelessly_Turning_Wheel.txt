The '''Tirelessly Turning Wheel''' was a shop in the city of [[Waterdeep]] in [[1370 DR]] that sold various curios from all across [[Faer√ªn]].

==Location==
This shop was located along [[Julthoon Street]], in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Structure==
The building housing this shop stood three stories tall.

==Services==
This shop sold a variety of different curios, some of them quite fragile and irreplaceable.

==Inhabitants==
This business was owned by the [[elf|elven]] couple [[Jhanaethe Mrhulaedir|Jhanathe]] and [[Ormsiir Mrhulaedir]]. They lived in the business with their son, [[Oenel Mrhulaedir]], who sometimes accidentally broke curios with his stocky build's passing.
