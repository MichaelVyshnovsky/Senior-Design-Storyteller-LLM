'''The Old Archer's Eye''' was a trading collective in [[Arrowmark]] that sold [[arrow]]s and [[bow]]s.

==Description==
The shop was a trading collective established by the best bowyers and fletchers in the area. It was a popular shop, with dozens of daily buyers, who were met with the shops distinct and pleasant scent of [[cedar]] combined with the smell of various [[oil]]s and [[varnish]]es. The work of each bower and fletcher was clearly marked and placed in its own case of glass. 

Due to the high quality of craftsmanship, the shop was expensive. The cost of a bow usually began at 125 [[gp]] and a single arrow 1 [[sp]]. Custom bows could be ordered but took longer to manufacture and were much more expensive, with costs beginning at 200 gp.
