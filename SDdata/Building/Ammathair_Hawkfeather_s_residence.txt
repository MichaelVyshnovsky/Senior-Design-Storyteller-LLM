'''Ammathair Hawkfeather's residence''' was a row house and the home of the [[sage]] [[Ammathair Hawkfeather]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The home stood two stories tall.

==Location==
Ammathair Hawkfeather's residence was located in the west section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on the western side of [[Swords Street]] just south of [[Keltarn Street]].
