'''Larissa Neathal's residence''' was a row house and the home of the courtesan [[Larissa Neathal]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The home stood two stories tall.

==Location==
Larissa Neathal's residence was located in the west section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on [[Mulgomir's Way]] and [[Lemontree Alley]].
