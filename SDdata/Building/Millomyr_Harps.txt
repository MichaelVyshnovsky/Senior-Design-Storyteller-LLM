'''Millomyr Harps''' was an instrument shop specializing in [[harp]]s in the city of [[Waterdeep]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
This shop stood on the eastern side of [[Golden Serpent Street]], in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Structure==
This building stood two stories tall.

==Structure==
===Interior===
Inside the building was a secret room that acted as the exit point to one of the ''[[teleport]]ers'' in [[Harpers' Hold]].

==Activities==
Owned by a member of the [[Harpers]], Millomyr Harps acted as a safe-house for that organization in addition to operating as a shop.
