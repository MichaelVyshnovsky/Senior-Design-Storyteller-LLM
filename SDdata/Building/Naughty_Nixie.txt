The '''Naughty Nixie''' was an [[inn]] and [[tavern]] found in [[Yhaunn]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Services==
The Naughty Nixie was one of the newer establishments in Yhaunn as of [[1358 DR]]. The [[adventurer]]s and caravan masters who favored the Naughty Nixie could expect excellent accommodations and moderate prices, especially when compared to the exorbitant [[Firedrinkers' Inn]].
