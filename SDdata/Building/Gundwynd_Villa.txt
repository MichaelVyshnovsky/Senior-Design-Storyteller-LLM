'''Gundwynd Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Gundwynd]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] on the northheast corner of where [[Mendever Street]] intersected [[Vondil Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The four buildings within the walled estate were arranged around a central garden with a building sitting in each corner. Each building was two or three stories tall.
