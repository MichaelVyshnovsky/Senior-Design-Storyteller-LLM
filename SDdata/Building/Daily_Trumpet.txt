The '''Daily Trumpet''' was a business located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood three stories tall.

==Location==
The Daily Trumpet was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]]. The business sat in the southern part of the Adventurers' Quarter with the front of the building along [[Kerrigan's Court]] while the rear of the building faced [[Snake Alley]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}
