Milshouns Stronghouses was a collection of three stone warehouses in Beliard around 1368 DR.
The warehouses were owned by Ahbhaer Mhilshoun, who provided storage facilities per day, tenday, or month. Milshouns Stronghouses were protected by a dozen guards, who hunted game in the hills to the north during their spare time.
* Storage space per chest for 20 gp a night
* Storage space per tenday was 50 gp
* Storage per month was 125 gp.
