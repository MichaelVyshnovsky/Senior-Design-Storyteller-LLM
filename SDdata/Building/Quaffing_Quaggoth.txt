The '''Quaffing Quaggoth''' was a [[tavern]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The tavern stood one story tall. 

==Location==
The Quaffing Quaggoth was located in the far southwest corner section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on the west side of [[Lackpurse Lane]] just south of [[The Reach (Waterdeep)|the Reach]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5260}}

==Inhabitants==
The Quaffing Quaggoth's bartender in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}, was a [[dwarf]] named [[Baklin]].
