The Quaffing Quaggoth was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The tavern stood one story tall. 
The Quaffing Quaggoth was located in the far southwest corner section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward on the west side of Lackpurse Lane just south of the Reach.
The Quaffing Quaggoths bartender in the , was a dwarf named Baklin.
