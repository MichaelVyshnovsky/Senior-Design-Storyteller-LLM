'''Summergarth Facilitations''' was a business in the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]]. 

==Geography==
The establishment was found on [[Leaping Dolphin Lane]]. 

==Inhabitants==
The business was owned by [[Halthara Summergarth]], who made barrels and crates and also owned a ship.
