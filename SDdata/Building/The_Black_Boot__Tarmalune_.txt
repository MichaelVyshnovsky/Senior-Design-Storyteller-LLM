The '''Black Boot''' was the most notorious tavern in [[Tarmalune]], one of the [[Windrise Ports]] of [[Laerakond]].

==Background==
The Black Boot doubled as a "darren," a Tarralune drinking, gambling, and wenching club. The owner was a large bald [[human]] named [[Lindelsan]], renowned for his popular homebrewed beer.

Many notorious and shady individuals in Tarmalune's society frequented The Black Boot, including [[Belyssa Erstros]] and [[Karadandel|Karadandel the Sly]].{{Cite organized play/LFR/Pyrophobia|15}}
