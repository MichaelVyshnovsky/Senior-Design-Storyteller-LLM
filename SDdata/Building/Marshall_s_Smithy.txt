'''Marshall's Smithy''' was a small blacksmith's shop located in the [[Bral/Low City|Low City]] of [[Bral]].

==Location==
The smithy was located at the intersection of [[Eel Street|Eel]] and [[Hammer Street]]s.

==Services==
The shop specialized in metal fittings for [[spelljammer]]s, such as [[Bolt (fastener)|bolts]], [[nail]]s, and other connectors, and as such, it catered to shipwrights of Bral. The store also provided [[axe]]heads and [[hammer]]heads primarily for use as [[tool]]s, but it could also be a source of weapons and [[arrowhead]]s.

==Staff==
The owner of the shop was a young man named [[Marshall]], who, though his main income came from nails and tools, was rather well-to-do.
