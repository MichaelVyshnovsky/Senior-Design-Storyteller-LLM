'''Nandar Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Nandar]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] in between [[Whaelgond Way]] and [[Brondar's Way]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The five buildings of Nandar Villa stood between two and three stories tall.
