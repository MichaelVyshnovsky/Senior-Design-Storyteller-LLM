'''Meiroth's Fine Silks''' was a clothing shop in the city of [[Waterdeep]]

==Location==
This shop fronted the east side of [[Snail Street]], in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Structure==
This shop stood three stories tall.

==Services==
Meiroth's Fine Silks sold [[silk]]s and clothing.
