'''The Sailor's Home''' was a modest inn in [[Velprintalar]], [[Aglarond]], in [[1368 DR]], kept by [[Mharus]].

Its patrons were seafarers and the less wealthy merchants. A room was two [[silver piece]]s per night, while a supper of fish, beef, or poultry was one [[copper piece]].
