'''Manycoins Moneylending''' was a [[manycoins services]] establishment located in [[Neverwinter]] in the 14{{Th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Manycoins Moneylending was located in the [[Blacklake District]].

==Description==
Manycoins Moneylending was a shop specialized in trade currency. For a small fee, people could change money to and from a different [[currency]]. The store was heavily protected by [[helmed horror]]s.

==Rumors==
According to [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]], Manycoins Moneylending was run by professional thieves.
