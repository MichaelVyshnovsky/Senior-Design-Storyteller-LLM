The '''Worried Wyvern''' was an inn in the town of [[Llorkh]] in the 1350s [[DR]], owned by [[Phintarn's brother|the brother]] of Lord [[Phintarn|Phintarn "Redblade"]].

Not long after Phintarn was murdered in a [[Zhentarim]] takeover shortly before the [[1357 DR|Year of the Prince, 1357 DR]],<ref group="note">The date of this unknown. ''[[Waterdeep and the North]]'' (dated [[1357 DR]]) only says this was "recently", implying sometime in 1357 DR or shortly before. his brother died mysteriously one night. The Worried Wyvern was razed the very next day.
