The Worried Wyvern was an inn in the town of Llorkh in the 1350s DR, owned by the brother of Lord Phintarn "Redblade".
Not long after Phintarn was murdered in a Zhentarim takeover shortly before the Year of the Prince, 1357 DR,The date of this unknown. Waterdeep and the North (dated 1357 DR) only says this was "recently", implying sometime in 1357 DR or shortly before. his brother died mysteriously one night. The Worried Wyvern was razed the very next day.
