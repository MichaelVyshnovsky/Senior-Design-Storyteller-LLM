Piatrans Clothiers was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The building stood a single story tall.
Piatrans Clothiers was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter on the east side of Weeping Maidens Run directly behind Madame Garahs Boarding House and Amranis Laundry.
