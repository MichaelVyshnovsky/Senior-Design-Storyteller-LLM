League Hall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the League of Skinners & Tanners.
The League Hall was located along Tower Trail, in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.
This guildhall stood two-stories tall.
On the second floor of this building was a tavern, open to only the members of the guild.
This guildhall operated as a tannery.
The League Hall was among some of the more notable and recognizable guildhalls in the Dock Ward. Like other guildhalls in the Dock Ward, it was considered by locals to be a landmark.
