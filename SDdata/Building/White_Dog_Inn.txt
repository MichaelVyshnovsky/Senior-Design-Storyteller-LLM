The White Dog Inn was an inn in the Tu Lung city of Balanzia around .
This inn was located in the southern portion of Balanzia, down the road from the homes and warehouse that were owned by a man named Xu.
Around , the White Dog was one of many buildings that were left intact in Balanzia as the city fell at the hands of the Shou Lung invasion of Tu Lung.The White Dog Inn is not explicitly stated in text to have not been destroyed in the incident that destroyed the city. However, on the "before-and-after" maps of Balanzia in Kara-Tur The Eastern Realms the building is still present on the "after" version. Therefore, it can be inferred that it was likely not destroyed. Centuries later, around , the building that once housed the White Dog Inn still stood within the abandoned cityand was recorded as such on a map made of the city in that year.
