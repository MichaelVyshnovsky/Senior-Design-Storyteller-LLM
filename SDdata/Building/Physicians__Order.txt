The Physicians Order was a guildhall in Daggerford that served as a meeting place for the towns apothecaries, healers, and morticians.
The hall was located in the center of town within the Caravan Quarter, quite close to the Clean Chin barber shop/mortuary
Visitors to the Physicians Order were granted access to a number of goods and services. They could purchase medicinal herbs and substances, employ the work of healers and surgeons, and even seek out magical cures to their ailments.
