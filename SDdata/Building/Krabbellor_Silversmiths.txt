Krabbellor Silversmiths was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The building stood two stories tall.
Krabbellor Silversmiths was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter. The front of the building faced Snake Alley, while the rear of the building faced the intersection of Grocers Lane and Weeping Maidens Run.
