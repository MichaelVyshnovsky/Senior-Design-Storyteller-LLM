}}The '''Diamond in the Dung''' was a large inn located in [[Hillsfar]] that catered to [[adventurer]]s despite the fact that this was considered an illegal activity.

==Location==
The inn was located in the eastern section of the city near the main gate, allowing tired adventurers to easily spot it upon entering the city.

==Services==
A stay at the inn cost 8 [[gp]] and that price included a room and meal. The inn's owner was willing to allow patrons to trade equipment for their stay as well.

==Inhabitants==
The Diamond in the Dung was owned by [[Oswul Farnf]] in [[1367 DR]].
