}}The '''Temple of the Faceless God''' was dedicated to the [[Faceless God]] of the [[Yikarian Empire]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}{{Cite dragon/241/Campaign Classics: The Roof of the World}}

==Location==
The temple was located in the [[yikarian]] capital of [[Nathong]] in the heart of the [[Land of the Yak-Men]].

==Activity==
The Temple of the Faceless God was the site where the ritual sacrifice of the [[Lotus Emperor]] occurred once every 20 years.
