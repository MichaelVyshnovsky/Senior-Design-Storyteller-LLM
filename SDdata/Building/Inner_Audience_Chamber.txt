The Inner Audience Chamber was within Castle Waterdeep.
There was at least one hidden tunnel leading out.
Mirt the Moneylender visited the Inner Audience Chamber in early Flamerule of  to warn Piergeiron that someone was slaying the Lords of Waterdeep, only to find the Open Lords daughter, Aleena, impersonating him. After she explained Piergeiron had already been attacked and nearly slain, they fled through a secret passage.
