'''Greenglade Tower''' was a popular and rather clean rooming house in [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
This inn was located in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of Waterdeep, in a courtyard south of [[Suldown Street]].

==Structure==
The Greenglade Tower stood five stories tall.
