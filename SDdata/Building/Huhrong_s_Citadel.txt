The Huhrongs Citadel, which was also called the Iron Lords castle, was a fortress and seat of government in the capital city of Immilmar in Rashemen in the .
The keep stood above Immilmar at the citys center.
This foreboding castle was made from stone and iron. Powerful water and earth spirits were believed to have played a part in its construction.
The Huhrongs Citadel was the ancestral home of Rashemens Iron Lord, or Huhrong, who, in 1367 DR, was Yvarrg Bearkiller. In addition to the Iron Lords family, his trusted advisors and commanders also inhabited the citadel. A trio of hathran wychlaran were also found within.
