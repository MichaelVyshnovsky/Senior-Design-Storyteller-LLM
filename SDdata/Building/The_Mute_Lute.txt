'''The Mute Lute''' was a shop located in [[Neverwinter]] in the 14{{Th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The Mute Lute was located in the northwestern end of the [[Blacklake District]], just across the street from [[Jaesor's Fineware Porcelain Works]], in the intersection between [[Hantor's Lane]] at the [[Street Of Storms]].{{cite web|author=[[Ed Greenwood]]|title=Ed's Twitter|url=https://twitter.com/TheEdVerse/status/967970877538275328|accessdate=2018-02-24}}

==Description==
The Mute Lute was popular across the Realms for its well-crafted [[lute]]s. The shop was built around an old oak tree. The Mute Lute got the name for a [[spell]] its owner, [[Rebeth Laereeryn]], could cast to silence all sound within its walls.

===Prices===
Common lutes at the Mute Lute cost 900 [[gp]], while custom-made lutes could cost up to 3000 gp.
