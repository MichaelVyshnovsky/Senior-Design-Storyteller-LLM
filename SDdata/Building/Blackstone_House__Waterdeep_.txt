'''Blackstone House''' was a row house located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The home stood four stories tall.

==Location==
Blackstone House was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] on northeast corner of where [[Telshambra's Street]] met the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Dead Trades Ward}}

==Services==
[[Mercenaries]] could be hired at Blackstone House.
