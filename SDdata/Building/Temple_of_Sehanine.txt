The '''Temple of Sehanine''' was a temple to [[Sehanine Moonbow]] in [[Leuthilspar]] on the island of [[Evermeet]].

==Structure==
The temple was a two-spire construction and, in [[1369 DR]], was run by [[Soliania Durothil]].
