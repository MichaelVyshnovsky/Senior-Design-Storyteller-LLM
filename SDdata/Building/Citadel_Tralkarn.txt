'''Citadel Tralkarn''' was the seat of power of the [[durthan]]s in [[Rashemen]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The fortress was located within the [[Erech Forest]] in northern Rashemen.

==Activities==
The durthans were able to construct Citadel Tralkarn in secret without alerting the hated [[hathran]]s.
