The '''Lesser Hall of Perfect Understanding''' was a large dormitory cave found within the [[Horseshoe Temple Oasis]] in the [[Quoya Desert]] of the [[Hordelands]] during the mid 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The cave was located on the far left side of the great chamber level of the temple. The dorms measured {{SI|35|ft|long=yes}} wide by {{SI|40|ft|long=yes}} long. Three passages served as entryways into the cave.

==History==
During the temple's heyday, the hall was used as personal quarters for all of the abbots who lived within.

==Inhabitants==
The Lesser Hall of Perfect Understanding was occupied by an [[oni]] named [[Halbasho|Halbasho the Smart]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.
