The '''Temple of Entropy''' was a house of worship devoted to [[Entropy]], a strange [[primordial]] striving to destroy all [[magic]].

==Location==
The Temple of Entropy was located in the city of [[Luthcheq]] in [[Chessenta]] in the {{YearlinkName|1357}}.

==Inhabitants==
The High Priest within the Temple of Entropy during this time was [[Maelos Karanok]].
