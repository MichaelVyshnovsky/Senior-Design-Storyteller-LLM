The Whirlpool was a former festhall, turned restaurant and theater, in the aquatic elf city of Myth Nantar.
This building stood in the Law Quarter of Myth Nantar.
The Whirlpool stood six stories tall.
In addition to serving food, the Whirlpool put on nightly dance and opera performances. Often times whales sang along to the the singing of the establishments mermaid and sea elf divas.
The Whirlpool had a reputation in Myth Nantar for being one of the premier places to go in the city for food and theater, with some saying that no other establishment in the city served better food.
