'''Nualorminoalh''' was a temple dedicated to [[Oghma]] in the city of [[Calimport]].

==Location==
The temple was located within the [[Ovohl Drudach]], of the [[Adlel Sabban]] district of Calimport.

==Structure==
This temple structure stood six stories tall.
