The Blue Baldric was a beauty shop and nightclub located in the city of Waterdeep during the late 14 century DR.
The Blue Baldric was located in the northeast section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward along Bazaar Street, being the middle building of a block that was bounded by Bazaar Street, Lamp Street, the Street of the Sword, and Warriors Way.
During the day the Blue Baldric operated as sort of beauty shop during the day and a club at a night. During daylight hours they additionally offered "pampering" in the form of scented bathes alongside servings of wine.
