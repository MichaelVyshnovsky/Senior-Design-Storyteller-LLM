The Abbey of the Rose was a secret temple to Amaunator in shadovar-dominated Sembia in the mid-to-late 15 century DR. Former, however, the site was sacred to Mask.
It was located in a valley in the Thunder Peaks, near the river Draal before it joined the river Arkhen, east of the Thunder Peaks.
The abbey was protected by the spirits of Elgrin Fau, led by Avnon Des.
However, in the , the Oracle released the spirits and so Sayeed and Zeeahd invaded the Abbey with their pack of spined devils.
