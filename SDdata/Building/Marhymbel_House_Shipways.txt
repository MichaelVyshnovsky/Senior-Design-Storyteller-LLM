'''Marhymbel House Shipways''' was one of the best shipwright workshops in the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]], based in [[Sarshel]] of [[Impiltur]] in [[1490 DR|1490s DR]].

==History==
By the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], Marhymbel House Shipways had been in business for two generations and was known as the fastest shipbuilders of rugged top-quality naval vessels that were reinforced for strength to weather any storm. Brother [[Ilund Marhymbel]] and [[Vromur Marhymbel]] were in charge for the Shipways.
