'''Firelust Fabrics & Tailoring''' was a clothiers shop located in the city of [[Yartar]]. It was run by the skilled and joyful tailors of the Firelust family, and remained under their proprietorship for generations.

==Location==
The shop was located in the center of the city, just west of the [[Fishyard]] marketplace.

==Services==
The Firelust family offered the finest tailoring work that was worth every [[coin]] of their rather steep prices. They were so adroit and worked with such collaboration that they could often compose an outfit and dress for a customer where they stood, in a matter of minutes.
