The '''Deep Temple of Dark Hope''' was a temple dedicated to [[Ibrandul]], the Lord of the Dry Depths.

==Location==
The temple was constructed far beneath the streets of [[Waterdeep]].

==Activities==
The temple was used as a stronghold or sanctuary by [[adventurer]]s exploring [[Undermountain]] and the [[Underdark]] if they paid the steep fee charged by Ibrandul's clergy. The temple was connected to the [[Dark Gateway]] via a ''[[gate]]''.
