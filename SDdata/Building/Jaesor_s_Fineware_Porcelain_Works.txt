'''Jaesor's Fineware Porcelain Works''' was a shop located in [[Neverwinter]] in the 14{{Th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Jaesor's was located in the northwestern end of the [[Blacklake District]], next to [[Dannar's Mechanical Marvels]] in the intersection between [[Hantor's Lane]] at the [[Street Of Storms]].{{cite web|author=[[Ed Greenwood]]|title=Ed's Twitter|url=https://twitter.com/TheEdVerse/status/967970877538275328|accessdate=2018-02-24}}

==Description==
Jaesor's was a shop specializing in porcelain works. This shop was popular among the well-known families of Neverwinter in the late years of the 14{{Th}} century.

===Prices===
A custom-paint plate cost 20 [[gp]], while a matched-set of 20 cost 45 gp.
