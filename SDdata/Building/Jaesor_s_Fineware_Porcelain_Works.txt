Jaesors Fineware Porcelain Works was a shop located in Neverwinter in the 14 century DR.
Jaesors was located in the northwestern end of the Blacklake District, next to Dannars Mechanical Marvels in the intersection between Hantors Lane at the Street Of Storms.
Jaesors was a shop specializing in porcelain works. This shop was popular among the well-known families of Neverwinter in the late years of the 14 century.
A custom-paint plate cost 20 gp, while a matched-set of 20 cost 45 gp.
