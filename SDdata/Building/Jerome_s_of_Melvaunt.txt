'''Jerome's of Melvaunt''' was a general goods store found in [[Phlan]] in [[1367 DR]]. The shop was named after its proprietor, [[Jerome]], who was in fact from [[Melvaunt]].

==Location==
The shop was located in the southwest section of the city.

==Activities==
Though Jerome purchased and sold general wares, he offered his services as a fence to those select customers who gained his trust.
