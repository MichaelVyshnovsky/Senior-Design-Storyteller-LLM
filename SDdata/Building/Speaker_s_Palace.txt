The Speakers Palace was the personal residence of the Speaker of Bryn Shander, the individual who represented the settlement to the rest of the Ten Towns.
This residence was located a bit south of the center of town.
The "palace" was made of dwarven design, constructed with stone walls and a slate roof. Its facade featured a notable colonnade that contributed to its out-of-place appearance among the other structures of Bryn Shander.
In the , a great battle took place in Bryn Shander between a barbarian horde and the folks of the Town Towns accompanied by the Companions of the Hall. Following the conflict, the Speakers Palace was gifted to the adventurer Regis.
