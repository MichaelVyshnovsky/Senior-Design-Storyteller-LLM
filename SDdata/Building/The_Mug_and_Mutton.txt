The '''Mug and Mutton''', which was also simply called '''the Mug''', was a raucous tavern located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The tavern was located in south Zhentil Keep near the center of the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] along [[Zhent Avenue]]. 

==Description==
A signboard depicting a drinking mug and a stuffed sheep was mounted to the right of the main door.

==Activities==
The Mug catered to all kinds of patrons who simply wished to eat and drink. Meal prices ranged between 6 [[sp]] to 2 [[gp]]. Most common drinks were 2 [[cp]], but more select beverages were 2 gp.

==Inhabitants==
The Mug and Mutton was owned by a man named [[Olford Thrax]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.
