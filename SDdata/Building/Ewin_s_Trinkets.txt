Ewins Trinkets, originally known as Earvins Treasures,
Under the ownership of its founder, the shop sold small items of a magical nature. Once it changed hands, Ewins Trinkets sold locally found treasures, gems, and jewelry.
The shop was founded by a wizard of the Arcane Brotherhood, Earvin Fastul, who fled Luskan following the destruction of the Host Tower of the Arcane.
