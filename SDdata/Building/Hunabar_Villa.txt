'''Hunabar Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Hunabar]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] at the intersection of [[Hassantyr's Street]] and [[Vhezoar Street]]. The western side of the villa compound also lay along [[Ilzantil Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The walled compound contained two buildings, both of which were located on the west side of the villa with the east side being composed of open gardens. Both buildings stood two storys tall.
