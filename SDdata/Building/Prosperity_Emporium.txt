The Prosperity Emporium was part of the Prosperity House complex located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The complex was located in south Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter on Zhent Avenue. 
Prosperity Emporium was a large mercantile shop owned by Malvred Silverguild that provided a wide range of goods and services. The shop carried anything that could be found in Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue.
