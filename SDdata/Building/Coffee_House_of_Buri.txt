The Coffee House of Buri was a small shop in Bandar al-Saadat in 1367 DR.
The establishment  was located near Usamah the Carpet Dealer in the Bandar al-Saadat marketplace.
Near the carpet dealers stand, the structure was buried behind a large line of stacked fruit.
The interior of the Coffee House of Buri was furnished with carpets and ragged cushions. Customers enjoyed a pleasant variety of sweet and overly spiced coffee.
Both Alix and Karbuqa were known to frequent the coffee house.
