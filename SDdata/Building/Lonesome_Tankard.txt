The '''Lonesome Tankard''' inn was considered among the finest of its kind in [[Eveningstar]]. Visitors were likely to meet [[Dunman Kiriag]], the friendly innkeeper who ran this historic, famous and often overcrowded inn in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=VGtC-p151><ref>

==Location==
The inn was located in the northeast section of Eveningstar.

==Description==
This two-story structure included a covered front porch, common rooms, and luxury rooms.

==Services==
The Tankard offered lodgings, drink, and a wide selection of dishes to its guests. Foods on offer included: [[braised beef stew]], [[cornmeal porridge]], crispy [[bacon]], [[ghallanda rations]], [[grilled turkey leg]], [[leek and potato soup]], [[pea soup]], [[poached sunfish]], [[roast boar shank]], [[smoke-cured ham]], brown [[bread]]; and the drinks menu consisted of [[bubbling brown froth]], [[grape]] [[juice]], [[broccoli]] juice, [[Dirty Kobold]], [[energy tonic]], [[ghallanda distillate]], [[dwarf|dwarvish]] [[sparkling apple cider]], [[Stormreach lemonade]], and [[milk]].

==History==
[[Darwell Kiriag]] was the owner of the Lonesome Tankard in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. During that time, a group of adventurers from another world set out to explore the infamous [[Haunted Halls]] with some possible help from a [[Harper]] agent [[Olarra Idlewist]], who posed as the inn's patron, and a [[Amaunator]]an [[spirit binder]] [[Melinde Agister]].
