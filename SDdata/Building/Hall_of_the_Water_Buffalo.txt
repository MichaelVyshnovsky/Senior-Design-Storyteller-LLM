The '''Hall of the Water Buffalo''' was a shrine dedicated to [[Yampa]] found within the [[Horseshoe Temple Oasis]] in the [[Quoya Desert]] of the [[Hordelands]] during the mid 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The Hall was located in the center of the upper galleries level of the temple. The Hall was roughly {{SI|20|ft|long=yes}} long. A pair of chambers were found along the hall, each measuring {{SI|15|ft|long=yes}} wide by {{SI|15|ft|long=yes}} long. The rearmost chamber contained a statue of Yampa the water buffalo.

==Inhabitants==
A pair of [[oni]] lived within the Hall of the Water Buffalo as of the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.
