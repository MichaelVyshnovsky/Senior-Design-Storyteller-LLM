'''Black Helm Tower''' was the home of [[Black Vizor]], one of the [[High Herald]]s of the organization known as the [[Heralds of Faerûn]].{{Cite web enhancement/Environs of Waterdeep|6}}

==Location==
Black Helm Tower was located about a day's ride (approximately 30 mi or 48 km) to the north and east of [[Daggerford]] in [[west Faerûn]], just north of the [[Delimbiyr]] river.{{Cite web enhancement/Environs of Waterdeep|3}}

==Structure==
This fortified compound sat on a small hill, affording it a minor strategic advantage.
