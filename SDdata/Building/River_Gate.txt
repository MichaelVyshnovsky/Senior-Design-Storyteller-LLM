The '''River's Gate''' was one of three entry points into the city of [[Daggerford]] in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The River Gate provided access to the city from the south near the shore of the [[Delimbiyr River]]. The gate was located at the junction of the [[Hill Road]] and [[Wall Street (Daggerford)|Wall Street]].

==Activities==
The River Gate was usually closed at night, especially during the flood season.

==Defenses==
The gate was only accessible after crossing a moat and bridge. The road leading through the gate passed beneath a single guard tower.
