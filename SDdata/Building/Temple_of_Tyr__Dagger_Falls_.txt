A temple of Tyr lay in Dagger Falls and was occasionally known to lend its services to adventurers who aided Randal Morn.
It was situated in Dagger Falls, in northern Daggerdale.
The temple of Tyr in Dagger Falls offered a few services to those willing and able to pay. Healing and spellcasting services were provided, if necessary.
In 1372 DR, the temple of Tyr had a good working relationship with the Lord of Daggerdale, Randal Morn, with a senior cleric of Tyr, Tunfer the Stout, operating as the Lords advisor.
