'''The Net of Pearls''' was a curios store in [[Marsember]], [[Cormyr]].

==Atmosphere==
It was owned and operated by [[Tannuth Ormbyr]]. It sold all kinds of antiques and bric-a-brac, such as statues, glassware, [[Purple Dragons|Purple Dragon]] arms and armor, [[Chessenta]]n items and all the materials necessary for fishing.
