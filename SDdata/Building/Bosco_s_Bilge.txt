Boscos Bilge was a tavern found in Jahaka Anchorage, the pirate bay located within the Jahaka Bay in western Chult. It was named after and run by Bosco Daggerhand.
The Bilge stunk of stale tobacco and spoiled food.
Though the quality was low, the tavern offered a limited variety of dishes, including shark and jungle snake. It catered to the various Jahaka pirate crews.
