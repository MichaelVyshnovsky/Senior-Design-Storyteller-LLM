'''Whistling Blades''' was a weapons shop located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood a single story tall.

==Location==
Whistling Blades was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] in a neighborhood just west of [[Snail Street]] and north of [[Blackstar Lane]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Dock South Ward}}
