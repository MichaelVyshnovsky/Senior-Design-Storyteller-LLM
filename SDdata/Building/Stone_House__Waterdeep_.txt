The Stone House was the guildhall of a Waterdhavian guild known as the Carpenters, Roofers, & Plaisterers Guild.
The Stone House was located along Telshambras Street,
This guildhall was a one-story building constructed of stone.
The Stone House was among some of the more notable and recognizable guildhalls in the Southern Ward, though some considered it to be an eyesore that was simply hard to miss.
