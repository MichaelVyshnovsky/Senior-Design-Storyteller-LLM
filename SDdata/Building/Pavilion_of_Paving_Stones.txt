The Pavilion of Paving Stones was a guildhall located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The guildhall stood three stories tall.
The Pavilion of Paving Stones was located in the western section of Waterdeeps Dock Ward in a neighborhood bordered by Lackpurse Lane to the north, Belnimbras Street to the south, Eel Street to the east, and Net Street to the west.
