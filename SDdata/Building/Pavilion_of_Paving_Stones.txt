The '''Pavilion of Paving Stones''' was a [[guildhall]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The guildhall stood three stories tall.

==Location==
The Pavilion of Paving Stones was located in the western section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] in a neighborhood bordered by [[Lackpurse Lane]] to the north, [[Belnimbra's Street]] to the south, [[Eel Street (Waterdeep)|Eel Street]] to the east, and [[Net Street (north)|Net Street]] to the west.
