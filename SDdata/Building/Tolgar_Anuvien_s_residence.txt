'''Tolgar Anuvien's residence''' was a row house and the home of [[Tolgar Anuvien]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The home stood three stories tall.

==Location==
Tolgar Anuvien's residence was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]].
