The Angry Coxswain was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The tavern stood a single story tall.
The Angry Coxswain was located in the central section Waterdeeps Dock Ward along the waterfront where Odd Street joined Dock Street.
The tavern was owned by a Mulan named Ramas Shehepset in the . The house brewmaster at this time was a duergar named Orth Ironchisel.
A magical portal existed within the tavern that Ramas and Orth used to ferry slaves to Mulhorand. The portal connected directly to a prison cell located in a market in Skuld.
