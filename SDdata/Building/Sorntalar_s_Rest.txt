Sorntalars Rest was the an inn located in the Amnian city of Crimmor. It was famous for offering a quiet place for guests desiring to rest up.
The inn was located in the Wheel Ward, near the western city walls.
Tapestries were hung from the thick walls, and halls were carpeted in order to dampen excessive noise, The owner attached a great importance to the quietness of her establishment, going so far as to ask noisy guests to leave.
