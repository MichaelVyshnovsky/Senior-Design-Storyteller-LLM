==Location==
Guard Hill stood in the northwest section of Skuld in the [[Skuld/Port District|Port District]] near the northern outer wall. It sat near the shore of the [[Alamber Sea]] and the merchant yards.

==Description==
The walled structure included a trio of guard towers.
