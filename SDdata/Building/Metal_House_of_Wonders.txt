Metal House of Wonders was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Splendid Order of Armorers, Locksmiths, and Finesmiths.
This building was located along Belnimbras Street and Gut Alley, in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.
This building stood two stories tall. It had a metal roof.
From the Metal House of Wonders, one could purchase adventuring gear, mundane armor, kits, and tools.
The Metal House of Wonders was among some of the more notable and recognizable guildhalls in the Dock Ward. Like other guildhalls in the Dock Ward, it was considered by locals to be a landmark.
