'''Point Dawn''' was the easternmost fortress in the chain of the [[Citadel of the Raven]] complex. It was named for the fact that it was the first part of the Citadel to feel the sun's rays in the morning.

==Description==
The fortress was dank and uncomforable.

==Inhabitants==
At maximum capacity the fortress could accommodate five hundred soldiers.
