'''Wands Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Wands]].

==Location==
[[File:North Ward-Gost Villa.png|thumb|right|270px|''Map showing the location of the Wands Villa (labeled <code>N55</code>) ''circa'' [[1372 DR]].'']]
The Wands Villa was located in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]],

==Structure==
Like many Waterdhavian villas, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden,{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2006)/07-06 - Waterdhavian Villas}}<ref group="note" name="evidence">Evidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps. with the entrance to the estate being a massive [[oak]]en gate. The building complex consisted of two high-quality buildings, one that was five stories tall and another that stood a three stories tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse. Notably, the mansion was adorned with twin towers<ref> over a large central chamber.

On the exterior walls of the estate's buildings were hung purple banners bearing the Wands family's insignia.
===Interiors===
The interior of the Wands mansion featured a long [[marble]] entry hall, leading to a sweeping staircase. The second floor featured a gallery. Inside the cellars of the estate, there stood a vault of artifacts that had [[permanency|permanent]] ''[[web]]'' [[spell]]s draped across their ceiling.

==Inhabitants==
In addition to the Wands family, the house was inhabited by [[iron golem]] servitors

==History==
In the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], the Wands family put a great many magical wards in place over the villa to protect the household's members from increasing attacks by creatures from the [[Far Realm]].
