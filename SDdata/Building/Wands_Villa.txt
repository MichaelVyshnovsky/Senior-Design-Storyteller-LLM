Wands Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Wands.
270px|Map showing the location of the Wands Villa (labeled N55) circa 1372 DR.
The Wands Villa was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep,
Like many Waterdhavian villas, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden,Evidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps. with the entrance to the estate being a massive oaken gate. The building complex consisted of two high-quality buildings, one that was five stories tall and another that stood a three stories tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse. Notably, the mansion was adorned with twin towers over a large central chamber.
On the exterior walls of the estates buildings were hung purple banners bearing the Wands familys insignia.
The interior of the Wands mansion featured a long marble entry hall, leading to a sweeping staircase. The second floor featured a gallery. Inside the cellars of the estate, there stood a vault of artifacts that had permanent web spells draped across their ceiling.
In addition to the Wands family, the house was inhabited by iron golem servitors
In the late 14 century DR, the Wands family put a great many magical wards in place over the villa to protect the households members from increasing attacks by creatures from the Far Realm.
