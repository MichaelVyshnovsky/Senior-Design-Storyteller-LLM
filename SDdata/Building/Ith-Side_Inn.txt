The '''Ith-Side Inn''' was a riverfront inn in [[Darromar]] commonly used by [[adventurer]]s.
{{TOC limit}}
==Location==
The inn sat in the southeastern corner of the [[Darromar/Black Quarter|Black Quarter]] of the city, overlooking the [[River Ith]].

==Structure==
The inn had a gated courtyard encompassed by {{SIAdj|8|ft|adj=tall}} walls topped with broken glass to deter thieves. The windows had shutters.

==Services==
Rooms in the inn were cheap, and quality of the inn reflected this. The inn included a stable. The staff included serving maids, who lit [[lantern]]s in the rooms for guests.

==History==
In the {{YearlinkName|1367}}, future-queen [[Zaranda Star]] was in Darromar (then Ithmong) to negotiate with the city's council. She stayed at the Ith-Side Inn. While there, she was drugged by a bottle of [[wine]].
