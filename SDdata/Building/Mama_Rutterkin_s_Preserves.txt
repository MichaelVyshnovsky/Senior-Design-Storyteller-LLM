Mama Rutterkins Preserves was a food store on the lowest level of the underground city of Skullport.
The shop was located next to the Dark Harvest Market and the Thrown Gauntlet. It was directly across from the Sanguine Hall and west of Shradins Excellent Zombies.
The interior of this shop , as well as the street outside of it, smelled strongly of vinegar. This was often a source of complaint from the businesss neighbors.
This shop specialized in selling a variety of preserves and pickled goods, with prices ranging from 5 cp to 5 sp.
The shop was owned and operated by a human woman named Gwylleth Rutterkin.
