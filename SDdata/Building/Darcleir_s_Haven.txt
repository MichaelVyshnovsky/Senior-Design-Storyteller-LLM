'''Darcleir's Haven''', or derisively known as the '''"The House of Peacocks"''' among the locals, was an exclusive eatery that stood on [[Suzail]]'s grand [[Promenade (Suzail)|Promenade]].

==Location==
Darcleir's Haven stood on the southeastern side of the crossing of the Promenade and the [[Court Close]]. The eatery could be easily found as it shared the same part of the Promenade as the Royal Court, and [[The Old Dwarf]] stood on the same block's eastern end.

===Atmosphere===
Darcleir's was known for its exquisite prices and exclusive clientele. Its guests were nobles, always flamboyantly dressed in accordance with Suzail's latest fashion trends, making the place scoffed at among commoners and lowborn. The restaurant was jam-packed with gossiping nobles daily.

==History==
As of the {{YearlinkName|1479}}, Darcleir's Haven was a usual haunt for [[Marlin Stormserpent]] of the noble [[House Stormserpent]] of [[Cormyr]].
