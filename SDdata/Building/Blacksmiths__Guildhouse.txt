The '''Blacksmiths' Guildhouse''' was the guildhall for the blacksmiths in the city of [[Daggerford]] in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The guildhall was located along the [[Trade Way (Daggerford)|Trade Way]] where [[Water Street]] connected in the southern section of Daggerford near the [[River Gate]].
