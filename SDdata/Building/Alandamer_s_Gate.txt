'''Alandamer's Gate''' was the northern gate of the city of [[Athkatla]] in [[Amn]].

==Location==
Alandamer's Gate stood on the border of two districts of Athkatla: the [[Athkatla/Wave District|Wave District]] to the west and the [[Athkatla/Temple District|Temple District]] to the east. The road that passed Alandamer's Gate ran north towards the grand temple complex of the [[Goldspires]] and the monastery [[Arbalest's House]].

==Activities==
All non-citizens who passed Alandamer's Gate had to pay an "any one coin" toll, of [[gold]] or [[silver]], to be allowed into Athkatla.
