The '''Westlight''', which was also called the '''Harbor Tower''', was a lighthouse found in the city of [[Westgate]] in [[1367 DR]].

==Location==
The Westlight was found at the end of long arm of land that separated Westgate's harbor from the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]].
