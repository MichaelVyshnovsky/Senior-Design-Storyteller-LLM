'''Tarm Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Tarm]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] at the intersection of [[Sulmor Street]] and [[Whaelgond Way]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
Three separate buildings within the walled compound were arranged around a central pond. All three building were three storys tall.
