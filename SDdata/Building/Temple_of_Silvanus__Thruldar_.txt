The '''Temple of Silvanus''' was a temple devoted to [[Silvanus]], the Oak Father, located in the town of [[Thruldar]] in [[Estagund]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The temple was one of the largest structures within Thuldar.
