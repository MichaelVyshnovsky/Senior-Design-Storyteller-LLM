The '''House of Textiles''' was the guildhall of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Most Excellent Order of Weavers & Dyers]].<ref>

==Location==
The House of Textiles was located along [[Nethpranter's Street]], in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
This guildhall stood two-stories tall.

==Reputation==
Like other guildhalls, the House of Textiles was considered by locals to be a landmark.
