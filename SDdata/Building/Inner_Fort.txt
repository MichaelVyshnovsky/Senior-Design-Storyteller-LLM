The '''Inner Fort''' was the main barracks and command center of the [[City Navy (Waterdeep)|City Navy]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
The Inner Fort was located on the eastern half of [[Deepwater Isle]].

==Structure==
This fortification stood three stories tall. It was connected to the heavily fortified [[Deepwater Wall]] that ran along the island's ridge.
