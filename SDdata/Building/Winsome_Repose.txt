The '''Winsome Repose''' was a riverfront inn in [[Zazesspur]] and one of the largest inns within that city.
{{TOC limit|3}}
==Location==
The inn sat in the western side of the [[Zazesspur/High City/Ships' District|Ships' District]] of the [[Zazesspur/High City|High City]] and overlooked the [[Sulduskoon River]].

==Structure==
The building was four stories tall, making it stand out from other locations on its street.

==Interior==
The inn was kept in fine condition. 

==Services==
The Winsome Repose surpassed many other inns in quality and served decent meals, making it a favorite among [[adventurer]]s. 

==Staff==
The innkeeper was a man named [[Quarlo]].

==History==
Future-queen [[Zaranda Star]] once stayed at this inn with her companions [[Farlorn Half-Elven]], [[Vander Stillhawk]], and [[Shield of Innocence]]&mdash;although the latter two slept in the stable with Zaranda's [[Golden Dawn|talking mare]].
