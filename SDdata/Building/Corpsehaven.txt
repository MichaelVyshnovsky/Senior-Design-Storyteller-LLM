Corpsehaven was a fortress in the Blood Rift. It was ruled over by the ultroloth Inthracis in the late 14 century DR.
Corpsehaven was located on the steep side of Calaas, the largest volcano in the Blood Rift.
Corpsehaven was a three-story library-fortress. As the name implied, it was built out of many thousands of magically preserved bodies, decaying but kept at the point of death and semi-sentient so they could still feel their pain and suffering and emit moans. Demons, devils, yugoloths, and mortals, all had been rivals and opponents of Inthracis.
