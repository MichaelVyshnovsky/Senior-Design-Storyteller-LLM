The '''River Trail Gate''' was the western entry point into lower half of the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The River Trail Gate sat on the western side of the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] on the southern bank of the [[River Tesh]]. It was situated on the [[Tesh Trail]] where the road entered the Foreign Quarter to connect with [[Slingshot Way]].

==Defenses==
The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers.

==Activities==
The River Trail Gate provided access to the Foreign Quarter from the west for land-based traffic.

==History==
The gate was completely sealed during [[Cyric]]'s attack on the city in the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, to protect the Foreign Quarter from attack.
