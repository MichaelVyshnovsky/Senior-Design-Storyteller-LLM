Yarth Stowage was storage facility found witin the city of Daggerford in the Western Heartlands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The compound was located in central Daggerford along Dukes Way just southeast of the market.
Yarth Stowage was one of the larger buildings found in Daggerford. The building stood five stories tall. Each floor was partitioned into secure rooms measuring  on each side.
In addition to the thick metal walls, the entire building was protected by a powerful ward that prevented anyone within the building from committing nefarious deeds.
Yarth Stowage was owned by the gnome Yarth.
