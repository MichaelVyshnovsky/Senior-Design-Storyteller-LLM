The '''Palace of the Cresting Wave''' was a temple to [[Istishia]] in the city of [[Calimport]].<ref name=Calimport-p63 />

==Location==
The temple was located in [[Calimport/Dock Ward#Aktorral Sabban|Markhib Drudach of Aktorral Sabban]] within [[Calimport/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]].<ref name=Calimport-p63 />

==Structure==
The building was an elaborate and ornate seven-story structure towering over most of the other buildings in the waterfront district.<ref name=Calimport-p63 />
