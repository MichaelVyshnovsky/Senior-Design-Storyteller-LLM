Galdeguts Boneyard was a glue factory, workshop and shop in Lannort in the 15 century DR.
The Boneyard sourced a wide variety of bones thanks to Morluth Galdeguts network of shady suppliers. These bones were worked into various alchemical ingredients, melted into glue, or sold wholesale for other purposes. It was linked by merchants map to Samrils Fine Blades in Secomber, who used bone (particularly the more durable bones of perytons, owlbears, dire wolves, and fire lizards) to carve pommels for weapons such longswords.
