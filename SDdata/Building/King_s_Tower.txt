The Kings Tower was a smooth-sided fortified construction on the west side of Marsember in Cormyr.
The buildings cobbled terrace was the largest open public space in the city. 
The tower was the home of the Crowns representative in the city which in 1367 DR was Bledryn Scoril
In the late 1400s DR, the Marsember Watch provided watchmen with Watch flans, which were steel meal tokens they could use to buy the cooperation of the hungry by letting them obtain a free full meal from the Kings Tower.
