Tym Waterdeep Limited, Tyme-Waterdeep, Tyme Waterdeep, Ltd. or simply TWL, as it was known among booksellers, was a large publishing business located in the city of Waterdeep.
The publishing company was a large, converted warehouse. The building was four floors high. 
The building was owned and run by Justin Tym in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Tym Waterdeep Limited was famous throughout the Realms as the publisher of all of the works of Volothamp Geddarm, including all his famous guide book, the ever-popular Cormyr A Novel and Evermeet A Novel by Greenwood Grubb. In the 15 century DR, in association with the Fellowship of Carters and Coachmen they published Volos Waterdeep Enchiridion.
