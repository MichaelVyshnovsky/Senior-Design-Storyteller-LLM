'''Othovir's Harness Shop''' was a store located in the town of [[Triboar]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Services==
The skilled craftsman [[Othovir]] crafted elaborately engraved harnesses out of [[elk]] skin and sold them within his shop. His goods were popular among [[Waterdhavian]] equestrian enthusiasts.
