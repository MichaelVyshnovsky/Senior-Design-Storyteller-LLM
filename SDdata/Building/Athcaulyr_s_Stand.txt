Athcaulyrs Stand was one of the wharves located in Lower City of Baldurs Gate.
Athcaulyrs Stand was the largest legless arrow-shaped wharf in Baldurs Gate. It protruded from the Eastway district into the Gray Harbor. The Eastern Wet street ran across the eastern side of the Gray Harbor and wrapped itself around the entire length of Athcaulyrs Stand. Directly at its southern end was a smaller in size Fishers Wharf
