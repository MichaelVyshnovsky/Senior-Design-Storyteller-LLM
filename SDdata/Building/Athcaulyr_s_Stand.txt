'''Athcaulyr's Stand''' was one of the wharves located in [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].

==Location==
Athcaulyr's Stand was the largest legless arrow-shaped wharf in Baldur's Gate. It protruded from the [[Baldur's Gate/Eastway|Eastway]] district into the [[Gray Harbor]]. [[The Eastern Wet]] street ran across the eastern side of the Gray Harbor and wrapped itself around the entire length of Athcaulyr's Stand. Directly at its southern end was a smaller in size [[Fisher's Wharf]]
