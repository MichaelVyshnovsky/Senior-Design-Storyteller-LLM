The '''Court of Proclamations''', or the '''second court''', was part of the complex of the [[Palace of the Enlightened Throne]] in [[Huzuz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
This large court was capable of holding 2,000 visitors at one time.

Important proclamations were announced here on a weekly basis. The nature of these announcements included awarding new titles, commendations, as well as announcing individuals who had earned disgrace and censure from the court.
