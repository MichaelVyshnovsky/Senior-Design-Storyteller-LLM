The Pilgrims Rest, which was also seen as the Pilgrims Rest, was an inn that was located in the city of Waterdeep.
The inn was located in the western section of Waterdeeps Sea Ward on the west side of Satchel Alley where it met Diamond Street.
The Pilgrims Rest was a three-story building. 
This comfortable inn was moderately priced, at least by Waterdeep standards. Many of the patrons stayed here while visiting Waterdeeps temples. A private room could be had for 9 gp per night and included stabling and a meal. Two-share rooms with a pair of double beds ran 6 gp per night, and a communal room with eight or more beds was 4 gp per night.
The inn was owned by Balaghast Brightlingar.
