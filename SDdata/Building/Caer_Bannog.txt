'''Caer Bannog''' was an imports shop in the city of [[Waterdeep]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
This shop was located on [[Mhaer's Alley]], in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Services==
Caer Bannog sold goods that were imported from the [[Moonshae Isles]].
