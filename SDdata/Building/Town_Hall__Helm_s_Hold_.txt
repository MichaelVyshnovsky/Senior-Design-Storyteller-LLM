Dragons Gauntlet
The Town Hall of Helms Hold, formerly the Dragons Gauntlet inn, was a public meeting place for the citys residents and its governing body.
It was located in the center of the city, not far from the Heartward marketplace and a short ways away from the Venturers Rest inn.
The hall was the official statehouse for the Holds council of Speakers, eight individuals who were elected among the citys residents. It also served as a public meeting place for the non-political citizens of Helms Hold. It was often the site of loud shouting matches that exemplified the uncompromising and individualistic nature of the populace.
