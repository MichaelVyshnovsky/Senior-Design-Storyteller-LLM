The House of the Black Lord was a temple of Bane in Mulmaster that came to be the center of worship of the Black Lord throughout the 15 century DR.
The temple was located on the east side of the city, not far off from the Zhents Quarter.
As of the late 1470s, the temple became a source of political strife between different factions that had formed within the ruling Council of Blades. While the councilors that shared in Banes worship created a powerful sect within the city, they were directly opposed by other belligerent members of the Blades, or those who shared a different faith altogether.
