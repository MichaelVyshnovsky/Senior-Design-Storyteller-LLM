The Wemic and Wineskin was a bustling tavern located in the town of Tulmon on the Lake of Steam in the late 14 century DR.
The tavern was well lit and usually so busy that it felt like a marketplace inside.
The Wemic and Wineskin was a popular spot among Tulmons younger fashionable crowd. The tavern offered a wide variety of beverages, but the only fare to be had was spiced buns.
