The '''Happy Hall of Fortuitous Happenstance''' was a temple to [[Tymora]]. 

==Geography==
The temple was located on a small hilltop in [[Yartar]], and was built like a huge windowed keep with dark and dreary stone walls. 

==Inhabitants==
Many folk in Yartar called the temple ''Two Hap Fort Hall'', or the ''Two''. The temple would sponsor adventuring parties to defend it from danger. As of [[1366 DR]], it was run by  [[Velantha Waerdar|High Priestess Velantha Waerdar]].
