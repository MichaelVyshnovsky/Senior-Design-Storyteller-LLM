The Blackstar Inn was an inn located in the city of Waterdeep during the 14 century DR.
The exterior windows were all barred for security, giving the inn the look of a fortress from the outside.
The inn was located on the east side of Gut Alley were it was intersected by Blackstar Lane in Waterdeeps Dock Ward.
The Blackstar Inn was owned by Asiyra Boldwinter during the mid-to-late 14 century DR. Armed guards protected the entryway and the stables.
