Ulbrinter Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Ulbrinter.
The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeeps North Ward on the south side of Delzorin Street. Vhezoar Street and Brondars Way also bordered the villa from the west and east respectively.
The walled compound contained three buildings. The two smaller structures sat in the northeast and southwest corner facing the larger central structure. The buildings were two or three stories tall.
