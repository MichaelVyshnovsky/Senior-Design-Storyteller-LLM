'''Heartward Hall''' was a temple of [[Sune]] located in [[Helm's Hold]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{cite book/Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide|85}}

==Location==
The temple was located on the outskirts of the [[Heartward]] market in the center of the city. The marketplace took its name from its proximity to the shrine.

==History==
Originally constructed a mere shrine, Heartward Hall was reformed as a temple shortly before the {{YearlinkName|1489}}.
