The Hall of rulership was the governments seat of power in the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 century DR following the citys destruction in the .
The hall was located in south Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter on Bridge Street. 
A lavish room called the Lords Room served as a meeting places for Zhentil Keeps rulers.
Lord Orgauth throne room was located within the Hall of Rulership. Other Lords of Zhentil Keep had offices here as well. A scribe named Zark worked out of the Hall of Rulership.
The Hall of Rulership was once a fortress.
A squad of 15 Zhentilar soldiers was present within the Hall at all times.
