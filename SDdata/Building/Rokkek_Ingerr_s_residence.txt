'''Rokkek Ingerr's residence''' was a row house and the home of [[Rokkek Ingerr]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The home stood two stories tall.

==Location==
Rokkek Ingerr's residence was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]] on the west side of [[Weeping Maiden's Run]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}
