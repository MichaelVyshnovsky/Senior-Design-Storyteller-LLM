The '''Great Imperial Library''' was one of the rooms of the [[Palace of the Purple Emperor]] in [[Raurin]]. It was of such singular notoriety that scholars knew of it even millenia afterwards: the [[dwarven]] [[geomancer]] [[Thormud Horn]] made special note of it while relating the fabled contents of the [[Palace of the Purple Emperor]] in the {{yearlinkName|1374}}.{{cite book/Darkvision|226|24}}
