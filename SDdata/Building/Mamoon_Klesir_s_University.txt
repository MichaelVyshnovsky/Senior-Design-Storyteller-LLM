'''Mamoon Klesir's University''' was a school located in [[Huzuz]], the City of Delights, in {{Yearlink|1367}}.{{AQ-time}}

==Location==
The university was found in Huzuz's [[Huzuz/Craft District|Craft District]] in the northeast corner of the city.

==Activities==
The school was run and owned by [[Mamoon Klesir]]. He only admitted member of Huzuz's nobility into his prestigious school.
