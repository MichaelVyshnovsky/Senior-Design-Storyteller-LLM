The Throne of Thought was an abandoned temple of Labelas Enoreth found in the city of Myth Drannor.
It was located on Eldansyrs Ride, on the northwestern border of Myth Drannors Sheshyrinnam district.
The five-story temples was built and as adapted tree-dwelling, a common form of architecture among the Cormanthan elves. Beneath it was an underground crypt-complex, with a tunnel that led to the Warriors Gate tomb.
