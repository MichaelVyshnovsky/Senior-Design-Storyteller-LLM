The '''Throne of Thought''' was an abandoned temple of [[Labelas Enoreth]] found in the city of [[Myth Drannor]].{{Template:Cite book/Cormanthyr: Empire of the Elves|74}}

==Location==
It was located on [[Eldansyr's Ride]], on the northwestern border of Myth Drannor's [[Sheshyrinnam]] district.{{Template:Cite book/Cormanthyr: Empire of the Elves/Map}}

==Structure==
The five-story temples was built and as adapted tree-dwelling, a common form of architecture among the [[Cormanthan]] elves. Beneath it was an underground crypt-complex, with a tunnel that led to the [[Warrior's Gate]] tomb.
