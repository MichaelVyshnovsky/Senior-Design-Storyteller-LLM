Brian the Swordmasters Smithy was a blacksmith shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The smithy stood two stories tall. 
Brian the Swordmasters Smithy was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward on the south side of Brians Street where it was joined by Fishwife Alley.
The smithy was owned by Brian the Swordmaster, a Lord of Waterdeep.
