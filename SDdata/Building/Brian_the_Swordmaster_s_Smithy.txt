'''Brian the Swordmaster's Smithy''' was a blacksmith shop located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The smithy stood two stories tall. 

==Location==
Brian the Swordmaster's Smithy was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] on the south side of [[Brian's Street]] where it was joined by [[Fishwife Alley]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Dock South Ward}}

==Inhabitants==
The smithy was owned by [[Brian|Brian the Swordmaster]], a [[Lord of Waterdeep]].
