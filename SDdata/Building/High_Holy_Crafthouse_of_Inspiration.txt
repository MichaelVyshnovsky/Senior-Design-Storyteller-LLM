The '''High Holy Crafthouse of Inspiration''' was a monastery dedicated to [[Gond]] the Wonderbringer in the city of [[Illul]] in [[Lantan]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was the heart of [[church of Gond|Gond's church]] in the Realms.

==Description==
It was a large walled monastery.

==Notable Inhabitants==
''Circa'' [[1370 DR]], this monastery was run by High Artificer [[Danactar]], Most Holy Servant of Gond. As the highest-ranking mortal [[cleric]] of Gond, Danactar was the supreme voice in ecclesiastical matters, at least on Lantan and among the Lantanese, and he was also leading councillor in Lantan's ruling body, the [[Ayrorch]].

==Activities==
The [[techsmith]]s of the church of Gond were based and trained at the High Holy Crafthouse.
