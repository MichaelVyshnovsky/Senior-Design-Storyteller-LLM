'''Yousouf's Grocery''' was a shop in the [[enclave]] of [[Eileanar]] that operated until its destruction in {{yearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}. It was notable for its ex-[[adventurer]] owner, [[Yousouf]], who had retired to a quieter life before he lost any more body parts.
