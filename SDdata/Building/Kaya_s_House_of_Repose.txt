'''Kaya's House of Repose''' was a prominent [[inn]] in [[Port Nyanzaru]], [[Chult]]. Visiting [[adventurer]]s were often referred there if they wanted a quiet and restful night.

==Location==
The inn was located in Port Nyanzaru's [[Port Nyanzaru/Market Ward|Market Ward]] just south of the [[Red Bazaar]].{{Cite adventurers league/A City on the Edge|42}}

==Services==
Kaya's House of Repose was known to be relatively opulent and quiet compared to the average inn in Port Nyanzaru, and as a result, charged a full [[gold piece]] for a night's stay. 

==History==
During the events of the [[death curse]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], Kaya's House of Repose was a favorite drinking and meeting spot for the [[Harper]] spymaster known as [[Soggy Wren]].{{Cite adventurers league/A City on the Edge|5}}
