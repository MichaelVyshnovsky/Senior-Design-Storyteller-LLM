The '''Captain's Fancy''' was a quiet and fine tavern in [[Yeoman's Loft]], [[Netheril]], in the 4{{th}} century before [[Dalereckoning]].

==Location==
The tavern was situated near the [[Harbor Lane]] and [[Elm Street]] in the southern part of the city.

==Structure==
The Fancy was surrounded by a beautiful garden.

===Atmosphere===
==Services==
The establishment was known as a frequent resting spot for [[spelljammer|spelljamming]] captains and served fine liqueurs and provender.

==History==
Around {{YearlinkConvert|3520|NY}}, [[Karsus]] tried to steal all the magic from [[Mystryl]] and caused the downfall of Netheril. When this happened, four spelljamming vessels exploded and crashed into the docking district, killing thousands of people, and possible destroying the Captain's Fancy tavern.
