The Captains Fancy was a quiet and fine tavern in Yeomans Loft, Netheril, in the 4 century before Dalereckoning.
The tavern was situated near the Harbor Lane and Elm Street in the southern part of the city.
The Fancy was surrounded by a beautiful garden.
The establishment was known as a frequent resting spot for spelljamming captains and served fine liqueurs and provender.
Around , Karsus tried to steal all the magic from Mystryl and caused the downfall of Netheril. When this happened, four spelljamming vessels exploded and crashed into the docking district, killing thousands of people, and possible destroying the Captains Fancy tavern.
