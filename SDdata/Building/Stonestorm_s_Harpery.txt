'''Stonestorm's Harpery''' was an instrument shop specializing in [[harp]]s in the city of [[Silverymoon]] in the [[Silver Marches]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. It stood on [[Goblinwood Rise]] in the city's [[Silverymoon/Southbank|Southbank]]. It was owned by [[Markovel Stonestorm]] and used strings made by [[Chesmyr Morrowynd]] ''circa'' [[1372 DR]].{{Cite web/My Slice of Silverymoon/Part 2}}
