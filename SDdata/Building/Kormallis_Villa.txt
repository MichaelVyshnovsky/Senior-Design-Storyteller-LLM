'''Kormallis Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Kormallis]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the eastern section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] on the south side of [[Hassantyr's Street]] where it was intersected by [[Ussilbran Street]]. [[Endcliff Lane]] passed by the back of the estate.{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The walled compound contained five buildings with one found in the center. All five buildings were either one or two storys tall.
