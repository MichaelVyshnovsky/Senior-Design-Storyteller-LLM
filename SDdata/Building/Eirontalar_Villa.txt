'''Eirontalar Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] of [[Eirontalar]].

==Location==
The Eirontalar Villa was located in the city block bounded by [[Diamond Street]], [[Delzorin Street]], [[Feather Street]], and [[Vondil Street]], in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

They had a second villa  on the north side of [[Hassantyr's Street]], in the middle of a block between [[Sul Street]] and [[Shield Street]]. It was a walled compound with three buildings and an inner garden.<ref><ref><ref group="note">The map in the 3.5-edition ''City of Splendors: Waterdeep'' displays two locations marked '$33' for the Eirontalar Villa. This second one may in error and intended to be another site, but this is difficult to determine. On other maps, the site is unmarked.

==Structure==
The compound was a walled estate that consisted of some two-story buildings and some green space.
