The '''Rose Altar''' was a consecrated shrine to [[Lathander]] the Morninglord, found in the city of [[Tilverton]].

==Location==
The altar was situated on a covered landing along the [[Market Stairs]] that led up to the city's Old Town, part of the lengthy [[Street of the Sorceress]].
