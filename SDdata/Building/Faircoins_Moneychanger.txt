The '''Faircoins Moneychanger''' was a shrine dedicated to [[Waukeen]] in the city of [[Marsember]].{{Cite dungeon/113/Practical Magic|37}}

==Location==
Faircoins was located on the mainland portion of Marsember, down the road from [[Chalanther House|the home]] of [[Vindala Chalanther]].

==Structure==
This shrine was small in size.

==Activities==
In addition to being a shrine of Waukeen, this building provided [[manycoins services]] to the Marsemban public.

==History==
By the mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]], this shrine had become abandoned by the [[Church of Waukeen]]. However, Lord [[Ildool]] maintained it as a public service to Marsembans for the purpose of its manycoins services.

==Rumors & Legends==
Some Marsembans speculated that the lights occasionally seen in the shrine during nighttime hours was Lord Ildool or his agents using it as a meeting place with less-than-savory individuals.
