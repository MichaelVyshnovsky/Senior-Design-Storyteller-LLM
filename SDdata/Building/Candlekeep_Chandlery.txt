'''Candlekeep Chandlery''' was a shop in [[Baldur's Gate]] that sold beautifying products and candles, both mundane and [[magic]]al in nature. The shop was located in the [[Seatower]] district of the [[Lower City]].

==Services==
{{Quote|''Longest Lasting Lamps and Magically Made Missives in the Lower City''|Slogan of the ''Candlekeep Chandlery''}}

Beyond cosmetics and soaps, the establishment's most famous product was its ''[[whispering candle]]''. It was crafted with a small [[fire mephit]] place within its tallow. As the candle burned, the mephit repeated back any phrases or noises it heard while the candle was being made.
