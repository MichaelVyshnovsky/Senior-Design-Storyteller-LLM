'''Ralagut's Wheelhouse''' was a general store in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of the city of [[Waterdeep]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
This shop fronted the western side of [[Snail Street]] and was located a block south of [[Shesstra's Street]].
