The '''Platter of Plenty''' was a restaurant in [[Marsember]], [[Cormyr]], owned by [[Blentra Whaelbuckler]].

==Location==
The Platter was built at the very center of [[Dawnturtle Isle]] in Marsember.{{cite web| url = https://web.archive.org/web/20220416130132/https://candlekeep.com/library/articles/sse/sse_050607-04.htmp| title = So Saith Ed May â€“ Jul 2004| accessdate = 25-11-2021| date = 25-11-2021}}

==Services==
It was a busy, alcohol-free establishment that opened around dusk and remained open until dawn. Notable dishes included [[Blentra's oysters and wild rice in mushroom soup]], and [[Blentra's cider and vinegar oyster sauce]] served with [[oyster]]s, but the restaurant served various roasts and platters and steamed vegetables with assorted sauces.
