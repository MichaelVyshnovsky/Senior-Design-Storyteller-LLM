'''Fortress Eyegad''' was a re-claimed fortress, located in the [[Spine of the World]], that was inhabited by the [[Skortchclaw]] tribe during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
The Skortchclaw [[orc]]s used a group of enslaved [[goblin]]s to mine [[mithral]] beneath its structure.

==History==
It was originally constructed and occupied by [[dwarf|dwarves]], but by the {{YearlinkName|1370}} it had been taken by the orcs.
