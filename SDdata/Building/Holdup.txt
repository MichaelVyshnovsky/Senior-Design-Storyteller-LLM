'''The Holdup''' was a [[tavern]] in [[Port Nyanzaru]], [[Chult]] that was popular with [[adventurer]]s.{{Cite adventurers league/Fester and Burn|3}}{{Cite adventurers league/Fester and Burn|16}}

==Location==
The tavern was located in Port Nyanzaru's [[Port Nyanzaru/Old City|Old City]].

==Services==
The Holdup was a popular drinking establishment, especially for adventurers as a last stop before setting out into the jungle. It was also a good place for adventurers to seek work.{{Cite adventurers league/Fester and Burn|26}}
