The Holdup was a tavern in Port Nyanzaru, Chult that was popular with adventurers.
The tavern was located in Port Nyanzarus Old City.
The Holdup was a popular drinking establishment, especially for adventurers as a last stop before setting out into the jungle. It was also a good place for adventurers to seek work.
By the late 15 century DR, the taverns proprietor, Miboro Yan, was much beloved by the people of the Old City and the adventurers who frequented her establishment.
Not long after the end of the death curse, In response, even the beggar princes were willing to pay for her safe return.
