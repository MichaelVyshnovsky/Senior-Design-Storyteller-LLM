'''Seaview House''' was a boarding house found in the town of [[Aithelar]] in [[Callidyrr]] on the [[Moonshae Isles]] in {{YearlinkName|1345}}.{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
Seaview House was located in southern Aithelar at the intersection of [[Aithe High Street]] and [[Blackdorn's Way]].
