The '''Jade Dancer''' was a [[festhall]] and [[tavern]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood three stories tall.

==Location==
The Jade Dancer was located in the southern section of the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] on the [[Dancing Court]] just off of the southern end of [[Slop Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5298}}
