The Caliphs Orphanage was a large center for housing orphans located in the city of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The Caliphs Orphanage was located within the Furdah Rustauk District in southwest Dhaztanar. The orphanage was located just down the street from the southern gate leading into the Madinat.
