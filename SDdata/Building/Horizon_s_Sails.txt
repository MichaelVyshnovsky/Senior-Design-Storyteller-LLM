'''Horizon's Sails''' was a shop located in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The shop was located along [[Two Flask Alley]].

==Activities==
Horizon's Sails specialized in fine and enchanted [[map]]s, charts, and logbooks. Some of these maps could not be found anywhere else and were said to be worth their weight in [[mithril]].

==History==
Around the 14{{th}} century DR, this business's ships sailed around the far off continents of [[Anchorome]] and [[Maztica]].
