Horizons Sails was a shop located in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep during the 15 century DR.
The shop was located along Two Flask Alley.
Horizons Sails specialized in fine and enchanted maps, charts, and logbooks. Some of these maps could not be found anywhere else and were said to be worth their weight in mithril.
Around the 14 century DR, this businesss ships sailed around the far off continents of Anchorome and Maztica.
