'''Wavesilver Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Wavesilver]].

==Location==
The villa was located at the corner of [[Ivory Street]] and [[Flint Street]] in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The villa compound was walled and composed of several two and four story structures. The estate included a large garden and pond.
