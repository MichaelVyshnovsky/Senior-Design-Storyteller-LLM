The House of the Lynx was a club where visitors of means could find hired companionship for a night, located in Suzail, the capital of Cormyr in the 15 century DR.
The House of the Lynx stood several doors down from Suzails grand Promenade and south from the Court Closes beginning at the stoop of The Winking Eye tavern. The brothel was known for its ladies "not sporting a face like an old boot" whose companionship was priced at ten lions and twenty more for a night-long affair and a choice activities that did not include flogging.
