The '''Crimson Brush''' was an art shop located in the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/The Map with No Names|9-10,12,18}}

{{quote|''Bood is beautiful. Its color highlights my art.''|
[[Gath]]}}

==Location==
The shop was located on the first floor of a two-story building, below an apartment.

==Structure==
===Atmosphere===
The Crimson Brush was organized like an art gallery, presenting paintings and painted trinkets on the walls and shelves. Most of the pieces exposed by the owner included blood-red colors or were blood-themed.

==Services==
The shop was used by Gath, the owner, to sell his own artwork.
