The '''Bit & Bridle''' was a quality inn located in the city of [[Lheshayl]], [[Shining Plains]].

==Services==
The Bit & Bridle offered typical guest services of food and lodgings. Along with the [[Saddle of the Plains]], Bit & Bridle was considered a moderately-priced establishment.

==History==
At some point before the {{YearlinkName|1370}}, [[Adamar Session]], [[Bard]] of [[Starmantle]], visited the Bit & Bridle inn during his twelve-year adventuring trek across the Reach.
