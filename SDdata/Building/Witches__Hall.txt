The '''Witches' Hall''' was a haven for [[Wychlaran]] in the capital city of [[Immilmar]] in [[Rashemen]] in the {{YearlinkName|1367}}.{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The hall was located on the south side of Immilmar.

==Description==
The support beams of this white-washed longhouse were carved into the shapes of [[dragon]]s, [[unicorn]]s, and hounds.

==Defenses==
Any non-Wychlaran found trespassing within the Witches' Hall were usually slain outright. Those spared, in the case of an accidental trespass, were exiled from Rashemen and had all memory of the land erased from their mind.

==Inhabitants==
[[Othlor]] [[Fydra Night-Tree]] lived within the Witches' Hall.
