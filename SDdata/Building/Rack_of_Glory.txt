The '''Rack of Glory''' was a temple dedicated to [[Ilmater]] located in the city of [[Yhaunn]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Inhabitants==
The temple was run by High Priest [[Inrimmon Othnil]]. Inrimmon was assisted by seven minor priests.
