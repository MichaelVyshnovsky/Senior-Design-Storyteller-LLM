'''Keep to Yourself''' was a tavern located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]] following the city's destruction in the {{YearlinkName|1368}}.

==Location==
The tavern was located in south Zhentil Keep outside of the [[River Trail Gate]] in the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] on the [[Tesh Trail]]. 

==History==
Keep to Yourself was constructed for travelers who did not wish to enter the city but still wanted a place to rest for the night. The tavern was an excellent source for wild rumors.
