'''Sundstone''' was the residence of the heads of the [[Belvrost]] clan, in the [[Gulthmere Forest]].

==Location==
Sundstone was near [[Baernwood]].

==Structure==
The location was a rambling, [[moss]]-girt building in the [[tree]]s, more cave than construction.
