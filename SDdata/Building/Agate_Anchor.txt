The Agate Anchor was a curio shop in the city of Tantras in the Vast around 1358 DR.
The shop lay on Dragonleather Street, in the southern part of the city.
The Agate Anchor was a vast curio shop that apparently stocked every non-magical item around.
Chuldos was the proprietor of the Agate Anchor, and Alaphale and Nuathue were guards.
