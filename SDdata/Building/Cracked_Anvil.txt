The '''Cracked Anvil''' was a smithy in the town of [[Port Llast]].

==Structure==
In front of the establishment there was notably a cracked anvil on display.

==Staff==
The Cracked Anvil was owned and operated by a [[dwarven]] blacksmith known as [[Haljal Throndor]].
