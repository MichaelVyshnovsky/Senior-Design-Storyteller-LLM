The Pantheon Temple of the Seldarine was a elven temple dedicated to the Seldarine located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The building stood four stories tall. 
The Pantheon Temple of the Seldarine was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward in a neighborhood that lay between Warriors Way to the west and the Street of the Sword to the east.
Biafyndar Loceath was an initiate of Corellon Larethian who worked within the temple. 
The only known surviving ruins of Aelinthaldaar could be found in the crypts beneath the temple.
