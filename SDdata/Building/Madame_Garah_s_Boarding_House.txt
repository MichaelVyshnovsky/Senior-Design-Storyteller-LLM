Madame Garahs Boarding House was a row house used as a boarding house located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The building stood two stories tall.
Madame Garahs Boarding House was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter on the south side of Slop Street.
