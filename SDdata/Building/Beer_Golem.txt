The '''Beer Golem''' was a [[tavern]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood two stories tall.{{Cite interactive atlas|3530 Safehaven Inn and Beer Golem Tavern}}

==Location==
The Beer Golem was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]]. The front of the tavern sat along [[Kerrigan's Court]] while the west side of the building touched [[Snake Alley]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}
