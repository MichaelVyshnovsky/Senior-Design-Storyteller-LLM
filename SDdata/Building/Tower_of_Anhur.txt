==Location==
The Tower was located in the southwest corner of the [[Skuld/City of the Gods|City of the Gods]] in the northcentral part of the city. 

==History==
As its name implied, the Tower of Anhur was once the home of an [[incarnation]] of [[Anhur]] who held the title of God-King in Mulhorand. It was constructed by slaves thousands of years ago as of the {{YearlinkName|1357}}.
