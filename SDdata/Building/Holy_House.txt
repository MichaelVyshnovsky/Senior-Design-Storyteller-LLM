The '''Holy House''' was an all-faiths shrine in the town of [[Voonlar]] in the [[Moonsea]] region.{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks archive|8}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 6: Local Faith}}

==Location==
The Holy House situated in the northwest corner of the Common Gardens, a park near the center of Voonlar.

==Description==
Holy House was an open pavilion with no walls, just a shingled roof held up by stone pillars. There was a plain stone block for an altar, flanked by [[brazier]]s. Nearby was a privy and a small but deep well that provided cool, drinkable water.

Overnight camping at the shrine was strictly forbidden by the local authorities.
