'''Tchazzam Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Tchazzam]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the western section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] on the northeast corner of where [[Diamond Street]] and [[Seawatch Street]] joined.{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The walled compound contained two buildings. The larger building stood on the north side of the estate. Each building stood between one and two stories tall.
