'''The Fountain of Anumka''' was a large fountain in the city of [[Tu Pe]] in [[Koryo]] around [[1357 DR]]. It lay in the central eastern part of the city, near the city wall. It was fed by a freshwater spring within the city and would enable the city to withstand a siege.
