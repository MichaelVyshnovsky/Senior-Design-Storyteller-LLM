'''The Belled Boot''' was a toy store in [[Reddansyr]] in the late mid-14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The toy store sat in the intersection of [[Bright Lane]] and [[Selver Street]].

==Inhabitants==
The store owner was [[Telgerd Belvrost]] as of {{yearlink|1370}}.
