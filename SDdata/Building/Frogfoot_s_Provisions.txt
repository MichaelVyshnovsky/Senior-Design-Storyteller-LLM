'''Frogfoot's Provisions''' was a supply store in [[Highcastle]], within [[High Dale]], during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. They provided finished goods, such as [[lantern]]s and [[tool]]s, and other traveling provisions. They were slightly more expensive than other stores in the Dales, selling goods at a 20% markup.
