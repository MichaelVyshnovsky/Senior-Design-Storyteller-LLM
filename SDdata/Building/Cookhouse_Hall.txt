'''Cookhouse Hall''' was a civic building located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood two storys tall.

==Location==
Cookhouse Hall was located in the far southeast corner of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] near the city's outer wall just southwest of [[Smuggler's Run]] at the end of [[Tower Trail]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Dock South Ward}}
