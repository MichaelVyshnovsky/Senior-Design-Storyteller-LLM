The '''Silent Sword''' was an inn found in the [[Port District]] of the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite document/Procampur City Expansion Packet|3}}

It was frequented by adventurers visiting the city. It was a much quieter place than the [[Happy Hippocampus]]. It had good quality service and moderate prices.
