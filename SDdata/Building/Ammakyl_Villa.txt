'''Ammakyl Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Ammakyl]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the eastern section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] on [[Vondil Street]] with the west side of the villa touching [[Shield Street]] and the east side touching [[Copper Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
There were four buildings within the walled estate with gardens in between. Two of the buildings were much larger than the others. Each building was one or two stories tall.
