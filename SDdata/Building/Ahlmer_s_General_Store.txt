'''Ahlmer's General Store''' was a shop in the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was owned and run by [[Ahlmer]].{{Cite document/Procampur City Expansion Packet}}
{{quote|You haven't been to Procampur until you've tried an Ahlmer's biscuit!|The shop's advertising slogan}}

==Location==
It stood in the [[Port District]].

==Services==
The prices were low and among its goods were fresh-baked [[biscuit]]s for which the store was famed.

In [[Alturiak]] in the {{YearlinkName|1372}},{{LCRB}} a strange well-dressed gentleman found wandering the streets of Procampur identified himself as "Lord [[Sellwin Wagglebottom]], Deputy Assistant Commander in charge of Perishable Supply Requisition for the Armies of [[Ravens Bluff]]" and asked for directions to Ahlmer's General Store.<ref>{{Cite organized play/LC/The Stolen Crown|13}}
