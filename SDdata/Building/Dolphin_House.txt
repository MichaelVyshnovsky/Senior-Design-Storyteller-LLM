The '''Dolphin House''' was an [[inn]] located in the city of [[Urmlaspyr]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Description==
This large establishment was carved from stone. The interior and furnishing were kept spotless and the service was exceptionally swift. Though filled with grand accommodations, the Dolphin House was moderately priced.
