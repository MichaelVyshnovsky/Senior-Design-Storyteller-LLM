==Description==
The tavern stood three stories tall.

==Location==
The Singing Sword was located in the northeast section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on [[Bazaar Street]] near the [[Market (Waterdeep)|Market]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5266}}
