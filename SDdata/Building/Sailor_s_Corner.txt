The '''Sailor's Corner''' was an [[inn]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The inn stood two stories tall.

==Location==
The Sailor's Corner was located in the western section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] in a small cluster of buildings that sat in the middle of [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] just south of [[Fishgut Court]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5309}}
