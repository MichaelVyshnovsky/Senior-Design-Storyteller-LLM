'''Jopalin's''' was a cafe in [[Baldur's Gate]].

As a love of drinking [[tea]] and [[coffee]] grew in Baldur's Gate, [[Jopalin]], the [[half-elf]] owner of the cafe, coverted the dive of a tavern into a trendy cafe that merchants would frequent to seem refined.
