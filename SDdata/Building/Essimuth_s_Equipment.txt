'''Essimuth's Equipment''' was a shop that sold tools and adventuring gear located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood two stories tall.

==Location==
Essimuth's Equipment was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]] where [[Weeping Maiden's Run]] joined [[Slop Street]] next door to the [[Temple of Good Cheer]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}

==Inhabitants==
The shop was owned and run by [[Essimuth]] in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.
