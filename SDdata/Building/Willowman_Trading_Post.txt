}}The '''Willowman Trading Post''' was a general store found in [[New Velar|Harrowdale]].

==Location==
The general store was found at the intersection of [[Lancegallop Lane]] and [[Dendever Street]].

==Atmosphere==
This rundown store was gloomy, dusty, and crammed with a large assortment of odd items. Bargains could be had for those searching for truly rare hardware. [[Volothamp Geddarm]] noted in particular a left-hand double-ended [[scythe]].
