The '''Warhorn Room''' was a room within [[Castle Obarskyr]] in [[Suzail]].

==Location==
The room was somewhere within [[Castle Obarskyr]].

==Interior==
The ''Royal Gorget of Battle'' had been on display there.

==History==
The ''[[Royal Gorget of Battle]]'' was held there until the fugitive [[wizard]] [[Elminster Aumar]] stole it, at some point close to [[Mirtul 5]] of the {{yearlinkName|1479}}. The theft was discovered by [[Ganrahast Aeiulvana]].
