'''Barrodar's Fantastic Fabrics''' was a [[fabric]] shop located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]] following the city's destruction in {{YearlinkName|1368}}.

==Location==
The shop was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] just off of [[Zhent Avenue]]. 

==Description==
Apartments were located about the shop where the proprietor lived.

==Inhabitants==
The shop was owned by its namesake [[Barrodar]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.
