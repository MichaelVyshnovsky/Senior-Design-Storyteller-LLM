Balthorrs Rare & Wondrous Treasures was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The shop stood a single story tall.
Balthorrs Rare & Wondrous Treasures was located in the southeast section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward on the west side of the Street of the Sword just north of Buckle Alley.
The shop was owned by its namesake, Balthorr Olaskos.
Magic items and stolen property could be acquired within the shop.
