'''Sherm's Herbs''' was a shop in [[Luskan]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The shop was overgrown with vibrantly colored plants.

==History==
Some time around [[1490 DR]], the herb dealer [[Sherm]] was running Sherm's Herbs in the Luskan market district.
