'''Shields Italazar''' was an armorsmithing business in [[Calimport]], [[Calimshan]] around the {{YearlinkName|1370}}.

==Location==
The shop was centrally located in the [[Taorahl Drudach|Taorahl]] drudach (neighborhood), part of the [[Eraré Sabban|Eraré]] sabban (district) of Calimport's [[Hook Ward]]. Shields Italazar was considered to be part of the [[Eye Tyrant's Khanduq]], even if it was located outside the [[khanduq]]'s main building.

==Structure==
Shields Italazar was a simple, one-story building.

==Services==
Famous for the craftmanship of its proprietor, [[Kahim el Tunril]], Shields Italazar mostly dealt in bulk armor orders for the guards of various [[pasha]]s. The business also made copies of individual armor pieces, particularly unique pieces that intrigued Kahim.

==Inhabitants==
The shop was owned and operated by Kahim el Tunril, who oversaw a small staff.
