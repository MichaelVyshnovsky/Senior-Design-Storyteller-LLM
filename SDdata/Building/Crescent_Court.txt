The '''Crescent Court''' was the [[Seldarine|elven]] temple of [[Sehanine Moonbow]] within the fabled city of [[Myth Drannor]].

==Location==
The temple was located on the [[Stroll of Minstrels]], in the city's [[Myth Drannor/Sheshyrinnam|Temple Ward]].

==Structure==
The temple itself was six stories tall, with one level that extended underground. Each of its towers were even taller, at eight stories tall each.

==History==
The temple was constructed in the {{YearlinkName|-1293}}, under direction of [[Miirphys Irithyl]], the fourth [[Coronal]] of [[Cormanthyr]].

==Rumors & Legends==
There persisted rumors that Coronal Miirphys remained within the temple grounds long after his death, as either some manner of [[ghost]], [[baelnorn]], or even as one of the [[Reverend Ones]].
