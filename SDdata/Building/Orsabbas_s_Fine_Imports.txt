Orsabbass Fine Imports was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The shop stood three stories tall.
Orsabbass Fine Imports was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Trades Ward on the northeast side of Vellarrs Lane.
In addition to their normal business, Orsabbass Fine Imports also dealt in stolen property.
