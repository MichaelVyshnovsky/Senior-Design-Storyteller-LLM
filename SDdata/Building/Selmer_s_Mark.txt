Selmers Mark was a large tavern located in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan in the late 14 century DR.
The tavern was located in the southeast section of Suldolphor.
Selmers Mark catered to thrifty patrons, making it a rarity in the Golden City. The tavern served basic beverages including watered ale and wines. Unruly customers were usually thrown to the street via the shuttered windows, much to the entertainment of everyone present.
The tavern was owned by its namesake, Selmer, in the . All of the servers in Selmers Mark were children who worked for room and board.
