Girdles, Bloomers, and Hose was a shop in the Central Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 century DR.
Girdles, Bloomers, and Hose was a one-story building in fair condition.
Girdles, Bloomers, and Hose was known for its high-quality undergarments and intimate apparel. Though it once enjoyed a reputation as Skullport’s premier destination for personalized service, by 1370 DR the shop has seen a decline in its prominence. Despite this, it continueds to offer some of the finest quality goods in the city, albeit at premium prices.
Girdles, Bloomers, and Hose was owned by Marni Sobor, a human and apprentice wizard who inherited the business following her mother’s tragic death in a street robbery. Cheerful and determined, Marni operated the shop primarily to support her two younger siblings.
