'''Bellister's Hand''' was a store in [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
This store was located on the major intersection [[Waymoot (Waterdeep)|Waymoot]], close to its warehouse [[Bellister's House]], in the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Structure==
This shop stood two stories tall.

==Services==
This business sold general goods.
