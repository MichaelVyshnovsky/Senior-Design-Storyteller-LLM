'''The Board Laid Bare''' was a small restaurant located in [[Neverwinter]] in the 14{{Th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The Board Laid Bare was located inside the city's northeast gate, in the [[River District (Neverwinter)|River District]].

==Description==
The Board Laid Bare served no [[beer]], [[wine]], or spirits. Instead, it offered a selection of fresh [[fish]]es and meat cooked in a variety of sauces, as well as fried [[vegetable]]s and [[potato]]es. The prices for such dishes were surprisingly low.

==History==
The Board Laid Bare may have been destroyed during the [[Ruining]] of [[1451 DR]].<ref group="note">Based on location, as most of the River District was destroyed during the Ruining.
