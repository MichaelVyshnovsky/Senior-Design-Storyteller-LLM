The Board Laid Bare was a small restaurant located in Neverwinter in the 14 century DR.
The Board Laid Bare was located inside the citys northeast gate, in the River District.
The Board Laid Bare served no beer, wine, or spirits. Instead, it offered a selection of fresh fishes and meat cooked in a variety of sauces, as well as fried vegetables and potatoes. The prices for such dishes were surprisingly low.
The Board Laid Bare may have been destroyed during the Ruining of 1451 DR.Based on location, as most of the River District was destroyed during the Ruining.
