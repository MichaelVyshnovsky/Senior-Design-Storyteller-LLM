'''Whiteturrets Manor''' was a manor located on the most exclusive street of [[Archenbridge]]. It could be rented by wealthy customers during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
This manor was built on the River Way, the most prestigious district of the city. It was built right next to the [[Bounty of the Goddess (Archenbridge)|temple of Chauntea]], and was close to the glen of [[Grave Hollow]]&mdash;a popular picnic place amongst the locals.

==Services (use for businesses)==
Whiteturrets was usually rented by exclusive guests, or to host parties. The price were steep, but decreased for longer duration or during winter time.
