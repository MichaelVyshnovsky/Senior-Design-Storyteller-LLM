Whiteturrets Manor was a manor located on the most exclusive street of Archenbridge. It could be rented by wealthy customers during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
This manor was built on the River Way, the most prestigious district of the city. It was built right next to the temple of Chauntea, and was close to the glen of Grave Hollow&mdasha popular picnic place amongst the locals.
Whiteturrets was usually rented by exclusive guests, or to host parties. The price were steep, but decreased for longer duration or during winter time.
