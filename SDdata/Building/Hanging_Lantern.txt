The Hanging Lantern was a festhall located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The building stood an impressive six storys tall.
The Hanging Lantern was located in Waterdeeps Dock Ward in a small neighborhood bordered by Ship Street to the west, Pressbow Lane to the east, and Oar Alley to the south.
Volothamp Geddarm revealed that the escorts within the Hanging Lantern were actually dopplegangers working for the Unseen.
The owner of the festhall in the , was Lady Cyrtue.
