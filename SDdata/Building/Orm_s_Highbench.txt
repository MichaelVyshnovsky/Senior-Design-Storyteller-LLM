'''Orm's Highbench''' was the headquarters of a trading company in the city of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
Orm's Highbench was located along the major intersection [[Waymoot (Waterdeep)|Waymoot]], in the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Structure==
This building stood four stories tall.

==Services==
One could hire the services of [[mercenaries]] at this establishment.<ref>
