'''Sarango's''' was a jewelry shop in [[Athkatla]]'s rich [[Athkatla/Gem District|Gem District]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Sarango's shop was located on the northern side of Sarbront's Walk, in a chaotic and clustered easterly Gem District. Across the street from Sarango's stood merchant [[Darastiir Thalongal]]'s abode.

==History==
By the late 14{{th}} century DR, Sarango's, along with a dozen other expensive jewelry stores "made Gem District glitter."
