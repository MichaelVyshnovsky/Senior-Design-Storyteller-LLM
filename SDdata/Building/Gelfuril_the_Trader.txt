'''Gelfuril the Trader''' was an unusual shop in [[Waterdeep]] that was owned by the merchant [[Gelfuril]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was a similar to a pawnshop, though its owner was adamant about it not being one.

==Location==
Gelfuril stood at the intersection of [[Sambril Lane]] and [[Smuggler's Run]],<ref> in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Structure==
This building stood a single story tall.

==Services==
This business operated on a barter system, with the proprietor being willing to sell and purchase nearly anything. He sold a wide variety of goods, These good were typically heavily used or old, but were offered at reasonable prices.
