Gelfuril the Trader was an unusual shop in Waterdeep that was owned by the merchant Gelfuril in the 14 century DR. It was a similar to a pawnshop, though its owner was adamant about it not being one.
Gelfuril stood at the intersection of Sambril Lane and Smugglers Run, in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.
This building stood a single story tall.
This business operated on a barter system, with the proprietor being willing to sell and purchase nearly anything. He sold a wide variety of goods, These good were typically heavily used or old, but were offered at reasonable prices.
