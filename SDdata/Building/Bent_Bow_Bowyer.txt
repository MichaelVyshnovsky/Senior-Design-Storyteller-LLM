The '''Bent Bow Bowyer''' was an excellent archery shop located in the [[Bell Market]] in [[Everlund]].

==Services==
Around [[1370 DR]], the shop was particularly well known for its [[everbright]] [[arrowhead]]s and custom [[bow]]s.
