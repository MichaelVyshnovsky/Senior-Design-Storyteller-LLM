The '''Resonant Horn Inn''' was an inn in the [[aquatic elf]] city of [[Myth Nantar]], operated by the [[shalarin]] couple of [[Po'ioa'nam]] and [[Tu'ara'thal]].<ref>

==Location==
This inn stood in the [[Myth Nantar/Dukar Quarter|Dukar Quarter]] of Myth Nantar.

==Structure==
This building stood two stories tall.
