Scupperden was an eatery located in the Dredge, the lowest level of Skullport. The establishment was known primarily for its stew made of fish, mushrooms and vegetable scraps which sold for 1 cp per bowl. The stew was described as somewhat alarming but unlikely to kill the eater.
Scupperden was a single room built from scavenged wood, sailcloth and rope. Inside, patrons lined up to a twenty foot long serving trough.
