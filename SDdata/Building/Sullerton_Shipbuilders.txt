'''Sullerton Shipbuilders''' was a shipyard that operated in the town of [[Daggerford]] during the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The Sullerton family business was established within a large warehouse located near Daggerford's River Gate. The gate was wide enough that ships could be moved down to the town's docks.

==Services==
Like the businesses that came before it, Sullerton Shipbuilders was solely dedicated on making ships that would traverse the [[Delimbiyr River]].

==History==
Around the {{YearlinkName|1485}}, the local logging industry was plagued with troubles and Sullerton was deprived of much-needed lumber. [[Wilma Sullerton]] sought to rectify the situation with the town's [[Council of Guilds]].
