Ilvastarr Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Ilvastarr.
The villa was located in the east section of Waterdeeps North Ward. It sat on southeast side of the intersection where Suldown Street met Saerdoun Street. A small portion of the west side of the villa abutted Nindabar Street.
The walled compound contained five buildings arranged around a central garden. The buildings were two and three storys tall.
