The Red Pillar Halls was an important bureaucratic location in the City of Brass in 1367 DR.
The Red Pillar Halls were found within the Furnace ward of the city.
The Red Pillar Halls served as the court of the Sultan of the Efreet. Additionally, bureaucrats collected taxes, made appointments, and dealt justice here. Any efreeti hoping to address their grievances here was subjected to an extensive search and needed to dish out hefty bribes to make it into the building.
