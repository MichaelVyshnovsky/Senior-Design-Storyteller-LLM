The '''Shippers' Hall''' was the guildhall of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Fellowship of Salters, Packers, and Joiners]].

==Location==
This building was located along [[Oar Alley]], in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
This building stood two stories tall.

==Reputation==
Like other guildhalls, the Shippers' Hall was considered by locals to be a landmark.
