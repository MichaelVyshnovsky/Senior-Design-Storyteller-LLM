The '''Rearing Hippocampus''' was an [[inn]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The inn stood two storys tall.

==Location==
The Rearing Hippocampus was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] on [[Adder Lane]] to the northwest of [[Gut Alley]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5309}}
