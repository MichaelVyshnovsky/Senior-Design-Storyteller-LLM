Litas Equipment was a general goods store located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the late 14 century DR.
The store was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the Foreign Quarter on Teshwave Street near the South Force Gate. 
A wide variety of equipment and goods could be purchased at Litas Equipment. In fact, aside from food, arms, and armor, anything that was carried by Auroras Emporium was found here as well.
Litas Equipment was owned by a young elf named Lita in the . She lived in a small apartment attached to the rear of the store.
