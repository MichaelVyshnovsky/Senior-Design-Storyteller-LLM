Dobbin Manor was the home of Earl Ruther Dobbin, Lord Mayor of Stagwick.
Dobbin Manor was guarded by a contingent of 50 soldiers.
In 1366 DR, the verbeeg runecaster Basil sneaked into the manor and stole a copy of A Full History of the Dobbins of Stagwick, by Neville Dobbin, the 35 Earl of Stagwick.
