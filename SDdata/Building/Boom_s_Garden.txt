Booms Garden was a unique clothing store, located in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.
This shop was located in along the culdesac The Boomgarden.
This structure was brick warehouse, whose bricks had been blackened in the past by smoke. It had a sky-blue awning above its front door.
This store sold a wide variety of unique clothing found nowhere else, in sizes ranging from small to extra large. They also offered special orders with complete measurements through Auroras Whole Realms Catalogue. Though that catalogued only offered a few of the most choice and tame items that the store had to offer.
The store offered a wide variety of armored undergarments,
Prior to falling under the ownership of the woman Kar this warehouse belonged to a failed gnome inventor.
After having long been stupefied by the existence of armored undergarments, Aurora tracked down Booms Garden and convinced its proprietor to do business through her catalogue.
