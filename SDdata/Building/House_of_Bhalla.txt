The '''House of Bhalla''' was a temple to [[Chauntea]] in [[Immilmar]], [[Rashemen]]. 

==Structure==
The House was a old medium sized stone building near the [[Iron Lord|Iron Lord's]] citadel. 

==Inhabitants==
Around [[1372 DR]], the temple was led by [[Dervarr|Brother Dervarr]].
