Omvudurth was a sprawling black fortress found within the Sunrise Mountains in southern Rashemen.
Omvudurth sat in the shadow of Mount Omvulag on the Mulsanyaar Plateau.
Crude dark stone was used to construct Omvudurths towers.
Omvudurth was the stronghold of a small army of goblins under the command of their chieftain Omvurr.
