The '''Tower of Stars''' was a wizard's tower on [[Traitor's Isle]] on the [[Sea of Fallen Stars]].

==History==
It was constructed and used by [[Jorunhast]], a former Royal Wizard of [[Cormyr]]. It was given its name because its walls were adorned with star mosaics.
