The '''Fiery Flagon''' was a stately old tavern in [[Waterdeep]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]] that served excellent food.

==Location==
The tavern was located on the west side of [[Seawatch Street]], across from the [[House of Inspired Hands]] at the intersection with [[Shark Street]].

==Interior==
It had a secret access tunnel to [[Skullport]] in its cellar.
