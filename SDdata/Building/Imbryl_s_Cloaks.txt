'''Imbryl's Cloaks''' was a [[cloak]] and garment shop in [[Amphail (village)|Amphail]], run by [[Imbryl]]. A group of women from the village gather here to make cloaks, [[breeches]] and [[rainspout]]s, which were hats with a large brim. The shop provides alterations to garments with a three-day waiting period.
