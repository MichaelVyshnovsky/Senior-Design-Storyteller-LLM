The '''Palace of Sly Ravens''' was the seat for the Caliph of [[Liham]], [[Harin al-Hayif]], in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Location==
Located in Liham, the palace did not stand out from the buildings surrounding it.

==Structure==
The Palace of Sly Ravens was made from stucco that had been white-washed. The most distinguishing feature of the palace was the bright red tiles that graced the entire floor of the building.

==Interior==
Modest murals decorated the walls, but the palace was not as opulent as other palaces within [[Zakhara]].

==Inhabitants==
The caliph of Liham called the palace home.
