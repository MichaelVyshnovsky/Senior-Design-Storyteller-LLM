'''Abas for All''' was a shop located in the [[Clothiers' District]] of [[Huzuz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Activities==
Abas for All sold a wide variety of hand-crafted [[aba]]s. They specialized in selling abas depicting [[dragon]]s and [[wyvern]]s since no other clothier in Huzuz could accurately depict the beasts on cloth.

==Inhabitants==
[[Gundar Silvershine]] owned and operated this establishment.
