'''Mermaid and Stars''' was a gambling den in the city of [[Waterdeep]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
This gambling hall fronted the eastern side of [[Copper Street]], three doors north of [[Sulmor Street]], in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of the city.
