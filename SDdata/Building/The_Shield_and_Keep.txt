The '''Shield and Keep''' was a tap house in [[Highcastle]] in late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was located on the south side of town, east of the road leading up to [[High Castle]] itself. It was extremely popular among the local Dalesmen and they expressed their loyalty with repeated patronage.
