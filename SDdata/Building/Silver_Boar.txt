The '''Silver Boar''' was an [[inn]] found in the town of [[Aithelar]] in [[Callidyrr]] on the [[Moonshae Isles]] in {{YearlinkName|1345}}.{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The inn was located near the center of town at the intersection of [[Callidyrr Ride]] and [[Blackdorn's Way]].
