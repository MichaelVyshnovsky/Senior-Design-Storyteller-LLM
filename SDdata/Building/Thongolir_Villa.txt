'''Thongolir Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Thongolir]].

==Location==
[[File:Sea Ward-Assumbar-Cassalanter-Dezlentyr-Eirontalar Villa.png|thumb|right|270px|''Map showing the location of the Thongolir Villa (labeled <code>$49</code>) ''circa'' 1372 DR.'']]
The Thongolir Villa was located in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]],

==Structure==
This building complex consisted of two high-quality buildings, one that was two stories tall and another that stood a single story tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse.

Like many Waterdhavian villas, it was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden.{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2006)/07-06 - Waterdhavian Villas}}<ref group="note" name="evidence">Evidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps.
