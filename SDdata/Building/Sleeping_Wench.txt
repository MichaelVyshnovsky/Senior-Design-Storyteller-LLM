The Sleeping Wench was a tavern in Dock Ward of Waterdeep. 
This tavern was located on the corner of Pressbow Lane and Snail Street.
The Sleeping Wench stood three stories tall.
This tavern could be rather rowdy, but less so compared to the Sleeping Snake and had a comparably better clientele. There was a greal deal of dancing on tables and music provided by horns and lutes.
