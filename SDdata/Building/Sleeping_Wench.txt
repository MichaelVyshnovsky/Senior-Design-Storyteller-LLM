The '''Sleeping Wench''' was a tavern in [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]]. 

==Location==
This tavern was located on the corner of [[Pressbow Lane]] and [[Snail Street]].<ref>

==Structure==
The Sleeping Wench stood three stories tall.

==Atmosphere==
This tavern could be rather rowdy, but less so compared to the [[Sleeping Snake]] and had a comparably better clientele. There was a greal deal of dancing on tables and music provided by [[horn]]s and [[lute]]s.
