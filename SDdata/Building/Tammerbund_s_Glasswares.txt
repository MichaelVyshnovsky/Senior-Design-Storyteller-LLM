Tammerbunds Glasswares was a glass goods shop in the city of Waterdeep in the late 14 century DR.
This shop was located along Mendever Street, in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep.
This shop stood three stories tall.
Circa 1372 DR, Tammerbunds was operated by a trio of gnome women.
