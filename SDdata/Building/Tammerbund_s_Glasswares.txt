'''Tammerbund's Glasswares''' was a [[glass]] goods shop in the city of [[Waterdeep]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
This shop was located along [[Mendever Street]], in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Structure==
This shop stood three stories tall.

==Inhabitants==
''Circa'' [[1372 DR]], Tammerbund's was operated by a trio of [[gnome]] women.
