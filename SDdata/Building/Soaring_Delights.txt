Soaring Delights was a restaurant located in the Castle Ward of Waterdeep during the 15 century DR.
The restaurant was built inside the hollow Hawk Man, one of the Walking Statues of Waterdeep, and was located on the first floor.
The dining area of the restaurant was elegantly decorated, including details such as candles lighting the tables.
The Soaring Delights served fine dishes made of fish, cheese and vegetables paired with wines. The dining room was managed by Shani and Vonda, two Turmishan sisters. In the kitchen Adeyo, Shanis husband, elaborated elegant culinary creations.
