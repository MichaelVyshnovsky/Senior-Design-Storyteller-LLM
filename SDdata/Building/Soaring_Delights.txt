'''Soaring Delights''' was a restaurant located in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/The Map with No Names|6-8,12}}

==Location==
The restaurant was built inside the hollow [[Hawk Man]], one of the [[Walking Statues of Waterdeep]], and was located on the first floor.

==Structure==
===Atmosphere===
The dining area of the restaurant was elegantly decorated, including details such as [[candle]]s lighting the tables.

==Services==
The Soaring Delights served fine dishes made of [[fish]], [[cheese]] and [[:Category:Vegetables|vegetables]] paired with [[wine]]s. The dining room was managed by [[Shani]] and [[Vonda]], two [[Turmishan]] sisters. In the kitchen [[Adeyo]], Shani's husband, elaborated elegant culinary creations.

==History==
Originally catering  to the building's well off residents, the restaurant was opened to the general public during the {{YearlinkName|1492}}.
