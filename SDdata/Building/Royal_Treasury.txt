The '''Royal Treasury''' of [[Cormyr]] held the wealth of [[Suzail]] and the entire nation.

==Location==
The treasury was located directly beneath [[Castle Obarskyr]].

==Defenses==
The Royal Treasury was rumored to be heavily warded with [[magic]], [[trap]]s, and monstrous guardians.
