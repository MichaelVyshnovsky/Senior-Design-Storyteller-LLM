The '''Drink''' was a seedy tavern in [[Tilverton]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The tavern was smoky, stale and dim, with light provided by a few high windows and [[oil lamp]]s, and fitted with wonky and warped furnishings. It was reasonably popular at night, but still very quiet.

==Location==
The Drink was hidden in the dark back alleys of Tilverton.

==Inhabitants==
The Drink was attended by the barfly [[Ponter]] and the professional thief and underworld agent [[Orrag]].
