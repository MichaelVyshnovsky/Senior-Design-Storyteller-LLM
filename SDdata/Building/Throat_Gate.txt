The '''Throat Gate''' was the eastern entry point into lower half of the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid–14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The Throat Trail Gate sat on the eastern side of the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] on the southern bank of the [[River Tesh]]. It was situated on the [[Throat (road)|Throat]] where the road entered the Foreign Quarter to connect with [[Bridge Street]].

==Defenses==
The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers.

==Activities==
The Throat Gate provided access to the Foreign Quarter from the east for land-based traffic, mostly from [[Yûlash]].
