The '''Temple of Chauntea''' was a house of worship dedicated to the [[Chauntea|Goddess of Agriculture]] located in the city of [[Almorel]] on the [[Lake of Mists]] in the [[Hordelands]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The Temple was located in the northwest section of Almorel near the shore of the Lake of Mists. It was one of the larger single buildings found in the city.
