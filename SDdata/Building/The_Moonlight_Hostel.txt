The Moonlight Hostel was a hostel and a soup kitchen located in the Crows End neighborhood of Ravens Bluffin the late 14 century DR.
The Moonlight Hostel was offering food and lodgings to its guests, but its most well-known operation was a charity that provided food for those unable to feed themselves.
The Moonlight Hostel was established by a Living City adventurer only known as Drakir Dwarf, who made a living by aiding the army of the Ravens Bluff and attacking enemy forces during the Three Women War in the Vast. In the , the Ravens Bluff Trumpeter reported that The Moonlight Hostels most recent charity drive raised over 200,000 gp.
