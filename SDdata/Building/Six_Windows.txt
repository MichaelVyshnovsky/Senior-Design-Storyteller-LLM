Six Windows was a boarding house located in the town of Triboar, within the Dessarin Valley.
Despite its name, the inn seemed to have dozens upon dozens of windows.
The building was pretty rundown and drafty.
Six Windows offered rooms for rent to those individuals who could not find a spot at the Everwyvern or Northshield House.
As of the , Six Windows was managed by Jaunda, who sold the items, clothes, and equipment that had been left within the inn over the years.
Around the , it was run by the senile old widow, Tolmara Hysstryn. She had a propensity for spying upon her guests through keyholes in the doors.
