The Burst Boil, which was also simply called the Boil, was a tavern located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the midâ€“14 century DR.
The tavern was located in south Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter just north of Zhent Avenue. 
The building appeared to be a simply hovel with the goblin word for "rest" scratched into the front door.
The Burst Boil only allowed humanoid patrons. Any humans entering the tavern had mugs of ale thrown at their heads and were told to leave. A demihuman who entered the Burst Boil risked being stabbed and killed. A bowl of gruel here, which including flies, cost 1 sp and an ale was 5 cp.
