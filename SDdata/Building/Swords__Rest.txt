The Swords Rest was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The tavern stood one story tall.
The Swords Rest was located in the northern section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward on the east side of the High Road where it was intersected by Telshambras Street.
The tavern was a good place to hire mercenaries.
Elgorel was the bartender at the Swords Rest.
