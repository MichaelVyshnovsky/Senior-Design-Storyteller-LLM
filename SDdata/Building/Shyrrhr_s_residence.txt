'''Shyrrhr's residence''' was a row house and the home of Lady [[Shyrrhr]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The home stood three stories tall. 

==Location==
Shyrrhr's residence was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on the south side of [[Waterdeep Way]]. The rear of the house faced an unnamed alley next to [[Gem Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5260}}
