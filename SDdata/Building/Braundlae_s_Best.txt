'''Braundlae's Best''' was a restaurant and tavern on the promenade below the [[Roving Dragon]] in [[Suzail]], [[Cormyr]] and staffed by [[Braundlae]].

==Food and drink==
The restaurant served live [[eel]]s in [[mint]]-and-[[lime]] hot sauce and [[sausage]] rolls with [[Silver dragon sauce]].
