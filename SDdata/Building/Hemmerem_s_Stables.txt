'''Hemmerem's Stables''' was a shop located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The three buildings that composed the stables were one and two stories tall.

==Location==
Hemmerem's Stables was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]]. The front of the stables sat along the west side of [[Slop Street]] while the rear of the buildings faced [[Kolat Towers]] and [[Weeping Maiden's Run]]. The southern building sat on the west end of [[Grocer's Lane]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}

==Inhabitants==
The stables were owned and run by [[Hemmerem]] in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.
