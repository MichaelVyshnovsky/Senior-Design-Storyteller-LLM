Hemmerems Stables was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The three buildings that composed the stables were one and two stories tall.
Hemmerems Stables was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter. The front of the stables sat along the west side of Slop Street while the rear of the buildings faced Kolat Towers and Weeping Maidens Run. The southern building sat on the west end of Grocers Lane.
