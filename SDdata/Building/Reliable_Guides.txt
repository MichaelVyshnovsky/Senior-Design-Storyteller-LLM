'''Reliable Guides''' was a shop located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The shop was located on the [[Throat (road)|Throat]] outside of the [[Throat Gate]] of the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]]. 

==Activities==
The shop hired out guides to take [[adventurer]]s through the ruins of Zhentil Keep following the city's destruction in the {{YearlinkName|1368}}. Guides cost 1 [[gp]] per day of use and usually consisted of children.

==Inhabitants==
The shop was owned a pair of brothers named [[Goartet]] and [[Jenzar]].
