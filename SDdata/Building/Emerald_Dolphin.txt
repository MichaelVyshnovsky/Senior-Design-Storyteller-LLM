The Emerald Dolphin was a quality bar located in the city of Iljack, Chondath.
Located near Iljacks Warehouse District, the Emerald Dolphin offered its guests quality and reasonably-priced drinks.
At some point before the , Adamar Session, Bard of Starmantle, visited the Emerald Dolphin during his twelve-year adventuring trek across the Reach. The drinking establishment was praised in the bards guide to the Vilhon Reach.
