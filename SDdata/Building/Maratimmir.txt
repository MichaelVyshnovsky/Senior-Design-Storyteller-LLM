'''Maratimmir''' was the [[wikipedia:en:Duchy|ducal]] seat of [[Ankaram]] and the official home of Duchess [[Vajra Valmeyjar]] of [[Tethyr]],<ref name=LoI1:T-p79> though she spent most of her time at the royal palace of [[Faerntarn]].<ref name=LoI1:T-p78>

==Location==
Maratimmir was located about 30 miles east of the city of [[Myratma]] along the southern bank of the [[River Ith]] within the confines of [[Calimmon|County Calimmon]].<ref name=LoI1:T-p79/>

==Structure==
The fortified palace was built in [[Calimshan|Calishite]] style. Some of the buildings on the grounds had been destroyed during the [[Tethyrian Interregnum#Reclamation Wars|Reclamation Wars]].<ref name=LoI1:T-p79/>

==History==
The palace was the former home of the duke of [[Ankaram|Ankramir]] before the [[Tethyrian Interregnum|Interregnum]].<ref name=LoI1:T-p79/>
