Erro Alhandrars Nets was a small shop that sold fishing nets imported from Sheirtalar and the Tashalar, located on Saerdoun Street in the North Ward of Waterdeep in the late 15 century DR.
Erro Alhandrars shop shared a three-story-and-an-attic building with a number of other businesses. The building stood on the southern side of Saerdoun Street, three doors west from the intersection with Thunderstaff Way. Other tenants of the office building were Tantra Jaressras Fine Gowns, Demra Samdros Best Beds, Laskalans Lamps and Lanterns, and Vressas Sweep Begone, as well as Tigers Eye at one point.
Erro Alhandrars Nets was established sometime before the building changed management in the , now in the hands of the gnome named Hardyn Glorjarn, who took over the buildings attic.
