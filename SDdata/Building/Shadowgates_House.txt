The '''Shadowgates House''' was an exclusive club located in [[Athkatla]]'s [[Athkatla/Gem District|Gem District]].

==Location==
The Shadowgates House was a large private building in the westerly part of the Gem District. It stood on the [[Alaith's Walk]], south of the famous [[Flamewater Fountain]] and immediately west of the [[Dancing Dolphins House]].

==History==
The Shadowgates House was already a well-known private club for Athkatlan wealthy and noble women by the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. The Shadowgates House was known to most city inhabitants as the place where "crones go to party and say nasty things in private."
