The '''Spice Gate''' was one of three terrestrial entry points into the city of [[Suldolphor]] in far eastern [[Calimshan]].

==Location==
The gate was located along the eastern side of Suldolphor's exterior wall, facing [[Talagath's Gauntlet]].

==Activities==
The Spice Gate remained open from dawn until dusk.
