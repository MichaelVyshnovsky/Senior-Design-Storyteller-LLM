'''Snome Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Snome]].

==Location==
[[File:Trollkill Street CoS-map.png||thumb|273px|''The location of Snome Villa (labeled 5) on a circa [[1368 DR]] map.'']]
The Snome Villa was located in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]],

==Structure==
Like many Waterdhavian villas in the North Ward, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2006)/07-06 - Waterdhavian Villas}}<ref group="note" name="evidence">Evidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps. with elaborate adornments. Inside the walls of the estate were two high-quality buildings that both stood two stories tall, one being a mansion and the other a guesthouse.
