Snome Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Snome.
|thumb|273px|The location of Snome Villa (labeled 5) on a circa 1368 DR map.
The Snome Villa was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep,
Like many Waterdhavian villas in the North Ward, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped gardenEvidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps. with elaborate adornments. Inside the walls of the estate were two high-quality buildings that both stood two stories tall, one being a mansion and the other a guesthouse.
