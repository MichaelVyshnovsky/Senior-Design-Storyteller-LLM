'''Vellard Sisters Shipwright''' was a shipbuilding business that operated out of the city of [[Tsurlagol]] in [[the Vast]], ''circa'' the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==History==
By the late 15{{th}} century DR, Vellard Sisters Shipwright was the main competitor of [[Selvard Haeleophar]], Master Shipbuilder of [[Telflamm]]. Vellard Sisters assembled three vessels at the time, delivering "bare-timbers but navigable" [[raker]]s or battle-[[caravel]]s per, one per eighteen days.
