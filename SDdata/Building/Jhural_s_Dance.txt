'''Jhural's Dance''' was a [[festhall]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The festhall stood three stories tall. 

==Location==
Jhural's Dance was located in the northwest corner of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on the southwest side of [[Swords Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5260}}
