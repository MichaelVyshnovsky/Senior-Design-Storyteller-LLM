'''Thond Glass and Glazing''' was a [[glass]]wares shop in the city of [[Waterdeep]], owned by [[Jhalassan Thond]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
This shop was located along [[Sleepers' Walk]], in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of Waterdeep.<ref>

==Structure==
This shop stood two stories tall.

==Services==
This shop sold various glassware goods.
