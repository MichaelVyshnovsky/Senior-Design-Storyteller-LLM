The Hidden House was a subterranean temple dedicated to Leira located beneath the city of Westgate in 1358 DR.
The temple was located in the northwest section of the city beneath the intersection of Wagonwheel Ride and Westgate Market Street.
The temple was run by High Mistmistress Halautha Immerstar. Halautha was assisted by 6 other priests and 4 followers.
