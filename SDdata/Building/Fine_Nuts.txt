Fine Nuts, which was also called Khostal Hannass Fine Nuts, was a shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The building was a single story tall.
Fine Nuts was located in the southeast section of the Dock Ward on Spices Street just to the west of where it was intersected by Wharf Street.
The shop specialized in the sale of nuts from all over Toril.
