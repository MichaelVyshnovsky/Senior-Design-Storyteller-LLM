The '''Mosque of the Venerable Kor''' was a temple dedicated to [[Kor]] in the city of [[Rog'osto]] in the {{YearlinkName|1367}}.{{AQ-time}}

==Structure==
This temple was constructed of white-washed [[limestone]] and covered in intricate, psychedelic mosaics of colored glass that reflected [[the sun]].

==Inhabitants==
The [[priest]]s that inhabited this temple were unusually pragmatic and open-minded.
