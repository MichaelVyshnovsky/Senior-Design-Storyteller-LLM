Sarsantyrs Tapestries & Draperies was a type of textiles shop in the city of Waterdeep.
This store was located along Vondil Street, in the North Ward of Waterdeep.
This building stood two stories tall.
Sarsantyrs was operated by the couple Embre Sarsantyr and Raustril Sarsantyr.
