'''Zulpair Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Zulpair]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] on [[Delzorin Street]] in between [[Diamond Street]] and [[Flint Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The walled compound contained three separate buildings with the largest standing in the southwest corner. Each building was three stories tall.
