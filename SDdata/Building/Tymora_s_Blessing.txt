'''Tymora's Blessing''' was a [[tavern]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The tavern stood a single story tall.

==Location==
Tymora's Blessing was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]]. The tavern was located along [[Snake Alley]] in the southwest section of the Adventurers' Quarter next to [[Helm's Hall]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}
