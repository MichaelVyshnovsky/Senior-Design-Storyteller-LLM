The Sheltering Hand was a temple to Tymora in Waymoot, Cormyr.
The temple provided healing services to those who took risks even if they werent followers of Tymora. The clergy believed that helping members of other faiths might one day lead them to follow Tymora. In addition to healing, patrons often received hot meals and a purse full of coins when they departed the temple.
The Chancepriest of the temple was a dignified man named Gothric.
Because of their affluent leadership, the temple housed a guardian golem that would defend the temple against any attack.
