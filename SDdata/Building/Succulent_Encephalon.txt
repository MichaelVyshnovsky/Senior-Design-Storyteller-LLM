The '''Succulent Encephalon''' was a performance eatery in the eastern [[Ring Caverns (Oryndoll)|Ring Caverns]] of [[Oryndoll]].

==Provender==
This [[festhall]] was well known throughout the city for the size of its menu. The festhall had brains from many different creatures available for consumption. 

==Entertainment==
The [[Nourisher Creed]] provided entertainment that was sensual in nature. Many of the eaters were high-ranking members of the Nourishers.
