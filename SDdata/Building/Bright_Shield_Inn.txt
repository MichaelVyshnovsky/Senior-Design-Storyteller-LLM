The Bright Shield Inn was an inn located in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan in the late 14 century DR.
The inn was located in the southwest section of Suldolphor near the Mists Gate.
The sprawling compound consisted of six connected buildings, all kept exceptionally clean. A metal shield was hung outside.
The Bright Shield Inn was popular among adventurers, mercenaries, and similar folk. Patrons had access to medical services, warm baths, and a series of secret rooms.
