'''Gost Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Gost]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the southwest section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. It sat on the southwest corner of [[Gelthoon Street]] where it met [[Golden Serpent Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
Two buildings faced each other from opposite sides of a garden within the walled compound. The buildings were three and four stories tall.
