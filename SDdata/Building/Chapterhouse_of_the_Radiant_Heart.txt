This building was the chapter house for the Waterdhavian branch of the Most Noble Order of the Radiant Heart.
This building was located along the east side of Whaelgond Way in the North Ward of Waterdeep, about two doors down from the Jardeth Villa.
The chapterhouse was constructed of stone and stood three stories tall with a cellar below ground. The entrance to the building was a set of double door, overshadowed by a roofed porch that was supported by stout stone pillars.
