'''Sharkroar, Horth Shalark's Broadsheets''' was a business located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood one story tall. 

==Location==
Sharkroar, Horth Shalark's Broadsheets was located in the north section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on the south side of [[Trader's Way (Waterdeep)|Trader's Way]] just north of the [[Market (Waterdeep)|Market]].

==Inhabitants==
The shop was owned by its namesake, [[Horth Shalark]] in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.
