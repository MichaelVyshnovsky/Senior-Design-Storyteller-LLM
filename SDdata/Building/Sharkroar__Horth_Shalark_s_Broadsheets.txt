Sharkroar, Horth Shalarks Broadsheets was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The building stood one story tall. 
Sharkroar, Horth Shalarks Broadsheets was located in the north section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward on the south side of Traders Way just north of the Market.
