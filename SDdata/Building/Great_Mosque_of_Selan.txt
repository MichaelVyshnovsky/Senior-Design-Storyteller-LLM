The '''Great Mosque of Selan''' in [[Medina al-Afyal]] was the largest mosque to the [[Zakharan pantheon|Zakharan]] goddess of beauty throughout the [[Zakhara|Land of Fate]] as of [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Location==
The Great Mosque was found in Medina al-Afyal on the island of [[Afyal]] in [[Bahr al-Ajami]] in [[east Zakhara]]. The structure flanked the city proper.

==Structure==
The Great Mosque also housed a university.

==Activities==
The Great Mosque was the center of worship for Selan's faithful in Zakhara.

==Inhabitants==
The priests who worshiped and taught here was all staunch [[moralist]]s, but they tolerated [[ethoist]]s and [[pragmatist]]s.
