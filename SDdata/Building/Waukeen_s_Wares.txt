'''Waukeen's Wares''' was a business located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood two stories tall.

==Location==
Waukeen's Wares was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]]. The shop was located on the western end of [[Grocer's Lane]] where it was joined by [[Snake Alley]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}
