'''Dunstul's Pump''' was a well-known landmark in the [[Athkatla/Trades District|Trades District]] of [[Athkatla]] in [[Amn]].

==Description==
Dunstul's Pump was a source of fresh drinking water available to all inhabitants of the city free of charge. It was also a popular meeting place for the locals.
