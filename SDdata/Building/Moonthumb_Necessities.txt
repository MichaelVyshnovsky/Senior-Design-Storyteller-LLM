'''Moonthumb Necessities''' was a general goods store that catered to [[adventurer]]s located in [[Tulmon]] along the [[Lake of Steam]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p165">

==Activities==
The shop was located on [[Ooloolar Street]] in Tulmon.<ref name=EotSS-p165"/>

==Activities==
The shop was known for its great selection of [[rope]]s and most Tulmonites came there for their rope needs. Additionally, just about any piece of adventuring equipment could be found with the shop's crammed confines or cellar.<ref name=EotSS-p165"/>

==Inhabitants==
Moonthumb Necessities was owned by [[Khaligeira Moonthumb]].<ref name=EotSS-p165"/>
