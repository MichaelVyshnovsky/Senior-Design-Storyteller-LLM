The '''Kraljaom Distillery''' was a famous producer of [[ale]]s, [[beer]]s, [[cider]]s, [[wine]]s, and other [[alcohol]], located in the western half of the city of [[Myratma]].

==Notable Products==
Many of the distillery's wares were known throughout [[Faerûn]], as they were sold both locally and through [[Aurora's Emporium]]. Among them were [[Purple Hills Cider]], [[Shanale]] lagers, and wines made from [[grape]]s found only in [[County Calimmon]].
