The Kraljaom Distillery was a famous producer of ales, beers, ciders, wines, and other alcohol, located in the western half of the city of Myratma.
Many of the distillerys wares were known throughout Faerûn, as they were sold both locally and through Auroras Emporium. Among them were Purple Hills Cider, Shanale lagers, and wines made from grapes found only in County Calimmon.
