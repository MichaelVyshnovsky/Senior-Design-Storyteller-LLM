The '''Halls of Memory''' were part of the complex of the [[Golden Mosque]] in [[Huzuz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Location==
This building could be found in the [[Pilgrims' District]] of the City of Delights.

==Structure==
The Halls of Memory was a small 2-storey building found behind the main hall of the mosque.

==Activities==
This building was often used for special ceremonies such as weddings, funerals, and special commemorations. The [[Khalil al-Assad al-Zahir|Grand Caliph]] was often married here.

Couples married here were believed to be blessed with luck and prosperity.
