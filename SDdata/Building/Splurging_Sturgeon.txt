The Splurging Sturgeon was an inn in the city of Baldurs Gate.
It was a small two-story building located in the northeast region of the city.
Rooms were spartan and the sheets threadbare, and the inn had a rowdy atmosphere, but for all that it was decent and cheap.
As of 1482 DR, the Splurging Sturgeon served as a popular location for seafood dishes from around Faerun.
