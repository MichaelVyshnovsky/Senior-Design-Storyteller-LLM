The '''Lodge of Luck''' was a shrine of [[Tymora]] in [[Halfhap]], on the edge of [[Cormyr]] and the [[Stonelands]].

It was an ostentatious structure built from white [[marble]]. ''Circa'' [[1369 DR]], it was overseen by [[Salbaril Fortunato]].
