The Gardens of Markharh was a public garden located in the city of Dhaztanar in Semphar during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The Gardens of Markharh were located within the Furdah Rustauk District in southwest Dhaztanar. The sat in the northeast section of the district just a block from the walls of the Madinat.
The small wall enclosed the gardens and they were accessible by a single road.
