'''Shipmasters' Hall''' was an [[inn]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The inn stood three storys tall.

==Location==
Shipmasters' Hall was located in the southeast section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] just off of [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]] and [[Cedar Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5309}}
