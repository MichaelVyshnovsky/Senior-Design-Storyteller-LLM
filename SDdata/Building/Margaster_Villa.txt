'''Margaster Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Margaster]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] within a neighborhood enclosed by [[Suldown Street]] and [[Vhezoar Street]]. Margaster Villa sat on [[Stabbed Sailor Alley]] and [[Shattercrock Alley]] within the neighborhood.{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The walled compound contained four buildings arranged around a central garden. All four buildings stood two storys tall.
