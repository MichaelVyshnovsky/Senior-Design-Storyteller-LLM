The Zhentilar Headquarters was the base of operations for the Zhentilar in Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The Headquarters was located in south Zhentil Keep in the central section of the Foreign Quarter along Zhent Avenue. 
A massive armory was located beneath the building. This area also contained cells reserved for soldiers who broke their oaths and citizens suspected of treason.
