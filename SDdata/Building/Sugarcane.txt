The Sugarcane was a seedy tavern in the city of Lheshayl, Shining Plains, circa 1370 DR.
The Sugarcane was located near the poor area of Lheshayl.
At some point before the , Adamar Session, Bard of Starmantle, visited the Sugarcane during his twelve-year adventuring trek across the Vilhon Reach. The establishment received a notable notion in the guide published by Adamar.
