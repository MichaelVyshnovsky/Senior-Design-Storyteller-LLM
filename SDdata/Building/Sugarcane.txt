The '''Sugarcane''' was a seedy tavern in the city of [[Lheshayl]], [[Shining Plains]], ''circa'' [[1370 DR]].

==Description==
The Sugarcane was located near the poor area of Lheshayl.

==History==
At some point before the {{YearlinkName|1370}}, [[Adamar Session]], [[Bard]] of [[Starmantle]], visited the Sugarcane during his twelve-year adventuring trek across the [[Vilhon Reach]]. The establishment received a notable notion in the guide published by Adamar.
