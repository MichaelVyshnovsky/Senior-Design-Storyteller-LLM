'''St. Noradnar's Hermitage''' was a remote [[Ilmatari]] hermitage named for and founded by the saint [[Noradnar]].

==Activities==
This hermitage was devoted to teaching its [[monk]]s a defensive form of martial arts that was developed by the founder, Saint Noradnar. This style of martial arts was centered around disarming and disabling one's opponents, using their own moves against them.
