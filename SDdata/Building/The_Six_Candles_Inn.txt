The '''Six Candles Inn''' was a discreet [[inn]] located in the city of [[Ordulin]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Location==
The inn was located in the northeast section of the city.

==Inhabitants==
The Six Candles Inn was old as of 1358 DR. Its poor lighting and dark rooms made it a perfect location for shady meetings.
