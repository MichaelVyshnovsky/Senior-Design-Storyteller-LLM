These people, Marella, Elizza, Moamar, and Rundell, were members of the warlord Myrkyssa Jelans army that waged war on the Vast just a year prior. Samman posted a bill that he sought a special magic wand. The criminals arranged a meeting and sealed the deal at the Trende Cafe.
