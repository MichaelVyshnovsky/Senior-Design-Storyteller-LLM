The Fief of the Lesser Lord was a large walled estate located in the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The estate was located near the center of the city just a stones throw from the Hanging Place and the Raumkremyl.
The Fief of the Lesser Lord was the home of Bludwite Sharp-pick, a dwarf noble in Amorel.
