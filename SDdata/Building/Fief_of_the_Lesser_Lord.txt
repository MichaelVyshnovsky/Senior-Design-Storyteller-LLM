The '''Fief of the Lesser Lord''' was a large walled estate located in the city of [[Almorel]] on the [[Lake of Mists]] in the [[Hordelands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The estate was located near the center of the city just a stone's throw from the [[Hanging Place]] and the [[Raumkremyl]].

==Inhabitants==
The Fief of the Lesser Lord was the home of [[Bludwite Sharp-pick]], a [[dwarf]] noble in Amorel.
