The Butchers Guildhall was the headquarters of the Waterdhavian guild of the same name.
This building was located along Way of the Dragon, in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.
This building stood two-stories tall.
This building contained a cold cellar for guild members to put their overstock in.
Around 1368 DR, the Butchers Guildhall was renovated.
The Butchers guildhall was among some of the more notable and recognizable buildings in the Dock Ward, considered by locals to be a landmark.
