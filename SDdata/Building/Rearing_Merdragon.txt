The Rearing Merdragon was a club in Suzail during the late 15 century DR.
The club was in Suzail.
There was more than one table at the clubs most expensive and overpriced room.
In the lead-up to the Council of the Dragon in the late  the vampire archmage Manshoon mind-controlled Lord Melder Crownrood while the latter was eating at the clubs most expensive and overpriced room.
To Manshoons knowledge, pets were not allowed. Meals included skewered roast quail.
The place numbered among the most overpriced and haughtiest in the city.
