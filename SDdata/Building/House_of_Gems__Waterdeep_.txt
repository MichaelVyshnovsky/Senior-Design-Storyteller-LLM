The '''House of Gems'''  was the headquarters of the [[Waterdeep|Waterdhavian]] [[Jewelers' Guild]].

==Location==
The guildhall was located on the north side of [[Gem Street]] in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]].

==Structure==
The House of Gems was a two-story row house that matched the architecture of most buildings in Waterdeep.
