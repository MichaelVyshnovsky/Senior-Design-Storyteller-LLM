Clockwork Wonders was a workshop and home in the Lower Trade Lanes of Skullport in the late 14 century DR.
Clockwork Wonders was a modest one-story building in fair condition.
The interior of the workshop was densely packed with an assortment of gears, springs, and unfinished devices.
Clockwork Wonders specialized in designing intricate clockwork mechanisms of various sizes and functions. The workshop was known for its clockwork prosthetics. They also created The Waterclock, in collaboration with local Gondar craftsmen.
Clockwork Wonders was owned by two skilled rock gnome inventors, Tohkkal and Tykkyl Burrwarden.
