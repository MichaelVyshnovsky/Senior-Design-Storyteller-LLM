The '''Institute for Sorcerous Imagery''' was a prestigious magic school in [[Eileanar]], the [[enclave]] of [[Karsus]].

==Services==
The college specialized in the magic of [[illusion]]s and [[enchantment]]s. The Dean of Teachers, [[Dillencraft]], taught alongside the body of tutors made up of the [[arcanist]]s [[Driander]], [[Galanti]], [[Sylberg]], [[Coletti]], [[Maleville]], and [[Holstmann]].
