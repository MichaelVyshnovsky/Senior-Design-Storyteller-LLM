The Hrelto was an upscale tavern of Suzail in the late 15 century DR.
The tavern was in Suzail.
In , prior to the Council of the Dragon, the lords Broryn Windstag, Kathkote Dawntard and Delasko Sornstern had offered much money at taverns frequented by servants of noblemen, including the Hrelto, for any enchanted largish hand axe belonging to a noble under the assumption such were likely blueflame items.
