The '''Temple of Anhur''' was a temple dedicated to the worship of [[Anhur]], the [[god]] of war within the [[Mulhorandi pantheon]].{{Cite interactive atlas|490 Skuld}}

==Location==
The temple was located in the the northwest corner of Skuld's [[Skuld/Central Ward|Central Ward]] in the north section of the city.
