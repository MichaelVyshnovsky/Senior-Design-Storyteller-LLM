Prosperity House was a sprawling complex located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The complex was located in south Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the Foreign Quarter on Zhent Avenue. 
Prosperity House included others buildings such as Silverguild Manor, the Prosperity Emporium, the Prosperity Caravan Company, and warehouses and staging grounds for the caravans.
A merchant named Malvred Silverguild owned the entire complex.
