The '''Farmers' Society''' was a guild hall in [[Daggerford]] that catered to the duchy's farmers and agriculturalists. It served over 200 individuals that lived within the town, and outside its walls in the surrounding farmlands.

==Location==
Appropriately enough, it was located in the [[Farmers' Quarter]] of Daggerford{{Cite interactive atlas|3990 Daggerford}}

==Atmosphere==
As the farmers of Daggerford treated each other as if they were family, their hall offered a strong sense of camaraderie.

==Activities==
The hall was essentially a meeting place where farmers could discuss their concerns and share their knowledge with one another.
