The Farmers Society was a guild hall in Daggerford that catered to the duchys farmers and agriculturalists. It served over 200 individuals that lived within the town, and outside its walls in the surrounding farmlands.
Appropriately enough, it was located in the Farmers Quarter of Daggerford
As the farmers of Daggerford treated each other as if they were family, their hall offered a strong sense of camaraderie.
The hall was essentially a meeting place where farmers could discuss their concerns and share their knowledge with one another.
