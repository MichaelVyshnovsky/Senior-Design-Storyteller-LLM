The Dragon Gate was one of two entry points into the portion of Zhentil Keep located on the northern banks of the River Tesh during the midâ€“14 century DR.
The Dragon Gate was located in northeast Zhentil Keep in the Common Quarter. The Dragon Trail outside of the city merged with the Street of Dragons at the Dragon Gate.
The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers. Furthermore, a Zhentilar guardpost and barracks was located just inside the gate.
The Dragon Gate provided access to northeastern Zhentil Keep via the Dragon Trail.
