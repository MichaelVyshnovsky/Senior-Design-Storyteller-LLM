'''Harborwatch Tower''' was one of the major torchtowers of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Trollwall]], located in the city's [[Deepwater Harbor]] and overlooking the naval harbor.Every tower connected to the Trollwall falls under the designation of "torchtower."<ref group="note" name="major">The sourcebook ''[[City System]]'' states that all torchtowers with names are "major towers".

==Location==
This tower was located directly south of the [[Watching Tower]] and north of the [[Sea Chain Gates]].

==Structure==
Harborwatch Tower stood four stories tall and was directly connected to the western Trollwall.
===Interior===
Being a torchtower,<ref group="note" name="torchtower" /> Harborwatch Tower had all the features that were common among them. This included a debriefing room, a small armory, holding cells, storage rooms for the holding of evidence or confiscated items, some bedchambers, and some garderobes.{{Cite web/theedverse/2021-10-17 - Waterdeep's Torchtowers}} Additionally, being a major torchtower, it had six small holding cells that could hold up to ten people, which were used in times of trouble.<ref><ref group="note" name="major" />

==Activities==
Like all other torchtowers, this structure had three beacon-fires that were constantly kept on standby.

==Inhabitants==
Unlike other torchtowers, which were manned by members of the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]], this building was garrisoned by members of the [[City Navy (Waterdeep)|City Navy]].
