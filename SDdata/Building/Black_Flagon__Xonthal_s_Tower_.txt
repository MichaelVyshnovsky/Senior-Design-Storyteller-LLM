The '''Black Flagon''' was a tavern and inn in the village of [[Xonthal's Tower (village)|Xonthal's Tower]] in the  [[Western Heartlands]].

{{Quote|Don't look forward to it too much, it is sure to disappoint you.|[[Volothamp Geddarm]]}}

==Interior==
The tavern had a rustic, backwoods appearance.

==Services==
In the estimation of famed traveler [[Volothamp Geddarm]], The Black Flagon was overpriced and served heavy cuisine caked in gravy.
