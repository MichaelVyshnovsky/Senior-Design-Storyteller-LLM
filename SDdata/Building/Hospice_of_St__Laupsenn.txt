The '''Hospice of St. Laupsenn''' was a temple of [[Ilmater]] in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] in the late 14{{th}} and late 15{{th}} centuries [[DR]].{{Cite adventurers league/Unsafe Harborage|8}}
{{quote|I once spent weeks convalescing in the Hospice of St Laupsenn after a nasty bout of ruddy pox. For all their kindness, leaving that place behind felt like freedom to me.|Gale}}

==Location==
The temple was located on the south side of [[Undal's Lane]], between [[Black Cat Way]] and [[Andamaar's Street]].

==Description==
The temple had bells that had a solemn sound and they could be used to mark the time.<ref>

==History==
In [[Marpenoth]]/[[Uktar]] of the {{YearlinkName|1368}}, the Ilmatari were among the Waterdhavian clergies who supported Prince [[Haedrak Rhindaun III]]'s claim to [[Tethyr]]. Some of the faithful joined his forces in the [[Reclamation Wars]].<ref>

In the late 1400s DR, [[Gale Dekarios]] was treated for the [[ruddy pox]] at the Hospice of St. Laupsenn.
