Zestras Hot Soups was an eatery in the city of Waterdeep in the 14 century DR.
Zestras was located down on Carters Way in Waterdeeps Southern Ward.
The shop offered its customers servings of salted sprouts, various soups, and spiced sausages in gravy.
Circa the , Fylgard "the Fat" Onister of South Ward, a merchant of ropes, fastenings, and trail-leathers, routinely visited Zestras after sunset and the closing of his stone, along with his sons.
