The '''Moonstone Palace''' (also known as '''Moonflower Palace''') was the home of Queen [[Amlaruil Moonflower]] in the city of [[Leuthilspar]] on the island of [[Evermeet]].

==Description==
It was a huge white structure made from [[marble]], [[alabaster]] and [[rock crystal|crystal]], surrounded by the [[guardian trees]], and situated above the city of Leuthilspar. Its featured slender towers, flying buttresses and sky bridges.

==Interior==
===Dome of Stars===
The throne room was a high and vaulted round chamber ringed with galleries and with a huge glass dome at its top. The dome was made of [[theurglass]] and was composed of a mosaic of stained panels, which turned clear by night in order to give a view of the stars. The room had a dark marble floor. The throne itself was gilded and hovered {{SI|6|ft|long=yes}} from the floor.

===Grounds===
The grounds of the palace were very large, and contained hedge mazes, gardens, parks, and lakes.

===Accommodation===
The rooms of the palace were expansive and offered very good views of Leuthilspar and the ocean and forest.

==Inhabitants==
Apart from Queen Amlaruil herself, the Council of Matrons frequented the palace, in addition to Amlaruil's top [[mage]]s, who had their own towers. There were several lesser palaces in which Amlaruil's family lived.
