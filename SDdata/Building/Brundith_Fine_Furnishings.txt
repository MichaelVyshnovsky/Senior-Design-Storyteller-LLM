'''Brundith Fine Furnishings''' was a shop in [[Athkatla]]'s rich [[Athkatla/Temple District|Temple District]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Brundith Fine Furnishings stood on the [[Godsmoot]], next door to the house of [[Thammas Thous]], a collector, and across the street from the manse of [[Marimmal Theer]], an impresario.

==Activities==
The shop offered its customer finely crafted furniture but its main specialty was various replicas, hidden compartments, and hiding places.
