'''Jardeth Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Jardeth]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] at the corner of [[Delzorin Street]] and [[Whaelgond Way]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The estate was composed of three separate buildings, each of which was two stories tall.
