'''Khammeral's Coins''' was a currency exchange located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building had a single floor.

==Location==
The business was located in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] on the eastern side of the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] just north of the intersection with [[Selduth Street]] and due west of the [[Waterdeep/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]].
