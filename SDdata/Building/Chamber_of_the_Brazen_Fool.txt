The '''Chamber of the Brazen Fool''' was a room inside [[Castle Obarskyr]] in {{yearlink|1357}}.

==Location==
It was distant from the [[Hall of Scrolls and Ledgers]].

==History==
On [[Marpenoth 17]] {{yearlink|1357}}, [[Vangerdahast]] commanded the [[Purple Dragon Knights]] close to him in the Hall of Scrolls and Ledgers to seal off the wing of the palace and then maintain a perimeter as far away as the Chamber of the Brazen Fool, after discovering a hand merged with the floor of the Hall of Scrolls and Ledgers.
