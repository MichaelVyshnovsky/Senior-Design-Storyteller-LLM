The '''Temple of Osiris''' was a temple dedicated to [[Osiris]] in the city of [[Skuld]].

==Location==
This temple was located in Skuld's [[Skuld/Central Ward|Central Ward]], along the southern wall that separated the district from the city's [[Skuld/Merchant District|Merchant]] and [[Skuld/Warehouse District|Warehouse districts]].

==Activities==
This temple acted as the entrance to Skuld's catacombs, the underground chambers wherein the city's citizens were buried.
