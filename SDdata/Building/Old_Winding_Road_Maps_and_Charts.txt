'''Old Winding Road Maps and Charts''' was a dusty shop in the city of [[Waterdeep]], in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], that was operated by the elderly [[Thorauntur Szalmast]].

==Location==
This business stood along the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]], in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Services==
As its name implied, the Old Winding Road sold both [[map]]s and nautical charts.

==History==
Following the death of Thorauntur, creditors looted the Old Winding Road.
