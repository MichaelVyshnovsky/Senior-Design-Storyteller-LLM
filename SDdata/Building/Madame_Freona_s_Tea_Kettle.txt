'''Madame Freona's Tea Kettle''' was an eatery and establishment in [[Phlan]] that catered to [[adventurer]]s and other travelers during the late 15{{th}} century. It was named after and run by the enigmatic Madame [[Freona]],{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|5}} and her five daughters.

==Atmosphere==
It had the reputation of being a safe and relaxing place for those looking to avoid the otherwise chaotic streets of Phlan.

==Services==
In addition to the food and drink offered, adventurers often used the Tea Kettle as a place to take on new jobs in town.

==History==
Some time during the 1480s [[DR]],{{ToD-time}} the [[Harpers]] of Phlan were looking for a band of adventurers to recover an illegally-acquired [[red dragon]] [[egg]] and return it to the Tea Kettle for their safekeeping.{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|6}}

Around that same time, an item of immense power was brought to Freona's Tea House. An agent of the [[Emerald Enclave]] named [[Buhrell Caah]] sought to take the item from its current owner, despite not knowing{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|23}} that it was in fact the tooth of a [[blue dragon]].{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|25}}

==Inhabitants==
Known patrons of Madame Feorna's Tea Kettle included:
* [[Esma]] and [[Eve]], two gossiping old ladies.
* [[Minnitha]], a [[dragonborn]] explorer and treasure-hunter.{{Cite adventurers league/Defiance in Phlan|24}}
* [[Schuyler]], a critic of the culinary arts.
* [[Surruk]], the [[wild elf]] protector of the [[Quivering Forest]].
