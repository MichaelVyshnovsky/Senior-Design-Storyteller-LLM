The '''South Force Gate''' was the southwestern entry point into lower half of the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The South Force Gate sat on the southern side of the [[Force Bridge]] that spanned the [[River Tesh]] where it met the [[Moonsea (lake)|Moonsea]].

==Defenses==
The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers.

==Activities==
The South Force Gate provided access to the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] from the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] and [[Zhentil Keep/Harbor District|Harbor District]] via the Force Bridge.
