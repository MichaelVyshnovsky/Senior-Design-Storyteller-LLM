'''Still Waters''' was an inn located in the eastern part of the village of [[Elmwood]] on the southern side of the [[Moonsea]].

==Structure==
It was a two-story building run by master brewer, [[Enrad Daleborn]], a friendly man whose [[wikipedia:ale|ale]] was sought after in the area and a favorite of the [[River Lis]] ferry crewmen.

==Inhabitants==
The inn was owned by Enrad Daleborn, an honest hardworking man of plain looks, who loved to brew the finest amber drink in the region. [[Elenawen]], one of his serving girls, aspired to become an acolyte of [[Sel没ne]]. While a serious-minded woman, Elenawen loved to flirt with charming [[human]] and [[half-elf]] men who frequented the establishment.

==Prices==
*A bed, bath, food, and drink for 3 gp
*Stable for horses an additional 2 gp
