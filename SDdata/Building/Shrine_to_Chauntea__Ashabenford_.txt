The '''Shrine to Chauntea''' was a small place of worship devoted to [[Chauntea]], the Great Mother, located in the town of [[Ashabenford]] in [[Mistledale]].

==Location==
The shrine sat along the northern side of the [[Moonsea Ride]] near the center of Ashabenford.{{Cite interactive atlas|3800 Ashabenford}}

==Inhabitants==
The shrine was maintained by a young [[priestess]] named [[Jhanira Barasstan]] ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1367}}.
