Lady Raes was an inn exclusively for women found in the city of Saerloon in the .
The inn was located near the center of Saerloon.
This establishment only allowed women, or those convincingly disguised as women, to enter. Lady Raes was meant to be a place of relaxation where women could gather and enjoy a respite from crude comments and lecherous hands. However, some of the clientele of Lady Raes could be just as rowdy and boisterous as men found in other inns.
