The Happy Hippocampus was an inn and tavern found in the harbor district of the city of Procampur in the Vast.
This building was located in Procampurs Harbor district. It had a number of small "bed-and-beer" houses huddled around it.
This building was around three stories tall, with a yellow roof slanting over it. In front of the entrance hung a shingle depicting a hippocampus with a beer stein in its mouth, with bubbles rising to the creatures side.
Together the tavern area and the bar measured  by . There were twelve tables in the tavern area, which would be moved around depending upon the size of groups. Various pieces of nautical equipment hung across the tavern walls, such as belaying pins, nets, and ropes with grappling hooks. Above the bar was hung a stern wheel and above the entrance was hung an astrolabe.
Behind the bar was a kitchen and pantry that measured  by . Inside was a staircase to the basement, where food and liqueur were store. This basement also had a small, yet well-stocked, wine cellar.
Situated next to the bar was a staircase to the second floor. This floor consisted of a large common room and seven guest bedrooms. The door to the third floor was typically locked during working hours, as that was where the staff lived.
Frequented by adventurers visiting the city,
The Happy Hippocampus mostly served seafood at cheap prices and quality. The quality of their drinks varied each night, as the establishments proprietor was often known to trade his homebrew beer for foreign alcohols.
Once the site of a failed brothel, this building was purchased by the mariner couple Bialtor and Rieggei Doogen. They founded Happy Hippocampus proper in the , following Bialtors discharge from the naval branch of the Diamond Legion.
