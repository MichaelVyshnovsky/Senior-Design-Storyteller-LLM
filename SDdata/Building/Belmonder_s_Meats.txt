'''Belmonder's Meats''' was a butcher shop located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The shop stood a single story tall.

==Location==
Belmonder's Meats was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] on the northeast side of the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] where it was joined by the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Dead Trades Ward}}

==Inhabitants==
The shop was owned by [[Morathin Belmonder]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.
