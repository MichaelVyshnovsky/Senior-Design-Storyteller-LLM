The '''Low Lantern''' was a tavern,  dance hall, and theater located in the village of [[Eveningstar]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The Iron Hand was located in the western section of Eveningstar along the [[High Road (Cormyr)|High Road]].

==Inhabitants==
[[Maea Dulgussir]] owned the Low Lantern.
