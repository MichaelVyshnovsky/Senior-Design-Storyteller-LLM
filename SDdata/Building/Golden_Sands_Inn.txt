'''The Golden Sands Inn''' was an inn found in the [[Amn]]ian city of [[Esmeltaran]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The inn was found in northwestern Esmeltaran near the northern gates.

==Services==
The Golden Sands provided very good services at appropriately high prices. It specialized in imported [[Calishite]] drinks, and was owned by the [[Golden Sands brewery]].
