The '''Red Sturgeon''' was a tavern located in the city of [[Almorel]] on the [[Lake of Mists]] in the [[Hordelands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The tavern was actually found just outside of Almorel's northeastern [[East Gate (Almorel)|East Gate]] along the [[Golden Way]].
