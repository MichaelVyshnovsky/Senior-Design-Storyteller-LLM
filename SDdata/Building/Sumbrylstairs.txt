'''Sumbrylstairs''' was an elegantly designed manor located in the [[Temple District (Ravens BLuff)|Temple District]] of [[Ravens Bluff]]. The sophisticated home was located where [[Crothmar's Lane]] intersected with [[Moorland Ride]] and [[Rhabie Promenade]] within the [[Swordspoint]] neighborhood. 

The front of the build was decorated with graceful pillars that led up to magnificent rooftop spires. 

==History==
As of [[1370 DR]], the manor served as the home to the [[elven]] adventurer, [[Talila Dawnsong]].
