The '''Oak and Spear''' was an alehouse in [[Highmoon]], [[Deepingdale]] in late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was predominantly patroned by travelers and minstrels, and was famed for its home-brewed [[ale]].

A draft of ale cost 8 [[cp]] or a recital of a good song.
