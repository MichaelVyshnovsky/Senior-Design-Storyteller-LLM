The '''Noble Hand''' was a temple of [[Helm]] in the city of [[Tsurlagol]] in [[the Vast]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Activities==
In addition to normal temple services, it operated as a school for training professional guards.

==History==
In [[1367 DR]], the Noble Hand was expecting to receive a shipment of [[evil]] artifacts from the [[Temple of the Vigilant Guard]] in the month of [[Calendar of Harptos|Kythorn]]. However, the Helmites and their cargo never arrived.

==Inhabitants==
Around [[1368 DR]], Noble Hand was led by High Watchful Weaponmaster [[Ellym Thourin|Ellym "Catsjaw" Thourin]]. It thrived under Ellym's leadership.

==Reputation==
Noble Hand was considered to be not only one of the five major temples of Helm in the Vast in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], it was considered the most prominent temple of Helm in all the Realms.
