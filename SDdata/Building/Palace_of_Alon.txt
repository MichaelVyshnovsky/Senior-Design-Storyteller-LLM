The '''Palace of Alon''' was the home of the Padishah and royal family of [[Medina al-Afyal]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Location==
The Palace was found in Medina al-Afyal on the island of [[Afyal]] in [[Bahr al-Ajami]] in [[east Zakhara]]. The structure flanked the city proper.

==Structure==
The Palace consisted of many smaller spired and domed structures, most of which were connected by causeways. It was considered to be one of the finest architectural achievements throughout the [[Zakhara|Land of Fate]]. Both magical and mundane methods were used during its construction.

==Inhabitants==
Five [[dao]] worked tirelessly to continually maintain the Palace.
