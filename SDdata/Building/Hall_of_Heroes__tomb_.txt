The '''Hall of Heroes''' was a tomb located within the [[Waterdeep/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]] in [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
The Hall of Heroes was located in the southern section of the City of the Dead.

==Description==
Blue [[marble]] was used for the tomb's walls. The interior was decorated with trophies of famous fallen [[warrior]]s including the [[sword]] hilt of [[Raurlor]]. One of [[Anacaster]]'s [[magic]]al portals within the tomb lead to a large grassy plane where the dead were interred in rows.

==History==
The tomb was used to honor those [[fighter]]s and [[warrior]]s of Waterdeep who died in battle.
