The Hall of Heroes was a tomb located within the City of the Dead in Waterdeep.
The Hall of Heroes was located in the southern section of the City of the Dead.
Blue marble was used for the tombs walls. The interior was decorated with trophies of famous fallen warriors including the sword hilt of Raurlor. One of Anacasters magical portals within the tomb lead to a large grassy plane where the dead were interred in rows.
The tomb was used to honor those fighters and warriors of Waterdeep who died in battle.
