'''Nestaur's Ropes''' was a store in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
This store stood on [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]], across from the [[Sleeping Snake]] and a few doors down from the [[Watermens' Hall]].

==Services==
Nestaur's Ropes sold gear for [[adventurer]]s,<ref> primarily specializing in [[rope]]s.
