The '''Mariners' Hall''' was the guildhall of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Master Mariners' Guild]].

==Location==
This building was located along [[Cedar Street]], just off the [[Way of the Dragon (Waterdeep)|Way of the Dragon]], in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
Mariners' Hall stood three-stories tall.<ref group="note" name="floors">Page 22 of ''Adventurer's Guide to the City'', part of the [[City of Splendors (boxed set)|''City of Splendors'']] boxed set, states that this building is two stories tall. But this is contradicted by the cited sources. The building was in a wattle-and-daub style
===Interior===
The main floor of the Mariners' Hall featured full tavern facilities, a private bar for visiting members of the Master Mariners' Guild. Elsewhere in the building were accommodations, also for visiting members of the guild.

==Reputation==
The Mariners' Hall was among some of the more notable and recognizable buildings in the Dock Ward, considered by locals to be a landmark.

==Trivia==
Behind a loose stone in the minaret atop this roof was a cavity where the [[wizard]] [[Coril]] hid their [[spellbook]]s.
