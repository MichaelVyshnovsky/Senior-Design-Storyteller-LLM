Corondors Tower was a tower located in Suldolphor in far eastern Calimshan near the Arnaden.
The tower was located along the perimeter wall in northwest Suldolphor north of the Golden Gate.
With its own granary, fortified walls, smithy, dungeon, and stables, Elaphs Tower was a fortress in its own right.
Corondors Tower served as a barracks for Suldolphors elite Flame Guard.
