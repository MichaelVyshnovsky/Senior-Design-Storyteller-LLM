The '''Place of Waiting Death''' was a hidden subterranean temple dedicated to [[Talona]] located beneath the city of [[Westgate]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Location==
The temple was located in the northwest section of the city near [[Castle Thalavar]] beneath a branch of the [[King's March]].

==Inhabitants==
The temple was run by Most Fatal Horror [[Ilua Yhestin]]. Ilua was assisted by four other priests and four followers.
