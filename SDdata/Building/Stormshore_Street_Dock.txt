'''Stormshore Street Dock''' was a dock in [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
It was a shorter dock located on the eastern side of the [[Gray Harbor]] at the end of [[Stormshore Street]] in the [[Brampton]] neighborhood of [[Baldur's Gate]].

==Description==
The [[Low Lantern]] tavern was housed within a ship permanently docked at Stormshore Street Dock.
