The '''Shinobugaoka''' was the top university in the city of [[Rukimbaru]] in [[Wa]] ''circa'' {{YearlinkConvert|1775|WY}}.

It was situated on a hilltop with views of the city. It included the largest library in the country, in which some of the books were thousands of years old.
