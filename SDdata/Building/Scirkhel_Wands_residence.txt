'''Scirkhel Wands residence''' was a row house and the home of [[Scirkhel Wands]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The home stood three stories tall.

==Location==
Scirkhel Wands residence was located in the southern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] on the southern side of [[River Street]] near the [[River Gate (Waterdeep)|River Gate]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Dead Trades Ward}}
