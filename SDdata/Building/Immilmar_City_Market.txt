[[Immilmar]]'s '''city market''' was a public square where trade occurred in [[Rashemen]]'s capital.{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The square was located near the center of the city next to the [[Huhrong's Citadel]].

==Description==
Traders and customers were protected from the elements by a great peaked ceiling that covered the entire square. The ceiling was elaborately carved and supported by decorated pillars.
