'''Athal's Stables''' were a stables in the city of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
These stables were located in the Waymoot neighborhood at the end of [[Coachlamp Lane]],<ref> in the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Structure==
Athal's Stables was a two story tall building.

==Services==
[[Mount]]s and gear related to them, such as [[bit and bridle|bits, bridles]], and [[saddle]]s, could be purchased here.
