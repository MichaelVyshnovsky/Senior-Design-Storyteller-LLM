The '''House of Healing''' was the guildhall of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Guild of Apothecaries & Physicians]].<ref>

==Location==
The House of Healing was located along the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]],

==Structure==
This guildhall stood three stories tall.

==Services==
In addition to being a headquarters of the Guild of Apothecaries & Physicians, this building acted as an emergency hospital. medicine, and substances that could be considered [[poison]]s. 

The building also acted as a minor school for student physicians.

==Reputation==
Some [[disease]]d individuals, or people in their service, were known to come all the way to Waterdeep just to visit the House of Healing. And like other guildhalls, the House of Healing was considered by locals to be a landmark.
