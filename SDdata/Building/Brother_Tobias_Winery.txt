'''Brother Tobias Winery''' was a local favorite winery in the city of [[Westgate]] on the [[Dragon Coast]].

==History==
By the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], Brother Tobias' wines were a staple in many of Westgate's inns and drinking establishments, including [[Black Eye Tavern]] and [[Bent Mermaid Inn]]. [[Brother Tobias' 1368]] vintage was considered the especially good year making these bottles popular due to their quality and cheap price.
