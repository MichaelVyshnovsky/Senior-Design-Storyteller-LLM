Brother Tobias Winery was a local favorite winery in the city of Westgate on the Dragon Coast.
By the late 14 century DR, Brother Tobias wines were a staple in many of Westgates inns and drinking establishments, including Black Eye Tavern and Bent Mermaid Inn. Brother Tobias 1368 vintage was considered the especially good year making these bottles popular due to their quality and cheap price.
