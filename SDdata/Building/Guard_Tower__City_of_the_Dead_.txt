This '''guard tower''' was one of the major torchtowers of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Trollwall]], located in the city's graveyard district, the [[Waterdeep/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]].Every tower connected to the Trollwall falls under the designation of "torchtower."

==Location==
This tower was located on the southeastern corner of the City of the Dead. Outside of the City of the Dead, in the [[Trades Ward of Waterdeep]], its exterior stood along [[Wall Way]].

==Structure==
This tower stood four stories tall.
===Interior===
Being a torchtower,<ref group="note" name="torchtower" /> this structure had all the interior features that were common among them. This included a debriefing room, a small armory, holding cells, storage rooms for the holding of evidence or confiscated items, some bedchambers, and some garderobes.{{Cite web/theedverse/2021-10-17 - Waterdeep's Torchtowers}}

==Activities==
Like all other torchtowers, this structure had three beacon-fires that were constantly kept on standby.

==Inhabitants==
Like all torchtowers, this structure was manned by members of the City Watch. Four members of the Watch were always stationed inside of the tower, while one was posted outside to watch for any trouble in the nearby alleyways abutting the City of the Dead.
