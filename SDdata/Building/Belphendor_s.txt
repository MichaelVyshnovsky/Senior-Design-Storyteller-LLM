'''Belphendor's''' was a shop in [[Khôltar]], [[south Faerûn]], that sold plumbing supplies, pipes, valves, and fittings ''circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks archive|68}}{{Cite web/Elminster Speaks/Part 54: Khôltar, Part 5, The Farrgaunlar}}

==Location==
Belphendor's was on the south side of [[Hael Way]] just to the west of [[Elduskryn's Fiery Creations]] and across the street from [[Anthormbrur]], the fancy home of the [[Horthander]] family.
