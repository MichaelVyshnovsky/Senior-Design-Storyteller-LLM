The '''House of Jemuril''' was a rowhouse It was kept closed to visitors and housed the collection of [[magical item]]s she had amassed over the course of her adventuring career.

==Location==
This house was located along [[Tower Trail]], in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
This building was two-stories tall.
