'''Sorkryn's Soft Hides of Scornubel''' was a trader and dealer in [[hide]]s and animals in [[Scornubel]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Services==
The business sourced hides and living beasts from eastern [[Amn]] and [[Tethyr]]. It was linked by [[merchants' map]] to [[Samril's Fine Blades]] in [[Secomber]], who used the cured hides for the hilt wrappings for weapons such [[longsword]]s.
