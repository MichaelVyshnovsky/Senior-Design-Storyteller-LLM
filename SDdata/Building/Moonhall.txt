'''Moonhall''' was the temple dedicated to the goddess [[Selûne]], The Night White Lady, located in the [[Athkatla/Wave District|Wave District]] of [[Athkatla]], the capital of [[Amn]].

==Structure==
Moonhall had a seven-sided conical shape.

==Inhabitants==
''Circa'' the {{YearlinkName|1370}}, Moonhall was run by the aging High Priestess and Lunar [[Aryn Gallowglass]]. She hailed from the nation of [[Tethyr]] and was a successful businesswoman who invested the coin from her three trade ships back into the temple.
