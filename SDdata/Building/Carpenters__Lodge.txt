The '''Carpenters' Lodge''' was the guildhall for the carpenters and other wood-workers in the city of [[Daggerford]] in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|3990 Daggerford}}

==Description==
The structure stood four storys tall.

==Location==
The guildhall was located in the northwest corner of Daggerford at the junction of [[Wall Street (Daggerford)|Wall Street]] and [[Horse Way]].
