The '''Blue Jack''' was a [[tavern]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The tavern stood a single story tall.

==Location==
The Blue Jack was located in the far southeast section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] just a few buildings north of the intersection with [[Waterdeep Way]]. The rear of the tavern sat on [[the Cat's Tail]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5262}}
