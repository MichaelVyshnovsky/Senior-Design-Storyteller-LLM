The Hungry Man, also known as the Hungry Man Inn,In the Shadowdale novel (1989), the inn is called the Hungry Man Inn, but in the 1995 sourcebooks Volos Guide to Cormyr and Cormyr the restaurant is called the Hungry Man.  was a restaurant located in the city of Arabel.
The proprietor was the elderly woman Zehla.
The establishment sold watered down ale and wine known as bullock juice.
