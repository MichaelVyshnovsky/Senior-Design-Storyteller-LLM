The '''Hungry Man''', also known as the '''Hungry Man Inn''',In the ''[[Shadowdale (novel)|Shadowdale]]'' novel (1989), the inn is called the ''Hungry Man Inn'', but in the 1995 sourcebooks ''[[Volo's Guide to Cormyr]]'' and ''[[Cormyr (sourcebook)|Cormyr]]'' the restaurant is called the ''Hungry Man''.  was a restaurant located in the city of [[Arabel]].

==Inhabitants==
The proprietor was the elderly woman [[Zehla]].

==Food and Drink==
The establishment sold watered down [[ale]] and wine known as ''bullock juice''.
