Cromach
Cromachs Smithy was a forge that operated within the town of Daggerford for well over a century.
While it was originally located in the towns Caravan Quarter,
The shop was originally founded by Cromach, a human blacksmith who knew better than to compete with the local weaponsmith at Dervals Bright Blade. Cromach had something of a flair for artistry, as his wares were highly valued for their aesthetics as well as their functionality.
Around the , the smithy was being run by the dwarf called Tholvar Cragjaw. He continued Cromachs legacy by crafting utilitarian metal items for Daggerfords citizens.
There was a saying during Cromachs times that his work was "as shaky as Wayfels best," referring to the the proprietor of Wayfels Smithy.
