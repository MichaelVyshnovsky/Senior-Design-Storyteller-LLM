'''Seawatch Tower''' was one of the major torchtowers of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Trollwall]], located in the city's [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]].Every tower connected to the Trollwall falls under the designation of "torchtower."<ref group="note" name="major">The sourcebook ''[[City System]]'' states that all torchtowers with names are "major towers".

==Location==
This tower stood on [[Westwall Street]], directly across from the western end of [[Grimwald's Way]].

==Structure==
This tower stood five stories tall along the western wall of the Trollwall.
===Interior===
Being a torchtower, Seawatch Tower had all the features that were common among them. This included a debriefing room, a small armory, holding cells, storage rooms for the holding of evidence or confiscated items, some bedchambers, and some garderobes.{{Cite web/theedverse/2021-10-17 - Waterdeep's Torchtowers}} Additionally, being a major torchtower, it had six small holding cells that could hold up to ten people, which were used in times of trouble.<ref><ref group="note" name="major" />

==Activities==
Like all other torchtowers, this structure had three beacon-fires they were constantly kept on standby.

==Inhabitants==
Seawatch Tower, like all torchtowers, was manned by members of the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]].
