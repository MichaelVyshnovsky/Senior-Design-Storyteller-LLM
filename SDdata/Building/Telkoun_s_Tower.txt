'''Telkoun's Tower''' was a [[wizard]] stronghold located in [[High Thorog]] in the [[Nether Mountains]].

==Description==
The stronghold consisted of one building with two double towers. Several [[gargoyle]]s defended the tower, paid and also charmed by [[Telkoun Adranther]]. There was a ''[[stasis sphere]]'' at the last floor of the tower.
