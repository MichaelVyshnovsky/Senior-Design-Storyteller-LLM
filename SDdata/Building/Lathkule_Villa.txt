Lathkule Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Lathkule.
The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeeps North Ward on the southwest side of the intersection of Whaelgond Way with Hassantyrs Street. The south side of the villa lay along the Street of the Manticore.
Four buildings faced each other surrounding a garden within the walled compound. The buildings were three storys tall.
