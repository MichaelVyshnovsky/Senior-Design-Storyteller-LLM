Hall of the Black Waves was an Umberlant temple in the city of Ruathym in the 14 and 15 centuries DR.
In the , when the Luskar waged war on the island of Ruathym and pillaged its city of the same name, the Hall of the Black Waves was left untouched by the invaders.
Around 1358 DR, the head priest of the temple was one Uther Jeroggean, a male shaman. In the late 15 century DR, sometime after the War of the Silver Marches, the rulers of Ruathym ruled from within the Hall of the Black Waves, such as First Axe Vok Dorrg.
