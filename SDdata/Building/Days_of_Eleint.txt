'''Days of Eleint''' was a tavern in the [[Amn]]ian city of [[Esmeltaran]] during the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The tavern was built on the western edge of Esmeltaran near [[Lake Esmel]].

==Services==
The Days of Eleint provided fair services for expensive prices. The place was decorated with the luxuries of [[Tethyr]]ian nobles taken during the [[Tethyrian Interregnum]].
