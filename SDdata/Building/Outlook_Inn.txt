The Outlook Inn was an inn found just outside of Selgaunts walls.
This large establishment was located just outside of Selgaunts west gate along the Way of the Manticore.
The Outlook Inn was a new and clean establishment as of 1358 DR. Since the inn was located outside of Selgaunts walls, they had no problems catering to adventurers when compared to the vast majority of inns and taverns found within the city proper.
