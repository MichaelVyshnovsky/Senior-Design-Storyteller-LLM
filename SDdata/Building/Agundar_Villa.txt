'''Agundar Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Agundar]].

==Location==
The Agundar Villa was situated on the corner of [[Copper Street]] and [[Hassantyr's Street]], in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
This building complex consisted of two high quality buildings, one that was three stories tall and another that stood a single story tall,Evidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps.
