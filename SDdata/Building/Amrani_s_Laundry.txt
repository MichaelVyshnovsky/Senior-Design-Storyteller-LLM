Amranis Laundry was a business located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The building stood one story tall.
Amranis Laundry was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter. The front of the building lay along the west side of Slop Street, while the rear of the building faced Kolat Towers on Weeping Maidens Run.
