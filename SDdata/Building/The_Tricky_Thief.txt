The Tricky Thief was a a magical miscellany and curio shop in the hamlet of Skull Crag in the Storm Horn Mountains of Cormyr in the 14 century DR.
The shop was located on the southern side of the village.
The Tricky Thief was filled to the brim with weird arcane paraphernalia, including stinging magical stones and inflated odd sound-making goat bladders.
In the mid-14 century DR, The Tricky Thief operated at odd hours with long lunch breaks, it catered to curious travelers of the High Road.
