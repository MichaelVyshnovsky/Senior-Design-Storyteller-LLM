The '''North Force Gate''' was the southwestern entry point into the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The North Force Gate sat on the northern side of the [[Force Bridge]] that spanned the [[River Tesh]] where it met the [[Moonsea (lake)|Moonsea]].

==Defenses==
The gate was flanked by a pair of guard towers. Furthermore, a pair [[Zhentilar]] guardposts were located just inside the gate.

==Activities==
The North Force Gate provided access to both the [[Zhentil Keep/Common Quarter|Common Quarter]] and [[Zhentil Keep/Harbor District|Harbor District]] in southwestern Zhentil Keep from the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] via the Force Bridge.
