Oraundro Nempeths was a shop in Khôltar, south Faerûn, circa the .
Nempeths was located on the east side of North Way near Handrornlar, the northern gate through the walls of Khôltar, the Iron City. It was sandwiched between Munsrums Ready Ladle to the north and Harhallos Warmhearth House to the south.
This building was made of stone and the shop had a relatively narrow front facing the North Way.
Oraundro Nempeths sold various binding cords, rope, twine, wire, and a few sizes of chain. It also carried metal rings used as fasteners and wall-mounts.
