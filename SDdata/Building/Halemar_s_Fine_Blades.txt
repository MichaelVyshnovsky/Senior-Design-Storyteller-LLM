'''Halemar's Fine Blades''' was a business in the city of [[Tantras]] in [[the Vast]], located in the northeastern part of the city, owned by [[Halemar]].

Halemar was a weaponsmith and armorer. During the [[Time of Troubles]] of [[1358 DR]], when Tantras expected war with the forces of [[Zhentil Keep]], Halemar was kept busy crafting materiel for the [[Guard (Tantras)|city's army]].
