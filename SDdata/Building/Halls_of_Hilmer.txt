The Halls of Hilmer was an armorers shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The shop stood a single story tall.
The Halls of Hilmer was located in the southeast section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward on the west side of the Street of Bells south of Buckle Alley. The rear of the shop faced Duirs Alley.
Magical and normal armor could be acquired within the shop.
