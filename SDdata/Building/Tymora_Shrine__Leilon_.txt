The '''Tymora Shrine''' was a temple to [[Tymora]] located in the town of [[Leilon]].{{Cite web/D&D Beyond/Sleeping Dragon's Wake}}

==Location==
The Shrine was located on the Leilon town square.{{Cite web/D&D Beyond/Divine Contention}}

==Services==
In addition to serving as a place of worship, the Shrine also served as the only place to purchase [[potion]]s in Leilon during the town's reconstruction  in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. Its inventory was inconsistent and restocked once a tenday. Generally, the Shrine was well stocked with ''[[potion of healing|potions of healing]]'', and often carried a few vials of potions of ''[[potion of climbing|climbing]]'', ''[[potion of growth|growth]]'', ''[[potion of water breathing|water breathing]]'', and ''[[potion of heroism|heroism]]''.

==History==
The original shrine of Tymora was one of the first major temples in Leilon, and stood in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century DR.{{Cite web/D&D Beyond/Storm Lord's Wrath}} The Shrine was overseen by [[Puck Caribdas]], who also sold potions out of the temple.

When Leilon was besieged by the [[Cult of Talos]] in the [[Battle of Leilon]], acolytes of the temple aided in the defense of the town and the townsfolk took care to protect the Shrine.
