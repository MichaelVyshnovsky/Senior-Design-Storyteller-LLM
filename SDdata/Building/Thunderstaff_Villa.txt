'''Thunderstaff Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Thunderstaff]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the north section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] where [[Thunderstaff Way]] met [[Immar Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The villa contained four separate buildings. The largest of the four sat in the northwest corner while the smaller three buildings were found in the southeast corner. The buildings stood two or four stories tall.
