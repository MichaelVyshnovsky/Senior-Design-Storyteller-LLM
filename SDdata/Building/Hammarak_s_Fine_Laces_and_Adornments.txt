Hammaraks Fine Laces and Adornments was a shop that sold feminine garments in the City of Splendors, Waterdeep, in the mid and late 14 century DR.
Hammaraks Fine Laces and Adornments was located in the third building west of Tchozals Race, on Calamastyr Lanes southern side. The shop took over its buildings first floor with rental floors above. The third floor above Hammaraks was rented out to an all-female drinking and dining club called The Lace Harp.
