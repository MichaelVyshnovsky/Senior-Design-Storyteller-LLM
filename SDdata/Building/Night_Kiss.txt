The '''Night Kiss''' was the most notorious tavern in [[Gontal City]].

==Background==
Its owner was a [[spellscar]]red [[tiefling]] named [[Narla Gulmaern]], who had the ability to disrupt [[arcane magic]] and [[ritual]]s with her mere presence, making the tavern a safe and popular place for important meetings and illegal activities alike.{{Cite dragon/366/Gontal|51}}
