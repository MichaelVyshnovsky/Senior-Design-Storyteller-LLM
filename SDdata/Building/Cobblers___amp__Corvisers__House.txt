The '''Cobblers' and Corvisers' House''' was the guildhall of a [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] of the [[Order of Cobblers & Corvisers|same name]].

==Location==
This guildhall was located along [[Soothsayer's Way]], in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]]. 

==Structure==
This guildhall stood two stories tall.

==Services==
This guildhall offered clothing services, specifically the selling or repairing of footwear of all forms.

==Reputation==
Like other guildhalls in Waterdeep, this building was considered by locals to be a landmark.
