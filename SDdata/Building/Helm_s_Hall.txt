Helms Hall was a temple dedicated to Helm, He of the Unsleeping Eyes, located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The temple stood three stories tall.
Helms Hall was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter. The temple sat on Snake Alley in the southwest section of the Adventurers Quarter next door and north of Tymoras Blessing.
