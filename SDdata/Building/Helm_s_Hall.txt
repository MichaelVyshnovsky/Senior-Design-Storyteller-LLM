'''Helm's Hall''' was a [[temple]] dedicated to [[Helm]], He of the Unsleeping Eyes, located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The temple stood three stories tall.

==Location==
Helm's Hall was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]]. The temple sat on [[Snake Alley]] in the southwest section of the Adventurers' Quarter next door and north of [[Tymora's Blessing]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}
