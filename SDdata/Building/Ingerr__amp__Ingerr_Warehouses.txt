Ingerr & Ingerr Warehouses was a warehouse compound located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
Each of the three buildings stood two stories tall.
Ingerr & Ingerr Warehouses was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Southern Ward within the Adventurers Quarter. The buildings sat in the southeast corner of the Adventurers Quarter on Slop Street and Kerrigans Court in between the Safehaven Inn and the Beer Golem.
