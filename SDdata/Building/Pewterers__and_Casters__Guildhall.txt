The '''Pewterers' and Casters' Guildhall''' was the [[guildhall]] of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] of the [[Guild of Trusted Pewterers and Casters|same name]].

==Location==
The Pewterers' and Casters' Guildhall was located along the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]], in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]]. Its back entrance stood along [[the Cat's Tail]] alleyway.

==Structure==
This guildhall stood one story tall.
