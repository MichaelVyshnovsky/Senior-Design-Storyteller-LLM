'''Darkwave Hall''' was a temple dedicated to [[Umberlee]] located in the city of [[Urmlaspyr]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Inhabitants==
The temple was run by High Priest [[Untharym Frostanar]]. Untharym was assisted by seven other specialty priests called [[waveservant]]s and four followers.
