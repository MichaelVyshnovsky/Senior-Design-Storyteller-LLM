'''Muddy Boots and Bloody Blades''', also known as '''Muddy Blades and Bloody Boots''', was a [[tavern]] in [[Vaasa]], located inside the [[Vaasan Gate]], in the mid-to-late 14{{Th}} century [[DR]].

==Structure ==
The sign outside the tavern, bearing its name, was crossed out and made to say "Muddy Blades and Bloody Boots". This reflected the frustrations of the bounty hunters whose work had been cut out and found not many monsters left to slay.

==Interior==
===Atmosphere ===
Muddy Boots and Bloody Blades was frequented by bounty hunters, who would trade ears of creatures such as [[goblin]]s and [[orcs]] for [[gold]].

==Services==
The tavern sold [[wine]] and [[ale]].
