'''Estelmer Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Estelmer]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] on [[Vhezoar Street]]. The villa also touched [[Sulmor Street]], [[Brondar's Way]], and [[Hassantyr's Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
Three buildings were arranged in an "L" pattern within the walled compound. The buildings were two or three storys tall.
