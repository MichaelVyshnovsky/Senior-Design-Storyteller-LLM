'''Scholar's Hope''' was a multi-faith temple-complex in [[Myth Drannor]] that served the followers of various gods of knowledge and scholarly pursuits. It was a place of worship with great significance the Realms, as it offered services to deities from the pantheons of [[elves]], [[dwarves]], and [[human|men]], one of the first of its kind in all of [[Faerûn]].{{Template:Cite book/Cormanthyr: Empire of the Elves|74}}

==Location==
The temples of Scholar's Hope were located on the west side of the [[Sheshyrinnam]] district of the city, on the northern side of [[Sunblade Street]].{{Template:Cite book/Cormanthyr: Empire of the Elves/Map}}

==Activities==
Unified by their faith of deities of knowledge, the clergy at Scholar's Hope maintained shrines dedicated to four different deities, each of were a member of a different pantheon. It housed places of worship for [[Corellon]], patron deity of the [[Seldarine]] and god of magic, arts, and music; [[Oghma]] and [[Deneir]], [[Faerûnian pantheon|Faerûnian]] gods of knowledge and literature respectively; and [[Dugmaren Brightmantle]], [[Morndinsamman|dwarven god]] of discovery, invention, and scholarship.For the purposes of this article, only the 2{{nd}} edition portfolios of these deities are included, as that is the given edition of ''[[Cormanthyr: Empire of the Elves]]''.

The core tenet of Scholar's Hope was the open sharing of knowledge between the unified peoples of these various faiths, as members of the great city of knowledge in which they all lived.

==Structure==
The unified temple-complex comprised three separate and distinct structures. The adapted tree-dwelling stood some {{SI|200|ft}} above ground level and housed the shrine to Deneir, the two-story building at ground level housed the library shared by the various faiths, and cellar beneath held the subterranean shrine to Dugmaren Brightmantle.
