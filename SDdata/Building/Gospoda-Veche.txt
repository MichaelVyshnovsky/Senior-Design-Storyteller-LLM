The Gospoda-Veche was a large government building located in the city of Almorel on the Lake of Mists in the Hordelands in the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The building and surrounding compound was located in northeast Almorel along the Golden Way.
In addition to serving as a meeting hall for the Gospoda, the Gospoda-Veche was also one of Almorels main armories.
