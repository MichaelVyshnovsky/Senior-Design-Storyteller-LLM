'''The Three Ravens''' was a nobles' club in [[Suzail]], [[Cormyr]] in the late 15th Century [[DR]].

==Atmosphere==
The club was a small, peaceful, stone-built establishment. It possessed private booths along the backwall.

==Services==
The Three Ravens provided drinks, and was often sought for quick and private discussions.

==History==
This club was considered a seat of power for a group of nobles consolidated under the Lord of House [[Dauntinghorn]].
