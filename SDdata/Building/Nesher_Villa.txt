'''Nesher Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Nesher]].

==Location==
The villa was located at the corner of [[Mendever Street]] and [[Chasso's Trot]] in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The villa compound was walled and composed of several two story structures arranged around a garden that included a small pond. The compound was quite large, covering more than half of the city block upon which is was founded.
