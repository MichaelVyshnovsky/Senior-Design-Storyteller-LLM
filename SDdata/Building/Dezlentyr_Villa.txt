'''Dezlentyr Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Dezlentyr]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] on the southeast corner of where [[Feather Street]] and [[Delzorin Street]] joined.{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The walled compound contained three separate buildings. Two of the buildings were much larger than the last. Each building stood between two and four stories tall.
