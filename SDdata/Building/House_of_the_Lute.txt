'''House of the Lute''' was a kaeth house in the city of [[Baldur's Gate]].{{Cite web/theedverse/2021-10-09 - House of the Lute}}

==Location==
This business was located along [[Ulvur's Lane]], halfway up the "bowl" above the harbor of Baldur's Gate.

==Services==
In addition to selling [[kaeth]], the House of the Lute had a small library with a good selection and a street-front area for [[minstrel]]s to perform at. On the upper levels of this building, one could receive instruction from masters in the way of [[bard]]ing. These lessons were conducted within soundproof rooms.
