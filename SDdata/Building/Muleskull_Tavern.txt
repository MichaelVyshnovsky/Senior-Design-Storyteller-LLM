'''Muleskull Tavern''' was a tavern that doubled as the guildhall of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Dungsweepers' Guild|Dungsweepers']].

==Location==
This building was located along [[Ship Street (Waterdeep)|Ship Street]], in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
This building stood two-stories tall.

==Reputation==
The Muleskull Tavern was among some of the more notable and recognizable buildings in the Dock Ward, considered by locals to be a landmark.
