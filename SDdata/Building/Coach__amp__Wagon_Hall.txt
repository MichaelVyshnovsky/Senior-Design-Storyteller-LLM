The '''Coach & Wagon Hall''' was the headquarters of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Wagonmakers' and Coach Builders' Guild]].

==Location==
This guildhall was located along the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]],

==Structure==
This guildhall stood three stories tall.

==Reputation==
Like other guildhalls in the Southern Ward, this building was used as a landmark by locals.
