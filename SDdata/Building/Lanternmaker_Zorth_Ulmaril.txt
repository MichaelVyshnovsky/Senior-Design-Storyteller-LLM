'''Lanternmaker Zorth Ulmaril's''' shop was located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood two storys tall.

==Location==
The shop was located in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] on [[Presper Street]] just south of the end of [[Bitter Alley]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Volo 5309}}

==Inhabitants==
The shop was owned by its namesake, [[Zorth Ulmaril]], in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.
