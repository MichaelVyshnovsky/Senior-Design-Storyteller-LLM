'''Stormhaven House''' was a temple dedicated to [[Talos]] located in the city of [[Urmlaspyr]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Inhabitants==
The temple was run by High Priest [[Thancrom Talastarr]]. Thancrom was assisted by four other specialty priests called [[Stormlord (Talassan)|stormlords]] and three followers.
