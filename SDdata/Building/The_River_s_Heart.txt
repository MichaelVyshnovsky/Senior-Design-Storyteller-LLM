'''The River's Heart''' was a shop that sold [[magic]]al items and other curiosities.

==Location==
This magic item shop was located just west of the north gate of [[Neverwinter]].

==Services==
The River's Heart had several items available for purchase, such as enchanted [[arrow]]s and [[battleaxe]]s. It also sold magic [[scroll]]s as well as the powerful ''[[dart of the hornet's nest]]''.
