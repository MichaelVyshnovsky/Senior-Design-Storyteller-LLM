The '''Lazy Moon Inn''' was an inn in the city of [[Tantras]] in [[the Vast]], owned by [[Faress]]. 

==Geography==
This inn was well located inside of Tantras, lying on [[Shendle Street]].

==History==
Around [[1358 DR]], it was always busy, a good quality establishment with only middling prices.
