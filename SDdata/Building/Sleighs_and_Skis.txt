'''Sleighs and Skis''' was a shop found in the capital city of [[Immilmar]] in [[Rashemen]] in the {{YearlinkName|1367}}.{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The shop was located near the center of Immilmar southeast of the [[Huhrong's Citadel]].

==Activities==
[[Dana Grayrunner]] was the shop's proprieter in 1367 DR. She often had [[Tholli Ironweaver]] craft the runners for her sleighs. [[Wychlaran]] commissioned various [[sleigh]]s and [[sled]]s from Dana that were eventually enchanted.
