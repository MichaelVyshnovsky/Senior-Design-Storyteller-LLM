'''Seaswealth Hall''' was the guildhall of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Fishmongers' Fellowship]].<ref>

==Location==
The Seaswealth Hall was located in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
This guildhall stood two-stories tall. It was rather unassuming.

==Services==
This guildhall controlled the city's lucrative open-air fish markets.

==Reputation==
Like other guildhalls in the Dock Ward, it was considered by locals to be a landmark.
