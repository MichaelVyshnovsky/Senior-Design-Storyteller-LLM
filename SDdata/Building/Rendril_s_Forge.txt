'''Rendril's Forge''' was a metalworking smithy in the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite document/Procampur City Expansion Packet|3}}

==Location==
It was found on [[Shards Street]] in the [[Port District]].

==Staff==
By [[1358 DR]], the business was owned by [[Opara Rendril]], a master metalsmith who, in particular, crafted high-quality [[longsword]]s and [[helmet|helms]], while her bracelets and belt-[[dagger]]s were famous around the [[Inner Sea]].
