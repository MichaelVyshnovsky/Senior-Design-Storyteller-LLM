The Tymoran Trails was the an inn located in the Amnian city of Crimmor. It was a noisy and crowded place, frequented by patrons looking to have some fun.
The inn was located on the Firesteep Square, in the western part of the River Ward.
This high building housed three floors of rooms above the ground floor.
The Trails was a popular place, crowded at every hour.
The inn employed several guards amongst its staff, who limited disorderly behaviors.
