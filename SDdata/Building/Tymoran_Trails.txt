The '''Tymoran Trails''' was the an inn located in the [[Amn]]ian city of [[Crimmor]].{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|30}} It was a noisy and crowded place, frequented by patrons looking to have some fun.

==Location==
The inn was located on the [[Firesteep Square]], in the western part of the [[Crimmor/River Ward|River Ward]].{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|26-27}}

==Structure==
This high building housed three floors of rooms above the ground floor.

==Interior==
===Atmosphere===
The Trails was a popular place, crowded at every hour.{{Cite dragon/334/Crimmor: City of Caravans|26-29}}

The inn employed several guards amongst its staff, who limited disorderly behaviors.
