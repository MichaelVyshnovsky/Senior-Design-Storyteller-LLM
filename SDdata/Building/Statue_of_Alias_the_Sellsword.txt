The Statue of Alias the Sellsword was a magically protected monument erected in the Market Triangle of Westgate in the late 14 century DR.
The Statue of Alias the Sellsword stood on the eastern side of the Market Triangle, several doors north from Westblade Guild Headquarters and Shalush Myrkeers Shop. A small sidewalk plaza surrounded the monument, and richly decorated buildings rose above it.
The monument was a large statue depicting a young and attractive woman in a heroic battle stance, adorned in armor and wielding a sword. With aid from Mintassan the Magnificent, friend and ally of Aliass, the monument was enchanted to be unaffected by the elements and resist any attempts to vandalize it. The statue was even protected from being spit upon, and its magics redirected the wet projectiles away from Alias. If any attempts to vandalize the statue took place, a magical message appeared at its base saying "This statue is magically protected from Night Masks and other destructive forces by Mintassan the Magnificent."
