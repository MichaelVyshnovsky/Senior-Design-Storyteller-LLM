The '''Legendary Pottery Shop''' was a pottery store in the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] in the late-15{{th}} century [[DR]].The date for all Year 1 RPGA sanctioned adventures is considered to be [[1479 DR]] for the purposes of this wiki.

==Services==
This store sold quite unusual and ornate pottery in a wide variety of colors, shapes, and sizes.
