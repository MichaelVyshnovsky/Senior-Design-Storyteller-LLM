Tyrintars Hall, previously known as Gargoyles Grim, was a grand festhall found in the city of Myth Drannor.
The building was located just north of Sixstar, the junction of six major city roads in the Kerradunath ward of the city.
The great hall was three stories tall, constructed with a triangular layout.
Tyrintars Hall was enchanted in such a manner that pleasing dance music continuously played throughout, which could be adjusted depending on the mood .
The hall was renamed after the Opening, in the . It honored Tyrintar, the paladin that served as one of the citys great heroes, who had a particular affinity for dancing.
