'''The Scarlet Slipper''' was an inn in [[Waterdeep]]{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|2|Realmslore}}

==Structure==
The old inn was a rambling building. It had stairs, indicating more than one floor.

==Interior==
The place was kept dim.

==Services==
The inn was renowned for the number of low-price [[coin-lass]]es that flocked to it, and it had a less exclusive reputation than other places.

==History==
[[Mirt the Merciless]] was a regular at the inn even as far back as {{yearlink|1321}}. He met [[Nalitheen]] there on [[Eleint 6]].
