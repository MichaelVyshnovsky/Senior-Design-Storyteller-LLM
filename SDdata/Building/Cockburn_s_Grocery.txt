Cockburns Grocery was a grocery shop located in the residential district of Phlan.
Originally a shop that sold household items and very little food,
Outside the building, the shop was signaled by a simple "Grocery" sign over the entrance. Inside, crates where stacked up to the high ceiling, which sported exposed framing.
