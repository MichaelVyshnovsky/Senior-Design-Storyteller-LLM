'''Hermuk's Allgoods''' was an excellent merchant's shop located in [[Melvaunt]].

While the store provided regular goods, along with some more exotic items from the region's black market, its proprietor [[Hermuk]] offered to fence goods brought to him by [[rogue]]s and adventurers.
