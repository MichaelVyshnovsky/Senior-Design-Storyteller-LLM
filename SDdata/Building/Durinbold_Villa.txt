Durinbold Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Durinbold.
The villa was located in the central section of Waterdeeps North Ward. It sat on the southwest corner of the intersection of Vhezoar Street and Sulmor Street. The west side of the villa sat on Ilzantil Street.
The walled compound contained three buildings and a large courtyard. The buildings were three or four stories tall.
