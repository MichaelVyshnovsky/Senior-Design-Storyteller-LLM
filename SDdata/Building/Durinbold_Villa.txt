'''Durinbold Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Durinbold]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the central section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]]. It sat on the southwest corner of the intersection of [[Vhezoar Street]] and [[Sulmor Street]]. The west side of the villa sat on [[Ilzantil Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The walled compound contained three buildings and a large courtyard. The buildings were three or four stories tall.
