The Hall of Pools and Mirrors was a shrine dedicated to Savras located within Candlekeep.
The shrine was hidden within the Inner Rooms where the most powerful magical tomes were stored within Candlekeep.
The Hall was lined with many magical and nonmagical scrying devices. Three of the crystal balls were actually crystaliches.
