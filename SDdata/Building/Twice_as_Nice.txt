'''Twice as Nice''' was a shop in the [[Sea Ward of Waterdeep|Sea Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
Located off the [[Street of Lances]], the building was in a constant state of disrepair. In the basement, there was a wooden door leading to a gate to [[Wyllowwood]] in [[Undermountain]].
