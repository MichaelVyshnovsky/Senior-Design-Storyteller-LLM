'''Market Hall''' was the guildhall of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Council of Farmer-Grocers]].<ref>

==Location==
The Market Hall was located along [[Trader's Way (Waterdeep)|Trader's Way]], in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
This guildhall stood two-stories tall.

==Reputation==
Like other guildhalls in the Castle Ward, Market Hall was considered by locals to be a landmark.
