'''Caer Westphal''' was a fortified castle that overlooked the city of [[Westphal]] on the island of [[Snowdown]] within the [[Moonshae Isles]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles}}

==Geography==
Caer Westphal was located on the southern coast on [[Snowdown Ride]] along the [[Sea of Swords]]. 

==Description==
The castle was similar in layout to [[Caer Callidyrr]] on a smaller scale.{{Cite dungeon/196/Backdrop: The Moonshae Isles|12-13}}

==Inhabitants==
The castle was ruled by Lady [[Erliza Daressin]], a [[vampire]] from [[Amn]], in the {{YearlinkName|1479}}.
