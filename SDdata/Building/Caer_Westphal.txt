Caer Westphal was a fortified castle that overlooked the city of Westphal on the island of Snowdown within the Moonshae Isles.
Caer Westphal was located on the southern coast on Snowdown Ride along the Sea of Swords. 
The castle was similar in layout to Caer Callidyrr on a smaller scale.
