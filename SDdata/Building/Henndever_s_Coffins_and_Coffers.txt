'''Henndever's Coffins and Coffers''' was a shop located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood two stories tall.

==Location==
Henndever's Coffins and Coffers was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] near the [[Waterdeep/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]] where [[Spindle Street]] joined the [[Coffinmarch]].
