The Sea Tower was a fortress in the city of Tantras in the Vast. It was the base of the Guard of Tantras.
The Sea Tower stood on the citys coast, on the southern side of the opening of the bay. It lay at the end of Whistleturn Way.
The Sea Tower was the citys main fortress, and held armories and training areas for soldiers.
