The Communal Longhouse was a government building found in the capital city of Immilmar in Rashemen in the .
The building was located in the northwest section of the city.
The Communal Longhouse was used by Immilmars elders for their meetings every tenday, or more frequently if urgent matters occurred. All citizens of Immilmar were allowed to attend such meetings, which frequently errupted in violence. When the elders were not using the longhouse the building served as a community center where guild meetings, feasts, and other social events were held.
