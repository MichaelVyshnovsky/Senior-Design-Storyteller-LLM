The '''Hospice of Wu the Mad''' was an [[inn]] located in the city of [[Almorel]] on the [[Lake of Mists]] in the [[Hordelands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas}}

==Location==
The inn was actually found just outside of Almorel's northeastern [[East Gate (Almorel)|East Gate]] along the [[Golden Way]].

==Inhabitants==
The inn was owned by a [[Shou]] named [[Wu (Shou)|Wu the Mad]] in {{Yearlink|1357}}.
