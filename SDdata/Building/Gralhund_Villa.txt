'''Gralhund Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Gralhund]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the northeast section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] wedged between [[Horn Street]] and [[Saerdoun Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
All three buildings of Gralhund Villa were three stories tall.
