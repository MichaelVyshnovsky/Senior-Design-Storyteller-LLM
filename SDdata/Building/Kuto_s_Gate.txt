Kutos Gate was the westernmost city gate in Phlan on the Moonsea North in the mid-14 century DR.
This double gate was named after a landmark of Kutos Well, found at the heart of reconstructed city of Phlan. The Troll Toss Tavern stood to the south, and a fortunetellers shop was to the northeast of Kutos Gate.
In the , Councilwoman Sasha of the Council of Ten, rendezvoused with the Heroes of Phan outside Kutos Gate before her journey to the lands of Thar, towards Taydomes Keep.
