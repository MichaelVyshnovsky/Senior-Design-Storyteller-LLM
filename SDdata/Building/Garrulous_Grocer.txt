The '''Garrulous Grocer''' was a grocery store located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The complex consisted of three buildings standing one, two, and three stories tall.

==Location==
The Garrulous Grocer was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] within the [[Waterdeep/Adventurers' Quarter|Adventurers' Quarter]] on the north side of [[Grocer's Lane]] across from where it was joined by [[Snake Alley]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Adventurers' Quarter}}

==Inhabitants==
The Garrulous Grocer was founded by [[Nindil Jalbuck]], a [[Masked Lord]] of Waterdeep.
