Dundars Fine Swords was a weapon shop that specialized in swords located in Tilverton in the .
The shop sat in the south-eastern part of Tilverton on a side street off the Street of the Sorceress.
The shop was owned by a retired fighter named Dundar. His sister Andalara helped finish the blades and make scabbards when she was not out hunting.
Around 1366 DR Dundar had expanded his craft and started selling axes, scythes, saws, and other bladed implements, eventually changing the shops name to Dundars Fine Blades. He was sometimes assisted by his sibling Andalara, who finished weapons and made scabbards. Dundar also provided weapons training for aspiring warriors, drawing on his own significant skill and experience.
