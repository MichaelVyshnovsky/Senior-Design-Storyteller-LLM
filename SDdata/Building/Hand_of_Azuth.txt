The '''Hand of Azuth''' was a temple dedicated to the [[Azuth|god of mages]] found along the [[Lake of Steam]] in the coastal city of [[Yhep]].<ref name=EotSS-p170">

==Inhabitants==
[[Lydeia Harauntan]] served as the high priestess of the Hand of Azuth during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. The temple was also the chosen house of worship for Yhep's ruling family.<ref name=EotSS-p170"/>
