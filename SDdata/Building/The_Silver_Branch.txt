The '''Silver Branch''' was a jewelry shop located in the village of [[Eveningstar]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The Silver Branch was located along the [[High Road (Cormyr)|High Road]] near the center of Eveningstar.

==Activities==
In addition to jewelry, the shop specialized in glasswork, art, and [[silk]]s.
