'''Spearpoint Tower''' was an crucial defensive structure located within the city of [[Tulmon]] along the coast of the [[Lake of Steam]] in [[south Faer√ªn]].

==Location==
The tower was located at the end of Tulmon's longest wharf along the harbor on the [[Bay of Balefires]].

==Inhabitants==
An estimated 3,650 soldiers of the [[Stone Spears]], Tulmon's standing army, used the tower as their main barracks.

==Defenses==
Spearpoint Tower was outfitted with an assortment of [[catapult]]s and [[ballista]]e that possessed sufficient range to harry any attacking ships. In order to keep their skills honed, the soldiers manning the war machines practiced by sinking leaky barges.
