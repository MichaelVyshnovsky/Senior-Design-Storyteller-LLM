The '''Red Burrow''' was an [[inn]] located in the [[halfling]] city of [[Beluir]] in [[Luiren]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name="tSS-p47>

==Activities==
The Red Burrow was one of the finest inns within Beluir. It was owned by [[Calcitro Burrow]] and his family.
