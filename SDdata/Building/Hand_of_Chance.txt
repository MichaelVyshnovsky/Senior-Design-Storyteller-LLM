The '''Hand of Chance''' was a shrine dedicated to [[Tymora]] in the city of [[Tilverton]] in [[Cormyr]].

==Description==
The Hand of Chance stood on [[Gateguard Road]] at the [[Upper City Gate]], the southernmost entrance to the [[Tilverton/Old Town|Old Town]] and the only non-stair passage to and from the knoll.
