The '''Shrine of the Bold''' was located in the great fortress of [[Sarahin]].{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|26-27}}

==Location==
The Shrine of the Bold was located deep within the [[Haunted Lands]] of [[Zakhara]] in Sarahin.

==Activities==
This shrine was dedicated to [[Hajama]]'s brother [[Najm]]. It was erected for the [[hobgoblin]]s, [[faris|farisan]], and few [[holy slayer]]s who worshiped the god of adventure.

==History==
Since the shrine was empty much of the time, it became a popular location for evening trysts between the holy slayers.
