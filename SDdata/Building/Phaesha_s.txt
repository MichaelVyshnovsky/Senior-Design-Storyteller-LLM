'''Phaesha's''' was a boarding house in the city of [[Arabel]] in [[Cormyr]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The building was located on the southwest{{Arabel map orientation}} corner of a block in the center of the city opposite the [[Ivory Jack]] tavern. It also sat across the street from [[Elhazir's Exotica]].
