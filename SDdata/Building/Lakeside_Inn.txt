The Lakeside Inn was an inn found in the village of Tinnir in Rashemen in the .
The Lakeside Inn catered mostly to fishermen and river travellers resting in Tinnir.
The inn was owned by a priestess of Bhalla named Vrulla.
The inn was guarded by the spirit of a dog named Nyella. Nyella often barked at strangers.
Bhalla was believed to have once visited the inn and blessed some of the rooms. Anyone sleeping in one of those rooms was supposed to have pleasant dreams and be blessed with love. Malar was also believed to have visited the inn and cursed some of the rooms so anyone sleeping in them had nightmares and a streak of bad luck. The rooms that were blessed and cursed were rumored to change each night.
