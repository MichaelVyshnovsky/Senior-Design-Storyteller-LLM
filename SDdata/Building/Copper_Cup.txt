The Copper Cup was a festhall located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The building compound was four and five stories tall.
The Copper Cup was located in the southeast corner of Waterdeeps Dock Ward on Smugglers Run and bordering the Southyard near the High Road.
The Copper Cup hosted annual guild-sponsored galas celebrating Fleetswake during the last days of Ches.
