The '''Hall of the Sages''' was a tomb located within the [[Waterdeep/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]] in [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
The Hall of the Sages was located in the southern section of the City of the Dead.

==Description==
The tomb was small and dusty.

==History==
As its name implied, the Hall of the Sages was reserved specifically for [[sage]]s. Approximately 80 sages were interred within as of the {{YearlinkName|1372}}. 

==Rumors & Legends==
A rumor persisted that some of the sages were buried with some of their rare and valuable [[tome]]s, making the place an enticing target for [[thieves]]. The rumor was spread by [[Laeral Silverhand]] and some of her allies in order to flush out black market dealers in forbidden lore.
