The House of Cogs and Hammers was temple devoted to Gond located in Tulmon along the Lake of Steam during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The main gate of the temple was crowned with a large clock where all of the gears and inner working were visible. Many of Tulmons children gathered around the gate to gaze in wonder at the small moving parts.
As one of Tulmons newest temples during that time, the House of Cogs and Hammers created quite a buzz among Tulmonites. Many eagerly supported the temples services of constructing and selling various tools and gadgets.
The House of Cogs and Hammers was run by Master Kalavdan Talast.
