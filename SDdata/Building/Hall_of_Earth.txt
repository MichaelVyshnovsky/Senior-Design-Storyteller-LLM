The '''Hall of Earth''' was located within the complex of the [[Palace of the Enlightened Throne]] in [[Huzuz]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

==Description==
The Hall of Earth was kept extremely dry.

==Activities==
This hall housed the [[noble dao]] who served the [[Grand Caliph]].

==Inhabitants==
Lesser [[dao]] and [[pech]] were present to attend the noble dao.
