'''Khaalvandar''' was a grand villa and guest residence owned by the royal [[Irithyl]] family of [[Cormanthyr]].

==Location==
It was located in the [[Myth Drannor/Cormanthor|Old City]] district of [[Myth Drannor]], immediately east of [[Castle Cormanthor]].

==Structure==
The lowest of the manse's four levels was built over {{SI|90|ft}} above ground level,

==Interior==
Khaalvandar was lavishly decorated with all manner of art and decorative sculptures. It housed the Lord-Speaker's library, that featured works that dated back to the ancient elves of [[Uvaeren]]. 

==Activities==
The mansion was often used a secret meeting place between the Myth Drannan [[elven high mage|High Mages]] and the [[Srinshee]]. 

==Defenses==
It was known to be protected by some of the most potent and powerful protective [[ward]]s in all of Cormanthyr.
