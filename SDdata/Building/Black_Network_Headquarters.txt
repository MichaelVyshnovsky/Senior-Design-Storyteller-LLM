The '''Black Network Headquarters''' was the base of operations for the [[Zhentarim]] in [[Zhentil Keep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]] following the city's destruction in {{YearlinkName|1368}}.

==Location==
The Headquarters was located in south Zhentil Keep in the eastern section of the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] just off of [[Bridge Street]]. 

==Description==
The building was a small mansion.

==Inhabitants==
[[Nimbaud]] was the head of the Zhentarim using the headquarters. A [[priest]] of [[Cyric]] named [[Synnda]] worked within the headquarters as well.

==Defenses==
Both the front and rear doors were guarded with ''[[Glyph of warding|glyphs of warding]]''. Six Zhentarim warriors armed with [[greatsword]]s guarded the building.
