'''Unicorn Run Boat Rental''' was a shop in [[Loudwater]] in the [[Delimbiyr Vale]] in the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
It stood on the bank of the [[Delimbiyr River]], opposite the [[Temple of Corellon Larethian (Loudwater)|Temple of Corellon Larethian]].

==Services==
In business by [[1358 DR]], the store rented out boats for use on the Delimbiyr. It was named after the [[Unicorn Run]], a tributary of the Delimbiyr located downstream of Loudwater.
