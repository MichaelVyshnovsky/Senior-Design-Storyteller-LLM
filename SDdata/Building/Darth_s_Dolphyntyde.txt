'''Darth's Dolphyntyde''' was a tavern located in the city of [[Waterdeep]] during the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The tavern was located on the eastern side of [[Wastrel Alley]] in Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] just four buildings south of [[Belnimbra's Street]].
