Gauntyl Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Gauntyl.
The Gauntyl Villa was situated along Ivory Street, across from Eltorchul Villa and next to the Temple of Beauty, in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep.
This building complex consisted of two high quality buildings, one that was two stories tall and another that stood a single story tall,Evidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps.
