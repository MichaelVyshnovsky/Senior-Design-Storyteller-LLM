}}'''Nanther Keep''' was the ancestral home of the [[Nanther]] family in [[Melvaunt]] around [[1367 DR]].

==Location==
The keep was located in the northeast section of Melvaunt.

==Description==
A beautiful garden was found within Nanther Keep. Some of the flora within the garden were used as [[Material component|spell components]].

==Defenses==
Twenty men-at-arms guarded the keep and the Nanther family.
