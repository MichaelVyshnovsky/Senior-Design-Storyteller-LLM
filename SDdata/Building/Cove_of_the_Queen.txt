The Cove of the Queen was a hidden pirate harbor-temple of Umberlee on the island of Mintarn in the Sea of Swords.
The Cove of the Queen was a harbor hidden from unknowing casual eye. The temple was used exclusively by the pirates of the region and they served the Wavemistress Royal, the high priestess of the temple.
The leader of the Umberlant devotees circa 1369 DR was Qalbess Frostyl.
