'''Dusk to Dawn''' was a traveling tavern in the late-15{{th}} century [[DR]]. The tavern's name derived from the fact that it always set up shop around dusk, then once dawn hit the proprietor would pack everything up.The date for all Year 1 RPGA sanctioned adventures is considered to be [[1479 DR]] for the purposes of this wiki.

==Description==
This tavern largely consisted of a [[tent]], [[cask]]s, and [[keg]]s.

==Geography==
Dusk to Dawn was always situated in the [[Mistshore]] neighborhood of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]]. It set up shop wherever there was a large open area.
