'''Redfires Inn''' was an [[inn]] in the city of [[Calaunt]] in [[the Vast]], operating around [[1358 DR]].

Redfires Inn was a big place, high quality, and popular with patrons of many different races, though it was costly. It featured room service by [[boar]]-pie cooks, [[wine]] vendors, and pleasure-girls from all over Calaunt.
