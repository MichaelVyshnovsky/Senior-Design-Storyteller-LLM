Kelvins Comfort was a tavern in Bryn Shander.
The tavern was located next to Bryn Shanders market square.
The tavern was famous for its signature drink known as Flamebeards Firebrandy. It also served mead from Good Mead and liquors imported from Mirabar.
Kelvins Comfort was owned by shield dwarf Ogden Flamebeard sometime after the War of the Silver Marches. A frequent patron was Baric Nylef while he searched for Dwarven criminal Worvil Forkbeard, who was known to be addicted to Flamebeards Firebrandy.
