The '''temple of Waukeen''' in [[Bezantur]] was a major center of worship of [[Waukeen]] in [[Thay]].

==Location==
This temple was located in Bezantur,

==Activities==
The temple was one of two major sites of Waukeen worship in Thay, the other being in [[Tyraturos (tharch)|Tyraturos]]. Waukeen's faith was popular among the Thayan middle-class as of the mid&ndash;14{{th}} century [[DR]], which made the [[Red Wizards of Thay]] uneasy about how they should treat the [[church of Waukeen]] within their realm.
