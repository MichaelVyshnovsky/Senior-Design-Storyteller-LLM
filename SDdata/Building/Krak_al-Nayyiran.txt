'''Krak al-Nayyiran''', or the '''Castle of Sun and Moon''', was a castle located in the [[City of Brass]] in [[1367 DR]].{{AQ-time}}

This keep was found in the [[Plume]] district of the city.

==Activities==
The finest of the [[Marrake al-Sidan al-Hariq ben Lazan|Sultan's]] military units were trained within the castle of Krak al-Nayyiran. Additionally, the most elite of troops from those units were awarded quarters here.
