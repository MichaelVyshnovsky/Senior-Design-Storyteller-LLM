'''Hothemer Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Hothemer]].

==Location==
The Hothemer Villa was located in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]],

==Structure==
Like many Waterdhavian villas, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden.{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2006)/07-06 - Waterdhavian Villas}}<ref group="note" name="evidence">Evidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps. Inside the walls of the estate were two high-quality buildings that both stood three stories tall, one being the mansion proper and the other a guesthouse.
