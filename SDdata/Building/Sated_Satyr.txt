The '''Sated Satyr''' was a tavern in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] in the {{YearlinkName|1372}}.

==Location==
The building was located at the junction of [[Trollkill Street]] and the [[Street of the Singing Dolphin]], directly in front of the [[North Tower]] (also known as "The Trolltower").

==Structure==
The Sated Satyr was a two-story, irregularly shaped building that fronted the streets or the alley on all sides but one. It adjoined the building next to it on Trollkill Street.
