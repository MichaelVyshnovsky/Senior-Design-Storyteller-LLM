Earth Mothers Bounty was a shop located in the Chauntean temple-farm of Goldenfields in the Dessarin Valley.
It was located near the main gates of Goldenfields, visible to all who passed within.
The shop was a stone building with an open facade. It featured a sign that proudly displayed the shops name Earth Mothers Bounty.
In front of the shop was a well-trodden roundabout decorated with a 20-foot-tall (6.1 meters) wooden statue of Chauntea. She was depicted as a rotund woman standing in a field of wheat.
The shop sold various types of seeds as well as decorative cornucopias to the abbeys visitors.
