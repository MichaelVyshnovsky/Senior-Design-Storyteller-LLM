The '''Lion Tower''' was one of the towers in [[Castle Obarskyr]].

==History==
Per [[Vangerdahast]]'s recollection in [[Alturiak]] of {{yearlink|1365}},{{cite book/Elminster in Hell/Hardcover 2001|2|Realmslore}} the Lion Tower had been built in {{yearlink|1306}} and much furniture was moved about. One of the rugs in the tower, bought in bulk from [[Wheloon]], wound up in a room last used by [[Amedahast]].
