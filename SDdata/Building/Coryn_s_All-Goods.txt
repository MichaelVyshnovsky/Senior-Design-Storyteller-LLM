'''Coryn's All-Goods''' was the only standing general store in [[Yûlash]] around [[1367 DR]].

==Activities==
Coryn's All-Goods was literally the place to buy and sell equipment in Yûlash. If Coryn didn't have it, then he could get it.

==Inhabitants==
The store was owned by a man named [[Coryn]].
