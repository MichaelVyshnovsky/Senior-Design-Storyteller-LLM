The '''Spires Against the Stars''' was the most holy site of worship for the faithful of [[Beshaba]], the Maid of Misfortune.

==Location==
This hilltop fortress was found in eastern [[Tethyr]] north of [[Saradush]].

==Inhabitants==
The Spires was run by Nails of the Lady [[Dlatha Faenar]] in the {{YearlinkName|1367}}.

==History==
The fortress was once the seat of power for a noble family, but was later overrun by [[vampire]]s.
