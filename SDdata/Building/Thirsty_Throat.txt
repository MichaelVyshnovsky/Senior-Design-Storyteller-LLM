The Thirsty Throat was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The tavern stood two storys tall.
The Thirsty Throat was located in Waterdeeps Dock Ward where Candle Lane and Zastrow Street met the Way of the Dragon.
A fence named Thunna worked out of the tavern. The Thirsty Throat was also a good place to hire mercenaries.
