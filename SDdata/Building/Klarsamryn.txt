'''Klarsamryn''' was the royal stronghold of the kingdom of [[Erlkazar]] in the capital city of [[Llorbauth]].

==Location==
The castle was located on the northeastern side of the capital city. From the roof of the complex, one could see farming fields to the east and the city's docks on [[Shalane Lake]] to the north.

==Structure==
Klarsamryn contained the royal palace on its grounds. It had an outer courtyard

==Interior==
The king's personal audience chamber was circular with a roof supported by pillars. It included a statue of [[Ondeth Obarskyr]], the founder of [[Cormyr]]. One of the rooms connected to this throne room was the king's private reading chamber.

==Inhabitants==
The stronghold was the home of the royal family of Erlkazar, which, in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]], included King [[Korox Morkann]] and his daughter, Princess [[Mariko Morkann]].

==History==
The castle was founded some 500 years before the city of Llorbauth arose around it.
