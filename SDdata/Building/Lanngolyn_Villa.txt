'''Lanngolyn Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Lanngolyn]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] on the southeast corner of the intersection of [[Delzorin Street]] with the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]]. The east side of their property was bounded by [[Ilzantil Street]].

==Structure==
The Lanngolyn Villa was a walled compound of one- and three-story buildings.
