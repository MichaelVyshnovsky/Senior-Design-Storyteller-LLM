'''Saern's Fine Swords''' was a business located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood two stories tall.

==Location==
Saern's Fine Swords was located in the northern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] on the east side of the [[High Road (Waterdeep)|High Road]] where the [[Winter Path]] and [[Burnt Wagon Way]] joined.{{Cite interactive atlas|Dead Trades Ward}}

==Services==
The shop dealt in weaponry to include the occasional [[magical]] variety.
