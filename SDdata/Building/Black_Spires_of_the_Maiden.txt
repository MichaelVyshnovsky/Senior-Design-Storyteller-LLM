The '''Black Spires of the Maiden''' was a temple devoted to [[Loviatar]], the Maiden of Pain, located in [[Amn]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The temple was located west of [[Ishla]] in the [[Vale of Wailing Women]] near the southern border with [[Tethyr]].
