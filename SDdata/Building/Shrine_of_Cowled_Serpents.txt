The '''Shrine of Cowled Serpents''' was hidden temple dedicated to [[Sseth]], the World Serpent, found in the [[Forest of Wyrms]].

==Location==
The temple was located underground beneath the western edge of the forest.

==History==
The temple was destroyed by [[human]] [[mage]]s long ago.

==Inhabitants==
Since its destruction, the temple was home to a variety of [[snake]]s. The [[Coiled Cabal]] explored the ruins in search of the legendary [[Naja Fountain]].
