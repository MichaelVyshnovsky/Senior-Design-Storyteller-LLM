The '''Gray Griffon''' was a [[tavern]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The tavern stood three storys tall.

==Location==
The Gray Griffon was located in the western section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] on the southwest corner where [[Lackpurse Lane]] was intersected by [[Eel Street (Waterdeep)|Eel Street]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Dock South Ward}}
