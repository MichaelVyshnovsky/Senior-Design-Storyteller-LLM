The '''Guardian's Post''' was a temple dedicated to [[Helm]] found along the [[Lake of Steam]] in the coastal city of [[Yhep]].<ref name=EotSS-p170">

==Inhabitants==
The temple was led by a [[paladin]] of Helm named [[Imingo Deiros]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].<ref name=EotSS-p170"/>

==Activities==
Much of the Guardian's Post's time and resources were spent monitoring for any [[beholder]] activity near Yhep which plagued the city during its past.<ref name=EotSS-p170"/>
