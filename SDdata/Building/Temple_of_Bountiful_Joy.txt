The '''Temple of Bountiful Joy''' was a temple dedicated to [[Isis]], the [[Mulhorandi pantheon|Mulhorandi goddess]] of agriculture and fertility.

==Location==
This temple was located in [[Skuld]], the capital city of [[Mulhorand]], within the city's [[Skuld/Central Ward|Central Ward]].<ref>

==History==
As of the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], it was considered to be among the most prominent temples of Isis, along with the [[Mystic Cornucopia]].
