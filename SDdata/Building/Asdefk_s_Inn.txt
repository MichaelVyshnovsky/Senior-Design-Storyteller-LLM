'''Asdefk's Inn''' was a popular inn and tavern in the capital city of [[Darromar]] in [[Tethyr]]. It was known for its extremely [[spicy]] [[Calimshan|Calishite]] and [[Tashalar]]an dishes, but was overpriced.

The building was located on the west side of [[Ithal Road|Star Street]] in the [[Darromar/Temple Quarter|Temple Quarter]] of the city, directly across from the [[Wheel Market]].
