'''Jhansczil Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Jhansczil]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the northeast section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] on the northeast corner where [[Copper Street]] met [[Thunderstaff Way]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
The walled compound contained four separate buildings. Each building stood one or three stories tall.
