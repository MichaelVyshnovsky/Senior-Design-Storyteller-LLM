Brabass Bakery was a shop in Ravens Bluff operated by Brabas Garlandros in the 14 century DR that sold pies and various pastries.
This bakery was located in the neighborhood of Altarside, in Ravens Bluffs Temple District, standing roughly two blocks away from the Singing Sword Inn.
Among the stores selection of pies was the common apple pie. The average cost of a pie at this establishment was around 1 gold piece.
