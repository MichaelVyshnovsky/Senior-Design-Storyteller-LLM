'''Brabas's Bakery''' was a shop in [[Ravens Bluff]] operated by [[Brabas Garlandros]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]] that sold [[pie]]s and various pastries.<ref group="note" name="name" />

==Location==
This bakery was located in the neighborhood of [[Altarside]], in Ravens Bluff's [[Ravens_Bluff/Temple_District|Temple District]], standing roughly two blocks away from the [[Singing Sword Inn]].<ref group="note" name="location" />

==Services==
Among the store's selection of pies was the common [[apple pie]]. The average cost of a pie at this establishment was around 1 [[gold piece]].
