'''Shalush Myrkeer's Shop''' was a general goods store located in the city of [[Westgate]] in [[1367 DR]].

==Location==
The shop was located at the intersection of [[East Market Street]] and [[Westgate Market Street]] in the south section of the city.

==Activities==
The shop was owned and operated by the merchant [[Shalush Myrkeer]]. This large shop stocked just about anything a prospective customer might be looking for.
