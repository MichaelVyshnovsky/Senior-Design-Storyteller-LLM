'''Sultlue Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Sultlue]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the north section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] in between [[Shield Street]] and [[Copper Street]] where they met [[Vondil Street]] at the border with the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
Each of the four buildings of Sultlue Villa were arranged in one corner of the compound. All four buildings stood four stories tall.
