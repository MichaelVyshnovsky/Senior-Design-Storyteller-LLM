'''Mother Salinka's House of Pleasure''' was a shabby, low-coin [[festhall]] in the [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. The festhall stood on [[Rainrun Street]] between the [[Yawning Portal]] and [[The Empty Keg]].
