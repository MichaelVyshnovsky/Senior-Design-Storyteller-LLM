The Gray Serpent, which was also seen as the Grey Serpent, was an inn located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The inn stood three stories tall.
The Gray Serpent was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Trades Ward on the eastern side of the Way of the Dragon.
A portal leading to Maldiglass Refuge was found on the roof of the Gray Serpent on the outside wall at the head of the back stairway.
