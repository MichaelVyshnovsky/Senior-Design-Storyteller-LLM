'''Selavar's Inn''' was a large inn of note in [[Suzail]], the capital city of [[Cormyr]].

==Location==
The establishment stood one block south of the grand [[Promenade (Suzail)|Promenade]], along the veering [[Court Close]]. It was located on the same eastern side of the Court Close as [[Zhaelun's Stockyard]].

==History==
Selavar's Inn was opened sometime before the {{YearlinkName|1356}}.
