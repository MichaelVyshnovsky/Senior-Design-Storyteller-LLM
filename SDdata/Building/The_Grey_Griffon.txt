The '''Grey Griffon''' was a tavern located in the city of [[Waterdeep]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Location==
The tavern sat at the intersection of [[Lackpurse Lane]] and [[Eel Street (Waterdeep)|Eel Street]] in southern Waterdeep within the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]].

==Description==
The building was a 3-story structure.
