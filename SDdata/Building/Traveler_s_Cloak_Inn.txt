Travelers Cloak Inn was an inn in Mulmaster on the Moonsea. 
The inn was a welcoming establishment where each room had a fireplace.
The Travelers Cloak was run by Dela and Vesvur Alder.
Vesvur was a Harper agent who reported to Chesslyn Onaubra.
*Room, dinner, and drink sold for 12 gp per night.
*Stabling of mounts for 3 gp per night.
