The '''apothecary''' in [[Triboar]] was a shop belonging to [[Tarmock Felaskur]] that sold medicine as of the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was also notable for its carbonated [[sweet water (beverage)|sweet water]].

==Location==
The shop was located on the east side of town north of the [[Evermoor Way]]. It sat across the street from the [[Northshield House]] inn.

==Structure==
The building was a sunken wood cottage covered in [[ivy]] and marked by a wooden sign in the shape of a [[potion]] [[bottle]]. A broken [[wheelbarrow]] sat out front just beneath the sign.

==Services==
The store sold [[herb]]al remedies and [[salve]]s of dubious effectiveness. It also sold sweet water for 1 [[gold piece]] per flask.
