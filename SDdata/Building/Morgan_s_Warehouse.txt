Morgans Warehouse was a warehouse in the Lower Port of Skullport in the late 14 century DR.
The warehouse was a two-story structure in fair condition.
The warehouse was known as one of the most secure and reliable depots in Skullport. Goods passing through Morgans Warehouse were well-protected, making it a trusted establishment for merchants seeking dependable storage.
