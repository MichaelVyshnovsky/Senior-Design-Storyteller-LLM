'''Urmlaspyrkey House''' was an [[inn]] located in the city of [[Urmlaspyr]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Description==
This was a snobbish inn that was devoid of cheer. The poor furnishings and accommodations combined with its expensive prices left the inn deserted most of the time.
