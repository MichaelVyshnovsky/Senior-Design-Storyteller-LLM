Rebeleighs Elegant Headwear was a clothing shop located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The shop stood a single story tall.
Rebeleighs Elegant Headwear was located in the southeast section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward on the west side of the Street of the Sword just north of Waterdeep Way. The rear of the shop sat on Zeldans Alley.
The shop sold clothing and specialized in fabulous headwear.
