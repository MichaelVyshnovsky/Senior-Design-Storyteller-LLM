'''Rebeleigh's Elegant Headwear''' was a clothing shop located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The shop stood a single story tall.

==Location==
Rebeleigh's Elegant Headwear was located in the southeast section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on the west side of the [[Street of the Sword]] just north of [[Waterdeep Way]]. The rear of the shop sat on [[Zeldan's Alley]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Castle Ward}}

==Services==
The shop sold clothing and specialized in fabulous headwear.
