Matteos was a renowned weaponsmith shop found in Phlan in 1367 DR. The shop was owned by the half-elven smith Matteo.
The shop was located in the southwest section of the city.
Matteos weapons were sought after by many soldiers and adventurers in the Moonsea region. The quality of these weapons was reflected in their high prices. The shop also did repair work, but only for weapons of exceptional caliber.
