The '''Fishscale Smithy''' was a blacksmith shop located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The smithy stood two stories tall.

==Location==
The Fishscale Smithy was located in the central section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] where [[Adder Lane]] met [[Gut Alley]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Dock South Ward}}
