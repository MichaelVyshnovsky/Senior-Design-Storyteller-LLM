The '''Pick and Lantern''' was a successful tavern in the [[Venter]] section of [[Skullport]]. The proprietor was a [[dwarf]] known as [[Wilgold Gunderbradt]] who also owned a flophouse behind the tavern. The tavern welcomed sailors and miners and was known for the collection of maps of [[Undermountain]] that adorned its walls.{{Cite dungeon/200/Backdrop: Skullport}}

==Description==
A ship lantern and miner's pick were painted on the side of the building.
