The Pick and Lantern was a successful tavern in the Venter section of Skullport. The proprietor was a dwarf known as Wilgold Gunderbradt who also owned a flophouse behind the tavern. The tavern welcomed sailors and miners and was known for the collection of maps of Undermountain that adorned its walls.
A ship lantern and miners pick were painted on the side of the building.
