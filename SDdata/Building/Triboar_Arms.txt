The Triboar Arms was a tavern in Triboar.
The tavern was located in the southwestern side of Triboar by the Long Road.
The tavern was popular among rangers and scouts who offered their services as guides.
The tiefling owner of the establishment, Nemyth, liked to lean at the buildings entrance cleaning a mug while nodding and smiling at passersby. The staff of the tavern was cordial, and prices were considered reasonable.
As of the late 1480s or early 1490s DR, the building had been burned to the ground and rebuilt twice.
