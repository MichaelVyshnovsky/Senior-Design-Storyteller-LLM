The '''Triboar Arms''' was a tavern in [[Triboar]].

==Location==
The tavern was located in the southwestern side of Triboar by the [[Long Road]].

==Interior==
The tavern was popular among [[ranger]]s and [[scout]]s who offered their services as guides.

==Services==
The [[tiefling]] owner of the establishment, [[Nemyth]], liked to lean at the building's entrance cleaning a [[mug]] while nodding and smiling at passersby. The staff of the tavern was cordial, and prices were considered reasonable.

==History==
As of the late 1480s or early 1490s [[DR]], the building had been burned to the ground and rebuilt twice.
