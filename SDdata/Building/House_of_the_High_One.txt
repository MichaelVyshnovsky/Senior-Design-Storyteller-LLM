The '''House of the High One''' was the most revered temple devoted to [[Azuth]] as of the {{YearlinkName|1367}}.

==Location==
The temple was located in [[Saerloon]] in the western section of the city.

==Inhabitants==
The House of the High One was run by six powerful [[wizard]]s including [[Helven]], [[Mirren]], [[Lhun]], [[Riilath]], [[Ormil]], and [[Thelcaunt]].
