The '''Eagles' Eyrie''' was a smaller, less-than-clean inn found in [[Highcastle]] in late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was popular among travelers in the area who were looking for a place to stay while on a budget.

==Prices==
A room for a night only cost 5 [[sp]].
