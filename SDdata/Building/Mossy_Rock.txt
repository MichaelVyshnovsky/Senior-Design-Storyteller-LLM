'''Mossy Rock''' was an [[inn]] in [[Port Nyanzaru]], [[Chult]].{{Cite adventurers league/A Walk in the Park|4}}

==Location==
The inn was located in Port Nyanzaru's [[Port Nyanzaru/Market Ward|Market Ward]].

==Services==
The inn offered modest accommodations for [[adventurer]]s,

==History==
During the events of the [[death curse]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]], Mossy Rock served as a meeting spot for members of the [[Emerald Enclave]], such as [[Screaming Wind]].
