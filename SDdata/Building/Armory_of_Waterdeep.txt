This building was '''the armory''' of the [[City Guard (Waterdeep)|City Guard]] in [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
The armory stood along [[Trollkill Street]] in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]], south of [[North Tower]] and the [[Sated Satyr]].

==Structure==
This structure stood three stories tall and was directly connected to the city walls that separated Sea Ward from the [[Waterdeep/Field Ward|Field Ward]].

==Activities==
Though this armory was primarily under the purview of the City Guard, the [[City Watch (Waterdeep)|City Watch]] also had access to it.<ref>
