Eotzi Gompa was a monastery (a gompa in the Tabotan language) of the Way of Enlightenment in Tabot.
When yeti emerged from the Wu Pi Te Shar mountains and raided Nafeen Gompa in , the dzong-pon (sheriff) of Thok district organized a hunting expedition against them, distributing printed handbills around southwestern Tabot. Among the first volunteers were 25 people from Eotzi Gompa, who quickly went to Nafeen.
