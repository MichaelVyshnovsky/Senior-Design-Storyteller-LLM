'''Sablehearth''' was the [[Irlingstar]] family mansion in [[Waterdeep]]. 

==History==
As of [[1374 DR]], the house featured rooftop gardens and was adjoined by the [[Delzimmer_(family)|Delzimmer]] townhouse to the south.
