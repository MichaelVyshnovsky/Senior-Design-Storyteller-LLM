'''Phull Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Phull ]].

==Location==
[[File:Trollkill Street CoS-map.png||thumb|273px|''The location of Phull Villa (labeled 4) on a circa [[1368 DR]] map.'']]
The Phull Villa was located in the [[Waterdeep/North Ward|North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]],

==Structure==
Like many Waterdhavian villas in the North Ward, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped garden{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2006)/07-06 - Waterdhavian Villas}}<ref group="note" name="evidence">Evidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps. with elaborate adornments.

==Inhabitants==
Besides the members of House Phull, this estate was inhabited by five [[watchspider]]s who guarded the family's vault. Whenever there was an intruder the watchspiders would attack in two pairs, while the fifth would ring a [[bell]] on the wall near the entrance.
