Phull Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Phull .
|thumb|273px|The location of Phull Villa (labeled 4) on a circa 1368 DR map.
The Phull Villa was located in the North Ward of Waterdeep,
Like many Waterdhavian villas in the North Ward, this was a walled estate featuring a landscaped gardenEvidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps. with elaborate adornments.
Besides the members of House Phull, this estate was inhabited by five watchspiders who guarded the familys vault. Whenever there was an intruder the watchspiders would attack in two pairs, while the fifth would ring a bell on the wall near the entrance.
