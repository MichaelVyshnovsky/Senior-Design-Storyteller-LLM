Danains hardware shop was a store located on the Promenade in Suzail, the capital of Cormyr in the 14 century DR.
Danains store was nestled between the posh Gilded Feather club to the west and the Witch-Dukes Bride, formerly known as the High Tankard tavern. The hardware shop stood on the southern side of the Promenade, three city blocks away from Cormyrs Royal Court.
