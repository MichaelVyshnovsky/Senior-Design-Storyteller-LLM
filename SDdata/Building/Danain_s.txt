'''Danain's hardware shop''' was a store located on the [[Promenade (Suzail)|Promenade]] in [[Suzail]], the capital of [[Cormyr]] in the 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
Danain's store was nestled between the posh [[Gilded Feather]] club to the west and the [[Witch-Duke's Bride]], formerly known as the [[High Tankard]] tavern. The hardware shop stood on the southern side of the Promenade, three city blocks away from [[Cormyr's Royal Court]].{{cite web| url = https://web.archive.org/web/20171021205244/https://candlekeep.com/library/articles/sse/sse_101112-05.htm| title = So Saith Ed 2010-2016| accessdate = 25-11-2021| date = 25-11-2021}}

==History==
Danain's hardware shop could be found on the maps of Suzail as early as the {{YearlinkName|1365}}.
