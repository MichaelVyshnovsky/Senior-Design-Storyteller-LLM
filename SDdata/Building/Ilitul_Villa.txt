'''Ilitul Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Ilitul]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the northwest section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] at the intersection of the [[Street of Whispers]] and [[Ivory Street]]. The east side of the villa was also located on the [[Street of the Singing Dolphin]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
Ilitul Villa was a triangular-shaped wall estate that contained a separate building at each corner. The largest building was on the north side of the villa. Each building was two stories tall.
