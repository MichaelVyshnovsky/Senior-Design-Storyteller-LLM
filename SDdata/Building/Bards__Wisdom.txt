The Bards Wisdom was a private library located in Calimport.
The business was located in Yaraf Drudach of Nasim Sabban within Quill Ward.
The Bards Wisdom was a well-built, two-story building.
The library housed a collection of books on topics of interest to bards, such as travel, legends from other countries, epic songs, etc. It also served as a consortium of sages.
The business was owned and operated by Kamal el Kitaeb, who was also a druzir within the city.
