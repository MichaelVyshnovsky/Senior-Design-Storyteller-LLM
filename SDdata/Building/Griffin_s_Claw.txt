The '''Griffin's Claw''' was a tavern found in the city of [[Procampur]] in [[the Vast]].

==History==
In [[1362 DR|the Year of the Helm, 1362 DR]], the [[bard]] [[Talamic]] could be found entertaining here, singing such songs as the ''[[Lay of the Purple Dragons]]''.
