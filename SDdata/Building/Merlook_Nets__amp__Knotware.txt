'''Merlook Nets & Knotware''' was a shop located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building was one story tall.

==Location==
Merlook Nets & Knotware was ironically located on the south side of the eastern end of [[Net Street (south)|Net Street]] in the central section of the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]].
