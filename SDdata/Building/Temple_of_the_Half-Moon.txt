This temple was chiefly staffed by Alamarayne Moonray.
In 757 DR, the High Moonmistress of the temple Niree Swifthands was captured by forces from the Bright Ladys Tower and executed in Chancelgaunt for her membership in the New Moon Pact. An inscription commemorating her death was carved into the temple using both Thorass and Dethek scripts.
