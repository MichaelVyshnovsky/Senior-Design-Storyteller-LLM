The '''Bridge of the Straw Merchants''' was actually a pair of bridges located in the city of [[Dhaztanar]] in [[Semphar]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The bridges were located in the southern section of the [[Furdah Rustauk District]] in southern Dhaztanar near the shore of [[Gbor Nor]]. They provided crossings for foot traffic over a pair of canals flowing toward the [[Madinat]] in the north.
