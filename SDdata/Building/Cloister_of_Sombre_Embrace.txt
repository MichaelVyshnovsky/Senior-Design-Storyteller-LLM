The '''Cloister of Sombre Embrace''' was a [[Church of Shar|Sharran]] [[temple]] located beneath the [[Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
[[File:Cloister of Sombre Embrace entrance.jpg|thumb|250px|The entrance to the cloister from the [[House of Grief]].]]
The Sharrans operated a false house of healing on the surface called the [[House of Grief]] and used it as a cover. The actual structure of the cloister was hidden beneath the house to avoid public attention.
