The '''Tower of Al-Kalb''', or '''Tower of the Dog''',  was located within the great fortress of [[Sarahin]] in the [[Haunted Lands]].{{cite book/Assassin Mountain/Holy Slayer Sourcebook|30}}

==Description==
Grey stucco covered the outside of the tower.

==Inhabitants==
This tower was given to [[Akim al-Kalaas]] when he joined the [[Everlasting]]. His servants, [[genie]]s and apprentices lived in the tower with him.
