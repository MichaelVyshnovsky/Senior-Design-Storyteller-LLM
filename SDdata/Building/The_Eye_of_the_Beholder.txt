The '''Eye of the Beholder''' was [[jewelry]] shop located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The store was located in south Zhentil Keep in the western section of the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]] on [[Twilight Street]] and [[Center Street]]. 

==Activities==
In addition to providing gem-cutting services, the owner of the shop was quite skilled with working various precious metals as well.

==Inhabitants==
The Eye of the Beholder was owned by a [[dwarf]] named [[Artagalor Goldbeard]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}.
