The '''Temple of Isis''' was a temple dedicated to the worship of [[Isis]], the [[goddess]] of [[magic]] and fertility within the [[Mulhorandi pantheon]].{{Cite interactive atlas|490 Skuld}}

==Location==
The temple was located in the the west section of Skuld's [[Skuld/Crafts District|Crafts District]] on the east side of the city.
