Telethar Leatherworks was a leather goods store owned and operated by Orgul Telethar, leader of the League of Skinners & Tanners.
Telethar Leatherworks was located along the Dock Street,Both Waterdeep and the North and City of Splendors (boxed set) state that this building is located "off the Tower Trail," but maps clearly show it as being along the Dock Street. in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep.
This building was two-stories tall.
As their name implied, Telethar leatherworks sold leather goods, in the form of adventuring gear and clothing. These included baldrics, belts, scabbards, straps, and suspenders.
