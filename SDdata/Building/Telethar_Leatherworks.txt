'''Telethar Leatherworks''' was a [[leather]] goods store owned and operated by [[Orgul Telethar]], leader of the [[League of Skinners & Tanners]].

==Location==
Telethar Leatherworks was located along the [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]],<ref group="note" name="Street">Both ''[[Waterdeep and the North]]'' and ''[[City of Splendors (boxed set)]]'' state that this building is located "off the [[Tower Trail]]," but maps clearly show it as being along the [[Dock Street (Waterdeep)|Dock Street]]. in the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
This building was two-stories tall.

==Services==
As their name implied, Telethar leatherworks sold leather goods, in the form of adventuring gear and clothing. These included [[baldric]]s, [[belts]], scabbards, straps, and suspenders.
