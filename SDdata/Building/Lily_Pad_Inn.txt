The '''Lily Pad Inn''' was a three-story floating inn in the [[Xiousing]] district of [[Marsember]], owned by the [[Shou]] known as [[Shiau Chen]]. The inn was built on a floating platform on the large pond known as the Zhao. Chen ran a [[rickshaw]] service out of the inn.{{Cite dungeon/195/Backdrop: Xiousing}}

==Food==
Among other dishes, the inn served fried [[vegetable roll]]s, boiled [[dumpling]]s and [[duck]]-stuffed [[sweet bun]]s.

==Drink==
The inn offered hot [[tea]] free of charge to all its patrons.

==Entertainment==
The inn was the performance venue of the renowned Shou twins [[Ping Chiang|Ping]] and [[Ling Chiang]]. Ping played a string instrument known as the [[erhu]] and Ling performed a fan dance.
