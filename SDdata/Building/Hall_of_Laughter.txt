}}The '''Hall of Laughter''' was a temple devoted to [[Lliira]] in [[Melvaunt]] around [[1367 DR]].

==Location==
The temple was found in the southeast corner of the city.

==Description==
This light and airy temple was the newest house of worship as of 1367 DR. It was also the finest temple devoted to Lliira throughout the [[Moonsea]] region.
