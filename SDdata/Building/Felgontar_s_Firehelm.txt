'''The Firehelm''' was a dining house in [[Marsember]] in [[Cormyr]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The tavern was at the intersection of [[Gelguld Lane]] and [[Tarnsar Lane]].

==Services==
The large dining house was popular, but respectable.{{Cite web/Candlekeep Forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2004)/07-18 - Marsember, Part 3}}

==Rumors & Legends==
In early spring of the {{yearlinkName|1373}}, when [[Aumun Tholant Bezrar]] and [[Malakar Surth]] threatened their fellow merchant [[Durexter Dagohnlar]] and his wife, [[Starmara Dagohnlar]], Malakar threatened cutting off Starmara's toes and giving them to [[Ponczer]] at the Firehelm to cook something with them.
