The Firehelm was a dining house in Marsember in Cormyr in the late 14 century DR.
The tavern was at the intersection of Gelguld Lane and Tarnsar Lane.
The large dining house was popular, but respectable.
In early spring of the , when Aumun Tholant Bezrar and Malakar Surth threatened their fellow merchant Durexter Dagohnlar and his wife, Starmara Dagohnlar, Malakar threatened cutting off Starmaras toes and giving them to Ponczer at the Firehelm to cook something with them.
