The Bowels of the Earth was a tavern located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The tavern stood two stories tall.
thumb|A map showing the Bowels of the Earth (labeled #198) on Simples Street.|320x320px
The Bowels of the Earth was located in the central section of Waterdeeps Trades Ward on the southeast corner of where Simples Street joined Snail Street.
The tavern was a well-known spot for hiring mercenaries.
