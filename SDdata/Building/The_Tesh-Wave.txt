The '''Tesh-Wave''' was a tavern located in the city of [[Zhentil Keep]] during the midâ€“14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
The tavern was located in south Zhentil Keep near the waterfront of the [[Zhentil Keep/Foreign Quarter|Foreign Quarter]]. Its location outside of the main defensive wall provided it with a fantastic view of the [[River Tesh]] and the ruins of Zhentil Keep on the north bank.

==Description==
The signboard for the Tesh-Wave depicted a large wave on a river.

==Activities==
Adventuring groups eager to explore the ruins of the city launched their ventures from the Tesh-Wave. Captains of river boats and larger vessels from the [[Moonsea (lake)|Moonsea]] frequented the tavern, making it a great place to book passage.

==Inhabitants==
The Tesh-Wave was owned by a man named [[Koleth]] in the {{YearlinkName|1369}}. A trio of serving wenches worked for him.
