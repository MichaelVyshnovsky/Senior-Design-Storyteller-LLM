'''Drakon Tavern''' was a small but quality inn and tavern located in the [[Baldur's Gate/Manorborn|Manorborn]] district of the [[Baldur's Gate/Upper City|Upper City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]]. It was located immediately next to the [[Gist Estate|Gist family estate]].
{{quote|Careful you have too much of the drink. You don't want to be stumbling through Baldur's Gate without your wits in good order. So, would you like a drink?|Drakon Tavern's bartender}}

==Description==
A small stone building that housed Drakon Tavern had only one story but two entrances; both led into its spacious taproom. The tavern offered several notable libations to its guests: [[mead]], [[Westgate Ruby]], [[Golden Sands Brews]], [[Shadowdark ale]], [[Suz-ale]], and [[Old One Eye]] [[beer]]. It offered several types of accommodations, from a cheap common room, to luxurious private bedrooms.
