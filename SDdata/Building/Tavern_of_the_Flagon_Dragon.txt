The '''Tavern of the Flagon Dragon''' was a tavern in the [[Castle Ward of Waterdeep|Castle Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]], not far from [[Blackstaff Tower]].

==Location==
The two story tavern was located in the northern section of the Castle Ward on [[Darselune Street]] about a block from the [[Sleepy Slyph]].
