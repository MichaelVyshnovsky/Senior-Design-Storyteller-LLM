'''Hammerhand's Wagons and Woodwork''' was a wainwright and carpentry business owned by [[Hammerhand Bucko]] in [[Shadowdale]].

==History==
In [[1374 DR]], Hammerhand sold and repaired wheeled vehicles, such as [[wagon]]s. His staff members also worked on carpentry projects ranging from jewelry boxes to large houses.
