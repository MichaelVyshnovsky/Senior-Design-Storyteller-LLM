Hammerhands Wagons and Woodwork was a wainwright and carpentry business owned by Hammerhand Bucko in Shadowdale.
In 1374 DR, Hammerhand sold and repaired wheeled vehicles, such as wagons. His staff members also worked on carpentry projects ranging from jewelry boxes to large houses.
