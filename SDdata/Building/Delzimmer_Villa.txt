'''Delzimmer Villa''' was the home of the [[Delzimmer (family)|Delzimmer]] [[Nobility of Waterdeep|noble family]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood four stories tall. 

==Location==
Delzimmer Villa was located in the northwest section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on the northeast side of [[Swords Street]] across from [[Blackstaff Tower]].
