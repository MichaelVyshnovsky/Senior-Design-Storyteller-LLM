The '''Walking Tower of Warglend''' was a motile tower during the mid–8{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
As of {{yearlink|761}}, the Walking Tower was located near [[Tower Tor]].

==History==
In the {{yearlinkName|761}}, the sage [[Tabarast]] of [[Moonshorn Tower]] reported that, amongst other signs from the Gods, the Walking Tower of had Warglend moved for the first time in a thousand years, since approximately {{yearlink|-250}}, and left its place in Tower Tor, moving instead to the middle of a nearby lake.
