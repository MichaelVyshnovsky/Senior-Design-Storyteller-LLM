'''Gerin's Breads''' was a bakery in the city of [[Waterdeep]] in the late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
This bakery stood on the [[Seawind Alley]] in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] of Waterdeep.

==Structure==
This structure stood two stories tall.
