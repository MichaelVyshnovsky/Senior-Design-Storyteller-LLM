Mhaalauvurs was a jug store in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.
This building stood at the northwestern corner of The Boomgarden, where it intersected with Fillet Lane.
Mhaalauvurs sold a wide variety of liquids within jug vessels the size of a human head. These included axle grease, cooking oils, scented washing soap flakes, slicewash (liquid disinfectants), and droot.
