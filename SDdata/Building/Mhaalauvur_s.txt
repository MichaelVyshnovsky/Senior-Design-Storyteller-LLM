'''Mhaalauvur's''' was a [[jug]] store in the [[Waterdeep/Southern Ward|Southern Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Location==
This building stood at the northwestern corner of [[The Boomgarden]], where it intersected with [[Fillet Lane]].

==Services==
Mhaalauvur's sold a wide variety of liquids within jug vessels the size of a [[human]] head. These included axle grease, cooking [[oil]]s, scented washing [[soap]] flakes, [[slicewash]] (liquid disinfectants), and [[droot]].
