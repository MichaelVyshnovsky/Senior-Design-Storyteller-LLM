The '''Theater of Joy''' was an old amphitheater complex repurposed as a temple dedicated to [[Lliira]], [[Milil]] and [[Sune]], located in the [[Crimmor]].

==Location==
The temple was built on the street known as [[The Dausann]], and was located next to the southern city walls.<ref group="note" name="temple">The temple is explicitly referred as being on the north end of the city, but all published maps contradicts that information.

==Structure==
The temple is composed of an amphitheater used for services, and of a smaller building storing divine reliquaries.

==Services==
By tradition and mutual agreements, the three clergies sharing the temple didn't officiate simultaneously.
* From dawn until highsun, the temple held services dedicated to [[Sune]].
* From highsun until dusk, services were dedicated to [[Milil]], including concerts.
* At night, the temple was dedicated to [[Lliira]].

At special occasions, such as when caravanning season began, the temple held huge parties dedicated to [[Waukeen]] in addition to its three regular deities. On such occasions, [[Lliira]] aspect as the Regent of the Merchant's Friend was celebrated.

==History==
The amphitheater was built by the [[Shoon Imperium]].

==Inhabitants==
The temple was inhabited by many [[priest]]s:
* [[Chynna Crytrapper]], a priestess of Lliira.
* [[Tahrun Harpson|Tahrun]] and [[Shyrros Harpson]], respectively bard and cleric, followers of Milil.
* [[Raven Sommersrace]], a charismatic priestess of Sune.
