'''Falthryn and Daughters Tannery''' was a workshop in [[Lannort]] in the 15{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Services==
The Tannery produced cured hides and [[leather]], the reason for it being outside and downstream of [[Loudwater]]. It was linked by [[merchants' map]] to [[Samril's Fine Blades]] in [[Secomber]], who used the products for hilt wrappings on weapons such [[longsword]]s.
