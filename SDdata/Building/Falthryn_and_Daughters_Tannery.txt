Falthryn and Daughters Tannery was a workshop in Lannort in the 15 century DR.
The Tannery produced cured hides and leather, the reason for it being outside and downstream of Loudwater. It was linked by merchants map to Samrils Fine Blades in Secomber, who used the products for hilt wrappings on weapons such longswords.
