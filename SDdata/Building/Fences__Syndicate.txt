The '''Fences' Syndicate''' was, for all intents and purposes, the guildhall for fences and other members of the black market within [[Daggerford]] in the [[Western Heartlands]] in the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].{{Cite interactive atlas|3990 Daggerford}}

==Location==
The guildhall was located at the main entrance into Daggerford through the [[Caravan Gate]]. It sat next to the [[Daggerford Inn]] on [[Wall Street (Daggerford)|Wall Street]] and [[Rogar Alley]].

==Activities==
Anyone wishing to do business within the guildhall was subjected to a thorough background check by a variety of [[magic item]]s or [[spell]]s.
