The Full Sails was a tavern that doubled as the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Most Diligent League of Sail-Makers and Cordwainers.
This building was located along Dock Street, in the Dock Ward of Waterdeep. It stood to the right of the residence of Arnagus the Shipwright.
This building stood three-stories tall.
The Full Sails was among some of the more notable and recognizable buildings in the Dock Ward, considered by locals to be a landmark.
