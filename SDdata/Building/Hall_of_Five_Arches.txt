The Hall of Five Arches, also referred to as the Tall Hall to its enormous size, was the guildhall of the Builders Guild in the city of Ravens Bluff.
This building was located along Moorland Ride, in the Sixstar neighborhood of the citys Uptown District.
This large building stood a full six stories tall Its overall architectural style was what those on some worlds would refer to as baroque.
The massive entrance to the building was met with a large array of high buttressed arches
