The '''Thayan Embassy''' was an [[Thayan enclave]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building stood four stories tall.

==Location==
The Thayan Embassy was located in the northwest section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] at the intersection of [[Trader's Way (Waterdeep)|Trader's Way]], [[the Sutherlane]], [[Mendever Street]], [[Heard Lane]], and [[Bazaar Street]].

==Services==
Various suits of [[armor]], weapons, [[magic item]]s, [[potion]]s, and [[scroll]]s could be purchased here.
