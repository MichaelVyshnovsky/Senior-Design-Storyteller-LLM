Tymoras Holy Hall of Good Fortune and Casino was a gambling establishment located in the city of Zhentil Keep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR before that citys destruction. The hall also served as a shrine to Tymora, the goddess of luck.
The casino was located in Zhentil Keeps Foreign Quarter in the section of the city located on the south bank of the River Tesh. It was located near the center of the quarter along Zhent Avenue.
Rare among the various gambling dens of Zhentil Keep, Tymoras Holy Hall of Good Fortune and Casino always played fair. The business did not extend any lines of credit and any payouts were usually modest.
