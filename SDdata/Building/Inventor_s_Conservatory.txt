The '''Inventor's Conservatory''' was a prominent magic school in [[Eileanar]] that operated until its destruction in the {{YearlinkName|-339}}.

==Services==
The college was focused on the [[inventive]] field of magical studies. The Dean of Teachers, [[Arconi]], oversaw and taught alongside [[Belair]], [[Dolci]], [[Koler]], [[Ballarini]], [[Agassaz]], [[Staufehaus]], and [[Clayton]].
