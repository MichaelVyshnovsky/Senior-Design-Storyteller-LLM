The Guild Paddock was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Stablemasters & Farriers Guild.
The Guild Paddock was located along Walltower Walk, in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep.
This guildhall stood two stories tall.
Mounts and gear related to them, such as bits, bridles, and saddles, could be purchased here.
Like other guildhalls, the Guild Paddock was considered by locals to be a landmark.
