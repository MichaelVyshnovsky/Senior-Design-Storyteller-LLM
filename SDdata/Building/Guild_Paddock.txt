The '''Guild Paddock''' was the guildhall of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Stablemasters' & Farriers' Guild]].<ref>

==Location==
The Guild Paddock was located along [[Walltower Walk]], in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]].

==Structure==
This guildhall stood two stories tall.

==Services==
[[Mount]]s and gear related to them, such as [[bit and bridle|bits, bridles]], and [[saddle]]s, could be purchased here.

==Reputation==
Like other guildhalls, the Guild Paddock was considered by locals to be a landmark.
