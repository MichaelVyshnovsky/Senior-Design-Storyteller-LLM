Waendels Wharf was one of the wharves located in Lower City of Baldurs Gate.
Waendels Wharf was the southern end of The Eastern Wet, south of Athcaulyrs Stand. Waendels Wharf was a thin dock with three legs that protruded from the border of the Brampton district into the Gray Harbor.
