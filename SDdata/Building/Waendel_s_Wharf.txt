'''Waendel's Wharf''' was one of the wharves located in [[Baldur's Gate/Lower City|Lower City]] of [[Baldur's Gate]].

==Location==
Waendel's Wharf was the southern end of [[The Eastern Wet]], south of [[Athcaulyr's Stand]]. Waendel's Wharf was a thin dock with three legs that protruded from the border of the [[Baldur's Gate/Brampton|Brampton]] district into the [[Gray Harbor]].
