The '''Glasscrafter's Hall''' was a guildhall found in [[Yhaunn]] in [[1358 DR]].

==Locations==
The Glasscrafter's Hall was located near the center of the city.

==Description==
This spired building held many fine furnishings and was a visual landmark within Yhaunn.
