'''Erladden Towers''' was a grand festhall found in the city of [[Myth Drannor]] ''circa'' the 7{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Location==
It was situated on the  [[Street of Song]] in the [[Myth Drannor/Kerradunath|Kerradunath]] district of the city.

==Structure==
The two-story building was built incorporating one of the city's great trees, extending upward one story from street-level.

==Services==
The venue hosted a number of galas and revels each and every [[tenday]], keeping on hand enough food, drink, and diversionary entertainment to accommodate 1000 guests a night for an entire month.
