Erladden Towers was a grand festhall found in the city of Myth Drannor circa the 7 century DR.
It was situated on the  Street of Song in the Kerradunath district of the city.
The two-story building was built incorporating one of the citys great trees, extending upward one story from street-level.
The venue hosted a number of galas and revels each and every tenday, keeping on hand enough food, drink, and diversionary entertainment to accommodate 1000 guests a night for an entire month.
