The Old Guildhall was the guildhall of the Waterdhavian guild known as the Cellarers & Plumbers Guild.
This guildhall was located along Gaustus Street, in the Trades Ward of Waterdeep. 
This guildhall stood three-stories tall.
The Old Guildhall was among some of the more notable and recognizable guildhalls in the Trades Ward. Like other guildhalls in Waterdeep, this building was considered by locals to be a landmark.
