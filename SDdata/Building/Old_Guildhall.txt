The '''Old Guildhall''' was the guildhall of the [[Guilds of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian guild]] known as the [[Cellarers' & Plumbers' Guild]].

==Location==
This guildhall was located along [[Gaustus Street]], in the [[Waterdeep/Trades Ward|Trades Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]]. 

==Structure==
This guildhall stood three-stories tall.

==Reputation==
The Old Guildhall was among some of the more notable and recognizable guildhalls in the Trades Ward. Like other guildhalls in Waterdeep, this building was considered by locals to be a landmark.
