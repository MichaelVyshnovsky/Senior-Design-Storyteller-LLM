'''Talnu's Ropeworks''' was a shop located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The building was two stories tall.

==Location==
Talnu's Ropeworks was located in the central section of the [[Waterdeep/Dock Ward|Dock Ward]] on the south side of [[Adder Lane]] across from the [[Rearing Hippocampus]].

==Activities==
The shop specialized in the sale of [[rope]]s and other gear suitable for [[adventurer]]s.
