The Saddlers & Harness-Makers Hall, also known simply as the Saddlers Hall, was the headquarters of the Waterdhavian guild of the same name.
This guildhall was located on Tulmasters Street, in the Southern Ward of Waterdeep.
This guildhall stood two-stories tall.
Tack such bits, bridles and saddles could be purchased here.
Like other guildhalls in the Southern Ward, this building was used as a landmark by locals.
