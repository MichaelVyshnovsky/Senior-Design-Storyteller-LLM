The Hall of Luck was a small, fortified temple of Tymora in the small town of Glister.
This temple stood on the north of the main street of Glister, near the towns west wall.
In the 14 century DR, the Hall of Luck was not as prosperous as the other temples in Glister, but it was well-to-do enough thanks to its popularity.
In the 14th century DR, this temple was inhabited by sixteen brothers and eleven priests. They were led by the High Priestess Ssaluntra Halamar.
