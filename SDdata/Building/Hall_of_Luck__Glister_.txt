The '''Hall of Luck''' was a small, fortified temple of [[Tymora]] in the small town of [[Glister]].

==Location==
This temple stood on the north of the main street of Glister, near the town's west wall.

==History==
In the 14{{th}} century [[DR]], the Hall of Luck was not as prosperous as the other temples in Glister, but it was well-to-do enough thanks to its popularity.

==Inhabitants==
In the 14th{{th}} century DR, this temple was inhabited by sixteen brothers and eleven [[priest]]s. They were led by the High Priestess [[Ssaluntra Halamar]].
