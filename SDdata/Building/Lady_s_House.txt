The Ladys House was the temple of Tymora in the Cormyrean city of Arabel.
As of the , the Ladys House was overseen by the overbearing High Hand of the Lady Darmos Lauthyr.
The avatar of Tymora herself appeared at the temple the Time of Troubles in the . She led a group of her faithful against a cleric they declared a heretic, when he caused great destruction by inadvertently conjuring a fireball when attempting to use healing magic on a wounded soldier. 
Over time the temple was rebuilt and gained many followers due to the presence of the Luck Maiden. Priest Lauthyr became quite demanding during this time of increased popularity among the Arabellan citizens, doubling the fees the temple imposed upon its faithful and demanding lordship from King Azoun IV.
During the time of its increased popularity the temple boasted two dozen priests and over 300 Tymoran devotees.
