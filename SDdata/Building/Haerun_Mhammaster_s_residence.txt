'''Haerun Mhammaster's residence''' was a row house and the home of [[Haerun Mhammaster]] located in [[Waterdeep]] during the mid-to-late 14{{th}} century [[DR]].

==Description==
The home stood three stories tall. The home was equiped with an impressive library. 

==Location==
Haerun Mhammaster's residence was located in the eastern section of Waterdeep's [[Waterdeep/Castle Ward|Castle Ward]] on the east side of the [[Street of Bells]].
