Haerun Mhammasters residence was a row house and the home of Haerun Mhammaster located in Waterdeep during the mid-to-late 14 century DR.
The home stood three stories tall. The home was equiped with an impressive library. 
Haerun Mhammasters residence was located in the eastern section of Waterdeeps Castle Ward on the east side of the Street of Bells.
