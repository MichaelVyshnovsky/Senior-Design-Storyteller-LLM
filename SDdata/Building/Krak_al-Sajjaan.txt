Krak al-Sajjaan was a qalat found in the Cities of the Ancients region of east Zakhara in 1367 DR.
This fortress was found toward the southern region of al-Sayaj Mountains. It stood approximately  slightly northwest of Kadarasto on the outskirts of the jungle.
Krak al-Sajjaan had a commanding view of the grasslands around the southern arm of the Nogaro River. It was unoccupied as of 1367 DR.
