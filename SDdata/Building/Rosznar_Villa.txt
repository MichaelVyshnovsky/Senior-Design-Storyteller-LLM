'''Rosznar Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Rosznar]].

==Location==
The villa was located in the northeast section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] in between [[Copper Street]] and [[Shield Street]] on [[Thunderstaff Way]] at the eastern end of [[Wrightstone Street]]{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
Four buildings were found within the walled estate with gardens separating them. Each building stood two or five stories tall.
