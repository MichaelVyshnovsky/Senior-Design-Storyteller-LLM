Zun Villa was the family estate of the Waterdhavian noble house Zun.
The villa was located in the east section of Waterdeeps North Ward. It sat on the eastern end of Tarsars Street on Saerdoun Street. The villa also touched the Cliffwatch just south of Endcliff Tower.
The walled compound contained two buildings located adjacent to each other. The buildings were two and four storys tall.
