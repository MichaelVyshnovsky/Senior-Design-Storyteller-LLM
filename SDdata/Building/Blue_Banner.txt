The '''Blue Banner''' was a [[tavern]] and [[inn]] located in [[Westgate|Westgate's]] [[Shou Quarter]]. The inn's growing popularity forced the owner to expand several times. 

==Location==
The Blue Banner was found in the south section of the city at the end of [[East Market Street]].
