The Planar Church of the Seven Heavens was a temple in the city of Bral, the sole city on the surface of the Rock of Bral, dedicated to the planar church of the same name.
This temple was located in the Temple District of the Middle City of Bral, across the road from the Planar Church of Olympus.
This temple was established sometime in the 14 century DR, being a newcomer among the faiths of Bral.
