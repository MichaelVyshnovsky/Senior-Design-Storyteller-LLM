'''Raventree Villa''', also known as '''Raventowers''',{{Cite web/Candlekeep forum/Questions for Ed Greenwood (2013)/Raventree Servants, Part I}} was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Raventree]].

==Location==
The villa was located at the corner of [[Sul Street]] and [[Chasso's Trot]] in the northeast section of [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]].{{Cite interactive atlas|Waterdeep}}

==Description==
Raventree Villa was composed of several structures reaching two and three storeys in height. The walled estate dominated much of the city block where is was located.
