'''Yi Woo's Clinic''' was a healing center located in the [[Xiousing]] district in [[Marsember]]. It was run by the [[Shou]] healer known as [[Yi Woo]]. The clinic would treat anyone, Shou or [[Cormyr|Cormyrean]], regardless of ability to pay.[[Dan Anderson]]. "Backdrop: Xiousing." ''[[Dungeon (magazine)|Dungeon]]'' #195. Renton, WA: Wizards of the Coast, October 2011.
