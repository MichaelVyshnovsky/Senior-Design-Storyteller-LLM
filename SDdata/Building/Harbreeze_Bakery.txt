The Harbreeze Bakery was a beloved eatery of Baldurs Gate, located within the marketplace of the Upper City during the late 15 century DR. It was run by the red-headed Ellyn Harbreeze, a woman who garnered as much knowledge as some information brokers.
The bakery offered such favorite delicacies as cinnamon cookies and full loaves of sugarbread highlighted by the flavors of foreign teas and spices. Their real commodity however was in information, specifically that regarding the social lives of the bakerys clientele.
It was one of the favorite mid-day stops for gossipy Baldurian patriar.
