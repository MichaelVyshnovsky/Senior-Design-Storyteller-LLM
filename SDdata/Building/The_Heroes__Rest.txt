The '''Heroes' Rest''', which was also called the '''Rest'''

==Location==
The inn was located in central Zhentil Keep within the southeast section of the [[Zhentil Keep/Inner Quarter|Inner Quarter]].

==Activities==
The Heroes' Rest was a noisy place but quite comfortable if you could get past all the raucous. The interior accommodations were good and pricing was moderate. The inn was owned by the [[Zhentarim]] who made notes of anyone passing through the establishment.
