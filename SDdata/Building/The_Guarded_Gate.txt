The '''Guarded Gate''' was the mint and treasury for the city of [[Ordulin]] in the {{YearlinkName|1358}}.

==Location==
The Guarded Gate was located in the center of the city.

==Description==
The entire structure was designed to thwart magical [[scrying]] or entry.

==Defenses==
Potent [[golem]]s and other magical constructions protected the Guarded Gate from intruders. A family of loyal [[wizard]]s lived within the structure to monitor all activity.
