'''Eltorchul Villa''' was the family estate of the [[Nobility of Waterdeep|Waterdhavian noble house]] [[Eltorchul]] and the home of the [[Eltorchul Academy]].

==Location==
The Eltorchul Villa was located in the [[Waterdeep/Sea Ward|Sea Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]], 

==Structure==
This building complex consisted of two high-quality buildings, one that was three stories tall and another that stood a single story tall,Evidence of this information can be seen in how the villa is depicted on maps.

===Interior===
One of the buildings of this estate housed a quite impressive library with a great deal of [[magic]]al information, rivaled only by those of [[Maskar Wands]] and [[Khelben Arunsun]].

==History==
In the early 14{{th}} century [[DR]], inheriting the fortune his family had garnered over the years through imports and [[spices]], Lord [[Thesp Eltorchul]] began to run a school for magic out of this family villa entitled Eltorchul Academy.
