'''Kraanfhaor's Door''' was an ancient [[elf|elven]] magic college in [[Talthalaran]] on the [[High Moor]]. It was one of the most ancient colleges, dating to millennia before the [[Descent]] of the [[drow]]. In the {{YearlinkName|1377}}, [[Q'arlynd Melarn]] found a clue to its location inside a [[kiira (disambiguation)|''kiira'']] found in the High Moor.
